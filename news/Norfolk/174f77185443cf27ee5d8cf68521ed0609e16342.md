Three teenagers arrested in connection with bike thefts in Thetford have been charged.

In November, local officers attended a property and recovered four stolen bikes. A teenage boy was arrested at the time and released on bail while enquiries continued.

Two further arrests were made as the investigation continued and three teenagers have now been charged with offences.

A 14-year-old boy is accused of four counts of theft between 17 September and 6 October incidents in Thetford.

A 15-year-old boy is accused of stealing two bikes in Thetford on 17 September and in Attleborough on 22 September

A 16-year-old boy is accused of three counts of thefts, in Thetford on 6 October.

The teenagers, who cannot be named for legal reasons, have been released on bail and will appear at Norwich Youth Court on Thursday 28 January 2021.
