Police can confirm that the body of a man has been discovered in Tibenham.

Officers were called to a field close to Low Road, at 12:40pm this afternoon (Thursday 31 December 2020).

A post-mortem examination will take place in due course to establish the identity of the man and the cause of death, which is currently being treated as unexplained, but is not believed to be suspicious.

While formal identification is yet to take place, the man is believed to be Anthony Hibble, who was reported missing yesterday (Wednesday 30 December). His next of kin have been informed.
