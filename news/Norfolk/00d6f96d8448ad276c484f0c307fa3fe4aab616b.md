Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious incident on Magpie Road yesterday morning (Tuesday 15 December 2020).

Officers were called at 10:48am to reports that a cyclist had fallen from his bike.

The cyclist, a man in his late 40s, was taken to the Norwich and Norfolk University with serious head injuries.

Officers are keen to hear from witnesses to the incident, or anyone who might have dash cam footage from the area at the time.

The cyclist is described as a white male, who would have been riding a grey bicycle, wearing a green fleece.

Anyone with information should contact Sergeant Dan Taylor at Bethel Street Police Station on **101** , quoting call 98 from 15th December.
