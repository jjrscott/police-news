Officers are appealing for witnesses following a boat theft in Cromer.

A shallow depth 1880s Cockling boat was stolen from outside a property on Brook Street between approximately 1pm and 3pm on Sunday 18 October.

Suspect(s) are believed to have made off in a blue or grey Ford Transit Van.

Officers are appealing for witnesses or anyone with any information concerning the incident to come forward. They are particularly keen to hear from anyone who may have seen the van in the area during the times stated.

Anyone with any information should contact PC Paul Bassham on the Broadsbeat Team on 101 quoting reference number 36/74117/20.
Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555111.
