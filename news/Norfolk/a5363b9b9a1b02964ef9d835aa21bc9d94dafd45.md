Police are appealing for information after the attempted theft of lead from a summer house roof.

The incident happened sometime between 11:30pm on Friday (27 November 2020), and 11am on Saturday (28 November).

An unknown suspect(s) forced entry into a secure, enclosed rear garden, at a property on Fox Road, Framingham Pigot.

It is believed that an unsuccessful attempt was made to remove lead from the summer house roof, after a chain and lock were cut to gain entry to the garden.

Anyone who may have seen suspicious activity in the area at the time, or anyone with information is encouraged to contact PC Mandy Shreeve at Norfolk Constabulary on 101, quoting crime number 36/83878/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555111.
