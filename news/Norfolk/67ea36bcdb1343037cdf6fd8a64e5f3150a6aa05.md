Thieves have stolen equipment belonging to the 1st Aylsham Scout Group.

A ride on mower, box trailer and an Ifor Williams trailer, were stolen from a field off Cawston Road, near to Woodgate Nursery, between 12pm on Sunday 18 October and 12pm on Friday 23 October 2020.

Anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area between the dates stated, or anyone with information, should contact PC Lucas Ward at Aylsham Police Station on **101** quoting reference number 36/75040/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111**.
