Motorists are advised of potential delays on the road over the coming days as they escort the following abnormal loads through the county.

**23 March**
Police will be escorting a boat, 4.8m wide and 22m in length from Gaymers Way, North Walsham to Falmouth. Leaving at 12.00 on the following route: Local Roads - B1145 - A149 - B1152 - A1064 - A47 - A140 - A14 - A12 Essex border.

Norfolk Police escort to A12 Copdock.

**24 March**
Police will be escorting a boat, 5.6m wide and 27m in length from Gaymers Way, North Walsham to Southampton. Leaving at 10.00 on the following route: Local roads - Gaymers Way - Folgate Rd - B1145 - A149 - B1152 - A1064 - A47 - A140 - A14 Cambs Border.

**27 March**

Police will be escorting vessels, 3.3m wide and 21m in length from Wymondham Rd, Wrenigham to Lincoln. Leaving at 10.00 on the following route: Local roads - Wymondham Rd- Silfield St - Silfield Rd - The Street - Wymondham Rd - B1113 - A140 - A11 - A47 - A17 Lincs.

Norfolk Police escort convoy from Wymondham Rd to A11 Thickthonre.
