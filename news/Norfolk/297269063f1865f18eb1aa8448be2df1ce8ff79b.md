Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision in Great Yarmouth in the early hours of this morning (Tuesday 2 March).

Officers were called to North Drive at approximately 3am following reports that a red Suzuki motorcycle had collided with a lamp post.

The rider of the motorcycle, a 17-year-old man, sadly died at the scene.

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or has any dashcam footage to come forward.

Anyone with information should contact the Serious Collision Investigation Unit on **101** or via email: [email protected] quoting incident number 26 of 2 March 2021.

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers 0800 555 111.
