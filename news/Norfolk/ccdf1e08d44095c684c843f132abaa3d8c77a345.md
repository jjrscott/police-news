Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision on the A1066, Thetford Road yesterday (Monday 16 November 2020).

Officers were called just after 5:30pm to reports of a collision between a green VW Golf, and a grey Skoda Octavia.

The incident took place between Rushford and Thetford, within a temporary speed limit stretch of road.

Both male drivers sustained serious head and chest injuries, and were taken to hospital for treatment.

Officers are keen to hear from witnesses to the incident, or anyone who might have seen the manner of driving of either vehicle.
