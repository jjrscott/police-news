Police are appealing for witnesses or dashcam footage in relation to a serious collision between a car and cyclist on Saturday afternoon (11 July 2020) on the A148 near the village of Aylmerton.

The collision happened at 4.35pm when a dark blue Peugeot 207 and a black pedal cycle collided on Holt Road.

The road was closed and the rider of the pedal cycle was taken to Addenbrookes hospital by air ambulance where he remains in a critical condition.

If anyone witnessed the collision or the manner of driving or riding near to the time of the collision, please contact PC Aaron Duffy on 101 quoting incident reference NC-11072020-322.
