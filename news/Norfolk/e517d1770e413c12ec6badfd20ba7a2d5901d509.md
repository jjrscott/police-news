Police have discovered the body of a man near Bawburgh Lakes.

Officers found the man inside a van at around 5.50pm on Thursday 25 February 2021.

Whilst formal identification is yet to take place, the family of James Gray, aged 55, who has been missing from Norwich since Monday 22 February, have been informed.

The death is being treated us unexplained; however, it is not believed to be suspicious and a file will be prepared for the coroner ahead of an inquest which will take place in due course.
