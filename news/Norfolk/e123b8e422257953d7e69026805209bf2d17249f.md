Four people have been charged in connection with supplying Class A drugs in Norwich.

Officers from the Norfolk Police County Lines Team executed warrants at four separate addresses in the Norwich area on Tuesday 23 March 2021,

Mobile phones, cash and crack cocaine was seized at a property in The Denes.

Mobile phones, cash, and a large amount of crack cocaine and heroin were seized from an address in Rose Drive in Cringleford.

Meanwhile mobile phones were seized from properties in Glenmore Gardens and Blackhorse Opening.

Seven people were arrested in total and four people have since been charged with conspiracy to supply Class A drugs between June 2020 and March 2021. They are:

Christopher MORRISON, aged 33 and of The Denes in Norwich.
Amenemhet ASANTE, aged 23 and of Rose Drive in Cringleford.
Tessa BOLAN, aged 28, of Blackhorse Opening in Norwich.
Alex WILLIAMS, aged 20, of no fixed address.

All four have been remanded into custody and were due to appear at Norwich Magistrate’s Court today (Wednesday 24 March 2021).

The three other people arrested – two men and a woman – have been released under investigation whilst enquiries are ongoing.
