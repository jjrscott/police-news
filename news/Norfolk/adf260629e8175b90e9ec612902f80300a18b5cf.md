Detectives in Norwich investigating a serious assault have released CCTV images of two men they would like to trace.

It follows an incident in Orford Place outside Debenhams on Thursday 19 September when the victim, a man in his 40s, was punched by another man, suffering a broken jaw.

Officers are keen to identify the men pictured in the CCTV images who may be able to assist officers with their enquiries.

Anyone with information should contact Detective Constable Bob-Walker Hall at Norwich CID on 101 quoting crime reference 36/65979/20.
