A man has been jailed for 20 weeks for assaulting a police officer.

Martin Bamford, aged 34 and of Trafalgar Court, Mundesley, appeared at Norwich Magistrate’s Court on Monday 9 November, charged with assaulting an emergency worker, driving whilst disqualified, driving without insurance and failing to provide a specimen.

The court heard how Bamford headbutted a police officer whilst being escorted at the Aylsham Police Investigation Centre on Saturday 7 November. The officer received minor injuries.

The incident happened following Bamford’s arrest for the driving offences on Cromer Road in Mundesley at approximately 5.15pm.

Bamford pleaded guilty to the charges and was sentenced to 20 weeks in prison.
