Police are appealing for witnesses following an aggravated burglary in Norwich yesterday (Monday 15 March).

The incident happened at approximately 12.50pm at a property on Clover Road. Three unknown suspects used weapons to force entry into the property causing significant damage. After entering the property, they fled the scene.

No one was injured as a result of the incident and enquiries to trace the suspects are ongoing.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the incident, or has any information concerning it. They are particularly keen to speak with anyone who may have dashcam or CCTV footage of the area during the times stated.

Anyone with information should contact DC Sarah Payne at Norwich CID on **101** , quoting crime reference number 36/16455/21.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
