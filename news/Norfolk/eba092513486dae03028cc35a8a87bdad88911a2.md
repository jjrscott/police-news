Police are appealing for witnesses or information after a car failed to stop after colliding with a garden wall in Gorleston overnight on Monday 21 December 2020.

The incident happened sometime between 9pm on Monday 21 December 2020 and 7.30am Tuesday 22 December 2020 at a property in Brasenose Avenue in Gorleston when the car hit a garden wall and summerhouse causing damage.

The car involved is believed to be a silver or grey Hyundai with damage to the front as car parts were left at the scene.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, has information or dashcam footage from the area at the time is asked to contact PC Ben Chard at Norfolk Constabulary on **101** quoting crime reference NC-22122020-86. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
