Police are appealing for witnesses after football pitches in Dersingham were damaged yesterday (Tuesday 16 February 2021).

It is believed the incident happened around 7.30am when suspects riding mopeds tore up the pitch, causing damage.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the incident or noticed anyone on mopeds in the area around the time stated.

Anyone with information should contact PC Glen Barnes at Hunstanton Police Station on **101** quoting incident number 38 of 16 February.
