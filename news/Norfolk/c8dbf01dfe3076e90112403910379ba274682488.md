A man has died following a collision on the A148 at Hillington which happened in the early of hours of this morning (Friday 15 January 2021).

Police were called at about 2.20am following reports a blue Audi A4 estate had crashed into a wall. The driver, a man in his 20s, was sadly pronounced dead at the scene and three other passengers, two men and a woman, suffered slight injuries.

Emergency services attended and the road remains closed this morning while scene investigations are carried out. It is likely to be closed until midday.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the collision, or has information concerning the driving manner of the Audi prior to the crash. Anyone with information should contact the Serious Collision Investigation Unit at Wymondham on 101.
