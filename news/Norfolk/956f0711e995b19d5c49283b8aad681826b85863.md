Police investigating a burglary at a derelict pub in Great Yarmouth have released CCTV footage.

It follows an incident at the Suspension Bridge, on Bridge Road, which happened on 11 September when a man entered the pub and stole two large wooden barrels and a hard drive.

Officers have released video footage captured by CCTV and are keen to hear from anyone who may recognise the man pictured.

Anyone with information should contact PC Richard Bennett at Op Solve on **101** quoting crime reference 36/65179/20.
