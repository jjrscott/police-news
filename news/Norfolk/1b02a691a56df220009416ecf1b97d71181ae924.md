Police are appealing for witnesses after a business premises in Great Yarmouth was damaged.

A number of windows on the Pizza Hut building on Gapton Hall Road were smashed at approximately 10.45pm on Wednesday 18 November 2020.

Anyone who may have witnessed the incident, or anyone with information, should contact PC Adrian Young at Gorleston Police Station on 101 quoting reference number 36/81567/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
