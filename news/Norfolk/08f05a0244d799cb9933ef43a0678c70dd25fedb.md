Police can confirm the deaths of a man and woman in Derheam are not being treated as suspicious.

Emergency services were called to a property in Becclesgate on Thursday 5 November around 12pm after concerns had been raised for the welfare of a couple.

The bodies of a man and woman, aged in their 70s and 80s, were found inside the property.

Post mortem examinations held last week established cause of death was related to natural causes.

A file will be prepared for the coroner in due course.
