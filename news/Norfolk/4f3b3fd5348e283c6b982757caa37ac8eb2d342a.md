Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Beeston Regis near Sheringham.

The incident happened overnight on Monday between 5pm and 7am on Tuesday (5 and 6 October 2020) when suspect(s) broke into a work yard on Britons Lane.

Offender(s) went on to break into four sheds stealing about 1,000 litres of red diesel and a large number of electrical tools and plant equipment worth several thousand pounds.

Police are keen to hear from anyone who may have seen people or vehicles at the site between the times stated, or may have been offered stolen items for sale.

Anyone with information should contact PC Charlotte Lilley quoting crime reference 36/70205/20 at Cromer Police Station on **101** or call Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
