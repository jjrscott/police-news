Police are appealing for information following a robbery in Norwich.

The victim, a man in his 50s, was on Marriotts Way at approximately 9.20pm on Sunday 18 October, when he was approached by two men who asked from some money.

One of the suspects was holding a knife at the time of the incident but both men made off when the victim waved his coat at them.

Both suspects are described as white, aged in their 20s and wearing dark clothing with dark face coverings.

Anyone who may have witnessed the incident, or anyone with information, should contact PC Katie Powell at Bethel Street Police Station on **101** quoting reference number 36/73628/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111**.
