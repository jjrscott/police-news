A 35-year-old man arrested in connection with a fatal collision in Swannington in June 2018 has been released under investigation.

The suspect was arrested in the Coltishall area this morning (Tuesday 20 October 2020) on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving.

He was questioned at Wymondham Police Investigation Centre and released while enquiries continue.

The collision happened on 7 June 2018 when a cyclist Sze-Ming Cheung, aged 44, was killed.

Following Mr Cheung’s inquest in July this year, the case was further reviewed with additional enquiries leading to today’s arrest.

## 20/10/2020 | 12:30

Police can confirm a 35-year-old man has been arrested in connection with a fatal collision in Swannington which happened in June 2018.

The man was arrested in the Colitshall area this morning (Tuesday 20 October 2020) on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving and taken to Wymondham Police Investigation Centre where he will be questioned.

The collision happened on 7 June 2018 when a cyclist Sze-Ming Cheung, aged 44, was killed.

Following Mr Cheung’s inquest in July this year, the case was further reviewed with additional enquiries leading to today’s arrest.
