The body of a man has been discovered on Gorleston beach.

Police were called at 10.25am on Saturday 30 January after a member of the public found the body on the shore line.

Whilst formal identification procedures are yet to take place, the family of 44-year-old Mark Bland, who was reported missing in Gorleston on Friday 29 January, have been informed.

The death is not being treated as suspicious and a file will be prepared for the coroner ahead of an inquest which will take place in due course.
