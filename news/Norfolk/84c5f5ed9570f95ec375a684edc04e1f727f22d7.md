Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Bracon Ash.

A ride-on lawnmower and a motorcycle were stolen from a property in Long Lane between 5pm on Saturday 5 December and 11am on Sunday 6 December 2020.

The motorcycle has since been recovered in Cringleford.

Anyone who may have witnessed any suspicious activity in the area between the times stated, or anyone with information, should contact PC Dave Johnson in the Op Solve Team on **101** quoting reference number 36/85778/20.
