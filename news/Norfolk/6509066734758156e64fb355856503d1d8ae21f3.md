Police can confirm a woman died in a collision on the A140 near Diss yesterday (Tuesday 15 December 2020).

It happened at about 11.25am at Dickleburgh when a blue Vauxhall Corsa, travelling towards Norwich, mounted the roundabout and overturned before coming to rest in a ditch.

The driver, a woman in her 80s from the local area, was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Emergency services attended and the northbound carriageway was closed while investigations were carried out. It was reopened at 5.30pm.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the crash or has relevant dashcam footage which could assist police with their enquiries.

Anyone with information should contact the Serious Collision Investigation Unit on **101** or email [email protected].
