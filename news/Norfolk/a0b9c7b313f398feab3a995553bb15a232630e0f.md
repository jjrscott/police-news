Police are appealing for information after six business premises were targeted by thieves in Long Stratton earlier this week.

Three shops in Swan Lane were broken into between 3am and 5am on Wednesday 24 March and cash was taken.

Another three premises on Wellesley Road were broken into between the same times and tools were taken from one of the properties.

Officers believe these incidents are linked and they are appealing for anyone who may have dashcam or CCTV footage in the area to contact them.

Anyone with information should contact DC Tiffany Haggith at Norwich CID on 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
