Six people arrested after a man was stabbed in Hobart Lane in Norwich earlier this week (Thursday 21 January 2021), have been questioned by police.

Two men and two women who were arrested in connection with the incident have been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Two juveniles from the London area have been released on police bail, with conditions not to enter Norfolk.

The investigation follows an incident that occurred on Thursday (21 January 2021), when officers were called to an address in Goldwell Road at approximately 2.45pm, to reports a man was at the location with stab wounds to his arms and legs. It was established that the man had been stabbed at approximately 2.20pm in Hobart Lane.

The victim, aged in his 50s, was taken to hospital for treatment, where he remains in a stable condition.

Anyone who may have information related to the incident should contact Norwich CID on **101** , quoting incident number 253 of Thursday 21 January 2021.

Alternatively call Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on **0800 555111**.
