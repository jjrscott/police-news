Police are appealing for witnesses following an attempted robbery in Norwich.

The incident happened at approximately 7pm in Riverside, near to the Coast to Coast restaurant, when the victim, a man aged in his 20s, was approached by two unknown men. One of the men grabbed the victim and demanded money. No money was taken.

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or anyone with information concerning it to come forward.

Anyone with information should contact PC Mark Cardow in Norwich CID on **101** quoting crime number 36/13478/21

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111**.
