Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Saham Toney where suspects stole tools and a Ford van.

The incident happened sometime between Monday 25 and Thursday 28 January 2021 at the Old Chapel in Bell Lane.

Offender(s) have gained access to the building, stealing keys before entering containers on the site. A number of tools including a generator, cement mixer, nail guns, concrete breaker, chainsaws and strimmers, have been stolen. A white Ford transit style van was also taken and has since been recovered in Suffolk.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the incident or noticed anything suspicious in the area between the dates stated.

Anyone with information should contact PC Austin Clarke at Watton Police Station on **101** quoting crime reference 36/6942/21 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
