Police are appealing for witnesses after a teenager was assaulted while on his paper round in Great Yarmouth.

The incident happened on Friday 29 January at about 7.20am when the victim, a 14-year-old boy, was approached by two other boys, thought to be aged about 16 or 17-years-old, on Drake Avenue.

The boys, who were walking with pushbikes, assaulted the victim who suffered pain to his jaw and a headache.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the incident or has information which could help officers with their enquiries.

Witnesses should contact PC Luke Demetriou at Gorleston Police Station on **101** quoting crime reference 36/5998/21. Alternative, call Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
