Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident of criminal damage at a primary school in Corpusty.

The incident happened in the early hours of Friday 1 January between approximately 3am and 4am, when suspect(s) smashed a number of the school windows.

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or has any information concerning it to come forward.

Anyone with any information about this incident should contact PC Russell Bradnam on **101** quoting crime reference 36/66/21.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555111.
