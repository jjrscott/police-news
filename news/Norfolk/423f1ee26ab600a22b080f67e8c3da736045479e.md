Police are appealing for witnesses to a collision in Harleston which is believed to have involved a stolen truck.

Officers were called at approximately 9.35pm on Thursday 18 March to reports a black Mitsubishi L200 had been in collision with a parked car on Redenhall Road. No one was injured in the incident.

The truck involved in the collision is believed to have been stolen from outside an address in Paddock Road earlier in the evening. It was then found burnt out in Mundys Lane in Mendham just before 11.50pm.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the collision on Redenhall Road, or saw any suspicious activity in the Paddock Road area of Harleston and the Mundys Lane area of Mendham.

Officers are also appealing for anyone who may have CCTV or dashcam footage in those areas yesterday evening.

Anyone with information should contact Sergeant James Butler at Harleston Police Station on 101 quoting incident number 441 of Thursday 18 March 2021.
