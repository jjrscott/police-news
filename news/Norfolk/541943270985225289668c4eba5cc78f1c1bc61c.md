Police are appealing for information following the theft of an electric scooter from a shop on Bridge Street.

A white, D8 Pro Electric Scooter was taken from the A T Johnson store, sometime between 9am on Monday 14th December, and 5pm on Tuesday 15th December 2020.

Anyone who may have seen suspicious activity in the area at the time, or anyone with information is asked to contact PC David Johnson in the Op Solve team on **101** , quoting crime number 36/88308/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111**.
