Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Aylsham yesterday (Wednesday 14 October).

Officers were called to Cawston Road shortly before 10am following reports of a single vehicle collision in which a red Ford Kuga left the road and collided with a bollard before coming to rest against a tree.

The driver, a man aged in his 70s, was taken to the Norfolk and Norwich University Hospital with serious injuries.

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or has any information concerning it to come forward. They are particularly keen to speak with anyone who may have been driving in the area during the times stated and has dashcam footage.
