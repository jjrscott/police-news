Police are appealing for information after the theft of a number of trailers from a secure storage area in Thetford.

Sometime between midnight and 7:30am yesterday (Tuesday 29 December 2020), three trailers and a mini digger were stolen from a business premises on Staniforth Road.

An unknown suspect(s) is believed to have forced entry into the area by angle grinding and cutting galvanised fencing, before driving a vehicle through, and towing the equipment away.

Over £25K worth of equipment was taken from the storage area during the incident, including:

• 14ft Ifor Williams Hiab trailer with ramps – labelled with ‘Premier paving & landscaping’, with small drill holes in side of trailer, and a black Honda engine.
• 10ft x 5.5ft Humbaur 3-way tipper - labelled with ‘Premier paving & landscaping’. Distinctive wheel that winds all the way down. Licence plate – AF57 0ZE.
• 10ft x 5ft Ifor Williams box trailer with barn and ramp doors – serial number: 660394.
• 1 x JCB Mini Digger, signposted with 'Premier paving & landscaping', and reflective orange dots on the back - serial number: JCB08008LH1930322.

Officers are asking anyone who may have seen the equipment, or suspicious activity in the area at the time, to contact PC Charlie Miller at Thetford Police Station on 101, quoting crime number 36/91104/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555111.
