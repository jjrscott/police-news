Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Great Yarmouth yesterday (Tuesday 27 October 2020).

The incident happened sometime between 4.50pm and 5.25pm outside the Asda store on Acle New Road. The victim, a woman aged in her 30s, was reportedly punched in the face a number of times, however no visible injuries were caused.

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or has any information concerning it to come forward.

Anyone with any information should contact Shaun Corbett at Great Yarmouth CIU on **101** or email: [email protected] quoting reference number 36/75954/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111**.
