Police are appealing for help to trace a man who is missing from Thetford.

Anthony Hibble, aged 32, was last seen in Nessa Close at approximately 4:40pm yesterday (Wednesday 30 December 2020).

Anthony is described as a white male, with ginger hair and beard. He is believed to have been wearing grey tracksuit bottoms, a jacket with dark and black stripes on it, bright red trainers, and a grey beanie hat.

Officers are concerned for Anthony’s welfare and would like to hear from anyone who may have seen him.

Anthony is expected to have been driving a Mercedes Benz CLS class – registration OE57 ODH.

Anyone with information should contact Norfolk Police on 101, quoting incident number 416 of 30th December.
