Police are appealing for information after an incident of criminal damage at a school in Fakenham.

Sometime over the weekend of Saturday 17 October and Sunday 18 October, paving slabs and roof tiles were damaged at Fakenham County Infant School.

The materials, 60 slabs and tiles in total, were due to be installed at the school on Norwich road.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have seen anything suspicious in the area at the time.

Anyone with information should contact PC Richard Dawson at Fakenham Police Station on **101** , quoting crime reference number 36/73744/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on **0800 555111**.
