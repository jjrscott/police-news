Police are appealing for witnesses to a serious collision in Great Fransham last week.

A red Vauxhall Corsa and a silver Ford Galaxy were involved in the collision on Beeston Road at around 5.50pm on Thursday 8 October 2020.

The male driver of the Ford Galaxy was taken to the Norfolk and Norwich University Hospital with chest injuries but has since been discharged.

Anyone who may have witnessed the collision, has dashcam footage, or saw the manner of driving of either vehicle prior to the incident, should contact PC James Kent at Dereham Police Station on **101** quoting incident number 350 of Thursday 8 October 2020.
