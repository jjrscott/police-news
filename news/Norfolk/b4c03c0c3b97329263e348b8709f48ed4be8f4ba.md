A man has been arrested following an assault in Attleborough.

A woman, aged in her 30s, was assaulted in the Connaught Recreation Ground between 4pm and 5pm on Saturday 13 March 2021. The victim sustained serious injuries to her leg which required hospital treatment.

A man, aged in his 40s and from the Cambridgeshire area, was arrested in connection with the incident on Tuesday 16 March. He remains in police custody at this time.
Initial enquiries have established those involved are known to each other.
