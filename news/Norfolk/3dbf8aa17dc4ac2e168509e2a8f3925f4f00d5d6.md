The Chief Constable is required by law to publish a set of accounts each year providing a breakdown of Norfolk Constabulary's financial performance and position.

The accounts for 2019/20, which have been inspected and audited by Ernst & Young, can be found below.

The OPCCN Chief Finance Officer, Jill Penn, and Norfolk Constabulary Assistant Chief Officer, Peter Jasper, said: "On behalf of the Police and Crime Commissioner for Norfolk and Chief Constable of Norfolk Constabulary, we confirm that the accounts published on 12 November 2020 are the final audited set of accounts."
