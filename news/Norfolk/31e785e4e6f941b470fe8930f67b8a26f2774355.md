Police are appealing for witnesses following a fight in Hunstanton in the early hours of Tuesday 20 October 2020.

The incident happened at around 1:05am, when a small group of men, believed to be in their 30s, were involved in an altercation on Waveney Road, near to a cemetery entrance.

One man suffered minor injuries to his head as a result of the fight.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who witnessed the incident, or has any information.

Witnesses should contact Sergeant Anne Curtis at Hunstanton Police Station on **101** quoting crime reference 36/73923/20.

Alternatively, they can call Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111**.
