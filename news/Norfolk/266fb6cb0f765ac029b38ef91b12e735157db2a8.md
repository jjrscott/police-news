A man has been charged in connection with supplying heroin and crack cocaine in Thetford.

Officers from the Norfolk Police County Lines Team, together with officers from the Metropolitan Police, executed a warrant at an address in Enfield on Tuesday 1 December. A man was arrested and mobile phones were seized.

Aime Mpukutu, aged 21 and of Maybury Close in Enfield, has since been charged with being concerned in the supply of heroin and crack cocaine in the Thetford area between Friday 4 September 2020 and Tuesday 6 October 2020.
