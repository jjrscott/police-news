Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Tunstead, near Coltishall, yesterday afternoon (21 January 2021).

It happened on the B1150, near to the junction with The Street at about 4.45pm, when a grey Mini Cooper travelling from North Walsham towards Coltishall left the roadside and crashed into a tree.

The driver, a man in his 30s, suffered serious injuries and was taken to the Norfolk and Norwich University Hospital for treatment.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the collision, has relevant dashcam footage or information concerning the driving manner of the Mini prior to the incident.

Witnesses should contact PC Karl Warren from the Roads and Armed Policing Team at Acle on **101** or email [email protected] quoting incident number 323 of 21 January.
