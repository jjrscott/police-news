Police are appealing for witnesses after an altercation in Norwich on Friday (13 November 2020).

The incident happened at around 9am on Friday (13 November) when three boys, believed to be aged in their late teens, were involved in an altercation in Ipswich Road, at the junction of Town Close Road.

One of the boys received stab wounds to his hand, arm and side in the incident and was taken to the Norfolk and Norwich University Hospital for treatment. He has since been discharged.

Detectives would like to speak to anybody who witnessed the altercation or has dash cam footage of the incident. In particular, they're keen to speak to the driver of a black Peugeot car who was in the area at the time.

Any witnesses or anybody with information about the incident or with dash cam footage is asked to contact DC David Block at Norwich CID on **101** quoting crime reference 36/80265/20. Alternatively, they can call Crimestoppers on **0800 555 111**.
