A man in his 50s has been detained under the Mental Health Act following an incident in Wells.

Police attended a property in Northfield Waye yesterday (Tuesday 19 January 2021) at about 11am following concerns for the welfare of a man at the address.

The man, who was in a distressed state, locked himself inside the property and a number of police resources were deployed to the scene including firearms officers and negotiators.

Following lengthy negotiations, officers entered the property shortly after 5am this morning (Wednesday 20 January 2021) and the man was detained under the Mental Health Act.

The incident was safely resolved and no one was injured.
