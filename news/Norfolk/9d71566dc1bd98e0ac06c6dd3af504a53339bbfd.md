Two teenagers have been arrested on suspicion of arson, after a public toilet was set alight in Sheringham.

The incident took place in the early hours of Tuesday morning (1st December 2020), on the East Promenade, when a public toilet was set alight, causing damage to the floor and walls of a cubicle.

Two teenage girls were arrested on suspicion of arson, in relation to the incident, and taken to Aylsham police station for questioning.

Both girls have been released on police bail, with curfew conditions, while an investigation continues.
