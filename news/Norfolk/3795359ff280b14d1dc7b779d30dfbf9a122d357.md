Police can confirm the body of a man has been found in water at Sparham overnight (Sunday 28 February 2021).

Officers were called at around 8pm yesterday (Saturday 27 February 2021) after reports a man in his 60s had gone missing from a property in Whitwell Road, Sparham.

Emergency services carried out a search but sadly the man was found unresponsive in Sparham Pools, Lyng Road at around 12.30am.

The death is not being treated as suspicious and a file will be prepared for the coroner in due course.
