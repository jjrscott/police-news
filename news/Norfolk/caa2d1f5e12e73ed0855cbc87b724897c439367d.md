Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Didlington last week (Monday 22 February).

The incident happened on a back road leading from Foulden to the A134 at approximately 7.30am. The victim, a male aged in his 70s, was pulled from his car, a green coloured Ford, before being assaulted by an unknown man.

The victim was punched and kicked before the suspect ran off. The victim suffered injuries to his face and hands as a result of the incident.

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or has any information concerning it, to contact PC Bethany Wagstaff on 101 quoting crime reference number 36/11561/21.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111.
