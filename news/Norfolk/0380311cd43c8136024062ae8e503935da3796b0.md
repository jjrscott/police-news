Police are appealing for witnesses to a road traffic collision on the A47 at Acle yesterday (Thursday 25 February 2021).

The incident took place at 8.40am when a silver Toyota car was travelling towards Great Yarmouth on the A47 at Acle when it collided with a crash barrier on the side of the road.

Nobody was injured.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed the collision or has dash cam footage from the area at the time. They're particularly keen to speak to anybody who may have seen a tipper-style lorry on the same stretch of road at the time stated above.

Anyone with information should contact PC Kate Wright at Roads and Armed Policing Team on **101** quoting incident number 62 of 25 February 2021.
