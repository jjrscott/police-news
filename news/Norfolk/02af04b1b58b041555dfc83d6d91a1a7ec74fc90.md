Police are appealing for help to identify and trace a man in connection with online offences.

The man (pictured) is believed to have links in the Norwich and Thetford areas and West Midlands.

He is described as around 35 - 40 years old with dark hair, clear shaven and believed to be of Bangladesh origin, speaking limited English.

Anyone who can help police identify the man or know of his whereabouts should contact Norfolk Police on 101 quoting crime references 36/45645/20 and 36/45652/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
