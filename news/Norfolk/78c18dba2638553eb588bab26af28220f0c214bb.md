Police are appealing for witnesses or information after a driver failed to stop following a collision with a teenager in Brundall yesterday morning (Wednesday 2 December 2020).

The collision happened on The Street, Brundall, near to the Co-op shop at 7.30am when a small grey/silver car came out of Links Avenue.

The driver failed to stop and the 13-year-old girl was taken to hospital with minor injuries to her ankle.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, has information or dashcam footage should contact PC Robert Haigh on 101 quoting crime reference NC-02122020-222.
