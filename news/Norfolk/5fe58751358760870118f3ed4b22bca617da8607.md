Police are appealing for witnesses or dashcam footage following an incident where a driver of a black Range Rover shouted verbal abuse at another driver.

The incident happened at 8.20am on Tuesday 9 March 2021 when the driver of a BMW was travelling on the inside lane of Unthank Road indicating right to turn into Upton Road.

The driver of a black Range Rover in the same lane opened his door and shouted verbal abuse at the driver, who had a child in the car at the time.

Anyone with information can call DC Tasmin Raines at Norfolk Constabulary on 101 quoting crime reference 36/15001/21.

You can also contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
