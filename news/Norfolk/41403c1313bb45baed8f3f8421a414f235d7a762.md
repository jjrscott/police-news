Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Gorleston.

It happened sometime between 5pm and 7.45pm on Saturday 21 November at an address in Beccles Road. The suspect/s gained access to the property by forcing open a bathroom window. Stolen items include cash and jewellery.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who may have seen anything suspicious in the area between the times stated or has information concerning the incident. They're asked to contact PC Luke Demetriou at Gorleston police station on **101** quoting crime reference 36/82378/20.

Alternatively, they can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111**.
