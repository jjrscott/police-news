Motorists are advised of potential delays on the road as they escort the following abnormal loads through the county on the 11 January at 09:30hrs.

From 09:30hrs, a 5.6m wide boat, 23m in length will be travelling from Gaymers Way, North Walsham to Southampton.

Local Roads – Gaymers Way – Folgate Rd – B1145 - A149 – B1152 – A1064 – A47 – A11 – A14 – Cambs Border A11 – M11 – M25 J25 anticlock

Norfolk Police to escort from Gaymers Way to M25 J25.
