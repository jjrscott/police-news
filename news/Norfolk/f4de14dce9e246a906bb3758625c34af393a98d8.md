Police are investigating after a man died in a house fire in Norwich last night.

Officers were called just after 9.40pm on Tuesday 2 February to assist the Norfolk Fire and Rescue Service following a report of a house fire at an address on Langley Walk.
A man, aged in his 50s, was sadly found deceased inside the property.

The death is being treated as unexplained at this time and a cordon remains in place around the property whilst officers establish the circumstances leading up to the fire.
