Police are appealing for information after an electric skateboard and pedal cycle were stolen in close proximity of each other.

The first burglary took place at a workshop in The Street, Trowse, which was broken into at around 4am on Wednesday 18 November 2020. A black Evolve GT Carbon electric skateboard, worth £1,800, a black Fast charger worth £200 and £100 worth of damage was done to the door and lock.

Between 11pm on Tuesday 17 November 2020 and 7am on Wednesday 18 November, a mountain bike was stolen from a shed in Newton Close, Trowse.

Anyone with information for the first incident should contact PC Lee Beckham on **101** , quoting crime number 36/81436/20 and on the second incident contact PC Adrian Craske quoting crime number 36/81435/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**
