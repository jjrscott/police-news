Police are appealing for information after a car was stolen during a burglary in the early hours of this morning (Thursday 28 January 2021).

At 3:30am this morning an unknown suspect(s) gained entry into a house on Norwich Road, and took a selection of property and car keys, including those for an Audi TT, and a BMW M3.

During the burglary, the dark blue BMW M3 car (pictured) was stolen from outside the property, and is believed to have been driven towards Dereham town centre.

The Audi TT car itself was not taken.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have seen the BMW since the burglary, which has a car registration number of CO03 NAS.

Anyone with information relating to the burglary or BMW should contact PC James Jarvis at Dereham Police Station on **101** , quoting crime number 36/5678/21.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on **0800 555111**.
