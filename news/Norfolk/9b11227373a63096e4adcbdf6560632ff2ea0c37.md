Police are appealing for help to identify a woman following a theft from Superdrug in Nelson Place, Dereham.

Bottles of perfume and aftershave to the value of approximately £160 were stolen from the store just after 2:30pm on Saturday 31 October 2020.

Officers have released a CCTV image of a woman they would like to speak to following the incident.

Anyone with information concerning the woman’s identity should contact PC Louise Watson at Dereham Police Station, on **101** , quoting crime number 36/76865/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
