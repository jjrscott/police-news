Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Trowse last week.

The garage of a property on Devon Way was broken into between approximately 4pm on Thursday 31 December 2020 and 10am on Friday 1 January 2021. A bike was stolen.

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or any suspicious activity within the area during the times stated to come forward.

Anyone with information should contact PC Amanda Shreeve in Op Solve on **101** quoting crime reference number 36/261/21.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555111.
