Police are appealing for information after fireworks were set off on a street in Great Yarmouth, causing damage to a nearby property.

At 11:50pm on Thursday 31 December 2020, fireworks were released in close proximity to a property on Albany Road.

As a result, fire damage was caused to nearby drain pipes and bins, while residential walls were also left with smoke damage.

Police would like to hear from those who may have information about the incident, or witnessed it.

Anyone with information should contact PC Faye Stocker at Downham Market Police Station on 101, quoting crime reference number 36/148/21.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555111.
