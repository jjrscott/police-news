Police are appealing for information after a rental van was seen driving dangerously in Wisbech on Tuesday last week (16 February 2021).

The incident happened between 6:30 â€“ 6:40am, on the A1122 at Outwell, when a silver VW Crafter van followed an ambulance travelling with its blue lights on.

The van is believed to have driven behind the ambulance through Salters Lode, driving over the speed limit, and making dangerous manoeuvres, causing other vehicles to move out of the way.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have seen the manner of driving of the van, or from anyone who may have dashcam footage of the incident.

Those with information or footage are asked to contact PC Hannah Price at Downham Market Police Station on **101**.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on **0800 555111.**
