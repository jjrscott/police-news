Police are appealing for information after the theft of a compact tractor from a field in the Redenhall and Starston area.

The incident happened at approximately 7pm on Monday 14th December 2020, when an unknown suspect(s) is believed to have pulled the tractor across a field towards the road using a winch.

The mini tractor is described as an orange Kubota L3200, with a vehicle registration number of AU17 ADX.

Anyone who may have seen the tractor, or suspicious activity in the area at the time is encouraged to contact PC Ady Craske at Norfolk Constabulary on **101** , quoting crime number 36/88349/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on **0800 555111**.
