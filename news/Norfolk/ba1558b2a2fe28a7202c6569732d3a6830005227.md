Witnesses are being sought to a burglary in East Harling where power tools were stolen.

The incident happened overnight between 5pm on Tuesday and 7am on Wednesday (20 & 21 October 2020).

Suspect(s) targeted an address on West Harling Road, breaking into sheds stealing a range of tools worth between £2,000 and £3,000.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone with information or relevant dashcam or private CCTV footage.

Anyone with information should contact PC Charles Large at Thetford Police Station on 101 quoting crime reference 36/74291/20.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
