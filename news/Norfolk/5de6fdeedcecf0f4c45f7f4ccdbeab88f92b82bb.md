A man has been jailed for dealing crack-cocaine and heroin in Gorleston.

Malcolm Hutchinson, aged 62 and of Suffolk Road in Gorleston, was sentenced to 39 months in prison at Norwich Crown Court on Tuesday 16 March after pleading guilty to two counts of possession with intent to supply a Class A drug at an earlier hearing.

Hutchinson was arrested on Wednesday 7 October 2020 after officers executed a warrant at his home address. A large quantity of crack-cocaine and heroin was seized, along with a quantity of cash and two mobile phones.
