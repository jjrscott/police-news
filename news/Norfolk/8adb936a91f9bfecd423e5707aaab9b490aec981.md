Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision along the A149 at Northrepps last night (Wednesday 17 March 2021).

Officers were called to North Walsham Road at approximately 10.30pm following reports that a silver Mini Cooper had collided with a tree before coming to rest on a verge.

Sadly, the driver, a man aged in his late teens died at the scene.

Police are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the collision, has any information concerning it or dashcam footage to come forward. They are particularly keen to speak with anyone who saw the Mini Cooper being driven prior to the collision in the Cromer and North Repps area.
