Police are appealing for witnesses following a number of attempted thefts from vehicles in Great Yarmouth over the weekend.

Suspect(s) smashed the window of a red Vauxhall Astra parked outside a property on Bright Close between approximately 11pm on Saturday 30 January and 8.30am on Sunday 31 January. Nothing was stolen. (36/6447/21).

Another two incidents happened when a white Ford Transit Connect and a white Mini Cooper, also parked outside properties on Bright Close had their windows smashed between approximately 1.45am and 2.30am on Sunday 31 January. Nothing was stolen. (36/6493/21 & 36/6515/21)

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incidents or any suspicious activity in the area during the times stated to come forward.

Please contact PC Grant Buchanan at Great Yarmouth Police Station on **101** quoting the relevant crime reference number.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
