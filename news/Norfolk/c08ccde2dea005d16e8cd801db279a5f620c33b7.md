Police are appealing for information after a teenager was assaulted in Caister-on-Sea, Great Yarmouth yesterday (Thursday 8 October 2020).

The incident happened outside the CO-OP shop on Yarmouth Road, at approximately 6pm, when the victim was assaulted by what is believed to be two males in their mid-teens.

The victim, also a male teenager, was punched and kicked after being knocked to the ground, resulting in several minor injuries to his head, back and wrist.

Personal items were also damaged during the assault, and the victim attended the James Paget University Hospital for treatment.

The two suspects are believed to have ran from the scene in the direction of Beach Road, Caister.

Officers are appealing for witnesses to the incident, or to anyone who may have dash cam or CCTV footage from the area at the time.

Anyone with information should contact PC Laura Fitzharris at Gorleston Police Station on **101** , quoting crime number 36/71007/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111**.
