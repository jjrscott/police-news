Police are appealing for help to identify a man after an assault in Dereham last month.

Officers were flagged down in Quebec Street shortly before 4am on Sunday 25 July 2020 by a member of the public reporting that a man had suffered a stab wound to the stomach. The victim, a man aged in his 20s, was taken to the Norfolk and Norwich University Hospital for medical treatment. He has since been discharged.

Now officers have released an image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the assault.

Anybody who recognises him or has any information about the assault should contact DC Clare Gardner at Thetford CID on **101** quoting crime reference 36/50170/20.

Alternatively, they can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**
