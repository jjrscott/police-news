Police are appealing for witnesses after a car was seen to be driving dangerously in Caister on Monday (1 March 2021).

The incident happened between approximately 12.45pm and 1pm on Caister High Street and involved a silver and blue Mitsubishi Pajero.

The vehicle reportedly rammed into a parked white Audi a number of times. When the Audi drove off, the Pajero continued to pursue the vehicle and rammed it again whilst driving erratically.

Three men, one aged in his 20s and the others aged in their 30s have been arrested in connection with the incident. They have been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or has any information concerning it to come forward.

Anyone with information should contact PC Lee Goss at Great Yarmouth Police Station on **101** quoting crime reference number 36/13174/21.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
