Police are appealing for any witnesses or information after marine equipment was stolen from a boat moored at Brundall Bay Marina.

The equipment is believed to have been stolen from the boat between 5.30pm on Wednesday November 4 to 12.30 on Saturday 14 November 2020.

The equipment taken and serial numbers, where known, are listed below (photos attached) :

1x Raymarine Axiom 12 multi-function display. Serial number: 770283

1x Raymarine i70s instrument display. Serial number:372040

1x Raymarine p70s autopilot display. Serial number:670615

1x Eltrim Trim tab display. Serial number: Unknown.

Anyone with information about the incident or stolen items should contact PC Paul Bassham on 101 quoting crime number: 36/80491/20 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
