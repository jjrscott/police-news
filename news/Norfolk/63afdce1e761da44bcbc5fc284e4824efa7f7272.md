Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in East Carleton last night (Tuesday 24 November).

A property in Intwood Lane was broken into sometime between 6.30pm and 6.45pm. Garden equipment including a hedge trimmer, a lawnmower, statues and a leaf blower were stolen.

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or saw anyone acting suspiciously in the area, to come forward.

Anyone with information should contact PC Rob McMahon at Wymondham Police Station on **101** quoting reference number 36/83024/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
