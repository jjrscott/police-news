Police are appealing for information after a number of horse saddles were stolen in Bergh Apton.

A total of 11 saddles were taken when a property in Church Road was broken into between 10.30pm on Sunday 15 November and 6.30am on Monday 16 November.

Anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area between the times stated, or anyone with information, should contact PC Tabitha Hornsey at Wymondham Police Station on 101 quoting reference number 36/80802/20.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
