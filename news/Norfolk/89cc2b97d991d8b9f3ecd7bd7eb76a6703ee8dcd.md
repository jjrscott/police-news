Police are appealing for information after printer ink cartridges valued at more than £1000 were stolen from two supermarkets this month.

The first incident happened at about 2.15pm last Thursday (21 January 2021) when two men stole two large gift bags filled with printer ink cartridges, worth approximately £957, from Sainsbury's in Pound Lane, Norwich.

The second incident took place at 2.15pm last Friday (22 January 2021) at Tesco in Thetford Road, Watton, when two men stole bags filled with printer ink cartridges and toothbrush heads worth approximately £983.

In addition, three men attempted to steal printer ink cartridges, worth £1,115, from Asda in Hall Road between 3.10pm and 3.25pm on Monday 11 January 2021.

Further attempts to steal printer ink cartridges were made at Tesco in Southend Road, Hunstanton, at 2.30pm on Thursday 21 January 2021, and from Tesco in Thetford Road, Watton, at 4.50pm on the same day.

Officers believes the offences may be linked and are appealing for any witnesses or anyone with information about the incidents to contact PC John Morgan or PC Paul Brown at Norfolk Police on **101**. Alternatively, they can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**
