Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision on the A140 at Newton Flotman this morning (Thursday 19 November 2020).

Officers were called at 8.50am after reports of a collision between a white VW Transporter van and black Yamaha motorbike. The motorcyclist was taken to hospital by ambulance with serious injuries.

Anyone with dashcam footage or who witnessed the collision or manner of driving before the collision can contact PC Chris Chambers on 101 quoting incident number NC-19112020-70.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555111
