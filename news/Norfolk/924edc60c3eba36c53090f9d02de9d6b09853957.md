Police are appealing for information after play equipment and trees were damaged in Hempnall.

Paint was put on the play equipment, floors surfaces and some near-by trees on Bungay Road on Thursday 25 February, and again on Sunday 28 February 2021.

Anyone who may have seen anyone acting suspicious in the area on those dates, or anyone with information, should contact the Op Solve Team at Norfolk Police on 101 quoting reference number 36/13908/21.
