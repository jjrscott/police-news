Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Coltishall on Friday 4 December 2020.

A silver Mercedes C220 was travelling along the B1150 towards North Walsham at approximately 7.20pm when it left the road and collided with a tree.

The driver of the car, a man in his 20s, and a female passenger, were taken to hospital with serious injuries. Two other passengers in the car suffered minor injuries.

Anyone who may have witnessed the collision, saw the manner of driving of the vehicle prior to the incident, or has dashcam footage, should contact Sgt Joe Simon at Acle Roads Policing on **101** quoting reference number NC-04122020-363.
