Police are appealing for witnesses following a fail-to-stop collision in Gorleston last night (Monday 9 November 2020).

It happened at 11.10pm on Beccles Road, near to the junction with Southtown Road, when a car and black Honda motorcycle were involved in a collision, causing the rider, a man in his 30s, to fall and suffer minor injuries.

The car, described as a large light-coloured family car, failed to stop at the scene and police are keen to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the incident or has relevant dash cam footage.

Anyone with information should contact PC Peter Hudson at Gorleston Police Station on 101 quoting incident number 458 of 9 November.
