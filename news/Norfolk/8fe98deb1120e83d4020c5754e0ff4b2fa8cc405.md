A Gorleston man has appeared in court charged in connection with two burglaries which happened in Bradwell earlier this month.

Justin Loades, 36, of New College Close, Gorleston, is accused of burgling a garage and summer house at a home in Briars Avenue between 10 and 11 March, stealing alcohol and a coat, and between 17 and 18 March breaking into a home in Jasmine Gardens stealing items including an XBOX, cash, scratch cards and keys.

He was arrested by police on Friday (19 March 2021) and questioned at Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre before being charged and remanded in custody. Loades appeared at Norwich Magistrates Court via video link on Saturday 20 March and was further remanded in custody to appear before Norwich Crown Court on Monday 19 April.
