Police are appealing for information after a motorbike was stolen from a garage in Walsingham.

The black Honda VTR 1000 (pictured) was stolen from a garage in Egmere Road between 1pm on Sunday November 8 2020 and 8am on Monday November 9 2020 alongside tools and three padlocks on the garage.

Anyone with information is asked to call PC Mandy Shreeve on 101 quoting crime reference 36/80027/20 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
