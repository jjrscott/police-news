Police are appealing for witnesses to a collision in South Wootton, King’s Lynn, on Friday 6 November 2020.

A Fiat car and a cyclist collided on Nursery Road, at approximately 8:10am.

The cyclist, a man in his 20s, sustained minor injuries and was taken to hospital for observation.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the collision, or from anyone who may have dashcam footage of the incident.

Those with information or footage should contact PC Daisy Riches at King’s Lynn Police Station on 101, quoting call number 52 of 6th November.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
