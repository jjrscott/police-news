09:43 16/12/2019

The Police, Fire and Crime Commissioner (PFCC) for Essex, Roger Hirst, has today, Monday 16 December, launched a public survey to find out the views of Essex residents regarding the policing element of council tax.

Mr Hirst wants to hear people's opinion before he sets out the budget plan for policing in the county in 2020/2021.

To find out more visit the PFCC's website www.essex.pfcc.police.uk
