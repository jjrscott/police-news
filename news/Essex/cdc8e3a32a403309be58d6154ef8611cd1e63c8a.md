#### A man has been charged following a serious assault in Colebrook Gardens, Loughton.

We were called just before 1am on Sunday 30 August, following a disturbance in which a man in his 30s was stabbed.

He remains in hospital in a serious but stable condition.

Sean Thompson, 32, of Colebrook Gardens, Loughton was arrested on Monday 31 August and later charged with attempted murder.

He has been remanded to prison and is due to next appear at Chelmsford Crown court on Tuesday 29 September.
