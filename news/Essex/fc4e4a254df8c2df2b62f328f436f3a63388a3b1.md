### A man is due to appear in court next year to face a charge in connection with a fatal collision in Colchester.

Stuart McClung, 40, died after a car was in collision with the Spinnker pub in Hythe Quay at around 12.50am on 3 November 2019.

A 41 year-old man was arrested.

Bandile Xozumti, of Waterside Lane, Colchester, has now been summonsed to appear at Colchester Magistrates' Court on 19 January to face a charge of causing death by careless driving.
