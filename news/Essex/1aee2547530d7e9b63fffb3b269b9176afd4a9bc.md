13:26 19/03/2020

### Seven men are due to appear in court at the end of this March to face charges in connection with reported disorder at a Southend United versus Barnsley match.

Officers have been investigating reports of a disturbance at the match at Roots Hall on 2 March last year.

Seven men have been summonsed to appear at Southend Magistrates' Court on 30 March to face a charge of violent disorder.

The men are:

 * Kyle Swann, 19, of Laithes Lane, Barnsley
 * Tom Bracken, 25, of Sheffield Road, Barnsley
 * Jonathan Senior, 47, of Calabria Grove, Barnsley
 * Cameron Rose, 22, of Little Stubbing, Wombwell, Barnsley
 * Darren Kennett, 53, of Newstead Road, Barnsley
 * Chris Kennett, 18, of Newstead Road, Barnsley
 * Chris Horbury, 20, of Station Street, Rotherham
