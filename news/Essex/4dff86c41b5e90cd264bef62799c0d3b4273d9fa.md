08:29 02/03/2020

#### Updated Tuesday 3 March 10:48

A man has been charged with the murder of a woman in Wimbish.

Mark Jozunas, 49, of Tye Green, Wimbish was arrested on Sunday 1 March following the death of a 78-year old woman at an address in the town.

He has now been charged with murder and is due to appear at Chelmsford Magistrates court today, Tuesday 3 March.

We are not looking for anyone else in connection with this incident.

Anyone with information is asked to please contact the Major Crime Team on 101 or
email scd.appeals.essex@essex.pnn.police.uk.

##### Original Monday 2 March

Detectives have launched a murder investigation following the death of a woman in, Tye green, Wimbish, Saffron Walden.

We were called at around 6.30pm on Sunday 1 March, following a concern for welfare for a woman.

Sadly, despite efforts from paramedics, the woman, was pronounced deceased at the scene.

A man in his 40s has been arrested on suspicion of murder and remains in police custody.

Anyone with information can contact the Major Crime Team on 101 or email scd.appeals.essex@essex.pnn.police.uk quoting incident 969 of Sunday 1 March.

Information can also be given completely anonymously to independent charity Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or visiting crimestoppers-uk.org.
