17:00 21/12/2020

Do any of these tools and equipment belong to you?

We recovered them while investigating a burglary in Chelmsford and believe they might be stolen.

If you recognise any of them, please contact Chelmsford CID quoting reference 42/138993/20.

Please also tell us which picture number relates to your item.

You can contact us online at https://www.essex.police.uk where you can also use the 'Live Chat' button to speak to an online operator between 7am-11pm.
