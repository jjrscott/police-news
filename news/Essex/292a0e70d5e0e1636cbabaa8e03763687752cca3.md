17:05 12/02/2021

Six people have been charged following a series of drugs raids in Braintree and Witham yesterday morning, Thursday 11 February.

They are all due to appear at Chelmsford Magistrates' Court on 26 March, charged with conspiracy to supply Class A drugs - crack cocaine and heroin.

They are:

Zena Fourie, 23, of Challis Lane, Braintree.

Danielle Greer, 28, of Northumberland Close, Braintree.
She is also charged with conspiracy to supply class B drugs, driving while disqualified and using a motor vehicle without insurance.

Lewis Passley, 37, of Easton Road, Witham.

Victoria Stanton, 31, of Easton Road, Witham.

Charles Stokes, 39, of Easton Road, Witham.

Christopher Tolchard, 35, of Challis Lane, Braintree.
He is also charged with possession of a class B drug.
