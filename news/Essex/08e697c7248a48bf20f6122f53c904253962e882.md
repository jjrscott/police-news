09:13 03/04/2021

**Two people have been charged in connection with a burglary in Dunow where jewellery and electrical items were stolen.**

It was reported someone had used a brick to gain entry to an address in Nursery Rise between 10.20am and 11.35am on Friday 26 March.

Items including an Omega watch, a Pandora bracelet, and other Tiffany jewellery were stolen.

Two people were later arrested.

Darryll Khazanchi, 28, of Ash Grove, Dunmow has now been charged with burglary and possession of a Class B drug.

Kaye Davey, 39, of no fixed address, has been charged with burglary.

The both appeared at Colchester Magistrates' Court on 29 March and are next due to appear at Chelmsford Crown Court on 26 April.

We are asking anyone who has information about the burglary and believes they may have been offered any of the items of jewellery for sale to contact us.

If you have any information you can submit a report online at https://www.essex.police.uk or use the 'Live Chat' button to speak to an online operator between 7am-11pm.

Please quote the crime reference number 42/53011/21.

You can also call us on 101 or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
