Two people have been charged by officers in Doncasterâ€™s Op Fortify team as part of an investigation into several reported shootings in the Conisbrough area last November.

Joe Edward Roberts, 20, of Windmill Avenue, Doncaster, and Nicola Butler, 44, also of Windmill Avenue, Doncaster, will appear before Sheffield Crown Court today (Wednesday 3 February).

Roberts is charged with three counts of attempted murder, three counts of possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life, arson with intent to endanger life, and violent disorder.

Butler is charged with two counts of attempted murder and two counts of possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life.

The charges relate to reported shootings in the Conisbrough area of Doncaster between Saturday 7 and Monday 9 November. Police were called to reports of firearms discharges in the Park Road, Daylands Avenue and March Vale Rise areas of Conisbrough.

A 19-year-old man suffered non-life threatening injuries in the incident on Daylands Avenue. Two men, aged 47 and 22, suffered non-life threatening injuries in the incident on March Vale Rise.
