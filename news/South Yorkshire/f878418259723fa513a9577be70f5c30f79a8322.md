_Date published: 20 December 2020 15:59_

 _Dated: 20 December 2020 15:58:25_

A forensic post mortem has been carried out today (20 December 2020) following the death of a man in Doncaster.

Officers were called to a hotel in the Lakeside area at around 2pm on Friday 18 December following a call made to the ambulance service that a 43-year-old man had died.

An investigation was launched to ascertain the exact circumstances around his death.

The post mortem established that there are no suspicious causes to his death. His family are being supported at this time by specialist officers.

A 41-year-old arrested on suspicion of murder has now been released from the investigation.
