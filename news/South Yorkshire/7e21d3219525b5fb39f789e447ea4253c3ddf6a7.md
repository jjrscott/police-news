_Date published: 04 January 2021 15:52_

 _Dated: 04 January 2021 15:51:51_

Detectives in Barnsley have charged a 30-year-old man in connection with the reported robbery of a milkman on Canal Street, Barnsley, on Wednesday 30 December.

It is reported that the victim was making deliveries when he was attacked with a bladed weapon and his vehicle was stolen. He suffered significant injuries to his hand, which required 26 stitches.

Daniel Bligh, of no fixed abode, appeared at Sheffield Magistrates' Court this morning (Monday 4 January) charged with robbery and possession of a bladed article.

Bligh has been remanded in custody to next appear before Sheffield Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 27 January 2021.
