_Date published: 09 December 2020 15:46_

 _Dated: 09 December 2020 14:00:14_

Police are appealing for witnesses after an 80-year-old woman sadly died following a collision in the Hoyland area of Barnsley on Thursday 3 December.

It is reported that at around 12:30pm, the 80-year-old pedestrian was involved in a collision with a car, a red Nissan Juke, on Hoyland Road.

The 80-year-old woman suffered critical injuries in the collision. Police, paramedics and the air ambulance attended the scene, but she sadly passed away.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the collision, particularly any motorists who were travelling along Hoyland Road who may hold dash cam footage, or properties not already spoken to in the local area that hold CCTV footage.

If you can help, please call 101 or email footage to enquiries@southyorks.pnn.police.uk quoting incident number 410 of 3 December.
