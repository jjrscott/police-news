_Date published: 26 January 2021 11:44_

 _Dated: 25 January 2021 18:12:40_

Traffic officers are appealing for witnesses and dashcam footage after a collision on the M1 on Friday morning (22 January).

At around 6.50am it is reported that two cars were in collision on the M1 southbound carriageway, about a mile before Woodall Services.

Officers have so far only identified one of the vehicles involved, a silver Seat Leon, the other vehicle is unknown.

The driver of the vehicle sustained minor injuries.

If you witnessed the collision or have any information about what happened then please contact us on 101 quoting incident number 138 of the 22 January 2021.
