_Date published: 25 July 2020 11:34_

 _Dated: 22 July 2020 15:40:54_

Our officers in Doncaster have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to in connection to reports of theft from a vehicle.

On Friday 17 July at around 6pm, a man was seen removing the number plates from a red Seat Ibiza parked on Great North Road in Woodlands.

Officers are keen to speak to the man in the image in connection to the incident. Do you recognise him?

If you have information that can help officers with their enquiries, please call 101 quoting crime reference number 14/107093/20.

Alternatively, you can stay completely anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers via their website Crimestoppers-uk.org or by calling their UK Contact Centre on 0800 555 111.
