_Date published: 04 February 2021 15:19_

 _Dated: 04 February 2021 15:19:13_

Two teenage boys have been charged with the murder of Lewis Williams in Mexborough.

The boys, aged 15 and 17-years-old, were charged yesterday evening (Wednesday 3 February) and will appear before Doncaster Magistrates’ Court today charged with murder.

The teenagers cannot be named for legal reasons.

Four other people have been charged with murder. Two boys, aged 16 and 17-years-old, who cannot be named for legal reasons, and two 20-year-old men. All four are currently on remand awaiting further court appearances.

You can continue to provide information about Lewis’ death directly to the investigation team via the online Major Incident Public Portal - https://mipp.police.uk/operation/14SY21A01-PO1 - or by calling 101 quoting incident number 495 of 11 January 2021.

You can also give information anonymously to independent charity Crimestoppers via their website – www.crimestoppers-uk.org – or by calling their UK Contact Centre on 0800 555 111.
