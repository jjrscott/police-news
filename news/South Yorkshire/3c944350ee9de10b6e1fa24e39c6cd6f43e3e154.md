_Date published: 16 June 2020 12:38_

 _Dated: 16 June 2020 13:37:59_

Officers in Intake have released CCTV footage following a burglary in which a bike was stolen.

It is reported that on Tuesday 9 June 2020 between 2:48- 4am, three men broke into the back garden of a property on Wadsworth Drive by climbing a fence. It is reported that they broke into a shed by forcing open the door and stole a black Carrera Vengeance mountain bike.

Police have released CCTV footage of men they would like to speak to in connection to the incident as they may hold information which could help them in their enquiries.

Can you help?

If you have any information which might help our officers, please call 101 quoting incident number 122 of 9 June.
