_Date published: 11 June 2020 11:52_

 _Dated: 11 June 2020 12:29:03_

Five people – three men aged 36, 34 and 34, and two 15-year-old boys arrested on suspicion of murder on Tuesday 9 June in connection with the death of 32-year old Stephen Georgeson have been released on police bail.

A sixth person, a 35-year-old man, who was arrested on suspicion of murder on Sunday (7 June) has also been bailed.

Stephen Georgeson died following a suspected serious assault in Barnsley on Friday (5 June) and sadly died on Sunday (7 June).

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 quoting incident number 650 of 5 June 2020.

You can also give information to independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously via their website – www.crimestoppers-uk.org – or by calling their UK Contact Centre on 0800 555 111.
You can also report online via the public portal- https://mipp.police.uk/
