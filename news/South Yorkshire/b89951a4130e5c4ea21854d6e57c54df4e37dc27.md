Officers in Doncaster have recovered property they believe to be stolen and are keen to reunite it with its owners.

During a house search in the Hyde Park area of Doncaster, officers recovered three mountain bikes and a signed football shirt.

Do you recognise it? If you believe the property to be yours, please email PC Charlotte Turner- Charlotte.Turner@southyorks.pnn.police.uk
