_Date published: 15 November 2020 10:00_

 _Dated: 15 November 2020 09:59:56_

A 30-year-old man is currently in police custody after being arrested overnight on suspicion of murder in Sheffield.

Emergency services were called at around 2.15am this morning (Sunday 15 November) to reports of a man laid in the street on Club Garden Road in the Highfield area of Sheffield.

Upon arrival, a 28-year-old man was found with serious injuries. He was taken to hospital but was sadly pronounced dead a short time later.

A 30-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of murder and is currently in police custody.

There is a police presence and cordon at the scene while officers carry out their initial enquiries, and this is expected to remain in place for some time.

Formal identification and a post-mortem examination is expected to take place later today.

If you hold any information that may help police, please call 101 quoting incident number 99 of 15 November 2020.
