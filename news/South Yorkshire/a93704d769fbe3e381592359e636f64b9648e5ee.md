_Date published: 06 March 2021 10:33_

 _Dated: 06 March 2021 10:32:43_

Detectives in Sheffield are investigating following a reported shooting in the Woodhouse area of Sheffield last night (Friday 5 March).

At around 10.40pm police were called to Ashpool Close, Woodhouse following reports of a shooting.

On arrival, officers discovered two men with injuries consistent with a shotgun discharge. They were both taken to hospital with non life-threatening injuries.

No arrests have been made and investigations are ongoing into the circumstances surrounding the incident.

There will be an increased police presence in the area today and throughout the weekend as investigations continue.

Police are keen to hear from anyone who may hold information which might help the investigation, particularly anyone who may have CCTV footage.

If you think you can help our officers, please call 101 quoting incident number 1054 of 5 March 2021.

Alternatively, you can stay completely anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers via their website www.crimestoppers-uk.org or by calling their UK Contact Centre on 0800 555 111.
