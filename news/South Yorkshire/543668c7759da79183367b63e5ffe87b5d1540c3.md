_Date published: 11 February 2021 12:58_

 _Dated: 11 February 2021 12:54:28_

A seventh person has been charged in connection to Lewis Williams’ death.

Stan Bentley, 22, of Bolton-Upon-Dearne in Rotherham has been charged with assisting an offender and remains remanded in police custody.

Lewis, 20, suffered fatal injuries following a reported shooting on Wath Road, Mexborough on Monday 11 January.

Bentley will appear before Sheffield Crown Court on 11 March 2021.

You can continue to provide information directly to the investigation team via the online Major Incident Public Portal - https://mipp.police.uk/operation/14SY21A01-PO1 - or by calling 101 quoting incident number 495 of 11 January 2021.

You can also give information anonymously to independent charity Crimestoppers via their website – www.crimestoppers-uk.org – or by calling their UK Contact Centre on 0800 555 111.
