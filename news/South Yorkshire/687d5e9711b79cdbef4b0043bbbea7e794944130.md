_Date published: 03 December 2020 20:11_

 _Dated: 03 December 2020 20:08:20_

Two teenagers, aged 14 and 16 have been charged in connection with the rape of a woman in Sheffield.

At around 6pm on 23 November, it was reported that a 21-year-old woman was raped by a group of four males in High Hazels Park.

Officers executed two warrants in the Darnall area of Sheffield yesterday and arrested four teenagers aged 13, 14, 15 and 16.

The 16 year-old boy has been charged with rape and the 14 year-old boy has been charged with attempted sexual assault.

The 13 and 15 year-old boys arrested on suspicion of rape yesterday have been released on police bail.
