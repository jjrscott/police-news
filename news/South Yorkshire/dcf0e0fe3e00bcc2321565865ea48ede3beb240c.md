_Date published: 18 September 2020 15:52_

 _Dated: 18 September 2020 16:51:48_

Officers in Rotherham have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with an assault in Grange Lane, Maltby area.

It is reported that on 17 August at around 9:30pm a taxi driver was driving four men from the Thrybergh area towards East Dene. The taxi stopped at an address in Grange Lane, Maltby, where three of the four passengers got out. The remaining man is then reported to have assaulted the driver, punching him repeatedly in the face. The man was removed from the taxi and the victim was able to drive away.

Enquiries are ongoing but officers are keen to identify the man in the image as they may be able to assist with enquiries.

Do you recognise him?

If you have any information, please contact 101 quoting crime reference number 14/123000/20 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
