_Date published: 25 July 2020 11:34_

 _Dated: 24 July 2020 10:52:01_

Police are appealing for information following a road traffic collision in the Grimethorpe area yesterday evening (Thursday 23 July).

It is reported that at around 8:50pm, a black Vauxhall Corsa collided at speed with the roundabout on Park Springs Road. The car subsequently crashed into the carpark of a nearby warehouse and damaged 25 parked vehicles.

The driver of the Corsa, an 18-year-old man, suffered life threatening injuries. He was taken to hospital via ambulance where he remains in a critical but stable condition.

Police now want to hear from anyone who may have information about what happened.

Were you in the area the time? Did you witness the incident? Did you see the car in the area prior to the collision?

If you can help our officers with their enquiries, please call 101 quoting incident number 884 of 23 July.
