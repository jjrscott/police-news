_Date published: 30 January 2021 14:40_

 _Dated: 30 January 2021 14:39:52_

Two people are in custody today (Saturday 30 January) after being arrested on suspicion of murder.

Emergency services were called shortly before 1am this morning to reports a man had been assaulted on South Road, High Green.

A 45-year-old man was found with serious injuries and was sadly pronounced dead while being taken to hospital.

A 25-year-old man and a 19-year-old woman have been arrested on suspicion of murder and both are currently in police custody.

Formal identification of the 45-year-old and a post mortem examination are yet to take place.
