_Date published: 28 February 2021 21:26_

 _Dated: 28 February 2021 21:25:36_

A 27-year-old man is currently in police custody after a reported firearms discharge in Sheffield earlier today (28 February).

Shortly before 5.15pm, police received multiple reports that shots had been fired at a vehicle on Yew Lane, Sheffield . Armed police responded and arrested a man from Sheffield, on suspicion of firearms offences.

No injuries have been reported, however there is thought to be damage to the car involved.

Sheffield Chief Inspector Helen Lewis said: “I appreciate this incident will have been incredibly concerning, but we have quickly arrested a man in connection with the incident. To reassure the local community, we will have armed police and neighbourhood officers in this area throughout the evening, speaking to residents and reviewing nearby CCTV footage in order to understand the exact circumstances.

“If you do want to report any information, or have any concerns, please speak to one of the officers. If you can assist with our investigation, please call 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously.”

The incident number to quote is 761 of 28 February.
