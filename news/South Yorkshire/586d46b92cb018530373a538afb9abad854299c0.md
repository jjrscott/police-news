_Date published: 02 September 2020 10:59_

 _Dated: 02 September 2020 11:57:08_

Detectives investigating the death of a 39-year-old man in Doncaster have today named him as Leighton Owen.

Shortly after 11pm on Wednesday 26 August, police were called to Grove Place, Balby and found the body of a man in a flat in Hatfield House.

A post-mortem examination carried out proved inconclusive and further tests are underway to determine the exact cause of Mr Owen's death.

Mr Owen's family are receiving support from specially trained officers. They have issued the following statement today: "Leighton was a kind loving person who will be sadly missed by his family and friends, he may be out of our lives but will always be in our hearts. He can sleep easy now he has been reunited with his Mum and Dad at long last."

Anyone with any information about Mr Owen's death is asked to call 101. The incident number to quote is 926 of 26 August.

Two women, aged 45 and 40, and a 21-year-old man, arrested on suspicion of murder, have been released under investigation as enquiries continue.
