_Date published: 01 December 2020 16:40_

 _Dated: 01 December 2020 16:33:54_

Officers in Barnsley investigating a knifepoint robbery at a shop in Cudworth have released CCTV images of two people they would like to speak to.

At around 7.40am on the morning of Saturday 21 November, it is reported that two men entered the Co-op on Barnsley Road and stole cigarettes.

During the incident, one of the men threatened staff with a large knife.

No-one was injured, but staff members were left distressed by what happened.

CCTV images have now been released of two men that officers would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

If you recognise these men or have any information that might help, please contact 101.

When you call, quote crime reference number 14/171849/20.

Alternatively, you can stay completely anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers via their website Crimestoppers-uk.org or by calling their UK Contact Centre on 0800 555 111.
