_Date published: 24 November 2020 08:10_

 _Dated: 24 November 2020 08:02:16_

Officers from our Roads Policing Team are appealing for witnesses and information following a road traffic collision involving a car and a pedestrian in Sheffield.

At around 6:40pm on Sunday 22 November, it is reported that a 62 year-old man was crossing Main Street in Darnall when a black Mercedes collided with him.

The driver of the Mercedes, a 29 year-old man stopped at the scene and is assisting officers with their enquiries.
The pedestrian suffered internal injuries and injuries to his pelvis and leg. He was taken to hospital by ambulance.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision, has dash-cam footage or who saw the Mercedes prior to the collision.

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 quoting incident number 845 of 22 November 2020.

Dash-cam footage can be submitted via email to enquiries@southyorks.pnn.police.uk, please include the incident number in your email.
