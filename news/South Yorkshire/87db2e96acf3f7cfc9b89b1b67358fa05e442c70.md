_Date published: 08 September 2020 09:22_

 _Dated: 08 September 2020 10:20:54_

Officers in Sheffield are asking for your help after 24 cars were damaged in an incident of criminal damage.

It is reported that between 8pm on Thursday 4 September and 6am on Friday 5 September, 24 cars parked overnight on Scott Road in Pitsmoor were scratched along their side panels.

Extensive enquiries are underway to find who is responsible, including CCTV analysis, but officers are keen to hear for anyone who may have information about what happened.

Were you in the area at the time? Did you witness the cars being damaged? Do you know who might be responsible?

If you can help, please call 101 quoting crime reference number 14/132086/20.

Alternatively, you can stay completely anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers via their website Crimestoppers-uk.org or by calling their UK Contact Centre on 0800 555 111.
