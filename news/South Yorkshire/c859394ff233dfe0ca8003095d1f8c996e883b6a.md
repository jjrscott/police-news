_Date published: 02 March 2021 18:12_

 _Dated: 02 March 2021 18:12:03_

Four men have been arrested following a reported shooting in the Westfield area of Sheffield yesterday evening (Monday 1 March).

At around 11.20pm, emergency services were called to Cowley Gardens following reports two men had been injured.

Two men, aged 25 and 26, were found with suspected gunshot injuries and taken to hospital. Their injuries are not thought to be life-threatening.

Four men, aged 31, 21, 20 and 19, have been arrested on suspicion of firearms offences.

Enquiries are ongoing but at this time, it is not thought that this incident is linked to any others.
