_Date published: 30 December 2020 14:19_

 _Dated: 30 December 2020 14:18:57_

Emergency services are on scene at an address in Southey, Sheffield following the unexplained death of a 38-year-old woman.

Police were alerted to the woman’s death by a call from the ambulance service shortly before 6am this morning (30 December).

Formal ID is yet to take place and the woman’s family are in the process of being informed. A post-mortem will be carried out later on today.

Detectives are working to establish the circumstances surrounding her death, and a 64-year-old man is currently detained on suspicion of murder. Officers will remain at the scene, in Southey Avenue, throughout the day to conduct enquiries.
