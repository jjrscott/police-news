_Date published: 16 December 2020 13:56_

 _Dated: 16 December 2020 13:55:48_

A Sheffield woman has today been charged with nine offences relating to malicious, threatening and abusive messages posted on social media.

Katie Louise Bell, of Stradbroke Road, is accused of sending the messages, targeting numerous recipients, between 31 December 2016 and 16 February 2020.

The 24-year-old faces two counts of stalking involving serious alarm or distress. These relate to two series of messages received by two different victims, one over a three-year period and one over ten months.

Bell also faces seven counts of sending malicious communications under the Communications Act. These charges relate to comments publicly posted on tweets shared by South Yorkshire Police and the Metropolitan Police between 8 November 2018 and 3 October 2019.
