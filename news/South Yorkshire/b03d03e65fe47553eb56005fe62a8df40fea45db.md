_Date published: 10 March 2021 15:50_

 _Dated: 10 March 2021 15:49:18_

A man has been arrested and charged with drug offences after the Barnsley fortify and tasking team searched an address on Nursery Street, Barnsley on Friday (5 March 2021). The activity came after UK Border Force intercepted a package destined for the address which contained approximately 6kg of suspected controlled drugs.

Officers conducted a search of the address on Friday, resulting in the seizure of a quantity of Methadrone (known as M-CAT) and Methadrone mixed with MDMA (also known as ecstasy). Other substances were also seized which are yet to be identified.

Mareks, Dzelz, 33, of Nursery Street was arrested and subsequently charged with conspiracy to import a class B drug, concerned in the supply of class B drugs, and concerned in the supply of class A drugs. He was placed before the courts on Saturday 6 March where he was remanded into custody.
