_Date published: 12 January 2021 15:24_

 _Dated: 12 January 2021 15:19:09_

An 18 year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder this morning (12 January) in connection to two firearms discharges in Sheffield last week.

On 6 January at around 8pm, it is reported that shots were fired at a property on Castledale Croft on the Manor. A 42-year-old woman and a 23-year-old man were inside the property and suffered minor injuries.

Shortly afterwards, we received reports of a second gunshot discharge on Prince of Wales Road.

Yesterday (11 January), a 27-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of attempted murder, he remains in police custody.

Detectives are still progressing the investigation and are still keen to hear from anyone with information or dash-cam footage.

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 quoting incident number 790 of 6 January. Dash cam or CCTV footage can be emailed to enquiries@southyorks.pnn.police.uk please quote the incident number in the title of the email.

You can also give information anonymously to independent charity Crimestoppers via their website www.crimestoppers-uk.org or by calling their UK Contact Centre on 0800 555 111.
