_Date published: 30 November 2020 11:15_

 _Dated: 30 November 2020 11:14:29_

Two people have been arrested after the body of a man was found in Doncaster yesterday (29 November).

Police were called to an address on Beckett Road, Wheatley, at 12.06pm after reports that a man had died.

On arrival at the scene, emergency services discovered the body of a 55-year-old man.

A 50 year old woman and a 34 year old man were arrested at the property on suspicion of murder and remain in custody as enquiries continue.

A post mortem is due to take place today.

Anyone with information should call 101 quoting incident number 355 of 29 November.
