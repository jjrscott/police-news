_Date published: 12 January 2021 16:47_

 _Dated: 12 January 2021 16:46:37_

Officers investigating the murder of a 20-year-old man in Mexborough yesterday (Monday 11 January) have made two further arrests.

A 20-year-old man and a 16-year-old boy, both from the Doncaster area, have today been arrested on suspicion of murder and are currently in police custody.

Emergency services were called to Wath Road, Mexborough at 4.26pm yesterday, where a 20-year-old man was found with suspected gunshot wounds. He was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

A 17-year-old boy arrested in the early hours of this morning on suspicion of murder remains in custody at this time.

Enquiries are ongoing and anyone with information is urged to contact 101 quoting incident number 495 of 11 January 2021 or call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or submit information via their website crimestoppers-uk.org.
