_Date published: 04 March 2021 13:57_

 _Dated: 04 March 2021 13:57:35_

We’re currently investigating an assault on a woman in the Gleadless area of Sheffield and need your help with our enquiries.

At around 12.20pm on the afternoon of Monday 15 February, an 25-year-old woman, was assaulted by two woman and a man, near to a bus stop on Gleadless Road near to the Newfield Green shops.

She was left with bruising to her face and injuries to her body.

The victim’s dog was also reportedly punched in the incident.

Enquiries are ongoing, but officers are keen to hear from anyone who saw what happened that afternoon or witnessed the attack.

If you have any information that might help us with the investigation, then please call 101 quoting incident number 395 of 15 February.
