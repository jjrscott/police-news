_Date published: 09 September 2020 10:06_

 _Dated: 09 September 2020 11:06:13_

Officers from Doncaster are appealing for witnesses and information following an assault in the Balby area.

It is reported that on Tuesday 23 June, a 51 year-old woman was walking along Warmsworth Road when she was assaulted from behind.

The woman received injuries to her face.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone with information. If you can help, please call 101 quoting incident number 893 of 23 June 2020.
