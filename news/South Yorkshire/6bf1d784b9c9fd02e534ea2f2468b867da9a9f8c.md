_Date published: 03 February 2021 12:55_

 _Dated: 03 February 2021 12:52:37_

A further two teenagers have been arrested in connection to the murder of Lewis Williams in Mexborough.
Lewis suffered fatal injuries following a reported shooting on Wath Road, Mexborough, at 4.26pm on Monday 11 January.

Yesterday evening (2 February) officers arrested a 15-year-old boy and a 17-year-old boy on suspicion of murder. They both remain in police custody.

The teenagers bring the total number of arrests in connection to the death of Lewis to 10.

Four people have been charged; Jack Parkes, 20, of Arnold Crescent, Doncaster, Ryan Nisbet of Springwood Road, Barnsley, and two boys, aged 16 and 17-years-old, who cannot be named for legal reasons, all charged with murder.
All four are currently on remand awaiting further court appearances.

The investigation into Lewis’ death continues and anyone who has information yet to speak to officers are asked to please get in touch. You can provide information directly to the investigation team via the online Major Incident Public Portal - https://mipp.police.uk/operation/14SY21A01-PO1- or by calling 101 quoting incident number 495 of 11 January 2021.

You can also give information anonymously to independent charity Crimestoppers via their website – www.crimestoppers-uk.org– or by calling their UK Contact Centre on 0800 555 111.
