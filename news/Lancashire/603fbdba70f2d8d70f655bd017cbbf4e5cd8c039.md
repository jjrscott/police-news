Sunday, October 28, 2018

Detectives investigating an assault in Burnley have charged a man.

We were called at 7:06am on Saturday (27th October) to reports of an assault on Hargreaves Street.

Officers and paramedics attended and a 32-year-old man was found on the floor with head injuries.

He was taken to hospital where he remains in a serious condition.
