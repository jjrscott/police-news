Friday, October 4, 2019

Good news. The 19 year old man missing from home in Blackburn has been found safe and well this morning (4 October).

He had last been seen on Tuesday 1st October at 1027hrs in the Cicely Lane area of Blackburn walking up hill towards Higher Audley Street.

Thanks to everyone who shared our appeal.
