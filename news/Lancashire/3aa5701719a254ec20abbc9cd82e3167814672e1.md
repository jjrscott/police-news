Sunday, November 25, 2018

A missing Preston man has been found safe and well.

Frank Richardson, 48, was missing from home in Preston.

Frank was last seen in the Eaves Lane area of Preston at around midday on Saturday, 24 November.

He was found safe and well in Preston this morning (25 November).

Thanks to everyone who shared our appeal.
