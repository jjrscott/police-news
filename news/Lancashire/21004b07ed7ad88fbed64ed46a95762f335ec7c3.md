Tuesday, November 27, 2018

A 27-year-old woman has died following a collision in Lancaster.

Police were called at 20:33hours last night (Monday 26 November) to reports a Vauxhall Corsa had been found crashed on Kellet Lane.

The vehicle was travelling from Nether Kellet in the direction of Halton when it collided with a wall.

Officers and paramedics attended but the driver- a 27 year old Lancaster woman – was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

#### Sergeant Adam Dawson of Lancashire’s Road Policing Unit, said: “My thoughts are with the woman’s family and friends at this incredibly tragic time.

#### “We do not believe any other vehicles were involved but continue to work to establish the full circumstances. We appreciate this happened in a rural location, but if you did see the collision, or the vehicle described, prior to the collision, please let us know.”

Anyone with information should call us on 101 quoting log number 1444 of 26th November.
