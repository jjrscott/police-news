Wednesday, October 24, 2018

A man who went missing from Leyland has been found.

Ryan Kennedy, 25 was last seen at 6am yesterday (October 23) in the St Annes Road area of Leyland.

He was found safe and well in Leyland today (October 24).

Thanks to everyone who shared our earlier appeal.
