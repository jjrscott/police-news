Wednesday, June 19, 2019

A SEVEN-year-old boy was injured in a collision in Bacup.

The incident happened on Pennine Road at about 4.45pm yesterday (Tuesday, June 18th) when the boy was involved in a collision with a Nissan Juke.

The youngster suffered a fractured collarbone and eye socket and was taken to Alder Hey Hospital.

Police are appealing for any witnesses or anyone with dashcam footage to contact them on 101 quoting log number 1060 of June 18th.
