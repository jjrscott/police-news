Tuesday, July 30, 2019

A 13-year-old boy who was missing from home has been found.

Levi Slade-Jordan had last been seen in the Preston area at around 9am on Friday (July 26).

He was found today (July 30) in the Leyland area.

Thanks to everyone who shared our earlier appeals for information.
