Thursday, September 26, 2019

**A 15 year old boy missing from the Preston area has been found safe.**

He was last seen around 1.20pm yesterday (Wednesday, September 25) in the Lancaster Road North area.

He was found safe and well this afternoon (26 September). Thanks to everyone who shared our appeal.
