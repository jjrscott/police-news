Saturday, October 12, 2019

We were called at 07:20 this morning (12 October) to reports that the body of a man had been found on the beach at Blackpool, opposite the Wedding chapel.

Emergency services attended the scene and enquiries are ongoing to identify the man, who is a white male believed to be in his 50s or 60s.

Anyone with information should call 101 quoting log number 319 of 12th October.
