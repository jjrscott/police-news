Tuesday, November 27, 2018

A MAN has been charged after a seven-year-old boy was sexually assaulted.

Dennis Walsh, 39, of Albert Street, Burnley is charged with sexual assault on a child under 13, false imprisonment and breach of a sexual harm prevention order.

Police launched an investigation after the boy reported he had been assaulted in a toilet cubicle at Sainsbury’s in Burnley on Sunday, November 25th.

Walsh was due to appear before Blackburn Magistrates’ Court this morning (Tuesday, November 27th)
