Friday, October 4, 2019

We are appealing for information following a serious collision in Freckleton yesterday.

We were called at 11:45 hours on Thursday (3 October) to reports of the collision on Preston New Road.

It involved a Vauxhall Insignia and a Honda CBR 600F motorbike.

The rider of the motorbike- a 39 year old man from Preston - suffered a head injury and a number of broken bones. He was taken to hospital where he remains in a serious condition.

#### Sergeant Martin Wilcock of Lancashire’s Tactical Operations Division, said: “This was a serious collision and our thoughts are with the rider who is injured.

#### “If you saw the collision itself or have any information that may assist our investigation, please get in touch as soon as possible.”

You can call us on 101 or email [email protected] Please quote log number 549 of 3 October.
