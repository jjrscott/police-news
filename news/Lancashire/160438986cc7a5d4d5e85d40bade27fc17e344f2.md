Friday, December 28, 2018

Emergency services are currently at the scene of a collision on Wigan Road in Chorley near the junction with Brookwood Way.

The collision happened at about 8.40pm today (Friday, December 28th) and involved a single vehicle, an Audi RS3.

Wigan Road is closed between Dawson Lane and Shawbrook Close and is likely to remain shut for some time. Please avoid the area if you can.
