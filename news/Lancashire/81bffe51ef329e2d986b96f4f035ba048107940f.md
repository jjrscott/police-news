Sunday, March 24, 2019

The 16 year old girl who was missing from home in Lancaster has been found safe and well.

She had last been seen on Tuesday (March 19th) at around 10.30am.

She was located safe and well this morning (24 March).

Thanks to everyone who shared our appeal.
