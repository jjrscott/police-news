Sunday, January 27, 2019

Police are appealing for witnesses following a robbery.

The robbery took place at an address on Mossy Lea Road, Wrightington between 8.15pm and 8.40pm on Saturday (Jan 26th).

The victim, a 58 year old man, was assaulted at his home address by two offenders demanding money. The two men then made off having stolen various items of property.

Detectives are keen to hear from anyone that might have been in the area of Mossy Lea Road at this time and saw any suspicious people or vehicles, or who was driving in the area and has dashcam footage.

If you can assist us with our enquiries, please contact Skelmersdale Police on 101 quoting log 1344 of January 26th or independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
