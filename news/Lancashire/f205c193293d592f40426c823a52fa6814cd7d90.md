Thursday, June 18, 2020

###### TWO men have been charged by detectives as part of an investigation into trafficking and sexual abuse of women and girls in Preston.

###### Police launched an investigation in 2019 following reports of sexual assaults on a number of girls.

###### And now two men have been charged in connection with the inquiry.

###### Amos O’Cheng (7/6/69) and Dickson Ngaunde (20/12/83) are charged with a number of offences.

###### O’Cheng, of Winckley Square, Preston is charged with:

 * ###### Rape a girl aged 13 / 14 / 15 - SOA 2003 (05/11/2019 - 08/11/2019)

 * ###### Rape a woman 16 years of age or over - SOA 2003 (05/11/2019 - 08/11/2019)

 * ###### Rape a woman 16 years of age or over - SOA 2003 (08/10/2019 - 11/10/2019)

 * ###### Rape a woman 16 years of age or over - SOA 2003 (01/10/2019 - 31/10/2019)

 * ###### Rape a woman 16 years of age or over - SOA 2003 (17/10/2019)

 * ###### Arrange or facilitate travel of another person with a view to exploitation (01/09/2019 - 08/11/2019)

 * ###### Arrange or facilitate travel of another person with a view to exploitation (01/09/2019 - 08/11/2019)

 * ###### Arrange or facilitate travel of another person with a view to exploitation (06/10/2019)

 * ###### False imprisonment (06/10/2019)

 * ###### Attempting to Supply Class A Controlled Drug (15/10/2019 - 18/10/2019)

 * ###### Supply a controlled drug of Class A - Cocaine (05/11/2019 - 08/11/2019)

 * ###### Supply a controlled drug of Class A - Cocaine (05/11/2019 - 08/11/2019)

 * ###### Supply a controlled drug of Class B - Cannabis (01/09/2019 - 08/11/2019)

######

###### Ngaunde, of Stanford Avenue, Brighton, Sussex is charged with:

 * ###### Rape a woman 16 years of age or over - SOA 2003 (17/10/2019)

 * ###### Arrange or facilitate travel of another person with a view to exploitation (06/10/2019)

 * ###### False imprisonment (06/10/2019)

######

###### The allegations relate to six female victims aged between 14 and 19 years of age.

###### Ngaunde appeared before Preston Magistrates’ Court on Thursday, June 18th and was remanded in custody.

###### O’Cheng is due to appear before the same magistrates’ court on Friday, June 19th
