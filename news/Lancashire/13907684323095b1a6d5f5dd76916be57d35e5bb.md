Monday, October 8, 2018

POLICE searching for a missing youngster in Blackburn have a found a seriously injured boy.

Officers were called to a factory in the Infirmary area of the town at just after 8am today (Monday, October 8th) after members of staff found the boy on the floor of the factory. At this stage it appears he had fallen through a roof.

The youngster is believed to be nine year old Leon Langford who went missing from home on Sunday afternoon.

His condition is described as serious.
