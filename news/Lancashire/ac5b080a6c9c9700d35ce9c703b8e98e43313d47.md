Saturday, April 25, 2020

DETECTIVES investigating the rape of a woman in Blackpool have this evening (Saturday, April 25th) charged a man.

David Dara, 28, of no fixed address, is charged with rape and will appear before magistrates in the town on Monday (April 27th)

Police launched an enquiry after a 20-year-old woman was approached by a man and taken into an alleyway off Hopton Road at the side of the Home Bargains Store on Tyldesley Road in South Shore before she was raped.

The incident happened at about 10.30pm on Thursday, April 23rd

A 19-year-old local man who had been arrested in connection with the enquiry has been released without charge.

We would like to thank everyone in the community who came forward to assist with our appeal for information. Your support is hugely appreciated, as always.
