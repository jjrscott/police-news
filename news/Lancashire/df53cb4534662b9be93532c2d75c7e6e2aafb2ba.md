Friday, March 29, 2019

POLICE are appealing for witnesses after a cyclist was left with serious injuries following a collision.

The man, 69, from Rishton, was riding his Cannondale Synapse along Stonygate Lane, Ribchester at about 10.40am on Thursday, March 28th when for reasons as yet unknown he came off his bike sustaining life changing injuries and was taken by ambulance to Royal Preston hospital for treatment. .

Sgt Steve Hardman, of the force tactical operations department, said: “This collision has left a man with some very serious injuries and I would appeal to any witnesses or anyone who has any dashcam footage to get in touch.”
