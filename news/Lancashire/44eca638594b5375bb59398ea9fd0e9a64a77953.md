Thursday, March 25, 2021

We’re appealing for help in finding teenager Shelby Wilson who has gone missing from Morecambe.

The 13-year-old was last seen yesterday afternoon around 3.30pm walking along Westminster Road.

Shelby is described as 5 ft tall, of slim build, with blonde straight hair and speaks with a slight Scottish accent. She was last seen wearing a black jumper and grey joggers.

As well as Morecambe she also has links to Lancaster.

PC Emma Walker of Lancashire Police said: “We are concerned for Shelby’s health and well-being and would urge members of the public to help us with any sightings of her. Likewise, if you are reading this Shelby then please get in touch with us.”

Anyone with information can contact police on 101 or 01524 596920 quoting log 1720 of March 24th.
