Saturday, June 29, 2019

We were called by the Ambulance Service at 00:35 hours today (29 June) to reports a man in his 20s had been found unconscious on Edgar St, Accrington.

Following enquiries, detectives believe the man had earlier been involved in an altercation in the vicinity of Max Cabs on Edgar Street.

The man was taken to Royal Preston Hospital with suspected head injuries. His condition is described as stable and his injuries are not as serious as first thought.

No arrests have been made.
