Friday, January 4, 2019

A missing Preston man has been found.

Ray MacKay, 81, was last seen at his home address in the Broughton area at around 14:15 hours on 4 January.

He was found safe in the Penwortham area this morning (5 January).

Thanks to everyone who shared our appeal.
