Tuesday, August 4, 2020

Have you seen Jack Parker?

Concern is growing for the 23-year-old who is missing from the Garstang Road area of Preston.

Jack is white and 5ft 11ins tall. He was last seen wearing a dark jacket with a fur collar, jeans and trainers.

Jack also has links to Lostock Hall.

PC Steve Connolly, of South Police, said: “We are growing increasingly concerned about Jack and I would urge anyone who sees him or knows where he might be to get in touch. I would also ask Jack himself to contact us if he sees this appeal.”

Anyone with information should contact police on 101 quoting log 1257 of today (August 4th) For immediate sightings call 999.
