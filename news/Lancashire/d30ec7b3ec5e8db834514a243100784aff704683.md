Friday, September 20, 2019

A wanted sex offender has been arrested.

Hassan Ali, 26, of Brierfield, was wanted for breaching his sex offender notification requirements.

He was convicted at Bolton Crown Court in March 2015 of sexual activity with a child.

Ali was arrested earlier today (Friday, September 20) in the Blackburn area.
