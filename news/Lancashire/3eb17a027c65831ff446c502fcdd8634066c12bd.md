Thursday, January 16, 2020

CONCERN is growing for a man missing from home in Blackpool.

Jonathan Parker-Waters, who also goes by the name John Kelvin Milnes, 44, was last seen leaving an address on Richmond Avenue in the town between 10am and 11am on Sunday (January 12th)

He is described as white male, approx 6'2", medium build, with short light brown hair.

He was last seen wearing a long black coat with O2 on the chest, waterproof trousers and dark coloured trainers. He was carrying a navy holdall.

We would appeal for anyone who sees Jonathan or knows where he might be to get in touch. We would also appeal directly to Jonathan to contact us if he sees this appeal.

Anyone with information should contact police on 101 quoting log 1400 of January 16th. For immediate sightings please call 999.
