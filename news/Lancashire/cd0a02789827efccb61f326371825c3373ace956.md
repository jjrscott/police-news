Wednesday, November 28, 2018

We are investigating an incident in Blackburn which has left three men seriously injured.

At 22:34 hours on Tuesday (27th November), we were called to reports of a serious assault on Rhyl Avenue. Three men were found with a number of serious injuries and have been taken to hospital.

Following enquiries, two men have been arrested and are currently in police custody.

#### Inspector Simon Lynch, said: “We are at the very early stages of our investigation and we would urge any witnesses – who may have seen or heard a disturbance in the area – to get in touch with us immediately.

#### “At this stage, we believe this to be an isolated incident. Officers remain in the area overnight and there will be an increased police presence into the morning.

If you have any information please call 101 quoting log number 1441 of 27th November.
