Thursday, June 27, 2019

A man has been arrested in connection with an attempt armed robbery in Great Harwood.

We were called at around 1pm on Saturday 8th June to reports a man had entered Coral Bookmakers on Queen Street.

The man was holding a knife and made demands for money.

No money was handed over.

A 39 year old man from Manchester was arrested last night (26 June) on suspicion of robbery. He is currently in police custody.
