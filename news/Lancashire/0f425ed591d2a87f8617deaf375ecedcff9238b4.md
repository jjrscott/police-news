Saturday, November 2, 2019

Police are growing concerned for the welfare of a missing teenager.

Millie Maguire, 15, was last seen at about 7.45pm yesterday (November 1st) on Whitegate Drive in Blackpool.

Millie, from Morecambe, is white with shoulder length hair that is usually in a bun. She was last seen wearing a black jacket, black leggings and black trainers.

She also has links to Lancaster.

Please contact Lancashire police on 101 quoting log number 1735 of the 1st November 2019 if you have any information. Call 999 for immediate sightings.
