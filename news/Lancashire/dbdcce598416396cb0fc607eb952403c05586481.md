Sunday, December 20, 2020

Detectives investigating a rape in Catterall have tonight (Sunday, December 20) charged a man.

Police were called around 3.50am yesterday (Saturday, December 19) to a report a woman in her 20s had been attacked off Tan Yard Road.

Following extensive enquiries, a man was later arrested by police in connection with the incident.

After consultation with the Crown Prosecution Service, Connor Fearon, 25, of The Parklands, Catterall, has been charged with rape, sexual assault and threats to kill. He is due to appear at Blackpool Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Monday, December 21).
