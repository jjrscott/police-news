Wednesday, March 31, 2021

A man has been charged by police investigating the murder of a woman in Blackpool.

Police were called by the Ambulance Service around 9.30pm on Monday (March 29) to a report of a stabbing at an address on Dinmore Avenue.

Officers attended and sadly the body of a woman, later named as Simone Ambler, 49, from Blackpool, was found inside the property. She was pronounced dead at the scene.

A Home Office post mortem gave a cause of death as multiple stab wounds.

A man was arrested at the address and taken into custody.
