Monday, February 3, 2020

Have you seen Shaun Horan?

Shaun, 48, was last seen at Mariner's Way in Preston this morning and concern is growing for his welfare.

He is described as white, five ft 8ins, slim to medium build, with dark hair. He usually wears a dark jacket and jeans.

Shaun's car, a blue Toyota Auris, was found this morning (Monday) near Preston docks and police are continuing to search the area.

We would appeal to anyone who sees Shaun or has information on where he may be, to get in touch. We would also urge Shaun himself to contact us if he sees this appeal.

Anyone with information should contact police on 101 quoting log 0212 of today (February 3rd) For any immediate sightings dial 999.
