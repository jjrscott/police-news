Wednesday, November 4, 2020

Lee Barr, 24, from Darwen, wanted in connection with an assault has now been arrested and charged.

He was arrested at an address in Fleetwood and later charged with actual bodily harm and criminal damage, following an incident in Darwen last month.

Barr was released on police bail to appear at Blackburn Magistrates on March 5th 2021.
