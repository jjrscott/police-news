Tuesday, January 8, 2019

THE M58 motorway is currently closed following a serious collision.

The collision involves mutliple vehicles and emergency services are currently at the scene.

A full closure is in place between J3 at Bickerstaffe and J4 at Skelmersdale in both directions. Please avoid the area where possible.

The closure will be in place for some time so please bear with us. We will update you when we can. Thank you for your continued patience.
