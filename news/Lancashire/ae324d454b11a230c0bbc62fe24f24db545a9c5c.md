Saturday, November 2, 2019

Have you seen Dean Haynes?

Concern is growing for Dean who is missing from home. He was last seen leaving the Wetherspoons pub in Morecambe at 6.15pm today (Saturday, November 2nd)

Dean, 45, is described as white, clean shaven with a shaved head. He was last seen wearing dark clothing including a black bomber jacket.

PC Emily Walker, of West Police, said: “We are growing increasingly concerned about Dean and I would urge anyone who sees him or knows where me may be to get in touch. I would also ask Dean himself to contact us if he sees this appeal.”

Anyone with information should contact police on 101 quoting log 1321 of November 2nd. For immediate sightings call 999.
