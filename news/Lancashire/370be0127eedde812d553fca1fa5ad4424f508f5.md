Thursday, June 14, 2018

POLICE are appealing for help to trace a wanted man after a woman was assaulted.

Officers want to trace Leon Uzan, 28, after a 54 year old woman was attacked at an address in Windmill Rise, Blackpool on May 12th.

The woman was punched in the face and left with two black eyes.

Uzan is white, short black hair, stubble, slim build, height 5’11”, and known to frequent the Blackburn area.

> Detective Constable Alex Summers, of Blackburn Police, said: “This was a serious assault which left a woman with significant injuries and we need the public’s help to trace Uzan so we can speak to him. I would urge anyone who sees him or knows where he might be to get in touch”

Any information can be passed to 101 quoting log 1356 of the 12th May 2018
