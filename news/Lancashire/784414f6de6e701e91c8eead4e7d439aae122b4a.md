Tuesday, December 18, 2018

We are appealing for witnesses after a collision in Preston left a cyclist with serious injuries.

The collision happened on the B6242 at the junction with Lancashire Way at about 5.10pm yesterday (Monday, December 17th)

A Vauxhall Astra collided with a pedal cycle leaving the rider, a 47 year old man from Preston, with serious injuries. He was taken by ambulance t the Royal Preston Hospital where he remains.

Sgt Paul Maguire, of Tac Ops, said: “This collision has left a man with some serious injuries and I would appeal for any witnesses or anyone with dashcam footage to get in touch.”

Anyone with information should contact police on 101 quoting log number 1030 of December 17th.
