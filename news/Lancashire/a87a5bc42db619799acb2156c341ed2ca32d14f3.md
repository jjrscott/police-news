Friday, January 4, 2019

A wanted Preston man has been arrested.

Police wanted to speak to Miguel Di Palma 21, after a report of violent disorder in Preston.

Police were called at shortly after 1am on Sunday 23rd December 2018 to reports a vehicle had collided with a building on Plungington Road and weapons had been produced.

Di Palma was arrested on Saturday (19th January) and has been bailed pending further enquiries.
