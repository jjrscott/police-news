Wednesday, April 10, 2019

Concern is growing for a missing man.

Darryl Watts, 29, was last seen at the Royal Preston Hospital at 4.30pm yesterday (Tuesday, April 9th)

He is described as white, 5ft 4ins, medium build, tanned, with brown short hair.

He was last seen wearing a red and black tracksuit top, black t shirt, and dark trousers. He was carrying a brown holdall.

Mr Watts has links to Lancaster, Morecambe and Rochdale.

PC Mark Emery, of Preston Police, said: "We are growing increasingly concerned about Darryl and i would urge anyone who sees him or knows where he might be to get in touch."

Anyone with information should contact police on 101 quoting log number 1077 of April 9th.
