Tuesday, October 29, 2019

Have you seen Ben Fay?

He is wanted for absconding from HMP Kirkham on Sunday (27 October).

Fay, 28, from Rochdale, was sentenced to four years in prison at Minshull Street Crown Court in June 2018 for aggravated vehicle taking, dangerous driving and possession of class A drugs with intent to supply.

Fay is described as being 6ft tall of medium build. He has brown eyes, receding brown hair and a Northern accent.

He has links to Rochdale and Lancashire.

If you have seen him or have any information about where he might be, please call us on 101 quoting log number 1530 of 27 October.

Alternatively you can contact independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
