Wednesday, December 30, 2020

Do you recognise this man seen here on dashcam footage?

We want to speak to him in connection with thefts from vehicles in Barnoldswick in the early hours of Boxing Day.

Items stolen include; money, a mobile phone and a CD player taken from a black Skoda, which was parked on Rook Street, Barnoldswick, at around 3.40am on December 26th.

A satellite navigation system and a radio were also reported stolen from a red Volkswagen Scirocco at around a similar time, on Skipton Road in Barnoldswick.

### PC Peter Burns of Lancashire Police said: “ _We are appealing for information in relation to the theft from vehicles in Barnoldswick where the windows were smashed to gain entry overnight on Boxing Day._

### _“We would like to identify the man in the footage and speak to him regarding the thefts. If you can identify him please get in touch with us. As always thank you for your help.”_

_Anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101 or email[email protected] quoting log reference 0363 or 0379 of 26th December._
