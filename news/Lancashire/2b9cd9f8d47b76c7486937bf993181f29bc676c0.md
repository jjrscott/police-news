Sunday, December 30, 2018

A man has been charged with a stabbing in Preston on Christmas Day.

Warren Givens, 44, of Blackpool Road, Preston is accused of Section 18 wounding and possession of a bladed article and will appear before Preston Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Monday, December 31st)

Police launched an investigation after a man was stabbed on Eldon Street in the city on December 25th. The victim suffered serious but not life threatening injuries.
