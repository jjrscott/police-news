Friday, May 31, 2019

Have you seen this man?

We’re growing concerned for the welfare of Chris Forshaw, 59, who is missing from home in Skelmersdale.

Chris was last seen at 14:50 hours yesterday (30 May) in the Liverpool Road area.

He was wearing a Joules polo shirt, blue jeans and brown suede shoes.

Chris is described as being 6ft2 in tall with grey short hair. He wears glasses and has a tattoo of ‘CF’ and a bird on his forearm.

Chris may be using a vehicle –a white Vauxhall Astra with the registration VU08NHT.

As well as Skelmersdale, he has links to Westhoughton, Bickerstaffe, Kirkby and Simonswood.

#### PC Sam Rigby said: “If you have seen Chris or know where he may be please get in touch with us as soon as possible.

#### “Chris – if you are reading this please let someone know you are okay.”

Anyone with information should call 01695 566160 or 101 quoting log number LC-20190530-1271
