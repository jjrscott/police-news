Thursday, May 25, 2017

A man has been charged with the murder of a woman in Chorley.

Police were called at around 10.25pm on Tuesday, May 23rd after the body of 30 year old Gemma Leeming was found deceased in a bedroom at an address on Hollinshead Street. A post mortem examination has shown the cause of death was strangulation.

Following consultation with the Crown Prosecution Service, Craig O’Sullivan, 39, of Chorcliffe Mews, Hollinshead Street, Chorley has been charged with her murder and is due to appear before Preston Magistrates’ Court tomorrow morning (Saturday, May 27th).
