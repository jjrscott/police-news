Serious sexual assault - Sydenham bypass, Belfast

Detectives are investigating the report of a serious sexual assault in the area of the Sydenham bypass in Belfast on the evening of Saturday 12th March.

A woman reported having been attacked by a man on Saturday evening between 10pm and 11pm. The man approached the woman who was walking country wards along the Sydenham Bypass, between Dee Street and Belfast City Airport.
