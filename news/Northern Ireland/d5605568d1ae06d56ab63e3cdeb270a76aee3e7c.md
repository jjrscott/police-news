* Belfast

Detectives have arrested two men following a hijacking in Cupar Street Lower in west Belfast on Sunday 25 October.

At around 2.15pm the driver of a white Transit van was pulled from his vehicle by an unknown man after making a delivery to a nearby house.

The van was subsequently located on fire on Ballygomartin Road a short time later.

The two suspects, aged 28 and 33, remain in custody this morning.

Anyone with information regarding this crime is asked to contact police by calling 101, quoting reference 930 25/10/20. You can also submit information online using our non-emergency reporting form via http://www.psni.police.uk/makeareport/. Information can also be provided to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 which is 100% anonymous
