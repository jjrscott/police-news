* Mid & East Antrim

Petrol bomb thrown at police: Castlemara, Carrickfergus

A petrol bomb has been thrown at a police vehicle in Carrickfergus.

The device was thrown by two masked men at in the Castlemara Drive area at around 2.20am this morning, Sunday 29 May. It struck the vehicle, bouncing off and igniting the road. No damage was caused to the police vehicle.

Anyone with information can contact police on 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
