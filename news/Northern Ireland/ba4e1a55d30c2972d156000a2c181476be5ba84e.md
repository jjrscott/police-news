* Lisburn & Castlereagh

Detectives from PSNI Major Investigation Team have launched a murder investigation following the discovery of a man's body at a house in Coulson Avenue in Lisburn .

Detective Chief Inspector Eamonn Corrigan said: "The 50 year old was found in his living room at around 8.45pm on Friday night, 21 June. A post mortem examination will be carried out in due course to determine the cause of his death.

Our enquiries are at a very early stage and I am appealing for anyone with information that could potentially assist the investigation to please get in touch by calling 101. Or alternatively, information can also be provided to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 which is 100% anonymous and gives people the power to speak up and stop crime."
