* Fermanagh & Omagh

A man who was critically injured in a collision in Newtownbutler at the weekend has now passed away.

Inspector Keir Kitt said: "The 21 year old was a passenger in a Peugeot 206 which crashed on the Clones Road at around 8.45pm on Sunday, 20 August.

The 22 year old driver of the vehicle was pronounced deceased at the scene. A third man, also in his 20s, sustained non-life threatening injuries.

We are continuing to investigate the circumstances of the collision. Witnesses or anyone with information can contact us by calling 101, quoting reference number 1280 20/08/17.
