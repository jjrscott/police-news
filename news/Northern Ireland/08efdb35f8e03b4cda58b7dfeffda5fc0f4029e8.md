Detectives appeal for information following an aggravated burglary in Ballywalter

Detectives are investigating an aggravated burglary in Ballywalter.

Two men armed with hammers entered a property in Meadow Park just before 7.30am this morning, Sunday 2 April.

They attacked two men who were inside but were overpowered by the occupants and fled.

Detective Constable David Cameron is appealing for witnesses, “We are interested in hearing from anyone who was in the area this morning or anyone with any information that could assist us. Please call 101 quoting reference 456 02/04/17.”
