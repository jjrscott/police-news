Detectives investigating the report a burglary in south Belfast today are appealing for information.

Detective Sergeant Corrigan said: "We received a report at 10:30am on Saturday 4th January that entry had been gained to a property in Inishowen Drive sometime between 3pm on Thursday 2nd January and the time the incident was reported to us.

"It was reported a sum of money was taken.

I want to appeal to anyone who was in the area between these times and who noticed any suspicious activity, or has information which may assist our investigation to call detectives at Musgrave on 101, quoting reference number 472 of 04/01/20."

Alternatively, information can also be provided to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 which is 100% anonymous and gives people the power to speak up and stop crime.
