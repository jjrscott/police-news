Arrest in relation to murder of Edward Gibson, West Belfast

Detectives from Serious Crime Branch investigating the murder of Edward Gibson in west Belfast in October 2014 have arrested a 27-year-old woman on suspicion of conspiracy to murder and withholding information.

She was arrested in the West Belfast area this morning and is currently helping police with their enquiries.

Mr Gibson died after being shot in an incident on 24 October, 2014, in the Clonfadden Crescent area of west Belfast.

We are keen to hear from witnesses of this incident

The Police Service of Northern Ireland is appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or anyone with any information that will assist the investigation to contact us on the non-emergency number 101. Information can also be passed anonymously via the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
