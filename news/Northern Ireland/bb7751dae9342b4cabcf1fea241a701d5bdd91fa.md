* Newry, Mourne & Down

Police can confirm that a woman has died following a collision on the Belfast Road in Downpatrick last night, Saturday, October 31st.

The single vehicle collision occurred at around 10.30pm.

Police would like to hear from witnesses or anyone who may have captured dashcam footage in the area at around the time of the crash. Please call 101 quoting reference 2378 of 31/10/20.
