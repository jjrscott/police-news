Police in Ards are investigating an arson attack on a vehicle in Blackwood Green.

A Vauxhall Astra was deliberately set alight, using accelerant, at around 10.25pm last night, Thursday 17 August. The boot of the vehicle was damaged by the flames.

Inspector Richard Murray said; “We are keen to hear from anyone with information about this crime. Officers can be contacted by calling 101, quoting reference number 1357 of 17/08/17.”

Information can also be given anonymously to the Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
