* Belfast

Assault - Ardoyne Road, Belfast

Police are appealing for information following the report of an assault on two male youths on Saturday, 21 May.

Shortly after 3.00pm it was reported that the two youths, aged 13 and 14, had been assaulted by two unknown males as they walked up the Ardoyne Road, Belfast.

Police are treating this incident as a sectarian hate crime.

Inspector Wise would appeal to anyone who may have witnessed this incident to contact Police at Tennent Street on the non-emergency number 101. Alternatively, if someone would prefer to provide information without giving their details they can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers and speak to them anonymously on 0800 555 111.
