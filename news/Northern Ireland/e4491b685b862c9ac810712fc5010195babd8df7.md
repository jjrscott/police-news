* Belfast

Major Investigation Team detectives are investigating the circumstances surrounding the death of a 49 year old man at Kings Road in Tullycarnet, Belfast last night, Thursday 17 October.
A 26 year old man has been arrested in the Belfast area and is currently in Musgrave Serious Crime Suite where he is being questioned by detectives.
A post mortem is due to take place to determine the cause of death and there are no further details at this time.
Detective Chief Inspector Darren McCartney said: "I would appeal to anyone who was in the Kings Road area last night between 6pm and 8pm and saw anything that may be of assistance to our investigation, please call 101 and ask to speak to detectives in Ladas Drive."
