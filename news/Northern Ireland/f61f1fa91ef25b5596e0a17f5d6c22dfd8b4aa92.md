Lisburn and Castlereagh police invite you to join them in their virtual carol service to celebrate Christmas. They are joined by special guests from Lisburn Central Primary School, Councillor Nicholas Trimble, Mayor of Lisburn and Castlereagh City Council, Christ Church Lisburn, Lisburn Food Bank, Our Lady and St Patrick's College Knock and Holy Trinity Church Ballylesson.

Merry Christmas from the Police Service of Northern Ireland's officers and staff in Lisburn and Castlereagh District.
