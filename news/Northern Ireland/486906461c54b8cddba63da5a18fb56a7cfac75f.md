Detectives from Serious Crime Branch investigating the murder of Conor McKee in north Belfast last January have arrested a 42 year old man.

The suspect was detained in the north of the city this morning and taken to a police station in Belfast for questioning.

31 year old Conor was found dead at his Glenpark Street home on 7 January by his mother. He had been shot a number of times with a shotgun.

**UPDATE - The 42 year old man was released unconditionally on the evening of Thursday 7 April.**
