Police are appealing for information following two cars being set alight in the College Grange area of Craigavon on the evening of Monday 4 April.

At approximately 11.20pm, police received a report that the two cars had been set alight by petrol bombs.

Sergeant Gibson said: "I would ask anyone who knows anything about this incident to contact Lurgan Police Station on the non-emergency number 101. Or if someone would prefer to provide information without giving their details, they can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers and speak to them anonymously on 0800 555 111."
