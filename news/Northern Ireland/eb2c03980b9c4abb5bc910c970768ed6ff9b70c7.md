* Belfast

Man charged with possession of firearms

Detectives from Serious Crime Branch investigating the murder of Stephen Carson at Walmer Street in south Belfast on Thursday, 25 February have charged a 39 year old man with possession of a firearm in suspicious circumstances.

He is due to appear at Belfast Magistrates Court on Wednesday, 2 March.
