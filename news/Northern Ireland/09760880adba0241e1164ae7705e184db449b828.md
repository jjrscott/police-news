* Belfast

Aggravated burglary - College Square North, Belfast

Detectives are appealing for information following an aggravated burglary at a flat in the College Square North area of Belfast yesterday, Saturday, 14 May.

Police received a report that sometime between 4.45pm and 5.00pm three males forced their way into the flat. One male stayed at the front door and the other two assaulted the male occupant. He received minor injuries to his head.

Detective Sergeant Michelle Griffin would ask anyone who was in the area and noticed any suspicious activity to contact Detectives at Reactive and Organised Crime at Musgrave PSNI Station on the non-emergency number 101. Or, if someone would prefer to provide information without giving their details they can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers and speak to them anonymously on 0800 555 111.
