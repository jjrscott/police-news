Arson & arrest, Drumgor Heights, Craigavon

A 35 year old man has been arrested following a car being set on fire in the Drumgor Heights area of Craigavon on the evening of Wednesday 16 March.

Chief Inspector Andrew Freeburn said: "At approximately 9.10pm, police received a report that a car was on fire. Police attended and at around 10pm a 35 year old man was arrested on suspicion of a number of offences including arson, threats to kill, common assault and assault on police.

"The man is currently in custody assisting police with their enquiries."
