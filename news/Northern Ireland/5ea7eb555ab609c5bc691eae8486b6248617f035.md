* Belfast

Update- Attempted aggravated burglary - Dhu Varren Crescent, North Belfast

The 26 year old man and 29 year old woman arrested following an attempted aggravated burglary that occurred in the Dhu Varren Crescent area of North Belfast in the early hours of this morning, Saturday 18 June have been released on bail pending further police enquiries.
