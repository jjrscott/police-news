* Belfast

Detectives are appealing for information following the report of a burglary at a property in the Causeway Meadows area of Lisburn on Wednesday, April 24th.

Detective Sergeant Melanie McGrory said: "Police received a report at around 7.20pm that two men had forced their way into the property. Two youths in the property at the time of the incident locked themselves in a room and raised the alarm. It is not believed anything was taken during the incident.

"Enquiries are continuing and we are appealing to anyone who witnessed anything suspicious in the area to contact detectives in Lisburn on 101, quoting reference number 1205 of 24/04/19.

Alternatively, information can also be provided to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 which is 100% anonymous and gives people the power to speak up and stop crime.
