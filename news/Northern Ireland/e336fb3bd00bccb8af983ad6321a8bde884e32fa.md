* Derry City & Strabane

Detectives in Derry/Londonderry are appealing for witnesses to an incident in Drumleck Gardens during the early hours of this morning, Sunday 26 May.

A 32 year old man was taken to hospital with serious injuries after he was assaulted and then struck by a van at around 1.40am.

The assailant then made off in the van.

Detective Inspector Conor McStravick said: "We would like to hear from anyone who was in the area or who may know the current whereabouts of the van, which is believed to be a blue Vauxhall. Detectives can be contacted at Strand Road police station by calling 101, quoting reference 225 26/05/19."
