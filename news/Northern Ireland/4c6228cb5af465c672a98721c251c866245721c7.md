* Ards & North Down

Motorcyclist killed in Ards crash named

A motorcyclist who died in a collision on Belfast Road, Newtownards on Sunday 31 July was 21 year old Jay Curtis Reardon from Holywood Hills.

The collision occurred at around 12.50pm on Sunday afternoon close to the junction with Milecross Road.

Inspector Jim McGrillen said: “Our investigation into the circumstances of the collision is at an early stage and I am appealing for witnesses. We would especially like to hear from the driver of a blue or purple Ford Kuga who is believed to have stopped at the scene but left prior to the arrival of police.

The number to call is 101, quote reference 663 31/07/16.”
