* Ards & North Down

Detectives investigating the murder of Colin Horner have arrested a man

Mr Horner was shot dead outside a Bangor supermarket in front of his young son on 28 May 2017.

Detectives arrested a 29 year old man earlier this morning, Friday 16 June, as part of an operation in the Newtownards area.

Detective Chief Inspector Alan Dickson said: “The suspect has been taken to the Serious Crime Suite at Musgrave police station in Belfast for questioning.”
