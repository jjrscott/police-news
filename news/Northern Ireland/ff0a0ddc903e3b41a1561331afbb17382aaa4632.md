* Belfast

Youth charged following death of woman after fatal collision, Colin Glen Forest Park

Police can confirm that, following the recent incident at Colin Glen Forest Park, a young mother of 3, Valerie Armstrong, has passed away surrounded by her family.

The family have requested privacy at this time to grieve for their loss.

A 17 year old male arrested in connection with the incident has been charged with a number of offences and is expected to appear before Belfast Magistrates Court tomorrow, Thursday 21 July. As is normal procedure all charges will be reviewed by the Public Prosecution Service.

A 15 year old male also arrested in connection with the incident has been released on Police bail pending further enquiries."
