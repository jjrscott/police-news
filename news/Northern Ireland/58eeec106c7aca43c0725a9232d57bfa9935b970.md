* Fermanagh & Omagh

Detectives investigating the fatal fire in Derrylin have charged a 27 year old male with four counts of murder and one count of arson with intent to endanger life.

The charges follows the blaze which broke out at a house in the Molly Road area of the village on Tuesday February 27, 2018.

The man is due to appear at Enniskillen Magistrates Court on Monday morning 5 March.
