Police charge two men over Carrickfergus murder

Detectives from PSNI Serious Crime Branch investigating the murder of George Gilmore in Carrickfergus on Monday 13th March have charged two men aged 28 and 35 with murder, two counts of attempted murder and possession of a firearm. Both men are due to appear at Belfast MC on Saturday 18th March.

As is normal procedure the charges are reviewed by the PPS.
