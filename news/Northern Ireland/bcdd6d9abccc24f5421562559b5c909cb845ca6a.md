* Causeway Coast & Glens

Fatal road traffic collision - Agivey Road, Aghadowey

Police investigating the fatal road traffic collision on the Agivey Road, Aghadowey on Saturday, 14 May in which a 19 year old man died are appealing for witnesses.

Inspector Ian Armour would appeal to anyone who was travelling on the Agivey Road at approximately 5.00pm and may have witnessed this incident to contact the Collision Investigation Unit on the non-emergency number 101. He would particularly like to appeal to any motorcyclists who had a helmet cam in operation and who may have captured this incident to contact police on the 101 number.
