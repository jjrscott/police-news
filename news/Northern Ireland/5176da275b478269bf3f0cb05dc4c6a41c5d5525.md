* Belfast

Detectives from PSNI's Major Investigation Team have arrested a man in connection with the murder of Jim Donegan.

Mr Donegan (43), was murdered as he sat in his car outside his son's school on the Glen Road in west Belfast on the 4th December 2018.

A 32 year old man was detained in the Springfield Road area earlier arrested on suspicion of murder and on suspicion of membership of a proscribed organisation.

He is currently in custody at Musgrave Serious Crime Suite where he is being questioned by detectives.
