* Belfast

Detectives from PSNI Major Investigation Team investigating the murder of a 53 year old woman in Whincroft Way, east Belfast have confirmed her identity as Alice Morrow.

Detective Chief Inspector Eamonn Corrigan said: "The 53-year-old's body was discovered in a property in Whincroft Way late last night. A post-mortem examination will take place but I am treating her death as murder at this time.

A 42-year-old male arrested on suspicion of murder and remains in custody.

I am appealing for anyone who was in the area of Whincroft Way yesterday and who witnessed anything or anyone with any information that can assist my investigation contact Detectives on 101 quoting reference 1345 10/03/19."
