* Belfast

Belfast Traffic and travel information Sunday 25 September

Road users in north Belfast are advised to allow additional time for their journey on Sunday 25 September. A parade will be taking place between 1.30pm and 3.30pm in the areas of Clifton Street and Crumlin Road.

Anyone travelling to the Mater Hospital complex between these times is advised to approach from Agnes Street and Cliftonpark Avenue.
