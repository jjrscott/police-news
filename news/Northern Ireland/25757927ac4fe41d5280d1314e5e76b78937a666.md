* Belfast

Assault - Falls Park, Falls Road, Belfast

Police are appealing for information following the report of an assault on a teenage girl in the Falls Park area of the Falls Road, Belfast yesterday, Saturday, 14 May.

At approximately 9.00pm as the girl was walking in the Falls Park area she was assaulted by a group of unknown females.

Police are treating this assault as a sectarian hate crime.

Inspector Kelly Moore would appeal to anyone who was in the area around this time and who may have witnessed this assault to contact Police at Woodbourne PSNI Station on the non-emergency number 101. Alternatively, if someone would prefer to provide information without giving their details they can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers and speak to them anonymously on 0800 555 111.
