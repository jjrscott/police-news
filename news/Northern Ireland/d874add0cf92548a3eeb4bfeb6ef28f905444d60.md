* Fermanagh & Omagh

Attack on Orange Hall - Plumbridge Road, Newtownstewart

Police are investigating an incident of criminal damage at Strawletterdallon Orange Hall, Plumbridge Road, Newtownstewart overnight last night, Tuesday 03 May.

Shortly after 7am this morning it was reported that a number of windows and the door of the hall had been damaged.

Police are treating this incident as a hate crime and Inspector Alison Ferguson is appealing for anyone with any information that can assist with the investigation to contact officers in Strabane Police Station on 101, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
