Man charged in connection with serious sexual assault – Peatlands Park Dungannon

Detectives from PSNI’s Rape Crime Unit, a serious sexual assault on a woman in the Peatlands Park area of Dungannon on Wednesday, 27 April, have charged a 23 year old male with rape.

He is expected to appear before Omagh Magistrates Court on Monday 02 May.
