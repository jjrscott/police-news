* Belfast

Aggravated burglary in east Belfast

Detectives are appealing for information following an aggravated burglary at a house in east Belfast overnight.

The property on Trillick Street was entered by three men armed with bats shortly after midnight.

The trio caused damage inside the house and threatened the residents.

Detective Sergeant Keith Wilson said: “We are keen to hear from anyone with information about this crime. Detectives can be contacted at the offices of Reactive and Organised Crime, Musgrave Police station by calling 101, quoting reference number 22 09/06/17.”

Information can also be given anonymously to the Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
