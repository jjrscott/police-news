* Derry City & Strabane

A pedestrian has died following a collision on the Foreglen Road near Claudy.

58 year old Eamon McCafferty was struck by a car at around 2am this morning, Saturday 18 August.

Inspector Peter Duncan said: “We are appealing for witnesses or anyone who was travelling on the Foreglan Road at around 2am to contact us. We are especially keen to hear from anyone who may have captured dashcam footage along the route at around the time of the collision.

Please call 101, quoting reference 154 18/08/18.”
