* Antrim & Newtownabbey

Detectives in Antrim are appealing for information following a report of a burglary in the Carnmoney Road area of Newtownabbey on Wednesday 27th March. ** _
_**

Detective Sergeant Colin Gray said: "It was reported that entry was gained by forcing a door at the rear of the property sometime between 3:30 pm and 6:30 pm. The female occupant was not at home at the time.

"Nothing was stolen during the incident, however damage was caused to a rear door.

"I am appealing for anyone with information to contact detectives at Antrim Police station on 101 quoting reference 1104 27/03/19."

Alternatively, information can also be provided to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 which is 100% anonymous and gives people the power to speak up and stop crime.
