* Mid & East Antrim

Security alert - Carrickfergus

The security alert in Carrickfergus has ended.

A suspicious object located in an alleyway between Lancasterian Street and Albert Road this morning was examined by ATO and was found to be nothing untoward.

Evacuated residents and businesses have been allowed to return to their properties.
