* Fermanagh & Omagh

Woman injured in crash - Lisnarick

A woman in her 40s has been taken to hospital following a collision, involving a single quad bike, in Lisnarick.

It’s understood the Kawasaki KLF 300 overturned on the Liscreevin Road at around 11.25am this morning, Tuesday 15 March.

Inspector Robert McGowan said: “The woman has sustained a serious head injury and is currently undergoing treatment. Her condition is believed to be serious.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or who saw the quad travelling on the road prior to the time of the report is asked to contact police on 101.”
