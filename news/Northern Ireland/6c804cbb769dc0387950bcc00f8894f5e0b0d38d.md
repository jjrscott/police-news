* Armagh City, Banbridge & Craigavon

Man charged with murder of Laura Marshall - Lurgan

Detectives from PSNI Serious Crime Branch investigating the murder of 31 year old Laura Marshall in the Victoria Street area of Lurgan have charged a 36 year old man to appear at Craigavon Magistrates Court on Friday 8 April.
