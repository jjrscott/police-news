A 44-year-old woman has been charged with criminal damage and is due to appear at Newry Magistrate's Court on 19 December.

As is normal procedure the charge will be reviewed by the Public Prosecution Service.

The charge is in relation to an incident of criminal damage caused to wreaths and wooden crosses that had been placed at a war memorial in the Sugar Island area of city.
