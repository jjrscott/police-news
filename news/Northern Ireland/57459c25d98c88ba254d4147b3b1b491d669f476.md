* Newry, Mourne & Down

UPDATE: Arrest in connection with Eugene Carr murder, Clogharevan Park, Bessbrook

A 30 year old man, arrested on Wednesday 1 June, as part of the investigation into the murder of 73 year old Eugene Carr from Bessbrook, has been released on police bail pending further enquiries.
