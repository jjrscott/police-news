* Mid Ulster

Detectives investigating a burglary at the home of a woman with severe physical disabilities in Dungannon last night, Saturday 03 November, have charged a 27 year old man with burglary.

He is expected to appear before Enniskillen Magistrates Court tomorrow morning, Monday 05 November.
