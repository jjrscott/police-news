* Belfast

Detectives investigating the murder of Robbie Lawlor have arrested a 36 year old man on suspicion of murder. The male was detained last night and remains in custody at present.

Robbie Lawlor was shot a number of times outside an address in Etna Drive in north Belfast on Saturday 4 April. He was pronounced dead at the scene.

The murder weapon has not yet been recovered.

This was a brutal killing and police are appealing for the public's help to bring the gunman to justice.

Anyone with any information about this murder is asked to contact detectives on 101 quoting reference number 562 04/04/20, whilst anyone with CCTV, dashcam or mobile phone footage recorded in the area of Etna Drive and Kingston Court on Saturday is asked to upload it to Major Incident Public Portal, which can be accessed from any device with internet access on https://mipp.police.uk/operation/PSNI20P04-PO1
