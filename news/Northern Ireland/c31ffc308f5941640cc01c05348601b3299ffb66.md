* Mid Ulster

Dungannon drugs seizure

Police have released a photograph of £400,000 worth of drugs seized near Dungannon on Saturday.

Three men aged 29, 39 and 53 were remanded in custody by Enniskillen Magistrates Court today as part of the investigation.

The £400,000 worth of cannabis and cocaine were seized along with cash during searches of houses and vehicles in the Dungannon area on Saturday.

We are keen to hear from witnesses of this incident

The Police Service of Northern Ireland is appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or anyone with any information that will assist the investigation to contact us on the non-emergency number 101. Information can also be passed anonymously via the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
