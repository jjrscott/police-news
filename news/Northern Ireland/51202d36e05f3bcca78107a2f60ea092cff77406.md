* Mid & East Antrim

A woman has been arrested after a man was stabbed in Larne.

The 53 year old victim sustained a number of wounds during an incident at a house on Coastguard Road at around 11pm last night, Tuesday 3 April.

He was given first aid at the scene by police officers before being transferred to hospital where is condition this morning is described as serious but stable.

A 56 year old woman was detained at the scene and remains in police custody assisting with enquiries. Detectives are appealing for anyone with information to get in touch. They are particularly keen to hear from anyone who was in the Coastguard Road area at around 11pm last night.

Detectives can be contacted by calling 101, quoting reference number 1462 03/04/18.
