* Mid & East Antrim

Detectives in Larne are investigating an arson attack on a car at Ardclinis Gardens in the early hours of Wednesday 13th September.

Detective Chief Inspector Dunny McCubbin said: “A parked Ford Focus car had a window broken and flammable liquid poured inside, before being set alight shortly after 1am on Wednesday morning. Damage was caused to the inside of the car as a result of the incident. We are currently investigating a possible link between this incident and other recent incidents in the Larne area.”

“I would ask anyone with any information to contact detectives in Larne on 101 quoting reference number 31 13/09/17 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.”
