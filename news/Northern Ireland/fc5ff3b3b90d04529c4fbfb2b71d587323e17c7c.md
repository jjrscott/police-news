* Belfast

Robbery and Theft - Albertbridge Road, Belfast

Detectives investigating a robbery at commercial premises in the Albertbridge Road, Belfast at approximately 11.50am on Tuesday, 12 April and a theft at another commercial premises on Albertbridge Road at approximately 11.35am on the same day have arrested two men aged 29 and 33.

Both are currently in police custody.

Detective Sergeant Keith Wilson would appeal to anyone who may have witnessed anyone acting suspiciously in the Albertbridge Road area between 11.30am and 11.50am to contact Detectives at Reactive and Organised Crime Branch at Musgrave PSNI Station on the non-emergency number 101. Or, if someone would prefer to provide information without giving their details they can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers and speak to them anonymously on 0800 555 111.
