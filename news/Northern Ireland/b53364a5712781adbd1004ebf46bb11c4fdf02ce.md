* Belfast

UPDATE: Man arrested on suspicion of brothel keeping: Cromwell Road, Belfast

A man arrested following the discovery of an alleged brothel in south Belfast has been released on police bail.

The 38 year old, who was detained at a house on Cromwell Road on the morning of Saturday 28 May, will return for further questioning at a later date.

Three women who were at the property have been receiving support and are to be spoken to by detectives.
