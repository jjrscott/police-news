* Belfast

Man charged in relation to £30k cannabis seizure, North Belfast

Detectives from Reactive and Organised Crime Branch investigating the seizure of cannabis with an estimated street value of £30k in Skegoneill, north Belfast in March, have charged a 47 year old man with importation of a class B drug, attempted possession of a class B drug and attempted possession of a class B drug with intent to supply.

He is due to appear at Belfast MC on Friday 22nd April.

As is normal procedure the charges are reviewed by the PPS.

This is the third man to be charged in relation to this investigation.
