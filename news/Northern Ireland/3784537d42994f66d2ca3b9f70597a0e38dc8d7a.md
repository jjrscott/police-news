* Derry City & Strabane

Police are appealing for information following the report of an assault in the Ballymagorry area of Strabane on Sunday, December 9.

Constable Moreland said: “Police are seeking to identify the male who we believe was the victim of an assault which is reported to have taken place at approximately 7.00am. We would urge him to come forward and speak to Police as we are keen to establish what took place that morning.”

“If anyone has any information regarding this incident please contact Strabane police on 101 quoting reference number 675 of 09/12/18.”

Alternatively, information can also be provided to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 which is 100% anonymous and gives people the power to speak up and stop crime.
