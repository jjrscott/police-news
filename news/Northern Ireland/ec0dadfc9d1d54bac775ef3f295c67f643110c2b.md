Earlier today (Thursday, 27 July) a Tweet was posted on the Chief Constable’s Twitter account. Head of PSNI’s Cyber Crime Centre, Detective Chief Inspector Michael Harvey, said: “At this time we believe there is a possibility the account was maliciously hacked. Enquiries are currently ongoing. There are no further details at this time.”
