* Mid & East Antrim

Detectives in Larne are appealing for information following an arson attack on two parked cars at Magee Park in the early hours of Sunday 10th September.

Detective Sergeant Pete Crothers said: “Police were contacted at around 3.20am and informed that a silver Peugeot 407 and a white Audi A4 had been set alight outside a house in Magee Park. Both cars were destroyed and the house extensively damaged as a result of the fire, which was extinguished by firefighters. Our enquiries are ongoing and I would appeal to anyone who noticed any suspicious activity in the area or who has any information to contact detectives in Larne on 101 quoting reference number 325 10/09/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.”
