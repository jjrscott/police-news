Man charged with 29 offences in connection with car thefts

A 29 year old man has been charged with a total of 29 counts of theft and is expected to appear in court in Newry tomorrow morning, Saturday 29 April.

As is usual procedure the charges will be reviewed by the PPS.

The charges relate to the theft of high value cars, worth an estimated half a million pounds in total, from locations across NI between May 2016 and February 2017.
