* Fermanagh & Omagh

Police are appealing for information following a report of arson at a house in the Camowen Cottages area of Omagh in the early hours of this morning (Saturday, July 25th).

At around 4.00 am this morning, Police received a report of a fire at a house in the Camowen Cottages, officers attended and the fire had already been extinguished. The property suffered significant damage in the kitchen of the property.

Enquiries are continuing and at this stage, the fire is being treated as deliberate.

Detectives would appeal to anyone who witnessed anything suspicious in the area to contact Police on 101, quoting reference number 315 25/07/20. You can submit a report online using the non-emergency reporting form via http://www.psni.police.uk/makeareport/you can also contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at http://crimestoppers-uk.org/.
