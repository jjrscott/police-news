Security alert, Strabane

Police are continuing to deal with a security alert in the Townsend Street area of Strabane. The alert began late last night and cordons are likely to remain in place until lunchtime while officers carry out further enquiries.

We understand the inconvenience this may cause and appreciate the patience and understanding of local people. We will keep you updated through our social media pages as the day progresses.

There are no further details at this time.
