* Armagh City, Banbridge & Craigavon

Men charged following £300,000 drugs seizure

Three males have been charged with drugs offences following the seizure of more than £300,000 of herbal cannabis.

The drugs were located during searches of a number of vehicles stopped by police on Derrymacash Road in Lurgan on Friday 22 April.

Detective Inspector Tom McClure from PSNI Organised crime Branch said: "The men aged, 32, 36 and 59, have each been charged with three offences.

They are due to appear at Lisburn Magistrates on Monday charged with possession of class B drugs, possession of class B drugs with intent to supply and being concerned in the supply of class B drugs."

As is usual procedure the charges will be reviewed by the PPS.
