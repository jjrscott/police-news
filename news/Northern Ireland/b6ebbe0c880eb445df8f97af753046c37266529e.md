Fatal Collision, Saintfield Road, Crossgar

Police can confirm that a man has died following a single vehicle collision on Saintfield Road, Crossgar.

The collision was reported shortly before 4am this morning, Sunday 2 April.

Police are seeking witnesses and would like to hear from anyone who was travelling on the Saintfield Road prior to 4am. Investigating officers can be contacted by calling 101, please quote reference 341 02/04/17.
