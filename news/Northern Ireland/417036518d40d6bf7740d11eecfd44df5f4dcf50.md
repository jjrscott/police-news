* Ards & North Down

A murder investigation has been launched after a man was stabbed to death in Bangor.

Chief Inspector Trevor Atkinson said: "The 47 year old was attacked outside a property in Enterprise Court shortly after 1am this morning, Thursday 31 October.

A 45 year old man was arrested at the scene and remains in custody at present. Anyone with information that could assist the investigation can call 101, quoting reference 61 31/10/19.

Alternatively, information can also be provided to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 which is 100% anonymous and gives people the power to speak up and stop crime."
