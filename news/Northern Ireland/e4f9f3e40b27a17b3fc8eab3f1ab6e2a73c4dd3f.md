* Mid Ulster

Police can confirm that a man has died following a one vehicle road traffic collision on the Newferry Road, near Bellaghy, on Thursday 25th October.

The collision occurred at around 5pm on Thursday when a BMW car with four men on board left the road. 22 year old Reece Kelly from the Draperstown area was pronounced dead at the scene whilst three other men were taken to hospital with one described as being in a critical condition.

The circumstances surrounding the collision are under investigation and officers have appealed to anyone who witnessed or who has dash-cam footage of the collision or who saw the blue BMW car being driven in the area before the collision to contact them at the Collision Investigation Unit on 101 quoting reference number 931 25/10/18.
