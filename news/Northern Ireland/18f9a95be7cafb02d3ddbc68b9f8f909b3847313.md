* Antrim & Newtownabbey

Hijacking, Grainon Way, Newtownabbey

Detectives in Antrim are appealing for witnesses and information about a Hijacking which occurred on Grainon Way, Newtownabbey at approximately 10.00pm on Thursday 28th July 2016.

During this incident a female was dragged from her vehicle, a black Vauxhall Zafira. The female received minor injuries during this incident. This vehicle was later found burnt out on the Crumlin Road, Belfast.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact Detective Constable Michelle McKeen at Antrim PSNI on 101 quoting reference number 1437 of 28/07/2016.

If you prefer to provide information without giving your details you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers and speak to them anonymously on 0800 555 111.
