Detectives from PSNI's Serious Crime Branch investigating the death of a 5 year old girl in Newtownabbey on Sunday, 15th December have charged a 31 year old man and a 25 year old woman with murder.

They have also been charged with causing GBH with intent and causing or allowing the death of a child.

They are due to appear at Belfast Magistrates Court on Saturday 21st December.

As is normal procedure all charges will be reviewed by the PPS.

We are keen to hear from witnesses of this incident

The Police Service of Northern Ireland is appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or anyone with any information that will assist the investigation to contact us on the non-emergency number 101. Information can also be passed anonymously via the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
