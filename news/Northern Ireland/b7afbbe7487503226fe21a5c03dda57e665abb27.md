* Mid Ulster

Police in Dungannon are investigating after a 27 year old man was assaulted in the Newell Road area of the town this morning, Sunday 25 June.

Inspector Neil Beck said: “The victim had just arrived at a local carpark at around 1am and had exited his vehicle when he was set upon by a group of men. He was punched and kicked to the ground, sustaining bruising to his face and body.

The victim’s car, a black Audi A4, was taken by his assailants.

Enquiries into this incident are ongoing. Witnesses are asked to contact police by calling 101, quoting reference number 87 25/06/17.”
