* Belfast

Three men have been charged in connection with an assault on a teenager in Belfast on Friday afternoon.

A 17 year old man remains critically ill in hospital following the attack a flat on the Donegall Road.

The three suspects, two aged 18 and one aged 20, were interviewed and charged with grievous bodily harm with intent.

All three are due to appear at Belfast Magistrates court on 6 May.

As is normal procedure the charges will be reviewed by the PPS.
