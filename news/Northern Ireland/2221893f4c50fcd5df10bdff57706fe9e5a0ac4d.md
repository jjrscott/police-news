* Belfast

Police investigating an incident at a flat in the University Street area of Belfast in the early hours of Saturday, 20 July, have charged a 20 year old man with grievous bodily harm with intent and possession of offensive weapon with intent to commit an indictable offence.

He is due to appear at Belfast Magistrates Court on Monday, 22 July.

As is normal procedure all charges will be reviewed by the PPS.
