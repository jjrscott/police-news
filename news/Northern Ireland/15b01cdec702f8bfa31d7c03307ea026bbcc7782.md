* Derry City & Strabane

Man dies in collision - Donemana

Police can confirm that a male in his twenties has died in a collision on Longland Road in Donemana this morning.

The silver Peugeot in which he was travelling struck a bridge at around 7.20am.

Inspector Ray Wilson said: “Another male who was also in the car was taken to hospital where he is currently receiving treatment for injuries that are not believed to be life threatening.

The occupants of a blue Transit van also travelling on the road at the time were not injured.

I am appealing for witnesses or anyone who was travelling on the Longland Road this morning to get in touch with police. Please call 101 quoting reference 204 22/07/16.”

The Longland Road remains closed between Cumber Road and Ballyarton Road.
