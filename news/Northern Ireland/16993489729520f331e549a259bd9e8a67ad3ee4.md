* Mid & East Antrim

Detectives from the PSNI's Major Investigation Team have charged a 32 year old man with murder. He is due to appear at Laganside Magistrates Court on Friday 3rd April.

Detective Inspector Millar, from the MIT, said: "Police were called to the scene of the sudden death of an 82 year old woman in the Dromaine Drive area of Larne on the evening of Monday 30th March. A post mortem examination has been carried out and we are now treating the death as murder."
