Charges following disorder in Agincourt Avenue, South Belfast

Three men have been charged following disorder that occurred in the Agincourt Avenue area of South Belfast in the early hours of Thursday 17 March.

A 21 year old man has been charged with disorderly behaviour, a 19 year old man has been charged with riotous behaviour while a 20 year old man has been charged with disorderly behaviour, obstructing police, resisting police and two counts of assault on police. All three are due to appear at Belfast Magistrates Court on Wednesday 13 April.

As is normal procedure, all charges are reviewed by the PPS.
