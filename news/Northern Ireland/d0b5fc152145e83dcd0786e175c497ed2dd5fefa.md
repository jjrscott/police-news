* Derry City & Strabane

Police can confirm that a 48 year old female pedestrian has died as a result of a road traffic collision on the Glenshane Road outside Derry/Londonderry in the early hours of this morning (Monday 18 September).

Inspector Gregory Smyth said: “We would like to hear from anyone who witnessed this collision. If you have any information that could help with our investigation, please contact police at Strand Road on telephone number 101, quoting reference 117 of 18/9/17.”
