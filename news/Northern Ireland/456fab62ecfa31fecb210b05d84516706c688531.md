Two men aged 62 and 40 have been charged with a range of drugs offences including possession of class A drugs, possession of class A drugs with intent to supply, importing class A controlled drug and being concerned in the supply of class A drugs.

A third man, aged 28, was charged with possession of class A drug and possession of a class A drug with intent to supply.

All three are expected to appear at Lisburn Magistrates court in the morning, Monday 1 June. As is usual procedure the charges will be reviewed by the PPS.
