* Mid & East Antrim

Police are investigating an attack on a house in Carrickfergus last night, Wednesday 17 July.

At around 11.55pm it was reported that a window had been broken and paint thrown at a property in Sunnylands Grove.

It's understood four males may have been involved.

Police are treating the incident as a sectarian hate crime and are appealing for information. Please call 101, quoting reference 2180 17/07/19.
