* Belfast

Serious assault - Andersonstown Road, Belfast

Police in West Belfast arrested two men following a disturbance on the Andersonstown Road on the evening of Monday 14th March.

The incident, which resulted in three men being injured and taken to hospital for treatment, was reported to police at around 6.15pm.

Inspector Davy McBride said: "The two arrested men are currently in police custody, arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm with intent and possession of an offensive weapon."

We are keen to hear from witnesses of this incident

The Police Service of Northern Ireland is appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or anyone with any information that will assist the investigation to contact us on the non-emergency number 101. Information can also be passed anonymously via the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
