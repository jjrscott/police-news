* Newry, Mourne & Down

A man has been charged with the murder of a man in Newry earlier this week.

Thirty three year old Brian Phelan was stabbed to death on Carrivekeeney Road shortly after 3pm on Thursday 26 July. A 27 year old man arrested shortly after the murder was subsequently charged to appear at Newry Magistrates Court on Saturday 28 July.
