* Antrim & Newtownabbey

Police revisit fatal collision scene - Collin Road, Ballyclare

This evening Police revisited the scene of a fatal collision which occurred on the Collin Road, Ballyclare on the evening of Monday, 2 May.

Constable Trevor Fletcher said, ”A black Volkswagen Amarok, a silver Vauxhall Vectra and a red Volkswagen Golf were involved in the collision in which a man in his 50’s died.

Constable Fletcher continued: “I would appeal to anyone who was travelling on the Collin Road at around 7pm on the evening of Monday, 2 May and who may have witnessed this collision to contact Police on the non-emergency number 101.”
