* Fermanagh & Omagh

Farm tragedy, Maguiresbridge

Police attended a farm in the Maguiresbridge area at around 8.30pm last night, Thursday 6 April, following a report of an incident involving a four year old child.

Inspector Glen Latimer continues: “The little boy was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Local PSNI will work closely with the Health and Safety Executive as they investigate the circumstances.”
