Above: Alan McCullough, the man who sadly died in the collision. ( _Photo provided by Alan's family._ )

Police can confirm a man has died from his injuries following a road traffic collision in Newtownabbey on Monday, 22 June.

29 year-old Alan McCullough from Newtownabbey was riding a motorcycle when he was involved in the one-vehicle collision on Church Road, which was reported just before 6.35am

He was taken to hospital for treatment of serious injuries following the collision, but he sadly passed away from his injuries last night (Tuesday, 7th July).

Inspector David Burns is appealing to anyone who witnessed the collision, or who may have captured dash cam footage, to contact them on 101, quoting reference number 249 of 22/06/20.
