Petrol bomb attack - Coagh

A petrol bomb has been thrown at a house in Coagh.

Scorch damage was caused to the front door of the property in Windsor Terrace at around 1.40am on Monday 2 May.

The male occupant, who was home alone at the time, was not hurt.

Detective Sergeant Mark Crothers said: “We are working to establish a motive for this incident and we are asking anyone with information to get in touch with us by calling the non-emergency number 101.”
