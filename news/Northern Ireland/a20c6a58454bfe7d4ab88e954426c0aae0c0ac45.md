* Mid & East Antrim

Businesses targeted in burglaries, Ballymena

Detectives are investigating a number of commercial burglaries in Ballymena.

A total of six business premises were broken into overnight between Wednesday 7 June and Thursday 8 June and various items, including cash, were stolen from each.

Three of the businesses are located on Broughshane Street, the other two on William Street and Larne Road.

Detective Sergeant Kerry Brennan said: “We are keen to hear from anyone with information about these crimes. Detectives can be contacted by calling 101.”
