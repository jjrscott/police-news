Larne Arson Attack

Detectives are appealing for information following an arson attack at a business premises in the Brustin Brae Road area of Larne on Wednesday 31st August.

Police recieved a report at approximately 7.00am stating that access to a building had been gained and six classic cars had been set alight casuing damage to the vehicles.

Sergeant Brian Caskey is appealing to anyone who has information abou the incident or who noticed any suspicious activity in the area to contact Larne Police Station on the non-emergency number 101, quoting reference number 177 on 31/08/16. Alternatively, if you someone would prefer to provide information without giving their details they can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers and speak to them anonymously on 0800 555 111.
