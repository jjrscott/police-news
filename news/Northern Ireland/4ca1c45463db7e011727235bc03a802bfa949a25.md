* Belfast

Men charged with robbery and theft, Belfast

Detectives from Reactive and Organised Crime Branch at Musgrave have charged two men aged 29 and 33 with robbery and theft. Both men are due to appear at Belfast MC on Thursday 14th April.

As is normal procedure the charges are reviewed by the PPS.

The charges are in relation to a robbery and theft at commercial premises on the Albertbridge Road in east Belfast on Tuesday 12th April.
