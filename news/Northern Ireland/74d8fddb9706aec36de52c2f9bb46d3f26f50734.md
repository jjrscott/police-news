* Derry City & Strabane

Police are investigating an arson attack at a farm building on Carnanreagh Road in Claudy on Sunday 12 April.

A shed containing expensive farming equipment was deliberately set alight shortly before midnight destroying the contents and the building. The fire also extensively damaged three vehicles parked nearby.

Sergeant Karen McGuinness is asking anyone with information about this crime to please contact police on 101, quoting reference 2855 12/04/20.
