* Lisburn & Castlereagh

Detectives are investigating the circumstances surrounding a fire at commercial premises at Glenavy Road, Upper Balinderry in Lisburn on Monday 25 February.

A report was received at around 11.40pm on Monday evening that a large shed in the area was on fire. A number of roads were closed whilst NIFRS dealt with the blaze which caused considerable damage to the property.

Detectives are appealing to anyone with any information about the incident to contact them at Lisburn on 101 quoting reference number 1586 25/02/19. Alternatively, information can also be provided to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 which is 100% anonymous and gives people the power to speak up and stop crime.
