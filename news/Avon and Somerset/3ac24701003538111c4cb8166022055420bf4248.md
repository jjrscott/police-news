We’re seeking witnesses to a serious road traffic collision in the Keynsham area.

It happened at about 5.15pm on 13 September in Broadmead Lane – outside Waitrose.

A Mercedes car and a Honda motorcycle were involved in the collision.

The motorcyclist received serious leg injuries and was taken to Southmead Hospital, where he remains in a critical but stable condition.

We’re keen to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time of the incident, particularly anyone who may have dashcam footage of the incident.

If you are able to help, please contact us, quoting reference 521912083
