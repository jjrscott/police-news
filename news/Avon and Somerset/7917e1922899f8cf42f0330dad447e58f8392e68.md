Appeal for witnesses

We’re appealing for witnesses after an assault in St George, Bristol.

The incident occurred on Clouds Hill Road just before 9.00pm on 24 July.

The victim was walking towards the town centre when the assault happened. The incident was reported by concerned witnesses and we would like to thank them.

We would like the victim to please come forward.

Police enquiries are continuing.

We’re keen to hear from anyone who may have information to help our enquiries. If so, please contact 101 and give the call handler the reference number 5219169696

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
