We’re investigating an incident in which an 18-year-old man has been stabbed in the Morley Street area of Barton Hill, Bristol.

The incident happened sometime between 11.10pm and 11.20pm on Saturday 19 October.

The victim was taken to hospital for treatment, where he remains in a stable condition.

We’ve cordoned off the area around where this offence took place to carry out further enquiries. Reassurance patrols will be taking place and any residents who have concerns should speak to their local neighbourhood officers.

If you can help, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number ** 5219242541 **

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
