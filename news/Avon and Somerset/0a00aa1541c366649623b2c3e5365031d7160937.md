Have you seen this quadbike which has been stolen from a property in Wrington?

The quadbike was stolen on Tuesday 9 July between 11.00am-2.45pm. It is a red TRX 350 FE5 quadbike with registration plate KX09 JWC.

The bike was stolen from the elderly owner’s rear garden. He uses it as a mobility aid.

We’re appealing for people to look out for the quadbike. We would also like anyone who may be offered one understand suspicious circumstances or for a cheap sale.

We would like to hear from anyone who may have CCTV or phone footage of anyone riding a quadbike like the one described above.

If anyone has seen this quadbike or knows of its whereabouts, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number 5219156032.

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
