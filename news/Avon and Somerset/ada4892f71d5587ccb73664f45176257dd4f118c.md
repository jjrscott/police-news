Officers have arrested Phillip Wright, wanted on warrant for breaching a court order, following a public appeal for information.

Wright, 43, of no fixed abode, was arrested by patrolling officers in Bristol on Sunday 23 August and was due before Bristol Magistrates' Court on Monday 24 August.

We'd like to thank everyone who got in touch with information or shared our appeal to trace him.
