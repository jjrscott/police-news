We're appealing for witnesses after two men were injured in a collision in Bristol last night, Wednesday 5 June.

It involved a pedestrian and a motorcyclist riding a blue and yellow motorbike. It happened just before 11.50pm on the A4 Bath Road, near the ITV West Country studios.

The road reopened at until about 5am on Thursday 6 June after emergency services dealt with the incident.

Both the rider and pedestrian suffered injuries. They remain in hospital in in a serious but stable condition.

If you saw the collision or have any other information which could help, get in touch quoting reference 5219125669

If you can help, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number ** 5219125669 **

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
