Appeal for witnesses

We’re appealing for witnesses following a serious road traffic collision in Somerset near to Shepton Mallet.

It happened yesterday (Wednesday 19 June) shortly after 11am on the A37 at Long Hill.

A Chevrolet Matiz was travelling southbound and a Rover 111 northbound.

The Matiz is reported to have crossed the centre of the road and was in a head-on collision with the Rover.

The woman driver of the Rover and two men who were in the Matiz all suffered potential life changing injuries and remain in hospital.

We’re keen to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time of the incident who may have information to help our enquiries. If you can help, please contact us, quoting reference 5219137452

If you can help, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number ** 5219137452 **

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
