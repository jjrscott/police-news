Appeal for witnesses

We are appealing for witnesses following a sexual assault in Bristol.

A 20 year old women was assaulted by a man on her way home from a night out on Thursday 9 May 2019 between 3.00am-4.00am near a bus stop on Parrys Lane.

The man is described as Asian, around 5ft 8in with dark short hair and wearing dark clothing.

We are particularly keen to speak people who were in the area at the time and who may have witnessed the incident.

If you were in the area at the time and saw anything, or you think you may have information that can help our investigation, please call 101 and quote reference number 5219101395.

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
