Three men have come forward following a police appeal for information over an assault in Bridgwater on Wednesday 7 August.

Our enquiries continue into the incident which began in the Golden Ball pub and continued in Dampiet Street. A 49-year-old man suffered cuts, bruises and broken glasses in the attack.

We're grateful to everyone who got in touch with information or shared the appeal.

If you have any other information which could help, get in touch quoting reference 5219182100.

If you can help, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number ** 5219182100 **

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
