Witness appeal following incident in Brean

We’re appealing for witnesses following an incident in Brean.

A public order incident happened in South Road, near to Pontins at about 12.10am on Monday (22 April).

Four people were injured – two requiring hospital treatment.

Three people – two men and a woman – have been arrested in connection with the incident. They have all been released on police bail, pending further enquiries.

We’re keen to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time of the incident.

In particular we would like to hear from anyone who may have mobile phone coverage.

If you can help, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number ** 5219087072 **

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
