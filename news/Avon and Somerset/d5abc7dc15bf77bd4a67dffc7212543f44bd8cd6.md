A wanted man has been arrested after handing himself in following a public appeal.

Jamal McBean was wanted in connection with a serious incident that occurred on Montreal Avenue, Horfield on Thursday 3 September.

He handed himself in at 9pm this evening, Sunday 6 September, and remains in police custody.

Officers would like to thank everyone who shared the appeal or got in touch with information.
