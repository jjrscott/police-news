 * 22 Sep 2019: Witness appeal after fight leaves two men in hospital - Bedminster

We've arrested a man after a fight which left two men in hospital with serious injuries.

It happened at the Three Lions pub in West Street, Bristol, at about 9.50pm on Saturday 21 September.

We arrested a 32-year-old man in Bedminster in the early hours of Wednesday 25 September on suspicion of wounding with intent. He remains in custody at the time of writing.

Both injured men have left hospital but need ongoing treatment.

A third man had stitches following the incident.

Our enquiries continue. If you can help get in touch quoting reference 5219218932.
