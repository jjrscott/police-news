A 19-year-old man has been charged with dangerous driving following an incident in Cheddar Gorge.

Ryan Peter White, of Weston-super-Mare, has been charged following a single-vehicle collision in the Gorge on the evening of 14 August 2020. Three people were injured after the car they were in struck the gorge side.

White is due to appear at Taunton Deane Magistrates' Court at a date to be confirmed.
