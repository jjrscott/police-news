Do you recognise this man? We would like to speak to him in relation to a racially aggravated assault that took place along Church Road, Bristol. The incident occurred on Thursday 11 July 2019 at 11:20am. The victim has received racist verbal abuse and was threatened and then chased, causing fear of serious harm to themselves.

The man is described as black, late 40's with short grey white hair and of slim build. He was wearing a dark long sleeved top with dark trousers and white trainers whilst carrying a small white and red children's style backpack.

If you can help, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number ** 5219157787 **

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
