Can you help?

We’re issuing a CCTV image of a red car in connection to a racially aggravated public order offence in Wells.

The incident happened between 11.20 – 11.25am on 17 July on Princes Road, Wells. After reports of two men in the car made racial comments towards a women who was walking her dog.

If you recognise the car pictured, or can help with our investigation, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number 5219163799

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
