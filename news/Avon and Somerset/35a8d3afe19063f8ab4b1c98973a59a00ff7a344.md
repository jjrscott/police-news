**Adam Kratjeta has been charged with possession with intent to supply Class B drugs.**

Mr Kratjeta, 27, from Wheatfield Place in Edinburgh, appeared at Bristol Magistrates Court on Saturday (7 September) charged with possession with intent to supply a Class B drugs.

He was charged in connection with an incident in Easton on Friday (6 September) in which three people were arrested having fled two vehicles and between five and six kilograms of Class B drugs – believed to be amphetamine – were seized.

Mr Kratjeta has been remanded in custody and is due at Bristol Crown Court on 9 October.

Two men were also arrested in connection with the incident and have been released under investigation.
