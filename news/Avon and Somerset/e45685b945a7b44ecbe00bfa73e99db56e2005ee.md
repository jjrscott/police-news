 * 14 Sep 2020: Patrols carried out after indecent exposure reports - Bath

A man has been arrested overnight by officers patrolling the Twerton area of Bath.

We've been targeting our patrols in response to a number of instances of indecent exposure in the area.

Patrolling officers spotted a man and made an arrest in Freeview Road at about 11.20pm on Thursday 24 September.

A man remains in police custody on suspicion of indecent exposure at the time of writing.
