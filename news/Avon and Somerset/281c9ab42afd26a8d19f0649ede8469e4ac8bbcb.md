Do you recognise this man?

Can you help us identify the man in this e-fit?

We're issuing it as we investigate a burglary at a commercial premises in Aztec West at about 11.55pm on Saturday 25 May.

A witness reported seeing hearing a bang and seeing a man climbing in through a smashed window. The suspect left empty-handed after being challenged.

He was a white man, aged between 25 and 30, over 6ft tall. He wore black trousers and a hoody.

The break-in was reported just after 12.30am on Sunday 26 May. A police dog and handler searched the area and Crime Scene Investigators attended.

If you recognise the man in the image or have any other information which could help get in touch, quoting reference 5219116310.

If you can help, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number ** 5219116310 **

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.
