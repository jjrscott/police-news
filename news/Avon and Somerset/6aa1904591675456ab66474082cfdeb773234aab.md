**Paul Matthews, 45, of Ashley Terrace in Lower Weston, Bath, is due at Bristol Magistrates Court later today (Tuesday 22 October) charged with burglary.**

Matthews was arrested on Sunday (20 October) and has been charged in connection with an incident earlier that day in which a number of items including a TV, wallet and bank cards were stolen from a property at Windsor Villas on the Upper Bristol Road in Bath.
