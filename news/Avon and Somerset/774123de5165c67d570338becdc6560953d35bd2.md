The prisoner who absconded from HMP Leyhill on Sunday evening, 22 September, has handed himself in.

Police officers in Kingswood, Gloucestershire, detained Dean Mellors, 52, this afternoon, Monday 23 September.

We'd like to thank everyone who called with information or shared our appeal to trace him.
