A man is due in court today, Monday 7 December, following a public appeal to trace him.

Aaron Powell, 30, of Claypiece Road, Bristol, is due before Bristol Magistrates charged with breaching a non-molestation order following his arrest on Friday 4 December.

We're grateful to everyone who got in touch with information or shared our appeal.
