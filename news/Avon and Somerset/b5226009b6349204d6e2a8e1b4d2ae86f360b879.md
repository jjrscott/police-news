We are appealing for witnesses to a two car collision in the Bridgwater area.

The incident happened at 9pm on Bank Holiday Monday, 26 August on the A38 Pawlett.Road.

A grey Audi A5 was in collision with a red Peugeot 2008, which is thought to have been following a combine harvester. The Audi was in collision with the rear of the Peugeot.

Two people injured in the collision were taken to Musgrove Park Hospital with minor injuries and released after treatment.
