Witnesses sought following fail to stop collision

We’re appealing for witnesses following a fail-to-stop collision in Keynsham last night.

The incident happened at about 6.30pm when a car was in collision with a pedestrian, who was walking in the direction of Charlton Road from Charlton Park.

The victim was taken to hospital, where he remains in a serious but stable condition.

A car – believed to have been involved in the incident – was later found burned out in a field near Kelston.

Police enquiries into the incident are continuing.

We are keen to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time of the incident who may have information to help our help our enquiries.

If you can help, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number ** 5219123507 **

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
