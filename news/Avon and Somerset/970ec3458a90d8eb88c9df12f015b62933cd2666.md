We’re appealing for witnesses following the theft and arson of a red Volkswagen Golf in Coleford, Frome.

The vehicle was stolen from the owner’s home address and set alight a short time after on Highbury Street, Coleford on Friday 26 July at around 2.50am.

Another vehicle was seen speeding away from the scene.

If you were in the area at the time and saw anything, or you think you may have information that can help our investigation, please call 101 and quote reference number 5219170749

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
