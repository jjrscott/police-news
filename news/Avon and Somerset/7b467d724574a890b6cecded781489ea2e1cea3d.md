**John O’Brien has been charged with five counts of dwelling burglary in and around the Bristol area.**

O’Brien, 44, of Kendon Drive in Horfield, Bristol, appeared at Bristol Magistrates Court last Thursday (17 Oct) charged with five dwelling burglaries.

He was arrested last Wednesday (16 Oct) and was charged in connection with a number of burglaries, including three properties on Brookfield Road, Cotham and another property on Arley Hill, Cotham as well as a property on Downend Road in Horfield.

O’Brien was granted conditional bail by the court and is due to appear at Bristol Crown Court on 18 November.
