Yamaha WR250F

We’re appealing for witnesses following the theft of a motorcycle (pictured) from a garage in Clifton, Bristol.

The incident happened between Friday 2 August and Monday 5 August on Belgrave Place.

The motorcycle is a white and blue Yamaha WR250F with gold handle bars.

During the burglary significant damage was caused to the victim’s car.

If you are able to help with our investigation, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number 5219179202.

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
