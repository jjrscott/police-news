Officers have arrested a woman in connection with a hate crime at a mosque in Stapleton Road, Bristol.

The religiously-aggravated public order incident happened at the Shah Jalal Jame Mosque on Monday 21 September.

The woman is currently on police bail. She has conditions not to go to any mosque in Bristol, pending a charging decision from the Crown Prosecution Service.
