**David Bevis, 35, of no fixed address in Bristol is due at Bristol Magistrates Court later today (Tuesday 22 October) charged with burglary.**

Bevis was arrested yesterday (Monday 21 October) and has been charged in connection with an incident earlier that day in which a property in Fairfield Road in Montpelier, Bristol, was burgled.
