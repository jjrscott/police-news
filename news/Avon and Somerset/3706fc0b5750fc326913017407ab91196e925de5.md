Do you recognise this man? We would like to speak to him in relation to a burglary at the British Heart Foundation, Marchfields Way, Weston-super-Mare. This happened between Saturday 17th August and Sunday 18th August 2019.

The unknown offender forced entry into the shop via the rear door. Once inside they entered the main office and tried to gain access to the safe but failed.

If you can help, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number ** 5219189971 **

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.
