 * 25 Sep 2019: Arrest after fight which left two men in hospital with serious injuries - Bedminster

We're appealing for witnesses after two men were seriously hurt in a fight in Bedminster.

It happened in the garden of the Three Lions pub in West Street, Bedminster at 9.50pm on Saturday 21 September.

Ambulance crews treated a number of people for injuries. Two men went to hospital with serious injuries. They are still in hospital getting treatment for lacerations not believed life-threatening. Another man went to hospital needing stitches for a 2.5 inch cut.

The area was cordoned off to allow for a forensic investigation of the scene.

Officers initially arrested a man in connection with the incident. He was later released for medical treatment.
