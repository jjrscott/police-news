Do you recognise this man?

We are issuing CCTV images of a man who we’d like to speak to in connection to a burglary of a unit on Dragon Court, Bristol.

The incident occurred between 8.30 – 9.00pm on Sunday 16 June, where a charity pot containing a large amount of cash has been taken.

We would like to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time or can help with the CCTV images. If so, please contact 101 and give the call handler the reference number 5219134958.

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
