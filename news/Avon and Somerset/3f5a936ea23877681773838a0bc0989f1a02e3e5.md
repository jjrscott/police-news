**Liam Kelly, 24, of no fixed address in Yeovil has been charged with possession with intent to supply Class A drugs.**

Kelly, was arrested at Mount Pleasant on 16 October and appeared at Yeovil Magistrates Court on Thursday (17 October). He was charged in connection with an incident in which a man was found in possession of a large quantity of Class A drugs believed to be crack cocaine and heroin.

He has been remanded in custody and will appear at Taunton Crown Court on a date yet to be set.
