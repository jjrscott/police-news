Do you recognise these men? We would like to speak to them in relation to a theft of a motorbike. This occurred at Old Weston Road, Flux Bourton, Bristol on Tuesday 2 July at 15:31pm. The motorbike was taken without consent of the owner and was then seen driving around Bristol.

The driver was wearing a dark jacket with the hood up and wearing jeans.

The rear passenger was wearing a dark top with the hood up and dark trousers.

If you can help, please call 101 and give the call handler the reference number ** 5219149526 **

You can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100 per cent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their Anonymous Online Form.

No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court or have to speak to police when contacting Crimestoppers.
