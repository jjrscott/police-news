Thames Valley Police is appealing for witnesses following a robbery in Didcot.

At around 7.15am on Tuesday (26/1) a man entered the Coop in Lockstock Place, Didcot and demanded money from the cashier threatening them with a knife.

An amount of cash was stolen.

The offender is described as a white man, believed to be in his forties. He was wearing a black shiny puffer jacket, black balaclava

Investigating officer Detective Constable Nicola Clark, based at Abingdon police station, said: "I am appealing for any witnesses to this incident, or anyone who has any information, CCTV or doorbell footage that might help with our investigation to please get in touch.

"You can make a report online or by calling 101 and quoting reference 43210034240.

"If you want to remain 100% anonymous you can also report to Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111."
