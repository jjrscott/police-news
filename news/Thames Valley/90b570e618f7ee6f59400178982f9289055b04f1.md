Two men have been charged with drugs offences following a warrant in Aylesbury.

Fayaz Zaman, aged 42, of St Catherine's Court, Aylesbury has been charged with one count of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs, and one count of possession of criminal property.

Daniel Sibley, aged 29, of Fowler Road, Aylesbury has been charged with one count of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs.

Both men were arrested following a warrant in Aylesbury on Wednesday (9/12) and were charged on Thursday (10/12).

They appeared at Wycombe Magistrates Court on Friday (11/12) and were remanded in custody to appear at Aylesbury Crown Court on 4 January 2021.
