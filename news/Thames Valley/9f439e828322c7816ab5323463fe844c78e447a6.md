A man has been charged following a stabbing at a shopping centre in High Wycombe.

Mourtaza Ahmed, aged 68, of Chichester Close, High Wycombe, was charged with two counts of assault occasioning grievous bodily harm and possession of a bladed article in a public place.

He will appear at High Wycombe Magistrates' Court later today (18/2).

The charges relate to a stabbing at Chilterns Shopping Centre on Tuesday (16/2) in which a 45-year-old man suffered stab injuries. He has since been discharged from hospital.
