A man has been charged in connection with an assault investigation in Aylesbury.

Tyrone Jonsyn, aged 26, of no fixed abode, has been charged with one count each of robbery, possession of a blade/sharply pointed article in a public place, theft from the person, attempted common assault on an emergency worker and threats to damage/destroy property.

It is in connection with an incident that occurred on Wednesday (6/1) in Paradise Orchard.

Jonsyn has been remanded in custody to appear at Aylesbury Crown Court on 8 February.
