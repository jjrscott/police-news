Following a Thames Valley Police investigation, a man has been charged in connection with an incident on Monday (29/3) where another man suffered stab wounds.

Jordan Hughes, aged 18, of Heroes Walk, Reading, was charged last night (30/3) with one count of Section 18 grievous bodily harm with intent and is due to appear at Reading Magistrates' Court today.

The charge is connection with an incident at around 2.40pm on Monday when a man aged in his fifties was stabbed, sustaining serious, but not life-threatening injuries.

Two other men, aged 22 and 44, both from Reading, have been released on conditional bail until 26 April.
