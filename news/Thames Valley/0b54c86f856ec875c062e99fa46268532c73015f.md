Following a Thames Valley Police investigation, a man has been charged with burglary in Chesham.

Tyler Strickland, aged 35, of Alexander Street, Chesham was charged with one count of burglary on Friday (27/11).

It is in connection with an incident that occurred in Hazelwood Close on Thursday (26/11) at around 3.40pm.

Strickland has been remanded in custody. A date for the next court appearance has not yet been set.
