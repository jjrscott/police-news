Thames Valley Police is appealing for witnesses following a serious injury road traffic collision in Oxford.

At around 11.45am yesterday (28/1) a white Ford Mondeo and a silver Peugeot moped where involved in a collision on Iffley Road.

The rider of the moped, a man in his sixties, sustained serious chest injuries and remains in hospital at this time.

Investigating officer, PC Matthew Hunt, of the Joint Operations Roads Policing Unit, said: "I am appealing for anyone who witnessed this incident to please come forward.

"I would also urge any motorists who have dash-cams and were in the local area around the time that this happened to check footage in case it has captured anything that may assist with this investigation.

"Anyone with information can contact Thames Valley Police by calling 101 or making a report online or quoting reference 43210037794.

"Alternatively, if you wish to remain anonymous you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or online."
