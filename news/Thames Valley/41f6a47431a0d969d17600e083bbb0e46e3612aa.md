A man has been charged with rape in connection with a Thames Valley Police investigation in Aylesbury.

Anthony Pemberton, aged 41, of Brookside, Oakley was charged via postal requisition with one count of rape.

It is in connection with an incident that occurred on 13 April 2019.

Pemberton is to appear at High Wycombe Magistrates' Court on 19 February 2021.
