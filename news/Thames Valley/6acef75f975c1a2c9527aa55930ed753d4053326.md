A woman has been arrested for multiple offences which occurred in Reading.

The woman, aged 33, of no fixed abode, was arrested yesterday (4/2) on suspicion of theft from a shop, possession of a controlled drug of class A and assault occasioning actual bodily harm.

It is in connection with a number of incidents that occurred between August 2020 and January this year in Reading.

She remains in police custody.
