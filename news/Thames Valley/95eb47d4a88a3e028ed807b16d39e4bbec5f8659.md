Thames Valley Police is appealing for witnesses following a robbery in Reading.

At 2.07am this morning (11/12) a man has withdrawn an amount of cash from the ATM at Morrison's on Basingstoke Road, Reading.

The male victim in his late twenties, has then walked towards Basingstoke Road, Reading where he has been approached from behind and assaulted and had cash taken from him.

The victim was taken to hospital where he remains and is being treated for his injuries, where he remains in a serious condition.

The offender is described as a white man, around twenty to thirty years old, wearing white hoody with the hood up, and he has facial hair. He was wearing a grey tracksuit bottoms and black shoes.

Investigating officer, Detective Constable Emma Pointon, said: "We are carrying out a thorough investigation into this incident in which a man has been seriously injured.

"We are appealing for anyone with information to please come forward, or anyone who might recognise the offender from the description to please get in touch."

"We would ask anyone with information to please call 101 quoting reference 43200418872 or if you wish to report anonymously you can call the independent Charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
