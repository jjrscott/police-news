Thames Valley Police is releasing CCTV images following a burglary in Conniburrow.

At around 11pm on Saturday 19 December, the front door of a property in Ramsons Avenue was forced open.

Clothes, electrical items and cash were stolen.

Civilian Investigator Charles Cox, based at Milton Keynes police station, said: "I am releasing these CCTV images as I am keen to hear from anyone who recognises the individual or the car depicted.

"If you have any information about these images or the incident, please get in touch using our online form or by calling 101, quoting reference number 43200433120. Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously via their website or on 0800 555 111."
