Thames Valley Police is appealing for witnesses following an assault in Marlow.

At around 6pm yesterday (25/2), an altercation broke out between a man in his fifties and three teenage boys outside Kingswood Parade on Marlow Bottom.

During the altercation the man and two of the boys fell into the road which resulted in minor bruising to the man but he did not require any medical treatment.

Investigating officer PC Joseph Bateman, based at High Wycombe police station said: "I am appealing for anyone who witnessed this incident, or has any information, to please get in touch.

"You can make a report online or by calling 101 and quoting reference 43210080363.

"Alternatively if you wish to remain 100% anonymous you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
