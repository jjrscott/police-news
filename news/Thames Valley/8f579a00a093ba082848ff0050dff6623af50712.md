Thames Valley Police is appealing for witnesses following an assault in Milton Keynes.

Officers were called to a report of an altercation between two men outside TJ Wines by Glovers Lane and Langcliffe Drive at around 7.15pm yesterday (31/3).

The victim, a man in his twenties, sustained facial injuries that required hospital treatment.
