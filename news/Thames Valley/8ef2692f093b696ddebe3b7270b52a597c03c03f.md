Thames Valley Police has charged a man with multiple offences that occurred in Windsor and Maidenhead.

Bradley McLauchlan, aged 22, of Ricardo Road, Old Windsor, was charged yesterday (9/2) with one count each of obstructing a PC in exercise of a Section 23A power to search a person for drugs, assault by beating of an emergency worker and using threatening, abusive and insulting words to cause harassment, alarm and distress.

It is in connection with an incident on 24 January in Windsor and on 8 February in Maidenhead.

McLauchlan has been released on bail to appear at Reading Magistrates' Court on 5 August 2021.
