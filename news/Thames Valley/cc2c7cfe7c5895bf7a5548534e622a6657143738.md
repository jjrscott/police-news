A man has been arrested following a fail to stop collision in Oxford on Saturday (6/3).

The man, aged in his 30s and from Oxford, was arrested on suspicion of causing serious injury by dangerous driving and has been released pending further enquiries.

A pedestrian, aged in her 50s, remains in hospital following the collision on Great Clarendon Street at the junction with Hart Street.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has information is asked to call 101 with reference 43210094276.
