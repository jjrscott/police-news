Following a Thames Valley Police investigation, a man has been charged with theft offences in Maidenhead.

Keith Perry, aged 29, of no fixed abode, was charged with two counts of theft from a motor vehicle and four counts of vehicle interference.

These charges are in connection with incidents that occurred between 22 October and 20 November this year in Maidenhead.

Perry has been remanded in custody to appear at Slough Magistrates' Court on 15 January 2021.
