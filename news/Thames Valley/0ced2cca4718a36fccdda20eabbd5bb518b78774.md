Three people have been charged in connection with a murder inquiry in Tilehurst.

Abas Khan, aged 23, of Helmsdale Close in Reading, has today (11/2) been charged with a single count of murder and one count of section 18 grevous bodily harm.

Additionally, two boys aged 16 and 17, have also both been charged with a count each of murder and one count of section 18 grevious bodily harm.

The charges are in connection with the death of 26-year-old Raheem Hanif in Dulnan Close in Tilehurst on Saturday evening (6/2).

Khan and both teenagers will appear at Reading Magistrates' Court tomorrow (12/2).

**Note to Editors**

Previously in the investigation, a 16-year-old boy was charged on Tuesday (9/2) with a count of murder and a count of section 18 grevious bodily harm.

Four other men, two aged 25 and two others aged 21 and 22, have been arrested in connection with the investigation and released on bail.
