A man has been charged with burglary following an incident in High Wycombe.

Gary Atkins, aged 43, of Skimmers Close, Holmer Green was arrested on Monday 22 February and later charged with one count of burglary (residential), one count of theft from a motor vehicle and two counts of vehicle interference.

He was remanded in custody and will appear at Aylesbury Crown Court on Thursday 1 April.

The charges relate to an incident on Sunday 21 February in which a property on Oaktree Close was broken into. Keys to a vehicle on the driveway were taken from inside and the car then stolen.
