A man from Slough has been charged with burglary offences which occurred in Windsor.

Brian Seagrove, aged 41, of Upton Park in Slough, was charged with two counts of burglary, and a single count of vehicle interference on Tuesday (23/3).

The charges are in connection with two incidents that took place in Windsor. The first was in Westmead on 29 January this year, with the second in Imperial Road on Monday (22/3).

Seagrove appeared at Reading Magistrates' Court on Wednesday (24/3) and pleaded not guilty.

His next court appearance will be at Reading Crown Court on 26 April.
