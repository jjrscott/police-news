During the Easter period there will be some changes to the Thames Valley Police Media Office opening hours.

The Media Office will be open as follows:

Friday, April 2: Closed

Saturday, April 3: Open 9am - 5pm

Sunday, April 4: Open 9am - 5pm

Monday, April 5: Closed

Tuesday, April 6: Normal office opening times resume.

Any enquiries sent to the Media Office while it is closed will not be answered until we re-open. Updates about any ongoing or urgent incidents will be published on the force website and social media channels.
