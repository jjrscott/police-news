Thames Valley Police is appealing for witnesses after a single vehicle road traffic collision in Banbury.

At around 3.25am on Sunday (28/3), a grey Ford Fiesta collided with a traffic island on Southam Road, located between the Dukes Meadow Drive roundabout and Hardwick Hill.

A 26-year-old woman from Banbury was arrested on suspicion of driving a motor vehicle when alcohol level above the limit and has been released under investigation.

Investigating officer, PC Callum Cooper, based at Bicester police station, said: "I am appealing for any witnesses to this incident to please come forward.

"I would also ask any motorists who were in the local area around the time this happened to please check any dash-cam footage in case it may have captured something that could assist our investigation.

"Anyone with information can contact Thames Valley Police by calling 101 or making a report online, quoting reference 43210130108.

"Alternatively, if you wish to remain anonymous, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their website."
