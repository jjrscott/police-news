Thames Valley Police is investigating a rape that happened at around 7.45pm in the play park between Bartholomew Road and Gaisford Road, Oxford on Saturday (12/12).

A woman, in her thirties, had met a man in the park, he has then forced himself upon her and subjected her to a sexual assault and raped her.

The victim is currently being supported by specially trained officers.

Investigating officer, Detective Constable Emilie Tomann of Force CID based at Oxford, said: "We are carrying out a thorough investigation following the rape of a woman yesterday in the play park between Bartholomew Road and Gaisford Road.

"We are aware the victim and the offender are known to each other and an arrest has been made in connection with this incident.

"We are appealing for anyone who may information around this offence to please come forward.

"In particular we are aware that there may have been a dog walker in the area at the time of this sexual assault and we would very much urge them to please come forward as they may have important witness information.

"Anyone with information should call 101 quoting reference 0484 of 13/12/20, or if you wish to report anonymously you can do so by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
