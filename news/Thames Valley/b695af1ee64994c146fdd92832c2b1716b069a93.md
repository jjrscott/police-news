Thames Valley Police is appealing for witnesses following an assault in Eynsham.

At around 8.15pm on Monday (22/2) the victims, two women in their early twenties, were running down Mill Street, Eynsham on the opposite side of the road to the Post Office when a light coloured van drove past and threw an egg at them.

The egg hit one of the victim's in the face causing injury and swelling to her face and a nosebleed. She did not require hospital treatment.

The van is described as a light coloured van, possibly caddy style.

Investigating officer PC Rachel Lundregan, based at Witney police station, said: "I am appealing to anyone who was in the vicinity of Mill Street and believe that they witnessed this incident or saw the van described to please contact the police.

"I would also ask anybody who was driving in the area to please check dash-cam footage and call us if it has picked up anything that could assist this investigation.

"You can contact us online or by calling 101, quoting reference number 43210075559.

"Alternatively, for 100% anonymity, you can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
