Thames Valley Police is appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Bracknell.

At around 12.40am on Tuesday (5/1) a number of windows and a door were smashed at a commercial premises in Milbanke Way and damage was caused to a lock.

Nothing was stolen during the incident.

Investigating officer DC Wasim Akram, based at Loddon Valley police station, said: "I am appealing to anyone who may have CCTV or dash-cam footage from the area of Milbanke Way in the early hours of Tuesday morning as they may have captured details that could prove vital to this investigation.

"If you have any footage or any information about this incident, please get in touch using our online form or by calling 101, quoting reference number 43210005014.

"Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously via their website or on 0800 555 111."
