Thames Valley Police media office will be closed from 4pm today (24/12).

The media office will also be closed on Christmas Day (25/12), Boxing Day (26/12), Monday (28/12), and New Year's Day (1/1).

Normal opening hours will be in place on Sunday (27/12), which are 9am until 5pm, and also 7am until 6pm on Tuesday (29/12), Wednesday (30/12) and Thursday (31/12).

You can contact the office during these hours by emailing _mediaoffice@thamesvalley.pnn.police.uk_
