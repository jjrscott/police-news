Following an investigation by Thames Valley Police two youths have been charged with murder.

A 15-year-old boy from Banbury and a 17-year-old boy from Milton Keynes have both been charged with one count of murder.

They cannot be named due to their age.

The charges are in connection with the death of 43-year-old Robin Williamson, of Awgar Stone Road, Oxford following an incident on 27th October 2019 in Woodfarm, Oxford.

The two boys appeared at Oxford Magistrates Court this morning and have been remanded in custody to appear before Oxford Crown Court on 31st July 2020.
