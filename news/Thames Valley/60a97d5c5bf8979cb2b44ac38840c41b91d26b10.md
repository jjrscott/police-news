Thames Valley Police is releasing an E-fit image of a man officers would like to speak to following an attempted burglary in Milton Keynes.

At around 5.45am on Sunday 17 January, the victims, a man in his forties and woman in her thirties, were at home in their property on Downs Barn Boulevard when they were woken by the sound of scraping at their front door.

When looking through the door viewer a man matching the E-fit description was seen trying to gain entry to the property. The victim shouted at the man and he fled the scene.

Staff investigator Charles Cox, based at Milton Keynes police station said: "I am appealing for any witnesses of this incident, or anyone who saw a man matching the E-fit provided, in the area at the time.

"If you recognise this man, or believe it could be you, please get in touch.

"You can make a report online or by calling 101 and quoting reference 43210021373.

"If you wish to remain 100% anonymous you can also call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111"
