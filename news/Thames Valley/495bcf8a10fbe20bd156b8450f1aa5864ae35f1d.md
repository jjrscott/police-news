Following a Thames Valley Police investigation, a man has been charged with multiple offences that occurred in Milton Keynes.

Glody Maki, aged 29, of Mere Road, Edington, Birmingham, has been charged with four counts of making threats to kill and one count each of false imprisonment, possession of a firearm with intent to cause fear of violence and being concerned in the supply of class A drugs.

The charges are in connection with a number of incidents that took place between 30 January and 3 February this year in Ramsons Avenue, Conniburrow, Milton Keynes.

Maki has been remanded in custody to appear at Wolverhampton Crown Court on 8 March.
