Thames Valley Police is appealing for the public's help in tracing a 17-year-old girl, who has been reported missing from High Wycombe.

Vanessa Matczak was last seen in Queen Victoria Road, High Wycombe, on Tuesday (2/3) at around 8pm.

She is originally from Manchester, and is a white female, approximately 5ft 4ins tall and has long black hair. She is believed to be wearing a dark puffer-style coat.

Investigating officer Detective Inspector Gill Fox, based at High Wycombe police station, said: "We are appealing to anybody who knows of Vanessa's whereabouts to please get in touch with Thames Valley Police.

"We are growing increasingly concerned for Vanessa's welfare, and would urge anybody who has any information that can help us to locate her to please call 101, quoting reference number 43210089554."
