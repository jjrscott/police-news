Following an investigation by Thames Valley Police, a man has been charged with a child sex offence.

Simon Clarke, also known as Saifuddin, of Church Road, Caversham has been charged with one count of engaging in sexual activity in the presence of a child _._

Clarke was arrested on Friday (18/12) and was charged yesterday (19/12).

It follows an incident on Thursday (17/12) between 8.20am and 8.30am when a man was seen performing a sexual act on himself in a car in Oxfordshire and this was seen by a victim who is a child.

Clarke has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Oxford Magistrates Court tomorrow (21/12).
