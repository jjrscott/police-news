Thames Valley Police is appealing for the public's help to trace a boy who is missing from Banbury.

Ahmed Idris, aged 15, was last seen on Thursday (3/12) at about 11.20am in Bramber Close.

When he was last seen he was wearing black jogging bottoms, a black North Face coat, grey reflective Nike trainers and a white t-shirt.

Ahmed is known to frequent the Croydon area of London.

Investigating officer, Detective Sergeant Robert Platt, based at Banbury police station, said: "I would like to appeal to anyone who has any information which could help us find Ahmed to get in touch.

"Ahmed, if you see this appeal, you are not in any trouble, we just need to know you are safe.

"Anyone with any information should call Thames Valley Police on 101, quoting reference number 43200408396."
