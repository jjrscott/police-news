Thames Valley Police is releasing a CCTV image of two men officers would like to speak to following a theft in Oxford.

Between 11am and 12pm on Wednesday 18 November 2020 the victim, an elderly woman, was shopping in Iceland in Templars Square Shopping Centre on Between Towns Road when she noticed her purse and an amount of cash was missing.

Investigating officer PC Abigail Kirkham based at Oxford police station said: "I am appealing for anyone who may have witnessed this incident, or recognise the men in this image, or if you believe it could be you, to please get in touch.

"You can make a report online or by calling 101 and quoting reference 43200384030.

"Alternatively, if you wish to remain 100% anonymous you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
