Two men have been charged following a burglary at a house in Maidenhead.

Constantin Balan, aged 26, of Ray Mill Road West, Maidenhead was arrested on Wednesday (24/2) and later charged with one count of burglary of a dwelling and two counts of fraud by false representation.

Andrei Marin, 37, of the same address, was charged with one count of receiving stolen goods and two counts of fraud by false representation.

They will appear at Reading Magistrates' Court today (26/2).

The charges relate to an incident in which a number of items were stolen from a house on Denmark Street in the early hours of Wednesday (24/2). A bank card taken from the address was also found to have been used to make a number of transactions shortly after the burglary.
