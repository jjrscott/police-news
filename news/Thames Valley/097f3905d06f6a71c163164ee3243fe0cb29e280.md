Thames Valley Police is releasing a CCTV image of a man officers would like to speak to following a theft in Oxford.

At around 4.05pm on Friday 18 December 2020 a stolen bank card was used to purchase goods from the One Stop shop in Barns Road, Cowley.

Investigating officer PC Samantha Woodward, based at Oxford police station, said: "I am appealing for the public's help in identifying the man in this CCTV image, as I believe he may have vital information in relation to this incident.

"If you recognise this man, believe it might be you or have any information, please get in touch with Thames Valley Police by making a report online or calling us on 101 quoting reference number 43200430980.

"Alternatively, you can contact independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
