Thames Valley Police is appealing for witnesses in connection with an investigation into an incident of attempted rape that occurred in Oxford.

At a time between 3pm and 4.30pm on Monday (15/3) the victim, a woman in her fifties, was sexually assaulted inside a property in Alice Smith Square.

She did not require hospital treatment and is being supported by specialist officers.

A 34-year-old man has been arrested in connection with this investigation and remains in police custody.

Investigating officer, Detective Constable Jamie Macaro, based at St Aldates police station, said: "This was a distressing incident for the victim and she is being supported by specialist officers at this time.

"I am appealing for anyone who has any information about this incident to please come forward.

"In particular, from our investigation so far, I am appealing for a female member of the public who may have seen a distressed woman in the area between 3pm and 4.30pm to please get in touch as we believe you may have important information.

"If anyone has information that they believe could assist this investigation, please call 101 or make a report online, quoting reference 43210112372.

"Alternatively, if you wish to remain anonymous, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers via their website or by calling 0800 555 111."
