Thames Valley Police is appealing for help in uniting a number of suspected stolen bikes with their owners.

On 10 January, officers searching a property in Abingdon discovered and seized 36 bicycles. Officers believe the bikes may be stolen and are keen to hear from their rightful owners.

PC Dan Townsend, based at Abingdon police station, said: "We would encourage anyone who has had their bike stolen in recent months and who believes it could be on this list to get in touch with us.

"We will be asking for proof of purchase and/or an identifying feature so we can ensure the right bike is given back to the correct person.

"If you think your bike may be here, please contact us on 101 with reference number 43210051829."
