A woman from Iver has been charged with a number of drug supply offences following a Thames Valley Police investigation.

Nicola Hollier, aged 32, of Langley Park Road in Iver was charged on Thursday (18/3) with two counts of possession with intent to supply crack cocaine, one count of possession with intent to supply heroin and two counts of acquiring, using or possessing criminal property, namely a quantity of cash.

The charges are in connection with incidents on the A308 (M) in Braywick on 17 March this year and Bulkeley Avenue, Windsor, on 16 September last year.

Hollier appeared at Reading Magistrates' Court on Thursday (18/3) and was remanded in custody to appear at Reading Crown Court on 19 April.
