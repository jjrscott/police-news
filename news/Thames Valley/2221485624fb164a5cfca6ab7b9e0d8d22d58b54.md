Thames Valley Police is releasing a CCTV image following an assault in Bletchley.

At around 6pm on Tuesday 24 November, a fight broke out outside Peri Peri Chicken on Queensway.

The victim, an 18-year-old man, was punched and kicked by a group of five to six men. One of the group was carrying a wooden baseball bat.

A number of the group, including the victim, sustained cuts but did not require hospital treatment.

Investigating officer PC David Smith, based at Bletchley police station, said: "I am releasing this CCTV image as I believe the person shown will have vital information about this incident.

"If you recognise him, please get in touch using our online form or by calling 101, quoting reference number 43200392763. Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously via their website or on 0800 555 111."

**Notes to Editors**

An 18-year-old man, a 19-year-old man and a 47-year old man, all from Bletchley, have been arrested on suspicion of affray and released under investigation.
