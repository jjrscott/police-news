Thames Valley Police is appealing for help in locating a wanted woman from Milton Keynes.

Shannon Roberts, aged 23, from Milton Keynes is currently wanted on recall to prison.

Roberts is white, with long, brown hair.

She is known to frequent Milton Keynes and has links to Reading.

Investigating officer, PC David Smith, based at Bletchley police station, said: "We are appealing for help in locating Roberts who is wanted on recall to prison.

"If anyone has seen her, or has information regarding her whereabouts, please get in touch with us.

"Anyone with information can contact Thames Valley Police by calling 101 or making a report online, quoting reference 43190389669.

"Alternatively, if you wish to remain anonymous you can make a report to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or online."
