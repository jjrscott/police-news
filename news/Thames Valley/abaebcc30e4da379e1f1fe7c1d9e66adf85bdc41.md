Officers investigating a serious assault in Shinfield Road, Reading this morning (1/1), have now made an arrest.

A 24-year-old man from Reading has been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder and is in police custody.

The arrest is in connection with the incident at around 2.30am this morning near to the Best Kebab shop when a man aged in his twenties was stabbed.

There will continue to be a police presence in the area while investigations continue, and anybody with any information or who believes they witnessed the incident, should call 101 or make a report online, quoting reference number 43210000149.
