Thames Valley Police is appealing for information following a robbery in Reading.

At around 9:20pm on Sunday 31 January, the victim, a woman in her 40s, was walking down Gordon Place towards Sherwood Street when a man approached her from behind.

He demanded she hand over her bag, which she did, before he fled the scene on foot.

The bag contained a bank card and a small purse.

The suspect is described as being of slim build, around 6ft and was wearing black or dark clothing with his hood up.

Investigating Officer Detective Constable Adele Taylor, based at Reading police station, said: "While the victim of this robbery suffered no injuries, she was left shaken by the incident and we are appealing for anyone with information to come forward.

"You can make a report online or by calling 101 and quoting reference 43210042717.

"Alternatively if you wish to report 100% anonymously you can do so by calling the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
