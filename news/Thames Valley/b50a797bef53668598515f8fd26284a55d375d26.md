PC Andrew Harper's family has now confirmed details of his funeral.

The funeral will be held at Christ Church Cathedral in ** ** St Aldate's, Oxford, at 11am on Monday 14th October.

Thames Valley Police is working closely with Andrew's family to ensure we help to plan the funeral to their wishes.

This is a private funeral but we appreciate that people will want to pay their respects along the processional route.

Full details of the funeral, including the processional route and road closures, will be announced closer to the date.

Following the church service, there will be a private cremation and wake for family only.

Please remember that members of the public can leave messages of condolence to Andrew's family by emailing PCAndrewHarperCondolences@thamesvalley.pnn.police.uk

Andrew remains in our thoughts every day and we are all committed to honouring his memory.
