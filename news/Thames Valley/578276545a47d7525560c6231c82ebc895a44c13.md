Thames Valley Police is releasing CCTV images of a man officers would like to speak to regarding an incident of sexual assault in Oxford.

At around 4.20pm on 12 December 2020, a 24-year-old woman was sexually assaulted in the Boots store in Oxford Retail Park in Ambassador Avenue. An offender touched her inappropriately over her clothing.

The victim was not injured during the incident.

Investigating officer, PC Louise Darvill of Cowley police station, said: "I am appealing for the public's help in identifying the person in this CCTV image, as I believe they may have vital information in relation to this investigation.

"If you recognise this person, or have any information relating to this incident that took place on December 12 last year, please get in touch with Thames Valley Police.

"You can do this by calling 101, or making a report online, quoting reference number 43200421153. You can also contact independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
