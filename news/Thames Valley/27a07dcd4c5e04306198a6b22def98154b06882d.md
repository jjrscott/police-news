Thames Valley Police has arrested a man in connection with a firearms incident that happened in Banbury in December last year.

The 35-year-old, from Birmingham, was arrested on Friday (12/2) on suspicion of possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life. He has been released on conditional bail until 12 March.

The arrest is in connection with an incident at around 11.30pm on Sunday 20 December, 2020, when a firearm was discharged a number of times towards a house in Sinclair Avenue in Banbury. No one was injured.
