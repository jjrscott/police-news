Thames Valley Police has arrested a man in connection with a sexual assault in Reading.

A woman was sexually assaulted in Caversham at about 4pm yesterday (15/1).

A 66-year-old man from Guildford in Surrey was arrested today (16/1) and is currently in police custody.

The force would continue to appeal to anyone with any doorbell, CCTV, dashcam, or mobile phone footage in and around the area of Ian Mikardo Way yesterday. If you have footage then please contact the force by calling 101 or visiting our website, quoting reference 43210019520.
