Three men have been charged with burglary following an investigation by Thames Valley Police.

Martin Stoakes, aged 42, of Bramston Road, Brent, John Cawley, aged 59, of no fixed abode and Patrick Cawley, aged 62 of Bridge Road, Brent, were charged with three counts each of burglary in relation to three incidents that took place in Slough on Saturday 6 February.

Stoakes was also charged with an additional three counts of burglary in relation to incidents in Slough last July.

All have been remanded in custody to appear at Reading Crown Court on Monday 8 March.
