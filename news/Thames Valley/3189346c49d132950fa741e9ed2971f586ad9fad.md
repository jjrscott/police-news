Following a Thames Valley Police investigation, a man has been arrested on suspicion of sexual assault that took place in Reading.

The 27-year-old, from Reading, has been arrested on suspicion of sexual assault and criminal damage. He has been released on police bail until 7 February.

It is in connection with an incident that took place in Clas Ohlsen store in Broad Street, Reading, on Saturday 2 January.
