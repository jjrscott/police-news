A missing man from Thatcham has been found.

Tuan Abdul, known as Tony, aged 77, was last seen in Thatcham yesterday morning (13/10).

He has since been found safe and well.

Thank you to Berkshire Lowland Search and Rescue for their help in looking for Tony.

Thank you also to everyone who shared our appeal to find him.
