The family of a man who was killed in a terrorist attack in Reading have issued a tribute.

James Furlong, aged 36, who lived in the Reading area, was killed following the incident in Forbury Gardens on Saturday (20/6) evening.

His parents Gary and Janet, have released the following statement:

"James was a wonderful man. He was beautiful, intelligent, honest and fun.

"He was the best son, brother, uncle and partner you could wish for. We are thankful for the memories he gave us all. We will never forget him and he will live in our hearts forever."

The family has asked that members of the media respect their privacy at this difficult time and allow them to grieve in peace.

They have no further comments to make at this time.
