The Ritchie family have issued a further tribute to Joseph Ritchie-Bennett, who was killed in the attack, which happened at about 7pm on Saturday June 20 in Forbury Gardens.

The Ritchie family said:

"We LOVED Joe so much and we are in such deep sorrow. We need all the prayers for Joe and the Ritchie and Bennett families."
