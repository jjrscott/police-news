A man has been charged with one count of murder following an incident in Oxford on Saturday (27/6).

John Francis Joyce, aged 30, of Redbridge Hollow in Oxford, was charged this afternoon (30/6).

The charge is in connection with an incident in Redbridge Hollow on Saturday morning when a 33-year-old man was found unresponsive at a property.

Sadly, the man, who has not yet been formally identified, passed away. The man's family is being supported by specially trained officers.

Joyce was arrested on Monday morning (29/6) and charged today.

He will appear before Oxford Magistrates' Court on Wednesday (1/7).
