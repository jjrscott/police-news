Following an investigation by Thames Valley Police, a man has been charged today (4/4) in connection with a murder investigation in Thatcham.

Christopher Minards, aged 35 of Station Road, Thatcham, has been charged with one count of murder and one count of being in possession of a bladed article in a public place.

It follows the death of a woman in her forties in Crookham Hill, Thatcham on Thursday (1/4).

Minards has been remanded and is due to appear at Reading Magistrates Court on Monday (5/4).
