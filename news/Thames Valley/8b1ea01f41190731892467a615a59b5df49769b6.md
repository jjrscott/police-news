Between 3pm and 5pm on Wednesday 10 March and between 9.30am and 12pm on Thursday 11 March the media office will be closed for training purposes.

As a result, there may be a delay in responding to media enquiries at these times, but we will respond on a priority basis as soon as possible.

**The Media Team**
