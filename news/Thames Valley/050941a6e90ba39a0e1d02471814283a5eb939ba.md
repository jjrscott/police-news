Thames Valley Police is appealing for witnesses following a robbery in Oxford.

Between 5.15pm and 5.30pm on Sunday (3/1) the victim, a 16-year-old boy, was walking along Titup Hall Drive when he was pushed into the alleyway that runs between Titup Hall Drive and Palmer Road.

The offenders demanded cash from the victim and stole his mobile phone before ordering him to run away.

An iPhone was stolen.

Both offenders are described as Asian men, aged in their late teens and around 6ft tall. They were both wearing face coverings and puffer jackets.

Investigating officer PC Rebecca Nightingale based at Oxford police station said: "I am appealing to anyone who may have information about this incident to come forward.

"I am particularly keen to hear from members of the public who were on Titup Hall Drive at the time of the incident.

"You can contact us using our online form or by calling 101, quoting reference number 43210002709. Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously via their website or on 0800 555 111."
