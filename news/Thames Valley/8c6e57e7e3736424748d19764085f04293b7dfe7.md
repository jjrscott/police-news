Thames Valley Police continues to appeal for witnesses following a burglary in Milton Keynes.

At around 2.45am on Friday (29/1) an offender has broken into a house on Hadrians Drive, Bancroft Milton Keynes.

They have taken car keys from the property and taken a black Nissan Qashqai.

Investigating officer, Detective Constable Lisa Pesquero, based at Milton Keynes police station, said: "Yesterday we appealed for information to trace a black Nissan Qashqai. Officers have now located this vehicle.

"Our investigation is ongoing and we are continuing to appeal for information around the burglary.

"If anyone has information we would ask them to please contact 101 or report online quoting reference 43210039029 or alternatively if you wish to report anonymously you can do so by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
