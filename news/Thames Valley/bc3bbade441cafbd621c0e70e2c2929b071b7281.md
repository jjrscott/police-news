A man has been charged with two offences in connection with an attempted rape investigation in Oxford.

Abdul Banaras, aged 34, of Alice Smith Square in Oxford, was charged last night (18/3) with one count of attempted rape and one count of assault by penetration.

The charges are in connection with an incident on Monday afternoon (15/3) inside a property in Alice Smith Square.

Banaras was remanded in custody to appear at Oxford Magistrates' Court today (19/3).
