Following a Thames Valley Police investigation a woman has been charged with drug offences in Marlow.

Natalie Seels, aged 40, of Wycombe Road, Marlow has been charged with one count each of possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of class A, namely heroin; possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of class A, namely crack cocaine; and possession of a controlled drug of class B, namely cannabis.

It is in connection with an incident that took place on 9 August 2019 in Malvern Close, High Wycombe.

Seels has been released on bail to appear at High Wycombe Magistrates' Court on 9 March.
