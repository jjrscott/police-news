Thames Valley Police is releasing an image of a man officers would like to speak to following an assault in Oxford.

At around 11.05am on Friday 11 December, the victim, a man in his fifties, was in Aldi car park at Horspath Driftway retail park in Cowley when he witnessed a man shouting at a woman in her car.

The victim approached the man, and a verbal altercation ensued between them. The man then pushed the victim in the chest and left the scene.

The victim did not sustain any injuries or require any medical treatment.

Investigating officer PC Louise Darvill, based at Cowley police station said: "I believe the man in this image may have vital information that could help with our investigation.

"If you witnessed this incident, or recognise this man, or believe it could be you, please get in touch.

"You can make a report online or by calling 101 quoting reference 43200419886.

"Alternatively, if you wish to remain 100% anonymous you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
