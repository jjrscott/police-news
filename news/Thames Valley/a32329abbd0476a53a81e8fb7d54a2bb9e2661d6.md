Officers from Thames Valley Police's Major Crime Unit have this evening (24/7) made a further arrest in connection with a murder investigation in Thame.

A 19-year-old man from Oxfordshire has been arrested on suspicion of murder and is currently in police custody.

Three others, a 15-year-old boy and 19-year-old man from Oxfordshire and a 19-year-old man from Buckinghamshire, remain in police custody after officers secured warrants of further detention from High Wycombe Magistrates' Court while investigations continue.
