Two men and a teenage boy have been remanded in custody after being charged with drugs offences last week.

20-year-old Dino Harrison and 43-year-old Ian Cross, both of Worcester Street in Gloucester, plus a 17-year-old boy from Stroud have all been remanded after appearing at court.

Harrison and the 17-year-old boy appeared at Cheltenham Magistrates' Court on Saturday (8 August) and Cross appeared today (Monday).

All three are now due to be appearing at Gloucester Crown Court on Friday 4 September.

Harrison was charged with being concerned in the supply of heroin, being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine, two counts of possession with intent to supply class A drugs, possession with intent to supply class B drugs and two counts of possessing criminal property.

Cross was charged with being concerned in the supply of heroin and crack cocaine whilst the 17-year-old boy was charged with two counts of possession with intent to supply class A drugs and possessing criminal property.
