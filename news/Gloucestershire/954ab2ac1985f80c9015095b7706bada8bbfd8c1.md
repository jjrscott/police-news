David Vernon, 59 and of Vittoria Walk in Cheltenham is due to appear at Cheltenham Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Thursday 5 March) after being charged with preventing a lawful and decent burial of a dead body.

The charge relates to an incident where a woman's body was discovered at a property on Aldridge Close in Cheltenham on 6 June 2019.
