Police are appealing for witnesses and dashcam footage following a serious injury collision in Cheltenham yesterday morning, Sunday 21 March.

The incident happened around 11am in the areas of College Road and London Road and involved a car, a blue Tesla, and a pedestrian.

The pedestrian, a 77-year-old woman, suffered serious injuries and was treated at the scene by paramedics before being taken to Southmead Hospital in Bristol via air ambulance for further treatment.

She remains in hospital today, Monday 22 March. The driver of the Tesla was uninjured.

Officers are now appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or has dashcam footage and has not yet spoken to police to make contact.

You can do this online by completing the update a crime report form on the Constabulary's website, quoting incident 172 of 21 March https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/ or by calling police on 101.
