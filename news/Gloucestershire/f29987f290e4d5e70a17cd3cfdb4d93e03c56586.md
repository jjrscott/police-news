A woman has been arrested on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving in connection with a collision on the A436 last month.

The woman, aged in her 30s and from Bromley, was arrested last Thursday, 30 January. She has since been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Police had been called to the A436 near Seven Springs at 8.36am on Sunday 26 January following a collision which involved a grey Volkswagen Polo and a blue Vauxhall Insignia estate.

The driver of the Volkswagen Polo, a woman aged 30 and from Oxfordshire, sustained serious injuries and was pronounced dead at the scene.

Any witnesses, or anyone with information who has not yet spoken to police, is asked to call 101 quoting incident 117 of 26 January.

Information can also be submitted to us online through our 'update a crime report' form by referencing the incident number: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
