Officers investigating a burglary in Stonehouse last week where a safe and numerous items were stolen are appealing for witnesses.

At some time between 10.30am on 31 December and 10.30am on 1 January unknown offenders forced entry to the rear of the property on Oldends Lane.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed suspicious activity in the area to come forward.

Stolen items include; a green Barbour jacket, Dyson V8 hoover, PS4 console and Grand Theft Auto 5 game, two pairs of ski boots, Gucci Dive watch, a sports collection album, purple Adidas tracksuit, black/camouflage work trousers and a Gel nail kit.

Anyone with information is asked to call police on 101 and reference incident 291 of 1 January.
