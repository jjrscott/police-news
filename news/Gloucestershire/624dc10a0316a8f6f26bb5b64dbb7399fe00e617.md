Officers in the Cotswolds are appealing for witnesses to a burglary which took place in Lechlade last week.

Police were called to Bryworth Lane in the town last Monday (28 September) after the homeowners had disturbed three men who were in their property.

The offenders gained entry to the property at around 4.50pm before being confronted by the homeowner and running away.

They were described as being three young men of slim builds who were wearing balaclavas and grey/black clothing.

Anyone who has information is asked to call 101 quoting incident 357 of 28 September.

Information can also be submitted by completing the following online form and referencing the incident number: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/

Officers have been conducting extra patrols in the area and residents are asked to please remain vigilant and report suspicious activity by calling 101 or 999 if the offenders are present.

Burglary prevention advice can be found here: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/cp/crime-prevention/residential-burglary/
