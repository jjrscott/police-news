A man aged in his 50s has died following a single vehicle collision in Lydney, Forest of Dean earlier this evening (Thursday 23 January).

Police were called by the ambulance service shortly before 5.05pm with a report a vehicle had collided with a tree on the B4231 Bream Road.

A collision investigation is underway and police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision, or who has relevant dashcam footage, to make contact.

Information can be given by calling police on 101 and quoting incident 362 of 23 January.

Road closures remain in place in Lydney and motorists are advised to find alternative routes.
