A man is in police custody following a speedy and co-ordinated team effort by officers responding to a burglary in Cheltenham yesterday.

Police were called to Montreal Drive shortly after 11.20pm last night following reports that a man was seen to have forced entry to a property before stealing a quad bike.

Following a co-ordinated response, a containment of the area was put in place and the man, aged 18 and from Cheltenham, was located on Princess Elizabeth Way before being arrested on suspicion of burglary and theft of a motor vehicle.

The quad bike was recovered as part of the arrest.

The man remains in custody pending further enquiries and anyone with information is asked to call 101 quoting incident 410 of 13 September.

Information can also be submitted by completing the following online form and referencing the incident number: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
