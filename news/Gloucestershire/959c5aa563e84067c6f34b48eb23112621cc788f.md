Officers are appealing for witnesses to a collision which took place in Gloucester earlier this week.

Police were called to Cole Avenue in Gloucester on Monday (13 January) following reports of a two vehicle collision which had taken place at around 4.50pm.

It was reported that a silver transit and white van had collided at the junction for Bristol Road causing the silver transit to spin and land on its side. The driver sustained minor injuries and was treated by paramedics.

The driver of the white van did not stop at the scene and officers are appealing for witnesses to the collision.

Anyone who was in the area at the time, has information on the white van or witnessed this taking place is asked to call 101 quoting incident 335 of 13 January.
