Police are appealing for witnesses following a hedge being set on fire in Broad Oak Way, Cheltenham yesterday (30 March).

At around 8.55pm police received a report of a hedge on fire in Cheltenham. Police and the fire service attended.

No suspects were found at the scene and CCTV in the area is being viewed as a part of the ongoing investigation.

Officers are asking anyone **who witnessed** the incident or any suspicious activity in the area around the time to contact them by completing the following form quoting incident 556 of 30 March: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/

Alternatively, you can call 101 quoting the same incident number.
