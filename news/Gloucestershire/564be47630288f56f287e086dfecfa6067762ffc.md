Two men appeared at court today (Tuesday) after being charged with drugs offences.

Gentian Brati, 31 and of The Docks, Gloucester and Akil Aga, 27 and of Gloucester appeared after being charged with possession with intent to supply class B drugs and the possession of criminal property.

Akil Aga was remanded in custody to reappear on Wednesday 20 May and Gentian Brati was bailed to reappear on the same day.

They will both be appearing at Gloucester Crown Court next month.

A quantity of money and suspected cannabis were recovered as part of the investigation.
