A man has been remanded in custody after appearing at court this morning (Monday) in connection with a significant drugs find in Cheltenham on Saturday (25 April).

Allen Goulding, 38 and of Fairview Street in Cheltenham, appeared at Cheltenham Magistrates' Court charged with possession with intent to supply cocaine.

He was remanded in custody and is due to appear at Gloucester Crown Court on Friday 29 May.

Just over 1.5 kilograms of suspected cocaine was seized as part of the investigation.
