A man is due to appear at court tomorrow (Thursday) after being charged with fraud and handling stolen goods.

Curt Lodge, 35 and of Kipling Road in Cheltenham was arrested by officers yesterday (Tuesday) before being charged and remanded to appear at court.

The charges relate to an investigation into a burglary in the St Marks area of Cheltenham and the use of a suspected stolen bank card.

Lodge will be appearing at Cheltenham Magistrates Court via video link.
