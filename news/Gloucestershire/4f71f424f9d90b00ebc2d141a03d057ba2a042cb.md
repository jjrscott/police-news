Over the past few days police have received two reports relating to incidents of indecent exposure at a park in Cinderford.

The latest was at around 12.30pm on Tuesday, 14 July where a man was seen to be exposing himself while in Linear Park off Valley Road.

Police attended and spoke to people within the park about the man however he was not located.

He was described as being a south Asian male, of a large build, had black hair which was down to his ears, had facial hair and he was wearing a sports style jacket.

Another incident happened on Sunday, 12 July at around 7pm where a man who was stood in some bushes by the old railway line exposed himself to a runner.

He was wearing a royal blue t-shirt, had dark hair and a moustache. The man was described as being around 5ft 10ins tall and aged around 50 to 60 years old.

Anyone with information about the incidents is asked to call police on 101 and reference 211 of 14 July.
