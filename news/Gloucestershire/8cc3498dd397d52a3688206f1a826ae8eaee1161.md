Officers investigating a robbery in Moreton-in-Marsh last month have released CCTV images in a public appeal.

Three images have been issued of a man police want to speak to in connection with the incident which happened in a shop on 8 November.

A man had entered the shop and after a struggle with a member of staff fled with cash from the till.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured is asked to call police on 101 and quote incident 285 of 8 November or submit information online to police here https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/

You can also give information anonymously by calling independent crime fighting charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 _._
