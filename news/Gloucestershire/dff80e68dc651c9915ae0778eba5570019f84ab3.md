Officers are appealing for witnesses after a transit van, trailer and mini digger were stolen from a farm in Twyning at the weekend.

Police were called to a farm in Churchend after it was reported that a White Ford Transit Van with the registration AG57 HWO had been stolen on Friday night (12 June) along with a trailer which had a red mini digger connected to it.

The vehicle is believed to have been stolen at around 11.30pm and CCTV footage shows two men in the area before the theft took place.

Anyone with information about the vehicle, or anyone who witnessed suspicious activity in the area is asked to call police on 101 quoting incident 114 of 13 June.

Information can also be submitted via our online form: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
