Officers are appealing for witnesses to a serious collision in the Matson area of Gloucester yesterday afternoon (Monday).

Police were called to Baneberry Road at the junction of Woodruff Close at 6.10pm following reports of a collision involving a pedestrian and car.

The pedestrian was taken to Southmead Hospital in Bristol with serious injuries and the road was closed for a number of hours whilst a collision investigation took place.

Investigating officers are appealing for anyone who was in the area at the time and witnessed the collision or has dashcam/CCTV footage to get in contact.

Anyone with information is asked to contact police through the following link referencing incident 274 of 30 November:

https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/contact/af/contact-us/us/a-crime-that-has-already-been-reported/

You can also call 101 quoting the same incident number.
