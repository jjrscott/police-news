Police in Gloucester have arrested a man in connection with a suspected sexual assault of a teenager in Coney Hill this morning (Friday 26 March).

Officers received a number of reports of a man acting suspiciously in the area.

A 41-year-old man, from Quedgeley, was arrested at midday on suspicion of sexual assault and remains in police custody at this time.

Police would like to thank the public for their support.
