Officers are concerned for the welfare of a 17-year-old boy who has been reported missing.

Ethan Cooney was reported missing from where he lives in Cheshire on 8 May, however he has links to Cheltenham and it is thought he has travelled here.

Officers have been informed of sightings in the Cheltenham and Evesham areas and due to his age and the amount of time he has been missing police are concerned for his safety.

Ethan is white, 5ft 7ins tall, of slim build and with short black hair.

If you see Ethan or know of his whereabouts please call police on 101 quoting incident number 352 of 10 May.
