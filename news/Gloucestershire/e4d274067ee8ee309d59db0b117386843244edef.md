Officers are appealing for witnesses to a serious collision which took place on the A40 near Andoversford on Saturday afternoon (8 February).

Police were called at 2.35pm following reports of a collision involving a blue Mini, grey Audi Q3 and a grey Isuzu pick-up truck.

The driver of the Mini, a local woman in hers 20s, was taken to Southmead Hospital via Air Ambulance and is receiving treatment for serious injuries.

The road was closed whilst a serious collision investigation was carried out and officers would like to hear from anyone who has dashcam footage or was in the area at the time and hasn't already spoken to police.

If you have any information or saw any of the vehicles involved in the lead up to the collision please call 101 quoting incident 209 of 8 February or submit this by using our 'update a crime report' online.
