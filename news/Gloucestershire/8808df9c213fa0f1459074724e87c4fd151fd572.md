Officers are appealing for the public's help to locate a man who has been reported missing from Parkend near Lydney.

Chris Weaver, aged 38 was last seen on Woodlands Rise shortly before 7pm last night (Monday).

Chris' car was found at Lydney train station and it's believed that he may have travelled to Cardiff via train

He is described as being a white male who is 5ft 5ins tall, of a slim build and has short brown hair.

When last seen he was wearing a beige hooded jacket, blue jeans and black shoes.

There are concerns for Chris' welfare and anyone who has seen or knows where he may be is asked to call 101 quoting incident 376 of 10 February.
