Debbie Hicks, aged 46 and of Stratford Road in Stroud, has been charged with using threatening, abusive or insulting words or behaviour likely to cause harassment, alarm or distress under Section 5 of the Public Order Act.

She has been released on bail and is due to appear at Cheltenham Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 30 March.

The charge relates to an incident at Gloucestershire Royal Hospital on Monday 28 December.
