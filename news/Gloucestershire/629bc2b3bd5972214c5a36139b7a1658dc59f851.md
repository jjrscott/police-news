Officers are investigating a burglary which took place in Cheltenham at the end of last month where sentimental jewellery was taken.

Between 5pm and 9pm on Saturday 27 February unknown offenders forced entry to a property on Winchester Way and stole a gold necklace and a pair of earrings.

Investigating officers and are now appealing for witnesses to any suspicious activity in the area at the time.

If you think you saw anything suspicious in the area at that time or have any information regarding this incident please contact police via this online form https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/ quoting incident number 545 of 27 February.

Alternatively, you can contact police on 101 quoting the same incident number.

Information on protecting your home from burglary can be found here: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/police-forces/gloucestershire-constabulary/areas/gloucestershire/campaigns/burglary/
