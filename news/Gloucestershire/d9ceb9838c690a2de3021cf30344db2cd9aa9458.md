Officers investigating the theft of a food van in Cirencester have released images in a bid to locate it.

It was reported that a trailer, which is being used as a mobile fast food van, was stolen from a property in Hatherop on Saturday 19 September.

CCTV enquiries show that a 4x4 or large estate vehicle was seen to be leaving the area with the trailer at around 12.15am.

The trailer has the registration TD02 MJD and anyone who has seen it or has information about the theft is asked to call 101 quoting incident 86 of 19 September.

Information can also be submitted by completing the following online form and referencing the incident number: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
