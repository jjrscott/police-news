A man has been charged in connection with an incident earlier this week where a teenage girl was grabbed in Chalford.

Robert Hall, 36 and of Stroud has today been charged with one count of kidnap (Saturday 6 June).

He has been remanded in police custody and is due to appear at Cheltenham Magistrates' Court on Monday (8 June).
