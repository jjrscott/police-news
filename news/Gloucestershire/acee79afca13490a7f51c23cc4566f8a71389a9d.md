The Gloucester Neighbourhood Policing Team are appealing for witnesses after a number of vehicles were damaged overnight on Monday/Tuesday.

It was reported yesterday that a number of vehicles which were parked on Tuffley Lane were found to have been damaged by unknown offenders.

The damage to the vehicles includes scratches to the bonnets which are believed to have been caused by sharp objects.

CCTV enquiries have taken place as well as house to house enquiries in the area however officers are asking anyone with information who hasn't already spoken to them to come forward.

Anyone who can help with the investigation or who has also had their vehicle targeted is asked to contact police quoting incident 228 of 23 February.

This can be done by calling 101 or completing the following online form: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report
