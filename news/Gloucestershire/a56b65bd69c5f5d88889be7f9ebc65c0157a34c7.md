Officers in the Forest of Dean are appealing for witnesses to an altercation which took place in Coleford at the end of last month.

Police were called to Gloucester Road on Saturday 31 October following reports that an altercation involving a number of people had taken place at around 9.30pm.

It was reported that the altercation broke out between a group of around five people and that a woman sustained facial injuries during this.

This took place near to the Feathers Public House and anyone who witnessed what happened or has information is asked to contact police quoting incident quoting incident 588 of 31 October either online at https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/contact/af/contact-us/us/a-crime-that-has-already-been-reported/ or by calling 101.
