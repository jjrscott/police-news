Police are appealing for witnesses after a member of the public reported seeing a car being driven erratically on Heron Way on Saturday afternoon (4 July).

Police received a called shortly after 6.35pm where it was reported that a small black car, possibly a Fiat or Hyundai, was swerving on the road and nearly mounted the path.

Officers are asking anyone who was in the area and has dashcam/CCTV footage, witnessed this taking place or has information on the car to please call 101 quoting incident 418 of 4 July.

Information can also be submitted via our online submission form: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
