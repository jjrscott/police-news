Officers from the Cheltenham Neighbourhood Policing Team are appealing for witnesses after a car was damaged in Montpellier last week.

Police were called to Montpellier Walk shortly after 11.55pm last Wednesday (24 June) following reports that a car had been damaged near to the main entrance of Montpellier Gardens.

Witnesses reported seeing a group of people who were involved in an argument taking glass bottles from a nearby bin and throwing them.

Investigating officers are asking anyone who witnessed the incident or has information to please call 101 quoting incident 693 of 24 June.

Information can also be submitted via our online submission form: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
