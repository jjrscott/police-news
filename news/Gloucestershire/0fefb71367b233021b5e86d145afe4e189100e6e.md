Officers investigating a suspicious fire in Gloucester last month have released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the incident.

Police and the Fire Service were called to The Strand on London Road on the afternoon of Saturday 14 December following reports of a fire inside the block of flats.

It was reported that a man had entered the premises between 2.20pm and 2.40pm before starting a fire in a communal corridor.

Anyone who recognises the man or knows who he may be is asked to call 101 quoting incident 257 of 14 December.

You can also contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at https://crimestoppers-uk.org/give-information/forms/give-information-anonymously
