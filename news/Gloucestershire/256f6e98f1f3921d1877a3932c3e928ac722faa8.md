Officers in the Cotswolds are appealing for witnesses after a large quantity of diesel was stolen from a coach company in Bourton on the Water last week.

Police were called to Pulham Coaches at Bourton Industrial Park after it was found that men had entered the site on multiple occasions and stolen 27,000 litres of diesel which has an estimated value of £30,000.

CCTV enquiries show that offenders, who were wearing dark clothing, attended the site at 11.09pm on Monday 1 February, 11.03pm on Tuesday 2 February after arriving in a large white, unmarked non-articulated lorry before leaving at 00.14am and also attended at 00.28am on Wednesday 3 February after arriving in a large white, unmarked non-articulated lorry and leaving at 1.56am.

Investigating officers are asking anyone who saw suspicious activity, has CCTV footage or information on the vehicles to contact police quoting incident 171 of 3 February.
