A black Land Rover Discovery, registration CK17 DVL, was stolen from a driveway on Evesham Road, Cheltenham last week following a burglary.

Have you seen the car? It was stolen around 1.20am on Tuesday 2 June after unknown offenders forced entry to a premises overnight.

An empty safe, sets of keys and alcohol were also stolen in the break in.

Anyone with information about the vehicle, or anyone who witnessed suspicious activity in the area, is asked to please call us on 101 and quote incident 107 of 2 June.

You can also submit information online by quoting the incident number: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
