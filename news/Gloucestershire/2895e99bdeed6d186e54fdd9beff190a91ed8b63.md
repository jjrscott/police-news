A man has been charged with drugs offences following proactive work by officers from the Cheltenham Neighbourhood Policing Team.

Bailey Johnson, 20 and of Moors Avenue, Cheltenham was arrested on Wednesday evening (22 July) after officers spotted him acting suspiciously

He was subsequently charged with being concerned in the supply of cocaine, crack cocaine and cannabis and has been remanded to appear at Cheltenham Magistrates' Court today (Friday 24 July).

A quantity of drugs was recovered as part of the investigation following the execution of a search at a nearby address.
