Police are appealing for witnesses following a single vehicle collision on the A38 Tewkesbury Road in Gloucester.

Police attended the scene of the collision at around 7.50pm this evening (Tuesday 30 March) where a green Mini had left the road. Two passengers from the vehicle left the scene before police arrived.

Investigating officers want to speak to anyone who saw two men leaving the scene around that time. One man was wearing a white t-shirt and the other wearing a dark coloured sleeveless top.

Officers are concerned for the two men's welfare following the collision.

If you witnessed the incident or two men leaving the scene around 7.50pm, please contact police on 101 quoting incident 513 of 30 March.
