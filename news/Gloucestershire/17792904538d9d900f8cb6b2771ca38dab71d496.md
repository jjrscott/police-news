A man has been charged in connection with a fire at a property in Tewkesbury on Sunday night.

Emergency services attended an address in Oldbury Road shortly after 10pm following reports of a fire in a flat.

No-one was injured.

Nicholas Perry, 59, of Oldbury Road, Tewkesbury was later arrested and subsequently charged with arson with intent to endanger life.

He is due to appear at Cheltenham Magistrates' Court by video link today, Tuesday 5 May.
