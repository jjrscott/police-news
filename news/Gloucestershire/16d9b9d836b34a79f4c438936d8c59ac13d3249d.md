Officers have issued a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with an assault outside a pub on West Market Place, Cirencester on Monday 31 August in Cirencester

Following other enquiries, officers are now turning to the public for help identifying the man.

Anyone who recognises the man in the image is asked to call 101 and quote incident 353 of 31 August.

Alternatively, information can be reported via our online form at www.gloucestershire.police.uk or anonymously via the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
