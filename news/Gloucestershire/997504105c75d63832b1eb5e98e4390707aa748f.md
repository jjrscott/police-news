A man has been charged in connection with an incident where threats were made in Gloucester on Monday (6 January).

Adrian Wilce, 61 and of Painswick Road, Gloucester has been charged with assault and making threats to kill.

He has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Cheltenham Magistrates' Court today, Wednesday 8 January.

The charges relate to an incident on Painswick Road where police received reports that a man had made threats whilst in possession of an axe.
