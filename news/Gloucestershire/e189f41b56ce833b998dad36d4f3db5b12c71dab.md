Police are appealing for witnesses and any CCTV footage after a pharmacy in Brockworth was broken into in the early hours of this morning, Monday 22 February.

At around 4.20am police were called with reports of a break in at a pharmacy on Abbotswood Road.

Unknown offender(s) forced entry through the sliding doors and stole cash and a number of medicines.

Investigating officers are asking anyone who witnessed anything suspicious in the area at the time to contact police.

They are also asking for anyone who believes they may have relevant CCTV footage or dashcam footage to contact them.

If you have any information please contact police using the following form and quoting incident 34 of 22 February: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/

Alternatively, phone 101 quoting the same incident number.
