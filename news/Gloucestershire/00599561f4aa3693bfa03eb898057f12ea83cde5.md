Officers are appealing for witnesses to a suspected arson which took place outside the Cam Parish Council Offices at the beginning of this month.

Police received a report that someone had set fire to a number of wooden planters outside the building which is located on Noel Lee Way.

It is believed that the fire was started by an unknown person at some point between 4pm on Tuesday 4 February and 8.30am on Wednesday 5 February.

If you were in the area at the time or have any information that may help with our enquiries please call 101 quoting incident 115 of 5 February.

Information can also be submitted by using the 'update a crime report' form on our website: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
