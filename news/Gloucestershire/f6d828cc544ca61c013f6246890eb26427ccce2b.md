A motorcyclist suffered serious injuries in a two-vehicle collision on the A48 near to Blakeney yesterday afternoon, Tuesday 9 March.

The incident happened around 3pm and involved a car and a motorcycle.

The rider of the motorcycle was taken to Southmead Hospital in Bristol by the ambulance service and he remains there today, Wednesday 10 March.

Investigating officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision near the Viney Hill junction, or for anyone who has dashcam footage, to contact them.

You can do this by completing the following form online, referencing incident 302 of 9 March https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/ or by calling police on 101 and quoting the same number.

Closures were put in place for a number of hours while emergency services were at the scene and these were removed last night.
