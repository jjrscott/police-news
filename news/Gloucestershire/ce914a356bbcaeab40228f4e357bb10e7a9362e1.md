Gloucestershire Constabulary is appealing for information that will help to locate a wanted man.

Odin Higgs, aged 33 and previously of Wintour Drive in Lydney is wanted on a warrant for failing to appear at court in 2017 in relation to assault offences.

Officers have completed a number of enquiries to locate Higgs such as conducting arrest attempts, house to house enquiries, liaising with partner agencies and sharing internal appeals but have been unable to locate him.

He is described as being 5ft 8ins tall, of a medium build, has short dark brown curly hair and a crooked nose.

He is known to frequent the Whaddon area of Cheltenham as well as Lydney and could also be in the Tewkesbury area.

If you see Higgs, or have any information on his whereabouts please contact Gloucestershire Constabulary on 101 quoting crime reference 027783/17.

You can also anonymously contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/give-information/forms/give-information-anonymously
