Officers are appealing for witnesses to a collision between a car and cyclist which took place in Longlevens earlier this week.

The collision is believed to have taken place on Innsworth Lane between 12pm and 1pm on Monday afternoon (23 November) and involved a silver/grey coloured Audi saloon and a man riding a green and black Scott Octane bicycle.

The cyclist sustained minor injuries as a result of the collision.

Anyone who was in the area at he time and witnessed this taking place or has information is asked to complete the following online form referencing incident 272 of 23 November: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/.

Information can also be submitted by calling 101 with the same incident number.
