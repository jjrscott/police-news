Officers investigating a burglary in Cheltenham are trying to identify the rightful owner of the pictured women's bicycle after it was seized from a man who has been arrested.

Police were called to Buckingham Avenue in Rowanfield shortly after 2.05pm this afternoon after it was reported that a member of the public had seen a man break into a property.

Officers from the Cheltenham Neighbourhood Policing Team attended and located the man nearby before seizing the bicycle from him as it was suspected to have been stolen.

A 43-year-old man from Cheltenham is in police custody after being arrested on suspicion of burglary.

Anyone who recognises the bike or has information on the burglary is asked to contact police through the following link referencing incident 204 of 15 January: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/contact/af/contact-us/us/a-crime-that-has-already-been-reported/ or by calling 101.
