At around 9.10am today (Thursday 6 February) a woman suffered injuries in a collision near to the junction of Flint Road with Caernarvon Road in Cheltenham.

The elderly pedestrian was taken to Gloucestershire Royal Hospital following a collision with a car and her injuries are not believed to be life threatening.

Flint Road was cordoned off for a number of hours to allow for a collision investigation to take place.

Investigating officers are appealing for any witnesses, or anyone with relevant dashcam footage, to call police on 101 and quote incident 81 of 6 February.

Information can also be submitted to police online through the 'update a crime report' form on their website by quoting the incident number: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
