On Friday (5 June) two suspected stolen bikes were recovered from a property in Cheltenham during a search and officers are now appealing to find the owners.

One is a lime green Pinnacle mountain bike with the serial number 021819763.

The second is a yellow and black Fantic electric bike with no serial number, but with the barcode number of 06T80695022.

A 19-year-old woman from Cheltenham was arrested on suspicion of handling stolen goods and has since been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

If you believe one of these bikes belongs to you please contact police on 101 and quote incident 181 of 4 June.
