Police are at an address in Kingsholm Road, Kingsholm in Gloucester following the death of a man in his 30s.

Police were called by the ambulance service at 8.05pm on Thursday 15 October and Gloucestershire Constabulary is in the early stages of an investigation.

At this time the death is being treated as suspicious. His next of kin and the coroner has been informed.

Anyone who has any information and has not yet spoken to police is asked to please contact 101 and quote incident number 415 of 15 October.
