Officers are appealing for witnesses to a collision which took place in Cheltenham earlier this week.

Police were called to A4019 Tewksbury Road near to Princess Elizabeth Way shortly after 4.20pm on Tuesday (25 February) following reports of a collision involving a cyclist and a car.

It was reported that this took place outside of the Blade Audi dealership.

Police are trying to locate the driver of the white car, believed to be a Nissan Qashqai with a dark bumper, and are appealing for witnesses to the collision.

Anyone who was in the area at the time, has information about the white car or witnessed this taking place is asked to call 101 quoting incident 274 of 25 February.

Information can also be submitted by using the 'update a crime report' form on our website: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
