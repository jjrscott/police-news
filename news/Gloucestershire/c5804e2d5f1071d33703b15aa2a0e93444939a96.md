Officers investigating a commercial burglary in Tewkesbury have issued a CCTV image of four men that they would like to identify in connection with the incident.

Police received a report of a burglary which is believed to have taken place at a unit on the Tewkesbury Business Park on Shannon Way between 11.45pm and midnight on Saturday (21 December).

It is believed that offenders forced entry to the premises before conducting an untidy search however it is unknown if anything was stolen during the burglary.

If you recognise the men pictured or have any information please call 101 quoting incident 10 of 22 December.

Information can also be submitted via our online form: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/soh/something-youve-seen-or-heard/

We are currently running an anti-burglary campaign and more information as well as tips on preventing yourself from becoming the victim of a burglary can be found here: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/police-forces/gloucestershire-constabulary/areas/gloucestershire/campaigns/campaigns/2019/burglary/
