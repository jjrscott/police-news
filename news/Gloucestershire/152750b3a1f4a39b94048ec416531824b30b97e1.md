Officers are investigating a burglary where a number of high value items were stolen from a flat in Cheltenham ** ** last month.

At around 5.30pm on Tuesday 5 January a flat in Wellington Square, Cheltenham was broken into and suspect(s) have stolen Macbooks, a game device and alcohol.

Officers are now asking people whether they saw or any suspicious activity in the area at that time.

Anyone who has any information is asked to call 101 quoting incident 331 of 5 January.
