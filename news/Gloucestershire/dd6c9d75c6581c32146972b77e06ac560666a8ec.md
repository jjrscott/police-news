A man is due to appear in court next month after being charged with burglary.

Bernard McDonagh, aged 27 and of Draycott in Cam was arrested yesterday morning (Thursday) before being charged with burglary with intent to steal.

The charges relate to a burglary which took place at a farm in Chalford on 29 October 2019.

McDonagh has been released on bail and is due to appear at Cheltenham Magistrates' Court on Friday 20 March.
