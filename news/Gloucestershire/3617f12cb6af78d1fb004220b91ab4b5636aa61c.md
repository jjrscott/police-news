Officers are appealing for witnesses to an assault which took place in Cheltenham town centre last month.

It was reported that a man was assaulted on Cambray Place at around 2.05am on Sunday 17 November and that he sustained a fractured eye socket and broken nose after being punched.

The offender was with a group of people at the time who are understood to have walked off in the direction of Rodney Road following the assault.

Anyone who was in the area at the time or witnessed this taking place is asked to call 101 quoting incident 298 of 17 November.
