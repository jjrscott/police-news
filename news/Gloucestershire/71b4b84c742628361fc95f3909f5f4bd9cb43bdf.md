Officers have released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with a burglary in Nailsworth near Stroud last month.

Police were called to Nailsworth Vape Shop on Cossack Square on Sunday 13 September following reports of a burglary overnight.

It is believed that a man forced entry to the premises before conducting an untidy search, stealing a quantity of e-cigarettes and vape liquids and leaving the area on foot.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured or has information about the burglary is asked to call 101 quoting incident 116 of 13 September.

Information can also be submitted by completing the following online form and referencing the incident number: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
