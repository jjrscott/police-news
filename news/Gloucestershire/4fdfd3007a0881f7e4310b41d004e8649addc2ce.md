Two people are in custody as part of a Cheltenham Neighbourhood Policing Team investigation into a burglary which took place at the end of last month.

Police were called with reports that a property had been broken into overnight on 28/29 December.

Officers who have been conducting enquiries attended a property this morning and arrested a 49-year-old man and 32-year-old woman on suspicion of burglary after they were linked to the incident.

Enquiries are ongoing at this time and officers are now working to identify the owners of three bicycles that were seized during the arrests.

Anyone who recognises the bikes is asked to contact police by completing the following form and quoting incident 68 of 27 January: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/

Information can also be submitted by calling 101.
