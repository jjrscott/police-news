A man is due to appear at court today (Friday) after being charged with a number of burglary offences.

Joshua Hayter, 22 and of no fixed abode has been charged with four counts of dwelling burglary and theft using distraction, two counts of burglary at business premises and one count of theft from a motor vehicle.

Hayter will be appearing at Cheltenham Magistrates Court via video link.

The charges follow a proactive arrest attempt by officers investigating a number of incidents in Cheltenham.
