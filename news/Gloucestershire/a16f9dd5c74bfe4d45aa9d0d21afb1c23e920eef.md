Officers are appealing for witnesses after a man was seen looking through a ground floor bathroom window as a woman was showering.

The incident occurred at Perryfield Court, Lansdowne, Bourton On The Water on Saturday 7 March at around 6.30pm.

It was reported that a man pushed open a window which was already ajar and watched a woman shower before being noticed and leaving.

The man is described as having a tanned complexion, aged in his late 30s to early 40s, clean shaven with a round shaped face, straight white teeth and dark hair.

Officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or anyone acting suspiciously in the area at the time to contact police on 101 quoting incident 380 of the 7 March.

Alternatively information can also be submitted here: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/soh/something-youve-seen-or-heard/
