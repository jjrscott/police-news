A person has died following a collision on the M5 near Quedgeley this morning.

Two cars collided between junction 12 and junction 11A northbound at around 8.10am today (Thursday 2 January).

The air ambulance attended but one person was declared dead a short time later.

Officers are currently making contact with their next of kin so further details cannot be released at this time.

The driver of the other vehicle suffered non-serious injuries but is being checked over at hospital.

The northbound carriageway remains closed between junction 12 and junction 11A while a serious collision investigation takes place.

Details of the diversion route in place can be found here: http://m.highwaysengland.co.uk/#news

The southbound carriageway is now open.
