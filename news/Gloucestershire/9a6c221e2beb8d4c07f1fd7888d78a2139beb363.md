Officers believe that the offenders were selective when searching the property before stealing items including batteries, charging cables and boxes to complete sets of tools.

The stolen tools are as follows:

 * Three Makita drills
 * A Makita nail gun
 * A Makita battery
 * A M-Tech M200 plastering machine
 * An AH battery set
 * A Makita radio
 * A Kres screw gun
 * A Makita battery charge
 * A Stanley storage box

Anyone who witnessed suspicious activity in the area, has been offered tools or has information is asked to call 101 quoting incident 90 of 5 March.

Alternatively, information can also be submitted by using the 'update a crime report' form on the Constabulary's website: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
