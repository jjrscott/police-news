Police are appealing for witnesses to an assault which happened in Kingsway, Gloucester last month where three men were repeatedly kicked and punched.

Between 9pm and 9.15pm on Thursday 13 August there was a verbal altercation between a group of three men and a large group of teenagers outside Tesco Express.

The group of 10 to 15 teenagers then followed the men and assaulted them outside Pizza Hut, causing them varying levels of injury.

The teenagers had been in the area for a large part of the evening with most being on foot and a couple on bicycles.

Investigating officers are now appealing for anyone with information which could assist with identifying the teenagers to come forward.

Information can be submitted to police online by completing the update a crime report from on their website and quoting incident 542 of 13 August: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/

You can also call police on 101, anonymously call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or provide information online: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/

The local policing team is aware of the ongoing anti-social behaviour issues in the area and will be patrolling on a regular basis.
