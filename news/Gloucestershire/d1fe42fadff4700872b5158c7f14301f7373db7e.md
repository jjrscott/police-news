A teenager has been charged in connection with an incident in Bruton Way, Gloucester over the weekend and remanded in custody.

Naphtali Campbell-Barrington, aged 19 and of no fixed address, has been charged with attempted murder, possession of a bladed article and robbery in relation to an incident on Saturday 20 February.

He appeared at Cheltenham Magistrates' Court via video link earlier today, Monday 22 February, and has been remanded in custody to next appear at Gloucester Crown Court on 22 March.

A man who required medical treatment in connection with the incident has since been discharged from hospital.
