Two men have been charged in connection with the stabbing of a man in Stroud on Saturday (6 February).

Liam Wells, 25 and of no fixed abode and Daniel Banks, 24 and of Kings Road in Rodborough have been charged with attempted murder and will appear in court via video link this morning (Monday).

The victim remains in hospital in a stable condition at this time.

A woman arrested on suspicion of assisting an offender has been released on police bail to return on 5 March.

If you have any information or witnessed any suspicious activity in Mathews Way, Stroud in the early hours of Saturday 6 February, contact police on 101 quoting incident 18 of 6 February.

Alternatively, you can complete the following online form: https://www.gloucestershire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/cor/tell-us-about-existing-case-report/
