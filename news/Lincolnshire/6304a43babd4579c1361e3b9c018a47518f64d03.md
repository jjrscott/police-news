We are appealing for witnesses following an assault in the Grantham area last night.

Between 6:30 and 7:00 pm on Tuesday March 23, we received reports of an assault that occurred in the area of Grantley Street and Launder Terrace.

A man sustained injuries consistent with a knife wound and was taken to hospital. Thankfully his injuries are not believed to be life-threatening.

We are appealing for any witnesses to the incident or anyone who may have CCTV or Dash Cam footage. If you can help, please contact us in one of the following ways below:

 * By calling our non-emergency number 101 quoting incident 404 of yesterday’s date.
 * By emailing force.control@lincs.pnn.police.uk or David.asher@lincs.pnn.police.uk – don’t forget to include incident 404 of March 23 2021 in the subject line.
 * Anonymously, via the independent charity, CrimeStoppers on 0800 555 111 or online https://crimestoppers-uk.org/

**Reference:** Incident 404 March 23 2021
