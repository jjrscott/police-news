We are appealing for witnesses following an assault against a vulnerable person in Gainsborough.

The incident occurred at a private address close to Gainsborough town centre. Thankfully the 35-year-old victim sustained no serious injuries and is being safeguarded.

A woman in her 30s has been arrested in connection with the incident.

Investigations are currently ongoing, and we are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or anyone who may have any information that can assist with our enquiries.

If you can help, please get in touch:

 * By calling our non-emergency number 101 quoting incident 480 of 01/04/2021.
 * By emailing force.control@lincs.pnn.police.uk – don’t forget to include incident 480 of 01/04/2021.
 * Anonymously, via the independent charity, CrimeStoppers, on 0800 555 111 or online.

**Reference:** Incident 480 of April 1, 2021
