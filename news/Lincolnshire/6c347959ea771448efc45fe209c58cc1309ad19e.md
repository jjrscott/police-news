We are appealing for information to help trace the individual shown in the CCTV photographs.

Officers would like to talk to him in connection with an assault on a 10-year-old boy who is believed to have been kicked and punched by an unknown male. He was not seriously hurt but was left shaken and upset.

The incident took place on Thursday 17th December at around 5.10pm – 5.20pm at Arlington Gardens in Grantham.

If you know who the individual shown is please contact us through one of the following ways:

 * By calling 101 quoting incident 344 of 17th December 2020
 * By emailing force.control@lincs.pnn.police.uk – don’t forget to include incident 344 of 17th December 2020 in the subject line
 * Anonymously, via the independent charity CrimeStoppers on 0800 555 111 or online
