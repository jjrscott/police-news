**Update, March 31**

The 30-year-old man who was arrested in connection with an assault in Boston Central Park has been released on bail.

Our investigations continue and we are appealing for anyone with any information to call us on 101, quoting incident 428 of March 28.

**Update, March 30**

A 30-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of GBH following this assault.

He has been remanded in custody and will be questioned in due course.

The victim remains in hospital.

Boston Central Park has now been reopened to the public.

We continue to appeal for witnesses. Anyone with any information should call us on 101, quoting incident 428 of March 28.

**Original release** :

We are investigating an assault in Boston Central Park in which a man in his 40s received serious injuries. He has been taken to hospital.

The assault was reported to us at around 5pm on Sunday 28th March.

The park has been closed to the public while our investigations continue. We will open the park as soon as our investigations conclude.

No arrests have been made at this stage.

Anyone with any information should call us on 101, quoting incident 248 of 28th.
