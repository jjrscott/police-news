Between 6:15-6:30 pm on Wednesday 16th December 2020, a 33-year-old male was travelling on his mobility scooter on Breedon Drive, Lincoln towards Honnington Approach.

Whilst on the junction, a male suspect wearing dark clothing exited a red Vauxhall Astra that was parked on the road and approached the victim.

The suspect then hit the victim with an iron bar numerous times, got back in his vehicle and drove away from the scene. The 33-year-old suffered minor injuries as a result.

We are appealing for any witnesses to this attack to come forward. We are also appealing for information on the registration details of the vehicle. If you can help:

 * Call 101 quoting reference 20000668141.
 * Email force.control@lincs.pnn.police.uk – don’t forget to include the reference number 20000668141 in the subject line.
 * Report it anonymously via the independent charity CrimeStoppers on 0800 555 111 or online.
