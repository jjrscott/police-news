We are appealing for witnesses and dashcam footage following a fatal collision at Ludford.

Following a call received at 11.24am yesterday (30th March) we attended an RTC on the B1225 about 200 metres south of its junction with the A631.

The collision involved two vehicles: A white Triumph motorcycle, and a Mercedes-Benz Sprinter van.

The rider of the motorcycle, a man in his 50s from the Scunthorpe area, was pronounced dead at the scene.

The road was closed for some time while emergency services attended the scene. It was re-opened at around 6.30pm.

If you saw the collision, or have dashcam footage, please get in touch as you may be able to help our investigation.

 * Call 101, quoting Incident 138 of 30th March;
 * Email force.control@lincs.pnn.police.uk putting “Incident 138 of 30th March” in the Subject line;
 * Call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555111.
