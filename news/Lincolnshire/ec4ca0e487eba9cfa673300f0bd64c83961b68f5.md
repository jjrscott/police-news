We are appealing for witnesses following a robbery at the Spar Store in Lincoln.

Between 9:45-10pm last night (2nd April), two males entered the Spar Store on Brayford Wharf and proceeded to take canned drinks from the shelves.

When challenged by an employee, one of the males produced a bladed weapon before running out of the shop. Thankfully there were no injuries reported.

Two male youths, aged 12 and 15, were arrested in connection with the incident. The 15-year-old remains in police custody and the 12-year-old has been released on police bail.

If you witnessed the incident or if you were in the area between 9:45-10pm last night, please get in touch in one of the following ways below:

 * By calling 101, quoting incident 434 of yesterday’s date.
 * By emailing force.control@lincs.pnn.police.uk – don’t forget to include incident 434 of April 2, 2021.
 * Anonymously, via the independent charity, CrimeStoppers on 0800 555 111 or online.
