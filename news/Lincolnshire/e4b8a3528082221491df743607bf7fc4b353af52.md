We are appealing for witnesses and dashcam footage following a fatal collision near Spalding.

At 8.18pm last night (20th March) we received a report that a silver Peugeot 207 was in a ditch on Weston Hills Road, Low Fulney. No other vehicles were involved in the incident.

The driver of the Peugeot, a man in his 30s, was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Two passengers in the car, a woman and a man, were both taken to hospital.

The road was closed for a time while emergency services attended, and was re-opened at around 3.50am this morning (21st March).

If you saw the incident, or have dashcam footage, please get in contact with us:

 * Call 101, quoting Incident 444 of 20th March;
 * Email force.control@lincs.pnn.police.uk putting “Incident 444 of 20th March” in the Subject line.
