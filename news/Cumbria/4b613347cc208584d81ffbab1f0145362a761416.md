Have you seen missing 15-year-old Katie Burgess, who is from the Kirkbride area?

Katie was last seen at around 1pm on Saturday 28th September on the Kirkbride to Carlisle Road.

Kate is around 5ft 6ins tall and of slim build, with long straight blonde hair and she has a lip piercing and a tanned face.

She is believed to be wearing a small pink dress with a dark-coloured puffa jacket with a fur hood.

Katie also speaks with a Liverpool accent.
