Posted on 10:04:00 on 29th November 2019

A 33-year-old man has been charged following an attempted robbery that happened on Telford Street, Barrow on Wednesday (27 November) at around 5.25pm.

Sean Walker of Smeaton Street, Barrow, will appear at South Cumbria Magistrates court this morning (Friday 29).
