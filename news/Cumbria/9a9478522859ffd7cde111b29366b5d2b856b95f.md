Posted on 10:25:00 on 23rd July 2020

A man who was wanted by Cumbria Police in connection with suspected drug supply has been located.

Kyle Annett, 25 of no fixed address has been charged with conspiracy to supply Class A controlled drug- Heroin, conspiracy to supply Class A controlled drug - crack cocaine and possession with intent to supply a controlled drug Class B - Cannabis.

He appeared at Barrow Magistrates yesterday (July 22) and has been remanded in custody.
