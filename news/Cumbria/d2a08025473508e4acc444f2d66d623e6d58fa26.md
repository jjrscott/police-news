Posted on 14:05:00 on 22nd March 2021

Police can confirm that the body of Edward Allanson was found in the area of Kirklinton, shortly after 12pm on Tuesday 16th March.

The death is not being treated as suspicious.

The family would like to thank those that helped in the search for Edward.
