Posted on 23:42:00 on 30th May 2020

Emergency services are currently responding to a large fire at the former Fusion nightclub building in Workington.

Police were alerted to a fire shortly before 11pm on Saturday (30 May) at the premises on Ladies Walk.

Nearby premises and homes on Ladies Walk and Coopers Walk have been evacuated whilst crews from Cumbria Fire & Rescue Service tackle the fire. There are no reported injuries at this time.

Washington Street is currently closed in both directions to assist fire crews.

A cordon is in place and members of the public are urged not to attend the scene.
