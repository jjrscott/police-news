Posted on 12:12:00 on 22nd August 2019

Police can confirm that 51-year-old James Graham from Threlkeld, Keswick, who was involved in a crash near Buttermere on the 2nd August, sadly died at the Royal Preston Hospital on the 15th August.

Mr Graham was riding his motorcycle at the time of the accident. No other vehicles were involved.

Any witnesses to the crash, which happened at approximately 7pm on the 2nd August, or anyone with dashcam footage, are asked to contact police on 101 or alternatively email 101@cumbria.police.uk referring to incident 232 of 2nd August 2019.
