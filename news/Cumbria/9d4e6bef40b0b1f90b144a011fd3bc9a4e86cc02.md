Posted on 14:56:00 on 22nd February 2021

Officers on Saturday 20th February stopped a vehicle travelling northbound on the M6 near junction 37.

The vehicle an Audi was searched by officers, a large amount of cannabis was located in the vehicle.

Qiu Chen, aged 23, of Bromsgrove Street, Birmingham was charged with possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class B - Cannabis.

Robert Leung, aged 65, of Bromsgrove Street, Birmingham was charged with possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class B - Cannabis.

Both are to appear at South Cumbria Magistrates this morning (22 Feb)
