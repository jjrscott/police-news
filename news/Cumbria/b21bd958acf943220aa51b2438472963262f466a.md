Posted on 15:46:00 on 27th May 2020

Officers are appealing for information following an assault that occurred at around 7pm yesterday (May 26) on Botchergate, Carlisle outside Marios takeaway.

A man in his 30s from Carlisle suffered serious injuries and was taken to Cumberland Infirmary Carlisle.

Enquiries are ongoing into the incident.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police on 101, email 101@cumbria.police.uk, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting log 188 of the 26th May 2020.
