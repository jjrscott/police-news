Posted on 13:38:00 on 1st March 2021

Police were call at 12:56pm today (1st March) to the report of a serious road traffic collision on West Tower Street, Carlisle, involving a vehicle and two pedestrians.

The road is closed whilst emergency services attend the scene.

The road may be closed for some time whilst investigations are conducted.

Congestion is to be expected in the area and road users are advised to plan their routes accordingly.
