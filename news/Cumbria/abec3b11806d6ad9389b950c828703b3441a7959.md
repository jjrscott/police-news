Posted on 15:28:00 on 14th June 2019

Police are investigating a serious collison on the A590.
Officers were called to the road at Levens at 4.53pm yesterday (June 13) following a report of a one-car collision.

The collision involved a Ford Focus, driven by a man, 61, of Grange Over Sands.

He was taken to the Cumberland Infirmary in Carlisle where he was today in a critical condition.

Police are appealing for witnesses who may have been in the area at the time to contact officers with any information.
Officers would also like to hear from anyone who has dashcam footage of the vehicle.
Call Cumbria Police on 101, referencing incident number 165 of June 13.
