Posted on 14:16:00 on 22nd November 2019

Police are currently dealing with a road traffic collision involving a pedestrian and a car at Bridges Retail Park, Whitehaven near B&M.

The pedestrian, a 91-year-old woman was taken to Cumberland Infirmary Carlisle with serious injuries.

The car involved was a Kia Rio driven by a woman, 27 of Frizington.

Motorists are advised to avoid the area as delays are expected and traffic is building up.
