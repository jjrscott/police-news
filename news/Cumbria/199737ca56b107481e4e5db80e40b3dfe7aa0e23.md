Posted on 08:38:00 on 28th August 2019

Police were called at around 3:30pm yesterday (August 27) to the report of a road traffic collision on the M6 northbound between junction 38 and 39.

The vehicles involved were a VW golf and a Mercedes CLA.

The driver of the VW a 29-year-old man from Bradford suffered minor injuries and was taken to Cumberland infirmary hospital. He was also arrested on suspicion of drink driving and dangerous driving and released under investigation.

The driver of the Mercedes a 50-year-old man from Coatbridge and his passenger were uninjured.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police on 101, email 101@cumbria.police.uk, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting log 157 of the 27th August 2019.
