Posted on 15:54:00 on 1st April 2021

A man was arrested following a two vehicle road traffic collision, which occurred on the A66 yesterday (31st March), near the Sportsman Arms. The 22-year-old from Carlisle, was arrested on suspicion of dangerous driving. He has been released under investigation.

Police were called to the scene at 5:38pm yesterday (31st March).

The incident involved a silver VW Golf and a quad bike.

Emergency services including police and ambulance attended.

A man in his 20's was taken to the Cumberland Royal Infirmary with injuries not believed to be life threatening, he is in a stable condition.

Police are appealing for witnesses, or anyone with Dashcam footage of the vehicles or location from around the time of the incident. Anyone with information is asked to contact officer 1636 Robbins on 101.
