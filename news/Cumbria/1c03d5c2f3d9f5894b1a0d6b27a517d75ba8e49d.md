Posted on 21:47:00 on 21st March 2021

Emergency services are dealing with a serious road traffic collision on the A596 near Micklethwaite.

Police were called today (21 March) shortly after 8.40pm following a collision between a HGV and a car.

The A596 is closed in both directions between the B5302 at Wigton and the A595 at Thursby Roundabout.

The road may be closed for some time. Motorists are asked to avoid the area.

Visit the traffic link map at www.cumbria.police.uk/traffic-link-map to keep up to date with the latest situation on the roads.
