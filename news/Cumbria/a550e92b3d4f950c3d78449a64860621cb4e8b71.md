Posted on 13:50:00 on 13th December 2019

Luke Walker

Police are concerned their appeal for missing Luke Walker, 30, who was last seen in the car park of the Mortal Man Inn, Troutbeck, at around 6pm on Monday (9 December).

Luke is described as 5ft 8ins, with black hair, a short beard and tattoos.

Police are appealing for anyone who may have seen Luke or the vehicle, or anyone with information to call police on 101. We would also like to urge Luke to contact us if he sees this appeal.
