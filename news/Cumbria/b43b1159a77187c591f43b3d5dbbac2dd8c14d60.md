Posted on 10:55:00 on 18th November 2020

A man has been charged following a robbery at McColls on Harrington Road, Workington at around 8am on the 4th November.

Mark Huddart, 29, of no fixed address has been charged with robbery and appeared at Carlisle Magistrates yesterday morning (Nov 17) and was remanded to appear at Crown Court on the 4th December 2020.
