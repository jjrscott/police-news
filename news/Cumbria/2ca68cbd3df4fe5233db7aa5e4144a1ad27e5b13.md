Posted on 13:08:00 on 3rd December 2019

Abrahams CCTV

Police are investigating the theft of clothes from Abraham's General Store, Kirkby Lonsdale that occurred at around 4:40pm on 26th November 2019.

Officers would like to speak with the person pictured as part of their investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact PC 2554 on 101, email 101@cumbria.police.uk, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting log number 91 of the 27th of November.
