Posted on 09:23:00 on 10th February 2021

Three men have been charged as part of an attempted murder investigation in Carlisle.

Viorel Pricope, 24, of Warwick Square, Carlisle, has been charged with attempted murder, affray, possession of a bladed article, perverting the course of justice and two counts of assault by beating.

Fernando Haliga, 23, of St Nicholas Street, Carlisle, has been charged with affray, possession of an offensive weapon and perverting the course of justice.

Costel-Mihaita Puiu, 25, of Warwick Square, Carlisle has been charged with affray.

All three men are scheduled to appear at North Cumbria Magistrates' Court today (10 February).

The charges relate to an incident that occurred inside a property on Warwick Square, Carlisle, on Saturday 6 February shortly after 9.30pm.

Anyone who has information on the incident is asked to contact police on 101 and ask to speak to an officer from the North Crime and Safeguarding Team. Alternatively you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
