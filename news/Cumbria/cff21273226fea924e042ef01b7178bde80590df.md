Posted on 11:22:00 on 22nd March 2021

Police are currently dealing with a serious one vehicle collision on Botchergate, Carlisle

The road is currently closed in both directions, closures are in place at The Crescent, Tait Street and Crown Street.

The road may be closed for some time. Motorists are asked to avoid the area.
