Posted on 09:01:00 on 19th August 2020

A man who was arrested at Lorton on Monday afternoon (17 Aug) has been charged with two offences.

Sebastian Parsons, 34, of no fixed address, has been charged with possession of a bladed article and a public order offence.

He has been bailed to attend Workington Magistrates' Court on 13 October, 2020.
