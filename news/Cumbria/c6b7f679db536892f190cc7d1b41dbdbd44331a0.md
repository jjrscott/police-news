Wanted - Gareth Skillen

Cumbria Police is appealing for the public's help tracing a man who is wanted on suspicion of assault.

Officers are investigating an assault which allegedly took place outside the Hunters pub in Cockermouth on 28 September 2019. He is also being sought for a second offence of resisting

Gareth Skillen is 37 and from Cockermouth.

Anyone who has information on his whereabouts should contact police or Crimestoppers anonymously.

Email: 101@cumbria.police.uk
Call non-emergency number: 101
Or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
Always call in an emergency 999
