Posted on 16:28:00 on 1st March 2021

A man had died following a fatal road traffic collision on West Tower Street, Carlisle.

Police were called to the incident at 12:56pm today which involved a white panel van and two pedestrians, a man and woman.

The woman has been air lifted to Preston Hospital with serious injuries.

The man was pronounced dead at the scene. A family liaison officer has been appointed for his family.

The road will remain closed whilst emergency services attend the scene and investigations are conducted.

Anyone with information regarding the incident, who has yet to speak to police, is asked to contact 101 quoting log number 90 of the 1st of March.
