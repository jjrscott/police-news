Posted on 09:48:00 on 23rd May 2019

Emergency services are currently at the scene of a road traffic collision involving two vans on the A590 between Greenodd and Haverthwaite.

The road is currently closed in both directions are drivers are asked to avoid the area.

An air ambulance is in attendance.

The incident occurred at around 9.50am today (23 May).
