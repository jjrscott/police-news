Posted on 11:52:00 on 21st January 2020

The body of a woman found at the Solway Firth, near to Cummertrees, Annan, on Saturday, 11th January has been formally identified as Janet McElrone, 51, of Eden Street, Carlisle.

There are no suspicious circumstances at this stage.

A file has been prepared for the coroner.
