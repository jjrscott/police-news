Posted on 08:18:00 on 26th September 2019

Emergency services are dealing with a three vehicle road traffic collision on the A66 near Appleby.

Police were called today (September 26) at 8.11am following a collision on the westbound carriageway just past the Appleby bypass.

The road is currently blocked.

Injuries at this time are believed to be minor.

Motorists are advised to avoid the area as delays are expected and traffic is building up.
