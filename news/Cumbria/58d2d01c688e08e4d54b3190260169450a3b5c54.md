Posted on 11:35:00 on 17th August 2019

Emergency services are dealing with a serious road traffic collision on the M6 near Penrith.

Police were called today (August 17) at 11.48am following a one vehicle collision on the southbound carriageway.

The M6 is currently closed southbound between junctions 40 and 41 and drivers are asked to avoid the area.
