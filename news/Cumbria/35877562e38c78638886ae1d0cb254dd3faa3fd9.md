**Update - **

**_ _**

**_Officers have made two arrests following an assault on Sandylands Road, Kendal._**

**_
An 18-year-old man from Kirby Lonsdale has been arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm and remains in police custody._**

**_ _**

**_And an 18-year-old man from Kendal has been arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm and remains in police custody._**

**Previous release - **

Police are currently dealing with an incident on Sandylands Road, Kendal following an assault that occurred at around 1:15pm today (Jan 20)

A 17-year-old boy from Kendal suffered injuries not believed to be life threatening and taken to Royal Lancaster Infirmary. It is believed the injuries were caused from a knife.

Parties involved are believed to have known one another.

Officers are in the area conducting ongoing enquiries.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police on 101, email 101@cumbria.police.uk, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting log 94 of the 20th January 2020. ****
