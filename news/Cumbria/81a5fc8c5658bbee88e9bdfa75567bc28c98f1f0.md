Posted on 15:49:00 on 23rd May 2019

The A590 has reopened following the earlier collision involving two vehicles.
One of the drivers has been airlifted to the Royal Preston Hospital where he was in a critical condition. The driver's family are aware.
The other driver was uninjured.
The collision happened at about 9.50am today (May 23).
Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward. Call Sergeant Martin Bainbridge on 101.
