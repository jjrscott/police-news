Posted on 15:35:00 on 9th May 2019

Police are concerned for a missing 15-year-old Carlisle boy.

Jordan Burgess was last seen in the area of Oaklands Drive, Carlisle, at approximately 2pm on Sunday (May 5).

He is described as a white male with short fair hair. He was last seen wearing a thin black or dark blue Berghaus puffer jacket, black tracksuit bottoms and a pair of grey or black-coloured trainers.

Police are appealing for anyone with information on Jordan's whereabouts to get in touch by calling 101.

Officers also ask Jordan if he sees this appeal, to get in touch to let them know he is safe and well.
