Posted on 16:23:00 on 27th June 2019

Police are appealing for witnesses following the death of a cyclist in the Haweswater Reservoir area today (27th June 2019). Police were called to the incident at 11:36am this morning.

A local 58-year-old woman died at the location. Her family have been informed.

Investigations are ongoing into the circumstances of her death which is currently being treated as unexplained.

Anyone with information regarding this incident, who has yet to speak to police, is asked to call 101 reference number 89 of the 27th June, or call CrimeStoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
