Posted on 14:37:00 on 12th July 2020

Officers are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision on Townhead Lane, Ravenstonedale, near to the junction for the A683 which occurred at approximately 4.08pm yesterday (11 July).

The rider of a black Triumph Rocket motorcycle, Ronnie Monks, aged 72, from the Worsley area of Manchester, died at the scene. Mr Monks family have been informed and are being supported by a specially trained officer.

Anyone with information is asked to call PC 353 Thompson on 101, or email: SCIU@cumbria.police.uk quoting log 155 of the July 11th.
