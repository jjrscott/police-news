Posted on 11:57:00 on 23rd February 2020

Police can confirm that the body of a man was found on land in the area of Sand Gate Marsh at about 3pm yesterday (22nd February).

Formal identification has not yet taken place and inquiries are ongoing.

The circumstances are not believed to be suspicious.
