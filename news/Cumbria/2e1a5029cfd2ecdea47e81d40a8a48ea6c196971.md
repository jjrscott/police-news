Posted on 05:38:00 on 27th June 2020

Police have charged a man with the murder of George Leon Blaney who died in Workington on Tuesday.

Sean Blaney, 37, of Shore Terrace, Salterbeck, was charged with murder in the early hours of this morning (27 June).

He is set to appear at North Cumbria Magistrates' Court later today.

The charge follows an extensive investigation by officers.
