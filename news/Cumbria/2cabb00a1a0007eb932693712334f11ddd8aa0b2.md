Posted on 11:55:00 on 10th August 2019

Police are investigating a burglary that happened in the Lamplugh area during the early hours of today (Saturday August 10).

During the burglary a silver Mercedes C200, registration KU07 UUA, was stolen.

Officers are appealing for information. They are asking anyone who sees this vehicle not to approach it but to contact Cumbria Police on 999 or report information anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
