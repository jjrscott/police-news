Posted on 09:30:00 on 21st May 2019

Police are in attendance at an incident on Aglionby Street, Carlisle, following a call to police at 10:00am this morning (21st May) due to concern for the welfare of an adult male.

Police, Ambulance and air ambulance are in attendance.

Aglionby Street is closed whilst emergency service attend the scene.

Congestion may be experienced in this area and road users are asked to plan their journey accordingly.

Visit the traffic link map at www.cumbria.police.uk/traffic-link-map to keep up to date with the latest situation on the roads.
