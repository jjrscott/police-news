Posted on 16:51:00 on 10th December 2019

Police investigating incidents in Kendal believed to be linked have today made an arrest.

A 55-year-old man from Kendal was arrested on suspicion of possession of a knife in a public place and committing an offence with the intention of committing a relevant sexual offence. He remains in police custody.

A 41-year-old man from Kendal was previously arrested on suspicion of sexual assault and has been released on police bail.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Kendal CID referencing incident 65 of 30 November 2019.
