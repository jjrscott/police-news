Posted on 10:55:00 on 27th April 2020

Detectives investigating reports of indecent exposure in Cockermouth have charged a man.

Michael Cowell, 28, of Cramlington in Northumberland, has been charged with three counts of indecent exposure.

This follows reported incidents on April 13.

Cowell was due to appear before Carlisle's magistrates' court today (April 27).

Officers continue to appeal for anyone with any information to contact the West Cumbria Crime and Safeguarding Team on 101, extension 6008.
