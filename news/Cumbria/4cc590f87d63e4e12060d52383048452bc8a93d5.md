Posted on 15:21:00 on 5th February 2021

Police have arrested a 19-year-old, local man on suspicion of arson following the reports of a number of vehicle fires and incidents, which police are currently investigating as being potentially linked.

The man was arrested at 12:10pm today (5th February) following multiple incident reports between 2:20am and 7:03am today.

Incidents reported include:

 * At 2:20am police received a report of an attempted burglary at Long and Small Garage and Car Wash in Flimby. Damage was caused to the property.
 * At 5:17am police received a report of two vehicles having been set on fire on Solway Trading Estate and three additional vehicles receiving fire damaged.
 * At 6:02am pPolice received a report of suspicious activity outside a property on Senhouse Street Maryport. There was no damage or injuries reported from this incident.
 * Police received a report from Fire and Rescue at 06:08am of a vehicle fire on Station Street, Maryport.
 * At 07:03 Police received a report of theft from a vehicle on Curzon Street, Maryport.

The man remains in police custody whilst enquiries continue.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have information regarding any of these incidents. To report any information please contact police on 101 or call CrimeStoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
