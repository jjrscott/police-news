Posted on 11:22:00 on 19th December 2019

Police are renewing their appeal to locate Luke Walker, 30, of Kendal who was last seen in the car park of the Mortal Man Inn, Troutbeck at 6pm on Monday 9th December.

Luke is described as 5ft 8 ins, with black hair, a short beard and tattoos.

Police are appealing for anyone who may have seen Luke, or anyone with information to call police on 101. We would also like to urge Luke to contact us if he sees this appeal.
