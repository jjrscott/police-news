Posted on 13:54:00 on 6th September 2019

Have you seen 17-year-old Daniel Stewart from Milnthorpe?

Daniel went missing on Thursday 29 August and was last seen in Kendal town centre the same day.

He is 5ft 3ins tall with short, dark brown hair.

He was last seen wearing a grey jogging top and bottoms and black trainers.

Officers are concerned for Daniel's welfare and would like to hear from anyone with knowledge of his whereabouts.

If you can help, contact police on 101.
