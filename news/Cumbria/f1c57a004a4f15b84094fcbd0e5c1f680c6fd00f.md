Posted on 20:17:00 on 13th August 2019

**Police have formally identified a man and a girl who died in a collision on the B5302 near Abbeytown.**

** **

**Andrew Frith, aged 58, and Jodie -Anne Frith, aged 15, both from Silloth, died following a collision involving a HGV yesterday (August 12) at about 1.30pm.**

** **

**Anyone with any information or who witnessed the collision is asked to contact PC2422 Motum or Sgt 1929 Bainbridge on 101 or email 101@cumbria.police.uk, quoting log 104 of the 12th August 2019.**
