Posted on 12:29:00 on 4th September 2019

Residents are invited to an event where they can speak to a range of agencies in Whitehaven next week.

The Partners and Communities Together morning offers people the chance to raise any concerns or share any ideas.

Agencies or people taking part include:

* Cumbria Police

* Cumbria Fire and Rescue Service
. Copeland Age and Advice Service

* Home Group

* Citizens Advice

* Unity

* Copeland Council's environmental health and licensing department

* Cumbria County Council's highways, traffic management and parking enforcement officers

* Copeland councillors for Whitehaven

The event is being held on Tuesday September 10, between 10am and 12.30pm, at St Nicholas Church cafe.

PC Kelly Pennington said: "Everybody is welcome.

"All the agencies can be spoken to face-to-face in a relaxed environment.

"Often agencies can work together to tackle problems the public report.

"So please come and see us, either individually or as a group."
