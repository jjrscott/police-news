Posted on 16:03:00 on 4th December 2019

Police are investigating an assault that occurred yesterday (Dec 3) at around 10pm in the area of Tangier Street, Whitehaven.

A woman from Whitehaven in her 40s suffered a head injury during the assault. She was taken to West Cumberland hospital and has since been discharged.

The suspect is described as a stocky man. Around 5'5 in height with short ginger hair.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police on 101, email 101@cumbria.police.uk, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting log 245 of the 3rd December.
