Posted on 13:20:00 on 16th June 2020

Police were called at approximately 11.16am on Monday 15th June 2020, to a report of a two vehicle road traffic collision on the U3038 near to Southwaite.

As a result of the collision, the driver of one of the motor vehicles Ronnie Robson aged 77 from Carlisle sadly died at the scene.

Ronnie was a father of two, and had two grandchildren. The family are currently being supported by a Family Liaison Officer.

Anyone with any information or who witnessed the collision is asked to contact Sgt 1962 Vickers of the Mobile Support Group on 101 or email 101@cumbria.police.uk, quoting log 69 of the 15th June 2020.
