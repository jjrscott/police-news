Posted on 14:37:00 on 3rd March 2021

A man who died following a fatal collision involving a white panel van and two pedestrians on West Tower Street, Carlisle, has been identified.

Terence Abson known as Terry, aged 62, of Lingside Crescent, Carlisle, was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Mr Abson's family have been appointed a family Liaison Officer.

The second pedestrian, a 56-year-old woman from Carlisle was airlifted to Preston hospital.

Her condition is described as serious but stable.

Officers were called to the collision at 12:56pm on Monday 1st March.

The police would like to thank all of those people who have already come forward with information to assist with their enquiries.

Anyone with any information or who witnessed the collision is asked to contact PC2685 Nicholson or Sgt 1929 Bainbridge of Mobile Support Group on 101 or email 101@cumbria.police.uk, quoting log 90 of 1st March 2021.
