Posted on 06:52:00 on 17th June 2019

Police have made a further arrest following an assault which occurred on College Street, Whitehaven, on the 14th June. Officers were called to the incident at 3.22am on the 14th.

A 30-year-old man from West Yorkshire, suffered serious head injuries. He remains the Cumberland Infirmary, Carlisle, in a stable condition.

A 23-year-old-man from Whitehaven, has been arrested on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm. He remains in police custody.

Two men, aged 44 and 35, who were arrested on the 14th June, on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm, have been released under investigation.

Officers continue to appealing for anybody who witnessed anything that might be relevant to the inquiry, who have yet to speak to police, to contact 101, referring to log 31 of June 14, or call CrimeStoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
