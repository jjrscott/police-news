Posted on 23:18:00 on 14th December 2019

Police are concerned for the welfare of Lauren Cheney, 16, who was last seen on Warwick Road, Carlisle at 1pm on Friday (13 December).

Lauren who lives in the Dumfries & Galloway area, is described as white, 5ft 1, of slim build, with very long light brown hair and is believed to be wearing a black puffer jacket with fur trim, a navy blue hoody, black leggings and white trainers.

Anyone with information is asked to call police on 101. Police would also like to urge Lauren to contact us if she sees this appeal.
