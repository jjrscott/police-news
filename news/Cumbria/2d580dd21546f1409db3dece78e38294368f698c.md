Posted on 13:31:00 on 3rd September 2019

Police have charged a woman with theft and making a false instrument with intent for it to be accepted as genuine.

Rachel Doran, of Lamb Lane, Egremont, is due to appear before Workington's Magistrates Court on 27th September 2019.

The charge follows allegations of offences committed between 13th September 2015 and 17th September 2017.
