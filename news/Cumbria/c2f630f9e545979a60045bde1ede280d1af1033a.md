Posted on 09:00:00 on 8th September 2019

Missing: Gerald Gibbons

Police are concerned for an 84-year-old missing man.

Gerald Gibbons was last seen at approximately 7am today (September 8) at his home address in Holme.

He is described as white, 5ft 7, of medium build and wears glasses. He is believed to be wearing a beige fleece, green/grey trousers and rust-coloured shoes.

It is also believed he may be travelling in a silver Audi A3 Sport, registration number: PJ16 VEK.

Anyone who has seen Mr Gibbons or the vehicle he is believed to be travelling in is urged to contact police on 101.
