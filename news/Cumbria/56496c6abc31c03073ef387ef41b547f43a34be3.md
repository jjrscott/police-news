Posted on 17:10:00 on 16th September 2019

Police and other emergency services have been called to a serious collision on the A683 at Middleton, near Sedbergh.

The collision, at 4.40pm, involved a car and a motorcycle.

The road is closed while emergency services deal with the incident.
