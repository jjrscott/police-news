Posted on 15:25:00 on 22nd March 2021

A man has died following a collision on Botchergate.

Police were called at around 10:15am today (March 22) to the report of a two vehicle collision on Botchergate, Carlisle.

The driver of one of the vehicles a man in his 80s from Carlisle was pronounced dead at the scene.

The road has been reopened.

Anyone with information or who witnessed the collision is asked to contact police on 101 quoting log number 34 of the 22nd March 2021. Alternatively you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
