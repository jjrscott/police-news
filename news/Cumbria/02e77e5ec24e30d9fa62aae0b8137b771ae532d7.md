Posted on 14:49:00 on 22nd March 2021

A man has appeared at court today following a number of attempted burglaries in north Carlisle.

Dean Kerr, aged 32, of no fixed abode appeared at North and West Magistrates today (March 22) charged with burglary dwelling and theft and seven counts of attempted burglary with intent to steal.

He has been remanded to appear at Carlisle Crown Court on the 19th April 2021.
