Posted on 10:57:00 on 4th July 2019

Police are investigating three burglaries that have occurred in Workington.

In the early hours of today (July 4) an offender has forced entry to the following three premises:

* Love Yours store on Steelemen Walk

* Home Bargains on Campbell Savours Way

* Royal George Inn on Cross Hill

Investigative inquiries, including crime scene investigation work, are ongoing. Clothing and money have been stolen from the businesses during these incidents.

Officers would like to hear from any witnesses or those who may have information. Those with information should contact police on 101 and ask to speak to an officer from the West Cumbria Crime and Safeguarding Team. Alternatively you can contact Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
