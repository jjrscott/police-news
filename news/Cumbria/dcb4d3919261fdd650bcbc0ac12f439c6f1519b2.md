Posted on 13:06:00 on 13th September 2019

At around 10.30pm yesterday (12th September) police received a report of three 19-year-old men who are not local to Cumbria, locked in the grounds of Carlisle Castle.

Fire attended the scene and the men were let out.

No one was hurt during the incident.
