Posted on 18:20:00 on 27th November 2019

Police are concerned for the welfare of 15-year-old Amy Whitelaw, who went missing from her home address in Scotby, near Carlisle this afternoon (27 November).

Amy is described as white, of slim build, with short dark brown hair and is believed to be wearing black leggings and pink trainers.

Anyone with information about Amy's whereabouts is urged to call Police on 101. Police are also asking Amy to contact us if she sees this appeal.
