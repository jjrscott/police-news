Posted on 09:27:00 on 13th November 2020

Police were called today at 8:40am to the report of a two-vehicle road traffic collision on Kingstown Road, Carlisle, outside the Coach and Horses.

The incident involved a Vauxhall Corsa and an Audi.

The female driver of the Audi is being taken to hospital with injuries not believed to be life-threatening.

There are delays in the area and drivers should plan their journey accordingly.
