Posted on 08:10:00 on 4th April 2021

Police can confirm that a body was found following a multi-agency search operation on the river Eden, near Lazonby.

Officers received a report of concern for the welfare and that a man fell into the water while out in a boat at 3.21pm yesterday (April 3).

The body of a man was found at around 8:45pm yesterday. Formal identification has not yet taken place.
