Posted on 09:18:00 on 17th December 2020

Police were called at 2:59pm on yesterday (Dec 16) to the report of a two vehicle road traffic collision on the A685 North of Grayrigg.

The collision involved a white Honda motorcycle and a white HGV.

As a result of the collision, the rider of the motorcycle, a 22-year-old-man from Kendal, sadly died at the scene.

His family have been informed and a Family Liaison Officer has been appointed.

The driver of the HGV was uninjured.

The road was closed for six hours whilst a forensic collision investigation and recovery was carried out

Anyone with any information or who witnessed the collision is asked to contact PC2291 Wilson or Sgt 1929 Bainbridge of Kendal Mobile Support Group on 101 or email 101@cumbria.police.uk, quoting log 129 of 16th December 2020.
