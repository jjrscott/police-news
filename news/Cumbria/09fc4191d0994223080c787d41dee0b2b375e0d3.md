Posted on 13:25:00 on 29th August 2020

**At 10.35am this morning (29 August) police were called to a report of a concern for the welfare of a man found by the river in the Melbourne Park area of Carlisle.**

** **

**Police attended and the man was pronounced deceased at the scene.**

** **

**Following initial investigations, officers are treating the death as unexplained but not suspicious at this time.**
