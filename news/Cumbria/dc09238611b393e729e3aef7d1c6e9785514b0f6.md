Posted on 20:07:00 on 19th June 2019

Emergency services have dealt with a fire at a Carlisle address this evening (June 19).

Police were contacted shortly after 7pm with a report of concern for the welfare of occupants of a house on Barrock Street after smoke was seen coming from the property.

All occupants were safely removed from the house with no reported injuries.

The fire was quickly put out by Cumbria Fire & Rescue Service.
