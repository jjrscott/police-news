Posted on 18:52:00 on 24th August 2020

Police are concerned for the welfare of missing 56-year-old David Kelson from Kendal who was last seen at in the area of Highgate, Kendal today (Aug 24) at around 10:30am.

David is described as having short white hair and was last seen wearing a top and tracksuit bottoms.

Anyone with information should contact police on 101. Officers would also like to urge David to contact this number if he sees this appeal.
