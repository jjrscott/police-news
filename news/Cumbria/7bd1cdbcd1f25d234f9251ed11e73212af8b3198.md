Posted on 13:22:00 on 27th October 2019

Following a road traffic collision earlier today (Oct 27), part of the M6 Northbound between junction 41 and 42 is currently closed and will be until early hours of tomorrow (Oct 28) while repair work is being carried out.

Highways are currently working on repairing damage to the carriageway.

A diversion is in place, via the A6 at junction 41.

Visit the traffic link map at www.cumbria.police.uk/traffic-link-map to keep up to date with the latest situation on the roads.

Police were called at approximately 6am today (Oct 27) to the report of a one vehicle collision to the M6 northbound.

The vehicle was a HGV, no one was injured.
