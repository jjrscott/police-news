Posted on 11:31:00 on 25th May 2020

Officers yesterday (May 24) in the early hours located a quantity of cannabis at a property on Lowther Street, Whitehaven.

Gjerhi Braculla, aged 39 from Archdale Place, New Maiden has been charged with possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of class B - Cannabis and producing a controlled drug of class B - Cannabis.

Xhoni Leka, aged 28 from The Dene, Wembley has been charged with possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of class B - Cannabis and producing a controlled drug of class B - Cannabis.

Both men appear at Carlisle's magistrate's court today (May 25)

Two men from Manchester aged 27 and 30 were arrested on suspicion of producing a controlled drug class B - Cannabis and have been released on police bail.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police on 101, email 101@cumbria.police.uk, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting log 27 of the 24th May 2020.
