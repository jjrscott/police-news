Posted on 15:43:00 on 24th November 2020

Police are currently dealing with a two-vehicle road traffic collision on Inkerman Terrace, Whitehaven.

The road is currently blocked in both directions.

No injuries have been reported.

Closures are in place at the top of Inkerman Terrace and Coach Road, drivers are advised to use the A595 or seek other alternative routes.
