Posted on 08:11:00 on 30th July 2019

Police are investigating a collision that occurred on the A591 Ambleside at 5:20pm, on Monday 29th July 2019. The collision involved a Volkswagen Golf, and a Suzuki Motorcycle both travelling towards Windermere.

As a result of injuries sustained, the rider of the Motorcycle, a local 48-year-old man, was declared dead at the scene.

The family have been informed, and are being supported by Specially Trained Officers.

At the time of the Collison the road was busy with vehicles, and many pedestrians were in the area. Anyone with information, particularly motorists who may have dash cam footage are asked to call PC 2392 Evans on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111,quoting log number 172 of the 29th July.
