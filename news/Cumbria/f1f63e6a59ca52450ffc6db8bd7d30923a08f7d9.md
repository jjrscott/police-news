Posted on 10:50:00 on 29th January 2021

A man who died following a following a fatal collision involving a single cyclist in the village of Westward has been identified.

Jeremy Richardson, aged 71, originally from Inverness had recently moved to Wigton was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Officers were called by NWAS to the collision at around 4:25pm on Thursday 21st January.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police on 101, email SCIU@cumbria.police.uk quoting log 148 of the 21st January 2020.
