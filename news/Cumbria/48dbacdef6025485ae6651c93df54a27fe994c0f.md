Posted on 13:48:00 on 17th June 2019

Officers are appealing for help identifying this man who is sought in connection with the theft of clothing from a shop in Bowness in Windermere.

The theft occurred on 28 May 2019 at a shop in Ash Street.

He is described as white with short, brown hair, a brown beard and he was wearing a dark coat with a hood.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact police referencing incident 142 of 28 May 2019.
