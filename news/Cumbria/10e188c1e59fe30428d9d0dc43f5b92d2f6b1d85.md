Posted on 10:50:00 on 9th October 2020

Two Albanian men have been arrested following a drugs warrant that was carried out this morning (9 October) in the King Street area of Wigton.

The West Area Drugs Unit and West Pro Active Support Group with assistance from the West Area Dogs Unit, entered the property at approximately 9.29am, where a large scale cannabis farm was located.

The two men were subsequently arrested on suspicion of the production of Cannabis and remain in custody.

Anyone with any information on suspected drug activity can contact police on 101, or email 101@Cumbria.police.uk. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
