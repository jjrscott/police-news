Posted on 14:52:00 on 27th August 2020

_Police were called at 10.13am this morning (27 August) following a collision involving a cyclist and a grey Nissan car on Main Street, Brampton._

_The cyclist, a man in his 50 's, sustained serious injuries. He was airlifted to James Cook Hospital in Middlesbrough, where he was in a serious but stable condition._

_The occupant of the car was uninjured._

_The road was closed for several hours while a forensic collision investigation and recovery was carried out. It has since been re-opened._

_Officers are keen to speak to a woman pedestrian who witnessed the incident but left in the direction of Front Street before leaving her details. _

_Anyone with any information or who witnessed the collision is asked to contact PC 2670 Woodcock or Sgt 1962 Vickers on 101 - or email the serious collision unit SCIU@cumbria.police.uk, quoting incident number 55 of 27 August._
