Posted on 07:44:00 on 15th September 2020

Police were called today (15th September 2020) at 7:30am to the report of a two vehicle collision on the A590 at Haverthwaite.

The incident involved a car and van.

Roads are closed in both directions whilst emergency services attend the scene and the vehicles are recovered.

Delays are to be expected and drivers are asked to plan their route and journey with this in mind.
