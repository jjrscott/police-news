Posted on 15:26:00 on 10th December 2019

Police are concerned for the welfare of Michelle Edgar, who was last seen at her home address in Watermillock, near Ullswater.

Michelle is described as 5ft2, of slim build with shoulder length sandy blonde hair.

Police are asking local residents of the area to check outbuildings.

Anyone with information is asked to call police on 101.
