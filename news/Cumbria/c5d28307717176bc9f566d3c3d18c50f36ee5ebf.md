Posted on 08:15:00 on 18th May 2019

Police are investigating an ATM theft which occurred at Booths, Windermere at around 1:30am this morning (18th May).

Police were alerted to the theft when members of the public report the incident to officers on the beat in the area.

Booths is open for businesses as usual today.

Specialist detectives are following multiple lines of enquiry and officers will be carrying out an investigation at the scene and surrounding areas today.

Police would like to speak to a man and a woman who may be able to help with enquiries. They were in the area of Booths around this time and approached members of the Booths staff on site to ask for directions for food outlets.

Police are appealing for any other witnesses, or any person who has seen anything they consider to be suspicious in or around that area.

Anyone with information should call DS 2010 Andy Lamb on 101, quoting incident number 28 of 18th May 2019. Alternatively you can contact Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
