Posted on 15:58:00 on 17th July 2020

**Police were called by the North West Ambulance Service at 7.35pm on July 15 to a report of concern for the welfare of a woman at a property at Clayton Avenue, Cleator Moor.**

**The woman, 43, was taken by ambulance to the West Cumberland Hospital where she was in a critical condition. She died last night.**

**The death is being treated as suspicious at this time.**

**Two men, aged 61 and 44, both of west Cumbria, have been arrested on suspicion of murder. They remain in custody today.**

**Detective Chief Inspector Craig Smith said: "We can confirm that two men are in custody following the death of a woman.**

**" Residents may notice a larger than normal presence in the area as inquiries are carried out."**

**Anyone with information can contact officers on 101.**
