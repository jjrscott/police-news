Posted on 15:56:00 on 23rd June 2020

At 1.25pm this afternoon (23 June) officers attended an address in the Garnet Crescent area of Salterbeck, Workington, following a report of the sudden death of a man inside the address.

Officers attended and have subsequently arrested a 37-year-old man on suspicion of murder.

The man is currently in police custody.

Officers are urging anyone with information about this incident who hasn't already come forward to contact police on 101, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 quoting log number 105 of today (23 June)
