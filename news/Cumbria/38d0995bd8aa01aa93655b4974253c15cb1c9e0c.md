Posted on 10:47:00 on 16th October 2019

Police can confirm officers were called at 8.55am today (October 16) following a fire at a property at Thwaites, near Broughton in Furness.

A man, 72, was taken to hospital following the fire. He is in a stable condition.

One person has been confirmed deceased. Officers are working to confirm their identity.

Joint inquiries are underway involving police and Cumbria Fire and Rescue Service.

Detective Inspector Matt Scott said: "We are treating the fire as unexplained at this stage.

"We are working closely with Cumbria Fire and Rescue investigators to establish the cause of the fire."

The A595 has reopened.
