Posted on 23:25:00 on 1st March 2020

Police are concerned for the welfare of 13-year-old Paul Willis-Hughes who was last seen in Kendal town centre on Saturday 29th February.

Paul is described as being 4ft 8in and was last seen wearing a blue Adidas, puffer jacket, red hoody and black joggers

Anyone with information should contact police on 101. Officers would also like to urge Paul to contact this number if he sees this appeal.
