Posted on 20:09:00 on 23rd March 2021

A man who died following a collision on Botchergate, Carlisle, has been identified.

Geoffrey Brown, 83, of Borland Avenue, Carlisle, was pronounced dead at the scene.

Officers were called to the collision at 10:15am yesterday (22 March) to the report of a two vehicle collision.

The collision involved a Honda Jazz and Ford S-Max.

The driver of the Ford was uninjured.

Mr Brown is father to two children Geoff and Jacqueline.

Anyone with any information or who witnessed the collision is asked to contact police on 101 or email 101@cumbria.police.uk, quoting incident number 34 of 22 March 2021.
