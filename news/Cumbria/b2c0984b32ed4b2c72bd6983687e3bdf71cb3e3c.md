Posted on 13:21:00 on 26th February 2021

John Parr

Police are concerned for the welfare of John Parr, 64, who was last seen in the Frizington area, at around 6:45pm on 25 February.

John is described as white, 5ft 6in tall, he has long grey hair tied back in a ponytail, a grey moustache and beard. He was last seen wearing a green sweater.

A police search is currently ongoing, which has been supported by an NPAS helicopter.

Anyone with information on John's whereabouts is urged to contact police on 101. Officers would also encourage John to contact this number if he sees this appeal
