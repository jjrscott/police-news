Posted on 20:38:00 on 13th August 2019

Police are investigating a sexual assault on a teenage girl in Carlisle city centre.

It happened at about 3pm today (August 13) in English Street.

Police received a number of reports that a man was acting inappropriately around the English Street area.

A man, 33, from Carlisle, was quickly arrested on suspicion of sexual assault.

Police are asking for anybody with any information to contact PC 2551 Smith on 101.
