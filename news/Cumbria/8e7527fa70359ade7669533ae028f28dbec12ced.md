Posted on 17:02:00 on 18th July 2020

**Police have today (July 18) charged a man with the murder of a woman in west Cumbria.**

** **

**Trevor Green, 44, of Cleator, is charged with the murder of Shelly Clark, 43, also of Cleator.**

** **

**A man, 62, who was arrested during the enquiry has been released with no further action.**

** **

**Green is due to appear before Workington 's magistrates' court on Monday.**

** **

**Police were called by the North West Ambulance Service at 7.35pm on July 15 to a report of concern for the welfare of a woman at a property in Cleator Moor.**

** **

**Shelly Clark died in the West Cumberland Hospital on Thursday night.**
