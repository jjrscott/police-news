Posted on 10:27:00 on 24th December 2020

Police were called at around 3:20pm yesterday (Dec 23) to the report of a two vehicle road traffic collision on the A590 at Lindale Brow.

The collision involved a Toyota Auris and Range Rover.

Cumbria Fire and Rescue and Air Ambulance were also at the scene.

A passenger in the Range Rover a 40-year-old man from Barrow suffered a head injury and was taken to Preston hospital.

There were no reported injuries to the driver of the Range Rover a 33-year-old man from Barrow

The driver of the Toyota a 77-year-old man from Barrow suffered life threatening injuries and was taken to Aintree Hospital via air ambulance.

A passenger in the Toyota a woman has injuries described as critical and was taken to Preston Hospital.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or has dashcam and was driving on the road around the time of the collision is asked to contact police on 101 or email the Serious Collision Investigation Unit - sciu@cumbria.police.uk quoting log number 145 of the 23rd December 2020.
