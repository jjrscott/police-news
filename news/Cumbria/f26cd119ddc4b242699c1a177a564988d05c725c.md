Posted on 15:26:00 on 29th January 2021

Police are appealing for witnesses following a fail to stop collision involving a car and a pedestrian yesterday (Jan 28) at around 1:30pm on Creighton Avenue, Carlisle.

Officers were called by NWAS to the report.

The pedestrian a 29-year-old man suffered significant injuries.

The vehicle involved was a Silver Vauxhall Corsa.

Anyone with any information or who witnessed or has footage the collision is asked to contact police on 101 or Crime Stoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 quoting log 85 for the 28th January 2021.
