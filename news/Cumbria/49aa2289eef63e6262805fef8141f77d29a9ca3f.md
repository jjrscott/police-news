Posted on 15:14:00 on 16th June 2020

Police were called at around 12:30pm today (June 16) to the report of an ordnance located at a property on Penrith Road, Keswick.

Officers are at the scene and as precaution a cordon is in place and a section of Penrith Road between Gretna Street and Blencathra Street junctions was closed.

EOD attended and the items were found to be casings.
