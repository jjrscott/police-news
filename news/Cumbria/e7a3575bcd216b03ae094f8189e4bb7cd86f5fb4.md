Posted on 12:41:00 on 16th February 2021

A Carlisle man has appeared at Carlisle Crown Court today (16 Feb) in connection with a fatal road traffic collision that occurred on A69 at Warwick Bridge on 27th April 2019.

Samin Khan, aged 75, of Scaleby Hill, Carlisle pleaded guilty today to death by due care. He pleaded guilty to failing to stop at an earlier hearing.

He will appear again on the 14th May at Carlisle Crown Court.
