Posted on 22:47:00 on 2nd August 2020

Have you seen missing Melmerby man James Ford?

James is 36 and was last seen leaving his home address at around 11am today (Aug 2).

Officers are concerned for his welfare and are urging for anyone wo sees him to call 999. If you have any other information which may assist please call 101.

James is described as 5ft 8ins tall, white and of medium build. He has short dark hair and was last seen wearing a black t-shirt, green gilet, dark pants and black trainers - possibly Converse. He was also carrying a black rucksack.

James has family in Australia and has recently moved to the area from London. He also has friends in the Bristol area.
