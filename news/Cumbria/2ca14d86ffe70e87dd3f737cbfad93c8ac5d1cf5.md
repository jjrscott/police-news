Posted on 19:34:00 on 31st July 2019

Cumbria Police is asking for witnesses to come forward following a fatal collision in Wigton this afternoon (31 July).

Police were called at around 4pm to the report of a road traffic collision at New Street, Wigton.

The incident involved a pedestrian and a white sprinter van.

The pedestrian, an 84-year-old local man, died a short time later. Family have been informed and they are being supported by police officers at this time.

The driver of the van is a 54-year-old man from North Cumbria. He was not injured and is assisting Police with the investigation.

Any witnesses are asked to contact PC2090 HYNES on 101 quoting incident number 127 of 31 July 2019.
