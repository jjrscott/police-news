Posted on 16:27:00 on 3rd August 2019

Cumbria Police are appealing for witnesses and dashcam footage as part of an investigation into an incident of reported dangerous driving this morning (3rd August).

Police were called to the incident at 7:32am, with the report of a vehicle, driving at speed, the wrong way on the M6 northbound, between Penrith and Shap.

Police are urging anybody who may have witnessed the vehicle driving, or who may have relevant dashcam footage to urgently make contact.

If you are able to assist please ring Cumbria police on 101 quoting reference number 66 of the 3rd of August, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
