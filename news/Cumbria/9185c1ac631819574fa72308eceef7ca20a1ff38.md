Posted on 15:21:00 on 5th February 2020

At approx. 11.30am this morning (5 February) there was a fatal one vehicle road traffic collision on the A5086 near to Eaglesfield.

A man in his 60s was pronounced dead at the scene. At this time the driver has not been formally identified, however specially trained officers are working with the drivers family. No one else was involved in the incident.

Any witnesses are asked to contact Sergeant 2265 of Cumbria Police Mobile Support Group on 101.

Cumbria Police would like to thank motorists for their patience whilst examination of the scene was undertaken.
