Posted on 11:48:00 on 11th June 2019

James Tweedy, 41, of Abby Street, Carlisle has been charged with attempted burglary.

Tweedy was arrested following an attempted burglary which occurred on the 7th June at Waterman's walk, Carlisle

Tweedy appeared at Carlisle magistrates today (11th June) and has been remanded in custody until 15th July for Crown Court, he entered no plea.
