Posted on 11:35:00 on 29th October 2020

Police are investigating an incident of robbery at the McColl's store on Harrington Road, Workington.

The unknown offender entered the shop at approximately 11pm last night (28 October) before reportedly threatening two members of staff with a knife. The offender then left the shop with a small amount of money.

The offender is described as a white male, around 5ft 10ins and of medium build. He was wearing dark clothing.

No injuries were reported in the incident. Enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone with information or anyone who witnessed this incident is asked to contact the West Cumbria Crime and Safeguarding team on 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 quoting log 258 of the 28 October.
