Posted on 10:54:00 on 20th October 2020

A man has been charged after being arrested in the early hours of yesterday morning (Oct 19) after being detained by a property owner

Police were called by an occupant of a nearby property at around 5:08am having detained a man.

Michael Torrance, 36, of Norfolk Road, Penrith was charged with burglary other than dwelling with intent to steal and two counts of burglary other than dwelling - theft. He is to appear at North and West Magistrates today (Oct 20).

The charges follow a burglaries at three properties in the Maidenhill area of Penrith.
