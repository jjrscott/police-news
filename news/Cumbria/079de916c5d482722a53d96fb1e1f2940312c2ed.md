Posted on 15:38:00 on 22nd March 2021

Police can confirm that a man's body was found on 24 March at a property in Grange-over-Sands.

The deceased has been formally identified as Andrew McIvor, who was reported missing on 22 March.

The circumstances of Mr McIvor's death are not believed to be suspicious. The Coroner has been informed.
