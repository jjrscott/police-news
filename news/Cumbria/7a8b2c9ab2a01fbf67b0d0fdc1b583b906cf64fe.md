Posted on 11:08:00 on 6th August 2019

Police are concerned for the welfare of missing 21-year-old Jadroslav Cermac who was last seen in the area of Winster on 3rd of August at 3pm.

Jadroslav is described as 6ft, stocky with short dark hair.

Anyone with information should contact police on 101. Officers would also like to urge Jadroslav to contact this number if he sees this appeal.
