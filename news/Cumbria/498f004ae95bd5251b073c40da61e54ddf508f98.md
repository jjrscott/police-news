Posted on 14:44:00 on 24th July 2019

Following new lines of enquiry police are releasing CCTV images in burglary and fraud investigation which occurred between the 31st May and the 1st June.

The burglary initially occurred at The Grange Hotel, Grange over Sands where several bank cards were taken.

A man purporting to be from the banks fraud team requested the pin code following suspicious activity on the card. The stolen card was then used at Barclays, Hanley, Stoke on Trent where a large amount of cash was withdrawn using the pin code.

Police would like to speak with the person pictured as part of their investigations.

Anyone with information or recognises the person pictured is asked to contact PC 2335 Matthews on 101, email 101@cumbria.police.uk or contact crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting log number 97 of the 1st June 2019.
