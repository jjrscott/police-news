Posted on 12:06:00 on 21st December 2020

The body of a woman found at Grange-over-sands, on Saturday 19th December has been formally identified as Ann Warren, 69, of Hampsfell Road, Grange-over-sands.

There are no suspicious circumstances at this stage.

A file has been prepared for the coroner.
