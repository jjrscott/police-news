Posted on 18:52:00 on 27th March 2020

A man has died following a collision on the A596 between Crosby and Crosby Villa today (Friday 27th March).
Police were called to the report of a two vehicle road traffic collision shortly after 9am.

The incident involved a motorcycle and a lorry.

As a result of the collision the rider of the motorcycled an 59-year-old man from the local area died.

The driver of the lorry was uninjured.
Anyone with any information or who witnessed the collision is asked to contact 101 or email 101@cumbria.police.uk, quoting log 34 of the 27th March 2020.
