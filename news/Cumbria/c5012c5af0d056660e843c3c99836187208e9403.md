Posted on 14:25:00 on 7th January 2021

CCTV

Police are releasing CCTV footage following an incident of theft that occurred at Aldi, Petteril Bank Road, Carlsile, between 12:45pm and 1pm on 19th December 2020.

During the incident, a quantity of alcohol was taken without payment being offered.

Officers are keen to speak to the man and woman pictured in support of their investigation.

Anyone with information is encouraged to contact PC 2893 on 101, quoting incident number 106 of 21 December. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
