Posted on 15:53:00 on 22nd January 2021

Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision involving a single cyclist in the village of Westward.

Officers were called by NWAS at around 4:25pm yesterday (Jan 21)

A 71-year-old man from Inverness was pronounced dead at the scene.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police on 101, email SCIU@cumbria.police.uk quoting log 148 of the 21st January 2020.
