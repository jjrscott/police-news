Posted on 14:22:00 on 6th December 2020

Police attended a licensed premises in Carlisle, yesterday (5th December), in the evening, following the report of an alleged breach of Covid regulations.

Cumbria Police are working in conjunction with local authorities, to establish if any breaches of the Covid regulations have occurred.

If breaches are found to have taken place, then necessary and appropriate enforcement will be actioned inline with Covid regulations and as a matter of public safety.
