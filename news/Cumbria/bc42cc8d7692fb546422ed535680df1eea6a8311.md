Posted on 11:13:00 on 16th December 2019

A man has been charged following an incident in Bootle on Friday night (13 December).

Brian Capstick, 48, of Newfield House, Ecclefechan, was charged with possession of a firearm with intent to cause fear or violence.

He was appearing at Workington's magistrates court this morning (16 December).
