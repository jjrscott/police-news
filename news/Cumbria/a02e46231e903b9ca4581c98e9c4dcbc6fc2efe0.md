Posted on 20:42:00 on 27th August 2020

Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident of assault that occurred at approximately 2:45pm today (Aug 27).

Officers were called to the area of Harrington road, and John street in Workington after reports of a man assaulting another man with a metal pole.

A 48-year-old man from Workington was arrested on suspicion of affray and actual bodily harm, he remains in police custody.

Enquires are ongoing to locate the second man.

Anyone with any information or who witnessed the incident or has dash cam footage of the incident is asked to contact the police on 101 quoting log 114 of 27th August 2020 or anonymously via crimestoppers on 0800 555 111
