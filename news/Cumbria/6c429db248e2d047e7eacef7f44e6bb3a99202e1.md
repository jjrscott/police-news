Posted on 07:22:00 on 1st September 2020

Emergency services are currently dealing with a multiple vehicle road traffic collision on the M6 in Carnforth.

Police were notified at 7.46am today (1 September).

All three lanes of the southbound carriageway may be closed for some time, causing traffic disruption.

Motorists are advised to avoid the area.

Visit the traffic link map at www.cumbria.police.uk/traffic-link-map to keep up to date with the latest situation on the roads.
