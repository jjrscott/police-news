Posted on 14:43:00 on 21st September 2020

A woman from Walney has been charged with perverting the course of justice.

Eleanor Williams, 19, of Teasdale Road, Walney, is currently on remand having been charged previously with seven counts of perverting the course of justice. She is due to appear at Preston Crown Court on 23 November 2020.

She is due to appear before Preston Magistrates' Court on Wednesday, 30 September 2020 in respect of the new charge.

The new charge relates to an offence alleged to have been committed on May 19, 2020.

The seven previous charges are alleged to have taken place between October 2017 and October 2019.
