Posted on 16:27:00 on 21st February 2020

Police are releasing CCTV footage in an attempted robbery investigation in Barrow on Sunday 9th February 2020.

At around 10.20pm Co-op Foods, Harrel Lane, an unknown person attacked two members of staff as they were closing the store.

Police would like to speak with the persons pictured as part of their investigations.

Anyone with any information or can identify the individual is asked to contact police on 101, email 101@cumbria.police.uk, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting log 264 of the 9th February 2020.
