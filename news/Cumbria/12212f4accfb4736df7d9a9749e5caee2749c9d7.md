Posted on 10:48:00 on 24th March 2020

Two men have been charged following a spate of burglaries in Seaton over the weekend (21-22nd March).

Ross Blacklock, 20, of no fixed abode, was charged with three burglaries of properties in the Cape Road, Croftfield Road and Inner Ling Road areas of Seaton as well as possession of a Class B drug.

Billy Joe Blacklock, 24, of no fixed abode, was charged with handling stolen property, theft, possession of a Class B drug and for violent conduct in a police station.

They have been remanded in custody to appear before Carlisle Crown Court in April.

We would like to thank the residents of Seaton for their help with our enquiries.
