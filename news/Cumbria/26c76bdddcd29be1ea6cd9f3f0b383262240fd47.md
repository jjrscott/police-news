Posted on 11:51:00 on 20th April 2020

Three people have been charged following a joint operation targeting county lines drug dealing on the 16th April.

Jack Hartley, aged 26 of Cowper Road, Merseyside has been charged with conspiracy to supply class A drugs and remanded to appear at court.

Amy Johnson, aged 26 of Cowper Road, Merseyside has been charged with conspiracy to supply class A drugs and remanded to appear at court.

Brendan Purcell, aged 45 of Earle Street, Barrow-In-Furness has been charged with conspiracy to supply class A drugs and remanded to appear at court.

All three are to appear at Preston Crown Court on May 15th.

A fourth man was arrested and released under investigation.
