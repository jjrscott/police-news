Posted on 08:36:00 on 3rd August 2019

The M6 is closed J38 - 37 in both directions following an incident of dangerous driving.

Police were called to the incident at 7:32am this morning, with the report of a vehicle driving the wrong way on the Northbound carriageway of the M6.

Police are in attendance at the scene. The road remains closed whilst investigations are carried out.

There have been no reported collision or injuries
