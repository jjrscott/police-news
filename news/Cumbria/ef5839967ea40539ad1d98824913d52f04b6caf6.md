Posted on 14:38:00 on 6th June 2019

Police are appealing for witnesses following a robbery at a jewellery shop in Carlisle city centre.

An investigation is underway after two robbers, wearing balaclavas, went into St Nicholas Galleries in Bank Street at about 2.20pm today (June 6).

A third person involved stayed in a vehicle - a blue Ford Fiesta, registration FM66 RYB.

One man working in the shop was assaulted during the robbery.

The robbers made off in the Ford Fiesta. It was abandoned in the Chiswick Street area in the city centre.

Detective Chief Inspector Helen Harkins said: "We would urge anybody who sees anyone they think might be these individuals not to approach them - but to phone 999 immediately.

"This is a busy part of the city centre so we would urge anybody who saw anything suspicious in this area to contact us.

"People can be reassured that enquiries are already well underway to find the people responsible.

"If anybody saw anything that might be relevant please contact us on the numbers below."

Call police with information on 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
