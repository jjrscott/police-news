Posted on 16:04:00 on 17th December 2020

A 22-year-old man who died yesterday (Dec 16) following a collision on the A685 North of Grayrigg has been named.

Police can confirm that Morgan Davidson from Kendal sadly died as a result of the collision.

Police were called at 2:59pm yesterday to the report of a two vehicle road traffic collision involving a white Honda motorcycle and a white HGV.

Officers are continuing their appeal for witnesses that were travelling on the road around the time of the collision.

Anyone with any information or who witnessed the collision is asked to contact PC2291 Wilson or Sgt 1929 Bainbridge of Kendal Mobile Support Group on 101 or email 101@cumbria.police.uk, quoting log 129 of 16th December 2020.
