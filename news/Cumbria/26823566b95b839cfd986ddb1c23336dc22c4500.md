Posted on 13:24:00 on 25th September 2019

A man who was wanted for failing to appear at court has been located in Oldbury in the West Midlands.

** **

David Lukac, aged 27, is now in prison after being caught by police yesterday (September 24) following information from a member of the public.

Lukac will serve two and half years in prison for an offence of fraud by false representation.

An appeal for information on his whereabouts was broadcast on the BBC's Crimewatch Roadshow on September 20.
