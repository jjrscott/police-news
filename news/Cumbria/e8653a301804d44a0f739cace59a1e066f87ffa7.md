Posted on 13:06:00 on 22nd February 2021

Police can confirm that the body of a man was found in the area Whitrigg, Bowness on Solway at around 3am this morning (Feb 22)

Formal identification has not yet taken place and inquiries are ongoing.

The circumstances are not believed to be suspicious at this stage.
