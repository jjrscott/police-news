Posted on 09:47:00 on 16th August 2020

Cumbria Police is appealing for information following an incident at an address in Thwaite Bank, Northside, Workington.

Police were contacted shortly after 6am this morning after occupants of the address found a man in their home this morning.

The man was chased from the address.

Officers have subsequently arrested a 43-year-old man on suspicion of aggravated burglary.

However, officers are keen to hear from anyone who has any knowledge of this incident or who saw anyone acting suspiciously in the area before or afterwards.

Please contact police referencing incident 57 of today (16 August).

Email: 101@cumbria.police.uk
Call non-emergency number: 101
Or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
Always call in an emergency 999
