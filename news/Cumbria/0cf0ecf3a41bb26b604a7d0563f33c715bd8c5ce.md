Posted on 12:54:00 on 21st August 2019

Police can confirm that a 41-year-old man, of no fixed address, has been arrested and is in custody, in connection with the assault in Coniston Drive, Workington, in which two people were injured in the early hours of this morning (21st August).

We are not looking for anyone else in connection with this incident.
