Posted on 09:00:00 on 27th November 2019

Cumbria Police is appealing for witnesses following a fatal road traffic collision which occurred on the A590 in the early hours of this morning (27 November).

The collision occurred at around 1.10am on the A590 between Haverthwaite and Backbarrow when a Kia car collided with the bridge.

At this time, it is not believed any other vehicles were involved.

The driver of the vehicle, a 23-year-old woman, is not believed to have suffered any serious injury.

The passenger, a 22-year-old man, was pronounced dead at the scene.

The road currently remains closed but is expected to reopen this morning. People are asked to be patient.

Anyone who witnessed the crash or who has any information which may assist police is urged to contact PS 1498 HILL or PC 563 WALSH referencing incident 5 of 27 November 2019.
