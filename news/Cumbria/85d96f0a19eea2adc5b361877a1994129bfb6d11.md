Posted on 05:36:00 on 28th October 2019

Police are currently dealing with an incident at Dixon's Chimney, Carlisle.

_ _

Officers became aware of a man trapped at height in the area.

_ _

Officers are currently working with other partner agencies to free the man.

_ _

Road closures are currently in place at Stanhope Road, Charlotte Street and Shaddongate where it meets Bridge Street (A595)
