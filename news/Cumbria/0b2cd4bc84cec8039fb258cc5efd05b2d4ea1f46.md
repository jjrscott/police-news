Posted on 15:05:00 on 28th August 2019

Police are currently dealing with a road traffic collision at the roundabout of junction 44 on the M6.

One lane is currently closed and motorists are warned there may be delays.

The incident involved a Kia Picanto and Audi. Injuries to those involved are not believed to be serious at this time.

Anyone who witnessed or has dashcam footage is asked to contact PC 1724 on 101, email 101@cumbria.police.uk, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting log 111 of the 28th August 2019.
