Posted on 11:10:00 on 17th July 2019

Police can confirm that the body of a man was found on land between High Harrington and Salterbeck, Workington, at about 10.50am today (July 17).

Formal identification has not yet taken place and inquiries are ongoing.

The circumstances are not believed to be suspicious at this stage.
