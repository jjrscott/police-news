Posted on 11:41:00 on 15th February 2021

A man has received a ticket for having an insecure load on a vehicle. The ticket was issued following an investigation into a large gas cannister found on the carriageway of Caldew Bridge, Carlisle, on the 9th February at around 3:49pm.

Police would like to thank everyone who responded to the appeal for information.
