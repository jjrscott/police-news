Posted on 12:45:00 on 2nd August 2019

Police were called to the report of a two vehicle collision at 10:44am this morning (2nd August), on St Georges Road, Millom.

The collision involved a Ford Edge and a Citron Picasso.

The driver of the Ford, a 79-year-old man from Haverigg, was airlifted to Preston hospital with serious injuries. A female passenger received minor injuries that were treated at the scene.

The Citron was unoccupied at the time of the collision.

The road is closed whilst a collision investigation is carried out.

Congestion is likely in the area and delays are expected. Drivers are asked to plan their journeys accordingly.
