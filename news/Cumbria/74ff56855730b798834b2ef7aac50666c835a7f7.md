Posted on 13:39:00 on 14th February 2020

Police are concerned for the welfare of 76-year-old Peter Wakefield who was last seen in the area of Edenhall today (Feb 14) at 11am.

Peter is described as 6ft and slim and was last seen wearing a beige jumper, black/grey trousers and a blue jacket.

Anyone with information should contact police on 101. Officers would also like to urge Peter to contact this number if he sees this appeal.
