Posted on 13:26:00 on 13th June 2020

Police are currently dealing with a two vehicle road traffic collision on the A590 near to the Newby Bridge service station, Ulverston.

The road has been closed between Newby Bridge roundabout and Meathop roundabout while emergency services attend the scene.

The public are advised to avoid the area.
