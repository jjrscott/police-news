Posted on 12:30:00 on 26th February 2020

Three men were arrested yesterday (Feb 25) in connection with a series of burglaries in the Kendal area.

A 25-year-old man from Kendal was arrested on suspicion of four counts of burglary and conspiring to commit burglary and remains in police custody.

A 27-year-old man from Kendal was arrested on suspicion of four counts of burglary and conspiring to commit burglary and remains in police custody.

A 28-year-old man from Kendal was arrested on suspicion of four counts of burglary and conspiring to commit burglary and remains in police custody.

Officers would like to remind the public to take steps to protect their property this can be simple things such as ensuring doors and windows are secure and leaving valuable items out of sight.
