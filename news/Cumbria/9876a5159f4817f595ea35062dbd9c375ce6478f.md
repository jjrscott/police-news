Posted on 14:39:00 on 30th August 2019

Police are in attendance to a collision on the M6 Northbound between J39 B6261 (Shap) and J40 A66 (Penrith). Police were called to the incident at 3:03pm which involved two vehicles.

Lane one is currently closed whilst emergency services attend the scene (lanes two and three are open)

Delays are likely in this area and drivers are asked to plan their journey accordingly.

It is raining heavily in places in Cumbria with strong winds, drivers are asked to drive to the road conditions in front of them, which may be slower than the speed limit.
