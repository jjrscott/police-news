Posted on 11:21:00 on 14th October 2019

Police are currently dealing with a road traffic collision on Victoria Road, Penrith.

The ambulance service are currently at the scene.

A closure is in place southbound at Roper Street and northbound at Kemplay Bank roundabout.

Motorists are advised to avoid the area as delays are expected and traffic is building up.

Visit the traffic link map at www.cumbria.police.uk/traffic-link-map to keep up to date with the latest situation on the roads.
