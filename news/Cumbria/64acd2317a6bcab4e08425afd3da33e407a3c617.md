Posted on 05:19:00 on 12th March 2020

Emergency services are dealing with a road traffic collision on the A590 near Lindal-in-Furness.

Police were called today (12 March) shortly before 4am following a collision involving a car and a pedestrian.

A section of the road is closed and a diversion is in place. HGVs travelling to Barrow are being diverted at Greenodd to use the A5092 and A595.

Motorists are advised to avoid the area.

Visit the traffic link map at www.cumbria.police.uk/traffic-link-map to keep up to date with the latest situation on the roads.
