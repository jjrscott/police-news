Posted on 07:50:00 on 3rd December 2020

 Police are concerned for the welfare of Laura Wilson, aged 35, from the Workington area.

Laura was last seen in the early hours of Thursday 3rd December, in the Mossbay Road area.

Police believe Laura may be in the Glasson, Maryport area.

Laura is described as a female, around 5 ft 8 in height with short, brown and blonde hair.

If anyone has seen Laura, or knows her whereabouts they are asked to report it to police on 101 quoting log 15 of the 3rd December 2020.

Police also ask Laura, if she sees this appeal, to contact them to let them know she is safe and well.
