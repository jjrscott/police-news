Posted on 16:19:00 on 25th November 2020

Four people were arrested after Barrow police carried out a drugs warrant to address community concerns.

Officers executed the warrant at Cote Ley Crescent yesterday (Nov 24), seizing drugs and cash as well as making the arrests.

Joseph Robertson, 18, of no fixed abode, Liverpool, was charged with possessing a class A drug with intent to supply, possessing cannabis resin and possessing criminal property.

He was remanded in custody ahead of an appearance before Barrow's magistrates' court tomorrow.
Two people - a woman and a teenage girl - who were arrested have been released under investigation while further inquiries are carried out.

Another man who was arrested was released with no further action.
