Posted on 13:06:00 on 19th May 2019

A pedestrian who was seriously injured in the collision on the A66 Stainburn Bypass, in the early hour of this morning (19th May), has died.

The pedestrian, a 37-year-old man from Cockermouth, had been take to the Cumberland Infirmary Carlisle, in a critical condition, but has since died.

The family are being supported by Cumbria Police. Any witnesses who have not already provided details are asked to contact PC 2344 Ross of Cumbria Mobile Support Group on 101.
