Posted on 14:21:00 on 13th February 2020

A man charged with conspiracy to supply a class A controlled drug has appeared at court.

Louis Simpson, aged 26, from Walton Road, Liverpool appeared at North and West Cumbria Magistrates on the 11th February.

He was remanded to appear at Carlisle Crown Court on 16th March 2020.
