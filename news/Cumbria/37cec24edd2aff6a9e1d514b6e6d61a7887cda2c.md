Posted on 07:25:00 on 3rd July 2019

Emergency services are currently dealing with a collision on the A595 Distington bypass.

The collision, involving a motorcycle and a car, happened just after 6am today (July 3).

The southbound carriageway is currently closed and may be shut for some time.

Motorists are asked to avoid the area if possible.
