Posted on 12:29:00 on 8th March 2021

Police are requesting the public's help in locating Kane Hull, 28, who is wanted for a recall to prison.

Kane is described as white, 5ft 8in tall, of average build and he has blue eyes.

Kane has links to Carlisle but could have travelled to any location.

Anyone who sees Kane should not approach him, but they should contact police immediately on 999.
