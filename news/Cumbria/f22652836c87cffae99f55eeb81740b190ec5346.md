Posted on 15:11:00 on 8th December 2020

CCTV

Police are releasing CCTV footage in an investigation of theft that occurred at Booths in Kendal on 3rd December.

A large quantity of baby food was taken from the store without payment being offered.

Officers are keen to speak to the woman pictured in order to support their investigation.

Anyone with information is encouraged to contact PC 2754 on 101, quoting incident number 125 of 3rd December. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
