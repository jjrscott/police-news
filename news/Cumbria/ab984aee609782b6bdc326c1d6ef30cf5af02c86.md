Posted on 11:17:00 on 12th March 2020

The A590 has now reopened following a collision near Lindal in Furness today (March 12).

Police were called shortly before 4am following a collision involving a car and a pedestrian.

The pedestrian, a man, 30, from the north east of England, was taken to the Royal Preston Hospital, where he was said to be in a critical condition.

Anyone who saw the collision or has any information should call 101.
