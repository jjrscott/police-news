Posted on 15:38:00 on 7th June 2019

Police and other emergency services are currently dealing with a collision in Ambleside.
The collision, on Lake Road at about 3.45pm, involved a car and a pedestrian.
Motorists are asked to avoid the area. Delays are expected for some time.
