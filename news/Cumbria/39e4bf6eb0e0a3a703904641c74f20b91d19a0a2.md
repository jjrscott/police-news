Posted on 14:20:00 on 19th December 2019

A man has been remanded in custody after appearing before a court charged with burglary.

Andrew Hall, 37, of Prince Charles Close, Penrith, is charged with burgling the Robin Hood pub, King Street, Penrith, on December 3.

Hall appeared before Carlisle's magistrates' court today (Dec 19). The case was adjourned until January 20, when it will be heard at Carlisle Crown Court.

Hall entered a not guilty plea.
