Posted on 10:47:00 on 17th March 2021

A man from Workington has appeared at North and West Cumbria Magistrate's Court today (March 17).

Matthew Ferguson, 38, of King Street, Workington, was charged with attempting to cause or incite a girl under 13 to engage in sexual activity, possession of a controlled drug of Class A - Heroin, Possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class C and assault by beating.

He has been remanded to appear at Crown Court on the 19th April.
