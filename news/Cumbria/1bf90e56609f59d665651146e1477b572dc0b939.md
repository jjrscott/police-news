Posted on 08:39:00 on 22nd February 2020

Mr Wilson, who suffers from dementia, was last seen at his home address in the Cark area of Cartmel at around 1am this morning (22nd February).

He is described as white, 5ft7ins tall, with light brown hair. He is believed to be wearing dark coloured joggers, a pyjama top and grey jumper.

Anyone who sees Arthur or anyone with information is asked to call police on 101.
