Posted on 22:09:00 on 14th February 2020

Police can confirm that the body of a man was found in the Edenhall area, at about 3:40pm today (Feb 14).

Formal identification has not yet taken place and enquiries are ongoing.

The circumstances are not believed to be suspicious at this time.
