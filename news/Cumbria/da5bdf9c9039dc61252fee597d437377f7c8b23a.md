Posted on 13:37:00 on 18th May 2019

Police are appealing for a witness to make contact, as investigations continue into an ATM theft that occurred at around 1:30am this morning (18th May), at Booth, Windermere.

A member of the public spoke to door staff at the Wheelhouse, Bowness, alerting them to the incident before they got into a taxi.

Police are appealing for this member of the public to contact DS 2010 Andy Lamb on 101, quoting log number 28th of the 18th May, or call CrimeStoppers, anonymously on 0800 555 111.
