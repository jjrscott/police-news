Posted on 11:22:00 on 12th April 2020

Police were called to an address in Brantwood Avenue, Carlisle, at 10.49am today (April 12).

A man was subsequently pronounced deceased.

A murder investigation is underway. Two men have been arrested and are in custody.

The public are likely to see a larger than normal police presence in this area as inquiries are carried out.
