Posted on 16:41:00 on 3rd February 2020

Detectives in west Cumbria have charged a man with 11 offences, including burglary and aggravated vehicle taking.

Colin Berry, 28, of Richmond Road, Workington, has been remanded in custody after appearing before Workington's magistrates' court today.

This followed investigations launched following a number of incidents in the Lamplugh, Rowrah and Frizington area overnight from Friday into Saturday (February 1).

Berry is charged with:

* Dwelling burglary.

* Non-dwelling burglary.

* Three counts of aggravated vehicle taking.

* Vehicle interference.

* Two counts of disqualified driving.

* Two charges of driving with no insurance.

* Shoplifting.

Berry's case will next be heard at Carlisle Crown Court at a later date.
