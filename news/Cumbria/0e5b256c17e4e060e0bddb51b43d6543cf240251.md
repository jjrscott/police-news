Posted on 20:00:00 on 7th October 2019

Concerns are growing for a 48-year-old man missing from the Carlisle area.

Edmond Pendrous, who lives in the city centre area, was last spoken to in the area of Castle Street this morning (October 7).

It is believed that he may be travelling in a silver Seat Leon, registration number: PY17 NCE.

Anyone who has seen Mr Pendrous or the vehicle he is believed to be travelling in is urged to contact police on 101.
