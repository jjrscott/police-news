Posted on 13:35:00 on 28th January 2021

Police have arrested two local teenagers following an incident of robbery.

The incident occurred around 6pm on 27 January, in a lane between Benson Row and Brentfield Way, Penrith.

It is reported that a woman was approached by two men and her handbag was taken.

A 15-year-old from Penrith was arrested on suspicion of robbery.

A 16-year-old from Penrith was arrested on suspicion of robbery.

They both remain in police custody.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who may have witnessed the incident or anyone with CCTV or dash cam footage in the area.

Anyone with information is encouraged to contact DS 2117 on 101, quoting incident number 156 of 27 January 2020. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
