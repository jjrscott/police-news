Posted on 12:48:00 on 23rd February 2021

The body of a man found at Whitrigg, in the early hours of yesterday morning (Feb 22) has been formally identified as Stephen Clingo, 63, of no fixed address.

There are no suspicious circumstances at this stage.

A file has been prepared for the coroner.
