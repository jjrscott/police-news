Police are issuing a CCTV image of a male they would like to speak to in connection with an incident of theft in Ipswich.

The incident occurred between 3pm and 5pm on Friday 27 November within the opticians at Boots in Tavern Street.

A man entered Boots and tried on a number of designer frame styles and then returned and stole a number of them.

The male suspect is described as being approximately 5ft 6ins tall and was wearing a light-coloured coat.

Officers would like to speak to the male pictured in the CCTV image about the incident as he may be able to help with the investigation.

Anyone who believes they recognise the male pictured in the CCTV image, or who has any information about this incident, is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/69082/20
