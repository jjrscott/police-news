Police are appealing for witnesses and information following an assault in Beccles.

On Saturday 5 September at approximately 9pm, two men were involved in an altercation outside the Wetherspoons pub on New Market.

A man in his 20s was assaulted and suffered minor injuries to his lip.

The suspect is described as a white male in his 40s, with short hair and grey stubble. He was wearing black shorts, a black top and a black cap.

Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to contact Suffolk Police quoting reference number 37/51977/20.
