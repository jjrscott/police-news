Police are appealing for witnesses to an attempted burglary that took place in Felixstowe.
It happened as the Forty 7 hairdressers on Orwell Road in the town at about 7.45pm on Sunday 29 January.
Two suspects went to the rear of the property and threw a rock smashing a rear upper window pane. No access was gained and they were then seen to flee the scene in the direction of Garrison Lane.
Anyone who witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it should contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/5355/18.
