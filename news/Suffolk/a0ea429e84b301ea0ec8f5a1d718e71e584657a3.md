Police are appealing for witnesses following a robbery that took place in Haverhill in the early hours of yesterday morning.

At approximately 3:30am, on Sunday 19 February, two men in their 30s and 20s, a teenage boy and a teenage girl walking along Hardwick Road were approached by several young men. They were ordered to empty their pockets and the teenage boy was reportedly struck in the face. A wallet was stolen and the suspects made off along the footpath towards the old railway line.
