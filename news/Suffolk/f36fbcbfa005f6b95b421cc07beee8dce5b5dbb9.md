An 86-year-old female pedestrian who died following a collision with a black Vauxhall Corsa in Sudbury is being named by police.

Officers were called by the Ambulance Service just before 6:50pm on Monday 6 November to reports of an incident on the A131 Melford Road.

Kathleen Butcher, from Sudbury, who received medical treatment at the scene, sadly died from her injuries.

Any witnesses are asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting reference CAD 343 of 6 November.

## Previous release | 6 November 2017

A woman has died following a serious road traffic collision in Sudbury.

Police were called by the Ambulance Service just before 6.50pm this evening, Monday 6 November, to reports of an incident on the A131 Melford Road.

A female pedestrian was involved in a collision with a black Vauxhall Corsa and sadly died at the scene.

The road is currently closed between Abbey Road and Chaucer Road whilst a collision investigation takes place.

Any witnesses are asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting reference CAD 343 of 6 November.
