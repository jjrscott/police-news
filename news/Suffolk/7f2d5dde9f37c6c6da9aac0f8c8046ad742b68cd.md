Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Earl Soham.

At some point between 11pm on Wednesday 3 March and 630am on Thursday 4 March offender(s) entered the property and stole a number of items from a gym located at the back of the property.

They include a set of Body Power Olympic weights (including bar) and two slam balls, three pairs dumb bells (12.5kg, 17.5kg, 20 kg) and 16kg kettlebell, three pairs dumb bells (22.5kg, 25kg, 30kg), a DeWalt twin battery pack, a DeWalt batteries and chargers, a DeWalt drill, four sets of lighter dumb bells and three kettlebells. The total value of the items stolen is an estimated £2000.

Any one who knows who was responsible or has knowledge of the whereabouts of any of the items stolen should contact Suffolk police quoting crime number 37/10727/21.
