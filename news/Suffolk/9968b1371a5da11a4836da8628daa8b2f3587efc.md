Police are appealing for witnesses and information after a boy was approached by a car driver in Ipswich.

The incident occurred at around 3:50pm on Wednesday 21 September in Hervey Street, at the junction with Cemetery Road.

A 12-year-old boy was walking alone when a man in a parked grey Vauxhall Astra told the boy to get into his car and he would be given sweets. The boy ignored the man and continued home.

The suspect is described as white with dark, spikey hair.

Anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time, or believes they may know who the man is, are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 reference 56733/16. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at http://www.crimestoppers-uk.org/.
