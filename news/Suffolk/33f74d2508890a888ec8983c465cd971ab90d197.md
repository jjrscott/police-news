Police are appealing for witnesses after a man was assaulted in Tavern Street in Ipswich on January 28.

Officers were called around 5.15pm on Saturday 28 to reports that a person had been assaulted. Officers attended and found a bin lorry worker had asked several youths to move their cycles as they were blocking access to the bins. There was then an altercation resulting in the man being assaulted. He sustained an injury to his head that required stitches.

Police investigating the incident are asking anyone who saw what happened, or anyone who may have information about those involved, to come forward. If you can help please contact Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 14700/17.
