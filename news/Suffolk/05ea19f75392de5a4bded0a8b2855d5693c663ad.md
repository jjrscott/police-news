Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Gislingham.

On Wednesday 4 January at approximately 2.45am, offenders forced entry to an outbuilding at a property in the Finningham Road/High Street/ Morleys Lane area, and took several items of gardening equipment.

A mini moto, Stihl chain saw, Stihl strimmer, Stihl hedge trimmer and pressure washer were stolen.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have seen any suspicious behaviour in this area at the time stated.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on **101** and quote reference number 37/10554/17.
