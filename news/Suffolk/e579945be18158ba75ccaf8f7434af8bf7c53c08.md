Police have arrested a man in connection with two burglaries that took place in Felixstowe, as officers investigate burglaries in and around the town.

The first incident took place sometime between Saturday 11 and Monday 13 March at a business premises in High Road East. A quantity of cash and a charity box were reported as stolen from the property.

The second break-in took place overnight between Monday 20 and Tuesday 21 March at a business property on Hamilton Road. A silver Acer laptop and a small amount of cash were stolen.

A 48-year-old man from Felixstowe has been arrested this afternoon, Friday 31 March, on suspicion of burglary and has been taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre where he remains in custody for questioning.
