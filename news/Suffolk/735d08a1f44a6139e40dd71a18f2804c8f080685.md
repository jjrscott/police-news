Police have made five arrests in connection with a suspected burglary in Ipswich.

Officers were called to an address on Old Foundry Road in the town following a call from a member of the public who reported some suspicious activity last night (Thursday 9 March) at about 10.25pm.

Four men, aged 32, 31, 19 and 17 and one woman aged 26 were all arrested on suspicion of burglary and have been taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning where they currently remain.

Police are appealing for members of the public who may have information relating to this incident to contact Ipswich CID on **101** or Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
