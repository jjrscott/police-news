A 46-year-old man has been charged in connection with an incident in Lowestoft.

Police were called shortly before 5.20pm on Friday 26 October to reports of an altercation between two men on Station Square and a man was taken to the James Paget Hospital for treatment for cuts to his head and neck.

A man was arrested and taken to Great Yarmouth police investigation centre for questioning.

Jason Hamilton, aged 46, of Suffolk Road in Lowestoft, has been charged with malicious wounding, possession of a bladed article in a public place and theft.
