Suffolk Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious single vehicle collision between Haughley and Wetherden over the weekend.

On Sunday 9 April at approximately 4.30am, it is believed that a black Ford Puma car was travelling towards the Haughley New Street area, on a side road close to the A14, towards the direction of Stowmarket.

The vehicle ended up on its roof, close to the Park Road junction at Wetherden. The male occupant sustained head injuries and was taken to the West Suffolk hospital for treatment.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the collision, or who may have seen the manner of driving of the vehicle beforehand.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting CAD reference 56 of 9 April.
