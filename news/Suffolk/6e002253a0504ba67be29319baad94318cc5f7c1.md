Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary at a home in Hadleigh on Good Friday.

The break in took place at approximately 3am on Friday 14 April at an address in Timperley Road. A mobile phone, a wallet containing bank cards and a quantity of cash, and a black Nike Manchester United zip-up jumper were stolen.

It is believed that the offender(s) entered and left the property to the rear of the building.

Enquiries are ongoing to identify those responsible and police are appealing for any witnesses or anyone with information to help bring the investigation forward to contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference 28936/17. Alternatively, for those who wish to remain anonymous, contact Crimestoppers on **0800 555 111** or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
