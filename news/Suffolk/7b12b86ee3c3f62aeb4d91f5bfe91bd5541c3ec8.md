Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary in Wickhambrook.

The incident occurred at some point between 4.30pm on Thursday 31 December and 10am on Sunday 3 January, at a property in Baxters Green.

During the times stated, an unknown suspect broke into a locked barn and removed an orange and black ride on mower - Kubota BX231 (registration AE20 BYU) - from within.

Police are reminding residents and businesses to review the security of their sheds, garages and outbuildings following a number of burglaries in the county over recent weeks.

For crime prevention advice, visit https://www.suffolk.police.uk/sites/suffolk/files/shedandgaragesecurity1.pdf and http://www.suffolk.police.uk/sites/suffolk/files/farmsecurity1.pdf

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/371/21
