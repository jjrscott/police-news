A 27 year man from Stowmarket has been charged with three burglaries and receiving stolen property

Samuel Rossiter of Haughley Road, Harleston, Stowmarket was charged yesterday Thursday 14 July, with three burglaries and receiving stolen property.

In the first burglary, which took place sometime between 11 October and 12 October 2015 at a property in Bury Road Stowmarket, items were stolen which included a Bosch Jigsaw, a circular saw, a Bosch battery drill charger and battery, a 3" angle grinder, a LED light and a blue roller bucket.

The second burglary took place on 12 October 2015, at a property in Bury Street, Stowmarket. A purse containing cash and bank cards, a mobile telephone battery pack, a packet of cigarettes, a Canon camera charger and a bottle of alcohol were stolen.

The third burglary took place on 29 June 2016 at a property in Cardinalls Road, Stowmarket. Computer and sporting equipment along with items of jewellery were stolen.

In addition, sometime between 7 May and 14 May 2016, Rossiter is charged with receiving stolen property.

Samuel Rossiter appeared before magistrates yesterday and has been remanded in custody. The case was committed to Crown Court on a date to be fixed.
