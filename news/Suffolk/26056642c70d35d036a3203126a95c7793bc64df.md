Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary in Haverhill yesterday afternoon, Wednesday 5 December.

The property on Colne Valley Road was broken into between 12.15pm & 6.20pm when the resident returned home and found that an untidy search had taken place of the main bedroom.

They had entered by an insecure bathroom window with items of jewellery potentially stolen.

Police are keen to speak with anyone who may have seen or heard any suspicious activity in the areas mentioned during the times given. If you have any information, please contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/70361/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
