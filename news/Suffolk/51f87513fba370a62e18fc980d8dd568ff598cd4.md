Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Ipswich at the weekend.

At some point between Friday 12 January at 12pm and Saturday 13 January at 1.45pm, offenders forced entry to a property on Highfield Approach via a rear door.

An amount of copper piping was removed from several areas of the house causing water damage to the property.

Anyone with any information should call South CID on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/2477/18.
