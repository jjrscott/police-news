Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Lowestoft.

At some point between 11pm on Saturday 3 March and 01:50am on Sunday 4 March, a 22-year-old man from Halesworth, was assaulted by what is believed to have been three unknown males in High Street.

As a result of the assault, the victim sustained multiple fractures to his jaw. He was later taken to the James Paget Hospital by paramedics for treatment.
