Police are appealing for witnesses or information after a car was set alight in Lowestoft.

The incident occurred at approximately 10.50pm on Friday 18 December, while the car – a grey Peugeot 306 – was parked in Norwich Road.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed any suspicious activity in the vicinity of Norwich Road at around the time of the incident.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/73333/20
