Officers are appealing for witnesses or information following an assault which took place near the junction of Bramford Road and Richmond Road in Ipswich.

The incident happened on Saturday 12 August at around 10.40am. An altercation took place between two men, one of whom was driving a Jaguar car and the other who was riding a bicycle.

It is reported that the cyclist verbally abused the car driver and then spat in his face before then hitting him. The victim sustained cuts and bruises to his face and arm.

The suspect is described as a man of mixed race appearance with a stocky build. He was believed to have been riding a sports type pedal bicycle.

Anybody who witnessed the incident or has any information are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 reference 49874/16. Alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
