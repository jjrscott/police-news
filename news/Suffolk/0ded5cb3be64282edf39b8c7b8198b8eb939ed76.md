A missing 76-year-old man from Haverhill has been found.

Robert Dunning was reported missing to police on Monday, 27 November. He was found in the safe and well in Cambridgeshire this afternoon.

We would like to thank the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release - 27 November

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 76-year-old man from Haverhill.

Robert Dunning was reported missing to police yesterday, Monday 27 November, but was last seen at his home in the Queens Street area of the town at 9.20am last Wednesday, 22 November.

Anyone who believes they may have seen Mr Dunning, or who has any information regarding his whereabouts, is asked to contact the duty sergeant at Haverhill Police Station on 101.
