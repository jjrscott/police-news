Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of public disorder involving two motorists on the A134 between Bradfield Combust and Sudbury.

It took place on Saturday 23 February at some point between 5.10pm and 5.30pm. The incident involved a red Kia Ceed and a silver BMW 320D where the driver of the Kia overtook and stopped ahead of the BMW on three occasions. On one occasion a bottle was thrown at the vehicle, causing two dents and a scrape to the windscreen. No one was hurt in the incident, although the driver of the BMW and two passengers were left shaken.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it should contact Sudbury police on 101, quoting crime number 37/10798/19.
