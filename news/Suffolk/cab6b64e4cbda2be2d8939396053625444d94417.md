Police are appealing for witnesses and information following an incident in which a man was stabbed in Lowestoft.

Officers were called by the East of England Ambulance Service at approximately 12:25am on Sunday 6 November, reporting that a man had been stabbed at a premises in Commercial Road.

The victim, a man in his thirties from Lowestoft, sustained stab wounds to the leg and arm and was taken to hospital where he is being treated for his injuries. He is described as being in a stable condition.

A scene is on at the address as police continue conducting enquiries.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Lowestoft CID at Suffolk Police on 101, quoting reference 37/71677/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
