A pedestrian remains in a serious condition in Ipswich Hospital today, Wednesday 27 June, after a road traffic collision in Ipswich.

Officers were called to Wellington Street at around 4.50pm yesterday, Tuesday 26 June, to a collision involving a blue Vauxhall Zafira and a pedestrian, who sustained a head injury.

Wellington Road was closed between the junctions of Bramford Road and Little Bramford Lane until around 9.20pm while emergency services were at the scene.

Any witnesses to the incident should contact Suffolk Police on 101, quoting incident number 317 of Tuesday 26 June.
