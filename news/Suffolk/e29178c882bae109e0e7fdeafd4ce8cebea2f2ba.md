Police are appealing for witnesses or information following an assault in Lowestoft.

The incident took place around 00.15am on Saturday 5 August in Normanston Drive. A man was struck on the back of the head by an unknown offender causing his glasses to be knocked off. The victim was not injured.

Anybody who witnessed the incident or who has any information are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 52967/17.
