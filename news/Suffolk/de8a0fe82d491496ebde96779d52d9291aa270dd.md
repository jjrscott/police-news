Police are naming a pedestrian who died following a fatal road traffic collision on the A14 at Sproughton.

Officers were called at around 5.50pm on Monday 26 December to reports of a collision involving a lorry and a pedestrian on the eastbound carriageway between junctions 54 and 55.

James Holt, aged 43 and of High Road, Trimley St Mary was sadly declared deceased at the scene.

Any witnesses to the incident are asked to contact Serious Collision Investigation Team on **101** , quoting CAD 196 of 26/12.

## Previous release - 26 December 2016

Police are currently at the scene of a serious road traffic collision on the A14 at Sproughton.

Officers were called at around 5.50pm today, Monday 26 December, to reports of a collision involving a lorry and a pedestrian on the eastbound carriageway between junctions 54 and 55.

The road is currently closed at junction 54 and diversions are in place.
