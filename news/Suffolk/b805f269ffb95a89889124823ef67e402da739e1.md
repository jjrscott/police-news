Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Lowestoft.

The incident took place at an address on Kilbourn Road in the town. Sometime between 11.45 am on Tuesday 28 February and 8.30am on Thursday 2 March suspect/s forced entry into the property and stole some items of jewellery and a safe.

Witnesses or anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time are asked to call Police on **101** reference 20496/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
