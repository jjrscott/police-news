Police are appealing for help to trace a man – described as bald, wearing dark blue jogging bottoms and a beige cardigan – after he indecently exposed himself to a woman in Newmarket.

The incident happened around 12noon on Tuesday 16 May in George Lambton Avenue, near the junction with Noel Murless Drive. The woman, in her 20s, was approached by the man who initially asked for directions but then exposed himself to her.

The woman walked away and reported the incident to police a short time later.

Police are asking anyone who may have seen the man in the area around this time or anyone with information about who he is to call Suffolk Police on 101, quoting crime number 35823/17.
