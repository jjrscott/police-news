Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary in Freckenham.

The incident occurred shortly after 5.10pm on Monday 14 December at a kennels in Elms Road.

At around the time stated, three unknown suspects, who were wearing rain jackets/hoodies, entered the kennels by climbing over a low fence. They let out several dogs from the kennels and then left taking three of the dogs. A female tri-coloured Jack Russell, a white and tan male Jack Russell and a male Cairn Terrier.

The male Jack Russell and the Cairn Terrier were located by a member of the public in Kent yesterday, Tuesday 15 December, and have now been returned to the owner. The female Jack Russell still remains outstanding at this time.

A number of enquiries have been carried out and officers are currently exploring whether this incident is linked to other dog theft incidents in the county.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who may have witnessed anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/72376/20.
