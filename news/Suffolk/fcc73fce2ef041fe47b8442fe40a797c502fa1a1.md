Police are issuing a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with an assault in Bury St Edmunds last month.

The incident took place at around 2.10am on Saturday 14 January at a licensed premises in St Andrews Street South.

The victim – an 18-year-old man – was assaulted by being struck in the face with what was believed to be a bottle. He was taken to hospital having sustained cuts to his forehead and cheek which required stitches.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured in the CCTV about this incident. He is described as white, aged between 20 and 25, of medium build, with medium-length slicked back blonde hair and a beard. He was wearing a green wax jacket and a white t-shirt.

Anyone who believes they know this man, or who has any information about this incident, is asked to contact the Bury St Edmunds Neighbourhood Response Team on **101** quoting reference 12204/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
