The sudden death of a man in Carlton Colville is not being treated as suspicious.

Officers were called by the East of England Ambulance Service to attend an address in the town at approximately 11.20pm last night, Saturday 30 December, following reports of a man in cardiac arrest.

The man, believed to be in his 70s, was pronounced deceased at the scene. His death remains unexplained, but following initial police enquires it is not believed to be suspicious.

A police cordon which had been in place at the address has been lifted and a file will now be prepared for the coroner.
