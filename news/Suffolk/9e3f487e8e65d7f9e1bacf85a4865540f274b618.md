Police are appealing for information after a fire was deliberately ignited in a bin storage room in Ipswich.

The incident occurred at around 2.15am this morning, Wednesday 2 November, at a block of flats in Grimwade Street.

Officers who had been called to a nearby minor disturbance, heard a noise and on closer inspection discovered the fire in the ground floor room.

The fire service were called and the police officers immediately began evacuating the building. The fire was extinguished by 3am and residents were allowed back in.

No one was harmed during this incident, but the storage room was severely damaged.

Anyone with information about this crime is asked to contact the Ipswich Neighbourhood Response Team at Landmark House on **101** quoting reference 64023/16, or Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
