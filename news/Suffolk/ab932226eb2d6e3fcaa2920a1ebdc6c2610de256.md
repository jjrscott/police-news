Police can confirm that a 43-year-old man from Newmarket has been found.

Jason Urbanowicz was last seen leaving his home address in Green Road, Newmarket at around 5am on Tuesday 12th December. He was located in Kent by police officers today, Wednesday 13 December.

Police would like to thank media and public for their assistance with this appeal.

## Missing man - Newmarket - 12 December

Police are appealing for help in tracing a missing 43-year-old man from Newmarket.

Jason Urbanowicz was last seen leaving his home address in the town at around 5am this morning, Tuesday 12 December, and has not been seen since.

Jason is described as white, approximately 5ft 9ins in height and of broad build. He is bald, has a dark coloured trimmed beard and, when he was last seen, was wearing a white checked shirt, navy jeans and a grey cable knit jumper.

It is believed that Jason travelled to Kent in his red Volvo C30 three-door car, however he is also known to have connections in Cambridge.

Officers are concerned for his welfare and are asking anyone who has seen Jason or who has any information on where he may be to contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting missing person reference PID 129022.
