Police are appealing for witnesses or information following the theft of an electric bicycle and charger unit in Otley.

At some point on Saturday 26 December, a black Carrera Crossfire E-Mens electric bicycle and charger unit were taken from an outside building while it was being charged in Charity Lane.

The bicycle had a black metal carrier over the black back wheel and the electric control on the front handlebars had been re-attached with velco and black plastic ties. The saddle was also slightly worn.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/74729/20
