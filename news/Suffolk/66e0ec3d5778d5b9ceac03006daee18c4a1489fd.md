Police are appealing for information or witnesses following a burglary that took place in Beccles.

At around 12.30am on Wednesday 28 March an offender entered a property in George Brown Way and stole an Xbox and a handset for a landline phone.

The offender is believed to have entered the property through an insecure kitchen door. They were seen to leave the property via a rear garden fence. The offender was wearing a hooded top.

Officers are appealing for anybody who saw anything suspicious during this time or who has any relevant CCTV footage to contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 37/16842/18 or use the ‘report something’ link at http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/report-something. Alternatively, you can call the charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
