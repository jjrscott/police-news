Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Bury St Edmunds.

It took place on Baron Road in the town at some point between 7.45am and 7.35pm on Wednesday 19 December.

The offenders have forced entry by smashing rear door glass panel and conducting an untidy search in the bedrooms. At this stage it appears items of jewellery and cash have been stolen.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw an suspicious behaviour should contact Suffolk Constabulary on 101, quoting crime reference 37/73306/18

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
