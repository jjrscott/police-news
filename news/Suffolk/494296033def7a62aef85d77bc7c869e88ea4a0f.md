Police have arrested a man in Ipswich in connection with drug offences.

Officers on patrol had reason to stop a vehicle in the Nacton area of the town yesterday afternoon at just after 4pm (Wednesday 25 September) on Romney Road.

The driver was searched under the Misuse of Drugs Act and a bag containing brown powder was located on the male in his trousers.

He was later released under investigation pending further enquiries.
