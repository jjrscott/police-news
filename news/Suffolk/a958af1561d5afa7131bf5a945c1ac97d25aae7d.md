Police are appealing for witnesses or information following an incident of criminal damage in Lowestoft.

At approximately 8pm on Sunday 14 March, a glass bottle was thrown at a window of a property, causing it to smash in Westhall Road.

Officers would be interested to speak to two young females who were seen in Westhall Road shortly after the incident as they may be able to help with the investigation.

The females are both described as being white, young, and were both wearing black clothing. One had long hair and was wearing a puffer jacket.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw two females matching the description above, should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/12595/21
