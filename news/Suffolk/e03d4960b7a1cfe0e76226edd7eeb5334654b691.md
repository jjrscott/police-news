Police are appealing for information after an incident of criminal damage to a vehicle in Lowestoft.

The incident is believed to have taken place at some point overnight on Thursday 22 into Friday 23 June.

The vehicle, a Toyota Yaris, had been parked outside a property in Rotterdam Road when a wing mirror was vandalised with such force that the glass shattered.

Any witnesses or those with information should call the Lowestoft Response Team at Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 45454/17.
