Officers are appealing for witnesses or information following two burglaries.The first incident took place on Tuesday 6 June sometime between 8.30am and 2.30pm at a property on Chelsworth Road, Felixstowe. Suspect/s forced entry to a rear window and stole jewellery and cash from within, including a round gold ingot.

The second burglary took place sometime between 5.30pm on Sunday 4 June and 4.30pm on Tuesday 6 June at an address on Ely Road, Ipswich. Suspect/s forced entry at the rear of the property but nothing is reported to have been stolen.

Detectives investigating are not are not being linkin the incidents at this stage.

Witnesses or anybody who has any information are asked to call Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference 39862/17 (Felixstowe) or 39855/17 (Ipswich)
