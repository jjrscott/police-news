A man has been arrested following a fire at a property in Haverhill.

At around 2pm on Wednesday 30 January, police received reports of a fire within a house in Lulworth Drive. The fire caused significant damage to the building but no one was injured as result of the incident.

A 54-year-old man was arrested a short time later on suspicion of arson and he was taken to Bury St Edmunds Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

The man has subsequently been released on bail and must return to police on Monday 25 February.

Police are appealing for any witnesses who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously or entering the property in Lulworth Drive to call Suffolk police on 101, quoting reference 37/5986/19.

Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
