Police are appealing for witnesses following an accosting incident in Lowestoft.

The incident occurred at approximately 7.45pm on Friday 3 April in Hollingsworth Road, when an unknown male in a black Mazda car approached a woman in her 20s.

The male continued to ask the victim to get into the car until a passer-by, another woman, walked her home.

The male suspect is described as being in his 30s and had a tanned complexion. He was driving an ‘06’ plate black Mazda and had with him a food delivery bag.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it should contact Suffolk police quoting crime reference: 37/19609/20
