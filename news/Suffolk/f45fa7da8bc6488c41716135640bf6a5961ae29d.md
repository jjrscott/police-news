Police are appealing for help to trace two men wanted by officers in Lowestoft.

36 year old Karl Taylor and 18 year old Bob (or Bobby) Price are wanted on recall to prison for burglary offences and are thought to be together in the Lowestoft area.

Karl Taylor is described as white, about six foot tall, of heavy build and has brown hair. Bobby Price is described as white, about five foot seven inches tall, of thin build, and has black hair and brown eyes.

If members of the public see them they should not approach them but call police immediately.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111.
