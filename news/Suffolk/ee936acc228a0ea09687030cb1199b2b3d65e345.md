A 13 year old girl from Lowestoft who was reported as missing last night has been found.

Shannon Wilson was last seen at 3pm on Sunday 24 February and reported to police as missing at about 11.30pm.

She has returned to her home address in Lowestoft late this morning (25 February).

Police would like to thanks the public and media for their help with this matter
