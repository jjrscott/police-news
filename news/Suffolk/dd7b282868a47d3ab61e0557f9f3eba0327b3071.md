Police are issuing CCTV images of a woman they would like to speak to in connection with an incident of theft in Ipswich.

The incident occurred between 3.55am and 4am on Saturday 7 March at Perfect Kebab House in Falcon Street, when the business owner used his set of keys to allow any remaining customers to leave before locking the door to close. As he turned away for a brief moment, the keys had been removed from the door.

Officers would like to speak to the woman pictured in the CCTV images about this incident as she may be able to help with the investigation.

Anyone who believes they recognise the woman pictured in the CCTV images, or who has any information about this incident, is asked to contact PC 179 Ives, Response Team 4 at Landmark House, quoting crime reference: 37/14398/20
