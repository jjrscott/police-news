A 90-year-old pedestrian who died following a road traffic collision in Woodbridge is being named by police.

The incident occurred just before midday on Monday 30 July at the junction of St John’s Hill and Castle Street, where a pedestrian was involved in a collision with a grey BMW 120 Sport.

Peter Grimer, of Victoria Road, Woodbridge, was taken to Ipswich Hospital for treatment, but sadly died following complications arising from his injuries on Wednesday 8 August. Peter was the widower of Margaret Grimer and known to his family as ‘Frog’.

Anyone with information or dash cam footage of the incident should contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team by calling 101 and quoting CAD 164 of 30 July.

## Previous release | 17 August 2018

A man has died following a road traffic collision in Woodbridge.

It happened on the junction of St John’s Hill and Castle Street on Monday 30 July just before midday.

The collision took place between a grey BMW 120 Sport and a pedestrian.

The pedestrian, a man in his 90s, was taken to hospital but sadly died from his injuries on Wednesday 8 August.

Anyone with information or dash cam footage of the incident should contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team by calling 101 quoting CAD 164 of 30 July, 2018.
