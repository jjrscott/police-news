Police are releasing two CCTV images of a man officers would like to trace following a theft from a business premises in Lowestoft.

During the morning of Wednesday 7 June it is reported that a man entered a shop on Westwood Avenue in the town and stole a bottle of champagne and some packets of meat.

Police are keen to speak to the man pictured in connection with the incident.

Anyone with information about who he is should call Suffolk Police on 101, quoting crime number 40230/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
