Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Lowestoft.

At some point between 10pm and 11.15pm on Monday 11 January, unknown suspect/s forced entry into a garden of a property in Wissett Way. Once inside, a green and black mini dirt bike KMXR 125 was stolen from an unlocked shed.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/1852/21
