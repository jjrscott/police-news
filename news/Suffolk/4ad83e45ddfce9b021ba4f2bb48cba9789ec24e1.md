Police are appealing for information after an incident of arson.

Fire crews were called to a gorse and undergrowth fire at Corton Road, Lowestoft, at around 3pm on Tuesday 15 May. It was dealt with and not thought to be suspicious but around half an hour after leaving the scene they were called back to another gorse fire within 50ft of the original incident.

Fire officers believe the second fire is unlikely to have been as a result of the original fire.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge about this incident should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime number 26535/18. Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
