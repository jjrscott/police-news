Police are appealing for information after an incident of criminal damage in Lowestoft.

At approximately 7:30am on Thursday 14 April, a man was reportedly seen damaging the rear door and front window of a white Volkswagon Polo parked in The Avenue, near to the Miller Close junction.

He was challenged by a member of the public before running off.

The suspect is described as white, of thin build and was wearing a dark hoody.

Witnesses or anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time or who has any information are asked to contact the Neighbourhood Response Team in Lowestoft on **101** quoting reference 28849/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111** or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
