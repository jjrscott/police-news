A 24-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a drug offence in Lowestoft.

Officers from the Operation Sentinel East team attended an address yesterday morning, Monday 28 September as part of an ongoing operation.

A search was carried out and five kilograms of cannabis was recovered from within.

Lewis Whitehouse, 24, of Pakefield Road, was arrested and was taken to Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

He was subsequently charged with possession with intent to supply Class B drugs.
