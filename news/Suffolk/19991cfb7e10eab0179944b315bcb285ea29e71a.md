A 58-year-old woman has been charged with seven counts of theft after an investigation by Suffolk Police.

Susan Ogden, of Cambridge Road in Felixstowe, a former carer, has been charged with seven counts relating to the theft of jewellery and/ or prescription medication from seven separate victims.

The charges include stealing 21 items of jewellery valued at up to £5,000 from one woman between 3 May and 26 May 2016, taking three rings valued at £3,200 from another woman between May and August 2016, stealing jewellery to the value of £2,500 from another woman between February and April 2017, stealing a £150 ring from another woman between September and November 2015, taking jewellery and prescription medication worth £100 from another woman, stealing a £50 watch from a sixth woman and stealing prescription medication from a seventh.

Susan Ogden has been bailed to appear at Ipswich Magistrates Court from 9.40am on 24 July.
