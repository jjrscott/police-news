Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Bury St Edmunds.

At some point between Wednesday 23 November at 12.30pm and Friday 25 November at 1pm, offenders broke into a residential property on Horsecroft Road and stole several thousand pounds in cash, along with some jewellery.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area at this time.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference number 37/68250/16.
