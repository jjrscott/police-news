A 46-year-old man has been charged in connection with a burglary in Hundon.

The offence took place sometime between 22nd and 28th November 2018 at a residential premises in Hundon. A credit card was reported stolen.

Darryl Barfield, formerly of Strasbourg Square Haverhill was charged with the offence and appeared at Ipswich Magistrates Court via video link on Saturday 22 December.

Barfield is due to appear before Ipswich Crown Court on 18 January.
