Police are appealing for witnesses and information after the theft of a vehicle in Ipswich.

The incident occurred sometime between 6pm on Friday 19 May and 8pm on Saturday 20 May. The vehicle, a moped, was parked in a car park in Yeoman Close, near the Hadleigh Road Industrial Estate.

The stolen vehicle is a Longja LJ 125 T-7 moped, primarily red in colour with registration number AY12 DPZ.

Police would like to hear from anyone who believes they may have seen this vehicle or anyone with information about this crime.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference 36570/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
