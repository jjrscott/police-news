A man who went missing from Red Lodge has been found.

Scott Courtney-Wines from was reported missing to police on Wednesday 22 November.

He was found in the safe and well on Friday 24 November.

We would like to thanks the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | Friday 24 November

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 38-year-old man from Red Lodge.

Scott Courtney-Wines was last seen at his home address in the village at around 10pm on Wednesday 22 November and has not been seen since.

Scott is described as white, 6ft tall, of slim build and currently has a shaved head and stubble. He usually wears a hat or a hooded top with the hood up.
It is thought he may have travelled to central London.

Anyone who believes they have seen Scott, or who has any information regarding his whereabouts, is asked to contact the duty sergeant at Mildenhall Police Station on 101.
