A missing 46-year-old man from Ipswich has been found.

Robert John Paul was last seen at 3pm near Heath Road in the town on Monday 10 August and he was reported missing to police shortly after 4.35pm on Tuesday 11 August.

Robert was found at 9.40pm yesterday, Thursday 13 August.

Police would like to thank members of the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 11 August 2020

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 46-year-old man from Ipswich.

Robert John Paul was last seen yesterday, Monday 10 August, at 3pm near Heath Road in the town and he was reported missing to police shortly after 4.35pm.

He is described as white, 5ft 7ins tall, of medium build and with light brown hair. He was last seen wearing blue shorts, black Nike trainers and a blue and orange striped shirt.

Robert John is described as being vulnerable and enquiries are ongoing to locate him.

Officers are concerned for Robert John's welfare and are asking anyone who believes they have seen him, or who has any information regarding his whereabouts, to contact Suffolk police on 101
