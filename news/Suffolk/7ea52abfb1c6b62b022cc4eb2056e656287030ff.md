Police were called to reports of a burglary in progress at a property in Bredfield near Woodbridge at around 7.50pm last night, Thursday 9 February.

Officers attended the scene quickly and located a suspect nearby. Two other male suspects ran from the location.

The suspect - a 34-year-old man of no fixed abode from west Suffolk - was arrested in connection with the incident and taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning. He has been released on bail until 10 April pending further enquiries.

Anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time, or who has any information about this crime, are asked to call South East CID on 101 quoting reference 16860/17, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org
