A missing 14-year-old girl from Ipswich has been found.

Lily Stephenson had last been seen at around 7.45pm on Monday 16 November, but returned home safe and well shortly before 7.10pm yesterday, Tuesday 17 November.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
