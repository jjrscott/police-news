Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary that took place in Shotley Gate.

It happened on Saturday 24 February at property in Bristol Hill at some point between 4:20pm and 7pm.

The offender forced a bolted door and then smashed a kitchen window to gain entry. Various items were stolen, including jewellery, a Makita drill and a collection of World War 1 medals.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge of the burglary should contact Ipswich CID on 101 quoting crime number 37/10737/18 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
