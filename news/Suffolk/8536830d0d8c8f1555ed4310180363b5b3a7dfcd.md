Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Ipswich.

The incident occurred on Spring Road, near to the junction of Cauldwell Hall Road, sometime between 11pm on Saturday 10 and 8:30am on Sunday 11 September.

The offender(s) forced entry into the property through an open window and a quantity of cash was stolen from a handbag.

Anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area between the times stated or anyone with information should contact Ipswich CID via Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 54815/16. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
