Police are appealing for witnesses following an altercation on Undercliff Road West in Felixstowe earlier this month.

A 38-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of assault occasioning ABH following the incident that happened near the junction with Sea Road around 11.30am â€“ 11.45am on Saturday 13 August.

A 39-year-old man was walking along Undercliff Road West when he was approached by a man and an altercation ensued. The 39-year-old sustained grazes to his legs, shoulder and arm in the incident.

The 38-year-old has now been bailed to return to police on September 14, pending further enquiries.

Police need to speak to anyone who may have seen what happened.

If you can help please call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 49836/16.
