Police are issuing CCTV images of a woman they would like to speak to in connection with an incident of theft that took place in Lowestoft.

At approximately 7.30am on Friday 17 August, an 80-year-old woman left her handbag and its contents in a trolley after shopping in Asda in Horn Hill.

The bag was later found nearby but a purse and an amount of cash had been stolen from within.Officers are issuing CCTV images of a woman they would like to speak to in connection with the incident.

Anyone who recognises the individual in the CCTV stills should contact the Incident Crime Management Hub on 101, quoting crime reference 37/47668/18. Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
