Police are appealing for witnesses or information following the theft of a moped in Corton.

The incident occurred at some point overnight yesterday, Monday 12 October and this morning, Tuesday 13 October in The Street.

The black moped had been parked on a drive but it was later found abandoned in a field nearby with its front headlight cowl removed.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious at the time is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/59518/20
