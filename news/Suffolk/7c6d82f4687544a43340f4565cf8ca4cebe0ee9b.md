Following a robbery in Leiston yesterday, a man has now been charged.

Police were called at 11.40am yesterday, 22 November, to reports of a robbery at a business premises on the High St. Nobody is thought to have been injured in the incident.

Toby Alexander, a 27 year old man, of High Street, Leiston area was arrested nearby on suspicion of robbery and was taken to Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre where he was questioned by detectives.

Alexander has been charged with attempted robbery and threatening a person with a knife. He is due to appear before Norwich Magistrates today.
