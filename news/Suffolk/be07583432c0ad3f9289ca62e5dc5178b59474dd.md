Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of arson that took place near Bury St Edmunds.

It happened near to Mount Road between Thurston and Bury St Edmunds on Sunday 29 July when four large stacks containing 260 bales of straw each were set alight by unknown means resulting in the loss of about 460 tonnes in total. The cost of damage is estimated to be in the region of between £20,000 to £40,000.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge of the incident should contact Bury St Edmunds police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/42556/18. Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
