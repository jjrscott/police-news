Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident of arson in Newton.

The incident occurred at some point between 2pm and 3.45pm on Saturday 6 June within a derelict building on land off Rectory Road.

During the times stated, a sofa was set alight within the building and the fire then spread to the roof.

No one was hurt as a result of the incident.

Officers are exploring the possibility that this incident may be linked to similar incidents in the Sudbury area.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/31228/20
