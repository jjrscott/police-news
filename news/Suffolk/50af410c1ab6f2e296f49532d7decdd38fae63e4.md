Police were called at just after 9.30am today, Monday 19 February, to reports that the body of a man had been discovered in the sea near to the pier in Felixstowe.

Emergency services are currently at the scene and an investigation into the circumstances is underway. At this time the death is not being treated as suspicious.

A file will be prepared for the coroner in due course.
