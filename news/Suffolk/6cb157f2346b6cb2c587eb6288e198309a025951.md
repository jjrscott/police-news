Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of criminal damage in Lowestoft.

It took place at some point from Sunday 20 December at 1030pm and Monday 21 December at 530am outside a property on York Road.

The victim's motorbike was tampered with whilst parked in front of their house. The damage consisted of handlebars being out of shape and the steering lock being very tight. The victim believes this was in an attempt to steal the motorbike.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour or knows who was responsible, please contact police quoting crime reference 37/74118/20

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
