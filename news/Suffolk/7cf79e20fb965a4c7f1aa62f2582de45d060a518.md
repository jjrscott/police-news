Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary in Wenhaston.

It took place at Wenhaston Primary School on Hall Road between Tuesday 3 October at 6.40pm and Wednesday 4 October at 7.15am. Damage was caused to a school window to gain entry and a small red key safe was taken.

Some items of paperwork were found discarded on a nearby verge and footpath.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour between the times stated or has knowledge of the burglary should contact PC 1885 Rob Stockman at Halesworth police station quoting crime reference 37/64954/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
