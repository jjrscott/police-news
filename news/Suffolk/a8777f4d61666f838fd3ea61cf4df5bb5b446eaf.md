Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of theft in Lowestoft.

It took place at the O2 store on London Road North at about 2.35pm on Friday 13 April.

Two males walked into the store and forcefully took 4 i-phones off the display. A member of staff attempted to intervene and was assaulted and pushed to the floor. He sustained a scratch to his hand. The two offenders then left the store with the four phones.

The two suspects are described as white males - one is five foot nine inches tall, of stocky build and clean shaven. He wore a blue jacket, blue trousers with a white stripes on the side, had burgundy trainers on and a dark baseball cap. The second was also described as five foot nine tall, also of stocky build and had a black jacket, with blue trousers, white and blue trainers and a dark baseball cap with light peak and writing on.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it should contact Lowestoft police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/19879/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
