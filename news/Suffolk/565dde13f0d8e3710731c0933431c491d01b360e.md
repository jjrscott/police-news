Officers are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Aldeburgh on Saturday 21 April.

Police were called at just after 4.10pm to reports that a collision that taken place outside the Co-Operative Shop, High Street in the town involving a white Mercedes van and a cyclist.

The cyclist, a woman in her 60s, sustained a head injury and a broken right shoulder and was taken by ambulance to Ipswich hospital where she remains in a stable condition.

Witnesses or anybody who has any information are asked to call Police on 101 quoting reference 265 of 21 April.
