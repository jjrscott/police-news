Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Worlington.

The incident occurred at some point between 6.30pm and 10.30pm on Friday 21 December, at a property in the vicinity of Isleham Road and Freckenham Road.

Offender/s forced open a window and once inside, an untidy search was carried out. Various items were stolen including, an amount of cash, a 12 bore Lincoln shotgun, a 22rf bolt action rifle and ammunition, as well as CCTV equipment.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity or has any information about the crime should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/73739/18.

Detectives would also like to hear from anyone who may have any dash camera footage captured between the times of 6.30pm and 10.30pm in the vicinity of Isleham Road.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
