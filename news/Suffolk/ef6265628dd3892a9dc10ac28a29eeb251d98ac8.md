Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place at Sproughton.

It happened at a property on Broomfield Common on Saturday 24 February at some point during mid-morning. The resident left her property at around 8.20am and returned home at 10am to find her Panasonic laptop stolen from the lounge.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge of the incident should contact Ipswich CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/10687/18 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
