Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Snape.

At some point between 7.30pm on Monday 6 April and 11am on Tuesday 7 April unknown offender/s entered a property in Wadd Lane.

Once inside, a set of keys belonging to a yellow Volkswagen Caravelle were taken and the van was driven away from the drive.

Offender/s also forced entry into several outbuildings by cutting the padlocks. A number of other items were stolen from within including a Stihl leaf blower, a Husqvarna chainsaw, a Honda FG205DE garden tiller, a Honda generator, Paslode nailer IM350, Stihl steel cut-off saw TS410, Makita cordless multi-tool, other hand tools, driving glasses, a Sprayway gore-tex waterproof jacket, Zeiss binoculars 10 x 40 (BGAT DIALY), waterproof trousers and a toolbox containing hand tools.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/20148/20
