The family of a man who died following a fall from a balcony in Ipswich have paid tribute to him.

Police were called just after 2.35am on Wednesday 24 June, to reports that a man had fallen from the third-floor balcony of a premises in Duke Street. He sadly died at the scene of the incident.

Subject to confirmation at formal inquest proceedings, the deceased person has been identified as Gary Henderson, aged 64, from Upper Dovercourt in Essex.

We have been asked to issue the following tribute on behalf of Mr Henderson’s sister, Kim Henderson:

“My whole family are devastated by the sudden death of my brother in these unexplained circumstances.

“Gary was a gentle and very caring man who would always help anybody in need.

“I’ve received a lot of calls from people touched by his kindness and that’s how he will always be remembered.

“I appeal to anyone with information that could help police inquiries to come forward and speak to the case officers.”

The police investigation into Mr Henderson’s death continues.

Three people have been arrested as part of the inquiry – all on suspicion of murder – and were taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning. Their details are as follows:

\- a 26-year-old man from the Harwich area, who was released under investigation

\- a 24-year-old woman from Ipswich, who was released on police bail until Wednesday 22 July

\- a 27-year-old man from Ipswich, who was released on bail until Thursday 23 July

Any witnesses, or anyone with information about this incident, is asked to contact the Major Investigation Team, quoting reference: 34924/20.

The police investigation into the incident continues, as detectives work to establish the full facts of what took place in the moments prior to Mr Henderson’s death.
