Police are appealing for witnesses or information after a teenage man was seriously assaulted.

Police were called at approximately 9:50pm on Saturday 18 July to reports of a fight involving a group of men on Commodore Road in the Oulton Broad area of Lowestoft. A 19-year-old man had been punched in the face causing him to fall to the ground.

Officers arrived at the scene and located the victim unconscious on the ground near to the Wolf Inn on the approach to the Commodore public house. The man was taken to hospital having sustained a fractured skull and slight bleed on the brain for which he is still receiving medical treatment as a result.

Witnesses or anyone with any information should contact Lowestoft CID quoting crime reference 37/40706/20.
