Police were called to attend a premises in Iceni Way, Haverhill at 1.44pm yesterday, Thursday 22 November, following reports that an industrial incident had occurred.

A man, in his 40s, had suffered fatal injuries and had died at the scene. Next of kin have been informed. No one else was injured and there is no wider threat to the public.

Officers are continuing to investigate alongside colleagues from other organisations.

Emergency services are still at the scene.
