Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Ipswich.
It happened at a property on Fircroft Road at some point between 11pm and midnight on Monday 2 April. Entry was gained to a semi-detached dwelling by using an implement on a locked UPVC door. Whilst inside the suspects were disturbed by a sleeping resident on a sofa in a living room within an annex on the side of the main house and the suspects left. Nothing was stolen in the incident.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity or has knowledge of the incident should contact Ipswich CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/17929/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
