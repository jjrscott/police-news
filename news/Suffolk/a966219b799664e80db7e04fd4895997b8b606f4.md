Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Haverhill.

The incident occurred between 9pm on Friday 25 September and 7am on Saturday 26 September within a business on the Charter House Trading Estate in Sturmer Road.

At some point during the times stated, force was used to gain entry and a number of items were stolen from within including two Makita impact wrenches, batteries, a charger for the batteries and fireworks.

Anyone who saw any suspicious activity in the area should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/56106/20
