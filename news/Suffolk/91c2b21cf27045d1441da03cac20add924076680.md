A man in his 40s has been taken to hospital with serious injuries following a road traffic collision in Levington.

Police were called just after 7am this morning, Wednesday 4 January, to reports of a two-vehicle collision between a car and a JCB on the A14 eastbound, just after the Seven Hills interchange.

Fire and ambulance crews were also in attendance and the roof of the car had to be removed to free the driver. He has been taken to Ipswich Hospital having sustained serious injuries.

One lane of the A14 is currently closed and any witnesses are asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on **101** quoting reference CAD 44 of today, Wednesday 4 January.
