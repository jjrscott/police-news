Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Holton St Mary.

Jewellery, cash and iPads were stolen from a property in Hadleigh Road after entry was gained through an unsecured door at some time between 1pm and 3pm on Thursday 31 May.

Anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area between the times stated, or anyone with information, should contact Ipswich CID on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/29808/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.

For further crime prevention advice please visit the Suffolk Constabulary webpage www.suffolk.police.uk or contact your local Safer Neighbourhood Team on 101.
