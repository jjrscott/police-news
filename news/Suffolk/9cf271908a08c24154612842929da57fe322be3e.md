Police are appealing witnesses after a burglary from a business premises in Haverhill.

The burglary took place on Saturday 5 September at the Cambridge Road Retail Park at around 2:30pm. Two people, a man and a woman, gained access to the yard at rear of the B&Q shop and stole a bicycle and attempted to steal a motorcycle. The suspects then left the scene in a black BMW car and drove the wrong way round a one-way system.

The male suspect is described as 6 feet tall and was wearing a dark hooded top. The woman is described as having pink hair and was wearing jeans.

The bicycle is described as a grey coloured Carrera mountain bike.

Officers would like to hear from witnesses or anybody who may have captured any relevant dash-cam footage, to contact police quoting reference 37/51862/20
