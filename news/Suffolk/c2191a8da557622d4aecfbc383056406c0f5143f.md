Officers are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Clare earlier today, Wednesday 21 March.

Police were called at 00:40am to reports of offenders forcing entry to the Co-Op shop on Market Hill. The suspects used a sledge hammer and an axe to smash windows and then stole a quantity of alcohol and cigarettes from within.

The offenders are described as three men who then left the scene in what is believed to be a black Audi saloon car.

Witnesses or anybody who has any information are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 15237/18. Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
