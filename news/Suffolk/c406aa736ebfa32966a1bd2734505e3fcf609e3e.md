Police are appealing for witnesses following a daytime burglary in Ipswich.

The incident occurred between 11.30am and 4.15pm on Tuesday 21 November at an address in Heathercroft Road.

A patio door was forced open to gain entry to the property and an untidy search was conducted inside, but it is not believed anything was stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity in the area, or who has any information about this crime, is asked to contact South CID at Landmark House on 101 quoting reference 74941/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
