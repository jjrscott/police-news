Police are appealing for information following an incident of indecent exposure in Saxmundham.

The incident happened on Tuesday, 12 September, shortly after 1pm in Brook Farm Road.

Three young children were playing in a school field when they noticed a male teenager urinating up against a fence outside the grounds. The children challenged the teenager who then indecently exposed himself to them before making off along the public footpath.

The teenager is described as white, pale-skinned with blonde hair. He was wearing a white or pink T-shirt with grey shorts, and was using a flip-style mobile phone which may have been red or black.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have been in the area and the time stated and seen someone matching this description, or who may have information to assist the investigation.

Anyone with information should contact the Neighbourhood Response Team in Halesworth at Suffolk Police on 101, quoting reference 60335/17.
