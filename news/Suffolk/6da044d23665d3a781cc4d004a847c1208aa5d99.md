Police are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision in Bury St Edmunds.

At around 3.45pm on Monday 17 December, a cyclist - a 10-year-old boy - was knocked from his bike by a car as he cycled across a zebra crossing in Mount Road.

The driver of the car, possibly a black Volkswagen or a small hatchback, stopped to assist but they did not leave their details.

Officers would be interested to hear from anyone who saw the collision or has any dash cam footage to contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting CAD 362 of 17 December.
