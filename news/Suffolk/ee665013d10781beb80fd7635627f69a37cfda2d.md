Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Ipswich.

The incident occurred between 4am and 4.30am on Sunday 11 November in Portman Road.

The victim - a man aged in 20s - was walking home when as he crossed the road, an unknown offender punched him in the face, causing him to fall to the floor. As a result of the assault, he sustained a fractured jaw bone.

Officers would be keen to speak to a man and woman who were witnessed on the junction of Portman Road and Crescent Road at around the time of the incident as they may be able to help with the investigation.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information should contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting reference 37/65405/18 or the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
