Police are appealing for witnesses and information following two incidents involving motor vehicles in Lowestoft.

The first incident occurred between 11pm on Wednesday 16 December and 5.55am on Thursday 17 December in Christmas Lane.

At some point overnight, a Mercedes A45 was entered by unknown means and a number of items were removed from within including clothing, a backpack, a number plate and a utility belt. The items were later found discarded nearby.

A further incident occurred during the same night and in the same lane.

At some point during the times stated, an unknown person gained entry to a Ford Fiesta, which was parked in driveway and a search was carried out. It isn’t known if anything was stolen.

Anyone with any information about these incidents or who may have seen anything suspicious is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting reference numbers: 37/72876/20 and 37/72877/20
