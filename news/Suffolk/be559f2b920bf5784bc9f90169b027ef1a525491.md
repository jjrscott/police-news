Police are appealing for witnesses or information following the theft of a bronze statue in Nettlestead.

The incident occurred at some point between 4pm on Thursday 7 January and 4.15pm on Friday 8 January, in the garden of a property in the village.

The solid bronze statue, which was overlooking a pond and featured and male and a female in an embrace, is described as being approximately 4ft tall.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/1347/21
