A 34-year-old man who had failed to return to Hollesley Bay prison following a period of home leave has been found.

Webster Mwenso was released on temporary licence to go to an address in Dartford, Kent yesterday, Sunday 11 March, and was due to return by 3pm today, Monday 12 March. He failed to return to the prison and so was reported as missing to police.

He has now been located in London after making contact with the prison and is understood to have a valid reason for not returning at the designated time.

## Previous release | 12 March 2018

Police are appealing for help to trace a Hollesley Bay prisoner who has failed to return following a period of home leave.

Webster Mwenso, aged 34-years-old, was released on temporary licence to go to an address in Dartford, Kent yesterday, Sunday 11 March, and was due to return by 3pm today, Monday 12 March. However, he has failed to return to the prison and so has been reported missing to police.

Anyone who may have seen Mwenso, or who has any information regarding his whereabouts, is asked to contact Suffolk Police on 101.
