Police are appealing for information following a robbery in Lowestoft last week.

On Monday 5 October, at approximately 5.45pm, a teenager was outside McDonalds on London Road North in a group when he was approached by two offenders.

One of the offenders forcefully grabbed his pedal bicycle and punched him in the face before leaving the scene in the direction of Bevan Street East.

The bicycle is described as black, white and teal in colour and is a Calibre Boss Nut model.

The two offenders are described as being 15 to 16 years old, around 5 feet 8 inches tall, and of skinny build. One was wearing a long-sleeved black tracksuit and one was wearing a long-sleeved blue tracksuit, both with hoods up and Covid masks.

Officers are investigating and would like to hear from any members of the public who may have witnessed this incident. Anyone with CCTV or dash cam footage or anyone who may have seen this bicycle or been offered a bicycle for sale matching this description is asked to contact Suffolk Police.

Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to contact Lowestoft CID quoting reference number 37/58043/20.
