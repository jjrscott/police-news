Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward following an incident of indecent exposure.

It happened at approximately noon on Wednesday 22 August, within the public toilets in The Guineas Shopping Centre.

It is reported that a male peered over a cubical and carried out a lewd act.

The offender is described as a white male, aged in his mid-20s, 5ft 10in tall, with a shaved head. He was wearing a white t-shirt and navy shorts.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting reference 37/48008/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
