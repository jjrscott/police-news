Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of burglary that took place in Ipswich.

It happened at a property on Larchcroft Road at some point between Monday 2 April at 11pm and Tuesday 3 April at 6.50am.

The suspect (s) entered via a bedroom window and conducted a tidy search with three keys to a grey Citroen Berlingo (Reg: NG13VNO) stolen along with the vehicle from the driveway at the side of the house. The vehicle contained specialist window cleaning equipment inside it. A wallet and handbag were also stolen in the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who saw any suspicious behaviour during the times stated should contact Ipswich CID on 101 or contact the charity Crimstoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
