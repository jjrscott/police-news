A 17 year old teenager has been arrested and charged with drug offences in Ipswich.

The male from Ipswich was arrested on Saturday 25 August on Bulwer Road in the town on suspicion of intent to supply a class A drug.

He was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning and subsequently charged with possession with intent to supply heroin, possession of cannabis and four counts of a breach of a criminal behaviour order.

He appeared before Ipswich Magistrates court on Monday 27 August where he was remanded to appear before the youth court on Thursday 30 August.
