A 34-year-old man reported missing from Aldeburgh has been found.

Robert Lye was last seen around 5.30am yesterday Wednesday 25 July in Linden Road.

Robert has been found safe and well this morning, Thursday 26 July.

Police would like to thank the public and media for their help in this matter.
