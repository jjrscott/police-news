Police have a man in connection after a distraction burglary that took place in Lowestoft.

It occurred at a property on Kimberley Road on Wednesday 23 January.

A male attended a home offering gardening work to the resident and subsequently stole money from a tin in the property.

35 year old Stephen Smith, from Romany Lane in Lowestoft was arrested on suspicion of burglary on Friday 1 February and taken to Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre for questioning. He was subsequently charged with burglary and possession of cannabis on Saturday 2 February.

He was remanded to appear before Ipswich magistrates court this morning (Monday 4 February).

A 25 year old woman was also arrested on 2 February in connection with the same incident on suspicion of burglary and subsequently released under investigation.
