Police are appealing for witnesses or information after the theft of a valuable lectern from a church in Newton Green, near Sudbury.

Sometime between 15th and 22nd March offender/s entered the All Saints Church, Church Road and stole a seventeenth century carved wooded lectern. The theft has recently been reported to police.

Witnesses, or anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time or has information regarding the whereabouts of the lectern, or who has been offered such an item for sale, are asked to contact police quoting reference 38786/20
