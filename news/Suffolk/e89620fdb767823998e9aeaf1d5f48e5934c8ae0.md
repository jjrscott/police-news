A 48-year-old man has been charged in connection with an incident in Pinewood on the outskirts of Ipswich at the beginning of this week.

Police were called just after 11am on Monday 1 January to reports that a man was causing damage to property with an axe at an address in Skylark Lane.

A number of officers attended the location, including armed units and a National Police Air Service helicopter, and a man was later arrested after being contained within the property and taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

## Previous release | 1 January 2018 1.25pm

A man has been arrested following an incident in Ipswich today.

Armed officers and the police helicopter attended Skylark Lane in the town, following reports of a man causing damage to property with an axe.

The man, believed to be in his 40s, was contained within a property and after a short while was arrested on suspicion of affray and taken into custody.
