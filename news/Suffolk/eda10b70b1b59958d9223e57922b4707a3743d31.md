A 56-year-old man has been charged in connection with a burglary and an assault following an incident in Felixstowe at the weekend.

The crime occurred at around 4.30am on Saturday 8 October at an address in Maidstone Road, where the residents discovered a man asleep in their living room.

When they woke him up he assaulted one of the victims and was found to be in possession of items of jewellery belonging to the victims.

Thomas Money, of no fixed abode, has been charged with burglary and assault by beating in relation to this incident.
