Police can confirm that the death of a man in Ipswich is being treated as not suspicious.

Officers were called at 5.50am this morning, Thursday 23 July, by the East of England Ambulance Service after a man was found unresponsive in Carolbrook Road near to the entrance of Ellenbrook Open Space by a member of the public.

Despite the efforts of a member of the public and paramedics the man, thought to be aged in his 40s, was sadly declared dead at the scene.

Police will prepare a file for the coroner in relation to the death of the male, which remains unexplained but not suspicious.

A police cordon at the scene has now been lifted.

Police are re-appealing for anybody who may have been in the area to contact them quoting CAD 47 of 23 July via:

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
