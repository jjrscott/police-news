Police are appealing for information after an attempted burglary in Lowestoft.

The incident took place just after 8pm at a home address on Waters Avenue in Carlton Colville on Sunday, 11 November.

It is reported that a man attempted entry into the garage at the property but left empty handed. It is thought he may have been disturbed at seeing a light on and music playing inside the garage.

The suspect is described as approximately 5ft 8ins in height and was wearing a beanie-style hat, short jacket and tracksuit bottoms with white writing to the lower part of the legs.

Witnesses or anyone with information to help identify the suspect, should call the Lowestoft Neighbourhood Response Team on **101** , quoting reference: 71079/16. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
