Police are appealing for information following a fail-to-stop collision in Lowestoft on Wednesday where a man in his 60s sustained grazing to his leg.

The incident happened in Waveney Road around 12.05pm on Wednesday, 17 May. The man was walking across the pedestrian crossing when he was clipped by a silver car that was being driven by an older man.

The man in his 60s sustained grazes to the back of his leg but did not require hospital treatment.

Police are asking the man driving the silver car, anyone with information about who he is, or anyone who saw the incident to call officers at Lowestoft by dialling Suffolk Police on 101, quoting CAD 293 of 17 May.
