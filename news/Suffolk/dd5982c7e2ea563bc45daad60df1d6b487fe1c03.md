Police are appealing for witnesses to a road traffic collision in Hintlesham. It happened at about 11.30pm on Saturday 10 March on the A1071.

A silver Ford Focus, heading towards Hintlesham from Hadleigh and a blue Ford Focus, heading from Hintlesham to Hadleigh, were in collision with each other just prior to Hintlesham.

The female driver of the silver Ford Focus and who is from Hadleigh sustained head injuries and was taken to Ipswich hospital for treatment. The male passenger in this vehicle was taken to Colchester hospital for treatment to head and facial injuries. The male driver of the blue Ford Focus, also from Hadleigh, sustained a broken ankle and pelvis and was taken to Ipswich hospital for treatment. His female passenger sustained chest and pelvis injures and was taken to Addenbrookes hospital for treatment.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, saw the vehicles leading up to the collision or has relevant dash cam footage of the vehicles should contact Suffolk Roads Policing at Marltesham Heath on 101.
