Police were called to an address in Newmarket late last night to reports of a stabbing.

It took place on George Lambton Avenue at about 11.20pm (Sunday 27 January) when two men approached a female victim and one of them stabbed her.

Police believe the incident was targeted with no wider threat to the public. The female victim, aged in her late 20s, sustained none life-threatening injuries. She was taken to West Suffolk hospital for treatment where she remains in a stable condition.

Anyone who may have seen the incident should contact Bury St Edmunds CID on 101, quoting incident reference 37/5364/19.
Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
