Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Lowestoft.

It happened on Friday 23 February at a property on Yarmouth Road at some point between 5.15pm and 5.45pm.

The offender smashed a rear kitchen window to gain entry and various items were stolen, including a mobile phone, jewellery items, some cash and bank/credit cards.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge of the burglary should contact Lowestoft CID on 101 quoting crime number 37/10534/18 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
