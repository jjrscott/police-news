Police are appealing for witnesses or information after a digger was stolen in Sudbury.

At some point between 5pm on Monday 18 and 7:30am on Tuesday 19 February, unknown offender(s) stole a red 1-12 Ton Takeuchi digger with a white cab from a building site in Bulmer Road.

Anyone who may have witnessed suspicious activity during the times stated are asked to call the Incident and Crime Management Team at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 9891/19 or use the online crime reporting page on the Suffolk Police website.
