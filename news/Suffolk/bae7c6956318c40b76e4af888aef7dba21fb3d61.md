A 73-year-old man arrested following the sudden death of Beryl Mary Taylor at Rattlesden in July has been re-bailed to return to police in November.

Police were called to an address in Birds Green in Rattlesden around 12.30pm on Monday 11 July following the death of Mrs Taylor, 70, who was known as Mary.

The 73-year-old man was initially arrested on suspicion of assisting a suicide. He was taken to Bury St Edmunds Police Investigation Centre and was then further arrested on suspicion of murder. He was interviewed and was initially bailed to return to police on August 24, however he has been now re-bailed to return to police on 17 November, to allow further enquiries to be carried out.
