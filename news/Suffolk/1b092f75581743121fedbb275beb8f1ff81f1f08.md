Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident of theft in Newmarket.

It took place at McColl’s in St Mary’s Square at approximately 8.40pm on Saturday 12 May.

An offender entered the shop and stole an amount of cash from the till before running out.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it should contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/25903/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
