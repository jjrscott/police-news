Police are confirming a man has died following a house fire at Sedge Fen, near Lakenheath.

Officers were alerted by the Fire Service just after 8pm on Monday 13 February to reports of the fire.

Emergency services attended the scene but the man was found deceased inside the home.

Although formal identification is still to be completed, the body is thought to be that of a man in his 80s.

At this time, the fire is not believed to be suspicious.
