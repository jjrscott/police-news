Police have arrested and charged three people with robbery of a convenience store in Ipswich.

The incident took place yesterday afternoon (Tuesday 10 April) at just before 1pm when three masked males entered the Premier Stores on Garrick Way in the town and stole two bottles of whiskey.

25 year old Ryan Hutchinson of Kipling Road in Ipswich was charged with robbery and criminal damage. 26 year old Jack Clougherty-Gallagher of Thackeray Road in Ipswich and 27 year old Lee Seaman of Byron Road in Ipswich were both charged with robbery.

They due to appear before Ipswich Magistrates Court this morning (Wednesday 11 April)
