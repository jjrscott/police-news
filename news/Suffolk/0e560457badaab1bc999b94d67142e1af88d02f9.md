Police are appealing for burglary that took place in Woodbridge.

It took place on Thursday 13 December at about 4.15pm at a business called Chenevix Jewellery on Market Hill.

An unknown offender has used unknown means to smash two panes of glass at the front to the store before removing a number of items of jewellery from the display.

If you witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious activity immediately prior to or just after the burglary or have knowledge of the incidents should contact Ipswich police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/72071/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
