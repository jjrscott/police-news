Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Worlingham.

The incident occurred shortly before 3.30am this morning, Tuesday 14 May, at One Stop in Hillside Avenue.

The offender/s forced entry via a door and once inside, an amount of cigarettes were stolen from a cabinet behind the till area.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity should contact DC Dave Colbridge on 101, quoting crime reference 37/26966/19 or email [email protected]

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
