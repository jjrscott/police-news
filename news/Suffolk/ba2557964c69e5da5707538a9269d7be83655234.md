Police are appealing for witnesses after a burglary in Offton near Ipswich.

The incident took place on Friday 11 September sometime between 10:15am and 1:00pm at an address on Ipswich Road.

Entry was forced via the back door and an untidy search took place. A number of items of jewellery, including a gold watch were amongst items stolen. It is believed that the offender/s also stole a red pillowcase to take the items away.

Witnesses or anybody with any information are asked to contact police quoting reference 52966/20
