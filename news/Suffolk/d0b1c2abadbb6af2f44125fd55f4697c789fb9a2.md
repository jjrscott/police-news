A 19-year-old man from Lowestoft is due to appear in court today following a robbery in the town earlier this month.

Police were called at approximately 10:45pm on Sunday 8 October following reports a teenage boy had been approached by a male who had threatened him with violence and who had stolen his mobile phone in an alleyway off Clapham Road South.

Travis Aldred, of no fixed abode in Lowestoft, was arrested on Wednesday, 25 October, and has been charged with robbery.

He is due to appear before Norwich Magistrates Court today, Friday 27 October.
