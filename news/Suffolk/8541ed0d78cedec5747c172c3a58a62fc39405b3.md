Two missing 16-year-old girls from Forest Heath have been found.

Ayonda Gasela from Red Lodge and Karma Stoneham from Mildenhall had both last been seen on Friday 1 February.

They were found safe and well in London yesterday evening, Tuesday 5 February.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with the appeal.
