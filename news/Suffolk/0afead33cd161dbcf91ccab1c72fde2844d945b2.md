A 56-year-old man from Ipswich who was reported missing, has returned home.

Alan Amos had last seen between 4am and 4.30am yesterday morning, Tuesday 29 December, when he left his home address to go to work.

Mr Amos returned home safely this afternoon, Wednesday 30 December.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
