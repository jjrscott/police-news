A 17-year-old boy has been charged in connection with a burglary at a home address in Haverhill.

The break-in took place in the early hours on Sunday 6 May at a property in Old Rope Walk.

At some point, a door window was smashed and a purse containing cards and an amount of cash was stolen.

The boy was arrested yesterday, Wednesday 16 May, in connection with this incident and was taken to Bury St Edmunds Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

He was subsequently charged with burglary and theft and remanded in custody.
