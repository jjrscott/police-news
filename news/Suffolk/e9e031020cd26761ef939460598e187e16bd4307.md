Police are appealing for witnesses to a road traffic collision which took place in Newmarket.

It happened at 3:35pm on Monday 2 July 2018 on Fred Archer Way. A black Ford Mondeo was travelling along Fred Archer Way towards the town centre when it was in collision with a male pedestrian. The pedestrian, aged in his late 50s, was then thrown into the side of a white stationary and unattended Ford transit van. The pedestrian sustained a fractured ankle and shattered knee.

Anyone with information, including dash cam footage, should contact the Roads Policing and Firearms Operations Unit by dialling 101, quoting incident number 307 of 2 July.
