Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Sapiston.

The incident occurred between 10.15am and 5pm yesterday, Monday 19 October at a property in the vicinity of Heath Road and Coney Weston Road.

At some point during the times stated, unknown suspect/s forced entry via a window. Once inside, a search was carried out and jewellery was stolen.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious at the time is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/60884/20
