Police are appealing for witnesses to an assault in Lowestoft.

It happened on Friday 23 October at about 7pm where a female victim, aged in her 20s was assaulted by having rocks thrown at her by four unknown suspects on Yarmouth Road near to the Methodist Church

One of the stones connected with her right cheek bone leaving a swollen red cheek. The suspects were described as male and all wearing black and wearing trainers.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour at the time or knows who was responsible should contact Lowestoft police, quoting 37/61746/20
