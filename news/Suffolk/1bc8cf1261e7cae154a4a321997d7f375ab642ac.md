Following consideration by the Audit Committee on Friday 23 September 2016 and external auditors, the Police and Crime Commissioner has approved his Statement of Accounts and the Annual Governance Statement 2016.

The Chief Constable has also approved his Statement of Accounts 2015-16 and the Annual Governance Statement 2016.

All of the documents are listed below:

The Police and Crime Commissioner for Suffolk - Statement of Accounts 2015-16 including Audit report

The Chief Constable of Suffolk Constabulary - Statement of Accounts 2015-16 including Audit report

Annual Governance Statement for the Police and Crime Commissioner for Suffolk 2016

Annual Governance Statement for the Chief Constable of Suffolk Constabulary 2016
