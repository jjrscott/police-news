Police can confirm that a missing 51-year-old from Newmarket has been found.

Patrick Pemberton, who was seen in the town on Saturday 13 June, was located earlier this afternoon, Thursday 18 June.

Officers would like to thank the media and members of the public for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 17 June 2020

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 51-year-old man from Newmarket

Patrick Pemberton was last seen at around 9pm on Saturday 13 June, in George Lambton Avenue, Newmarket. He was reported missing to police the following day.

Patrick is described as white, 5 feet 5 inches tall, clean shaven, with receding hair and wears glasses. Patrick is partially sighted and walks with a white stick. It is not known what he may have been wearing.
