Police can confirm that a missing 32-year-old man from Needham Market has now been located.

David Read was reported missing following a road collision in Church Road, Tostock near Bury St Edmunds which took place in the early hours of Sunday 3 June. He presented himself to police in Stowmarket yesterday, Tuesday 5 June. Officers will be speaking to the man regarding the circumstances of the collision in due course.

Police would like to thank the public and media for their help in this matter.
