Police are appealing for witnesses to a serious sexual assault that took place in Newmarket.

The incident took place at some point between Wednesday 24 March at 10.45pm and Thursday 25 March at 1am in the area of a skate park, off George Lambton Avenue in the town.

It is thought the victim, a male aged in his late 30s, may have been unconscious for a period of time following the assault before reporting it the police. However, the victim did not require any hospital treatment.

The victim is being supported by specialist trained officers as detectives have been undertaking enquiries.

Enquiries are on-going and anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious activity or knows who was responsible should contact police, quoting crime reference 37/14565/21.
