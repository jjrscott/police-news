Motorists are advised they may experience delays on Wednesday 17 February as police escort the transportation of an abnormal load through the county.

The 5m wide, 23m long and 4.6m high deck mould weighing 44,000kg will move from Oyster Yachts, Tunstead Road, Hoveton to Poole, Dorset at 11am.

 **The route will be as follows:**

Local Roads -Tunstead Rd - Horning Rd West - A1151 - A149 - B1152 - A1064 - A47 - A11 - A14 Cambs Border
