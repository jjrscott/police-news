Police are appealing for witnesses or information following an incident of suspected arson in Hoxne.

At just before midnight on Wednesday 6 September it was reported that an agricultural building situated on a track off Green Street (B1116) near Hoxne was alight.

The barn was almost totally destroyed as a result of the fire. Nobody was injured.

Officers are appealing for witnesses who may have seen anybody acting suspiciously in the vicinity at the time, or anybody who has any information to contact Suffolk Police on 101 reference 59341/17.
