Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Ipswich.

It took place between 3.50pm on Wednesday 27 January 2021 and 12midday on Thursday 28 January at a property on Heath Lane.

It is believed the offender(s) broke a window on a front door and gained access to the property. An untidy search was then conducted where a safe was broken into and an Indian gold necklace and bracelet were taken.

The offender(s) then exited through the bathroom window.

Anyone who saw any suspicious activity, has any knowledge of the incident or knows the whereabout sof the items stolen they should contact Ipswich CID, quoting 37/4560/21.

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Phone – Call 101

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
