Police are appealing for witnesses after a distraction theft in Ipswich.

The incident occurred yesterday (Monday 8 March) around 12.20pm.

The elderly victim, a lady in her 80s, was unpacking her shopping into the boot of their car at a supermarket on Felixstowe Road. The victim was approached by a male who started to talk to her. Another male then reached into her bag and stole a card holder containing cards. The males then ran away. The bank card was used at a nearby ATM.

One suspect is described as being is over 5ft tall, late 30s/early 40s, was well built, with light brown hair, brown eyes, and was wearing a white face mask.
The second male was approximately 23 to 24 years old.

Anyone who has any information about this theft are asked to contact Suffolk Police quoting crime reference 37/11403/21
