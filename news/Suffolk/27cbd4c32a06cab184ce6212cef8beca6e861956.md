Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place at a property in Great Waldingfield.

The incident took place on Tuesday 7 March at some point between midday and 3.15pm.

The offender(s) accessed the property via the dining room windows and made an untidy search of the premises with some items of silverware taken.

The resident was not present in the property, which is located on the edge of the village, at the time of the burglary.

Anyone with any information or saw any suspicious activity during the times stated should contact DC Barry Teare at Bury CID on **101** or call Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111**.
