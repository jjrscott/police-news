Officers are appealing for information following a burglary that took place in Hollesley.

Police were called at just after 2 am this morning to reports that a burglary had taken place at a business premises on The Street in Hollesley.

Entry to the property had been forced, a cabinet broken into and an unknown quantity of cigarettes stolen from within. It is understood that bottles of alcohol have also been taken.

Anybody who saw or heard anything suspicious in the area around this time or who has any information are asked to call Suffolk Police CID on 101, quoting reference 41725/17.
