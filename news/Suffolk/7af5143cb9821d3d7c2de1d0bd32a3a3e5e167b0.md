Police are appealing for witnesses or information after a number of wheelie bins were set alight in Bungay.

Three incidents occurred approximately between 7pm and 11.45pm on Sunday 6 December in Queens Road and a further wheelie bin was discovered alight shortly before 12.10am on Monday 7 December in Manor Road. Although, it is believed the fourth bin belonged to a property in Hillside Road West.

Officers attended the scene and are continuing to carry out enquiries in the area.

Anyone with information about these incidents is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/70726/20
