## Previous release | 27 June 2018

Police have made a further three arrests after a man was stabbed in Haverhill on Saturday 23 June.

The arrests were made earlier this morning (Wednesday 27 June) in the Notttinghamshire area with a 25 year old man, and two 21 year old men all arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm. They remain in police custody for questioning.

Officers were called to the incident which took place at around 6.10pm in the car park near to the entrance to Haverhill Rugby club on School Lane in the town. The victim, a man in his 30s, was with a friend in a white Vauxhall Astra car. Both men got out of the vehicle in the car park and were approached by a group of five or six males. An altercation is then thought to have taken place and one of the males stabbed the victim with a knife. Both the victim and his friend then got back into the car and drove it a short distance down School Lane before calling emergency services.

The victim sustained a single stab wound to the chest which punctured his lung and damaged his heart. Following surgery the victim is now in a stable condition in hospital at this time.

Two people arrested on Sunday 24 June - an 18-year-old man from Haverhill arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm with intent and a teenage girl, also from Haverhill, arrested on suspicion of assisting an offender, have now been released under investigation whilst police enquiries continue.

Witnesses or anybody with any information regarding the incident are asked to call police on 101, quoting reference 34609/18. Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.

Police have arrested two people after a man was stabbed in Haverhill yesterday, Saturday 23 June.

Officers were called to the incident which took place at around 6.10pm in the car park near to the entrance to Haverhill Rugby club on School Lane in the town. The victim, a man in his 30s, was with a friend in a white Vauxhall Astra car. Both men got out of the vehicle in the car park and were approached by a group of five or six males. An altercation is then thought to have taken place and one of the males stabbed the victim with a knife. Both the victim and his friend then got back into the car and drove it a short distance down School Lane before calling emergency services.
