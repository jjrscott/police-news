Police are appealing for information following the theft of a motorcycle in Mettingham.

On Tuesday 9 August, at some point between 8.25am and 9.10am, a motorcycle was stolen from the roadside near to the Watch House Hill and Vicarage Lane junction on the B1062.

The motorcycle is a black Honda CBR 900 with the number plate R218 GBR.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have any information as to the whereabouts of the vehicle or who may have seen the motorcycle removed from the location.

Any information to Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference number 37/49431/16.
