The arrest follows two incidents at the same property in Burton End between 4.45pm on Friday 10 July and 6.10am on Saturday 11 July.

At some point during the times stated, a front door and two locked bedrooms were forced and a number of items were stolen from within including various items of food and an amount of cash.
