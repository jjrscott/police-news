Following thefts from more than a dozen motor vehicles in Ipswich in the past few weeks, officers have arrested two men in connection with these incidents.

John Alderson aged 30 from Ipswich has been charged with two offences of thefts from cars and one offence of interfering with a motor vehicle.

Another man from Ipswich aged 27 has been bailed in connection with a number of thefts from vehicles.

Alderson appeared at Ipswich Magistrates court this morning, Friday 11 November. He pleaded guilty and was given a six month community order to wear an electronic tag and obey a curfew between 7pm and 7am. In addition he was ordered to pay Â£230 in fines and costs.

The other man has been bailed to return to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre on 21 December to allow further inquiries to be carried out.
