Police are appealing for information or witnesses following a theft and criminal damage in Haverhill.

The incident took place at the cemetery on Withersfield Road in the town at some point between Thursday 25 January and Friday 26 January. A grave was tampered with and a candelabra and small statue have been stolen.

Witnesses or anybody who has seen any suspicious activity in the vicinity of the cemetery are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101, reference 5260/18. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
