A man who died following a road traffic collision in Ipswich is being named by police.

Officers were called at around 10.25pm on Saturday 23 March to reports of a collision between a pedestrian and a silver Ford Fiesta in Star Lane, just past the junction with Foundation Street.

Ambulance personnel were also in attendance, but the pedestrian involved sadly died at the scene.

Pending formal Coronerâ€™s inquest proceedings, police have provisionally identified the deceased as Robert Wolski, aged 53.

Mr Wolski was a Polish national who was visiting the area from his home in Norway.

Any witnesses, or anyone driving in the area with a dashcam fitted in their vehicle, are asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Unit on 101 quoting reference CAD 451 of 23 March.

## Previous release | 24 March 2019

Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal road traffic collision in Ipswich.

Police were called at around 10.25pm last night, Saturday 23 March, to reports of a serious collision on Star Lane.

A Silver Ford Fiesta was travelling along Star Lane when it was involved in a collision with a pedestrian.

Emergency services were called to the location, however the pedestrian was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Witnesses or anybody who may have any relevant dash-cam footage are asked to call the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting reference 451 of 23 March 2019.
