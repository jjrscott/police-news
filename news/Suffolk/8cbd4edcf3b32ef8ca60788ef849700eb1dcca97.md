Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Ipswich.

The incident took place on Wednesday 20 June at a property in Onehouse Lane between 12.30pm and 12.45pm.

The victim left the address at 12.30pm and when she returned 15 minutes later discovered an intruder in the property who is believed to have forced a rear door.

The woman then left the property and ran to a neighbour to raise the alarm. It is not thought that anything was taken in the incident.

The suspect is described as a white male, aged 25-35, 5ft 9ins tall and with shoulder length light to medium brown hair. He was wearing a light coloured top and had a bandana with a diagonal pattern around the lower half of his face.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact Suffolk Police on 101, quoting reference 37/33925/18, or contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
