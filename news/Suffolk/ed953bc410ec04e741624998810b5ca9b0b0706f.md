Police are releasing two CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with an incident in Ipswich.

The incident took place between 11pm and 11.15pm on Sunday 22 November in Clarkson Street.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured in the CCTV images about this incident as he may be able to help with the investigation.

Anyone who believes they recognise the man pictured in the CCTV images, or who has any information about this incident, is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/67878/20.
