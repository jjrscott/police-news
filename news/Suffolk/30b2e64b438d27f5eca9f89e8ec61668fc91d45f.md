A 64-year-old woman who was involved in a road traffic collision in Beccles earlier this autumn has died.

The incident occurred at around 3.40pm on Tuesday 2 October in Gosford Road, where a green Citroen Xsara Picasso was involved in a collision with a mobility scooter.

The rider of the scooter was taken to the James Paget Hospital for treatment having sustained injuries including a broken hip, cracked vertebrae and cracked ribs.

She was discharged from hospital in November, but was readmitted on Tuesday 4 December after becoming unwell at home and sadly died later the same day.

It cannot be confirmed at this stage if her death is definitely as a result of the collision and police enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone who witnessed the collision is asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting reference CAD 214 of 2 October.
