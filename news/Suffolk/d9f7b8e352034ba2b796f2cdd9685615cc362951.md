Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary in Haverhill on Wednesday 5 December.

The property on Hamlet Road was broken into between 3pm and 10pm when the resident returned home and found that an untidy search had taken place of the rooms.

The suspects had entered by a bathroom window with cash taken from a bedside chest of drawers.

Police are keen to speak with anyone who may have seen or heard any suspicious activity in the areas mentioned during the times given. If you have any information,please contact Haverhill police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/70363/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
