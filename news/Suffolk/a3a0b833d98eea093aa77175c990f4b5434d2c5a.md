Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a commercial premises overnight in Haverhill.

Officers heard an alarm going off at Screwfix on Hollands Road in the town last night at about 10.30pm (Tuesday 4 July) and discovered that the premises had been broken into. A rear loading bay roller shutter had been damaged where a large hole had been cut in it.
