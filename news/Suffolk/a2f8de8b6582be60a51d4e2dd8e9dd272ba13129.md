Police are appealing for witnesses to an accosting that took place in Lowestoft.

It happened on Saturday 24 November at about 4pm on Snape Drive.

The female victim, an 18 year old teenager, was walking along Snape Drive when a white van drove slowly along the road towards her. An occupant of the vehicle then wound down the window and the two male occupants of the vehicle told her to get into the van.

She refused and the drivers didn't get out of the van. The van then drove off.

Anyone who has knowledge of the incident or has knowledge if it should contact Lowestoft police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/68429/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
