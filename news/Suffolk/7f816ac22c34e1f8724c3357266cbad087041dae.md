Police are issuing CCTV images of a male and a vehicle that officers want to trace in connection with a burglary at a property in Brampton in June.

The burglary happened between 5am and 6am on 21 June and the individual and the vehicle were captured on CCTV at the property at Station Road in Brampton around this time.

A 65-inch Samsung TV was stolen in the burglary and this was UV marked with postcode and other details on the back of the TV.

Anyone who may recognise the male or the vehicle, or who may have any information that can help, is asked to call Suffolk Police on **101** , or 01473 613500 if calling from outside the county, quoting crime number 39780/16.
