Police are appealing for witnesses after a man was assaulted in Lowestoft.

It happened on Tuesday 22 August at about 11.30pm. A man in his late 20s was walking down Oxford Road when he got into an altercation with four other men resulting in the lone male victim being assaulted.

He sustained bruising and swelling to the face and ribs.

Anyone who witnessed the assault or has knowledge of it should call PC Shane Mingay at Suffolk police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/57368/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
