Two men have been charged with going equipped and attempted theft following an incident at Martlesham Heath.

Police were called by a member of the public just after 11pm on Wednesday 13 July, who reported seeing two men acting suspiciously under a truck at premises in Gloster Road. The two were detained in the early hours of yesterday, Thursday 14, and taken into custody at Martlesham Police Investigation Centre.

Costel Parpala, 26, of no fixed abode, and Mihaita Bucar, 21, also of no fixed abode, have now been charged with attempting to steal a catalytic converter and with having articles for use in the course of, or in connection with theft, namely adapted tools to take off catalytic converters.

Both are due to appear at Ipswich Magistrates Court this morning, Friday 15 July.
