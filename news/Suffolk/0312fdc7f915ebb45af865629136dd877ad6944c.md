Officers are appealing for information following a burglary in Ipswich.

The incident took place on Pelican Close in the town sometime between 10am and 3.30pm on Wednesday 21 June.

Suspect/s entered the address and stole a black wallet and bank cards.

Anybody who witnessed anything suspicious at the time above or who has any information are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 43407/17.
