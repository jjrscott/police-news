A 41-year-old man has been sentenced in connection with a number of separate bicycle theft incidents in the town.

Robertas Jasinskis, 41, of Civic Drive, Ipswich, appeared at Ipswich Magistrates’ Court on Monday, 14 December, where he pleaded guilty to stealing six bicycles in the town between 26 August and 27 October. He also asked that seven other offences were taken into consideration.

Officers from the Operation Shere team carried out a number of enquiries and a man was arrested Wednesday 2 November.

The man was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning and Robertas Jasinskis was subsequently charged in connection with a bicycle theft incident on 27 October at Cardinal Park.

He had previously been charged in connection with five other bike theft incidents, all occurring in Ipswich.

Jasinskis was sentenced to 12 weeks' imprisonment, suspended for 12 months.

He was also ordered to pay a total of £400 in compensation.
