Detectives are appealing for information following a burglary at a home in Syleham where jewellery and cash were stolen.

The break-in happened at the home in Wingfield Road between 1pm on Monday 3 and 8pm on Tuesday 18 October, with entry being gained via the back of the property and a search being made.

Several hundred pounds in cash, around 200 Euros and jewellery items were taken. Police are asking anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area between these times or anyone with information about those responsible to call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 61531/16. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers, anonymously if required, on **0800 555 111**.
