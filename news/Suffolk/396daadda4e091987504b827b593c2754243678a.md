Two absconders from Hollesley Bay prison have been located.

22-year-old Eammon Kenevan and 39-year-old Darren Mellor were reported as missing yesterday after they were both out on temporary licence on a visit Ipswich.

Both were located in Stowmarket earlier this afternoon (11 August) and will now be returned to the prison system.

Police would like to thank the public and the media for their help with this matter.
