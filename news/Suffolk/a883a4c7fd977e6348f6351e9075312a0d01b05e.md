Two people have been charged in connection with drug offences in Newmarket, Bury St Edmunds and Mildenhall.

It follows warrants conducted in Newmarket and London on Wednesday 10 February. Two males were arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of drugs and were taken to a north London police station for questioning.

Rolls Samba, aged 26 and from Stamford Hill and Djo Muzembe, aged 23 from Barking were both subsequently charged with being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine and heroin.

Samba was remanded to appear at Thames Magistrates Court with Muzembe appearing at a later date.

Anyone who has information about drug use in their community is asked to contact their local policing team on 101 or alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
