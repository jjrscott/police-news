A man has contacted police in connection with an image released following an alleged assault in Bentley last month.

An appeal to trace the man was issued after a man claimed to have been pushed to the ground after encountering a group of four men riding trailbikes in woodland.

Following the appeal a man has contacted police and enquiries are progressing. We would like to thank you for your help with this appeal

## Previous release - 26/09/2016

Officers have issued a photograph of a man they would like to speak to following an alleged assault, which took place in woodland in Bentley last month.

At around 8:15pm on Wednesday 17 August, a man aged in his 70s encountered a group of four men riding trailbikes. He was pushed to the ground by one of the men and whilst no medical treatment was required, the victim was left shaken by the incident.

The man depicted in the photograph is described as white, with dark hair and of large build.

Anyone with information on the identity of this man should contact PC Robert Le-Mare at Southern Area Neighbourhood Response Team on **101** , quoting reference 50556/16.
