Police are appealing for witnesses and information following the theft of a motorcycle in Lowestoft.

The incident occurred between 6.50am and 3pm on Thursday 17 December in a car park outside Wilko in London Road North.

The black and white Lexmoto motorcycle was subsequently located the following day near Stanley Street and returned to the owner.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who may have seen the motorcycle being pushed through the town on Thursday 17 December between the times stated.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who may have seen anything suspicious is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting reference number: 37/73012/20
