Police are releasing CCTV images following a burglary in Ipswich.

On Saturday 15 December at around 5pm, offenders forced entry to a house on Goldsmith Road by breaking a window.

Offenders conducted an untidy search and a number of items of jewellery were taken, as well as some DVDs and a set of new curtains, still in their packaging.

Officers would be interested in hearing from anyone who may be able to identify the individual in the CCTV images.

Police would also like to hear from anyone who may have dash cam footage in the Goldsmith Road, Byron Road or Shakespeare Road area on Saturday 15 December between 4.45pm and 5.30pm.

Any information to Suffolk Police on 101, quoting reference number 37/72515/18.
