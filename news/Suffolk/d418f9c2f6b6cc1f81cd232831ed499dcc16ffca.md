Police are appealing for information after a burglary at a business in Stanton.

The crime occurred between 12 midday Saturday 19 December and Monday 21 December. Security equipment captured three or four suspects wearing dark clothing break into a business on Shepherds Grove Industrial Estate, using an angle grinder type tool to remove door hinges.

A Yamaha 700 Raptor quad bike registration FH60VFT, a red Westward S1300 ride on lawnmower, a SIP airmate 3HP/150-SRB air compressor and a quantity of Evergreen tyres were stolen. The vehicle used in this burglary by the offenders was possibly a white LDV Maxus van .

If you saw the vehicles being taken or have any information about where they are now, please contact Suffolk Police quoting crime reference 37/73725/20 via –
