Police are appealing for witnesses after a Sinnis Stealth motorcycle was stolen from an address in Lowestoft.

The incident occurred sometime between 10:30pm on Wednesday 3 and 7:30am on Thursday 4 May outside a property in Selby Street. The motorcycle had been locked using a heavy duty chain on the rear wheel, which was also taken.

The stolen vehicle is a Sinnis Stealth 125CC Qingqi motorcycle, registration number KX12GEY.

The motorcycle is black with a blue trim, and has distinctive gold suspension bars at the front. It is described as having a couple of tears on the leather seat.

Police would like to hear from anyone who believes they may have seen this vehicle or anyone with information about this crime. Calls should be made to East CID at Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference 32902/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
