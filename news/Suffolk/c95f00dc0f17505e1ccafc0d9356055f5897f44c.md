A missing 16-year-old girl from Saxmundham has been found.

Harriette Grant had last been seen on Wednesday 20 February by family at her home address.

She was found safe and well this morning, Saturday 23 February.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 22 February 2019

Police are appealing for help in tracing a missing 16 year old female from Saxmundham.

Harriette Grant was last seen on Wednesday 20 February 2019 by family at her home address.

Harriette is described as a white female, 5’6" tall, with shoulder length brown hair and of medium build. She is possibly wearing dark clothing and a coat.
Police are concerned for Harriette’s welfare and enquiries are ongoing to locate her. Anyone who has seen her or who has any information on where she may be to contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting missing person reference 129558.
