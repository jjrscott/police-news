Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Ipswich.

It happened at a property on Tolworth Road at some point overnight between 11.30pm on Sunday 22 July and 6.45am on Monday 23 July.

During the hours of darkness unknown suspect(s) entered the detached property by breaking the glass in rear patio door.

Whilst the occupants have been asleep suspect(s) have taken a men's wallet, i-pad and handbag from the ground floor before leaving via the same patio door. The handbag has subsequently been located in a garden of a neighbour with all its contents.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge of the incident should contact Ipswich CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/41196/18. Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
