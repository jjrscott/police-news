Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident of arson.

Officers were called by the fire service at around 3.45pm on Monday 26 November, following reports of a fire in a derelict building in College Street, Ipswich.

Road closures and diversions were put in place while fire crews dealt with the incident.

Police are investigating the incident and would like to hear from any witnesses, or anyone who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area at the time of the offence.

Anyone with information is asked to call Suffolk police on 101 quoting CAD reference 260 of 26 November.

Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
