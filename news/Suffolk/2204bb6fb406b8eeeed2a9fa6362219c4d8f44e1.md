Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Hemingstone.

The incident occurred between 10pm on Friday 13 November and 9.30am on Saturday 14 November at a property between Church Lane and Main Road.

At some point during the times stated, unknown offender/s forced entry into a secure garage and stole a KTM 250 orange motorbike (registration OU67DLV) from within.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who may have seen a motorbike matching this description for sale is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/66112/20
