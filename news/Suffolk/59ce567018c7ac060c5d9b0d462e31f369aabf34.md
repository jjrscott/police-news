Police have arrested a man following a burglary in Ipswich.

A B&B in Christchurch Street was broken into shortly before 5am today, Wednesday 12 December, and an amount of food was stolen.

A 44-year-old man was arrested earlier today on suspicion of burglary, for possession of a knife/bladed article and for possession of a Class B drug. He was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning where he currently remains.
