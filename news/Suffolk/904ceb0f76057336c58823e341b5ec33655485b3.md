Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of criminal damage in Lowestoft.

It happened on Monday 23 November at about 3.30pm in Eastwood Avenue where damage was sustained to a car.

A male on the bike rode out in front of her whilst she was driving her car forcing her to break. He subsequently made threats to her and caused damage to her car by hitting it with his bike’s handlebars.

A group of four other males were on bikes were also with the suspect.

The offender is described as white, five foot eight inches tall, was about 19 years old, and was wearing a black coat, a black baseball cap. He had blonde hair.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or know who was responsible should contact Lowestoft police, quoting crime reference 37/68037/20.
