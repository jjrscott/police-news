Police are appealing for witnesses to a fail to stop collision in Sudbury.

At approximately 1pm on Tuesday, 16 February, a black car collided with a parked black Ford Fiesta but failed to stop at the scene.

The incident occurred in Reynolds Way and police are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed or have knowledge of the incident to get in touch.

Anyone who has information should contact police quoting CAD 137 of the 16 February.
