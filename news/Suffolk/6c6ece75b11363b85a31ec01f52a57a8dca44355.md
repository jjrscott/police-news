A prisoner who absconded from a medical facility in Ipswich has been located.

32-year-old David Elsom, serving an indeterminate sentence for robbery and assaults with the intention to rob, was reported missing to police on Monday 17 September.

He was located in Ipswich earlier this afternoon (Friday 21 Sept) and has been arrested and taken into custody at Martlesham Police Investigation Centre.

Police would like to thank the public and the media for their help with this matter.
