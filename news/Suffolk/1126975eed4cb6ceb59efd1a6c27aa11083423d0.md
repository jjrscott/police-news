A man has been charged in connection with burglary and vehicle offences in Lakenheath.

The incidents all took place on 13 March in Lakenheath. It was reported to police that a man entered a house and an outbuilding on the High Street in the town , with the intention of stealing. On the same day a Peugeot 405 car and a Ford Transit van were interfered with in Lakenheath

Anthony Vittles of Peterhouse Close, Mildenhall, has been charged with two counts of attempted burglary and two counts of interfering with a motor vehicle. In addition, Vittles has been charged with possession of a class B drug (cannabis)

Vittles has been remanded in custody and is due to appear before Ipswich Magistrates Court on 28 March.
