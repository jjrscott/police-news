Motorists are advised they may experience delays on **Tuesday 23rd March from 12pm** , as police escort the transportation of a boat 4.8m wide, 4.6m high, 22m long, weighing 32,000kg.

The load will be escorted from Gaymers Way, North Walsham to Falmouth.

The route will be as follows: Local Roads – B1145 – A149 – B1152 – A1064 – A47 – A140 – A14 – A12 Essex Border

Norfolk Police escort to A12 Copdock

 **Delays can be expected along these routes.**
