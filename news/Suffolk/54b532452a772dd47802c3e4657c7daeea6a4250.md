Police are appealing for witnesses or information following the theft of a car in Sudbourne near Orford.

At some point between 1pm on Wednesday 15 April and 7am on Thursday 16 April, unknown offender/s stole a blue Kia Sportage, registration LA18 YKP from a property in Snape Road.

Entry was also gained into a garage and a set of exercise weights were removed but were later found discarded nearby.

Police are reminding residents to review the security of their sheds, garages and outbuildings following a number of burglaries in the county over recent weeks.

For crime prevention advice, head to: https://www.suffolk.police.uk/sites/suffolk/files/shedandgaragesecurity1.pdf

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/21465/20
