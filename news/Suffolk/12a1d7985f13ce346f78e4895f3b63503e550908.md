A Home Office post mortem examination carried out on Wednesday 26 September on the body of a man in his 50s has proved inconclusive with further tests needing to be conducted.

The man died in hospital on Saturday 22 September after emergency services were called to London Road North in Lowestoft on Friday evening (21 September) to reports that a man had been found on a bench.

An investigation is being carried out and the death is still being treated as unexplained, but is not believed to be suspicious.

Anybody who has any information which could assist the investigation is asked to call police on 101 quoting incident number 36/59140/18.
