Police are appealing for information or witnesses following a burglary that took place in Little Waldingfield.

Sometime between Thursday 15 March and Friday 23 March offender/s forced entry to a property on The Street (B1115) between Little Waldingfield and the junction with Lavenham Road (B1071) and stole items of jewelry.

Officers are appealing for anybody who saw anything suspicious during this time or who has any relevant dash-cam footage to contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 37/15870/18 use the ‘report something’ link at http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/report-something. Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
