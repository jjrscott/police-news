Police investigating recent burglaries at business premises in Woodbridge have arrested a woman in connection with four of the incidents.

Three of the burglaries occurred at premises in The Thoroughfare overnight between Friday 12 January and Saturday 13 January, with the fourth break-in taking place in Tide Mill Way overnight between Wednesday 17 January and Thursday 18 January, with over Â£250 in cash stolen as a result of these crimes.

Officers have subsequently arrested a 22-year-old woman from Woodbridge on suspicion of burglary in connection with these offences. She was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning and has been released under investigation pending further enquires.

Anyone with information about these burglaries is asked to contact Woodbridge Safer Neighbourhood Team on 101 quoting reference 2396/18, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
