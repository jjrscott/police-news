Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a home address in Ipswich.

The break-in occurred between 11am and 4pm on Tuesday 23 January at a property in Wherstead Road.

At some point between the times stated, offender(s) forced open a door before carrying out a tidy search. Nothing is believed to have been stolen.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any knowledge of it is asked to contact Ipswich CID on 101 quoting reference 4371/18 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
