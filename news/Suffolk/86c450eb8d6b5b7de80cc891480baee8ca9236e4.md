Police are appealing for witnesses following a single vehicle collision in Bacton, near Stowmarket.

Officers were called shortly after midday on Tuesday 27 December to reports that a car had gone into a ditch in Cow Green, off the B1113.

The driver and passenger of the red Peugeot 206 sustained minor injuries and an investigation into the circumstances of the collision is underway.

Police are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed the collision or driving manner of the vehicle to contact Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting CAD 146 of 27 December.
