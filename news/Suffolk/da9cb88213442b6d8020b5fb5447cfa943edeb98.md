Police are appealing for witnesses following a robbery in Ipswich at the weekend.

On Saturday 17 October at approximately 5pm, a victim was walking on Hawthorn Drive with his friend when he was approached by an unknown male on a moped asking for directions.

The unknown male made threats to the victim and demanded his phone. Having taken the phone, the suspect made off from the scene in the direction of Birkfield Drive.

The suspect is described as a white male with curly ginger hair, around 16 or 17 years old and approximately 5 feet 9 or 5 feet 10 inches tall. He was riding a green and white moped.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious at the time is asked to contact the Police quoting reference number 37/60426/20.
