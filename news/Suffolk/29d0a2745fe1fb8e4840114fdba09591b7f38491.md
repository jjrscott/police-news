Officers are appealing for witnesses after a man was racially abused in Haverhill.

The incident took place on Monday 7 September at around 7pm just off the High Street.

In a loading area behind the row of businesses a youth who had been climbing on the walls was involved in a verbal altercation with the victim, a man in his 30s. The suspect used racially abusive language.

Witnesses or anybody who has any information are asked to contact police quoting reference 52173/20
