A missing 15-year-old girl from Ipswich has been found.

Josie Brennan had last been seen at 4am yesterday morning, Tuesday 19 June, but was found safe by police in London in the early hours of this morning, Wednesday 20 June.

Officers would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
