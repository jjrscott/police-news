Police are appealing for witnesses after a 23-year-old woman was subjected to racial abuse in Lowestoft.

The incident occurred during daylight hours on Thursday 1 February in the Clapham Road South area of the town.

The victim was walking along the road when another woman, who was known to her, is alleged to have shouted racially abusive remarks towards her.

Anyone who witnessed this incident is asked to contact Lowestoft Police Station on 101 quoting reference 6351/18, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
