Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of indecent exposure that took place in Ipswich.

It happened in Whitehouse Park on Norwich Road on Thursday 2 November at about 4pm when a man was witnessed committing a lewd act by two children.

The suspect was wearing grey tracksuit bottoms and a grey jumper.

Anyone who saw the incident or has any knowledge of it should contact Suffolk Police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/72796/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
