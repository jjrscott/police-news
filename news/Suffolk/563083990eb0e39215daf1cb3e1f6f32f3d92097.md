Detectives are investigating a burglary at a home in Stowmarket, where cash and jewellery were stolen.

The break in occurred at an address in Lockington Road, sometime between 9:15am and 9:50am, on Wednesday 21 December.

Entry was forced to the rear of the property and officers are keen to speak to anyone who may have been in the area and who may have seen or heard anything suspicious.

Police would also like to trace a man seen in the vicinity of the house at around 9:50am. He is described as white, in his 40s and was wearing a black baseball cap, black trousers and black jacket, which was just below waist-length.

Witnesses or anyone who may know the identify this man are asked to contact Bury St Edmunds CID by calling Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 72957/16, or call Crimestoppers, anonymously if required, on **0800 555 111**.
