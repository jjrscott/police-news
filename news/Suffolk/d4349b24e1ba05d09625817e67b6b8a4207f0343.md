A man has been charged in connection with a robbery that took place on Hadleigh Road, Ipswich. On 14 December at around 2.30pm a teenage boy walking along the road met a number of people who he began talking with. During this encounter the victim reported being struck and his iPhone and belt were stolen.

The man has also been charged in connection with a separate incident that took place in Whitehouse Park, Ipswich. A teenage boy walking through the park on Monday 12 December at around 8pm encountered a group of five or six people who began shouting at him. It is reported that one man from this group then assaulted the boy.

Morgan Smyth, aged 18 years of age of Spenser Road, Ipswich was arrested on 16 December and charged with robbery and assault. Smyth has also been charged with using threatening/abusive words/behaviour, criminal damage and resisting a police officer in the execution of his duty.

Smyth has been remanded and is due to appear at Ipswich Crown Court on 16 January 2017.
