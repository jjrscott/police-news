Officers are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary at a business in Ipswich.

The incident took place at a Co-Op Daily shop on Handford Road in the town at around 2.45am on Friday 16 March. Offender/s forced entry by smashing a rear window. However, the security alarm was triggered and the suspect/s left the scene without entering the property. Nothing was stolen.

Anybody who witnessed the burglary or who saw anything suspicious at the time above are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 14306/18. Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
