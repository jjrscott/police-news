Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of indecent exposure in Tuddenham in Ipswich

It happened on Fynn Lane on Monday 12 November at about 4pm.

A female in her 30s was walking her dog and was near Tuddenham Fountain when she heard a whistle. She looked around and saw an unknown male in the distance, who had exposed himself. She then left the scene. The offender is described as in his mid 20s to mid 30s, had dark hair and was wearing a red or maroon coloured top and jeans.

Anyone, who witnessed with incident or saw any suspicious activity during the times stated should contact Ipswich CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/65725/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
