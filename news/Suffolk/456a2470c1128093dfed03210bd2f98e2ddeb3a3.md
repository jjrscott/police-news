Officers are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Mildenhall

Sometime between 6pm on Sunday 12 August and 11.40am on Monday 13 August, offender/s entered the house on Macpherson Robertson Way in the town and stole a handbag. The bag is described a brown leather and contained a purse and cash.

Witnesses or anybody with information or who saw anything suspicious at the time is asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 46063/18.

Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or through their online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
