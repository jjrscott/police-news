Police are appealing for information after a burglary in Hadleigh.

It took place on Pond Hall Road on Wednesday 14 March between 2.30pm and 5.20pm.

The suspect went to the rear of the property, forced a rear ground floor window to the kitchen and stole a silver MacPro laptop.

Detectives would like to hear from anyone who was in the area during this time who may have seen someone acting suspiciously in the area or carrying a laptop.

Any information to Ipswich CID via 101 quoting crime reference number 37/14049/18. or alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
