Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Ipswich.

The incident occurred at around 2.50am yesterday, Wednesday 5 June, at a property in Foxhall Road.

The offender/s forced a door and once inside, a handbag containing car keys, house keys, a pair of sunglasses and other miscellaneous items were stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity should contact T/DC Nick Cook on 101, quoting crime reference 37/31991/19 or email [email protected]

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
