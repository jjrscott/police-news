Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of theft from a motor vehicle that took place in Stowmarket.

At about 7.35pm on Creeting Road East from Monday 19 March a secured vehicle, a Fiat van, has been entered by unknown means and a variety of tools have been stolen from the vehicle. Just prior to the incident a silver Vauxhall Vectra was seen to pull alongside the van with an occupant of the vehicle seen to interfere with the Fiat van.

Anyone who witnessed the theft, saw the Vauxhall Vectra prior to the incident or has knowledge of the incident should contact Stowmarket police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/15094/18 or alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
