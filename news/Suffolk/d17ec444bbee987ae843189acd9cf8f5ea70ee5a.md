Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Bury St Edmunds.

At some point between Tuesday 14 February at 10.30pm and Wednesday 15 February at 5pm, offenders gained entry to a property on Hardwick Park Gardens by unknown means and stole a quantity of cash.

Police would like to speak to anyone who may have heard or seen anything suspicious in the area at the time of the offence.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting crime reference number 37/18243/17.
