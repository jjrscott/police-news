Police are appealing for witnesses following the theft of a motorcycle in Bury St Edmunds last week.

On Tuesday 9 February, at some point between 3.30pm and 7.30pm, offenders stole a motorbike that was parked outside a property on Corsbie Close.

The bike is described as a black Honda Ruckus 49cc motorbike.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious at the time is asked to contact the Police quoting reference number 37/6772/21.
