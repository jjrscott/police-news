Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident where a dog jumped up at a child in West Stow.

The incident happened on Wednesday 3 March at about 9.30am in King’s Forest. A dog jumped up at a six year old child and bit them – no injury was caused.

The down owner is described as being aged in her 40s, about five foot five inches tall, with blonde or light brown hair in pony tail. She had two dogs with her and described as being 'scruffy' and mottled brown in colour.

Enquiries are on-going and anyone with information should contact Mildenhall police, quoting crime reference 37/10698/21

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
