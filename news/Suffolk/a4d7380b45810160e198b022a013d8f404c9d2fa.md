Police are appealing for witnesses and information following incidents of criminal damage to vehicles in Ipswich over the weekend

At least four vehicles parked in Back Hamlet sustained deliberate scratches to the paintwork and it is thought the damage is likely to have occurred at some point between 5pm on Saturday 3 and 8am on Sunday 4 June.

Motorists whose vehicles were damaged on the same night and who haven’t already reported it are urged to get in touch.

If you think you know the person or persons responsible, or if you or heard anything suspicion in the area between the relevant times, please call Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference 39506/17. Alternatively, this can be reported online via the ‘Report a Crime’ page.
