Police have arrested three males in connection with several theft, fraud and burglary offences.

Officers conducted a warrant at a property at about 7am yesterday morning (Thursday 5 November) in the Whitehouse area.

The offences all relate to criminal activity in the south east area of the county including Woodbridge area.

A search of a premises at the location also saw officers recover around £5,000 in cash and as well as over 50 power tools.

A 33 year old male, 57 year old male and a 27 year old male were all arrested on suspicion of fraud by false representation, interference of a motor vehicle, two counts of theft and theft of a motor vehicle.

The three males, were taken to Martlesham Police investigation Centre for questioning. All three have been released on police bail to answer on 3 December 2020.

Anyone who recognises any of the serial numbers or markings in the attached images of some of the items recovered is asked to contact Ipswich police, quoting crime number 37/52353/20
