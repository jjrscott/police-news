Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Sudbury.

It happened on Thursday 7 June at some point between 1.45pm and 3.45pm at a property on Melford Road.

A stone garden ornament was thrown through the rear window pane of a bedroom at a bungalow before the suspect(s) entered the property. They made an untidy search of a bedroom stealing a quantity of jewellery, including rings and necklaces, before they left the property.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or has any knowledge of the burglary should contact Bury St Edmunds CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/31179/18 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
