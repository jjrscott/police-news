A 19-year-old man from Ipswich has been charged with a burglary which occurred in Barham at the beginning of this week.

The incident occurred at some time between 1.45pm and 9.50pm on Monday 22 January at a house in The Crescent.

A rear patio door was smashed to gain entry to the property and a number of jewellery boxes were stolen from within.

Vasile-Gabriel Calici, of Yeoman Close, was arrested in connection with this incident at 12.30am yesterday, Tuesday 23 January, after officers out on patrol had cause to stop a vehicle in Shakespeare Road, Ipswich.

He was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning and has subsequently been charged with the following three offences: burglary; driving without insurance; and driving otherwise than in accordance with a licence.
