Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision in Freston on Saturday.

The incident occurred at around 2pm on Saturday 20 October, when a cyclist was involved in a collision with a silver Volkswagen Polo on the B1456.

The cyclist, who was taken by ambulance to Ipswich Hospital, sustained leg, face and a possible head injury as a result of the collision.

Any witnesses to this collision are asked to contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting reference CAD 262 of 20 October. Alternatively, email [email protected]
