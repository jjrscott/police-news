Police are appealing for be vigilant after a distraction theft that took place in Woodbridge.

It happened on Wednesday 16 December at about 4pm in a business premises on Market Hill in the town.

A woman entered the office and distracted staff by dropped her handbag on the desk before retrieving it and in the process scooping up a mobile phone which she took when she left the office.

Anyone who thinks they have experienced a similar distraction theft should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/73124/20.

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
