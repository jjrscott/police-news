Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in the village of Cockfield, near Bury St Edmunds.

The burglary took place between 5.30pm and 6pm on Monday 10 December. A home in Windsor Green was entered by forcing the glass in rear door. An untidy search has taken place in the upstairs rooms. Jewellery and car keys were stolen the home, and a red BMW 320I edition M sport BMW was stolen from the driveway. The vehicle has since been found elsewhere in the county.

Were you driving in this area of the A134 between Bury St Edmunds and Sudbury between these times and have any dashcam footage? If you have any information about this burglary and theft of this vehicle, please contact Western Area CID on 101 quoting reference 37/71440/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
