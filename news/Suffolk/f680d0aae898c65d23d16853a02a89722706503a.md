Police are asking anyone who was in the area of St Mary’s Church in Haverhill High Street in the early hours of Friday 26 May to come forward after a man reported being assaulted and threatened.

The man, in his 20s, told police he had been in the churchyard between 12.15am and 12.30am when a man – described as black, around 5ft 8ins tall, of medium muscular build, of similar age, with a silver stud in his eyebrow, and wearing dark clothing – approached him, grabbed him and threatened him.

The suspect then let him go. Nothing was stolen.

Police are asking anyone who may have seen any incident in the area around this time or anyone with information about the man involved to call west Suffolk CID by dialling Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 37443/17.
