Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident in Reydon where a man approached a teenager.

It happened on Wednesday 12 December at about 6.40am on Wangford Road when a child aged 16 walking to work whilst still dark became aware of a man walking behind her.

She quickened her pace and the male copied her. The suspect then disappeared for a brief time before reappearing again causing victim to be fearful and run. It is not known where the male went after this. The teenager was unhurt.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw an suspicious behaviour should contact Suffolk Constabulary on 101, quoting crime reference 37/72448/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
