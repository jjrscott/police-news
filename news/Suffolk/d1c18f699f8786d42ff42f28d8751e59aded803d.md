Police are appealing for witnesses to a fail to stop road traffic collision that took place in Woodbridge.

It happened on Wednesday 7 November at about 5.30pm on the Quayside at the junction with Hamblin Road.

A male pedestrian, in his late 50s, was in collision with a possible silver 4 x 4 vehicle that failed to stop at the scene The victim sustained a fractured pelvis and was taken to hospital for treatment.

Officers are appealing for any drivers with dash-cam footage of the incident to come forward in an attempt to identify the suspect. Please call 101 and quote incident number CAD 296 on Wednesday 7 November.
