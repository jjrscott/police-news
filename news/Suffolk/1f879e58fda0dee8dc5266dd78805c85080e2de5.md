Police are appealing for witnesses following the theft of a mountain bike in Felixstowe.

The incident occurred at around 3.25pm on Friday 27 March in Wadgate Road, outside Felix Fish Bar and Grill.

The ‘BOSS’ mountain bike is described as having an orange and blue frame.

Anyone with information about this theft, or who believes they have seen a bike matching this description, is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting reference: 37/19677/20
