Police have arrested a man in connection with an allegation of exposure near to the seaside in Felixstowe.

Officers received a report of an incident which occurred at around 6.25am on Tuesday 20 October, in Undercliff Road West.

The victim states that as she was walking through the Felixstowe Seafront Gardens, she was passed by a man who was exposing himself.

Yesterday morning, Wednesday 21 October, officers investigating this allegation arrested a 40-year-old man from Felixstowe on suspicion of exposure.

He was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning and was subsequently released under investigation.

Any witnesses, or anyone with information about this incident, is asked to Felixstowe Safer Neighbourhood Team, quoting reference: 61029/20.
