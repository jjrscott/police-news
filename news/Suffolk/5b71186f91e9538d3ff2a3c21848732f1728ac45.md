Police are appealing for witnesses or information after a number of items of equipment were stolen from a combine harvester in Bardwell.

At some point between midnight and 6am on Friday 17 July, a John Deere controller screen, a satellite receiver and a moisture meter were removed from a John Deere combine harvester T560i after an unknown person forced entry into the cab while it was left unattended on a farm near The Green.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/40372/20
