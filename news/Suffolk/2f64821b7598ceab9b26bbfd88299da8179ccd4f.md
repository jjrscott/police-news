Police investigating a burglary in Hollesley have arrested three people.

Shortly before midday yesterday, Thursday 15 June, a vehicle was stopped in Belstead Road, Ipswich, and a quantity of cigarettes and alcohol was seized.

The driver, a 30 year-old-woman from Ipswich was arrested on suspicion of taking a motor vehicle without consent, burglary and handling stolen goods. A 29-year-old man from Sutton was arrested on suspicion of burglary and handing stolen goods, and a 32-year-old woman from Eyke was arrested on suspicion of handing stolen goods.

All three were taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning and have now been released under investigation pending further enquiries.
