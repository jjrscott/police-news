Police are appealing for witnesses after a reported assault in Lowestoft.

The incident took place on Friday 11 September at 8:30am in the Boston Road area of the town. Police received reports of two men in their 30’s arguing in the street and it is believed that one man assaulted the other. Nobody was reported injured as a result.

Witnesses or anybody with information are asked to contact Lowestoft Police quoting reference 52929/20
