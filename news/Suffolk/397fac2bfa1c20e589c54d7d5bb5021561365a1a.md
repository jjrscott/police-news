Officers are appealing for information or witnesses following a fail to stop collision in Newmarket.

Police were called on Thursday 7 June at around 10.30pm to reports of a collision involving a cyclist and a car/vehicle near to the Moulton Paddocks. The woman cyclist was travelling on the B1506 towards Newmarket and was hit from behind by a vehicle that failed to stop. The victim sustained minor injuries which required hospital treatment.

Anybody who witnessed the incident or who has dash cam footage are asked to call Police on 101 quoting reference CAD 537 of 7 June.
