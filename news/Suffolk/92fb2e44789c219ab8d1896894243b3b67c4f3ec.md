Police can confirm that a missing 20-year-old man from Ipswich has been found.

Daniel Hare was located in the town late this afternoon, Friday 15 February.

Officers would like to thank the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 15 February 2019 - 1.25pm

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing person who has gone missing from Ipswich.

Daniel Hare, aged 20, was last seen at 5.20pm yesterday (Thursday 14 February).

He is described as white, of a slim build, six foot tall, with brown hair, and was believed to be wearing a large, dark coloured puffer jacket with the hood up and possible wearing a grey woolly hat when last seen.

He frequents Ipswich and Felixstowe and in-between locations, especially on foot.

Enquiries are on-going to locate Daniel and anyone who has seen him, knows of his whereabouts or has seen a person matching the description given should contact Suffolk Constabulary on 101.
