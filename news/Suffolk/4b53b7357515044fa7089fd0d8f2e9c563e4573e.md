Police are appealing for witnesses or information after four flashing beacons were stolen from work vans in Lowestoft.

Entry was gained to a yard at Waveney Norse in Rotterdam Road, at some point between 1pm on Monday 20 April and 5am on Tuesday 21 April.

The four flashing amber beacons were stolen from the tops of Citroen vans and are described as being approximately two and half to three foot in length.

Anyone with information about this incident should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/22296/20
