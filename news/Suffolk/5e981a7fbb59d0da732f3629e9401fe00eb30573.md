Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary in Worlingworth.

The incident occurred at some point between Monday 21 December and Tuesday 22 December at Worlingworth Community Centre in Shop Street.

At some point during the dates stated, unknown suspect/s forced entry into a store room in the community centre and once inside, a ride on lawn mower was stolen.

Police would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed the lawn mower being pushed across the field and then taken away in a vehicle.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who may have seen anything suspicious is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting reference number: 37/74103/20
