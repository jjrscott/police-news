A male who police want to speak to in connection with an incident of voyeurism in Ipswich has been identified following a CCTV appeal.

Officers received a report from a security guard stating he saw a male using a go-pro camera to view under the skirts of woman in a shopping centre on Tavern Street in Ipswich. When the guard approached the male he ran off leaving behind the ‘go-pro’ camera he was using.

The male pictured in the CCTV image has now been identified and will be interviewed by officers in due course.

## Previous release | October 10 2017

Police are appealing for help to identify a man following a reported case of voyeurism.

Officers received a report from a security guard stating he saw a male using a go-pro camera to view under the skirts of woman in a shopping centre on Tavern Street in Ipswich.

When the guard approached the male he ran off leaving behind the ‘go-pro’ camera he was using.

Police are carrying out enquiries into the incident and anyone who recognises the male in the attached images should contact PC Shane Hughes at Ipswich on 101 quoting crime reference 37/ 47740/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
