Police are releasing CCTV images of two men they would like to speak to in connection with an incident of assault in Felixstowe.

At around 3:30pm on Saturday 5 May, police received reports of an altercation which took place outside a food kiosk in Sea Road.

A suspect is alleged to have shouted before assaulting a person outside the kiosk. Another victim was later assaulted in Platters Road by the same suspect.

A second victim was also verbally assaulted and pushed in the chest by another suspect a short time later outside the kiosk.

Anyone who recognises the individuals in these images should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime reference number 34438/18.

Alternatively, contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
