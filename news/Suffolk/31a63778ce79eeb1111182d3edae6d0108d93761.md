Police are appealing for witnesses after a single vehicle collision in Felixstowe.

Officers were called at approximately 12:25pm on Wednesday 7 June to reports that a container lorry had overturned on the eastbound A14, just after the Dock Spur roundabout.

The road was blocked for almost three and a half hours as emergency services attended to the scene, particularly affecting traffic heading towards the Port of Felixstowe. The driver, a man in his 40s, sustained cuts and bruises and was taken to hospital by ambulance.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the incident or who saw the manner of driving of the lorry beforehand.

Anyone with any information should contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team at Suffolk Police on **101** quoting CAD reference 174 of Wednesday 7 June.
