Police are urging residents to remain vigilant to any suspicious persons or activity, following the theft of a large planter pot in Mendlesham.

The incident occurred at some point overnight on Sunday 21 March at an address in Ducksen Road.

~The pot is pictured, and is described as grey which contained lavender seedlings.

Police are urging anyone who may have information regarding this incident, or who may have bought one from someone online, to contact Suffolk Police quoting reference 37/14239/21 via

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
