Police are appealing for information after a quantity of jewellery was stolen in a burglary at a home in Beccles.

The burglary happened at an address in the Meadow Gardens between 11am on Friday 28 April and midnight on Saturday 29 April.

The home was broken into and searched. Among the items taken was a pink Chanel handbag and items of jewellery including a Rolex watch and various other items of jewellery of high value.

Police are investigating the break-in and are keen to hear from anyone who may have noticed any suspicious behaviour in the area on Friday 28, or anyone who may have been offered items of jewellery for sale since.

If you saw anything out of the ordinary in the vicinity around this time or if you have any information about who stole the items or where they are now, please contact the East CID Team by dialling Suffolk Police on 101, quoting crime number 31799/17.
