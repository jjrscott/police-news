Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Martlesham.

The incident took place at some point during the day on Monday 25 June at a property in Farriers Close.

An unknown suspect is believed to have entered via an insecure first floor window and stolen a quantity of jewellery.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact Suffolk Police on 101, quoting reference 37/35202/18, or contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
