Police have made four arrests following a burglary in Ipswich.

The break-in occurred at a property in Hervey Street at some point between 10am and 4pm on Sunday, 22 October.

The following day, Monday 23 October, officers arrested a 32-year-old man, two 18-year-old men and a 17-year-old girl – all from Ipswich – in connection with the incident and all were taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

The 32-year-old man and 17-year-old girl have since been released under investigation pending further enquiries, and both 18-year-old men have been bailed to return to police on Monday 20 November.

Anyone with information relating to the burglary is urged to contact Ipswich CID at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 68899/17.
