Police are appealing for witnesses after a privately-owned skip was taken from a layby in Bramford.

The skip was removed without the owner’s permission from a layby on Loraine Way close to the Suffolk Waterpark on Monday 21 or Tuesday 22 September.

It is described as an HL5 model, green and yellow in colour with no branding.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious at the time is asked to contact the Police quoting reference number 37/55285/20.
