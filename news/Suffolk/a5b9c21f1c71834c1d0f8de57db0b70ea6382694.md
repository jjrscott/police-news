Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Hitcham near Wattisham.

It took place on Tuesday 7 November at some point between 9.25am and 3.15pm at a property on Water Run.

At some point during the times stated the offender(s) smashed a small window in the sitting room to gain access to the property. Various items were stolen including a small amount of cash, a Gucci bag and a Gucci watch.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious activity or has knowledge of the incident should contact Bury St Edmunds CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/72033/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
