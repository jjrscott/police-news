Police are releasing CCTV footage following criminal damage to vehicles in Lowestoft back in November.

At some point between Friday 17 November at 5pm and Sunday 19 November 11.30am, exterior damage was caused to vehicles parked on Clapham Road Central.

Police are issuing this footage having exhausted all other lines of enquiry.

Anyone who recognises the individual in the images should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/74842/17.
