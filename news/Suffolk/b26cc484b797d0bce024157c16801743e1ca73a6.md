Police are appealing for witness to incidents of thefts and attempted thefts of motorbikes in Lowestoft.

One incident took place on Saturday 27 March at about 3.30am on Cambridge Road where an attempt was made to steal a motorbike – a grey Javelin 125cc. It is thought three males were in the area dressed in dark clothing and on two motorbikes, with bolt croppers also seen. (crime number 37/15039/21)

Elsewhere, on Rose Court in Lowestoft, a grey Yamaha bike was stolen at some point between Saturday 27 March at 130am and 835am on Sunday 28 March where two people cut the security chain on the bike. (37/15120/21

In Kessingland at a property on Church Road a Suzuki Bandit was taken from an address in the early hours of Saturday 27 March but was located the same morning in the Cooper’s Drive area.

Police are keeping an open mind if they are linked and any witnesses or who saw anything suspicious should contact Lowestoft police, quoting the relevant crime number.

Owners of motorcycles are reminded of the following crime prevention security advice on the Suffolk police website:
