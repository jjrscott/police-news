A missing 16-year-old teenage boy from Stowmarket has been located.

Jamie Stevens was reported missing to police shortly after 10.15pm on Sunday 14 February but he was found yesterday evening, Monday 15 February.

Police would like to thank the media and the public for their help with this matter.

## Previous release | 15 February 2021

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 16-year-old boy from Stowmarket.

Jamie Stevens was last seen at around 2.45pm yesterday, Sunday 14 February and he was reported missing to police shortly after 10.15pm.

He is described as white, 5ft 8ins tall, of slim build and with brown hair and blue eyes. He was last seen wearing a black puffa jacket, with a dark hoody underneath, grey tracksuit bottoms, a black The North Face baseball cap and black Nike trainers. He was also carrying a bag.

Jamie has links to Bury St Edmunds and Newmarket and it is believed he could be in either town.
