Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Fornham St Martin.

The incident took place at some point between 12.30pm and 4pm yesterday, Tuesday 18 June, in Barton Hill. Offender/s forced entry via a side door and then searched the upstairs rooms.

A safe was forced open and a number of items were stolen including jewellery, watches and an amount of cash.

Officers are appealing for any witnesses who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously or any vehicles in the area of Barton Hill, Thetford Road or Russell Baron Road between 12.30pm and 4pm, yesterday.

Anyone with information or dash-cam footage should visit www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update quoting reference 37/35223/19 or call Suffolk police on 101. Witnesses can also contact DC Hughes by emailing [email protected]

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
