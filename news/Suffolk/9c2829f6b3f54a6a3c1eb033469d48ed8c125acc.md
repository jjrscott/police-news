Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Otley.

At some point between 3pm on Thursday 2 April and 5pm on Saturday 4 April, unknown offender/s forced entry into a locked garage within the village and once inside, a black and yellow Stia Estate 710 ride on lawn mower was stolen.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/19680/20
