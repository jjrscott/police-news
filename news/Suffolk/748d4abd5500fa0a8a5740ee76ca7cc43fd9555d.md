Police are releasing CCTV images of a two men officers would like to speak to following a theft from a vehicle in Coddenham.

During the early hours of Monday 13 February at an address on School Road two men stole a Sat Nav device and some CDs from within a car. On the same night a Vauxhall Zafira vehicle was stolen from an address also on School Road which was later recovered. In addition, police are investigating links between these incidents and three other reports of thefts from outbuildings and another attempt to interfere with a vehicle, all in Coddenham which took place around the same time.

Police are keen to speak to the men pictured in connection with these incidents.

Anyone with information about who the men are should call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 17577/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111** or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
