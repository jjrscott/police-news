Police are appealing for witnesses to a theft of a dinghy from Ramsholt.

It took place at some point between Tuesday 1 December and Tuesday 15 December when a Quicksilver dinghy model A-A270EA3N was taken Ramsholt Quay from the storage area of the quay

Anyone who witnessed the theft or has knowledge of the current whereabouts of the dinghy should contact Ipswich police, quoting crime number 37/73195/20.
