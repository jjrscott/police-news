Officers are appealing for information following a burglary at a rural address near Bury St Edmunds where cash was stolen.

The incident took place in the vicinity of the A143 Bury Road between Stanton and Ixworth at some point between 5pm and 8:15pm on Saturday 1 July. Offender(s) forced entry into the property, where a safe was broken into and a large sum of cash was stolen, including foreign currency.

Police would like to speak to anyone who may have heard or seen anything suspicious in the area at the times indicated above, or who may have information about those responsible.

If you can help, please contact Western Area CID at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 45526/17.
