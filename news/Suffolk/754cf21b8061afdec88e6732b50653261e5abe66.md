Police are appealing for witnesses following two thefts in Carlton Colville.

The first incident took place on Tuesday 3 April during the day. Offender/s stole a handbag from the skate park in Carlton Meadow Park. The handbag contained a car key for a Land Rover Evoque and a document that identified the victim's address, among other items.

The second incident occurred between 6:28pm and 11:31pm the following day, Wednesday 4 April. It is believed that offender/s used the key to steal a Land Rover Evoque from outside a property in Brook Close, Carlton Colville.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge about either of these incidents should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime number 18433/18. Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
