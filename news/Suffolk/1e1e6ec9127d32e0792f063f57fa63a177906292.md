Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision in Felixstowe.

Officers were called at approximately 3:20pm on Tuesday 21 November to reports of a collision involving a pedestrian and a motorcyclist at the junction of Orwell Road and Ranelagh Road.

The pedestrian, a woman in her thirties, sustained minor injuries to their arms and legs.

Police would like to trace two women who may have been in the area at the time of the incident. They may have vital information to assist the investigation.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or saw the manner of driving should call Felixstowe Safer Neighbourhood Team at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting CAD 247 of Tuesday 21 November.
