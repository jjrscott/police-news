Police are appealing for help to trace the driver of a vehicle and any witnesses following a fail-to-stop collision on the A137 at Tattingstone on Saturday 10 December.

Officers were contacted around 11.30pm on Saturday 10, following the incident which happened around 11.10pm, close to the turning to Belstead village.

A motorcyclist â€“ a man in his 30s - suffered a foot injury after a car pulled out of a junction and collided with the Honda motorbike. The car then drove off towards Manningtree, with the driver failing to stop or leave details.

Police are appealing for any witnesses to the incident and are asking the driver involved or anyone who may have information about the vehicle or the driver to come forward.

If you can help please call PC Ben Towns at Suffolk Police by dialing **101** and quoting **CAD 412** of December 10.
