Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Lowestoft.

It took place at some point between 9.45am and 10.40am on Monday 19 March at a property on Normanston Drive.

The offender has gained access to private rear garden of a property from a public park behind the premises. The offender then threw a stone through the glass pane of a large patio door and entered building. A brief search of property has taken place with a jewellery box containing items of jewellery stolen from a bedroom.

Anyone who saw the incident or any suspicious behaviour at around the times stated should phone Lowestoft CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/14961/18 or alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
