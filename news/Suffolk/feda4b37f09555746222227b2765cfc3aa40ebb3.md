Two teenagers arrested after a 28-year-old man was robbed on the Sir Bobby Robson Bridge at West End Road in Ipswich have now been bailed to return to police in September.

Police arrested the two on Sunday night on suspicion of robbery following the incident, which happened between 12midnight and 12.30am on Sunday morning, 24 July. The 16-year-old boy and 18-year-old man, both from Ipswich, were taken into custody at Martlesham and were interviewed by detectives. Both have now been bailed to return to police on September 8.

Officers are continuing to ask anyone who may have seen the robbery and assault of the 28-year-old to come forward. Anyone with information is asked to call Ipswich CID by dialling Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 46229/16.
