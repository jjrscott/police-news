A 49-year-old man has been charged in connection with an incident in Lowestoft on Friday.

On Friday 26 October at 11.40pm, police were called to High Street in Lowestoft following reports that a man had been stabbed in the chest. A man in his 40s was taken to hospital for treatment and remains in a stable condition.

A man was arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm and taken to Great Yarmouth police investigation centre for questioning.

Subsequently, James Bowler of Crown Street West in Lowestoft was charged with grievous bodily harm, assault and possession of an offensive weapon and was remanded in custody.
