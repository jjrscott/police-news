Police are appealing for witnesses and information after a woman reported being sexually assaulted in Ipswich.

The incident occurred at some point between 3am and 4am on Saturday 1 July.

The woman, in her teens, had been walking alone along Falcon Street when she was approached by a man asking for directions. He continued trying to engage in conversation as she walked in the direction of The Corn Exchange and Crown Street and is reported to have attempted to kiss her and put his hands inside her clothing.
