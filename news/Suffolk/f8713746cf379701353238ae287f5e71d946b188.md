***Following initial enquiries, some of the details of this appeal have been amended***

Police are appealing for witnesses after a vehicle failed to stop following a collision in Ipswich.

The incident occurred at 7.15pm on Monday 29 July, westbound on the A14, **just after the Orwell Bridge and adjacent to the off-slip to the A137 at Wherstead, J56.**

A **white** lorry was involved in a collision with a grey Seat Ibiza, **which resulted in the car mounting a grass verge of the slip road.**

The lorry, **which had a trailer attached** , failed to stop and drove away.

The driver of the seat Ibiza was left shaken and with minor injuries as a result of the collision.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision or directly after it, or anyone who may have captured any dash-cam footage of the vehicles.

Anyone with any information should contact the Roads and Armed Policing Team at by quoting CAD 403 of 29 July.

## Previous release | 8 August 2019

Police are appealing for witnesses after a vehicle failed to stop following a collision in Ipswich.

The incident occurred shortly before 7.15pm on Monday 29 July, westbound, close to the Orwell Bridge and the A137 turn off for Manningtree and Tattingstone.

A red lorry was involved in a collision with a grey Seat Ibiza, which resulted in the car mounting a kerb and subsequently colliding with a tree.

The lorry, which had no trailer attached, failed to stop and drove away.

The driver of the seat Ibiza was left shaken and with minor injuries as a result of the collision.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision or directly after it, or anyone who may have captured any dash-cam footage of the vehicles.
