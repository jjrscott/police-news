Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of criminal damage to a motor vehicle in Beccles.

It happened at some point overnight at Providence Place from Monday 19 February into Tuesday 20 February.

An offender damaged the paint work of a black BMW X3 by using sharp item to scratch paint.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or saw any suspicious behaviour should contact Lowestoft police station on 101, quoting crime reference 37/9911/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
