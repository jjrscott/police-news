Police are appealing for witnesses after a 14-year-old girl was approached by a man in Trimley St Mary last week.

The incident occurred at around 4pm on Friday 13 November in High Road, near to the junction with Gaymers Lane.

The girl was walking along when a man approached her and tried to engage her in conversation, before making inappropriate comments towards her.

He is described as white, aged in his mid-40s to early 50s, approximately 5ft 10in tall, of heavy build and bald. He was wearing a red t-shirt and dark-coloured jeans.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact the Crime Coordination Centre, quoting reference: 67391/20.
