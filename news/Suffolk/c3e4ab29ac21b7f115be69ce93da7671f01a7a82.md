Police are appealing for help to identify a man officers want to speak to in connection with a sexual assault in Exning.

The incident happened on Friday 22 June on Oxford Street at about 1.20pm when the male victim, aged in his early 20s, was approached by suspect who indicated he needed help.

Whilst walking with the male, the suspect touched him in appropriately. The victim was unhurt and then fled the scene.

Anyone who recognises the male depicted in the stills or has knowledge of the incident should contact Mildenhall police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/34409/18. Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
