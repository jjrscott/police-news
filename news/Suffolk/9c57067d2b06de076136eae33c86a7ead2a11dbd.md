A 21-year-old man has been jailed for 22 months at Ipswich Crown Court today, Tuesday 12 December.

Meshack Christopher of Millard Terrace, Heathway, Dagenham pleaded guilty to being concerned in the supply of class A drugs, namely crack cocaine and heroin.

Following a misuse of drugs warrant which was executed by the Scorpion Team in Haverhill on 19 May 2016, Meschack Christopher was arrested and subsequently charged in connection with the possession and supply of class A drugs.
