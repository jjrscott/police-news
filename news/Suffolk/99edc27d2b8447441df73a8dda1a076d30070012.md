Police were called today, Tuesday 14 November, at around 1.50pm, to reports that a man had been assaulted at an address in Foundation Street in the town.

The man, thought to be in his fifties, is understood to have sustained cuts to his back and his head. The victim has been taken to hospital for treatment but his injuries are not believed to be serious.

A scene is now on at the address whilst an investigation into the circumstances of the incident gets underway.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact South CID on 101 quoting reference CAD 203 of today, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
