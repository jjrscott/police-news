A man has been arrested in connection with a theft from a vehicle in Ipswich.

Overnight, between Thursday 11 October and Friday 12 October, in Back Hamlet, Ipswich, an insecure vehicle was entered and a wallet containing an amount of cash and a bag were stolen. Bank cards were then used at various locations.

A 37-year-old man was arrested on Wednesday 24 October on suspicion of theft from a vehicle.

He was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning and subsequently released under investigation.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact South CID at Landmark House in Ipswich on 101 quoting reference 59985/18, or the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
