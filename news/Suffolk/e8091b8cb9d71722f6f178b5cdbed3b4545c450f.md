Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Bramford.

It happened at a property on Ravens Lane at some point on Friday 20 October.

The female victim left the property secure and locked at 1pm before returning at 5pm to find that a search of the property had taken place with a large double pane of glass in the patio doors smashed. Various items of jewellery had been taken from a wooden box.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity in the location between 1pm and 5pm or has knowledge of the incident should contact DC Sally King at Ipswich CID on 101 quoting crime reference 37/68519/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
