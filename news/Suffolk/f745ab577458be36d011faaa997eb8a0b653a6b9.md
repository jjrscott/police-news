A woman has died following a serious road traffic collision in Elveden.

Police were called at 12.10am this morning, Friday 24 November, to reports of a single vehicle collision on the B1106.

A blue Honda Civic collided with a tree approximately a mile north of the Centre Parcs entrance. Ambulance and fire crews were also in attendance, but the driver – a woman aged in her 20s – sadly died at the scene.

The road was closed whilst a collision investigation took place and reopened at around 8am.

Any witnesses are asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting reference CAD 4 of 24 November.
