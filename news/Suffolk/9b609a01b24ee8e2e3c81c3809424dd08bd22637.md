A 38-year-old man is due to appear before magistrates this afternoon after being charged with three home burglaries in the Trimley area.

Sebastian Ochocinski, of Russell Road in Felixstowe, was arrested around 6.50am yesterday morning, Wednesday 21 December, following a burglary at a home in Stennetts Close in Trimley St Mary.

He was taken into custody at Martlesham where he was interviewed by detectives.

He was subsequently charged with three home burglaries;

The burglary at Stennetts Close in the early hours of Wednesday 21 where a handbag and contents, two iPads and an iPhone were stolen.

A burglary at a home in Langstons in Trimley St Mary between 11 and 12 December where a purse, wallet and electrical devices were stolen.

A burglary at Mill Close, Trimley St Martin, between 19-20 December where a mobile phone, two tablet computers and a wallet were taken.

He was also charged with possession of amphetamine and possession of cannabis.

He has been detained to appear before Ipswich Magistrates this afternoon, Thursday 22 December.
