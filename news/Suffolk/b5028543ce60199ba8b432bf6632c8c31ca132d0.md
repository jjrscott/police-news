Police are looking to speak to a male in connection with an incident of theft that took place in Woodbridge.

It happened on Tuesday 16 June at about 7.40pm at the Shell petrol station on Grove Road.

A male entered the premises and requested a pack of cigarettes and whilst staff member has turned to go to cigarette display the offender put his hand through gap of perspex screen and grabbed a handful of notes that had been taken from till for banking by staff member. the male paid for the cigarettes but leaves with the notes.

Anyone who recognises the male in the CCTV still attached should contact Woodbridge Safer Neighbourhood Team, quoting crime reference 37/33384/20.
