Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Poslingford near Clare.

The incident took place between 2pm and 4.30pm on Monday 16 July at a property in The Street.

An unknown offender forced a rear door to gain entry and made an untidy search.

A Gamo Phox air rifle and items of jewellery were stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious persons or who has any information about this crime is asked to contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting reference 39666/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
