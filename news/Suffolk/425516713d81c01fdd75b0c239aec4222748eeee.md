Police are appealing for witnesses after a sexual assault in Bury St Edmunds last week.

The incident occurred between 4.30pm and 5pm on Thursday 13 December in Angel Hill.

The victim, a woman aged in her 20s, was walking near to the Abbey Gate when a man approached her and grabbed her bottom.

The suspect is described as white, aged in his late 30s, around 5ft 9in tall, of skinny build and with short dark hair. He was wearing dark clothing and pushing a bicycle.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact Bury St Edmunds Safer Neighbourhood Team on 101 quoting reference 72171/18.
