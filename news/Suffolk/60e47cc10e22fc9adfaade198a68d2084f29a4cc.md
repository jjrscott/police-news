Police are appealing for witnesses following two burglaries and an incident of criminal damage that took place in Felixstowe.

Sometime between 5pm on Monday 20 March and 8pm on Tuesday 21 March, offender/s forced entry to a business property on Hamilton Road in the town. A window was smashed at the rear and a silver Acer laptop and a small amount of cash were stolen.

The second burglary took place at a business premises on Gainsborough Road and is believed to have also happened overnight on Monday night. Suspect/s used a large concrete plinth to force entry to the front door. A number of GHD and Cloud Nine boxed hair straighteners were stolen.

Officers are looking at possible links between these two incidents.

Police have also received a report of criminal damage to a vehicle on Ranelagh Road. Sometime between 10 pm on Monday 20 March and 8.40am on Tuesday 21 March, the passenger window of a green Volvo V40 car was smashed and the glove box was opened. It is not thought that anything was stolen.

Witnesses or anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time are asked to call Police on **101** reference 24235/17 (Hamilton Road) 24197/17 (Gainsborough Road) or 24249/17 (Ranelagh Road) Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111** or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
