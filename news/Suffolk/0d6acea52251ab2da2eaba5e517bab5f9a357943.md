A man questioned by police in connection with the death of a woman in her 40s from Felixstowe has been released under investigation

The 58-year-old man also from Felixstowe was arrested yesterday (Wednesday 12 July) evening after the woman was found critically ill on the floor at her home where she has believed to have been for some time.

She died in hospital earlier this afternoon. A post mortem is due to take place tomorrow.

A cordon remains in place around an address at Leeward Court in Felixstowe and police enquiries continue.

Anyone with information about what happened is asked to call the Major Investigation Team by dialling Suffolk Police on 101, quoting crime number 48027/17.
