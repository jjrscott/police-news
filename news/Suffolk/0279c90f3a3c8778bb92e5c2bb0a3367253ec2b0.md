Police searching for a man missing from Lowestoft have found him.

Robert Chittenden who is aged 40 was reported as missing to police this morning at about 7.30am (Wed 17 October) after failing to turn up for work.

He was located earlier this afternoon in the Blythburgh area.

Police would like to thank the public and media for their help.
