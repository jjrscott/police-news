Police are appealing for witnesses following a daytime burglary in Hintlesham.

The incident occurred between 10:30am and 1:30pm on Thursday 21 June at a house in the vicinity of George Street.

An unknown suspect entered the property via an insecure door. Once inside, a search was carried out and jewellery was stolen.

Police are interested in identifying a white car-derived van and male driver believed to be in the area at approximately 12:40pm.

The male driving the van is described as white and of a slim build. He was wearing a blue t-shirt, dark trousers and trainers.

Anyone who witnessed anything suspicious on that day, or who has any information about this crime, is asked to contact South CID at Landmark House on 101 quoting reference 34209/18.

Alternatively, contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
