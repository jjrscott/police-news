Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of indecent exposure that took place in Lowestoft.

It happened on Tom Crisp Way near to a wooded area called Blackheath Woods on Friday 3 November at about 3pm when a man exposed himself to a teenage girl.

The suspect is described as aged in his 40s or 50s, had dark stubble and he was wearing a dark baseball cap, jeans and a jacket.

Anyone who saw the incident or has any knowledge of it should contact Lowestoft police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/71401/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
