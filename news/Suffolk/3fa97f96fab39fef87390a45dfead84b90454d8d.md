Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Cardinal Park, Ipswich on Saturday 30 July.

The incident took place in a nightclub at around 1.00am. A man was assaulted by another man in the toilets and sustained cuts and bruises.

Witnesses or anybody who has any information are asked to call Suffolk Police on **101** reference 47839/16. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
