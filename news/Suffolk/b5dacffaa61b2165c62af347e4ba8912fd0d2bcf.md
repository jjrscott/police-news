Police have arrested a male on suspicion of assault following an incident in Beccles.

It took place Tuesday 16 March where a teenage pupil was walking home from school when they were approached by a teenage male who made abusive comments to them before hitting them on the head. This took place on Rigbourne Hill.

A 14 year old male was arrested on Wednesday morning and taken to Great Yarmouth Police investigation Centre for questioning. He was released on police bail pending further enquiries to answer on 13 April.

Anyone who witnessed the assault should contact Lowestoft police, quoting crime number 37/13034/21
