Police can confirm no further action will be taken against a 73-year-old man who was arrested following the sudden death of Beryl Mary Taylor at Rattlesden in July last year.

Police were called to an address in Birds Green in Rattlesden around 12.30pm on Monday 11 following the death of Mrs Taylor, 70, who was known as Mary.

The 73-year-old man was initially arrested on suspicion of assisting a suicide.
