Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Stutton.

Jewellery and a bank card were stolen from a bedroom after a patio door was smashed at a property in Cattsfield between 9am and 1.25pm on Wednesday 11 April.

Anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area between the times stated, or anyone with information, should contact Ipswich CID on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/19544/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
