Four people have appeared in Ipswich Magistrates’ court this morning, (Friday 25 May) charged with drugs offences.

On Wednesday 23 May, officers from the Cyber and Serious Organised Crime Unit arrested six people on Elderberry Road in Red Lodge.

A kilogram of cocaine and over £30k of cash was seized.

Four people were charged and remanded to appear in court this morning: Klaus Saliraj, aged 26 and of Pasteur Close in London, Adenis Laho, aged 27 and of no fixed address in London, and Ryan Oyeyemi, aged 26, from the Bury St Edmunds area were charged with being concerned in the supply of controlled drugs. Lacey Taylor aged 23, of Elderberry Road in Red Lodge, was charged with being concerned in the supply of controlled drugs and possession of cannabis with intent to supply.

Two other people have been released under investigation pending further enquiries.
