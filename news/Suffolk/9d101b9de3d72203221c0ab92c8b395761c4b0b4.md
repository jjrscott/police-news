Two people have sustained life-changing/life-threatening injuries following a serious collision on the A1120 in Earl Stonham.

Police were called at around 4.45pm this evening, Sunday 1 April, to reports of a head on collision involving two vehicles.

Both drivers are believed to have suffered life–changing or life-threatening injuries and have been taken by air ambulance to Addenbrooke’s Hospital. Paramedics and firefighters were also in attendance.

The road is currently closed in both directions.

Any witnesses to the incident are asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting reference CAD 265 of 1 April.
