A missing 15 year old boy has been found safe and well.

Tyler Clarke, who has links to Melton and Combs, was last been seen at around 12.30pm in Russell Road, Ipswich on Saturday 25 March.

Tyler was found safe and well in Ipswich yesterday, Monday 28 March.

Police would like to thank members of the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.
