Police are appealing for witnesses following a robbery in Ipswich.

On Saturday 16 February at approximately 9.55pm, a woman was walking along Austin Street when a man standing outside Ladbrokes tried to attract her attention. When she stopped to check the time on her phone, the man approached her and snatched it from her, before running away in the direction of Stoke Street.

The suspect is described as of slim build and a tanned complexion, around 5 foot 7 or 5 foot 8 inches tall, aged in his mid-thirties with stubble and wearing a green or brown hooded top.

Anyone who may have witnessed the incident or who may have any information to assist the investigation should contact Ipswich CID on 101, quoting crime reference number 37/9426/19.
