Police are appealing for witnesses or information after a man sustained a facial injury following a night out in Lowestoft.

Officers were called to a licensed premises in Royal Terrace at approximately 00:50am on Saturday 16 September to reports a man in his 20s had sustained a cut to his face, thought to be consistent of an assault.

The victim required hospital treatment but has been unable to recall events of that night.

Police are investigating the circumstances surrounding his injury and are working to understand what has occurred.

Anybody who may have information to assist the investigation is asked to call Lowestoft CID at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 61127/17.
