Police are appealing for witnesses to an assault in Cowlinge.

It took place on Tuesday 10 November at about 10:30am on Red Dock Lane.

The victim, a male in his early 50s reported that an argument developed with a male on Parklands.

The male jumped into his vehicle and Drove at the victim. The vehicle hit the victim causing injuries to his knee, lower leg and back

Officers would like to speak to the male driver of a white transit van that may have witnessed the assault taking place.

Anyone with information is asked to contact police, quoting crime reference 37/65422/20
