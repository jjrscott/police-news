Officers are appealing for witnesses after an arson in Lowestoft which took place early this morning.

Police were called by the Suffolk Fire and Rescue Service at 1.30am today Thursday 23 August, to reports of a fire in a wheelie bin next to an unoccupied house on Bevan Street West. Nobody was reported injured as a result of the fire.

Witnesses or anybody who saw anything suspicious are asked to call Lowestoft CID on 101 quoting reference 37/48090/18. Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
