A missing 69-year-old man from Lowestoft has been found.

John Cone was last seen at around 8am on Sunday 25 October but he was found earlier this afternoon, Wednesday 28 October.

Police would like to thank members of the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 27 October 2020

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing man from Lowestoft.

John Cone, who is aged 69, left home at about 8am on Sunday 25 October and has not been seen since. There was a sighting of his vehicle, a silver Ford C Max reg: JC54 ONE in Southwold between 12:03pm and 12:09pm on Monday 26 October.

He is described as of being medium build and is about five foot, 10 inches tall, has grey, shoulder length hair and wears glasses. When last seen he was wearing a maroon jacket, blue jeans and had his arm in a splint and has use of a crutch.

Anyone who has seen him or knows of his whereabouts should contact the duty sergeant at Lowestoft police on 101.
