Detectives are appealing for information following a burglary at a home near Lowestoft where jewellery and cash were stolen.

The break-in happened at an address in Hall Lane, Blundeston, sometime between 9:15am on Friday 2 December and 6:45pm on Sunday 4 December, with entry being gained via the back of the property and a search being made.

Two safes and a significant amount of jewellery and cash were taken.

Police are asking anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area between these times, or anyone with information about those responsible, to call Lowestoft CID on **101** quoting reference 69841/16. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers, anonymously if required, on **0800 555 111**.
