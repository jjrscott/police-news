Police are appealing for witnesses to a fail to stop road traffic collision in Ipswich.

It took place on Crown Street in Ipswich on Saturday 7 April at about 4.05pm. A black Honda Civic failed to stop after it was in collision with a 40 year old man on a Harley Davidson motorbike near to the bus station. The motorcyclist sustained just minor injuries.

Anyone who witnessed the incident should contact Ipswich Roads Policing at Martlesham Heath PHQ quoting CAD number 260 of 7 April 2018.
