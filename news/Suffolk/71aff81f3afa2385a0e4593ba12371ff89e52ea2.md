Police are appealing for witnesses to incidents of criminal damage to motor vehicles in Bungay.

The latest incident happened at some point between 10pm and 11pm on Thursday 15 March in Staithe Road.

An offender damaged the paint work of a red Ford Fiesta by using sharp item.

Police would like to speak to a man seen in the vicinity of the vehicle. He is described as white and around 5ft 9in tall. He was wearing all dark clothing and a black puffer jacket. The stitching of the jacket was in the shape of an upside down V.
