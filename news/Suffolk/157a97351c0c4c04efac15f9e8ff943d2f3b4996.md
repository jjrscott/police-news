Police are appealing for witnesses after gravestones at a church were damaged with spray paint.

The incident took place at All Saints Church in Hitcham. Offender/s had covered two headstones with a gold/yellow spray paint. It was reported to police on Wednesday 14 October but is not known when the offences took place.

Witnesses or anybody who has seen anything suspicious or who has any information are asked to contact police quoting reference 37/59938/20
