Detectives are appealing for information following a burglary at a home in Bungay where a Mac Book computer, a drill and jigsaw set and a Samsung curve screen TV were stolen.

The burglary happened at the home in Outney Road at sometime between Saturday 31 December and Wednesday, 4 January.

Police are asking anyone who may have seen someone with the items or anyone with information about who took them or where they are now to call Waveney CID by dialling Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 10778/17. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers, anonymously if required, on **0800 555 111**.
