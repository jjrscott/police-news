Police are appealing for information after a burglary at a jewellers in Ipswich.

At some point between 5:15pm on Saturday 25 and 7:30am on Monday 27 February, offenders gained entry to a property on Tavern Street by smashing a glass door.

A safe was stolen from inside the building, which contained a quantity of cash, watches and jewellery.

Police would like to speak to anyone who may have heard or seen anything suspicious in the area at the times indicated above.

Anyone who saw the incident or anyone with information about those responsible should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting crime reference number 20104/17.
