Police have charged two men and a woman in Lowestoft in connection with drug offences.

It follows a pro-active police operation yesterday, Wednesday 27 February, by the East Scorpion team. Three people arrested at an address in the High Street in the town on suspicion of drug offences. All three were taken to Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

Alex Walsh, aged 43 years and Dominique Luxford, aged 29 years, both of High Street Lowestoft have both now been charged with possession with intent to supply Class A drugs, being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs, and possession of Class A and Class B drugs.

Nick Platten, aged 48 of High Street,Lowestoft was charged with being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs, and possession of Class A drugs.

All three people are due to appear before Norwich Magistrates Court today, Thursday 28 February.
