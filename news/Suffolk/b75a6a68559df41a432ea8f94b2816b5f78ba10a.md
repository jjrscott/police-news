A missing 14-year-old girl from Bury St Edmunds has been found.

Chloe Humpage was reported missing yesterday, Tuesday 7 November in Bury St Edmunds but has returned home safe and well this afternoon.

Police would like to thank the media and the public for their assistance.

## Previous release | 8 November 2017

Police are appealing for help in tracing a missing 14 year old female from Bury St Edmunds.

Chloe Humpage left her address giving no indication of where she was going and was last seen heading off into Bury St Edmunds Cemetery at 2:50pm on Tuesday 7 November 2017.

Chloe is described a white female, 5’ 4” tall slim build; she is described as having very long dark brown straight hair with a pale complexion. Chloe was last seen wearing a black zip up hoodie with black jeans and black boots.

Officers are concerned for her welfare and are asking anyone who has seen Chloe or who has any information on where she may be to contact Suffolk Police on 101.
