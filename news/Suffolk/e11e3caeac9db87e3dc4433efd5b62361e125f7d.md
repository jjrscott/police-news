Police are urging pet owners to be vigilant after two rabbits were taken from a hutch in Little Blakenham.

At some point between 5pm on Thursday 10 December and 7am on Friday 11 December, two adult pet rabbits were removed from a garden at a property in the vicinity of Somersham Road.

One of the rabbits was black and the other one was white and brown.

Two ferrets were also let out of their cage overnight at a neighbouring property.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who may have witnessed anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/71716/20
