Police are appealing for witnesses after a burglary in Brantham.

The incident took place on Monday 16 November at the Constable Park development, Bergholt Road. Sometime between 4:30pm and 9pm entry was forced to the site. Four lodges, the café and toilet block were broken into. A quantity of sanitary ware, worth approximately £4000 was taken along with a number of hand and power tools.

Witnesses or anybody with any information are asked to contact police quoting reference 37/66759/20
