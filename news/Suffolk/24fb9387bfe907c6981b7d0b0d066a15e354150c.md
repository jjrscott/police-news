An area of Thetford Forest was cordoned off this afternoon, Thursday 26 January, following the discovery of a live mortar and a 500lb bomb around 12:30pm yesterday.

A one mile exclusive zone was placed around the Kings Forest area and personnel from the Bomb Disposal Squad completed a controlled detonation around 1pm.

All restrictions have now been lifted and police would like to thank the public for their patience.

## Previous release | 26/01/2017 - 12:16

The live mortar and a 500lb bomb discovered in Thetford Forest yesterday, Wednesday 25 January, are to be detonated in a controlled explosion.

A one mile exclusive zone has been placed around the area at Kings Forest and the Bomb Disposal Squad is to complete the planned explosion around 1pm today.

Suffolk Police are requesting people avoid the area between West Stow and the A11, along the B1106 from 12:15pm until further notice.
