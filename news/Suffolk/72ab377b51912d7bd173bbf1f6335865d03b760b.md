Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at Bentley near Ipswich.

It happened at some point overnight between Monday 8 January at 5pm and Tuesday 9 January at 7am at a farm outbuilding on Bergholt Road.

Offenders entered the outbuilding by force by forcing open secured wooden doors then stole a Stihl hedgecutter(serial no 43662969), a Stihl leafblower (serial no 437815132) and Stihl chainsaw (serial no 183700179).

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious activity or has knowledge of the items stolen should contact Ipswich police quoting 37/51309/17 on 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 08000 555111.
