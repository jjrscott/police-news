Motorists are advised they may experience delays on **Wednesday 24th March** as police escort two abnormal loads.

The first is a transportation of 6 units 4.94m wide, 4.95m high, 15m long, weighing 44,000kg.

The load will be escorted from Peterborough to Churchfield, Monks Eleigh.

A14 Cambs Border – A1214 – A1071 – A1141 – Church Hill – Church Field – Local Roads to Site

Suffolk Police to escort from A14 J50 layby to Monks Eleigh.

The second is a transportation of a boat, 5.6m wide, 4.9m high, 27m long, weighing 38,000kg.

The load will be escorted from Gaymers Way, North Walsham to Southampton.

Local Roads - Gaymers Way - Folgate Rd - B1145 - A149 - B1152 - A1064 - A47 - A140 - A14 - Cambs Border

 **Delays can be expected along these routes.**
