Three people have been charged following a robbery in Felixstowe yesterday afternoon (Saturday 24 February).

This relates to an incident on Orwell Road at the junction with Highfield Road in Felixstowe at around 2.20pm on Saturday 24 February, when police received a report that an elderly woman was approached from behind by a male who snatched her handbag before being driven away with others in a vehicle.

Nicholas Faux, aged 33 and of Highfield Road in Felixstowe, Kiel Faux, aged 29 and Rachel Vincent, aged 32, both of Elmcroft Road in Ipswich, were arrested and all three have been charged with robbery.
