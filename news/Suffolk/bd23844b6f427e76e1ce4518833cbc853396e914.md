Police in Ipswich have arrested two men in connection with drug offences.

The arrests were made in Christchurch Park yesterday afternoon (Thursday 28 Sept) after police officers spotted suspicious activity between two males in the park.

Both males were challenged by officers as they were walking towards the Crown Street exit.

A suspected wrap of cannabis was recovered in the incident. The other male was searched and was found to be in possession of around Â£500 in cash and a mobile phone.

A 20 year old male and a 17 year old male were both arrested on suspicion of supply of drugs of and taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

They have both been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Elsewhere in the town yesterday morning, in a separate incident, a 49 year old man was arrested for drug offences following a warrant at an address on Moore Road.

Officers made the arrest yesterday morning on suspicion of possession of class B or C drugs yesterday morning and possession with intent to supply class A drugs.

He was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning and was subsequently released under investigation pending further enquiries.
