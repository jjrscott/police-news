A missing 77-year-old man from Kesgrave has been found.

John Beal had last been seen in Grange Farm at around 6am yesterday morning, Monday 2 July.

He was found safe and well by police in Tattingstone yesterday evening.

Officers would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
