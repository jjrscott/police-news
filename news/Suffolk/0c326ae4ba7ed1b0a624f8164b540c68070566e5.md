Police appealing for help to find a missing 25-year-old man from Ipswich have found him.

Miral Alin Solosi was last seen in Reading Road at around 7pm on Friday 6 July. He was located in Romania safe and well on Wednesday 18 July.

Police would like to thank the media and public for their help with this matter.
