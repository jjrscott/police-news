Police searching for a man wanted in connection with an assault and theft of a motor vehicle has been located.

30-year-old John Caulder was arrested in Bury St Edmunds on Saturday 15 December.

Caulder was questioned at Bury St Edmunds Police Investigation Centre and was later released under investigation pending further enquiries.
