Police are appealing for witnesses after a two-vehicle road traffic collision on the A12 at Saxmundham on Monday evening.

The collision happened at the A12 cross roads at the B1119 Rendham Road around 6.50pm on Monday, 10 October, when a Mercedes C270 was in collision with a Peugeot 206.

A woman in her 40s who was driving the Peugeot was taken to hospital where she was found to have serious injuries. A young boy in the same vehicle also sustained injuries and was taken to hospital. He was kept in as a precautionary measure. A man in his 80s who was driving the Mercedes was also taken to hospital for treatment for minor injuries and chest pain.

Police are appealing for witnesses to the collision and are asking anyone who can help to call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting CAD reference number 320 of October 10.
