Police have arrested and charged a man in connection with drug and weapon offences in Ipswich.

The arrest was made at 12.30pm yesterday, Monday 1 June, while officers were on patrol in around Christchurch Street and Cemetery Road.

Officers stopped and searched an 18-year-old man in Christchurch Street and he was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

Sonny Stirton, 18, of no fixed abode, was subsequently charged with possession of a bladed article in a public place and possession of a Class B drug.
