Officers are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision that took place in Stowmarket last night.

Police were called at 5pm on Thursday, 20 October to reports of a collision on the B1115, Stowupland Road, Stowmarket near to the Mortimer Road roundabout involving a Ford Transit van and a pedestrian.

Emergency services attended and the pedestrian, a man in his 30s was taken to hospital with serious injuries. Initial reports indicate that the man may have run towards the road from the direction of the Eider Close area.

Witnesses are asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team (SCIT) on **101,** reference 228 of 20 October.
