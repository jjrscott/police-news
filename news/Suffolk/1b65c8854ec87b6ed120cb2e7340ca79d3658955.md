Police are appealing for witnesses following a three vehicle collision in Ipswich.

On Friday 2 February, at approximately 12.05pm, a collision occurred on Hadleigh Road, near to the Hyntle Close junction, involving a silver Audi A3, a black Honda Civic and a red Suzuki motorcycle.

Police, East of England Ambulance and the East of England Air Ambulance attended and the motorcycle rider, a man in his 30s, was taken to Addenbrookeâ€™s hospital in a critical condition with head and pelvic injuries.

The road was closed until around 6pm.

Police would be interested in hearing from any witnesses to this collision, or anyone who may have seen the manner of driving of the vehicles beforehand.

Anyone with any information should call the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101, quoting incident number 168 of 2 February.
