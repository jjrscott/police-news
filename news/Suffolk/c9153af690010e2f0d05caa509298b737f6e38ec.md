Police are appealing for witnesses after a Lexus convertible was stolen following a burglary in Ipswich.

The incident occurred overnight on Friday 28 March at a property in Valley Road. Offenders gained access to the home and removed keys from the property before stealing the vehicle from the driveway.

The stolen vehicle is a silver Lexus IS 250 convertible, registration number MMW168.

Police would like to hear from anyone who believes they may have seen this vehicle or anyone with information about this crime. Calls should be made to Southern Area CID at Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference 25775/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
