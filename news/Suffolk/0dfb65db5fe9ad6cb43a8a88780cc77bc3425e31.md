Police were called to a farm near Eye at Thorndon on Thursday 14 February at about 6pm following reports that an industrial incident had occurred.
A man, in his 70s, had sadly died at the scene. Next of kin have been informed. No one else was injured.

Emergency services have been present at the scene during the evening. Officers are continuing to investigate alongside colleagues from other organisations, including the Health and Safety Executive.
