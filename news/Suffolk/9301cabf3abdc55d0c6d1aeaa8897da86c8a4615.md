A 23-year-old man has been given a 10 year prison sentence after admitting a number of burglary offences and possession of bladed weapon.

James Preston of Bardfield Road, Colchester pleaded guilty to the following offences,

 * burglary at an address in East Mill Green, Bentley
 * attempted burglary at a property in Capel Road, Bentley
 * aggravated burglary with intent at an address in Pound Lane, Capel St Mary
 * possession of a meat cleaver in a public place
 * assault of a police officer

Police were called on Wednesday 1 February, to reports of a burglary in progress at a property on Capel Road, Bentley. Officers quickly attended and located and detained the suspect who had fled the scene on foot towards the A12.

Preston appeared at Ipswich Crown Court yesterday, Thursday 9 March and was given a 10 year prison sentence.
