Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Otley.

Overnight on Wednesday 5 January, offenders forced entry to a home on The Green, and conducted an untidy search of the property before stealing a quantity of cash.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have seen or heard any suspicious activity in the area before or at the time stated.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference number 37/10975/17.
