Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Ipswich where jewellery was stolen.

The incident took place at a home address in Stone Lodge Lane at some point between 8am and 4:40pm on Wednesday 11 October.

Access was forced, with offenders gaining entry by smashing a window towards the rear of the property.

Items stolen include a quantity of cash, a wooden jewellery box which contained costume jewellery, a silver Samsung Galaxy mini mobile phone, blue Samsung Galaxy mobile phone and jewellery.

Jewellery stolen include:

路 opal stud earrings

路 a diamante chain-link bracelet

路 Pandora star bangle bracelet with diamantes

路 Pandora double rope bracelet on leather, with up to seven charms including a Sagittarius horoscope charm, a silver heart, a blue and white-coloured charm, a jade glass charm and a Christmas teddy bear charm.

Anybody who witnessed anything suspicious during the times stated, or who has information that may help with enquiries are asked to call South CID at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 66477/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org
