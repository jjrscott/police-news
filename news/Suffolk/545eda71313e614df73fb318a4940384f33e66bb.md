Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a business premises in Brandon last week.

The incident occurred at overnight between Wednesday 3 January and Thursday 4 January at The Factory Shop in London Road. The intruder alarm was activated around 4am and police were called to the scene shortly after this time.

The offenders caused a significant amount of damage to a number of windows and also the roof in their efforts to break in. Once entry had been gained a safe containing a quantity of cash was stolen.

Any witnesses, or anyone with information about this burglary, is asked to contact the Forest Heath Neighbourhood Response Team at Mildenhall Police Station on 101, quoting reference 667/18, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
