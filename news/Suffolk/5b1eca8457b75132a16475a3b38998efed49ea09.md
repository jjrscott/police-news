Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Holbrook.

On Friday 5 May, at some point between 9.30am and 11.00am, offenders forced entry to a property on Firebronds Road by smashing a window.

Following an untidy search, a gold pendant and earring set were stolen.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have seen or heard any suspicious activity in the area before or at the time stated.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference number 37/33164/17.
