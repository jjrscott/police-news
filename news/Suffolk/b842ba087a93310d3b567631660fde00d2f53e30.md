Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident of indecent exposure in Melton.

The incident took place in the vicinity of Dock Lane around 11:15am on Friday 20 January. A woman was walking along a footpath by the river wall when a man reportedly exposed himself and acted indecently.

The man is described as white, under 40 years old and of heavy build. He was clean shaven and was wearing a dark anorak and a green beanie hat.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have seen someone matching this description in the area at the time stated, or who may have information to assist the investigation.

Anyone with information should contact the Incident and Crime Management Hub at Suffolk Police on 101, quoting reference 13467/17.
