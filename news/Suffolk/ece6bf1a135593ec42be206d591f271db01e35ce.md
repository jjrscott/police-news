A quantity of jewellery was stolen in a burglary at a home in Mill Lane in Felixstowe yesterday.

The break-in happened between 10am and midday yesterday, Monday 10 April. Someone smashed their way in via the front of the property and made a search before stealing items including watches, rings, earrings, necklaces and bracelets.

Police are releasing photographs of some of the items taken and officers are asking anyone who may have been offered similar items for sale to get in touch.

If you saw anything suspicious in the Mill Lane area around this time or know where the jewellery is now, or have any information about who stole the items, please contact detectives at police headquarters by calling Suffolk Police on 101, quoting crime number 28107/17.

Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers, anonymously if required, on **0800 555 111**.
