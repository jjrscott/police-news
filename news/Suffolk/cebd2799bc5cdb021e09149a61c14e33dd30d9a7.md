Police are appealing for witnesses regarding a burglary that took place on the Saturday 6 March 2021 between 6.20pm and 7.30pm on Main Road in Kesgrave.

Offenders have gained access by breaking a double glazed window on the rear patio doors of the property, turning a key on the inside of the door and then entering. A search of the property has then taken place resulting in items of jewellery being stolen.

Anyone who saw any suspicious activity, was driving in the area at the material time with dash camera footage, has any knowledge of the incident or knows the whereabouts of the items stolen should contact Ipswich CID quoting investigation reference 37/11113/21.
