Police are appealing for witnesses after an arson incident at a property in Ipswich.

Officers were called at 7.30pm yesterday evening, Monday 6 March, to reports that a window at a flat in Dandalan Close had been smashed and furniture inside set alight. The Fire Service were also in attendance.

The fire was quickly extinguished and none of the occupants suffered any injuries.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact Ipswich CID on101 quoting reference CAD 315 of 6 March, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
