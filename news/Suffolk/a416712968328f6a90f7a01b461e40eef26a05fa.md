Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Bury St Edmunds.

Suspect/s forced entry to the rear of a property in Oliver Road sometime between 7.50am and 4.50pm.on Saturday 18 February. A search was made of the property and a sum of cash was stolen.

Witnesses or anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time are asked to call Police on **101** reference 18423/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
