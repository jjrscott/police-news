Police are appealing for witnesses after three vehicles were damaged in Stowmarket.

Between 11am and 9.20pm yesterday, Wednesday 19 August, three vehicles – a Volkswagen Golf, Ford Fiesta and Ford C-Max – were damaged in Prentice Road.

An unknown person smashed a windscreen, damaged and smashed wing mirrors, dented the bonnets and broke a headlight, while they were parked in the same road.

Anyone who saw any suspicious activity in the area should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/48270/20
