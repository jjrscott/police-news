A Hollesley Bay prisoner who absconded on Wednesday 12 October has been recaptured.

Stuart Guildea, 39, who was serving a sentence for robbery, theft and burglary, was reported as missing following a roll call at 8pm that night.

He was detained by officers at Bury St Edmunds yesterday, Wednesday 9 November.

We would like to thank you for your help with this appeal.
