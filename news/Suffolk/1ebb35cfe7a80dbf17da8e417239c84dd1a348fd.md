Residents in Sudbury and Stowmarket will have the chance to meet their local police officers this week.

The Sudbury Safer Neighbourhood Team (SNT) will be holding a community engagement event this Wednesday, 8 March, at the Tesco Superstore in Springlands Way between 10:30am and 12:30pm.

Stowmarket SNT officers are holding a community engagement event the following day, Thursday 9th March, at the Tesco Superstore in Cedars Link Road between 10am and 1pm.

Local residents and shoppers are invited to come along to meet their local officers to air any concerns they have within their community and ask for crime prevention advice.
