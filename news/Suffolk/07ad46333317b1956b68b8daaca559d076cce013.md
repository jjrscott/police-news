Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Troston.

At some point between 6am on Tuesday 12 November and 5am on Wednesday 13 November, an unknown person forced a lock on a garden shed at a property in the vicinity of Church Lane and The Street and an electric bike was stolen from within.

The bike is described as black with white and red writing. It also had a black and orange pannier attached to the back of the bike.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting reference number 37/69089/19
