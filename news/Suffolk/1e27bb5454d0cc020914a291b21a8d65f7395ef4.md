Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Sudbury.

At some point between Thursday 16 November at 9.30pm and Friday 17 November at 7.30am, offenders forced entry into commercial premises on Old Market Place.

A safe was stolen from the property, containing cash, documents and keys.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have seen or heard any suspicious activity overnight on the 16 November, or who may have any other information to assist the investigation.

Anyone with information should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/74207/17.
