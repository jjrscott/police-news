Police are issuing photographs of items of property in an effort to return them to the rightful owners.

The items, which may have been stolen, were recovered by officers as part of their enquiries into recent burglaries in both the Brandon area and Norwich area in Norfolk. These include watches, an iPad, a Ford car key and a pair of multi-tools, among other items.

Anyone who believes they own any of the items in the photographs is asked to contact West CID at Bury St Edmunds Police Station on 101, quoting reference 37/69155/18.
