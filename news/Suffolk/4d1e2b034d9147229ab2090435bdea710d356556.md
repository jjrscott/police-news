A prisoner from Hollesley Bay who failed to return has been located.

Ben Tonge, 30, was reported missing to police today, 14 December, after failing to return following a period of home leave.

He was found in Ipswich.

He will be returned to the prison in due course.

Police would like to thank the public and media for their help with this matter.
