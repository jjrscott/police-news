Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary at a home address in Haverhill.

The break-in occurred at some point between 11am and 4:50pm on Wednesday, 18 October, on the A143 Wratting Road, near Churchill Avenue.

Entry was forced by smashing a glass panel to the rear of the property and a quantity of jewellery and handbags were stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity during the times stated or has knowledge of the incident should contact CID West at Suffolk Police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/68107/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
