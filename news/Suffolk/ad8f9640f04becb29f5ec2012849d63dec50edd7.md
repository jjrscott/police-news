Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Ipswich.

The incident took place sometime between 12pm and 12:30pm on Wednesday 5 April at an address in Lackford Place.

The victim, a man in his 50s, returned home to discover two people – a man and a woman – in his property. They pushed passed him and ran out via the front door. A laptop, a black Rotary watch in a brown case and two bottles of aftershave were stolen.

The male suspect is described as white with a tanned complexion, between 25 – 30 years’ old, approximately 5ft 6ins in height and of medium build. He was unshaven and wearing a black baseball cap with a white logo and curved peak, black trousers and a black padded coat.

The female suspect is also described as white with a tanned complexion, between 20 and 30 years’ old, of thin build and approximately 5ft 7ins in height. She was wearing a dark-coloured coat, with white and black leggings, and her dark brown hair was pulled back in a ponytail.

The offenders were both reported as speaking a foreign language.

Witnesses or anybody with any information are asked to call Southern Area CID at Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference number 27253/17.
