Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident of arson in Felixstowe.

The incident occurred at some point between 11pm on Friday 9 October and 12.25am on Saturday 10 October, within an outdoor historic seafront shelter in Sea Road.

During the times stated, two waste bins within the building were set alight by unknown offender/s. The fire spread and caused significant damage to the building.

Officers have carried out a number of enquiries and are in the process of reviewing CCTV footage as the investigation continues.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact Felixstowe Safer Neighbourhood Team, quoting crime reference: 37/58923/20
