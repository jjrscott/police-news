Police are appealing for witnesses following a daytime burglary in Stratford St Mary.

The incident occurred between 8.40am and 2.50pm yesterday, Wednesday 20 June, at a house in the vicinity of the Strickmere road area.

A patio window was smashed to gain entry to the property and all of the rooms were searched. It is not yet known what has been stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious persons or vehicles in the village on that day, or who has any information about this crime, is asked to contact South CID at Landmark House on 101 quoting reference 33947/18.

Alternatively, contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
