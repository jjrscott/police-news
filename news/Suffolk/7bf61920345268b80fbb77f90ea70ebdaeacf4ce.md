Police are appealing for witnesses after an assault in Haverhill.

It happened on Friday 4 May at around 8.30am in Withersfield Road, when a nine-year-old girl walking to school was approached by a man.

The man said she should go with him then grabbed her right shoulder. The girl managed to walk away from the man, who did not follow her, and was not injured. When she got to school she told her teachers about the incident.

The suspect is described as an adult white male, 5ft 5in tall, of skinny build with short, black greasy hair. He had a black beard and was wearing a blue polo-type shirt.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it should contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/24263/18. Alternatively, they can call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
