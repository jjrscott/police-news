Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident in Kesgrave where a man was bitten by a dog.

The incident took place on Sunday 14 February at around 12.30pm, in a public space adjacent to Dr Watson Lane and Main Road.

Two dogs, believed to be a French Bull Dog and a Boston Terrier, were being walked off-lead by a woman. It is not known which of the dogs bit the man.

Anyone who knows the owner of the dogs, witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/7473/21, via:

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Phone – Call 101

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
