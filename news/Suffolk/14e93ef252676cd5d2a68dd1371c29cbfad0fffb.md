Police can confirm that wanted man Daniel Dale has been located.

The 28 year old was wanted by police after breaching the conditions of his court bail and also in connection with robbery and burglary offences in Haverhill.

He was arrested in the Haverhill area earlier today. Police would like to thank the media and the public for their help with this appeal.
