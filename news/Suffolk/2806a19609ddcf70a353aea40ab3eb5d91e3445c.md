Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary at a business in Framlingham.

It took place on Tuesday 6 February at just before 3pm on Station Road.

A suspect entered the India Curry restaurant while staff were absent. The suspect went behind the serving counter and stole a large red tip jar, an orange metal charity jar both containing large quantities of cash and three large bottles of spirits from the bar areas. The suspect also made an unsuccessful attempt to break into the shop cash till using a knife that was obtained from the kitchen area of the restaurant.

Police have released two CCTV stills of an individual they would like to speak to in connection with the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has knowledge of the crime should contact Halesworth police station on 101, quoting crime reference 37/7339/18 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
