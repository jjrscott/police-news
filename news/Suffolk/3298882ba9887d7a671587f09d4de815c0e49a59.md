Police can confirm that a missing 14-year-old girl from Lowestoft has been found.

Gracie-May Gullen was located in the town safe and well this morning.

Officers would like to thank the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | Wednesday 23 January

Police are appealing for help to trace a 14-year-old girl missing from Lowestoft.

Gracie-May Gullen was last seen in Norwich Road Lowestoft at around 8pm yesterday, 22 January and has not been seen since.

Gracie-May is described as white, of slim build with long brown hair (possibly worn in a pony tail) When last seen she was wearing black tracksuit bottoms.

Officers are concerned for her welfare and are asking anyone who may have seen Gracie-May or who has information as to her whereabouts are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 quoting CAD 274 of 22 January.
