A man has been jailed for three months for a burglary in Felixstowe.

46 year old Mark Lewis of no fixed abode but from the Felixstowe area was jailed at Ipswich Magistrates Court on Thursday 20 January after pleading guilty.

He was arrested on suspicion of burglary (non-dwelling) after a report of suspicious activity at a buisness premises on Walton Avenue in the town at about 5.15am on Wednesday 13 January. He was subsequently charged with burglary and was remanded to appear before court on Thurs 20 Jan where he pleaded guilty to the offence and was sentenced to 12 weeks in jail.
