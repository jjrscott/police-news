A 28-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with assault, public order and criminal damage offences which occurred in the Beccles area.

Between 31 July and 27 August, the male used words and behaviour contravening the Public Order Act, committed criminal damage to a Perspex screen, assaulted a member of the public and, when arrested, assaulted a police officer.

Peter Davidson-White of Cucumber Lane, Beccles, was arrested yesterday, Thursday 27 August and was taken to Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

He was subsequently charged with assaulting an emergency worker, assault, two counts of using threatening words or behaviour and criminal damage.
