Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of criminal damage that took place in Lowestoft.

It was reported to police just after midnight on Saturday 11 November.

An offside front window and windscreen of a white Ford Focus parked on Reeve Street had been smashed with damage also sustained to the bonnet.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it should contact Lowestoft police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/72714/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
