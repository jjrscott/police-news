Officers are appealing for witnesses or information following an assault that took place in Ipswich.

The incident took place on Friday 9 June at around 9.50pm on Bramford Road, near to the junction of Rendlesham Road.

The victim, a man, went inside a retail premises whilst his girlfriend waited outside. Two unknown men then confronted the woman before walking away in the direction of Norwich Road. The victim approached the men who then assaulted him, knocking him to the floor. The suspects then ran off.

The man sustained a broken jaw which required hospital treatment.

The first suspect is described as being in his late 20s to mid 30s, 5 feet 8 inches tall, of stocky build with short black hair, curly on top. He was wearing a blacket jacket, red jumper and dark blue jeans.

The second suspect is described being in his late 30s about 5 feet 11 inches tall, of average build with short, receding hair. He was wearing a blue jacket, black jumper and light blue jeans.

Both men are reported as speaking an East European language to each other.

Anybody who has witnessed the incident or who has any information are asked to call Ipswich CID on **101** quoting reference 41425/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111** or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
