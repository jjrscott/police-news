Suffolk Police are appealing for information following a road traffic collision in Crowfield.

At some point between the evening of Thursday 23 March and the morning of Saturday 25 March, it is believed that a vehicle was in collision with the village hall on Stone Street.

The collision caused extensive damage to the walls of the building, along with some damage to the interior services and kitchen area.

Police would like to hear from anyone who might have heard or seen the collision take place.

If anyone has any information about the incident, they are asked to contact the Traffic Justice Unit at Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting reference TJU 169559.
