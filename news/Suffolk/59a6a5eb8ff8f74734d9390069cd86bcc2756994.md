Suffolk Police are appealing for help in locating the owner of a gold ring found in Beccles.

The gold ring is described as a vintage saddle design and was found by officers from the Sentinel team in the town on Monday 23 November.

Anyone who recognises the ring described or who has any information to assist is asked to contact PC Richard Sievers via the Suffolk Police website or via 101 quoting reference number 37/68097/20.
