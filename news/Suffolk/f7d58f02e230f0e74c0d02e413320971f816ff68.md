Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision in Lowestoft.

On Thursday 30 November at approximately 12:40pm, officers were called following reports that a grey Ford Fusion car had been involved in a minor collision with a wall on Yarmouth Road near to High Street Surgery.

It was initially believed that the driver, a woman in her fifties, may have suffered a medical episode prior to the collision. The air ambulance attended the scene but the woman was taken by road ambulance to hospital. The woman sadly passed away a short time later.

It is now thought that the death could be related to the collision itself and police are asking that anybody who witnessed the collision or the manner of driving prior to the incident to come forward.

Anyone with information is asked to call PC 356 Metcalf at the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101.
