Police can confirm that a man's body was found in Pakefield this morning, Sunday 17 February.

Officers were called at approximately 7:45am by the East of England Ambulance Service to reports that a body of a man in his 70's had been discovered on the beach in Pakefield.

The death is currently being treated as unexplained but is not believed to be suspicious. Enquiries to establish the circumstances remain ongoing. Next of kin have been informed.
