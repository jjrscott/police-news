Police are appealing for witnesses to a theft of a pedal bike in Bury St Edmunds.

It took place at some point between Tuesday 23 March at 11pm and Wednesday 24 March at 7am at a property on Sicklesmere Road.

Overnight a 'Whyte' mountain bike, coloured green, was removed from the garden of the victim. The bike was locked to a secure mount bolted to a solid wall. This was located behind a 6' garden gate. The bike was worth about £750.

Anyone who saw or heard any suspicious behaviour or knows of the whereabouts of the bike should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/14421/21.

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
