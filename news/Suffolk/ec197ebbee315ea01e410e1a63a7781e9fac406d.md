Detectives from Suffolk Police have charged a man following a robbery in Ipswich in June.

Lubomir Kroscen, 32, of Elliott Street in Ipswich, has been charged with robbery following an incident on Burlington Road on Tuesday 20 June at 12.30am, where a wallet was stolen.

He has been remanded in custody with a next court hearing date of 23 August.
