Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place In Ipswich.

It happened at some point between 12.30pm and 9.30pm on Sunday 15 October.

The offender(s) entered a property on Tomline Road by climbing onto a water butt and accessing the property via an open upstairs bedroom window.

They stolen three watches, a a large quantity of cash and a burgundy coloured make up bag.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity during the times stated or has knowledge of the incident should contact PC Daniel Steel at Ipswich police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/67507/13 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
