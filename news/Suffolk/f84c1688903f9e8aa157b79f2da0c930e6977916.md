Following a single vehicle collision on the A143 in Stanton, near Bury St Edmunds this morning, Friday 14 Oct, it has been confirmed that a man in his 80s has sadly died.

Police were called by the East of England Ambulance Service at 10.55am today, to reports that a Citroen C5 car had collided with a tree. The man driving the car was treated by the ambulance service but died at the scene. A woman passenger in her 80s has been taken to West Suffolk Hospital with serious injuries.

It is believed that the man was feeling unwell shortly before the collision.

Police are appealing for witnesses to the collision and are asking anyone who can help to call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting CAD reference number 134 of 14 October.

## Previous release | 14/10/2016

Emergency services are currently on the scene of a serious collision on the A143 in Stanton near Bury St Edmunds.

Police were called by the ambulance service at 10.55am today, Friday 14 October to reports of a single vehicle collision. The road is currently closed.

Police are appealing for witnesses to the collision and are asking anyone who can help to call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting CAD reference number 134 of 14 October.
