Police are appealing for witnesses after receiving a report of rape occurring in Ipswich town centre at the weekend.

Officers were called in the early hours of yesterday morning, Sunday 18 September, by a woman who alleged that she was raped at some time between 1am and 3am, at a location between Old Cattle Market and Upper Orwell Street.

The victim is being supported by specialist trained officers as efforts to identify the offender are taking place.

Anyone with information about this crime is asked to contact the Major Investigation Team on 101 quoting reference 56118/16, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
