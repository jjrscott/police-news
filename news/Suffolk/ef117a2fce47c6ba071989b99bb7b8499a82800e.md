A 25-year-old man is due to appear before Ipswich Magistrates tomorrow, Friday 17 November, charged with drug offences in the town.

Yusef Ahmed was arrested yesterday, Wednesday 15 November, in the Tower Hamlets area of London and taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre where he has subsequently been charged with conspiracy to supply class A drugs.

He has been detained in custody pending an appearance before Ipswich magistrates tomorrow.
