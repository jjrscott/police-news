Officers were called today around 4.40 am today, Tuesday 5 September to reports that a burglary was in progress on a boat in the marina on New Cut East, Ipswich.

Police swiftly attended and witnessed a man fitting the description given walking towards Stoke Bridge. It is believed that the suspect then lowered himself into the water at the quayside and hid in a void under New Cut East. The suspect was located a short time later and was detained on suspicion of burglary.

The man, in his thirtes, was taken to hospital to be assessed before being brought to Martlesham Police Investigation centre for questioning by detectives.

Emergency services including East of England Ambulance Service, Suffolk Fire and Rescue and HM Coastguard also attended the incident.
