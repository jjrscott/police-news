Police can confirm that a fatal house fire in Yoxford is not being treated as suspicious.

Emergency services were called to a property in Grove Side shortly before 5.20am on Saturday 15 December.

The body is still yet to be formally identified and a post mortem examination will take place in due course.

A seal was put in place at the property, while both police and fire investigators attended but it has now been lifted.

## Previous release | Saturday 15 December

Police are continuing with their investigations following a fire in Yoxford this morning, Saturday 15 December.

Emergency services were called shortly before 5.20am to reports of a fire at an address in Grove Side where a body was discovered inside the property.

The death is currently being treated as unexplained and the cause of the fire is unknown at this stage.

A seal remains on the property while Crime Scene Investigators (CSI) and fire investigators carry out enquiries to establish the cause.
