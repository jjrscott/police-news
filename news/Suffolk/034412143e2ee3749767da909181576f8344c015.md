Three people arrested after a woman in her 60s was robbed at knifepoint in Tuddenham Road in Ipswich on Wednesday have been bailed to return to police in September.

The three - a 31-year-old man from the Stowmarket area, a 31-year-old woman from Ipswich and a 25-year-old man from Ipswich â€“ were arrested in the early hours of yesterday, Thursday 4 August, after the woman was approached, threatened with a knife and had her handbag stolen around 5.15pm on Wednesday 3.

They were taken into custody at Martlesham Police Investigation Centre where they were interviewed by detectives in connection with the incident. All three have now been bailed to return to police on September 20, to allow further enquiries to be carried out.

Police are asking anyone who may have information about the incident to call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 48255/16.
