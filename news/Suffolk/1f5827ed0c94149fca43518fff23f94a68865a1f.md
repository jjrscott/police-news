Police are appealing for witnesses following an arson attack at a retail park in Ipswich.

The incident took place between 12am and 12.20am on Wednesday 7 September at Orwell Retail Park in Ranelagh Road.

A display shed outside the front of a retail premises was deliberately set alight which also caused damage to the doors and signage.

Any witnesses, or anyone with information about this incident, is asked to contact PC Ryan Brunning at the Southern Area Neighbourhood Response Team on **101** quoting reference 54064/16, or Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
