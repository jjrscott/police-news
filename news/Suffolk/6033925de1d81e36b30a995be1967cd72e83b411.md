Police are appealing for witnesses and reminding dog owners to keep their pets under control, after sheep were killed in Gedgrave.

The incident occurred at some time between Thursday 21 January and yesterday, Wednesday 27 January, in a field near to the river wall.

Two pregnant ewes were attacked and killed in a suspected dog attack. As a result, officers would remind dog walkers to ensure they keep they their pets under control when walking through fields with livestock in.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact the Rural Crime Team, quoting reference: 4327/21.
