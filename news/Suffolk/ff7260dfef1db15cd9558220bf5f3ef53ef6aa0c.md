A missing 39-year-old woman from Beccles has been found.

Kerri Francis had last been spoken to on the phone at around 10am yesterday, Wednesday 9 October, and she was reported missing to police a short time later.

Kerri was found safe and well at 10am this morning, Thursday 10 October.

Officers would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 9 October 2019

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 39-year-old woman from Beccles.

Kerri Francis was last spoken to on the phone at around 10am today, Wednesday 9 October, and she was reported missing to police a short time later.

She is described as white, 5ft 2ins tall, of slim build and with shoulder length blonde hair. She also has a butterfly tattoo on her right shoulder and a piercing in her left eyebrow.

Kerri was wearing a white long-sleeved top and blue jeans.

Officers are concerned for the welfare of Kerri and are appealing for anyone who may have seen her or know of her whereabouts to contact Suffolk police on 101.
