Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Trimley St Mary.

At some point between 10:50am and 11:40am on Sunday 26 February, a home address on High Road was broken into.

The incident took place near the junction with Park View and entrance to the property was gained via the rear of the building. Collectable coins and a small amount of cash was stolen.

Police would like to speak to anyone who may have heard or seen anything suspicious in the area at the time of the offence.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting crime reference number 19767/17.
