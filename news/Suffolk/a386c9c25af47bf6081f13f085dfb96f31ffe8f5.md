On behalf of the Police and Crime Commissioner for Suffolk and Chief Constable of Suffolk Constabulary, I confirm that the status of the draft Statements of Accounts for the year-ended 31 March 2017 is unaudited, and the Statements of Accounts as published may be subject to change.
