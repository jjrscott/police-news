Emergency Services are currently at the scene of a single vehicle collision in Felixstowe.

Police were called at approximately 12:25pm today, Friday 17 November, to reports that a lorry had overturned on the eastbound A14, just after the Dock Spur roundabout.

One lane is currently closed, however both lanes are expected to shut for recovery which will affect traffic heading towards the Port of Felixstowe. Traffic will instead be diverted via the A154 Candlet Road.

The driver is believed to have sustained minor injuries.

An investigation into the circumstances of the incident is now underway. Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting CAD reference 175 of today, 17 November.
