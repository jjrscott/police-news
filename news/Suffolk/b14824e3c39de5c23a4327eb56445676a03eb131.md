Police are appealing for information following an incident of theft in Rushmere, near Lowestoft.

At some point between 4pm on Monday 1 April and 6am on Tuesday 2 April, unknown offender/s cut the padlock off a gate and gained entry to a farm.

Once inside, several barns were entered and the following items were stolen: A 20 litre diesel can; Bosch angle grinder; Karcher HDS-7/10-4M hot pressure washer with 10 metre extension hose; Scheppach HC53DC 50 litre twin cylinder air compressor; Suzuki Eiger Quadrunner 4x4 400cc petrol red quad bike, model LT-F400FK5; Bosch 4.5 Inch 240v angle grinder and various hand tools.

A Stiga ride on lawnmower was also removed from the site but was left damaged close to the roadside.

Anyone who has witnessed the sudden arrival of such items or who may have been offered the items for sale should contact PC Samantha Plummer on 101, quoting crime reference 37/19179/19 or you can provide an online update via the force website http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update or email [email protected]

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
