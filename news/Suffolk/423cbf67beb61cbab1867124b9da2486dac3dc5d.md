Police are appealing for witnesses or information following the theft of a mountain bike in Ipswich.

At some point between 5pm and 8pm on Friday 9 October, a locked blue GT mountain bike was stolen from outside Goals in Grimwade Street.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/59034/20
