Police are appealing for witnesses to an assault that took place in Bury St Edmunds.

It happened at about 3.30pm on Tuesday 11 September in the vicinity of Hospital Road.

The male victim, aged 13, was walking alone when the male suspect has emerged from within a group of girls and called his name. When the victim has turned around suspect has punching him to the face causing him to fall and stamped on him.

He sustained a significantly split lip, a cut and bruised gum, a minor cut to one elbow and minor graze to the back. In addition to this he has suffered some memory loss following falling to the floor where he hit his head.

Anyone who saw the incident or has knowledge of it, should contact via 101 quoting crime references 37/ 52199/18. Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
