Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Ipswich in which four shotguns were stolen.

The incident occurred between 6pm and 6.45pm on Wednesday 24 October at a property in Porter Road.

At some point during the times stated, offender/s forced entry by breaking a rear window. A safe was broken into and jewellery and an amount of cash were stolen from within. A locked gun cabinet was also forced open with four shotguns stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity or who has any information about the crime is asked to contact South CID on 101 quoting reference 37/61586/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
