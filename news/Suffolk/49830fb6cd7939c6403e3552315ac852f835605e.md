Three teenagers have been arrested after two men were assaulted in Hadleigh at the weekend.

The incident occurred at around 12.45am yesterday morning, Sunday 1 July, in High Street.

A 31-year-old man was struck with a glass bottle causing a deep laceration to his face and he was taken to hospital for treatment. A 42-year-old man was punched to the face during the same incident, causing swelling to his cheek.

A 17-year-old boy and two 19-year-old men â€“ all from Hadleigh - have been arrested on suspicion of assault causing grievous bodily harm in connection with the incident. The 17-year-old has been released on police bail and one of the 19-year-olds have been released under investigation pending further enquiries. The other 19-year-old remains in police custody for questioning.

Any witnesses to the incident are asked to contact South CID at Landmark House on 101 quoting reference 36200/18, or the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
