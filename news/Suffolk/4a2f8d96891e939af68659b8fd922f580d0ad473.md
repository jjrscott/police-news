An 18 year old man has been arrested and charged with two counts of vehicle taking in Lowestoft.

It follows two incidents that took place at some point between 15 and 16 February this year The first saw a green Suzuki Motorbike stolen from Wollaston Road in the town and was left disgarded nearby with smashed lights, slashed seat and wiring pulled out. The second incident took place also at some point between 15 and 16 February this year when an orange Honda moped was taking from the front garden of a property on Sands Lane in Lowestoft and was recovered in a nearby property on the morning of 16 February.

An 18 year old was arrested yesterday afternoon on suspicion of aggravated vehicle taking (Wednesday 4 April) and taken to Great Yarmouth Police investigation Centre for questioning.

Tyler Ezobi of Sands Lane in Lowestoft was charged with two counts taking a motor vehicle without consent to appear before Norwich Magistrates' Court Norwich today Thursday 5 April.
