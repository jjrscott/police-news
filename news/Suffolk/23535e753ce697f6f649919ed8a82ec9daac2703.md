Tributes are being paid to the victim of a fatal road traffic collision in Bury St Edmunds.

Officers were called at 2.40am on Friday 14 July, to a report that a blue Peugeot 306 had collided with a pedestrian on the westbound carriageway of the A14, between junctions 43 and 42.

Alexander Beck, aged 27 and from Sheffield, sadly died at the scene.

Suffolk Police have been asked to issue the following tribute of behalf of his family:

"We are devastated at the sudden death of our beloved son and brother, Alex. He was a son and brother to be proud of and we will miss him dearly."
