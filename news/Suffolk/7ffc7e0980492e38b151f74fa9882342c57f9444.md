Officers are investigating a burglary at an amusement centre in Felixstowe.

The break in happened at around 5:30am yesterday, Tuesday 10 January, at an address on Sea Road.

Entry was forced via the side of the building. Two coin pusher machines were damaged and a quantity of cash was subsequently stolen.
It is thought the suspect made off on foot.

Police would like to speak to anyone who may have seen anything suspicious around the time stated. Anyone with information should contact Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting reference 11509/17. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers, anonymously if required, on 0800 555 111.
