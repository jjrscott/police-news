Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Lowestoft.

It happened on Tuesday 15 May at some point overnight when two pedal cycles were stolen from a garage on Rembrant Close.

The cycles are described as a white Schwin racing bike and a white Giant XTC carbon mountain bike.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity, or has knowledge of the whereabouts of the bike should contact Lowestoft police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/27785/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
