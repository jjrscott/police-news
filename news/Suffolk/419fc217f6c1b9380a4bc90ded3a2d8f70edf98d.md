Police are appealing for witnesses to a theft of boat from Woolverstone Marina.

The boat, a Ribeye A600, was taken at just after 5pm on Sunday 6 December from the marina and was seen being towed away by a vehicle.

The boat was on the hard-standing for winter storage. It is six metres in length RIB (rigid inflatable boat) complete with a Yamaha 150hp outboard, The engine serial number was Yamaha: 6HP-1001259.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour at the time of the incident or knows of the boat’s current whereabouts should contact Ipswich police, quoting crime reference 37/71421/20.
