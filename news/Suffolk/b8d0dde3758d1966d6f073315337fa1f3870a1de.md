Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Hadleigh.

It took place at a property on Ipswich Road at some point overnight between 10pm Wednesday 6 March and 7am on Thursday 7 March.

An unknown suspect gained access to a garage band stole a Yamaha YFZ50 quadbike and Stihl MS180 chainsaw worth in total £2,300.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any vehicle parked in a nearby layby overnight, saw any suspicious activity or knows of the bike’s whereabouts should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/13512/19

Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
