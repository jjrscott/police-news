Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Lowestoft.

The burglary took place on Sunday 8 July at a residential property on Kirkley Cliff Road near to Rectory Road in the town. Sometime between 2pm and 2.45pm entry was forced to the rear and numerous items of jewellery were stolen including wedding rings and gold earrings.

Witnesses or anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time are asked to contact police on 101 quoting reference 37944/18. Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
