A 42-year-old prisoner who absconded from Hollesley Bay has been arrested.

Robert Maxfield had been missing since approximately 4.45pm on Saturday 28 November, but he was detained earlier today, Sunday 29 November, in Northamptonshire.

Suffolk police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
