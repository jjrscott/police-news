Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of criminal damage in Lowestoft.

It happened at some point between 9.30pm at 18 December and 7.30am on Thursday 27 December.

An unknown person entered the secure Lowestoft dock near to Hamilton Road and climbed onto a fishing boat called the Serene Dawn. Using a sharp instrument they caused considerable damage to the fishing nets on this vessel, causing in the region of Â£5,000 damage to at least two trawler nets.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge regarding the incident should contact Lowestoft police on 101,quoting crime reference 37/74793/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
