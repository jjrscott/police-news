Police can confirm that they have identified a man sought in connection with a theft in Martlesham in October.

The CCTV image appeal was issued following an incident where a sum of cash belonging to a previous customer was taken from a cashpoint on Anson Road on 5 October 2017.

The man pictured in the images has subsequently been located and is being spoken to.
