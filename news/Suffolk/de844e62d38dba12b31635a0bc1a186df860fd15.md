Police are appealing for information following the theft of a bike in Ipswich.

The theft occurred at some point between 1.30pm and 3pm on Tuesday 14 May near Kenny’s café in Nacton Road.

An unknown person removed the locked black Carrera mountain bike by unknown means.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity or has information should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime reference: 37/27654/19.

Alternatively, contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

For further crime prevention advice regarding cycle security, visit: https://www.suffolk.police.uk/sites/suffolk/files/cyclesecurity_0.pdf
