A 77-year-old man reported missing from Kesgrave has been found.

John Beal was last seen in the Grange Farm area at 7pm yesterday, Tuesday 26 June, but has now been located.

Police would like to thank the public and media for their help in this matter.

## Previous release | 27 June 2018

Police are appealing for help to find a missing 77-year-old man from Kesgrave.

John Beal, who suffers from dementia, was last seen in the Grange Farm area at 7pm yesterday, Tuesday 26 June, and police and family members are concerned for his welfare.

He is described as white, 5ft 6ins tall, of medium build, balding and with a mole on top of his head. He was last seen wearing black jogging bottoms, a dark short-sleeved shirt and tan moccasins.

Police are asking anyone who has seen him or has any information on his whereabouts to contact Suffolk Police on 101.
