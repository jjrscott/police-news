Police can confirm that a 38-year-old missing woman from Beck Row has been found.

Emma Nicholls was last seen in the village at 3pm on Sunday 19 August and was reported missing to police on Tuesday 21 August.

Emma was located in Brandon yesterday evening (Wednesday 22 August).

Officers would like to thank the public and media for their assistance with this appeal

## Previous release | 22 August 2018

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 38-year-old woman from Beck Row.

Emma Nicholls was last seen in the village at 3pm on Sunday 19 August and was reported missing to police yesterday, Tuesday 21 August. It is believed that she may now be in Brandon.

Officers and family members are concerned for Emma’s welfare and are appealing for anyone who believes they may have seen her to make contact.

Anyone with information on Emma’s whereabouts should call the duty sergeant at Mildenhall Police Station on 101.
