Police can confirm that a 76-year-old missing man from Bury St Edmunds has been found.

James McNair was found safe and well this afternoon, Monday 24 December.

Officers would like to thank the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | Monday 24 December

Police are appealing for assistance with locating a missing man from Bury St Edmunds.

James ‘Jim’ McNair, aged 76, was reported missing today, Monday 24 December, and was last seen at 12.45pm in the Glastonbury Road area, believed to be on his way to the Bristol Road area.

James is white with short silver hair, glasses and wears hearing aids in both ears. He was last seen wearing a blue/grey jacket, a blue shirt, olive green cardigan with beige trousers and brown boots.

Jim is described as vulnerable and officers and dog units are conducting a search of the area.

Anyone with any information or any sightings should contact Suffolk Police on 101, quoting incident number 188 of Monday 24 December.
