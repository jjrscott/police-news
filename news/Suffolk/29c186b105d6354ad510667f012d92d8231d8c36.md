Officers are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Rede near Bury St Edmunds.

The incident took place, yesterday, Sunday 17 June, on The Green sometime between 11.45am and 5pm. Offender/s entered the rear of the property and stole items including cash and jewellery.

Witnesses, or anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time above, or who has any information are asked to call police on 101 quoting reference 33246/1
