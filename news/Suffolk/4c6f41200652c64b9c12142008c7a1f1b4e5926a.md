Police searching for missing 52-year-old Richard Hull can confirm that the body of a man was discovered near to Leavenheath this afternoon, Sunday 21 January.

Officers, assisted by members of Suffolk Lowland Search & Rescue and specialist search dogs, conducted a search of the area today. The body of a man was later discovered in a wooded area close to the village earlier this afternoon.

A scene is currently in place and investigations continue into the circumstances leading up to the man's death.

Formal identification is yet to take place but the man is believed to be missing person Richard Hull, who was reported missing to police yesterday, Saturday 20 January.

A file will be prepared for the coroner in due course.

## Previous release 21 January

Police are appealing for help in tracing a missing 52-year-old man from Leavenheath

Richard Hull was last seen on Wrights Way, Leavenheath around 12.30pm yesterday, Saturday 20 January. He was reported missing to police later that afternoon.

Richard is described as white, slim build, 5 feet 8 inches tall with short mousey coloured hair. When last seen he was wearing a brown Crombie type coat, navy trousers an off-white fleece top and brown shoes.

Officers are very concerned for his welfare and are asking anyone who has seen Richard or who has any information on where he may be, to contact Suffolk Police on 101
