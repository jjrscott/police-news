Police are releasing CCTV images of two people officers would like to trace following a theft from a business premises in Ipswich.

Around 1.30pm on Thursday 7 September it is reported that a man and a woman entered a shop on Carr Street in the town and put a package of computer software and a computer game, into their pram and then left the shop without paying..

Police are keen to speak to the man and woman pictured in connection with the incident.

Anyone with information about who the man or woman are should call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 37/60450/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
