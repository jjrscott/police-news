Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in East Bergholt.

The incident occurred between 8:15am and 5:50pm on Wednesday 20 June at a house in the vicinity of Quintons Road.

An unknown suspect forced entry into the property via a door. Once inside, a search was carried out and a Rolex watch was stolen. It is not yet known if anything else was taken.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious persons or vehicles in the village on that day, or who has any information about this crime, is asked to contact South CID at Landmark House on 101 quoting reference 33948/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
