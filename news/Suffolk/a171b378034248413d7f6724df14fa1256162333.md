Police are appealing for information after the theft of a bicycle in West Ipswich.

The theft took place at a residential address on Wallace Road, at some point between 10pm on Thursday 27 October and 10am on Friday 28 October.

It is thought the suspects accessed the property via the garden gates and removed the bike from the rear of the house.

The bike is described as a bright blue and green hybrid cycle, with a serial number of AAS08 23032 engraved into the frame.

Witnesses or anyone with information relating to this theft is asked to contact the Ipswich Safer Neighbourhood Team on **101** , quoting reference 63133/16.
