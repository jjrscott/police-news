A missing 15-year-old girl from Ipswich has been found.

Beyonce Turay had last been seen in Norwich Road at 3.30pm on Monday 18 June but has now been found safe and well.

Officers would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 22 June 2018

Police are appealing for help in tracing a missing Ipswich teenager.

Beyonce Turay, aged 15, was last seen in Norwich Road at 3.30pm on Monday 18 June. It is believed she may have travelled to London.

Beyonce is described as black, 5ft 8ins tall, of medium build and with dark brown shoulder length hair with a top knot. She was last seen wearing light blue ripped jeans, a black and white Nike tracksuit top and white and grey Nike trainers.

Officers are concerned for the welfare of Beyonce and are appealing for anyone who may have seen her of know of her whereabouts to contact Suffolk Police on 101.
