Detectives are appealing for information following a burglary in Kirkley where a quantity of jewellery was stolen.

The break in occurred at some point between 4:30pm and 9pm on Wednesday 8 February at an address in London Road South, near to the junction with Kendal Road.

It is thought entry was gained through a window to the side of the property.

Police would be interested in speaking to anyone who may have seen or heard any suspicious activity in the vicinity of London Road South and Kendal Road during the times indicated above.

Anyone with information should contact Lowestoft CID on **101** , quoting reference 16718/17. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers, anonymously if required, on **0800 555 111**.
