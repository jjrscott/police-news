Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary south of Bury St Edmunds.

The break in happened at a home address near Nowton Road, Nowton, at some point between 7:40am and 5:30pm on Monday 5 December, with entry forced to the rear of the property.

An untidy search was then made and items stolen include jewellery and two laptops.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who may have seen or heard any suspicious activity in the area. Anyone with information is urged to contact Bury St Edmunds CID on **101** , quoting reference 70069/16.
