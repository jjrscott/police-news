Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary, theft and collision in Trimley St Mary.

Officers were called at 11.15pm last night, Sunday 19 March, to reports that a white Vauxhall Vectra car had collided with a building on High Road.

The driver of the vehicle, reportedly a woman aged between 20 and 40 years of age, with dark hair, who was wearing light coloured clothing like pyjamas, left the scene and ran off in the direction of Station Road. Nobody is thought to have been injured as a result of the collision.
