Police are appealing for witnesses or information following an incident of theft in Stoke by Nayland.

At some point between 5pm on Tuesday 13 November and 7.30am on Wednesday 14 November, unknown offender/s stole a Mitsubishi 14KW ECODAN Air Source Heat Pump (model number PUHLIDHW14UHA2BS and serial number 09U01336) from a construction site in School Street.

Anyone who has witnessed the sudden arrival of such an item or who may have been offered the item for sale are asked to call Suffolk police on 101 quoting reference 66318/18.

Alternatively, contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
