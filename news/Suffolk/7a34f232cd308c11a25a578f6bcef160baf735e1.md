A 21-year-old Lowestoft man is due to appear in court this morning charged with burglary.

Officers were called at approximately 9:15pm on Tuesday, 31 January, to reports of a burglary at a licensed premises in White Lanes, Kessingland.

Joseph Haskins, of High Street Kessingland, was challenged by staff who detained him on suspicion of stealing a quantity of alcohol and police were called.

He was taken into custody at Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre where he was later charged with a number of offences, including burglary and two counts of assault.

He is due to appear before Great Yarmouth Magistrates Court this morning, Monday 3 April.
