Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a number of catalytic thefts in Haverhill yesterday evening (Monday 15 March).

The thefts occurred around 8pm and 8.25pm in the evening. Witnesses reported seeing three unknown males wearing balaclavas in Clements Lane. The offenders have jacked up a Lexus vehicle and removed the catalytic converter.

The offenders have left in a black Audi saloon type vehicle - crime reference 37/12751/21.

Catalytic converter thefts also occurred in Hollands Road when a Mercedes vehicle had the cat converter removed - crime reference 37/12753/21, and in Crowland Road, a Honda vehicle had the cat converter removed - crime reference 37/12757/21.

In Moon Hall Lane around 8pm on Monday 15 March number plates were stolen from a black BMW and it is thought these were used on the Audi vehicle used by the offenders -37/12760/21.

If you noticed any unusual activity or have any information about these thefts please contact Suffolk Police quoting crime references above via -

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Phone – Call 101
