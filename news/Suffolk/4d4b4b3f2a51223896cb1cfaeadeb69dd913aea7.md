Two people have been arrested on suspicion of criminal damage in Ipswich.

Officers were alerted to banging noises just after midnight (Friday 20 November) on Ainslie Road in the town.

They located two males trying car door handles on parked and unattended vehicles. Enquiries established around seven vehicles had sustained damage to their wing mirrors.

Two males both aged 20, were arrested on suspicion of criminal damage and taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning where they remain.
