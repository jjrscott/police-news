Police are appealing for witnesses following the theft of a bike in Ipswich.

The incident occurred between 4pm and 5pm on Friday 8 May in Felixstowe Road.

At some point during the times stated, a locked red Chris Boardman Roadster bike was stolen from within the bicycle storage area at Sainsbury’s Warren Heath.

Anyone with information about this theft, or who believes they have seen a bike matching this description, is asked to contact Suffolk police quoting reference: 37/25531/20
