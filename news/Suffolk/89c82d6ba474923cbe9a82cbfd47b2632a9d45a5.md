Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary in Felixstowe.

The break-in took place in Dellwood Avenue at some point between 7:25am and 8:25am on Friday 30 June.

Entry was forced to the rear of the home and a search of the property was made. A quantity of cash and jewellery, including an antique ladies yellow gold watch with a white face and brown leather strap, were stolen.

Anyone who witnessed anything suspicious at the times indicated, has any information about the incident, or who may know individuals trying to sell a watch that matches the description above, are asked to contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference 45219/37.
