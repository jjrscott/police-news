Police are appealing for help to locate the owner of various items of medieval re-enactment gear in Ipswich.

The items were found yesterday (Monday 14 December) at about 12.15pm by a member of the public at water treatment works on Sproughton Road.

Anyone knows who they belong to or has information at to how they got there should contact Ipswich police, quoting CAD 121 of 14 December 2020.

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
