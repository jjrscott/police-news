Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Elmswell.

The incident occurred at some time between 4pm on Friday 20 November and 8.50pm on Sunday 22 November, in Rose Lane.

Entry was gained to a house and items of jewellery and cash were stolen from within.

Detectives would like to hear from anyone with information about this crime and in particular anyone who may have witnessed any suspicious activity in the vicinity of Rose Lane on Saturday 21 November, at around 12.10pm or 6.40pm.

Any witnesses are asked to contact West CID at Bury St Edmunds Police Station, quoting reference: 67865/20.
