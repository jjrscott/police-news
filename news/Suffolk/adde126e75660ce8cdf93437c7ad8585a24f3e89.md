Police are appealing for witnesses in connection with a robbery in Bury St Edmunds.

It happened on 28 June just after 11pm when a man aged in his 70s was robbery of his money.

It is not exactly clear where the attack took place but possibly in the alley from Kings Road in Bury St Edmunds to Waitrose car park. A man meet the victim near to shops in Arc shopping centre a few minutes before the attack where a small quantity of money was taken from the victim. He was taken to West Suffolk hospital for treatment to a back injury and has now been released.

The offender is described as white and aged in his 40s.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it should contact Bury St Edmunds CID on 101 quoting crime reference 37/35956/18. Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
