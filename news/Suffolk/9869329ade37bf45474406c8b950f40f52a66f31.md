Anthony Hagger who was reported as missing to police last year in October as been located.

The 55 year old went missing after being released from Hollesley Bay prison on temporary licence to receive treatment at Ipswich Hospital on 11 October.

Hagger, who is serving a life sentence for firearm and robbery offences, handed himself in to Exeter police yesterday evening (29 January) and will be returned to the prison system.

Police would like to thank the media and the public for their assistance with this appeal.
