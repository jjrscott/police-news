A missing 26-year-old man from the Leiston and Saxmundham area has been found.

Kadeem Walton was reported missing on Wednesday 7 February and had not been seen since.

He presented himself to officers this afternoon, Friday 2 March, and is safe and well.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
