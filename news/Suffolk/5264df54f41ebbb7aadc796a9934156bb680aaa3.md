Police are appealing for witnesses to a theft of a caravan that took place from Ingham.

The incident happened at some point on Thursday 27 July at 2.45pm and 3.20pm at a property on The Street when the caravan was stolen whilst parked behind a locked gate.

The caravan is a two berth Swift Challenger 500 with no windows at the back and a large swift on the back. It also had a piece of orange metal where the wheel lock has been cut sticking out of the wheel.

Anyone who saw any suspicious activity in the area during the times stated or has knowledge of the eft should contact Suffolk police on 101 and quote crime number 37/51737/17.
