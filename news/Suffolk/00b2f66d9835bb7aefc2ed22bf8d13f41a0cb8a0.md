A 77-year-old man has died following a flat fire in Ixworth.

Police were contacted by the Fire Service shortly after 1.30am today, Saturday 6 July, following reports of fire at a property in Peddars Close.

A joint police and fire investigation into this incident is now taking place, though the death is not being treated as suspicious at this time.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact Suffolk Police quoting reference CAD 36 of 6 July.
