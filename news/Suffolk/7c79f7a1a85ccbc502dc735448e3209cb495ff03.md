Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident where a dog bit the hand of a male in Ipswich.

It took place on Wednesday 24 March at about 7pm on Fore Street.

A 17 year old male was skateboard when a dog, described as a Border Collie on a lead, jumped up at the male and bit him on the hand, causing it to bleed.

The woman walking the dog is described as five foot seven inches tall, was wearing a green coat, jogger bottoms, and aged in in her late 40s or early 50s.

Anyone who knows the owner of the dog should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference37/14844/21
