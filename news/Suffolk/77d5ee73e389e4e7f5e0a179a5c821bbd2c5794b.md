Police are asking residents to be extra vigilant and to report any suspicious activity following a series of incidents in the Hadleigh area over the last few days.

A business premises in High Street was broken into between 5pm Wednesday 12th & 9.30am Thursday 13th December & cash was stolen from inside. (CAD reference 96 of 13th December)

At around 2.30am this morning, Friday 14th December, another premises on High Street was broken into. A glass door panel was smashed and a number of electrical items were stolen. (Crime reference 37/72148/18)

Also in Hadleigh, an orange Hitachi ZX27U-2 mini digger was stolen from a site near Bridge Street sometime between 29th November & 12th December. (CAD reference 177 of 13th December)

Nearby in Holton St Mary, a house was broken into in Hadleigh Road between 4.45pm yesterday, Thursday 13th & 7.40am this morning, Friday 14th December. A number of power tools were stolen. (CAD reference 53 of 14th December)

If you have any information about any of these incidents or noticed any unusual activity nearby please contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting the relevant crime or CAD reference.

Alternatively, use the online crime reporting link - http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/report-something.

Officers are urging residents to review security for their homes, outbuildings & businesses. Security advice can be found on the ‘advice’ pages of the Suffolk Police webpage www.suffolk.police.uk.
