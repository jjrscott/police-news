Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a home address in Mildenhall.

The break-in occurred between 6:30pm and 10:30pm on Monday 27 November at a property in Macpherson Robertson Way.

At some point between the times stated the offender(s) forced entry by smashing the back door before an untidy search was carried out. Various items were stolen including a pair of Nike trainers, a jar and an amount of cash.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any knowledge of it should contact Bury St Edmunds CID on 101, quoting crime reference 76192/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
