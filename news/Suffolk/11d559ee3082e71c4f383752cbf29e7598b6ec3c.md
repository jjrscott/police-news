Police are appealing for information after the windows to an allotment were smashed in Lowestoft.

The incident took place in Water Lane at some point between 5pm on Friday 14 and 8am on Monday 17 July.

Three panes of glass were smashed, believed to have been cause by air rifle pellets.

Police are reminding members of the public that air weapons are not toys and that any irresponsible use is taken very seriously.

Those with information regarding this incident of criminal damage are urged to contact the Neighbourhood Response Team in Lowestoft on 101 quoting reference 49019/17.
