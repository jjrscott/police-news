Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a business premises in Woodbridge.

The incident occurred at approximately 11:45pm on Thursday 9 November at an address on Cumberland Street, close to the roundabout where the road becomes Ipswich Road.

Offender(s) forced entry to an outbuilding by smashing a window and conducted a search of the property but fled the scene as the alarm was activated. It is believed that nothing was stolen.

Witnesses, or anyone with information related to the incident, are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 72582/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
