A 24-year-old man has been charged in connection with a burglary that took place in Ipswich earlier this year.

The incident occurred between Tuesday 6 March and Saturday 10 March at an address in Waterloo Road.

The back door was forced open to gain entry to the property and items including two guitars, a carriage clock and a television were stolen from inside.

Sorin Berlea, of Chevallier Street, Ipswich, was arrested by officers investigating this crime on Saturday 7 July and taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning.
