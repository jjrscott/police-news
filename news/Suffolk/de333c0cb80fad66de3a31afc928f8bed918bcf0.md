Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident of criminal damage and theft in Belstead, near Ipswich.

At some point between Sunday 27 April at 4pm and Wednesday 30 April at 7am, offenders gained entry to a building site on Grove Hill in the village.

Damage was caused to an excavator, wood shredder and a dumper truck, and an amount of diesel was stolen from the site.

Police would like to hear from anyone who saw any suspicious activity in the area at or around the times stated.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/23304/18.
