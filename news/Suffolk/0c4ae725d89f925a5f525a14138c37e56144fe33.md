Officers are appealing for witnesses after a motorhome was stolen in Newmarket.

Sometime between 7.00am on Saturday 2 June and 8pm on Thursday 5 June a motorhome vehicle was stolen from a driveway on Tannersfield Way.

The blue and white Transit motorhome, registration number FX09 CFE, contained a Honda EU30is generator and a large amount of equipment for go-kart racing including helmets, gloves, racing suits, transponders, boots and cameras etc

Witnesses or anybody who has any information are asked to call police on 101 quoting reference 31607/18. Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555
