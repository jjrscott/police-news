A 16-year-old girl from Ipswich is no longer missing.

Niamh Simons had last been seen at 1.40pm on Monday 9 November, but returned home safe and well yesterday evening, Tuesday 10 November.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
