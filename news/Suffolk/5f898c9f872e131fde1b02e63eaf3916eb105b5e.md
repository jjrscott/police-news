A teenager who went missing from Ipswich has been found.

Matas Vaitekunuas was reported as missing to police on Monday 20 November.

He has now been found safe and well.

We would like to thank the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | Friday 24 November

Police are appealing for help to trace a 14 year old teenager who has gone missing from Ipswich.

Matas Vaitekunuas was reported as missing to police on Monday 20 November and was last seen by his mother at the home address on Victoria Street on Friday 17 November and last seen by a friend on Sunday 19 November.

He is described as slim build, with short spiked brown hair and is about 4 foot, 11 inches tall.

Anyone who has seen Matas, a teenager matching the description given or knows of his whereabouts should contact Ipswich police on 101.
