Police are appealing for witnesses following a robbery in Woodbridge.

The incident took place in Jetty Lane at 9.10pm yesterday, Wednesday 8 August.

A 14-year-old girl was walking alongside the car park when she was pushed to the floor, dropping her mobile phone and money. An unknown offender then picked up the items and ran away. As a result of the incident, the victim sustained a small graze to her knee.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious activity or has knowledge of the incident should contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/45046/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
