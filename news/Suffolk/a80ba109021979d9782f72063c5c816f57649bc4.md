INSPECTION OF THE STATEMENTS OF ACCOUNTS OF THE POLICE AND CRIME COMMISSIONER FOR SUFFOLK AND THE CHIEF CONSTABLE OF SUFFOLK CONSTABULARY

The Accounts and Audit (England) Regulations 2015 – Section 16

Audit Commission Act 1998 – Section 14

Notice is given that the audit of the Statements of Accounts of the above-mentioned bodies for the financial year ended 31 March 2017 has been concluded. The Statements of Accounts are available for inspection by any local government elector for the area of the specified bodies at Suffolk Police Headquarters, Martlesham Heath, Ipswich, Suffolk, IP5 3QS on working days on and after 2 October 2017, between the hours of 10.00am and 4.00pm when any such elector may make copies of the Statements. The Statements can be found here.

Copies of the Statement of Accounts will be delivered to any local government elector upon request.

Chris Bland
Chief Finance Officer for the Police and Crime Commissioner and the Chief Constable of Suffolk Constabulary.
