Police are appealing for witnesses following a robbery in Ipswich yesterday, Tuesday 28 February.

At approximately 12:30pm three men approached a man in his 20s, who was walking along Norwich Road near the junction of Benezet Street, and asked him for the time.

The victim has reported he was then pulled into an alcove on Gaye Street, threatened with a knife and ordered to give the men his watch.

The first man is described as black, approximately 5ft 9ins (1.8metres) tall, of skinny build and was wearing dark clothing including a hoody-style jumper and tracksuit bottoms. He was carrying a silver draw-string bag and a small bag across his body.

The second man is described as white, approximately 5ft 7ins (1.75 metres) tall and of slim build. He was wearing a black puffa-style jacket and a black baseball cap.
