Police are appealing for witnesses or information after a teenager was assaulted in Lowestoft.

The incident took place on Friday 18 January at around 7.30pm in Gunton Community Park, behind Montgomery Avenue.

A male teenager was playing football with two friends in the park when an unknown male and female approached him. The male then punched him in the face, causing him to sustain a cut lip.

The male suspect is described as white, with a tanned complexion, aged in his mid-teens, 5ft 11ins with black hair. He was wearing a grey tracksuit with no hood.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or saw any suspicious behaviour should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/4238/19.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
