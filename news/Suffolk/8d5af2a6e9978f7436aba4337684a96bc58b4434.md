Police are appealing for witnesses after a burglary that took place in Cotton near Stowmarket.

It took place at a property in Broad Road at some point between 4.10pm and 6.55pm on Thursday 25 January.

During this period unknown offender(s) have entered the property by smashing a glass pane in a rear patio door.

An untidy search of the upstairs was carried out with a number of items of jewellery stolen.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any knowledge of it should contact West CID in Bury St Edmunds Police Station on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/4773/18.
