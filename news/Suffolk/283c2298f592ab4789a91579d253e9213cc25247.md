Police are appealing for witnesses after religious silverware and an iPad were stolen from a parked car in Lackford.

The incident occurred between 4pm and 5.30pm on Monday 27 April, when an unknown person entered a parked Citroen C3 within the car park at Lackford Lakes.

A bag containing a black case with an antique solid silver chalice and paten, and an iPad were stolen from within.

Anyone with information about this theft, or who believes they have seen any items matching the descriptions, is asked to contact Suffolk police quoting reference: 37/23533/20
