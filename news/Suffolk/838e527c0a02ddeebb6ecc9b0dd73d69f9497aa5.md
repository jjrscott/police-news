Police have charged a second man in connection with a robbery that took place in Pakefield on Friday 18 May 2018 at about 7.15pm. Two males entered the Ladbrooke’s shop on London Road South where a quantity of cash was taken from the till. Both males then left the shop walking up London Road South with the cash. A 34 year old man was arrested on Tuesday 10 July and taken to Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre for questioning. Grant Webster of Ratcliffe Lane in London was subsequently charged with robbery yesterday. 31 year old Sonny Booty of Rutland Drive in Hornchurch was also charged with robbery.on 30 June. He was remanded into custody to appear before Ipswich Magistrates Court today (Wednesday 11 July).

## Previous release I 24 May 2018

Police are appealing for help to identify two people they want to speak to in connection with a robbery that took place in Pakefield on Friday 18 May 2018 at about 7.15pm.

Two males entered the Ladbroke’s shop on London Road South with an older white male that was referred to as ‘Dad’. One of the two males then jumped across the counter and snatched a quantity of cash from the till. Offenders attempted to assault the manager of the store, who was uninjured, when he intervened. Both males then left the shop walking up London Road South with the cash.

Anyone who may has knowledge of the incident or saw the crime should contact Lowestoft CID on 101 quoting ref 37/27121/18 or the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
