A prisoner from Hollesley Bay who absconded in October has been located.

Anthony Smith, aged 46, was reported missing to police on Tuesday 23 October, after being absent from a roll call and is serving an eight year, six month sentence for burglary.

He was found in the Brentwood area of Essex yesterday morning (Wednesday 5 December)

He will be returned to the prison in due course.

Police would like to thanks the public and media for their help with this matter.
