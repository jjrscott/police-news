Police are appealing for information after outbuildings at a property in Kelsale were broken into and items including bicycles and tools were stolen.

The incident occurred at some time between 11pm on Thursday 19 November and 8am on Friday 20 November, at a property in East View.

Entry was forced to a number of outbuildings at the property and items were stolen including the following:

\- a black, yellow and blue Cube Hanzz pro Mountain Bike, with 26in wheels
\- a blue Transition TR500, with Fox 40 forks and 27.5in wheels
\- a Stihl MS 390 chain saw with a 50cm chain bar
\- a Makita DAB radio
\- a Leatherman multitool

Photographs of some of the stolen items can be seen in the gallery below.

Anyone with information about this burglary, or who may have been offered items matching the descriptions above to purchase, is asked to contact the Crime Coordination Centre, quoting reference: 67386/20.
