Police are appealing for information after two mountain bikes were stolen from a home address in Beccles following a burglary.

The break in occurred at a premises in Grove Road at some point between 10:30am on Tuesday 26 and 1:30pm on Wednesday 27 September. It is thought access was gained via the rear of the property.

The first bike is described as white and blue, with ‘Ride’ adorned on the left handle bar grip and ‘Harder’ on the right hand grip; the second is a black Scott Tampico bike.

Anyone with information regarding the theft is urged to call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 63619/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
