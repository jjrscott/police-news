Police can confirm that a 51-year-old woman from Ipswich has been found.

Lesley Edmunds was located in the town yesterday evening (Monday 22 July).

Officers would like to thank the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 22 July 2019

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 51-year-old woman from Ipswich.

Lesley Edmunds was last seen at around 12.30pm yesterday, Sunday 21 July, at her home address in the town. She was reported missing to police shortly after 7.20pm last night.

Lesley is described as white, 4ft 11ins, of a slim build, with shoulder length blonde hair and blue eyes. She was wearing a white top, blue hoody and jeans.
