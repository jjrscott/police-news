Police can confirm that a woman believed to be in her 80s has sadly died in a road traffic collision in Stanstead, near Long Melford.

Police were called at 3.15pm yesterday Monday 26 June, to reports of a serious road traffic collision on the B1066 Lower Street, between a grey BMW and a female pedestrian.

The East of England Ambulance service and the East Anglian Air Ambulance also attended the scene.

The driver of the BMW, a woman, suffered minor injuries and was assessed at the scene by Ambulance staff.

The road was closed in both directions to allow an investigation to take place, but has now reopened.

Any witnesses to the collision should contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on **101** quoting reference number 306 of yesterday, Monday 26 June.
