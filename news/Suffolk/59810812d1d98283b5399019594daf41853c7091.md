A man police wanted to speak to in connection with an ongoing investigation in Bury St Edmunds has been identified after an image of him was issued.

Officers had appealed for this individual to come forward to assist with their enquiries and he contacted police on Saturday 12 May and was subsequently arrested and interviewed. He has been released on bail until Monday 4 June pending further enquiries.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 11 May 2018

Police are issuing an image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with an ongoing investigation in Bury St Edmunds.

The man was wearing a white ‘Thrasher’ t-shirt and blue shorts.

Officers are appealing for this individual to come forward as he may be able to help with their enquiries. Alternatively anyone who believes they know him is asked to make contact.

Calls should be made to Bury St Edmunds Safer Neighbourhood Team on 101 quoting reference 24662/18.
