A man who was reported missing from Lowestoft yesterday, has been found.

David White, 77, had last been seen in the Lowestoft area at around 1.30pm yesterday, Tuesday 16 October.

He was found safe and well in the evening.

Police would like to thank the media and the public for their help with the matter.

## Previous release | Tuesday 16 October

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 77-year-old man from Sprowston in Norfolk.

David White was last seen in the Lowestoft area at around 1.30pm today, Tuesday 16 October.

He is described as white, 5ft 2ins tall, of medium build and is clean shaven with short hair. He was last seen wearing a long brown coat and navy trousers. He also wears a hearing aid and glasses.

David lives in Norfolk and it is believed he may try and travel back there by public transport.

Anyone who has seen David, or who has any information regarding his whereabouts, is asked to contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting CAD 230 of today.
