Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision in Leavenheath.

At 1:35pm yesterday, Thursday 3 May, officers were called following reports of a collision between a car and a motorcycle on the A134.

The motorcyclist, a man in his 40s, sustained head, spine and neck injuries and was taken to Addenbrookeâ€™s Hospital by Essex and Herts Air Ambulance where he remains in a critical condition.

A road closure was put in place between Stoke Road in Leavenheath and Bear Lane in Nayland while police and emergency services were at the scene. Traffic was diverted through Stoke by Nayland.

Police are appealing for witnesses or anyone who may have information concerning the manner of driving of either of the vehicles beforehand.

Anyone with any information should contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting incident number 189 of 3 May.

## Previous release | 3 May 2018

Emergency services are currently at the scene of a serious road traffic collision in Leavenheath.

Police were called at 1:35pm this afternoon, Thursday 3 May, to reports of a collision between a car and motorbike on the A134.

A road closure is in place between Stoke Road in Leavenheath and Bear Lane in Nayland. Traffic is being diverted through Stoke by Nayland. Motorists are advised to avoid the area if possible.

Any witnesses to this incident are asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting reference CAD 189 of 3 May.
