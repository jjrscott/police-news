Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident in Lakenheath.

Officers were called to a report of three men trying to smash the windows of the Co-op store in High Street at around 00.50am today, Friday 20 July.

The three suspects are believed to have smashed their way through the front door and left a short time later carrying a quantity of cigarettes in white bags.

The suspects made off in a white Ford Transit-type vehicle.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting reference 37/40477/18. Alternatively contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
