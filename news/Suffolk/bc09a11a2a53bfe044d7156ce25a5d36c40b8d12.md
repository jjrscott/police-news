Police are appealing for witnesses to an attempted arson incident in Newmarket.

It took place on Sunday evening (20 December) at about 8.15pm at the Guineas Shopping Centre on Fred Archer Way in the town.

Unknown person(s) made attempts to set light to pieces of cardboard and its believed they have used white spirit as an accelerant. Five youths were also thought to have been seen running from the area at the time.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour or knows who was responsible, please contact Newmarket police quoting crime reference 37/73738/20

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
