Police are appealing for witnesses to a serious road traffic collision in Beccles last week.

Officers were called at around 8.10am on Friday 19 October to reports that a white Toyota Yaris had been involved in a collision with a red Ford Fiesta on Queen Elizabeth Drive, at the junction with Duke Street.

The driver of the Toyota Yaris, a woman in her 80s, was taken to the James Paget Hospital with serious chest injuries. She remains in hospital in a serious but stable condition.

The Ford Fiesta driver sustained minor injuries and was also taken to the James Paget Hospital, but has since been discharged.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who may have witnessed the collision, or the driving manner of the vehicles before the collision, or who may have any has dash-cam footage.

Anyone with information should contact PC Callum Walchester at Acle Roads and Armed Policing Team by calling 101 and quoting reference CAD 60 of 19 October.
