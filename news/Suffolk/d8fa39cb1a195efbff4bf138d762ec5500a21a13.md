A missing 12-year-old boy from Stowmarket has been found.

Travis Rowney was last seen earlier today, Monday 10 August, at 4pm in the town and he was reported missing to police shortly after 7.10pm.

Travis was found shortly after 10pm this evening.

Police would like to thank members of the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | Monday 10 August

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 12-year-old boy from Stowmarket.

Travis Rowney was last seen earlier today, Monday 10 August, at 4pm in the town and he was reported missing to police shortly after 7.10pm.

He is described as white, 5ft 4ins tall, of slim build, has freckles, short light brown hair and blue eyes. His hair is currently shorter than pictured.
