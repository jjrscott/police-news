Police can confirm that the body of a man has been found in Stutton this morning.

Officers were called at 7.20am, today (Friday 16 October) following reports that a body of a man had been found in the water near Alton Water Sports Centre in Holbrook Road.

The fire service and ambulance service were also called to the location but the man was sadly pronounced deceased.

The death is being treated as unexplained at this time but police do not currently believe there are any suspicious circumstances.

A file will be prepared for the coroner in due course.
