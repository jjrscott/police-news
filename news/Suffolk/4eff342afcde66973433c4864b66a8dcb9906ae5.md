Police have arrested a 36-year-old man who was wanted.

Frank Benet, who has links to the Lowestoft area, was wanted for burglary offences and was also wanted on recall to prison for breaching the terms of his licence.

He was located and arrested on Friday 8 June and taken to Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

Benet of no fixed abode was subsequently charged with burglary at a property on Newland Close in Lowestoft in March, and three other burglaries that took place in May. One was on Aldeburgh Road in Leiston, a second on School Lane in Heckingham and a third at a property on Burstall Lane in Sproughton.

He appeared before Ipswich Magistrates Court on Saturday 9 June and remanded.

Police would to thank the media and the public for their assistance with the wanted appeal.
