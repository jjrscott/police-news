A missing 41-year-old man from the Ipswich area has been found.

Jamie Boreham had last been seen in Ipswich at approximately 9am on Tuesday 1 May.

He was found safe and well by police in Colchester yesterday evening, Tuesday 8 May.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
