Police are appealing for information following a collision in Felixstowe at the weekend.

On Saturday 13 January at approximately 2.50pm, a black VW Golf and a motorcycle collided on Undercliff Road West.

The motorcycle rider, a man in his 40s, was taken to hospital with serious injuries.

Police would be interested in speaking to any witnesses to the collision.

Anyone with any information should contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting incident number 234 of 13 January.
