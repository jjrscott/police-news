A young woman has died following a collision on the A47 north of Lowestoft yesterday.

The woman, who was on foot, was in collision with an HGV on the north bound carriageway, near Jay Lane, around 2.50pm yesterday, Monday 22 May.

Emergency services attended and the young woman, from Lowestoft, was taken to the James Paget hospital with serious injuries, however she died just before 10pm last night.

The northbound carriageway on the A47 re-opened around 7.25pm last night, after police carried out an initial investigation at the scene.

Police would like to thank those who have already called in and continue to ask anyone who saw the incident to call the Serious Collision Investigation Team at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting CAD number 261 of Monday 22 May.
