A 21 year-old man who was charged following a burglary in Sudbury has entered a guilty plea at Ipswich Crown Court.

The burglary took place on Monday 14 November at an address on Waldingfield Road. A wallet, purse and car keys were reported stolen from the property.

Shaun Henson, aged 21 from Tilbrook Drive, St Helenâ€™s, Merseyside was arrested on 18 November and charged on 19 November with theft of a wallet, purse and car keys, dangerous driving, driving a vehicle whilst disqualified and driving without motor insurance. He was also charged with the theft of four sets of keys from a vehicle in Sudbury on 13 November.

Henson appeared at Ipswich Crown Court yesterday, 19 December 2016 and was given a 996 days (just over 2 years and eight and half months) prison sentence.
