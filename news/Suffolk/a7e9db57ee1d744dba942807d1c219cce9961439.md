Police are releasing a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with an incident of theft in Stowmarket.

The crime took place in the early hours of the morning on Friday 10 November at a business address in Combs Ford.

A CCTV camera had its wires cut and was then removed from the front of the building.

Officers are issuing an image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the theft.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 73025/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
