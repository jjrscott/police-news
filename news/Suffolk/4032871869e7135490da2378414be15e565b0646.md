A man has been charged in connection with burglary at a house in Lowestoft.

The break-in took place between Wednesday 14 March and Friday 23 March at a property in Chaulkers Crescent.

At some point between these dates, a kitchen window was forced and a number of items were stolen.

Christopher Edgington, 21, of Eastwood Avenue, Lowestoft, was arrested yesterday, Tuesday 24 April, in connection with this incident and taken to Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre for questioning.
