A 29-year-old man from Lowestoft is due to appear before magistrates this morning charged with robbery and a number of other offences.

Ben McRoberts, of no fixed abode, was arrested yesterday, Friday 10 January, in connection with an armed robbery incident at an address in the Surrey Street area of Lowestoft on Tuesday 7 March.

He was taken into custody and has now been charged with the robbery of a man on March 7, where cash, a mobile phone and a bank card were stolen, possession of an imitation firearm in relation to the same incident, a burglary on March 6, also in Surrey Street, where a guitar, a suitcase and speakers were stolen, and possession of an imitation firearm and possession of a knife yesterday, Friday 10 March.

He has been held in custody overnight to appear before Norwich Magistrates from 10am this morning, Saturday 11 March.
