A 33-year-old man who absconded from Hollesley Bay prison has been detained in Sussex.

James Regan was found to be missing from the prison at around 8.30am yesterday morning, Saturday 13 January.

He was arrested last night just before midnight in Hastings and will now be returned to the prison system.
