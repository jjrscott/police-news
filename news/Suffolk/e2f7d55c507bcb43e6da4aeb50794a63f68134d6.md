Police can confirm that a man has died following an incident in Walberswick.

Emergency services were called at 3:20pm today, Sunday 7th February, to reports of concern for safety of a man in the sea off Walberswick beach.

HM Coastguard, East of England Ambulance Service and police attended the scene.

The man in his 50s, who is thought to have been kite-surfing, was found by members of the public on the shore. Despite receiving medical attention, the man sadly died at the scene. His next of kin have been informed.

An investigation into the incident is continuing but at this stage there are not thought to be any suspicious circumstances

A file will be prepared for the coroner in due course.

Witnesses or anybody with any information is asked to contact Halesworth Police, quoting CAD 189 of 7th February via,

Phone – Call 101

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
