Police appealing for witnesses to an incident of indecent exposure that took place in Beccles.

It happened on Thursday 18 March at about 3pm in Black Boy Meadow in the town.

The victim, a woman in her 30s, saw a male indecently expose himself by a tree before he walked off laughing as he went.

The suspect is described a black, aged25 to 30 years old, about five foot , six inches tall, of medium build and was clean shaven. He was wearing a black bomber jacket and a blue top and light blue denim jeans

Anyone who knows who was responsible or has saw the incident at the time should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/13485/21.
