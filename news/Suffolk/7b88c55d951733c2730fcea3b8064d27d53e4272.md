The body of a man has been found in Ipswich.

Police, NPAS helicopter and Suffolk Lowland Search and Rescue personnel had been conducting searches for missing 57-year-old man Derrick Chapman when they discovered the body in woodlands near Bobbits Lane at approximately 11:20am this morning, Saturday 1 July.

Mr Chapman was reported to police as missing from his home address at approximately 1pm yesterday.

The body is believed to be Derrick Chapman, but formal identification is yet to take place. The death is not being treated as suspicious and a file will now be prepared for the coroner.
