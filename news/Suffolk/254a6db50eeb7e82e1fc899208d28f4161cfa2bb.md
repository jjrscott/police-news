Police are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision in Framlingham yesterday, Wednesday 10 May.

Officers were called by the Ambulance Service just before 4:20pm to reports of a collision between a car, a black Ford Focus, and a pedestrian in Riverside.

A woman in her 90s suffered serious injuries and was taken to Ipswich Hospital for treatment.

Witnesses to the incident are asked to contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting CAD 294 of 10 May.
