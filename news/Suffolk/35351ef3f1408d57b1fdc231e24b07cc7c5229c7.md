Police have arrested and charged a 35 year man with robbery and burglary offences.

Antonio Wells of no fixed abode was arrested yesterday afternoon in Ipswich and taken to Marltesham Police investigation centre for questioning.

He has been charged with robbery of a restaurant in Ipswich on Thursday 8 February on Duke Street where a quantity of cash was stolen. He has also been charged with burglary of a business on Wentworth Road in Ipswich on 8 February where a large amount of power tools were stolen, a further burglary on Felixstowe Road In Ipswich on the same day where a power drill was stolen and a third burglary on Roebeck Road in Ipswich at some point between 8 and 9 February where car keys were stolen and fishing equipment and also charged with theft of a motor vehicle.

He was remanded in custody to appear before Ipswich Magistrates Court this morning (22 February).
