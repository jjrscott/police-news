Police are appealing for information after buildings were vandalised near to The Esplanade in Lowestoft.

At some point between Thursday 24 December and Sunday 27 December, guttering on beach huts along the lower promenade was pulled down.

This was followed by another incident which took place between Sunday 27 December and Tuesday 29 December, where guttering on Claremont Pier was also damaged.

Any witnesses, or anyone with information about these incidents, is asked to contact the Crime Coordination Centre, quoting reference 74744/20.
