Officers are appealing for information after a burglary in Ipswich.

The incident took place at a property on Trent Road in the town on Thursday 3 January sometime between 8.30am and 10.30pm.

Offender/s smashed the back door to gain entry and stole four televisions and a Playstation console. Vehicle keys were also stolen which were then used to take a grey Vauxhall Insignia car, index mark AV61 BYB, parked on the driveway.

Witnesses or anybody who has any information are asked to call Police on 101 quoting reference 564/19. Alternatively call the charity Crimestopppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
