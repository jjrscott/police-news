Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Sudbury.

The incident happened at some point between 4:30pm and 6:40pm on Tuesday 23 February at a property on Windermere Road.

The offender(s) is believed to have gained access to the property via an open window. An untidy search was then conducted and bank cards and checking books were taken.

Anyone who saw any suspicious activity, has any knowledge of the incident or knows the whereabouts of the items stolen they should Bury St Edmunds CID, quoting 37/9171/21 via

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Phone – Call 101

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
