Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place at Great Glemham.

It took place at some point between Monday 8 March at 10.30pm and Tuesday 9 March at 4pm.

The offender(s) gained entry to a garage by cutting the padlock and stealing a mountain bike – described as a 2016 grey and orange On-One Codeine make and an Apple laptop.

Anyone who saw any suspicious activity or knows who was responsible should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/11846/21.Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
