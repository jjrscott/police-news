Officers are appealing for witnesses or information after a burglary at a church in Newmarket

The incident took place sometime between 8.30pm on Sunday 9 September and 9.15am on Monday 10 September, at All Saints Church, All Saints Road in the town.

Offender/s forced entry to the church by smashing a valuable Victorian arched stained glass window. A cupboard and a donations box were then broken into and a small amount of cash stolen.

Witnesses or anybody with any information are asked to call Police on 101 quoting reference 52905/18. Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
