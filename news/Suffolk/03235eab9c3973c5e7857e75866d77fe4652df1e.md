Police are appealing for witnesses after a burglary in Newmarket.

The incident occurred at some time between 9.30am on Friday 4 May and 1.30pm yesterday, Monday 7 May, at an address in Bury Road.

Entry was gained to the property by unknown means and items of jewellery and perfume were stolen from within.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity in the area during the times stated above, or who has any information about this crime, is asked to contact West CID at Bury St Edmunds Police Station on 101 quoting reference 24778/18. Alternatively call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
