Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident in Pakefield.

A boy, of high school age, was walking along London Road at approximately 7.50am yesterday, Monday 3 December, when as he passed a man, the man asked if he would like to buy a new bike from the back of a grey or white van. The boy then walked away.

The man is described as white, approximately aged in his 60s, between 5ft 10ins and 6ft, of a medium build and with grey hair. He was wearing a dark coloured shirt, jeans and was clean shaven.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or saw any suspicious behaviour prior to the incident should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/69900/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
