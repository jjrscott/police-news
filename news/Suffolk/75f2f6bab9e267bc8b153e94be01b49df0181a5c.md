Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident in Felixstowe.

The incident occurred at around 7.15pm on Sunday 4 November in Dellwood Avenue.

A two-year-old child was leaving a fireworks display with her family when an unknown male, who was potentially under the influence of alcohol, grabbed her around the wrist and asked her a few questions. The mother of the child then pulled her away and the male ran off.

The male is described as having an olive complexion, aged between 30 and 40 years of age, with a shaved head, stubble on the face/light beard, of a medium build and approximately 5ft 5ins. He was wearing blue jeans and a grey top and had an accent.

Anyone who has knowledge of the incident should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/64202/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
