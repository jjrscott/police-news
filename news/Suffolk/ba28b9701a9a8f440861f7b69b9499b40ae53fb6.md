Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of theft that took place in Brandon.

It happened at some point overnight between 5pm on Tuesday 15 May and 9am on Wednesday 16 May on Cherry Lane in Brandon. Lengths of copper pipe were stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour should contact Bury St Edmunds CID on 101, quoting crime number 37/27663/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800555111.
