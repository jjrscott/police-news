Police are appealing for information following reports that several vehicles were damaged in Lowestoft

Police received reports that eleven vehicles were damaged in The Street Lowestoft. The damage occurred between 6pm Monday 3 and 7am Tuesday 4 December. Wing mirrors were smashed, some wing mirror covers were removed, and some vehicle doors were scratched and dented.

Were you in or near the area during this time? Did you see anyone acting suspiciously, or hear any smashing noises? If you have any information about these crimes or know who was responsible, please contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting crime reference 37-70017-18 or use the online crime reporting link - http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/report-something.
