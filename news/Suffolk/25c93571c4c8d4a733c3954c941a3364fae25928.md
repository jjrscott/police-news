Police are appealing for information after an incident of criminal damage to a vehicle in Lowestoft.

At some point between 7pm on Friday 7 and 12:30pm Sunday 9 July, a Vauxhall Corsa car parked in the High Street sustained deliberate dents to its bonnet and roof.

The black Vauxhall Corsa had been parked at the top end of the High Street and police are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident of criminal damage or anyone acting suspiciously around the times stated to get in touch.

Witnesses or those with information should call the Lowestoft Response Team at Suffolk Police on 101, quoting crime number 47808/17.
