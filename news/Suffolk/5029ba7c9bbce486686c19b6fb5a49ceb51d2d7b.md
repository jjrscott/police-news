Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a home address in Bradfield Combust near Bury St Edmunds.

The break-in occurred on Tuesday 7 November at some point between 8.15am and 7pm at the property in The Street.

Entry is thought to have been gained by climbing through a kitchen window.

Offender (s) made an untidy search and stole a TV, laptop, cameras and a quantity of cash.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any knowledge of it should contact Bury St Edmunds CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/72049/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
