Police are appealing for witnesses to a theft of a lawn mower from a property near Woodbridge.

The incident happened at some point between Sunday 27 December and Wednesday 30 December at a property on Fox Hill at Hollesley.

The victim reported that an unknown person gained entry to a garden shed by forcing off the lock and removed a ride-on lawn mower from inside. The make and model is a Countax C300H Tractor.

Anyone who saw any suspicious activity in the area or knows the whereabouts of the mower should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime number 37/75214/20
