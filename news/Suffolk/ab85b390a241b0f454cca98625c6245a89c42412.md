Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Raydon.

The incident occurred at some point between 4pm on Sunday 13 December and 7am on Tuesday 15 December at a property in the vicinity of Upper Street.

During the times stated, unknown offender/s forced entry via a window and once inside, a number of items were stolen including various tools, industrial equipment and two Samsung televisions.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who may have seen anything suspicious is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting reference number: 37/72481/20
