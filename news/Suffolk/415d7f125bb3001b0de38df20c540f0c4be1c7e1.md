Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision involving a motorcyclist at Tuddenham on Thursday 13 April.

The incident happened at 4.25pm when a red Honda CRF 450cc motor-cross bike was in collision with a tree on Green Road.

Paramedics were called to the location but despite efforts to save him the male victim, 53 year old Wayne Austin who is from Cheshire, sadly died at the scene.

Anyone who witnessed the collision who has not yet spoken to police should contact PC Allie Cotton from the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101, quoting CAD 332 from Thursday 13 April.
