Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Eye.

The incident occurred between 7.30am and 6.40pm on Wednesday 23 January at a property in Victoria Hill.

At some point during the times stated, unknown offender/s forced entry by smashing a window and once inside, an untidy search was carried out and a number of collectable coins were stolen.

Furniture was also damaged during the incident.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity or heard anything during the evening should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/4558/19.

Alternatively, contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
