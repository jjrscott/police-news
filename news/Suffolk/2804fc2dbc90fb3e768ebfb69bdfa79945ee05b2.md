Police are appealing for witnesses to criminal damage to vehicles in the Ipswich area.

They all took place on Moffat Avenue overnight Tuesday 5 January into Wednesday 6 January.

The wing mirror of a Toyata Yarris was damaged and knocked off, whilst parked up outside a resident address overnight (crime number 37/861/21)

There was another report in the same road of a blue Toyota Yarris also having its wing mirror damaged on the same night. (37/853/21) A third incident the same evening, on the same road also saw a Peugeot 107 have its wing mirror damaged. (37/898/2)

Anyone who knows who was responsible or saw any suspicious activity in the area they should contact Ipswich police quoting the relevant crime number
