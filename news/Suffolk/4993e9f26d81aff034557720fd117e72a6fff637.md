A 42-year-old man from Narborough, Norfolk has been arrested and charged after a car was stolen in Lakenheath.

A Mercedes C320 car was taken from outside the victimâ€™s address on Hallmark Close, Lakenheath in the early hours of Friday 4 September. The following day, Saturday, at approximately 6:40pm officers stopped the stolen car being driven on Yarmouth Road, Lowestoft and arrested the driver on suspicion of theft. A quantity of drugs were also located in the vehicle.

Shaun Vittles of Swaffham Road, Narborough, Norfolk was taken to Great Yarmouth Investigation Centre where he was questioned by officers. Vittles was subsequently charged with theft, driving without motor insurance, driving whilst disqualified, possession of class A and class B drugs and breaking a bail condition not to be in the front seat of a motor vehicle.

Vittles appeared before Norwich Magistrates Court on 14 September and was remanded into custody. He is due to appear before the same court on 8 October.
