Police are appealing for witnesses following a daytime burglary in Brantham last week.

The incident occurred between 7.30am and 3.30pm on Tuesday 12 June at a house in The Street.

A side door was forced open to gain entry to the property and items of cash and jewellery were stolen from inside.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious persons or vehicles in the village on that day, or who has any information about this crime, is asked to contact South CID at Landmark House on 101 quoting reference 32245/18.

Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
