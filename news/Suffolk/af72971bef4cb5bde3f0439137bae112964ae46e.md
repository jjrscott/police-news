A 32-year-old man who absconded from Hollesley Bay Prison has been arrested.

Saun Moorehouse was found to be missing from his room in the morning of Sunday 21 June. He is serving a six year, five month sentence for conspiracy to commit robbery.

Moorehouse was subsequently arrested by police in Hertfordshire on 7 July. He has now been returned to the prison system.
