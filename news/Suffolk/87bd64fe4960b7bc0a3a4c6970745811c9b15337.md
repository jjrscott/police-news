Suffolk Police are appealing for witnesses to an alleged incident of careless driving on the A12 near Marlesford.

On Monday 6 March at approximately 2.55pm, police were contacted to reports of a vehicle driving erratically on the northbound carriageway of the A12.

The vehicle is described as a black Corsa, and it is believed to have been travelling from the Wickham Market area towards Farnham.

If anyone witnessed the manner of driving of a vehicle of this description at the time specified, they should contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference number 37/21327/17.
