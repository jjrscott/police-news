Police have located a teenage girl missing from the Red Lodge area. 16 year old Ayonda Gasela was last seen on Sunday 30 December.

She was located at Cambridge train station yesterday evening (Thur 3 January).Police would like to thank the media and the public for their help with this matter.

## Previous release I 3 January 2018

Police are appealing for help to locate a 16 year old teenager missing from the Red Lodge area.

Ayonda Gasela left home at 4pm on Sunday 30 December saying she was going to a party in Mildenhall. She was reported as missing to police on Tuesday 1 January 2019.

She is described as black, five foot three inches tall, of slim build with black shoulder length hair.

When last seen she was wearing a light brown fur coat, black trousers and had a caramel coloured hand bag and also wore black jeans and a white jacket.

She is also known to have links to London and Norfolk, including Norwich and Great Yarmouth.

Enquiries are on-going to locate her and anyone with information who may have seen her or had knowledge of her whereabouts should contact Suffolk Constabulary on 101, quoting CAD 217 of 1 January 2019.
