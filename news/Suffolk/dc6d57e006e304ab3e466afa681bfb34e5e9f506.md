Police are re-appealing witnesses after a man was seriously assaulted in a Lowestoft park.

The incident took place on Saturday 5 September at Bleach Park, on the junction of Winnipeg Road and Church Road at around 2:30pm.

After an altercation involving a number of children, an older youth punched one the children’s dad in the face. The suspect then walked off in the direction of Water Lane.

The victim, a man in his 50s, sustained a broken jaw and was taken to hospital. The injuries require surgical treatment.

Police are looking to trace a family who were in the park at the time of the incident and may be able to assist the investigation.

Witnesses or anybody with any information are asked to contact Lowestoft CID quoting reference 37/51733/20
