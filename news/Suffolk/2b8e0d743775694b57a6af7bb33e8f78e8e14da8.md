Police are appealing for witnesses to an assault that took place in Lowestoft.

Two 14 year olds males were walking through Nicholas Everett park on Sunday 21 October at about 12.45pm when a group of males began giving them both verbal abuse. One of the males from the group then tackled one of the 14 year old causing him to fall. No serious injury was caused.

The male is described as a white male, around 14 to 16 years old, with light brown scruffy hair and was wearing a black and grey t-shirt, black joggers with white stripes on and red trainers. He was also on a red bike.

Anyone who has knowledge of the incident, or saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk Constabulary on 101, quoting crime reference 37/61122/18

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
