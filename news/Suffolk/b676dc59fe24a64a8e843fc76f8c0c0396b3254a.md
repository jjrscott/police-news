Police are appealing for witnesses to a robbery that took place in Newmarket.

It happened on Wednesday 6 December at around 6.50pm, when a man was approached and threatened by two men in Green Road.

One of the men had a knife in his hand and demanded the victim hand over any valuables. His rucksack was searched and a letter stolen.

The offenders then walked off along Green Road and on to The Avenue.

The suspects were both wearing balaclavas and gloves.

Anyone who witnessed either incident, saw any suspicious activity in the area or has knowledge of the incident should contact Bury St Edmunds CID on 101 quoting crime reference 37/77958/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
