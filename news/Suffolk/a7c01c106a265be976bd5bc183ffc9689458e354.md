Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Letheringham.

The incident occurred between 10am on Tuesday 13 November and 5pm on Thursday 15 November at a property.

At some point during the times and dates stated, offender/s forced entry via a door and made an untidy search within. A cream leather jewellery box containing a number of gold bracelets, rings, pearl necklaces and earrings were stolen. A gold pencil with a name inscribed on it was also taken.

A number of silver items were also stolen, including a presentation plate, a warrant pendant, a christening mug and a bottle top in the shape of a figure of a boy.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity or who has any information about the crime is asked to contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting reference 66258/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
