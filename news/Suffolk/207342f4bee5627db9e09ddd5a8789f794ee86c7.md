A man who died in a road traffic collision at Rougham near Bury St Edmunds is being named by police.

Officers were called at 12.53am on Saturday 6 July, to a report of a single-vehicle collision involving a Mercedes Sprinter van on the A14.

The 37-year-old male driver was sadly declared dead at the scene. Next of kin have been informed.

Pending formal Coroner’s inquest proceedings, police have provisionally identified the deceased as 37-year-old Kieran Mayhew of Southcourt in Aylesbury.

Anyone who witnessed or has any information about this incident is asked to contact Suffolk police quoting reference CAD 24 of 6 July.

Witnesses to the collision are asked to visit http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update. Alternatively, call the Serious Collision Investigation Unit on 101.

Anybody who may have relevant dash-cam or mobile phone footage are asked to visit https://www.suffolk.police.uk/dashcam
