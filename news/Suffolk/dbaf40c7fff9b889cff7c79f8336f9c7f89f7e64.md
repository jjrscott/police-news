A missing 15-year-old girl from Bury St Edmunds has been found.

Chloe Humpage had last been seen at around 4.45pm on Wednesday 5 September, but was found safe and well by police in Bury St Edmunds this afternoon, Friday 7 September.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
