Officers are appealing for witnesses or information after a number of vehicles were damaged in Ipswich.

The incidents of criminal damage all took place sometime between 11.15pm on Sunday 23 September and the early hours Monday 24 September. It is reported that offender/s travelled in a silver estate car, possibly in convoy with another vehicle, along Dumbarton Road, Sidegate Lane, Dales View Road, Rushmere Road and Humber Doucy Lane, leaning out of the window using what is described as a baseball bat to damage parked vehicles. Fourteen vehicles are reported to have sustained damage to wing mirrors.

Witnesses or anybody with relevant dash-cam footage or who has any information relating to the incidents are asked to call Police on 101 quoting reference 37/54844/18. Alternatively, contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
