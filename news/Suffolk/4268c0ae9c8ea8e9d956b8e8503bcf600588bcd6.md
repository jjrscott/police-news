Police are appealing for witnesses or information following the theft of a bicycle in Ipswich.

At some point between 7pm on Wednesday 25 November and 7am on Thursday 26 November, a locked black Specialized road bicycle was stolen from a communal outside area of a secure residential building in Sheldrake Drive.

The bicycle was also fitted with Mavic wheels.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/69011/20
