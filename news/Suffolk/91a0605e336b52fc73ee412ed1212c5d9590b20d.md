Two men have been charged in connection with a serious assault in Ipswich.

The alleged incident occurred on Friday 3 November at approximately 10.45pm on St Stephens Church Lane in the town centre, where a man in his 20s sustained a number of wounds to his arms and legs that required medical attention.

26-year-old Jamie Phillips and 18-year-old Ryan Page, both of Turret Lane, Ipswich, were arrested and have both been charged with grievous bodily harm.

They have been remanded in custody to appear at Ipswich Magistrates Court tomorrow (Monday 6 November) at 10am.
