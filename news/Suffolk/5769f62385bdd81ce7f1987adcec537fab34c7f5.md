Police are appealing for witnesses and information following an assault in Lowestoft.

On Friday 7 October at approximately 11.20pm, a man was walking along Yarmouth Road in Lowestoft when he was approached by an offender who asked him the time.

When the victim looked down at his phone, the offender reportedly punched the man in the face. The victim fell to the ground unconscious and suffered a cut to his cheek as well as severe bruising to his left eye.

The offender is described as a white male aged approximately 25 years old, wearing a dark blue hoodie and light coloured jeans or trousers.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting crime reference number **37/59691/16** or alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111.**
