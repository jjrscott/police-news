Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident of criminal damage in Stowmarket.

The incident happened on Sunday 13 May at about 5.30pm.

The victim, a man in his 20s, left a shop on Ipswich Street to return to his car, a red Seat Arosa, when he was shouted at by a group of teenagers. When he went to drive off the group attempted to block the car. They also punched the windows of the vehicle and kick the car door.

The group then followed the car back to Jubilee Avenue in the town where they again kicked the car once it had been parked up and the two occupants had left it. No one was hurt in the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the incident should contact Stowmarket police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/26074/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
