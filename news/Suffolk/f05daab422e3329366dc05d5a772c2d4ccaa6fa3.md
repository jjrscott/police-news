Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Sudbury.

The incident took place at a taxi rank near Club Infinity in East Street on Saturday 18 August between 1:30am and 1:50am. The victim, a man in his 20s, received a punch to the head, which caused him to fall to the ground after a verbal altercation. It is then alleged that an offender continued to punch the victim.

As a result of the incident, the victim was taken by ambulance to Colchester Hospital after he sustained a broken jaw, an injured eyelid and various facial injuries.

Later the same day, a 38-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of assault causing grievous bodily harm. He was taken to Bury St Edmunds Police Investigation Centre for questioning and he was subsequently released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Police would be keen to speak to anyone who may have seen the incident take place between the times stated.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information should contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting reference 37/46986/18 or the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
