Police are issuing a further witness appeal after two people were seriously injured following a collision at Beck Row near Mildenhall.

Officers were called to the scene on the A1101 at Beck Row at 3.32am on Thursday 7 January following reports that a blue Honda Accord had collided with road signage.

Two people remain in Addenbrookes’ hospital following the collision, one person is in a serious but stable condition, the second is in a critical but stable condition.

The road was closed in both directions for an investigation to take place.

Police are particularly interested in hearing from any drivers who may have been travelling on the A1101 during the early hours of Thursday 7 January around the time of the incident.

Any lorry drivers who briefly stopped at the scene are asked to contact police with any information that could help with the investigation.

Anyone with any information should contact PC 1981 Taylor at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 23 of Thursday 7 January.

## Press release | 7 January 2021

Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious single vehicle collision at Beck Row.

Officers were called to the scene on the A1101 at Beck Row at 3.32am today (Thursday 7 January) following reports that a car had collided with road signage.

The incident was attended by a volunteer SARS paramedic, fire and ambulance.

Two people have been transported to Addenbrookes’ hospital by ambulance with serious injuries.

The road has been closed in both directions for an investigation to take place.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 23 of today, Thursday 7 January.
