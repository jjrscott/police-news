Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Sudbury.

The incident is believed to have taken place in the Infinity nightclub in East Street on Sunday 8 July between 3am and 3.20am. The victim, a man in his 20s, reported that he was pushed to the floor and kicked in the head by another man.

As a result of the incident, the victim sustained a cut to his head and bruising to his face.

A short time later, officers arrested a 26-year-old man on suspicion of assault. He was taken to Bury St Edmunds Police Investigation Centre for questioning and has been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Police would be keen to speak to anyone who may have seen the incident take place between the times stated.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information should contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting reference 37/37778/18 or the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
