Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary in Ipswich.

It happened at some time between 4.45pm on Wednesday 13 June and 9.45am on Thursday 14 June at a property in Belmont Road.

A rear patio door was smashed to gain entry to the property before the suspect(s) made a search of two upstairs bedrooms, apparently looking for jewellery. It is not clear at this stage if anything was taken.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting reference 37/32662/18, or the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
