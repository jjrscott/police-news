Police are appealing for help to identify a man in connection with the a theft of a pedal cycle in Lowestoft.

The incident happened on Friday 15 September at some point between 9am and 4pm when a blue and green Carerra pedal cycle which was left secure outside the Water Lane Leisure Centre was stolen.

Anyone who recognises the individual in the still or has knowledge of the incident should contact Lowestoft police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/61477 /17or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
