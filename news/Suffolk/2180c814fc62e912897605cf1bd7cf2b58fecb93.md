Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident in Lowestoft.

The incident took place on Gordon Road, near to Clapham Road South, in Lowestoft on Saturday 8 December at around 12.40pm.

Two vehicles were stationary waiting to join the traffic near the roundabout. It is reported that a man driving a purple Ford Fiesta got out of his vehicle and approached the driver of a black Passat car and began shouting and swearing at him. The driver of the Ford Fiesta then got back in his vehicle and left the scene.

Witnesses or anybody who has any information or relevant dash-cam footage regarding the incident are asked to call Lowestoft Police on 101 quoting reference 71158/18. Alternatively, cal the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
