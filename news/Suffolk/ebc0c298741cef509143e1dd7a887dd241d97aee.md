Police are appealing for help to trace three males that police officers want to speak to in connection with theft offences in Lowestoft.

The first incident took place on Thursday 4 March at the Co-Operative store on London Road in Pakefield (37/11056/21). Three males entered a shop and filled their coats with meat, while one of them also filled a bag with meat before leaving the store, making no attempt to pay.

A second incident took place on Saturday 6 March at the One Stop Store on Carlton Road in Lowestoft (37/11052/21) where three males entered the shop and filled their coats with meat, while one of the trio also filled a bag full of alcoholic drink, before leaving the store making no attempt to pay.

Anyone who recognises the individuals in the image, should contact Lowestoft police, quoting the relevant crime number.

Phone – Call 101

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
