Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Creeting St Mary.

The incident occurred between 3.30pm and 6pm on Wednesday 27 January, at an address in All Saints Road.

Entry was gained to the property via the back door and an untidy search of the bedrooms and kitchen was carried-out, with a gold watch stolen. A shed was also searched.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who saw any suspicious persons or vehicles in the village on Wednesday afternoon. They would also like to remind residents to be vigilant in their communities and to ensure they keep doors and windows secured.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact West CID at Bury St Edmunds Police Station, quoting reference: 4397/21.
