Police are appealing for information after a teenage boy reported being sexually assaulted by a man in Lowestoft.

The incident occurred at approximately 8:50am on Tuesday, 12 September, on Lovewell Road.

The boy had been standing by a push bike when the man is said to have approached him from behind and grabbed his bottom over his clothes before walking away in the direction of Beaconsfield Road.

The man is described as white, possibly in his 80s, and approximately 5ft 2ins. He was wearing a brown suit jacket with brown trousers and black shoes, and was wearing two hearing aids.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have seen anyone matching this description in the area at the time stated, or who may have information to assist the investigation.

If you can help please call the Incident and Crime Management Hub at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 60994/17.
