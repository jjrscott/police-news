Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident in Lowestoft.

Officers were called to Raglan Street, Lowestoft, at around 8.40pm yesterday, Monday 2 July to reports of a disturbance and an incident of criminal damage.

In what is believed to be a targeted attack, a Ford Focus was driven at speed down the road before two unknown males got out of the vehicle.

The males then proceeded to shout before one of the males caused criminal damage to a door of a property. It is then believed the males got back into the car and reversed down the road.

One of the males is described as wearing a red/orange crash helmet with a black hooded/long sleeve top and either black tracksuit bottoms or jeans. He was also thought to be in possession of a knife. The other male is described as white, with brown hair and wearing a beige t-shirt and jeans. He was believed to be holding a baseball bat.

Officers were quickly dispatched and a search was made of the area but no vehicle was found.

Police are appealing to anyone who has information relating to the incident, in particular anyone who may have any CCTV or dash-cam footage, to contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting crime reference 36681/18

Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
