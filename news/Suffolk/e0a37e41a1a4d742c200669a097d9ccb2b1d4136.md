Suffolk Police are appealing for witnesses following vandalism of 28 vehicles in Lowestoft.

On Saturday 30 September, between approximately 1am and 3am, offenders caused tyre damage to vehicles parked in: Spexhall Way, Stoven Close, Blyford Road, Crestview Drive, Weston Road, Wissett Way, Frostenden Crescent, Westhall Road, Village Way and Henham Road.

Police would be interested in speaking to anyone who may have seen or heard anyone acting suspiciously in these areas, during or around the time stated.

Anyone with any information to assist the investigation should contact PC Mark Brown at Lowestoft SNT on 101, quoting crime number 37/64404/17.
