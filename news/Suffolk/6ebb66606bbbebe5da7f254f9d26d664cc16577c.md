Police are appealing for witnesses to a robbery in Stowmarket that took place late last night (Thursday 22 June).

The male victim, aged in his 30s had just returned to his home address in Chaffinch Way just before 11pm when he was set upon by three males as he went into the property. The three offenders pinned him to the ground, using cable ties to restrain him and stole a large quantity of money and jewellery before fleeing the scene.

The suspects were all described as wearing black hooded tops and black cargo trousers.

Enquiries into the incident are continuing and anyone who has any information, saw an suspicious activity around the time of the incident should contact Western Area CID on 101 quote reference 37/43545/17 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
