Police are appealing for information after criminal damage to and theft from a vehicle in Homersfield, near Harleston.

The incident occurred at some point between 6:30am on Tuesday 2 and 5:30pm on Wednesday 3 May. The vehicle, a white transit van, had been parked in a lay by on the B1062, near to the junction with Back Lane.

The rear number plate, a generator and DeWalt cordless drill were among the items stolen from the van. A wing mirror was broken off, and a tyre was also punctured.

Officers are investigating the motive behind the incident and are appealing for anyone who may have seen anything suspicious in the area between these times to get in touch.

Those with information should call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 32948/17.
