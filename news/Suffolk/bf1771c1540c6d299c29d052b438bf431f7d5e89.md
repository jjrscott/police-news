Police investigating an arson in Haverhill have arrested two people.

Officers were called on around 3.40am yesterday, Monday 10 September, to reports of a large fire in a derelict building on Hamlet Road in the town.

Two 17-year-old boys were arrested yesterday afternoon and brought to Bury St Edmunds Police Investigation Centre for questioning. Both have been released under investigation while enquires continue.

Witnesses or anybody with any information is asked to contact West CID on 101 quoting reference 51849/18. Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
