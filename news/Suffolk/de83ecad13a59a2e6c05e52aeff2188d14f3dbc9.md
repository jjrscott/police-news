Police are issuing a CCTV image of a man they would like to trace in connection with a theft in Bury St Edmunds.

The incident occurred at around 1:30pm on Saturday 7 November at the Homebase shop on Easlea Road. A man and woman entered the shop together and the man put a number of tools into a bag and left without paying. The value of the items taken is approximately £483.

The man is described as white, in his 30s, 5 feet 10 inches tall, with short black hair and unshaven. He was wearing a black and grey fleece, black shorts and dark shoes.

The woman is described as white in her 30s, small build with long dark hair in a pony tail. She was wearing an orange jumper, blue jeans and white trainers.

Anyone who believes they know the man pictured in the CCTV, or who has any information about this incident, is asked to contact the Crime Coordination Centre quoting reference: 65117/20
