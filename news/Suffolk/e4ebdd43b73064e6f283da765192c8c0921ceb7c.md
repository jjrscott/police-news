Police are appealing for witnesses or information following an incident of theft in Stowmarket.

At around 1.20am on Tuesday 11 December, unknown offender/s stole a number of decorations from a Christmas tree in Ipswich Street.

Officers would be interested to speak to two females and a male who were seen in the market place at around the time of the incident as they may be able to help with their enquiries.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting reference 37/71463/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
