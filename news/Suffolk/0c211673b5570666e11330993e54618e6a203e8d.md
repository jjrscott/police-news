Police are appealing for witnesses after a road traffic collision in Halesworth.

Officers were called shortly before 8pm on Thursday 19 July following reports of collision between a blue Toyota Aygo and a pedestrian in Norwich Road.

The victim, a woman in her 70s from the Halesworth area, was taken by air ambulance to the James Paget Hospital in Gorleston having sustained a serious head injury.

Witnesses or anybody who may have any dash-cam footage are asked to call PC 1202 Tyler in the Roads Policing Unit on 101 quoting reference CAD 407 of 19 July.
