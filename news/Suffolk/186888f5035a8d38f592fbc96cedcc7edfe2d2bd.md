Police are appealing for witnesses following criminal damage to three vehicles in Oulton Broad, Lowestoft.

The incidents took place at Hall Road on Friday 17 February sometime between 6pm and 11pm. Unknown offenders, smashed a window of a Range Rover Evoque vehicle, broke a rear window of a Volkswagen Golf car and also a damaged a wing mirror of a BMW Z4 car.

Officers would like to trace a dark coloured 4x4 pick-up style vehicle that was seen in the street at around 7.45pm as the occupants were seen acting suspiciously.

Anybody who witnessed the incident or who saw anything suspicious at the time or has any information relating to the pick-up vehicle are asked to contact Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting reference 18381/17.
