Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Bury St Edmunds.

At some point between 11.30pm on Thursday 4 June and 3am on Friday 5 June, an unknown person entered a property in Ridley Road via an open window and once inside, they stole a Macbook pro, a Playstation 4 and controller, a black pair of Prada trainers a white pair of Tommy Hilfiger trainers, a blue pair of Nike trainers with yellow shoelaces and an electric scooter.

The scooter was later found nearby by the victim.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/30969/20
