A man has been arrested in connection with several incidents of criminal damage in Needham Market.

A 41-year-old man from Needham Market has been arrested by police in connection with nine incidents of criminal damage in Stowmarket Road and Anderson Close areas of the town, all of which took place between July and September this year. Offences includes vehicle and property damage.

The man was arrested on Tuesday 26 September, and has now been released under investigation pending further enquiries.
