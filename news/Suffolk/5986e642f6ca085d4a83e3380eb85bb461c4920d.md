Police have located a 56-year-old woman missing from Kedington.

Joanne Bytham was last seen at her home address at 9:30am on Tuesday 8 January.

She was located safe and well in the Great Yarmouth area, yesterday 9 January. Police would like to thank media and public for their help with this appeal.
