A man who was reported missing from a Hollesley Bay roll call yesterday morning has been arrested.
23-year-old Tom Scott was detained by officers in Essex this evening (Sunday 3 January) and will be returned to the prison system.
Suffolk Police would like to thank the media and public for their help with this matter.
