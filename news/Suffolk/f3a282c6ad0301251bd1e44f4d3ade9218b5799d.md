Police are appealing for witnesses and information after a car was stolen following a burglary in Woolpit.

The incident occurred at some point between 10:30pm on Monday 26 and 6:30am Tuesday 27 June at a property in Steeles Road.

Offenders forced entry to the home and removed keys from the property before stealing the vehicle, a black BMW 323, from the driveway.

The vehicle has since been recovered, after a member of public reported an abandoned vehicle in the vicinity of Chilton Way.

Police would like to hear from anyone who has information about the incident, particularly if they know the identity of those responsible. If you can help, please contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 44416/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
