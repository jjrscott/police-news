Police are appealing for witnesses to a robbery that took place in Lowestoft.

It happened on Sunday 8 October at about 8pm on Mount Pleasant in the town.

A man, aged in his 60s, was walking home when he was approached from behind and was hit to the head. He fell to the floor and offender then had some cash taken from the pocket of the jeans he was wearing.

The victim sustained some possible cracked ribs in the incident. The suspect is described as tall, slim, aged in his late 20s, and was wearing a dark jumper and possibly dark jeans.

Anyone who witness the incident, has any knowledge of it or saw any suspicious activity at the time of the incident should contact DC Lee Cudmore at Lowestoft CID on 101 quoting crime reference 37/65969/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
