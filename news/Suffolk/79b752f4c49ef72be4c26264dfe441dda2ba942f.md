Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Haverhill

It happened at some point between Monday 8 March at 4.30pm and Tuesday 9 March at 7am at a building site on School Lane. Overnight, the suspects gained access to the site by cutting the lock to main gate and have started the dumper truck which has then been driven into a container causing, in total, around £2,000 damage.

Anyone who heard any suspicious activity or knows who was responsible should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/11572/21

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
