Police are appealing for witnesses or information following the theft of a box trailer in Ipswich.

The incident occurred between 5pm on Friday 9 October and 6pm on Saturday 10 October in the Scout Association car park in Britannia Road.

The box trailer is described as grey, small and had a flat tyre.

Anyone with information about the incident, or who believes they have seen a box trailer matching this description, is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/59389/20
