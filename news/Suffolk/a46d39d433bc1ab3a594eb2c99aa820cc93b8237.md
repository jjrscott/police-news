Police were called to the Port of Felixstowe at 11:45pm last night, Thursday 19 January, to reports of an incident in the boiler room of a ship that was docking.

One person has sadly died as a result of this incident and another is currently in hospital receiving treatment. The death is not being treated as suspicious and there will be a joint investigation between the Police and the Maritime and Coastguard agency.
