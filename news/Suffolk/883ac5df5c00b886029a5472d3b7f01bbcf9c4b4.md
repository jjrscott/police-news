A 17 year old teenager who went missing from Ipswich has returned home.

Lily Williams returned to her home address at around lunchtime this afternoon (Friday 30 November) after being reported as missing earlier this morning.

Police would like to thank the media and members of the public for their help with this matter.

## Previous release I 30 November 2018

Police are appealing for help to trace a 17 year old teenager who is missing from Ipswich.

Lily Williams left her home address on Allenby Road sometime last night (Thursday 29 November) after 10pm and was reported as missing to police earlier this morning.

She is described as of thin build, about five foot, four inches tall, with long brown hair and has various piercings, including a studded lip bar in the centre of her lip and a piercing in her top right ear.

Enquiries are on-going to locate her and anyone who has seen Lily, or a person matching her description or knows of her whereabouts should contact police on 101.
