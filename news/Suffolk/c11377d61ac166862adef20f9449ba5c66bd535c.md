Suffolk Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Walsham-le-Willows.

At around 4.30pm on Saturday 18 November, offenders entered a property on The Causeway and carried out an untidy search of an upstairs bedroom before leaving empty handed.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have seen or heard any suspicious behaviour in the area at the time.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting crime number 37/74318/17.
