Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Stanton.

The incident took place at a bus shelter at The Street on Monday 23 January, at some point between 7:15pm and 7:45pm. A teenage boy, who was with three friends, was approached by a man. After a short verbal exchange the man is reported to have punched the boy who sustained cuts and bruises to his face.

Officers are appealing for public help to trace the man who is believed to have left the scene as a passenger in a burgundy car. He is described as white, approximately 25 – 30 years’ old and of heavy build with a shaved head and was wearing a blue jumper with jeans.

Witnesses or anybody who has any information are asked to contact the Incident and Crime Management Hub at Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 13880/17.
