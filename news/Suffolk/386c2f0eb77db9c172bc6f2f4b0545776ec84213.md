A 50-year-old man from Lowestoft has appeared in court charged with three burglary offences in the town.

Franklyn Mathurin, of Kirkley Cliff Road, was charged with two dwelling burglaries and one attempted burglary at a business premises.

All three incidents occurred on Tuesday 31 July at locations on High Street.

Mathurin was arrested by detectives investigating the crimes on Thursday 2 August and subsequently charged in connection with them.
