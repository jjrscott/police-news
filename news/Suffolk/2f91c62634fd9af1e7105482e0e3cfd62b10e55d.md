Police are appealing for information following a burglary that took place in Lowestoft.

Suspect/s forced entry into a charity shop on Pier Terrace overnight on Saturday 6 August, smashing the front door with an iron grate. An untidy search was then conducted and a small amount of cash was taken.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact Police on 101, quoting reference number: 48684/16. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org
