Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious traffic incident in Leavenheath.

Officers were called shortly after 7:40pm on Saturday, 10 June, to reports a woman had fallen from a moving vehicle on the A134, near Honey Tye.

The vehicle, a white Audi Q7, was travelling northbound in the Sudbury direction when the woman left the vehicle.

The woman, who is in her 20s and who was travelling as the passenger, sustained a serious head injury and was taken to Royal London Hospital by air ambulance where she remains in a serious but stable condition.

An investigation into the circumstances around the incident is ongoing and police are appealing for any witnesses to get in touch.

Witnesses are asked to call the Serious Collision Investigation Team at Suffolk Police on **101** quoting CAD 414 of Saturday, 10 June.
