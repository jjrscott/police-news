Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Ipswich.

It took place at a property on Southgate Road at some point between 10am and 6.30pm on Monday 12 November.

The property had been entered via rear French doors which had been smashed. An untidy search had taken place in the property and various items of jewellery had been stolen.

Anyone, who witnessed with incident or saw any suspicious activity during the times stated should contact Ipswich CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/65630/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
