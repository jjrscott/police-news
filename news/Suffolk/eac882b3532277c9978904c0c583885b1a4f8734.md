Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident of criminal damage that took place in south Lowestoft.

At approximately 3pm on Saturday 18 March a window was damaged at a property in London Road South, near to the junction of Parade Road South.

A 39-year-old woman from Lowestoft is currently on police bail in connection with the incident until Wednesday 19 April.

Anyone who has any information that may help bring enquiries forward are asked to contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference 23698/17.
