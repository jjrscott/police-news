Police are releasing CCTV images of a man they would like to trace following a robbery in Haverhill.

The incident occurred at approximately 7:30am on Thursday 4th March at the McColls newsagent in the High Street.

The offender entered the store armed with a screwdriver and demanded staff to open the till. He then left the premises on foot, taking a quantity of cash with him. The member of staff was not injured during the incident.

The suspect is described as white, approximately 5ft 6ins in height, of slim build and believed to be in his early 20s. He was wearing dark bottoms and a white hooded coat, with dark sleeves.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured in the CCTV images about the incident as he may be able to help with the investigation.

Anyone who believes they recognise the man pictured, or who has any information about this incident, is asked to contact West CID at Bury St Edmunds Police Station on 101, quoting crime reference 10610/21 via

## Haverhill – Appeal following robbery - 4 March 2021

Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a robbery in Haverhill this morning.

The incident occurred at approximately 7:30am today, Thursday 4th March, at the McColls newsagent in the High Street.

The offender entered the store armed with a screwdriver and demanded staff to open the till. He then left the premises on foot, taking a quantity of cash with him. The member of staff was not injured during the incident.

The suspect is described as white, approximately 5ft 6ins in height, of slim build and believed to be in his early 20s. He was wearing dark bottoms and a white hooded coat, with dark sleeves.

Enquiries are ongoing and anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious behaviour prior to the incident or afterwards, or has further knowledge of the robbery that could assist should contact Bury St Edmunds CID on 101, quoting crime reference 10610/21 via
