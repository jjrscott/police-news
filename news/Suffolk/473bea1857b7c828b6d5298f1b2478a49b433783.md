Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary in Martlesham.

It happened at a property on Viking Heights at some point between Wednesday 24 March at midday and Thursday 25 March at just after 11am.

A window of a bedroom in the property was smashed and it appears a search of the main bedroom had taken place. It’s thought the only item stolen was a gentleman’s wrist watch.

Anyone who saw any suspicious behaviour, knows who was responsible should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/14688/21
