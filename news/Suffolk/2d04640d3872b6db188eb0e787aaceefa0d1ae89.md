Police are appealing for witnesses or information after three static caravans were damaged in Ellough near Beccles.

At some point between 4pm and 6pm on Tuesday 28 July, an unknown offender damaged three caravans by throwing large bricks at the doors and windows within a compound in Benacre Road.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/43022/20
