Police are appealing for information after two cycles were stolen from a shed in Mildenhall.

Unknown person(s) gained entry to a shed by cutting the padlock in North Place in North Terrace. A man’s black Calibre Line 29 mountain bike and a lady’s grey and yellow Calibre 2 Cubed pedal cycle were stolen from within. The theft took place between 9pm Sunday 20 December and 9am Monday 21 December.

If you saw the cycles being taken or have any information about where they are now, please contact Suffolk Police quoting crime reference 37/73747/20 via:

Email - [email protected]

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
