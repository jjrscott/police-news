Police are appealing for information after a 19-year-old woman reported she was assaulted in South Lowestoft.

The incident took place at approximately 4pm on Sunday 20 November as the woman walked along Rectory Road, in Kirkley.

The woman has reported that she was approached by a man who shouted at her before grabbing and punching her face. She sustained scratches, swelling and bruising.

The suspect is described as a white man, in his early to mid-20s and of slim build. He was wearing a grey Adidas tracksuit.

Police are looking into what happened and would like to speak to anyone who may have been in the area at the time, regardless if they saw the incident or not. Please contact the Lowestoft Response Team at Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting reference 67311/16.
