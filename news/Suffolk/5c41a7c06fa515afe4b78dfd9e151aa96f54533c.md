One person has died in a fatal road traffic collision in Clare.

The single vehicle collision happened at about 12:15am on the A1092 Cavendish Road this morning (Friday 27 November).

A Vauxhall Corsa was traveling from the direction of Clare to Cavendish and collided with the roadside bank. Emergency services attended the scene and the road was closed until about 7.15am while initial investigations were conducted and clear ing of the road. A woman in her early 30s sadly died at the scene. She was the sole occupant of the car and no-one else was hurt.

Anyone who witnessed the collision is asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Unit on 101, quoting CAD 4 of 27 November 2020.
