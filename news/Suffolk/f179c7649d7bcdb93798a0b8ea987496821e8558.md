Police are appealing for witnesses to an assault that took place in Lowestoft.

It happened on Saturday 5 May at some point between 7pm and 8pm.The victim, a man aged 21, was walking from walked from Broadview into the Wherry Hotel car park when he was approached by two males one of whom has punched him in the face. It caused a double fracture of the jaw, causing him to lose consciousness. According to the victim he regained consciousness to see the two suspects ride away on pedal bikes up Bridge Road towards the train station. The victim sustained a cut lip and to his gums and was treated in hospital as a precaution, but has now been released.

Anyone who has knowledge of the incident or saw any suspicious activity should contact Lowestoft police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/24477/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
