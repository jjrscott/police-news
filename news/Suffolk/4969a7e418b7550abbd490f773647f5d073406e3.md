A missing 34-year-old man from Wetheringsett. has been found.

Luke Roberts was last seen leaving his home address in Wetheringsett at around 8am on Tuesday 9 May

He presented himself to police in Norfolk yesterday afternoon, Wednesday 10 May, and is safe and well.

Police would like to thank the public and members of the media for their assistance with this appeal.
