A missing 31-year-old man from Halesworth has been found.

Stephen Wilson had last been seen in the town on Monday 11 June and yesterday, Tuesday 19 June, he presented himself to police in Norfolk.

Wilson of Poppy Close, Loddon was then arrested by police in connection with an incident of arson that took place in Market Place, Halesworth on 11 June and was brought to Wymondham Police Investigation centre for questioning by detectives.

He has now been charged with arson endangering life, common assault, breach of a restraining order and taking a vehicle without consent of the owner.

Wilson has been remanded to appear at Norwich Magistrates Court, today, Wednesday 20 June.
