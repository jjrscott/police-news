Officers are appealing for witnesses following a burglary and an attempted burglary in Felixstowe.

The burglary took place sometime between 6pm on Monday 30 January and 6.30am on Tuesday 31 January at a business premises on Hamilton Road, Felixstowe. A suspect/s forced entry at the rear of the building and made a search of the premises before stealing a quantity of cash.

An attempted burglary took place at an adjacent business sometime between 5.30pm on Monday 30 January and 8am on Tuesday 31 January. Suspect/s attempted to force entry to the rear of building, damaging a window.

Detectives are investigating links between both incidents.

Witnesses or anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time are asked to call Police on **101** reference 15073/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111** or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
