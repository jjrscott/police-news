Detectives are investigating a burglary at a home in Pakenham, near Bury St Edmunds, where cash and jewellery were stolen.

The break in occurred at an address in Manor Ga **r** th and is thought to have taken place at some point between 7am on Saturday 3 December and 9:45pm on Monday 5 December.

Police believe entry was forced via the rear patio doors and detectives are keen to speak to anyone who may have been in the area who may have seen anything suspicious.

If you can help please contact Bury St Edmunds CID by calling Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 70074/16, or call Crimestoppers, anonymously if required, on **0800 555 111**.
