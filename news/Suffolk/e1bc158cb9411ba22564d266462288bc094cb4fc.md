Officers are appealing for help or information following reports that a number of vehicles in Ipswich were damaged overnight.

Police were called in the very early hours of today, 00.25am, Thursday 1 February, to reports that wing mirrors on sixteen vehicles parked on Kings Avenue in the town had been damaged.

Anybody who saw or heard anything suspicious last night are asked to call Ipswich Police on 101 quoting reference CAD 12 of 1 February.
