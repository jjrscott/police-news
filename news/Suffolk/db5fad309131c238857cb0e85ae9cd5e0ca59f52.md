A 15-year-old girl from Needham Market who went missing after she was dropped off at Ipswich Railway Station last night has returned home safe and well.

There were concerns for Amy Harrold who had last been seen around 8pm yesterday, Friday 31 March, but she returned home this afternoon, Saturday 1 April.

We would like to thank you for your help with this appeal.
