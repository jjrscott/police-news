A motorcyclist who died following a road traffic collision in Sudbury last week is being named, as tributes are paid to him.

Police were called shortly before 7.50am on Thursday 8 September, to reports of a collision between a lorry and a motorbike on the A134.

Gary Parker, aged 49, of Sheepshead Hill in Great Cornard, was sadly declared deceased at the scene.

Suffolk Police have been asked to issue the following tribute to Mr Parker:

## Previous Release - 08/09/2016

A man has sadly died following a serious road traffic collision in Sudbury.

Police were called shortly before 7.50am this morning, Thursday 8 September, to reports of a collision between a lorry and a motorbike on the A134.

The rider of the motorbike has sadly been declared deceased at the scene.

The road will remain closed between Shawlands Avenue/Northern Road roundabout and Joes Road (towards Newton) whilst a collision investigation takes place.

Any witnesses are asked to call the Serious Collision Investigation Team on **101** quoting reference CAD 55 of 8 September.
