Officers were called today, Wednesday 9 May, at around 4.30pm to reports of a sudden death in Linnet Road, Bury St Edmunds.

Police can confirm that body of a man in his 50s was found in the front garden of a house.

A scene is in place and the road is closed whilst an investigation gets underway into the circumstances of what has happened. At this time the death is being treated as unexplained but is not thought to be suspicious.

Anybody who has any information which could assist are asked to call police on 101 quoting CAD reference 305.
