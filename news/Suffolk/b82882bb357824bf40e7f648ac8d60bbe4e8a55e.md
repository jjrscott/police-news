A 12-year-old girl reported missing from her home in Hundon earlier today has been found safe and well.

Claire Powell was located this afternoon after being reported missing from her home in Folley Road at 7am today.

Police would like to thank the media and the public for their help with this matter.

## Previous release | July 5 2018

Police are appealing for help to find a missing 12 year old girl from Hundon.

Claire Powell was reported missing at 7am today from her home address in Folley Road.

Her behaviour is described as out of character and as such police and family members are concerned for her welfare.

She is described as white, with curly auburn hair and a freckled face. She may be wearing a black vest top, white shorts, a black and white blouse and white and blue plimsolls. She may also have a sweatshirt with her with a pink bauble and rabbit design and be riding a blue mountain bike.

Anyone who has seen Claire, or who has any information regarding her whereabouts, is asked to contact Suffolk Police on 101.
