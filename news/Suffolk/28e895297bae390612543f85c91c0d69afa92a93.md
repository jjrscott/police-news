Police have charged a man in connection with drug offences in Bury St Edmunds

A man was arrested by officers in Hertfordshire yesterday (13 January) for being concerned in supplying controlled drugs. The arrest was made as part on an on-going operation set up by Suffolk Constabulary with the Met Police to tackle County Lines drug dealing that operate between London and Suffolk.

27 year old Zac Patterson of Iverson Road in London was charged with being concerned in the supplying cocaine and heroin from 1 December 2019 to 30 March 2020.

He is due to appear before Hatfield Magistrates' Court today (14 January) .
