Police are appealing for information after a burglary in Ipswich.

The incident took place between 10.30pm on Wednesday 11 July and 6.20am on Thursday 12 July at a property in Cliff Lane.

It is believed an unknown offender created a gap in a neighbour’s fence to gain access to the property and then entered the victim’s home though an unsecured kitchen door.

A work bag, wallet and watch were reported stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious persons or who has any information about this crime is asked to contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 38709/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
