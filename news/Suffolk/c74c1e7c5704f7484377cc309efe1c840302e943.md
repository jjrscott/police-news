Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a home in Woolpit.

The break in is believed to have taken place at some point between 10pm on Tuesday 13 and 7:15am Wednesday 14 June at an address in Steeles Road.

A grey Carrera Hybrid bicycle and a quantity of cash were stolen.

Police are investigating the burglary and are keen to hear from anyone who may have noticed any suspicious behaviour in the area at the times indicated.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting crime reference number 41695/17.
