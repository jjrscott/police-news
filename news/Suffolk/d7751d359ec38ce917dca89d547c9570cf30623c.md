Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward following the theft of a black Ford Focus at a car park in Dennington, near Framlingham.

The vehicle was stolen at some point between 7pm on Tuesday 9 August and 8am the following day whilst it was parked at The Square.

Anyone who may have seen or heard anything suspicious in the area between the times stated or anyone with information about the theft is asked to contact the Police on **101** quoting reference 49276/16.
