Police are appealing for information after an unknown person has smashed a glass panel in the front door of a property in Norwich Road.

The damage occurred in the early hours of this morning, Wednesday 6th January at approximately 1.20am.

A spanner type tool was found nearby.

If you noticed any unusual activity nearby, heard the sound of smashing glass, or have any information about this crime, please contact Suffolk Police quoting reference 37/809/21 via:

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Email - [email protected]

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
