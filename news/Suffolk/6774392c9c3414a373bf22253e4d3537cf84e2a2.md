Police are urging motorists to be vigilant and are appealing for information after two catalytic converter thefts took place in Ipswich.

A catalytic converter was stolen from a Ford Galaxy while it was parked outside a property in Mornington Avenue. The theft occurred sometime between 11.03am Wednesday 11 March and 9am yesterday, Thursday 12 March. (37/15592/20)

A catalytic converter was stolen from another Ford Galaxy parked on Cedarcroft Road sometime between 9pm Wednesday 11 March and 7am yesterday morning, Thursday 12 March. (37/15445/20)

Any witnesses or anyone with any information about these incidents should contact Suffolk police, quoting the relevant crime reference number.
