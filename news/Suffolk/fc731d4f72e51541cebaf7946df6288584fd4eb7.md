Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a home address in Ipswich.

The break-in occurred at some point between 1am and 6:20am on Friday 27 October at the property in Great Whip Street.

Entry is thought to have been gained by climbing through an unlocked ground floor bathroom window.

Offender(s) stole a HP laptop and charger, a Sony camera and a black ladies purse containing a large amount of cash.

Anyone with information regarding the incident is urged to call DC Donna Whelan-Smith Suffolk Police on 101, quoting crime number 37/69845/17 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
