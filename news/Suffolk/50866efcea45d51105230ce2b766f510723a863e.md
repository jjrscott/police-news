Officers from Lowestoft CID have charged two men from Lancashire in connection with drugs importation offences.

36-year-old Steven Brogan of Eskbank in Skelmersdale and 33-year-old Anthony Reilly of Yewdale in Skelmersdale have both been charged with importing class A drugs.

The charges are in connection with a multi-agency response involving police, HM Coastguard, the RNLI and Border Force that took place off the Suffolk coast on Wednesday 30 September, where two men were located in the North Sea with a jet ski.

The two men were searched and arrested and taken to Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre for questioning.
