A missing 22-year-old woman from Lowestoft has been found.

Chelsea Lance was reported missing to police on Thursday 26 November but she was found safe and well in the town yesterday afternoon, Friday 27 November.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 27 November 2020

Police are appealing for assistance in locating a missing woman from Lowestoft.

22-year-old Chelsea Lance was reported missing yesterday evening (Thursday 26 November) after missing a medical appointment.

Chelsea is described as 4 feet 10 inches tall, with straight brown hair and is pregnant.

Police are concerned for her welfare and would like to locate her as quickly as possible.
