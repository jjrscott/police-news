A 53-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of arson with intent to endanger life following a fire at a home in Haverhill.

Police were contacted by the fire service around 8.45pm yesterday, Thursday 7 July, reporting that the fire at a home in Eastern Avenue was believed to be suspicious.

A man was taken to the West Suffolk hospital with minor burn injuries and was discharged in the early hours of this morning, Friday 8 July.

Police are treating this as an isolated incident.

The 53-year-old man arrested remains in custody at Bury St Edmunds Police Investigation Centre where he will be interviewed by officers in connection with the fire.
