A 26-year old man has been sentenced over the serious assault of a man in Ipswich last November.

On Friday 3 November, at approximately 10.45pm, a man in his 20s was seriously assaulted on St Stephens Church Lane in Ipswich.

The victim sustained a number of wounds to his arms and legs that required medical attention.

Following a police investigation, Jamie Phillips, of Turret Lane in Ipswich, was arrested and charged with grievous bodily harm. He appeared at Ipswich Crown Court yesterday (23 April) after admitting the offence and was sentenced to 7 years and 6 months in jail.
