Police are appealing for witnesses to a Mildenhall burglary where items of jewellery were taken.

The incident happened at some point between 5.45pm and 7pm on Friday 4 December at a property on Wentworth Drive.

The address was entered believed to be the back doors of the property, with damage caused to the rear window when gaining entry. An untidy search was carried out by the suspect(s) with various items of jewellery stolen.

Anyone who saw any suspicious activity at the time stated or has knowledge of who committed the incident or knows of the whereabout of the items of jewellery stolen, should contact Bury St Edmunds CID, quoting crime reference 37/64087/20. Images of the items stolewn are below in the gallery.
