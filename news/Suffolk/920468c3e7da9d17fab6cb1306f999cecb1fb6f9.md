Police have arrested a 17 year old teenage male in connection with a robbery in Ipswich.

The incident happened at about 6.50pm on Thursday 26 July on Franciscan Way.

A group of people assaulted a male and female both in their 40s and a woman and male in their 20s with a mobile phone and a bank card stolen in the incident.

All four individuals were taken to hospital for a check-up - the two people in their 40s both sustained minor injuries in the incident.

Police believe this was a targeted assault and police would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it.

The 17 year old male was arrested on suspicion of assault and taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning where he remains.

Anyone with information should contact Ipswich CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/41963/18. Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
