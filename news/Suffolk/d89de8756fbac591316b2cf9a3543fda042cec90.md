Officers are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Corder Road, Ipswich.

Sometime between 11.30pm on Thursday 16 August and 6.30am on Friday 17 August, offender/s entered the house via an unsecured window and exited via a rear door.

Items stolen include a black purse, passport, jewellery, Playstation 4, laptop and a quantity of cash.

Witnesses or anybody with information or who saw anything suspicious at the time are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 46890/18.

Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or through their online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
