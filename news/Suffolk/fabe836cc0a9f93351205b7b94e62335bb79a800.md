Police are appealing for witnesses following a fail to stop road traffic collision in Copdock.

The collision occurred on Thursday 3 October at around 3.50pm, southbound on the A12 at Copdock, opposite the Best Western Hotel.

A white van with blue writing on the passenger side and rear collided with a silver Volkswagen Polo. The driver of the van did not stop at the scene.

The driver of the Volkswagen Polo was left shaken but was not injured.

Officers would be keen to speak to anyone who may have witnessed the collision or who may have captured any dash-cam footage at the time of the incident.

Witnesses should visit http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update. Alternatively, call the Hadleigh Safer Neighbourhood Team on 101.
