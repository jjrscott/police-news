Police are issuing CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the theft of a charity box in Ipswich.

The incident occurred between 1pm and 3pm on Wednesday 3 January at The Station Hotel in Burrell Road.

A donation box belonging to Make-A-Wish was stolen from the bar, which is believed to have contained over Â£100.
