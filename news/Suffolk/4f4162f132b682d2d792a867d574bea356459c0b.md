A 41-year-old man has been charged in connection with three separate bike theft incidents in the town.

The first incident occurred between 4.30pm and 5.30pm on Wednesday 26 August, outside Matalan in Ranelagh Road. A bike was stolen after its lock was cut.

A second incident occurred between 3.45pm and 4.15pm on Sunday 30 August in Anchor Street. A lock was cut and the bike was stolen.

A further incident occurred shortly before 2.30pm on Saturday 5 September, outside Warren Heath Residential Home in Felixstowe Road. A Carrera Vendetta bike was stolen after it was left unlocked.

Officers from the Operation Shere team carried out an investigation and a man was arrested yesterday morning, Monday 12 October. He was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

Robertas Jasinskas, 41, of Ancaster Road, Ipswich, was subsequently charged in relation to the three incidents.
