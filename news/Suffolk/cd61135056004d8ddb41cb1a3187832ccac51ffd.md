Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary in Ipswich.

The incident occurred at approximately 7:40pm on Sunday 30 April at a home address in Digby Road.

The victim became aware that a man was attempting to gain access into his home via the rear doors, and challenged him. The suspect fled the property and it is believed that nothing was stolen.

A search of the area was carried-out, assisted by the police dog unit, but the suspect was not located.

He is described as around 6ft in height, approximately 20 – 22 years’ old and of athletic build. He was dressed all in black.

Witnesses or anybody with any information are asked to call Ipswich CID at Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference number 32243/17.
