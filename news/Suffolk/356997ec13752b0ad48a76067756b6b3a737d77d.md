Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place at Market Weston near Diss.

It happened on Wednesday 8 August at some point between 9.30am and 4.40pm at a property on Church Road.

The offender(s) forced entry by using a pair of short handled garden sheers and a spade/shovel. This has been used on a single window at the side of the property into the dining room, where a tidy and untidy search has been conducted. Access was also gained to a safe by unknown means. Jewellery, paper documents and a laptop have been stolen from the address.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious activity or has knowledge of the whereabouts of any of the items stolen should contact Bury St Edmunds police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/45043/18.Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
