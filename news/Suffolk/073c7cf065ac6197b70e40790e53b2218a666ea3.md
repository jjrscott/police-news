A missing 18-year-old man from Ipswich has been found.

Kurtis Lloyd had last been seen at 10am on Sunday 11 March at his home address in South East Ipswich.

He was located safe and well by police in Stowmarket this morning, Tuesday 13 March.

Officers would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
