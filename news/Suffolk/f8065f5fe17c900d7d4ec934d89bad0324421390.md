Police are appealing for witnesses to an attempted burglary that took place in Ipswich.

It happened on Monday 26 November at about 10.50am at a property on Discovery Avenue.

Two males were disturbed in the victim's kitchen during the attempt during this time one of the males picked up a knife and swung it towards the female victim, causing them a minor injury to their arm.

Following the attack both males fled from the scene through the back garden.

The offenders are described as follows: Male, white, in his late teens with a dark puffer jacket and the hood pulled up with a black scarf around his nose and mouth and wore black bottoms. The second male is described as wearing a black or dark navy puffer jacket with a black scar around his neck and about six foot tall.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious behaviour prior to the incident or has knowledge of it should contact Ipswich CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/68315/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
