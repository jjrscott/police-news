A missing 74-year-old woman from Mildenhall has been found.

Anne Hutchinson was reported missing to police shortly before 9.45pm on Monday 24 August and she was found yesterday, Wednesday 26 August, at around 6.40pm.

Police would like to thank members of the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.
