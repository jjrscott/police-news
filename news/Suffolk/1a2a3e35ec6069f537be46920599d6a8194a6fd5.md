A man has sadly died following a road traffic collision on the westbound A14 at Creeting St Mary.

Police were called around 7.15pm on Friday 20 January, to reports a car had collided with a lorry close to the A140 Beacon Hill junction.

Both vehicles caught fire and the man, believed to be the driver of the car, died at the scene.

The road was closed and diversions were put in place while an investigation got underway and reopened at 3am.

Police are investigating what happened and are asking for witnesses to call the Serious Collision Investigation Team at Suffolk Police on **101**.
