Six people have been reported for the offence of hare coursing following an incident in Mid Suffolk.

Police were called at 9.30am yesterday morning, Thursday 8 December, to reports of a group of people with dogs and two vehicles, on a field near to Lyle Close in Elmswell.

A number of police units were immediately sent to the area, supported by the National Police Air Service helicopter, and began searching for the suspects.

Officers detained the first suspect at around 10.25am and by 11am six adults and one child had all been detained. Two vehicles – a Subaru Impreza and a Honda CRV – were both seized under the Wildlife and Countryside Act.

The six adults have all been reported for consideration of the offence of trespassing in pursuit of game.

Their details are as follows:

 * A 46-year-old man from Basildon
 * A 41-year-old man from Northampton
 * A 38-year-old man from the Northampton area
 * A 32-year-old man from the Northampton area
 * A 23-year-old man from Northampton
 * A 20-year-old man from Northampton

Police enquiries into the incident continue and any witnesses are asked to contact the Stowmarket Neighbourhood Response Team on **101** quoting reference 70550/16.
