A missing 57-year-old woman from Beck Row has been found.

Beatrice Priestney had last been seen on Thursday 16 March in Bury St Edmunds, but has now been located in Spain following enquiries by police.

Police would like to thank members of the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.
