Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of suspected arson and criminal damage following an incident in Ipswich.

It happened early on Saturday 14 April at some point between midnight and 2.30am at the Moto Works yard on Stoke Hall Road.

Five scooters, four Yamaha models and one Suzuki model, at the premises had been pushed over and damaged and a further scooter, a Yamaha model, had been moved to another part of the yard and set alight and damaged.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge of the incident should contact Ipswich police on 101, quoting crime number 37/20231/18 or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 080055511.
