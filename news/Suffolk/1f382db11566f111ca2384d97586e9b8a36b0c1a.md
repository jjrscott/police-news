Police are appealing for witnesses and information following an assault in Lowestoft.

The incident occurred at around 4.30pm on Thursday 7 January at the junction with St Margarets Road and Park Road.

The victim – a woman aged in her 20s – was walking back from the shops nearby, when she was approached by three unknown males. Two of the males then spat on her and the group continued on their way.

As a result of the incident, the woman was not physically harmed but was left shaken

The three males were all described as being approximately 5ft 3ins tall, aged between 16 and 17-years-old, all wearing either jeans or tracksuits. One of them wore a blue and dark red jacket and all three had dark hair.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/1212/21
