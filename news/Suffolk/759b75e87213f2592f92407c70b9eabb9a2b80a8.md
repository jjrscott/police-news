Police are currently at the scene of a serious road traffic collision in Boxford.

Officers were called at 12.15pm this afternoon, Friday 16 February, to reports of a two vehicle collision on the A1071, involving a Ford Transit van and a Ford Galaxy.

A female passenger in the Ford Galaxy was airlifted to Addenbrookeâ€™s Hospital having sustained potentially life-changing injuries, with the driver of the Ford Galaxy taken to Ipswich Hospital for treatment to what are believed to be minor injuries.

The road is currently closed whilst a collision investigation takes place and local diversions have been put in place.

Any witnesses to this incident are asked to contact the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting reference CAD 167 of 16 February.
