Police are appealing for witnesses or information following a burglary in Weybread.

At some point between 8.30am on Wednesday 15 April and 10am on Thursday 16 April, unknown offender/s gained entry to a shared site for Waveney Windsurfers & Small Craft Club, Beccles Sea Cadets and Waveney Water Lions in Withersdale Road.

Once inside, access was gained to a metal container and two storage sheds and a number of items were stolen from within including generators, an electric pump, mowers, outboard motors, GPS sat maps, buoyancy aids, ride on mower, water pump, water pipe, two Avon rowing dinghies, wooden oars, deep cycle batteries, a trailer, two kayaks and stakes, among other items.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/21495/20
