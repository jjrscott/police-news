Police have charged a man from Newmarket with common assault.

A 47 year old man was arrested on Wednesday 4 July and taken to Bury St Edmunds Police investigation Centre for questioning.

Iftikhar Ali of New Cheveley Road in Newmarket was charged yesterday (Thurs 5 July) with assault. It follows an incident where a 15 year old girl was assaulted on 25 June in Princess Way in the town. The victim was shaken but unhurt in the incident.

He has been released from police custody and bailed to appear before Ipswich magistrates court on Monday 23 July.
