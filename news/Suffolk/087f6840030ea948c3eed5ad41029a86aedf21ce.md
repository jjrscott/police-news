A missing 15-year-old girl from Sudbury has been located.

Evie Sims was reported missing after leaving home during the evening of Monday 25 February and failing to return.

She was found safe and well last night, Tuesday 26 February.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
