Officers have charged a 23 year old man following an incident in Bentley.

Police were called at 11.30am on Wednesday 1 February, to reports of a burglary in progress at a property on Capel Road. Officers quickly attended to locate the suspect who had fled the scene on foot towards the A12. The road was closed for a short time in both directions.

James Preston of Bardfield Road, Colchester has been charged with the following offences:

 * burglary at an address in East Mill Green, Bentley
 * attempted burglary at a property in Capel Road, Bentley
 * aggravated burglary with intent at an address in Pound Lane, Capel St Mary
 * attempting to rob a man of his vehicle in Capel St Mary
 * possession of a meat cleaver in a public place
 * assault of a police officer

Preston is due to appear before Ipswich Magistrates Court today, Friday 3 February.

## Previous release | 01/02/2017

Officers have arrested a man following an incident in Bentley near Ipswich this morning.

Police were called at 11.30am today, Wednesday 1 February, to reports of a burglary in progress at a property on Capel Road. Officers quickly attended to locate the suspect who had fled the scene on foot towards the A12. The road was closed for a short time in both directions but has now re-opened.

A 23 year old man from Colchester has been detained in connection with the incident and will be taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning by detectives.

Officers would like to thank members of the public for their assistance.

Anybody who witnessed the incident is asked to call Ipswich CID on **101** quoting CAD 137 of today.
