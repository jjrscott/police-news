Police are appealing for witnesses following a series of thefts and criminal damage in Woodbridge.

The incident occurred between 00:01 and 00:30am on Monday 27 November at Seckford Golf Club in Woodbridge.

Two offenders removed six EZ-GO golf buggies, before driving them away to the top of the driving range. Five batteries were stolen from the buggies, with the sixth buggy being found half a mile down the road, also with its battery removed.

Extensive damage has also been caused to the buggies as a result of the battery thefts.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity in the area, or who has any information about this crime, is asked to contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 76036/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
