Three men have been charged with drug offences in Lowestoft.

Officers were called to an address on Britten Road in the town on 18 February and arrested three men on suspicion of drug offences.

Richard Chivers, 43 of Burgate Road, Felixstowe has been charged with possession with intent to supply class A drugs (crack cocaine and heroin). He has been bailed to return to Ipswich Crown Court on 20 March 2017.

Michael Barclay, 41 of Britten Road, Lowestoft has been charged with possession with intent to supply class A drugs, (crack cocaine and heroin), dishonestly using a quantity of electricity and allowing a premises to be used to supply or attempting to supply controlled drugs. Byron Paris, 21 of Star Lane Ipswich has been charged with possession with intent to supply class A drugs, (crack cocaine and heroin). Both men have been remanded in custody.

All three men are due to appear before Ipswich Crown Court on 20 March 2017.
