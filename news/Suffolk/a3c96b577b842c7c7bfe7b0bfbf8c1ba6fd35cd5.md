Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Felixstowe where a quantity of jewellery was stolen.

The break in happened at a home address in Lodge Farm Drive between 9:50am and 12:45pm on Wednesday 4 January, with entry being gained to the rear of the property and a search being made.

The items taken include a gold ring with cluster of diamonds; an aquamarine ring with three stones in between diamonds, set in gold; a gold chain with pig charm, in gold with red eyes; an amethyst brooch set in gold with pearls surrounding the stone and various other gold chains and bracelets.

Police are appealing to anyone who may have seen or heard anything suspicious to come forward and would also like to hear from anyone who may have been offered any similar items of jewellery. Anyone with information should contact Southern Area CID on **101** , quoting reference 10600/17. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers, anonymously if required, on **0800 555 111**.
