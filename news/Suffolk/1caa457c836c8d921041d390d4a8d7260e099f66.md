A man has been charged with drug offences in Ipswich.

Officers made an arrest with the Metropolitan police in the Chadwell Heath area of East London yesterday (Wed 17 February) on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs and he was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

28 year old Akim Rowe of Cavendish Gardens, Romford was charged with being concerned in the supply of Class A (heroin and crack cocaine) drugs and for failing to stop for police, relating to a separate incident on 6 October in Ipswich.

He was remanded to appear before Ipswich Magistrates Court this morning (Thursday 18 February).
