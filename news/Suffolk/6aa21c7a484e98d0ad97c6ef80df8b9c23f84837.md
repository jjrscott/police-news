A missing 12 year-old-boy from Wetherden has been found.

Paul Clarke had last been seen at 9.30pm on Wednesday 25 January, at his home address in Wetherden, near Stowmarket.

Paul has been found safe and well in Kent this afternoon, Thursday 26 January.

Police would like to thank members of the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.
