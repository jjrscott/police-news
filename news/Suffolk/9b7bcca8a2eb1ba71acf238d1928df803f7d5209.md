Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Onehouse near Stowmarket.

The incident took place between 8.30pm and 8.45pm on Thursday 2 August at a property in Ash Road.

An unknown offender/s gained entry via an insecure rear door. A search was made of the kitchen but it does not appear that anything was stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity or who has any information about this crime is asked to contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting reference 43739/18.

Alternatively contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
