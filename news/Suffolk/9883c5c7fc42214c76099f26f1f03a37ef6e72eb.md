Police were called on Friday 3 February at around 11.30pm to reports of a disturbance at an address on Thompson Drive, Rendlesham.

On arrival officers discovered a large number of cannabis plants inside the premises.

A 64 year old man and a 47 year old man both from the North Essex area have been arrested on suspicion of cultivating cannabis. A 37 year old man from Felixstowe has also been arrested in connection with this incident.

All three men have been bailed to return to police at Martlesham Investigation Centre on 23 March 2017.
