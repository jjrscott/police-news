Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Knodishall, near Saxmundham.

At some point between 8pm on Tuesday 1 May and 10:30am on Thursday 3 May, offender(s) smashed a ground floor window to gain entry into a property. Once inside, the suspect(s) made a tidy search and stole various items from within, including a shotgun that had been locked in a cabinet, an air rifle, electrical tools and a camera lens.

The shotgun is a Browning B525 12G O/U, with the serial number 573912W.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any knowledge of it is asked to contact Lowestoft CID on 101 quoting crime reference 37/24061/18. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
