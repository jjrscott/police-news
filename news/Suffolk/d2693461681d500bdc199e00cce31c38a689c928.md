Police have arrested one man in connection with counterfeit goods in Lowestoft this morning (Fri 4 Dec).

Following information from a member of the public officers attended a business premises on London Road South at about 9am and recovered a quantity of boxes containing suspected counterfeit cigarettes without excise duty being paid.

Cigarettes and tobacco were seized by officers from the Lowestoft Safer Neighbourhood Team.

A 38 year old man was arrested and taken to Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre for questioning where he remains.
