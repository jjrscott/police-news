Police are issuing CCTV images of three males they would like to speak to in connection with a burglary in Lowestoft.

The incident occurred at around 12.35am on Wednesday 28 October at O2 in London Road North.

At around the time stated, three males kicked the top window of the front door at O2 before entering and attempting to take mobile phones and tablets but they left empty-handed.

The first suspect is described as male. He was wearing a grey/light back jacket with black arms, grey tracksuit bottoms and white trainers.

The second suspect is described as male, 6ft tall, with short black hair and had a tanned complexion. He was wearing a scarf covering his mouth, a blue baseball style jacket with mustard-coloured arms and buttons down the middle. He was also wearing dark bottoms and grey trainers.

The third male was shorter than the other two, also had a tanned complexion and was wearing a dark grey jacket with the hood up, a blue jumper with a white diamond-shaped logo on the front, dark bottoms and black trainers.

Officers would like to speak to the three males pictured in the CCTV images about the incident as they may be able to help with the investigation.

Anyone who believes they recognise the males pictured in the CCTV images, or who has any information about this incident, is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/62552/20
