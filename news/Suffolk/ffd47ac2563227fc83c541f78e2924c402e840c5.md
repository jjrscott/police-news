Police are appealing for witnesses in relation to a burglary that took place in West Row near Bury St Edmunds.

It happened at some point between 8pm and 10pm on Saturday 7 October at a property on Hurdle Drove.

The offenders) gained entry by smashing a glass rear patio door. Items stolen include cash, clothing and a computer. It is believed there were four offenders who were possibly using a silver coloured car.

Anyone who has knowledge of the incident or saw any suspicious activity during the times stated should contact DC Anna Harvey at Bury St Edmunds CID on 101 quoting crime reference 37/65711/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
