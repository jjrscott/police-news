Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward following a public order incident in Lowestoft.

On Sunday 21 October just before 6pm, a report was received of a man spitting near to a bench on London Road North in the town.

A man has been interviewed in connection with the incident.

Police are continuing to appeal for witnesses and would particularly like to hear from any witnesses who may have footage of the incident.

Any information to Suffolk Police on 101, quoting reference number 37/60977/18.
