Police are appealing for witnesses following a robbery in Lowestoft yesterday (Thursday 26 July).

Officers were called to Regent Road in the town at approximately 11.35am on Thursday 26 July, following a report of a robbery.

An offender approached the victim, an elderly woman, and snatched her handbag, containing a mobile phone, keys and purse. He ran from the scene on Regent Road across Katwijk Way towards Clapham Road Central.

The offender is described as a white male, aged between 16 and 24 years old. He was wearing a black t-shirt and black shorts and was carrying a sports drawstring bag over his shoulder.

A man has been arrested in connection with the offence but has been released under investigation.

Anyone with any information should contact DC Chris Thompson at Lowestoft CID on 101, quoting crime reference number 37/41870/18.
