Three men have been remanded in custody after being charged with the supply of drugs in Ipswich.

Twenty-four year old Amiadul Islam, 23-year-old Daniel Kaganda and 27-year-old Aidid Mohamed were arrested yesterday in the Newham area of east London and brought into custody at Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

They have subsequently been charged with conspiracy to supply Class A drugs and have been remanded in custody to appear before Ipswich Magistrates Court tomorrow, Saturday 8 July.
