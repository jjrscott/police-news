An 81-year-old man who went missing from Stowmarket yesterday has been found.

Thomas Pritchard was reported as missing to police at 12.20pm yesterday, Tuesday 22 January but was located safe and well yesterday afternoon.

Police would like to thanks the media and public for their help with this matter.
