Police are appealing for information after a wheelie bin store was set alight in Southwold.

Around 60 bins belonging to Waveney Norse were destroyed or damaged in the fire, which took place at some point overnight between Monday 23 April and Tuesday 24 April at the bin store in Southwold Pier car park in North Parade.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge about this incident should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime number 22245/18. Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
