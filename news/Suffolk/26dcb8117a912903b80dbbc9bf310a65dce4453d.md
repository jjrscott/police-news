Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident of voyeurism in Lowestoft.

The crime occurred at around 10.30pm last night, Wednesday 4 July, at an address in Oulton Road North.

The woman living at the property reported seeing a figure standing outside her bedroom window looking in at her. When she approached the window to investigate the person then ran away. It is not known if the suspect was male or female.

Anyone who witnessed anyone acting suspiciously in the area of Oulton Road North last night is asked to contact Lowestoft Police on 101 quoting reference 37163/18.
