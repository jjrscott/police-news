Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Ipswich.

Officers were called shortly before 6pm on Friday 15 June after receiving reports that a male was witnessed allegedly assaulting a female in the vicinity of St Margaret’s Street.

A 30-year-old man was arrested a short time later on suspicion of common assault. He was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning but was later released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Officers would be keen to speak to anyone who may have seen the alleged incident taking place between the times of 5:45pm and 6pm in St Margaret’s Street and on the junction of St Margaret’s Green.

Anyone who witnessed the alleged incident or has any information should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting reference 32906/18 or the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
