A 35-year-old man from Haverhill who was reported missing, has returned home.

Jason Hewitt had last been seen at his home address at around 1am yesterday morning, Thursday 21 January, and was thought to have left on foot in the early hours.

Mr Hewitt returned home safely yesterday evening.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
