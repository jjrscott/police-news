Police are appealing for information after a quantity of jewellery was stolen in a burglary at a home near Eye.

The burglary happened at the address in the Hestley Green area between 8.45am and 11pm on Friday 27 January.

The home was broken into and searched. Among the items taken were items of jewellery including a Rolex watch and various other items of jewellery of high value.

Police are investigating the break-in and are keen to hear from anyone who may have noticed any suspicious behaviour in the area on Friday 27, or anyone who may have been offered items of jewellery for sale since.

If you saw anything out of the ordinary in the vicinity around this time or if you have any information about who stole the items or where they are now, please contact Waveney CID by dialling Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 14544/17.
