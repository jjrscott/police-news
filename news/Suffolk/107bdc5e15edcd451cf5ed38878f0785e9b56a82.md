A 59-year-old man has been arrested in connection with a dangerous driving incident in Haughley.

Police were contacted at approximately 5:20am this morning, Friday 30 June, to reports a lorry travelling on the westbound A14 had collided with the central reservation and crossed into the opposite carriageway.

One lane remains closed on either side of the carriageway as repairs are carried out.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision, has dash cam footage of the incident, or may have seen the manner of driving of the lorry.

Anyone with information should contact the Roads Policing Team at Suffolk Police on 101, or via the ‘Report Something’ page on the Constabulary website https://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/report-something, quoting CAD 43 of 30 June.
