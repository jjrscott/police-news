Police are appealing for information after a schoolgirl was approached by a man in a car in Stowmarket.

The incident occurred at some time between 3pm and 3.20pm on Monday 20 February at the top of Cormorant Drive, near the junction with Creeting Road.

The girl was approached by a man in a black Citroen car who tried to speak with her before driving off.

The man is described as white, aged in his late thirties and with black/brown hair. He was wearing a white shirt, tie and suit jacket.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who saw a man matching this description or a black Citroen car around the time stated above.

Any witnesses are asked to contact Stowmarket Safer Neighbourhood Team on **101** quoting reference 19145/17.
