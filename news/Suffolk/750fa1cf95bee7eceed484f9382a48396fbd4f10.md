Police are appealing for witnesses after a burglary in Fornham St Martin.

The incident occurred between 11.30am on Thursday 6 December and 11.45am on Saturday 8 December at an address in Birkdale Court.

The glass in a rear patio door was smashed to gain entry to the property and cash and jewellery were stolen from inside.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity in the area, or who has any information about this crime, is asked to contact West CID at Bury St Edmunds Police Station on 101 quoting reference 70948/18, or the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
