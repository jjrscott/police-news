Police are appealing for witnesses after two people were assaulted in Felixstowe.

The incident occurred at around 4.40pm on Monday 20 November in Victoria Street, close to the junction with Cobbold Road.

It is alleged that during the altercation a man and woman – both aged in their 30s – were assaulted by two other men, before a member of the public intervened.

Officers were called and subsequently arrested two men - aged 32 and 26 and both from Felixstowe - on suspicion of assault. They were taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning and have been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Officers are appealing for any witnesses to this incident to make contact. Anyone with information is asked to call Martlesham Custody Investigation Unit on 101 quoting reference 74677/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
