Four men are being questioned by police following an alarm activation early this morning (Monday 2 October).

At approximately 1am police received reports of a burglary in progress at a premise on Cherry Lane in Browston.

On arrival three vehicles were seen leaving the location. They were stopped and four males were arrested in connection with the incident:

 * 19 year old man from Great Yarmouth
 * 24 year old man from Lowestoft
 * 19 year old man from Lowestoft
 * 27 year old from Lowestoft

They all remain in custody at Great Yarmouth Police Investigation Centre awaiting questioning.
