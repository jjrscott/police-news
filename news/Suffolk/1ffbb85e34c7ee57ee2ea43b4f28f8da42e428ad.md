Police are appealing for information following a burglary at a home in Debenham.

The burglary occurred at a home in Gardeners Road at some point between 10pm on Wednesday 23rd and 7.30am this morning, Thursday 24th September.

A quantity of cash was stolen together with a mobile phone, 2 bottles of alcohol and a set of keys.

Can you help?

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference number: 37/55714/20

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Phone – Call 101

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.

Officers are reminding residents to ensure doors and windows are secure overnight. Security advice can be found via the Suffolk Police website www.suffolk.police.uk or via this link
