Motorists are advised they may experience delays on Thursday 4 May as police escort the transportation of an abnormal load through the county.

The 5.2m wide, 24.9m long and 5.9m high cruiser weighing 59,000kg will move at 9am from A14 Cambs border to Ipswich Haven Marine, Key Street, Ipswich.

 **The route will be as follows:**

A14 Cambs border - A1303 via Newmarket - B1506 Rejoin A14 - A1308 - B1113 Needham Market - Gt Blakenham - Lorraine Way - Bramford Way - Sproughton Village - A1071 - A1214 - A137 - West End Road - Bridge Street - Haven Marina

Delays can be expected along these routes.
