*Update : recaptured by police on Sunday 18 December*

Police are appealing for help to trace a Hollesley Bay prisoner who was reported missing this morning.

Michael White, aged 20, was last seen at 11.30pm on Friday 16 December, and was reported missing following a prison roll call at 6.30am on Saturday 17 December. He is serving a sentence of 3 and a half years for robbery.

White is described as a white male, 5 foot 10 inches tall, with blue eyes and brown hair. He is of slight build and is clean shaven.

Anyone with any information as to his whereabouts is asked to contact Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting CAD reference number 100 of today, Saturday 17 December.
