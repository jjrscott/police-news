Police are releasing CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to following an incident of making off without payment in Beccles.

The incident took place at around 8.30 am on Saturday 29 October at a business on George Westood Way.

A man driving a silver Renault Megane, displaying index number ET04ZBN, filled the car with more than 50 litres of unleaded petrol. The man then drove off without making any attempt to pay. It is believed that the number plate may be false.
