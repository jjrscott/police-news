Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a home address in Ipswich.

The break-in occurred between 11:30am and 3pm on Wednesday 24 January at a property in Foxhall Road.

At some point during the times stated, offender(s) forced entry by smashing glass patio doors before carrying out an untidy search. Items of jewellery were stolen.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any knowledge of it is asked to contact Ipswich CID on 101 quoting reference 4565/18 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111
