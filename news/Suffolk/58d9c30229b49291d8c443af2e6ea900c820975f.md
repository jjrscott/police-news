Officers are appealing for witnesses following a fail-to-stop collision in Melton earlier today, Tuesday 13 September.

The collision took place on Wilford Bridge Road, Melton at approximately 8.25am. A teenage boy on a bicycle collided with a tractor and trailer which failed to stop.

The cyclist multiple deep lacerations, face, arms, hands elbows and legs which required hospital treatment.

Witnesses or anybody with any information are asked to call Suffolk Police on **101** reference CAD 65 of 13 September. Alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111** or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org
