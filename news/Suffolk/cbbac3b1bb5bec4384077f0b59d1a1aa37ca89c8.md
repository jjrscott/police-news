A 16-year-old missing person from Ipswich has been found.

Emily Hodgins – who is also known as Kieran – had last been seen at home at around 9pm on Sunday 1 April.

Emily has been found safe and well in Norwich this evening, Tuesday 3 April.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
