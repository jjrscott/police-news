Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary that took place in Hitcham on Boxing Day.

Sometime between 12 pm midday and 10.15pm on Monday 26 December, offender/s forced entry to the rear of an address on Bury Road, Hitcham. An untidy search was made of the property and cash and jewellery were stolen. The jewellery is of great sentimental value to the victims.

Witnesses or anybody who saw anything suspicious are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 73728/16. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
