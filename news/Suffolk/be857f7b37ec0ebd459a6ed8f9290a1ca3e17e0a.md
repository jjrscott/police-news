Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious road traffic collision in Bury St Edmunds yesterday evening (Thursday 9 March).

Suffolk Police received a call at 5.20pm reporting a collision between a blue Yamaha motorcycle and a blue Volvo V70 on Oakes Road at the junction with Davoren Walk.

The motorcyclist, a man in his 50s, was taken to Addenbrookeâ€™s hospital, where he is undergoing treatment for a life changing injury.

The road was closed following the collision and reopened just after 9pm.

Police would like to hear from any witnesses to the collision, or who may have seen the manner of driving of either vehicle beforehand.

Anyone with any information should call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting CAD reference number 324 of Thursday 9 March.
