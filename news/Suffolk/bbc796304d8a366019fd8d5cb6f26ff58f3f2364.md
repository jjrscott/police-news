Police are appealing for witnesses after a burglary that took place in Elmsett.

It took place at a property on Mill Lane at some point overnight between Monday 15 January at 8pm and Tuesday 16 January at 8am. During this period unknown offender(s) have entered victim's garage, by forcing the back window, climbed through and then kicked the back door of the garage to open from the inside. The offenders have poured PVA glue and oil all over the garage and stolen a two high value cycles stolen. One was an Orbea Orca 2014 black carbon fibre cycles with blue paint and white writing on. The second was of the same paintwork but a Orbea Onix brand. The total value of the cycles stolen was Â£10,000.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has knowledge of it or knows of the whereabouts of the two cycles stolen should contact Ipswich police on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/3059/18.
