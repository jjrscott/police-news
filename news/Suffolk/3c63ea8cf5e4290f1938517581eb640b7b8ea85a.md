Officers are appealing for witnesses following two thefts in Tostock.

The first incident took place on Monday 19 March at around 3.30am. Offender/s entered a Vauxhall van parked on Church Road and stole a large number of power tools from within, including drills, saws and grinders.

A short time later a blue Mercedes A Class car index mark KR07CXD was stolen from Tostock Park Stud on Church Road. Three other vehicles at the same address were also entered by unknown offender/s but nothing was stolen from within.

Witnesses or anybody with any information about either of these incidents is asked to contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 14928/18 (theft of power tools) or 14919/18 (theft of Mercedes car) Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
