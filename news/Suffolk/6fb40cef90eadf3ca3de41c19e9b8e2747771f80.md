Police are issuing CCTV images of a male and a female that officers want speak to in connection with an incident of suspected fraud that occurred in Lowestoft.

The individuals were captured on CCTV in a local shop in the town on Saturday 23 September 2017. A stolen debit card was used in the incident.

Anyone who may recognise the male and female is asked to call Lowestoft CID on 101 quoting crime number 37/62705/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
