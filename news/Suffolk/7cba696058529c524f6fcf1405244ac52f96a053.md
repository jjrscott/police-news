Suffolk Police have charged a 19-year-old man in connection with an incident in Henley earlier this month.

Brandon Pitcher-Jolly, of no fixed abode, has been charged with: burglary, aggravated vehicle-taking, dangerous driving, driving without insurance and driving without a licence.

The charge relates to an incident on Saturday 9 June where a house was broken into on Ashbocking Road and a vehicle taken.

Two other people have been arrested in connection with the incident and released under investigation. Pitcher-Jolly appeared in court yesterday (Monday 18 June) has been bailed to appear at Ipswich Crown Court on 3 July.
