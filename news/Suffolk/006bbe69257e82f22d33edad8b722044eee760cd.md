Police can confirm that a 20-year-old missing man from Lowestoft has been found.

Daniel Deverill was found safe and well this afternoon, Saturday 17 November.

Officers would like to thank the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | Friday 16 November

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 20-year-old man from Lowestoft.

Daniel Deverill was last seen on Waveney Drive, Lowestoft, at around 4.30pm on Friday 16 November. He was seen walking towards the town centre and library area.

Daniel is described as white, of slim build with short dark brown hair and goatee beard. He was last seen wearing dark clothing and a dark hoody.

Anyone who has seen Daniel or anybody with any information is asked to contact Suffolk police on 101.
