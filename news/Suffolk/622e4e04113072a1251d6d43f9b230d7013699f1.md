Police searching for a teenager who went missing from Ipswich has been located.

15 year old Arminas Nauseda went missing from his home address in the town on Friday 9 November.

He was located in Colchester yesterday afternoon (Sunday 9 December).

Police would like to thank the public and media for their help with the matter.

## Previous release I 6 December 2018

Police are growing increasingly concerned for the welfare of a 15 year old missing teenager from Ipswich.

Arminas Nauseda went missing from his home address in the town on Friday 9 November.

He is believed to have links to Stowmarket and Colchester.

He is described as six foot, one inch tall, of slim build and with curly brown hair.

There have been occasional sightings of him in Ipswich in recent days but police are concerned for his welfare and enquires are on-going to locate him.

Anyone who has seen him, seen a person matching the description given or has information as to his whereabouts should contact Suffolk Constabulary on 101.
