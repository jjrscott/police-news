Police are appealing for information following an incident of indecent exposure in Lowestoft.

It took place at around 8am on Wednesday 26 October on Whapload Road when a man was reportedly seen acting indecently.

A 23-year-old man from the Lowestoft area has been arrested in connection with the incident and has been released on police bail until Monday 28 November pending further enquiries.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who may have noticed anything suspicious in the area and ask them to contact Lowestoft Neighbourhood Response Team on 101, quoting reference 62783/16.
