Officers are appealing for information or witnesses following an incident of criminal damage in Great Waldingfield.

Sometime between 9am on Monday 3 April and 5pm on Monday 17 April, at a business premises on Waldingfield Airfield, suspects entered and caused damage to a Hitachi excavator machine and a Liebherr powered shovel. The windows of the digger were smashed, and the dashboard damaged. Both vehicles were covered in graffiti using green spray paint. The damage is estimated to be in the region of Â£20,000.

Witnesses or anybody who saw anything suspicious at the time or who has any information are asked to contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference 29795/17. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111** or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
