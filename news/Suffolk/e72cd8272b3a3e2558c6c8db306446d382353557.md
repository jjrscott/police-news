The arrest was made shortly before 7pm on Saturday 18 July, while officers were on patrol in the town.

Officers stopped and searched a man in Orford Street and he was taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning.

Arthur Edwards, 50, of Bramford Lane, was subsequently charged with three offences of possession with intent to supply Class A and B drugs.
