Police are appealing for witnesses or information following the theft of a trailer in Stradbroke.

The incident occurred between 10pm yesterday, Monday 30 November and 8.15am today, Tuesday 1 December outside a property in Farriers Close.

At some point during the times stated, a silver Paxton trailer with a cage and white wheels was stolen from the rear of a white Citroen Berlingo van (registration plate AP53 UHL), while it was parked on the road outside.

Anyone with information about the incident, or who believes they have seen a trailer matching this description, is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/69548/20
