Police can confirm that a 63-year-old missing woman from Lowestoft has been found.

Stephanie Parker was last seen at her home address at 11:30am yesterday and reported as missing to police.

Stephanie has been located safe and well in Norwich this afternoon, Wednesday 20 June.

Officers would like to thank the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
