Officers are appealing for information following a racially aggravated assault.

The incident occurred on Monday 10 June at around 7.15pm in North Hill, Woodbridge.

A 14-year-old boy was walking home when he became aware that an unknown male was approaching him. The male then verbally abused the boy before punching him in the stomach, causing pain and discomfort. The male then ran off down Bredfield Street.

The male offender is described as white, 5ft 11ins, aged between 30 and 40 years of age, of medium build, bald and with a ginger beard. He was wearing a grey jacket, dark jeans and white trainers.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or anyone with information is asked to visit www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update quoting crime reference 37/34343/19, or telephone 101 or contact PC Mortimer by emailing [email protected]

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
