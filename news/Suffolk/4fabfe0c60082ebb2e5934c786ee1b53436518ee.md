Police have charged a teenager following a burglary in Ipswich.

Claydon High School was broken into at around 10.10pm on Tuesday 15 January, and a computer monitor was stolen from within.

A 14-year-old, who cannot be named for legal reasons, was charged with burglary.

She was released on police bail and is due to return to Ipswich Youth Court on Thursday 7 February.

A 15-year-old, who was initially arrested on suspicion of burglary, will face no further police action at this time.
