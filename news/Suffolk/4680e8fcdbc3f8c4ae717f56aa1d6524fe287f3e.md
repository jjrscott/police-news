A missing 81-year-old man from Wangford has been found.

Roy Beckett had been missing from his home address in the village since 2.30pm yesterday afternoon, Wednesday 3 January, but has now returned home safe and well.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with their appeal.
