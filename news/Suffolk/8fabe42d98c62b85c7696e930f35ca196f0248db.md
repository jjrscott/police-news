A missing 23-year-old woman with links to Ipswich has been found.

Payton McCarry was last seen at approximately 7am yeserday morning, Sunday 3 June, in Bramford Road, Ipswich.

She was found safe and well by police in Ipswich in the early hours of this morning, Monday 4 June.

Officers would like to thank members of the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.
