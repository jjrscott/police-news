Police are appealing for information after a pre-school was vandalised with dog faeces in Ipswich.

The incident occurred overnight between Thursday 8 September and Friday 9 September at the premises in Dryden Road.

Dog faeces was smeared over the front door window, behind the door handles and in the keyhole. A number of dog faeces bags had also been thrown about and hung on door handles and off fences.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact the Ipswich West Safer Neighbourhood Team on **101** quoting reference 54544/16, or Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
