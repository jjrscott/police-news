Three people have been arrested in connection with drug offences in Bury St Edmunds.

The arrests were made on Saturday 5 January at a premises on Westley Road in the town at about 12.15pm

Officers entered the property with a 26 year old woman, a 19 year old male and a year old 54 year old man all arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply drugs.

A large amount of what is believed to be class A wraps and quantity of bank notes were located within property.

The three individuals were taken to Bury Ste Edmunds Police Investigation unit for questioning.

19 year old Ralph Levins of Westley Road in Bury St. Edmunds was subsequently charged on Sunday 6 January with supplying of a quantity of cocaine and a quantity of diamorphine.

He appeared before Ipswich Magistrates Court this morning (Monday 7 January) at 10am where he was remanded to appear before Ipswich Crown Court on 4 February at 10am

The 26 year old woman and 54 year old man were both released under investigation pending further enquiries.
