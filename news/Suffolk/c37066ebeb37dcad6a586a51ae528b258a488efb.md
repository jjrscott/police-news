Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Kessingland.

At some point between Sunday 25 March at 4pm and Monday 26 March at 7.30pm, offenders broke into outdoor aviaries at an address on Catchpole Close, taking 20 canaries and some miniature quail.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area between the times stated.

Anyone with any information should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/17059/18.
