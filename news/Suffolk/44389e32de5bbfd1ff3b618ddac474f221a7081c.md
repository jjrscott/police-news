Police are appealing for witnesses after a cyclist was injured in a road traffic collision in Ipswich.

The collision took place yesterday, Monday 25 February at around 6.40pm on the roundabout at the junction of Tuddenham Road and Colchester Road. A black VW Golf car was involved in a collision with a bicycle. The cyclist, a man in his 40s sustained minor injuries to his arm.

Witnesses or anybody who may have any relevant dash-cam footage are asked to call the police on 101 quoting reference 455 quoting reference CAD 375 of 25 February.
