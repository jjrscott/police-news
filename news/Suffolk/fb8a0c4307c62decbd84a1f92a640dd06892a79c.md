Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Wrentham near Beccles.

It happened at some point between 3.15pm on Tuesday 22 August and 3pm on Wednesday 23 August when entry was gained to a barn off the High Street by the offender(s). They broke down a wooden wall and removed various power tools including a power saw, a tile cutter, a rotavator, a nail gun and a welder and two generators.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious activity or has knowledge of the whereabouts of the items stolen should contact PC Mike Kerridge on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/57036/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
