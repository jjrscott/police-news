Police are appealing for witnesses following a theft of a flag in Stowmarket.

At some point between Thursday 10 August at 5pm and Friday 11 August at 8am, the Stowmarket flag has been taken from the flag pole on Market Place in the town centre. Police would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed the incident, or who has any information that may assist with the investigation. The flag is white and features the Stowmarket crest.

Anyone with any information about the whereabouts of the flag should contact officers at Stowmarket police station on 101 quoting crime number 37/54643/17.
