A missing 10-year-old boy from Bury St Edmunds has been located.

Charlie Manning was last seen at 10.30am today, Wednesday 23 December, but he was located safe and well later this afternoon.

Police would like to thank the media and the public for their help with the matter.

## Previous release | 23 December 2020

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 10-year-old boy from Bury St Edmunds.

Charlie Manning was last seen at 10.30am today, Wednesday 23 December, and he was reported missing to police at 1pm.

Charlie is described as white, 4ft tall and with auburn hair. He was wearing a navy blue coat, jeans and black Nike trainers.

Anyone who believes they have seen Charlie, or has any information regarding his whereabouts, is asked to contact Suffolk police on 101.
