Police are in attendance at a road traffic collision at a railway crossing in Thorpe Lane, Trimley St Martin.

Police were called to the incident, involving a vehicle and a train, at around 11.15am. The road is currently closed.

Ambulance and fire crews are also in attendance. One person was taken by ambulance to Ipswich Hospital but is not believed to have suffered serious injuries.
