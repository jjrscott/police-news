A missing 15-year-old girl from Ipswich has been located.

Kai Rader was last seen at home on Tuesday 8 December at around 11.50am but she was found in Stratford, London earlier today, Tuesday 22 December.

Police would like to thank the media and the public for their help with the matter.

## Previous release | 11 December 2020

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 15-year-old girl from Ipswich.

Kai Rader was last seen at home on Tuesday 8 December at around 11.50am and she was reported missing to police shortly before 10.10am the next day, Wednesday 9 December.

Officers believe Kai may have travelled to either Essex or London.

Kai is described as white, 5ft 5in tall, with hazel eyes and shoulder-length purple hair. She may be wearing a long grey coat with black sleeves and a black fur collar with white trainers.

Anyone who believes they have seen Kai, or has any information regarding her whereabouts, is asked to contact Suffolk Police on 101.
