Two men from Ipswich were arrested yesterday morning (Wednesday 7 October) in connection with burglaries of commercial premises across the county.

A man in his 20s from Ipswich was arrested on suspicion of burglary, theft and possession of class A drugs.

A second man aged in his 40s, and also from Ipswich, has been arrested on suspicion of theft.

The arrests are in relation to burglaries of commercial premises across the county and to the theft of a vehicle registration plate.

The two men were arrested and questioned by detectives at Martlesham Police Investigation Centre and have been released on bail until 3 November 2020.

Enquiries are going.
