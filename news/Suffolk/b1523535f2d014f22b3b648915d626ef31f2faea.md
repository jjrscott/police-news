Police are appealing for information following an attempted burglary in Ipswich.

On Tuesday 21 November between 7.30am and 1.20pm, offenders caused damage to a garden gate at a property on Maybury Road and attempted to force entry to the property via a rear door.

The offenders left empty handed.

Police would like to hear from anyone who may have seen or heard any suspicious activity in the area at the time of the offence.

Anyone with any information should contact Ipswich CID on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/74861/17.
