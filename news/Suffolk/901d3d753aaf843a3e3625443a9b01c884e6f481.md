Police have released a image of man they would like to speak to following a burglary in Ipswich.

The break in occurred at some point between 9:30pm and 10pm on Tuesday 23 May, at a premises in Felixstowe Road â€“ near the Bixley Road roundabout.

Access was gained via an insecure door and a handbag and mobile phones were stolen.

Police are keen to speak to the man pictured, who is wearing a distinctive jacket, in connection with the incident and are appealing for public support to identify him.

Anyone with information, or who knows the identity of the man, is urged to contact Suffolk Police on **101** quoting reference 37025/17. Alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Officers are also urging residents to ensure doors are locked and windows are closed, even if you are in another area of the house, to help minimise the risk of being targeted by thieves.
