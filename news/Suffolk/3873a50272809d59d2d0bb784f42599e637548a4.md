Police are appealing for witnesses following two thefts from a motor vehicle in Bury St Edmunds.

At some point between Wednesday 11 November at 6pm and Thursday 12 November at 9pm, four alloy wheels were stolen from a grey Audi A6, parked on Martin Reed Walk.

At some point between Wednesday 11 November at 4pm and Thursday 12 November at 8.15am, four alloy wheels were stolen from a BMW X4 on Redwood Drive in Bury St Edmunds.

Officers believe these two incidents are linked and would be interested to hear from anyone who may have seen or heard anything suspicious in the area at the times stated above.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious at the time is asked to contact the Police quoting reference number 37/65737/20 or 37 65692/20.
