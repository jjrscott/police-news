A missing 23-year-old man from Bury St Edmunds has been found.

James Williamson – known as Jimmy – had last been seen at 9.45pm yesterday, Thursday 8 February.

He was found safely by police in the vicinity of the B1106 near Elveden this morning, Friday 9 February.

Officers would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
