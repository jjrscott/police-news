Police are appealing for witnesses after a motorbike was stolen in Mildenhall.

The theft took place overnight between Saturday 11 March and Sunday 12 March at a garage block in Emmanuel Close.

A garage door was forced open and a black Honda motorbike was stolen from within. The bike was discovered at 7.40am on the Sunday morning in woodland near to Fir Tree Court with damage to the ignition wiring.

Any witnesses are asked to contact the Neighbourhood Response Team at Mildenhall Police Station on **101** , quoting reference 22448/17.
