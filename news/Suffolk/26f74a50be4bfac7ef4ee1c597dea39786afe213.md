Police can confirm that an 80-year-old missing woman from Ipswich has been found.

Brenda Dean was located in the town yesterday afternoon (Wednesday 3 October).

Officers would like to thank the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | Wednesday 3 October

Police are appealing for help in tracing a missing 80-year-old woman from Ipswich.

Brenda Dean was last seen in Parliament Road, Ipswich, shortly before 1.45pm today, Wednesday 3 October.

Brenda is described as a white female, of slim build and around 5ft 8in tall. She has white bobbed hair and has missing front teeth. She is believed to be wearing a navy blue skirt and a beige, sleeveless cardigan with a top underneath.
