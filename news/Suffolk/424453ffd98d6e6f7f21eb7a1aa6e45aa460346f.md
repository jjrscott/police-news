Police are appealing for information after a burglary in Ipswich.

The incident took place sometime between 7:00pm on Friday 3 January and 1:00pm Saturday 4 January at a property on Bulstrode Road in the town. A front window was forced and offender/s stole a ‘memory box’ which contained items of great sentimental value to the owners. The box is yellow with a butterfly pattern and 'Sweet Deams' lettering. In addition, a Nintendo Wii console, a glass angel and toy bricks were taken.

Anybody who noticed any unusual activity or who has information as to the box’s whereabouts are asked to contact Ipswich CID quoting crime reference 37/835/20 via
