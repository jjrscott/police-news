Police are appealing for witnesses to a series of thefts from motor vehicles that took place in Debenham.

They all occurred overnight Wednesday 1 November into Thursday 2 November.

Two vehicles, both transit vans that were parked on Field Way, had power tools taken from them, while a third incident also on Field Way saw another transit van had some soft drink stolen. On the same road a Â£20 note was stolen from a milk bottle left on a doorstep.

An incident on Low Road saw the doors on a Renault van forced open before all the power tools were stolen, while a silver transit van also parked on Low Road also had its power tools removed from the vehicle.

On Sackvylle Street in Debenham a Fiat Skudo van had its front door forced open with a blunt instrument with a variety of power tools were taken. Also on Sackvylle Street a Ford transit van had its power tools stolen from the vehicle.

Anyone who witnessed the incidents, saw any suspicious activity or has knowledge of the incidents should contact Halesworth police on 101, quoting reference crime references 37/71195/17, 37/71135/17, 37/71021/17, 37/70980/17, 37/71141/17, 37/70984/17, 37/71267/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
