Police are appealing for witnesses after a collision involving a tractor and a parked car in Metfield.

The incident took place at around 9.10pm on Sunday 14 August in The Street (B1123) at the junction with Christmas Lane.

A tractor pulling a long trailer carrying bales is reported to have collided with a green Ford Escort cabriolet, dragging it a short way and causing significant damage to the car.

Officers are attempting to identify the driver of the tractor and are asking them – or any witnesses to the incident – to make contact.

Anyone with information is asked to contact the Traffic Justice Unit on 01473 613888 ext: 5193 or be emailing [email protected] quoting reference 98029.
