Suffolk’s Police and Crime Commissioner, Tim Passmore is encouraging Ipswich residents to come along to a public meeting to talk about crime in the town in the first of his ‘Your Police Your Say’ series of meetings.

The PCC will be joined by Assistant Chief Constable, Rachel Kearton.

The meeting will be held at Nansen Road Baptist Church, Nansen Road, Ipswich IP3 9HN on Thursday 10 May at 7pm.

This is the first of seven meetings over the coming months, there will be one is each of the seven borough and district council areas. There’s no need to book. Just turn up. Tea and coffee will be served from 6.40pm.

Ipswich:Thursday 10 May - Nansen Road Baptist Church, Nansen Rd, Ipswich IP3 9HN

Forest Heath:Thursday 24 May - Brandon House Hotel, High Street, Brandon IP27 0AX

Suffolk Coastal:Thursday 7 June - Town Hall, Felixstowe IP11 2AG

Babergh: Wednesday 20 June - Hadleigh Town Hall, Hadleigh, IP7 5DN

Waveney: Monday 9 July - Wherry Hotel, Bridge Rd, Oulton Broad, Lowestoft NR32 3LN

Mid Suffolk: Wednesday 11 July - Cedars Hotel, Needham Road, Stowmarket IP14 2AJ

St Edmundsbury: Thursday 12 July -The Newbury Community Centre, St Olaves Road, Howard Estate, Bury St Edmunds IP32 6RW
