A 52-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of arson with intent to endanger life following a fire at a home in Little Gipping Street in Ipswich last night.

Police were contacted by the fire service around 8.45pm yesterday, Wednesday 14 June, to reports a fire had been started outside a home there and that the blaze had spread to the inside of the property.

Officers attended and the 52-year-old, from Ipswich, was arrested around 9.20pm. He remains in custody at Martlesham Police Investigation Centre where he will be interviewed about how the fire started.

Police are asking anyone who may have seen anything suspicious in the area between 7pm and 8.45pm yesterday to call Suffolk Police on 101, quoting CAD number 423 of yesterday, Wednesday 14 June.
