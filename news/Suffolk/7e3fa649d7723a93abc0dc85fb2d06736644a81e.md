Police are appealing for witnesses following a daytime burglary in Capel St Mary.

The incident occurred between 3.30pm and 5.15pm on Wednesday 23 November at an address in Thorney Road.

The property was entered by offender(s) climbing over a side passageway gate. A rear patio door was smashed to gain entry and an untidy search was conducted inside. Cash and jewellery was stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity in the area, or who has any information about this crime, is asked to contact Ipswich CID on 101 quoting crime reference 75456/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
