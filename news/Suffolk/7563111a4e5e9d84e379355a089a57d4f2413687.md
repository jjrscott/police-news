A man who died after a vehicle left the A12 at Wangford on Sunday has been named as Ian Rayner, 63, from Sotherton, near Beccles.

Police were called just after 5.10pm on Sunday, 26 June, to reports a Vauxhall Vectra car had left the A12 carriageway at London Road, Wangford, and come to rest in vegetation there.

Emergency services attended but Mr Rayner, from Sotherton Corner, died at the scene.

Police are asking anyone who may have seen what happened who has not yet spoken to officers to call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting CAD number 285 of Sunday 26 June.
