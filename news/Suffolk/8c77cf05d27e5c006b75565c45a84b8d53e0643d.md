Officers are appealing for witnesses or information following a road traffic collision in Hadleigh.

Police were called at 1:40pm on Saturday 23 June to reports of a single vehicle collision in Matilda Groome Road.

The vehicle, a Ducati motorcycle, was travelling along Matilda Groome Road when it left the road and collided with the side of a house.

The rider, a man in his 50s, sustained serious injuries and was taken to Addenbrooke’s Hospital. The man’s condition is currently believed to be stable and he remains in hospital.

Police are appealing for witnesses or any drivers with dashcam footage captured at around the time of the collision to contact PC Dawson of the Roads Policing & Firearms Operations Unit on 101 quoting reference 186 of 23 June.
