Police can confirm that a 75-year-old missing woman from Stratford St Andrew has been found.

Lesley Creese was found safe and well yesterday evening, Thursday 27 December.

Officers would like to thank the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | Thursday 27 December

Suffolk Police are appealing for assistance in locating a 75-year-old missing woman from Stratford St Andrew.

Lesley Creese was last seen at 12pm today at an address on Great Glemham Road in Stratford St Andrew and is believed to have left the location in a silver/grey coloured VW Polo.

Lesley is white, 5ft 6 inches tall and was last seen wearing a brown coat and grey trousers with brown shoes.

Lesley is vulnerable and officers are extremely concerned for her welfare.

Any sightings should be reported to Suffolk Police on 101 quoting incident number 199 of today, Thursday 27 December.
