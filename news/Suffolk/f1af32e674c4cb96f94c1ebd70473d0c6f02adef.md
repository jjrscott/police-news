Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident of public order in Bury St Edmunds.

The incident took place on Friday 14 September at around 4pm in Cornhill.

Officers were called following reports that a group of males were involved in an altercation with the occupants of a vehicle.

An 18-year-old man, a 17-year-old boy, two 16-year-olds boys and a 15-year-old boy were all subsequently arrested and taken to Bury St Edmunds Police Investigation Centre for questioning. All five have since been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Witnesses or anyone who has knowledge of the incident should contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting crime reference 37/52944/18.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
