Police are appealing for witnesses to the theft of a gator vehicle and power tools from Barham.

It happened at some point between Sunday 24 January at 6pm and Monday 25 January at 8am at Barham Lakes off Penthouse Road.

Offender(s) broke into two 20ft shipping containers used for storage with a John Dere gator vehicle (index WA13FKG) and power tools stolen.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity or knows the whereabouts of the vehicle or power tools should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/3932/21.

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Phone – Call 101
