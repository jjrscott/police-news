Officers are appealing for information or witnesses following three burglaries in Felixstowe which took place on Friday 16 June.

The first took place sometime between 9.40am and 3pm at a property on Beatrice Avenue in the town. Entry was forced to the front of the house and items of jewellery were stolen including gold wedding rings and a gold belcher chain.

The second burglary happened on Links Avenue sometime between 12pm and 4.30pm. Suspect/s forced entry to the rear of the property by smashing the patio doors and stole items of jewellery.

The third burglary took place at an address on Picketts Road sometime between 8.45am and 3pm. Suspect/s forced entry to the property and it is not believed that anything was stolen.

Police are investigating possible links between the incidents.

Witnesses or anybody who has any information are asked to call Ipswich CID on 101 quoting reference 42172/17 (Beatrice Avenue), 42176/17 (Links Avenue) or 42165/17 (Picketts Road)

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
