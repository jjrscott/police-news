Police are appealing for information following the theft of two Kawasaki motocross motorbikes.

On Friday 21 September at around 1.15am, two motocross bikes were stolen from a locked outbuilding at a residential address in the Ipswich area.

The bikes are described as two green and black Kawasaki motocross bikes, 125cc and 500cc.

Anyone who may have seen motocross bikes matching this description or been offered any for sale should contact Suffolk Police on 101, quoting crime reference number 37/54205/18.
