Two people have been arrested on suspicion of drug offences in Ipswich.

The arrests were made yesterday evening (5 July) after officers received intelligence of suspected drug related activity near to a wooded area close to Cornflower Close in the town.

A 21 year old from Ipswich was arrested on suspicion with intent to supply class A drugs and also on suspicion of money laundering and suspicion of possession of cannabis. A 20 year old, also from Ipswich was arrested on suspicion with intent to supply class A drugs.

The two suspects were taken to Martlesham Police Investigation Centre for questioning and have now been released under investigation.
