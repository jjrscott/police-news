Four people charged with murder in connection with the death of Ipswich man Joe Pooley have been remanded to appear before Ipswich Crown Court tomorrow (Friday 1 November).

Joe Pooley, aged 22, from the town, was found at around 10.30am on Monday 13 August 2018 in the River Gipping near to London Road. A post-mortem examination carried out on 14 August determined that the likely cause of death was immersion in water.

34 year old Sebastian Smith of no fixed abode was charged with murder on Wednesday 30 October. He appeared before Norwich Magistrates Court by live link on Wed 30 October and was remanded into custody.

29 year old Sean Palmer of South Market Road in Great Yarmouth was charged with murder on Wednesday 30 October. He appeared before Norwich Magistrates Court today (Thursday 31 October).

25 year old, Lisa-Marie Smith of Ramsay Road in Hawick, Roxburghshire and 29 year old, Becki West-Davidson of Foxhall Road in Ipswich were also both charged with murder earlier today (31 Oct).

Both appeared before Ipswich Magistrates Court today (31 October) and were remanded to appear before Ipswich Crown Court tomorrow.
