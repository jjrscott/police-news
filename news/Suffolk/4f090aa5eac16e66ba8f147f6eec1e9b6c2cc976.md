Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Ipswich.

The incident occurred between midnight and 12.10am yesterday, Thursday 13 August, when a male was assaulted by two other males in Burrell Road near to Willoughby Hill.

As a result of the incident, the victim sustained bruising and minor cuts.

Anyone who has knowledge of the incident or saw any suspicious activity in the area should contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/46605/20
