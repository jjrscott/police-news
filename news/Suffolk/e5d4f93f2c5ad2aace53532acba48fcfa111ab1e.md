Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident in which a man in Newmarket was assaulted.

The incident took place on Saturday 25 August sometime between 5.15am â€“ 5.45am on the High Street in the town. It is reported that a male motorist driving along the road was flagged down by a man. It is then understood that a third man arrived on the scene and assaulted the driver of the car, punching him in the face.The victim sustained a suspected broken jaw and facial swelling that required hospital treatment.

Anybody who witnessed any part of the incident or who may have relevant dash-cam footage captured at the time are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 48604/18. Alternatively, call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
