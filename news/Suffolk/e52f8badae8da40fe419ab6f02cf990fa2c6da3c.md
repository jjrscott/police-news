Emergency services are currently on the scene at Market Hill, Sudbury, following reports of a suspicious package found in a rucksack at Barclays Bank.

Police were called shortly after 1.10pm this afternoon. The building has been evacuated of people. A cordon is in place. Members of the public are asked to avoid the area.
