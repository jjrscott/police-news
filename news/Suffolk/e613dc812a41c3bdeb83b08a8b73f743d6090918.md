Police are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision on the A134 at Nayland on Sunday.

Around 2pm on Sunday, 31 July, a BMW car was in collision with a Suzuki motorcycle on Horkesley Hill at the Bear Street junction.

The 25-year-old woman riding the motorcycle sustained serious injuries including fractured/ broken legs and cuts and bruises including a significant cut to her arm. She was taken to Colchester hospital.

The man driving the BMW, in his 70s, sustained cuts and bruises.

Police are asking anyone who may have seen the collision to call Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting CAD 222 of July 31.
