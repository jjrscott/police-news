Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a home address in Elmswell.

The break-in occurred between 12pm and 11pm on Friday 5 January at a property in Borley Crescent.

At some point between the times stated, offender(s) forced entry by smashing a rear double glazed door before carrying out an untidy search. Various items were stolen.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any knowledge of it is asked to contact West CID at Bury St Edmunds Police Station on 101 quoting reference 1023/18 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
