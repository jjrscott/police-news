A missing 14-year-old girl from Ipswich has been found.

Kai Rader was last seen at home on Friday 9 October but was found in the town earlier today, Monday 19 October.

Police would like to thank members of the public and the media for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 13 Ocotber 2020

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 14-year-old girl from Ipswich.

Kai Rader was last seen at home on Friday 9 October and is believed to have travelled to Essex to visit a friend.

Kai is described as white, 5ft 5in tall and with shoulder-length hair.

Anyone who believes they have seen Kai, or has any information regarding her whereabouts, is asked to contact Suffolk Police on 101.
