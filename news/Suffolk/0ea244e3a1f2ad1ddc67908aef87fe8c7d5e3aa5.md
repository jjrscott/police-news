A missing 15-year-old teenage girl from Bury St Edmunds has been found.

Leah Vine had last been seen at around 10pm on Saturday 30 January at home, but she was located yesterday, Monday 1 February.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
