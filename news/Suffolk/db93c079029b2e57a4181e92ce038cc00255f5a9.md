Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Felixstowe.

The incident happened at a social club in the High Road West area of town, around 10:45pm last Thursday, 17 December.

It was reported to police that a man in his 50s assaulted a woman in her 60s, and officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed this incident to come forward.

Those with any information should contact Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting reference 72211/16.
