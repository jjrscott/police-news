Officers are appealing for witnesses or information following an incident in a public house in Newmarket.

Police were called on Saturday 5 May at around 8pm to the White Hart Hotel, High Street where a large fight was taking place. It is reported that it began in the hotel and spread to the beer garden and onto the street.

Officers attended and arrested a 25 year-old-man from Cambridge on suspicion of affray. He was later arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm, actual bodily harm and common assault. The man has been released under investigation whilst enquiries continue.

One man sustained facial injuries and was taken to hospital for treatment.

Police would particularly like to hear from anybody who may have video footage, photographs or dash-cam footage of the incident. Material can be emailed directly to [email protected] Witnesses or anybody with any information are asked to contact Police on 101 quoting reference 37/24509/18.
