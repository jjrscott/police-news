Suffolk Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision in Great Cornard.

On Sunday 27 November at approximately 3.45pm, a collision occurred on Poplar Road, close to the junction with Shawlands Avenue, between a Honda CB600 motorbike and a stationary vehicle.

The rider suffered a suspected head injury and was transported to hospital.

Police would like to speak to any witnesses to the collision or anyone who may have seen a motorcycle matching this description being ridden in the area prior to the collision.

Anyone with any information should call Police on **101,** quoting reference number 234 of 27 November.
