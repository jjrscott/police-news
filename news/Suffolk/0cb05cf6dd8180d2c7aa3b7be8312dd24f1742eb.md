Police can confirm that a missing 18-year-old man from Ipswich has been found.

Mamo Mohammed, who was last seen in the town yesterday, Monday 8 June at around 2.20pm, was located earlier today.

Officers would like to thank the media and members of the public for their assistance with this appeal.

## Previous release | 8 June 2020

Police are appealing for help to trace a missing 18-year-old man from Ipswich.

Mamo Mohammed was last seen in the area of Heath Road, Ipswich at 2.20pm yesterday Monday 8 June.

Mamo is described as middle eastern, 5ft 6in tall, with short black wavy hair and a tanned complexion. He was wearing all black clothing – black puffa jacket, black jeans and a black baseball cap.

Officers and his family are concerned for Mamo’s welfare and have asked if anyone has seen him or has any information regarding his whereabouts, to contact the duty sergeant at Ipswich Police Station on 101.
