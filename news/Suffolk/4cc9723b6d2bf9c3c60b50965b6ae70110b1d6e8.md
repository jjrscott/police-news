Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Wetherden, Stowmarket.

The break-in occurred at a home in Stowmarket Road yesterday, Monday 10 July, at some point between 2pm and 4:40pm.

Access was forced to the rear of the property and a quantity of cash, passports and jewellery was stolen.

Police are investigating the burglary and are keen to hear from anyone who may have noticed any suspicious behaviour in the area at the times indicated.

Anyone with any information should contact Western Area CID at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting crime reference number 47566/17.
