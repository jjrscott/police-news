Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary that took place in Ipswich.

The incident occurred at some point between 8:30am and 2:30pm on Monday, 22 March, at a residence on Belstead Road, near to the junction with Holyrood Close.

The suspect(s) gained access to the property by smashing a window and stole a number of items including games consoles, iPhone, Gucci handbag, a watch and a quantity of cash.

Anyone who heard any suspicious activity or knows who was responsible should contact South CID at Landmark House in Ipswich, quoting crime reference 37/14039/21 via

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999
