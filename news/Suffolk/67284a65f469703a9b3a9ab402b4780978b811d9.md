Police are appealing for help to locate a woman they want to speak to in connection with a theft in Sudbury.

The incident took place on Friday 9 November at sometime between 2pm and 2.40pm at the Aldi shop on Girling Street in the town.

A female customer had her purse stolen from a handbag which was hung on the back of her shopping trolley.

Officers have now released CCTV images of a woman they would like to speak in connection with the incident.

Anyone who may recognise her or has any information concerning the incident should contact Suffolk police on 101, quoting crime reference 37/65249/18

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
