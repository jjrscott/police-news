Police are appealing for witnesses after a sheep was slaughtered in Washbrook.

The discovery was made at lunchtime on Wednesday 11 October in a field off The Street.

The owner found the remains of one of the ewes thrown over the fence into woodland. The carcass had been taken, leaving the remainder of the animal behind.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed any suspicious activity in the area between Monday 9 and Wednesday 11 October.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact PC Kevin Stollery at the Rural and Wildlife Crime Unit on 101 quoting reference 66490/17.
