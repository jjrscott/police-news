Police are appealing for witnesses of a single vehicle road traffic collision on the A47 at Corton.

It took place at Rackham's Corner at around 10pm on Friday 5 Feb 2021 and involved a red Honda Civic vehicle.

As a result of the collision one male, aged in his 30s, sustained serious head and spinal injuries and damage to his knee but is in a stable condition at hospital.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or who has with dashcam footage, please contact Suffolk Constabulary on 101 quoting reference SC-05022021-414.
