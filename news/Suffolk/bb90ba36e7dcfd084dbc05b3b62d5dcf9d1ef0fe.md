Police are appealing for witnesses to a road traffic collision that took place in Lowestoft.

It happened at 7.50pm on Wednesday 4 July on Raglan Street close to the junction of Camp Road.

A white 249cc Jinling quad bike was travelling south on Raglan Street when it was in collision with a stationary Vauxhall Corsa. The rider, a 26 year old man from Lowestoft, was thrown from his vehicle and struck the Corsa causing him serious head injuries. The victim was taken by air ambulance to Addenbrookes hospital for treatment.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or anyone has dash-cam footage should contact Roads Policing on 101 and quoting incident number 429 of 4 July 2018.
