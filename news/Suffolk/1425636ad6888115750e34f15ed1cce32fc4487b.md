Police are appealing for information following a burglary at a rural address near Badwell Ash.

The break-in occurred at some point between 11am and 6:30pm last Wednesday, 24 May.

Access to the premises was gained via an insecure window and items stolen include a safe, a quantity of jewellery, cash and prescription medication. It is believed the offender left via the rear of the property.

Police are issuing a warning that the medication could be dangerous if they are taken by anyone they are not prescribed for.

Anyone who saw any suspicious persons or vehicles in the vicinity of this incident is urged to contact West CID at Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 37212/17.

Police are continuing to urge members of the public to secure their homes and report any suspicious activity in their communities. If you believe a burglary to be in progress always dial 999.
