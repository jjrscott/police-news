Police are appealing for any witnesses to come forward after the theft of a dog near Bury St Edmunds.

The incident took place on Monday 6 July at some point between 1pm and 2.20pm when a Cocker Spaniel was stolen from the garden of a farm in Holywell Row. The dog was left in a pen enclosure in the garden.

Called 'Penny', she is seven years old and has a black fur with a white bib and lip markings.

Anyone with any knowledge of the theft or knows of the whereabouts of the dog should contact Mildenhall police, quoting crime reference 37/38055/20

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
