Police searching for a missing 20 year old Lee Rudhall from Haverhill have found the body of a man.

The body was discovered in the early hours of this morning by officers (16 November) in a wooded area of the town.

He has not been formally identified but the family of Lee have been informed.

The death is not being treated as suspicious and a file will now be prepared for the coroner.
