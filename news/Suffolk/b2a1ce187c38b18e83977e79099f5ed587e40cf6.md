Police are appealing for witnesses to the theft of canaries from an aviary in Bungay.

The incident took place overnight from 23 November at 5pm to Tuesday 24 November at 9am from a property on Wherry Road.

They are described as Norwich canaries and 13 were stolen.

All the birds have ring tags on their legs with ‘PBB’ and the year either 18, 19 or 20 on them.

Anyone who saw or heard any suspicious behaviour or has knowledge of their whereabouts should contact Suffolk police , quoting crime reference : 37/68244/20.

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
