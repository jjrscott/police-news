Police are investigating a burglary at a business address in Lowestoft.

The break in took place at a Pakefield shop on London Road at some point between 5:30pm on Tuesday 15 November and 8am on Wednesday 16 November.

The suspect forced entry at the rear of the shop and stole various items, including fishing equipment, and the store till, which was later recovered on a nearby beach.

Police are appealing to anyone who may have seen or heard anything suspicious to contact Lowestoft Neighbourhood Response Team on 101 quoting reference 66511/16, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
