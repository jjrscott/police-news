Police are appealing for help to identify a man following a burglary in Ipswich.

It happened on Saturday 14 July at a property on Ashely Street in the town. The victim went to bed at 11.30pm and when she woke the next morning at about 8.10am the back door to her address was open. It is thought a quantity of cash was taken in the incident.

Anyone who recognises the individual in the still or has knowledge of the incident or saw any suspicious activity should contact Ipswich CID on 101, quoting crime reference 37/39228/18.Alternatively contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
