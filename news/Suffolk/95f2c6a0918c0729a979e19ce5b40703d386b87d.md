Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident of theft of a motor vehicle in Bury St Edmunds.

It happened at some point between 9am on Friday 25 August and Monday 28 August at 10am at a property on The Street in Holywell Row. Whilst occupiers were away their property was entered by fthe front wooden door being forced off and a black BMW 435i Gran Coupe taken in the incident.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour, has knowledge of the incident or the whereabouts of the vehicle should contact DC Andrew George on Bury St Edmunds CID quote crime reference 37/57408/17.
