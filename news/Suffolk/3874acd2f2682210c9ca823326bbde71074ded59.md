## Previous release

Two people have died following a fire at a home in Stanley Street in Lowestoft this morning.

Police were contacted by the fire service just after 1.15am this morning, Monday 26 June.

On police arrival one person was being given CPR and a second person was located and brought out of the premises by fire crews. Both were taken to the James Paget hospital but both died a short time later.

Formal identification will take place in due course. A police and fire investigation into the cause of the blaze is underway and a cordon is currently in place at the scene.

Anyone who may have any information about the incident is asked to call Suffolk Police on 101, quoting CAD number 24 of today, 26 June.
