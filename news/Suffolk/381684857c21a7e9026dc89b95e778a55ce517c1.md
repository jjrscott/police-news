Police are appealing for witnesses following two burglaries that occurred on the outskirts of Ipswich earlier this week.

Both crimes took place on Monday 14 December in Pinewood.

Between 10.10am and 11.10am a home in Bramblewood was broken into, where a hole was smashed in the glass of a rear door to gain entry. An untidy search was undertaken and a laptop and items of jewellery were stolen.

The second incident occurred between 8am and 6.55pm in Broad Meadow, where a house was broken into via a kitchen window. The upstairs was searched and a quantity of US dollars were stolen.

If you have any information about these burglaries please contact South CID at Landmark House, quoting the crime references as below:

72273/20 for Bramblewood. Email: DC Morling

72371/20 for Broad Meadow. Email: DC Thorn

Website – http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update

Phone – Call 101

Crimestoppers – Contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form: www.crimestoppers-uk.org

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call **999**
