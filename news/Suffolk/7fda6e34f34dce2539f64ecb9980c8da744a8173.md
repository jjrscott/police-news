Police are appealing for witnesses and information following an incident of theft from a motor vehicle in Barham.

The incident took place overnight between Sunday 7 April and Monday 8 April in Woolner Close.

At some point, an unknown offender forced entry into a Mercedes and stole a bag of archery equipment from within.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity should contact PC 265 Trudie Beckwith on 101, quoting the crime number 37/20093/19 or you can provide an online update via the force website http://www.suffolk.police.uk/contact-us/existing-report-update or email [email protected]

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
