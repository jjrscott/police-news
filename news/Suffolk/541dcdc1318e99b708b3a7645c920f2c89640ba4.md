Police are appealing for help to locate a man they want to speak to in connection with a suspected racially aggravated public order offence.

The incident happened at the Market Place in Stowmarket on Tuesday 1 January at about 12:50am. A taxi driver was verbally abused and had his vehicle damaged whilst he was trying to deal with a female passenger who was being sick.

Anyone who recognises the individual depicted in the images or has knowledge of the incident should contact Stowmarket police, quoting crime reference 37/46/19.

Alternatively, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
