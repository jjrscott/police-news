Police are appealing for witnesses following an aggravated burglary that took place in Brettenham near Hitcham.

It happened at about 2pm on Wednesday 17 January at a property in The Street.

Three unknown male suspects knocked on the door of the property and asked for directions to Ipswich. When the victim gave directions, one of the males threw a liquid substance into his face, which was later found to have caused no lasting damage.

The offender(s) then forced their way into the home and made an untidy search of the property before taking a quantity of cash.

The suspects are described as having possibly an Asian appearance.

Anyone who witnessed any suspicious behaviour at around the time of the incident or has knowledge of the incident should contact Ipswich CID on 101 quoting crime reference 3250/18 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 55511.
