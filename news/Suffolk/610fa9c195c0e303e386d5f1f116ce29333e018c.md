Police are appealing for witnesses to three burglaries in the Eye area.

One took place overnight at some point between Friday 26 March at 5pm and Saturday 27 March at 1.30pm. A workshop was targeted in the Wilby area with a chainsaw and petrol cans stolen. The petrol cans were located nearby later on. 37/15251/21

The second one on Friday 26 March where a barn was targeted with petrol cans stolen and keys to a Landrover were also taken. c/n: 37/15287/21

In Palgrave, on Denmark Hill, police received a report on Saturday 27 March that a garden shed was broken into with garden equipment stolen. 37/15320/21

Anyone who knows who was responsible, has knowledge of the incident should contact Suffolk police quoting the relevant crime number.
