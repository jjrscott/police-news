Police are appealing for information following an incident of indecent exposure in Worlingham, near Beccles.

At approximately 12:30pm on Tuesday 12 September, a woman was walking her dogs along Marsh Lane when she saw a man sat on the ground in the hedgerow, near a gate that leads into fields, exposing himself and acting in an indecent manner.

The man is described as white, aged in his 20s, approximately 6ft 3ins and of muscular build. He has black hair with tight curls and was wearing a red waterproof jacket with grey shorts, and was carrying a black and red rucksack.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have been in the area at the time stated and seen someone matching this description, or who may have information to assist the investigation.

Anyone with information should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference 60359/17.
