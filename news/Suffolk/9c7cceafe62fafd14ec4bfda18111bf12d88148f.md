Emergency services are currently on the scene of a serious collision in Little Livermere.

Police were called by the East of England Ambulance Service at 10.06am this morning to reports of a two vehicle collision involving a motorcycle and a van in Little Livermere near to the junction of Green Lane and Rymer Lane.

The air ambulance is on the scene and at present the A134 is closed at the junction with Green Lane.

Witnesses are asked to called Suffolk Police on **101** quoting CAD 104 of today.
