Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Great Cornard this morning.

The burglary took place at Co-op in Canhams Road at around 4am today, Friday 13 September.

Two unknown offenders forced entry via the front door and once inside, they broke into a cigarette kiosk and stole a quality of tabaco products. They then left and got into a dark coloured saloon type car and exited the car park by turning left into Canhams Road and then drove off in the direction of Head Lane.

The first suspect is described as tall and of a medium build. They were wearing a light grey Nike hooded jumper with a white Nike motif, dark jeans, black trainers and a balaclava.

The second suspect is described as tall and of a medium build. They were wearing a grey Superdry style coat, blue jeans, navy trainers and a balaclava.

Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to contact Suffolk police quoting reference number 37/55180/19.
