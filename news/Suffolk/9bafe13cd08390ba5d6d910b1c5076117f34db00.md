Police who arrested three girls in connection with an arson investigation in Bury St Edmunds have all been released under investigation.

Emergency services attended the incident on Sunday 28 October on Beetons Way, where it is believed that some roofing materials situated in the grounds of a school caught fire.

The girls, all under 15, have all been released under investigation, pending further enquiries.

Anyone who may have witnessed the incident or who has any information relating to it should contact Suffolk police on 101 quoting reference number 37/62362/18.

## Previous release | 29 October 2018

Police have arrested three girls in connection with an arson investigation in Bury St Edmunds.

Emergency services attended the incident on Sunday 28 October on Beetons Way, where it is believed that some roofing materials situated in the grounds of a school caught fire.

The school has not been damaged in the fire and is open as normal.

Three girls have been arrested on suspicion of arson and are in custody where they will be questioned by detectives.

Anyone who may have witnessed the incident or who has any information relating to it should contact Suffolk Police on 101 quoting reference number 37/62362/18.
