Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Ipswich earlier this week.

The incident occurred at some time between 10.30am and 1.25pm on Tuesday 8 May at an address in Woodbridge Road, in the vicinity of North Hill Gardens.

Entry was gained to the property by forcing open the front door and a quantity of jewellery was stolen from within.

Anyone with information about this crime, or who witnessed any suspicious activity in the area on that day, is asked to contact South CID at Landmark House on 101 quoting reference 24936/18. Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
