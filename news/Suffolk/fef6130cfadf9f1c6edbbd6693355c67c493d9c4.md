Police are appealing for witnesses to an incidents of a male exposing himself in Leiston.

They took place in the Quakers Way area of the town at 3.20pm on Tuesday 11 September.

A male indecently exposed himself to a teenage girls in two separate incidents. He is described as a white male aged 30 to 40 years of age, 5’8’’ in height and of a big build and wearing dark, baggy trousers.

One of the victims also reported she saw a second male exposing himself on Aldeburgh Road. The male was in a white van with no sign writing. The male stood up in the front of the van with his phone in his hand and indecently exposed himself.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, knows who the suspect may be or has knowledge of it should contact 101, quoting crime reference 37/52222/18. Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
