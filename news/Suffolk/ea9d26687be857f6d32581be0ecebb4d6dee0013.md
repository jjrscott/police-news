Police are appealing for witnesses following the theft of a mountain bike in Ipswich.

The incident occurred at around 5.30pm on Sunday 27 April in Hines Road.

At some point, a locked yellowy-green and black Voodoo Bizango mountain bike was stolen from the bike rack outside Aldi.

Anyone with information about this theft, or who believes they have seen a bike matching this description, is asked to contact Suffolk police quoting reference: 37/23576/20
