Officers are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Bramford.

The incident took place on Bullen Lane, at the Bramford village end, on Monday 30 October at around 5.30pm.

A suspect knocked on the door and window of the property before then climbing through a rear window. The householder in the property,disturbed by the noise, then shouted out and the offender left via another window. Nothing was stolen.

The offender is described as having long straggly brown hair and was wearing a grey jacket.

Witnesses or anybody who saw any suspicious activity in the vicinity are asked to call Suffolk Police on 101 reference 70456/17.
