Police are appealing for help to trace a man they want to identify following an attempted theft and attempted sexual assault in Haverhill.

The incident occurred at some time between 3am and 3.45am on Sunday 1 April in High Street.

The victim – a woman aged in her 20s – was sitting on a bench outside Select Fashion when she was approached by a man who tried to grab her handbag and in the process also got hold of her arm and tried to kiss her. She was not harmed during this incident.

The suspect is described as white, aged in his mid 30s, of skinny build, and with dark hair. He was wearing a black leather jacket and spoke in a foreign language.

Anyone who believes they know who this man is, or who has any information about this incident, is asked to contact the Incident and Crime Management Hub on 101 quoting reference 18357/18. Alternatively contact the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
