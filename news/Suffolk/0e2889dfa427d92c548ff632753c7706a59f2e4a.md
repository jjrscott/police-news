A missing 86-year-old man from Ipswich has been found.

Wilhelm Thiebes was reported missing from his home in the Stoke Park area this morning, Thursday 3 August.

He has been found safe and well in the area of Ipswich waterfront this afternoon.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
