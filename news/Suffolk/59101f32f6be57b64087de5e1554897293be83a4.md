Police are appealing for information following a shop burglary in Debenham.

On Sunday 3 September at some point between 2am and 2.20am, offenders broke into a shop on Chancery Lane, by smashing a glass pane in the front door.

Offenders stole a large quantity of cigarettes before making off from the scene in a vehicle.
Police would be interested in hearing from anyone who may have seen or heard any suspicious activity in the Chancery Lane area at the time of the offence.

Anyone with any information or saw any suspicious behaviour during the times stated should contact DcCAndrew Thawley at Lowestoft CID on 101 quoting crime reference number 37/58358/17 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
