Police are appealing for witnesses after an assault in Stoke-by-Clare. The incident took place on Sunday 15 November between 11:00am and 11:20am outside Wixoe Mill on the main road between Stoke by Clare and New England.

A man driving his car came across a woman and a man standing in the road who were speaking to each other. The driver slowed down and the pedestrians moved out of the way. A verbal altercation then took place involving the woman pedestrian and the male driver. The woman then threw a small bag at the driver. Nobody was harmed as a result of the incident.

The woman is described as white, in her 60s, of slim build with shoulder length, curly grey hair. She was wearing a black top and a plum coloured jacket.

Witnesses or anybody who recognises the description of the woman are asked to contact Haverhill Police quoting reference 37/66276/20
