Police are appealing for information after two motocross bikes were stolen during a garage burglary in Wyverstone.

The incident occurred between 10.45pm on Monday 25 June and 5.25am on Tuesday 26 June.

The garage door was forced open to gain entry and an orange KTM motocross bike, along with another motocross bike, a large amount of Snap-on tools, a generator and two disc cutters were stolen from within.
