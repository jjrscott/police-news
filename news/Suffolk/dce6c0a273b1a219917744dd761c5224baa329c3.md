A 48 year old Suffolk man who downloaded child abuse images has been given a suspended prison sentence.

Trevor Collett of Orchard Green in Beccles appeared before Ipswich Crown Court on Friday 23 November.

Police officers went to his home in November 2017 and seized various computer items, Ipswich Crown Court heard.

When the devices were examined they were found to contain indecent images of children and a number of extreme pornographic images and movies were also found.

Collett admitted to making and possession of indecent images of children and possessing extreme pornographic images.

He was given a 10 month prison sentence, suspended for two years, 80 hours unpaid work and a 30 day rehabilitation requirement.

He was also made the subject of a sexual harm prevention order and ordered to sign the sex offenders register for 10 years.
