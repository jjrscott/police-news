A second prisoner who absconded from Hollesley Bay this weekend has been arrested in Sussex.

Robert Butler, aged 34, was reported missing to police at around 8.30am yesterday after being absent at a roll call.

He was arrested in Hastings this morning, Sunday 14 January, and will now be returned to the prison system.
