Police in Ipswich are appealing for witnesses after over 20 vehicles were vandalised at the weekend.

The offences are believed to have occurred overnight between Friday 12 May and Saturday 13 May in roads to the east of the town centre.

Officers are linking 22 reports of criminal damage, occurring in Belle Vue Road (11 crimes), Oxford Road (seven crimes), and Woodville Road (four crimes). The damage is consistent with the vehicles having been scratched or ‘keyed’.

Anyone with information about these offences is asked to contact the Incident and Crime Management Hub at Police Headquarters on **101** quoting reference 35003/17, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
