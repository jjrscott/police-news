A missing 92-year-old man from Woodbridge has been found.

Peter Moore had last been seen at Stansted Airport train station at around 2.30pm yesterday afternoon, Thursday 4 January, but has been found safe and well by police in the Woodbridge area this morning.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
