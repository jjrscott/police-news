Police are appealing for witnesses or information after a cat was seriously injured in Exning.

The Maine Coon cat was found on Saturday 31 October and was taken to a vet with serious internal injuries. It is believed that the cat was shot with an air gun pellet, possibly up to 72 hours beforehand in the vicinity of Oxford Street.

As a result of the incident, the cat required surgery and may not recover from his injuries.

Anyone with any information about this incident or who saw anything suspicious is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference 37/63418/20
