A missing 14-year-old girl from Ipswich has been found.

Lily Stephenson had last been seen at around 2.30pm on Monday 18 January, but she was located in the town earlier this morning, Thursday 21 January.

Police would like to thank members of the public and media for their assistance with this appeal.
