A 23-year-old man has been charged with the rape of a woman in Lowestoft.

Police were called to Marine Parade just before 2pm on Thursday 30 March after a member of the public reported a woman had just left a flat there in a distressed state and told him she had been raped. The 23-year-old was arrested a short time later.

Officers have been working with the woman, in her 30s, since then.

Dele Djabula, of Marine Parade in Lowestoft, has subsequently been charged with rape and is due to appear before Norwich Magistrates this morning, Saturday 1 April.

## Previous release

A 23-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of rape following an incident in Lowestoft.

Police were called to Marine Parade just before 2pm yesterday, Thursday 30 March, after a member of the public reported a woman had just left a flat there in a distressed state and told him she had been raped.

Officers attended and the 23-year-old man, from Lowestoft, was arrested a short time later. He remains in custody where he will be interviewed in connection with the incident.

Police have been working with the woman, in her 30s, to get an account of what happened and enquiries are continuing.

Anyone who may have been in the area around this time yesterday who may have information that could assist should call the Lowestoft safeguarding unit by dialling Suffolk Police on **101** , quoting crime number 26034/17.
