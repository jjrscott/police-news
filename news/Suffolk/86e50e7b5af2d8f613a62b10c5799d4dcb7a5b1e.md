Officers are appealing for witnesses following a serious road collision in Tuddenham, near Mildenhall yesterday.

Police were called at 5.00pm on Tuesday 27 March to reports of a collision involving three vehicles near to the junction of Higham Road and High Street. A Honda motorbike travelling into Tuddenham collided with a black Vauxhall Zafira car which was travelling in the opposite direction.

A third vehicle, a Mazda CX5, was damaged as a result of the collision.

The motorcyclist, a man in thirties from Beck Row, was taken by ambulance to Addenbrookeâ€™s Hospital having sustained a serious head injury. The man remains in a critical condition.

Nobody else was injured.

Witnesses to this incident or anybody who make have dash-cam footage are asked to call the Serious Collision Investigation Team on 101 quoting reference CAD 279 of 27 March.
