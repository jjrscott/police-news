Police are issuing a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with an incident in Beccles.

The incident occurred at around 10pm on Friday 18 September within Beccles Kebabs in Hungate Lane.

A male entered the fast food restaurant when he became irate and then shouted and swore. He then picked up a bottle of hand sanitiser and threw it at staff.

The male is described as white, approximately aged in his 40s to 50s, with grey hair and a grey beard. He was wearing a white zip up top with a blue t-shirt underneath, dark denim jeans and white trainers.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured in the CCTV image about this incident as he may be able to help with the investigation.

Anyone who believes they recognise the man pictured in the CCTV image, or who has any information about this incident, is asked to contact Suffolk police, quoting crime reference: 37/54935/20
