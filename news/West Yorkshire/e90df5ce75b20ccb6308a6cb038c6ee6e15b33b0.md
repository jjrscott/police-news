Monday, 14 December 2020

Police are appealing for information following a fatal road traffic collision in Wakefield.

It happened at about 5.50pm on Saturday, when a silver Chevrolet Matiz was in collision with a male pedestrian outside the Star Inn pub in Stanbridge Lane.

The pedestrian, who was 16, was taken to hospital with serious injuries, but sadly passed away in hospital.

The Major Collision Enquiry Team are investigating the collision and would like to hear from anyone who witnessed it, or who may have footage which could assist their investigation.

They can be contacted on 101 or via the West Yorkshire Police website. The incident reference is 13200621557.
