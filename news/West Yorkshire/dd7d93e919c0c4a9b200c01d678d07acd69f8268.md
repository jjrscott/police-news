Tuesday, 15 December 2020

Police investigating a burglary in Huddersfield have released details of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the offence.

Craig Hughes, who is 39, has links to Newcastle-upon-Tyne.

He is described as being about 5ft 8ins tall and of medium build.

Officers would like to speak to him about the offence which occurred in the Moldgreen area in November 2019.

Numerous enquiries have been made into his whereabouts and it is believed he may have since left Kirklees.

Anyone with information which may assist in locating him is asked to contact Kirklees CID on 101, or via the options on the West Yorkshire Police website.

Information can also be reported anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers.
