Police are issuing a picture of a man they want to speak with in connection with an attempted burglary in Huddersfield.

The Kirklees District Crime Team would like to speak with anyone who can help identify the man pictured as they continue to investigate an attempted burglary offence at a property on Crescent Road, Birkby at about 4.30pm on Friday June 26.

A suspect had entered the property but left empty handed after he was seen by the householder.

The suspect made off in the direction of Birkby Hall Road.

Anyone who can identify the man pictured or who has any information about the offence should contact PC 3678 Hewitt at the Kirklees District Crime Team on 101 referencing crime number 13200322284.

Information can be given online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat or anonymously to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
