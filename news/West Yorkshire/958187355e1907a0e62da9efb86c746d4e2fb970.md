Tuesday 12th January 2021

Coroner's officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Richard Seymour.

Mr Seymour aged 82yrs lived in the Meanwood area of Leeds and died of natural causes on the 4th of January 2021.

Anyone with information is asked to call Coroner's officer Dawn Powell on 01924 292301.
