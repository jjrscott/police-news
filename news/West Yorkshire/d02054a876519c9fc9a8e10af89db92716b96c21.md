**Fridayt 19 March 2021**

Police in Halifax are appealing for information following reports an altercation in the Skircoat area.

Officers were called to a report of a fight involving a large group of males on Saville Park Road at about 4pm on Wednesday. (17 March)

One person was taken to hospital with injuries not believed to be life-threatening.

Two people have been arrested in connection with the incident and enquiries are ongoing.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or may have information that will assist in enquiries to contact 101 or via the website www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101LiveChat quoting log 1323 of 17 March.
