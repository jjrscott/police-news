Wednesday 17th February 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Malcolm Horsfall Veal from Halifax.

Mr Veal, aged 81 years, passed away on the 5th February 2021.

Anyone with information is requested to contact Coroner's Officer Vicki Illingworth during the hours of 9am - 5pm, Monday - Friday.
