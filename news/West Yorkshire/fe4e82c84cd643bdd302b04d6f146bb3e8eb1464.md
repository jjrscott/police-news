Friday 5 March 2021

Police have released images of two men they wish to speak to in relation to a burglary in Huddersfield.

Entry was forced into a home on Mount Avenue, Salendine Nook, Huddersfield, between 11.40am and 12.50pm on Wednesday 3 March 2021.

The suspects carried out an untidy search before leaving with property.

They were in a Volkswagen Golf car, which was seen to make off along Lindley Moor Road towards Outlane and Junction 23 of the M62.
