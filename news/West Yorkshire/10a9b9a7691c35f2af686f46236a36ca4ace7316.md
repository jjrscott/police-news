**Wednesday 7 October, 2020**

Detectives have charged a man for attempted murder following an incident this week where a man was stabbed.

On 5 October, police were called to reports of a 34-year-old man found with injuries consistent of stab wounds to be chest and legs.

He remains in hospital being treated for his injuries.

A 27-year-old man was arrested at the scene and he has now been charged with attempted murder.

Cameron Hussain, of Greaves Street, Bradford will appear at Bradford Magistrates Court this morning.
