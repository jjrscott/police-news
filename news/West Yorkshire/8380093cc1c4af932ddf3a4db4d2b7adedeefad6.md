**Tuesday 15 December 2020 **

Police are appealing for witnesses as enquiries continue into a serious assault in Bradford.

Detectives would like to speak to anyone who saw or has information about an incident near the NCP car park on Hall Ings in Bradford on 12 December between 5pm and 5:45pm.

It took place after the victim, a 35 year-old-man, was attacked by another male and suffered a knife wound to his lower back.

The suspect then made off, leaving the victim with an injury which required hospital treatment.

He was described as a male of dual heritage, 5”10, slim build, wearing a black hoodie and dark jeans.

> PC Rebecca Brown of Bradford District CID, said:
>
> “We are conducting a number of enquiries into this serious assault including potential motives for the attack which left the victim with a stab wound which needed hospital treatment.
>
> “Anyone who saw the incident or the suspect just before or after the attack is asked to contact Bradford District CID on 101 referencing crime number 13200621361.”

Information can also be given online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat or anonymously to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
