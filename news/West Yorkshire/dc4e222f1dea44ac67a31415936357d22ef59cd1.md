Tuesday 16th March 2021

Coroners Officers are appealing for help in tracing the relatives of Richard Charles Hudson.

Mr Hudson, aged 45 years old and born on 7th May 1975, passed away on 13th February 2021.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact the Bradford Coroners Office on 01274 373721.
