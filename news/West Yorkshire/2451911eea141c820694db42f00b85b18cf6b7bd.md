**Friday 12 March, 2021**

Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious road traffic collision in Oakworth, Keighley yesterday.

Officers were called at around 4:50pm on Thursday 11 March to reports of a collision on Hob Cote Lane at the junction with Denby Hill road in Keighley.

Three vehicles were involved â€“ a silver VW Golf and a black VW Golf collided with a grey Ford Finesse.

A 20-year-old woman was taken to hospital with serious injuries. Her condition is not thought to be life threatening.

The other two male drivers in their 20s suffered minor injuries and were taken to hospital for medical treatment.

Police are appealing for witnesses and in particular to a driver of a Ford Transit van that was believed to be in the area at the time.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, has any dashcam footage or any further information is asked to contact the police via 101 or online quoting log number 1237 of 11 March.
