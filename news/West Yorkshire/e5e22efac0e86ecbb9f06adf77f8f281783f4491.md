Tuesday 5th January 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Juozas Gaigalas from Bradford, originally from Lithuania.

Mr Gaigalas, aged 72 years, passed away on the 23rd December 2020.

Anyone with information is requested to contact the Coroner's Office on 01274 373721 during the hours of 8am - 4pm, Monday - Friday.
