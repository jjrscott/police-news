**Wednesday 22 April, 2020**

Police are issuing images of a man they wish to identify as part of enquiries into a robbery at a convenience store in Huddersfield.

Kirklees CID are appealing for information regarding the robbery at the Marsh branch of Bargain Booze on Westbourne Road on the evening of Tuesday April 21st.

The incident took place at about 7.08pm after a man entered the shop and then browsed the aisles.

He then went to the back of the counter and made threats to a member of store staff and demanded money.

The suspect escaped a few moments later with a quantity of cash.

Enquiries remain ongoing regarding the robbery and officers would like to speak to anyone who saw the man pictured either entering the store, exiting and leaving the area, or hanging around outside in the moments before the robbery.

Anyone who has information is asked to contact Kirklees CID on 101 referencing crime number 13200198076.

Information can also be given online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat or anonymously on 0800 555 111 to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
