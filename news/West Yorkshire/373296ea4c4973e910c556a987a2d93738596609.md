Thursday 21 January 2021

Police in Wakefield are appealing for information to help trace a man who is wanted on warrant for failing to attend court.

Darren Jepson, 42, from Pontefract was due to attend court on Thursday 7 Jan 2021.

He is charged with a driving offence and possession of Class A drugs.

Officers have made extensive enquiries into his whereabouts and are appealing for the public to assist.

He is described as 6ft, broad build with short grey hair and a Yorkshire accent.

Police believe he may be in the West Yorkshire area but also has links to Leicester.

Anyone who has seen him or who has any information that could assist in tracing him is asked to contact via 101 quoting reference 13210010823 or online via www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
