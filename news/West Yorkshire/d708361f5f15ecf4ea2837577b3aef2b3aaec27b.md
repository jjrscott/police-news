**Friday 15 January 2021**

Police are appealing for anyone who sees a missing teenager to get in contact.

Joshua Wakefield

Joshua Wakefield, 16, is from Blackpool and was last seen 31st December 2020.

It is believed he is in either the Dewsbury or Batley areas of West Yorkshire.

Joshua is described as being white male, about 5ft 4ins tall and of slim build.

He has black hair which is now believed to be shaved as per the image.

Anyone who sees Joshua is asked to call police on 101 or use the contact us options at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contactus quoting reference 13210005820.
