Wednesday 23rd December 2020.

Coroner's officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Robert Varela.

Mr Varela, aged 82yrs from the Lovell Park area of Leeds, is believed to have originated from Spain. He died on the 18th of December 2020 and his death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Coroner's officer Dawn Powell on 01924 292301.
