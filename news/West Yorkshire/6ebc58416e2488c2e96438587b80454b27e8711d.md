**Wednesday 9 December 2020**

Police were called to Hudson Avenue in Bradford at 8.34 this morning to reports of a one vehicle road traffic collision.

A female pedestrian has suffered serious but not believed to be life threatening injuries.

Another female pedestrian and two children are receiving medical treatment at this time.

The road has since reopened.

Any witnesses to 101 or www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contactus quoting log 269 of December 9.
