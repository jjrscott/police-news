**Tuesday, February 2, 2021**

Police are continuing to appeal for information to help trace a woman who has gone missing in Leeds.

Jodey Carroll, aged 36, from Morley, was reported missing at 7.30pm yesterday (1/2) after last being seen at about 8pm on Sunday (31/1).

Missing: Jodey Carroll

She is described as 5ft tall with blonde hair with green ends. She was last seen wearing a thin black or green coat, black high-heeled boots, jeans and a top.

Detective Inspector James Entwistle, of Leeds District CID, said: “There are concerns for Jodey’s welfare and we urgently need to find her and check that she is okay.

“We are carrying out extensive enquiries to locate her and would like to hear from anyone who has seen her or who has any information that could assist.”

Anyone with information is asked to contact Leeds District CID via 101 quoting log number 1479 of February 1 or online via www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat
