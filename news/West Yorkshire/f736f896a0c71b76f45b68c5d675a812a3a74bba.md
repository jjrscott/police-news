Wednesday 31st March 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Winifred ( also known as Wendy ) Mair-Darch from Leeds.

Ms Mair-Darch, aged 87 years, passed away on the 28th March 2021.

Anyone with information is requested to contact the Coroner's Office on 01924 292301.
