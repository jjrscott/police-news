Tuesday 15th December 2020.

Coroner's officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Anthony Martin.

Mr Martin, aged 79yrs who lived in the Cross Green area of Leeds, died on the 8th of December 2020. There are no suspicious circumstances to his death.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Coroner's officer Sarah Halloran on 01924 292301.
