**Wednesday, 24 March 2021 **

Police are appealing for information following a fire in Huddersfield which has left three people in hospital.

It is believed to have started at about 6am yesterday (Tuesday) at an address in Clare Hill.

Six people received hospital treatment. Three of whom remain there today, where they are all said to be critical but stable.

Enquiries suggest the fire was deliberate and three males have been arrested in connection with the incident.
