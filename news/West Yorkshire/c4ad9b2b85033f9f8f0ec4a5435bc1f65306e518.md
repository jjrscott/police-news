**Monday, March 8, 2021**

Police are appealing for information to help trace a man from Leeds who is wanted for recall to prison.

Barry Riley, aged 39, has been wanted for recall to prison since December following his release from prison in October after serving a sentence for theft from a shop.

Wanted: Barry Riley

He is also currently wanted on suspicion of a theft from shop offence in February this year and on warrant for failing to appear at court in North Yorkshire.

He is described as white, 5ft 10ins tall, slim build, with short brown hair and blue eyes.

Anyone who sees him or who has any information that could assist in locating him is asked to contact officers via 101 quoting reference 13200618911 or online via www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
