**Thursday 11 March 2021**

Police are appealing for witnesses after a serious collision on Listing Lane in Cleckheaton.

Officers were called at 19.52 yesterday (March 10) to reports two vehicles had been in collision.

A Fiat 500 and Toyota were travelling in opposite directions when the collision occurred.

The driver of the Fiat 500 a 20 year old woman has been taken to hospital in a critical condition.

The driver of the Toyota was taken to hospital for non life threatening injuries.

Officers are asking for any witnesses to the collision to come forward and make contact.

Officers are particularly keen to hear from anyone who has dash cam / CCTV footage of either vehicle leading up to the collision.

Anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101 or use the contact us options at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contactus quoting log 1541 of March 10
