Wednesday, 6th January, 2021

Police in Halifax are appealing for witnesses following a theft offence.

The incident happened on Saturday 2 January at 16.30pm on Keighley Road, between the junctions of Grove Avenue and Athol Road.

A car pulled up alongside a 23 year-old female with a pushchair, and three men then got out of the car and demanded her purse. The fourth man, the driver, remained in the car during the incident.

The 23 year-old victim, handed over her purse and the car drove off down Old Lane, Halifax.

The car is described as a small silver 5-door hatchback.

No injuries were sustained, but the victim was understandably shaken by the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or particularly anyone with relevant dashcam footage is asked to contact the police as soon as possible via 101 quoting log number 2162 of 02/01. Information can passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
