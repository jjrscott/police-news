Wednesday 17 March 2021

Police in Calderdale are appealing for information following a racially aggravated public order offence on George Square, Halifax.

The incident happened outside the Wilkinsons store at around 16.50pm on Friday 12 March.

A security guard attempted to detain a man, who was suspected of stealing from the store.

The suspect assaulted the security guard and shouted racial abuse before leaving the area on foot.

A 38-year old man has been arrested and bailed pending further inquiries.

Officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information which may assist in enquiries to contact 101 or online via www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101LiveChat quoting crime reference number 13210125495.

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555111.
