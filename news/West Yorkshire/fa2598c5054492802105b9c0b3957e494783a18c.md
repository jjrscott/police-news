Saturday 20 February 2021

Police are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision in Bradford.

The incident happened at around 12.10am today (Saturday) on Canal Road, when a black Audi RS3 collided with a wall.

The vehicle was travelling in the direction of Shipley, from Bradford.

Both the driver and passenger of the vehicle sustained serious injuries and were taken to the Leeds General Infirmary. The injuries are not considered to be life threatening at this time.

Enquires are still ongoing.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, may have dash cam footage which will assist the investigation or who saw the movements of the car immediately prior to it is asked to contact 101, quoting log 0011 of 20/02, or report online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat.
