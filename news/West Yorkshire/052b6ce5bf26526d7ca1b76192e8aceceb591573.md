**Saturday 9 May 2020**

Three men who were arrested after a cannabis farm was located in Slaithwaite on Tuesday 5 May have been charged with conspiring to produce a class B drug.

Gjovalin Preka (34) of Kingfisher Drive, Barnsley and Coman Ndue (27) and Armando Ara (23) of Watling Street, Cannock all appeared at Leeds Magistrates Court on May 7.

They were all bailed and are due back at Leeds Crown Court on June 4.
