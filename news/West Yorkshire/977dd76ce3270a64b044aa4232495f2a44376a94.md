Thursday 17th December 2020.

Coroner's officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Henry Stones.

Mr Stones, aged 78yrs who lived in the Fitzwilliam area of Pontefract, died on the 12th of December in hospital of natural causes.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Coroner's officer Steve Jarvis on 01924 292301.
