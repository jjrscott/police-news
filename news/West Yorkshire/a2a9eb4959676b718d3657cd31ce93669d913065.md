Wednesday 6th January 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Ronald Nichols from Keighley.

Mr Nichols, aged 72 years, passed away on the 22nd December 2020.

Anyone with information is requested to contact Jayne Dawson on 01274 373721 during the hours of 8am - 4pm, Monday - Friday.
