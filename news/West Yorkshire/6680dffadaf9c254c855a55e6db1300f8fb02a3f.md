Wednesday 9th December 2020.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Dareen Bridge from Huddersfield.

Mrs Bridge, aged 76 years, passed away on the 8th December 2020.

Anyone with information is requested to contact Coroner's Officer Lynsey Atkinson during the hours of 8am - 4pm, Monday - Friday, on 01274 373721.
