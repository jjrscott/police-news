Friday 26 March 2021

Police are appealing for information after a man was found with stab wounds in Wakefield yesterday (Thursday).

The man, 25, was taken to hospital for further treatment after he was found in Miners Court in New Sharlston at just after 3pm yesterday.

He was later discharged.

The incident is believed to have occurred in Wakefield Road, close to the Millpond Petrol Station.

Wakefield CID are investigating the incident and anyone with information is asked to contact them on 101 or by using the options on the West Yorkshire Police website.

The crime reference is 13210149146.

Information can also be reported anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers.
