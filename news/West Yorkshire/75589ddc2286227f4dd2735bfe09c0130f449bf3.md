Police are appealing for witnesses after a man was stabbed on Copper Beach Avenue, Bramley.
Officers were called to the road at around 0320am this morning (Saturday 13 February) to reports of a man being attacked by a number of men.
The victim was left with serious but not believed to be life threatening injuries.
Detective Inspector James Entwistle from Leeds CID, said:
“I would appeal to anyone who witnessed the incident or saw any suspicious activity around this time to call police.”
Call police on 101 quoting log 226 of Saturday 13 February
