Tuesday 23 March 2021

Police are appealing for witnesses following a fire at the BT Exchange in Hemsworth.

It is believed to have happened at about 11pm on Sunday (21 March) and caused significant damage to a building in Westfield Road.

It is believed to have been started deliberately and Wakefield District CID are appealing for anyone who saw anything suspicious in the area at around the above time to get in touch.

Anyone with information which may assist enquiries can contact Wakefield CID on 101, quoting crime reference 13210141953, or use the contact options on the West Yorkshire Police website.

Information can also be reported anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers.
