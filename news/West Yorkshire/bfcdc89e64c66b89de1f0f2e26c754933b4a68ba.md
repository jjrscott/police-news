**Thursday October 1, 2020**

Police are appealing for information to trace Scott Rimmer who is wanted in connection with a robbery in Wakefield.

Wakefield District Police would like to speak to anyone who has information to help them locate the 31-year-old who is from the Crigglestone area of Wakefield.

Officers have been carrying out enquiries to locate him for several weeks and want to speak to him in connection with a robbery in Kettlethorpe in September, in which a male victim suffered injuries.

Scott Rimmer is still believed to be in the Wakefield area and anyone who has any information about him is asked to contact Wakefield Police on 101 referencing crime number 13200466090.

Information can also be given online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat or anonymously to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
