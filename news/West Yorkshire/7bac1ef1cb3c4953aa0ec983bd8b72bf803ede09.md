Monday 15th February 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Darrell Priestley from Halifax.

Mr Priestley, aged 53 years, passed away on the 5th February 2021.

Anyone with information is requested to contact Coroner's Officer Vicki Illingworth on 01274 373721 between the hours of 9am - 5pm, Monday - Friday.
