Tuesday 2nd February 2021

Coroner's officers are asking for help to trace the relatives of Mr Graham Watson.

Mr Watson, aged 71yrs, lived in Castleford and died on the 20th of January. His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Coroner's officer Dawn Powell on 01924 292301
