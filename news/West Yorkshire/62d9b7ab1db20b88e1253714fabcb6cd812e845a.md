Friday 15 January 2021

Police are appealing for information to help trace Ashley Robinson who is wanted in connection with the assault of an emergency worker.

Ashley Robinson, aged 19, from Bradford is also wanted on a recall to prison, for burglary offences.

Extensive enquiries have been ongoing to locate him by Bradford District Police.

Police are urging the public to get in touch with any information.

Anyone who has seen him or who has any information that could assist in tracing him is asked to contact Bradford District Crime Team via 101 quoting reference 13200645130 or online via www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
