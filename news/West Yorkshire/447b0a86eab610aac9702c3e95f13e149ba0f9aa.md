**Thursday 1 April, 2021**

Police are appealing for witnesses following an attempted robbery in Recreation Park, Eccleshill.

The incident happened on Sunday 21 March at around 22:15 when a man in his 20s was walking in the park.

Two male suspects approached the male victim from behind and assaulted him by kicking him and also hit him.

The suspects attempted to check the pockets of the victim’s jacket; nothing was stolen.

The suspects then fled the scene.

The first suspect is described as a white male, 6ft, stocky build, wearing a matching tracksuit and had his hood up.

The second suspect is described as a white male, around 5ft 10”, stocky build, bigger build than the first suspect and he was also wearing a tracksuit with hood up.

The male victim suffered bruised ribs as result of the incident.

The area in the park was dark and poorly lit and police are urging the public to be vigilant in the area.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or has any information is asked to contact Bradford District Crime Team via 101 quoting crime reference number 13210142860 or online.

Information can also be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
