**Monday 21 December 2020 - 22.25**

Tara Whelan from Halifax who was the subject of a earlier missing persons appeal has been found safe and well.

Police would like to thank everyone who shared the appeal across social media.

A 39 year old man who was arrested in connection with her disappearance has been released from custody.
