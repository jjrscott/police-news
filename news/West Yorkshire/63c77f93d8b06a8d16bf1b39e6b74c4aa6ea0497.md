Wednesday 15th April 2020,

Police in Leeds are appealing for information after the driver of a white transit van failed to stop at the scene of a collision on East Grange Drive yesterday (14/04).

Just before 5:30pm, a cyclist was struck by the vehicle, which subsequently made away.

The man in his twenties who was on the bike sustained serious injuries and taken to hospital for treatment.

Officers investigating the incident are keen to hear from anyone who was driving in the area at the time and may have captured the incident on Dashcam footage.

Likewise, any other witnesses are asked to contact the non-emergency number 101 or use the online live-chat facility available via the Force website.

If you can assist in the enquiries, please reference PC 6042 Gibson of the Roads Policing Unit and log 13200185127.
