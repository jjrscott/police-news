Monday 4th January 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Janesse Tineke Mousea from Bradford.

Miss Mousea, aged 53 years, passed away on the 31st December 2020.

Anyone with information is requested to contact Coroner's Officer Carol Taylor on 01274 373721 during the hours of 8am - 4pm, Monday - Friday.
