Wednesday 3rd February 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of George Lawson from Leeds.

Mr Lawson, aged 86 years, passed away on the 29th January 2021.

Anyone with information is requested to contact the Coroner's Office on 01924 292301.
