**Tuesday 8 December 2020**

Police are appealing for witnesses after they were called to reports of ongoing disorder in Tesco car park on Haugh Shaw Road, Halifax.

Officers were called at 9.45pm yesterday evening (7 December) to reports that a number of males armed with baseball bats were smashing up vehicles in the car park.

On arrival officers viewed store CCTV, three men, two aged 23 and one aged 25 have been arrested on suspicion of violent disorder and remain in police custody at this time.

> Inspector Jim Graham from Halifax Central Neighbourhood Police Team said:
>
> ‘We believe this to be a targeted and isolated incident.
>
> ‘My officers are conducting reassurance patrols in the area. I would like to appeal to anyone who may have information about the incident yesterday to come forward.
>
> ‘Our enquiries are ongoing.’

Anyone with information is asked to contact police via 101 or use the live chat facility at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contactus quoting reference 13200612549.

Information can also be given anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111
