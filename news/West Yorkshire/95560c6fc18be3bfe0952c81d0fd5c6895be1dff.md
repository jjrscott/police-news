**Friday 18 December 2020**

Police are appealing for witnesses of an assault of a man in Elland.

It happened between 3pm and 3.30pm on Monday 14 December on Brooksbank Gardens, Elland.

The victim, 54-year-old man, suffered injuries to his head and back and was taken to hospital.

Police are keen to speak to anyone who witnessed the assault or who was in the area at the time who may have seen anything suspicious to get in touch.

If you can assist this investigation then please call 101 or use the contact via the West Yorkshire Police website https://www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101LiveChat , quoting crime reference 13200624389

Information can be also given via Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
