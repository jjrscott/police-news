Tuesday 16 February 2021

Police in Leeds are appealing for information to help trace a man who is wanted on a recall to prison.

Andrew Fawcett, 31, from Beeston is wanted for breaching the conditions of his licence.

Officers have conducted extensive enquiries into his whereabouts and are now appealing for the public to assist.

He is described as white male, slim build, short brown hair and a black tattoo on the left-hand side of his neck. It is believed that he may have a bandage on the rear of his head.

Police believe he may be in Leeds City Centre and the surrounding areas.

Anyone who has seen him or who has any information that could assist in tracing him is asked to contact via 101 quoting reference 13210032173 or online via www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. **
