Wednesday 17 March 2021

Police are appealing for information following a fatal road traffic collision in Keighley.

The collision occurred at approximately 11.05am this morning, Wednesday 17 March on Skipton Road, Steeton when a D.A.F cement truck travelling West on Skipton Road collided with a pedestrian.

The pedestrian, a 42-year old male, was taken to hospital where he later died of his injuries.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or anyone who may have dash cam footage is asked to contact 101 or via the website www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101LiveChat quoting reference 13210133608.
