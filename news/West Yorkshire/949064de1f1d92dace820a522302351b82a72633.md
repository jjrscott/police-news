**Wednesday 3 March, 2021**

Police are appealing for information to trace wanted man George Runicman in Bradford.

He is wanted for burglary and arson offences in the Bradford District.

He is believed to be in the Bradford area.

Anyone with any information as to his whereabouts is asked to contact the police via 101 quoting crime reference number 13210052820.

Information can also be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
