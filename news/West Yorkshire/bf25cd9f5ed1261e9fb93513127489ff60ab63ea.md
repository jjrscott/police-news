**Wednesday 2 September 2020**

A 20-year-old man has been charged with arson following an incident at an accommodation block on Servia Road in Leeds on 29 August.

Bradley Starkey, of Woodview Street, appeared before Leeds Magistrates Court yesterday.

He has also been charged with possession of a bladed article in a public place, and a separate offence of burglary on 21 August.
