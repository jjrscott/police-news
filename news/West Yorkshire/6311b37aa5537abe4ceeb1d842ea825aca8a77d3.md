Thursday 3 December 2020

Police are appealing for information about the possible whereabouts of Leeds man Nicky Walker.

Walker is 24-years-old and is wanted for recall to prison, plus breaching a court order.

Police have carried out extensive enquiries to locate Walker but have so far been unsuccessful in finding him.

Anyone who may know where Walker is or who sees him is asked to contact West Yorkshire Police on 101 or via Live Chat, quoting reference number 13200495447.
