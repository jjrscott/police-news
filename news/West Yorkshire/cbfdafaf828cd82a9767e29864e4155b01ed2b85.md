**Wednesday, 10 February 2021**

Police are appealing for information on Darren Harper, who has been reported missing from his home in Bradford.

Darren, who is 53, was last seen at his Thorpe Edge home at about 2.53pm today.

He is described as being about 5ft 8ins tall and of stocky build.

He is believed to be wearing black jeans, walking boots and a charcoal grey winter coat.

He is a keen walker and is known to travel long distances on foot. However, there are concerns for his welfare and enquiries are being made to locate him.

Anyone with information which may assist is asked to contact police in Bradford on 101, quoting log 1016 of 10 February.
