Monday 1 March 2021

Detectives in Kirklees are issuing CCTV images of a group of males they want to identify in connection with a robbery in Birkenshaw.

At around 6.55pm on Monday 22 February, police were called to an incident at Old Lane Newsagents, Birkenshaw.

Three males entered the shop and were verbally abusive towards the shop keeper and then attempted to steal items from the shop.

The shop keeper confronted the males and was assaulted causing facial injuries.

Detective from Huddersfield CID are continuing with enquiries and would like to hear of any sightings of the group in the area or if anyone can identify the individuals captured on CCTV it would be greatly appreciated.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Kirklees District Crime Team via 101 quoting crime reference 13210092003 or online via www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
