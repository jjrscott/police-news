**Friday 1 January, 2020**

Police have charged a teenager in connection with a serious assault in Slaithwaite.

The incident happened on Wednesday 30 December around 5pm when an 18-year-old man was stabbed in the arm on Bank Top.

A 16-year-old male, from Huddersfield was arrested and he has now been charged with Section 18 wounding with intent.

He will appear at Leeds magistrates court on 2 January.

====================================================================================================

**Thursday December 31, 2020**

Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious assault in Slaithwaite on Wednesday night.

Kirklees CID would like to speak to anyone who saw or has information about the incident on Bank Top in Slaithwaite on December 30 in which an 18-year-old man was injured.

It is believed the assault occurred at about 5pm after the victim became involved in an altercation with a male.

The victim was stabbed in the arm by the suspect who ran from the scene.

Officers attended the location and following enquiries, arrested a 16-year-old male on suspicion of wounding. He remains in police custody.

The victim later attended hospital for treatment to his arm.

Anyone who has any information about the incident is asked to contact Kirklees CID on 101 or online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat.

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
