Wednesday 27th January 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Jefferson Brennan from Todmorden, born in Leigh, Lancashire.

Mr Jefferson, aged 52 years, passed away on the 19th January 2021.

Anyone with information is requested to contact Jayne Dawson on 01274 373721 during the hours of 9am - 5pm, Monday - Friday.
