**Wednesday 3 February 2021**

Police are appealing for witnesses after a teenager was assaulted at Keighley bus station on Friday 29th January 2021.

The incident happened between 3pm and 4pm near to the Cenotaph.

A 14-year-old was left with injuries that required hospital treatment.

> PC 648 Mercer of Bradford District Investigation Team Police, said:
>
> “The incident happened at a busy time of day and I am appealing for anyone who saw the incident to call police on 101 quoting crime reference 13210049407.
>
> “We are particularly keen to speak to anyone who may have recorded the incident on a mobile phone or other digital device.”
