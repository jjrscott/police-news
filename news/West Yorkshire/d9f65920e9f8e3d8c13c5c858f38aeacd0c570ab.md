Thursday 10th December 2020

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Ahmed Awan from Bradford.

Mr Awan, aged 82 years, passed away on the 27th November 2020.

Anyone with information is requested to contact Coroner's Officer Arran Arblaster on 01274 373721 during the hours of 8am - 4pm, Monday - Friday.
