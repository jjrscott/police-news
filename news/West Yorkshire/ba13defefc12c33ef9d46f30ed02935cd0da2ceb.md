Friday 18th December 2020

Coroners Officers are appealing for help in tracing the relatives of Mr John Durkin.

Mr Durkin, aged 79 years old, passed away on 8th December 2020.

Anyone with any information regarding the relatives of Mr Durkin should contact Coroners Officer Gemma McDermott on 01274 373721.
