Monday 22nd March 2021

Coroners Officers are appealing for help in tracing the relatives of Michael Robinson from Shipley.

Mr Robinson, aged 41 years old, passed away on 26th February 2021.

Anyone with any information regarding Michaels relatives should contact the Bradford Coroners Office on 01274 373721.
