Wednesday 17th March 2021

Coroner's officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Michael Kent.

Mr Kent, aged 70yrs lately lived in the Chapeltown area of Leeds but is believed to have lived in Horsforth before that. His cause of death is unknown at this time.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Coroner's officer John Bracewell on 01924 292301.
