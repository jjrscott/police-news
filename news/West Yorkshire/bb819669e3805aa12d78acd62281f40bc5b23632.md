**Tuesday 15 December 2020**

Police in Bradford are appealing for information about the possible whereabouts of Michael Crossley, aged 36.

Crossley is wanted on a recall to prison for breaching his licence conditions. He is thought to be in the Bradford area.

Officers have been carrying out a number of enquiries but so far have not been able to locate him.

Anyone who has seen him or who has any information that could assist in tracing him is asked to contact officers via 101 quoting reference 13200464888 or online via www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat.

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
