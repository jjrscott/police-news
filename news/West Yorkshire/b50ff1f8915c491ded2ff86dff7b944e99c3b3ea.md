**Friday, 19 March 2021**

Police have issued an image of three males they would like to speak to about a robbery in Leeds.

It happened in Darfield Crescent at about 8.15pm on Thursday, 4 February.

The suspects asked the victim for money and then took his mobile phone when he declined.

Anyone who may recognise these males is asked to contact the Leeds District Crime Team on 101 or by using the contact options on the West Yorkshire Police website, quoting crime reference 13210060294.

Information can also be reported anonym to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.
