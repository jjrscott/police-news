**Monday 18 January 2021**

Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident on Saturday 10 October at The Works pub, Hollins Lane, Sowerby Bridge.

At around 8pm, a man aged in his 50s and 43 year-old woman were involved in an altercation outside The Works pub.

The incident was reported to the police and the officers have recorded an offence of racially aggravated assault.

Officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed the altercation to come forward.

Anyone with information should contact the police on 101 or via the West Yorkshire Police website at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contactus quoting the crime reference 13200508559.

Information can also be reported anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.
