**Thursday 14 January 2021**

A West Yorkshire Police Staff Member has been charged with making an indecent photograph/ pseudo-photograph of a child.

Carl Hardcastle who works in Protective Services Crime in the Bradford area will appear at Leeds Magistrates Court on 25 January 2021.

Mr Hardcastle is currently suspended from the force.
