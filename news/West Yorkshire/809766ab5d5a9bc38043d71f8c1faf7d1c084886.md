**Wednesday September 30, 2020**

Detectives are appealing for witnesses following a reported firearms discharge in the Deighton area of Huddersfield

Police have been conducting enquiries in the Glenfield Avenue area of Huddersfield since Tuesday evening (September 29) following a report of a firearms discharge there.

Officers from the Homicide and Major Enquiry team are investigating after a man attended at hospital at about 9.30pm on Tuesday evening with none life threatening injuries to one foot consistent with a firearms discharge.

The male continues to be treated and enquiries into the circumstances of the incident are ongoing.

Anyone who has information or who saw or heard suspicious behaviour in the Glenfield Avenue area between 9pm and 9.20pm on Tuesday September 29 is asked to contact the Homicide and Major Enquiry Team on 101 referencing police crime number 13200489018.

Information can also be given online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat or anonymously to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
