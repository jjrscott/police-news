**Monday February 8, 2021**

Police are issuing an Efit of a man they want to identify in connection with an attempted burglary in Dewsbury.

The Kirklees District Crime Team would like to speak to anyone who can identify the man pictured as investigations continue into an attempted burglary on Windsor Close in Dewsbury on November 24, 2020.

The offence took place after a man attempted to open the door of a flat but was unsuccessful.

He was spotted by the owner and made an excuse before leaving.

The man was described as white, between 25-30, 5ft 8ins to 6ft tall, bald and of a muscled build. He was wearing dark work pants and an orange high vis jumper.

Anyone who recognises the man is asked to contact DC 3578 Montgomery on 101 or via email at [email protected]

Information can also be given online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat or anonymously to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
