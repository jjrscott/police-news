**Thursday 4 February, 2021**

Detectives are investigating following a stabbing in Bradford last night.

Police were called at 23:04 (3 February) to reports of a man being injured on Great Horton Road.

A male suspect, armed with a knife, assaulted another male.

The male suspect fled the scene and ran towards Grove Terrace.

A 38-year-old man suffered a serious injury to his leg and was taken to hospital via ambulance where he underwent treatment.

His injuries are not thought to be life threatening.

The suspect is described as a white male, in his late 30s, was clean shaven and had a distinctive large head and possible facial deformity. He was wearing black jeans, black hoody with hood up and black trainers.

Enquiries are ongoing to establish the circumstances of the incident.

Anyone who was in the area, witnessed the incident or has any further information is asked to contact Bradford CID via 101 quoting crime reference number 13210058503 or online via https://www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contact-us

Information can also be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
