Thursday 25 February 2021

Police are appealing for information about Shauna Smith, who has been reported missing from Halifax.

Shauna, who is 15, was last seen in the Queens Road area at about 3.15pm yesterday (Wednesday).

She is described as being about 5ft 2ins tall with blonde/brown hair which is usually pulled back in a bobble.

It is possible she may have travelled to Warwickshire or Manchester and officers are making enquiries to locate her, as there are concerns for her welfare.

Anyone who can assist in locating Shauna is asked to contact police at Halifax on 101 or by using the contact options on the West Yorkshire Police website, quoting log reference 1008 of 24 February.
