Friday 26 March 2021

Police in Bradford are appealing for information to help trace a man who is wanted on a recall to prison.

Aurangzeb Haq, 50 from Bradford is also wanted for breach of a restraining order.

Officers have carried out extensive enquiries and have not been able to locate his whereabouts and are asking the public to assist with enquiries.

He is believed to be in or around the Bradford area.

Anyone who has seen him or who has any information that could assist in tracing him is asked to contact officers via 101 quoting reference 13210048133 or call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
