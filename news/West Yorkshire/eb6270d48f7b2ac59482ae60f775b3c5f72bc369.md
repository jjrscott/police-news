**Friday 21 August 2020**

Police are issuing CCTV stills of men they want to speak with in connection with an assault and disorder incident in Batley.

Kirklees District CID would like to speak with anyone who has information about the three men as enquiries continue into the incident on Benny Parr Close on Sunday June 21.

The incident took place between 8.55pm and 9.05pm culminating in fighting between a group of males.

One man received serious head injuries in the incident.

Detectives have so far arrested six men and a woman as part of the ongoing investigation.

Anyone who can identify the three men pictured (two of whom are pictured twice) or has any information about the incident is asked to contact Kirklees District CID on 101 referencing crime number 13200310317

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
