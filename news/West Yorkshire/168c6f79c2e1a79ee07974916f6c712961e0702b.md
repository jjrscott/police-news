**Friday, 29 January 2021 **

Jay Newsome has been located and charged with the burglary he was wanted in connection with.

He has since appeared before magistrates where he was remanded into custody.

We are grateful to everyone who shared our appeal.

**Wednesday January 20, 2021**

Police in Kirklees are appealing for information to trace Jay Newsome who is wanted by officers.

Kirklees CID would like to hear from anyone who has information which may help them trace the 30-year-old who they want to speak to in connection with a burglary offence committed in Batley last November.

Jay Newsome is from the Dewsbury area and numerous enquiries have been ongoing there and elsewhere in Kirklees to find him.

Anyone who can assist enquiries to locate him is asked to contact Kirklees CID on 101 referencing crime number 13200583235.

Information can also be given online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat or to anonymously to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
