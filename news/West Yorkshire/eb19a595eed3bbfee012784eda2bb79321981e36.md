**Thursday January 28, 2021**

Police are issuing images of a man and woman they want to speak with in connection with a burglary in Huddersfield.

The Kirklees District Crime Team would like to speak to anyone who can identify the man and woman pictured as they continue enquiries into a break-in at a house on Ray Street Huddersfield on September 12, 2020.

The offence took place between 11am and 12.30pm after suspects broke into a property and stole a TV.

Anyone who can identify the man and woman pictured or who can assist the police investigation is asked to contact the Kirklees District Crime Team on 101 and quote crime number 13200458944.

They can also e-mail DC 3578 Montgomery at [email protected]

or make a report online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat

Information can also be given in total anonymity to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
