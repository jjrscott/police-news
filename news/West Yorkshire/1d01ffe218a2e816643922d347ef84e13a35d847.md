**Wednesday 13 May, 2020**

A 30-year-old man is due to appear in court this morning following an incident in Cleckheaton on Monday.

Drew Trudgill, of Turnsteads Drive, has been charged with two counts of false imprisonment and two counts of making threats to kill.

He will appear before Leeds Magistrates Court this morning.
