Wednesday, 2 December 2020

Police are appealing for information following a serious collision in Wakefield.

It happened at about 4.50pm yesterday (Tuesday) when a blue Hyundai i10 travelling towards the M1 northbound slip road at Junction 39 on the Durkar Interchange Roundabout left the road and collided with a lamp post.

An 89-year-old local man who was a passenger in the vehicle was taken to hospital for further treatment.

His injuries are believed to be potentially life threatening.

The driver was uninjured.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or who may have footage of the vehicle prior to it is asked to contact the Roads Policing Unit on 101 or by using our contact options on the West Yorkshire Police website.

The log reference is 1025 of 1 December.
