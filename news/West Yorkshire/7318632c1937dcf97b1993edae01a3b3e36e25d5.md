**Tuesday, November 3, 2020**

Police are appealing for information to help trace a man who gone missing in Leeds.

Tendai Mupawaenda, aged 22, from Belle Isle, was last seen on October 23 and there are concerns for his welfare.

Missing: Tendai Mupawaenda

Anyone who has seen him or who has any information that could assist in tracing him is asked to contact officers via 101 quoting log number 1213 of October 27 or online via www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat
