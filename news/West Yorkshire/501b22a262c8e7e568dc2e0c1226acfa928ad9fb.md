Wednesday, 9 December 2020

Police are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision in Bradford.

It happened at about 8.33am today (Wednesday) when a black Suzuki Swift was involved in a collision with four pedestrians in Hudson Avenue, close to the junction with Hudson Crescent.

Two adults and a child received serious injuries and are receiving further treatment in hospital.

A further child suffered minor injuries.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, or who has footage which may assist its investigation is asked to contact Roads Policing on 101, or by using the options on the West Yorkshire Police website.

The log reference is 269 of 9 December.
