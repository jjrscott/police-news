**Wednesday, 24 March 2021**

Police are appealing for information after a man was stabbed in Huddersfield.

Officers were called to reports of a fight in Catherine Road at about 4.45pm yesterday (Tuesday) and found a 26-year-old man with serious injuries.

His injuries are not life threatening and he remains in hospital.

Kirklees District CID are investigating the incident, which is being treated as a targeted attack, and are appealing for anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area at around the time stated to contact them.

The local neighbourhood policing team have increased patrols in the area to offer reassurance.

Anyone with information which may assist this investigation can contact Kirklees CID on 101 or by using the options on the West Yorkshire Police website.

The crime reference is 13210145333.

Information can also be reported anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers.
