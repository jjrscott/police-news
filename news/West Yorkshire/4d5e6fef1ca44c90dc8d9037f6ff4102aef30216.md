**Monday, March 29, 2021**

A man who was the subject of a wanted appeal has been arrested and charged over a domestic-related incident in Leeds.

Karl Lewis, aged 23, of Valley Road, Pudsey, appeared at Leeds Magistrates Court on Saturday (27/3) charged with two counts of false imprisonment, two counts of assault occasioning actual bodily harm and an attempt to pervert the course of justice. He was remanded in custody pending a further hearing.

He was previously the subject of wanted appeals on March 1 and March 8.
