**Monday, November 30, 2020**

Police have released CCTV images of two men they want to identify in connection with a series of burglary offences in the Tingley area of Leeds.

Detectives from Leeds District Crime Team are investigating incidents in Folly Hall Road and Landseer Avenue in the early hours of Tuesday, November 24, where doors were tried at a number of addresses and coats were stolen from the front porch of one property.
