Saturday, 5 September 2020

Yiting Zhang, who was the subject of a missing persons appeal yesterday has been located.

Thank you to everyone who shared our appeal.

Friday, 4 September 2020

Police are appealing for anyone who sees a missing woman to get in contact.

The last confirmed sighting of Yiting Zhang, 22, (who was a student at Huddersfield University studying Accountancy) was on 20 July at her student accommodation block on Kings Bridge Road, Huddersfield.

The last confirmed contact with her was on Thursday 20 August and there are concerns for her welfare. She was reported missing when she failed to meet family on the 21st August 2020.

It is thought that she could be in the Whitechapel area of London with enquiries potentially placing her there as recently as Bank Holiday Monday (31 August).
