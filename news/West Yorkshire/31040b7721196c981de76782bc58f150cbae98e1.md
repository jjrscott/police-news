**Tuesday March 30, 2021**

Police have issued a picture of a man who has died following a house fire in Huddersfield.

Reece Schofield (24) from Huddersfield died in hospital on Monday after receiving serious injuries in a fire at an address on Clare Hill on Tuesday March 23.

His death follows that of Samantha Mills (31) from the Huddersfield area who passed away in hospital last Friday as a result of injuries sustained in the blaze.

A 26-year-old woman remains in hospital but is now recovering.

Craig Collier, aged 34, of Clare Hill in Huddersfield, has been charged with arson with intent to endanger life and perverting the course of justice.

He has since appeared at magistrates and has been remanded in custody ahead of a further hearing at Leeds Crown Court.

West Yorkshire Police’s Homicide and Major Enquiry Team is continuing to appeal to anyone who was in the Clare Hill area at about 6am on Tuesday 23 March 2021 to contact them on 101, or via LiveChat.

The crime reference is 13210144132.

Information can also be reported anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers.

**Monday, 29 March 2021**

Detectives investigating a house fire in Huddersfield have confirmed a second fatality.

Reece Schofield, who was 24, was taken to hospital following the blaze at an address in Clare Hill last Tuesday (23 March).

He was pronounced dead earlier today.

Samantha Mills, who was 31, was also injured in the fire and died in hospital on Friday.

One person remains in hospital where their condition is said to be stable.

**Monday, 29 March 2021**

A woman who was taken to hospital following a house fire in Huddersfield has died.

Samantha Mills, who was 31 and from the Huddersfield area, was one of six people taken to hospital following the blaze at an address in Clare Hill last Tuesday (23 March).

She died in hospital on Friday.

Two people remain in hospital today (Monday).

Craig Collier, aged 34, of Clare Hill in Huddersfield, has been charged with arson with intent to endanger life and perverting the course of justice.

He has since appeared at magistrates and has been remanded in custody ahead of a further hearing at Leeds Crown Court.

West Yorkshire Police’s Homicide and Major Enquiry Team is continuing to appeal to anyone who was in the Clare Hill area at about 6am on Tuesday 23 March 2021 to contact them on 101, or via LiveChat.

The crime reference is 13210144132.

Information can also be reported anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers.
