Thursday 3 December 2020

Robert Bruce is wanted for breaching a court order issued by Leeds Magistrates Court.

Bruce is 33-years-old and from the Armley area of Leeds.

Police have carried out extensive enquiries but have so far been unsuccessful in locating him.

Anyone who may know where Bruce is or may have seen him recently is asked to contact West Yorkshire Police on 101 or via Live Chat, quoting reference number 13200464031.
