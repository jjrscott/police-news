**Saturday April 3, 2021**

Police are appealing for information to locate a man reported missing in Leeds.

Leeds District Police would like to speak to anyone who has seen or has information about Kazimierz Tomica (50) from the Woodhouse area.

He was reported missing during the early evening of April 1 and officers have concerns for his welfare.

The 50-year-old is described as white, 6ft tall and of slim build with brown hair a full beard and blue eyes. He is Polish and speaks with a Polish accent.

Enquiries have been ongoing in Leeds city centre to locate Kazimierz and anyone who has seen him of has information about him is asked to contact Leeds District Police on 101 referencing police log 1421 of April 1.

Information can also be given online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat
