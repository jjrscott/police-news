Tuesday 9th March 2021.

Coroner's officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Mr John Humble.

Mr Humble, aged 71yrs, died on the 20th of February at his home address. His death is of natural causes and there are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone with information is asked to call Coroner's officer Nina Roper on 01924 292301.
