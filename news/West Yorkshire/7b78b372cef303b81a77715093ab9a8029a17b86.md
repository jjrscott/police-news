**Tuesday 1 December 2020**

Police are appealing for witnesses after a road traffic collision involving three vehicles on Farrar Lane in Leeds.

A red Fiat Panda failed to stop for officers in Bramhope.

It then travelled along Otley Road when it approached the junction with Farrar Lane.

The Fiat Panda was in collision with two other vehicles and Volkswagen Tiguan and Ford Transit Van shortly before 5pm yesterday (30 November.)

The driver of the Fiat Panda was taken to hospital with serious but not life-threatening injuries. The passenger suffered minor injuries and has since been released from hospital.

The occupants of the other vehicles did not require hospital treatment.

Officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision or who saw the vehicles prior to the collision to come forward.

Anyone with dash cam footage of the incident is asked to contact police.

Information can be given to the Roads Policing Unit via 101 or use the contact us options at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contactus quoting reference 13200600036 or log 781 of 30 November.
