**Monday, 22 February 2021**

Paul Capuvanno, who was the subject of a wanted appeal, has been located.

He has also been charged with a number of offences and has been remanded in custody to appear before Bradford Magistrates today (Monday).

Thank you to everyone who shared our appeal.
