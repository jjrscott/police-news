**Friday 26 March, 2021**

Police are appealing for anyone who sees a missing Bradford man to get in contact.

David Briggs, 31, of Clayton, was last seen at Lavinia Terrace, Clayton at 0645 am today (Friday 26 March).

He is described as white, 5’8” tall, of medium build and with dark brown short hair.

When last seen he was wearing a dark tracksuit and white trainers.

Anyone who sees him is asked to call police on 101 quoting log 221 of Friday 26 March.
