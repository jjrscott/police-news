**Tuesday 19 January, 2021**

Police in Bradford have now charged a man who was subject of an earlier wanted appeal.

Ashley Robinson, 19, of Harewood Street, Bradford, has been charged with assault of an emergency worker and will appear at Bradford Magistrates Court this morning.

He has also been recalled to prison.

**Monday 18 January, 2021**

Ashley Robinson who was subject to an appeal has been located.
