**Monday January 4, 2021**

Police in Kirklees are appealing for information to help trace two men in connection with a fraud offence.

Gheorghe Pongori

Officers are seeking the whereabouts of Gheorghe Pongori, 33 and Calin Singeeran, 31 from Dewsbury.

Despite extensive enquiries and checks on addresses, officers have been unable to locate them.

Anyone who knows their current whereabouts or who may have information that could assist in tracing the two men is asked to contact on 101 or use the live chat facility at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contactus quoting crime reference 13200140192.

Calin Singeeran

They can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
