**Monday 2 November 2020**

Police and other emergency services are continuing to search the River Aire and surrounding areas after a report of a man seen entering the water this morning.

It is reported the man entered the water at 0422am and officers are becoming increasingly concerned for his welfare.

DI Richard Coulton of Leeds CID, said:

“We are urgently trying to trace this man – the river is particularly swollen at the moment given the recent weather conditions and we are doing all we can to try and locate him.”

Officers would like to appeal to anyone with information about the incident including anyone who saw the male enter the water.

Call police on 999 quoting log 154 of November 2.

**Monday 2 November 2020**

Police and emergency services are currently in attendance at the River Aire in Leeds after a report of a man entering the water at 4.22 this morning.

The incident remains ongoing.

A number or surrounding roads in the area of Dock Street are closed whilst the operation is ongoing.

Officers would like to appeal to anyone who may have seen anything in the area this morning.

Any information can be given via 101 or on the contact us options at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contactus quoting log 154 of November 2.
