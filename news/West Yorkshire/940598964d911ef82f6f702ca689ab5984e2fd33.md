**Friday, December 11, 2020**

Police searching for a missing woman from Leeds have found a body.

Officers were called to Musgrave View in Bramley this afternoon (Friday 11 December) where a woman’s body was found.

Formal identification has taken place and it has been confirmed as Sara Magari, aged 38 who was reporting missing from her home in Leeds on Thursday.

Her family have been informed and the Coroner’s Office has been notified. There are no suspicious circumstances.
