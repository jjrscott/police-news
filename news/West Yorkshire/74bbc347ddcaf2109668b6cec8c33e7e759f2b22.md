Police are appealing for witnesses after a collision on Cemetery Road in Bradford.

Officers were called at around 6.40pm on Sunday November 1 to reports of two vehicles in collision.

A black Audi S3 motor Vehicle and a grey Toyota Avensis motor vehicle were in collision between the junctions of Glendare Terrace and Wilkinson Terrace.

The driver of the Toyota suffered serious injuries and he was taken to hospital.

Anyone who witnesses the collision itself or anyone who saw the vehicles prior to the collision is asked to contact PC 483 Jon Moss of the Western Area Roads Policing Unit by calling 101 or using the live chat facility at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contactus quoting 13200547749
