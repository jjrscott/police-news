Wednesday 23rd December 2020.

Coroner's officers are trying to trace the relatives of Anthony Howarth.

Mr Howarth aged 67yrs, lived in Knottingley and died on the 19th of December. It is believed he was born in Barnsley. There are no suspicious circumstances to his death.

Anyone with information is asked to call Coroner's officer Chris Clarkson on 01924 292301.
