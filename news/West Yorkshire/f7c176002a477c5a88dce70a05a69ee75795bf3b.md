Wednesday 20 January 2021

Police investigating an incident where the front door of a house was set on fire in Knottingley are appealing for witnesses.

The incident happened at around 9pm on Saturday 17 October 2020, at a house on Windermere Drive, Warwick Estate.

The occupants were home at the time of the incident, but no injuries were reported.

Enquiries have now identified two people, a man and a woman, that officers are keen to speak to in connection with the offence.

The male suspect is described as white, late teens/early twenties, slim build, 5ft7-5ft10 with short dark hair.

The female suspect is described as white, early twenties, medium build, brown hair tied up in a bun and was believed to be wearing a long light-coloured cardigan.

It is believed that the suspects were picked up by a dark coloured 4x4 type vehicle shortly after the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the offence or may have information that will assist in the investigation is urged to get in contact.

If you believe you can assist this investigation you can call 101 or via the website www.westyorkshirepolice.co.uk/contactus quoting crime reference 13200521085. Information can also be reported anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555111 or online.
