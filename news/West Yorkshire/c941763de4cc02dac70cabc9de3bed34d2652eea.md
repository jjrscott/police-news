**Saturday, December 26, 2020**

Police are appealing for information following a road traffic collision in Bradford which caused extensive damage to a business, and where the occupants of the vehicle fled the scene.

At 3.02pm yesterday, police were called to an incident where a white Range Rover had collided with Wibsey Osteopathic Clinic, in Moore Avenue, Wibsey.

The occupants of the vehicle ran off from the scene. None were thought to be seriously injured.

The vehicle was recovered for forensic examination and enquiries are ongoing to trace those involved.

PC Dan Williamson, of West Yorkshire Police Roads Policing Unit, said: “This was a serious collision which is lucky not to have resulted in serious injury. However a business premises has been extensively damaged and investigations to identify the driver are ongoing. I would urge that person to take the opportunity to come forward now.”

Officers liaised with structural engineers to make the building safe.

Anyone with any information that could assist the investigation can contact officers via 101 quoting log 674 of December 25.
