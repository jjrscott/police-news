**Tuesday, January 5, 2021**

Detectives have released CCTV images of a man they want to identify after a bank card stolen in a burglary in Leeds was used in Shipley.

The card was taken when thieves broke into a house in Guiseley between December 8 and 9 and stole two cars parked outside after taking the keys and other items.

The man shown in the CCTV images used the card in a shop in Windhill on December 9.
