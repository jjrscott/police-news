Wednesday 17th March 2021

Coroners Officers are appealing for help in tracing the relatives of Ronald Alcock from Bingley.

Mr Alcock, aged 75 years old, passed away on 2nd March 2021.

Anyone with any information is encouraged to contact the Bradford Coroners Office on 01274 373721.
