**Tuesday 10 November 2020**

Police are issuing a CCTV still of a man they want to speak with in connection with attempted burglary in Huddersfield.

The Kirklees District Crime Team would like to speak with anyone who can assist their ongoing investigation into an attempted burglary on Hanson Lane, Lockwood on Tuesday October 20.

It took place between 10.50am and 5pm during which times suspects attempted to force their way into the property.

Anyone who recognises the man or who has any information about the offence is asked to contact PC Paul Hewitt of the Kirklees District Crime Team on 101 referencing crime number 13200534986 or contact the force online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent Crimestoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
