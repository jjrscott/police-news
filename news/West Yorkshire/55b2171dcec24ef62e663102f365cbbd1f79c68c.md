Tuesday 16th February 2021.

Coroner's officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of June Briggs.

Ms Briggs aged 86yrs, lived in the Morley area of Leeds and died on the 5th of February 2021. There are no suspicious circumstances to Ms Brigg's death.

It is believed that she was from Dewsbury originally.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Coroner's officer Chris Clarkson on 01924 292301.
