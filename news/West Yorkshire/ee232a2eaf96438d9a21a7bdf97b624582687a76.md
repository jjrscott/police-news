**Tuesday 13 October 2020**

Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Normanton in which a car was stolen.

The grey Volkswagen Golf was stolen from outside an address in Fairfield Avenue between 8.40pm and 9.10pm on Thursday, 8 October.

Suspects are believed to have taken the keys for the vehicle after gaining entry to a nearby house.

Anyone with information which may assist the investigation is asked to contact police in Wakefield on 101, or by using the options on the West Yorkshire Police website.

Information can also be reported anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

The crime reference is 13200505510.
