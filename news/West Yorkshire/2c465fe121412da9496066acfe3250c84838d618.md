Wednesday 10th February 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Paul Hamer from Huddersfield.

Mr Hamer, aged 64 years, passed away on the 31st January 2021.

Anyone with information is requested to contact Coroner's Officer Catherine Toner on 01274 373721 during the hours of 9am - 5pm, Monday - Friday.
