**Wednesday 27 January 2021**

Police are appealing for witnesses after a Mosque was burgled in Todmorden.

Suspects broke into the Mosque on Eagle Street sometime between 11pm on Friday 22 January and 12.30am Saturday 23 January.

Suspects made off with cash from the safe.

A black or dark coloured three door hatchback was seen in the area at the time of the offence. It was then seen travelling along Stansfield Road, White Platts Street and Joshua Street around that time.

Enquiries are ongoing.

> Inspector Ben Doughty of Calderdale Valley’s Neighbourhood Policing Team said:
>
> ‘We are carrying out enquiries into this burglary through CCTV and house to house.
>
> ‘I would appeal to anyone who saw anything suspicious on Eagle Street or a dark coloured vehicle on the night of Friday January 22 to get in touch and help us with the appeal.’

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police on 101 or use the live chat www.westyorkshire.police.uk/contactus quoting crime reference 13210036921
