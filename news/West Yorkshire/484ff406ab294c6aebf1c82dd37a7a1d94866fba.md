Monday 30th November 2020

Coroners Officers are appealing for help in tracing the relatives of Mr Ahmed Awan from Bradford.

Mr Ahmed, aged 82 years old and originally from Pakistan, died on 27th November 2020.

Anyone with any information should contact Coroners Officer Arran Arblaster on 01274 373721.
