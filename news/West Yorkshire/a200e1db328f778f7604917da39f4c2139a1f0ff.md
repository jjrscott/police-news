**Saturday 14 November, 2020**

Police are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision in Queensbury last night.

Officers were called at 18:30 on Friday 13 November to reports of a collision between a Renault van and a pedestrian on the Brighouse and Denholme Road junction with Mill Lane.

The van was travelling along the Brighouse and Denholme Road through Mountain in the direction of Keelham when the collision happened.

Emergency services attended the scene.

The 63-year-old female pedestrian suffered a serious injury to her foot and was taken to hospital for urgent medical treatment.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, saw the van prior to the collision or has any dashcam footage is asked to contact the police via 101 quoting log number 1488 of 13 November.
