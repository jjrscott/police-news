**Saturday 24 October 2020**

Police are issuing images of males they would like to speak to following an incident in which objects were thrown at cars in Castleford.

The Castleford Neighbourhood Policing Team would like to speak to anyone who recognises the men or has any information about the incident in the Square on Saturday September 19.

It took place at about 9.30pm on Saturday, 19 September, when missiles were thrown at passing traffic by males on the pedestrian precinct.

No damage is believed to have been caused, but a number of people reported the incident to police and a crime of public nuisance has been recorded for further investigation.

Anyone who recognises any of these males or has any information about the incident is asked to contact the Castleford Neighbourhood Policing Team on 101, quoting crime reference 13200505722.

Information can also be given online at www.westyorkshire.police.uk/101livechat or reported anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
