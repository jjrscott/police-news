Wednesday 17th February 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Malcolm Hales from Halifax.

Mr Hales, aged 79 years, passed away on the 7th February 2021.

Anyone with information is requested to contact Coroner's Officer Vicki Illingworth on 01274 373721 during the hours of 9am - 5pm, Monday - Friday.
