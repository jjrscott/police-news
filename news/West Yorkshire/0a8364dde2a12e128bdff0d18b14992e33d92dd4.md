**Monday 19 October, 2020**

Police are appealing for witnesses following reports of a sexual assault in Bingley.

The incident happened on Thursday 15 October at around 19:15 on Keighley Road, at the junction with Queens Road.

A 63-year-old woman was walking and as she got to the junction of Queens Road and Keighley Road, she was approached by a male suspect who was in a vehicle.

The male got out of the car and made sexual comments to the female victim and grabbed her, before the victim ran off.

The car he was in was a dark coloured 4x4 and inside the car were a large and a small dog on the passenger seat.

Anyone who was in the area at the time, witnessed anything suspicious or has any further information, is asked to contact the police via 101 quoting crime reference number 13200517493.

Information can also be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
