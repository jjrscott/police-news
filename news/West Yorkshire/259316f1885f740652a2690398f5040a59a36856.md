**Wednesday, 27 January 2021**

Police investigating a burglary in Leeds have released details of a man they would like to speak to.

Officers would like to speak to Donovan Young, 27, in connection with the offence which happened in Osmondthorpe in September 2019.

Extensive enquiries have been made to locate Donovan, who is described as being about 6ft 3ins tall and of slim build.

He is believed to have links to the Halton Moor area and possibly Harehills too.

Anyone who can assist police in locating Donovan is asked to contact police in South Leeds on 101 or by using the contact options on the West Yorkshire Police website.
