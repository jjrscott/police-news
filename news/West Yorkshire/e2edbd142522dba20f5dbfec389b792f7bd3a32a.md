Friday 26 February 2021

Police in Kirklees are appealing for information after reports of suspicious activity in Hade Edge.

Kirklees CID are appealing to anyone with information to try and identify a group of males seen in and around the Hade Edge area in the early hours of the morning on Wednesday (24 February).

Officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity in the area or that may have CCTV footage.

Anyone with any information or CCTV footage that may assist with the enquiry is asked to contact Kirklees CID on 101 referencing 13210094499 or email **[email protected].**

Information can also anonymously to the independent CrimeStoppers charity on 0800 555 111.
