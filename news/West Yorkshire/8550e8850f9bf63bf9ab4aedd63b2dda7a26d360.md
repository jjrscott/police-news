**Friday, 19 March 2021**

Police are urgently appealing for information on Jonathan Bowes, who has been reported missing from Bradford.

Jonathan is 27 and is described as being of slim build and around 5ft 8ins tall.

He was last seen wearing a black Nike tracksuit and trainers.

There are concerns for his welfare and anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101, or by using the options on the West Yorkshire Police website.

The log reference is 1401 of 18 March.
