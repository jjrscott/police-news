**Wednesday, 24 March 2021 - Update 18.35**

Police searching for a missing Pontefract woman have found a body.

Although formal identification hasn’t taken place the family of Beverley O’Connor have been made aware of the development and police are continuing to offer support to the family.

There are not thought to be any suspicious circumstances surrounding the death and a file will be prepared for the Coroner.
