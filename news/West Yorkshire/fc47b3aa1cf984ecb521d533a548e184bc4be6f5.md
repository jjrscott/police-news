**Sunday 30 August 2020**

Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious road traffic collision in Halifax last night (29/08).

At around 11.10pm, a blue Citroen C2 car was travelling on Turner Avenue North, Ovenden, in the direction of Raw Lane when it was in collision with a parked grey Peugeot 308.

The driver of the vehicle, a man in his 30s, sustained serious injuries in the collision and remains in hospital.

Officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision or saw the vehicle driving in the area prior to the collision to get in touch.

The Roads Policing Unit would also like to hear from anyone who has dashcam footage of the vehicle driving in the area prior to the collision or footage of the collision itself.

Anyone with information should use the 101 online chat service or call 101, quoting log 2079 of 29/08.
