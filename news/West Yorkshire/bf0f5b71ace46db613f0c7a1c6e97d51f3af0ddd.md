Monday 4th January 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Adrian Charlesworth from Bradford.

Mr Charlesworth, aged 53 years, passed away on the 29th December 2020.

Anyone with information is requested to contact the Coroner's Office on 01274 373721 during the hours of 8am - 4pm, Monday - Friday.
