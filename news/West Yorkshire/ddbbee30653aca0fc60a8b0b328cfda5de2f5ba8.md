**Friday 15 January, 2021**

Police in Bradford are appealing for information to trace Christopher Windle who is wanted in connection with offences in the District.

Christopher Windle, aged 20, from Halifax failed to attend a court hearing last month and also another court hearing in January, for offences including robbery, burglary, attempted burglary and assault of an emergency worker.

Bradford District Police have been conducting extensive enquiries to locate Windle.

Officers are urging members of the public to come forward with any information to assist with enquiries.
