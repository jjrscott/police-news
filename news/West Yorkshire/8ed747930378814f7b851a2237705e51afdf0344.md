Tuesday 9th February 2021.

Coroner's Officers are appealing for information to trace the relatives of Peter Walsh from Bradford.

Mr Walsh, aged 83 years, passed away on the 31st January 2021.

Anyone with information is requested to contact Coroner's Officer Jayne Dawson on 01274 373721 between the hours of 9am - 5pm, Monday - Friday.
