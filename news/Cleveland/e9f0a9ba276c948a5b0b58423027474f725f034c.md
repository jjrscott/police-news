The break-in occurred overnight from Friday 27th September into Saturday 28th at a property on Roseberry Road.

A child's scooter, golf clubs and a hedge trimmer was stolen. (Pictured: child's scooter)

Anyone with information, including nearby private CCTV, is asked to contact Cleveland Police via the non-emergency number 101, quoting the event number 167464. Crimestoppers can also be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at http://www.crimestoppers-uk.org/.
