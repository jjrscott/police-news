08:22 09/10/2019

The first incident occurred overnight between 6pm on Friday 21st September and 11:30am on Saturday 22ndSeptember. £200 cash, two MacBook Air laptops, one HP laptop, one pair of Valentino trainers, one pair of Alexander McQueen trainers, one pair of blue football boots and an iPhone was stolen.

The second incident occurred overnight between 4pm on Sunday 6th October and 7am on Monday 7th October. £140 cash, £400 worth of alcohol and three football shirts, including a signed Liverpool shirt, was stolen.

Anyone who witnessed the incidents or has dash-cam footage is asked to contact DC Andy Bennett via the non-emergency number 101 and quote the event number 163480 for the first incident and 172744 for the second incident. Crimestoppers can also be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at http://www.crimestoppers-uk.org/.
