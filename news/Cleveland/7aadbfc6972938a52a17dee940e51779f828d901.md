14:34 15/01/2020

Officers continued a second search on Briar Road in Thornaby today in connection with the suspected murder of Naheed Khan, 43.

The searches potentially could have led detectives to information regarding Naheed's whereabouts.

A tent was put in place to protect the searches, however it was determined that no evidence was found to assist the investigation further.

Detectives will be planning further searches early this year.

Naheed, 43, has been missing since early May bank holiday 2018 had links to Stockton, Middlesbrough and Thornaby. She is described as Asian, of slim build and around 5ft 6" tall, with long, dark hair.

Anyone with information is asked to call Cleveland Police on the non-emergency number 101.

Alternatively, people can visit https://mipp.police.uk/ and provide information direct to the investigation team.
