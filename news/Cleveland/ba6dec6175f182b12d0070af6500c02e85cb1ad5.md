07:52 01/10/2019

Officers have arrested two women aged 42 and 30 on suspicion of burglary following an incident on Woodland Street in Stockton.

A TV and a pram were amongst the items taken from the property.

The two women currently remain in custody.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident to contact Cleveland Police on 101 quoting 168710.

Independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
