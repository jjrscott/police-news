15:18 06/12/2019

Stockton Neighbourhood Officers conducted a drugs warrant at a property on Georgiana Close in Thornaby this afternoon.

Class B drugs, a Rolex watch, CS spray and hundreds of pounds worth of cash was seized from the property.

A Linkin Park CD case containing electronic scales (which officers believe were used to weigh drugs) was also seized.A man, 34, was arrested at the property and currently remains in custody.

Speaking of the warrant, Sergeant Ewan Barclay said: "This afternoon's drugs warrant was a success and the team are happy with the items seized from the address.

"We always act on intelligence provided to us from members of the public and we will do everything we can to reduce criminality and the misery caused by drugs activity in our communities."

Anyone reporting information regarding drugs activity in their local area can contact Cleveland Police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
