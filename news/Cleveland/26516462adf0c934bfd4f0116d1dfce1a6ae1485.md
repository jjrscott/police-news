15:47 09/04/2020

Officers have arrested five men and one woman on suspicion of murder in connection with the incident on Smith Street in Stockton on Friday 3rd April at 10:45pm.

A man aged 42 sadly died in hospital on Sunday 5th April as a result of serious injuries. Specialist officers are supporting the man's family during this difficult time.

Five men aged 20, 26, 28, 23 and 22 have been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

A woman aged 30 currently remains in police custody.

Police are continuing to appeal for anyone with information regarding the incident, or for any key workers or members of the public who were out for essential travel in the area and time of the incident to please come forward with information.

Detective Sergeant Lindsey Dale from Cleveland Police can be contacted on 101 quoting 051824.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at: www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
