11:31 21/01/2021

At around 7:15pm yesterday, Wednesday 20th January, Stockton Neighbourhood Officers sighted a vehicle reportedly driving at excess speed in Billingham.

Officers stopped and searched the vehicle and seized class A drugs and cash. The 29 year old male driver was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply class A drugs.

In connection with the arrest, officers then searched a property on Wollaton Road in Billingham where suspected class A drugs, drugs paraphernalia, an air weapon and three samurai swords were seized from the address.

A woman aged 29 was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of class A drugs.

Both the man and woman have been released pending further enquiries.
