10:14 10/07/2020

Officers from the Stockton Neighbourhood team executed a drugs warrant at a property on Stranton Street in Stockton yesterday afternoon, Thursday 9th July.

A quantity of class B drugs were recovered from the premises and a woman aged 42 was arrested on suspicion of drugs offences and released with a caution.

Officers also worked in partnership with the housing company in charge of this property following the warrant and the premises is now unoccupied and boarded up until further notice. ** **

This is part of Cleveland Police's Operation Phoenix; a summer long operation to improve public service, prioritise vulnerability, tackle serious violence and prevent crime.
