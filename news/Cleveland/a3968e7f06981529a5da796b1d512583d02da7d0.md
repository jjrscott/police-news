11:06 16/06/2020

Officers from the Stockton Neighbourhood team conducted a number of patrols in the Parkfield, Oxbridge and Newtown areas of Stockton yesterday, Monday 15th June.

The patrols were conducted after officers had received complaints from local communities regarding anti-social behaviour and drugs activity in these areas.

As a result of the patrols a man was reported for summons for reportedly being in the possession of class A drugs, a driver of a vehicle was reported for being in the possession of class B drugs and a small amount of class B drugs were recovered from a property.

Anyone who wishes to report drug activity in their local area can contact Cleveland Police on 101, or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or online at http://crimestoppers-uk.org.
