08:16 03/10/2019

Officers from Stockton CID have arrested a man, 43, on suspicion of theft from motor vehicles.

The arrests were made in connection with incidents on Mulberry Wynd and Ashmore Gardens in Stockton.

A satnav and money were amongst the items reported to be stolen.

The man currently remains in custody.

Police would ask anyone with information regarding the incidents to contact Cleveland Police on 101 quoting 167987 (Mulberry) or 168001(Ashmore).
