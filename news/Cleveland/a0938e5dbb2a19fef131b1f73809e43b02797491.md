11:09 21/08/2020

Police are appealing for information and private CCTV/ dash cam footage after an American Bulldog puppy was stolen from the yard of a property on Ridsdale Avenue in Roseworth, Stockton, yesterday, Thursday 20th August.

The incident occurred at around 7:30am when two men wearing balaclavas took the 10 month old puppy called Kaos from the kennel in the yard of the property.

The suspects made off in a blue MG vehicle (shown in the image) which was later abandoned in the Bowesfield Lane area of Stockton and officers seized it.

Officers are appealing for anyone who sighted the vehicle prior to or after the incident who may have information or private CCTV/ dashcam footage to contact police as a matter of urgency.

Anyone with information regarding the incident, those responsible or anyone who may have seen the puppy advertised for sale is asked to contact Cleveland Police on 101 quoting 135702.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at: www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
