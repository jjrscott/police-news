13:33 13/01/2020

Police are appealing for information following a suspected arson attack at a property on Moorcock Close, Middlesbrough.

A touring caravan was found alight at around 03:20am on Saturday, 4th January. The fire brigade attended and there were no injuries reported.

Officers are asking anyone who has information regarding the incident on nearby private CCTV to contact PC Richard Roughton on the non-emergency 101 number, quoting the event number 001838. Crimestoppers can also be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
