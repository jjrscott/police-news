10:46 16/09/2020

Stockton Neighbourhood Officers seized drugs, weapons, a quad bike, motorbike and a Mercedes vehicle yesterday from two properties in Stockton and Billingham.

Two drugs warrants were executed at properties on Durham Road in Stockton and Kirby Close in Billingham.

Class A and B drugs were recovered from the premises and a male aged 44 was arrested on suspicion of drugs offences and has been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

The Stockton neighbourhood team will continue to act on intelligence from local communities.

If you have any information about criminal activity in your area please contact us on 101, Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at crimestoppers-uk.org.
