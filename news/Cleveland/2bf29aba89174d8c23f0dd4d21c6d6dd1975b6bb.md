15:26 19/12/2019

Police are appealing for information after a man suffered injuries in a serious assault in Stockton.

The incident occurred at around 6pm on Monday 16th December on Lawrence Street, near to the junction of Yarm Lane in Stockton.

The man in his 40s was attacked by two unknown males with weapons and as a result suffered injuries. He received treatment at hospital and has since been discharged.

Officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident to contact Stockton CID on the non-emergency number 101, quoting the reference 213312. Crimestoppers can also be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
