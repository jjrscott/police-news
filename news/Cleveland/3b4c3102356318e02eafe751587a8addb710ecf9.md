12:27 18/12/2020

Officers from Stockton CID have charged a man aged 28 with assault, possession of a bladed article and harassment. He will appear at Teesside Magistrates Court today, Friday 18th December.

The reported charges were made following an alleged incident on Tilery Way in Stockton on Monday 14th December.

A man was taken to hospital with lacerations to his face as a result of the reported incident and he was later discharged.
