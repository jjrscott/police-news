15:13 12/08/2020

Officers from the Middlesbrough Neighbourhood team attended the Sports Village in Middlesbrough yesterday evening, Tuesday 11th August after receiving reports of anti-social behaviour in the area.

On police arrival, two off road bikes which were believed to be involved in ASB were seized as shown in the photograph.
