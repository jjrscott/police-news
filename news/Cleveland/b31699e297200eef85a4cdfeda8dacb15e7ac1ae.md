14:46 20/12/2019

Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision that occurred on the Coast Road (A1085) towards Redcar, by the junction of Hawthorn Road. The collision occurred at around 2:50pm on Wednesday, 18th December.

A white Hyundai 10SE, white Suzuki Swift and a stationary grey Chevrolet Aveo were involved in the collision.

The 67-year-old female driver of the Hyundai was taken to James Cook University Hospital and received treatment for head injuries and has since been released.

The 73-year-old driver of the Chevrolet sustained minor injuries.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or anyone with dash-cam footage or private residential CCTV is asked to contact PC Jason Malcolm via the non-emergency number 101, quoting the event number 214217.
