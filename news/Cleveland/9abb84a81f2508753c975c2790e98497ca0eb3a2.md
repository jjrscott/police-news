Police are releasing CCTV images of two males they would like to speak to in connection with a burglary at KFC on the Lanyard in Hartlepool.

The incident occurred on Sunday 20th October between 3:45am and 4:30am.

Thousands of pounds worth of cash and a charity box containing around Â£200 was taken.

Please note that the timing shown on the CCTV images is not accurate.

Anyone with information regarding the males or anyone with dash cam/ CCTV footage from the Church Street area of Hartlepool between 03:30am and 04:30am on Sunday 20th October is asked to contact PC Leon Webster from Cleveland Police on 101, quoting event number 180726.
