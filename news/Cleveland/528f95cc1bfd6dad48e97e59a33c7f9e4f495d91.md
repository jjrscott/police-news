09:11 04/11/2019

Police are appealing for witnesses to a collision which occurred in Middlesbrough on Saturday 2nd November at around 10:30pm.

The collision involved a silver Honda Accord and a pedestrian on Linthorpe Road.

The 36-year-old pedestrian was taken to James Cook University Hospital and received treatment for leg, shoulder and head injuries.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or has any dash cam footage is asked to contact PC Matthew Holman on the non-emergency number 101, quoting the reference number 189135.
