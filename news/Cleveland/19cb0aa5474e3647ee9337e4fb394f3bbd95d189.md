08:21 07/10/2019

Police have arrested a further two people in connection with an incident on Yarm Road in Stockton on Friday 27th September.

A woman died following an incident at around 11pm when it is believed the woman spoke to the occupants of a vehicle.

Four men aged 17, 18, 18 and 24 were arrested in connection with the incident and all four have now been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

The woman's death is currently being treated as unexplained.

Officers are appealing for any taxi drivers or vehicles with dashcam footage to contact them on 101 quoting 167119.

Independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
