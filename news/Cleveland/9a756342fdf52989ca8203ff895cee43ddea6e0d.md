11:30 07/07/2020

Yesterday afternoon, Monday 6th July, officers from the Stockton Neighbourhood team stopped a silver Golf vehicle on Ware Street in Stockton.

The 29 year old male driver was arrested on suspicion of drugs and driving offences and the vehicle was seized.

The man has been released under investigation whilst enquiries continue.
