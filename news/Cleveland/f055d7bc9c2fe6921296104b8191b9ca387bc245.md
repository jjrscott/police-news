10:51 27/12/2019

Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision that occurred on Owton Manor Lane, Hartlepool at around 4:45am on Wednesday 25th December.

A grey Nissan Juke and a pedestrian were involved in the collision.

The 18-year-old pedestrian was taken to James Cook University Hospital where he currently remains receiving treatment for a broken leg.

Officers are also investigating whether the pedestrian was in collision with a second vehicle.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or anyone who may have dash cam footage or private CCTV of the incident is asked to contact PC Martin Tranmer via the non-emergency number 101, quoting the event number 217822.
