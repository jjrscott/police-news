Yesterday, Thursday 19th June, officers from the Stockton Neighbourhood team executed a drugs warrant at a property on Marsh House Avenue in Billingham.

Cannabis and other items believed to be used for drugs activity were recovered from the premises (as shown in the image below).

A man aged 38 was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply class B drugs, malicious communications and other offences and he has been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Do you have information about drugs activity in your area? If so, call us on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, or online at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
