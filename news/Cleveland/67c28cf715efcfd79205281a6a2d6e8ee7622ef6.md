14:41 21/01/2020

Police are appealing for a member of the public to assist in their investigation of a burglary in Hartlepool.

Officers were called to a report of a burglary at the Pools Surplus Store on Park Road at around 1:45am on Saturday 21st December. It is believed the suspect left the scene prior to police arrival.

An unknown member of the public located the damaged till from the burglary and handed it into the store. Officers are appealing for this person to please come forward as they may be able to assist officers with enquiries.

If you handed the damaged till to the store, or know the person who did, please contact PC Lorene Haworth on the non-emergency number 101, quoting the reference 215699.
