10:28 21/01/2020

Police received a report of a burglary on Wheatear Lane in Ingleby Barwick at 3am this morning.

It was reported that two people wearing balaclavas entered a property and stole an air weapon and knives.

Response officers, the Dog Unit, Road Policing officers and colleagues from NPAS attended the scene and shortly after, PD Elsa located two males reportedly hiding in the bushes near to Tesco.

The males, both aged 17, were arrested on suspicion of burglary dwelling and theft and currently remain in custody.

The air weapon and knives were recovered.

Anyone with information regarding the incident or anyone with private CCTV footage from the area of the incident is asked to contact Cleveland Police on 101 quoting 011146.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at: www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
