08:28 21/10/2019

The collision involved a Vauxhall Agila and a Fiat Punto on the A174 High Street Eastbound.

A 27-year-old passenger of the Vauxhall Agila was taken to James Cook University Hospital to receive treatment for leg injuries.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or has dash-cam footage is asked to contact PC Stewart Shepherd via the non-emergency number 101, quoting the event number 176744. Crimestoppers can also be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at http://www.crimestoppers-uk.org/.
