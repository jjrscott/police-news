10:58 27/01/2021

A 19-year-old man has been arrested after a report of a break in at the Pig Iron Public House on Corporation Road, Middlesbrough, in the early hours of this morning (Wednesday 27th January).

Officers received the report around 5:25am and attended the scene, where a 19-year-old man was located and arrested.

The man remains in police custody at this time.

Any witnesses are asked to contact Middlesbrough CID on 101, quoting event number 013782.
