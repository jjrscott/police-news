Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Stockport.

Darrell Spear, aged 64, died on Windermere Road, Stockport, on Tuesday 22 September 2020.

It is believed that Darrell may have family living in Somerset.

Anyone with information about Darrell's family are asked to call the police coroner's office in Stockport on 0161 856 7978.
