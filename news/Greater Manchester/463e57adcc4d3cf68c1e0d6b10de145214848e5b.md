Andrejus Bukatas (02/10/89) of no fixed address and Marekas Garlauskas (08/06/1984) of no fixed address have been charged with section 18 assault.

They have been remanded to appear before Manchester Magistrates Court on Saturday 16 November 2019.

Shortly before 1 am on Sunday 10 November 2019, police were called to reports that a 47-year-old man had been found unconscious on Whitworth Street in the City Centre.
