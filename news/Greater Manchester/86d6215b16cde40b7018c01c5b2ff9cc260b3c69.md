Appeal

Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Oldham.

Eric Jones, 71, passed away at his home address on Nursery Road, Failsworth on Thursday 10 September 2020.

It is believed Eric may have had a brother who lives in the Oldham area.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Eric's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Oldham on 0161 856 8095.
