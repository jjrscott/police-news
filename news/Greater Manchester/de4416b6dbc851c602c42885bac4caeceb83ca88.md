Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a woman from Ancoats in Manchester.

Susan McDonagh, 58, passed away at her home at Woodward Court in Manchester on Wednesday 15 April 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

If anyone has any information about Susan's next of kin they should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 2334.
