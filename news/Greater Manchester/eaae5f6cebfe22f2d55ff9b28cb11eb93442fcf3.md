Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Wigan.

John Kilmartin, aged 93, sadly passed away at Ancliffe Residential Care Home in Wigan on Wednesday 20 May 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with any information should contact the Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 4687.
