Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a woman from Oldham.

Elsie Scholes (12/06/27) passed away on Tuesday 22 October 2019 at Royal Oldham Hospital and resided on Sportsmans Drive, Oldham.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone with information about Elsie's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Heywood on 0161 856 8565.
