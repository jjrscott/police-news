Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the family of a man from the City Centre of Manchester.

Thomas Corlett, aged 72, sadly passed away at his home address on Booth Street West in the Chorlton-on-Medlock area of the city on Thursday 26 March 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Thomas' next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 3897.
