Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Newton Heath.

John Boyle, aged 80, who was originally from Scotland passed away at his home address on Droylsden Road, Newton Heath on Thursday 9 January 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about John's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 6069.
