A man has been charged with driving, drug and firearms offences.

Ilyas Abudaber (19/3/1999), of no fixed abode, has been charged with the

following:

 * Dangerous driving
 * Driving without insurance
 * Driving otherwise than in accordance with driving license
 * Handling stolen goods
 * Possession of firearm with barrel less than 30cm
 * Possession of ammunition without Firearms certificate
 * Two counts of possession with intent to supply class A drugs
 * Possession with intent to supply class B drugs
 * Possession of criminal property

He will appear at Manchester Magistrates Court today (Tuesday 15 October 2019).

These charges follow officers engaging in a pursuit with an Audi A3 in Moss Side on Sunday 13 October 2019.
