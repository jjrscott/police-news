Amir Maqbool (12/07/91) of HMP Manchester has been charged with two counts of possession of a mobile phone device in prison and will appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court on Tuesday 12 November 2019.

On 28th June 2019 police were made aware that prison officers had confiscated a phone.
