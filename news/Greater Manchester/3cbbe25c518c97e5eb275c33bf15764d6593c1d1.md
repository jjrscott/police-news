Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Bolton.

Melvyn Harrison, 70, passed away at the Royal Bolton Hospital on Sunday 31 May 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Melvyn is believed to have family who reside in Manchester.

Anyone with information about Melvyn's next of kin should contact the Bolton Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 4687.
