A man has been charged with carrying a knife in Rusholme.

Abdul Kalam (02/03/71) of Bates Street, Longsight, Manchester has been charged with the possession of a bladed article and will appear at Manchester Magistrates Court On Tuesday 30 June 2020.

Shortly after 7am on Monday 18 May 2020 police were called to report of a man armed with a large knife on Wilmslow Road.

The man was disarmed by police and no one was injured.
