Charged

A man has been charged following a warrant in Middleton.

Ricci Ferrari (21/10/1984) of Alcester Close, Middleton has been charged with two counts of conspiracy to supply class A drugs and two counts of money laundering.

He has been remanded in custody before an appearance at Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court today (Friday 22 January 2021).

At around 5am on Thursday 21 January 2021, officers executing a warrant at an address on Alcester Close arrested one man and seized a car, cash and designer clothing.
