At around 5pm on Monday 11 January 2021, police received a report of an earlier burglary at a business on Garden Street, Ramsbottom.

An investigation was launched and officers established that, at around 11.50am on Saturday 9 January 2021, two men entered the property and stole a quantity of cash.

PC David Green, of GMP's Bury district, said: "I would like to appeal to these men or anyone who can help us identify them to contact us.

"I would also like to use this opportunity to advise businesses to be vigilant and monitor entrants to their properties, even during opening hours."

Anyone with information should contact police on 0161 856 8204 quoting 1976 11/01/21 or the independent charity - Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
