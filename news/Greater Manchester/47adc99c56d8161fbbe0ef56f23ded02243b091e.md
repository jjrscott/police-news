Ricardas Jonusas (03/04/88) of Balby Road, Doncaster has been charged with murder.

He has been remanded to appear before Bolton Magistrates Court on Monday 16 December 2019.

At around 11.35pm on Friday 13 December 2019, police were called to reports that a man had been stabbed at an address on Mornington Road, Bolton.

Emergency services attended and the man was treated at the scene by paramedics, but despite their best efforts, the man sadly died a short time later as a result of his injuries.

The man has since been identified as 35-year-old Laurimas Ruzanov.
