At around 7.15pm on Sunday 26 July 2020, police were called to reports of a disturbance on Henbury Street, Manchester. Emergency services attended and a 17-year-old boy was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

He has since been formally identified as Mohamoud Mohamed.

A 17-year old boy has been arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to murder and remains in custody for questioning.

Four teenage boys - three aged 17 and one aged 19 - have been charged in relation to this murder.

Detectives are continuing to appeal to anyone with any information to get in touch as soon as possible.

Anyone with information should contact police on 0161 856 9283 or the independent charity - Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.

Information can also be shared via our Major Incident Public Portal: https://mipp.police.uk/operation/06GMP20A38-PO1
