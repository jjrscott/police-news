Did you witness a robbery in Radcliffe?

Officers are appealing for information after a robbery at a house on Gate Lane on Friday 16 August 2019.

At around 10.50pm three masked men forced their way into the property with crow bars and removed jewellery from the occupants, before searching the address and stealing more jewellery.

An iPad and iPhone were also stolen from the property.

The offenders fled the scene in a small dark coloured car.

Anyone who has any information about the robbery should call police on 0161 856 8223 quoting incident number 3108 of 16/08/2019.

Details can also be passed on anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
