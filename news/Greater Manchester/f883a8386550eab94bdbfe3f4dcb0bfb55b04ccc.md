Appeal

Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the family of a man from the Old Trafford area of Manchester.

Riaz Rio, 83, passed away at his home on Clayton Close, Old Trafford on Saturday 26 December 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed he may have family living or possibly working in the South Manchester area.

Anyone with information about Riaz Rio's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Stockport on 0161 856 9696.
