Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Lowton in Wigan.

David James, 54, passed away at his home address on Sandy Lane, Lowton on Saturday 25 January 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

If anyone has any information about David's next of kin they should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 4687.
