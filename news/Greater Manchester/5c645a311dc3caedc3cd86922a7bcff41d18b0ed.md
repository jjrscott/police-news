Shortly before 4pm on Tuesday 1 October 2019, police were called to reports of a collision on Ashton Old Road, Manchester involving a man and a HGV.

The pedestrian was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

A number of road closures are in place as enquires are being carried out and commuters are advised to avoid the area where possible.

No arrests have been made in connection with the incident.

Anyone with information about the collision should contact GMP's Serious Collision Investigation Unit on 0161 856 4741, quoting incident number 1900 of 01/10/2019. Alternatively information can be passed on via the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
