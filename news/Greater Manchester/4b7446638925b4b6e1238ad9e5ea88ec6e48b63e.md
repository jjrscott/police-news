Stuart Watson (15/04/1981) of Kingsway Park, Davyhulme has been charged with robbery and bailed to appear before Manchester Magistrates' Court on Thursday 7 November 2019.

Jack Yarwood (20/12/1985) of Water Street, Salford has been charged with aggravated burglary and handling stolen goods. He has been remanded to appear before Manchester Minshull Street Crown Court on Tuesday 17 December 2019.

A third man, aged 32, was arrested on suspicion of aggravated burglary and released under investigation.

At around 8.50 pm on Monday 16 September 2019, police were called to reports of break in at a property on Dunham Road, Warburton.

The offenders smashed through a glass patio door with machetes and a crowbar and threatened the victim and his family. The offenders then attacked the victim before taking a quantity of jewellery and cash. They then fled the scene in victim's Mercedes AMG.
