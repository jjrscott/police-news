Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man who lived in the Berwick area of Manchester.

Mike (also known as Patrick) Bitumazala, aged 52, sadly passed away at his home on Manila Walk on Monday 19 October 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

He is believed to have been born in Kinshasa in the Democratic Republic of Congo and may have a son who resides in the UK.

Anyone with information about Mr Bitumazala's immediate family should contact the police coroner's office in Manchester on 0161 856 6069.
