Mohammed Altaf, 58, who was often known as Alex, passed away at his home address on Woodvale in Middleton on Tuesday 1 December 2020.

He is believed to have previously lived in the Cheetham Hill area and also the Prestwich area of Manchester.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Mohammed's next of kin should contact the Police

Coroner's Office in Rochdale on 0161 856 5996.
