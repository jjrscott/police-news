David Brown (18/12/1993) of Argyle Street, Hindley has been charged with one count of criminal damage and one count of possession of Class-B drugs.

He will appear before Wigan and Leigh Magistrates Court on Monday 13 July 2020.

At around 9am on Saturday 18 April 2020, police were called to reports of a disturbance on Arundel Street, Hindley.

Police attended and enquiries have established that a disagreement occurred between the occupants of a property and the occupants of a car.
