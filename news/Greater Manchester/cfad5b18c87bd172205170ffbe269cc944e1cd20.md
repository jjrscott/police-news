Darren Roache (07/03/86), of no fixed abode, has been charged with sexual assault and remanded in custody.

He is scheduled to appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court on Monday 9 November 2020.

At around 10.35pm on Sunday 25 October 2020, police received a report that a woman had been sexually assaulted in an alleyway off Grimshaw Lane, Middleton.
