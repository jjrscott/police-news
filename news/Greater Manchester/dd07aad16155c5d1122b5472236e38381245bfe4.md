At around 4.40am this morning (27 August 2019) police were called to reports of a collision between a Toyota Prius and a pedestrian on Deansgate, in Manchester City Centre.

The driver left the scene following the collision.

The pedestrian - a man who is believed to be in his 20s - has been taken to hospital with potentially life changing injuries.

Shortly before 7.20am, a man was arrested on suspicion of serious injury by dangerous driving, dangerous driving and failing to stop after a collision.

There are road closures in place on Deansgate which are expected to remain in place for several hours. Commuters are advised to avoid the area where possible.
