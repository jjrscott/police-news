A man has been charged with a number of offences including armed robbery in Oldham.

Cameron Bland (13/03/1992) of no fixed address has been charged with the following offences -

 * Two counts of criminal damage
 * Two counts of assault
 * Four counts of possession of a bladed article
 * Assault on a police officer
 * Two counts of public order offences
 * Six counts of theft
 * Five counts of robbery

He appeared at Manchester and Salford Magistrates on Monday 4 November 2019 where he was remanded.

He will reappear at Minshull Street Crown Court on Monday 2 December 2019.

The charges follow an investigation by Oldham's Operation Gladiator team into a knife point robbery at McDonalds on Lucas Street on Tuesday 29 October 2019 and another knife point robbery at Co-op, Grotton on Thursday 31 October 2019.
