Leroy Panton (05/07/1980) of Westminster Street, Levenshulme has been charged with the murder of 67-year old Hyacinth Morris and will appear before Manchester City Magistrates Court today (Tuesday 1 September 2020).

At around 8:30pm on Thursday 7 May 2020, police were called to reports of a concern for welfare at an address on Westminster Street, Manchester.

Emergency services attended and Hyacinth was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.
