69-year-old John Wilson was found deceased on Monday 21 October at his home address on Edale Close, Bowden.

He is known to have previously lived in Morecambe and London for several years.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about John's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Stockport on 0161 856 9677.
