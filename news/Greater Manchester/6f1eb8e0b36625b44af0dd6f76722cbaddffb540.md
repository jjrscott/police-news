A man has been charged in connection with the death of a woman in Oldham.

Daniel Smith (27/10/1979) of Park Road, Oldham has been charged with murder.

He has been remanded into custody ahead of an appearance at Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court today (Monday 8 March 2021).

Officers were called around 12.15pm on Thursday 4 March 2021 to a report of concern for the welfare of a woman at an address on Ashton Road, Oldham.

Emergency services attended and a woman was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

The incident has been referred to the IOPC as per standard procedure following previous police contact.
