Stephen Coleman, 67, passed away at Salford Royal Hospital on Tuesday 14 April 2020. There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed that Stephen has two brothers, James Coleman and Anthony Coleman, who live in the Manchester area.

If anyone has any information about Stephen's next of kin they should contact the Bolton Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 4687.
