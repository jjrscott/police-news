Police are appealing for the public's help to trace family of man from Bury.

Steven Jones, 51, passed away at an address on Walmersley Road, Bury on Thursday 21 May 2020.

His home address was on Moorside Avenue in Ainsworth.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Steven Jones next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Bury on 0161 856 8565
