Two men have been charged with the murder of a man in Wythenshawe.

Joseph Stott (23/11/1986) of Hampshire Road, Partington has been charged with murder.

Aiden Matthews (31/08/1989) of Lawton Moor Road, Wythenshawe has been charged with murder and possession of an offensive weapon.

They appeared at Manchester Magistrates' Court this morning (Monday 8 June 2020) and have been remanded into custody where they're awaiting their next court date.

At around 6pm on Thursday 4 June 2020, police were called to Wythenshawe Road in Wythenshawe to reports that a man had been seriously assaulted.

23-year-old David Allan was taken to hospital, where he sadly died the following day.
