Police are appealing for the public's help to trace family of a man from Denton.

Wilfrid Moxon, 92, sadly passed away at his home on Ann Street in Denton and was found on Friday 17 April 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed Wilfrid possibly has cousins living in the Lincolnshire area.

Anyone with information about Wilfrid Moxon's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Stockport on 0161 856 9677.
