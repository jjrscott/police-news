Appeal 03

Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the family of man from the Oldham area.

Raymond Wright, aged 87, passed away at home on Stoneleigh Street, Oldham on 13 March 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Raymond's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Heywood on 0161 856 7307.
