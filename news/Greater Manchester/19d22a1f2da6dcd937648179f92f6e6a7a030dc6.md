Oliver Wolstenholme (25/01/1985) of Ashurst Road, Wythenshawe has been charged with child grooming and:-

One count of making an indecent image of a child relating to 368 category A images.

One count of making an indecent image of a child relating to 480 category B images.

One count of making an indecent image of a child relating to 656 category C images.

One count of possessing extreme pornographic images relating to 26 images.

He has been remanded to appear at Manchester Magistrates Court on Thursday 19th March 2020.
