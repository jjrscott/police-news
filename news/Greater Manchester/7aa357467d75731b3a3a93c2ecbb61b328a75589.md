A man has been charged with sexual assault in Manchester city centre.

Thomas Sabato (06/06/1988) of no fixed address was charged with sexually assaulting a 23-year-old woman on Monday 4 November 2019.

Sabato has been remanded in custody and will reappear at Manchester Crown Court on Monday 16 March 2020.

Shortly after 4.40am on Sunday 5 October 2019, police were called to Bugle Street, Deansgate to a report of a sexual assault on a 23-year-old woman.
