A 15-year-old boy from Longsight who cannot be named for legal reasons and a 16-year-old boy from Longsight - who also cannot be named for legal reasons - have been charged with violent disorder.

They will both appear at Salford and Manchester Magistrates Court today (Wednesday 27 May 2020).

At around 6:15pm on Monday 25 May 2020, police were called to reports of a disturbance on Meldon Road, Manchester.

Enquiries have established that there was a fight between a group of people.
