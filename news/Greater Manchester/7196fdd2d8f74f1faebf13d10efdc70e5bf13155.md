Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a woman who lived in Longsight.

Joan Brawley, aged 73, passed away at her home on Bickerdike Court in Longsight on Tuesday 17 December 2019.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone with information about Joan's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 6816.
