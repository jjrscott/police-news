A man has been charged following a series of burglaries in the Hulme area of Manchester.

Dean Lawrence Pottinger (11/04/1980) of Hamilton Road, Manchester has been charged with four counts of burglary, one count of aggravated burglary and two counts of assault.

He has been remanded to appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court on Monday 9 September 2019.

In August 2019, police responded to a string of burglaries targeting businesses in the Hulme area, including one where property was stolen from a fast food restaurant.
