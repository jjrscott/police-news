Marley Stevens (26/05/1992) of Smith Grove, Ashton-under-Lyne has been charged with conspiracy to commit robbery.

Jamal Miah (01/04/1997) of Richmond Street, Ashton-under-Lyne has been charged with perverting the course of justice.

Both men have been remanded in custody and will appear today (Tuesday 12 May 2020) before Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court.

These charges are in relation to six reported robberies taking place at convenience stores since the lockdown restrictions were implemented on Monday 23 March 2020.
