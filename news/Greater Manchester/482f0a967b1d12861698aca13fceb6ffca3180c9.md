Michael Burke 06/04/81 of Winchester Road, Stretford has been charged with conspiracy to commit robbery.

Daniel Adamson 21/01/1986 of Hurst Avenue, Sale has been charged with conspiracy to commit robbery.

Robert Lewis 22/03/74 of Keswick Road, Stretford has been charged with conspiracy to commit robbery.

Connor Kedie 30/04/98 of Rookfield Avenue, Sale has been charged with conspiracy to commit robbery.

Christopher Burke 08/04/85 of Greenwood Close, Timperley has been charged with conspiracy to commit robbery.

They have been remanded in custody to appear today (Friday 22 November 2019) before Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court

The charges relate to 29 armed robberies across Greater Manchester and Cheshire between May 2019 and November 2019.
