A 17-year-old boy, who cannot be named for legal reasons, has been charged with the below offences:

\- Two counts of common assault
\- Possession of a bladed article
\- Possession of an imitation firearm with intent to cause fear or violence
\- Section 47 Actual Bodily Harm
\- Intentional harassment, alarm or distress.

He will appear before Wigan and Leigh Youth Court on Thursday 27 August 2020

Between Friday 3 July 2020 and Friday 14 August 2020, police were called to four separate incidents.
