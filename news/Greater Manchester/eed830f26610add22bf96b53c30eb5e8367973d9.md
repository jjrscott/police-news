A woman has been charged after a man was stabbed and two police officers were assaulted in Leigh.

Drew Pattinson (08/05/97) of no fixed abode has been charged with section 18 assault and two counts of assault on emergency workers section 1.

She will appear at Bolton Crown Court on Monday 2 December 2019.

At around 6.15pm on Friday 1 November 2019 officers were called to reports of a stabbing on Charles Street, Leigh.

While arresting a woman at the scene, two responding officers were assaulted by the woman.
