Jason Bursk (12/12/68) of Overton Close, Radcliffe has been charged with two counts of attempted rape and one count of sexual assault in relation to an incident that occurred on Albion Street in the early hours of Friday 15 November 2019.

He has also been charged with two counts of trespass with intent to commit a sexual offence and one count of sexual assault for three incidents that took place between January 2012 and July 2019.

He will appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court today (Thursday 21 November 2019).

Shortly before 4.30am on Friday 15 November 2019 police were called to an address on Albion Street, Radcliffe to reports that an intruder had sexually assaulted a young girl.
