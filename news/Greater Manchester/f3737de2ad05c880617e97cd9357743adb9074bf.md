Can you help trace the family of a man from Bury?
Police are appealing for the public's help to trace family of a man who lived in Ramsbottom and is believed to have links to London.
Mikal Yaniv (formally Michael Lubell) , 77 , passed away at his home on Great Eaves Road in Ramsbottom on the Saturday 11 April 2020.
There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.
Anyone with information should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Heywood on 0161 856 9096.
