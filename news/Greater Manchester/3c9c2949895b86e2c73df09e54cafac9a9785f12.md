Police are appealing for the public's help to trace family of man from Heywood, Rochdale.

Joseph Lee Healey, 89, passed away at an address on Bury Old Road, Heywood on Saturday 21 November 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Joseph's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Rochdale on 0161 856 8497.
