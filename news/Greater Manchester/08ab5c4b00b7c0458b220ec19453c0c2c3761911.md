Officers enforcing parking regulations in the Hollingworth Lake area

Neighbourhood officers have been assisting the council's civil enforcement officers around the Hollingworth Lake and Rakewood Road car parks and residents' parking zones.

 They have engaged with the community to provide reassurance, and advice to visitors of the lake.

The two officers have stopped 173 vehicles down Rakewood Road, 120 of these vehicles were turned around from restricted parking areas.

Please be considerate of local residents when planning your trip to the lake.
