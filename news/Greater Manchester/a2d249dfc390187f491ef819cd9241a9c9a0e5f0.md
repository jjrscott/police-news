Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man who lived in Stockport.

Jack Preston, 74, sadly passed away at his home on Cresgarth House, The Crescent in Davenport on Friday 6 March 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Jack's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 9677.
