Charged

A man has been charged in connection with a firearm incident in Manchester.

Peter Gallagher (04/04/1977) of Langport Avenue, Longsight has been charged with two counts of possession of an imitation firearm with intent to cause fear, one count of driving whilst disqualified, one count of driving without insurance and two counts of malicious communications.

He has been remanded in custody before an appearance at Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court today (Friday 29 January 2021).

Officers were called around 7.25pm on Wednesday 27 January 2021 to Portland Crescent, Manchester to a report of a suspected firearm discharge and a man making threats.
