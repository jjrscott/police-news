Police are appealing for the public's help to trace a wanted man from Clayton.

Killien Walker (26/01/1998) is wanted in connection with an assault and a breach of a restraining order that occurred in Openshaw on Wednesday 18 December 2019.

Walker is known to have links to Clayton, Droylsden, New Moston and Failsworth in Greater Manchester.

Anybody with information regarding the whereabouts of Walker should contact police on 0161 856 5883 or the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
