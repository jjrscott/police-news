Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Cheetham Hill.

Vincent Shortt, 73, passed away at his home on Elvate Crescent, Manchester on Monday 20 April 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

If anyone has any information about Vincent's next of kin they should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 2334.
