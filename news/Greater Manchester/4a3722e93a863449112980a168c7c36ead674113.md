A man, who police believe to be Walter Moskal, 63, passed away at his home address on Elmwood Grove in Bolton on Monday 25 May 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed that Walter is possibly of Russian descent.

Anyone with information about Walter's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Bolton on 0161 856 5746.
