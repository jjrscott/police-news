A man has been charged following a burglary in Manchester.

Phillip Coudyoe (30/09/62) of Woodnewton Close, Manchester, has been charged with burglary.

He appeared before Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court on Monday 27 April 2020.

He has been remanded in custody and is awaiting a court date for his next appearance.

On Saturday 5 January 2019 police were called to a report of a burglary at a property on Acheson Street in Manchester.

A suspect entered the property and took several items before they fled the scene.
