Itshaan Ali (20/10/02) of Church Lane, Moston has been charged with possession of an offence weapon.

He has been remanded in custody and will appear before Manchester Magistrates Court on Monday 22 March 2021.

Police from GMP's City of Manchester division are investigating a number of incidents which are believed to be linked.

On Saturday 20 March 2021, police were called to reports of a man with a knife on Denison Road.
