Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the family of a man from the Hollinwood area of Oldham.

John Sheridan, aged 73, passed away at his home address on Chamber Road, Hollinwood on Sunday 22 December 2019.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about John Sheridan's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Heywood on 0161 856 8565.
