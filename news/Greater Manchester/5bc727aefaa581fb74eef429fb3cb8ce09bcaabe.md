A man has been charged after a series of indecent exposures in Trafford.

Takunda Bere (19/10/97), of Ascot Avenue, Sale in Trafford, has been charged in relation to 14 indecency offences and four driving offences.

He has been remanded and will appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court today (Tuesday 7 July 2020).

Between 22 June 2020 and 28 June 2020 a number of indecent exposures were reported to Greater Manchester Police in the Trafford area of Greater Manchester.

On 28 June 2020 a man allegedly drove a car dangerously, without a licence, without insurance and failed to stop.
