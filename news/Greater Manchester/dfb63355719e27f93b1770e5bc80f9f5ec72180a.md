Charged 10

A woman has been charged in relation to the death of a man in Rusholme.

Katie McMullan (19/08/92) of Carnforth Street, Rusholme, Manchester has been charged with assisting an offender.

She has been bailed and will appear at Manchester Magistrates on 9th September 2020.

At around 1.15am on Tuesday 14 July 2020, police were called to a report of a disturbance at a property on Carnforth Street, Rusholme.

Officers arrived and found the body of Leroy Hanley, aged 59, within the property.
