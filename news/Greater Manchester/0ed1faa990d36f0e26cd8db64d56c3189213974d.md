Police are appealing for the public's help to trace family of a man from Oldham.

Jeffery Walker, aged 82, died at Tree Tops Nursing Home, Oldham on Saturday 31 August 2019.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Jeffery's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Oldham on 0161 856 8095.
