Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of a kin of a man from Moston.

Stephen Modrzynski, 70, passed away at his home address on Teddington Road on Thursday 23 July 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Stephen's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 6816.
