At around 6.30pm last night (Tuesday 17 December 2019) officers were called to reports of a black VW Golf colliding with a 27-year-old man who was crossing the road on Stockport Road - close to the junction of Cromwell Grove.

The man was attended to by emergency services and remains in hospital with life-threatening injuries.

A 26-year-old man was arrested at the scene on suspicion of causing serious injury by dangerous driving and has since been released from police custody under investigation.

Anyone with any information regarding this incident or has dashcam footage should contact police on 0161 856 4742 quoting incident number 2454 of 17/12/2019 or contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
