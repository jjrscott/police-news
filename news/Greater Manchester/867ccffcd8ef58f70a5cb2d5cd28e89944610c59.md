Charged

Three men have been charged in connection with a firearms discharge in Bolton.

Sabir Abdulkadir (01/02/2000) of Rawcliffe Street, Manchester has been charged with attempted murder. He has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Manchester & Salford Magistrates' Court today.

John Lee Robinson (24/02/1985) of no fixed abode, has been charged with affray and remanded in custody before appearing at Bolton Crown on Wednesday 9 December 2020.

Abdul Said (01/01/2000), of Langdon Close, Bolton has been charged with possession of class A with intent to supply. He has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Bolton Crown Court on Friday 11th December 2020.

Around 01.20am on Saturday 7 November 2020 officers were called to a report a firearm discharge on Lindfield Drive, Bolton.
