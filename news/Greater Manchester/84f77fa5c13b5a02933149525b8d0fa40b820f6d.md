Christopher Pearson, aged 57, passed away on Wednesday 22 January 2020 at Church Walk Nursing home, Cavendish Road in Rochdale.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed that Christopher was originally from the Bradford area.

Anyone with information about Christopher's next of kin should contact the police coroner's office in Heywood on 0161 856 8095.
