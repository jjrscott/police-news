Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Moston.

Ronald Paul Carnegie, 68, passed away at an address on Broadlands Walk, Moston on Monday 20 January 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

If anyone has any information about Ronald's next of kin they should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 3485.
