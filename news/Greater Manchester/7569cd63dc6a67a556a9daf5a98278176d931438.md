Anthony Jackson, 71, passed away at his home in Norfield Close, Dukinfield on Tuesday 7 April 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

If anyone has any information about Anthony's next of kin they should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Stockport on 0161 856 7978.
