Police are appealing for the public's help to trace family of a woman from Urmston, Trafford.

Olga Sanderson, 94 years old, passed away at home on Bradfield Road, Urmston on 18 March 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Olga Sanderson's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Stockport on 0161 856 9677.
