A man has been charged after a burglary in Davyhulme in Trafford.

Phillip Mitchell(29/04/1973 ), of no fixed address, has been charged with burglary and breach of restraining order.

He has been remanded and will appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates today (Tuesday 21 April 2020).

In the early hours of Sunday 19 April 2020, a man entered a property on Lowood Avenue, Davyhulme and stole a number of items from the garage
