Police are appealing for the public's help to trace a wanted woman from Moss Side in Manchester.

Angela Andrews (29/08/1962) is wanted for possession with intent to supply class A drugs.

Andrews is known to have links to the South Manchester, Salford and Trafford areas of Greater Manchester.

Anybody with information regarding the whereabouts of Angela Andrews is asked to contact police on 0161 856 6197 or contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
