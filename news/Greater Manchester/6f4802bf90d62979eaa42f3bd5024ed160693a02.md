Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from the Eccles, Salford area.

Stephen Roberts, 59, passed away at his home on Liverpool Road, Eccles Salford on Tuesday 14 July 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Stephen's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Bolton on 0161 856 4687.
