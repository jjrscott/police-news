Three men have been charged in connection with criminal damage in Stockport.

James O'Brian (07/08/1979) of Hubbards Terrace, Limerick, Ireland has been charged with criminal damage and fraud by false representation.

Thomas Harty (12/09/1988) of Abbeycourt, Limerick, Ireland has been charged with criminal damage and fraud by false representation.

James O'Brian (03/08/1985) of Church Street, Castleisland, Ireland has been charged with criminal damage and fraud by false representation.

All three appeared at Stockport Magistrates' Court on Monday 3 August 2020 and have been remanded into custody where they're awaiting their next court date.

At around 10.40am on Friday 31 July 2020, police were called to a report of bogus tradesmen causing damage to an address on Nelstrop Road, Stockport.
