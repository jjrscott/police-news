Simara Knopka (19/09/1981) of Earlsway, Macclesfield has been charged with disqualified driving and no insurance. She has been remanded in police custody to appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court today (Friday 6 November 2020).

On Thursday 5 November 2020, police were made aware that a woman who was believed to be disqualified from driving was driving a car on Bridge Street in the city centre.
