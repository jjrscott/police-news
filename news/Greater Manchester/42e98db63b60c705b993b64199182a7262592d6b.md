Police are appealing to members of the public for their help tracing the next of kin of a man from the Harpurhey area of Manchester.

David Marsden, aged 49, sadly passed away at his home address on Beech Mount Road, Harpurhey, on Friday 27 March 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with any information about David's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 3485.
