Harold Pennington, 86, passed away at his home in Mottram Towers, Stockport on Wednesday 1 April 2020. There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed Harold may have had a wife called May Pennington who previously passed away.

If anyone has any information about Harold's next of kin they should contact the Stockport Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 7978.
