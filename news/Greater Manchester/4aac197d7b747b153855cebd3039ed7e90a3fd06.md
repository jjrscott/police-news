During the eleventh day of GMP's drink and drug drive campaign, officers made 10 arrests, resulting in four people being charged.

Jagdish Singh (29/08/1986) of Webberley Road, Fallowfield has been charged with driving while under the influence of alcohol. He is due to appear at Manchester & Salford Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 7 August.

Krisztian Kalanyos (18/03/1986) of Higher Market Street, Bolton has been charged with driving while under the influence of alcohol, no insurance and driving while disqualified. She is due to appear at Bolton Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 23 July.

Ian Platt (27/11/1980) of Tewkesbury Close, Stockport has been charged with failing to provide a specimen. He is due to appear at Stockport Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 24 August.

Christine Oakes (20/09/1971) of Lord Street, Salford has been charged with failing to provide a specimen. She is due to appear at Manchester & Salford Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 31 July.

Five people have been released under investigation and one person was released with no further action.
