Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Manchester.

Russell Barry Taylor, 64, passed away at his home address on Victoria Terrace in Longsight on 15 September 2019.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Russell Barry Taylor's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 3897.
