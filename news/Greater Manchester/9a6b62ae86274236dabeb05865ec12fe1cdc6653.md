Police are appealing for the public's help to trace family of a woman from the Monsall area of Manchester.

Eron Katumba, 57, passed away at North Manchester General Hospital on the 20 March 2021.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone with information about Eron's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 2334.
