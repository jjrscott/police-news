Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the family of a woman from Radcliffe.

Christine Hodgson, aged 72, passed away at her home on Irwell Street on 21 March 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone with information about Christine's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in in Heywood on 0161 856 8555 or 0161 856 9096.
