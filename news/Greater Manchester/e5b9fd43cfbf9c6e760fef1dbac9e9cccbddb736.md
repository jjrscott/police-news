A Manchester man has been charged with attempted murder.

Adil Butt (25/07/86), of Saxelby Drive, has been charged with attempted murder.

He has been remanded and will appear at Manchester Magistrates Court this morning (Wednesday 29 January 2020)

Police were called by the ambulance service shortly after 1.10pm on Monday 27 January 2020 to a property on Saxelby Drive, Manchester.

Emergency services attended and a woman in her 30s was taken to hospital with serious stab wounds.
