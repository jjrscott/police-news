A man from Stockport has been charged with two burglaries.

Stephen Connolly (16/04/1978), of Hardcastle Street, Stockport has been charged with two counts of burglary in a dwelling.

He has been remanded to appear at Stockport Magistrates Court on Saturday 9 May 2020.

Between Friday 7 and Friday 8 April 2020 police received two reports of burglary in the Shaw Heath area of Stockport.
