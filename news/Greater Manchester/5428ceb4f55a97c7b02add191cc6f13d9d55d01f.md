Appeal

Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man who passed away in Oldham.

Christopher Medley, 48, passed away at an address on Garden Terrace, Royton on Sunday 30 August 2020.

Police believe that Christopher may have a sister called Andrea who possibly owned a pub in Oldham.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Christopher's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Bury on 0161 856 8095.
