Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from the Miles Platting area of Manchester.

Mohammed Huzaien, 41, passed away at his home on Woodward Court in Manchester on Saturday 11 April 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

If anyone has any information about Mohammed's next of kin they should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 3897.
