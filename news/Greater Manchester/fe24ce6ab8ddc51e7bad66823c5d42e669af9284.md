Police want to speak to this man

Police investigating a burglary in the Egerton area of Bolton have released the image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the incident.

Officers were called at around 5.40pm on Friday 15 May 2020 to reports that a man had entered an address and stole in excess of Â£30,000's worth of jewellery and watches.

The burglary is believed to have occurred earlier during the day while the house was unoccupied.

No arrests have been made and enquires are ongoing.

Detectives are encouraging members of the public to contact them with any information they may have to help progress their enquiries.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured is encourage to call police on 0161 856 5761 quoting incident 1988 of 15/05/2020. Details can also be passed anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
