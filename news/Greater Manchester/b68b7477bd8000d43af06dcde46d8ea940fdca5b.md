Allan Wright, 77, passed away at his home on Water Street in Ashton-under-Lyne on Monday 20 April 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed Allan may have relatives living locally in the Tameside area.

If anyone has any information about Allan's next of kin they should contact the Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 9696.
