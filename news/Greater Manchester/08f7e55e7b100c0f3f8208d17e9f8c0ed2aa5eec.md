Barbara McMillan, aged 73, passed away at her home on Erskine Street on Saturday 10 October 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

It is believed that Barbara may have family in Scotland.

Anyone with information about Barbara's next of kin should contact the Manchester Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 3485.
