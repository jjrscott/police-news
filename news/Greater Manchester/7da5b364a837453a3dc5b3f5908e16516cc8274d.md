A boy has been charged after an assault on a paramedic in Manchester City Centre.

A 17-year-old boy, who can't be named for legal reasons, has been charged with one count of assault on an emergency worker and will appear at Manchester Youth Court on Monday 9 March 2020.

On Sunday 1 March 2020, police were called to reports of an assault on a paramedic who was assisting a patient outside McDonald's on St. Ann's Square in Manchester City Centre.
