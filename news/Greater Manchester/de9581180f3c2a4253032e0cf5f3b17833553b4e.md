Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the family of a woman from Rochdale.

Isabel Bernie Grindrod (22/08/1936) who was originally from Scotland passed away at her home in the Hurstead area of Rochdale on Wednesday 30 October 2019.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone with information about Isabel's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 9096.
