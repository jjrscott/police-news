Charged

A man has been charged in connection with two sexual assaults in the Stockport and South Manchester areas.

Mark Bolton (28/04/1963) of Woodheys, Stockport has been charged with two counts of sexual assault by touching.

He has been released on bail and is due to appear at Manchester Minshull Street Crown Court on Tuesday 29 September 2020.

On Tuesday 25 and Wednesday 26 August 2020, police received reports that two women had been sexually assaulted by a man on public transport in the Stockport and South Manchester areas.
