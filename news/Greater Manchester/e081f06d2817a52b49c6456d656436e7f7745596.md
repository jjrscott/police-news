Four people have been charged following warrants in Ramsbottom and Rochdale.

Imad Ul-Haque (25/04/89) and Holly Palmer (13/10/1990) both of Croft End Place, Ramsbottom have been charged with

•Money laundering
•Participating in the activities of an organised crime group
•Conspiracy to supply class A controlled drugs

Ibad Ul-Haque (26/12/92) of Leamington Street, Rochdale and Sabrah Ul-Haque (26/01/92) of Armstrong Hurst Close, Rochdale have been charged with

•Participating in the activities of an organised crime group
•Conspiracy to supply class A controlled drugs

They are all appearing at Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court today.

On Thursday 10 December 2020 officers executing warrants in Ramsbottom and Rochdale arrested four people and seized money and electronic devices.
