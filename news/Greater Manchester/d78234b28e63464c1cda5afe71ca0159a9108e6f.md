A man has been charged with Section 18 Assault in Longsight.

A 16-year-old boy who cannot be named for legal reasons has been charged with section 18 assault and will appear at Manchester Magistrates court today (Thursday 17 October 2019).

Shortly after 4pm on Tuesday 15 October 2019 police were called to reports that a 17-year-old boy had been stabbed on a bus on Wilmslow Road, Manchester.

The boy was taken to hospital with two stab wounds to his thighs and has been discharged from hospital.
