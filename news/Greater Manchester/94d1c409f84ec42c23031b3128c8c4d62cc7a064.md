Officers are appealing for the public's help to trace a wanted Colin Hughes from the Manchester area.

Colin David Hughes (05/01/1975) is wanted after failing to appear at Manchester Crown Court on 11 April 2019 after being charged with an assault that allegedly took place in the Wythenshawe area.

Hughes is known to have links to the Stockport, Trafford and Bolton areas of Greater Manchester and Blackpool.

Anybody with information regarding the whereabouts of Colin Hughes is asked to contact police on 0161 856 6197 or contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
