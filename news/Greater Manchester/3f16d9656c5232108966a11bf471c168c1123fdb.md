Police are appealing for the public's help to trace family of woman from the Longsight area of Manchester.

Gladys Gilman, 88, passed away at Victoria Nursing Home on Friday 27 December 2019.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone with information about Gladys next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 1376
