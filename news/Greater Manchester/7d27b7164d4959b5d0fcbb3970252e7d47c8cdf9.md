Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the family of a man from Withington.

Dr. Tony Matthews, 66, passed away at his home on Brookleigh Road on Friday 26 March 2021.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Tony's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 3485.
