Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the family of a man from Manchester.

Kenneth Wright, 76, passed away at his home address on Aniline Street, Manchester on 11 February 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Kenneth's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 3897.
