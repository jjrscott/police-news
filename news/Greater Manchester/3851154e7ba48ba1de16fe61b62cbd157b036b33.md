A man has been charged with aggravated burglary and the handling of stolen goods.

Jack Steven Yarwood (20/12/85) of Regent Road, Manchester has been charged with aggravated burglary and the handling of stolen goods and will appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court today (Tuesday 1 October 2019).

Shortly after 9.00pm on Monday 16 September 2019, a group of men wearing balaclavas forced their way into a house on Dunham Road, Warburton and stole a car and other items.

On 20 September 2019, police recovered a vehicle at an apartment in Salford which had been stolen from a home on Ridgeway Road, Timperley, Trafford.
