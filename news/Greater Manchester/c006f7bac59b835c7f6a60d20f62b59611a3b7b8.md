An eighth person has been charged in relation to the death of Alexander John Soyoye in the Moston area of Manchester in November.

Ismael Correia (10/08/2001), of Cavanagh Close, Manchester, has been charged with single counts of murder and violent disorder.

He has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Manchester Magistrates' Court on Saturday 26 December 2020.

Just after 7pm on Thursday 5 November, police were called to reports of a disturbance on Birchenall Street, Moston.

Officers attended and established that Alexander John Soyoye had been stabbed. He was sadly pronounced dead at the scene despite the best efforts of emergency services.

Six other men and a teenage boy have also been charged with murder and have been remanded into custody ahead of further court appearances.
