A man has been charged following the death of a man in Salford.

Shortly after 9pm on Saturday 26 September 2020, police were called to reports of an assault at a property on Leicester Road in Salford.

It is believed two men were involved in an altercation which resulted in one of the men being seriously injured.

Emergency services attended and 49-year-old Lee Warren Oates was taken to hospital with serious head injuries. He sadly died in hospital on Friday 2 October 2020.

Jack Kent (21/07/1999), of Leicester Road, Salford, has been charged with murder.

He will appear at Manchester Crown Court on Monday 26 October.
