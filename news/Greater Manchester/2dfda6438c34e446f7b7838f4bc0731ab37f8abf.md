CCTV 1

In February 2020, police received a report of a sexual offence against a woman.

An investigation was launched.

In September 2020, a man - who officers want to speak with - was captured on CCTV.

Detective Inspector Andrew Naismith, of GMP's Bury district, said: "As soon as we received this report in February 2020, we launched an investigation to establish the full circumstances.

"Enquiries were ongoing when we received CCTV of a man who we want to speak with.

"Since receiving this CCTV, we have exhausted a number of options to identify the man shown and we are now appealing for him or anyone who knows who he is to contact us or Crimestoppers."

Anyone with information should contact police via 0161 856 8158 or 19269@gmp.police.uk quoting 1816 of 13/02/20 or the independent charity - Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
