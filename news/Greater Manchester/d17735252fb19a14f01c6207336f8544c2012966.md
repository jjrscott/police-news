Police are appealing for the public's help to trace family of man from the Chorlton area.

Peter Leigh, aged 58, passed away at home on Lockton Close on the 23 March 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Peter Leigh's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 2334.
