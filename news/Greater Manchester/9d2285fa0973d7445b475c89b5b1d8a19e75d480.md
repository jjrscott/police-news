A man has been charged following a robbery and burglary in Stockport.

Matthew Bayley (19/10/90), of no fixed address, has been charged with burglary and robbery.

He has been remanded and will appear at Stockport Magistrates Court today (Thursday 7 November 2019).

On Saturday 14 September 2019 police received a report of burglary and robbery in the Romiley area of Stockport.
