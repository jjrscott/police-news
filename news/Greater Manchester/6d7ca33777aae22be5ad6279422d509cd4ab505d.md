Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a Manchester man.

Peter Leigh, 58, passed away at home on Lockton Close, Chorlton in Manchester on Monday 23 March 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

The Police Coroner's Officer is now appealing for help to locate any relatives of Peter.

Anyone with information should call Paul Devine Coroner's Officer on 0161 856 6816. or e mail paulj.devine@gmp.police.uk
