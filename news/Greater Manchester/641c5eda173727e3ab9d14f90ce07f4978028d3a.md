Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the family of a woman from the Hulme area of Manchester.

Naomi Chin, aged 68, sadly passed away at her home address on Chorlton Road on Thursday 2 April 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone with information about Naomi's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 3897.
