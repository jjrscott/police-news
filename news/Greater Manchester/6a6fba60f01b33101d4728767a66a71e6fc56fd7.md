Robert Francis Morrison, 73, sadly passed away at his home address on Birkdale Gardens, Bolton, on Monday 2 March 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed Robert was originally from Belfast, Northern Ireland.

Anyone with information about Robert's next-of-kin is asked to call the Police Coroners Officer in Bolton on 0161 856 5037.
