Police are appealing to the public for help to identify the next of kin of a man from the Mossley area of Tameside.

Kok Ooi, aged 46, sadly passed away at Stepping Hill Hospital on Sunday 2 August 2020. He had been an in-patient there for the past 18 months.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Kok was born in Singapore and may have relatives living in the Tameside area.

Anyone with any information about Kok's next of kin should contact the police coroner's office in Stockport on 0161 856 9696.
