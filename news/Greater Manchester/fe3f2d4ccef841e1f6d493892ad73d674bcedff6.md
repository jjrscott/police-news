A man has been charged after a stabbing in Manchester.

Milo Lyndsay (17/02/1986) of Longport Avenue, Manchester has been charged with two counts of section 18 assault and possession of an offensive weapon.

He was remanded and will appear at Manchester Magistrates Court today (Friday 31 January 2020).

At around 1.45am on Thursday 30 January 2020 police were called to a report of a stabbing at a nightclub on Wilmslow Road in Withington.

Two men, aged 22 and 21, were taken to hospital with serious injuries.
