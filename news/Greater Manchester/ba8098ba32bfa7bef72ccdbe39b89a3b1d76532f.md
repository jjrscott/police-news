A man has been charged following a warrant in Buxton yesterday (1 December 2020).

Officers from GMP's Serious and Organised Crime Group searched an address on Walker Brow as part of an investigation into the supply of firearms.

Aaron Melville (03/08/2000), of Walker Brow, Buxton, has been charged with possession of a firearm.

He will appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court tomorrow (3 December 2020).
