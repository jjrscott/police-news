Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man who lived in Wigan.

Thomas Longridge, 80, sadly passed away in Westwood Lodge Care Home on Sunday 8 March 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Thomas's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 4687.
