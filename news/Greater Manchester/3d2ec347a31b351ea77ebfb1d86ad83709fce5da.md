Two men have been charged after a serious assault in Moss Side.

Mark Raymond (15/04/1970) of Bowness Road, Timperley has been charged with false imprisonment and robbery and will appear at Manchester Crown Court On Sunday 3 May 2020.

Mark Hart (16/01/1993) of Catherston Road, Mosside, Manchester has been charged with section 18 assault, false imprisonment and robbery and will appear Manchester Crown Court On Tuesday 5 May 2020.

At around 2pm on Sunday 5 April 2020 police were called to a report of concern for the welfare of a man in Moss Side.

A 24-year-old man was assaulted by two men armed with numerous weapons at a property in Moss Side.
