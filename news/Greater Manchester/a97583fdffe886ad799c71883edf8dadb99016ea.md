Five men have been charged following a violent dispute in Edgeley.

Callum Taylor (16/04/1999) of Bodmin Crescent, Brinnington has been charged with involvement in a violent disorder.

Sam Emmerson (17/05/1998) of Lanegate in Hyde has been charged with involvement in a violent disorder.

Scott Phillips (08/11/1994) of Wessenden Bank East, Offerton has been charged with involvement in a violent disorder.

Westley Holland (02/09/1992) of Laburnum Way, Cheadle Heath has been charged with involvement in a violent disorder.

Baillie Blomeley (25/02/1999) of Victoria Park, Offerton has been charged with involvement in a violent disorder.

The five men will appear at Stockport Magistrates Court on Monday 13 January 2020.

On Monday 26 August 2019 a violent dispute broke out on Northgate Road between Stockport County and Chesterfield supporters prior to a football match between the two clubs at Edgeley park the same day.
