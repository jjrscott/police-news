During the ninth day of GMP's drink and drug drive campaign, officers made 10 arrests, resulting in two people being charged.

Radoslav Bosela (11/03/1994) of Valpy Avenue, Bolton has been charged with driving while under the influence of alcohol and having no insurance. He is due to appear at Bolton Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 30 July.

Roy Thomas (28/07/1963) of Begonia Avenue, Bolton has been charged with failing to provide a specimen. He is due to appear at Bolton Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 30 July.

Six people have been released under investigation and two people were released with no further action.

**Court results**

Kazza Hussain (28/05/1988) of Nixon Street, Rochdale, who was charged with driving while under the influence of alcohol, driving while disqualified and having no insurance, appeared at Manchester & Salford Magistrates' Court on Friday 28 June and received a 12-week custodial sentence and disqualified from driving for 24 months.

Nema Ndikumana (01/04/1987) of Clanfield Road, Liverpool, who was charged with driving while under the influence of alcohol, appeared at Manchester & Salford Magistrates' Court yesterday (Tuesday 2 July) and was disqualified from driving for 24 months, ordered to undertake 80 hours of unpaid work and to pay costs totalling Â£171.
