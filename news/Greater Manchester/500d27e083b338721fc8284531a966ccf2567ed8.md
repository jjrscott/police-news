A man has been charged following a stabbing in woodland in Offerton, Stockport.

Joshua Hallows (09/11/98) of Warren Roadd, Cale Green in Stockport, has been charged with one count of attempted murder and one count of grievous bodily harm with intent.

He has been remanded in custody and will appear at Stockport Magistrates today (Monday 7 September 2020)

At 4.35pm on Tuesday 1 September 2020, police were called to reports of a stabbing in woodland off The Fairway, Offerton.

Those injured received non-life-threatening injuries.
