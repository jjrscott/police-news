Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Manchester.

Gordon Fredrick Elms, 59, passed away at his home address on Mabfield Road, Manchester on Monday 17 February 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

If anyone has any information about Gordon's next of kin they should contact the Manchester Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 1376.
