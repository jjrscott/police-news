A man has been charged after officers were spat at whilst responding to an incident in Withington.

Samuel Konneh (13/06/1981), of Easthorpe Close, Withington, has been charged with:

 * four counts of attempt section 18 assault
 * one count of common assault
 * one count of section 4 harassment

He has been remanded and will appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court today (Saturday 28 March 2020).

On Thursday 26 March 2020 officers were called to Yew Tree Road in Withington, Manchester, to reports that a woman had been assaulted.

Whilst responding to the incident, four officers had blood spat at them.
