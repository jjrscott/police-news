Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Bolton.

James Parker, aged 93, sadly passed away at his home address on Royston Avenue in Bolton on Wednesday 17 June 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about James' next of kin should contact the police coroner's office in Bolton on 0161 856 4687.
