Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Bolton.

Michael Brigden, 58, passed away at his home address on Lancaster Close on Thursday 14 November 2019.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Michael's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Bolton on 0161 856 4685.
