A man has been charged with a firearms offence in Manchester.

John Fleck (16/02/1989) of Farnborough Road, Manchester has been charged with a Section 16A firearms offence and will appear at Manchester Magistrates Court today (Monday 16 March 2020).

At around 11.55pm on Friday 13 March 2020 police were called to a report of a firearms discharge on Winstanley Road in Manchester.

Several gunshots were fired on the street. No one was injured during the incident.
