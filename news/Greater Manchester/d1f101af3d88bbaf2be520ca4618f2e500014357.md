Appeal

Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Didsbury.

Gerard Veto, 87, passed away at his home address on Palatine Road on Monday 28 September 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Gerard's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 2334.
