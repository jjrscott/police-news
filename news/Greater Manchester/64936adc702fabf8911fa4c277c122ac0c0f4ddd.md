Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Manchester.

Lee Hirschmann, 61, of a care home in Northern Moor, passed away on Monday 6 January 2020.

He is known to have links to Blackley and Moston and may have brothers living in Manchester.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Lee's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 6069.
