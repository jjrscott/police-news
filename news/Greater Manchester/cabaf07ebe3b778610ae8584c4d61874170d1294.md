Charged

A man has been charged in connection with an assault in Gorton.

Inocencio Moto (17/08/90) of St Phillips Road, Gorton has been charged with Section 18 and Section 47 assault.

He has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Manchester Magistrates' Court this morning (Thursday 26 November 2020).

At around 4.25pm on Tuesday 24 November, officers were called to a report of a disturbance on Hyde Road, Gorton.

Officers attended and two people were taken to hospital, one with life changing injuries.
