54-year-old Brian Anton Mag Uidhir, also known as Maguire, was found deceased at his home on Empress Court, Old Trafford on Tuesday 5 November 2019.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

He is understood to have grown up in the Belfast area of Northern Ireland and is thought to have a brother living in that area.

Anyone with information about Brian's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Stockport on 0161 856 9677.
