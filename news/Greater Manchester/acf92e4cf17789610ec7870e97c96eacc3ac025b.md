Police attended a property on Worsley Road, Eccles yesterday morning (Wednesday 17 June 2020) following a report of concern for the welfare of a man.

A 35-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of possession of a knife. He remains in custody for questioning.

Bomb disposal officers attended and carried out a controlled explosion of the suspicious package found at the property as a precaution.

Nearby buildings were evacuated, however residents have since been allowed to return to their homes.

A scene remains in place whilst enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone with information should contact police on 0161 856 5345, quoting incident number 2270 of 16/06/2020.

Reports can also be made anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
