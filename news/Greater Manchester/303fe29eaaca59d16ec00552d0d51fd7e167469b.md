A Trafford man has been charged in connection with an investigation into child sexual abuse.

Anthony Hook (25/07/1960) of Stretford Road, Urmston, has been charged with eight counts of sexual activity with a child.

He will appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court on Wednesday 8 July 2020.

This relates to an ongoing investigation into child sexual abuse in the Manchester area after reports were made to police in February 2019. The charges relate to four victims.
