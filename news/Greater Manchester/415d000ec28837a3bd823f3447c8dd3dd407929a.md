Police are appealing for the public's help to trace a wanted man from Manchester.

Trent Taylor (15/04/1984), from Hale, is wanted in connection with an assault that took place on Millhalf Close, Hulme on Friday 18 December 2020.

Taylor is known to have links to the Moss Side, Hulme and Wythenshawe areas of Greater Manchester.

Anybody with information about his whereabouts should contact police on 0161 856 4275 or contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
