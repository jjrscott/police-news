Officers have released images of jewellery worth £12k stolen in a burglary in Manchester in a bid to find those responsible.

A property on Great Western Street in Rusholme was burgled between 3pm and 7pm on 21 October 2020.

Sentimental jewellery worth £12,000 was taken.

The homeowners were inside the address at the time of the incident but thankfully no one was injured.

PC Michelle Higginbottom, from GMP's City of Manchester district, said: "The items of jewellery stolen were of great sentimental value to the family and have been in the family for a number of years.

"We're doing all we can to trace those responsible and we're keen to locate the stolen jewellery.

"Anyone who may have seen the jewellery or anyone with any information should call us on 0161 856 6139 quoting crime number CRI/06A2/0019691/20.

"Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
