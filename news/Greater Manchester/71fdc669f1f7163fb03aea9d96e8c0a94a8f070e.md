Nico Logan (21/03/1993) of Shayfield Road, Manchester was charged with one count of conspiracy to possess a firearm with intent to endanger life, two counts of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs and one count of being concerned in the supply of Class B drugs.

Jordan Gabriel (26/02/1992) of Bolam Close, Manchester was charged with one count of conspiracy to possess a firearm with intent to endanger life and three counts of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs.

Both men have been remanded to appear before Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court today (Friday 19 March 2021).

On Thursday 18 March 2021, officers executed warrants in Wythenshawe and Warrington as part of Operation Foam.

A quantity of cash, drugs and other property was seized.
