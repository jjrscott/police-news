Awa Zongo Bn (01/01/91) of Atherton Road, Wigan has been charged with attempted murder.

She has been remanded to appear before Wigan and Leigh Magistrates Court on Monday 16 December 2019.

At around 10.35am on Saturday 14 December 2019, police were called to reports of stabbing on Atherton Road, Hindley Green.

A 39-year-old woman who was injured during the incident remains in hospital.
