Desmond McKenna (07/08/66) of Hopton Court, Hulme has been charged with seven counts of robbery and six counts of possession of an offensive weapon.

He appeared before Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court on Monday 23 March 2020.

He has been remanded in custody and is awaiting a court date for his next appearance.

This charge is in relation to seven armed robberies which occurred at commercial properties between Wednesday 11 March 2020 and Friday 20 March 2020.
