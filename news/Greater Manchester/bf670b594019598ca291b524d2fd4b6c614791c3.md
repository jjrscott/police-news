Detectives are appealing for witnesses after a man was assaulted by two people in Stockport.

At around 8.30pm on Saturday 15 August 2020, a 19-year-old man was repeatedly punched in the face in a smoking area at the Friendship Inn on Stockport Road in Romiley.

The suspect then left the pub and when the 19-year-old left the pub, he was assaulted again by another man whilst running for a taxi.

He suffered serious facial injuries.

The suspects are described as two white males.

Anyone with information or anyone who may have seen anything is asked to call 0161 856 9923 quoting incident number 1911 of 17/08/20.

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
