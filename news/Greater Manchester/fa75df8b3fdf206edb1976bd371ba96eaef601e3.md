Charged

A teenage boy has been charged following the knifepoint robbery of a car in Wigan.

A 17-year-old boy - who cannot be named for legal reasons - has been charged with:

 * One count of robbery
 * Possession of a bladed article
 * Taking a vehicle without consent
 * Driving without a licence and insurance

He appeared at Manchester and Salford Youth Court on Tuesday 24 August 2020 and has been remanded in custody until a further appearance on Wednesday 2 September 2020.

At around 1.45pm on Friday 21 August 2020, police were called to a report of a knifepoint robbery on Westwood Lane, Wigan.
