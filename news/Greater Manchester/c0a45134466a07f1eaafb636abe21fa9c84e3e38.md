Do you recognise these men?

Police have released CCTV images following a disturbance in Wigan.

On Sunday 21 June at around 2.20am, police were called to College Street, Leigh following a report of a burglary and assault.

Officers attended and enquiries have since established that two men had entered the property and shot at residents with a BB gun.

One person received minor injuries that did not require hospital treatment.

The offenders left empty handed.

Detective Sergeant Sarah Bell of GMP'S Wigan District said: "One person was left with minor injuries to the face as a result of this incident and we're doing everything we can to trace those responsible.

"We have released images of two men we'd like to speak to as part of our enquiries so I would urge them or anyone who recognises them to get in touch.

"I would also ask anyone who witnessed anyone acting suspiciously in the area around the time of the incident to contact police."

Anyone with any information should contact police on 0161 856 7254 quoting incident 2942 of 21/06/20 or the independent charity - Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
