Officers have released CCTV images of two men they want to speak to in connection with an assault in Manchester.

It is understood that the incident took place shortly after 10pm on Wednesday 14 October on Chester Street where a man in his 20s was injured and taken to hospital.

Two men have been charged in connection with this assault but officers are keen to speak to the men pictured as part of their enquiries.

Anyone with information or anyone who recognises the men in the CCTV images is asked to call 0161 856 4409.

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
