A former teacher has been charged with non-recent sexual offences.

David Warburton (18/05/49) of Fields Road, Lancashire has been charged with indecent assault.

He is scheduled to appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates' Court on 24 March 2021.

In January 2020, police received a report of a sexual offences against a teenage boy in the 1980s.

Anyone with information should contact police quoting 1503 08/01/20 or the independent charity - Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
