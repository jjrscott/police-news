Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a woman from Stockport.

Patricia Robertson, 99, passed away at her home on Kenley Lodge in Bramhall on Saturday 14 December 2019.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

If you have any information about Patricia's next of kin please contact the Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 7711.
