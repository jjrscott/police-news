Jaroslav Marchewka (30/12/1989) of Cleveland Road, Crumpsall has been charged with section 18 assault.

He was remanded into custody and is appearing at Manchester & Salford Magistrates' Court today (Tuesday 2 June 2019).

Shortly before 11.20pm on Saturday 1 June 2019, police were called to reports that a man had been stabbed at an address on Lansdowne Road, Crumpsall.

He remains in hospital where he is in a serious but stable condition.
