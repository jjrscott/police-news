Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Bolton.

Kevan Clegg, 50, passed away at his home address on Solent Drive, Bolton on Sunday 29 December 2019.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Kevan's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Bolton on 0161 856 4685.
