Nezar Abukhrais (26/07/ 1985) of Shipton Street, Bolton has been charged with:

 * One count of causing death by careless driving
 * One count of causing death whilst unlicensed
 * One count of causing death whilst uninsured
 * One count of failure to stop following a collision
 * One count of failure to report a collision

He has been remanded to appear before Bolton crown court on Tuesday 24 December 2019.

Shortly before 9pm on Saturday 23 November 2019, police were called to reports of a fatal collision outside the Royal Albert infirmary on Wigan Lane in which a 53-year-old woman was killed in a fatal collision.
