Three people have been charged in relation to an investigation into serious crime following two serious assaults in Rochdale.

Jeffrey Ojo (27/01/01) of Newick Walk, Manchester has been charged with conspiracy to commit murder.

Gedeon Kalumda (21/01/02) of William Lister Close, Manchester has been charged with conspiracy to commit murder.

A 17-year-old boy from Manchester, who cannot be named for legal reasons, has been charged with conspiracy to commit murder.

All three have been remanded in custody to appear before Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court today (Saturday 6 February 2021).
