Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Bolton.

James Naylor, age 63, passed away at his home on Valley View, Bromley Cross, Bolton on Monday 27 April 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about James Naylor's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office on 0161 856 4687.
