Kathleen Kurt, aged 75, sadly passed away at her home address at Saint Matthews Grange in the Halliwell area on Thursday 19 March 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone with any information regarding Kathleen's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Bolton on 0161 856 4687.
