Police are currently at the scene of a serious assault in Wythenshawe.

Officers were called at around 6.30pm to a report of an assault at a shop of Sale Road, Wythenshawe.

Officers attended and a man aged in his 30s was located nearby on Rackhouse Road and taken to hospital, where he remains for treatment of serious head injuries.

A man has been arrested on suspicion of assault and remains in custody for questioning.

Enquiries are ongoing.
