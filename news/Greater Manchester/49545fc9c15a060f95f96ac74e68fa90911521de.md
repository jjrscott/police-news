Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Harpurhey.

Jean Claude Nzikou, 50, passed away at his home address in Harpurhey, Manchester on Monday 15 June 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Jean Claude's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 4155.
