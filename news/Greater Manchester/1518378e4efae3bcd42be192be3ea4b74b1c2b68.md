Police are appealing for the public's help to trace family of man from Bury.

Dennis Wall, 49, passed away at his home address of Brick Street, Bury on Tuesday 28 January 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Dennis Walls next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Bury on 0161 856 8095.
