Police are appealing for footage and images following the incident at Manchester Arndale Shopping Centre.

The public and media can help us by sending any images or footage they may have taken at the Arndale via this website at http://www.ukpoliceimageappeal.co.uk/.

Anyone who may have information that could assist the investigation should call 0161 856 0394. In an emergency dial 999.

We will keep you updated.
