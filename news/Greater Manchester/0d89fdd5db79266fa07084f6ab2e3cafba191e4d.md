Charged

Two men have been charged with drug offences.

Mateo Lezi (11/08/1996) of no fixed abode, has been charged with possession of class B drug with intent to supply.

Andy Kola (10/12/1997) of Edmund Court, Sheffield, has been charged with possession of class B drug with intent to supply.

They have both been remanded in custody before appearing at Stockport Magistrates' Court today (Tuesday 8 December 2020).

On Monday 7 December 2020 officers from GMP's Tactical Vehicle Intercept Unit seized a van on Chapman Street, Openshaw and a quantity of cannabis was recovered.
