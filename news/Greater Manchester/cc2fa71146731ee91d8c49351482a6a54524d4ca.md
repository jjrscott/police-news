Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man who was originally from Wiltshire.

Robert Alexander Slater, aged 70, died at his home address on Botha Close in Manchester on Friday 6 December 2019.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Robert's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 1376.
