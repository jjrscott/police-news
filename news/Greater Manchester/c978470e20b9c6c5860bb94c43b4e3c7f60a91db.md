A man has been charged after officers were called to reports of a house party in Ashton.

Macauley Orme born (22/05/1995) of Currier Lane, Ashton has been charged with possession of a controlled substance.

He has been bailed to appear before Tameside Magistrates' Court on Thursday 21 May 2020.

On Thursday 23 April 2020, police were called to reports of a house party taking place on Currier Lane.

A Closure Notice was granted by the Court which will remain in place until Friday 1 May 2020 and prohibits anyone other than the tenant from entering the property.
