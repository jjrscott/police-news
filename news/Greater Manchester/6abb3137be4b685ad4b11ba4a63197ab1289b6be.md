The following five men have been charged with conspiracy to supply class A drugs namely heroin:

 * Stephen King (21/05/1977) of Crown Wood Court, Bamfurlong
 * John Hayden (20/03/1964) of Roman Road, Ashton-in-Makerfield
 * Ian Washington (04/04/1969) of Edge Green Lane, Golborne
 * Andrew Tait (30/09/1981) of Tram Street, Platt Bridge
 * Paul Adair (10/12/1975) of Simkin Street, Wigan

All five have been remanded in custody before being due to appear at Manchester Magistrates' Court on Monday 3 February 2020.

On the afternoon of Friday 31 January 2020, specialist officers seized a van at Keele service station and subsequently arrested five men on suspicion of conspiracy to supply class A drugs.
