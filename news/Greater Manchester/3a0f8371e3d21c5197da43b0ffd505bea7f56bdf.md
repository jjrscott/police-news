Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Crumpsall.

David West, 56, passed away at his home on Westhorne Fold, Crumpsall on Friday 3 April 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

If anyone has any information about David West's next of kin they should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 2334.
