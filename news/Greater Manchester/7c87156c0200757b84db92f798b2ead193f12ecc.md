Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the family of a man from the Whalley Range area of Manchester.

Anthony Turner, aged 78, died at Abbotsford Care Home, Carlton Road Manchester on Saturday 28 December 2019.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Anthony's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Manchester on 0161 856 1376.
