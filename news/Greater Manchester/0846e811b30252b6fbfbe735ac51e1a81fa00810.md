Wanted

Phillip Baxter (27/03/1951), of no fixed abode, is wanted for failing to comply with the conditions of his release from prison after being released in November 2019.

Baxter is known to have links to the Stockport area of Greater Manchester as well as the city centre. He is also believed to have links to Southport.

Officers are advising members of the public not to approach Baxter if they see him but to contact police immediately.

Anyone with information regarding Baxter's whereabouts should call police on 0161 856 9975. Details can also be passed anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
