Gareth Eardley (30/10/82) of Field Walk, Hale has been charged with two counts of attempted murder, making threats to kill, assault and possession of an offensive weapon.

He has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court today (Monday 30 November 2020).

Shortly before 10.05pm on Saturday 28 November 2020, police were called to reports of people fighting outside a property on Field Walk in Hale. Officers attended and two men, aged 39 and 46, were taken to hospital with serious injuries. A 50-year-old woman was treated at the scene for minor injuries.
