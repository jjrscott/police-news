Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the next of kin of a man from Radcliffe.

Karl Johnson, 55, passed away at his home on Ainsworth Road, Radcliffe on Thursday 30 April 2020.

There are believed to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Karl's next of kin should contact the Police Coroner's Office in Heywood on 0161 856 8095 or 0161 856 9096.
