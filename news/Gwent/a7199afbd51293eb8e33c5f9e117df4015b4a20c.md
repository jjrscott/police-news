We're appealing for information following an incident that occurred in the vicinity of Pill Millennium Centre this afternoon, Thursday 18th February.

Between 3-3.30pm a 28 year old man sustained injuries to his head, chest and back.

He was taken to the University Hospital of Wales for medical treatment. His condition is not life threatening.

Officers are carrying out enquiries and ask that anyone with information relating to this incident calls 101 quoting 2100057967, or direct messages us on Facebook or Twitter.
