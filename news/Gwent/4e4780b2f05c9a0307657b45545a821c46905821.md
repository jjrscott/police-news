21-year-old Brandon Montel Crosdale was sentenced to three years and four months in prison after pleading guilty to possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class A - crack cocaine, in the Newport area.

Brandon Crosdale from the Newport area was sentenced at Newport Crown Court on Monday 4 January 2021.

Officer in the case, PC Tyler Price said: "We welcome this sentence handed by the court. This is a strong message that Gwent Police will not tolerate people targeting the most vulnerable in our communities and profiting from the demise of others."
