Gwent Police is appealing for information to find 27-year-old Shabaz Ali, from #Newbridge, who breached his licence conditions after being released from prison on 8 December last year.

Ali received a 7-year 9-month sentence for aggravated vehicle taking at Cardiff Crown Court in July 2015.

Due to the fact Ali, who was released on licence, breached his conditions he has now been recalled to prison.

Shabaz Ali also has contacts in the Newport and Birmingham areas.

Anyone with any information is asked to call 101, quoting 2100005674, or you can direct message Gwent Police on Facebook or Twitter. You can also call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
