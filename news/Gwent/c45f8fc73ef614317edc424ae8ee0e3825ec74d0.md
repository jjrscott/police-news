A Brynmawr man has been jailed for 30 months for Class B drug production and drug supply.

Gytis Babenskas, aged 22, was sentenced at Cardiff Crown Court on Friday 4 December 2020.

He pleaded guilty to:

Production of Class B Drug (Cannabis)

Being Concerned in the supply of Class B Drug (Cannabis)

PC Liam Crandon explained: "The neighbourhood enforcement team in Blaenau Gwent are targeting those involved in this type of organised criminal activity and we hope this sentence shows that the production and supply of controlled drugs within our communities will not be tolerated, and we will endeavour to bring these offenders to justice.

"If you have any information about the production or supply of drugs in your community, you can call us on 101 or report anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
