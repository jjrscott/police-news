Six men have appeared in court charged with conspiracy to supply class A drugs - cocaine.

The men, aged between 32 and 50, appeared at Newport Magistrates' Court on Saturday, 20 March.

A 38-year-old from Barking, London was also charged with possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of class A - cocaine.

They have all been remanded until their next court appearance on April 12.

The men were arrested as part of an operation which took place on Thursday, 18 March in which warrants took place at addresses in Gwent, Cardiff, Oxford, Kent and London.
