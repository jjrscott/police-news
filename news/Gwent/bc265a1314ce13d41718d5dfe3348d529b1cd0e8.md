We're appealing for information following a break in at a commercial premises on Baneswell Road in Newport.

Hairplay was broken into sometime between the evening of Saturday 5th December and the morning of Monday 7th December.

Items stolen include:

3 new GHD hairdryers in boxes (like pictured)

7 GHD hair straighteners (4 of which were new and in boxes - like pictured)

1 iPad with a smashed screen and a Hairplay screensaver

Approximately 30 Wella hair products

If you have been offered any of these items or have any information relating to this incident, please call us on 101 quoting 2000443971, or DM us.
