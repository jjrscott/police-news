We're trying to find the driver of a car that was involved in a collision with a pedestrian in the Ebbw Vale area yesterday evening, Tuesday 8th December.

It happened at approximately 4.40pm at the roundabout between Cwmdraw Industrial Estate and Gibbons road in Newtown.

The pedestrian, a 52-year-old woman, received serious injuries and was taken to hospital where she is currently receiving treatment.

The car involved, which was silver in colour, didn't stop and continued to drive in the Newtown direction.

Anyone with any dash cam footage or who was in the area at the time is asked to call police on 101, quoting 2000445872, or you can direct message on Facebook or Twitter. You can also call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
