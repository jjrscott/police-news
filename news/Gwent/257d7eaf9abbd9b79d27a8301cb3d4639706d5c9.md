We're appealing for witnesses to a road traffic collision in Newport.

Officers attended a road traffic collision involving two vehicles in Charles Street, Newport, shortly after 9.15am this morning.

The collision, which involved a Citroen Berlingo and a Peugeot 307, took place on Charles Street at the Junction of School lane and Talbot lane.

Two people were taken to hospital for treatment.

We're appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision to get in contact or anyone with dash-cam footage of the area at the time.

Anyone with information can call us on 101 quoting log 74 - 16/02/2021.
