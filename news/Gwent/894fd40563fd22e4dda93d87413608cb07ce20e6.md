Officers have recovered a number of items from a house in Newport which are suspected to have been stolen.

We are appealing for information to return the items to their owners.

The items include a men's Tommy Hilfiger brown leather strapped watch, men's black coloured watch, ladies' Citizen eco-drive gold and silver coloured watch and a ladies' Tissot gold coloured watch.

Also recovered were a silver necklace and a Mark Benevolent Fund Steward 1978 with Berks and Oxon county badge and a Mark Benevolent Fund Steward 1980 badge.

If you think any of the items could belong to you, please contact us on 101 quoting reference 2000448541, or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

You can also send us a direct message via the Gwent Police Facebook or Twitter pages.

A 26-year-old man from Newport has been arrested on suspicion of burglary and has been released on bail.
