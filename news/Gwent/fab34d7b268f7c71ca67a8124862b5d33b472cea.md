We are appealing for witnesses following a fatal road traffic collision which took place in Pontllanfraith this morning.

The collision, involving a pedestrian and a blue coloured Toyota Yaris, took place in Pen-Y-Bryn Terrace/Bryn Road at around 8.25am, November 9.

The pedestrian, a 74-year-old woman from Blackwood, was pronounced dead at the scene.

Her family are being supported by specially trained officers.

We are appealing for anyone with any information to get in contact. Any motorists who may have any dash-cam footage of the area at the time of the collision is also asked to contact us.

Anybody with any information can contact us on 101, quoting reference number 2000408067. You can also contact us via direct message on Facebook or Twitter.

Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
