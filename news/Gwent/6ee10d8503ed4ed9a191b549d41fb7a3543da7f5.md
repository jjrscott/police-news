The 50 year old Newport man who was arrested following the robbery that took place at Lifestyle Stores on Pentonville Newport on Wednesday (20th January), has been charged with robbery and appeared at Newport Magistrates Court this morning.

The 37 year old man from Newport, also arrested on suspicion of robbery, was released on police bail pending further enquiries.
