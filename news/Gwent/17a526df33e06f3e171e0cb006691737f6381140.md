We're appealing for information following an assault that occurred on Commercial Road, Newport on 10th March.

Just before 7pm, a 23 year old man was assaulted by another man near Antalya Kebabs. He sustained head injuries requiring hospital treatment and is now recovering at home.

This man was captured on CCTV in the Commercial Road/ Alexandra Road area around the time of the incident and officers are keen to identify and speak with him.

Anyone with information that could help this investigation is asked to call 101 quoting 2100084940 or to direct message us on Facebook or Twitter.
