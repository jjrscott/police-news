We're appealing for information to locate 40 year old Neil Norman from Aberbargoed, who has breached his licence conditions after being released from prison on 7th January 2021.

Norman received a 3 month prison sentence for assault at Cardiff Magistrates Court on 13th November 2020.

Due to the fact that Norman, who was released on licence, breached his licence conditions he has now been recalled to prison.

If you have any information please call 101 quoting 2100048205, direct message us on Facebook or Twitter, or call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
