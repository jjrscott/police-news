Officers are appealing for information following an assault that happened in Newport on Friday 1st January.

A man suffered serious head injuries after being assaulted by a group of other men, on Corporation Road, sometime just after midnight and 2am.

It happened near the junction for George Street Bridge.

If you can help call us on 101, quoting 200000203, or you can DM us. You can also call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
