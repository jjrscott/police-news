A Liverpool man has appeared in court charged with possession with intent to supply cocaine.

The 31-year-old from Kirkby was arrested on Friday, 19 March, after a car was stopped on the A40 in Abergavenny.

He has since been charged with possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of class A - cocaine and appeared at Newport Magistrates' Court this morning.

He was remanded until his next court appearance on 19 April.
