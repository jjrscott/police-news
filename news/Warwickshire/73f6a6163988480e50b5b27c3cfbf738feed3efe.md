A man has been charged in connection with an incident of violent disorder in Nuneaton on Friday (27 December) afternoon.

Syed Abbas, 19, from Grange Road, Small Heath, Birmingham, has been charged with violent disorder and possession of a blade in a public place.

He is due to appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court today (Tuesday 31 January).

Police enquiries into the incident are ongoing and they continue to appeal for information.
