Two men have been arrested following the theft of a van from Kenilworth yesterday (Monday 23 March).

The van was stolen from Woodland Road at around 1.40pm.

The two 23-year-old men who are from Derby and Bedworth, were arrested on suspicion of theft after they were stopped by officers from West Midlands Police on the M6 a short time later.

Anyone with information that could help police with their enquiries should call Warwickshire Police on 101. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
