Incident 50 of 2 March 2020

Police are appealing for witnesses to a serious collision that occurred at 07.20 hours this morning, Monday 2 March 2020 on Wishaw Lane, Curdworth.

Police, fire and ambulance including the air ambulance attended the scene of the collision between two cars, a Peugeot and a Ford Kuga.

The female driver of the Peugeot suffered serious injuries and was taken to Queen Elizabeth Hospital by land ambulance for further assessment and treatment.

Initial reports indicate the male driver of the Ford suffered minor injuries.

Any witnesses are please asked to call Warwickshire Police on the collision line 01926 415415 quoting incident 50 of 2 March 2020.

Road closures were put in place to allow emergency services to attend the scene at the Wishaw Lane junction with Blindpit Lane and the Wishaw Lane junction with the A4097 Kingsbury Road.
