Warwickshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a collision in Bulkington on Wednesday morning (26 February).

At around 8.15am, a collision took place between a black Smart Fortwo and a blue Kymbo motorbike on Nuneaton Road, at the junction with Weston Lane.

The rider of the motorbike sustained serious injuries and is currently in hospital.

If anyone witnessed the collision, or either vehicle prior to the incident, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 61 of 26 February 2020.
