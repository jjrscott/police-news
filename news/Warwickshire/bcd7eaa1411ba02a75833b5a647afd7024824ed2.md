Two people have been charged with in connection with drugs offences in Rugby.

Scott McGreggor, aged 37 of Calvestone Road, Cawston, was charged yesterday (17 December) with possession with intent to supply Heroin.

Karen Brown, aged 64 of Rounds Gardens, Rugby, was charged yesterday with supply of class A controlled drugs Heroin.

McGreggor appeared at Warwickshire Magistrates' Court yesterday and will next appear at the same court on 3 January 2020.

Brown will also appear at the same court on 3 January 2020.

The charge is in connection with an incident at an address on Calvestone Road, Cawston on Monday (16 December).
