Warwickshire Police is appealing for help to locate a Nuneaton man.

Officers would like to speak to 35-year-old Alex White in connection with an incident of assault in December in the Nuneaton area. He may have information which could assist with enquiries.

Alex is white, 6'1", of a broad build with short black hair which is likely to be shaved.

He has connections to Nuneaton.

If anyone has seen Alex or knows of his whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 31 of 23 December 2020.
