A man has been charged in connection burglary in connection with two incidents in Warwick and Leamington.

Stefan Watkins, aged 44 of Theatre Street, Warwick, was arrested yesterday (Thursday 28 May).

He has been charged with two counts of burglary, and appeared at Warwickshire Magistrates' Court this morning (Thursday 29 May). He has been remanded in custody and will next appear on 11 June 2020.

The charge is in connection with an incident on Price Road, Leamington, on 11 May 2020 and an incident on Gresham Avenue, Warwick, on 16 May 2020.
