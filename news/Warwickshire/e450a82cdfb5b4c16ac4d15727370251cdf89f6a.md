Updated News Post

**Please see updates below in bold.**

Five people have been arrested after warrants were executed this morning (Wednesday).

As a result of concerns raised by the local community, officers carried out warrants at a property on Atherstone Road in Hartshill and on Stratford Avenue in Atherstone.

A quantity of a substance - believed to be class A drugs - was removed. A vehicle - suspected to be stolen - was recovered from Stratford Avenue.

Two 18-year-old men from Birmingham has been arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply class A and theft of motor vehicle.

A 17-year-old boy of Birmingham has been arrested on suspicion of supplying a controlled drug and attempted theft of a vehicle.

A 39-year-old man from Atherstone was arrested on suspicion of theft. **He has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.**

A 37-year-old woman from Atherstone was arrested on suspicion of theft. **She has been further arrested on suspicion of being concerned in supplying a controlled drug.**

All five individuals remain in police custody at this time.
