Warwickshire Police is appealing for information to locate a 30-year-old man who is wanted for failing to attend court.

Nathan Douglas was due to appear at Warwickshire Crown Court on 15 September in connection with a charge of escaping lawful custody. He did not attend and a warrant has now been issued.

Douglas is described as white and of a medium build.

If anyone has seen Nathan Douglas or knows of his whereabouts please call Warwickshire Police on 101.

Information can also be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
