A man has been charged with three counts of assaulting an emergency worker and remanded in custody after staff were allegedly spat at and hit in Nuneaton custody block yesterday (Monday 30 November) morning.

Andrew Shirley, 25, of Thorncliffe Way, Nuneaton had been arrested after an alleged incident of criminal damage in Nuneaton.

He was also charged with two counts of criminal damage. The window of a police car was smashed during the alleged incident.

Shirley will appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court today (Tuesday 1 December).
