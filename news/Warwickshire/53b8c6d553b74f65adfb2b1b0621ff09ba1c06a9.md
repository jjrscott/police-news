A post-mortem has now taken place of a woman who was sadly found deceased in Rugby at the weekend.

Officers were called to a property on Featherbed Lane shortly after 3pm on Sunday (12 April).

A post-mortem has now concluded natural causes.

A 47-year-old man who was arrested in connection with the incident has been released without charge.

Det Insp Collette O'Keefe from Warwickshire Police said: "A thorough investigation has been conducted, and the death is not being treated as suspicious.

"I would like to thank the public for their help and support with enquiries. Our thoughts remain with the lady's family during this difficult time."
