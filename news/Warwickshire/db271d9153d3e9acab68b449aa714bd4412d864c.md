Officers have released images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with a car key burglary in Bulkington.

At around 11.15am last Thursday (2 April), an individual entered a property on Coventry Road, Bulkington, and stole the keys to a white Jaguar F Pace.

The car was then taken from the drive.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured as he may have important information which could assist with enquiries.

If anyone recognises the man or has any information which could assist with enquiries, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 127 of 2 April 2020.

Alternatively, information can be given to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their website.
