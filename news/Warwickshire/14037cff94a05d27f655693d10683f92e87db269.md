A Nuneaton man has been charged with vehicle offences in connection with a series of incidents in Nuneaton and Hinckley.

This includes the alleged theft of a car from Roston Drive, Hinckley and alleged interference with motor vehicles in Ivanhoe Avenue and Aberdeen Road, Nuneaton.

Ricky Aucott of Aberdeen Road has been charged with aggravated vehicle taking, two counts of driving without insurance, making off without payment and two counts of vehicle interference.

The offences are alleged to have occurred in October and November 2020.

The 31-year-old is due to appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court today (Wednesday 11 November).
