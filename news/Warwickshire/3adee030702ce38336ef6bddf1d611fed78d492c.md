Incident 176 of 5 March 2020

Police have been called to a serious collision involving a pedestrian and **blue Ford Focus saloon** that occurred in Vicarage Street, Nuneaton just before 1300hours today, Thursday 5 March 2020.

The pedestrian who is from Nuneaton is believed to have suffered serious injuries and was taken by ambulance to University Hospital Coventry & Warwickshire for further assessment and treatment.

A 40 year old man has been arrested on suspicion of drink driving and taken to police custody where he is awaiting questioning.

Police are warning the public in Nuneaton to expect significant delays to traffic that may affect the rush hour as people travel home. Please plan an alternative route avoiding the ring road in Nuneaton if you can.

Any witnesses to the collision are please asked to call Warwickshire Police on 01926 415415 quoting incident 176 of 5 March 2020.
