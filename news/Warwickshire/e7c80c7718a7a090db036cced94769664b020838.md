SHARED ON BEHALF OF WEST MIDLANDS POLICE

A man and a teenager have been arrested from separate addresses in Rugby on suspicion of the commission, preparation and instigation of acts of terrorism.

The 16-year-old boy and 62-year-old man are being questioned at a police station in the West Midlands.

The arrests were pre planned and intelligence led.

WMCTU is conducting the investigation with the support of Warwickshire Police.
