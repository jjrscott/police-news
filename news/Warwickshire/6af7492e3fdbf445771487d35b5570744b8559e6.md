Warwickshire Police is appealing for the public's help in locating 42-year-old Marcin Bober, who has links to Rugby.

Officers would like to speak to him in connection with a burglary in the town last year.

He is 6ft, with brown hair and brown eyes. He wears glasses.

He is believed to have connections to Coventry and Cheshire.

If anyone has seen Bober or knows of his whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101.

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or by visiting their website www.crimestoppers-uk.org
