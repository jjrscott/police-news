Four men have been charged in connection with an incident on Balcombe Road, Rugby last Sunday (7 February).

 * Callum Grace, aged 21 of Saunton Road, Rugby.
 * Samuel Wash, aged 20 of Warwick Road, Wolston.
 * Donawan Guirirab, aged 20 of Paradise Street, Rugby.
 * Farouq Semakula, aged 20 of Oakfield Road, Rugby.

All four have been charged with attempted murder, and will appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court this morning (Saturday 13 February).

Enquiries are continuing into the incident and there may be a heightened police presence in the area over the next few days.
