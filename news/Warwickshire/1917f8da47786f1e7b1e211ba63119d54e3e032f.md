Following a warrant in Rugby this morning (Thursday 11 June), man has been arrested on suspicion of class A drug offences.

Officers executed the warrant at a property in Wilf Brown Close at around 7am, following intelligence received.

A large quantity of cash, suspected cannabis and suspected evidence of supply of class A drugs were seized from the address.

A 30-year-old man has been arrested and is currently in police custody.
