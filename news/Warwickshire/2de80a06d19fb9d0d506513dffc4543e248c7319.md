Warwickshire Police is appealing to the public to help locate a Nuneaton man.

Officers would like to speak to 37-year-old Darren Whitmore in connection with an assault in December.

Darren is white, 6', and of a slim build. He has bright blue hair and a large tattoo of a panther on his left arm.He has links to Nuneaton and Bedworth.

If anyone has seen him or known of his whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101, quoting incident 228 of 3 January 2021.
