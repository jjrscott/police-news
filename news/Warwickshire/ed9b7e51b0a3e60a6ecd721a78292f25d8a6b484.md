Two men have been charged with the theft of a car from Bermuda Road, Nuneaton on Wednesday (22 January).

Daniel Godsell and Craig Thomas Cresswell were arrested in Tomkinson Road, Nuneaton, after officers spotted a suspicious vehicle.

Cresswell, 30, of Norman Place Road, Coventry will appear at Warwickshire Magistrates' Court today (Friday 24 January).

Godsell, 34, of Station Street East, Coventry, will appear at the same court on 28 February 2020.
