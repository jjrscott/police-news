Three men have been charged with drugs offences in Rugby.

Damian Morris, aged 31 of Claremont Road, Rugby was arrested on Tuesday (28 April) and later charged with possession with intent to supply heroin, possession with intent to supply heroin and crack cocaine and possession of an offensive weapon. He was remanded in custody and appeared at Warwickshire Magistrates' Court yesterday (Wednesday 29 April).

James Howson, aged 19 from Hounds Gate, Nottingham and James Main, aged 19 from Cherhill Close, Nottingham were also arrested on Tuesday and later charged with intent to supply heroin and crack cocaine. They both appeared at Warwickshire Magistrates' Court yesterday.

All three have been remanded in custody and will next appear at Warwick Crown Court on 27 May 2020.
