Three people from Nuneaton have been have been charged following an incident in the town.

David Hurst, 50, of Oaston Road, was charged with being concerned in supply of heroin, assault by beating of an emergency worker and possessing a controlled drug of Class B - Cannabis /Cannabis Resin.

Rex Paul Morris, 56, of Lister Street, was charged with being concerned in supply of heroin, supplying a controlled drug of Class A - Heroin and possessing a controlled drug of Class B - Cannabis /Cannabis Resin.

April Hope, 47, of Tomkinson Road, was charged with being concerned in supply of heroin and possessing a controlled drug of Class B - Cannabis /Cannabis Resin.

They have been remanded and are set to appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court today (Friday).

It follows an incident on Lister Street in Nuneaton yesterday (Thursday).
