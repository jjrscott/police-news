A teenage man was assaulted and robbed while he worked on a property in Coton Road, Nuneaton yesterday.

The male suspect and three other males approached the man and carried out the attack at around 2.40pm. They stole power tools before fleeing in the direction of The Rose Inn and then leaving in a green vehicle heading towards the town.

The victim suffered face injuries.

Police would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the incident or saw anyone acting suspiciously in the area around the time. They would also like to speak to anyone who may have CCTV or dash cam footage from the area around the time of the incident.

Anyone with information can call 101 quoting incident 252 of 18 July 2020. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
