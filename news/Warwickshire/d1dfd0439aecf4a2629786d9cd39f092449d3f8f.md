An 80-year-old man has died when his car left the carriageway on the A46 near Stratford Upon Avon.

The collision, which occurred close to the Alcester Rd roundabout, was reported to police at 9.47am today (Friday 25 September).

Despite the efforts of paramedics, the man was declared dead at the scene.

The man's next of kin has been informed.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or has dash cam footage is asked to call 101 quoting incident 83 of 25 September 2020.
