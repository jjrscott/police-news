19 people from across Warwickshire have been charged with drugs and firearms offences by the force's Serious and Organised Crime Unit (SOCU).

This follows a week of action across the county involving more than 200 officers.

Of the 16 men and three women arrested several have been remanded in custody and others will appear in court in November.

Two men and two women have been charged with supplying Class A drugs and possession of prohibited firearms. 15 other people have been charged with Class A and B drugs offences.

Last week, officers also recovered several kilo's of Class A and Class B Drugs.
