Police arrested a 28 year old man on suspicion of drink driving following a collision between a car and a lorry on the A446 Lichfield Road near the Belfry, Wishaw at 02.35 hours on 12 December 2019.

Following the collision, a man was taken to Birmingham Heartlands Hospital for treatment to a head injury. He has been de arrested while he receives treatment.

The road was closed for a short time to allow for collision investigators to attend the scene and for vehicles to be recovered.

Any witnesses are please asked to contact Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident 20 of 12 December 2019.
