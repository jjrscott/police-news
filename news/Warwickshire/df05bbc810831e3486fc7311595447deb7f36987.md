The family of three year old Briya Kaur Gill who died after a car collided with her in Brunswick Street, Leamington on Friday 17 July have issued the following tribute.

"To all our family, friends and well-wishers near and far. Thank you for your kind words and sympathy. We truly appreciate the outpouring of love, for now, please respect our privacy and give us time to grieve. Our world has lost its colour and our lives will never be the same. Thank you to all of the staff at Birmingham City Children's Hospital, the care and overwhelming support they provide is not repayable and we cannot express our gratitude to them. To continue to support them with all their efforts, if you would like to donate please do so by following this link:

https://www.justgiving.com/fundraising/briya-kaur-gill

Our beautiful girl has become the most beautiful angel.

Briya touched the hearts of every person she met and she lived a lifetime within her 3 years."
