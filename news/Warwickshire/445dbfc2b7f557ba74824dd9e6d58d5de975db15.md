A man who died in Rugby on Thursday evening (31 October) has been formally identified.

Cain Jackson, aged 24, sadly died following an incident on Meadow Road at around 6pm.

Investigating officers are continuing to appeal to anyone who may have witnessed the incident. Anyone with information is asked to call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident 376 of 31 October 2019.

Information can also be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org

*A 17-year-old boy who was charged with murder on Sunday appeared at Warwick Magistrates' Court yesterday morning (Monday 4 November). He has been remanded in custody and will appear at Warwick Crown Court tomorrow (Wednesday 6 November).
