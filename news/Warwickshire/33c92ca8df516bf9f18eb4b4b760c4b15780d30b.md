Updated News Post

Two males have been arrested following a car key burglary in Tanworth-in-Arden.

It happened around 2.10am on Tuesday (4 February) at a property on Bell Field.

The stolen car was located in West Midlands where it failed to stop for police. Two suspects were subsequently detained by officers.

A 16-year-old boy from Birmingham was arrested on suspicion of burglary.

An 18-year-old man from Birmingham was arrested on suspicion of burglary and possession of drugs - Class B/C.

They have been bailed until 2 March.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time of the incident.

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 quoting incident number 12 of 4 February.
