Two men have been charged with multiple drugs offences after a search was carried out at a property in Nuneaton on Friday (29 January).

John Harrison, 53, of Queen Elizabeth Road, Nuneaton, was charged on Saturday (30 January) with the following offences:

 * being concerned in supply of heroin
 * being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine
 * possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class A - heroin
 * possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class A - crack cocaine
 * acquiring/using/possessing criminal property

Delroy Facey, 59, of Atherstone Road, Hartshill, was charged with:

 * supplying a controlled drug of Class A - heroin
 * possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class A - heroin
 * possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class A - crack cocaine
 * being concerned in the supply of heroin
 * two counts of being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine
 * acquiring/using/possessing criminal property

Both men were remanded to appear at Warwick Crown Court on 4 March 2021.
