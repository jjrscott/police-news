Detectives have released a CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with an armed robbery.

The incident happened on Friday 12 February at 8.20 at a property in Melbourne Court, Croft Pool, Bedworth.

A number of males have forced entry to the property, assaulted a man and demanded cash. They fled the scene empty handed.

Detective Constable Dave Moorman from Warwickshire Police CID said: "This was a terrifying ordeal that left a man with nasty injuries to his face.

"We have been working hard to trace the suspect and would appeal for anyone who may recognise this male to get in touch with us.

"We appreciate it is not the best photo but we hope someone will recognise him.

"Likewise, if anyone witnessed anything suspicious in the area at the time of the incident, please call us on 101 quoting incident 369 of 12 February 2021. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
