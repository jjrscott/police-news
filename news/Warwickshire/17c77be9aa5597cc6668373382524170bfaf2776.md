A Leamington man will appear in court in connection with social media messages allegedly sent to a political candidate in the run up to the General Election in December 2019.

The 30-year-old has been requisitioned to appear at Warwickshire Magistrates' Court on 3 July 2020 charged with causing to be sent by a public communication network an offensive, indecent, obscene or menacing message or matter.

The messages were allegedly sent between 10 December and 12 December 2019.
