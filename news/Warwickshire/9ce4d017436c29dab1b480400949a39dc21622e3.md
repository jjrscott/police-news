Four people have been arrested on suspicion of drug offences following three warrants that were carried out in Rugby yesterday morning (Monday, 30 November).

Officers forced entry to properties on Plowman Street, The Sidings and Smith's Orchard following intelligence received in relation to drugs supply at the three addresses.

A quantity of suspected class B drugs believed to be cannabis, along with cash and mobile phones were seized from the properties.

Three men, aged 26, 30 and 42, and one woman aged 34, all from Rugby, have been arrested on suspicion of supplying or offering to supply a controlled class B drug.

They have been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Warwickshire Police is committed to tackling drug offences within the county. Anyone with concerns about the supply of drugs in their communities is asked to contact us - reports can be made via the website - Warwickshire.police.uk, on 101 or in an emergency always call 999.
