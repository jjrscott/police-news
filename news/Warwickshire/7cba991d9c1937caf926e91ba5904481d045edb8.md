Incident 374 of 4 December 2019

Warwickshire Police arrested a 30 year old man from Coventry on suspicion of drink driving after the vehicle he was driving was involved in a collision at 7.30pm on Wednesday 4 December.

Fortunately no one was injured in the collision that occurred between a white Ford Transit van and a silver Vauxhall Corsa at the top end of Tavistock Street in Leamington Spa.

Officers arrived and the driver of the white van was arrested on suspicion of driving a motor vehicle when alcohol level is above the limit.

Any witnesses are please asked to contact Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident 374 of 4 December 2019.
