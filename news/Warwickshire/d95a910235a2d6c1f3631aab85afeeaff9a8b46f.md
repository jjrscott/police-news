A man has been charged with drugs offences in Bedworth.

George Jones, aged 21 from The Mews, Bedworth, was arrested at around 12.30pm yesterday (Wednesday 14 October) on Park Road in the town.

He was charged overnight with possession with intent to supply cocaine, possession with intent to supply heroin and possession of cannabis.

He was remanded in custody and will appear via videolink at Coventry Magistrates' Court this morning (Thursday 15 October).
