Warwickshire Police is appealing for the public to help locate a Nuneaton man.

Officers would like to speak to 20-year-old Callum Jephcote in connection with an assault in the town on Wednesday (27 November).

He is described as white, of a large build, around 5ft 8ins. He has ginger hair and a tattoo of a star on one of his wrists.

If anyone has seen him or knows of his whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 42 of 27 November 2019.

Alternatively, information can be given to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their website.
