A man has been arrested in Leamington on suspicion of being in possession of Class A drugs.

A 44-year-old man from Leamington was arrested yesterday (Wednesday 9 September). He has been released on under investigation whilst enquiries continue.

Detective Constable Paul Luke from Leamington CID said: "We are committed to disrupting the supply of drugs in our community, and are working hard to tackle these offences.

"If anyone has any concerns about the supply or distribution of illegal drugs in their community, please report these to us via our website or by calling 101."

Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. Always call 999 in an emergency.
