Warwickshire Police is appealing for witnesses or information following a robbery in Nuneaton on Friday night (21 February).

At around 9.25pm, three males entered a shop an off licence on Bull Street.

They assaulted a member of staff and threatened another, before leaving the premises with a quantity of cigarettes and alcohol. They also caused significant damage to the shop.

The member of staff sustained bruising.

It's not believed any weapons were used.

Investigating officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the offence. If anyone has any information, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 427 of 21 February 2020.

Alternatively, information can be given to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their website.
