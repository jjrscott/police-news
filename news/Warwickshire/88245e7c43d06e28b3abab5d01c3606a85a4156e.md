A man has been charged with two counts of producing cannabis.

Luca Rosi, 36, of No fixed abode is accused of growing the class B drugs at properties in Hayle Close, Nuneaton and Bayton Road, Bedworth.

Rosi will appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Friday 18 December).

A 25-year-old man from Coventry arrested in connection with the same alleged offences has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
