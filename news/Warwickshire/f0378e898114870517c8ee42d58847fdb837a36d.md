**Please see update in bold below.**

A teenager has been charged with a number of offences following an incident on Bridget Street in Rugby on Tuesday (8 December).

The 17-year-old boy from Rugby, who cannot be named for legal reasons, was charged with:

• possession with intent to supply a drug of class A
• possession with intent to supply a drug of class B
• possession of a knife blade/sharp pointed article in a public place

**He went before magistrates on Wednesday (9 December) where he was remanded on conditional bail to appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court on 28 January 2021.**
