Incident 261 of 3 December 2019

Officers arrested a 55 year old man from Rugby on suspicion of drink driving after members of the public alerted Warwickshire Police to a vehicle they believed was being driven erratically on the A426 near The Avon Mill at 3.25pm on Tuesday 3 December.

Officers arrived to find a grey Ford van had been in collision with a stationary vehicle. Fortunately no one was injured in the collision.

A 55 year old man was arrested on suspicion of driving a motor vehicle when alcohol level is above the limit.

Officers would like to thank the public for their assistance in alerting them to a suspected drink driver and for their help at the scene of the collision.

Any witnesses to the incident are please asked to contact Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident 261 of 3 December 2019.

**Issued: 11.15am 4 December 2019, HN Corporate Communications**
