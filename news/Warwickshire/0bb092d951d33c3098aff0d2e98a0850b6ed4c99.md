As part of an investigation into the theft of a car from Nuneaton, Warwickshire Police has issued a CCTV image of someone they want to speak to in relation to the incident.

PC Georgina Carvey said: "At around 12.30pm on 27 February a man is reported to have stolen a Skoda Yeti from Roxborough Road.

"We're appealing for anyone who recognises this man or with any other information that could help with our enquiries to call us."

If you can help, call Warwickshire Police on non-emergency number 101. You will need to give the call handler the incident number 155 of 27 February 2020.

Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
