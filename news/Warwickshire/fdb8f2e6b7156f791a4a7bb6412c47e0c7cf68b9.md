Warwickshire Police has released an image of a man officers would like to speak to in connection with an incident on Hillmorton Lane, Rugby last month.

Officers would like to speak to the male pictured, as he may have information in relation to an indecent exposure at around midday on 17 October.

If anyone recognises him or has any information in relation to the incident, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 366 of 17 October 2020.
