Warwickshire Police is continuing to investigate an arson which took place in Nuneaton last month.

In the early hours of Wednesday 30 December, a lit firework was put through the window of a property on Park Avenue.

Thankfully the damage was not serious and there were no injuries.

Enquiries have so far not been able to identify a suspect so officers are now appealing to the public for help.

If anyone witnessed the incident or may have any CCTV in the area, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 18 of 30 December 2020.

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their website.
