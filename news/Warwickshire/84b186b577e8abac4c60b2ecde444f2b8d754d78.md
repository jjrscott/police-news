Police are appealing for information after an armed robbery in Kenilworth.

The incident occurred at around 9am today (Friday 12 February) at the Post Office in Moseley Road.

A man walked into the store and threatened staff with a knife before leaving with cash from the till.

Detective Sergeant Ollie Deakin from Warwickshire Police CID said: "Thankfully nobody was injured in this incident but this was a frightening experience for the victim.

"We're keen to speak to anyone who was in the area at the time."

Anyone with information can call 101 quoting incident 65 of 12 February 2021, or call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

**UPDATED** **9.40am, 13/02:** A 16-year-old boy from Kenilworth has been arrested in connection with the below. He is currently in police custody.
