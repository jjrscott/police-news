Detectives investigating a knifepoint shop robbery in Nuneaton have launched an appeal for information.

It is understood two suspects entered One Stop in Higham Lane at around 9.55pm on Monday (28 October) and threatened a worker with a knife and a hammer.

They left with a quantity of cash.

Police Staff Investigator Len Bell from Nuneaton CID said: "Being confronted with a knife is a terrifying ordeal.

"Fortunately, no-one was injured, but we need to find those responsible as soon as possible."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Warwickshire Police on 101. Quote incident 407 of 28 October 2019.

Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
