Officers investigating the deaths of a man and a woman in their Nuneaton home have confirmed a post mortem carried out on the couple was inconclusive and further tests will be required.

The investigation into the death of the couple - who were in their 80s - was launched on Sunday 15 November after their bodies were found in their house in Bucks Hill.

Following enquiries detectives have now confirmed they will not be treating the deaths as suspicious and a file will now be passed to the coroner.
