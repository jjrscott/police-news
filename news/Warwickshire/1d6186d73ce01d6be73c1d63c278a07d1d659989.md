Two people were arrested yesterday (Thursday 12 September) in connection with an ongoing murder investigation in Bedworth.

A 48-year-old woman from Bedworth was arrested on suspicion of attempting to pervert the course of justice and possession of a prohibited weapon, and a 35-year-old man from Bedworth was arrested on suspicion of attempting to pervert the course of justice.

Both have been released on bail.

The arrests are in connection with an investigation into the death of a 33-year-old man at a house in Bulkington Road, Bedworth, on Monday 6 May this year.
