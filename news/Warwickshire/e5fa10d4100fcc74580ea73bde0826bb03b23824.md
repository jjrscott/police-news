Lea Hiatt, aged 48 of no fixed address, was arrested on 5 February and charged today (Wednesday 17 February) with:

•Two counts of attempted burglary

•Dangerous driving

•Burglary dwelling

•Burglary other than dwelling

•Going equipped to steal

•Driving without insurance

•Driving otherwise in accordance with a license

•Receiving stolen goods.

The charges are in connection with incidents between 2018 and February 2021.

He has been remanded and will appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court today (17 February 2021).
