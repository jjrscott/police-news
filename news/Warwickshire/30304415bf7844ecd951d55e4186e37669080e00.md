Officers are appealing for help to locate 30-year-old Patrick Pritchard, who they would like to speak to in connection with an assault on 21 October.

It's believed he may have information which could assist with enquiries.

Patrick is white, 6", of a slim build. He has dark blonde/ginger hair and a beard, and 10 tattoos.

He may have connections to Nuneaton and Coventry.

If anyone has seen him or knows of his whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101, quoting incident 4 of 21 October 2020.
