Warwickshire Police is appealing to the public to help locate a 36-year-old man from Leamington.

Officers would like to speak to Ross Timlin in connection with an assault and breach of a restraining order on 13 August.

Timlin is described as a white, around 5'11" tall, of a medium build, with short cropped hair

If anyone has seen Timlin or knows of his whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 264 of 13 August 2020.
