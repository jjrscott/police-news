Warwickshire Police is appealing for witnesses following an incident in Kenilworth last month.

At around 7pm on Tuesday 17 December, a vehicle mounted the pavement near to the Jet island.

There were no reported injuries but it's believed two male pedestrians were nearby at the time, and officers would like to speak to them as potential witnesses.

The pedestrians are described as a black man, and a man wearing dark clothing.

The vehicle is believed to be a dark-coloured Mercedes or BMW.

If anyone has any information about the incident, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 402 of 17 December 2019.

**Issued:** HCB, Corporate Communications
