Thank you to everyone who shared our appeal to find Connor Dawes, 25, from Nuneaton who was wanted in connection with a robbery at a shop in College Street, Nuneaton on Wednesday (15 July) afternoon.

Following our appeal, a 25-year-old man presented himself at Nuneaton Police Station and was arrested on suspicion of robbery.

Enquiries are ongoing. Anyone with information should call 101 quoting incident 249 of 15 July 2020. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
