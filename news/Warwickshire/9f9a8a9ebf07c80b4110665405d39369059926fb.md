Investigating officers are appealing for witnesses to a robbery in Nuneaton.

A 15-year-old boy was on Copperbeach Road in the Camp Hill area around 6.30pm on Wednesday (4 December) when he was approached by an unknown individual.

The individual is reported to have threatened the teenage boy before demanding and taking his mobile phone.

He is described as a white male, around 6ft tall, of slim build. He was wearing a black zip up hoodie and black jogging bottoms.

Enquiries are ongoing and we are keen to speak to anyone who witnessed the incident, who was in the area at the time, or who might have CCTV footage.

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 quoting incident number 347 of 4 December.
