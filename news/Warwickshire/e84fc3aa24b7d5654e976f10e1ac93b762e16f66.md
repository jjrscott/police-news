Two people have been arrested after a quantity of class A drugs were seized during a warrant in Bedworth.

The warrant took place at a property on Newtown Road yesterday morning (Tuesday 11 February).

A significant quantity of what is believed to be cocaine was seized, along with a quantity of cash.

A 51-year-old man and a 52-year-old woman were both arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply cocaine. They have been released under investigation.
