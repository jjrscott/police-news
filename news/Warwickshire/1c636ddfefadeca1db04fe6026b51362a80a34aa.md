A man and woman have been arrested following an incident in Rugby yesterday afternoon (Wednesday 31 March).

At around 1.50pm, officers witnessed suspicious behaviour on Victoria Street.

Suspected crack cocaine and heroin were recovered, and a 19-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of drugs and criminal damage. He is currently in police custody.

A search of a nearby property recovered further suspected drugs and cash, and a 37-year-old woman was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of drugs. She has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
