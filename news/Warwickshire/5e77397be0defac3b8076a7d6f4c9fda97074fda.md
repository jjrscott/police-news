The family of a woman who died in Studley has released an image of her.

Ann Mowbray, aged 80, was sadly found deceased in a property on Allendale Crescent on Monday morning (17 February). A post-mortem result has concluded she died of stab wounds.

Ann's family has today released the below image of her. They have asked that their privacy is respected at this difficult time.

**Note to editors:** Ronald Mowbray, 82, of Allendale Crescent, Studley, has been charged with murder. He will next appear at Warwick Crown Court on 20 March.
