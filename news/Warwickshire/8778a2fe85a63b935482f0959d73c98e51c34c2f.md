A man has been arrested following a warrant in Nuneaton.

Officers from Nuneaton's Proactive CID team, Safer Neighbourhood Team, (SNT), Tactical Support Team and the force's dog unit carried out a search warrant at a property on Gilfil Road shortly before 9am this morning (Friday).

A quantity of what is suspected to be Class B drugs were seized.

A 26-year-old man from Nuneaton was arrested on suspicion of possession of class B drugs. He remains in police custody at this time.

Anyone with concerns about the supply of drugs in their community is asked to contact us.

Reports can be made via the force's website, on 101 or in an emergency always call 999.
