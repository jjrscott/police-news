A man has been charged with burglary in connection with three incidents in Kineton overnight on 4 and 5 October.

Marc Lelli, aged 38 of Pitwick Court, Banbury, was arrested yesterday (Wednesday 9 October).

He was charged this morning (Thursday 10 October) with three counts of burglary. He attended Coventry Magistrates' Court where he has been remanded, and will next appear at Warwick Crown Court on 7 November.
