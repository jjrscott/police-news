Thank you to everyone who shared our appeal to identify a male in connection with an alleged incident of racial abuse in King Street, Bedworth at around 8.30pm on Saturday 25 July. Following the appeal, a male contacted police and will now be spoken to by officers.

Enquiries are ongoing. Anyone with information is asked to contact Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident 427 of 25 July 2020or make an anonymous report through Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
