Warwickshire Police is appealing for witnesses or information following a robbery in Nuneaton at the weekend.

At around 2.30am on Saturday (21 March), a man in his 30s was confronted by two males in a car on Leyland Road.

The victim was then assaulted and had his wallet and mobile phone stolen from him.

He sustained facial injuries which required hospital treatment.

The suspects are described as two white men in their 30s, around 6ft, and were both clean shaven.

Enquiries are ongoing and officers would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed the offence or the vehicle in the area. If anyone has any information, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 38 of 21 March 2020.

Alternatively, information can be given to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their website.
