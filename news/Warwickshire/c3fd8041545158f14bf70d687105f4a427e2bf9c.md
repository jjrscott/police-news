A man has been charged following an incident on Chestnut Field in Rugby yesterday morning (Thursday).

Wesley Myrie, of Rounds Gardens, was charged with possessing a knife blade/sharp pointed article in a public place and obstructing/resisting a constable in the execution of his/her duty.

The 32-year-old was remanded in custody to appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court today (Friday).
