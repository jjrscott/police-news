Investigating officers are appealing for witnesses after a man exposed himself to a woman in Warwick.

A man is understood to have been on Myton Road around 6.20am on Friday (5 March) when he approached a woman in her 40s and exposed himself.

He was thought to be white, around 5ft 8in, of stocky build, in his 50's with balding hair. He wore a green jacket, blue jeans and is described as speaking with a posh accent.

The woman was uninjured but left shaken by the incident.

Investigations are ongoing and officers are keen to speak to anyone who saw the incident or who saw anyone acting suspiciously in the area at the time.

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 quoting incident 37 of 5 March.
