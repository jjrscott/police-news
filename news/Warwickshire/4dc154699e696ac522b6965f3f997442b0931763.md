Officers have arrested three men from Walsall - aged 21,22 and 26 - on suspicion of theft of fuel from lorries.

It comes after officers from the operational patrol unit spotted them acting suspiciously on the B4429 Ashlawn Road in Dunchurch in the early hours of this morning.

The three remain in custody while enquiries continue.

Anyone with information that could help police with their enquiries can call 101 quoting incident 1 of 24 November 2020. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
