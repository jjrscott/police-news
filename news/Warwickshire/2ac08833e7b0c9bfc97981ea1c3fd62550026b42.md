Warwickshire Police is appealing for help to locate a Nuneaton man.

Officers would like to speak to 23-year-old Rory McAdoo in connection with an assault in the town on 30 September.

Rory is described as white, 5'9", of a slim build. He has short blonde hair, a blonde beard, and has tattoos on his right arm.

He is believed to frequent the Hilltop and Camp Hill areas of Nuneaton.

If anyone has seen him or knows of his whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting 289 of 30 September 2020.

**Issued:** HCB, Corporate Communications
