A man has died following an altercation outside a house in Kenilworth.

Police responded to the report of the incident in Hyde Road at 10.29pm on 13 September.

A 66-year-old man from Kenilworth was taken to hospital with a serious head injury. He died in hospital on Saturday (21 September) and a manslaughter investigation has been launched in order to establish the full circumstances.

Immediately following the incident a 67-year-old woman from Kenilworth and 46-year-old man from Kenilworth were both arrested on suspicion of assault and subsequently released under investigation.

A post mortem has been conducted but further tests are required to establish the exact cause of death.

The deceased's next of kin has been informed.

Anyone with information that could help police with their enquiries should call 101 quoting incident 512 of 13 September 2019. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Posted Sam Cook, Communications Officer
