A Rugby man has been charged with drugs and weapon offences.

Scott Satturley, aged 46, of no fixed abode, was charged on Monday (30 November) with two counts of possession of a controlled Class A drug, one count of possession of a controlled Class B drug and one count of possession of a bladed article in a public place.

He has been bailed to appear at Warwickshire Magistrates Court on 22 December 2020.

The charges are in connection with an incident in in Brandon on Sunday, 29 November.
