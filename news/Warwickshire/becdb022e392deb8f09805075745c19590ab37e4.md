Police investigating a report of criminal damage in Southam have issued an appeal for witnesses.

Officers would like to hear from anyone with information about a brick being thrown through the window of a house in Springfield Grove.

The incident happened at around midday on Monday.

The offender is described as being a white male, around 15-16 years old and about 5ft 3ins wearing a black or grey tracksuit with the hood up.

Anyone with information is urged to contact Warwickshire Police by going to www.warwickshire.police.uk/ro/report/ or by calling 101. Please quote incident 136 of 19 October 2020.

Information can also be passed anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111, or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org
