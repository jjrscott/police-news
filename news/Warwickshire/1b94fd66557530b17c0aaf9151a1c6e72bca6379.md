Warwickshire Police has released an image of a man officers would like to speak to, in connection with a robbery in Nuneaton in March.

At around 2.30am on Saturday 21 March, a man in his 30s was confronted by two males in a car on Leyland Road.

The victim was assaulted and had his wallet and mobile phone stolen from him. He sustained facial injuries which required hospital treatment.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured, as he may have information which could assist with enquiries. If anyone recognises him or has any information on the incident, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 38 of 21 March 2020.

Alternatively, information can be given to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their website.
