Warwickshire Police is appealing for help to locate 24-year-old Natasha Wood.

Officers would like to speak to her in connection with a harassment incident in Rugby earlier this month.

Natasha is white, around 5'7", of a slim build, with long dark hair. She speaks with a Birmingham accent and has links to Rugby.

If anyone has seen Natasha or knows of her whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 266 of 3 of April 2020.
