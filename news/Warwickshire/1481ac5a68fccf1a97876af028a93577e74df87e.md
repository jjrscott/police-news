Officers have charged Jasbinder Gahir, 57, and Rohan Gahir, 23, both from Maidenhead, with murder, following the death of a woman in Leamington Spa on Monday (24th August).

Both men have been remanded in custody and will appear at Coventry Magistrates Court this morning (Thursday 27th August).

Officers are not looking to speak to anyone else in connection with this incident.
