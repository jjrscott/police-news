A man has been charged in connection with a burglary at a flat in Leamington.

Cash and a safe were stolen during the burglary which occurred in Brunswick Street at some time between 13 and 17 April.

Paul Dean, 53, of Rugby Road, Leamington has been charged with burglary. He appeared at Coventry Magistrates' Court today (Tuesday 21 April) where he was bailed to appear at Warwick Crown Court on 22 May 2020.
