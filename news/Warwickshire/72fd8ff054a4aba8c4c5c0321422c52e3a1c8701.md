A man has been arrested in connection with an incident of violent disorder in Nuneaton.

The 19-year-old man from Birmingham was arrested on suspicion of violent disorder.

Warwickshire Police arrested the man the incident in Pool Bank Street at around 2.30pm on Friday (27 December).

Officers responded to a report of a violent disorder involving a number of people. Following the incident a teenage man was taken to hospital with a stab wound to his upper arm. His injuries are not considered life-changing and he has since been released from hospital.

Warwickshire Police has asked for anyone with CCTV, dash-cam or phone footage of the incident, or any information which could help with their enquiries to please contact them on 101.

Alternatively, you can pass information on via Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111.
