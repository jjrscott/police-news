Four males from Rugby are to appear at Warwickshire Magistrates' Court charged with section 18 - wounding with intent and violent disorder.

The males are:

 * Alanzo Scarlett, 22, of Claremont Road
 * Cheetij Pun, 20, of Morton Gardens
 * Bryan Junior Ayamba-Ashu, 21, of Phipps Avenue
 * A 15-year-old boy who can't be named for legal reasons

They have been charged in connection with an incident in Clifton Road, Rugby on 31 July. A teenage man was treated for a stab wound following the incident.

Scarlett, Ayamba-Ashu and the 15-year-old boy are set to appear at court on 5 February 2021 while Pun will appear before magistrates on 26 February 2021.
