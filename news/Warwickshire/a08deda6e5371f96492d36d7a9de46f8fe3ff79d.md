Warwickshire Police wants to speak to the man pictured below in connection with an allegegation of assault.

Officers say that Joshua Brandy (also known as J) from Atherstone also has connections to Nuneaton, Bedworth and Coventry.

Officers are appealing to the public for help.

The alleged incident occurred at a property in Atherstone on 10 February.

If anyone has any information as to where the 26-year-old could be they are being urged to contact the police immediately.

He is 5 foot 4 inches tall and of slim build with dark hair and a beard.

If you see Brandy call 999. If you have any information that could help police find him call 101. You can quote incident number 41 of 10 February 2021.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
