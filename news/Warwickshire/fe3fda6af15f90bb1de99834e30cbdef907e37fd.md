A man has been charged with possession of an imitation firearm with intent to cause fear of violence, threats to kill, affray and criminal damage.

David Shuttlewood, aged 35 of High Cross Road, Lutterworth, was remanded in custody overnight and will appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court today (Friday 28 August).

The charge is in connection with an incident on Newtown Road, Bedworth, at around 6.30pm on Wednesday (26 August).
