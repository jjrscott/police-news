We are appealing to the public to help us find wanted man Joseph Banks.

Banks, 27, from Atherstone, is wanted in connection with a blackmail in the town.

He is also wanted for failing to appear Warwickshire Magistrates' Court in October to face a charge of possession of class B drugs.

Banks has connections to Polesworth, Tamworth and Grendon.

If you see Banks call 999. If you have information on his whereabouts call 101. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
