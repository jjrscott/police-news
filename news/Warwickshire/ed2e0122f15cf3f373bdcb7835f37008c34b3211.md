Warwickshire Police is appealing for information to help locate a Nuneaton man.

Officers would like to speak to 30-year-old Patrick Pritchard in connection with an assault in the town on 9 May 2020.

Pritchard is white, with short brown hair and a ginger beard. He is approximately 6ft tall, and may have a black and white dog with him. He has connections to Coventry.

If anyone has seen him or knows of his whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 549 of 9 May 2020.
