Officers investigating the theft of power tools in Rugby Road, Church Lawford on Saturday (20 June) afternoon are trying to identify a tractor driver who attempted to block the thieves in.

At around 2.55pm the victim reports a blue Vauxhall Astra containing two males pulling up at the location. One of the men got out and stole the tools.

A passing farmer tried to block the vehicle in with his large red tractor. Police are now trying to identify the tractor driver as he may have information that could help them identify the thieves.

Anyone with information that can help police with their enquiries can call 101 quoting incident 326 of 20 May 2020. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
