Were you outside the Fuzzy Duck pub in Nuneaton in the early hours of Saturday morning (11 July)?

We're appealing for anyone who was in the area around 12.30am and witnessed an assault to get in touch.

A man is his 20s was standing as part of group when a verbal disagreement is understood to have taken place with another group.

The victim was reportedly punched to the face - causing him to fall and hit his head on the pavement.

He suffered a cut to the head and facial injuries, which are not believed to be life-threatening at this time.

Enquiries are currently ongoing and officers would like to speak to anyone who can help or may have been in the area at the time of the incident.

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 quoting incident number 13 of 11 July.
