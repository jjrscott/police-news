Warwickshire Police is appealing to the public to help locate a missing man from Dordon.

Officers are currently trying to locate 33-year-old Denholm Price, who was last seen on Sunday (23 August).

Denholm is white, and is described as having a slim build with brown hair and facial stubble.

He was last seen wearing blue shorts and a blue and white checked short-sleeved shirt.

Denholm is thought to have connections to Dordon, Polesworth and Tamworth.

If anyone has seen Dordon or knows of his whereabouts, please do not approach him but call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 382 of 23 August 2020.
