Warwickshire Police is appealing for help to locate a 27-year-old man.

Officers would like to speak to Curtis Flint in connection with a burglary which took place in Bedworth in February.

He has short, dark-coloured hair and is described as being of a medium build and around 5ft 7in tall.

He has connections to Nuneaton & Coventry, and also to Hinckley in Leicestershire.

If anyone has seen Flint or knows of his whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 145 of 21 February 2020.
