A man has been charged with assaulting an officer following an incident in Rugby in February.

Jason Killick, aged 50 of Avondale Road, Brandon, Coventry, was charged with Assault Police - Assault occasioning actual bodily harm (ABH) last Tuesday (12 May 2020). He will appear at Leamington Magistrates' Court on 17 July 2020.

The charge is in connection with an incident on Little Church Street, Rugby, on 15 February 2020.
