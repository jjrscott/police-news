A Nuneaton man has been charged with drugs offences.

Jordan Bates, aged 19 of Hickman Road, Nuneaton was charged on Friday (23 October) with being concerned in the supply of cocaine, being concerned in the supply of heroin, possession with intent to supply cocaine and possession with intent to supply heroin.

He has been remanded in custody and will appear at Wolverhampton Crown Court on 20 November 2020.

The charges are in connection with an incident in Nuneaton on 22 October 2020.
