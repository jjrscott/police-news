A 29-year-old man from Coventry has been arrested in connection with an alleged sexual assault of a woman in Leamington last weekend.

The incident took place in Dormer Place at the junction with the Parade at around 10.30pm last Saturday (1 August).

The man was arrested last night.

Enquiries are ongoing. If anyone has any information and has not yet spoken to officers, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident 484 of 1 August 2020. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
