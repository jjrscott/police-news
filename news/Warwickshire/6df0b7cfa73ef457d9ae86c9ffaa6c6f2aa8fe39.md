A man has been charged with conspiracy to murder in connection with an incident in Ryton-on Dunsmore last year.

John Kiely, aged 40 of Darlingscote Road, Shipston-on-Stour, was charged yesterday (20 October).

He was remanded in custody and appeared at Warwickshire Magistrates' Court today (Monday 21 October).

He will next appear at Warwick Crown Court on 18 November 2019.

The charge is in connection with an incident on the A445 Leamington Road on 16 October 2018.
