Police are appealing for information about the theft of a converted horse trailer from Long Itchington.

The trailer, which had been converted into a catering trailer, was stolen from a car park in Southam Road sometime between 4pm on 20 December and 3pm on 21 December.

The below photos of the trailer have been released and officers investigating what happened are asking for anyone who sees the trailer or knows of its whereabouts to come forward.

If you have information please call 101 quoting incident 263 of 21 December 2020. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
