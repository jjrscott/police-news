Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Nuneaton in the early hours of this morning (Thursday 20 February) where cash was stolen.

The burglary took place at Bunches Florist in Coleshill Road at around 3am. Two offenders with their faces covered forced entry to the shop before stealing the cash and fleeing.

Police are appealing for anyone with information to contact them on 101 quoting incident 61 of 20 February 2020. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
