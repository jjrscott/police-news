Officers are currently in attendance on Grosvenor Road and Boughton Road, Rugby, following an incident at around 5pm this evening.

A man in his 20s has been taken to hospital, his injuries are not believed to be life-threatening.

A 16-year-old boy and a 17-year-old boy have been arrested on suspicion of possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life.

Det Insp Martyn Kendall said: "This is currently being treated as an isolated incident, and an investigation has been launched.

"Two males are currently in police custody. More information will be issued when available."
