Investigators have issued an appeal for information following a suspected arson in Alderman's Green.

Warwickshire Police was called at 4.07am on Monday 22 March to a report that a car and van were on fire while parked in Parrotts Grove.

Officers attended the scene along with fire crews who extinguished the blaze.

Officers are appealing for anyone who was in the area around the time of the incident and saw anything suspicious or unusual to contact them.

Anyone with information should call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident 14 of 22 March 2021.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, or you complete the online form.
