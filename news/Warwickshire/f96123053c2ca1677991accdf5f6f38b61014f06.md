Warwickshire Police is appealing for witnesses or information following a collision in Elliot's Field retail park on Friday (22 November).

At around 5.10pm, a blue Volvo XC90 was in collision with a pedestrian in the car park outside Marks and Spencer.

The pedestrian, a woman in her 70s, sustained injuries and was taken to hospital by ambulance.

Investigating officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision. If you have any information, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 361 of 22 November 2019.
