A man and a woman have been arrested on suspicion of drugs offences following a warrant in Rugby yesterday (Thursday 25 June).

At around 12.40pm, officers from Rugby Proactive CID executed a search warrant at a property on Fosterd Road in Rugby.

A quantity of suspected class A drugs, drug paraphernalia and cash were seized.

A 60-year-old woman and a 31-year-old man were both arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply class A drugs. They have been released under investigation.

Warwickshire Police is committed to tackling drug offences within the county. Anyone with concerns about the supply of drugs in their communities is asked to contact us - reports can be made via the force's website, on 101 or in an emergency always call 999.
