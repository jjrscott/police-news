As part of the force's ongoing work to tackle illegal drugs within our communities, officers from the Offender Management Unit have arrested a 20-year-old man from Wolverhampton.

The man was arrested at an address in Atherstone yesterday morning (Thursday 12 September) on suspicion of possession of crack cocaine with intent to supply.

A quantity of cash and crack cocaine was also seized from him.

The man has been released under investigation, enquiries remain ongoing.

**Issued: 11.10am, Friday 13 September, HCB, Corporate Communications**
