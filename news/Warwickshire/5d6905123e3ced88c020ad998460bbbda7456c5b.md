Warwickshire Police is appealing for witnesses following an exposure incident in Atherstone yesterday evening (Wednesday 17 March).

At around 5.45pm, a man is reported to have exposed himself in Royal Meadow Park.

The man is described as white and in his mid 30s.

Enquiries are currently ongoing but anyone who was in the area or noticed anyone acting suspiciously at the time is asked to call 101 quoting incident 305 of 17 March 2021.

Information can also be given anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or reporting via their website.
