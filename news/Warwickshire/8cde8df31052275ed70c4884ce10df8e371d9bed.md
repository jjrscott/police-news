A woman has been charged in connection with an incident in Stratford on Monday night (6 April).

Donna Hurford, aged 34 of Masons Road, Stratford, was charged last night (Tuesday 7 April) with the following:

• Two counts of theft from shop

• Two counts of common assault

• One count of assault by beating

• One count of assault on emergency worker

• One count of racially aggravated public order

She was remanded in custody and will appear at Warwickshire Magistrates' Court by virtual link today (Wednesday 8 April).

The charges are in relation to an incident on Birmingham Road on Monday night (6 April) and an incident at a medical premises yesterday (Tuesday 7 April).
