Warwickshire Police is appealing for help to locate a missing man from Rugby.

61-year-old Keith Appleton was last seen at 9:45am yesterday (Monday 18 January) on Newbold Rd, Rugby.

Keith is white, 5'10", of a slim build.

He is believed to be wearing a black jacket with a hood, a shirt and jumper, smart suit trousers and formal shoes. He may also be carrying a clip board.

Officers and Keith's family are growing concerned for his welfare. If anyone has seen him or knows of his whereabouts, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 220 of 18 January 2021.
