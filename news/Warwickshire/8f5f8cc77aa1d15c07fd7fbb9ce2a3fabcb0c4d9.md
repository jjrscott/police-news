Five people have been arrested for suspected drugs offences following a warrant in Leamington this morning (Monday 21 September).

At around 9.50am, officers entered a property on Gordon Street.

Suspected class A drugs were seized from the property, and five people were arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of class A drugs.

A 21-year-old man, a 34-year-old man, a 36-year-old man, a 50-year-old man and a 50-year-old woman are all currently in police custody.
