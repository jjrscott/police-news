Devon Bailey, 21, of Gray Street, Northampton, has been charged with possession of crack cocaine with intent to supply, possession of heroin with intent to supply, possession of a knife in a public place and acquiring criminal property.

Bailey was arrested on Monday (30 September) afternoon after officers from the Rugby Offender Management Unit spotted a suspected drug deal while on patrol in Market Place, Rugby.

The officers seized crack cocaine, heroin, a knife, a mobile phone and cash.

Bailey appeared at Warwick Magistrates' Court on Tuesday (1 October) and was remanded in custody to appear at Warwick Crown Court on 29 October 2019.
