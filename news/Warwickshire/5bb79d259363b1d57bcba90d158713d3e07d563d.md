A man has been arrested in connection with an attempted arson at a home in Nuneaton.

The 43-year-old from Bedworth was arrested this morning (Monday 2 September) on suspicion of conspiracy to commit arson and is in police custody.

It comes following an alleged attempted arson attack on a property in Ansley Road at around 9.45pm on Wednesday (28 August).

Warwickshire Police is appealing to anyone with information to get in touch. Anyone with information can call 101 quoting incident 433 of 28 August 2019. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
