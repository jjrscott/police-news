Warwickshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision near Bearley this morning (Friday 5 March).

At around 5.50am, emergency services were called to a report of a white Vauxhall Corsa in collision with a telegraph pole on Birmingham Road.

The driver of the car - a man in his 60s - was sadly pronounced deceased at the scene. His next of kin has been informed.

There were no other injuries.

Investigating officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision or who may have any dashcam footage. If you have any information, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident 27 of 5 March 2021.
