Two people have been arrested in connection with drugs offences in Nuneaton.

At around 4pm on Monday (30 November), officers stopped a Volkswagen Passat on Manor Court Road.

Two occupants were searched, and a large quantity of cash and a mobile phone were found in the car.

A 28-year-old man and a 44-year-old man, both from Birmingham, were arrested on suspicion of concerned in the supply of controlled drugs.

Following a subsequent search of a property linked to one of the males, a further substantial amount of cash was seized from a bedroom.

Both men have been released on police bail while enquiries continue.
