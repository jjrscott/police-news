Three men and a teenage boy are due to attend court in connection with a motorbike theft in Stratford in June.

All four have been sent postal requisitions to attend Warwickshire Magistrates' Court on 12 October 2020.

The incident took place in Bridgefoot, Stratford at around 4pm on 2 June 2020.

 * A 17-year-old boy from Stoke-on-Trent will face charges of theft of a motor vehicle, attempted theft of a motor vehicle, aggravated vehicle taking and dangerous driving.
 * A 20-year-old man from Birmingham will face charges of theft of a motor vehicle, attempted theft of a motor vehicle, aggravated vehicle taking and dangerous driving.
 * A 21-year-old man from Birmingham will face charges of theft of a motor vehicle, attempted theft of a motor vehicle, aggravated vehicle taking and dangerous driving.
 * A 23-year-old man from Solihull will face charges of theft of a motor vehicle, attempted theft of a motor vehicle and dangerous driving
