An appeal has been launched after a woman was racially abused in the car park of a Rugby supermarket.

The incident occurred at Aldi in Hillmorton Road at 10.35am yesterday morning (Wednesday 18 March).

The woman - who is in her 40s - was abused by a man as she parked her car.

Police believe someone witnessed the incident and they are keen to speak to that person.

If you have any information that could help with this investigation please call 101, quoting incident number 129 of 18 March 2020.

Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
