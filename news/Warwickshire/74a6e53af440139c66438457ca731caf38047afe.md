Warwickshire Police is appealing for dash cam footage or witnesses to a collision involving a pregnant woman and a car on Lower Lodge Avenue, Rugby at approximately 11.23hrs on Wednesday 4 November 2020

The collision is believed to have occurred when the female pedestrian was looking to cross from the footpath on Spinney Lane onto Lower Lodge Avenue, opposite some parked cars.

Any witnesses or anyone with dashcam footage is please asked to contact Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident 127 of 4 November 2020. Information can also be provided anonymously through independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111
