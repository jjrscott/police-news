Six people have been arrested following a warrant at a house in Leamington yesterday morning (Thursday 7 January).

At around 8.20am, officers executed a warrant at a property on Rugby Road.

A significant amount of suspected Class A drugs, Class B drugs and cash was seized from the location, and six people were arrested. They are:

•A 29-year-old man from Warwick was arrested on suspicion of possession of class B or C drugs.

•A 29-year-old man from Coventry was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in supplying controlled drugs.

•A 31-year-old woman from Leamington was arrested on suspicion of possession With Intent to supply - Class A

•A 45-year-old man from Leamington was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in supplying controlled drugs.

•A 24-year-old woman from Nuneaton was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in supplying controlled drugs.

•A 52-year-old man from Warwick was arrested on suspicion of possession of concerned in supplying controlled drugs.

All are currently in police custody.

Anyone with any information concerning suspected drug dealing in their area is asked to report it to the force via our website or by calling 101.
