Three people have been arrested after a man was injured in an incident in Leamington.

Police were called to Clemen's Street at 7.35pm yesterday (Wednesday 13 November) following a report of disorder.

Officers found a man in his 50s with a head injury.

The man was taken to hospital. His condition is not thought to be life-changing or life-threatening.

A short time later a 24-year-old man of no fixed abode and a 21-year-old man from Leamington were arrested on suspicion of assault.

A 21-year-old woman from Leamington has been arrested on suspicion of assault and possession of class A drugs with intent to supply.

Inquiries continue to establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Witnesses should call Warwickshire Police on 101, quoting incident 360 of 13 November 2019. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
