Warwickshire Police is appealing for help to locate a missing 39-year-old man.

Jamie Dixon was reported missing from the Upper Holly Walk area of town yesterday afternoon (Wednesday 24 February).

He is 5'11", and has short black hair, stubble and blue eyes.

Officers and Jamie's family are growing concerned for his welfare and would ask anyone who has seen him to please call Warwickshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 250 of 24 February 2021.
