Warwickshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision in Nuneaton on Saturday afternoon (11 April).

At around 1.50pm, a single vehicle collision took place on Kingswood Road, involving a grey Yamaha MT125cc motorcycle.

The motorcyclist, a man in his mid-20s, was taken to hospital where he sadly died on Sunday. His next of kin has been informed.

Road closures were in place whilst emergency services were in attendance.

If anyone witnessed the collision, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 275 of 11 April 2020.
