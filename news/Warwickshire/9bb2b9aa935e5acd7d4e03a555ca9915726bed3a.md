A man has been arrested on suspicion of drugs offences in Rugby.

At around 1.35pm yesterday (Wednesday 27 May), a suspected drug deal was witnessed on Norman Road. A quantity on controlled drugs and cash were recovered, and a 22-year-old man from Birmingham arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of class A controlled drugs. He has been released on bail.

Warwickshire Police is committed to targeting drug offences and county lines drug-dealing in our communities. If you have information or concerns about people involved in drug dealing or vulnerable people being exploited by drug dealers in your area, we would like to hear from you.

Information from the public is vital because it helps officers to get a better picture of the problem and who is involved. Anyone with any concerns or information on suspected drug dealing is asked to contact police on 101 or report via the website. In an emergency, always call 999.
