A teenage boy has been arrested and charged in connection with a car key burglary in Bulkington on Sunday (19 January).

A 17-year-old boy was charged last night with burglary dwelling, taking a car without consent, and being carried in a motor vehicle taken without the owner's consent.

He will appear at Coventry Magistrates' Court this morning (Tuesday 21 January).

The charge is in connection with an incident on Dingley Road, Bulkington, at around 3pm on Sunday.
