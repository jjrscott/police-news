Emergency services are currently at the scene of a light aircraft crash at Wellesbourne Airfield.

Police were called shortly after 9.30am this morning (Saturday 4 September).

Two people were inside the aircraft - there are no reported serious injuries.

The Civil Aviation Authority has been informed.
