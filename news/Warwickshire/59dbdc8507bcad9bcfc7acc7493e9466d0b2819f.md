Warwickshire Police is appealing for witnesses or information following a suspected arson in Leamington last month.

Between 6pm and 9pm on Tuesday 29 October, it's reported a doorstep was set on fire in Wych Elm Grove. This is currently believed to be suspicious.

There were no reported injuries.

This is currently being treated as an isolated incident.

If anyone has information or witnessed any suspicious behaviour in the area, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 455 of 29 October 2019.

Alternatively, information can be given to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their website.
