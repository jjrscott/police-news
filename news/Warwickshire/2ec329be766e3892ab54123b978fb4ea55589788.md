A 25-year-old man from Rugby is set to appear in court after being charged with drugs offences.

Joseph Shallcross, of Murray Road, was charged with possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of class A - heroin and possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of class A - crack cocaine.

He was remanded and is set to go before Coventry Magistrates' Court today (Wednesday).

It follows an incident on Wells Street in Rugby yesterday (Tuesday).
