A further arrest has been made in connection with the investigation into the death of 34-year-old Carl Moorhouse in Leamington on Thursday 28 May.

A 24-year-old man from Birmingham was arrested today (Wednesday 17 June) on suspicion of murder. He is currently in police custody.

Eight other people who were previously arrested in connection with the offence (a 21-year-old man, a 22-year-old man, a 25-year-old man, a 26-year-old man, two 29-year-old men, a 33-year-old man and a 34-year-old woman) remain on police bail whilst enquiries are ongoing.
