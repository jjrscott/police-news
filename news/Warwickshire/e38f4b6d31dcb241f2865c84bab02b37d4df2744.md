A man has been charged with conspiracy to commit GBH in connection with a Leamington murder investigation.

Javantry Scott, aged 23 of Osborne Road, Birmingham, was charged on Friday (19 March). He appeared at Coventry Magistrates' Court on Friday and will next appear at Warwick Crown Court on 7 April.

The charge is in connection with an investigation into the death of 34-year-old Carl Moorhouse in Leamington on 28 May 2020.

Seven other people have also been charged in connection with this investigation: https://www.warwickshire.police.uk/news/warwickshire/news/2021/march/two-charged-with-murder--leamington/
