Two men have been arrested on suspicion of drug offences after their car was stopped in Tachbrook Road, Leamington.

Officers seized a large quantity of what they believe to be cannabis and cocaine, and cash after the car was stopped yesterday (Sunday 23 February) afternoon.

Two men from Warwick, aged 36 and 22, were arrested on suspicion of possession of drugs with intent to supply.

The 36-year-old was bailed until March while enquiries continue.

The 22-year-old was released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Anyone with information that could help police with their enquiries should call 101 quoting incident 223 of 23 February 2020. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
