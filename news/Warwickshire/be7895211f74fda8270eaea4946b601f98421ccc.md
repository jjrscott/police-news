A teenager has been arrested on suspicion of drugs offences in Rugby.

At around 3pm yesterday (Tuesday 14 April), officers stopped a 17-year-old boy from Leicester on Lawford Road.

A quantity of suspected drugs were found, and he was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of class A controlled drugs. He has been released on bail whilst enquiries continue.
