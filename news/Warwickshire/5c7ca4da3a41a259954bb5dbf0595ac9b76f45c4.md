A 16-year-old boy from London has been charged in connection with an incident on Tachbrook Road, Leamington on 15 January.

The boy was charged this morning with kidnap and perverting the course of justice.

He has been remanded in custody and will appear at Warwick Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Monday 3 February).
