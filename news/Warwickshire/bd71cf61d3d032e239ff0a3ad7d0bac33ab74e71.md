Warwickshire Police is appealing for witnesses or information after a man was found injured in Rugby.

At around 9.40pm on Friday 29 November, a man in his 20s was found injured on Bilton Road.

He was taken to hospital where he received treatment for a broken leg and facial injuries. He has since been discharged.

Officers are currently investigating how the man sustained his injuries, and would like to speak to anyone who may have seen him on the night.

We would also like to speak to the driver of a small red car, which is believed to have been at the scene at the time with its hazard lights on.

If anyone has any information, please call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 447 of 29 November 2019.
