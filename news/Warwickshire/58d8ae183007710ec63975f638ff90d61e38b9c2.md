Police are appealing for witnesses and information following an assault.

A women in her 40s suffered injuries to her face during the attack in Overview Way, Rugby.

At around 6.15am on Saturday (18 July) witnesses report seeing a man push the victim to the floor and then kick her.

Police believe a large number of people were nearby waiting for a lift following the night shift at Hermes. They are keen to speak to anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information that could help police with their enquiries.

Anyone who saw what happened or has any conformation should call Warwickshire Police on 101 quoting incident 102 of 18 July 2020. Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
