Officers are appealing for public help in identifying this man after a distraction theft in the city centre on Saturday 17 October.

At 4pm the victim was in a restaurant in Market Street, Leicester, when two people approached him and asked for directions. Shortly afterwards he discovered his mobile telephone had been stolen.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who recognises the man in the image as he may be able to assist with their enquiries.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact 101, quoting crime number 20*548375.
