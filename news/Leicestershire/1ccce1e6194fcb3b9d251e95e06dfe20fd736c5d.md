A motorhome has been stolen from a property in the Azalea Drive area of Burbage at around 11.10pm on Sunday 29 December.

The silver coloured motorhome is an Auto Trail 635 Sport. It was parked on the driveway of the house when it was taken.

The registration of the motorhome at the time it was taken was MM19 XSR.

If anyone has any information about who may be responsible for taking it, or has seen a motorhome that looks similar to the one pictured, please contact Leicestershire Police on 101.

Quote incident number 19*695146.
