Three people have been arrested following a stabbing in Leicester city centre on 20 December.

On 30 December, an appeal was issued asking for public help to identify three men in connection with the incident in Granby Street.

A 17-year-old boy was taken to hospital for treatment and later discharged.

Two men aged 19 and a 17-year-old youth were arrested on connection with the incident.

All three, who are from Leicester, have been released on bail and enquiries are continuing.

Officers would like to thank those who shared our appeal.
