Officers have released details of items they would like to reunite with their owner after a search warrant was executed in Leicester.

On Friday 12 March a warrant was executed at a property on West Street and a number of items that officers believe to have been stolen were recovered.

A 38-year-old man from Leicester was arrested for handling stolen goods and was released under investigation.

The items are:

 * Dewalt knee pads
 * Makita handle
 * Orange Bosch box
 * Armorgard Ox box
 * Premier Products mini - vacuum cleaner
 * Oerlikon Welder TDC 160
 * Bosch GST 60 PB Jigsaw
 * High voltage cabling
 * 5 star case - by design
 * Mizuno Golf shoes
 * Callaway golf bag containing various Mizuno golf clubs

If anyone recognises these items and can provide proof of ownership, they should contact 101 quoting reference 21*139968.
