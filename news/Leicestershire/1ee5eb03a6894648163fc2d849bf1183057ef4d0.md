Detectives have named a man who was fatally stabbed in Hinckley.

Police were called at around 12.55am yesterday morning (Monday 7 October) to a report of a man who had been stabbed and found injured in Sword Drive, Hinckley.

Officers attended the location and the man was pronounced dead at the scene.

The man has been formally identified as 18-year-old Joshua Taylor.

A post-mortem examination has been carried out and found Joshua died of a single stab wound.
