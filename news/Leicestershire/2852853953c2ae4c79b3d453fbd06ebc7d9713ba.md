A man has been charged with causing death by careless driving following a collision in Loughborough last year.

Matthew Allen, 27, of Tyler Avenue, Loughborough, has been charged in connection with the incident which happened in Holt Drive, Loughborough, on 11 November last year.

Melvin Shephard, 64, of Loughborough, died at the scene of the incident.

Allen is due to appear at Leicester Magistrates Court tomorrow (1 November).
