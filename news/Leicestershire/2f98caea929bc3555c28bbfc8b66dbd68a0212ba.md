A number of people charged with drugs offences as part of the Operation Lionheart enforcement activity have appeared at Leicester Magistrates' Court today (Wednesday 29 May).

**Byron Pratt** , 18, of Limehurst Avenue, Loughborough, has pleaded guilty to two counts of supplying class A drugs and one count of being concerned in the supply of class A drugs.

**Laura Harley** , 41, of Chestnut Road, Glenfield, has pleaded guilty to one count of supplying class A drugs.

They will be sentenced on 1 July at Leicester Crown Court.

**Elizabeth Nash** , 42, of Burfield Street, Leicester, pleaded guilty to a section five public order offence which happened on 9 April 2019. She was given a conditional discharge for six months.

**Josh O 'Neill **gave no indication of plea and will be appearing in court again on a date yet to be set.
