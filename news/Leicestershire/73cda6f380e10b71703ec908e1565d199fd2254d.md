A Leicestershire Police staff member has been charged with misusing force computers.

Ajit Singh, 48, of Leicester, appeared at Leicester Magistrates' yesterday (22 August) charged with one count of computer misuse between 2002 and 2018.

He was convicted of the offence and will be sentenced at the same court on 12 September.
