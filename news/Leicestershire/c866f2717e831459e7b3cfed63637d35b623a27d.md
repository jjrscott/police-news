Some of our cases this month which have led to charges and/or convictions in court.

**_Convictions_**

**_Gang jailed for total of more than 160 years for large scale drug supply_**

Thirteen members of a 25 strong Leicester based organised crime group were sentenced for their role in large scale drug supply across the county.

The 12 men and one woman - who were convicted or pleaded guilty to offences at earlier hearings - appeared at Leicester Crown Court this month for sentencing.

Officers from East Midlands Special Operations Unit (EMSOU) spent an undeterminable number of hours investigating the whole group, who used dedicated phone lines in order to facilitate the supply and distribution of heroin, cocaine and crack cocaine throughout the county between July 2017 and September 2018.

More information: https://www.leics.police.uk/news/leicestershire/news/2021/february/gang-jailed-for-total-of-more-than-160-years-for-large-scale-drug-supply-across-leicestershire/

**_Man who sexually abused two girls sentenced to 16 years imprisonment_**

A man who sexually abused two women when they were just young girls has been sentenced to 16 years imprisonment.

Martin Roberts, 70, of Oxford Street, Leicester, committed the offences in Leicester on various occasions between 1973 and 1992.

In 2018, both victims reported matters to police and an investigation began.

More information: https://www.leics.police.uk/news/leicestershire/news/2021/february/16-year-sentence-for-man-who-sexually-abused-girls-more-than-40-years-ago/

**_Man who raped woman in her home jailed_**

A man who raped a woman at her home has been sentenced to six years imprisonment.

Jamie Russell, 31, of Rancliffe Crescent, Leicester, was found guilty of rape following a trial at Leicester Crown Court.

_Man jailed for firearms and drugs offences_

A 43-year-old man has been jailed for thirteen-and-a-half years after pleading guilty to firearms and drugs offences.

Keon Campbell - who is also known as Leon Hall - was arrested in August 2019 by police while driving his car in Cooks Lane, Wigston. Information received suggested he had been involved in the supply of class A drugs.

As part of the investigation, two addresses in Blaby linked to him were searched by police. Officers subsequently found pistols, ammunition and drugs.
