Do you recognise these men?

Officers would like to speak to them following a report of a theft at a store in Aylestone Road, Leicester.

The incident happened on the morning of Sunday 23 June. A man was reported to have stood next to a woman while a second man stole her purse from her handbag.

The purse contained cash and cards.

Officers believe the men pictured can help with enquiries and are appealing to them, or anyone who knows them, to get in touch.

Anyone with any information should contact 101 quoting incident 19*325291
