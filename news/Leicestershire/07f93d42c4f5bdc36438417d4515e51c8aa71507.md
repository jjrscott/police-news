Officers are appealing for information after a burglary in Loughborough where a bankcard was stolen and later used.

Sometime between 2pm and 8pm on Sunday 28 June, a house in Hazel Road was burgled. Stolen from the house was a handbag containing bank cards.

One of the bankcards was fraudulently used in a shop in Leicester Road, Loughborough around 8.15pm later that same day.

PC Alan Summers is investigating the incident and asking for anyone who has any information about the incident or the people responsible to get please get in touch.

Anyone with information can contact police on 101 quoting 20*329401
