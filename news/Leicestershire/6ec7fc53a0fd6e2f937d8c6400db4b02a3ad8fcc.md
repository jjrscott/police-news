Updated News Post

Detectives have made an arrest after a man was found with stab wound injuries inside an address in Braunstone.

Police were called at 10.11pm on Tuesday 15 December by the East Midlands Ambulance Service reporting their attendance to the incident in Gooding Avenue.

The victim, 22, was taken to the Queen's Medical Centre in Nottingham for treatment to serious injuries. He has since been discharged.

The suspects are believed to have fled the scene before emergency services arrived.

A 27-year-old Leicester man has been arrested on suspicion of attempt murder and has been released on police bail pending further enquiries.

Detective Sergeant Jo Elliott is overseeing the investigation. She said: "Despite this arrest, we are still keen to speak to anyone who was in the area at around 10pm on this Tuesday and can recall seeing or hearing anything suspicious.

"If you haven't as yet spoken to police with what information you have, it's not too late so please give us a call."

"Any information you have may help our investigation."

Anyone with information can call 101 quoting incident 615 of 15 December.
