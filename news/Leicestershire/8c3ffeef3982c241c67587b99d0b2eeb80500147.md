Updated News Post

A man has been charged after a serious sexual assault near a Leicester park on Thursday (23 May).

Gurpal Singh Gill, 33, of no fixed address, has been charged with rape.

He will be appearing before Leicester Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Wednesday 29 May).

The charge relates to an incident in the area of Watermead Park at 6.20pm on Thursday.
