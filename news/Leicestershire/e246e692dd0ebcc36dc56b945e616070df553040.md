A man and a woman have been charged with possession of class A drugs with intent to supply and possession of criminal property.

Daniel Stringer, 32, of Coleman Road, Leicester, and Kelly Snutch, 32, of London Road, Leicester have both been charged with the offences in connection with incidents which happened in Leicester on Monday 27 January.

Stringer has also been charged with driving while disqualified, driving without insurance or a MOT and fraudulently using a vehicle registration.

Snutch has also been charged with possession of class B drugs.

Stringer has been remanded into custody and Snutch has been released on police bail. Both are due to appear at Leicester Magistrates Court today (Wednesday).
