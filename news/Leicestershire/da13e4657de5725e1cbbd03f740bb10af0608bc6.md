Police are aware of a video which is circulating on social media showing a boy being assaulted and robbed in a Leicester Park.

The incident, which happened in Spinney Hill Park on Tuesday 9 February was only reported to police, by a third party, at 9.36am on Friday 12 February.

A 14-year-old boy was assaulted by another boy in the park. He sustained minor injuries during the incident which did not require hospital treatment.

As soon as the report was received officers carried out enquiries to determine the exact circumstances surrounding the report and the following day (Saturday 13 February) arrested a 16-year-old boy on suspicion of robbery.

He has since been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Officers are working closely with a local school to address any issues and to identify anyone else who may have been involved in the incident.

Anyone with information about what happened can call 101 quoting crime reference number 21*86799.
