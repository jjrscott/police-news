Officers investigating the theft of some antiques during a burglary in Shepshed are hoping someone may recognise the items and help them find them.

The items pictured were stolen sometime between Monday 10 August and Tuesday 11 August from Armstrong Mills Antique Centre in Charnwood Road.

Following enquiries a 47-year-old Nottingham man was arrested in connection with the incident, he has since been released pending further enquiries.

If you have been approached by someone selling these items or have unknowingly purchased any of them please contact PC 4482 Lena Jones on 101, quoting crime number 20*415906.
