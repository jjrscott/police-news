Officers investigating following a fight in Webster Road in Leicester continue to appeal for witnesses.

Police were called at around 9pm on Friday (30 August) by a member of the public reporting a group of approximately 20 people fighting in the street.

It is believed the fight occurred as a result of an argument between residents of the estate.

Officers attended the scene but the group had left the area.

Two boys, 14 and 17, who were arrested, at the scene, on suspicion of affray have since been released under investigation.

PC Dave Smith is the investigating officer, he said: "Our enquiries into the circumstances surrounding this incident are continuing and we would like to hear from anyone who was in the area and saw what happened or who was involved.

Anyone with information is asked to contact PC 706 Dave Smith on 101 quoting crime reference number 19*462424.
