**An 18-year-old man has been charged with the murder of Leah Fray.**

**Curtis Moyse, of Avonside Drive, Leicester, has also been charged with rape and arson with intent to endanger life.**

**Police were called to a report of a fire at a flat in Mere Road, Leicester just after 7am on Easter Sunday (21 April) where the body of Leah, 27, was found inside.**

**Moyse was remanded in custody to appear at Leicester Magistrates ' Court today (27 April).**
