Updated News Post

A man arrested on suspicion of attempted murder has been released on police bail.

Police attended a premises in Wellington Street, Leicester, yesterday morning (5 August).

A 42-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of two counts of attempted murder.

He has been released on bail and enquiries are continuing.

The arrest related to an incident at a property in Stephenson Drive, Leicester, during the early hours of Thursday 11 July.

Two men were reported to have suffered gunshot wounds in the incident and were taken to hospital. They have both since been discharged.
