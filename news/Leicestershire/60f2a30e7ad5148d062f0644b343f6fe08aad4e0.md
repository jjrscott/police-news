A 20-year-old man, who was charged as part of Operation Lionheart, has pleaded guilty to supplying cocaine.

At Leicester Magistrates' Court today (31 May), Ryan McGeechan, of Brook Street, Shepshed, admitted to one count of supplying a class A drug on 4 October 2018.

He is due to be sentenced at Leicester Crown Court on 21 June.
