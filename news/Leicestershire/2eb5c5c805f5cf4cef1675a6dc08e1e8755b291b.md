Officers have released an image of a Rolex watch they would like to return to its owner.

The watch was recovered as part of an investigation into a series of aggravated burglaries.

Anyone who recognises this watch should contact 101 quoting reference 21*13791.

Potential owners will need to provide proof of ownership.
