A man has been charged with robbery following incidents in Leicester and Coalville.

32-year-old Levi Muttock of no fixed address has been charged with two counts of robbery and two counts of possessing a bladed knife.

He was remanded in custody to appear at Leicester Magistrates Court on Thursday 18 March.

The charges relate to incidents at commercial premises in Loughborough Road, Leicester on Saturday 6 March and Broom Leys Road, Coalville on Saturday 13 March.
