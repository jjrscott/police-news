A 19-year-old man has been charged with causing grievous bodily harm with intent following a stabbing in Braunstone Frith last Wednesday (25 November).

The charge relates to an incident at the junction of Elsworthy Walk and Cuffling Drive.

Callum Beane, of Kelbrook Close, Leicester, is due to appear at Leicester Magistrates' Court today (Friday 4 December).

He was arrested by police on Wednesday (2 December).
