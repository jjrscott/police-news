Searches are continuing for a teenage boy who has been reported missing.

Beck Morgan, 17, is believed to have left his home near Oakham during the early hours of yesterday morning (Wednesday).

He has not been seen since and his family and officers are extremely concerned for his welfare.

_**A passport photo of Beck Morgan**_

Enquiries are ongoing to find Beck including searches across the area. CCTV is also being checked.

Officers are continuing to appeal to anyone with any information about Beck to make contact.

Beck is described as white, 6ft tall, of slim build with short brown hair and brown eyes. He has black painted fingernails and is wearing a ring.

It is believed he will be wearing dark clothing and possibly a grey hoodie and brown walking boots.

Anyone with any information should call 101 quoting incident 77 of 24 February.
