Officers are appealing for information to help find a man who has been reported missing.

Hoang Hai Nguyen was reported missing from the Eyres Monsell area of Leicester on the morning of Sunday 28 March.

He has not been seen since and officers are extremely concerned for his safety and welfare.

Hoang is described as being around 5ft 4ins tall, of medium build with short hair and brown eyes.

He was last seen wearing a navy blue Parka coat with fur around the hood, light blue jeans, a dark-coloured baseball cap and Nike trainers.

Hoang is Vietnamese and does not speak English.

Anyone with any information in relation to Hoang is asked to contact 101 quoting incident 198 of 28 March.
