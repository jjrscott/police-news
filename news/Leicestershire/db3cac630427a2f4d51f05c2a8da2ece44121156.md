Officers are appealing for public help to find who was responsible after wing mirror glass from a number of vehicles was stolen in Leicester.

The thefts took place in the early hours of Tuesday (3 November).

Around 20 sets of mirrors were stolen from vehicles in Stokes Drive, Copeland Avenue, Duncombe Road, Heathley Park Drive, Tatton Close, Greenwich Close and Brading Road.

Officer assigned to enquiries in connection with the reports found a bag containing a number of wing mirrors in a nearby road and efforts are continuing to return them to the vehicle owners.

Detective Sergeant Sian Harris-Ley is investigating. She said: "Enquiries are being carried out to find who was responsible for this spate of thefts and we are appealing for public help to do so.

We would like to hear from anyone who may have CCTV footage or dashcam images capturing any suspicious activity in these areas overnight on Monday into Tuesday."

Anyone with information can call 101 quoting crime reference number 20*579791.
