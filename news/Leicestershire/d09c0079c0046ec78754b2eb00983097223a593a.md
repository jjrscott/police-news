A former Leicestershire Police Community Support Officer has appeared in court charged with five offences.

Scott Hitchings, 43, of Leicestershire, appeared at Leicester Magistrates' Court today (26 September) where he pleaded not guilty to three counts of making indecent photographs of a child and two counts of possessing extreme pornography, all between 25 April 2018 and 13 May 2018.

He is now due to appear at Leicester Crown Court on 11 November 2019.
