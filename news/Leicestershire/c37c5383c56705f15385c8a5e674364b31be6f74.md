Updated News Post

A 16-year-old boy who was reported missing yesterday evening (Monday 9 September) has been found safe and well.

Macauley Curcher was reported missing at 7.50pm after he failed to return home from college in Melton.

He was found a short while ago in the Countesthorpe area.

Officers thanked everyone who had shared their appeal and assisted with enquiries.
