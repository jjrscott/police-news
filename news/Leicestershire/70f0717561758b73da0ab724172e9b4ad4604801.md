A 29-year-old man has been charged following a fire at a property in Birstall.

Bashkim Nuredini, of no fixed abode, has been charged with arson, abstracting/using electricity without authority and production of a controlled class B drug - cannabis.

He is due to appear at Leicester Magistrates' Court today (26 April).

The charges relate to a report of a fire at a house in Queensgate Drive on the morning of Saturday 13 April.
