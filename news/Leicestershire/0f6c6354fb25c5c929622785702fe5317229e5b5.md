Updated News Post

A man has been charged with wounding with intent following an incident in Imperial Avenue, Leicester, yesterday morning (Saturday).

Jordon Smith, 22, of Charles Street, Leicester, has been charged with wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm after a 19-year-old man was taken to hospital with stab wound injuries. The man has since been discharged from hospital.

Smith has also been charged with attempting to wound a woman with intent to cause grievous bodily harm. This is in relation to the same incident.

Smith has been remanded into custody and is due to appear at Leicester Magistrates Court on Monday (30 November).
