Updated News Post

Two men have been charged with possession of class A drugs with intent to supply.

Ethan Ricketts, 28, of Melbourne Street, Coalville, and Travis Aldred, 21, of Melbourne Street, Coalville, were both charged with the offence today (Tuesday) after being arrested in the Coalville area yesterday.

Ricketts has also been charged with possession of criminal property, namely cash.

Aldred has also been charged with possession of class B drugs with intent to supply.

Both have been remanded into custody to appear at Leicester Magistrates Court tomorrow (Wednesday).
