A 24-year-old man has been charged with four offences following a fire in Leicester.

Police were called to an address in Wolverton Road, off Narborough Road, just after 1.30am on 15 February. Officers were informed cannabis plants were found inside the property, but subsequently perished in the fire.

Yesterday (Tuesday 9 April), Marek Kaminski, 24, of Wolverton Road, was charged with:

 * One count of being concerned in the production of cannabis
 * One count of abstracting/using electricity
 * One count of criminal damage
 * One count of possession of cannabis
