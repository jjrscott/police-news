Officers have released images of items they have recovered and wish to reunite with their owners.

The items were found in a van that was recovered after being stolen from Earl Shilton on Monday 21 December around 8.20pm.

The items recovered include a white Apple iPad in a red case, a brown Burberry handbag, a Breitling Bentley watch, a pear face Aurora watch and jewellery in an Aurora box.

PC Rebecca Mathers said: "We are continuing investigations to establish the full circumstances of this incident.

"The items recovered are of substantial value and we would like to return them to their rightful owners.

"We would ask anyone who recognises these items to please get in touch."

Anyone with information should contact 101 quoting reference 20*679180.

Potential owners of any item will need to provide proof of ownership.

You can also visit https://www.leics.police.uk/ro/report/.
