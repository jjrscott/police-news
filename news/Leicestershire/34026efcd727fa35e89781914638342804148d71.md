A man who died following an incident in Syston has been named as 24-year-old Connor Black.

Police were called by East Midlands Ambulance Service at 11.06pm yesterday (Saturday) following a report that a man had been stabbed.

Officers attended and Connor was found inside a property in St Columba Way.

_**Connor Black (Family photo)**_

Connor was taken to the Queens Medical Centre, Nottingham, by ambulance where he sadly died during the early hours of Sunday morning.
