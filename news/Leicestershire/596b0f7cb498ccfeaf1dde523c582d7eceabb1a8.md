Updated News Post

Three men have been charged with violent disorder after a fight in Osmaston Road earlier this week.

Ion Stefan, 42, Danut Stefan, 39, and Ilie Tanase, 35, have been remanded to appear at Leicester Magistrates' Court today (25 May).

Tanase has also been charged with GBH section 18 wounding.

An 18-year-old man who was arrested on suspicion of affray has been released on police bail pending further enquiries.

The charges relate to a report of a fight in Osmaston Road, Leicester on Thursday evening (23 May).

Two men were taken to the Leicester Royal Infirmary to be treated for non-life-threatening injuries and have since been discharged.
