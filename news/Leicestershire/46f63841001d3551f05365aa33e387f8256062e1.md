Detectives investigating a burglary in Hinckley have released an image of a man they wish to speak to in connection with the incident.

It happened at an address in Ashby Road at around 1.30pm on Thursday 3 September.

The front door of the property was smashed and a quantity of jewellery - including bracelets, necklaces and earrings - were taken.

Some items were later found in Factory Road.

A 21-year-old man from Milton Keynes was arrested in connection with the incident and later released on bail.

Detective Constable Kate Pitt, from Hinckley CID, is the investigating officer. She said: "I would like to speak to the man pictured as I believe he may have information about this incident.

"Do you recognise him or do you know who he is? If so, then please get in touch."

Call 101, quoting reference 20*460571.
