Officers have released an image of a bicycle that was recovered in Leicester in the hope of reuniting it with the rightful owner.

The bike, which is suspected to be stolen, was recovered in Kingsmead Road at around midday on Thursday (19 November).

The bike (pictured) is a silver Trek and appears to be in good condition.

If you believe this to be yours and can provide proof of ownership please get in touch.

Call 101 quoting crime reference 20*612764.
