Updated News Post

A 15-year-old girl reported missing from Melton Mowbray has been found.

Natalie Bird, who was last seen leaving her home on 30 December, has been found safe and well in the Oxfordshire area earlier today (3 January).

Thank you to all who helped by sharing our appeal to find Natalie and contacted us with information.
