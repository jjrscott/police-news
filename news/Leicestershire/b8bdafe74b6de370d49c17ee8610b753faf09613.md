Do you recognise these men?

Officers would like to speak to them following the theft of mobile phones from the Carphone Warehouse store in Belvoir Shopping Centre, Coalville.

The incident happened at around 11.50am on Sunday 12 May. Four men entered the store, stealing a number of mobile phone handsets before leaving the premises.

They then ran through the centre towards Belvoir Road.

No-one was injured in the incident and no weapons were reported to have been used.

Enquires have been ongoing and officers are appealing to the men pictured, or anyone who recognises them, to come forward as it is believed they can help with the investigation.

Anyone who has any information in relation to the theft should also contact police.

Call 101 quoting incident 19000241437.
