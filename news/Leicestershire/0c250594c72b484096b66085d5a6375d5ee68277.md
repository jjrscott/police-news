Updated News Post

A man has been charged with conspiracy to receive stolen goods following an operation in Burbage and Leicester Forest East yesterday (Tuesday 1 December).

Matthew Newell, 29, of Freemans Lane, Burbage, will be appearing before Leicester Magistrates' Court today (Wednesday 2 December).

The charge relates to offences which occurred between 15 July and 1 December this year.
