Six men have been charged after an assault in East Leicester, last August.

Mustafa Shirzad, 27, of Melton Road, Leicester, has been charged with an offence under Section 4 of the Public Order Act, possession of offensive weapon and assault.

Mohammed Coachi, 41, of Melton Road, Leicester, has been charged with an offence under Section 4 of the Public Order Act and possession of offensive weapon.

The following men have been charged with an offence under Section 4 of the Public Order Act:-

• Mirwais Khan, 27, of Melton Road, Leicester,
• Yousaf Khan, 24, of Melton Road, Leicester,
• Talhah Khan, 20, of Honeysuckle Lane, Langley Green, Crawley, West Sussex and
• Hashim Khan, 29, of Lockerbie Avenue, Rushey Mead, Leicester.

The men are due to appear before Leicester Magistrates' Court today (Monday 22 March).

The charges relate to an incident on 17 August when a 29-year-old man sustained injuries to his arm after he was assaulted with a metal pole in Melton Road.
