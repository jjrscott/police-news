A Leicester man is due in court charged with firearms offences.

Eche Waldron, 23, of Tatlow Road has been charged with possession of a firearm/prohibited weapon and possessing a firearm during a five period following suspended sentence of three months or more being passed.

Waldron has been remanded to appear at Leicester Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Saturday 16 January).
