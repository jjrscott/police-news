A dozen men have been charged with being involved in a conspiracy to supply class A drugs.

They were arrested following a proactive operation by the East Midlands Special Operations Unit (EMSOU) across Leicestershire yesterday (Thursday 14 January).

The following men appeared at Leicester Magistrates' Court today (Friday) and have been remanded into custody:

 * Erlind Bashukaj, 26, of Marquis Street, Leicester
 * Besfort Agaj, 28, of Watkin Road, Leicester
 * Hysni Sokolaj, 48, of Bath Lane, Leicester
 * Bujar Hykaj, 27, of Bath Lane, Leicester
 * Arland Rexhaj, 25, of Burgess Street, Leicester
 * Kismet Memia, 32, of Canal Street, Leicester

The following men have been charged and will appear at Leicester Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Saturday):

 * Bekim Gjongecaj, 23, of Lee Street, Leicester
 * Erdilant Kucana, 26, of Castle Street, Hinckley
 * Liridon Agaj, 26, of Marquis Street, Leicester
 * Bledar Cakraj, 24, of Narborough Road, Leicester
 * Ervin Sinani, 32, of Castle Street, Hinckley
 * Ermal Kucana, 19, of Castle Street, Hinckley

Seven other men were also arrested as part of the operation.

Three men have been released on bail pending further enquiries, two men were dealt with for immigration offences and two other men were interviewed by police and then referred to immigration services.
