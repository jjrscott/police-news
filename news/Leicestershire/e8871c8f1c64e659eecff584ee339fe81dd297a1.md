Officers have released images of a number of bikes which they suspect to be stolen to reunite them with their rightful owners.

Following information received officers attended an address in the Whitteney Drive area of Eyres Monsell on Thursday 11 March and after a search of the property a numbers of bikes were recovered from the back garden.

A 44-year-old man and a 14-year-old boy have been arrested on suspicion of theft and have been released under investigation.

Sgt Ben Widdowson, from South Leicester Neighbourhood Policing Area, said: "We believe these bikes to be stolen and have therefore released photos of them in the hope to return them back to their rightful owners.

"If you suspect one of these bikes is yours and can provide proof of ownership please get in touch with us."

You can contact police on 101 quoting 21*137791.
