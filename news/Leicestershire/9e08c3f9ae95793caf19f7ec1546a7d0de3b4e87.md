Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Gaddesby yesterday.

The incident happened at around 2pm yesterday (28 June) in Ashby Road and involved a tractor and a motorcycle.

The motorcyclist was taken to the Queen's Medical Centre in Nottingham where he remains in a serious but stable condition. The tractor driver wasn't injured.

Detective Constable Alison Briance, from the force's Serious Collision Investigation Unit, said: "Both vehicles were travelling in the same direction towards Ashby Folville at the time of the collision.

"I'd be keen to speak to anyone who may have seen the collision or either vehicle beforehand. Alternatively, you have any dash cam footage of the collision please also get in touch with police."

Anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101 quoting incident 347 of 28 June.
