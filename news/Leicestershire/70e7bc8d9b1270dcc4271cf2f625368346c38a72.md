A man has died following a two-car collision in Whatton Road, Hathern, near Loughborough.

Officers were called at 11.10am yesterday (Tuesday) to the incident which involved a green Toyota Avensis car and a green Range Rover.

A man, aged in his 80s, who was driving the Toyota car, was taken to the Queen's Medical Centre in Nottingham following the incident with serious injuries. The man has since died in hospital.

No other injuries have been reported.

Road closures were put in place in the area of Whatton Road, Hathern Road and the A6 Derby Road following the collision. The roads have since been reopened.

Enquiries remain ongoing into the collision.

Detective Constable Pete Davies said: "We have spoken to a number of people following the incident and are appealing to anyone with any information, who has not yet spoken with us, to make contact.

"Were you in the area around the time of the incident? Did you witness the collision or did you see either of the vehicles involved prior to the collision. Do you have any dashcam footage from the area around the time of the incident which may assist with our enquiries?

"Anyone who does have any information should contact 101 quoting incident 176 of 14 January.
