A man has been charged with the murder of Stuart Morris.

Martin Green, 49, of Heathcott Road, has been charged this afternoon (Friday) with the offence and remanded into police custody.

He is due to appear at Leicester Magistrates Court tomorrow (Saturday).

The charge relates to the death of 50-year-old Mr Morris. Mr Morris' body was found inside a flat in Heathcott Road on Tuesday afternoon (14 April).
