A 41-year-old man has been charged after a house was broken into in Loughborough.

Dean Foster, of Blackbrook Road, Loughborough, was charged on Thursday 18 July with burglary of a house in Adam Dale.

The incident happened around 11.40pm on Sunday 26 May.

Foster appeared at Leicester Magistrates' Court on Friday and has been bailed to appear at Leicester Crown Court in next month.
