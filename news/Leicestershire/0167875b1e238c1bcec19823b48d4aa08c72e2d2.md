A 27-year-old man has been charged after a woman was raped in New Walk earlier this week.

Nathaniel Gray of Gaul Street, Leicester, has been charged and will be appearing before Leicester Magistrates' Court today (Thursday 22 August).

The charge relates to an incident on New Walk during the early hours of Tuesday (20 August).
