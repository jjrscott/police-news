Updated News Post

An investigation is ongoing following a report of a stabbing in Leicester city centre yesterday afternoon (Tuesday 23 March).

Officers were called to the Gallowtree Gate area at 3.34pm where a man was found to have suffered stab wound injuries.

East Midlands Ambulance Service were also in attendance and the man was airlifted to hospital with serious injuries - he remains in hospital where his condition is described as non-life-threatening.

Detective Constable Ian Button is the investigating officer. He said: "Enquiries are being carried out to establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident. Officers are checking CCTV in the area and speaking to witnesses to piece together what happened.

"We continue to appeal for anyone who was in the vicinity of Gallowtree Gate yesterday afternoon and saw or heard anything suspicious to come forward. Your information could prove invaluable to our investigation.

"If you have information about what happened and have not yet spoken to us please make contact."

Anyone with any information can call police on 101 quoting incident 370 of 23 March.
