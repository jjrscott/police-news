Updated News Post

A 21-year-old man has appeared in court charged with a number of drug offences.

Ashley Bates, of Tedworth Green, Leicester has been charged with two counts of possession with intent to supply class A drugs, one charge of possession with intent to supply class B drugs, one charge of possession of criminal property and one charge of assault of an emergency worker.

He appeared at Leicester Magistrates' Court today (22 June) and has been remanded to appear at Leicester Crown Court on Monday 27 July.
