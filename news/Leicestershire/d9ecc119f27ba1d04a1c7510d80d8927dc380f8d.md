Following an appeal issued in relation to an assault at a premises in Abbey Street, Leicester, on Saturday 18 January, two men pictured have now been identified.

A 23-year-old man, of Leicester, has been arrested on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm. He has since been released under investigation.

A 28-year-old man, of Leicester, has been voluntarily interviewed in connection with the incident.

Enquiries remain ongoing.

Thank you to everyone who shared the appeal and provided information.
