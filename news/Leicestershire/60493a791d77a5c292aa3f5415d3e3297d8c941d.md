Officers are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision in Leicester earlier this week.

The collision happened at around 4.45pm on Monday 6 July on New Parks Way close to the Scudamore Road roundabout and involved a blue Suzuki motorbike and a light coloured vehicle, which failed to stop at the scene.

The motorcyclist received hospital treatment for a dislocated arm and a fractured collar bone.

Both vehicles were travelling city bound towards Meridian Leisure Park at the time of the collision.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, the events leading up to it or knows the driver of the vehicle which failed to stop is asked to contact police on 101 quoting 20000345164.
