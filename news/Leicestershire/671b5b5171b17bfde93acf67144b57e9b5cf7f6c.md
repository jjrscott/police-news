Officers are appealing for information after a reported theft from a lorry near Lutterworth.

It was reported that between 8pm on Wednesday 17 February and 6am on Thursday 18 February a curtain-sider lorry was cut open when parked in a layby on the A4304 Lutterworth Road near Walcote.

A substantial amount of goods including parts for Tesla cookers were stolen.

Detective Constable Nick Cox said: "We would like to hear from anyone who has dash cam footage from the area on the night of the incident.

"Or anyone who may have driven along Lutterworth Road that night and might have seen anything suspicious."

Anyone with information should call 101 quoting reference 21*96871.

You can also visit https://www.leics.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/.
