Some of our cases this month which have led to charges and/or convictions in court.

**_Convictions_**

**_Man sentenced to life imprisonment for murder_**

Ishaq Hassanjee was sentenced to life imprisonment after being found guilty of murder following a stabbing in the Highfields area.

_**Ishaq Hassanjee**_

Hassanjee was ordered to serve a minimum of 19 years after being convicted of the murder of 19-year-old Hassan Jama.

More info: https://www.leics.police.uk/news/leicestershire/news/2021/january/bartholomew-street-stabbing-19-year-sentence-for-man-found-guilty-of-murder/

**_Man jailed for manslaughter after injured pensioner died following burglary_**

A 20-year-old man who pleaded guilty to manslaughter after an injured pensioner died following a burglary in his own home has been jailed.

Jack Tebbutt, of Central Avenue, Wigston, Leicester, appeared at Leicester Crown Court where he was sentenced to five years and 10 months imprisonment.

_**Jack Tebbutt**_

Tebbutt admitted the manslaughter of 66-year-old Howard Staff. He had also pleaded guilty to one count of burglary and a further charge of perverting the course of justice at an earlier hearing.

https://www.leics.police.uk/news/leicestershire/news/2021/january/wigston-man-jailed-after-pleading-guilty-to-manslaughter/

**_Man convicted of attempted murder of former partner_**

A 26-year-old man who twice ran over his former partner was convicted of attempted murder on Friday 15 January.

Zack Coleman knocked over his victim in Sturdee Road, Leicester, on the morning of 3 December 2019, before turning his vehicle around and running over her again. He then fled the scene.

Coleman is due to be sentenced at Leicester Crown Court on 8 March.

More info: https://www.leics.police.uk/news/leicestershire/news/2021/january/man-found-guilty-of-attempting-to-kill-ex-partner/

**_Woman admits Leicester stabbings_**

Hannah Burton, 25, appeared at Leicester Crown Court on Thursday 7 January where she admitted two counts of section 18 assault causing grievous bodily harm.

This followed an incident in Healey Street in Leicester on 15 April last year.

Burton is due to be sentenced on Tuesday 16 February.

More info: https://www.leics.police.uk/news/leicestershire/news/2021/january/woman-admits-leicester-stabbings/

**_Man jailed after admitting stabbing_**

A man has been sentenced to seven and a half years imprisonment after admitting stabbing a man inside a property in Leicester.

Jordan Smith, 22, of no fixed address, had stabbed the man to the back during an incident at a property in the Westcotes area of Leicester, on the morning of Saturday 28 November.

_**Jordan Smith**_

More info: https://www.leics.police.uk/news/leicestershire/news/2021/january/man-jailed-after-admitting-stabbing/

**_Man sentenced to five years for robbery and affray_**

Liam O'Brien, 29, of Waterside Close, Loughborough, was jailed for five years after pleading guilty to robbery, affray and possession of a knife blade/sharp pointed article in a public place.

O'Brien had forced his way into an address in Thringstone on 9 July last year, subsequently threatening the occupants with a knife.

_**Liam O 'Brien**_

More info: https://www.leics.police.uk/news/leicestershire/news/2021/january/loughborough-man-sentenced-to-five-years-for-robbery/
