Officers are appealing for public help to find a 15-year-old girl who has been reported missing.

Natalie Bird was last seen leaving her home in Melton Mowbray at 5.40pm yesterday (30 December).

She has not been seen since and Natalie's family and officers are extremely concerned for her welfare.

Natalie is described as around 5ft tall, of slim build, with shoulder length dark hair.

Anyone with any information in relation to Natalie's whereabouts should contact 101 quoting incident 458 of 30 December.
