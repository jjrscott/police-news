Updated News Post

Updated on 11/4/19 at 9:39 a.m.

Incident number: 19*181424

Four youths arrested in connection with a series of incidents of damage in Coalville have been released under investigation.

Police are investigating a number of incidents of damage in the London Road and Park Road area which are reported as happening between 2am and 4am yesterday morning (10 April).

The damage consists of windows on numerous vehicles being smashed.

Four youths aged 15, 16, 16 and 17 were arrested on suspicion of criminal damage and have since been released under investigation.

PC Daniel D'Araujo, the investigating officer, said: “We are investigating eight confirmed reports of damage.

“We know that incidents such as these can have a negative effect on local communities and neighbourhood officers will be carrying out reassurance patrols in the surrounding area.

“I would encourage anyone who may have witnessed these incidents or anyone who has been a victim but hasn’t as yet contacted police to please get in touch.”

Anyone with information or to report further incidents is asked to contact police on 101 quoting 19*181424.

**If you have information in response to this appeal please contact us on 101. You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Your personal details won't be taken, information isn't traced or recorded and you will not be required to go to court.**
