Four men have been charged after a violent disorder outside the King Power Stadium in October last year.

The following have all been charged with affray:-

 * Trevor Marks, 40, of Savoy Wood, Harlow, Essex
 * George Mason, 26, of Pakenham Road, Waterlooville, Hampshire
 * Joe Braden, 19, St Peter's Street, Croydon
 * Darren Little, 36, of Station Road, Ratby, Leicester

They are due to appear before Leicester Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Friday 20 September).

The charges relate to an incident on Saturday 27 October, 2018, where a number of people were involved in a fight after the Leicester City and West Ham United match.

Six men were charged with affray in May and are currently going through the legal process.
