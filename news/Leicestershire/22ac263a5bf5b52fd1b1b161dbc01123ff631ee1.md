Officers have released CCTV images to help identify a man they wish to speak to in connection with a burglary in Leicester.

Between 4.35am and 4.50am on Wednesday 3 February, it was reported that a man forced entry to an unoccupied property on Glenfield Road.

CCTV captured a man searching the property. No items were stolen but over Â£600 worth of damage was caused.

Detective Constable Rachel Chapman said: "We are carrying out investigations to establish the full circumstances of this incident.

"CCTV has captured clear images of a man inside the property and we urge anyone who could help identify him to please get in touch."

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 quoting reference 21*69333.
