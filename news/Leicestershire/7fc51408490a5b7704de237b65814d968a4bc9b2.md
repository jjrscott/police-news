A man has been charged with sexual assault in connection with an incident in Melton on Saturday (16 January).

Krystian Surmaj, 22, of Melton, has been remanded into custody to appear at Leicester Magistrates Court tomorrow (Friday).

The charge is in connection with an incident reported to have happened in the area of Elmhurst Avenue.
