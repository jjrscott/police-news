Officers are appealing for information after arson and criminal damage were reported near Hinckley on Tuesday 23 February.

It was reported that around 2.45am a van was set alight and the windows and doors of an outbuilding were smashed and graffiti sprayed on the walls at a property on the A5 near the junction of the B4114 for Wolvey Heath.

Criminal damage was also reported at the property on Sunday 7 February when vehicles were damaged.

Detective Sergeant Fraser Macintosh said: "We believe this incident may involve someone from the local area and the offender suffered an ankle injury during the incident.

"We would like to speak to anyone who may have any information that could help us identify a motive for this incident.

"We would also urge anyone with dash cam footage from the area at the time of the incident to please get in touch."

Anyone with information should contact 101 quoting reference 21*106085.
