A man has been charged with murder following the death of a 24-year-old man in Syston.

Kaylon Wilson, 21, of St Columba Way, Syston, has been charged with the murder of Connor Black.

The charge relates to an incident at a property in St Columba Way on Saturday evening (27 February).

Wilson has been remanded into custody and is due to appear at Leicester Magistrates Court this morning (1 March).
