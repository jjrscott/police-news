A man has been charged with burglary after incidents in Leicester.

37-year-old Rickie Lee of St Marks Road, Derby has been charged with three counts of burglary of a non-dwelling.

He was remanded in custody to appear at Leicester Magistrates Court on Thursday 18 March.

The charges relate to incidents in Fosse Park Avenue on Saturday 13 March, Meridian Way on Sunday 14 March and Ryder Road on Wednesday 17 March.
