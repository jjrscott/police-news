Officers have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the theft of a push bike in Leicester.

The incident happened between 3.20pm and 3.40pm on Thursday 11 February on East Park Road.

It was reported that the bike was left unattended outside a commercial premises and was stolen by a man who was seen nearby.

PC Baljit Singh said: "This incident happened in the middle of the afternoon when people are likely to have been in the area.

"We would like to speak to anyone who may recognise this man from the CCTV or from anyone who may have seen him in the area at the time of the incident."

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 quoting reference 21*85747.

You can also visit https://www.leics.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/.
