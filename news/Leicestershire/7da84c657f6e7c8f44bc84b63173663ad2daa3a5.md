Two Loughborough drug dealers have pleaded guilty to a total of five offences after being detained as part of Operation Lionheart.

Jordan Raybone, 22, of Victoria Street, Loughborough, pleaded guilty to four counts of supplying class A drugs and one count of possession with intent to supply a class B drug.

John Moon, 47, of Pinfold Gate, Loughborough, pleaded guilty to one count of supplying a class A drug.

Both defendants appeared at Leicester Crown Court yesterday (29 July) and admitted the offences.

Raybone is due to be sentenced on 23 August and Moon on 6 September.
