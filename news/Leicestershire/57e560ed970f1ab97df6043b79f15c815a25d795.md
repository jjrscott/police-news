A man has been charged after a non-stop collision in Measham in July last year.

Nathan Wileman, 31, of Wash Lane, Ravenstone, Coalville, has been charged with Section 18 Grievous Bodily Harm, assault and dangerous driving.

He is due to appear before Leicester Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 10 March.

The charges relate to an incident in the High Street in Measham on Saturday 4 July. Two people were injured during the incident and discharged after treatment in hospital.
