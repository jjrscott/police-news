A Telford man has pleaded guilty to assaulting a key worker and criminal damage.

Thomas Essex, aged 27 from Dawley was arrested on Saturday Morning (18 April) following reports that an ambulance worker was punched and the wing mirror of the ambulance was broken.

Essex is due to be sentenced on 22 May.
