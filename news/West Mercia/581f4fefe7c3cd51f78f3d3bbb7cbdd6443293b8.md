**We are appealing for any information and any witnesses to come forward including any dashcam footage following a fatal road traffic collision on the A46 Evesham between Bidford and Twyford yesterday (Wednesday 3 November).**

At around 10.55am a grey Mazda, green Renault Clio and white Man artic lorry collided. The three vehicles were all travelling southbound on the A46 dual carriageway from the direction of Bidford to Twyford.

Sadly one man in his 30s has died in the collision. His Next of Kin has been informed.

An investigation is underway to establish the exact circumstances of the collision. If you have any information please report any information on our website or call 101 and quote incident number 161i of 3 November 2020.
