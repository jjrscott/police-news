Kevin Mathers, 46, from Hereford, has entered into a guilty plea to incite a child to engage in sexual communication and attempting to cause a child to look at an image of a person engaging in a sexual activity today (Wednesday 26 August).

He appeared virtually at Kidderminster Magistrates' Court and will be remanded until his sentencing on Tuesday 22 September.
