Police are appealing for witnesses after a man died in a collision in Shropshire.

The collision happened around 8.55am yesterday morning (Tuesday 14 January) in Church Street in Broseley.

For reasons yet to be established, the vehicle left the road and mounted the pavement where it collided with a brick wall.

The driver, a 78 year old man, was pronounced dead at the scene.

No other vehicles were involved in the collision.

Enquiries are on-going and police are appealing for witnesses.

Anyone with information is asked to contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 102s 140120.
