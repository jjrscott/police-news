**Five** men, all from Poplar Lane in Smethwick, have been charged in connection with attempting to cause grievous bodily harm as part of an investigation into an incident on Wylds Lane in Worcester at the beginning of the month.

The incident happened at approximately 4:15pm on Sunday 2 August at the junction of Vincent Road and Wylds Lane when a 21-year-old suffered serious injuries.

Aurica-Marian Muti, aged 18, Christi-Daniel Stefanescu, aged 19, Ionut-Alexandra Tudor, aged 18, Remus-cosmin Vladutoaia, aged 24, all appeared at Kidderminster Magistrates Court via virtual live link on Saturday (August 8). They were remanded in custody.

**A 32-year-old man from Poplar Road in Smethwick, has been charged with causing grievous bodily harm and causing injury by dangerous driving in connection to the incident.**
