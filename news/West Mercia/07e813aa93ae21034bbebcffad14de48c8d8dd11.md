Officers are appealing for witnesses after a serious collision on Pulley Lane in Shrewsbury.

Emergencies services were called around 8am this morning (Tuesday 29 October 2019) to reports of a collision between a red tractor and a red moped.

The rider of the moped (a man in his 20s) suffered serious injuries and was taken to hospital by air ambulance where he continues to receive medical care.

If you witnessed the incident or have any information in relation to it please contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 070s of 29 October 2019.
 **
Issued by Alex Gale, Communications Officer at 11.20am on 29 October 2019**
