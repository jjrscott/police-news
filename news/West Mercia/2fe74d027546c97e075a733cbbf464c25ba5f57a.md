We are appealing for witnesses following an altercation in Worcester City Centre in the early hours of this morning (23 August).

At approximately 4.25am a report was made by a member of the public for a disorder at The Foregate, Worcester. A man is believed to have stabbed a man and chased him down Angel Street to The Cross. The offender is believed to have gone down St Nicholas Street towards The Trinity.

DS Chris Buffery said "We believe this is an isolated incident, the victim has been treated in hospital for two stab wounds.

"We are appealing to anyone who may have seen this incident or have information that might help our enquiries. Please call us on 101 quoting reference 131 I 230820."
