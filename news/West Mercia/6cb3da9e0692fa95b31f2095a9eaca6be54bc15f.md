We are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision on Hagley Wood Lane, Clent on Sunday 12 April 2020.

The incident occurred at around 00.40am, when a white BMW 3 series, registration ending BNJ exited the carriageway and collided with a tree.

The driver and all four passengers sustained injuries. Three of the passengers were taken to hospital. Two of the passengers were later released from hospital; a 19-year-old woman remains in critical condition.

Investigating Officers are appealing for information from the public including dash-cam footage.

PC Scott Gee said: "We are keen to speak with anyone who may have seen the incident on Hagley Wood Lane, in Clent, during the early hours of Saturday 12 April so that we can get a better understanding of what occurred.

"We are especially keen to see any dashcam footage from the area at the time of the incident."

Anyone with information should email PC Scott Gee on _scott.gee@westmercia.pnn.police.uk_ quoting incident number 0024s 12 April 2020.

Alternatively, if you want to give information but don't want to give your name you can call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or use their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.

Crimestoppers are not part of the police, when you contact them you won't be identified. The only person who knows you have given information is you.
