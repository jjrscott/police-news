Two boys aged 15 and 16 from Kempsey and Evesham have been arrested and charged in relation to a burglary on 28 April in Worcester.

One has been charged with burglary, assault of a constable, assault of an emergency worker, use of mobile phone while driving, driving a vehicle without a license, use of a vehicle on the road, and taking a vehicle without consent.

The second burglary of a property and being in a vehicle without the owner's consent.

Both are due to appear at Worcester Magistrates on the 2 June 2020 for sentencing.
