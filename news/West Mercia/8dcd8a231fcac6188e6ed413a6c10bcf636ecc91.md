A 35-year-old man has been charged in connection with a drug offence.

Allan Golding, of Hillside Road, Liverpool, was charged yesterday (Thursday 9 July) with possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class A - Heroin, and is currently in custody. The charge is in connection with an incident on the M5 on Wednesday 8 July.

He will appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court via video link later today.

Three men aged 21, 25 and 22 who were arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply a controlled drug have been bailed until 6 August.
