We are appealing for information following a break-in at a local school in Fairfield, Bromsgrove.

Overnight offenders gained entry to Fairfield First School, between 9.30pm on Monday 2 October and 7.30am on yesterday (3 October) offenders forced entry. While on the premises a number of iPads have been stolen.

If you have any information on the incident, saw any suspicious behaviour in the area or have been offered any iPads for sale that do not seem legitimate. Please contact PC Evans on 01562 826111 quoting reference 85i3November.
