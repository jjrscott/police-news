Following a rapid response by West Mercia Police's South Worcestershire Burglary Team, a man has been charged with attempted burglary and assault occasioning actual bodily harm.

Jonathan Shire, of no fixed abode, Worcester, was charged with the offences following an incident in Gorse Hill, Worcester on Thursday 10 December.

The 30-year-old is due to appear in Worcester Magistrates' Court this morning (Friday 11 December).
