Three men have been arrested for a serious robbery in Redditch.

At around 5.40pm this evening (Wednesday 6 May) police were called to Winslow Close to reports that a man had been attacked and suffered serious head injuries.

He was taken to the Queen Elizabeth Hospital where he is currently in a critical condition.

Firearms officers arrested three men from Redditch, a 21-year-old, 32-year-old and 25-year-old, on Other Road.

Anyone with information should call 101 quoting Incident 528S 060520 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
