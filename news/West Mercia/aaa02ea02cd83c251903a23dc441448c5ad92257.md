Police investigating the death of a woman in Upton-upon-Severn have released a man on bail.

A 44-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of murder after a 25-year-old woman was found unresponsive in Causeway Lane on Saturday 15 November.

The cause of the woman's death is not yet known.

The police investigation continues and we are supporting the victim's family at this time.
