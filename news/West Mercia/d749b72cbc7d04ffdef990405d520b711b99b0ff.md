The incident happened around 5.30pm on Wednesday 11 December at the junction of Park Avenue and Church Street in Madeley.

An 87 year old woman had her handbag grabbed from her shoulder, causing her to fall to the ground, knocking her head and causing her to suffer a broken arm.

Any witnesses can ring West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 538s 111219 or alternatively Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
