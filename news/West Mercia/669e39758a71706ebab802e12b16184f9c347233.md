At around 5.15pm on Sunday 3 May, officers were called by the West Midlands Ambulance Service to support them after a paramedic was reportedly assaulted.

Inspector Lee Page said: "Officers who responded to the call at a property in Hewell Road, Redditch arrested a man in his 50s also from Redditch on suspicion of one racially aggravated public order offence, four assaults on emergency workers (two on paramedics and two on police officers) and breaching a Criminal Behaviour Order (CBO)."

The man remains in police custody at this time.
