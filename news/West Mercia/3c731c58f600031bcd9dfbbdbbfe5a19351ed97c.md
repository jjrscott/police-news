Police are appealing for witnesses after a collision in Market Drayton.

The collision happened on the A53 near Fordhill Farm between Market Drayton and Tern Hill around 8.55pm last night (Wednesday 15 July).

Two vehicles were involved, a Citroen C3 and Landrover Discovery.

The drivers of both vehicles, a 28-year-old man and a 35-year-old man, and a passenger, a 32-year-old woman, were taken to hospital.

The 28-year-old man and 32-year-old woman suffered serious injuries.

Officers are carrying out enquiries and are appealing for witnesses, including any motorists who were on the road around the time of the collision and have Dash Cam.

Anyone with information is asked to contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 679_I_150720.
