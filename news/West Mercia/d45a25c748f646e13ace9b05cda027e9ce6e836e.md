Police are appealing for witnesses and dashcam footage following a fatal collision in Whitney on Wye.

At around 12.50am yesterday (Sunday 9 August) a man in his 40s was involved in a collision on the B4350 and sadly died at the scene.

The collision involved a Skoda and a Honda which was towing a speedboat in the direction of the A438 towards Brecon.

Anyone with information is urged to contact police on 101 quoting incident 0065 of 9 August or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Alternatively information can be reported on the West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
