Officers are appealing for witnesses with any information to get in touch following a road traffic collision in Rubery.

The collision happened at the car park of the New Rose & Crown pub, New Road in Rubery around 5am on Saturday 11 July.

A White Ford Eco Sport car appears to have collided with a wall at the pub resulting in serious injuries to the driver. The 35 year old man from Birmingham remains in hospital in a serious condition.

If anyone witnessed the collision or has any information that can help officers in their investigation, please call 101 and quote incident number 00765i of 11 July 2020.
