Officers are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision involving two vehicles on the A38 at Hanbury Turn, Bromsgrove.

The incident happened on Thursday 6 August at 4.40pm when a silver VW Transporter motor caravan and a black Mercedes were in collision.

The driver of the transporter was taken to hospital, where he currently remains.

Investigating officers are appealing for information from the public including dash-cam footage.

PC Simon Carpenter said: "We're keen to speak to anyone who may have seen the incident.

"We would especially like to see any dash-cam footage from the area at the time of the incident. If you have any information please contact us."

Anyone with information can report information on this website under the section Tell us About or call on 101, quoting incident number 501_I_ 06082020.
