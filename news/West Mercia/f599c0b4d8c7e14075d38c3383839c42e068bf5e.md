A man has been charged in connection with drugs offences in Redditch

Safdar Hussain, aged 48 of Parsons Road, Redditch has been charged with one count of possession with intent to supply cannabis, one count of producing a controlled drug and one of acquiring/using/possessing criminal property.

A warrant was executed by Worcester Magistrates Court on 3 May 2019 at his home address.

He is due to appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court on 7 January 2020.
