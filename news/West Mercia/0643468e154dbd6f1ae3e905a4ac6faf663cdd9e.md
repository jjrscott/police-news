We are appealing for witness to an attempt rape of a woman in Redditch. The incident is believed to have happened some time between 9pm and 10pm on Wednesday (25 December) near to a block of flats in Bushley Close, Redditch.

Detective Inspector Sarah Squires from West Mercia Police said: "We take reports of this nature very seriously and a thorough investigation is underway.

"I would urge anyone who may have witnessed this incident or saw anything suspicious in the Bushley Close area on the evening of Wednesday 25 December to please come forward."

Anyone who may have witnessed the incident or has any information that could help police with their enquiries is asked to call 101 quoting incident number 477s of 25 December.

Alternatively, you can contact independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website www.crimestoppers-uk.org.

Anyone who is a victim of any sexual assault who feels they may need support, or may not want to contact the police can contact West Mercia Police's Victim Advice Line which is a free, independent and confidential support service. People can call 0800 952 3000 or visit www.victimadviceline.org.uk
