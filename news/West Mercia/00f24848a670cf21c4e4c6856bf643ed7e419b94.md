Police are appealing for witnesses to a collision involving a vehicle and a bicycle in Lickey.

Officers were called to Rose Hill at around 4pm on Tuesday 19 May after a cyclist suffered minor injuries following a collision with a Toyota.

Anyone with information is urged to contact 101 quoting incident number 380S 190520 of 19 May. Alternatively information can be passed anonymously to Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555111.
