Police are appealing for the public's help following a theft in Bromsgrove on Wednesday 13 May.

Several pieces of surveying equipment were stolen from a vehicle on the A38 near Slideslow Close between 9am and 11am. The vehicle was also damaged during the break in.

Police have carried out enquiries and are now asking for the public's assistance.

Officers are urging any witnesses or anyone with dashcam footage to contact police on 101 quoting incident 175s of 130520 or alternatively information can be given anonymously to Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555111.
