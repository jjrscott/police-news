We are appealing for witnesses following a collision last Friday (17 July) in Malvern.

At approximately 9.50am on Poundbank Road, a vehicle has driven towards a grey Nissan Note at speed causing the driver to swerve into a Renault Clio, the first vehicle has left the scene.

The 79 year old driver of the Nissan Note, later attended Worcester hospital with chest pains, she suffered a fractured sternum and deflating lung but is now in a stable condition

If you saw the incident or saw the car leave the scene would like to hear from you, please contact us on 101 quoting reference 160 I 170720.
