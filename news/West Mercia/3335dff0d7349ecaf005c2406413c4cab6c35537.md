Detectives investigating the serious assault of a 24 year old man from Hereford have charged three people.

The incident happened on Blackthorn Close, Belmont on Tuesday 18 February.

Damien Kidley aged 27 (06/12/1992) from River View, Ross on Wye, Herefordshire, Kara McManus aged 22 (12/12/1997) from Whitecross Road, Hereford and Berdardus Goodall aged 18 (11/04/2001) of no fixed address were all charged with section 18 assault and robbery.

The three people charged are to appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court this morning (Friday 21 February).

Two further people were arrested as part of the investigation; a 17 year old man from Hereford on suspicion of assault and robbery and was released under investigation and a 60 year old man from Ross on Wye on suspicion of assisting an offender and was bailed.
