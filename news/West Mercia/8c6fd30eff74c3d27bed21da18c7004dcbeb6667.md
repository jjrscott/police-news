Around 3pm this afternoon (Friday 2 April) we were informed a helicopter had crashed in a field off Bromyard Road in Ledbury, Herefordshire.

Emergency services are currently at the scene.

Two people were on board the helicopter and while the extent of injuries is not yet known, they are not believed to be serious.
