A man has been charged with a number of driving offences in relation to a non-stop fatal collision in Coton near Whitchurch.

The collision happened on the B5476 on 31 of July, 2020 and Ian Edwards, aged 58 sadly died at the scene.

Aaron Rogers, aged 22, from Whitchurch, has been charged with causing death by careless driving, failing to Stop after a road traffic collision, failing to report a road traffic collision, driving without a licence and driving without insurance. He will appear at Telford Magistrates Court on Tuesday 23 February.
