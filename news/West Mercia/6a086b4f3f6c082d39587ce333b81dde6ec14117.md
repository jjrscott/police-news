Following an investigation by West Mercia Police, a 35 year old woman has been charged with obstructing police and in charge of a dog dangerously out of control causing injury.

Victoria Biddle from Lowesmoor, Worcester was arrested and then charged after her dog to attacked officers who were attending an incident at her address on Thursday 23 April. One officer was bitten in the leg although he didn't require significant medical treatment. All three dogs at the address were later seized by officers and are now in kennels.

She has been released on bail and is due to appear at Worcester Magistrates' Court on 28 May 2020.
