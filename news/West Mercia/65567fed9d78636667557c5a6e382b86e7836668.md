Following deaths of two men on Thursday (21 May) last week in Bridgnorth, the second man has now been named as 67 year old Kevin Davies, who was found deceased in a field at Knowle Sands Caravan Park.

A post mortem has shown Mr Davies died as a result of stab wounds sustained to his chest. His death is linked to the death of 73 year old found near the B4555, enquires confirm the deaths are linked.

Mr Davies' family has paid tribute to him.

"We are deeply saddened by the loss of our beloved husband, dad, grandad and great grandad.

"He was a kind, caring, wonderful family man who will be greatly missed by all his family and friends. We would like to thank ambulance staff and police officers for their support, and to all those that went to help Kevin at the time."

The family respectively request privacy at this time.
