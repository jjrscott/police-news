**Specialist firearms officers carried out a an operation today to detain a man at Leominster Train station.**

Inspector Mike Phillips said: "We would like to reassure the public that this operation involving both covert and uniformed officers was successful and tried to also minimise disruption to the public. However we do appreciate that some members of the public at the train station may have found the incident unsettling and I would like to thank everyone for their understanding and patience while officers detained one man who is now in custody."

Issued: AG, Corporat Communications, 2.45pm Saturday 21 September 2019
