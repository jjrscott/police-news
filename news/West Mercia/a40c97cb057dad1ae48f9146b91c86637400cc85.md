Police are appealing for witnesses after a burglary at a service station in Newport.

The burglary happened at Nix Service Station on Forton Road between 3am and 4am this morning (Tuesday 4 August). A quantity of cigarettes were stolen.

Officers are carrying out enquiries and appealing for witnesses. Anyone with information can report this to West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 83_I_040820 or fill in the online form here.

Alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
