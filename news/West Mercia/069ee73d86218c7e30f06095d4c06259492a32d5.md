A man has been charged with possession of firearm or imitation firearm with intent to cause fear of violence.

David Richard Chatfield, aged 65 of Hinton Road, Hereford appeared at Hereford Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 17 September where he was remanded in custody to appear before Worcester Crown Court on 15 October.

The charge is in connection with an incident at an address on Hinton Road, Hereford on Monday 16 September.
