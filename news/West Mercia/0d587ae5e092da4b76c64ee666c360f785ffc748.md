Earlier this afternoon (Wednesday 30 October) police attended an address in Rondel Street in Shrewsbury over concerns for a 16 year old boy who was found unconscious. The boy was taken to hospital by the ambulance service where he has sadly died. Enquiries are on-going, however, at this stage police do not believe there is any third party involvement in the death.
