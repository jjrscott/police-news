Detectives are appealing for witnesses after a tent was set on fire in Shrewsbury.

The incident happened around 9.10pm on Sunday 17 January in Old Potts Way, a 28 year-old woman was assaulted and a tent set on fire on land underneath the railway.

The 28 year-old suffered minor injuries as a result of the assault.

A 38 year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of arson with intent to endanger life and assault.

Enquiries are on-going.

Any witnesses are asked to contact West Mercia Police via the Tell Us About section at www.westmercia.police.uk or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
