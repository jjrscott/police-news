Police are appealing for help to locate missing Thiraviyam Arulanantham from Albrighton.

The 37-year-old, who also goes by the name Justin, was last seen on Albrighten Business Park on 29 December at around 9.30am.

He is around 5ft 4ins, of a slim build with dark hair and was last seen wearing a hat, shorts, flip flops and a jacket.

Anyone with information is urged to contact 101 quoting reference 117i of 29 December or call Crimestoppers on 0800 555111. Alternatively information can be provided through the Tell Us About section of our website.
