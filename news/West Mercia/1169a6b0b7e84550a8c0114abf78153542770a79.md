Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Hereford.

At around 4pm on Tuesday 15 September a woman was reportedly assaulted by a man at a pub on East Street.

The two people then walked through king Street towards Ware Road.

The woman is described as in her 20s with blonde hair and was wearing black jeans and espadrille shoes. There is currently no description of the man involved in the incident.

Anyone with information is urged to contact police on 101 quoting incident 00041i of 15 September or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Alternatively information can be passed through the online reporting function on West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
