Two teenage boys who went missing from Droitwich and hadn't been seen since Tuesday 7April have been located.

Joss Camden and Koa Hawkes-Wagstaff, each16 years old and from Droitwich have both been located and found to be safe.

On behalf of West Mercia Police and boys' families, thank you to everyone who shared our appeal to find them.
