West Mercia Police is appealing for witnesses or information following a collision at Crowgreaves, Bridgnorth, on Sunday 13 October.

Officers were called to the scene at just after 12.30am and discovered a blue Vauxhall Zafira had travelled across the carriageway and into a verge.

The driver, a 37-year-old man, who was the only person inside the vehicle, was treated at the scene by paramedics and then taken to hospital.

Investigating officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision to call West Mercia Police on 101, quoting incident number 28s of 13 October 2019.
