We are appealing for witnesses following a collision on Birchfield Road, Redditch on Tuesday 9 February.

An e-scooter and a pedestrian collided at around 5.15pm; ambulance and police attended.

A 29 year-old man and a 17 year-old girl were taken to hospital.

Investigating Officers are appealing for information from the public including dash-cam footage.

PC Hegenbarth said: "We are keen to speak with anyone who may have seen the incident on Birchfield Road at 5.15pm on the 9 February, or saw the e-scooter being driven prior to the collision in the surrounding area so that we can get a better understanding of what has occurred."

"We are especially keen to see any dash-cam footage from the area at the time of the incident. If you have any information please report on our website."

Anyone with information should call police on 101, or our website, quoting incident number 463i of 9 February.

If you have information but don't feel comfortable speaking to police, you can speak to the independent charity Crimestoppers. It is 100% anonymous, they never ask your name and they cannot trace your call or I.P address. You can contact them online or by calling 0800 555 111.
