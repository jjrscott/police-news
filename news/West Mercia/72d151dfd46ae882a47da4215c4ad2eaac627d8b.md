Police investigating an arson attack have arrested a man.

Around 5.40pm on Sunday 7 June three wheelie bins were set on fire at the back of the Maddies Cafe behind Market Approach in Wellington, Telford.

Earlier today (Tuesday 9 June) a 24 year old man was arrested on suspicion of arson. He is currently in police custody.
