On Thursday 14 November between 9pm - 9.30pm near Holyoakes Close, a man has approached a woman asked her for cash before making off with her black bag in the direction of Wynyates Centre.

The offender is described as 6ft tall of a skinny build with a black hoody and trousers, he also had a black bandana covering his face and he carried a large kitchen knife.

DS Paul Willetts from Kidderminster said "This type of incident is rare and we are asking for anyone with information or who was in the area at the time that may have seen something to get in contact via 101 quoting OIS: 0736S141119."
