Two men have both been charged with grievous bodily harm and threats to kill.

Paul Gibbons aged 42 from Sutton Coldfield and brother Mark Gibbons aged 46 and also from Sutton Coldfield, have been charged and remanded to Kidderminster Magistrate's Court and will appear in court today (16 June).

They were charged in relation to an incident that occurred in Stourport on Sunday 14 June in which a man was badly beaten.
