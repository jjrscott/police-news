As part of the on-going investigation, two more men have been charged in connection with the murder of a 22 year old man from Guinness Close, Redditch on 15 July.

Mohammed Hussain who is 24 years old and from Oakfield Drive, Birmingham has been charged with Murder and conspiracy to commit Robbery and has been remanded into custody. Faisel Fiaz, 20 years old and from Millsbro Road, Redditch has also been charged with Murder and conspiracy to commit Robbery.

Both men will appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court tomorrow (25 July).

While the investigation continues there will be an increased presence of officers in the area and anyone with any information or concerns can speak to an officer or call 101.
