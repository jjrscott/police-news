Police are appealing for anyone who has CCTV or witnessed a fire on Halesfield industrial estate in Telford on Sunday 31 May to come forward.

At around 11pm officers were called to reports of a fire involving several pallets in one of the industrial units.

Colleagues from Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service attended and extinguished the fire.

Enquiries are still on-going but police urge anyone with information or footage to contact Detective Sergeant Stephen Goddard on 101 quoting incident umber 867s of 31052. Alternatively information can be given to Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555111.
