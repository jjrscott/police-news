West Mercia Police officers are appealing for information in connection with an incident at the Telford Tigers v Bradford Bulldogs ice hockey game on Saturday 18 January at Telford Ice Rink.

There was an altercation between two players on the ice which has resulted in a member of the Bradford Bulldogs receiving facial injuries, which required medical treatment.

The incident took place during the match at around 8pm.

Det Sgt Danielle Logan said: "We are actively seeking information from the attendees to support our investigation.

"If you have any information regarding this please call 01952 214736 quoting reference 312s 200120."
