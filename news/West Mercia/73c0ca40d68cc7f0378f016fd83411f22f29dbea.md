Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision on Spennells Valley Road, Kidderminster yesterday (Wednesday 11 December).

Officers were called at 11.30pm to reports that a white BMW has been in collision with a blue Saab on Spennells Valley Road in the direction of Comberton Hill roundabout.

Sadly, the driver of the BMW, an 22 year old man, died at the scene. His next of kin has been informed.

The driver of the Saab was uninjured.

Officers are keen to speak to any member of the public who may have witnessed the incident or may have dash cam footage.

If any member of the public has seen anything can they ring 101, quoting reference 0748S 111219 .
