Police are appealing for witnesses after a road incident on the A5 near Shrewsbury.

At around 11.20am on Sunday 11 October 2020 a white BMW and a Honda Motorbike were on the westbound carriageway of the A5 By Pass in Shrewsbury when the motorists were involved in an altercation.

It was reported that the motorcyclist stopped on the carriageway and confronted the BMW driver at Dobbies roundabout. They continued on their journeys and then stopped again prior to Woodcote roundabout (Royal Shrewsbury Hospital turning) where the motorcyclist has confronted the BMW driver and allegedly caused damage to the BMW.

Anyone with dash-cam footage or has witnessed the incident should contact PC Wagg on

07870219414 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Alternatively information can be passed online through our website.
