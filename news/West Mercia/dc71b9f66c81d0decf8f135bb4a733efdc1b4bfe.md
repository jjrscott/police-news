West Mercia Police release CCTV images to appeal for witnesses of an assault outside a bar located on New Road, Kidderminster at around 3am on Saturday 2 November 2019.

A man in his 20s received serious injuries to his face and foot after being struck by two/three men believed to be in their 20s.

PC Luke Catmur said: "In the early hours of Saturday 2 November we believe an assault took place outside Nu Bar on New Road, Kidderminster.

"We are releasing CCTV images that may help to jog people's memories that were in the area at this time that may have seen an altercation. If you did see an anything please contact us on 101."

Enquiries are currently ongoing but anyone who witnessed the incidents is asked to call police on 101 quoting number 104ss 020119
