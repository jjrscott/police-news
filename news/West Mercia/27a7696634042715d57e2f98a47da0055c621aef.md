Officers in Hereford are appealing for witnesses after two groups of men were reportedly fighting on Saturday 26 September.

The incident happened on Union Street in Hereford at around 10pm in the middle of the road by The Ruby. Officers are keen to speak all those involved and while some of those who were present have been spoken to, they looking to speak to all those involved.

If anyone witnessed the incident or recognises anyone form the CCTV image, please contact us here
