We are appealing for witnesses including dash-cam footage following a road traffic collision where two cars collided at the junction of Breachers Lane and Icknield Street Drive (B4497) in Redditch.

The incident occurred last night (Wednesday 17 February) at around 8.20pm.

Police, Fire service and Ambulance were all in attendance.

Icknield Street Drive, Breaches Lane, and nearby roads Morsefield Lane and Bartleet Road were closed for around four hours, and are now reopen.

Two men both aged 19 years-old were conveyed to hospital by ambulance.

Investigating Officers are appealing for information from the public including dash-cam footage where a black Audi TT and a red BMW 118d collided.

Anyone with information should call police on 101 or report via our website, quoting incident number 587i of 17 February.

If you have information but don't feel comfortable speaking to police, you can speak to the independent charity Crimestoppers. It is 100% anonymous, they never ask your name and they cannot trace your call or I.P address. You can contact them online or by calling 0800 555 111.
