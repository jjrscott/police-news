Breaking News Post

Officers have made an arrest following multiple reports of a man exposing himself to people around Blakedown, Clent and in areas along the border with West Midlands and Staffordshire.

Sergeant Lisa Hornberger said: "We were called to the latest incident at Broome Lane on Thursday 11/02/2021 at around 15:30. A man in his 20s from Kingswinford has been arrested on suspicion of indecent exposure and public indecency and is in police custody.

"I would like to reassure our communities that we take these reports very seriously and we encourage any victims or witnesses to come forward and all reports are dealt with sensitively. If you have any information that can help in this investigation please contact PC 21512 Williams at Kidderminster Police station via 101 or online via our reporting page here.
