Officers were contacted earlier this afternoon (Friday 10 April) after a woman was reportedly witnessed assaulting a child while walking along Chedworth Drive in Worcester.

Following an investigation, a woman in her 30s from Worcester has been arrested on suspicion of assault and is in police custody.

Detective Sergeant Dave Hall said: "I would like to thank the community for their support and to the member of the public for reporting this incident to us. The child did not require medical attention and is safe, staying with a friend of the family."
