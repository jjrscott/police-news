We are appealing for witnesses following an assault at around 7.10pm this evening (6 February) in the area of Bembridge, Telford.

A 26 year old man was assaulted by a number of youths, following the incident he realised he had suffered multiple knife wounds. Police are currently conducting enquiries in the area. The victim is at hospital undergoing assessment

We would like to thank the residents in the area for their cooperation and are appealing for any witnesses to the assault or for any information concerning it.

Please contact us on 101 quoting reference 641s 060220.
