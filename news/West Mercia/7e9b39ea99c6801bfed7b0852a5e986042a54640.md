A man has been arrested after cars were damaged in Coalbrookdale.

Around 4am this morning police received a number of reports cars were being smashed in and around Darby Road.

A 47 year old man has been arrested and is in police custody.

Patrols have been increased in the area to offer reassurance to local residents.

Enquiries are on-going and police are appealing for witnesses.

Anyone with information should contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 56 120320 or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
