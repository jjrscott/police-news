A local man has been charged with fraud offences in Telford.

Armardeep Rana, 30, from Far Valens in Hadley, has been charged with four counts of fraud. He has been remanded in custody to appear at Kidderminster Magistrates' Court on Monday 1 June.

The charges related to incidents where local residents have been duped into handing over money.
