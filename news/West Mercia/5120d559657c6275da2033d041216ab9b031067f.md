Six men have all been charged on suspicion of attempt murder.

Including 21, 22, 23, 25 and 28-year-olds from Worcester, along with a 30-year-old from Birmingham have all been charged.

It follows an incident in Wylds Lane, Worcester on Saturday 23 November.

_*A seventh man, aged-28-year-old has been released with no further action._
