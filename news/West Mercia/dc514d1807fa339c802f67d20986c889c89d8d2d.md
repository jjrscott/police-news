**A man is in custody following an armed robbery at the Mare & Colt in Summerfield on Monday 26 October.**

Detective Inspector Kirsty Hickling said: "A man in his 40s from Birmingham was arrested on suspicion of robbery last night (28 October) by West Midlands Police officers in connection with the robbery at the Mare & Colt in Summerfield and is in their custody.

"I would ask anyone with any information please still get in touch by reporting online here or by calling 101 and quote incident number 00575i of 26 October 2020."
