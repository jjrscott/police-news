Officers are appealing for any witnesses who may have seen a man acting suspiciously in Spennells Valley Road, Kidderminster, to please contact us.

The incident happened around 5.30pm on Tuesday 9 March when a man was seen by witnesses acting suspiciously near to the bushes on the road. Officers are keen to hear from anyone who was driving or riding any vehicles with recording devices along Spennells Valley Road or any of the nearby roads and who may be able to help with their investigation.

If you have any information or footage from in car or bike devices, please contact us via our online reporting page here quoting incident number 00546i of 9 March 2021.
