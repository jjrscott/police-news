Police have made a fifth arrest in relation to the attempted murder in Stourport.

A 27-year-old man was arrested today (Wednesday 16 December) in connection with the incident which occurred on Friday 11 December in Crossway green. Three men were injured during the incident and two of them remain in hospital.

This follows four arrests which were made on Monday (14 December). Three men aged 48, 18, and 21 and a 21-year-old woman, were also arrested on suspicion of murder and have since been bailed.
