Updated News Post

Officers are appealing for witnesses to a serious assault in the Wylds Lane area of Worcester over the weekend.

The assault happened between 11pm on Saturday the 23 November and 1am on Sunday the 24 November 2019 in Wylds Lane area of Worcester where a 22 year old man suffered serious injuries as a result and continues to be treated in hospital.

Detective Chief Inspector Andy Bailey stated: "Whilst we acknowledge that this is a very serious incident I would like to reassure the local community that we are working hard to apprehend the offender(s) responsible for this assault.

"We have increased our patrols within the Wylds Lane area and I would encourage anyone with any information that may help or anyone who may have witnessed this incident to call West Mercia Police on 101 and quote incident number 54S of the 24 November 2019."

Issued by Alex Gale, Communications Officer at 7.35pm on Sunday 24 November 2019
