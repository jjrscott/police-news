We are currently dealing with an incident at an address in Woodlands, Evesham.

Inspector Peter Nightingale said: "Emergency services are at the scene and a man has been taken to hospital by air ambulance after suffering stab wounds.

"We would like to reassure the public that this is believed to be an isolated incident and we currently have a number of officers in the local area as investigations continue."
