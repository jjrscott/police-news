A Shrewsbury woman has been charged in connection to an incident at a Co-Op in Bomere Heath.

The incident happened at around 7.30pm on Saturday 30 November at the convenience store on the Shrewsbury Road.

Adina McLean, 21, of Crowmere Green, Shrewsbury has been charged with robbery and possession of an intimation firearm and will appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court this morning (Monday 9 December).
