A woman has died following a collision in Telford.

The incident happened at around 10.15am today (Tuesday, 11 August) on the A442 near River Lane in Waters Upton.

A 48-year-old woman suffered fatal injuries as a result of the collision.

The road is currently closed to allow emergency services to deal with the incident.

Sergeant Alexander Saxton said: "Our thoughts are with the woman's family and friends at this difficult time. An investigation is underway to establish the circumstances of the collision."
