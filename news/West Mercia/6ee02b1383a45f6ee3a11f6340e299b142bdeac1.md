A woman who was found dead at an address in Pembridge, Herefordshire, has been formally identified.

Officers were called to the property at around 12.20pm on Friday 29 January where sadly Ann Turner, aged 82, died at the scene.

A 56 year-old man, who was arrested on suspicion of murder following the death, is currently still detained under the Mental Health Act. Officers have confirmed that they are not looking for anyone else in connection to this incident.

Please note - Ann Turner's family have requested privacy at this time.
