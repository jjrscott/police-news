Officers are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision on the A46 Twyford to Evesham on Saturday 30 November.

Emergency services were called around 11.40pm to reports of a collision between vehicle and a bicycle travelling towards Hinton on the Green.

The ambulance service attended but the cyclist unfortunately died at the scene. The driver of the other vehicle was not injured.

If you witnessed the incident or have any information in relation to it please contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 823s of 30 November 2019.
