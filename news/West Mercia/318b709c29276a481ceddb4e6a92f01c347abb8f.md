Police recovered suspected stolen property and want to reunite the items with the owners.

It is believed the property has been stolen overnight Tuesday 5 May - Wednesday 6 May from the Wellington area.

A 38 year old man was arrested on Regent Street, Wellington on Wednesday 6 May.

A number of items were seized including; three watches, three rings and some foreign coins.

Anyone who thinks any of the property belongs to them, can contact Pro-Active Criminal Investigation Department, Malinsgate, Telford on 01952 214793 and quote 22/GA/11064/20.
