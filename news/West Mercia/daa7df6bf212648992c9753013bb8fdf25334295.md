Police were called to a man acting suspiciously parked in a car on Shap Drive, in Worcester at around 8am this morning (Thursday 3 September).

On arrival the man and the vehicle were no longer in the area. Police are carrying out reassurance patrols and thoroughly investigating this incident.

It is believed the man was driving a black VW Golf car and is described as having dark hair, tall, wearing a black bomber jacket gilet and black t-shirt.

If you have information or saw the man or car in the area this morning please contact PC Hopes on 101 quoting reference 71 I 030920

You can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Alternatively information can be passed through the online reporting function on West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
