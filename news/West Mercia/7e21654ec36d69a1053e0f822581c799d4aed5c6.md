Following two breaches of Criminal Behaviour Order CBO and Assaulting an Emergency Worker, Donna Barnes, 43 from Worcester has been arrested and charged before a remand court video linked to Kidderminster Magistrates Court

Donna Barnes was arrested yesterday (21 April) from a supermarket in St Martins Quarter after breaching the terms of CBO and spitting at officers. She has been committed to prison for 16 weeks and an additional four weeks for breaching the CBO.
