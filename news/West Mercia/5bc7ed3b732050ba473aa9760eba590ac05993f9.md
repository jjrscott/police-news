We are appealing for witnesses following a robbery at Costcutters in Bromsgrove.

At around 8.10pm on Monday 2 November it is believed that two men wearing balaclavas entered the store on Stourbridge Road, Bromsgrove and threatened staff.

The men, carrying crowbars took cash from the till and cigarettes before leaving the scene.

They are described as:

A white man around 6ft tall, a stocky build wearing all black clothing, a black beanie hat, black gloves and face covering.

The second man was white, around 5ft 10" tall with a stocky build. He was wearing all black clothing including a black beanie hat, face covering and was carrying a black rucksack.

If you have any information regarding the incident or saw any suspicious behaviour in the area please report via our website quoting reference 506_i_02112020.
