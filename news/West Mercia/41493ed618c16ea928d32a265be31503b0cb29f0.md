Three men have been charged with drug related offences following their arrests in Rowley Regis, Birmingham and Wednesbury in the West Midlands.

Syed Alom, aged 32, of Highfield Road, Rowley Regis, Mohammed Islam, aged 27 of No Fixed Address, and Shakir Ali, aged 32 of Sycamore Road, Wednesbury, were all charged with conspiracy to supply Class A drugs between January 1 2018 and 16 December 2020.

They attended a virtual court at Kidderminster Magistrates Court on Thursday 17 December and were remanded into custody.

The arrests were made on Tuesday 15 December.
