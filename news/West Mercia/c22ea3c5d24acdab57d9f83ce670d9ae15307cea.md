Police are appealing for information after an assault on the Forge Retail Park in Telford.

The incident happened around 3pm on Tuesday 25 August in the car park of McDonalds.

A 21-year-old woman was grabbed by a man who then smashed her phone.

A 24-year-old man has been arrested.

It's believed those involved are known to each other.

Officers are carrying out enquiries and are appealing for any witnesses.

Anyone with information is asked to contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 00420_I_250820 or alternatively information can be reported online under the Tell Us About section www.westmercia.police.uk or passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
