People in Evesham this evening may have been aware of an increased police presence around the Burford Road area at about 7.30pm.

Inspector Fergus Green said: "I would like to reassure the public that the armed officers and other policing units that will have been seen around the Burford Rd area of Evesham this evening were responding to reports of a concern for man's welfare.

"I am pleased to say that the situation has been resolved without incident and the report fully investigated. However, high visibility patrols will remain in the area to provide community reassurance throughout the evening."
