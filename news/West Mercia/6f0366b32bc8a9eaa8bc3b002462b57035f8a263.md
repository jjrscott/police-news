**Updates in bold**

Mark Chilman, aged 51 from Pencombe, Bromyard, has been charged with murder and remanded to Kidderminster Magistrates' Court to appear tomorrow (Thursday 17 December).

He was charged in relation to an incident that occurred in Cotheridge on Saturday 12 December.

A 27-year-old man remains in custody. **He has now been released on bail.**
