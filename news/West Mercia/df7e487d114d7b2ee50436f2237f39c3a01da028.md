Officers in Kidderminster are investigating an incident of arson that happened on Monday 10 February.

At around 3am a van parked on Farfield, Kidderminster was set alight. The vehicle was not able to be salvaged.

PC Connor Chambers said "Reassurance patrols have been and will continue to be conducted in the local area to safeguard residents.

"We are appealing for witnesses to the incident or any suspicious behaviour prior to the incident are asked to contact me on 07900608441 quoting reference number 40s 100220. We will be making contact with anyone who has already contacted us in due course. Alternatively reports can be made anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111"
