A 30-year-old-man, from Sparkhill, Birmingham, has been arrested and is currently in police custody.

The arrest is in connection with an incident that took place in the Wylds Lane area or Worcester, between 11pm on Saturday 23 November and 1am on Sunday 24 November 2019 where a 22-year-old man suffered serious injuries as a result.

He is currently stable and continues to be treated in hospital.

Worcestershire Local Police Commander, Superintendent Damian Pettit said: "The investigation is still on-going and we currently have a man in custody who is helping us with our enquiries.

"I understand this has had a large impact on people in the area and would like to extend our thanks to the community for assisting with our enquiries. We continue to have an increased presence within the area and I would encourage anyone with any information that may help to call West Mercia Police on 101 and quote incident number 54s of the 24 November 2019 or alternatively Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
