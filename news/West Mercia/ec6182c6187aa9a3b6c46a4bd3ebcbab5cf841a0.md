**Police are appealing for witnesses to reports of a man being racially abused in a supermarket on Alvechurch Highway, Redditch on 10 November 2019.**

The victim was reportedly verbally abused while shopping in the Sainsbury's on Abbey Trading Centre in Redditch. Officers would like to speak to the man in the image as they believe he can help in their investigation.

If you have seen him or know who he is, please contact 101 and quote incident log 375s of the 10 November 2019.

**Issued by Alex Gale, Communications Officer at 3.35pm on Thursday 14 November 2019**
