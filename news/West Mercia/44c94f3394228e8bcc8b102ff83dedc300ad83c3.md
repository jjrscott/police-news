Officers are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision on the A456 Tenbury Wells road towards Worcester.

Emergency services were called around 3.10pm on Wednesday 20 November 2019 to reports of a collision between two cars. The collision happened about one mile outside of Tenbury Wells.

One of the drivers, a man in his late 80s sadly died in the collision. It is believed he suffered a medical episode at the wheel, losing control of his vehicle and striking the other car. The driver of the second car suffered neck injuries but is in a stable condition.

The family of the man who died have been notified and officers are supporting them at this very sad time.

If you witnessed the incident or have any information in relation to it please contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 434s of 20 November 2019.
 **
Issued by Alex Gale, Communications Officer at 10.45am on Thursday 21 November 2019**
