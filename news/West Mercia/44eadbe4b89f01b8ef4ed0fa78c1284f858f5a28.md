A 48 year old man from Kidderminster, Jason Baker has been charged with three offences.

Two of the offences (common assault of an emergency worker) relate to purposefully and forcibly breathing into two police officer's faces after disclosing he had contracted Covid-19. This followed his arrest on suspicion of assault for which he has also been charged.

Baker appeared at Kidderminster Magistrates court this morning (21 April) where he was remanded to again appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court on 28 April and then at Worcester Crown Court on 18 May.
