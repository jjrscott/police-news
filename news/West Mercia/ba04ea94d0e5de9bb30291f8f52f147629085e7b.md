A further three men have been arrested in connection with an incident that occurred in Wylds Lane over the weekend.

A 21-year-old, 23-year-old and 25-year-old, all from Worcester, have been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder and are currently in police custody.

Three men continue to remain in police custody. (A 22-year-old man and 28-year-old man, both from Worcester and 30-year-old from Sparkhill, Birmingham).

The arrests are in connection with an incident that took place in the Wylds Lane area or Worcester, between 11pm on Saturday 23 November and 1am on Sunday 24 November 2019.

A 22-year-old man sustained potentially life changing injuries and he is currently in a stable condition in hospital.

Detective Chief Inspector Ian Wall said: "I would like to reassure the community we have now made six arrests in connection with an assault that occurred in Wylds Lane, Worcester over the weekend (23-24 November). The individuals remain in custody, while we continue to progress our enquiries."

"Thank you to members of the public that have helped us with our enquiries. The investigation is on-going and I would like to take this opportunity to ask anyone who may have seen or heard anything in relation to the incident that we would welcome your information - no matter how small. You can report this either by visiting Worcester Police Station, calling 101 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
