We are appealing for witnesses after a two vehicle collision on A438 Stoke Edith to Tarrington, Hereford, yesterday afternoon (Wednesday, 16 October).

The incident happened at around 3.55pm when a black Honda CBR motorcycle and a blue Renault Clio, were travelling towards Tarrington.

The rider of the motorcycle a 21-year-old was taken to hospital with serious injuries but is now in a stable condition.

The 18-year-old driver of the Clio sustained minor injuries.

Anyone with any information or dash cam footage is asked to contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting incident number 462s of 16 October 2019.
