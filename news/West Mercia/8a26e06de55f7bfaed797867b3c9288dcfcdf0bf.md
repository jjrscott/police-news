Police are appealing for witnesses after a cyclist was pushed from his bike near Beckford.

At around 3.25pm on Wednesday 7 October between Ashton Under Hill and Beckford a man in his seventies was pushed from his bike on Ashford Road by the occupants of a blue Ford Focus car. He suffered serious injuries to his leg and shoulder and is currently still in hospital in a stable condition.

Anyone with information is urged to call 101 quoting incident 692i of 7 October or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Alternatively information can be passed through the online reporting function on West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
