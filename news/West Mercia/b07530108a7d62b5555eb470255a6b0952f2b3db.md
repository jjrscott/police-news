The burglary happened between 12pm and 4pm on Friday 25 October in Kings Acre Road, Hereford.

Offenders gained entry via the rear of the property and multiple items have been stolen including; A Racemaster and a Fieldmaster Belstaff Jackets, a Pocket watch engraved with "Southern region railway", an Omega seamaster limited edition engraved "HO02", An Omega Seamaster Aquaterra serial number "8696" and a Rolex explorer 2 engraved "HO02".

PC Phil Millichip from Hereford said "This was obviously an incredibly distressing incident for the victim and I would urge anyone who may have seen these items for sale or that can help with our enquiries to please get in contact with us."

Anyone with information can contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 559s 251019 or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
