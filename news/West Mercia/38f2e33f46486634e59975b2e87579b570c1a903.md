Breaking News Post

A man from Kidderminster has pleaded guilty at Kidderminster Magistrate's Court today to possessing ammunition for a firearm without a certificate.

Finley Carse, 21 years old and from Carlton House, Oxford Street was arrested on Wednesday 2 September at his home following a police search in which a quantity of ammunition was found.

Carse was sentenced to two months in prison which was suspended for two years.
