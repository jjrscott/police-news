Officers in Droitwich are appealing for any information regarding the whereabouts of Matthew Marsh.

Marsh is currently thought to be sleeping rough in the Droitwich area and is known to sometimes stop in the Westlands Estate in Drotwich.

Marsh is wanted for five thefts from shops between 16 and 18 October 2020 as well as being wanted for a recall to prison.

If you've seen Marsh or know where he may be please call us on 101 or contact us here
