At approximately 6pm yesterday (16 June) police were called to an incident at an address on Church Street, Kington.

A 56 year old man has been taken to hospital to receive treatment for injuries he sustained in an altercation. A 57 year old man has been arrested and taken to Hereford custody.

DS Jamie Bullock said "This was a serious incident that we believe may have been seen by people in the local area,
"We are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed it to please contact us on 101 quoting reference 558S 160620."
