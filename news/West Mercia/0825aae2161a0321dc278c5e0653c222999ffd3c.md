Police are appealing for witnesses following a stabbing in Redditch on Saturday 1 August.

At around 2am a man in his 40s was walking from the direction of The Park Inn pub on Evesham Road towards Pool Bank with his son when a man approached the pair and stabbed the victim. He was left with injuries to the face and was taken to hospital for treatment.

The offender is described as white, with short brown hair and was wearing a fluorescent orange jacket.

Anyone with information is urged to call police on 101 quoting incident 115 of 1 August or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Alternatively information can be reported on the West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
