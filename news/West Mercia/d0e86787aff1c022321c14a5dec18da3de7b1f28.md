We are appealing for witnesses following a collision on Salt Way, Hanbury.

At 3.30pm yesterday (25/6) a blue Honda Civic has left the carriage way at the junction between Salt Way and Hanbury Road and has been in collision with a white Winnebago.

Did you see this incident take place, do you have any information that will help our investigation. Please contact PC Alcock on 101 referencing 500s 250620.
