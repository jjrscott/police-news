A man has been charged in connection with an assault that took place outside the Fownes Hotel in Worcester on Monday 25 May.

Tobi Hurding, aged 31, from Birmingham, was charged yesterday (Monday 25 May) with wounding with intent to do grievous bodily harm and robbery.

He will appear at Kidderminster Magistrates' Court today (Tuesday 26 May).

The 29-year-old man that sustained injuries has now been released from hospital.
