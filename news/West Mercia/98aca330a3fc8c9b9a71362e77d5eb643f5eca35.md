Officers from West Mercia Police are appealing for witnesses after a cyclist died on a country lane near Lawley Hill, Leebotwood, Church Stretton, Shropshire.

Police, the ambulance service and fire service were called to a report of a collision between a green tractor and a cyclist at just before 10.30am today (Thursday 24 October).

The male cyclist was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

If you witnessed the incident or have any information in relation to it please contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 203s of 24 October 2019.
