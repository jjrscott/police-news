Mark Kendall of Breakback Road, Bromsgrove, has been charged with the attempted murder of Dayle Warman on Sunday 1 December 2019 in Villiers Road, Bromsgrove.

The 36 year old appeared charged with the offence at Kidderminster Magistrates Court on Thursday, 13 February and was released on bail with conditions until 12 March when he will appear at Worcester Crown Court.

Detective Inspector Jim Hopkins said: "I would like to thank the public for their support in this investigation."
