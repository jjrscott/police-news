Three men are due to appear at Kidderminster Magistrates' Court today following an incident in Queens Road, Bridgnorth, on Tuesday 7 January.

Eddie Jones, aged 30, of Shelton Avenue, Warlingham, Surrey, has been charged with two counts of robbery, receiving stolen goods and possession of criminal property.

Acer Bridges, aged 38, of High House Lane, Tardebigge, Worcestershire, has been charged with two counts of robbery, receiving stolen goods and possession of criminal property.

Elias Taylor, aged 42, of Garlands Lane, Barlestone, Nuneaton, has been charged with two counts of robbery, receiving stolen goods and possession of criminal property.
