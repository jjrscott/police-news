A 28-year-old man from Gloucestershire has been charged with assaulting two police officers.

Thomas Ramm was arrested on suspicion of common assault of two emergency workers after officers reported being coughed at on Thursday 23 April in Pershore. He was later charged.

Ramm, of Mickleton, Chipping Campden, has been bailed and is due to appear at Worcester Magistrates Court on Thursday 4 June.
