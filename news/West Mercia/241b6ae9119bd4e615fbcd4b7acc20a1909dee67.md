#### Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision in Shrewsbury.

The collision happened at 3.10pm yesterday afternoon (Monday 24 August) between a motorbike and car on Whitchurch Road at Heathgates.

The motorbike was travelling from Heathgates roundabout in the direction of Battlefield when the collision happened at the traffic lights.

The motorbike rider, a woman in her 20s, suffered serious injuries and was taken to hospital for treatment. Her injuries are not believed to be life-threatening.

Enquiries are on-going into the collision and police are appealing for witnesses, including any motorists in the area with dash cam.

Anyone with information can contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 00335_I_24082020 or alternatively information can be reported online under the Tell Us About section of the West Mercia Police website www.westmercia.police.uk
