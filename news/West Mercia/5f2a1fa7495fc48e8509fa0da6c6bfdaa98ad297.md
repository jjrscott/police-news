We are appealing for witnesses including dash-cam footage following a road traffic collision where a car and a motorbike collided near to the **local public house** located by the roundabout where Rose Hill meets Groveley **Lane** , **in Rednal** , Bromsgrove.

The incident occurred today (Monday 21 October) at around at around 7.15am.

A 20-year-old man was conveyed to hospital by ambulance. His injuries are not thought to be life threatening.

Investigating Officers are appealing for information from the public including dash-cam footage where a grey Renault Clio registration beginning RX5 and a blue Suzuki GSX-R motorbike registration beginning BU69 collided.

Anyone with information should call police on 101, quoting incident number 73s of the 21 October 2019.

Alternatively, if you want to give information but don't want to give your name you can call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or use their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
