The man arrested in connection with the death in Telford yesterday (Saturday 20 March) has been interviewed and has now been released from custody on Police Bail.

At around 12:30am Police were called by the Ambulance Service following the death of a woman in her fifties at her home in Malinslee.

We would like to reassure the community that this is believed to be an isolated incident and enquiries are continuing to establish the cause of death.
