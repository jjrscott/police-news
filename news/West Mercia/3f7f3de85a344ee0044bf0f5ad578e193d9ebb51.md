We are appealing for witnesses following a three vehicle Road Traffic Collision (RTC) on Parsonage Way, Worcester on Tuesday 11 August at around 5.50pm.

A beige Fiat 500 registration ending AWP, a blue Mini Cooper registration ending NVX and a white Mann breakdown recovery truck registration ending WWF collided.

Three people have been taken to hospital. A 20 year-old man and 19-year-old woman remain in a critical condition in hospital. A 21-year-old man remains in hospital with injuries that are not thought to be life threatening.

Investigating Officers are appealing for information from the public including dashcam footage.

Operational Patrol Unit Sergeant Matt Digger said: "We are keen to speak with anyone who may have seen the incident on the Parsonage Way, in Worcester.

Unfortunately two people remain in critical condition, and a third remains in hospital with serious injuries not thought to be life threatening.

"We are especially keen to see any dashcam footage from the area at the time of the incident. If you have any information please report it via our website or 101."

 To help with our investigation we're keen to speak to anyone who witnessed the incident or has any other information that could help with our enquiries.

Anyone with information should call police on 101 or use the 'report' section on westmercia.police.uk, quoting incident number 585_I_11082020.

Alternatively, if you want to give information, but don't want to give your name, you can call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or use their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
