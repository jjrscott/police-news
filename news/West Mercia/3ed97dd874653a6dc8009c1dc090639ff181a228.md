Officers in Redditch are appealing for any information or witnesses to get in touch after a man was reportedly assaulted on Christmas Day.

PC Gemma Sparkes said: "The man in his early 20s, was reportedly assaulted in the upstairs bar at Pop World, a night club in Redditch in the early hours of Christmas Day. The victim was near to the bar when he was hit with a bottle around the head and knocked unconscious. He was taken to hospital where he received treatment for his injuries.

"If anyone has any information or witnessed the incident please contact West Mercia Police by calling 101 and quoting 169s of 25 December 2019."
