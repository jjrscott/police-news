Police are carrying out enquiries after five car fires in Brookside, Telford.

Officers were called around 1am early this morning (Wednesday 23 October) to reports of three cars on fire in Brackenfield and two cars on fire in Brindleyford.

It's believed the fires were started deliberately and are being treated as arson.

Enquiries are on-going and police are appealing for witnesses.

Patrols have been increased in the area to offer reassurance to local residents.

Anyone with information should contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference on 13s 231019 or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
