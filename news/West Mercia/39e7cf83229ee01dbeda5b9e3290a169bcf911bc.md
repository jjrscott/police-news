Officers are appealing for any information following a large disorder that took place in the early hours of Sunday 28 June in Exhall Close, Redditch.

There were reports from members of the public that at least ten people were involved and that there were weapons present. A 21 year old man visiting the area was taken to hospital with a head injury and has since been discharged.

If anyone has any information about the incident could they please contact PC Richard Britton on 101 quoting incident number 0034s of the 28 June 2020.
