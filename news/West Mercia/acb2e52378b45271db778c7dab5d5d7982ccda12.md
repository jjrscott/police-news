**A man has been charged with burglary following a number of linked offences in Charford, Bromsgrove.**

Mark Eades, of Humphrey Avenue, Bromsgrove, was arrested on Friday 19 March and has been charged with burglary following an investigation into a number of break-ins in the Charford area.

The 41-year-old has been remanded into custody and is due to appear in Crown Court on 19 April 2021 after being denied bail at Kidderminster Magistrates on Saturday 20 March.
