Officers are appealing for witnesses following reports of an assault in Evesham on Friday 13 December.

The assault happened at the Bridge Inn, Boat Lane, Offenham in Evesham at around 11pm. A fight reportedly broke out when the landlord attempted to eject a group of people from the pub.

During the incident several of those involved received serious injuries and were taken to hospital.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who has any information is asked to call 101 and quote and incident number 0613s of 14 December 2019. You can also give information anonymously via Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555111'
