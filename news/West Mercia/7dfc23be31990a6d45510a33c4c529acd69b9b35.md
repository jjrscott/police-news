West Mercia Police is appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision in Stourport this morning (Thursday 26 September).

Shortly after 7am, a collision took place between a lorry and a pedestrian on Worcester Road.

The pedestrian, a man in his forties, sustained serious injuries and sadly died at the scene. His next of kin has been informed.

Road closures were in place whilst emergency services were in attendance.

Investigating officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the incident. If you have any information, please call West Mercia Police on 101 quoting incident number 72s of 26 September 2019.
