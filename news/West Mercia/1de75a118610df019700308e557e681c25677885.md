We are pleased to report missing 15 year old Brian Walsh from Hindlip, Worcestershire, has been found safe and well.

Thank you to everyone who has shared our appeal to find him. The appeal was key in finding Brian, after a member of the public reported a sighting following seeing the media appeal.
