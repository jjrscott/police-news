A 30 year-old man, from Wichenford, Worcestershire, has been arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to murder; and a 28 year-old man, from Worcester, has been re-arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to murder.

The arrests have been made this morning (Thursday 21 January) in connection with the murder of Neil Parkinson, who was sadly found dead within a vehicle in Cotheridege, Worcestershire, on Saturday 12 December 2020.

One man has been charged with murder and remains in custody until he appears in Worcester Crown Court in August 2021.
