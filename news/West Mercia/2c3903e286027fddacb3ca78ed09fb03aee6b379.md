Ernie Taylor, 33 years old and from High Street, Stourport was arrested on Saturday July 4 after allegedly spitting at police officers when they attended reports of a violent incident in Stourport.

Taylor was charged with assaulting an emergency worker and criminal damage. He was remanded into custody and due to appear at Kidderminster Magistrates court today (Monday 6 July).
