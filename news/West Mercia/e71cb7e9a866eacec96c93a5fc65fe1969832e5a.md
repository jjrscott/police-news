Officers are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision involving a car and a bus on Bromyard Road, Worcester.

The incident happened on Monday 18 May at 7.30am when a red BMW and bus collided.

Investigating officers are appealing for information from the public including dash-cam footage.

PC Danny Somner said: "We are keen to speak to anyone who may have seen the incident near to the junction of Blakefield Gardens. A woman in her 50s was taken to hospital with serious injuries.

"We are especially keen to see any dash-cam footage from the area at the time of the incident. If you have any information please contact us."

 Anyone with information should call on 101, quoting incident number 0072s of 180520.

Alternatively, if you have any information but don't want to give your name you can call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
