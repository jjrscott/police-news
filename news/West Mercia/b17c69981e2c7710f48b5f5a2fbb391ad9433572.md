Police are appealing for the victim of an assault on Daddlebrook Road, Aveley to come forward.

At around 9.50am last Friday (28 August) the victim, a man in his eighties, was involved in a verbal altercation with another man.

He punched the victim and threw him to ground leaving him with an arm injury.

The offender drove off in a silver estate and is described as medium height, in his late forties wearing a black t-shirt and dark trousers.

Anyone with information is urged to call 101 quoting incident 141 of 28 August or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Alternatively information can be passed through the online reporting function on West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
