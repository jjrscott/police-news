Three men have been jailed after a man was attacked with a baseball bat in Wellington.

On December 8 Habib Mohammed and Idris Mohamed confronted a 46 year old man in Regent Street before punching and kicking him to the ground. The pair were then joined by Ammir Fiaz, who arrived at the scene in a vehicle and attacked the victim with a baseball bat.

The victim suffered considerable cuts and bruising as a result of the assault.

On Thursday (19 September) the three men were sentenced at Shrewsbury Crown Court.

Habib Mohammed, 23 from Windsor Road in Arleston was sentenced to two years in prison for ABH and witness intimidation, Ammir Fiaz, 23 of Dawley Road in Arleston was sentenced to 15 months for ABH and possession of an offensive weapon and Idris Mohmammed, 20 of Windsor Road in Arleston was sentenced to three years and three months for ABH and possession with intent to supply class A.
