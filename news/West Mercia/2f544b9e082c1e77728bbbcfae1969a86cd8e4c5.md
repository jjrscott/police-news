Updated News Post

**UPDATED** At around 8am this morning (Saturday 14 November) we received reports of a serious collision on the A441 in Alvechurch involving a car and two bicycles.

Two people, both cyclists, were seriously injured in the collision and sadly, one of the victims, a man in his 40s, died. The other victim, a man in his 20s continues to receive medical care at hospital and is in a critical condition.

**A woman in her 20s from Redditch has been arrested on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving and has been released pending investigation. The collision resulted in the closure of the A441 which then remained closed for several hours.**

If you witnessed the incident, have dashcam footage or have any information that can help officers with their enquiries, please contact the officer in the case, PC 3779 Boswell at Bromsgrove on 101 quoting incident number 00093i of 14 November, or by using our online reporting function on West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About here
