West Mercia Police is appealing for witnesses or information following a reported exposure in Worcester on Friday 23 August.

Between 5pm to 6pm, it's reported a man exposed himself on a field at Power Park, Worcester.

The man is described as white, aged between 40 - 50 years, around 5ft 8in with a bald head and large build. He was wearing red rugby polo shirt and black jeans, and had a small striped shopping bag with him.

PC Molly-Ann Smith said: "A number of enquiries are currently ongoing, and we are keen to speak to anyone who saw the incident or who may have witnessed anyone acting suspiciously in the area at the time.

"Anyone with information is asked to call West Mercia Police on 101, quoting incident number 582s 23 August 2019."

One 44-year-old man, from Worcester has been arrested for outraging public decency and released on bail.

Alternatively, information can be given to the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.

Issued by Alex Gale, Communications Officer at 4pm on Thursday 5 September 2019
