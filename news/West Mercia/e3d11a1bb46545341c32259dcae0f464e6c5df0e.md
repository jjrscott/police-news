Police are appealing for witnesses following an incident in Ross-on-Wye town centre on Wednesday (29 July).

At around 1.50pm there was an altercation between two men. A man approached a BMW car on Broad Street, before verbally assaulting the occupants.

There was then a physical altercation between the man and a man in the vehicle in the street, near to WHSmiths.

Any witnesses place or anyone with information is asked to contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting 335 I 29072020. Alternatively information can be reported on the West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
