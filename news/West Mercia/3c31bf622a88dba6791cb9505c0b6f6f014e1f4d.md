Three men are in custody following an incident in Queens Road, Bridgnorth this morning (Tuesday 7 January).

Police officers were called at just after 10am after they received reports that a property had been burgled. Two people were assaulted by the intruders and a quantity of cash and a bottle of whiskey were taken during the burglary.

Detective Chief Inspector Mike Nally said: "This was a particularly nasty burglary where a couple were assaulted in their home. We have arrested three people in connection with this incident and they are currently in police custody."
