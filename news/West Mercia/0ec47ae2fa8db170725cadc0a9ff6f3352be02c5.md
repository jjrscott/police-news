At around 7am on Wednesday 25 March officers received a call that there had been a reported burglary at a commercial premises in Bromsgrove.

Following an investigation at the property on St. Godwalds Road in Bromsgrove a number of items have been stolen including a white VW van and some motor cross motorbike equipment.

 Pictured: Stolen bike

PC Mike Smith said: "We would like to hear from anyone who has seen saw the stolen vehicle being through Bromsgrove around 6am on Wednesday 25 March. The registration of the stolen van is AF62 ZWP and attached is a photo of the motor cross bike that was stolen.

"If you have any information that may help with our investigation or witnessed anything suspicious please call West Mercia Police on 101 and quote incident number 039s of 25 March 2020."
