A body has been recovered by officers searching for a missing Telford woman.

49-year-old Amanda Caldwell has been missing since the end of November.

Enquiries have been carried out to find her, however sadly, earlier today (Monday 11 January), officers carrying out searches recovered a body.

While formal identification has yet to take place it is believed to be Amanda and her family has been informed.
