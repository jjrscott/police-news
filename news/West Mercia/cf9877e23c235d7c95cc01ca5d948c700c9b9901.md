**West Mercia Police is appealing for witnesses or information following a collision at the ****Junction of the A449 and B4224 near to Old Gore, Ross on Wye.**

At around 2.45pm on Monday 23 September, a collision took place between a Kia Picanto car and a Peugeot Expert on the A449 near to old Gore.

The driver of Kia, a woman in her 50s, sustained serious injuries and was airlifted to hospital where she remains. The driver of the other vehicle sustained no serious injuries.

Road closures and diversions were in place whilst emergency services were in attendance.

Investigating officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision or who may have any dashcam footage. If you have any information, please call West Mercia Police on 101, quoting incident number 450s 23 September 2019.

**Issued by: AG, Corporate Communications at 8.55am on Tuesday 24 September 2019**
