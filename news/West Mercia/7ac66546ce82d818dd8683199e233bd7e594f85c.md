We are appealing for information following an incident in Belbroughton, Worcestershire. At around 3.40pm on Tuesday (15 September) an 11 year old boy was in collision with a passing vehicle.

The boy was crossing the road after getting off the bus and has been in collision with a white Nissan Qashqai on Heath End Road, Belbroughton.

The boy received serious injuries but is in a stable condition in Birmingham Children's Hospital.

We are appealing for any witnesses to contact PC Matthew Hunt via 101 using reference 00427i of 15 September or report to us online via www.westmercia.police.uk Tell Us About section. If you have any dash cam footage of this incident please submit via our Operation Snap campaign https://www.westmercia.police.uk/police-forces/west-mercia-police/areas/west-mercia/campaigns/campaigns/2019/operation-snap/
