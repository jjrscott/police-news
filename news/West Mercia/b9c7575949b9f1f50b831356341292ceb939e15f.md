West Mercia Police is appealing for witnesses following an assault on Wednesday 29 April.

The incident happened on the number 58 Diamond Bus when one passenger, a woman, verbally abused and physically assaulted another female passenger around 5pm. When the incident occurred the bus was travelling from Woodrow onto the Studley Road towards the bus station on Ipsley Street.

A woman in her 50s from Redditch was arrested on suspicion of assault and of possessing a controlled drug. She remains in custody.

PC Megan McGeough said: "We're particularly concerned that the motivation for this assault was racial and if anyone has any information that could help us with our investigation please call 101, quoting incident reference 0380s of 29 April 2020.

Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org
