Police investigating the death of a woman in Upton on Severn in Worcester are appealing for witnesses.

Police were called around 8am yesterday morning (Saturday 17 November) after a 25 year old woman was found unresponsive in Causeway Lane.

The cause of death is not yet known.

Enquiries are continuing and police are appealing for anyone who was in the area between 11pm on Friday (15 November) and 8am on Saturday (16 November).

A 44 year old man arrested on suspicion of murder remains in police custody after detectives were granted extra time to question him.

Senior Investigating Officer, Detective Inspector Jim Bayliss said: "Our thoughts remain with the family of the woman who has died at this incredibly difficult time. We have specially trained officers who are offering support.

"Our enquiries have continued throughout today as we work to establish the circumstances around the woman's death and I would ask anyone who was near the fire station in Causeway Lane between around 11pm on Friday night and the early hours of Saturday to get in contact with us as they may have information that can help with our enquiries."

Anyone with information can ring West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 150s 161119 or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
