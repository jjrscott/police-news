Two people from Hereford were remanded to prison today. They were both arrested on Sunday 5 July following an allegation they attacked a police officer with a pair of scissors in an attempt to stab them before throwing boiling water over them.

Craig Rowe (41 years old) has been charged with attempted GBH and Hannah Currie (36) has also been charged with attempted GBH as well as assaulting an emergency worker by beating.

Both live in Mayberry Avenue in Hereford and appeared at court this afternoon (6 July).
