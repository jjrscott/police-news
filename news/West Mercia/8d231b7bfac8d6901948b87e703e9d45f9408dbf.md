In the early hours of this morning (Thursday 19 November) we received several calls in what was believed to be two men approaching cars suspiciously in Warndon Villages, Worcester.

Worcester response officers attended immediately after the calls and steadfastly searched the area.

A 31-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of theft from motor vehicle and is currently in police custody.

Inspector Al Denny, said: "We take these sorts of incidents very seriously and understand the lasting and devastating effect it can have on members of our community."

The investigation is on-going, if you witnessed any suspicious incidents or have CCTV or doorbell footage please contact us via our website.
