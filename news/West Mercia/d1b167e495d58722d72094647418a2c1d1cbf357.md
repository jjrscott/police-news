Earlier today police responded to reports a woman was being threatened by a man with a knife in Hadley Road in Telford.

The woman has been taken to hospital, the extent of her injuries are not yet known, however are not believed to be life-threatening. A second person, a man, was also taken to hospital, his injuries are not believed to be life-threatening.

Enquiries are on-going and a search of the local areas is being carried out to locate a named suspect. Initial enquiries suggest the incident is domestic related.

Patrols have been increased in the area to both assist with enquiries and offer reassurance to the local community.

Chief Inspector Graham Preece said: "Earlier this morning we responded to an incident in Hadley Road and are carrying out searches to locate a named suspect.

"There is an increased presence in the area as we carry out enquiries and patrols have been increased to offer reassurance to the local community."

Anyone who was in the area around 6.15am this morning, including any motorists with dash cam footage, are asked to get in contact with police on 101 quoting reference 70s 141019 or alternatively Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
