Following an police investigation, a 38 year old woman from Abbeydale in Redditch has been charged with 11 offences and appeared before a district judge at Kidderminster Magistrates Court today (13 May).

Jamila Akhtar was arrested at Redditch Train Station on Tuesday 12 May on suspicion of making threats to kill and harassment.

Subsequent to her arrest she was charged with four harassment offences and seven public order offences.

She has been remanded to prison and will appear before Kidderminster Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 19 May.
