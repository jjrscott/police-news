West Mercia Police is appealing for witnesses after a white Ford Transit Connect van failed to stop for police in Commercial Road, Hereford at around 11.30pm on Saturday 25 January.

We are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed the van driving in and around the area and are especially keen to hear from anyone with dash-cam footage of the incident, including footage that shows the van before or after it failed to stop for police.

Anyone that may have any information, especially dash-cam footage is asked to get in touch with PC Mansfield by calling Hereford Police Station on 01432 347119, quoting incident number 0769s of Saturday 25 January.

Information can also be given anonymously to the Independent Charity, Crimestoppers, by calling 0800 555 111 or by visiting their website: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
