West Mercia Police have released a statement following an assault on a woman in Bromyard, Herefordshire.

Inspector James Ashton said: "At approximately 11.30am on Saturday 23 November we received reports that a woman canvassing for the General Election on the High Street in Bromyard had been assaulted.

"Officers attended and spoke to the victim who highlighted that she didn't wish for charges to be brought. We continue to engage with both parties to ensure an appropriate outcome for the victim."

Any witnesses to the incident that haven't yet spoken to officers are asked to contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting incident number 265s of November 23.

You can also give information without giving your name by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or by visiting their website.
