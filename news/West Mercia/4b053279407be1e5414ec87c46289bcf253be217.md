Updated News Post

**Update: All four men have been bailed with conditions not to enter any hospital in England unless they have a medical reason to do so. **

We have arrested four men today (Tuesday 12 January) for suspicion of causing a public nuisance.

A 31 year-old from Bromsgrove, 34 year-old from Bromsgrove, 34 year-old from Droitwich and 37 year-old from Kidderminster currently remain in police custody.

The arrests have been made in relation to several incidents across hospitals within Worcestershire and Birmingham, Bromsgrove town centre and a park in Bromsgrove that have taken place between 31 December 2020 and 9 January 2021.
