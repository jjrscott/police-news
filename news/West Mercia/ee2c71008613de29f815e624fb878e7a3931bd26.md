Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Telford.

The burglary happened around 8pm on Monday 30 September in Finchale Avenue in Priorslee.

Three men broke into the property and stolen a pair of Nike trainers.

It's believed the offenders then made off in a red car.

Officers are carrying out enquiries and appealing for witnesses.

Anyone with information should contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 22/91581/19 or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
