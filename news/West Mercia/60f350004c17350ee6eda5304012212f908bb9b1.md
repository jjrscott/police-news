A man has suffered serious injuries after a house fire in Oswestry.

Police were called to a fire in College Road around 6.50pm last night (Wednesday 23 October).

A 50 year old man was taken to hospital with serious injuries.

A second person in the house was not injured.

The cause of the fire is not yet known and police are working with fire investigators.
