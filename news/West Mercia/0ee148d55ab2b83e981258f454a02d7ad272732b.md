#### Officers are appealing for witnesses or information after three statues were stolen from Ullingswick, near Hereford.

It is believed the offenders removed the three Brian Alabaster statues sometime between midnight on Friday 5 February and midnight on Sunday 7 February 2021.

Anyone who has any information about the theft is asked to contact us via our Tell us About section on our website www.westmercia.police.uk or call us on 101 quoting incident 22/9948/21.

Alternatively, if you have information but don't feel comfortable speaking to police, you can speak to the independent charity Crimestoppers. It is 100% anonymous, they never ask your name and they cannot trace your call or I.P address. You can contact them online or by calling 0800 555 111.
