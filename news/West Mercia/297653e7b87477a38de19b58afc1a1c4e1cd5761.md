Police are appealing for witnesses after a burglary in Crabbs Cross, Redditch, where a number of sentimental jewellery items were stolen from a house.

The burglary happened on Evesham Road, between 6.50am and 12.23pm on Friday 11 October.

The offenders gained entry to the property and stole a quantity of jewellery including a gold ring with a pearl and two rubies set in it, a grey pearl necklace and a man's Omega gold and stainless steel watch.

Anyone with information is asked to contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 0282-S 11 October 2019 or alternatively Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
