The identity of the man killed at an address in Bromsgrove on Monday 14 October has now been confirmed.

The man has been formally identified as Wayne Coventry. Wayne was 36 years old.

Detective Inspector Lee Holehouse said: "I'd like to ask that the privacy of Wayne's family be respected at this very difficult time.

A woman from Birmingham was arrested on suspicion of murder on Wednesday night (16 October).

**Issued by Alex Gale, Communications Officer at 4.07pm on Friday 18 October 2019**
