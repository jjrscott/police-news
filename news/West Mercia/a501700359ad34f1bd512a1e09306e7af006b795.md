Following searches for a missing teenager at Shavers End Quarry in Dunley, near Stourport, we can confirm that sadly a body has been located.

Detective Inspector Justin Taylor: "It's with a heavy heart that we can confirm that a body has been found in our searches at the quarry. Whilst formal identification has not yet taken place we have updated the family and the coroner has been informed."

Searches for the 17-year-old began at the quarry after police were informed he was missing at around 4.45pm yesterday (Wednesday 12 August).
