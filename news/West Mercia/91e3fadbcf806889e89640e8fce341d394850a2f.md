Police enquiries are ongoing after a man suffered stab wounds in an incident in Woodlands, Evesham, yesterday (Thursday 19 November).

Detective Inspector Tony Garner said: "The victim, a 44-year-old man from Evesham, was airlifted to hospital and remains in a stable condition.

"Officers remain in the area and additional patrols are being carried out to offer reassurance to the local community. The incident is believed to be isolated with no risk to the wider public. If anyone was in the area at the time and saw anything suspicious, we would ask them to get in contact with us."

Anyone with information on this incident should ring West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 334i of 19 November or report online under the Tell Us About section. Alternatively, information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
