West Mercia Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident of dangerous driving in Worcester on Christmas Eve morning (Tuesday 24 December).

A report has been received that, at around 11.45am, a deep cherry red 4x4 Land Rover had driven over Worcester Bridge and onto New Road and was then driving erratically near the Cricket Club and Cripple Gate Park.

If you were travelling through the city around this time and saw the incident please contact DC Shelley Aplin on 01905 727470.

You can also submit dashcam footage via our Operation Snap webpage here: https://www.westmercia.police.uk/police-forces/west-mercia-police/areas/west-mercia/campaigns/campaigns/2019/operation-snap/
