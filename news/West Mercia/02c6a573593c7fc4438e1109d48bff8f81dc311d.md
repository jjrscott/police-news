Last night (3 November) we received a report of an assault at Brickfields Park, Worcester between 11.30pm and midnight.

The 35 year old man was approached by one man in his late teens to early twenties, with short blonde/brown hair, around 5ft 10ins and of a medium build, he wore a Nike hoodie and tracksuit bottoms and made off towards the main road.

The victim sustained minor injuries. __

DC Laura Griffiths from South Worcester CID said "We are appealing to the community to speak to the witness who kindly walked the victim home We feel they may have information that can support in the investigation. If this is you or you have any information relating to this incident, please contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 0007S 041119

Alternatively, information can be passed to the independent charity Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111 or via their website.
