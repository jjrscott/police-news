As part of our response to adapting to how we work remotely and safely during these testing times, the Regional Cyber Crime Unit is hosting an event to help individuals and businesses keep themselves protected against Cyber Crime.

**Cyber Security Q &A**

On Wednesdays 10:00am - 11:00am the Regional Cyber Crime unit will be hosting live 'Cyber Security Q&A' Sessions via Sli.do that allow people to ask any questions that they have regarding Cyber Security. This is open to the public but also available internally.

To ask a question the link is: https://app.sli.do/event/jvodjdcu . All questions will be answered live from 10:00am on Sli.do, along with a quiz to check peoples current knowledge around Cyber Security.
