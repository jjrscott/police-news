On Friday 22 November a man in his fifties was assaulted by a man at Barnards Green, Malvern.

At around 3pm, the victim was sat inside the 'Clock Shelter' bus shelter, when a man approached and assaulted him.

We are appealing for witnesses who may have seen this assault or have a description of the offender.

Anyone with information that may help police should contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 0829s 221119 or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
