A man has been arrested after police were called to a domestic incident in Evesham this morning.

Around 9am this morning police were called to Summerfield Gardens, during the incident a man climbed on to the roof of a property in the street and threw tiles into the road.

The road was closed while police dealt with the incident.

A man in his 20s has been arrested in connection with incident.
