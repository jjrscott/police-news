A JCB Telehandler and 4x4 pick up truck were stolen from farm land in South Worcestershire last week.

Overnight last Thursday (4 June) to Friday( 5 June) a JCB telehandler was stolen from a property on Upton Road, Powick & a Blue Mitsubishi pickup truck was also stolen from an address on Station Road, Worcester.

The vehicles have since been recovered in Leigh Sinton. Police are appealing for information from anyone who may have seen the vehicles or seen suspicious behaviour in the area.

West Mercia Police has a commitment to tackling these kind of rural crimes. Rural crime can have a huge impact on the communities, business and the individuals involved.

There are a few ways in which you can keep your property safe;

 * lock equipment away in a secure building or part of a building when not in use
 * invest in a secure storage toolbox
 * install a burglar alarm on buildings where equipment is kept
 * always lock vehicles when left outside and keep the keys in your possession
 * keep expensive items and vehicles out of sight when not in use
 * consider using hitch locks, wheel clamps or ground anchors
 * mark your tools and equipment and register them
 * keep a record of all valuable items
 * consider fitting outside security lights to deter thieves.

If you have information regarding the thefts please contact DC Max Voyce through 101 Quoting OIS - 0082S 050620
