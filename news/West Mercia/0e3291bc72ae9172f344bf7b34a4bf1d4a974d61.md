We are appealing for witnesses following a robbery at Asda, Donnington.

At around 10pm on Friday (24 July) night two teenage boys were approached in an alleyway near the supermarket. A 16 year old boy and a man believed to be 18 threatened the two boys with a knife and demanded their belongings and phones before leaving the scene.

The older of the two had dark hair, around 5ft 8ins tall, of a slim build, he wore a dark jacket, the second also of a slim build around 5ft 10ins, he was wearing a grey tracksuit.

If you have any information regarding the incident or saw any suspicious behaviour in the area please contact DS Bentley from Malinsgate Station quoting reference 862_i_24072020.
