A 15-year-old-boy from Redditch has, this evening (Wednesday 8 July), been charged with four counts of attempted murder.

He has been remanded into police custody and will appear at court tomorrow morning (Thursday 9 July) via video link.

The charges relate to a suspected arson in Redditch on 14 June 2020.

The teenager cannot be named for legal reasons.
