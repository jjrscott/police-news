Police are appealing for witnesses after a woman was assaulted in Telford.

The assault happened around 4pm on Wednesday 30 September in Plough Road in Wellington.

The victim, a 78-year-old woman, was walking from King Street towards Church Street when she was approached by a man who grabbed at her wrists.

The man made off in the direction of Wellington town centre turning left on to Church Street at the end of Plough Road.

He is described as white, slim build and approximately 5ft 7inches tall with light brown hair.

The woman suffered bruising as a result of the incident.

Enquiries are on-going and police are appealing for witnesses.

Anyone with information should contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 00732_I_01102020 or information can be reported online under the Tell Us About section of www.westmercia.pnn.police.uk.

Information can also be reported anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
