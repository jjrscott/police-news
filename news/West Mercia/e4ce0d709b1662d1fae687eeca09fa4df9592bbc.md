West Mercia Police appeal for witnesses following an altercation in supermarket carpark Redditch.

On Sunday 26 January, at approximately 8pm the occupant of a white Vauxhall Corsa and the occupant of a grey BMW have had an altercation in the supermarket carpark on Jinnah Road, Redditch.

PC Zowey Marshall from Redditch Station said "We are keen to speak to anyone who might have been in the car park at the time, as there were a number of cars seen in the area. If you have information please email me on Zowey.Marshall@westmercia.pnn.police.uk"
