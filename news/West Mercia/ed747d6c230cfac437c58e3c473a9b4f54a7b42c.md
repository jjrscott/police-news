A police investigation was launched after it was reported that on the 12 October catalytic converters had been taken from two vehicles parked at Worcestershire Royal Hospital.

Three men, all from Leicestershire, have been arrested and bailed.
Chief Inspector Gareth Morgan, said: "We take the theft of catalytic convertibles very seriously and know what a devastating effect these crimes can have on individuals within our community."
