Police are currently assisting the fire service at an address in Salters Lane in Redditch following reports of a house fire.

Two people were rescued from the fire and have been taken to hospital for treatment.

The public are asked to avoid Salters Lane while emergency services deal with the incident.

A joint investigation between police and the fire service is ongoing.

An investigation into the cause of the fire has been commenced by the Police and Fire Service. Anyone who may have information relating to this fire is asked to Call 101, West Mercia Police and quote Incident 00328_I_26092020.
