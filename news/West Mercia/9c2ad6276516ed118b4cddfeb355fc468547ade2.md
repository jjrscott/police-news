A man has been charged with attempted murder after a woman suffered knife wounds in Telford.

In the early hours of Monday police responded to a report a woman was being threatened by a man in Hadley Road in Telford.

Steven Carless, 60, of Ketley Park Road has been charged with attempted murder, kidnap, stalking, affray and possession of a bladed article and is due to appear at Kidderminster Magistrates' Court this morning.
