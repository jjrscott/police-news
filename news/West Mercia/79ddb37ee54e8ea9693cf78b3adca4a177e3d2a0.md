Detectives investigating the disappearance of a Bromsgrove woman are appealing for information.

Zobaidah Salangy, aged 28, was last seen at her home address in Charford on Sunday 29 March. A 42-year-old man has now been arrested on suspicion of murder in connection with the woman's disappearance and is currently in custody.

The investigation is still on going and West Mercia Police are appealing for any information.

The public are urged to contact West Mercia Police on the major incident public portal, an online website which has been set up specifically to receive information here:

https://mipp.police.uk/operation/22HQ19D76-PO1

The public can also call 101 quoting incident 0521S 040420 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800555 111.
