Police are appealing for witnesses to a fatal collision in Whitchurch, in Shropshire, this morning (Tuesday 22 December).

The incident happened at around 7.50am on the B5935 and involved a white Ford Transit and Mercedes lorry.

A pedestrian, who was loading items into the back of the lorry, died at the scene. Our thoughts are with his loved ones, who will be supported by a specially trained Family Liaison Officer.

The ambulance service attended but sadly the man, died at the scene and our thoughts are with his loved ones at this difficult time.

The road reopened at around 12.40pm.

Witnesses to the collision that haven't spoken to officers, and anyone with dash-ca footage is urged to call PC Symonds on 07813055976 or Crimestoppers on 0800555111. Alternatively information can be reported online anonymously through tell us about section of our website and referencing incident number 66i of 22 December.
