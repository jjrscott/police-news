Police were called to reports of a disturbance, where four men are believed to have entered an address in Baxter Walk, Malvern at around 5.30am this morning (Monday 6 July). No one has been harmed during the incident.

Officers attended, including armed officers, and carried out a search of a home and surrounding area which proved negative.

A cordon remains in place and officers will remain in the area to offer reassurance.

Detective Inspector Tony Garner said: "We know that a significant police presence can cause concern, and I would like to reassure local residents that a search of the area has been carried out, but that at this time nothing untoward has been identified.

"We are particularly interested to hear from anyone who may have heard or seen anyone acting suspiciously, or any unusual vehicle activity in the area."

He continued: "Officers will remain in the area into the evening to carry out patrols and to provide reassurance to the local community."

Investigations are on-going, anyone who may have heard or seen any unusual activity in the area at the time is asked to call West Mercia Police on 101 quoting incident number 0053_I_06072020. Alternatively you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
