A Birmingham man has been charged with offences after a 21-year-old man was injured on Wylds Lane in Worcester on Sunday August 2.

Marius Anton Roman, aged 32 and of Poplar Road in Smethwick, has been charged with causing grievous bodily harm and causing injury by dangerous driving. He will appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court via live link today (Wednesday 12 August).

Last week four other men, also from Smethwick, were charged with attempting to cause grievous bodily harm in connection to the incident. They were remanded in custody.

Detectives investigating the incident continue to appeal for anyone who witnessed the incident, which happened at around 4.15pm on Sunday 2 August at the junction of Vincent Road and Wylds Lane, to contact them on 101 quoting incident number 564i of August 2 or by using the "tell us about" form on the West Mercia Police website.

You can also give information anonymously by calling Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their website.
