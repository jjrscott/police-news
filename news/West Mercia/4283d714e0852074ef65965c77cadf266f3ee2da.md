Police are appealing for the public's help to locate a wanted man from Telford.

Tobias Graham, aged 29, is wanted for domestic abuse offences in both Shropshire and the West Midlands.

He is described as 5ft 11ins with short black hair.

Anyone with information on his whereabouts is urged to contact Telford Patrol 101 ext 771220 or Crimestoppers on 0800555111. Alternatively information can be reported anonymously through the Tell Us About section of our website.
