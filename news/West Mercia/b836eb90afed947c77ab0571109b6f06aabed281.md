The man who was killed when his car collided with another vehicle has been named.

On Wednesday 20 November Paul Springer sadly died when his vehicle was involved in a collision with another car on the A456 Tenbury Wells road while heading towards Worcester.

Paul, 88 years old was a retired Chartered Engineer from Stourbridge and is believed to have suffered a medical episode which led to the collision.

Paul was a private, family man and was the father of two daughters and six grandchildren.

The family wish for their privacy to be respected at this time.
