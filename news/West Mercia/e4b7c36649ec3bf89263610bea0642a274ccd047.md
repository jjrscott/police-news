Police are appealing for witnesses after a man was reportedly assaulted in Redditch.

The assault happened at around 10.35pm on Christmas Day on Mount Pleasant near Parsons Road.

Officers investigating the report believe the man may have received some facial injuries as a result of the incident. He may also have been bleeding and police are keen to trace the victim to check on his welfare as well as progressing the investigation.

Anyone with information or video/dashcam footage is asked to contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 0465S of the 25 December 2019. Alternatively information can be passed to CrimeStoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
