We are currently at the scene of a serious collision on the A5 at a roundabout between Oswestry and Gobowen at 10.25am this morning.

The incident involved a car and a motorbike. The driver of the car is not believed to have suffered any injuries however the motorcyclist has sustained significant injuries and the air ambulance attended to take him to Royal Stoke Hospital.

The road remains closed while the area is being made safe- please avoid the area.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or has dashcam footage is asked to contact us by calling 101 quoting incident 170S of 11 March.
