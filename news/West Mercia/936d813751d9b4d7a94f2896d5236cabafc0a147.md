Two men have been arrested after an incident on the roof at a college in Shrewsbury yesterday.

Police were called around 1.10pm yesterday afternoon (Monday 20 April) to reports of two men on the roof of Shrewsbury College in London Road.

A cordon was in place around the college with a section of London Road closed to allow police to deal with the incident safely.

Two men were arrested on suspicion of criminal damage and have been released on police bail.
