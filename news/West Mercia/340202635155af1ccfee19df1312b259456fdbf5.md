Concerns are growing for missing person, Brent Laight from Redditch.

The 39-year-old went missing near Worcestershire Royal Hospital at around 8am yesterday (Friday 15 January).

He has links in both Worcester and Redditch and is described as white, with blue eyes, short brown hair and is around 5ft 11ins tall. He was last seen wearing blue jeans, a blue coat, black sketchers, glasses and a navy coat with a fur edge.

Anyone with information on his whereabouts is urged to contact 101 quoting reference 00138i of 15 January. Alternatively information can be reported on the Tell Us About section of the website.
