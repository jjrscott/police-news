Earlier today (Monday 23 September) police officers carried out a warrant at an address in Church Street, Kidderminster.

The warrant was carried out after we received information from the community.

A substantial quantity of cannabis was seized.

Wyre Forest Safer Neighbourhood Team Inspector Dave King said:

"Today Kidderminster officers and specialist officers from West Mercia's task Force carried out a warrant in Church Street, Kidderminster."

We acted upon information from the community and seized a substantial amount of cannabis."

"We will continue to act upon information from the community would ask members of the public to inform us of any information about any drug dealing or anything that concerns you in your community, call us on 101 and we will act accordingly."
