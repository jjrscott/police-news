Officers are appealing for the victim of a serious incident to please come forward after he was threatened in Redditch on Friday 26 February.

The incident took place on Alcester Street in Redditch town centre at around 3pm when a man was threatened by two men. The incident was witnessed by a member of the public who reported it to the police and subsequently two men were arrested a short time later.

Detectives are keen to identify the victim to progress the investigation and to ensure he has all the support he needs.

The victim and anyone else with any information regarding this appeal can contact officers via 101 quoting incident number 00345i of 26 February 2021.
