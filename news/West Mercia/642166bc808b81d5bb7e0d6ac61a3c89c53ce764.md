Do you recognise the man in the CCTV image?

We are appealing for the public's help in identifying the man in the photo as we believe he can with our investigation into an assault in Bromsgrove.

The incident happened in KUMU nightclub in Bromsgrove in the early hours of 14 March 2020.

The victim, a man in 20s from Reditch was punched in the face so hard he lost consciousness. The victim has also received serious injuries to his face and teeth.
