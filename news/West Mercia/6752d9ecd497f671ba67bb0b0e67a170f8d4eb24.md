The trial of a West Mercia Police officer, Michael Darbyshire, 54, has resulted in a hung jury today (Wednesday 31 March 2021) after a seven day trial at Worcester Crown Court.

The jury were unable to reach a majority vote. A retrial is set for 28 February 2022.

We are unable to comment further at this time.
