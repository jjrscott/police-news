A woman has been charged with murder in Worcester.

Jessica Craine, aged 49, of Hillside Close, Worcester, has been charged with the murder of a 69 year old woman at an address in Green Lane, Worcester.

The offence took place on Tuesday 9 March 2021. No other individuals are being sought in connection to this murder.

Craine has been remanded to appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court today (Thursday 11 March).

The family of the woman who died are being supported by specialist police officers.
