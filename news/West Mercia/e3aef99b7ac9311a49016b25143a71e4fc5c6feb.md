At around 5.40pm on Wednesday 6 May officers were called to Winslow close after a man was attacked and suffered serious head injuries.

He was taken to the Queen Elizabeth Hospital where he is currently in a stable condition.

Three men were arrested on Other Road later that evening and have been released under investigation.

Police are keen to speak to anyone who may have witnessed the incident or have CCTV or dash-cam footage from between 5pm and 6pm in or around the area of Winslow Close and Other Road.

Anyone with information or footage should contact Dc Tony Flannagan on 07773 045114 or by mail anthony.flannagan@westmercia.pnn.police.uk. Or DC Phil Wood on 07967302060 or by emailing phillip.wood.3017@westmercia.pnn.police.uk
