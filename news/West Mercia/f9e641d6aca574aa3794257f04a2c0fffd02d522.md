The incident happened in the early hours of Wednesday 1 January at around 00:20am outside the Unicorn Pub in Hollies Road in Little Dawley.
Two men were injured during the disorder and attended hospital, they've since been discharged.
Officers are carrying out enquiries and are appealing for witnesses.
Anyone with information is asked to contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 29s 010120 or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
