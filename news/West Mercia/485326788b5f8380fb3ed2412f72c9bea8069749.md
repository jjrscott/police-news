Updated News Post

Wanted man Jakub Gadomski, has now been located, arrested and charged.

Jakub Gadomski, 24 years old and of no fixed address, was located in a house in Evesham in the early hours of Thursday 9 July. He was arrested and taken into custody at Worcester Police Station and later charged with domestic violence offences including harassment and criminal damage.

Detective Constable Harriet Lloyd said "Gadomski appeared via video link to Kidderminster Magistrates Court where he was given conditional bail to reappear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court on 20 August.

"We would like to thank the public and community in Evesham for sharing our appeal."
