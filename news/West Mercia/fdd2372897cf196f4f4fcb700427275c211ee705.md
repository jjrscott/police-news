Following an incident on Saturday 28 March outside a supermarket in Hereford where a security officer was assaulted a 26 year old man has been charged.

Levi Doherty from Bath Street, Hereford was arrested on Saturday from his home address and has been charged with obstructing a person from carrying out a function under the regulations-Corona virus and assault by beating.
