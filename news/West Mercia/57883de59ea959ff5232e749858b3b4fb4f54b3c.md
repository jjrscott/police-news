We are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Copcut, Droitwich on Saturday night.

At around 10.10pm on Saturday 16 January a BMW K1200 motorbike and a silver ford fiesta collided at the Pulley Lane junction with the A38 at Copcut, Droitwich.

We are keen to speak to anyone who may have seen the collision or has dash-cam footage of the incident.

Anyone with information is urged to contact police on 101 quoting reference 00635_i of 16 January. Alternatively information can be passed anonymously using the Tell Us About section of our website here.

Issued: MC, Corporate Communications
