Officers are appealing for information and any witnesses to come forward following a serious collision on the M5 yesterday (15 July).

The incident which involved a lorry that collided with the central reservation, happened on the M5 between junction 8 and junction 9 at 2.10pm. The motorway was closed in both directions with long delays for several hours for repairs and removal of the vehicle.

If you were passing the scene or have dashcam fitted to your vehicle and may have witnessed or recorded the incident please call us on 101 and quote incident 00326i of the 15 July 2020.
