Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Abbey Fields in Telford on Friday 30 October.

Between 5pm and 6pm a 31-year-old man was attacked on Stirchley Avenue. The offender was described as a white male, approximately 6ft tall, of a slim build with mousey brown hair. He looked to be between 16 and 18-years-old, and was wearing dark jogging bottoms, a dark jumper and a grey t-shirt. He was seen with two other men and one woman.

The victim suffered an injury to his jaw which needed hospital treatment.

Anyone with information is urged to contact the police on 101 quoting incident 503i of 30 October or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Alternatively information can be provided through the Tell Us About section of the website.
