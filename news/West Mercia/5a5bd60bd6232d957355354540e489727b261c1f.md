Officers are appealing for witnesses or information regarding a burglary that happened on Wednesday 25 February in Broseley.

PC Smith said: "We would ask the public in the local area if they saw anyone acting suspiciously around Benthall Road between 1pm and 3pm. During this time a house was burgled and number of items stolen.

"I would ask anyone who has any information or who witnessed anything suspicious to please get in touch on 101 and quote incident number 457s of the 26 February 2020."
