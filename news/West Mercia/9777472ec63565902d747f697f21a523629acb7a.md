Police have charged Nezam Salangy, aged 42, of Austin Road Bromsgrove, with murder in connection with the disappearance of 28-year-old Zobaidah Salangy who has not been seen since Sunday 29 March.

Anyone with information is urged to contact West Mercia Police on the major incident public portal, an online website which has been set up specifically to receive information here: https://mipp.police.uk/operation/22HQ19D76-PO1

The public can also call 101 quoting incident 0521S 040420 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800555 111.
