Updated News Post

Following a detailed search for a missing man from Redditch, we can confirm that sadly, the body of a man has this afternoon been found.

Inspector Craig Harris said: "It is with sadness that I can confirm that a body has been found in our search for Trevor Matthews. Whilst formal identification has not yet taken place we have updated and the family."

Searches for the 56 year old had been ongoing since officers were informed he had last been seen yesterday (Friday 2 October) at around 10pm.
