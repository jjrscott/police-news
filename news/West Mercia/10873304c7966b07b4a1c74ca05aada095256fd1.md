At around 1pm on Sunday (5 January) we were called to reports of an accident between a vehicle and a mobility scooter near Crossley Retail Park, Kidderminster.

A blue Nissan Juke and a mobility scooter collided on the carriageway near to the A456.

The roads around the junction were closed from around 1pm to 5pm.

Ambulance and air ambulance attended. A man in his 70s was flown to the Queen Elizabeth in Birmingham with serious, potentially life changing injuries.

PC Scott Gee said: ""We are keen to speak with anyone who may have seen the incident that occurred on the ring road near to the Crossley Retail Park, in Kidderminster, at around 1pm on Sunday (5 January) that involved a Nissan Juke and a mobility scooter so that we can get a better understanding of what has occurred.

"We are especially keen to see any dashcam footage from the area at the time of the incident. If you have any information please call 101."

If you have any information regarding the accident or dash cam footage please contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting 300s of 5 January.
