Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Dawley in Telford.

At around 3pm on Tuesday 22 September on Dawley Green Way a silver Vauxhall Astra left the roadside and a woman in her forties suffered serious injuries.

The fire service and ambulance service attended and the woman was taken to Royal Stoke Hospital where she remains in a critical condition.

Anyone with information or dash-cam footage is urged 101 quoting incident 00398i of 22 September or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800555111. Alternatively information can be passed through the online reporting function on West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
