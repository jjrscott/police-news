Emergency services are responding to an incident on Stourport Road, Kidderminster.

The incident happened at around 15:10pm this afternoon (Thursday 1 October) after a young child was injured in a collision with a car.

The child has been taken to hospital with potentially life changing injuries. The road remains closed at this time and we're grateful for the public's patience.

Anyone who witnessed the incident is asked to contact us via 101 quoting incident number 00408 of 1 October 2020.
