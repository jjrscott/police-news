Concerns are still growing for missing Amanda Caldwell, and police are issuing a new appeal to help locate her.

The 49-year-old from Telford has been missing since 30 November and despite officers searching tirelessly she has not yet been found.

She is described as white, approximately 5ft 6 ins tall, medium build with short bobbed purple/grey hair. It's believed she may be wearing jogging bottoms, a sweatshirt or hoodie and trainers.

Enquiries are on-going to find her and police are asking anyone who has seen her to get in contact.

Anyone with information can report this to West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 00589i 30 November or online under the Tell Us About section of www.westmercia.police.uk.
