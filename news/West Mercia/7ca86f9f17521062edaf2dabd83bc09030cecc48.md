Officers are appealing for any witnesses to come forward and help with their investigation into the reports a woman was assaulted in Redditch.

The incident happened on Tuesday 12 January around 7.45pm in the Asda car park on Jinnah Road in Redditch.

As the victim was putting away shopping into her car she was aware of three young men wearing masks stood close by to her. Once they walked past her one of the young men returned and physically assaulted her. The victim screamed for help and all three ran off.

If you were in the car park and saw anything suspcious or maybe have dash cam footage from your vehicle from around that time officers would like to hear from you. You can contact us here and include the incident number which is 00134i of 12 January 2020.
