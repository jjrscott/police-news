A body has been located by officers searching for a missing Worcester man.

84 year-old Keith Hemming has been missing since Thursday (11 March).

Enquiries have been carried out to find him, however, sadly yesterday afternoon, officers carrying out river searches have located a body under the water.

A specialist police diving team have recovered the body today (Tuesday 16 March).

Formal identification has yet to take place, but it is believed to be Keith and his family has been informed.
