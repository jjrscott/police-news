Police are appealing for witnesses after two men chased a group of children in Stourport.

The incident happened around 3.30pm on Monday 15 February in Layamon Walk.

Two men chased a group of four children, aged between 8 and 14, with what is believed to be a machete and a knife.

No-one was injured in the incident.

Enquiries are on-going into the incident and police are appealing for witnesses.

The suspects were both wearing black clothing and hats, one had a green jacket on.

Anyone with information is asked to contact PC Reece Miller reece.miller@westmercia.pnn.police.uk using reference 22/11781/21. If you do not wish to speak to police you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or online www.crimestoppers-uk.org
