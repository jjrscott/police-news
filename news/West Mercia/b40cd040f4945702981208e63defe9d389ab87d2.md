As part of the on-going investigation into the murder a 22 year old man from Guinness Close, Redditch on 15 July, a man has been charged and another man arrested.

Adam Carpenter, 20 years old and from Wharrington Hill, Redditch has been charged with conspiring to commit robbery and has been remanded into custody. Carpenter was arrested in Weston-super-Mare at around 3am on Saturday 18 July and brought to Kidderminster Police Station and will appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court tomorrow (22 July).

Another man in his 20s and from Redditch was arrested at around 12.30pm today (21 July) on suspicion of murder and also remains in police custody.

While the investigation continues there will be an increased presence of officers in area and anyone with any information or concerns can speak to an officer or call 101.
