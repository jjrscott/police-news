Two men have been arrested after a vehicle was stopped in Oswestry.

The vehicle was stopped in Cabin Lane on Thursday 26 November.

Drugs were seized from the vehicle.

Two men aged 23 and 30 were arrested.

Mohammed Abid, 23, from Davey Road in Birmingham and Abdul Majid, 30, from Freer Road in Birmingham have been charged with possession with intent to supply class A drugs and appeared via virtual court this afternoon. They are next to appear at Shrewsbury Crown Court on 6 January, 2021.
