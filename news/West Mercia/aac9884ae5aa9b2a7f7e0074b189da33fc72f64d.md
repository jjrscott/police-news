Updated News Post

Two people have been charged with drugs related offences following arrests that were made on Wednesday 1 July on London Road, Worcester.

Lucy Cannaway, aged 39 of London Road, Worcester, was charged with acquiring, using or possessing criminal property and two charges of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs.

Finlay Clarke, aged 19 of Croft Street, Lincoln, was charged with acquiring, using or possessing criminal property and two charges of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs.

They are due to appear Kidderminster Magistrates Court today (Friday 3 July).

UPDATE at 12.35pm - both Lucy Cannaway and Finlay Clarke have been remanded in custody and will appear at Worcester Crown Court on Wednesday 29 July.
