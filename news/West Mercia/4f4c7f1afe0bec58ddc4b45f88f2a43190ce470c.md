We are appealing for witnesses following a collision on A46 Evesham bypass on Saturday 5 December.

At approximately 2.20pm on the A46 between A44 Broadway and A46 Cheltenham Road, a silver Vauxhall Zafira travelling towards Stratford and a white Ford Focus travelling in the opposite direction have been in collision.

Both drivers were taken to the Queen Elizabeth Hospital, Birmingham with potential fractures to their legs.

If you saw the incident or have dash cam footage we would like to hear from you, please contact PC Edwards on 01527 583761 quoting reference 343i 5Dec.
