Police are appealing for witnesses to a collision in Malvern on Wednesday, 27 January.

At around 5.30pm on Abbey Road a man in his seventies was struck by a vehicle and the driver failed to stop. The pedestrian suffered serious injuries to his leg and was taken to Worcestershire Royal Hospital where he currently remains in a stable condition.

Any passers-by, or motorists who had driven down the road around the time of the incident, are urged to come forward.

Anyone with dash cam footage or information should call PC Hughes on 101 ext 7733404 quoting reference 00049i of 27 January, or alternatively report online using the Tell Us About function of our website.
