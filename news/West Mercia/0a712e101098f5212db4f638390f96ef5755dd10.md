Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision in Redditch.

At around 10.20pm on Thursday 4 March a red Vauxhall Astra was travelling on the A441 Alvechurch Highway when it left the road. Emergency services attended but sadly the driver, a 27-year-old man, died at the scene.

Anyone with information, CTTV or dashcam footage is urged to call 01527 583766 quoting incident 0676i of 4 March or report anonymously online through the Tell Us About section of our website.
