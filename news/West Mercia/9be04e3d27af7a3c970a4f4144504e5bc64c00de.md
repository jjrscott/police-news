#### Police are appealing for the public's help to locate a missing Telford woman.

Amanda Caldwell, 49, was reported missing yesterday evening.

She is described as white, approximately 5ft 6 inches tall, medium build with short bobbed purple/grey hair. It's believed she may be wearing jogging bottoms, a sweatshirt or hoodie and trainers.

Enquiries are on-going to find her and police are asking anyone who has seen her to get in contact.

Any one with information can report this to West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 00589_I_30112020 or online under the Tell Us About section of www.westmercia.police.uk.
