Officers are appealing for witnesses following a fatal incident on the A438 in Letton, Herefordshire on Friday 8 November 2019.

Police were called around 12.30pm to reports of a serious incident at the junction of the A438 and Kinley Lane.

The A438 at Letton was blocked between the A4111 junction and Letton Court while emergency services attended the scene.

The man, in his 40s, is believed to have sustained serious injuries following attempts to repair his fork lift truck at the side of the road and sadly died at the scene.

If you witnessed the incident or have any information please contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting incident 318s of Friday 8 November 2019

Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

**Issued by Alex Gale, Communications Officer at 1.25pm on Monday 11 November 2019**
