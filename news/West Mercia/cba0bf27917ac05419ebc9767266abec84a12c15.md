Police looking to trace a man wanted on suspicion of rape have made an arrest.

This morning a 39 year old man was arrested on suspicion of rape in Huddersfield. He is currently in police custody.

Detective Inspector Rich Roberts said: "I'd like to thank members of the public for sharing our appeal so widely. A 39 year old man has been arrested and is currently in police custody."
