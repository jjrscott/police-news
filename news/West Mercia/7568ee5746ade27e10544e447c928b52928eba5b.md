Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision in Frankton in Shrewsbury.

At around 2.10pm on Saturday 17 October a go kart was being ridden through Wood Lane when it collided with a brick wall. The driver, a man in his sixties, suffered head injuries.

Anyone with information should call police on 101 quoting incident 359i of 17 October or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800555111.

PC Steve WOOTTON from the Roads policing Unit is keen to speak to any witnesses. Alternatively information can be passed through the online reporting function on West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
