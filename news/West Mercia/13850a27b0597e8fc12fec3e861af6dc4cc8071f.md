Police are appealing for witnesses after a collision on the A442 in Shropshire.

The collision happened around 9.30pm on Saturday 12 October on the A442 between Horton and Bridgnorth.

Two vehicles collided on the junction with Ackleton and Worfield.

Considerable damage was caused to both vehicles.

The driver and passenger of one of the vehicles, a man and woman in their 60s, were taken to hospital for treatment. Both are described as critical but stable, however their injuries are not believed to be life-threatening.

Enquiries are on-going and police are appealing for witnesses. Any witnesses, including any motorists who were on the road on Saturday night and have Dash Cam, should get in contact with West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 777s 121019.
