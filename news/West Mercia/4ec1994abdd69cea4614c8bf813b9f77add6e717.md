Police are appealing for witnesses after a store on Wrekin Retail Park, in Arleston, Telford, was burgled on Sunday 6 January.

A number of items were stolen during the burglary, which was reported at just after 6.20pm.

PC Rob McNally said: "We believe the offenders left the retail park in a Ford Transit vehicle between 6.15pm and 6.30pm. I would like to appeal to anyone with any information about this incident or anyone who may have seen anything suspicious in the area at around the time of the incident, to get in contact."

Anyone with information is asked to call West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 521s of 5 January or Crimestoppers anonymously 0800 555 111.
