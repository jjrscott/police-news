Officers have launched an investigation following a house fire in Sutton Hill, Telford.

The fire service alerted officers to the fire at a home in Summerhill at around 10.20pm on Saturday 19 September.

A man, who was inside the house when the fire broke out, was treated for minor smoke inhalation but did not require hospital treatment.

Police Constable Sophia Walker, said: "The exact cause of the fire is yet to be determined but investigating officers are treating it as suspicious. We would like to appeal for witnesses to this incident or if anyone has information relating to it, we would ask them to please get in contact."

Anyone with information is asked to contact PC Walker on 01952 214 611 quoting reference 00758i of 19 September 2020 or they can report online at www.westmercia.police.uk via the Tell Us About section or alternatively information can be passed anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
