We are appealing for information following an incident on Ipsley Street, Redditch at 2am Sunday (26 July).

A young brunette woman wearing a brown/beige dress with black handbag got into a dark coloured vehicle parked outside The Warwick Bar and Nightclub at 2.05am, the vehicle later turned right onto Hewell Road at about 2.06am

If you saw the individual get into the vehicle or have information please contact Redditch CID via 101.
