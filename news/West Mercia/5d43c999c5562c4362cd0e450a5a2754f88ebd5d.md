Euan Short, 21, of Flyford Flavell, has entered a guilty plea to causing death by dangerous driving and causing serious injury by dangerous driving on Friday 21 August at Worcester Crown Court.

He is due to be sentenced on Friday 2 October at Worcester Crown Court.

This is in relation to a road traffic collision that occurred in Flyford Flavell on 2 August 2019.
