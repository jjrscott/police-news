We are appealing for information following a collision on Sillins Avenue, Redditch.

At around 9.13pm on Friday (2 October) a silver Citroen C2 was travelling towards Beoley Road East when it has been in collision with a pedestrian.

The pedestrian was taken to the Queen Elizabeth Hospital, Birmingham in a critical condition, he remains in hospital.

Two 18 year olds have been arrested in connection with this collision.

If you saw this incident, or have dash cam footage that might help our investigation please contact Sergeant Bate on 101 quoting reference 0625 I of 2 October or visit https://www.westmercia.police.uk/ and submit information via the tell us about section.
