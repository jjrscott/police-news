We are appealing for witnesses following a single car collision on Tuesday (5 May) in Droitwich.

At around 5.20pm a black Vauxhall Corsa was travelling along A38 Roman Way near Copcut Boulevard.

The vehicle has left the carriageway and travelled onto the grass verge before coming to rest down the embankment.

If you saw this incident and have any information please contact police on PC Cadwallader on 01527 583763. quoting reference 492s 050520.
