Two people have been arrested and cannabis plants seized after addresses was searched in Malvern.

On Tuesday (6 October) an address in Worcester Road, Malvern & Highfield, Callow End were searched.

Around 100 cannabis plants were found in one address and a further 20 at a second address, the plants have an estimated street value of approximately Â£120,000 and were seized by police.

A man, aged 55 and woman aged 38 were arrested at the addresses on suspicion of cannabis cultivation, they have both been released on bail.

Worcester Duty Inspector Tony Bush, said: "Acting on information from the community we have disrupted these cannabis cultivations which is part of Protect - our response to tackling serious and organised crime.

He continued: "We want to make our communities even safer and will continue to act upon all information we receive from the community."
