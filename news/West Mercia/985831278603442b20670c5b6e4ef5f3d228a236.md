West Mercia Police is appealing for witnesses or information following a collision near Tong yesterday afternoon (Monday 16 September).

At around 2pm, a collision took place between a Mercedes MCL320 car and a Volvo HGV on the A41, Whitchurch.

The driver of the car, a man in his 60s, sustained serious injuries and was airlifted to hospital.

Road closures were in place whilst emergency services were in attendance.

Investigating officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision or who may have any dashcam footage.

If you have any information, please call West Mercia Police on 101, quoting incident number 356s of 16 September 2019.
