The identity of the man killed at an address in Redditch on 15 July has now been confirmed.

The man has been formally identified as Colton Bryan who was 22-years-old.

Detective Inspector Mark Walters said: "I'd like to ask that the privacy of Colton's family be respected at this very difficult time."

Three people have been charged with offences, and remanded in custody, as part of the investigation into Colton's death.
