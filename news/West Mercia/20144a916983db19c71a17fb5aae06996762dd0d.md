At around 8pm last night (Tuesday 12 May), officers were called to a property in Lock Close, Redditch following reports of an altercation between two men.

Once officers arrived at the house they found a 57-year-old man with a head injury, who was taken to hospital by ambulance.

Inspector Lee Page sad: "An investigation is underway and I can confirm that a man in his 20s from Redditch has been arrested on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm with intent. The victim has remained in hospital and at this time his injuries remain life threatening.

"I would like to reassure the local community that this is an isolated incident and that they can expect to see an increased police presence in and around Lock Close as we continue our enquiries and investigation of the scene.

"I would ask that anyone with any information please gets in touch with us by calling 101 and quoting incident number 0592s of 12 May 2020."
