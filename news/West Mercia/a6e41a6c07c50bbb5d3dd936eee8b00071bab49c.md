Four people have been charged after officers were assaulted during violent disorder in Brookside in Telford.

Today (Thursday 3 September) Michael Manu, aged 18 of Brackenfield, was charged three counts of assaulting an emergency worker, Kyan Tyne-Voyce, aged 18 of Brandsfarm Way and Landing Conteh, aged 18 of Castlecroft, in Stirchley, have both been charged with two counts of assaulting an emergency worker and Rebbeca Hitchin, age 31 of Burford was charged with obstructing the police. The incident occurred at around 5.30pm on Tuesday 1 September whilst officer were patrolling in Burford.

All four appeared virtually at Telford Magistrates Court this morning and were conditionally bailed until Thursday 17 September.
