Officers are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision involving a pedestrian and a car on the A449 in Powick, Worcestershire.

The incident happened on Wednesday afternoon at 3pm when a grey Audi was in a collision with a pedestrian.

Investigating officers are appealing for information from the public including dash-cam footage.

Sergeant Matt Digger said: "We are keen to speak to anyone who may have seen the incident near to the junction of **Powyke Court Close**. A five year old girl was taken to hospital with serious injuries where she remains receiving treatment.

"We are especially keen to see any dash-cam footage from the area at the time of the incident. If you have any information please contact us."

Anyone with information should call on 101, quoting incident number 464s of 24 June 2020.

Alternatively, if you have any information but don't want to give your name you can call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
