Overnight between Friday 29 and Saturday 30 November a 22 year old man has been assaulted in Tramps (nightclub), Angel Place, Worcester.

The victim has sustained a fractured eye socket and cheekbone and may require surgery.

PC Rob Swoosh, from Worcester Police Station said " we are appealing for witnesses who may have been in the club on Friday night and may have seen this altercation take place.

"Please contact us on 101 using reference 389s 301119 or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
