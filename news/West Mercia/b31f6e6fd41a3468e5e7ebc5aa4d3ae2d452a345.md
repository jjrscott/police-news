A man has been arrested for a drug offence following the execution of a warrant in Shrewsbury.

Officers carried out the warrant at Worcester Road at around 5am this morning (Wednesday 20 May).

A 35-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply controlled drugs and remains in custody at this time.

Detective Sergeant Steve Miller said: "Despite the restrictions of the current situation we are continuing to carry out warrants such as this but are taking the necessary precautions. It is important that we continue to pursue those concerned in the supply of drugs and work to keep the community safe."
