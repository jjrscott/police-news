Police are appealing for witnesses after an assault in Arleston in Telford.

At around 1.30pm on Sunday 20 September a man was attacked on Windsor Road by two other men and suffered minor injuries.

A 50-year-old man from Telford has been arrested on suspicion of assault and he remains in custody at this time.

The investigation is still ongoing and officers are continuing to patrol the area.

Any witnesses or anyone with information is urged to contact police on 101 quoting incident 0355i of 20 September of 15 September or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Alternatively information can be passed through the online reporting function on West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
