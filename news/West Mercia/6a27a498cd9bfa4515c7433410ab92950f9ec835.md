A woman has been arrested in connection with a murder investigation.

The investigation was launched after a man was killed in a house in Humphrey Avenue, Bromsgrove on Monday 14 October.

A 37 year-old woman from Birmingham was arrested last night (Wednesday 16 October) in Perry Barr, Birmingham on suspicion of murder and is in police custody.

 **Issued by Alex Gale, Communications Officer at 08.55am on 17 October 2019**
