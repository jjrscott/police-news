A Redditch man will appear in court this week after allegedly spitting at a police officer in an incident on Tuesday (June 30).

57-year-old Guy Turner of Hewell Road, Redditch has been charged with assaulting an emergency worker and breaching a Criminal Behaviour Order (CBO).

He has been remanded in police custody and will appear at Kidderminster Magistrates court on Thursday 2 July 2020.
