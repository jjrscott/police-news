Officers are appealing for information and any witnesses to come forward following a serious collision on the A46 at Hinton Green, near Evesham.

The incident, which involved a Nissan Micra and Renault Clio, happened on Friday 31 July at just before 10.45am.

A teenage girl and a boy, who were passengers in the Clio, were both taken to hospital.

Sergeant Steve Hastie said: "We are keen to speak with anyone who may have seen the incident so that we can get a better understanding of what occurred.

"We are especially keen to see any dashcam footage from the area at the time of the incident."

Anyone with information is asked to report through the Tell Us About section on this website using reference 195 of 31072020 or call 101.
