A man has been charged with assault after an attack in Arleston in Telford.

The incident occurred at around 1.30pm on Sunday 20 September when a man was attacked on Windsor Road by two other men and suffered minor injuries.

Saqib Khan, aged 21 from Harvey Crescent was charged with s18 GBH and was today (Thursday 24 September) bailed by the courts.
