Police in Shropshire are appealing for witnesses to a serious collision on the road between Whittington and Ellesmere yesterday evening (Thursday, 10 October).

Officers were called at 7.30pm to the A495 near to the entrance of Hardwick Hall and found that a white VW Caddy had left the road before coming to stop in a field on its roof.

The driver, a 35-year-old man, was the only occupant in the vehicle. He suffered serious injuries and was taken to the

Royal Stoke University Hospital.

If you were driving in the area and witnessed the collision please contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 673s of 10 October 2019.
