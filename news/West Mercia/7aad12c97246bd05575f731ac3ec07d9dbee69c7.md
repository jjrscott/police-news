The charge is in connection with the death of 36 year old Wayne Coventry who was found dead at an address in Bromsgrove on Monday 14 October.

Cordelia Farrell, 37, from Birmingham, was charged with murder this evening (Friday 18 October).

She has been remanded in custody and will appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court tomorrow morning (Saturday 19 October)

**Issued by Alex Gale, Communications Officer at 5.58pm, Friday 18 October 2019**
