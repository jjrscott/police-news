Three men will appear at Kidderminster Magistrates Court this morning (Friday 13 March) after a shot was fired at a police car in Worcester on Tuesday (10 March).

Ciaran Irvine, 19, of no fixed abode in Warwickshire, Kyle Ryley, 21, of Highlands Road, Hinkley, Leicestershire and 20-year-old Gerard Doherty of Oxford Road in Ryton-on-Dunsmore in Warwickshire have all been charged with: attempted murder, possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life, possession of a firearm, possession of an offensive weapon.

Ryley has also been charged with dangerous driving and driving whilst disqualified.
