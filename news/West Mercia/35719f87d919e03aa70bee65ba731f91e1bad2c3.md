Police attended a disorder on Load Street Bewdley in the early hours of this morning (25 June).

Two 15 year old boys from Kidderminster were arrested, one on suspicion of drunk and disorderly, one on suspicion of assault and a 31 year old man from Droitwich on suspicion of obstructing police.

The investigation is ongoing, if you saw this incident or have any information please call on 101 quoting incident number 12S250620.

Please contact Crimestoppers if you wish to report anonymously 0800 555 111.
