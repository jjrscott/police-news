Police are appealing for the public's help to find Gareth Rogers who is wanted for driving offences.

The 26-year-old failed to appear at Telford Magistrates Court on Thursday 3 September for a series of driving offences including dangerous driving and driving whilst disqualified on 30 May.

Anyone with information on his whereabouts is urged to call 101 to contact PC 22357 Deacon or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800555111. Alternatively information can be passed through the online reporting function on West Mercia Police website under the section Tell Us About https://www.westmercia.police.uk/
