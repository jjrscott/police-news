Police in Redditch are appealing for witnesses after a man was attacked by a border collie outside Redditch Library, Market Place, Redditch.

It is reported that the collie attacked and injured the local man who had bent down to stroke the owners other dog a Chihuahua when the black and white border collie although muzzled has bitten the man on his shoulder on Monday 4 November between 4.45pm and 5.15pm.

PC Stokes said: "We've been making local enquiries into this incident and we're really keen to hear from anyone who can help us to identify the owner of the dog."

Anyone with information that could help locate the dog and its owner are asked to call West Mercia Police on 101, quoting incident reference number 0196S of 5 November 2019. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or at www.crimestoppers-uk.org
