Officers are appealing for the public to get in touch if they have seen Todd MacDonald or know where he is.

MacDonald, 23 years old and from the Batchley area of Redditch is wanted after failing to appear at Kidderminster Magistrate's Court where he was due to be heard on charges of handling stolen goods.

MacDonald is white, 5'10" tall, medium build with short and untidy brown hair and may have recently travelled to Birmingham.

If you have any information about his whereabouts please call 101 or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
