Officers are appealing for the public's help in locating a man missing from Telford.

There is growing concern for 59-year-old Steven Johnson who is 5ft 9in tall and was last seen near to the Shakespeare Inn, in Coalport, at around 7.40pm on Friday 19 March.

If you have seen Steven or have any information as to where he may be, please call us on 101 quoting incident 479i of 21 March.
