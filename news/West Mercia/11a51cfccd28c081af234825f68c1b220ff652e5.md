We are appealing for information following reports of an assault during the early hours of Saturday 1 February on Birchfield Road.

Two men were walking from the White Heart Pub in the direction of the Seven stars pub, when an altercation started between a member of the public and one of the two men, the second man was then assaulted. The two men received scratches and one man has a broken arm.

It is believed there were people in the area at the time of the incident, if anyone witnessed the incident of has any information regarding the circumstances please let us know via 101 quoting reference 0146S 010220.

**Issued AS Corporate Communications **
