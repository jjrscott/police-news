West Mercia Police is re-appealing for witnesses following a collision near the M5 at Strensham on Monday 30 September 2019

At around 8.30am, a grey 15 reg Skoda car was in collision with a grey Kawasaki zg1400 with a black top box, the rider wore all black motorcycle clothing. The two vehicles both exited Junction 8 of the M5 motorway southbound slip road and the collision occurred travelling around the island.

The motorcyclist, a man in his late 50s, sustained serious injuries and was airlifted to hospital where he is still receiving treatment and is in a serious but stable condition.

The Skoda driver was not injured.

Road closures were in place whilst emergency services were in attendance.

Investigating officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision. If you have any information, please call West Mercia Police 101 quoting incident number 95s of 30 September 2019.

**Issued: AS, Corporate Communications at 11.10am on Wednesday 16 October 2019**
