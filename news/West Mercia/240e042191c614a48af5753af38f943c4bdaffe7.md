A police investigation was launched after it was reported to us that during the evening of 20 September a gamekeeper in Overbury, Worcestershire, had seen a man with what was believed to be an air rifle.

The man, along with two other individuals departed the area.

A 44-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life and burglary non-dwelling. He is currently in police custody.

Detective Inspector Dave Knight, said: "Thankfully, no persons or animals were seriously injured in this incident. We take poaching crimes and burglary very seriously and know what a devastating effect these crimes can have on our community."
