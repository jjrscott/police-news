A man has been charged with attempt murder, wounding with intent and possession of an offensive weapon in a public place.

Martin Young, aged 57 from Kington, has been charged and remanded to Worcester Crown Court to appear at a later date.

He was charged in relation to an incident that occurred in Kington on Tuesday 16 June in which a man sustained serious injuries.
