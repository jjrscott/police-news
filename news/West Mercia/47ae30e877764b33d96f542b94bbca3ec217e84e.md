The body of a woman has been found near Upton on Severn in Worcester.

Police were called around 8am this morning after a 25 year old woman was found unresponsive in Causeway Lane, near to the fire station.

Enquiries are on-going and a police cordon is in place.

Next of kin has been informed.

A 44 year old man has been arrested on suspicion of murder and is currently in police custody.

Extra officers are in the area.

Detective Inspector Jim Bayliss said: "Our enquiries are very much in their early stages and at this stage the woman's death is being treated as suspicious. A man has been arrested and is currently in police custody.

"Extra officers will be in the area, both to carry out enquiries and to offer reassurance."

Anyone with information can ring West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 150s 161119 or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Note to Editors:

The death is not connected to localised flooding in the area.
