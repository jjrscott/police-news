We are appealing for witnesses following a collision between a marked police car and a car in Kidderminster.

Two weeks ago at around 2pm on Wednesday 7 October at the roundabout on The Ringway and Comberton Hill an orange Vauxhall Mokka and a marked police car have been in collision.

If you saw this incident and have information or dash cam footage of this incident, please contact us using reference 322 I 7 October online via the tell us about section and dash cam footage can be submit through Operation Snap.
