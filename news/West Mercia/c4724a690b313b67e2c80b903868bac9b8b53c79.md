Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision in B4176 near to Rudge Heath, Bridgnorth yesterday (Thursday 5 December).

Officers were called at 7.30pm to reports that a grey X Type Jaguar had collided with a pedestrian on the B4176.

Sadly, the pedestrian, a man in his 70s, died at the scene. His next of kin has been informed.

PC Tom Symonds from West Mercia Police said: "While investigations are ongoing, we are appealing for any witnesses who may have been in the area at the time of the incident and may have dash cam footage to please come forward.

"Our thoughts remain with the victim's family at this difficult time."

Any witnesses are asked to contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 0676S of 51119.

Alternatively, information can be given anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
