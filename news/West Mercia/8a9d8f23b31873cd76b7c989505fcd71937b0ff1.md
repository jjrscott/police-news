West Mercia Police is appealing for witnesses following a collision near the M5 this morning.

At around 8.30am, a grey Skoda car was in collision with a motorbike collision on the roundabout between J8 of the M5 onto the M50.

The motorcyclist, a man in his late 50s, sustained serious injuries and was airlifted to hospital where he is currently receiving treatment.

The Skoda driver was not injured.

Road closures were in place whilst emergency services were in attendance.

Investigating officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision. If you have any information, please call West Mercia Police 101 quoting incident number 95s of 30 September 2019.
