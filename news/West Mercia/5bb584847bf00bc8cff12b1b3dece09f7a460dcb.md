A woman has been charged in connection with drugs offences in Herefordshire.

Louise Taylor aged 48, of The Crescent, Kington, was charged with one count of possession of a class B drug and one count of possession of a class A drug.

Taylor was arrested after a warrant was carried out at a property in Kington on Tuesday 5 May.

She is due to appear at Hereford Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 3 June **.**
