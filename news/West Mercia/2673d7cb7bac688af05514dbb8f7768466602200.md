We are appealing for witnesses and dash-cam footage following a fatal collision in Bewdley yesterday lunchtime.

At around 1.08pm on Monday 18 January three vehicles involved in a collision outside West Midlands Safari Park on the Kidderminster Road, Bewdley. The collision involved a white Peugeot Boxer van that was towing a white Vauxhall combo van and a brown Vauxhall Insignia.

A man in his 60s received medical treatment at the scene, he was conveyed to hospital by ambulance where he was later unfortunately pronounced deceased. His family have been informed and are being supported by specially trained officers.

If you witnessed the incident, have dash-cam footage, or have any information that can help officers with their enquiries, please contact PC Di Stevens from the Roads Policing Unit at Bromsgrove on 07773 046415 or email diane.stevens@westmercia.pnn.police.uk
or by reporting online on our website under the Tell Us About section here, quoting incident number 233_i of Monday 18 January.
