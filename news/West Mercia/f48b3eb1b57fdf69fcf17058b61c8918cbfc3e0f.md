We are appealing for witnesses following a collision in Arleston on Saturday night.

At around 9.50pm on Saturday (13 March) on Dawley Road, Arleston a blue Renault Clio has been in a collision with a 27 year-old pedestrian.

The Clio was travelling southbound towards Lawley from Arleston, the pedestrian has been taken to the Queen Elizabeth Hospital with significant head injuries, which are believed to be life threatening. She remains in a serious condition.

The driver was uninjured.

We are keen for anyone with dash cam footage to submit this to us via Operation Snap on our website. If anyone has information regarding this incident or saw a pedestrian in the area at the time of the incident please contact the officer in charge PC Simon Measor on 07870158597 quoting reference 683i 13 March.
