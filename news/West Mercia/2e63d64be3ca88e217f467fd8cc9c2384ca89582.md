West Mercia Police is appealing for witnesses following a serious collision just outside Elsinore on Glyn Road in Selattyn, Oswestry.

At around 2.30pm on Wednesday 4 August 2019 a motorbike was in a collision with silver VW Amarok car on Glyn Road.

The motorbike rider, a 17 year old man from Oswestry suffered serious injuries and was taken to hospital via air ambulance where he is currently receiving treatment.

The road was closed for a short time while the emergency services attended the scene.

Investigating officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the collision. If you have any information, please call PC Mark Hobden at West Mercia Police on 101, quoting incident number 435s of 4 September 2019.

Issued: 8.40am, Thursday 5 September 2019, Alex Gale, Corporate Communications
