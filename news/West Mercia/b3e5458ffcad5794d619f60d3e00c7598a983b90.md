Police investigating the theft of a bank card have released an image of a woman they would like to trace.
Officers are keen to speak to the woman pictured as it's believed she may be able to help police with enquiries.
The woman, or anyone, who recognises her, is asked to contact PC 21209 Williams on 101 ext 7712157.
