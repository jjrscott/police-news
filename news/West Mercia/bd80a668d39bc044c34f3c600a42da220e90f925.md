Police are appealing for information after a man was assaulted in Oswestry.

The incident happened around 9.20pm on Saturday 11 July at the cross roads of Leg Street, Cross Street and Oswald Road.

A 52-year-old man suffered a fractured jaw after being assaulted by a group of males and females following a verbal altercation.

Officers are carrying out enquiries and appealing for anyone with any information to get in contact.

Any witnesses can ring West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 766 I 11072020 or alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
