**Following the assault on Friday evening (20 September) on New Street in Worcester city centre in which a man was stabbed, police have now made four arrests in connection with the incident.**

Detective Sergeant Maxine Rawlins said: "I'm pleased to say that following the assault on Friday of a man in the city centre, three people have now been arrested for assisting an offender; a 45 year old man from Worcester (released on bail until 18/10/2019), a 44 year old woman from Worcester (released under investigation) and a 54 year old man from Worcester (released under investigation). A 39 year old man from Worcester has also been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder (released on bail until 20/10/19).

"Our investigation is on-going and proceeding well however I would ask anyone with any information to please get in touch with us by calling 101 and quoting incident number 630s of 20 September 2019"

Issued by Alex Gale, Corporate Communications at 10am on Tuesday 24 September 2019
