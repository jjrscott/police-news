Police are appealing for witnesses after a fatal collision in Newport, Shropshire.

The collision happened around 5.35pm last night in Salters Lane when for reasons yet to be established a pedestrian was hit by a car.

The pedestrian, a man in his 50s, suffered fatal head injuries and was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Officers are carrying out enquiries and appealing for witnesses.

Sergeant Marc Whild said: "We're carrying out enquiries to establish the circumstances around the collision which has sadly seen a man lose his life and our thoughts are with his family at this awful time."

Any witnesses are asked to contact Sergeant 3231 Marc Whild on 101 ext 5785 or online at www.westmercia.police.uk/contact/af/contact-us/us/contact-us/id-like-to-contact-an-officer-or-member-of-police-staff.
