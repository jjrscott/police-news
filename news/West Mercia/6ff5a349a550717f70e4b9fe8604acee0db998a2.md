A Birmingham man has been charged with attempted grievous bodily harm, dangerous driving and having no insurance following an incident in Wythall in which a police officer was injured.

The incident happened on Three Oaks Road on Tuesday evening (28 January).

Khazeer Ali, 27, of Russell Road, Hall Green, Birmingham appeared at Kidderminster Magistrates' Court this morning (Friday 31 January) and was remanded in custody. He will be appearing at Worcester Crown Court on March 2.

Three other men from Solihull were arrested as part of the investigation:

 * An 18-year-old was arrested on suspicion of assault
 * A 20-year-old was arrested on suspicion of assault and failing to stop when directed
 * A 22-year-old was arrested on suspicion of assault, failing to stop, dangerous driving and attempted theft of a vehicle

These three men have been released under police investigation.
