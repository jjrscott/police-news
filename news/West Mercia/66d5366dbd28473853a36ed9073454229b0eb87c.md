Officers investigating a report of a suspected sexual assault in Worcester, that took place on Sunday 5 January, would like help from the public to further their enquiries.

The incident happened between 4.15pm and 5.15pm on Sunday 5 January, in Cripplegate Park, Worcester.

Officers would like to speak to a man that they believe can help them with their enquiries.

He is described as an Asian man, in his late 20s, around 5ft tall with an average build. He has short dark hair, a short thin black beard and moustache.

He was wearing dark brown jogging bottoms, a red t-shirt and black hooded jacket.

If anyone recognises the description of him or saw an incident take place in Cripplegate Park on Sunday 5 January please contact PC Phillips at West Mercia police by email: joshua.phillips@westmercia.pnn.police.uk quoting incident number 0313S 060120
