Officers searching for missing 16 year old Leila Belacel are happy to report she has been found safe and well.

Leila was located late this evening (Sunday 4 October) and is now safely with officers. We would like to thank both the public for sharing our appeal so widely and the other police forces involved in the search for her, for their assistance.
