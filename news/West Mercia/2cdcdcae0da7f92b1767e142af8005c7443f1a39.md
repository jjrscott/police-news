Following several incidents of violence and disorder both inside and outside of Hereford FC's grounds at the Hereford FC and Stockport County FC match on 6 April 2019, the following have been charged with Violent Disorder and Common Assault -

 * Michael Whitehead, 35 years old from Stockport, charged with Violent Disorder
 * James Good, 33 years old from Stockport charged with Violent Disorder
 * Michael James, 39 years old from Stockport charged with Violent Disorder
 * James Thornhill, 24 years old from Stockport charged with Violent Disorder
 * Jason Rowlinson, 39 years old from Stockport charged with Violent Disorder
 * Macauley Malaney, 20 years old from Stockport charged with Violent Disorder
 * Sam Emmerson, 21 years old from Stockport charged with Violent Disorder
 * Clive Symonds, 30 years old from of Hereford charged with two counts of Violent Disorder
 * Billy Jenson, 25 years old from of Hereford charged with Violent Disorder
 * Lee Prince, 27 years old from of Hereford charged with Violent Disorder
 * Andrew Beavan, 52 years old from of Hereford charged with Violent Disorder
 * A 17year old man from Hereford charged with Violent Disorder and Common Assault

All of the defendants are due to appear at Hereford Magistrates Court on 30/10/2019.
