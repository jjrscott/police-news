Police are appealing for witnesses after motorbike rider was seriously injured in a collision.
The collision happened around 4.55pm on Thursday 21 November in Harlescott lane in Shrewsbury near to Lidl between a motorbike and a car.
The motorbike rider, a man in his 50s, was taken to hospital with serious injuries, however, they are not believed to be life-threatening.
Officers are carrying out enquiries and are appealing for information.
Any witnesses should ring West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 507s 211119.
