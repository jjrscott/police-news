Police are appealing for witnesses to a racially aggravated incident in Lickey.

It happened around 3pm on Sunday 5 July in the area of The Monument on Monument Lane, when the victim saw two men on motorbikes. She believed the motorcyclists were riding their bikes anti socially on the grass and asked them to move away.

PC Jo Jinks said: "When the victim challenged the motorcyclists they racially abused her verbally before riding off towards the Beacon area of the Lickey Hills. We are appealing for witnesses to this incident."

Anyone with information should contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting 22/56224/20 or report online via www.westmercia.police.uk Alternatively information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
