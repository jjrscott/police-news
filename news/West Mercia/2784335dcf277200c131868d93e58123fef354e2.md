In the early hours of this morning (Tuesday 5 January) response and specialist traffic officers arrested three men travelling in what was believed to be a stolen vehicle on the Powick roundabout in Worcestershire.

This follows burglaries at three homes in Malvern overnight.

Three young men, all aged 17, were arrested on suspicion of theft of motor vehicle and burglary. They are currently in police custody.

The investigation is on-going, if you witnessed any suspicious incidents or have CCTV or doorbell footage please contact Detective Inspector 3704 Dave Knight on 01905 332819. You can also provide information via our website.
