West Mercia Police is appealing for witnesses after a white VW Golf **exited a car park in Burcott Lane, Bromsgrove, ** **when police attempted to detain the occupants** at about 2.35pm on Tuesday (28 January).

The vehicle **exited the car park and** was then in a collision with a black Peugeot 206 car before the driver and passenger exited the vehicle.

Detective Sergeant Alex Farmer, said: "Unfortunately a member of the public has been injured during a collision with another vehicle. She was taken to hospital along with her passenger. Both received medical treatment for their minor injuries and have since been discharged.

He continued: "If you saw the white VW Golf, registration starting AD13 prior to the collision or driving in the Bromsgrove area we would like to hear from you especially those with dash-cam footage."

One man in his 40s from the West Midlands was arrested concerned in the supply of Class A drugs and was later released under investigation.

Anyone with information is asked to call Detective Sergeant Farmer on 01562 826287 or email Alex.farmer@westmercia.pnn.police.uk, quoting incident number 362s of Tuesday 28 January.

Information can also be given anonymously to the Independent Charity, Crimestoppers, by calling 0800 555 111 or by visiting their website: www.crimestoppers-uk.org
