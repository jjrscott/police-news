A man has been charged and three more people have been arrested in connection with the murder of Naasir Francis earlier this week.

Lawrence Morgan, 24, of Crompton Close, Nechells has been charged with murder, three counts of attempted murder and possession of a prohibited weapon.

He appeared before Birmingham Magistrates court this morning and has been remanded to appear a Birmingham Crown Court on Tuesday 1 September.

A 17-year-old boy has been arrested on suspicion of murder whilst a 48-year-old man and a 44-year-old woman have been arrested on suspicion of assisting an offender.

All three were arrested in Surrey and will be transported to the West Midlands for questioning.

A 22-year-old man arrested on suspicion of murder continues to be questioned by detectives.

A 39-year-old man and a 21-year-old woman who were arrested yesterday (28 August) on suspicion of assisting an offender have been released pending further investigation.

Naasir, aged, 22, was fatally shot in Lozells at around 12:30pm on Wednesday (26 August).

Naasir Francis

Detective Chief Inspector Jim Munro, from the force’s homicide unit, said: “I would continue to urge anyone who has any information who has not yet spoken to us to get in touch.

“Naasir’s family and friends are devastated by his death which has also left the community sad and shocked.

“Local neighbourhood police teams will continue their increased presence in the area over the weekend."

Anyone with information can contact us via Live Chat on our website or by calling 101 anytime. Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111 - tell us what you know not who you are.
