We're appealing for witnesses or dashcam footage after a driver died following a collision in Perry Barr yesterday (23 August).

His silver Nissan Almera is believed to have hit the back of a van and lamppost before flipping onto its roof in Walsall Road.

The 70-year-old driver was sadly confirmed dead soon after the collision, near the junction of Perry Avenue, at around 2.15pm.

We're trying to piece together the circumstances around what happened and are keen to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time.

Tests will be carried out to establish if a medical episode contributed to the collision. No-one else was injured.

Sergeant Dean Caswell, from our Serious Collision Investigation Unit, said: "Our thoughts are with the man's family at this extremely difficult time.

"We're trying to establish the circumstances around the collision and need to hear from anyone with information which can assist our enquiries.

"We are particularly interested in hearing from anyone with dashcam footage. We appreciate the patience everyone showed while road closures were in place."

Anyone with information is asked to contact the serious collision unit by email at FL_ COLLISION _INVEST@west-midlands.pnn.police.uk, call 101 or Live Chat via WMP Online between 8am and midnight. Please quote log 2332 of 23 August.
