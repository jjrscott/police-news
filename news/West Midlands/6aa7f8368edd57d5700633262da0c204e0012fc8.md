We’ve arrested two men after launching an investigation into gun shots being fired in Ladywood around 3.30pm yesterday (25 July).

We’ve recovered two bullets from Kelsall Croft and Sherborne Grove although no-one is known to have been injured.

Following enquiries we later arrested two men, aged 24 and 26, on suspicion of possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life. They remain in custody for questioning.

The investigation is ongoing and we're keen to hear from anyone with mobile phone or dash cam footage.

Anyone with information is asked to contact us via Live Chat or by calling 101. Quote 20BW/174718L/20.
