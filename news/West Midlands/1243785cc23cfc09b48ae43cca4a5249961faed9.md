Detectives investigating the fatal stabbing of Thomas Rogers in Birmingham have charged a fourth man with murder.

Macauley Welby was arrested in Mansfield yesterday in connection with the death of 26-year-old Mr Rogers.

Pictured: Thomas Rogers

The 22-year-old, of no fixed address, was brought to the West Midlands for questioning and charged to appear before Birmingham Magistrates Court today (24 September).

Mr Rogers was found seriously injured near the junction of Bristol Road and Mill Pool Way, Bournbrook, on 22 August. He sadly died shortly after his arrival at hospital.

Three other men - including two of Mr Roger's brothers, have already been charged with murder.

We're continuing to ask for anyone with information to contact us via 101 or through Live Chat on our website. Please quote 3112 from 22 August.
