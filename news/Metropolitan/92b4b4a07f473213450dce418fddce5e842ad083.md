Feb 25, 2021 12:11 GMT

**Detectives have charged five men with firearms offences following a proactive operation by the Met’s Flying Squad on Wednesday, 24 February.**

The following were charged on Thursday, 25 February with conspiracy to supply firearms.

[A] Daniel Ngoo, 29 (23.05.1991), of Edington Road, Abbey Wood, London

[B] Stephen Donkor, 44 (07.06.1976) of Seally Road, Grays

[C] Malacai Aaron Yexley, 25 (24.08.1995) of Laindon, Basildon

[D] George Clark-Chesaites, 32 (21.06.1988), of Laindon, Basildon

[E] David Heal, 56 (25.05.1964) of Alexandra Road, Great Wakering, Southend-On-Sea

All five are due to appear at Westminster Magistrates’ Court on Thursday, 25 February.
