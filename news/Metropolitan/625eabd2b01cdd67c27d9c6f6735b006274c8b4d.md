Jan 07, 2021 10:56 GMT

**A man has been charged in connection with a violent robbery at a shop in Feltham.**

Daniel Oldcorn, 42 [25.7.78] of Milford Road, Southall, was charged on Thursday, 7 January with robbery, grievous bodily harm and two counts of being in possession of a knife/bladed article.

He is remanded in custody to appear at Uxbridge Magistrates Court on Thursday, 7 January.

\+ Police were called at 10:15hrs on Wednesday, 6 January to reports of a robbery at a commercial premises at Hampton Road West, Feltham.

Officers and London Ambulance Service attended. At the scene a man, aged 50s, was found suffering a knife wound. His injuries were not life-threatening.
