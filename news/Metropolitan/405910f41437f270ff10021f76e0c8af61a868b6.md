Dec 31, 2020 09:55 GMT

**Police are appealing for information after a collision in Newham left a man with serious injuries.**

On Tuesday, 3 November at 16:00hrs on Newham Way a white Mercedes Sprinter Van collided with an orange Duke motorbike whilst travelling eastbound.

The van moved from lane three to lane two, approximately 50 meters west of the junction with New Barn Street, when it collided with the motorbike.

The rider, a man in his 30s, suffered a dislocated shoulder and fractured ankle.

The driver of the van stopped at the scene. There were no arrests.

Anyone with information or footage of this incident is asked to call 101 quoting reference CAD 5004/03NOV20.
