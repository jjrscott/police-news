Apr 01, 2021 14:00 BST

**A man has been charged following an investigation into an organised crime group alleged to be involved in the conspiracy to import heroin and cocaine into the UK.**

Brook Mckoy, 27 (20.01.1994) of Ferntower Road, Highbury was charged with conspiracy to supply heroin and cocaine on Thursday, 25 March. He attended Highbury Magistrates Court on Friday, 26 March.

He has been remanded to appear in custody and will appear at Snaresbrook Crown Court on Friday, 23 April.
