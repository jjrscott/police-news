Apr 02, 2021 20:50 BST

**A man has been charged in connection with a series of high value burglaries in Westminster.**

Jugoslav Jovanovic, 23 (16/01/1997), of Milan, Italy was charged on Friday, 2 April with conspiracy to commit burglary, conspiracy to commit money laundering and attempting to convert criminal property.

He was remanded in custody to appear at Westminster Magistrates' Court on Saturday, 3 April.

Jovanovic was arrested at Heathrow Airport on the afternoon of Friday, 2 April following extradition from Italy.
