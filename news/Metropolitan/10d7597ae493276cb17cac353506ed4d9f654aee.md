Apr 02, 2021 11:36 BST

**A man and a woman have been charged with conspiracy to supply cocaine, a class A drug, after warrants were carried out in south London and Bedfordshire.**

Robert Hunt, 34 (13.12.1986) of Cranborne Close, Bedford, has also been charged with possession with intent to supply cocaine, possession with intent to supply a class B drug (cannabis) and possession of criminal property.

Joanne White 54 (13.12.1966) of Moggerhanger, Bedfordshire, was also charged with possession of a class B drug (cannabis).

They were both arrested on Thursday, 1 April after warrants were executed by Met officers as part of Operation Eternal. Hunt and White are due to appear at Westminster Magistrates' Court on 3 April.
