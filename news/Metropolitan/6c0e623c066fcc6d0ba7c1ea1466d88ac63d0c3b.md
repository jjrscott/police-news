Apr 01, 2021 17:10 BST

**Six men have been charged after officers** **executed eight search warrants** **in London and Hertfordshire.**

All six were arrested during raids in Barnet, Wandsworth and Borehamwood on Wednesday, 31 March. They appeared in custody at Willesden Magistrates' Court on Thursday, 1 April charged as follows:

Daniel Bracellari, 33 (25.02.88) of no fixed address, was charged with conspiracy to produce a Class B drug, namely cannabis.

Ilir Ntiso, 34 (16.01.87) of Lismore Boulevard, Hendon, was charged with conspiracy to produce a Class B drug, namely cannabis.

Korant Kitan, 31 (08.01.90) of Finchley Lane, Hendon, was charged with conspiracy to produce a Class B drug, namely cannabis.

Gerti Lici, 30 (11.06.90) of Victoria Road, Hendon, was charged with conspiracy to produce a Class B drug, namely cannabis.

Vasil Stefa, 33 (20.10.87) of Victoria Road, Hendon, was charged with conspiracy to produce a Class B drug, namely cannabis and production of a Class B drug, cannabis.

Ernest Gozhdari, 26 (14.07.94) of Boscombe Road, Tooting, was charged with production of a Class B drug, cannabis.

They have all been remanded in custody to appear at Harrow Crown Court on Thursday, 29 April.

A 27-year-old man was cautioned for possession of cannabis. Three men aged 24, 30 and 34, were released under investigation.

Enquiries are ongoing.
