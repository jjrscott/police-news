**Oluwakemi Badare, 36 (28.07.84), was charged with the murder of her four-year-old son Kingswealth Gbenoula Bayode on Sunday, 27 December.**

She appeared at Bromley Magistrates' Court on Monday, 28 December.

A further hearing was held at the Old Bailey today, Wednesday, 30 December. Badare was not in attendance.

Police were called by London Ambulance Service at 07:49hrs on Sunday, 27 December, to a report of the death of a child at a flat in Invermore Place, SE18. The boy was pronounced dead at the scene.
