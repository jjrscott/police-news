Mar 01, 2021 12:21 GMT

**Five people have been charged with a number of offences following breaches of the Coronavirus regulations. The dates of the breaches range from 29 August 2020 to 31 December 2020 at numerous locations within London.**

They are all due to appear at Westminster Magistratesâ€™ Court on Thursday 20 May 2021.

 **Piers Corbyn, aged 73 (10/03/1947) of East Street, SE17 was charged with the following offences:**

On 29 August 2020 in Trafalgar Square he held/was involved in holding a gathering of more than 30 people on land in public outdoor place during emergency period contrary to regulations 5B(1), (2)(b)(iii) and 8(1) and (4) of the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Restrictions) (No.2) (England) Regulations 2020.

On 29 August 2020 in Trafalgar Square he participated in a gathering of more than 30 people on land in public outdoor place during emergency period in England contrary to regulations 5(1)(b)(iii) and 8(1) and (4) of the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Restrictions) (No.2) (England) Regulations 2020.

On 26 September 2020 in Trafalgar Square he held/was involved in holding a gathering of more than 30 people on land in public outdoor place during emergency period contrary to regulations 5B(1), (2)(b)(iii) and 8(1) and (4) of the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Restrictions) (No.2) (England) Regulations 2020.

On 26 September 2020 in Trafalgar Square he participated in a gathering of more than six people in any place during emergency period in England contrary to regulations 5(1) and 8(1) and (4) of the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Restrictions) (No.2) (England) Regulations 2020.

On 24 October 2020 in Westminster he held/was involved in holding a gathering of more than 30 people on land in public outdoor place in the Tier 2 area contrary to regulation 5(1)(a) and (2) and paragraph 3(1) and (5)(b)(iii) of Schedule 1 to the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Local COVID-19 Alert Level) (High) (England) Regulations 2020.

On 24 October 2020 in Westminster he participated in an outdoor gathering of more than six people in the Tier 2 area contrary to regulation 5(1)(a) and (2) and paragraph 2(1) of Schedule 1 to the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Local COVID-19 Alert Level) (High) (England) Regulations 2020.

On 28 November 2020 in Westminster he held/was involved in holding a gathering of more than 30 people on land in England contrary to regulations 10(1) and (5)(b)(iii) and 20(1)(a) and (2) of the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Restrictions) (England) (No. 4) Regulations 2020.

On 28 November 2020 in Westminster he participated in a gathering in public outdoor place of more than two people in England contrary to regulations 9(1)(a) and 20(1)(a) and (2) of the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Restrictions) (England) (No. 4) Regulations 2020.

On 31 December 2020 on the South Bank, Lambeth he held/was involved in holding gathering of more than 30 persons on specific public outdoor land in Tier 4 area contrary to regulation 10(1)(a) and (2)and paragraph 5(1) and (5) of Schedule 3A to the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Restrictions) (All Tiers) (England) Regulations 2020.

On 31 December 2020 on the South Bank, Lambeth he participated a in gathering of more than two people in public outdoor place in Tier 4 area contrary to regulation 10(1)(a) and (2) and paragraph 4(1)(a) of Schedule 3A to the Health Protection (Coronavirus, Restrictions) (All Tiers) (England) Regulations 2020.
