December 14, 2020

Share by email

Detectives investigating a burglary in which a mobile phone was stolen have made an arrest.

The incident took place around 9.40pm on Friday 11 December at an address in Sandfield Road in Lenton.

A 39-year-old man has now been arrested in connection with the incident.

Detective Inspector Kayne Rukas from the force's Burglary Team said: "We were called following a report of a man leaning into an insecure ground floor window and stole a mobile phone.

"It was reported at around 9.40pm he entered a house from a living room window and a stole a phone, then ran away from the scene.

"Officers searched local CCTV footage and a man was located and arrested swiftly.

"A 39-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of burglary. He remains in police custody.

"This positive result was thanks to excellent work by a number of officers to locate the man and make the arrest.

"Nottinghamshire Police takes burglary extremely seriously and we continue to work hard and thoroughly investigate all incidents reported to us."
