A man is due to appear in court in connection with a robbery where a woman was punched in the face.

The victim had a mobile phone taken in the incident in Sherbrooke Road, Carrington, at 4.40pm yesterday (Saturday 26 December).

Kyrie Michael, of Perlethorpe Avenue, Sneinton, has today been charged with robbery and burglary in connection with the incident.

The 25-year-old has also been charged with burglary in connection with a separate incident in Ravensmore Road, Carrington, at around 5.15pm yesterday.

He has also been charged with possession of cannabis and has been remanded in custody to appear at Nottingham Magistrates' Court today (Monday 28 December).

Detective Inspector Ed Cook, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: "A number of officers from different teams have pulled together on this investigation which has now led to a charge."
