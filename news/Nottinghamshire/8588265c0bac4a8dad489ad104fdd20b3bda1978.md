Detectives investigating a burglary at a city centre bar have charged a man in connection with the incident.

A quantity of alcohol was reported to have been taken from Bar 101 in St James Street on 22 February 2021.

Gregory Bailey, 47, of Comyn Gardens, St Ann’s, is due to appear at Nottingham Magistrates’ Court tomorrow (Monday 8 March) charged with burglary as part of an investigation into the report.

Detective Sergeant Nick Wood, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “Nottinghamshire Police takes burglary very seriously and will always thoroughly investigate reports.

“We have been working hard to establish the circumstances of this incident since it was reported to us and are pleased to have charged a man as part of our investigation.”
