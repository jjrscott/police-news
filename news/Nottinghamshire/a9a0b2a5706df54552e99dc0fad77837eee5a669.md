Detectives investigating a burglary in Worksop are appealing for information.

It's believed thieves gained entry into the house in Baulk Lane between 9.45am and 11am on Tuesday (15 December).

The house was ransacked and cash and bank cards were stolen.

Detective Sergeant Jamie Moore, said: "We're asking residents if they saw anything suspicious in the area around the time it happened, no matter how small.
