January 27, 2021

Share by email

Officers have made two arrests after a woman was approached by two men in a car and robbed of her bank card and mobile phone.

The men, aged 21 and 22, have been arrested on suspicion of robbery following the incident called just before 4.10pm yesterday (26 January 2020) to Edward Street in Kirkby-in-Ashfield, and remain in custody.

The woman was reportedly walking along the street when two men pulled up got out of a car. They are then said to have approached her and made threats before taking her phone and card from her.

Detective Sergeant Jamie Moore, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: "This is a positive update in our investigation into this incident, which was extremely frightening for the woman.

"We take such incidents extremely seriously and we would reassure people that officers are dedicated to investigating reports of robbery and tackling this across the county.

"We continue our enquiries into what happened yesterday and welcome any information which could help our investigation.

"Anyone who saw the incident or has any information is urged to contact Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident 403 of 26 January 2021.
