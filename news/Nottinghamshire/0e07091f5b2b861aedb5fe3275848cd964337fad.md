Officers responding to a report of a disturbance in Arboretum acted quickly to arrest and charge a woman in connection with the incident.

Police were called after a woman was reported to have been shouting explicit words and obstructing a tram in Noel Street at around 2.10pm on 11 February 2021.

Angela Hopkins, 38, of Waterford Street, was charged with threatening behaviour and was bailed to appear at Nottingham Magistrates’ Court on 3 March 2021.

Neighbourhood Policing Inspector Amy English, said: “We are committed to keeping the local community safe and our officers are always quick to respond to concerns from the public.

“If you see something suspicious in your area, please report it to Nottinghamshire Police on 101 or in an emergency, dial 999.”
