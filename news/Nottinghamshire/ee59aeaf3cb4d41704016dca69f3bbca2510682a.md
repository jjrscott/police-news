Police are seeking the public’s help after a 17-year-old was assaulted by two male suspects in St Ann’s.

The victim was with a friend in Pym Street when he was rugby tackled to the floor. The victim and his friend managed to run off and neither were injured.

The incident reportedly happened around 11.30pm yesterday (Wednesday 17 March 2021).

Inspector Karl Thomas, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “Our enquiries into this assault are ongoing and we’re appealing for anyone who was in the area around the time and who may have seen what happened to please call us.

“We would also like to hear from any drivers who may have captured footage of the incident. If you have any information which might help us with our enquiries please call Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 961 of 17 March 2021, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.”
