November 29, 2020

Share by email

A fourth man has been arrested by police investigating a recent shooting.

The arrest is in connection with an ongoing investigation after a 23-year-old man was shot in the arm near to Hickings Lane Park, Stapleford, on the night of Wednesday 18 November.

The victim was taken to hospital with non-life-threatening injuries and has since been discharged.

An 18-year-old was arrested yesterday on suspicion of attempted murder.

Two men, both aged 21, have already been arrested on suspicion of the same offence and released on bail.

A third man, aged 58, has also been arrested on suspicion of assisting an offender and is currently on police bail.

Detective Sergeant Charlotte Henson, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “We understand that this incident came as a shock to local residents and would like to thank them for their ongoing cooperation during our investigation.

“Our enquiries are still ongoing and we are urging anyone who has any information which may help us with our investigation, and who haven't already come forward, to please get in contact with us.

"If you saw anything or possibly captured dashcam footage of the incident please call Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 749 of 18 November 2020, or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
