Share by email

Two people were assaulted when a man smashed his way into their home and demanded money.

The incident took place shortly after 8.20pm on Thursday evening at an address in Hawthorn Close, Edwalton.

A suspect broke in by smashing glass at the rear of the property before assaulting and demanding money from the victims.

After a brief struggle he fled the scene empty-handed and drove away in a waiting vehicle

The victims sustained minor injuries but were not seriously hurt.

Detective Sergeant Luke Todd, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “This was a frightening incident for the victims who thankfully were not seriously injured. Officers remain in the scene and we are working hard to find the person responsible for this crime.

“I’d also like to reassure local residents that additional police patrols will be conducted in the area over the coming days. House-to-house enquiries are underway and we’d like to hear from anyone who saw or heard anything suspicious before, during and after the incident.

“We would also like to hear from anyone that may have witnessed the vehicle leaving the location or have dashcam footage”

If you have any information on this incident please call 101 quoting incident 642 of 3 December.
