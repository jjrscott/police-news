A suspect arrested by police within minutes following reports of a disturbance in Radford has now been charged with drug possession offences.

Officers were called to the Walter Street area shortly after 4.20pm on Tuesday (23 February 2021). It was reported that a man had been involved in an altercation with two other men.

They quickly arrived on scene and arrested a man nearby in connection with the report.

Johnny Bryan, 44, of Tennyson Street, Radford, has now been charged with possession of Class A and B drugs. He has been bailed to appear at Nottingham Magistrates' Court on 11 March 2021.

Sergeant Janine Barnes, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: "We take drug-related crime extremely seriously and will always investigate reports and take appropriate actions against those suspected to have committed such offences.
