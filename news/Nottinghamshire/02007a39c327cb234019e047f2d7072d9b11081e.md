Police are in attendance in the B600 near Jubilee Hill, Selston, following reports of a serious collision involving three cars.

Nottinghamshire Police was called at around 10.18am this morning and officers are at the scene.

The B600 is closed in both directions while emergency services work to establish the circumstances.

It is unclear at this stage if anyone has been seriously injured in the incident.

The road will remain closed until it has been cleared.
