January 5, 2021

Share by email

Detectives investigating a suspicious incident in Eastwood have appealed for information, footage and witnesses to aid them in their enquiries.

A scene was in place at a house in Three Tuns Road after a reported burglary.

The incident is believed to have taken place between 7pm and midnight on New Year’s Eve and it is unclear whether or not anything was taken.

Detective Inspector Gayle Hart, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “The victim in this case in an incredibly vulnerable person and we are doing everything we can to establish what happened.

“A burglary was reported to Nottinghamshire Police on 3 January and as such, it is very early days in our investigation. We still working to establish the circumstances around what actually happened.

“I’d ask anyone who may have seen something suspicious between between 7pm and midnight to get in touch as soon as possible.

“Anything information you have, no matter how minor you think it may be, could help us piece together what happened.

“The victim is being supported by specially trained officers while we make our enquiries.”

Anyone who witnessed the incident, has any footage or information should contact Nottinghamshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 424 of 3 January 2021.
