Two men were arrested after police were called to a fight in a residential street.

Officers were called to Victoria Street, Mansfield, shortly after 4pm yesterday (Wednesday) afternoon after several people were reported to be fighting.

Two men, aged 37 and 39, were arrested on suspicion of affray and taken to hospital for treatment. They were both released shortly afterwards and taken into police custody.

Investigations continue.

Inspector Nick Butler, district commander for Mansfield, said: “This kind of public disorder is completely unacceptable and will always generate a very swift police response. We are working to understand exactly what happened during this incident and would greatly appreciate hearing from any witnesses who have not already spoken to us.”

If you have any information about this incident please call 101 quoting incident 456 of 17 February. Alternatively you can all Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
