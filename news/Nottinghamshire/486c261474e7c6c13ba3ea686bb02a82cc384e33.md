January 28, 2021

Share by email

Police recovered shotgun ammunition from a stopped car and arrested a man in connection with the find.

Officers quickly responded to a report of a suspicious vehicle in Valley Road, Basford yesterday evening.

The car was stopped by officers who used tactical contact to bring it to a halt.

The vehicle was search and ammunition was recovered.

A 35-year-old man was then arrested on suspicion of unlawful possession of ammunition without a certificate.

Detective Inspector Jon Scurr said: “Officers did a fantastic job to bring this incident to a safe conclusion and arrest a man in connection with the incident.

“Detectives are now investigating the circumstances of the ammunition and a man remains in police custody for questioning as part of our enquiries.

“Nottinghamshire Police takes reports of firearms offences very seriously and will always thoroughly investigate incidents in order to take appropriate action against those who have committed such offences.

“I’d ask that any members of the public report any concerns to 101 or in an emergency dial 999.”
