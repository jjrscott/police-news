Detectives investigating a burglary in Mansfield have arrested a 20-year-old man.

Officers from Nottinghamshire Police were called to a report that a man was said to have stolen the keys to a flat in Tideswell Court, Mansfield at 4.30am this morning (27 February) before breaking into the property.

Various items were taken including two coats, various tools, a designer bag and alcohol.

A man was arrested nearby following police enquiries.

Detective Sergeant Gary Hewson, said: "Response teams were immediately on scene and made an arrest within minutes.

"Burglaries are taken extremely seriously and we will always do everything we can to establish who might be behind these crimes

"Our enquiries continue while the arrested man remains in custody."
