Officers were quick to respond to a report of an industrial accident yesterday morning.

A man was reported to have suffered injuries that required hospital treatment in the incident, which happened at around 10.30am in Trent Lane.

Police attended and the investigation has been passed to the Health and Safety Executive, who will now investigate the circumstances.

Police Sergeant Peter Shaw, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “Emergency services acted quickly to arrive at the scene of this report.

“A man was taken to hospital for treatment of injuries that are not thought to be life-threatening.

“The investigation will now be taken over the by the Health and Safety Executive.”

A HSE spokesperson said: “HSE was informed of an incident by Nottinghamshire Police yesterday and attended the site to make initial enquiries.

“We are now investigating the incident further and are gathering evidence on site. We have also taken action to control any ongoing risk.”
