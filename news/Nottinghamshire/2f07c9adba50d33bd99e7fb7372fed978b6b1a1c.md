Share by email

Three teenagers were arrested after a boy had his bike stolen.

The victim was pushing his bike along Chesterfield Road South shortly after 3pm on Tuesday when we was approached by a group of young men and shoved to the body and face.

An offender than took his bike and rode away in the direction of Mansfield town centre.

A 19-year-old man and two 17-year-old boys were arrested suspicion of robbery shortly afterwards.

The victim was not seriously hurt.

Detective Sergeant Mark Shaw, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “This was an upsetting incident for the victim and we are currently working to establish what happened. Robbery is an incredibly serious offence that we will always investigate thoroughly.

“To this end we would like to speak to anyone who saw what happened and would ask them to call us on 101 quoting incident 405 of December 1.”
