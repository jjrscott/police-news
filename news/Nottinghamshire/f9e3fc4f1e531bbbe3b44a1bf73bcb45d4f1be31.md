Share by email

A delivery driver was assaulted and his car stolen during a violent attack on a residential street.

He had been delivering food to an address in Beech Avenue, Mapperley, at around 8.15pm on New Year's Eve when he was chased, struck repeatedly with a meatal weapon, and forced to hand over his car keys.

The attacker, who was wearing a black hooded top and tracksuit trousers, then drove away in the victim’s car – a black VW Golf.

The victim is currently being treated in hospital.

Detective Sergeant Emma Pollard , of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “This was a nasty and sustained attack that left a man needing hospital treatment. We are obviously extremely keen to find the person responsible and would like the public’s help to being them into custody.

“So if you witnessed any part of this incident, or saw the driver’s car immediately after the incident, please get in touch us as soon as possible.”

If you have any information about this incident please call police on 101 quoting incident 562 of 31 December.
