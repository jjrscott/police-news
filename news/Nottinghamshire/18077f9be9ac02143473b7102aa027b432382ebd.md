Emergency services have been called to a report of a fire at a gas substation in Trowell.

Nottinghamshire Police, Nottinghamshire Fire and Rescue Service and Cadent were called to the blaze in Pit Lane just after 5.50pm today (Tuesday 16 March 2021)

Inspector Ben Lawrence, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “Following advice from gas safety experts a number of properties within 200 metres of the fire have been evacuated while emergency services deal with the incident and residents have been relocated to a nearby community centre and parish hall.

“No injuries have currently been reported and police have cordoned off the area while investigations continue.

“We would advise people to please stay away from the area while the incident is being dealt with.”
