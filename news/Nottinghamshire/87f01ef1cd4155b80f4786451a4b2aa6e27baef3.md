Police investigating an alleged robbery have arrested a suspect.

The incident took place in Crompton Acres, West Bridgford, shortly after 4.30pm on Monday 30 November, when an offender threatened to stab his teenage victim unless he handed over money.

Police arrested a 13-year-old boy on suspicion of robbery this morning and he remains in custody.

Detective Sergeant Luke Todd, said: “Incidents like this cause a great deal of alarm and distress to victims and can also be a meter of great concern to local communities.

“That’s why we take them so seriously and dedicate whatever resources we need to bring suspects into custody and to get justice for victims.”
