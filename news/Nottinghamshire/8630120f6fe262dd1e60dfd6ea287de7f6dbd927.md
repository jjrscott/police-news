A man has suffered head injuries following a collision on the A52 (Derby Road) where his car has hit a tree.

The road has had to be closed following the serious incident, with emergency services at the scene to treat the man.

Chief Inspector Annie Reavley said: "Emergency services were called to the A52, opposite Hawton Crescent, on Derby Road towards the city from Wollaton at around 11.15am this morning.

"Officers have closed the road near to Crown Island following the incident. The man's injuries are thought to be life altering.
