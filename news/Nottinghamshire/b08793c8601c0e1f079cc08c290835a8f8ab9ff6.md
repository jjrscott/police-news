A suspect has been charged and remanded in connection with a report of a large fight in a Ruddington pub.

Quaid Gayle, 29, of Broxtowe Lane, Aspley, has been charged with affray. He appeared at Nottingham Magistrates' Court today (Saturday 9 January 2021) and was remanded in custody to next appear at Nottingham Crown Court on 8 February 2021.

Police were called to reports of a fight at The Red Heart in Easthorpe Street at around 9.20pm on 19 September 2020.

Officers quickly attended and conducted enquiries into the incident.

Detective Sergeant Luke Todd, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: "We want to reassure people that violence of this nature will not be tolerated and we will always look to take action against those who commit such offences in our communities."
