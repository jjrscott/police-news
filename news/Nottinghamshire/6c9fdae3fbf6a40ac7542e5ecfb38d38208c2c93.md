March 12, 2021

Share by email

Police investigating a report a man threatening a driver with what may have been a gun quickly made an arrest in connection with the report.

Officers were called to Hayden Road in Sherwood at around 11pm last night (Thursday 11 March) after a car was reported to have attempted to ram in to another vehicle.

It was then alleged that a driver pointed gun in the direction of another driver before the victim fled the scene.

Eagle-eyed officers spotted a car suspected to be involved in the incident and quickly arrested a 21-year-old man on suspicion of possession of a firearm in connection with the report.

Detectives are now appealing for anyone who may have seen something suspicious in the area at the time to report it as soon as possible.

No one was injured in the incident.

Detective Sergeant Christopher Berryman, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “This incident will undoubtedly be concerning for residents but officers did a fantastic job to quickly make arrests in connection with the report.

“We are exploring the possibility that a gun was pointed at a driver as part of the incident as part of our enquiries.

“Nottinghamshire Police takes firearms offences very seriously and thoroughly investigates reports in order to take appropriate action against offenders.

“I’d now appeal for anyone who witnessed something suspicious in the area at the time to get in touch as soon as possible.”

Anyone who witnessed the incident, has any CCTV or dash-cam footage in the area at the time should contact Nottinghamshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 762 of 11 March 2021.
