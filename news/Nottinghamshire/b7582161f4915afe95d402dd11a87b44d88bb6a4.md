Share by email

A man has been charged with drink-driving after officers were called to an incident in Hucknall.

The response team attended a report of a disturbance in the Annesley Road area at around 1am today (17 December 2020).

They located a van with the engine running in the Annesley area and the driver was arrested on suspicion of a drink-drive related offence.

Jonathon Lowton, 39, of Annesley Road in Hucknall, was charged with the offence this morning.

He has been bailed to appear at Magistrates Court in February 2021.

Ashfield Response Sergeant Mark Westlake, said: "We take a zero tolerance approach to drink-driving at any time of the year and during the force's Christmas campaign, we have extra measures.

"We are working hard to take dangerous drivers off the roads before they can cause any harm.

"We also welcome information from the public if they suspect someone is drink or drug driving."

You can call Nottinghamshire police on 101 with any information.
