Police have charged a man after a student was allegedly robbed of his electric scooter in Radford.

James Francis, 19, of Glyn Road, Hackney, London, has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Nottingham Magistrates' Court today (Monday 1 March 2021) charged with robbery.

Nottinghamshire Police received a report that an 18-year-old man was travelling home on his electric scooter when he was threatened by two men before one of them stole his scooter. The alleged incident happened in the Hilditch Way/New Road area of Radford at around 5.45pm on Thursday (25 February 2021).

Detective Sergeant Nick Wood, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: "The charge follows a determined investigation into this reported incident which left the victim very shaken.

"Nottinghamshire Police takes these types of offences very seriously and will always strive to put suspects before the courts."
