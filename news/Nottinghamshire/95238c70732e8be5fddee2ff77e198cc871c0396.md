A man has appeared in court charged on connection with a robbery at a convenience store in Nuthall.

Victor Rickett, 37, of Thornbury Way, Nottingham, is charged with robbery, taking a vehicle without the owner's consent, driving without insurance and driving without a licence.

The charges relate to a report of a robbery at the McColls store in Ash Crescent at around 10pm on Tuesday (29 December 2020)

Rickett appeared at Nottingham Magistrates' Court on Thursday (31 December 2020) and was remanded in custody to appear at Nottingham Crown Court on 28 January 2021.

Police Investigator Adam Karimjee, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: "We are working hard to identify and trace two further suspects in connection with this incident.

"Our enquiries are ongoing and I would urge anyone who saw the incident and who hasn't yet come forward to please get in touch with us by calling Nottinghamshire Police on 101 and quoting incident number 676 of 29 December 2020. We'd also like to hear from anyone who has any footage or any information that could aid us in our investigation."
