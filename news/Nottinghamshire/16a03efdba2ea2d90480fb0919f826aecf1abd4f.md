Share by email

A man has died following a report of concerns for safety.

Officers from Nottinghamshire Police were called to Upper College Street in Nottingham City Centre just after 4.40pm to a report of concerns for a man’s safety.

Inspector Paul Hennessy, who is overseeing the incident, said: “Officers are at the scene of the incident and we are working to establish the circumstances of what has happened.

“Anyone who has information who has not already spoken to officers should get in touch with us.”

Anyone who saw what happened should call Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 488 of 10 December 2020.
