An arrest has been made after man was reportedly kicked and punched before having money taken from him.

Police were called to a report of a robbery at an address in Rosemary Street, Mansfield just before 10.05pm yesterday (26 March 2021).It's reported a man had been demanding money and that a small amount of cash was taken during the incident.

Officers arrested a 35-year-old man on suspicion of robbery. He remains in custody.

Detective Sergeant Lauren Morgan, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: "An arrest has been made in connection with the reported robbery and we continue our enquiries into this incident.

"This was frightening for the victim and we are absolutely dedicated to investigating this and understanding the circumstances around what happened.

"However, we believe this is an isolated incident and that there is no wider risk to the public, and we would reassure people that we will thoroughly investigate where there is such a report.

"We would encourage anyone with information to get in touch."

Anyone who saw the incident or has any information is urged to get in touch on 101, quoting incident number 888 of 26 March 2021.
