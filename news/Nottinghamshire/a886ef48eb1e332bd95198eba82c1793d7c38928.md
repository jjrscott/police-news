A motorbike and a van collided in Chesterfield Road South at the junction with Paulsons Drive at around 6.20am this morning.

Two men who were on the bike have been taken to the Queen's Medical centre with serious injuries.

Both roads are closed while the serious collision investigation takes place.

Motorists are being advised to avoid the areas.
