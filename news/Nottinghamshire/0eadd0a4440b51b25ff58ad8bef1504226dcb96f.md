A 27-year-old man has been arrested in connection with a number of serious offences after members of the public reported seeing man with a knife.

Officers were called to the incident in the Forest Yard and Norfolk Street areas of Worksop at around 3.50pm on Friday (26 February) where a man was said to be threatening people with a knife.

A man tried to flee from response teams before being arrested nearby on suspicion of possession of a bladed article. He was further arrested on suspicion of possession wtih intent to supply controlled drugs following searches near to the incident location as well as at a house.

Detective Sergeant Ricky Ellis, said: "Response teams quickly attended this incident resulting in a speedy arrest.

"Further enquiries resulted in the man also being arrested in connection with some serious drug offences.

"Our officers will always investigate the full circumstances around an incident and will leave no stone unturned in order to gather all pieces of evidence."

The man remains in custody while enquiries continue.

If you witnessed the incident or have any information that could help, please call us on 101 quoting incident 481 of 26 February. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
