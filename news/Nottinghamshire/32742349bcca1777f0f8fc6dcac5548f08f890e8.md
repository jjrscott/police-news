Officers investigating a report of a car stolen from a city centre car park would like to speak to this man as part of their enquiries.

The black Nissan Quashqai was reportedly taken from an NCP car park in Stoney Street at around 8am on 27 September 2020.

Today, police have asked for the public’s help to identify a man who may hold information about the incident.

Police Constable Angelo Gibaldi, of Nottinghamshire Police’s City Centre team, said: “As a force, we are committed to tackling vehicle crime and theft and will always investigate such incidents thoroughly.

“If anyone has any information about this incident or recognises the man pictured, please contact Nottinghamshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 296 of 27 September 2020.”
