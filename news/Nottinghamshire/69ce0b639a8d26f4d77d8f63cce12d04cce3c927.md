Police are appealing for witnesses and information after an antique pram was stolen in a suspected robbery.

Officers say the distinctive white pram was taken from a woman in Gladstone Street, Nottingham, shortly before 10.30am yesterday (Saturday) as she took it for a private sale.

Detective Sergeant Nigel Malik, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “We are working to understand what happened here and would like to speak with anyone who saw what happened before, during and after this incident

“The antique white pram in question is very distinctive looking and would have seemed out of place. We are eager to speak to anybody who may have seen it.”

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to call police on 101 quoting incident number 213 of Saturday 28 November.
