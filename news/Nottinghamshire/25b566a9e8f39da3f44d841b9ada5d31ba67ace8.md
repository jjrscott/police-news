February 18, 2021

Share by email

Officers investigating a report of criminal damage and bike theft would like to speak to the people pictured as part of their enquiries.

Police were called to the Cycle Hub in Station Road, Beeston after two men were reported to have broken the door to the premises in order to gain entry and steal bikes.

The incident is believed to have happened at around 12.42pm on Thursday 21 January 2021.

Nottinghamshire Police has now appealed for anyone who recognises the people pictured or has any information that could aid officers in their investigation to get in touch as soon as possible.

Police Constable Phoebe Lau, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “Criminal damage and theft are taken very seriously by Nottinghamshire Police and we will always investigate reports made to us.

“We are now working to establish the circumstances of this incident and I would encourage anyone with any information to come forward.”

If you recognise the people pictured or have any information please contact Nottinghamshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 21000068260.
