December 19, 2020

Share by email

A man and a woman have sadly died following a crash in Selston.

Police were initially called to the B600 at around 10.18am this morning to a report of a serious collision involving three cars.

The man and woman were pronounced dead at the scene and a woman has been taken to hospital for treatment of serious injuries.

A man has been taken to hospital as a precaution and is not believed to be seriously injured.

The B600 near Selston is expected to be closed for some time until it has been cleared.

Inspector Hayley Crawford, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “This is an incredibly sad incident that has resulted in two people losing their lives and one person in a critical condition.

“We are now working hard to establish the circumstances and I would appeal to anyone who has any information, witnessed the incident or has any dashcam footage to get in touch as soon as possible.

“Our thoughts are with the families of the man and the woman who have died.”

Nottinghamshire Fire and Rescue Service, Group Manager Tom Archer said: "This is a tragic incident which sadly led to the death of a man and a woman along with one person seriously injured. Our thoughts are with the family and friends and local community at this time.

"Crews worked extremely hard alongside colleagues from Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service, EMAS and the Police in very challenging circumstances.

"This is a timely reminder that if you are travelling this season please do so safely."

If you witnessed the incident or have any information or footage that could aid officers in their enquiries, please contact Nottinghamshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 199 of 19 December 2020.
