December 22, 2020

Share by email

Police arrested two men and a woman after swiftly attending reports of a fight in a block of flats in Bestwood.

A man was hit over the head with a baseball bat during the incident which happened in Monroe Walk shortly after 11.15pm yesterday.

Officers administered first aid at the scene before he was taken to hospital. His injuries are not believed to be serious.

A 57-year-old man and 50-year-old woman were arrested on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm with intent and a 58-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of affray.

Sergeant Neil Langham, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: "Our enquiries are ongoing to establish the circumstances of this incident. It’s believed those involved in this incident are known to each other and I’d appeal to the public to get in touch if they have any information that could aid us in our investigation.

“Violent crime has no place on our streets and we will always take swift and proportionate action against those who commit such offences."

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information that could aid officers in their investigation should contact Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 719 of 21 December 2020, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
