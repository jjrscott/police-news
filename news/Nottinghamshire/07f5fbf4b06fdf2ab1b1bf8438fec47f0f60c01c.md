Police are questioning a teenager after a man was knocked unconscious and suffered a fractured eye socket, jaw and wrist during an assault in the Warsop area.

Officers investigating the assault on the victim, who is aged in his 20s, arrested a 17-year-old boy in Warsop, during the early hours of today (Sunday 21 March 2021), on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm with intent.

He remain in custody in connection with the attack which happened on 7 March 2021.

Detective Inspector Kim Binns, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: “This was a brutal attack which left the victim with a number of broken bones.

"Violent behaviour of this nature will not be tolerated in our communities and will do all we can to identify those responsible for such offences.”
