Police have charged a man with possession of a knife in connection with an alleged incident at a block of flats in Balderton.

Dominic Howitt, 24, of Bridge Court, Balderton, has been bailed to appear at Nottingham Magistrates' Court on 25 March 2021.

The charge follows a report of an incident in Bridge Court shortly before 6pm on Monday (8 March 2021).

Detective Sergeant Andrew Hall, of Nottinghamshire Police, said: "The force takes knife-related offences extremely seriously and will always strive to bring suspects before the courts."
