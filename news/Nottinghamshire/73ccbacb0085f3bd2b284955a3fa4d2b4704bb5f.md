A man has pleaded guilty to the manslaughter of a woman who died after being assaulted in a city centre car park.

Deborah Jones, 48, also known as Deborah Hendrick, died at the Metro Car Park in Union Road on 1 August 2020.

Gary Parnell, 59, of Lytham Gardens, Top Valley, entered his guilty plea at Nottingham Crown Court yesterday and will next appear at Nottingham Crown Court for sentencing on 11 January 2021.
