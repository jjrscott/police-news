**Officers investigating an arson attack in Chippenham in September have arrested and charged a man.**

Kris Cleverley, 32, of Moyne Close, Devizes, was charged yesterday with arson with intent to endanger life in relation to the incident that occurred in Charter Road on September 22.

He appeared at Swindon Magistrate's Court this morning and has been remanded in custody. He will next appear at Swindon Crown Court on December 29.

 _Published on Tuesday, December 1, 2020_
