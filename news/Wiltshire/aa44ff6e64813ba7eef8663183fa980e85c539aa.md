We are appealing for witnesses to a serious road traffic collision on the A361 south of Trowbridge yesterday (28/02) in which an 18-year-old man sustained life threatening injuries.

Shortly after 4pm, on the A361 Southwick Junction with Poplar Tree Lane, a motorcyclist on a Yamaha 125cc was in collision with a Vauxhall Zafira - leaving him with serious injuries.

He was taken to Southmead Hospital by air ambulance where he is currently being treated for his injuries. His next of kin have been informed.

The occupants of the Vauxhall Zafira were uninjured.

Anyone who may have witnessed the incident or who may have dashcam footage is asked to contact us on 101 quoting log 234 of yesterday (28/02)

 _Published on 1 March 2021_
