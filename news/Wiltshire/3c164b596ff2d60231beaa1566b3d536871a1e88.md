We've charged a man in connection with the incident in Ridge Green, Swindon, on Tuesday (16/6).

Howard Jones, 59, from Shrivenham Road, has been charged with:

 * Possession of a firearm or imitation firearm whilst committing or being arrested for a schedule 1 offence
 * Robbery
 * ABH
 * Assault on an emergency worker
 * Breach of bail

He has been remanded in custody and is due before Swindon Magistrates' Court tomorrow morning.
