**We're appealing for witnesses after one of Warminster's public Christmas trees was damaged by vandals. **

At approximately 10pm yesterday (17/12), offenders damaged the tree which is standing in the Three Horse Shoes Walk Mall.

Thanks to CCTV and local intelligence our officers have a good idea who the suspects are and are continuing to make enquiries today (18/12).

To help with our investigation, if you heard or saw anything suspicious in this area yesterday evening please contact us on 101 quoting crime reference number 54200124985.

 _Published on Friday 18 December 2020._
