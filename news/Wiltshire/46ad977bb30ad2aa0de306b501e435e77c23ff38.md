We've charged a man in connection with the serious assault in Swindon on Thursday (03/09).

Patrick Gaff, 48, of Beaulieu Close, Toothill, has been charged with causing Grevious Bodily Harm (GBH) and possession of a knife in a public place.

This relates to the assault in Crawford Close, Freshbrook where a 31-year-old man was assaulted and required hospital treatment.

Gaff is due before Swindon Magistrates' Court this morning.
