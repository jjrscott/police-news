**We're appealing for witnesses following an assault on a man at an address in Pinehurst. **

Officers were called at approximately 6.30pm today (19/12) to the property on Pinehurst Road following reports that a man had been stabbed.

A 24-year-old man was taken by ambulance to hospital with non-life threatening injuries.

People may notice an increased police presence in the area while our enquiries continue.

If you heard or saw anything suspicious in the area before or after this time this evening, please contact us on 101 quoting log number 263 of today.

Alternatively, you can report anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

 _Published on Saturday 19 December 2020_
