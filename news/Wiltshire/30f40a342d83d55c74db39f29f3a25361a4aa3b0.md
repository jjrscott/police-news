We are aware of a potential issue affecting the 101 number for non-emergency calls. This seems to be a national issue and engineers are already working to resolve the problem.

If you are having trouble trying to get through to us on 101 please DO NOT call 999, as this needs to be reserved for emergencies.

Please call either 01380 861101 or 01793 267101, or you can report a crime to us online at www.wiltshire.police.uk/report_a_crime

Posted: 14:00, 19 June 2020
