**Three men have been charged with drug offences following a series of raids in East Swindon yesterday (25/02). **

Ewan Burroughs, aged 18, from Green Road, Upper Stratton was arrested late last night and has subsequently been charged with being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs, possession with intent to supply Class B drugs and money laundering. He has been remanded in custody.

Kieran Steptoe, aged 20, from Tansley Moor, Swindon has been charged with intent to supply Class B drugs and has been released on conditional bail.

Liam Lewis, aged 22, from Tansley Moor has been charged with possession of Class B drugs and has been released on unconditional bail.

An 18-year-old man who was arrested yesterday on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs has been released under investigation.

A 22-year-old woman arrested at a property in Swindon yesterday has been released with no further action to be taken against them.

 _Published on Friday 26 February 2021. _
