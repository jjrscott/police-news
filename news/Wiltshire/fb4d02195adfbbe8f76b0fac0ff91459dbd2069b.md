This letter is being delivered from today (19 July 2018) to households in the Amesbury and Salisbury area. It is from Police and Crime Commissioner for Wiltshire and Swindon Angus Macpherson, Leader of Wiltshire Council Baroness Scott of Bybrook OBE, and MP for Salisbury and South Wiltshire John Glen and is an update following the major incidents.

It contains public health advice and contact details, and restates the view of all the agencies involved that public safety remains paramount.

The letter also includes details of two further public meetings next week.
