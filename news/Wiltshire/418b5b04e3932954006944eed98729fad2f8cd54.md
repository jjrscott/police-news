**We're appealing for witnesses after an address in West Swindon was burgled. **

During the early hours of Sunday 29 November, unknown offenders entered the property on Kimbolton Close and stole several electrical tools and hand tools.

PC Thomas Scourfield said: "We would like to speak to anyone who may have heard or seen anything suspicious in this residential area in the early hours of Sunday morning.
"Also, we are noticing a series of burglaries in the West and North Swindon areas of the town recently and would appeal to home-owners to be vigilant and ensure their property is left secure when going out. "
