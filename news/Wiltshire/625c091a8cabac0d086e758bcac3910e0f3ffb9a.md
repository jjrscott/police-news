**We're appealing for information regarding the whereabouts of a woman who is missing from Salisbury.**

Kelly Shipsey, 29, was last seen at her home address in Salisbury on 31 January and we believe she could be in the Southampton area, but also has connections to Trowbridge and Swindon.

She is described as white, approximately 5 feet 1 inch tall, of slim build with dyed black hair and hazel eyes. Please see picture attached.

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 and reference number 54210013422. In an emergency call 999.
