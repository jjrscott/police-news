**We're appealing for witnesses following an assault on a busy road in Salisbury. **

At approximately 6.30pm on Monday 21 December, an altercation took place on Fisherton Street between two people, one of whom was then assaulted by the other.

A 37-year-old local man was arrested in relation to this incident and has been released on conditional bail.

We are continuing to make enquiries.

If you heard or saw anything suspicious in this area at this time, please contact us on 101 quoting crime reference number 54200126310.

 _Published on Thursday 24 December 2020. _
