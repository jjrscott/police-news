**We are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision on Saturday (09/01) afternoon in Ludgershall.**

At approximately 3.15pm we were called to the report of a single-vehicle collision involving a white Mitsubishi Colt which had ended up on its roof on Biddesden Lane.

The driver, who has been described as a white male, aged in his 20s, wearing a grey tracksuit, got out of the vehicle and left the scene on foot.

The vehicle was reported stolen from an address in Ludgershall the same day.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or has any information in relation to the identity of the driver or the vehicle being stolen, is asked to call us on 101 and quote crime reference number 54210002450.

 _Published on Tuesday, January 12, 2021_
