Officers are appealing for information following the theft of a quad bike from a property in Long Preston.

The incident occurred at Skirbeck Farm near to the A65 between 6pm on Wednesday, 6 June and 6am on Thursday, 7 June. The offenders are believed to have demolished part of a dry stone wall approximately 200metres from the farm entrance and gained access through the field.

A green Honda TRX 500FM quad bike has then been removed through the damaged wall. The bike is less than a year old.

In particular, officers are appealing for information about any vehicles or people involved. It is possible that passing traffic may have witnessed this incident due to the close proximity to the A65.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Michael Haydock. You can also email Michael.Haydock@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12180100563 when providing information.
