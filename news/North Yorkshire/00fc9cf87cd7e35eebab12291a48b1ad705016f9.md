Police are appealing for witnesses after a woman died in a single-vehicle collision on the Castle Howard estate.

The collision occurred at about 9.40pm on Monday 4 May 2015 when a silver Volkswagen Touran collided with a stone archway on a public road that runs through the estate.

The woman was sadly pronounced dead at the scene by paramedics.

Senior investigating officer Traffic Constable Mark Hutchings, of the Major Collision Investigation Team, said: "If you witnessed the incident, or saw the silver VW Touran involved prior to the collision, and have not already spoken to the police, please contact me as you may have important information that could assist the investigation."

Anyone with information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police - dial 101, select option 2 and ask for TC Martin Hayes or the Major Collision Investigation Team. Alternatively, information can be emailed to martin.hayes@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12150073743 when passing information.

**7 May 2015**
