North Yorkshire Police has issued e-fits of two men they would like to speak to in relation to the robbery and assault of a woman who was driving along the A172 toward Stokesley, in the early hours of Sunday 18 September 2016.

The 22-year-old local woman was driving along the A172 towards Stokesley at 1am on Sunday morning. As she approached a set of crossroads near Faceby, she noticed the vehicle behind her was flashing its headlights.

She pulled her car over and two hooded males got out of the following vehicle and approached her car. They proceeded to rob the woman, took money from her and physically assaulted her before leaving.
