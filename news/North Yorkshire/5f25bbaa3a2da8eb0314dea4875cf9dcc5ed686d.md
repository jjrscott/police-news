North Yorkshire Police are appealing for information to help them trace a man in York.

At approximately 5.30pm on Thursday 23 March 2017 a 14-year-old girl was walking back from York city centre along Wigginton Road in York.

As she turned onto the cycle path which runs under Wigginton Road towards Crichton Avenue, she was approached by a man dressed in dark clothing. Police are wanting to trace the man to determine the full circumstances around the incident.

Police are appealing to anyone who was in the area at time who may have seen the man, or anyone with any information which could assist the investigation, to contact them on 101 and ask to speak to A/DS Kevin Goodman or email Kevin.Goodman@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170049168.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
