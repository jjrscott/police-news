North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of tools and plant material that occurred at Hookstone Woods near the Yorkshire Showgrounds in Harrogate

The theft took place at the Hookstone Woods near the Yorkshire Showgrounds sometime between 2pm on Sunday 3 July 2016 and 7am Monday 4 July 2016 and involved offenders gaining access to a secure construction site forcing a secure container and removing a quantity of tools and plant material.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
