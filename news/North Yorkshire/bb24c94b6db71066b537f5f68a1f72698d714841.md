29-year-old Abrar Khan who was living temporarily in York has today (8 January 2018) appeared at York Magistrates' Court charged with rape and robbery following an incident in York on the evening of 2 January 2018.

He has been remanded in custody to appear again at Crown Court on 5 February 2018.
