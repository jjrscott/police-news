North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a motorbike theft that occurred in York.

It happened between 3pm on Thursday 19 January and 5.30am on Friday 20 January 2017 and involved a black Kawasaki motorbike which was stolen from outside Drummond House, St Johns Walk in Heworth. The bike was abandoned a short distance away on a cycle path near to Heworth Green.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident. In particular, if anyone witnessed the motorbike being pushed from Drummond House towards the cycle path then they are urged to get in touch.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Louise Neville-Beck. You can also email: louise.neville-beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-20012017-0054.
