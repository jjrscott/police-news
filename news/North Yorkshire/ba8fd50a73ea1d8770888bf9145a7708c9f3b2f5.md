The 15 year old girl, 16 year old boy, 33 year old woman and 37 year old man who were arrested in connection with the tragic death of Josh Reeson have been released on bail with conditions, pending further enquiries.

Anyone who has any information which could help the investigation into Josh's tragic death, or anyone who saw a group of teenagers in the Fulford area of York in the early hours on Sunday (27 September 2020) is asked to contact Police. Dial 101, press 1 and pass information onto the Force Control Room, quoting reference number 12200169915.

If you know anyone who deals drugs or you are concerned about someone who is taking drugs, please get in touch on 101. The police are here to help.
