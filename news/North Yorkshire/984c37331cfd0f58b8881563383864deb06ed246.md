North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary that occurred in Ingleton, North Yorkshire.

Overnight between Monday 28 August and Tuesday 29 August 2017, offenders forced entry into the Community Outdoor Swimming Pool at Ingleton, causing damage to the buildings and stealing cash once inside.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1389 Anne Jefferson. You can also email anne.jefferson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170154113.
