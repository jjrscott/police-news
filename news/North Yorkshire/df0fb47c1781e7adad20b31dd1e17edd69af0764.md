Police are appealing for witnesses after an 8 year old girl was assaulted by a cyclist outside her home in Carlton, near Selby.

It happened at around 4.30pm on Thursday 15 October in Mill Lane. The cyclist is said to have hit the child over the head.

The cyclist is described as a white man wearing black cycling lycra, a black helmet and bright orange glasses. The bike he was riding is also described as black.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who has any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, press option 2na d ask to speak to PC Janet McGrevy or emailjanet.mcGrevy@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contact anonymously on 0800 555 111.
