North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision which occurred outside the Blue Dolphin Holiday Park, Filey.

North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision which occurred outside the Blue Dolphin Holiday Park, Filey.

The incident occurred at around 5.45pm on Thursday, 27 September outside the holiday park on Bowes Lane. It involved a red Jaguar XF and a blue Volkswagen Passat estate which collided head-on.

Both front seat passengers were taken to hospital for treatment to serious injuries. One passenger remains in hospital.

Officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or the events surrounding it to contact them with information. Officers are also keen to speak with anyone who has dash-cam footage of either the event or which shows the manner of driving of either vehicle prior to the collision.

If you can assist officers with their investigation, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Traffic Constable PC Will Lane. You can also email will.lane@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12180181113 when providing information.
