North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a man in a car exposed himself to a woman on a country road in the Malton area.

It happened on Welham Road heading towards Leavening near to Watts Quarry, at around 11.40am on Tuesday 23 February 2016.

The man, who was in a small, bright red Nissan car, exposed himself to the woman after stopping to speak to her.

The driver of the vehicle was a white man in his 40s with short, swept back, jet black hair.

Anyone with information that could help to identify the man or anyone who has also have been targeted by him is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC David Meer or email _david.meer@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160032428
