North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a suspicious incident that occurred in South Milford on Easter Monday.

Police are appealing for witnesses on Westfield Lane, South Milford, in relation to suspicious circumstances between 7.15pm and 7.30pm hours on Monday 2 April 2018.

The incident involved a blue Citroen Cactus was parked near to a house being built on Westfield Lane, and three men wearing high-visibility outer clothing. The men came from the direction of School Lane and left in the same direction.
