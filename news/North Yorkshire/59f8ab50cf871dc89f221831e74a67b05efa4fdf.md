North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Hawsker, near Whitby.

It happened overnight between the 11th and 12th May 2015. Entry was forced to an outbuilding and a quadbike and strimmer were stolen from within.

The quadbike stolen is a Yamaha Big Bear 350cc in dark green. The strimmer is a Stihl FS55 in orange.

In particular, we are trying to trace the whereabouts of the stolen property and identify person/s responsible for the burglary.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Melanie Smith. You can also email melanie.smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150078107.
