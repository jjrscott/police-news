North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred at Thor's TIPI, which is in the ground of The Principal Hotel in York.

It happened in the early hours of the morning between 1.30 and 2am on Friday 2 August 2019 and involved thieves breaking into an outbuilding and committing a commercial burglary.

Police have released a CCTV image of three men they would like to speak to as they believe they will hold information which would assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises the men pictures, or who has information that could assist the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and quote reference number 12190140988. You can also email force.control@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190140988.
