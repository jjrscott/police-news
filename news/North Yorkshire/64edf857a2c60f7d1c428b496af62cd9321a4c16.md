Last modified: 30 June 2017 at 06:49pm

Officers have issued two CCTV images and are asking for the public’s help to identify a man and a woman following the theft of items from Exelby Services at Coneygarth near Leeming Bar.

On 11 June 2017, at around 6pm, a number of items including a toy and a phone charger were taken from the store without any attempt being made to pay for them.

Officers would like to speak the man and woman shown in the images in connection with the incident.

The two entered the shop with a two young children – a boy and a girl and left the services in a grey van which was towing a caravan.

Anyone who can help identify them is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Karen Miston, or email Karen.mitson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170102304 when passing on information.
