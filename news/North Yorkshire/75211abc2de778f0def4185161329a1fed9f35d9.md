North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about damage that occurred in Malton.

It happened at a business premises on Market Place, Richmond, between 5pm on Friday 25 September 2015 and 8am on Saturday 26 September. Unknown offenders have caused damage to an overhanging antique light.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone seen causing the damage.

Anyone with any information that can assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Louise Neville-Beck. You can also email Louise.Neville-Beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
