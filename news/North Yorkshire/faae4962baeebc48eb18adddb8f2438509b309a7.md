Police have released CCTV images of two men they want to speak to regarding a burglary at a commercial premises in Skipton.

At around 2.20am on Sunday 6 August 2017, the premises were broken into by suspects smashing the glass window of the front door of The Home Company, High Corn Mill, Chapel Hill, Skipton.

The suspects left the scene on foot in the direction of Skipton High Street empty-handed.

Police are appealing to anyone who has any information which could assist the investigation or anyone who recognises the men in the CCTV to contact them on 101, select option and speak to PC David Gasson. You can also email David.Gasson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170138720.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
