North Yorkshire Police would like to speak to the man pictured regarding the theft of a number of meat products from Sainsburys supermarket on Ramshill Road in Scarborough.

The man was seen to enter the shop at around 5.45pm on Saturday 18 March 2017. He is described as being about six feet tall, slim build with dark stubble on his face. He was wearing a grey beanie hat, black bomber jacket over another black jacket and dark blue chinos.

Anyone who recognises the man, or who has any information which could assist the investigation is asked to call 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to PC Liam Cromack. You can also email Liam.Cromack@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170046411.

You can also contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
