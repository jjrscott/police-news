North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage caused to a red Nissan Micra that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Greenstead Road between 8:00pm on Thursday 20th August and 5:00pm on Friday 21st August when damage was caused to the bonnet while it was parked on the street overnight.

The elderly victims of this incident are clearly upset by the actions of the person who caused this damage and we are appealing for the public's help to identify the person responsible.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC20 Mike Tinsley.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150148199.
