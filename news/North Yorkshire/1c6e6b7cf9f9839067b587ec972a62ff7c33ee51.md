Last modified: 20 August 2020 at 09:19am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an affray and criminal damage incident in York.

The incident occurred between 9pm on Tuesday 18 August and 1am on Wednesday 19 August 2020 on Micklegate in York city centre.

A 52 year old man was arrested on suspicion of affray and criminal damage and later released on conditional bail.

North Yorkshire Police is requesting the public's assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident. In particular, officers are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed or have footage of the incident, or who may have had property damaged during it.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1428 You can also email 1428@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200143871.
