North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a woman was subjected to comments of a sexual nature whilst walking in the city centre

The incident happened around 6pm on Monday 25 November 2019 on the footpath which runs alongside the River Ouse, close to Leeman Road and the Royal Mail Office in York. A woman was walking along the footpath when a man made comments of a sexual nature towards her as she walked past. The woman also saw the man again on the pedestrian ramp leading to Aldborough Way.

The man is described as medium build, wearing dark clothing and riding a dark-coloured bicycle. Police are appealing for anyone who was in the area at the time and might have seen the man or been subjected to similar comments.

Anyone with information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police by calling 101 and quoting reference number: 12190216811.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
