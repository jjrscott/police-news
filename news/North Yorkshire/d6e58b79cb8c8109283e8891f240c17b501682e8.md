Updated 8pm 15 February 2017 The search of Whitby leisure centre has been completed and nothing suspicious was found. The cordons have been lifted and it is safe to return. Our thanks go to people who were inconvenienced by the incident for their patience and understanding.

****************************************************************************

Whitby leisure centre has been evacuated as a precaution following a security alert reported at 5.15pm on 15 February 2017. Police are on scene and are carrying out a search of the premises with the help of specialist search dogs. We'll keep you updated.
