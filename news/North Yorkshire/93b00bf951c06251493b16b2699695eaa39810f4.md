Last modified: 9 May 2018 at 10:42am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a road rage incident on the southbound carriageway of the A1(M).

The incident involved two vehicles and occurred at around 3pm on Wednesday, 5 April near junction 50 (Ripon).

Both drivers left their vehicles in lane three for a period of time.

Police received reports of a disturbance taking place and officers are urging any witnesses to contact them with further information.

Anyone who can assist with the investigation is urged to call 101, selecting option 2 and asking for officer Akram. Information can also be provided by emailing raheel.akram@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to provide information anonymously the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12170057834 when providing information.
