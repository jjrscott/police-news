Police are appealing for witnesses after a robbery at a shop in Scarborough.

At about 10.35am today, Thursday 30 June 2016, police received a report that two men had entered the e-cigarette shop on Newborough, Scarborough, and threatened the manager with a knife. They forced him to empty the safe of a quantity of cash. One of the men then punched the manager before they fled with the safe and till takings.

One of the men is described as 6ft 3ins - 6ft 4ins, of slim build, wearing a black hooded top, a black bandana covering his face, and a baseball cap. The other man is described as 5ft 10ins - 5ft 11ins, of a stockier build, wearing similar clothing but possibly a purple bandana covering his face.

After they left the shop, it is believed they turned left down Newborough.

Anyone with any information about the incident is asked to contact DC Alex Parker or Scarborough Serious Crime Team by dialing 101, and selecting option 2. Alternatively, phone Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12160114871.
