North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Old Town, Scarborough.

It happened on Overton Terrace between 09:30am - 5:30pm on Sunday 24th May.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 219 Helen Crane. You can also email helen.crane@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150086078
