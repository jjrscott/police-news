North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempt house burglary that occurred in Sutton in Craven.

It happened on Crofters Mill, Sutton in Craven between 31 January and 4 February 2016 and involved an attempt to force the door handle on patio windows at the rear of a house in a quiet cul de sac, fortuantely no entry was gained on this occasion.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Susan Marklew. You can also email susan.marklew@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160020409
