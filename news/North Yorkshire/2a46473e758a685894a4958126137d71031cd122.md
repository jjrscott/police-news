Last modified: 2 December 2019 at 06:08pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a vehicle that was damaged in Forge Valley, Scarborough.

It happened on Seavegate, Forge Valley, Scarborough on Saturday 9 November 2019 at around 1.30pm. A member of the public was parked in one of the car parks off the main road when an off road motorcycle has wheel-spun covering the front of the car in mud and stones. This has caused damage to the front of the car including a cracked windscreen.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and quote incident number 12190207372.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190207372.
