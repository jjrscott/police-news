Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a serious assault in York.

At about 8.50pm today, Friday 3 February 2017, a man suffered a head injury in an assault in Landalewood Road, in the Rawcliffe area of York.

The victim was taken to York Hospital for treatment. The area has been cordoned off to allow for forensic enquiries.

Officers are urgently appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident, or has any information about it, to get in touch. In particular, they would like to speak to anyone who saw mopeds being ridden in the area around the time of the assault.

Please dial 101, select option 1 and quote incident number 486 of 3 February.
