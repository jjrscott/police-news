North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a inverter battery charger worth £3,000 from an unattended fishing vessel in Scarborough.

It happened on the North Pier harbour, Sand side, between 10/03/2018 - 11/03/2018.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul O'Neill. You can also email Paul.O'Neill@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-15032018-0344
