North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Cycle theft that occurred in Pickering.

It happened at a business premises on Westgate Carr in Pickering between 7am and 8pm on the 16 March 2016 and the cycle was stolen from a rack on the outside of the business premises.

The cycle was a Cannon Dale trail bike SL3 in black. It had hydraulic disc brakes and front suspension. The words Cannon Dale were in a dull white colour on the frame.

If anyone is offered a cycle for sale matching the description in the local area or has information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Louise Neville-Beck. You can also email Louise neville-beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160046871.
