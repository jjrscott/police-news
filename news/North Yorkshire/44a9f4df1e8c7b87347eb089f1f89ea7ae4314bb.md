North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following the theft of a number of bicycles in the Bootham area of York.

The thefts happened at around 8pm on Saturday 6 February 2021.

Officers attended the area and recovered a number of bicycles and we are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident and to identify any owners of the stolen bicycles.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation, or anyone who has had their bike stolen from the Bootham area of York, should contact North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, press 1 and speak to the Force Control Room, quoting reference number 12210054551.

A 16 year old boy from Malton was arrested in connection with the thefts and has been released under investigation, pending further enquiries.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
