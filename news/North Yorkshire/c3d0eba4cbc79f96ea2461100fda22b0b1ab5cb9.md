Detectives investigating a fatal stabbing have charged five men with the murder of 26 year-old Scarborough man Solomon Robinson (pictured).

Mr Robinson was found with serious injuries outside Scarborough Town Hall on St Nicholas Street at 2am on Sunday 20 October 2019 and taken to hospital where he sadly died.

North Yorkshire Police arrested ten people on suspicion of murder.

Two 18 year-olds, a 20 year-old, a 21 year-old and a 22 year-old have been remanded in police custody and are due to appear at York Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 23 October 2019 charged with murder.

A 16 year-old boy, two 20 year-old men, a 21 year-old man and a 28 year-old man have been released on conditional bail.
