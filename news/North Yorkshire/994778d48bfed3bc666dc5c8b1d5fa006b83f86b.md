On Monday 19 March 2018, your Neighbourhood Policing Team will be holding a Crime Prevention / Property Marking Event at Clifton Moor Church and Community Centre between 11am and 3pm.

PCSO's from the local policing team, will be on hand to offer crime prevention advice and promote property registration on the National Mobile Property Register IMMOBILISE.

They will also be offering FREE property marking on bikes, tools, electronic equipment and other items.

We would like to encourage residents to register their valuables on the National Mobile Property Register and we would also like to encourage residents and businesses to sign up to our Community Messenger. So Come along and PCSO's will be happy to assist you.

Address: Clifton Moor Church and Community Centre, Rivelin Way, Clifton Moor, York, YO30 4WD
