North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a criminal damage that occurred in Scarborough.

The incident happened between Seamer Road at Falsgrave and Crossgates roundabout between Thursday 18 and Sunday 21 October 2018 in which graffiti was spray painted on numerous building and structures along the route.

If anybody has any information which would assist the police identify the person/s responsible for this damage, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to Chris Hunter. You can also email chris.hunter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180200270
