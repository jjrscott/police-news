North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a serious assault that happened in Filey.

The assault happened on West Road, Filey, close to the junction with Ash Road at around 5.20pm on Saturday 26 November and involved a man being assaulted by another man suspected to be in possession of a knuckle duster.

The victim was struck with a blunt object and bitten by a dog during the incident but did not require hospital treatment for his injuries.

One man has since been arrested and is currently on police bail.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone that might have witnessed the incident to come forward.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Tom Eaglestone. You can also email Thomas.eaglestone@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160214011.
