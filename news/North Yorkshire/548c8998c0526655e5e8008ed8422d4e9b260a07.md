North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage that occurred in Selby.

The incident took place between 6pm on Sunday 28 May and 7am on Tuesday 30 May 2017 at the Persimmon building site on Staynor Hall.

It involved offenders smashing a rear window and the glass on the dashboard gauges of a forklift and pulling out wiring inside the driver’s cabin.

They also damaged the fencing and set off and emptied eight fire extinguishers into the drains causing a blockage.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public’s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Heidi McLoughlin. You can also email Heidi.McLoughlin@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170093356
