Last modified: 26 September 2016 at 12:29pm

North Yorkshire Police are appealing for information regarding a robbery and assault which took place in the early hours of Sunday 18 September 2016.

A 22-year-old local woman was driving along the A172 towards Stokesley at 1am on Sunday morning. As she approached a set of crossroads near Faceby, she noticed the vehicle behind her was flashing its headlights.

She pulled over and two hooded males got out of the following vehicle and approached her car. They proceeded to rob the woman, took money from her and physically assaulted her before leaving.

DC Neil Brodhurst said â€œWe are asking for anyone who may have been travelling along the A172 at the time, or who were in the area between midnight and 2am, who may have witnessed anything or noticed anything suspicious to contacts us."

"Thankfully incidents of this nature are very rare in North Yorkshire and I would like to reassure the local community that officers from the local safer neighbourhood team are continuing patrols in the area, to gather any further information about the incident and provide reassurance to local residents."

Anyone who could assist the investigation is asked to call 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to DC Neil Brodhurst. You can also email Neil.Brodhurst@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
