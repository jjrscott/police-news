Last modified: 9 September 2019 at 10:21am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary at a York city centre store at the weekend.

It happened at the size? shoe store on Feasegate at around 11.30pm on Friday 6 September 2019.

The thief or thieves broke a number of windows at the back of the shop before entering the store room and taking several pairs of ladies' Nike trainers, valued at about £1,000.

We are requesting the public’s help to establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Michael Parker. You can also email michael.parker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190166047.
