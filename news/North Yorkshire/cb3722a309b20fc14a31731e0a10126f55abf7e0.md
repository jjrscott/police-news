Police are appealing for information after a road traffic collision and assault near York.

It happened at about 4pm on 9 June 2017 and involved three vehicles - a grey Peugeot, a white Jaguar and a red VW.

It occurred on the eastbound A64 prior to the A19 Fulford interchange, York, in lane two.

The collision was a minor collision one between the three cars, but resulted in an assault by one of the drivers.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing any of the vehicles prior to the collision, and/or the assault, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Tony Barnes. You can also email tony.barnes@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170100596.
