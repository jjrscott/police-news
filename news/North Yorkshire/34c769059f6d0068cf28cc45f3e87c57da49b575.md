North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following the theft of a children's jacket that occurred in McDonalds, York.

It happened at around 4pm on Friday 19 May 2017 in McDonalds on Boroughbridge Road, York.

It involved a woman who was seen taking the jacket from a chair in the restaurant, placing it in her bag before leaving the premises.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Damion Liversidge. You can also email damion.liversidge@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170086223
