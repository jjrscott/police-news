Police have released an e-fit image of a man they would like to speak to regarding a robbery were a man was threatened and his iphone taken.

At approximately 11.15pm on Sunday 12 November 2017, the 43-year-old male victim from York was walking down Hamilton Drive in York with his iphone in his hand.

When he approached the entrance to the rugby club on Hamilton Drive, a man approached him and attempted to take the victims iphone and threatened him with violence. The suspect took the victims phone and made off in the direction of New Lane. The victim was left unharmed.

The suspect is described as a white male, 5ft 9in tall with a slim build and thought to be around 25-years-old. He has dark hair, which is shorter at the back and longer at the front with a centre parting.

He was wearing dark Adidas tracksuit bottoms, a dark coloured hooded top with the hood up and Adidas trainers.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured in the e-fit, or who has any information which would assist the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to DC Gary Blackburn. Alternatively you can email Gary.Blackburn@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Please quote reference 12170203909.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
