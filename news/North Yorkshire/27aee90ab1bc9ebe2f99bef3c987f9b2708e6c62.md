Last modified: 22 May 2018 at 02:53pm

Police are now in a position to confirm that the collision which occurred at junction 50 of the A1M near Baldersby at 7am this morning (Tuesday 22 May 2018 ) was a fatal collision.

The collision occurred between an articulated lorry and a car transporter, which were both travelling northbound. Sadly the driver of the articulated lorry died at the scene. Police are not yet in a position to release his identity.

Officers from the Major Collision Investigation Team are appealing for any witnesses who were travelling along that section of the road, who saw either vehicle prior to the collision, or anyone who may have captured dash cam footage to contact them.

Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to Hamish Halloway. You can also email Hamish.Halloway@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number NYP-22052018-0060.
