Last modified: 9 May 2018 at 10:46am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a fail to stop collision that occurred on Westwood in Scarborough on Friday 8 July 2016, at around 2.30pm.

A dark coloured vehicle was involved in a collision with an elderly female who was knocked to the floor and the driver of the vehicle did not stop.

In particular, we are appealing for a member of the public who stopped to assist the female to contact the police as they may be able to provide important information.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Vicky Mason. You can also email vicky.mason@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160124883.
