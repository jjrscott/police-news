Last modified: 22 January 2020 at 05:01pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a violent assault that occurred in Vodka Revolution Bar, Coney Street, York in the early hours of Monday 20 January.

At around 1.15am, a 19-year-old man was punched and kicked whilst on the floor, just outside the toilets inside the bar.

A local man aged 20 has been arrested in connection with this incident and has been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

It is believed there will have been a number of witnesses to this violent assault, which left the victim with substantial facial injuries requiring hospital treatment.

We are asking for anyone who saw this incident to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Stuart Hodge. You can also email 001596@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200010772.
