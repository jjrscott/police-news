North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred on Jute Road, York on 19 February 2019.

Offenders have taken items belonging to the victim including some high value watches and rings. The most identifiable being a Raymond Weil watch with 3 names engraved on the rear of the watch along with a personal message.
