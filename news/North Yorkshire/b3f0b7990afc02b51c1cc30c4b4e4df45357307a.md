Police are appealing for information and witnesses after a bag containing a large amount of cash was stolen in Harrogate.

Between 12.30pm and 12.45pm on Wednesday 14 December 2016, a purple Karrimor rucksack was taken from a premises in Cheltenham Crescent, Harrogate.

The bag contained a large amount of cash, as well as invoices, keys and a wallet.

Officers are carrying out enquiries, and are appealing for anyone who was in the area at the time, or has any information about the theft, to contact them.

Please call North Yorkshire Police by dialling 101, select option 1, and quote reference number 12160224839. You can also phone Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
