North Yorkshire Police investigating the shooting of a hen harrier on Threshfield Moor.

The incident occurred on Threshfield Moor near Grassington at approximately 10.45am on Monday 27 January 2020 where two members of the public witnessed an incident in which they believe a male hen harrier was shot.

North Yorkshire Police officers have been conducting enquiries and a man was arrested in connection with this incident, then later released under investigation

Anyone with information about this incident or who may have seen anything in the area shortly before the bird was shot please call North Yorkshire Police on 101 quoting reference number: 12200015792

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

This is the second incident of this type to take place in the last six months, with another hen harrier believed to have been shot in October 2019 near Keasden.
