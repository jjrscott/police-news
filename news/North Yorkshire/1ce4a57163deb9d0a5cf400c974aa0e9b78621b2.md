Detectives in Scarborough want to talk to Gareth David Keogh in connection with a robbery in the town.

The 33-year-old is from Castleford, West Yorkshire but has connections with Filey and Scarborough.

Police believe Mr Keogh _(pictured below)_ may currently be in one of several areas, including Scarborough, Castleford or Bridlington.

He is described as white with ginger hair and blue eyes and is around 6ft tall.

Anyone who recognises Mr Keogh or knows of his whereabouts is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and select option 1. Please quote reference number 12170104120.
