Last modified: 6 January 2020 at 06:31pm

North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about an assault which occurred at a pub on Northallerton High Street.

It happened at Mr Grey’s public house on Northallerton High Street in the early hours of Wednesday 18 December 2019 and involved a local man being struck on the back of the head with a pool cue.

The victim was taken by ambulance to Darlington Hospital where he received treatment for a serious head injury.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.In particular, we would like to speak to anyone who was in the pub at the time who may have witnessed the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to Detective Constable Mike Cope. You can also email michael.cope@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190230664

A man has been arrested in connection with this incident. He has since been released on bail pending further enquiries.
