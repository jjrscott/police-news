North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the attempt theft of a trailer that occurred at a farm near to Lunds, Sedbergh.

Some time between 6pm on Saturday 25 November and 11.30am on Sunday 26 November 2017 an attempt was made to steal a cattle trailer from a farm near to Lunds, Sedbergh. It would appear that the suspects have been disturbed, leaving the trailer at the side of the road.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for James Bowie. You can also email james.bowie@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170212210.
