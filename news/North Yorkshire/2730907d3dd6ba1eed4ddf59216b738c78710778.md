Heading out in the snow today? Here are five things you should always do before you set off to drive in snow or ice.

####

####

* * *

#### Make sure your windscreen is cleared inside and out.

* * *

#### Clear snow from the roof as it can slide onto the windscreen and block your vision or fall into the path of oncoming traffic.

* * *

#### Clean your lights, front and back, and check that they are working.

* * *

#### Check your fuel levels and make sure you have enough to keep you going in case of unexpected delays.

* * *

#### Lastly, allow extra time for your journey and where possible try to keep to major roads.
