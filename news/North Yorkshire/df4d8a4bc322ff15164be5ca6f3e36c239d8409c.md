Further to the message published about difficulties with the 101 non-emergency number, we are pleased to inform you the issues were rectified shortly after 8pm last night. We apologise for the temporary delay in getting through to us.

We are currently experiencing intermittent issues with the 101 non-emergency phone number. Please bear with us if you are delayed in getting through. Emergency calls to 999 are not affected, but we would ask people not to call 999 unless it is a genuine emergency. We hope to have the problem resolved as soon as possible
