North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about damage to a vehicle that occurred in Granville Street, Skipton.

It happened between 8.50am and 5.35pm on Monday June 19 2017 and involved a black VW Golf.

The car was 'keyed' at some time during this period.
