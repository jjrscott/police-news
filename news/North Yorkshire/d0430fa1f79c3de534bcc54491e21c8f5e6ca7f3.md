North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary at Braeburn School, Eastfield.

It happened at around 11.30pm on Monday 1 April 2019.

A double-glazed door was smashed using a paving slab and offenders entered the nursery of the school. The offenders made an untidy search of the nursery before making off prior to police arriving. A number of other windows at the school were also smashed.

Some of the nursery children's work was also damaged.
