Last modified: 13 June 2018 at 03:37pm

Multiple agencies are working together to assist residents and business owners in the City and surrounding areas.

Overnight 605 properties were visited leading to 92 people being evacuated and taken to the local rest centre at Archbishop Holgate School, Hull Road, York.

Military assistance is currently in place with 25 military personnel assisting with sandbagging in Cawood and 100 military personnel assisting with sandbagging and evacuation in York.

North Yorkshire Police are continuing to ask people to not travel unless absolutely necessary. If you do have to travel please plan your route and do not attempt to drive through flood water.

The latest flood warning information can be access here _https://flood-warning-information.service.gov.uk/map_

You can also follow @EnvAgencyYNE and #floodaware on Twitter for the latest updates. Or call Floodline on 0345 9881188.

Keep up to date with the weather forecast and weather warnings at _www.metoffice.gov.uk_ or tune in to your local BBC radio station.

Travel information can be found at _www.bbc.co.uk/travel_

Follow our advice about driving in wet conditions _http://www.northyorkshire.police.uk/winterdriving_

For local information about sandbags and other services, please contact your local council.
