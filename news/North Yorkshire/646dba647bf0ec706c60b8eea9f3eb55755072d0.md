North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary in Gate Helmsley.

Tools were taken from Langford Garage between 3.05am and 4.20pm on Wednesday 27 September 2017.

A set of ratchet and sockets in a red case were taken. Thieves also took an infra-red camera used for diagnosing electrical wiring faults on vehicles.
