Last modified: 9 May 2018 at 10:42am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred on the outskirts of Gilling West, Richmond.

Between the hours of 7.50am and 6.20pm on Thursday 20 July 2017, a property on the outskirts of Gilling West, Richmond was broken into. The suspects have entered the property by smashing a window and whilst inside the property they have stolen various items including jewellery and clothing.

Police area appealing to anyone who witnessed the incident, saw any suspicious activity in the area or has any information about the burglary to contact them.

Anyone with any information should call 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 433 James Bowie. You can also email james.bowie@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170127806.
