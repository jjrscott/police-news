Police have issued CCTV images of a man and woman they would like to trace following a house burglary in Burton Leonard, Ripon.

It happened at approx 3pm on Friday 9 February 2018 when a Tag Heuer watch of sentimental value was stolen from a property on Wigby Close.

Officers would like to speak to the man and woman pictured on CCTV as they may have important information that could assist the investigation.

If you recognise them please contact North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, select option 2 and ask for Lana Beardsley, Ripon Police or email lana.beardsley@northyorkshirepolice.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, you can call Crime stoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12180024154 when passing on any information.
