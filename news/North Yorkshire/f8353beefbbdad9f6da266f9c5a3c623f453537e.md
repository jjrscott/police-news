North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of lead that occurred in Skipton.

The lead was taken from the roof of the Cancer Research shop in Caroline Square at between 5.45pm Monday 12 October and 9.30am Tuesday 13 October 2015.

Police are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone seen acting suspiciously or suspicious vehicles around Devonshire Place.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1771 Andrew Hanks You can also email Andrew.Hanks@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150181303
