Emergency services are currently at the scene of serious collision at Northallerton involving a woman pedestrian and a lorry.

The incident was reported to the police at 12.02pm today (Friday 4 September 2020) and occurred on Brompton Road at the roundabout near Kwik Fit and Marks and Spencer.

Ambulance, fire and police are working at the scene and local diversions have been put in place.

Motorists and pedestrians are advised to avoid the area until further notice.

Witnesses to the collision are asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1, and speak to the Force Control Room. Please quote reference number NYP-04092020-0183.
