Last modified: 9 May 2017 at 03:16pm

Police have released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to regarding the theft of eight bottles of Persil detergent from the Coop Service Station at Crosshills.

The man was seen to enter the store on Monday 27 March 2017. Eight bottles of detergent were removed and the man made no attempt to pay for the items.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured or who has information which could assist the investigation is asked to contact PC Andrew Stoyles on 101 or email Andrew.Stoyles@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170051648.

Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.
