North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about two caravans who were broken into, in the Runswick Bay area.

The incident happened between the 10 to14 April 2016 at a caravan site in the Runswick Bay Area. Two caravans were entered and a number of items were stolen.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information and would appeal to anyone in the area who may of been offered any of the following items: fishing rods, a marine radio, a rescue radio, a rechargeable Dyson vacuum cleaner and a Garmin fish finder.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Louise Neville-Beck. You can also email Louise.neville-beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160065129
