North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a domestic assault that occurred on Foreshore Road in Scarborough at around 11pm on Monday 24th September 2018 which left a woman with facial injuries.

The assault in the vicinity of the shelters on Foreshore Road in Scarborough at approximately 11pm on Monday 24th September 2018 and involved a white man, 5'8″, aged mid 50's, medium build and a white woman, 5'4″, aged mid 30's.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to Scarborough Investigation Hub. You can also email InvestigationHubScarborough@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180178779.
