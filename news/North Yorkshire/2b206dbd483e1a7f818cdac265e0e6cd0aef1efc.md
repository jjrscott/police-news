Last modified: 30 August 2019 at 02:22pm

Members of the community in Skipton and the wider Craven district are invited to an event to help keep themselves and others safe.

North Yorkshire Police will be at the joint event next week with North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service and Age UK North Yorkshire and Darlington.

The ‘Safe and Well in Craven’ event will take place at the Swadford Centre in Swadford Street, Skipton. As well as police and fire officers, Age UK and NatWest staff will be there. Advice and information will be available on crime prevention and personal safety, fraud awareness and combatting social isolation. Free property marking will also be available, using the police's dot peen property marking machine.

It will run from 10am to 3pm on Saturday 7 September. Drop in at any time to meet the team.
