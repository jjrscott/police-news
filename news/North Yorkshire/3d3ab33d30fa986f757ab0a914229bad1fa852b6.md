﻿﻿Police have released CCTV footage of a woman they would like to speak to regarding the theft of approx £60 worth of meat.

__On Sunday 16 October 2016 at 4.25pm the woman was seen to enter the Tesco Express store on Low Street in Sherburn-in-Elmet. CCTV captures the woman taking approx £60 worth of steak and leave the store having made no attempt to pay.

Anyone who recognises the woman or who has any information which could assist the investigation is asked to contact PC 1216 Sally Smith on 101. You can also email sally.smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk, please quote reference 12160188642.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
