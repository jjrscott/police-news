Last modified: 6 July 2018 at 02:18pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened at Topps Chinese restaurant on Falsgrave Road, between midnight on 5/5/28 and 08:00 5/5/18 and involved persons attacking the lock of the front door to gain entry to the property.

Numerous items have been taken including a Louis Vuitton handbag.

In particular, we are appealing for information from any members of the public who may have been around that area and saw anything or anyone suspicious.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Michael Barker. You can also email Michael.Barker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180120549
