Last modified: 2 July 2020 at 12:23pm

Police are appealing for dashcam footage of a collision between a car and scooter in Harrogate.

The incident involved a green Lambretta which was part of a group of around 40 riders.

The scooter and a silver BMW collided on Leeds Road on Thursday 25 June 2020 at around 6.50pm.

The rider of the scooter sustained serious but not life-threatening injuries.

Police are investigating the incident and are appealing for anyone with dashcam footage to email Dean.Barrett@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote incident number 12200107719 when sharing information.
