Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:47am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after wing mirror was smashed off a black Land Rover Discovery that was parked on North Road in Ripon.

It happened at around 11pm on Friday 11 August 2017 when the vehicle was left near to the entrance of the old auction market.

In particular, we are appealing for any passers-by between 10.30pm and 11pm, either on foot or in vehicles, to help us identify those responsible.

There was a large group of youths believed to be walking in the direction of the town centre at around the time of the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Noel Edwards. You can also email Noel.Edwards@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170142326
