North Yorkshire Police is appealing for recent burglary victims to come forward if they recognise any of these recovered items (pictured).

The items include a number of watches, items of jewellery, a pair of GHD hair straighteners and ‘Festival of Britain’ crown coin

They were handed to police after a member of the public found them inside an old pillowcase in Aberford Woods last week.

Officers in North Yorkshire are appealing for any recent burglary victims to come forward if they believe any of the items could be theirs.

If you recognise any of the items pictured, or if you have any information that could help with the investigation, please email Craig.Davies@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Please quote reference number 12200162755 when providing any information.
