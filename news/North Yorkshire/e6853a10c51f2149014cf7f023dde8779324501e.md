North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incidence of vehicle intereference that occurred in High Bentham.

On two occasions in November a vehicle parked on KING STREET, HIGH BENTHAM has had some of the wheel nuts loosened/removed. This potentially could have led to very serious consequences for the driver and passengers.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Pc 830 Barbara Parsons. You can also email barbara.parsons@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160203779.
