North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a garage burglary that occurred in Ripon.

It happened on Russell Dixon Square, Ripon overnight between Thursday 25 January 2018 and Friday 26 January 2018 where two motorbikes and two quad were stolen.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Sarah Hargreaves. You can also email sarah.hargreaves1995@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180014766
