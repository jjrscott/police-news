It happened at the junction of Duncombe Place and St Leonard's Place, York at approximately 6.55pm on Wednesday 18 April 2018 and involved three men and a woman. Three men have been arrested and bailed pending further enquiries.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
