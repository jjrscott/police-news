North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident that occurred on the park ground behind Carnot Street in York

It happened at around 6.45pm on Tuesday 13 August 2019 and involved an altercation between a young female and three young males.

No one was injured during the incident.

Police attended and the female suspect was located nearby and arrested. She has since been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Ellison or email david.ellison0041@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or online at crimestoppers-uk.org.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190149066.
