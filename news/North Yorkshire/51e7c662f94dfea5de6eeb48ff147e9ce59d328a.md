North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred at Martin Cheeseman Court in Acomb, York.

Thieves broke into a flat at Martin Cheeseman Court between 6pm and 9.15pm on Tuesday 10 September 2019 and stole an amount of money from the property.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident and are appealing for any witnesses who may have information which would be helpful to the investigation, to contact police. Officers would be particularly interested to hear from anyone in the area who has CCTV footage which may of captured the thieves as they made their way in and out of the property.

Anyone with information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and speak to the Force Control Room. You can also email Laura.Webster@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
