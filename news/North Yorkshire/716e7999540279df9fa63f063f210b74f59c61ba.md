Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:09am

Police have issued CCTV of two people they would like to talk to after handbag thefts in a Harrogate nightclub.

Some time between 12.15am and 2.15am on Sunday 16 October, unattended handbags were targeted by thieves in the Viper Rooms nightclub in Parliament Street.

All personal items were removed from one handbag, including a blue Smythsons purse, a long black stole, an iPhone in a green case, various cards and a photograph with sentimental value. Another handbag, a dark brown Louis Vuitton bag with a leather strap and a chequered square pattern, was also taken.

Police would like to speak to a man and a woman pictured on CCTV in the nightclub, as they may have important information that could assist the investigation.

If you recognise either person, please contact police. Dial 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Brendon Frith, or email brendon.frith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12160188427 when passing information.
