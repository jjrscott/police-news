Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:17am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision that occurred on A172 at Stokesley.

It happened at 6.50pm on 25 November 2016 and involved a blue Vauxhall Vectra and a blue Ford focus. The Vectra was later reported stolen from the Stokesley area.

Minor injuries were caused to the driver and passenger of the Ford Focus.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicles prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the Vauxhall Vectra, and if anyone recalls seeing the vehicle in the local area on the day of the collision.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, quoting North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160213836.
