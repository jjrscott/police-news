Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision on the A64 near Murton, York, involving a HGV tipper and a yellow Ford Transit pick-up.

The crash happened on the west-bound carriageway at around 1pm on Wednesday 10 June 2015. Both vehicles were travelling in the same direction and came to rest approximately 15 feet down a grass verge following the crash. Both vehicles sustained extensive damage as result of the impact.

The driver of the Ford Transit suffered minor head injuries and was treated at York District Hospital.

Police are appealing to anyone who witnessed the collision or saw either of the vehicles prior to the crash, to contact them.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Traffic Constable Mark Patterson. Or email mark.patterson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12150096830.

The road was closed until 6pm to allow the vehicles to be recovered.
