North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information about a theft from a shop in Scarborough.

Officers want to speak to a man pictured by CCTV after a man an a woman entered a department store and stole several items.

It happened at Boyes at 1.15pm on 26 May.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180084819.
