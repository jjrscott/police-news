North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a moped that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Alma Parade between 9pm on 28 August and 8.30am on 29 August 2017 and involved the theft of a blue and silver Pulse BT 49 moped, registration number YX12 GDK.

We are appealing for information about the theft and the current whereabouts of the moped.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Twyford. You can also email David.twyford251@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170154176.
