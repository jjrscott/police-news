North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about two burglaries that occurred in Whitby.

Both Burglaries occurred overnight Monday 23rd February into Tuesday 24th February 2015.

Overnight, offenders have gained entry to Beavers furniture store on Stakesby Vale and stolen an amount of cash from within.

Also, at around 03:50hrs on Tuesday offenders have gained entry to the post office on Upgang Lane by breaking the font window. A quantity of cigarettes were stolen.

Offenders have been described as three males dressed in dark clothing and carrying a large bag.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident. In particular if anyone has been offered or bought "Cheap" cigarettes.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Neale Graham at Whitby CID. You can also email neale.graham@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
