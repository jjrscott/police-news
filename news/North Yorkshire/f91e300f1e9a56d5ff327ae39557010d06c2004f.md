Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:14am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a serious road traffic collision that occurred on the A168 near Marton Cum Grafton.

It happened at 7.40pm on Wednesday 1 August 2018 involving a cyclist and a red car on the A168 at the junction of Limebar Lane, Maton Cum Crafton.

The cyclist was taken to Harrogate Hospital by ambulance and discharged after spending two days in hospital. The patient is recovering at home having suffered broken bones and ribs.

We are appealing for witnesses/dash cam footage of the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

In particular, we are appealing for information about a red-coloured vehicle travelling on the A168 towards Boroughbridge prior or after the collision at around 7.40pm on Wednesday 1 August 2018.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1498 Galbraith. You can also email Jonathan.Galbraith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180143537.
