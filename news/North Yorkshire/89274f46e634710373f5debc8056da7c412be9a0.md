North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a vehicle was damaged in Selby.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about damage caused to a front window and scratching to the driver's side of a black BMW while parked on Millgate, Selby overnight on 13/08/15 into the morning of 14/08/15.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Lee SMITH. You can also email Lee.smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150141787.
