Last modified: 30 December 2016 at 04:00pm

Police are appealing for information to help identify the man in the CCTV images. Officers want to speak to the man in connection with an incident which happened on Christmas Eve, Saturday 24 December 2016.

The incident happened at 11.45pm on Victoria Road outside the Scarborough Police Station, when a 60 year old man from Scarborough was physically assaulted and sustained a serious facial injury.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who was in the area at the time is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police.

In particular, officers would like to speak to a man and a woman, who were seen talking to the man in the CCTV images around the time of the incident.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC1394 Jane Brewster. You can also email Jane.Brewster@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160232332.
