North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Malton

It happened at Goody's Hair and Beauty Salon on Castlegate, Malton between 10.30pm on 30 December 2016 and 8.10am on 31 December 2016. A number of hair products and styling tools were stolen.
