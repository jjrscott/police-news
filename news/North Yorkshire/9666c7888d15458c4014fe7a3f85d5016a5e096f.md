Police are appealing for information after a man with a knife attempted to rob a convenience store near Skipton.

The hooded man entered the Spar shop in Cross Hills carrying a kitchen knife and a plastic bag.

He told a member of staff to fill the bag with money, but he ended up leaving empty-handed. Fortunately, nobody was injured.

The incident was reported at around 7.55pm last night (Wednesday, 16 March 2016).

Anyone with information is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting crime reference 12160046299.

You can also contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
