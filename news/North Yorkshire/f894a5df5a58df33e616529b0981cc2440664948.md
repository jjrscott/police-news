North Yorkshire Police are investigating following a report that a 13 year old girl was approached by two men in a white van on her way to school

The incident happened at around 8.15am today, Friday 30 September 2016, on Halfpenny Lane in Knaresborough.

Officers are conducting enquiries to establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

If anyone saw anything suspicious in the area please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Please quote reference number NYP-30092016-0112 when passing on any information.
