North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a number of reports that items were stolen from tents on 27 July 2019 at Crow's Nest Caravan Park.

It happened in the early hours of Saturday 27 July 2019 when empty tents were entered and a number of items stolen including phones, beer and shoes.

Police are appealing for anyone who was staying at Crow's Nest at this time and was either a victim or has information regarding the incidents.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Rushworth. You can also email Jacob.rushworth@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190136773
