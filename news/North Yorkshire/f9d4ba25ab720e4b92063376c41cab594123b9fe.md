Last modified: 8 March 2018 at 04:30pm

Police in Scarborough are appealing for witnesses and mobile phone footage following a fight in Eastfield on Wednesday afternoon.

Officers were called to the fight involving a group of girls on Link Walk, Eastfield between 3.10pm and 3.30pm on Wednesday 7 March 2018.

Police are appealing to anyone who witnessed the incident and has any mobile phone footage to contact them, rather than share it on social media.

Anyone who can assist the investigation on asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Scarborough Investigation Hub.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12180039433 when passing on information.
