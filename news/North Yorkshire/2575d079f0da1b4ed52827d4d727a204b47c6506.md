North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from a vehicle that occurred in the Lady Edith's Avenue area of Scarborough.

Edit this information to suit your appeal

General crime appeal

It happened at around 5am on 12 September 2017 and involved two men tampering with a grey BMW 730 and stealing items.

We are appealing for information regarding any sightings of two men acting suspiciously in the area around the time of the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Peter Beard. You can also email peter.beard@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-12092017-0035
