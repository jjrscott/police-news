Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident which took place at Kuda nightclub in York, which has led to a man sustaining serious injuries to his leg.

The incident took place in the early hours of Thursday 7 April 2016 at around 2.20am. The victim, a 26-year-old local man, was dancing on the dance floor of the club when it's believed he was struck. He then sustained an assault which broke his leg in two places. He has since had to undergo surgery to pin his leg back together.

Police are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed the incident or anyone who has any information which could assist the investigation to contact Detective Constable 1377 Cherry of the York Serious Crime Team on 101. Please quote reference 12160059759. Alternatively you can email adam.cherry@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

You can also contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
