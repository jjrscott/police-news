Officers are appealing for information after a tyre on a police van was damaged in Pickering.

The incident happened on Smiddy Hill on Friday 15 December when officers were undertaking a drugs operation targeting drug dealing in the local pubs. The van, a white Vauxhall Vivaro, was parked at 10pm and officers returned to the van to find a tyre slashed at 11pm.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who has information is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Gemma Thwaites.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170223979 when passing on any information.
