A man has appeared at court following a 24 minute police pursuit in the York and Selby areas.

John Leonard Frame, aged 40, of Victoria Road, Scarborough, was arrested at 1.45am on Monday 11 June 2018 after a blue Ford Galaxy people carrier was forcibly stopped by a tyre-bursting stinger device and a police vehicle.

The pursuit began at the Fulford interchange in York at 1.21am and ended on Hillam Common Lane in Selby.

A police helicopter assisted the operation.

Following questioning in custody, Frame was charged and appeared at York Magistrates’ Court in relation to:

 * taking a vehicle without the owner’s consent from Scarborough
 * dangerous driving on the A64, A19, B1222 and A63
 * driving while disqualified
 * driving without insurance

The case has been committed to York Crown Court on 3 July.

Frame was remanded in custody.
