North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about vehicle tyres that have been targeted in Hellifield.

****

It happened on 9 August 2015, at 11.30pm on Midland terrace in Hellifield. A white man wearing a bright yellow polo shirt was seen running away from the vehicles.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC David Ridler.

You can also email David.Ridler@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150138094
