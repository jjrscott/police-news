Police are appealing for witnesses and information following an assault at Kennedy’s Bar in York.

The incident happened at around half past midnight on Saturday 14 October, when a 41 year old man was assaulted, resulting in a fractured eye socket.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or who was in Kennedy’s on Little Stonegate at the time and might have some information, is asked to contact police.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC 41 Dave Ellison or email david.ellison0041@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170185133 when passing on any information.
