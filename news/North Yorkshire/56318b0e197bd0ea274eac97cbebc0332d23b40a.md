North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic collision that occurred at the A19 Easingwold roundabout at the southern end of the by pass.

At around 6.50pm on Saturday 28 January 2017 a road traffic collision occurred on the A19 Easingwold by pass at the southern end.

The collision involved a black Jaguar and a white Suzuki Vitara and a red saloon car. The Jaguar and red saloon vehicle were travelling south bound and the Vitara was travelling north. The collision occurred on the roundabout. The driver and passenger of the Vitara received minor injuries and were seen by ambulance at the scene.

Police are appealing for witnesses to the collision or if anyone saw any of the vehicles prior to the collision to contact Tc149 Garbutt on 101 quoting reference 12170015866. Alternatively you can email Chris.Garbutt@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
