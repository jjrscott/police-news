North Yorkshire Police is appealing for members of the public to come forward after gin was stolen from Morrisons supermarket in Eastfield.

The incident happened at around 12.50pm on Friday 9 December 2016 when a man stole a box of gin and left without paying, leaving the area in a Vauxhall Vectra.

Police are appealing for witnesses, in particular they want to speak to members of the public who saw the vehicle leave Morrisons and passed the registration number to Morrisons staff.

Two men in their forties later arrested in Scarborough Town Centre and have been bailed pending further enquiries.

If you are the people who assisted the staff, or have any other information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask forRob Henderson. You can also email Robert.Henderson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160221802.
