North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a motorbike that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened overnight 23rd-24th April on Russet Groove, Scarborough. The motorbike stolen was a red, white and green Aprilia RS450 motorbike. It had a broken chain.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help locate the bike and the offenders.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 219 Helen Crane. You can also email helen.crane@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150067267
