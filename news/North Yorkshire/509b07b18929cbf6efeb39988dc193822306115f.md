Last modified: 22 December 2018 at 03:28am

Officers are appealing for information, witnesses and dash-cam footage following a serious road traffic collision on Pot Bank, Beckwithshaw, Harrogate.

The incident occurred around 9.35am on Friday December 21 and involved a grey Lamborghini and a white Kia. The driver of the Lamborghini, a man in his 30s from West Yorkshire received serious injuries and is currently in hospital in Leeds.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who was driving in this area who may have seen the vehicles prior to the collision as this could assist with their investigation.

Anyone who can assist with the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police and ask for TC1470 Brett or email Gemma.Brett@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or Ts 1170 Halloway or email Hamish.halloway@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk quoting reference 12180236660.
