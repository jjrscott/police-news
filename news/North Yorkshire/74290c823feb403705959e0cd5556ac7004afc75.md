Last modified: 13 February 2017 at 01:37pm

Police in the Selby area are appealing for witnesses following a single vehicle collision in which a woman in her forties was injured.

It happened on the A63 between Cliffe and Hemingborough at around 4.25pm on Sunday 12 February 2017.

It involved a brown Vauxhall Meriva that left the road and came to rest on its side.

The woman driver of the car was rescued from the vehicle and taken to hospital with serious but not life-threatening injuries and has since left hospital.

Police are appealing to anyone who saw the car driving along the A63 in the minutes leading up to the collision or who witnessed the actual collision to contact them.

Anyone with any information is asked to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Dean Turner of Tadcaster Roads Policing Group. Or email dean.turner@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12170024759 when passing on information.

The road was closed and reopened at around 7.15pm.
