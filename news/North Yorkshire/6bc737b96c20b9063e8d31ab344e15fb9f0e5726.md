North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from motor vehicle that occurred in Cayton Village, Scarborough.

It happened on Ivy House Close junction with Harford Road, Cayton between 5pm and 7pm on Sunday 3 April 2016. Offender/s have gained entry to a parked and unattended van and stolen numerous tools from within.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the incident itself in addition to persons selling Matika tools and specialist plumbing tools including a Anton gas analyser and Rothenberger freeze machine.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Georgina Hanson. You can also email Georgina.Hanson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160057637.
