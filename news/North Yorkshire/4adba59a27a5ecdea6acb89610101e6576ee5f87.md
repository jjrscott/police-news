North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Whitby.

It happened at The Chocolate Box, Golden Lion Bank, Whitby around 4:30am on Saturday 29th August 2015. The glass panel in the shop door was smashed and chocolate and sweets were stolen from inside.

A 24 year old woman form Whitby was arrested in connection with the incident and has been released on police bail.

We are continuing enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
