Police want to talk to two men pictured by CCTV after a sat nav and dash cam were taken from a shop in York.

The incident took place at around 1.50pm on Friday, 10 November at Maplin Electronics.

The Garmin sat nav and the Nextbase dash camera were both taken from displays on the shop floor.

Officers have released CCTV images of two men they wish to speak to in connection with their investigations.

Anyone who recognises the men or has any other information that could help police is urged to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Emily Clark. You can also email PC Clark on Emily.Clark@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote incident number 12170202816 when sharing information.
