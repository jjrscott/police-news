North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a stolen motor vehicle that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Westbourne Park overnight between 24 and 25 August and involved the theft of a white Mercedes Sprinter van with high visibility hatch markings on the rear doors. The vehicle registration is KM11AEF.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any sightings of the vehicle between 11pm on 24 August 2015 and 9am on 25 August 2015.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Zoe Coutts. You can also email Zoe.Coutts@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150150652.

1 September 2015.
