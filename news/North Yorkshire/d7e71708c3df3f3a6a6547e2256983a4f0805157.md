Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic collision that occurred on Malton Road, near Elmfield Avenue in York, on Saturday 3 June 2017.

The collision occurred at 4pm on Saturday 3rd June 2017 and involved a blue BMW 130d, driven by a 34-year-old woman from York and a silver bicycle, ridden by a 56-year-old man from York.

The 56-year-old man was taken to York District Hospital. However, very sadly, eight days later on Sunday 11 June 2017, he died at his home address.

Officers are appealing for witnesses to contact them, so they can establish the full circumstances around the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, or who recalls seeing the cyclist or BMW prior to the collision, is asked to contact Police as soon as possible, to assist the investigation.

Please dial 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to TC 1470 Gemma Wood. You can also email Gemma.Wood@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170096518.
