Edit this information to suit your appeal North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information regarding a violent incident that occurred in Pickering on Smiddy Hill , between midnight and 02:00hrs of Sunday 5th April 2015.

It happened on Smiddy Hill , Pickering between midnight and 0200hrs on Sunday 5th April 2015 and involved a large group of unidentified adults .

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the identity of persons involved.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC931 Walmsley. You can also email Jeremy.walmsley@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150054967 . ***************************************
