Police are appealing for witnesses to a fatal collision which occurred on the A64 just Scarborough side of Scampston Bridge, Rillington.

The collision occurred at 2.10pm on Sunday 7 February and involved five vehicles; a silver Citroen Berlingo, a black Mondeo, a grey Mini Cooper, a black Mazda and a blue Peugeot 206.

Sadly the driver of the Citroen Berlingo, an 87-year-old local man was pronounced dead at the scene.

A 63-year-old woman sustained minor injuries, she was taken to Scarborough District Hospital for treatment and discharged the same evening.

The other three drivers were uninjured.

Police are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the collision or seen the vehicles prior to the incident, to contact them on 101 and select option 1 and speak to the Force Control Room.

Alternatively you can email _Dave.Seavers@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

Please quote reference 12160022554.
