North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Burglary that occurred in Whitby.

Overnight Thursday 13 into Friday 14 August 2015, offender(s) have approached the "Unique" watch shop, situated at the top end of Flowergate and smashed the shop front window. Offender(s) have then removed a quantity of watches from within and made off.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who has been offered one of these watches.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Neale Graham at the Invesigation Hub. You can also email Neale.Graham@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150141389.
