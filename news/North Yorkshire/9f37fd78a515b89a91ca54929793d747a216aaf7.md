Officers are appealing for information following a single vehicle collision between Skipton and Carleton which occurred during the early hours of Saturday, 12 January.

Officers are appealing for information following a single vehicle collision between Skipton and Carleton which occurred during the early hours of Saturday, 12 January.

The collision occurred between Skipton and Carleton, near to Carleton Bridge, at around 2am and involved a black Seat Ibiza. The driver and sole occupant of the vehicle, an 18-year-old man, was pronounced dead at the scene.

Officers are appealing to anyone who saw the vehicle prior to the incident to contact them.

Anyone with information is asked to contact the Major Collision Investigation Team on 01609 643185 or by emailing Laura.Cleary@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or Kirsten.Aldridge@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote reference 12190006765 when providing information.
