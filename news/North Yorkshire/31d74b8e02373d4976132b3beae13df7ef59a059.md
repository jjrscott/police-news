North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about car that was damaged in Whitby.

It happened at Railway Cottages, off Windsor Terrace, Whitby, between 10am and 11.45am on 30 April 2015 and involved damage being caused to a white vehicle.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Whitby police.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150070883.
