North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a garage burglary that occurred on Northstead area of Scarborough.

It happened overnight 06/07/15-07/07/15 on Newlands Avenue and involved three mountain bikes being stolen from a garage.

The cycles are described as mens Apollo Slant mountain bike in black with white forks, a womens Trek 3700 mountain bike in blue and a mens Giant Boulder 510 mountain bike in dark blue.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Georgina Hanson. You can also email Force.Control@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150114848.
