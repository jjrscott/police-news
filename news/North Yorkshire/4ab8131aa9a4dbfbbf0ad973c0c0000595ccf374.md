North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in York.

The incident happened on Low Ousegate between 2.30pm and 3pm on Saturday 4 November 20017. It involved the victim, a female cyclist aged in her late 40s, and an unknown male suspect.

In particular, we are appealing for information to help identify the man. He is described as a white, aged between 20-30 years of age, approximately 5ft 9in tall with short spiked hair. He was wearing a grey jumper.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 1, and speak to the Force Control Room. You can also email 000673@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-05112017-0246 when providing details about this incident.
