Police are appealing for witnesses after a man was robbed in York city centre.

The robbery occurred on Duncombe Place, by the postbox next to the taxi rank, on Saturday 14 November, just before 10pm.

The victim, a 25-year-old man, was approached by a man, punched and made to hand over a quantity of cash and a Rolex watch. He was not seriously injured.

The area would have been busy with members of the public, and police are appealing for anyone who was in the area at the time and witnessed the robbery, or has any information about it, to contact them.

Please call 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Louise Harness or the York Serious Crime Team, or emaillouise.harness@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12150202605 when passing information.
