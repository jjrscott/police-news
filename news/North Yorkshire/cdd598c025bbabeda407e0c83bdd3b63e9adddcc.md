North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a collision on the A6055 near Ferensby.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a collision on the A6055 near Ferensby.

The incident occurred between 11pm and midnight on Sunday, 10 March around a quarter of a mile north of Ferensby and involved a black Renault car.

Officers are appealing to anyone who either witnessed the collision or the events surrounding it to contact them with information. They are also asking anyone who stopped to assist the driver following the incident, whether or not they witnessed the collision, to contact them.

Anyone with information which could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Julie Brown. You can also email Julie.brown@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Information can be passed anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12190043997 when providing information.
