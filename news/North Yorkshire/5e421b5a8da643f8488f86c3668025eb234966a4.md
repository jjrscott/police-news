Police would like to speak to the man pictured after a woman was punched and spat at in a bar in York.

View image

The incident took place in Revolution on Coney Street in the city centre. A man thumped the victim and spat in her face before leaving the bar.

Police want to identify the man pictured by CCTV and would also like to hear from anyone else who witnessed the incident, which took place shortly before 1am on Sunday, 15 May 2016.

If you have any information, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option two and ask for Rachel Shaw. Please quote reference number 12160084496.
