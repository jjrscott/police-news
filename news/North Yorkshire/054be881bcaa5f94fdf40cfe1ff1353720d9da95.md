North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred at Whitby Town Football Club between 6.10pm on 20 March 2016 and 10.15am on 21 March 2016.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC33 Cope. You can also email force.control.northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160049228
