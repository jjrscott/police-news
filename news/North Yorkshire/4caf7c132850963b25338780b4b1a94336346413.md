North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about damage to a window that occurred on Millgate, Selby.

It happened on Millgate, Selby between 10:00 pm on Saturday 19 August 2017 and 6:30 am on Sunday 20 August 2017 and involved a bathroom window being smashed a property on the street.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who has any information is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to Nicola Knowles.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170148055 when passing on any information.
