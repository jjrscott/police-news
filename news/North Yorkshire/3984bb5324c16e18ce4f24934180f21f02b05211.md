Last modified: 16 December 2019 at 01:42pm

Police are warning residents to be extra vigilant following a series of burglaries at churches and village halls in the north of York.

Between 13 and 14 December, North Yorkshire Police received reports of burglaries at a number of churches and public buildings in the area.

The premises targeted were:

Incident number 12190228352 – St Margaret Clitheroe, Holly Tree Lane, York – extensive damage caused

Incident number 12190228361 – St Mary's Church and village hall, The Village, Haxby, York – extensive damage caused

Incident number 12190228434 – Church of Holy Evangelists

Incident number 12190228435 – St Mark's Grove, York – cash taken and damage caused

Incident number 12190228595 – Village Hall, 31 North Lane, Haxby, York

Incident number 12190228275 – Lakeside School, Oakdale Road, York – damage caused to three doors

One suspect has been described as extremely tall.

Anyone with information about any of these crimes is asked to call North Yorkshire Police on 101 and quote the relevant incident number.

Please report any suspicious behaviour immediately.
