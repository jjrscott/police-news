North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred at a commercial premises at the rear of Kings Road, Harrogate.

At around 12.30am Friday 6 December 2019, thieves broke into the property and have stolen an amount of sound equipment.

CCTV captured images of four young men in dark clothing and hooded jackets at the rear of the premises.

Police are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone or any vehicles which were seen to be acting suspiciously in the area. Officers would particularly like to hear from anyone who has any information which would assist them to determine the identities of the four young males that seem near the premises.

Please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to PC Jackie Thornborrow. You can also email Jackie.thornborrow@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190222750.
