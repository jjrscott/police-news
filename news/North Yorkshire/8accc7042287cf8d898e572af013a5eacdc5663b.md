North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Nelson Street at approximately half past midnight Saturday 24 June, 2017 and involved a fight between a number of men and women.

A 28 year old man and a 42 year old woman, both from Scarborough, were arrested in connection with the incident. They have both been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
