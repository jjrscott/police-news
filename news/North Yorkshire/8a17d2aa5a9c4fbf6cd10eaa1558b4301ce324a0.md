Two men have been arrested on suspicion of supplying class A drugs in the Craven district of North Yorkshire.

At about 2pm on Thursday 16 April 2020, officers from North Yorkshire Police's Operational Support Unit - who were on patrol near Skipton - noticed a black Volkswagen Golf on Main Street, Cross Hills.

A short time later, the car was parked in a supermarket car park nearby. Officers spoke to the driver and passenger, and searched them. They found both had with them suspected Class A drugs, and one also had a large amount of cash.

Both were arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class A drugs, and were taken into custody.

The men, aged 35 and 31, have both been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
