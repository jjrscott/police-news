Last modified: 13 April 2018 at 03:54pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision on Westfield Way, Norton.

The collision happened within close proximity to the Karro Foods site at around 6.15am on Saturday 31 March 2018 when a Volkswagen Bora collided with a stationary HGV.

The 20 year-old passenger in the VW Bora suffered serious injuries. He remains in hospital in a stable condition.

Police investigating the collision are appealing for information and witnesses.

Anyone who witnessed this incident or anyone who may have dash-cam footage of the incident are encouraged to contact the Major Collision Investigation Team.

Please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for the Major Collision Investigation Team. Or email MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12180054503 when passing on information.
