North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a house burglary that occurred in Richmond, North Yorkshire.

It happened at Theakston Lane between 9.45pm and 11pm on Wednesday 8 November 2017. The house was broken into and property was stolen.

As part of the investigation, officers are appealing for information about any vehicles or persons in the area of Theakston Lane during this time. We are also asking motorists who travelled on this road to check dash cams for any suspicious behaviour.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Antony Kent. You can also email antony.kent@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170201158
