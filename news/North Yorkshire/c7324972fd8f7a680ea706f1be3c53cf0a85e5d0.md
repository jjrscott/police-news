North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary in Gargrave.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary in Gargrave.

The incident occurred at a property on the High Street between Thursday, 27 September and Saturday, 6 October. Offenders gained entry to the property through rear patio doors by using a garden fork to pry them open.

Anyone who witnessed anything suspicious in this area during this time frame is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Michael Smith (352). You can also email Michael.Smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, then information can be passed to the independent charity Crimestoppes on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12180186944.
