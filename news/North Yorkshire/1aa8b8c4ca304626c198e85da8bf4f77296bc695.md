North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses after a car was damaged in Harrogate.

****It happened on Strawberry Dale, Harrogate, somtime between 1.15pm and 2.20pm on 18 February 2015.

The victim's Audi A4 was "keyed" while it was parked opposite Blueline taxis.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Tom Lister. You can also email Thomas.Lister1001@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150027297.
