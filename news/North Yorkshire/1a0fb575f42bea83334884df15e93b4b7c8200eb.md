North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened at the Alma Inn on Alma Parade at 2.30am on 9 September 2015 when the suspect broke into the inn and removed charity boxes for the RNLI and Cancer Research.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the break-in or has any information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul GIBSON-HODGES. You can also email Paul.Gibson-Hodges@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150158752 .
