Last modified: 1 March 2021 at 03:43pm

Police have issued CCTV of two men they would like to speak to following the theft of an Audi A3 in Sutton in Craven and the subsequent collision before the vehicle was abandoned on Burnside.

It happened on North Street, Sutton In Craven between 4pm and 8.20pm on the 24 November 2020. At around 8.20pm the vehicle was in collision with two unattended vehicles on Carleton Street Skipton.

Officers are asking members of the public to get in touch if they recognise the people in the images as they believe they will have information that will help the investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Andrew Ingram or email Andrew.Ingram@northyorkshire.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12200208645 when passing on information.
