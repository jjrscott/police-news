North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following shed burglary on Jervis Road, York.

It happened overnight between 6pm, 22 Ocotober and 8am, 23 October.

High Value fishing tackle including a Map 14 meter carbon fibre rod, carp rods, a fishing tent and other equipment was taken.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, You can also email karen.britton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170189940
