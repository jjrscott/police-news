Last modified: 3 June 2020 at 10:00am

An image of a pedal cycle stolen from a property in York has been released in the hope someone will know the whereabouts of the stolen bike.

The bike was stolen from the rear yard of a property on Sutherland Street on Monday, 20 April and is described as a metallic blue Raleigh Ridgeback Metro and has a 21 inch frame.

Anyone who has seen a pedal cycle like the one in the image or who knows someone who has a bike like this and believes it could have been stolen please contact North Yorkshire Police.

Anyone who could assist with the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for 1390 Kennedy. You can also email Philip.Kennedy@northyorkshire.police.uk

If you wish to provide information anonymously then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12200066304
