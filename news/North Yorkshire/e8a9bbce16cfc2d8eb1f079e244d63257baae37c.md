North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a house burglary that occurred in Bradley.

It happened at Silsden Road, Bradley near Skipton at approx 1.00am on Sunday 7 February 2016 and involved entry being gained to a house whilst occupants in bed, suspects have removed personal items and also the keys to a high value vehicle which has been recovered shortly after in the Bradford area.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about a small light coloured van (car derived type possibly) seen in the area at the time

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Susan Marklew. You can also email susan.marklew@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160022194.
