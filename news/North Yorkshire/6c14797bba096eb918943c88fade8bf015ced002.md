Police are appealing for information after a man was assaulted in a Selby nightclub.

A man was punched several times by another man, who then ran off.

Officers believe the attack - which took place at around 11.20 or 11.30pm in Main Street Nightclub on Saturday 9 September - was unprovoked.

The suspect is described as white, around 5ft 8ins and a muscular build. He is in his late 20s or early 30s with short dark hair and was wearing a bright pink polo shirt at the time.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Lucy Hammond.

You can also email PC Hammond at Lucy.Hammond@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote incident number 12170162775 when passing on information.
