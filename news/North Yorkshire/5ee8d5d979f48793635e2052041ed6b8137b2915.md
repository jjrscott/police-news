Around 3pm on Thursday 24 December in the Coop store on Market Place, Bedale, a man entered the shop and took a number of bottles of alcohol then left without paying.

North Yorkshire Police has released CCTV images of a man officers would like to speak to as we believe he may have information that could help this investigation.

If you can identify the man pictured or have any other information which may assist the investigation, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC 1255 Jack DODSWORTH or email 001255@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12200244559 when passing on information.
