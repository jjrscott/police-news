Last modified: 3 June 2019 at 04:50pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Skipton.

It happened outside The Fleece Inn on Keighley Road between 3am and 3:30am on Bank Holiday Monday 27 May.

A man was knocked unconscious and received injuries to his head. The attacker then flagged a taxi and made his escape.

In particular, we are appealing for any information about this attacker and the taxi he fled the scene in.

Anyone with information is asked to call 101, select option 2, and ask for Sohail Tariq. You can also email Sohail.Tariq@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190095119.

The injured man was taken by ambulance to hospital where he was treated and discharged following treatment.
