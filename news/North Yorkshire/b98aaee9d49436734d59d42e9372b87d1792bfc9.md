Police have issued CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with a theft from a shop on St Sampson Square, York.

At around 1.00pm on Saturday 19 November 2016, two men entered Richer Sounds on St Sampson Square. One of the men left the store with a NovaFidelity hi-fi system, making no attempt to pay for it.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured in the CCTV still as he may have important information that could assist the investigation.
