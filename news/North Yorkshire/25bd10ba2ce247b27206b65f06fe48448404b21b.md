North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a house burglary that occurred in Farnhill near Skipton.

It happened at Main Street, Farnhill at approx 0215hrs on 080116 and involved suspects breaking into a large detached house by forcing a ground floor window, gaining access and making a search of the property. They also entered a secure outbuilding where tools were removed from but these were left at the scene

In particular, we are appealing for information about any persons or vehicles seen in the area acting suspiciously around the relevant time

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Susan Marklew. You can also email susan.marklew@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160004039.
