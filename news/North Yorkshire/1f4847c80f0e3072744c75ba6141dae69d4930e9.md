North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred on the A629 roundabout, near Kildwick.

It happened at Kildwick roundabout in traffic queing towards Skipton at approximately 5.30pm on Friday 28 August 2015 and involved a female driver of a black BMW cabriolet and a male passenger of a blue Fiat Panda.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the male seen leaving a static blue Fiat Panda at the roundabout approaching the female's black BMW vehicle which was static directly behind and assaulting the female.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Mark Browne. You can also email mark.browne@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150151532.
