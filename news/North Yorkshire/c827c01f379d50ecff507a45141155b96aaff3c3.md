North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempted robbery that occurred in York.

It happened at around 8pm on 17 April 2019 when the victim was walking from Bede Avenue onto Crichton Avenue and noticed a male on a red bike coming towards her on the footpath.

As the male got closer to her he took hold of her handbag, which was being carried across her body, and demanded that she gave him it causing an injury to her neck.

The suspect is described as a white male, around 21/22 years old, scruffy looking, with mousey brown hair and approximately 5ft 7 inches tall of medium build. He was wearing a dark grey tracksuit and was riding a red mountain bike.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Emily Clark. You can also email Emily.clark@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190069441
