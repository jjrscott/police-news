Police are appealing for witnesses after a dog was injured in a collision in Selby.

The incident occurred at around 10am - 10.20am on Friday 11 September, in Leeds Road, Selby.

The dog was hit by a mint-green coloured car, being driven by an elderly man, which did not stop following the collision.

The dog suffered bruising and was taken to a vet, and will require a further check-up.

Anyone who saw the collision, or has any information that could assist this investigation, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Please dial 101, selection option 2 and pass information to the Safer Neighbourhood Service Desk, quoting reference number NYP-11092015-0174.
