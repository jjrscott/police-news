This is an update following the sudden death of a man in his 60s who was found in Piccadilly, York in the early hours of Wednesday 22 August 2018.

Police believe that there are no suspicious circumstances surrounding the manâ€™s death and are not looking for anyone else in connection with his death. Officers will prepare a report for the coroner.

The examination of the scene is now complete and the area has now reopened.

Officers are in the process of contacting the man's next of kin.

To recap, police were called by the ambulance service at around 4.30am on the morning of Wednesday 22 August 2018 after members of the public came across an injured man outside Tesco in Piccadilly, York.

Despite the efforts of members of the public and medical staff at the scene, the man was sadly pronounced dead at the scene a short time later.
