Officers are appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a white Renault van that occurred in Petch Garth, Kirkbymoorside.

The incident happened between 8pm and 11pm on Thursday 31 August 2017.

The vehicle was stolen from outside the owner's property and we are appealing for information about anybody seen in the area acting suspicious at the time of the offence.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul O'Neil.l You can also email Paul.O'Neill@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-31082017-0588.
