North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred in Ripon.

We are appealing for witnesses for a commercial burglary that took place in the early hours of Friday 25 November 2016 between 01.10am and 01.14am at Simply Beds situated on Dallamires Lane in Ripon. A substantial amount of cash was stolen from an onsite safe along with a petty cash box.
Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andrew Tiffany. You can also email andrew.tiffany@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160213175
