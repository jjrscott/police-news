Police are asking for the public's help to identify the man in this CCTV image.

It follows an incident in Huntington Spar store on 8 November 2019 when bottles, cans and tins were thrown at members of staff before an amount of alcohol was stolen.

Officers would like to speak to the man has he could have information that would assist the investigation.

Anyone who can help identify him is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and pass information for incident 12190206409. Or email nikki.jackson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

if you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
