Following the below appeal, a man has been identified and charged in connection with the incident. Thank you to all those who provided us with information.

**Original release: CCTV appeal after bus window smashed**

Police in York have issued a CCTV image of a man they want to speak to after a bus window was smashed.

At around 5pm on 20 December 2017 a York First Bus with passengers on board was travelling over Ouse Bridge when a passing cyclist punched a window and smashed it. No-one on board was injured.

Officers are appealing to any members of the public who can help identify the man shown in the CCTV image to contact them.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Emily Clark or email Emily.Clark@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800555111.

Please quote reference number 12170226468 when passing on information.
