Last modified: 11 March 2021 at 06:45pm

Following the incident on the A1M this morning, the road re-opened at around 9.30am.

A minor road traffic collision occurred shortly after 7.30am, and officers are appealing for anyone who saw the incident involving a lorry and a van, to get in touch.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to Roads Policing Officer Matthew Harvey or email Matthew.Harvey@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number NYP-11032021-0051 when passing on information.

**Original message posted at 8.30am 11/03/21**

A1M currently closed between J42 and J43

The A1M is currently closed between J42 and J43 to allow police and other emergency services to deal with an ongoing incident.

Motorists are advised to avoid the area and find alternative routes.

NYP-11032021-0051.
