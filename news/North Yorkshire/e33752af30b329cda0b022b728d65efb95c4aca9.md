North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses to an altercation that occurred between two men in York.

The incident happened at 8am on Monday 13 March 2017 on Bishopthorpe Road, York.

The male driver of a large black vehicle has got out of his vehicle near the shops on Bishopthorpe Road and has confronted and attacked another man walking on the pavement. The driver got back into his vehicle and left in the direction of Scarcroft Road.

The driver of the large black vehicle is described as a white man aged in his mid 40's.
