North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault on a shopkeeper on Skipton High Street.

It happened at approximately 4.40pm on Monday 15 May, outside the Just Jeans shop on Skipton High Street. A shopkeeper was assaulted in what appears at this stage to be an unprovoked attack. The shop windows were also smashed during the incident.

A 37-year-old man has been arrested and released while still under investigation.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for any witnesses of the incident to come forward with any information to help our enquiries.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Ben Brittain. You can also email PC Brittain on ben.brittain266@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170083607.
