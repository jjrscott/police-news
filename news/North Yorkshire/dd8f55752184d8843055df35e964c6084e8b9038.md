Police investigating the theft of meat from a shop in Scarborough have issued a CCTV image of a woman they would like to speak to.

On Monday 24 July the woman entered Iceland, Vernon Road, Scarborough and put nine steaks in her bag before leaving the store without making an attempt to pay for them.

Police have issued CCTV stills of a woman they would like to speak to in connection with the theft, as she may have important information that could assist the investigation.

If you recognise the woman pictured on the CCTV, please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for Michael Barker or email Michael.Barker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170130456.
