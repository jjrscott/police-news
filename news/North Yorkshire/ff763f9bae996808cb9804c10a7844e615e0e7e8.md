North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about damage to vehicles that occurred in Eastfield.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a damage to the wing mirrors of parked vehicles that occurred on High Garth and Dale Edge, Eastfield.

It happened at Dale Edge and High Garth between 10pm and 10.30pm on 7 July 2015. It is believed there were around eight people involved in the incidents.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the identification of the offenders.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 598 LAVERICK. You can also email david.laverick@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150115407 when passing on information about the incident.
