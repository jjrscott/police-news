Last modified: 28 January 2020 at 01:09pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a minor collision outside Morrisons Supermarket, Dunslow Road in Eastfield.

It happened at 2.25pm on Tuesday 21 January 2020 and involved a blue Vauxhall Corsa and a pedestrian.

The Vauxhall Corsa is believed to have collided with the pedestrian before leaving the scene. The pedestrian has suffered bruising and attended Scarborough Hospital but has since been discharged.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

In particular, we are appealing for information from members of the public who have witnessed the incident and obtained the registration number of the vehicle.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Ben Stanyon. You can also email Ben.Stanyon857@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200014239.
