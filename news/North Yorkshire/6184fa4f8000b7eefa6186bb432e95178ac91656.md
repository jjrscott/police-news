Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision on Wentedge Road near Kirk Smeaton.

The incident happened at around 6.20pm on Thursday 13 April 2017 and involved a Red Mercedes A180 and a Blue Vauxhall Corsa.

Eight people were injured in the collision, three women aged 36, 19 and 18, three girls aged 10, 8 and 5 and two boys aged 5. All of the casualties were taken to hospital, some with minor injuries, others with more serious, but not believed to be life threatening, injuries.

The road remained closed for around 4 hours whilst collision investigators examined the scene.

Police are appealing for any witnesses to the collision or anyone who saw either of the cars prior to prior to it, to get in touch.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to Traffic Sgt 1268 Paul Cording or Traffic Constable 1113 Dean Turner.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170063426 when passing on any information.
