North Yorkshire Police is seeking to identify the owner of an electric bike which has been recovered in York.

In the early hours of Saturday 24 June 2017, officers in York seized an electric bike which they believe to be stolen. The bike was recovered on Lindsey Avenue and is believed to have been taken from the Bouthwaite Drive area. Police are seeking to identify the owner of the bike.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 292 Alastair Foy. You can also email alastair.foy@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170110274.
