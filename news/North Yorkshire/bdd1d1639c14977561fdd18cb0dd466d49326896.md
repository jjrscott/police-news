Police are appealing for information to identify the driver of a vehicle after they failed to stop following a damage only collision with a grey Skoda FABIA.

At 6.50pm on Friday 3 February 2017 the grey Skoda Fabia was travelling east on the A1237. When the driver was stationary at the roundabout with the B1363 Wigginton Road, near Roko Gym, a vehicle has driven into the back of the Skoda causing damage to the vehicle and then failed to stop to exchange details.

Police are appealing to any drivers who were on this stretch of the A1237 at the time who may have witnessed the collision or who may have any details regarding the other vehicle to contact PC 1707 Allan Fenney on 101 or email Alan.Fenney@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170019581.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111
