North Yorkshire Police need your help to locate the owner of a recently found electric cycle.

The Neighbourhood Policing Team for Acomb have recently located an abandoned electric cycle and are appealing for the public's help to locate the owner of this cycle.

The electric cycle is a Viking MT Tobin - black framed with green and grey detailing.

If you think this is your cycle please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1815 Nicola Manning. You can also email nicola.manning@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Proof of ownership will be required.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180122892.
