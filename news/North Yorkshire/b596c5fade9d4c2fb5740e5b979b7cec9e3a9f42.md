Last modified: 22 November 2018 at 03:59pm

North Yorkshire Police has been made aware of some planned protest activity by an organisation known as Extinction Rebellion, which will affect York City Centre from 11am on Friday 23 November 2018.

It is understood that the activity may affect a major road in the city and one of the bridges across the River Ouse.

Residents, businesses, visitors and transport providers are advised that the activity may cause some traffic disruption in central York.

North Yorkshire Police will continue to work alongside the City of York Council and other community partners to ensure any disruption is minimised.

To keep up to date with the police operation, people are advised to visit the North Yorkshire Police website and social media accounts - facebook.com/NorthYorkshirePolice and @NYorkPolice
