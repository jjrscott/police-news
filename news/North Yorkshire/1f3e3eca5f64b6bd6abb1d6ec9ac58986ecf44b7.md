Last modified: 15 March 2021 at 03:12pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a garage burglary.

The incident took place on Calf Close in Haxby between 10.30pm on Sunday 21 February and 7.30am on Monday 22 February 2021 when the locks were broken on a residential garage and a Vespa motorbike was stolen, together with a Dewalt drill and electric screwdriver with charger.

Police are appealing for anyone who may have information about the burglary or who may have seen any of the items for sale.

If you have information that could assist the investigation, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Adam Shire. You can also email adam.shire@northyorkshire.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12210064376
