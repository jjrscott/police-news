North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from unattended motor vehicle that occurred in Whitby.

It happened at Fairmead Court, Whitby between 16:30 hours on Thursday 5th February 2015 and 09:50 hours on Friday 6th February 2015 and involved entry being gained to a parked Talbot motor home.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any persons seen in the vicinity of Fairmead Court or Larpool Lane area in suspicious circumstances or if seen carrying electrical equipment.

We are also appealing for information about any persons offered any of the following items for sale or know of their whereabouts:

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
