North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after damage was caused to a vehicle in Scarborough.

The offence occurred at around 9.30pm on Sunday, 26 March after a man slashed all four tyres and smashed the windscreen of a removals vans on Gladstone Road.

The offender is described as a white man, of average build and height. He was wearing a dark hooded top, possibly leather, with the hood pulled uo.

Anyone with information on who is responsible for this damage is asked to contact officers on 101, selecting option 2 and asking for Lauren Green or Adam Varney. You can also email lauren.green262@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Information can be passed anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
