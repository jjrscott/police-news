North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following an incident that occurred in York city centre

It happened at 1pm on Saturday 2 June 2018, near to the Hopgrove flyover on Stockton Lane, when a man in a grey Ford Mondeo stopped in live traffic and got out of his vehicle to confront the people in a vehicle behind him.

The driver of the grey Ford Mondeo shouted abuse at the woman in the other vehicle and her 14 year-old daughter for driving slower than 60mph after pulling out of junction.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
