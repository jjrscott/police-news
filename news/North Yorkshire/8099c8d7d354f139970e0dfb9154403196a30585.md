Last modified: 30 March 2021 at 03:49pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Burglary that occurred near Alma Square, Scarborough.

The incident happened sometime between Friday the 13 of March and Monday the 15 of March 2021.

A number of Milwaukee hand held power tools valuing approximately £2500 were taken from a property which was being refurbished.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident. In particular, we are appealing for information about the location of these tools and any potential sightings of suspects.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Alex Warren. You can also email alex.warren@northyorkshire.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12210077827
