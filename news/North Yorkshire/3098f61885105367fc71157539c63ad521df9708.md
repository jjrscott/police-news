Police are appealing for any witnesses to or information regarding an assault on a 19-year-old man from Skipton which occurred on Monday 27 February 2017.

At around 9pm, the 19-year-old man was walking down a snicket way on Sharpaw Avenue, near Tesco in Skipton when he was approached by a youth who then punched him repeatedly in the face.

The offender then made off in the direction of Great Wood School. He was described as approx 17-years-old, around 5'10" and of slim build. He was wearing grey jogging bottoms, a grey jumper and a dark coloured body warmer with a hood.

The 19-year-old victim was left with cuts and bruises to his face.

Anyone who was in the area at the time who may of witnessed anything which could assist the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and ask to speak to the Force Control Room. Please quote reference 12170033963.
