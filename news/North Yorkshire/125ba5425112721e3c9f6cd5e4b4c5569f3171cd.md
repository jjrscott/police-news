North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a purse that occurred in Skipton.

It happened within Morrisons Supermarket, Broughton Road, Skipton between 09.40 - 10.40am on Wednesday 9 November 2016 and involved a suspect dipping into the handbag of a member of public and stealing a purse containing an amount of cash.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public’s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are keen to speak to a male who was in Morrisons at the relevant time. He is described as 5'5″ in height, aged late 30's to early 40's, average build with short, light brown coloured hair, wearing a grey anorak.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Gasson. You can also email david.gasson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160203289.
