North Yorkshire Police can confirm that the death of 25-year-old Claire Lucas, whose body was found at a property on Fourth Avenue on Monday 2 July 2018, is no longer being treated as murder.

The 35-year old man who was arrested on suspicion of murder, has been released from police bail.

The police investigation is ongoing to determine the full circumstances around Claire’s death.

Detective Chief Inspector Mark Pearson of North Yorkshire Police said:

“I understand that the local community have been deeply affected by Claire’s death. I’d like to reassure them that this is a continuing investigation and we are committed to our task of determining the full circumstances around Claire’s death."
