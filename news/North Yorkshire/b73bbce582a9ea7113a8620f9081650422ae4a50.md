Last modified: 14 December 2018 at 10:19am

Police are appealing for information after a 98-year-old woman pedestrian was injured in a collision involving a BMW X5 in York.

It happened at approximately 10.40am on Thursday 13 December 2018 on East Parade at its junction with Harcourt Street.

Officers are appealing to anyone who was in the area and witnessed the collision to contact them.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Traffic Constable Richard Harrison or email Richard.harrison@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12180231665 when passing on information.

The woman sustained head, chest and abdominal injuries and was taken to York District Hospital for treatment.
