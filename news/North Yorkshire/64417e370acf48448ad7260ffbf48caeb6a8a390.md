North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary in Skipton.

It happened at Coach Street carpark overnight on29/30 January and involved the coffee house in the carpark having its rear fire exit smashed and entry gained where an amount of cash was removed with a laptop computer.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any witneses that may have seen anyone acting in a suspicious manner in the area of the coffee shop on the night in question.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Andrew Middlemiss. You can also email _andrew.middlemiss@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160017237.
