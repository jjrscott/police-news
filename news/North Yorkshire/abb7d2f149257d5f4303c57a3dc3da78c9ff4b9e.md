Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a number of burglaries and attempted thefts in Farnhill, near Cross Hills, overnight between 9 November and 10 November 2015.

All of the incidents occurred between 10pm on 9 November and 5.45am on 10 November in Grange Road, Farnhill.

A house was burgled and a garage at a different property was also burgled. Nothing was taken from either property. An insecure vehicle was searched at a separate address and there was an attempted theft of a trailer from a fourth targeted property.

Anyone with any information or who saw anything suspicious in the area is asked to contact the police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Andrew Middlemiss or emailAndrew.middlemiss@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
