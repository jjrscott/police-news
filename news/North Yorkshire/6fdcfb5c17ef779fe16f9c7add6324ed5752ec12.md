Officers want to speak to the man in connection with the theft, which happened between 4.15pm and 5.15pm on 25 March 2017. A number of changing room lockers were broken into at the hotel and a quantity of cash and personal valuables were stolen.

Anyone who recognises the man in the CCTV images, or who has any information, is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Rachel Shaw.

Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170053990 when passing on any information.
