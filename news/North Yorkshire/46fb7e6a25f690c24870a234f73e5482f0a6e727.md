Police want to trace two men after outdoors clothing was stolen from a shop in Whitby.

Two men entered Hall and Coates on Church Street and one of them asked to try on a pair of boots.

While the shop assistant was in the store room, a second man took a number of "Sealskinz" items, including gloves, hats and socks, valued at £200 to £300 and left without paying.

The other man left the shop shortly afterwards.

It happened at around 12.10pm on Saturday (16 September).

Police would like to speak to two men pictured by CCTV in connection with their investigations.

If you recognise the men, have any information about the incident, or have been offered the described items for sale, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Marie Williams.

You can also email PC Williams on Marie.Williams1212@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote incident number 12170166285 when passing on information.
