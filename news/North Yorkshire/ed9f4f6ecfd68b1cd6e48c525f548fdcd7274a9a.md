North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a woman was hit by a vehicle on South Parade

The incident happened around 4.20pm on Monday 25 November 2019 when a 65 year-old woman was crossing the road from the direction of MW Darwin & Sons on South Parade, Northallerton, towards Shine Hair and Beauty on Thirsk Road.

The woman was struck on her right side by a vehicle, causing her to fall onto the ground and the vehicle is then believed to have left the scene towards East Road. The woman sustained minor injuries and attended the Friarage Hospital for treatment.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information, anyone who may have witnessed the assault or any CCTV from local premises to help us establish the full circumstances of this incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police by calling 101. If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190216912.
