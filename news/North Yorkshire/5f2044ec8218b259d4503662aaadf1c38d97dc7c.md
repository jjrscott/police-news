Police have released CCTV images of four men they would like to speak to, in relation to the theft of a number of items from Mainsgill Farm Shop in East Layton.

The four men were seen to enter the shop at around 1pm on Sunday 25 March 2018. Three of the men were seen to leave the store shortly after in a silver Mercedes-Benz whilst one left on foot, heading towards the A66.

A number of items totalling £500 had been taken, including three coats valued at £360 and three steaks worth £60.

Anyone who recognises any of the men, or who has any information which could assist the investigation, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to Michael McVay. Please quote reference 12180051731.

You can also email Michael.McVay556@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800555111.
