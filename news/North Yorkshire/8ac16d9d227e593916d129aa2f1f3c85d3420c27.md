Police are appealing for information following a collision on Hall Lane Harrogate.

The incident happened at 10.30am on Friday 9 October 2015 and involved a cyclist and two vehicles.

The cyclist, a local 24yr old woman, was riding her black and green pedal cycle towards Bilton Lane. A medium sized red/orange hatchback car was travelling in the same direction and a white Ford Transit type vehicle was coming in the opposite direction.

The red/orange hatchback car collided with the cyclist and failed to stop. The cyclist received slight injuries to her left foot and ankle.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who has any information that could assist the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, press option 2 and ask to speak to TC Rob Roberts or email rob.roberts@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk .

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150180141 when passing on information about the incident.

Monday 12 October 2015.
