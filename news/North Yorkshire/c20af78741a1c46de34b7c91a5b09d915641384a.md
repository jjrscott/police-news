At around 5.05pm on Monday 16 May 2016, an Asda delivery driver parked his van on Westbourne Grove.

He was at the rear of his van when he was attacked by two men and was knocked to the ground. He suffered swelling to his lip as a result of the incident.

The men then jumped into his van and stole his wallet before leaving the scene.

The men are described as white, both over 6 ft tall, of medium build and aged in their late 20s.

One man was wearing a grey hooded top, dark jeans and dark trainers. The other was wearing a dark coloured hoodie, grey jogging bottoms and dark shoes.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information that could help the police investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for DC Steve Williams at Scarborough Investigation Hub.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160085443 when passing on information.
