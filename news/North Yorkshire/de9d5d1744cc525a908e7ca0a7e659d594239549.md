North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in a nightclub in York.

It happened at the Kuda Nightclub on Clifford Street at around 2.45am on 27 December. A man suffered an injury to his mouth after an altercation near to the main bar where it is alleged he was headbutted by another man.

The club was very busy at the time, with around 400 people in the venue.

The attacker is described as a white male with ginger hair who made off immediately after the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Gareth Evans. You can also email gareth.evans@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150229284
