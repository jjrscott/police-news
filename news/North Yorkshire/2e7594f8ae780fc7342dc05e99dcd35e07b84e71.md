Police are searching for two men in connection with a burglary at a Bedale shop. Officers were called to reports of a break-in at a shop in North End at around 6am on Friday.

A member of the public found a necklace on the street near a neighbouring shop and reported it to police.

Officers attended the scene and found that thieves had forced their way in and taken items of jewellery and watches.

They believe two men were involved and investigations are currently ongoing.

North Yorkshire Police is urging anyone with information to get in touch on 101, ask for Peter Henderson and quote incident number 12160173290.
