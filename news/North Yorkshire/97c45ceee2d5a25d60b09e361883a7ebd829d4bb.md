Police are appealing for information after Â£75 worth of make-up was stolen from a shop in Richmond.

At around 9.15am on Friday 27 February 2015, a man entered Boots, in Market Place, and put seven bottles of Bourjois foundation in his bag before leaving the store without paying.

Officers have released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the incident and are urging anyone who can help identify him to come forward.

Anyone who can help officers with their enquiries should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for PC Alex Newsome or Richmond police.

If you prefer not to give your name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150032947 when passing information about this incident.
