Police are appealing to anyone who can help to identify a man with a distinctive leg tattoo shown in this CCTV image.

Officers want to speak to him after a woman's mobile phone was taken from the self-service till area at Lidl's Seamer Road store in Scarborough.

It happened at around 2.30pm on 4 November when the victim left her purple iPhone 11 with a clear case at the tills.

Anyone with any information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1, and quote reference number: 12200194208.

Alternatively, please email laura.robinson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk with any information.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
