Police in York have issued CCTV footage in a bid to identify a man they want to speak to in connection with a burglary in Poppleton.

Officers have carried out extensive enquiries to identify the man, including liaison with neighbouring police forces, but he remains unidentified.

The burglary happened on 11 October 2017 when a large amount of jewellery was stolen from the house.

Anyone who can help the police identify the man is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for York Investigation Hub.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12170182759 when passing on information.
