Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:24am

Police are investigating the theft of high-value jewelry following a burglary near Selby.

Two distinctive Breitling watches (similar to the attached pictures) were taken from the address in Monk Fryston.

Thieves smashed their way through a door and also stole other items of jewellery as well as a black Arai motorcycle helmet.

The incident took place between 9am and 3pm on Monday, 11 July 2016.

Anyone with information relating to the event or items taken is urged to call DC Pete Wilson on 01904 669808 and quote incident number 12160122789. You can also contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
