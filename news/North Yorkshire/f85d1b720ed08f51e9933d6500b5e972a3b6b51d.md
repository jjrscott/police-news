North Yorkshire Police is appealing for dash-cam footage after a white Audi A3 crashed near Dalton on Tees on the afternoon of Wednesday 1 August 2018.

The vehicle was travelling along the A167 from Northallerton towards Darlington between 3.30pm and 4pm when it overtook another vehicle before leaving the road and coming to rest in a hedge.

Police are appealing to anyone who was travelling along the road from Northallerton to Darlington and saw the manner of driving of the Audi or has any dash-cam footage of the vehicle to come forward.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Northallerton Serious Crime Team.

Please quote reference 12180141414 when passing on information.

The 18-year-old male driver of the vehicle left the scene of the collision and was later arrested.
