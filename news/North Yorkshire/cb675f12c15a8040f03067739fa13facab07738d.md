North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a fire that occurred in Norton.

It happened on Langton Road between the evening of Friday 13 January and the morning of Monday 16 January 2017. The incident involved the suspects setting fire to one of the rooms at the old Brooklyn Centre, which is currently under development.
