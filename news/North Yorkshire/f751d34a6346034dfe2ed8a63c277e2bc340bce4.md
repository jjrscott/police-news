Tributes have been paid to a man who died in a fatal collision near Richmond.

Gordon Hall, 58, from Spennymoor, died in a collision involving his black Harley Davidson motorcycle and a red Citroen C3. It happened on Saturday 23 March 2019 at around 2.15pm on the B6271, near Kiplin Hall between Great Langton and Scorton. An inquest was opened and adjourned last week.

Gordon's family have described him as "a loving husband of Sandra and devoted father of Charlotte".

His wife, Sandra Hall, said: "Gordon was a fun, loving and generous husband. I could not have asked for any more. We had so many exciting adventures together and I will treasure the memories forever."

His daughter Charlotte said: "My Dad was so kind, supportive and loving. He was the best Dad that I could have ever wished for and he will always inspire me in everything I do."

Police are continuing to appeal for information about the collision. Anyone who witnessed it, or has dash-cam footage of the vehicles involved prior to it, should phone 101 or email TC David Minto on david.minto@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12190052366 when passing information.
