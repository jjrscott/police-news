Last modified: 7 October 2020 at 01:04pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information in connection with a spate of incidents in the village of Middleton Tyas over the last year, which are all believed to be connected.

Several vehicles have been damaged, a grave attacked and graffiti with offensive words has been spread around the village including the local school gates, appearing 31/08/2020 and again on 06/10/2020.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Sharon Findlay. You can also email Sharon.Findlay@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200175748.
