North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Harrogate.

It happened at Auto Discounts on Regent Parade between 17:30 hours on 19th April and 07:00 hours on 20th April and involved the front window of the store being smashed and a push bike stolen from the window display. The push bike is described as a Raleigh Helion 2.0 Mens mountain bike in blue, with front suspension.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about this incident and whether any member of the public has seen anyone acting suspiciously in that area. We are also interested to know if anyone has seen a push bike like the one stolen for sale since the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC COOKE 663. You can also email Eleanor.cooke@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
