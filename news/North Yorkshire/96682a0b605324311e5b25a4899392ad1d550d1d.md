North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred at a restaurant in Scarborough town centre.

It is believed that thieves broke into the premises on Queen Street at approximately 1.30am on Saturday 4 January 2020. On gaining entry to the property by kicking in the front door, an untidy search was made and the till was stolen.
