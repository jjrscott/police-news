Detectives are investigating a violent incident in Selby which left a man and woman in hospital.

It was reported to North Yorkshire Police at 5.16am today (Saturday 24 September 2016) by Yorkshire Ambulance Service.

Following enquiries, two men aged in their early-30s and one man aged in his mid-20s have been arrested in connection with the investigation.

The injured man and woman are receiving treatment at hospital.

The circumstances surrounding the incident are unclear at this stage.

Officers are working to establish the full facts with house-to-house and forensic enquiries on-going in a number of locations in Selby.

Witnesses or anyone with information that could assist officers are to come forward without delay.

Please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for York Serious Crime Team.

If you would prefer to remain anonymous, call Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.

Please quote reference number 12160174009 when providing details about this incident.
