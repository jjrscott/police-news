Did you assist with a broken down vehicle on Walmgate on Wednesday 24 April?

Officers are appealing for witnesses who may have seen two men pushing a dark coloured saloon car from Thistleton Court onto Margaret Street at the junction of Walmgate at around 5.15am on Wednesday 24 April. It is believed the men had stolen a distinctive white Da Vinci bike from the area and may have put it into the vehicle.

Both the men were white and in their twenties. The first man had dark hair and was wearing a black tracksuit with a hood. The second man had brown hair and was wearing a light grey or white t shirt with an emblem on the chest.

Officers are especially keen to speak to the driver of a small white van that stopped to assist the two men start the car on Walmgate before the men then drove off in the direction of Lawrenece Street.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police. Dial 101 press 2 and ask to speak to Hanneke Hart. Alternatively you can email Hanneke.Hart@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
