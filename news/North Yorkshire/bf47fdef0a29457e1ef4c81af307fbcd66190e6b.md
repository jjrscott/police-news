Update: Police have launched an arson investigation following the incident below.

Police are keen to hear from anyone who saw anyone acting suspiciously in the area at the time, or anyone who has any other information that could help the investigation.

You can contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1 and quote reference 12200202265. You can also contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

**Original statement: 8.30am 17/11/20**

A number of households had to be evacuated after a fire in Pickering town centre overnight.

North Yorkshire Police were called by the Fire and Rescue Service shortly after 10pm last night, Monday 16 November 2020, to assist with a caravan on fire in the area of Southgate, Pickering.

Officers put a cordon in place, which included closing the A170, to keep the public safe and allow the emergency services to work at the scene.

A small number of properties, in the immediate vicinity of the fire, had to be evacuated. The fire was brought under control, and the road was re-opened and families returned home by 2am.

Enquiries are ongoing into the cause of the fire.
