Police are appealing for information and witnesses after a man was injured in a collision on the A64.

The man was walking on the A64 from Malton to Ganton in the early hours of Monday 30 March 2020, when he was hit by a car. He suffered injuries to his head, hands and leg.

The injured man cannot remember precise details of the incident. However, it is believed to have taken place at the traffic lights in Sherburn, near some roadworks, some time between 2am and 3am.

Anyone who was on the A64 between Malton and Scarborough in the early hours of Monday 30 March, and may have information or relevant dash-cam footage, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Zoe Todd. Quote reference 12200052318 when passing information.
