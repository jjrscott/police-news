North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a burglary in which a vehicle, tools and money was stolen in Birkin, Selby.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a burglary in which a vehicle, tools and money was stolen in Birkin, Selby.

The incident occurred between 4.50pm on Monday, 7 January and 7.50am on Tuesday, 8 January at a business premises on Haddlesey Road.

The stolen vehicle is a white Vauxhall Vivaro campervan with the registration HG61 KBV and anyone who believes they may have seen this vehicle is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police.

Information can be provided to police by calling 101, selecting option 2 and asking for PC Longhorn. You can also email Thomas.Longhorn@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to provide information anonymously, you can contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the reference 12190004213.
