Police attended a property Carrnock Close, Huntington, this morning. A 27-year-old man was arrested at 10am on suspicion of possession with intent to supply a class A drug, and being concerned in the production of a class A drug.

As a precaution, specialist personnel have attended to test suspected drugs and secure the scene. Members of the public are asked to avoid the area while the emergency services work at the site. There is no identified risk to the wider public.
