Last modified: 16 April 2019 at 07:41pm

Police are appealing for witnesses and dash-cam footage following a fatal collision on the A66 at the junction with Kirby Hill, Richmondshire.

The collision occurred at 9.45am on Friday 22 March 2019, and involved a red VW Touran, which was foreign-registered, and a red Scania HGV.

Sadly, one of the occupants of the VW, a woman in her 50s, died following the collision. The other occupant was seriously injured.

Anyone who was travelling on the A66 and saw the collision, or the manner of driving of a foreign-registered VW or a red Scania, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police.

Please phone 101, or email the Major Collision Investigation Team, MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk, or TS Julian Pearson, julian.pearson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Quote reference number 12190051568.
