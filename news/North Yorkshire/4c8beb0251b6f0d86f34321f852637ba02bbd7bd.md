In the early hours of Friday 1 January 2021, police received a report of a sexual offence that had occurred shortly before midnight, near Millennium Bridge in York.

A teenager was arrested in connection with the incident later the same day. He has since been released on bail while police enquiries continue.

Anyone who has information which could assist the investigation, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference 12210000072 when passing information.
