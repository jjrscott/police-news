A man will appear in court today charged with arson in connection with a fire at a fish and chip shop in Selby.

The 40-year-old Selby man will appear at York Magistrates Court this morning. He is charged with arson with intent at Abbey Fisheries, on Gowthorpe, on 21 March 2019.

He was arrested by North Yorkshire Police officers in Selby just before 6pm on 22 March 2019.
