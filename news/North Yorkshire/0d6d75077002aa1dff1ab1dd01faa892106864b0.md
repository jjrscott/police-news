North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred in Northallerton.

Officers are trying to track down the owners of a garden spade believed to have been used in the burglary.

They are asking members of the public to look at the photograph of the spade to see if they recognise it as their own.

The spade is of good quality with a wooden handle.

It is well used and has an identifiable weld repair on the blade.

If you recognise this spade, which you may have lost or had stolen please contact Northallerton CID

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Kate Jones. You can also email Kate.Jones@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number [12140177121].
