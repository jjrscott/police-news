Police investigating the theft of meat from two shops in Sherburn-in-Elmet have issued a CCTV image of a man they want to speak to about the thefts.

View image

On the night of 6 October 2016, meat was stolen from Tesco and Spar on Low Street in Sherburn-in-Elmet.

Officers want to speak to the man as he could have important information that could help the investigation.

Ayone who can help identify the man shown in the image is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Nicola Gill.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160182313 when passing on information.
