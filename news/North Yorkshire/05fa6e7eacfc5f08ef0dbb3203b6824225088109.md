North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred in York.

Police in York are investigating a burglary at York Wholesale Meats on York Business Park, Nether Poppleton, which occurred between 9.40pm and 11.40pm on 23 January 2020.

It involved a group of people who broke into a container and stole thousands of pounds worth of steak.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about a Vauxhall Vivaro van which was seen around the area during the time of the burglary and any people acting suspiciously.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jordan Kiernan. You can also email Jordan.kiernan@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200013265.
