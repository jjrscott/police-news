North Yorkshire Police are appealing to the public for information to assist in identification of a man who entered a 27-year-old woman’s home in Colburn and assaulted her.

At around 7.30pm on Tuesday 23 August 2016, the victim was sitting in the lounge of her house in Beechwood Grove with the rear patio doors of the property open. A man then entered the property via the open doors and upon confirming the identity of the woman – he grabbed her by her hair and punched her twice in the face, causing facial injuries. He then left the house via the back garden.

The man is described as between 27 and 31 years of age, white, 5’7’’ in height, medium build with short dark hair and a pale complexion with gaunt cheeks and stubble on his face. He is also described as having a yellow/stained tooth on the upper left side of his mouth.

Anyone who may recognise the e-fit or description or any witnesses who were in the Beechwood Grove area that evening are asked to dial 101 and ask to speak to PC Sean Godfrey. You can also email Sean.Godfrey@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk – please quote reference 12160152886.

You can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 .
