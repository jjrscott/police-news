North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of alcohol from Asda that occurred in Northallerton on Saturday 23 April.

It happened on Brompton Road, Northallerton at around 11.55am on Saturday 23 April 2016 and involved a number males leaving Asda store with stolen alcohol in a trolley and then loading the alcohol into the boot of a silver estate car parked nearby on Byland Place area.

Three males have been arrested in connection with this incident and enquieries are continuing.

Police are particularly keen to speak with a couple who walked past the Asda store and spoke with a staff member outside on the pavement regarding the silver estate car and the suspect males loading alcohol into the boot.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Northallerton Police.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
