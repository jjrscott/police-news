North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Harrogate on Saturday 17 December 2016.

It happened on Strawberry Dale Terrace between 1.15pm and 5pm on Saturday 17 December 2016 and involved entry being forced into the property. A safe containing a large amount of money and some jewellery was taken.
