Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision involving a car and a pedestrian in York.

At about 5.50pm on Monday 18 January 2016, a collision occurred on Elvington Lane, York. A silver Lexus car collided with a male pedestrian, leaving him with leg injuries that required hospital treatment. He has since been discharged from hospital.

At the time of the collision, both the pedestrian and the car were travelling towards Elvington, between the Grimston Bar roundabout and York Maze.

Police are appealing for anyone who saw either the pedestrian or the car prior to the collision to get in touch.

Please phone North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for TC Roger Walker, of the Roads Policing Group at Tadcaster. Quote reference number 12160010291 when passing information about this incident.
