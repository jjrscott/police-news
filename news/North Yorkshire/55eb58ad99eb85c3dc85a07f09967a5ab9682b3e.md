North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision that occurred on Hull Road just by the Shell garage.

It happened by the Shell garage at around 9pm on Tuesday 3 November 2020 and involved a black Renault Clio and a black Nissan X-Trail.

The Clio left the scene without exchanging details with the other party involved.

The occupants of the X-Trail received minor injuries that did not require hospital treatment.
