Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a traffic collision on the A19 near Crathorne yesterday evening (Monday 11 July).

The incident happened shortly before 7pm and involved a grey Mazda 6 and an HGV lorry.

As a result of the collision, the 41 year old man form Middlesbrough who was driving the Mazda, was taken to James Cook Hospital with serious injury. His condition is described as critical but stable.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident, or who saw either vehicle prior to the collision, to get in touch. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to TC 149 Chris Garbutt, or email _chris.garbutt@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

Please quote reference number 12160122947 when passing on information about the incident.
