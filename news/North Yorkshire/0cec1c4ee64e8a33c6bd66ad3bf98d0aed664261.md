Police in York are appealing for information after thieves broke into a cash machine and stole money.

The incident happened in the early hours of this morning. Officers were called to Vyner Street off Haxby Road at 4.20am.

Detectives believe the thieves forced their way into the machine. It is not yet known how much money was taken.

Crime scene investigators attended and extensive enquiries are currently taking place to catch the culprits.

North Yorkshire Police is urging anyone who saw someone acting suspiciously in the area at the time or has any other information that could help the investigation to get in touch on 101, select option 2 and ask for York CID.

You can also call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12190092402 when sharing information.
