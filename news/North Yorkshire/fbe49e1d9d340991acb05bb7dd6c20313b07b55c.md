Here is some information about the ongoing incident in Pickering this evening

Police were called at around 7.50pm on the night of Tuesday 20 October 2015 to reports of a robbery on Manor Drive in Pickering.

The victim was threatened by five men who entered his caravan and stole valuables before leaving the scene in a van. The victim was not injured during the incident.

Police officers began searching the area around Pickering, Malton and the roads leading out of the area. Air support was provided by helicopter from the National Police Air Service.

Two men were arrested a short time later and have been taken into police custody.

At the time of writing searches are being carried out to locate the remaining suspects.

If anyone witnessed the incident or has any information that could help the police investigation, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and pass information to the Force Control Room.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 0504 of 20 October 2015.

9.55pm 20 October 2015

**UPDATE**

A third man has been arrested in connection with the incident and is en route to custody.
