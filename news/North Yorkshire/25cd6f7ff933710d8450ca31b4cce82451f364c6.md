Police are investigating a burglary in Tadcaster in which a large amount of cash and several items of distinctive jewellery was stolen

The incident happened at a property on Doncaster Road between 7.30am and 6pm on Thursday 28 July 2016.

The images show some of the stolen items, a Cartier three band wedding ring, a silver Gucci watch, Links of London Pearl, and a Jamie gold hearted necklace.

Other items stolen include a Tiffany cross necklace and Links necklaces (silver)and a Marc Jacobs necklace with red heart charm.

A Rolex watch which holds great sentimental value was also stolen. The watch is described as vintage style 1980 to early 90’s with a black face with a steal and gold coloured bracelet and worth around £5,000.

Anyone who saw anything suspicious in the area on the day of 28 July 2016 is asked to contact police. Also, anyone with any information about the jewellery is also urged to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to Claire Massheder in York Investigation Hub or email _Claire.massheder@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
