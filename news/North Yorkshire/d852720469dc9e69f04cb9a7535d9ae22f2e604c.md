North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Garage Burglary which occurred in the village of Fairburn.

It happened at Cut Road between 9am and 4pm on Thursday 14th May.

Stolen from the garage were an oragne KTM off road motorcycle and two high value pedal cycles made by Crosstrainer, one white and one black.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Graham Smith. You can also email graham.smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150079952.

Monday 18 May 2015.
