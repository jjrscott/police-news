Police are appealing for witnesses after a fatal collision in York this morning.

At about 5.30am on Saturday 8 September 2018, police and ambulance services attended a single-vehicle collision on the A59 Boroughbridge Road near to the junction with Plantation Drive, in York York.

The collision involved a single rider on a silver-coloured Piaggio moped who was travelling outbound towards the ring road.

Sadly, despite efforts by a local passing fire crew, the rider, a 48-year-old man from the York area, was pronounced dead at the scene.

The road was closed for five hours while officers from the Roads Policing Group and Forensic Collision Investigation Unit conducted examinations at the scene.

Police are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the collision, seen the vehicle prior to the collision, or have dashcam footage of the incident, to contact the North Yorkshire Police Major Collision Investigation Team on 101, quoting the incident reference number 12180168057.
