Last modified: 12 October 2020 at 12:15pm

Police are appealing for witness or anyone who may have dashcam footage a road traffic collision involving a cyclist and white Skoda Yeti.

The collision occurred at the roundabout joining Stepney road, Sandybed Lane and Stepney Drive in Scarborough.

It happened at approximately 1.40pm on Tuesday 6 October 2020. The cyclist was travelling over the roundabout towards Stepney Drive when a white Skoda Yeti collided with the cyclist as it traveled over the roundabout from the direction of Stepney hill towards Stepney road.

Officers are asking members of the public to get in touch if they believe they will have information that will help the investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Sophie Milner or email Sophie.Milner@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12200175687 when passing on information.
