North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses who were in Mrs Lofthouses Book Emporium, 2 Queen Street Scarborough on Sunday 8th October 2017.

At around 2.45pm an incident occured which involved a man who had entered the shop with a quantity of books and a member of staff.

Officers are conducting enquiries to establish the circumstances around the incident and are appealing for anyone with information that could assist the investigation to contact police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jade Gallagher-Barrass. You can also email Jade.Gallagher-Barrass597@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170180608.

Date 17th October 2017
