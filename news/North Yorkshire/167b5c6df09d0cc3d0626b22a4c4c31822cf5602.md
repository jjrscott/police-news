North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic collision that occurred that occurred on Castlegate near to the traffic lights with Butcher Corner in MALTON on the 2 December 2015.

It happened at approximately 4pm when a yellow Citroen C2 was stationery at the lights and was struck from behind by a large black vehicle, possibly a Land Rover 4Ã—4.

The driver of the Citroen suffered minor injuries.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the black 4 x4 .

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1288 Nick Ham or email to _Nicholas.ham@Northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150214433.
