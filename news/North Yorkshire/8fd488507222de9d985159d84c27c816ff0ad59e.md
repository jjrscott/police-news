North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after power tools were stolen from a vehicle

The incident happened on Beechfield Road, Richmond at 10.45pm on Monday 4 November 2019 and involved a white Ford Transit van. The van's rear doors were forced open and power tools stolen.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for James Bowie. You can also email james.bowie@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190203928.
