Last modified: 26 September 2016 at 12:20pm

Police want to speak to anyone who witnessed a collision near Harrogate in which a motorcyclist died. The incident took place at about 5.30pm yesterday.​

The motorcyclist was traveling west on a white and silver BMW from Sicklinghall towards Netherby, on Kirkby Lane.

A black Volkswagen Polo was travelling in the opposite direction.

The motorcyclist – a man in his 50s from the Ripon area – came off his bike and, sadly, was pronounced dead at the scene.
Investigations are ongoing and officers are not currently in a position to release his identity.

Police request that anyone that saw either of these vehicles prior to the collision contact TC Mark Mullens by calling North Yorkshire Police on 101 or via e-mail _Mark.mullins@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

Please quote reference number 12160175076 when passing on information.
