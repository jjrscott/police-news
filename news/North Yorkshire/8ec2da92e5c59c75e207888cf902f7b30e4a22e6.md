Police in Scarborough have issued a CCTV image of a woman they would like to speak to following a disturbance inside the Mist Bar and Lounge.

It happened on 17 December 2017 when two women were removed from the premises during which one of them became verbally abusive and a doorman was assaulted.

Anyone who can help identify the woman is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Rachael Atkinson or email Rachael.Atkinson1954@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12170224931 when passing on information.

The doorman sustained a minor injury to his leg.
