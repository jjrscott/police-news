North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Hemingbrough near Selby on 26 July 2019.

The burglary took place between 4pm Thursday 25 July and 6am Friday 26 July 2019 at a bungalow on Chestnut Rise in Hemingbrough. The resident was not at home during this time.

The suspect/s have gained access to the property by smashing the window of a conservatory at the rear of the address. A number of items of antique jewellery have been stolen and the suspect/s have then left the address in an unknown direction.

Police are appealing to anyone who may have been in the area at the time and who may have information that would help to establish the full circumstances surrounding this incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC306 McGreevy. You can also email tim.mcgreevy@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190135849
