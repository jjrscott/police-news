Police have released a CCTV image of a woman they would like to speak to in connection with the theft of household items from a shop in York.

At about 5.30pm on Saturday 28 January 2017, a woman entered B&M Stores in Stirling Road, Clifton Moor. She walked round the store, selected various items, including a TV and a mirror, placed them in a trolley and left the store without making payment.

Officers would like to speak to the woman pictured on CCTV, as she may have important information that could assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises her should contact North Yorkshire Police. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Michael Halstead or York Police, or email michael.halstead@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12170015825 when passing information.
