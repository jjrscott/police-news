North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a MAFIA BMX specialised pedal cycle that occurred in West Row in Scarborough

It happened on West Row in Scarborough between 7.30pm and 9pm on 9 January 2016. It is believed unknown offender(s) have entered the communal area of the address and stolen the cycle.

The cycle is described as chrome in colour with a BMX-type frame. It has a Black MAFIA sticker which is situated on the frame, and a black gel seat with a green sticker underneath. The BMX also has 20inch wheels, white tyres with a tri-coloured rim. The BMX also has red and black grips on the handles.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 330 Verrill. You can also email ashley.verrill@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160005209
