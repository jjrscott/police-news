Police in Harrogate have issued CCTV images of a man they want to speak to following a theft from the Yorkshire Event Centre.

On the afternoon of 18 March 2018, a purse and a wallet were stolen from a room at the centre.

Officers are appealing to anyone who can help identify the man in the images to contact them.

Anyone with information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Helen James or email helen.james@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12180046734 when passing on information.
