North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary in Settle.

It happened at Three Peaks Cycles at approximately 1.30am today (Monday, 12 August).

The offenders smashed the front window with a sledge hammer and stole an orange coloured Saracen Levarg 2019 gravel bike.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Burgess. You can also email David.Burgess@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190147887
