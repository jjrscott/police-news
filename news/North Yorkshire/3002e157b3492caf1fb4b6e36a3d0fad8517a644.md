North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a damage-only collision that occurred on the A173 on the outskirts of Great Ayton.

It happened at about 6.50am on 1 April 2016 and involved a white Peugeot Partner van and a small Suzuki, dark in colour.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle(s) prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the small Suzuki car with a registration plate similar to WD54 YDX.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Chris Villiers. You can also email chris.villiers@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160055985.
