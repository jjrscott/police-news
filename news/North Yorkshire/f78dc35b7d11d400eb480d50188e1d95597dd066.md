Police investigating the theft of alcohol from a shop in Stokesley want to speak to a man pictured by CCTV.

A man entered the Co-op store in the high street at around 5.45pm on Friday, January 5 and stole three bottles of spirits.

Police want to speak to a man pictured by CCTV in connection with their investigations.

Anyone who recognises the man in the images or has any other information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Michael Wilson.

You can also email PC Wilson on Michael.Wilson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote incident number 12180002833.
