Police are appealing for information to help identify the man in the CCTV image, following a “ringing the changes” scam in Starbucks on Coney Street, York.

Officers want to speak to the man in connection with the incident, which happened at around 6.10pm on Saturday 11 February 2017.

A member of staff at the till was asked by an unknown man to change £200.00 in £10 note denominations to £200 in £20 note denominations. The man was seen to place some notes in his back pocket on two occasions during this and the store has now reported a loss.

Anyone with any information about the identity of the man is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC592 Lewis.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170024007 when passing on any information.
