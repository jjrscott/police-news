North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a new ramp at the skate park was damaged by graffiti on 22 July 2019.

It happened at the Skate Park, Norton Road, Malton on 22 July 2019 and involved graffiti being applied to one of the new ramps.

The police are looking to speak to a man who we believe has information that would assist with the investigation. He is believed to be aged 20, around 6ft tall and was wearing a light blue t-shirt, white trainers and black trousers with one of the legs tucked into his sock. He was also wearing a dark coloured sun hat and was using a skateboard.

Anyone who can help identify him or has any other information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jane Jones 1492. You can also email: jane.jones@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190133038.
