North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a damage only/minor collision that occurred on Queens Parade at Scarborough.

It happened between 8pm on 03/04/15 and 10am on 04/04/15 and involved a stationary Vauxhall Astra in black registered to Germany parked outside the Clifton Hotel being hit by an unknown vehicle which failed to stop. There was substantive damage caused to the Vauxhall Astra.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Pc 596 Gillon. You can also email leah.gillon@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150054450.
