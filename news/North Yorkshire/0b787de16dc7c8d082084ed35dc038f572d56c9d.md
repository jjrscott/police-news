Last modified: 9 June 2016 at 01:20pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a camera belonging to a female visiting the area. The camera was stolen whilst the victim was in the Abbey Tea Rooms in Whitby on the afternoon of the 19th April 2015.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance.

In particular, we are appealing to the public to see if they are able to identify the male shown in the images attached to this appeal. It is believed that this male may be able to assist us further with our enquiries.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Tom Rollinson. You can also email tom.rollinson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150064423.

**14 May 2015**
