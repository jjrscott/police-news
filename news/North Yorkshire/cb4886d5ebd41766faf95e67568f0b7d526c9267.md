North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a suspected indecent public exposure incident that occurred on Wigginton Road in York.

It happened near the York Cycle Orbital Track, at the junction/path leading to Wigginton Road, at around 7.45am on Saturday 8 July 2017.

A lone female was walking from the cycle track to Wigginton Road when she found a man on the side of the path appearing to expose himself.
