Police are appealing for information following an incident of sexual assault on a York bus

The incident happened between 3.40pm and 3.52pm on the number six bus to Clifton at Clarence Street yesterday, Wednesday 23 January 2019.

Two 15-year-old girls were assaulted by a man who stroked and squeezed their bottoms. The girls were standing on the bus and the man was sitting on the seat next to them.

The man is described as white, aged in his 40s with a moustache and stubble. He was wearing a black beanie and a black puffa style jacket.

It is believed the man also assaulted other women on the bus.

Officers are appealing for information and ask anyone who was on the number six bus, to get in touch. Officers are also keen for anyone else who was assaulted by the man to report it to police.

Dial 101, press 2 and pass information to PC1371 Ruth Atkins or email

Ruth.atkins@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
