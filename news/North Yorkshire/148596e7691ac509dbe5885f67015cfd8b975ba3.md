Last modified: 25 October 2018 at 12:30pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a spate of recent burglaries that have taken place across York.

Offenders are targeting properties that are undergoing building work/restoration and stealing tools from within the properties.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone who maybe selling cheap power tools.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Richard Tempest. You can also email richard.tempest@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-25102018-0069.
