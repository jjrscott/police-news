Officers are appealing for information after two vehicles were damaged in the village of Eppleby.

The offences occurred overnight between Tuesday, 19 May and Wednesday, 20 May when two vehicles were damaged after a considerable amount of paint was thrown over them.

Anyone with information which could assist with the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for James Bowie. Alternatively, you can email james.bowie@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to provide information anonymously, then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the reference number 12200083684 when providing information.
