Last modified: 27 June 2016 at 02:10pm

Police are appealing for witnesses after two cyclists were injured in a collision near Skipton.

They collided with a Ford Transit van on the A629 at around 11am on Saturday (25 June 2016).

The make cyclist sustained back injuries and the woman received injuries to one of her legs. Both were taken to Leeds General Infirmary and their condition was described as “not life-threatening”.

The road was closed for several hours while police and other emergency services dealt with the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, or saw anything in the moments leading up to it, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference number 12160111918.
