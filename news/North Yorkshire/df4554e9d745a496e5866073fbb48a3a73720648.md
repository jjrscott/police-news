We are now in a position to identify the man who died in a collision on the B6265 Dibble's Bridge last weekend.

He was George Ballard, 41, from Ilkley.

The collision occurred at about 8.30am on Sunday 2 August 2015, when a pedal cycle heading towards Grassington, from the direction of Pateley Bridge, collided with the bridge.

Officers are continuing to appeal for anyone who witnessed the collision to contact them.

Anyone with information is asked to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Traffic Constable Martin Mulcaster, or emailMartin.Mulcaster@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk orMCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote reference number 12150133243 when passing on information regarding this incident.

**5 August 2015**
