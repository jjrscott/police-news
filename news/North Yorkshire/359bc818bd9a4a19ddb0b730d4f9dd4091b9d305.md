North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a large amount of alcohol that occurred in Skipton.

It happened at the Tesco superstore in Craven Street at about 11.30pm on Monday 20 January and involved three white men all aged in their mid to late 30s.

The thieves removed the security tags on the bottles of alcohol before placing them into large bags for life and leaving the store. The men then fled after being challenged and ran onto Craven Street then into the alleyway by Christ Church Primary School.

Male 1 is of stocky build with short dark hair and was wearing a mid blue puffer jacket.
Male 2 is of slim build and was wearing a black baseball cap, black zip up jacket and black trousers.
Male 3 is described as skinny, wearing a grey hooded top and grey jogging bottoms.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200011008.
