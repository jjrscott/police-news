Last modified: 28 September 2020 at 12:07pm

Police are appealing to anyone who may have been offered a Capra AL mountain bike for sale or has any other information that could assist their investigation.

The bike was stolen in what police are treating as a robbery investigation after the victim had visited Forge Valley in Scarborough on the evening of 13 September.

Stolen Capra AL mountain bike

After returning to his vehicle in the wooded area, the suspects have pushed him from behind before stealing his bike.

Police are appealing to anyone who has been offered the bike for sale, knows where it is now, or was in Forge Valley on the evening of 13 September and can assist the police investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Paul Southgate, or email Paul.Southgate@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestsoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12200160759
