North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage that occurred in Scarborough

It happened on Sussex Street, Scarborough at approx. 01:45 in the early hours of 5 August 2018 when a brick was thrown at a resident's door by two males. The incident has caused considerable distress to the occupant of the property.

Officers are appealing to anyone who was in the area at the time and either witnessed the incident, or has any CCTV or dash-cam footage from the area.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Stephen Mortimer. You can also email Stephen.Mortimer@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180146378
