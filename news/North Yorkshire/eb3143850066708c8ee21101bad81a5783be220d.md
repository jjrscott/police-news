**UPDATE**

**Thank you to everyone who responded to our appeal, Mr Johnson 's family have now been located.**

*******

Stephen Johnson, 53, from York was found deceased at his home on Thursday 16 December 2019 after concerns were raised for his welfare. There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Police are looking to locate Mr Johnson’s brothers who are believed to live in Scarborough and Darlington.

Family members of Mr Johnson, or anyone who can help contact them, are asked to contact the Coroner’s Office at North Yorkshire Police on 01609 643123.
