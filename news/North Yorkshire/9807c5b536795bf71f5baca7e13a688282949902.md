North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Haxby, York.

It happened at the bus stop on the Village street in Haxby, opposite the Memorial Hall.

The incident happened between 10pm and 10.15pm on Saturday 3 October and involved a group of six men. A altercation broke out between the men which resulted in two suffering suffering facial injuries.

One of the victims of the incident received treatment for his teeth at York District Hospital.
