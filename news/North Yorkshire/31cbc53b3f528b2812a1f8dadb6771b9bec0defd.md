Last modified: 3 August 2020 at 05:12pm

Police in Whitby are appealing for witnesses after a man suffered facial injuries during a disturbance in the early hours of Friday morning (31 July).

The incident happened in Runswick Avenue at around half past 12 in the early hours of Friday.

Police were called to reports of a disturbance in the residential street shortly after midnight. On arrival, they found a man in his fifties with injuries to his face that required hospital treatment including stitches.

Officers are appealing to anyone who was in the area at the time and witnessed the incident or has any information that could assist the investigation to contact them.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, selection option 2 and ask for Scarborough CID. Or email Nicholas.Burton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12200130753 when passing on information.

A woman in her forties has been arrested in connection with the incident and released on conditional police bail to allow for further enquiries to be carried out.
