North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about damage to a motor vehicle that occurred in Eastfield, Scarborough.

It happened on Dale Edge, Eastfield between 6.10 - 6.20pm on 11th September 2015 and involved damage being caused to a red Vauxhall Astra

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the offender who can only be described as being a white male wearing a green parka style jack.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Scarborough Police.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150160412.
