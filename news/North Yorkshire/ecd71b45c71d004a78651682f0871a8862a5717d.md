Police are investigating the death of a seven-year-old girl in York.

Police were called shortly after 4.30pm on Monday 9 January 2017 to an address in Alness Drive, York.

Officers attended and upon visiting a scene nearby, they found a seven-year-old girl with life-threatening injuries. Ambulance attended and the girl was taken to York District Hospital, where she sadly died a short time later.

A 15-year-old girl has been arrested in connection with the incident. She remains in police custody for questioning.

Officers remain at the scene while the investigation to establish the full circumstances surrounding the death of the girl continues.
