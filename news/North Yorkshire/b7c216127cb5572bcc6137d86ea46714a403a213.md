On Wednesday 29 June 2016 a road traffic collision occurred on the A172 near the junction with the B1365 Thirsk Road Stokesley.

The incident happened at around 7.50am and involved a red Ford Transit van, a white Ford Transit van, a silver Vauxhall Vectra and a white Kia Picanto.

The red Transit van and Kia were travelling towards Stokesley and the white transit van and Vectra were travelling in the opposite direction.

As a result of the collision, two of the male drivers, aged 41 and 32, both from Middlesborough, were taken to James Cook Hospital with serious injuries. They were both discharged from hospital later that day. Two passengers, a 22 year old woman and a 31 year old man, both from Middlesborough, also attended hospital with serious but not life threatening injuries.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who saw the vehicles prior to the collision is asked to contact police.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to t TC149 Chris Garbutt, or email _Chris.Garbutt@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_.

Please quote reference number 12160114158 when passing on information .
