Around 2.50pm on 1 December 2020, a man entered the Iceland store on Fulford Road in York, selected three bottles of spirits and then left the shop without paying.

North Yorkshire Police has released a CCTV image of a man we would like to speak to as we believe he may have information which could help this investigation.

If you recognise the man in the image or have any other information about this incident, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Huang Yan or email Huang.Yan@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12200214389 when passing on information.
