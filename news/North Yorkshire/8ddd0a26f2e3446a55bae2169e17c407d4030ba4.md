North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred on Carr Lane, East Heslerton.

It happened on Carr Lane between 17:00hrs on Thursday 17 September 2015 and 17:00hrs on Friday 18 September 2015 and involved unknown offenders gaining entry into a portable cabin shed where numerous items of horse tack have been stolen.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspect vehicles or persons seen in the area around these times

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1634 Collins. You can also email emma.collins@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150165110.
