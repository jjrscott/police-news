North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of an Ifor William trailer and power tools from a farm barn in Giggleswick.

The incident happened between 2.30pm on Saturday 19 March and 9am on Sunday 20 March.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, and ask for PC957 Ann Earnshaw or email Ann.Earnshaw@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160048692
