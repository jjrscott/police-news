Police have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to regarding damage to stock at the One Stop Shop on North Marine Drive in Scarborough.

Police have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to regarding damage to stock at the One Stop Shop on North Marine Drive in Scarborough.

At around 7.10pm on Tuesday 28 March 2017 the man pictured was seen to enter the shop. Following an altercation with a member of the public, a fridge was kicked over and items were knocked off a shelf, causing damage to stock.

Police would like to speak to the man pictured, to determine the circumstances around the incident.

Anyone who recognises the man, or has information which would assist the investigation is asked to contact PC Jade Gallagher-Barrass on 101. Alternatively you can email Jade.Gallagher-Barrass597@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170052471.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
