North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information in relation to a collision that occurred on Falsgrave road outside the Spar shop in Scarborough.

The incident happened on Thursday 14 December 2017 on Falsgrave Road between 12:00pm and 12:30pm. It involved a blue motorbike, driven by a man, and a pedestrian. The pedestrian, a 25 year old woman, was carrying a small child at the time of the collision.

The rider came off the bike but appeared uninjured. He then left the scene without speaking to the pedestrian or giving his details.

The 25 year old woman went to Scarborough Hospital with bruising to her foot. The child was uninjured.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information which could assist police in identifying the motorbike or the rider.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul O'Neill. You can also email Paul.o'neill@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170223521 when passing on information.
