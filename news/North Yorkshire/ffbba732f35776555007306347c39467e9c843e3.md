Last modified: 23 March 2020 at 08:40am

Police are appealing for witnesses after a fatal collision in Carlton, near Selby.

The single-vehicle collision happened at around 8pm on Sunday 22 March 2020 on the A1041 High Street as it leaves the village of Carlton.

It involved a Yamaha motorcycle which was travelling from Carlton towards Snaith. The 27-year-old male motorcyclist was pronounced dead at the scene.

The road was closed for a number of hours whilst police examined the scene.

Officers are appealing for witnesses, or anyone who may have dashcam footage of the motorcycle prior to the incident. If you have any information which may assist the investigation, please email Andrew.Ingram@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Please quote North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200048649.
