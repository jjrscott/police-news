A man has appeared in court charged with an assault in Acomb on Saturday 5 May 2018.

The 38-year-old man has been charged with assault occasioning actual bodily harm to a man in his 50s in Acomb. He appeared at York Magistrates Court on 7 May.

Anyone who witnessed any incident in the York Road area between 6pm and 7pm on Saturday 5 May, and has not already spoken to police, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101. Ask for PC Neel Seth and quote incident number 12180078004.
