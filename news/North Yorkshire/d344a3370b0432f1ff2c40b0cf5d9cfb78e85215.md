North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred inside Pizza Pronto in Richmond Market place on Sunday 31 July 2016 between 12.30 -12.45 am

At this time there was a disturbance between two young women and several older men. The shop was fairly busy at the time and so witnesses may recall a loud argument between the men and women, which resulted in them being asked to leave the shop. If you were present and can remember this incident , North Yorkshire police would be grateful if you could contact us with any information.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the descriptions of the men involved and information regarding the circumstances leading up to the disturbance

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Northallerton Investigation Hub You can also email kate.jones@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160136498
