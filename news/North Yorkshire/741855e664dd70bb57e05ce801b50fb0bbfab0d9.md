UPDATED

UPDATE

The technical issues were resolved in the early hours of this morning (3 August) and calls are being answered as normal.

****************************************************************************

Our Force Control Room is currently experiencing some technical difficulties.

Staff are working hard to take calls. 999 calls are taking priority and are being answered.

Calls to 101 are being answered, however due the technical issues, delays in answering these calls could be experienced.

If your call is not urgent or not an emergency, please consider calling back later when delays have been reduced.

If your call is an emergency please call 999. All emergency calls are being answered.

We will provide further update as soon as normal service resumes.

(Wednesday 2 August 2017 - updated 9.40pm)
