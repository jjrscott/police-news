North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary in Settle.

It happened at Carrs Billington on the Sidings Buisness Park at Settle overnight on Wednesday the 17 May 2017. It involved someone breaking a hole in a wall to gain access to the property and stealing a large quantity of animal medication.

The offenders were in the property , which backs on to Settle Train Station, for quite some time and may have made some noise getting in.

Anyone who saw or heard anything suspicious around this time , or has been offered such items for sale, is asked to contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Max Barton. You can also email max.barton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170085146
