UPDATE - Serious collision on A169 Whitby to Pickering road, near the Hole of Horcum

We are now in a position to confirm that sadly a 33-year-old man from Whitby died as a result of the collision between a black Renault Twingo and a white DAF HGV lorry on the A169 at Saltergate Bank on Tuesday 1 March 2016.

Police are appealing for anyone who saw the collision, or either of the vehicles involved, to dial 101 and ask to speak to TC Matthew Tanfield of the Major Collision Investigation Team or email _Matthew.Tanfield@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

Please quote reference 12160036735.

**2 March 2016**

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Emergency services are currently dealing with a serious crash between a car and a lorry on A169 Whitby to Pickering road.

It happened at around 2.35pm today (Tuesday 1 March 2016) between the Hole of Horcum and Lockton.

Ambulance and fire crews have been attending to three casualties. A man and a woman in the car, and a man driving the HGV.

Local diversions have been put in place to allow to the police to investigate the scene and for the vehicles to be recovered.

Witnesses are asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1, and speak to the Force Control Room. Please quote reference NYP-01032016-0256.

**1 March 2016 - 4.35pm**
