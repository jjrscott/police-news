Police are appealing for information after a Land Rover was stolen in Craven.

The vehicle is a Land Rover Defender 110 TD5 in heritage green with a white roof, a black access ladder to the back of the vehicle and three silver galvanized steel roof bars. The registration is LL55 YGK.

It was stolen from Gargrave, near Skipton, between 02/10/2020 and 06/10/2020.

Police are searching for this vehicle. Anyone who has seen it or has information is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and select option 1, quoting reference number 12200175846.
