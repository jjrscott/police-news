North Yorkshire Police are appealing for information regarding an incident which occurred between a pedestrian and a female pedal cyclist on Monday 22 February 2016 at 4.30pm outside of Tang Hall News on Tang Hall Lane, York.

Police are appealing for the female pedal cyclist to come forward, as she may hold some vital information which may assist an investigation into some unexplained injuries that the pedestrian has sustained.

Police would like to reassure the female cyclist that she is wanted as a witness to assist the investigation and request that she dials 101 and asks to speak to DS Fionna McEwan of the Serious Crime Team in York, quoting reference 12160031740. Alternatively she can email _Fionna.McEwan@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

Crimestoppers can also be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
