Police are appealing for information after a woman was sexually touched in York.

At around midnight on Saturday 13 October 2018 the woman was in York City Centre when she hailed what she believed to be a taxi near Pavement in York.

A silver coloured car stopped for her and the male driver asked her to sit in the front of the car.

On arrival at her destination the driver leaned over to the woman and touched her inappropriately.

He is described as Arabic in appearance and has dark hair and a well-trimmed dark beard. He is described as having thick eyebrows and a distinctive nose. He is believed to be approximately 6 feet tall and of a slender build and a smoker.

Anyone who has any information which could assist the investigation is asked to contact police on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to PC 632 Bolton. Please quote reference 12180191482.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
