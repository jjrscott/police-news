North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic collision that occurred in Skipton.

It happened at 6.55pm on Tuesday 21 March on Greenacres close to the junction of Meadow Rise.

We are appealing for the driver of the vehicle involved in the incident to come forward or anyone who may have seen a runner being hit. Police are particular keen to speak to the driver of a car who may of turned left onto Meadow Rise at the time of the collision and who may of witnessed something. The runner suffered minor injury.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andrew Ingram. You can also email Andrew.Ingram@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170049402
