Last modified: 29 November 2019 at 04:28pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred outside the Lord Roseberry Pub on Westborough in Scarborough.

It happened at between 7.15pm and 7.45pm on Wednesday 27 November 2019 and involved two male victims who were subject to an unprovoked attack by the two male suspects. Both victims sustained serious facial injuries.

The two male suspects have been arrested and bailed pending further enquiries.

We are requesting the public's assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident. In particular we are appealing for information about anyone who witnessed the incident

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1685 Kerry Burton. You can also email Kerry.burton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190218052.
