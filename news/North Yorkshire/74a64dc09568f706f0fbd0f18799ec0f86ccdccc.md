Police attended a property at The Flatts in Sowerby,Thirsk at around 8.20pm on Thursday 5 November, following a report of a concern for safety.

Upon arrival, officers found an elderly man with serious injuries. The 85-year-old local man was taken to James Cook hospital where he remains in a critical condition.

A 29-year-old man from Thirsk has been charged with attempted murder and theft and will appear at Northallerton Magistrates Court on Monday 9 November.
