Allotments holders in the Middletown area of Skipton are being urged to be vigilant after some incidents of criminal damage.

A large greenhouse was damaged at the allotments at sometime between 4pm on 29 January 2015 and 8.30am on 30 January 2015.

Damage was also caused to two glass panes for no apparent reason other than vandalism.

Anyone with information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for Skipton police.

If you prefer not to give your name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150016350 when passing information about this incident.

**18 February 2015**
