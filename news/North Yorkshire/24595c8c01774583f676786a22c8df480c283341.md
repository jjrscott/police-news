North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a purse theft which occurred at Marks & Spencer, Harrogate.

View image

The incident occurred during the afternoon of Monday 26 September 2016 at Marks & Spencer, Cambridge Street, Harrogate. A woman reached into a shopper's handbag and removed a purse. The suspect is white, around forty to fifty years old, wearing a distinctive black and grey coloured patterned three quarter length coat, a dark coloured floppy peaked hat and a blue coloured scarf.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Janine Walker. You can also email janine.walker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160175666.
