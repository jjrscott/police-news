Police are appealing for witnesses after a serious road traffic collision on Askwith Moor Road, Otley.

The collision occurred at around 1.20pm on Tuesday 6 October 2015, on Askwith Moor Road near to the junction of Low Snowdon, Otley.

It involved a single pedal cycle, which was travelling towards Blubberhouses.

The pedal cyclist, a man from the Harrogate area in his forties, suffered serious injuries as a result of the collision.

Traffic Constable Mark Brook, of North Yorkshire Police's Roads Policing Group, said: "If you saw the incident - or the cycle involved prior to the collision - I urge you to come forward as you may have important information that could assist the investigation.

"In particular I would like to appeal to the female of a green Land Rover that stopped at the scene, and who may be able to offer vital information in respect of the collision."

Anyone with information which could help this investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Traffic Constable Mark Brook or Skipton Roads Policing Group. Alternatively, information can be emailed toMark.Brook@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12150177081 when passing information about this incident.
