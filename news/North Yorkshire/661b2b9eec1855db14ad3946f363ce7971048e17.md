North Yorkshire Police are appealing for the rightful owner of a bronze, resin statue to come forward.

The statue was found by a member of the public in a ditch near Thirsk and is thought to be one of a small number made.

It features a man's body wrapped in chains with a beast's head.

If you believe the statue is yours, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Tina Fiorentino or emailtina.fiorentino@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote reference number 12150157166 when calling.
