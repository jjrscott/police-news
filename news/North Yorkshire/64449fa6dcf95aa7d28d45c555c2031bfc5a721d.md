North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following an assault on a woman in her 30s in York.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following an assault on a woman in her 30s in York.

The incident occurred at around 12.40am this morning (Thursday, 17 January) on Coney Street. The woman suffered a minor head injury as a result of the assault and was taken to hospital for treatment.

An 18-year-old man has been arrested in connection with the incident and remains in police custody at this time.

Anyone who witnessed the assault or the events surrounding it is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police either by calling 101, selecting option 2 and asking for PC 783 Hardwick or by emailing Ashley.Hardwick@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, if you wish to provide information anonymously, this can be done through the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12190009753 when providing information.
