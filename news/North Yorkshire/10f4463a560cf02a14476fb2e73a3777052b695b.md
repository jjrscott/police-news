Police are appealing for information about a wanted man with links to Leeds.

North Yorkshire Police are searching for Spencer Osbourne, 23, in connection with Class A drugs offences.

Extensive enquiries are are ongoing to locate Osbourne, who has links to Leeds and may be in the West Yorkshire area.

If you see Osbourne, or know where he is, please contact North Yorkshire Police immediately on 101, quoting reference number 12190226061.
