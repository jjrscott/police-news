Police are appealing for information about a burglary that occurred at a hotel in Gargrave.

It happened at the Premier Inn (Anchor Inn), Hellifield Road, Gargrave, at about 8.40pm on Thursday 7 April 2016, and involved two men stealing money from the till at the hotel reception. The men are described as being of Eastern European in appearance.

Police are appealing for information to help identify two men pictured on CCTV from the hotel, as they may have important information that could assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises the men on the CCTV pictures should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Martin Carson. You can also emailmartin.carson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160060261.
