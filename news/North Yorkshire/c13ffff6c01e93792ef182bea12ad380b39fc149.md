North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a series of broken wing mirrors in Pickering.

Eight vehicles had their wing mirrors damaged overnight on New Year's Eve, between about 9.30pm and 7am on New Year's Day.

Three vehicles were parked at the end of Norman Close; two vehicles were parked at Brant Hill, one at the top and one at the bottom; three vehicles were parked in the layby outside Pickering Railway Station on Park Street, about 100m from the vehicle at the bottom of Brant Hill.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Meer. You can also email david.meer@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160000347.
