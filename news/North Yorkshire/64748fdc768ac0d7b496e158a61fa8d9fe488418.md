Entry forced to residential property on Acre Road.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for any witnesses or information which may help officers in the investigation of a burglary to a residential address on Acre Road, Cowling shortly before 4am on Sunday 30 August 2020.

Officers believe burglars may have been attempting to steal the resident's car keys but were unsuccessful. If you have any information or CCTV footage which could help North Yorkshire Police establish the full circumstances of this incident then please call 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 249 Wehrle.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200151341.
