Last modified: 8 March 2017 at 05:10pm

Police are appealing for information regarding a report of a serious sexual assault that took place in the Lower Clark Street area of Scarborough at around 9.30pm Friday 3 February 2017.

The victim, a girl in her mid-teens from Scarborough, had been assaulted near the William Street car park. She is currently receiving support from specialist officers.

Police are appealing for anyone who was in the area between 9pm and 10pm to contact them on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to DC Lucie Welford. Alternatively you can email Lucie.Welford@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12170019729.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
