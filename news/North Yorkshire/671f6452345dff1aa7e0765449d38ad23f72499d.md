Last modified: 9 October 2018 at 03:58pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a mountain bike from outside a Scarborough supermarket.

The GT Mountain bike was stolen between 12pm and 9pm on Thursday 4 October 2018, from outside Sainsbury's on Falsgrave Road, Scarborough.

The bike had been locked up, and the lock was cut.

The attached photograph shows a bike of the same model - the only difference is the red crank on the pictured bike is actually black on the stolen bike.

Anyone with information about the theft is asked to contact PC Phil Charlton. Please dial 101, select option 2, or email philip.charlton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180186255.
