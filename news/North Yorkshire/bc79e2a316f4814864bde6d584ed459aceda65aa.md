North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a woman was hit by a car on Falsgrave Road in Scarborough.

At around 9.40am on Saturday 3 February 2018, a vehicle described as a white estate car emerged from an internal shop car park and collided with the woman who sustained a minor injury to her arm.

Officers are appealing for witnesses and to anyone who can help identify the vehicle and the occupants.

The vehicle contained two men and one of them apologised to the woman before it drove off.

If you are one of the occupants of the car or have any information that could assist the investigation, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 336 HINCHLIFFE. You can also email David.Hinchliffe@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180021357.
