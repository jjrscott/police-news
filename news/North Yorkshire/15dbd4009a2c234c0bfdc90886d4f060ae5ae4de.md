North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a house burglary that occurred in Leyburn.

It happened on Matthew Terrace, Harmby Road, Leyburn between 22.30 pm on 11 December and 08.00 am 12 December 2018, when the suspects entered the property and took a wallet, laptop and bottles of alcohol.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Michaela Godridge. You can also email Michaela.godridge@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-12122018-0298.
