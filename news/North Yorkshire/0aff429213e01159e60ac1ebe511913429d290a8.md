North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage to a vehicle that occurred in Whitby.

It happened at a car park at the rear of Kirby Flats, East Terrace, Whitby, between midnight and 7am on Friday 2 August 2019, and again on Sunday 4 August, when a red Mercedes C220 estate was damaged on two separate occasions. The vehicle has deep scratching to the paint work, a tyre has been punctured and the vehicle has been defaced with spray paint.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any person who may have been seen acting suspiciously in the car park between the times mentioned above.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Craig Martin. You can also email Craig.Martin@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190141083.
