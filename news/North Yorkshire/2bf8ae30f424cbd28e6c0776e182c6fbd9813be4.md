Have you seen 41 year old Anthony Kelly?

Kelly, of no fixed abode, is wanted for failing to answer bail following three serious assaults in Middleham in July of this year.

It is believed Kelly could be in the Middleham area, or he could have travelled to Northern Ireland.

If you think you might have seen Kelly, or have any information as to his whereabouts, please contact police.

Dial 101, press 1 and pass information to the Force Control Room.
