Last modified: 29 June 2020 at 12:46pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an altercation that occurred on York cycle track near to Heworth village.

It happened on York cycle track near to Heworth Village at approximately 1pm on 12 June 2020 when a man in his thirties threatened another man with a bladed article.

The suspect is described as a man in his thirties, 6'3″ to 6'4″ tall and of slim build. He had short shaven dark hair with a dark goatee style beard. He was wearing a grey jacket, grey jogging shorts and trainers as well as a gold watch and gold ring.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jamie Gibson. You can also email Jamie.Gibson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200099184.
