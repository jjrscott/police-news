North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred in York City Centre.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident which occurred overnight between Tuesday 16 and Wednesday 17 May 2018. The commercial premises, located on the corner of New Street and Coney Street, has been accessed via the rooftop and entry forced through a skylight. The incident occurred between the hours of 17:30 - 07:30 and offenders have made off on foot with a number of small items and cash.

Police are appealing for information from anybody on New Street or Coney Street during this time, that has witnessed any suspicious behaviour or any persons present on the rooftop.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Alicia Oakes. You can also email alicia.oakes@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180086051.
