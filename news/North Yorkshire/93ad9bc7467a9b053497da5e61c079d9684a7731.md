North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a shop theft that occurred at the Matalan store on Clifton Moor in York.

At around 12.20pm on Monday 13 August 2018, a man and a woman have been captured on CCTV entering the store and selecting a large amount of items totalling in the region of £600.

They then left the store, without offering any form of payment.

Police are requesting the public’s assistance to help identify the man captured in the CCTV image, as they believe he has information which would assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises the man, or who has any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and ask to speak to Police Constable Johnathon Weeks, collar number 0541. Please quote reference number 12180150872.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
