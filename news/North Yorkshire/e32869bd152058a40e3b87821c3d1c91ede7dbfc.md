North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic collision that occurred on Wharfedale Place in Harrogate.

It happened at 12:30pm on 12/04/15 and involved a Red '05' plate Ford Fiesta which collided with a parked vehicle before leaving the scene.

We are appealing to the driver of the vehicle and to anyone who witnessed the collision or can help identify the driver or occupants of the red car, to come forward.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask forStephen.Ayres@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150059821.
