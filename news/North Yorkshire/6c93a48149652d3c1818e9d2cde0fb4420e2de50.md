Last modified: 24 December 2019 at 11:45am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from an unattended motor vehicle that occurred in the Rawcliffe area of York.

It happened on Shelley Grove at 7.25pm on Wednesday 18 12 December 2019 and involved two male suspects stealing £450 worth of power tools from a parked van.

The suspects made off in a white panel van.

We are requesting the public’s assistance, in particular we are appealing for information to establish the identity of the suspects involved and details of the vehicle they used during the incident.

The men were dressed in dark clothing with head and face coverings. They were driving a white panel van which approached from Rawcliffe Lane and left the area along Eastholme Drive in the direction of Bowness Drive.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Kristoffer Griffiths. You can also email kristoffer.griffiths@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190231917.
