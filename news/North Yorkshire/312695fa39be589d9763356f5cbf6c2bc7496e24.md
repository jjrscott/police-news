North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident of criminal damage to a Ford Focus car that occurred in Pickering.

It happened on Maudon Avenue overnight between Monday 13 April and Tuesday 14 April 2015.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help with identifying the offenders.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Anna Rees.

You can also email anna.rees@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number nyp-14042015-0130
