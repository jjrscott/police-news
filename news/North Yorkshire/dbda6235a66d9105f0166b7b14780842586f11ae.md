A man suffered a broken jaw after he was punched to the ground in an assault in York,

It happened at around 2.50am on Saturday on the footpath between York Cabs and the Subway shop in Blossom Street.

The offender then left the scene. No description is available.

Anyone who witnessed the assault should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference 12190015411. You can also email Abid.Tarvaz@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
