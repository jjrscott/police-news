North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after antiques were stolen from a Pickering shop.

It happened at Pickering Antiques on Southgate at approximately 1:25pm on the 6th March 2015 and involved two men entering the store and one of them gaining entry to a display cabinet before selecting numerous items of valuable china and walking out of the store without making payment.

The first suspect is described as white, aged between 35-45, of slim build with short dark hair wearing a suit, shirt and tie and sunglasses.

The second suspect is described as white, aged 60-70, medium build with grey hair.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Stephen Ashley You can also email stephen.ashley@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
