North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following the theft of a mini excavator from Thirsk Industrial Park.

It happened on York Road between 3.30pm and 5.30pm on Sunday 26 March 2017 and involved a white long wheel based van and two men, one dressed in all black clothing and white footwear and the second dressed in a light grey hoody, dark bottoms and white footwear.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious activity on York Road or Sutton Road, Thirsk at around 3.30pm or if you saw a white van parked in the layby on Sutton Road.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andy Grainge. You can also email andy.grainge@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170051467.
