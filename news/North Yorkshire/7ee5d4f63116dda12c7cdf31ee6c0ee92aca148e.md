North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an arson that occurred on Manor Road , Pickering

It happened at the rear of a property on Manor Road at around midnight on Sunday 7 February 2016 and involved a shed at the rear of a terraced property being set alight , and totally destroyed by fire .

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious persons or vehicles in the area of Manor Roaad during the early hours of Monday 8th February 2016.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1278 Linda Coles. You can also email linda.coles@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160022981
