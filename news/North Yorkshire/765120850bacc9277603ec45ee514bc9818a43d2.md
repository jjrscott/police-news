Officers are currently in attendance on the A66 at Richmond following a collision between a lorry and a car.

The incident occurred on the westbound carriageway at around 12.30pm.

Both carriageways are currently closed in the immediate area while emergency crews attend the scene and motorists are advised to find alternative routes.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or the events surrounding it are asked to contact North Yorkshire Police quoting reference NYP-19052017-0177.
