North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses to contact them following a theft from a Coop supermarket in Scarborough

A male entered the store on Newlands Park Drive, Scarborough at around 5.30pm on Sunday 13 September 2015 and allegedly stole a quantity of meat.

Enquiries are currently ongoing and police are requesting anyone who witnessed the alleged theft to contact them to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, they are keen to speak to a customer who alerted staff.

Anyone with information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Belinda Williamson. You can also email belinda.williamson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150161748.
