North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Road Traffic Collision that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Coldyhill Lane at the junction with Hirstead Road at 6.30pm on Sunday 24th May 2015. The collision involved a car and a motor cycle and the motor cycle was found to be stolen.

Two people were riding the motor cycle at the time and both made off on foot prior to police arrival.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the two people who were riding the motor cycle.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1148 Martin Pullan. You can also email martin.pullan@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150086066.
