Northallerton police have issued photographs of some property which they suspect has been stolen.

View image

The items have been recovered as part of an ongoing investigation into burglaries and officers want to reunite them with their rightful owners.

The items include:
•Two statues of a small boy and girl with their eyes closed and their hands behind their back.
•A bronze statue of a girl and a boy dressed in 1930s - 1940s clothing, the boy is wearing a flat cap.
•Numerous commemorative coins.

If you have recently suffered a burglary and believe that the items are yours, please contact North Yorkshire Police.

Please call 101, select option 2 and ask for Northallerton CID.

Please quote reference 12140167430 when passing on information.

If you have any information about any burglaries, you can also pass information anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
