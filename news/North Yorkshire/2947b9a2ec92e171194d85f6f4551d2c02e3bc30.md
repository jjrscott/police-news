North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic incident that occurred on the on the A1237 York ring road.

It happened between the A59 roundabout and the B1224 roundabout near Knapton at approximately 8.20pm on Wednesday 4 January 2017 involving a grey Landrover Discovery and a black Toyota Yaris.

The two occupants of the Toyota were taken to hospital by ambulance with serious injuries. The Landrover driver escaped uninjured.

Sgt Paul Cording of the Force Roads Policing Group said “If you saw either of the vehicles prior to the collision or witnessed the collision itself then please contact us”.

If you have any information, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for the York Major Collision Investigation Team. You can also email MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170002169.
