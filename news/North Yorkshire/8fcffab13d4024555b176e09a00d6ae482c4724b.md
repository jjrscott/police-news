North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary at farm outbuildings that occurred at a premises near Ugglebarnby, near Whitby.

It happened between midnight and 8am on Tuesday 14 April 2015 and involved entry being forcefully gained to several outbuildings on the farm.

Several items of property were stolen including a Stihl Strimmer and a Dewalt Drill.

We are appealing for information from anyone who knows about the burglary, is offered the stolen goods for sale or who knows where they are now.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Nicola Knowles.

You can also email nicola.knowles@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150060932.
