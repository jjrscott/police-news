Last modified: 5 August 2019 at 11:45am

Officers from North Yorkshire Police are investigating a burglary which took place overnight at the Thomas the Baker shop in Market Street, York.

The shop was broken into sometime between 4.30pm yesterday (4 August) and 4am today.

It is believed an unknown amount of cash was stolen from a safe within the premises.

Police are appealing for anyone who saw or heard anything suspicious in the area last night or early this morning to call 101 and quote reference number 12190143045.

If you wish to remain anonymous you can contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or online at crimestoppers-uk.org

Please quote reference number 12190143045 when passing on information.
