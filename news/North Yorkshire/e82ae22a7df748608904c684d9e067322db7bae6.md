North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a motor vehicle in Scarborough.

The victim parked their car, a black AUDI A4 S-LINE on Marine Drive close to the Scarborough Water Work Pump Station on the Castle headland at 18:00 hours on Saturday 9th July 2016. On returning to their vehicle at 19:30 hours, the vehicle was no longer there and had been stolen.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, and quote reference NYP-09072016-05313.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
