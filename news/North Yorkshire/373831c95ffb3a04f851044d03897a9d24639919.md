Police in York are appealing for witnesses and information about an incident of criminal damage at Costcutter on Allerton Drive, Nether Poppleton, York, on Tuesday 27 October 2020 at approximately 7.30pm. This is following antisocial behavior inside the the shop when a group of teenagers have been asked to put masks on by the shop staff and have then caused damage to the shop window.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the identities of those responsible.

The teenage boy who kicked the door is described as white with pale skin, aged about 16, clean shaven, around 5ft 9in tall and with a slim build. He was wearing a dark hooded "puffa" jacket with his hood up, and light grey loose-fitting jogging bottoms.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Harry Mallett. You can also email harry.mallett@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
