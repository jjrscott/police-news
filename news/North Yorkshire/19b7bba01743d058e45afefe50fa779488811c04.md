Police in Scarborough are appealing for information after chain saws were stolen from a shop on Roscoe Street.

It happened at Dobson Garden Machinery between 1am and 3am in the early hours of 31 October 2018 and it is believed that two or three suspects in a white, light goods van were involved.

Officers are asking anyone who has any information or saw any suspicious activity in the area in the early hours of Wednesday morning to contact them.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1997 FORSYTH. You can also email Matthew.Forsyth1997@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180203363.
