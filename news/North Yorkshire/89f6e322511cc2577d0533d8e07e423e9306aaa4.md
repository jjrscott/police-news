North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary in Northallerton.

It happened on Mowbray Road at sometime between 6.30pm and 11pm on 8 May 2015.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about two men, who were seen in the area around the time of the break-in.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Northallerton Investigation Hub.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150076156.
