Last modified: 17 May 2017 at 01:27pm

Police have issued CCTV footage of two men they would like to speak to following a serious assault on two men in York earlier this month.

At about 4.15am on Monday 1 May 2017 an altercation occurred in the Castlegate area of York involving two men being attacked by a group of men and women.

Both men were found unconscious with serious injuries. One of the men was taken to York Hospital for treatment where he remained for two nights.

One of the men that police would like to speak to is described as white, with dark short hair, of muscular build and was wearing a dark T-shirt, blue jeans and black Adidas trainers.

The second man is described as white, smaller in height, average build, with dark short hair. He was wearing a black shirt, blue jeans with lighter colour patches above knees.

Officers are urgently appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident, or was in the area at the time, to get in touch.

In particular, they are appealing to anyone who can help identify the two men in the CCTV stills to come forward.

If you have any information which could assist the investigation please dial 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to Amanda Bostwick or email Amanda.Bostwick@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170074267 when passing on any information.
