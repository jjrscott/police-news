North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Newton on Ouse, York.

It happened in Newton On Ouse between 2100hours on 25/08/15 to 0815hours on 26/08/15.

In particular, we are appealing for information about three high value bikes.

1) E Bike STOR

Black and White with a white frame

Serial Number - KA60335150

Value £3000

2) TREK bike 7.7KX

20inch frame

Black frame

Serial Number - WTU162G0166F

Value - £1500

3) TREK bike

Orange Wheels, Grey frame

Serial Number - WTU123D0469E

Value - £800

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Lucy Tate. You can also email Lucy.Tate@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150149851.
