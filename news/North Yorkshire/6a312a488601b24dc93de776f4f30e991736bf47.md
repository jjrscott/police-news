North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Sutton In Craven.

It happened on Main Street, Sutton in Craven between 00:00hrs - 04:00hrs on Wednesday 6th july 2016 and involved unknown offender/s who enter via the back door and removed a Samsung television from the lounge.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 352 Michael Smith You can also email Michael.Smith352@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160118974.
