Police are appealing for information to locate the family of a man who died in Scarborough last week.

James Hutchinson, 74, from Scarborough, died on Saturday 14 December 2019. There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Police enquiries to date have not revealed any next of kin for him. They may be in the London area.

Family members of Mr Hutchinson, or anyone who can help contact them, are asked to contact the Coroners Office at North Yorkshire Police on 01609 643168.
