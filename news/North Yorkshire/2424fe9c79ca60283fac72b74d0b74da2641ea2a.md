North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred in Cross hills, Keighley.

It happened on Skipton Road at approximately 4am on Sunday 27 September 2020 and involved the Co-op Petrol Station being broken into and cash tills being stolen from the premises.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any person seen acting suspiciously on Skipton Road or the Kildwick roundabout on the A629 in or around that time.

The suspect is described as male, of large build and was wearing a black hoody, with hood up and dark blue jeans. He was possibly carrying a sledge hammer and / or long black metal boxes believed to be the till drawers from the premises.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should email christopher.bullock@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for 1493 Chris Bullock.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200169838.
