North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Ripon.

It happened at Ripon City Football Club between 1pm and 7.30pm on Tuesday 8 September 2020 and involved a door to the storage shed being forced open and bike that was being stored inside was stolen.

The stolen bike is described as being a grey Marin mountain bike with full suspension. The bike has sellotape on the handles with RockShox front suspension.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we would like to speak to a male and female, aged between approx. 13-18 years old, who were seen close to the shed where the incident took place just prior to the time of the report.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Ryan Rudd. You can also email ryan.rudd@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200157572.
