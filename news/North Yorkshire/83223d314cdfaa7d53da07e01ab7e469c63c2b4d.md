Police are appealing for information to help identify the men in the CCTV images.

Officers want to speak to the men in connection with a purse theft from a café in Thirsk.

The incident happened at around 1pm on Saturday 3 September 2016, when an elderly woman has withdrawn an amount of cash from a cashpoint on the High Street in Thirsk and placed her purse in her shipping trolley.

The woman has then gone to the White Horse Café in the Market Place. At the café, the woman has been distracted by a man, while another man stole the purse from the woman’s shopping trolley.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to Safer Neighbourhood Service Desk or e-mail SNservicedeskSNSD@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160160239 when passing on any information.
