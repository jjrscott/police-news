Police are appealing to trace the owner of a JCB Mini Excavator and IFOR Williams Trailer which are believed to have been stolen.

Police stopped a vehicle that was towing the JCB and trailer at around half past midnight today, Wednesday 23 March 2016, on the A63 at Monk Fryston.

A 34 year old man form the Castleford Area was arrested in connection with the incident and remains in police custody pending further enquiries.

Anybody with any information that could assist the investigation is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press option 2 and ask to speak to PC 192 Jill Rawson at the York Investigation Hub.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160050269 when passing on information about the incident.
