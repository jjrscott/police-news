North Yorkshire Police is appealing for anyone who has been offered Jewellery stolen from a property in Great Ayton.

Stolen during a Burglary between 11/03/2016 and 13/03/16 from Great Ayton are two items of jewellery that have significant sentimental value:

One White gold wedding band with the words MIZPAH engraved on the inside.

One gold locket containing black & white photos of victims parents.

A suspect has been identified, arrested and charged with this offence however the jewellery has not been recovered

If you have any information which could assist the investigation then call North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to DC 911 Matthew Mitchell or email matthew.mitchell@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers annonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160044145.
