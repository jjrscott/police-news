North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary which occurred at 14 Rainbow Lane , Malton.

It happened at about 12.30pm on 30 March 2016 at where a white male, aged approx 18 years of age was seen attempting to gain entry to 14 Rainbow Lane via an insecure bathroom window. Police are appealing for any information which would assist them in establishing his identity.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andy Grant. You can also email Andy.Grant@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Thursday 31 March 2016
