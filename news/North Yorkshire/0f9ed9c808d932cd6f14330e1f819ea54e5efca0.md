Officers are appealing for information after damage was caused to a window at McDonalds on Dunslow Road in Eastfield.

Officers are appealing for information after damage was caused to a window at McDonalds on Dunslow Road in Eastfield.

The window was damaged by a young man at around 6.30pm on Thursday, 21 February.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who knows the identity of the person responsible is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police with information.

Anyone who can assist with the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Colin Irvine. Alternatively you can provide information by emailing colin.irvine@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190032810.
