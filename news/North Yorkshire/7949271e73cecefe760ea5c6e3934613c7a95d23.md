North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in York on Hull Road.

The incident took place at around 11pm on Tuesday 31 July 2018 on Hull Road in York near the Beeswing Pub and resulted in a taxi driver being assaulted.

The driver has not suffered from any injuries, but he is very shook up by the incident.

Police would like to speak to three men, who had got into his car asking for a lift, who may have information which would assist the investigation.

The only information that is known about the suspects is that they were three white males, one of which was wearing a white T-shirt.

Anyone who was in the area at the time, who may have witnessed the incident, or anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1983 Lucy Watson. You can also email Lucy.Watson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180140829
