Officers are appealing for witnesses and information relating to a serious collision which occurred on the A629 yesterday (Wednesday, 6 June).

Officers are appealing for witnesses and information relating to a serious collision which occurred on the A629 yesterday (Wednesday, 6 June).

The collision occurred between Skipton and Kildwick at the Cononley Lane end when a silver and grey vehicle collided at around 8.15pm.

The silver car was travelling from the direction of Kildwick towards Skipton and collided with the grey vehicle which was travelling in the opposite direction.

It is also believed that there may have also been a red vehicle was involved in the incident.

Officers are asking anyone who either witnessed the incident or the manner of driving of the vehicles prior to the collision to contact them with information. Officers are also keen to speak with anyone driving in the area around the time of the incident and who has dash cam footage which may help the investigation to contact them.

Anyone who can assist officers with their investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 quoting reference 12180100373.
