North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic collision that occurred on A65 between Clapham and Austwick involving a van and a cyclist.

The van failed to stop at the scene of the incident.

It happened at about 1.35pm on Thursday 27 September 2018 and involved a 68-year-old male cyclist and a white van.

The cyclist was taken by ambulance to Lancaster Royal Infirmary with a suspected back injury after being knocked from his cycle.

The driver of the van failed to stop after the incident.

Police are appealing for witnesses to this collision that may have dash cam footage of the van involved, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

The stretch of road remained open during the police investigation.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Burgess. You can also email David.Burgess@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180180922
