North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft and subsequent fraudulent use of a bank card in York.

The bank card was taken from the victims handbag when it was left unattended in the study room of the Spring Lane Building at the University of York on Thursday 10 May 2018.

The card was then used on a number of occasions over the next day (Friday 11 May 2018) to purchase items totalling over Â£100 from Tesco in Low Ousegate, Morrisons at Foss Islands and Sports Direct and Poundland at Monks Cross.

Police are wanting to speak to the man capture on the CCTV footage, as officers believe he may hold information which would assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured, or who has information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to PC Rout. You can also email 000536@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180085623.
