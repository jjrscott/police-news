Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:16am

Police are appealing for witnesses after a vulnerable man was assaulted on Cockpit Hill in Brompton, Northallerton.

The incident occurred at around 3.50am on Saturday 2 June 2018 as the 40-year-old victim was walking along Cockpit Hill.

He was set upon by two men who punched and kicked him to the ground during which he sustained cuts and bruises to his head and body.

The offenders are described as both being adult white men. One is around 5ft 9in tall with light-coloured hair and wearing a t-shirt and grey shorts. The other was dressed all in dark clothing.

Officers are investigating the circumstances of the incident which appears at this time to have been an unprovoked attack.

They are appealing for witnesses to come forward along with anyone who may be able to identify the two suspects.

If you can help, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Graeme Gale. Or email graeme.gale@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.

Please quote reference number 12180097139 when providing details about this incident.
