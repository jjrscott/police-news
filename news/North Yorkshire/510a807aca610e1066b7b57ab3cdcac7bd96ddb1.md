Police are investigating an altercation in York in which a 24 year old man suffered facial injuries that required hospital treatment.

The incident happened at 7.10pm on Friday 17 March 2017, on Barfield Road in York and involved three men.

It is believed that there were a number of witnesses present during the time of the incident and officers would like these witnesses to come forward.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press option 2 and ask to speak to PC 192 Jill Barnard or email jill.barnard@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote crime reference number 12170045423 when passing on information about the incident.
