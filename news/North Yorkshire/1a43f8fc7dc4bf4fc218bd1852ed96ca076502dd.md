Police have released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to following the theft of a case of cider from the Co-op convenience store at the Esso petrol station on Harrogate Road, Ripon.

The man was seen to enter the store at approx 10.30pm on Friday 15 September 2017. He selected a case of cider and left the store without making an attempt to pay for the items.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured or who has information which could assist the investigation is asked to contact Gary Anderson on 101 or email Gary.Anderson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170166250

Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.
