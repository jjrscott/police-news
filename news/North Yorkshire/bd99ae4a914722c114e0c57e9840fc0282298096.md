Over recent days Police in Ryedale have recovered items they suspect to be stolen but with no owner identified.

Over the past 2 weeks items of property have been recovered by Police in the Ryedale area. These items include a petrol generator, handheld cement mixer, hydraulic nail gun, a small air compressor and an angle grinder.

Police ask that local tradespeople and residents check their own property and tools are where they should be and report any missing items to the police quoting reference number 12170004966 .
