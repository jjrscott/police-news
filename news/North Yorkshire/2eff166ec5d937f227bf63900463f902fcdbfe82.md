North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage to a vehicle that occurred in Skipton.

It happened on DUCKETT STREET SKIPTON at around 01:30 hours on 07/03/15 and involved a stone being thrown through the window of a parked vehicle,

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from a male and female who have possibly witnessed the incident who were in the area at the time.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1771 Andrew Hanks You can also email andrew.hanks@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150037352
