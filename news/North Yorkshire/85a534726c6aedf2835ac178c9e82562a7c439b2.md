Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a serious sexual assault in York in the early hours of Sunday 10 April 2016.

The incident happened at around 2-2.30am on Haxby Road at the junction of Kitchener Street.

The offender is described as a man in his early thirties, with a bald head, stubble on his face and wearing a dark coloured shirt or top.

The victim, a woman in her twenties from York, is being supported by specialist officers.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who was in the area of Haxby and Kitchener Road between the hours of 1.30am and 2.30am is asked to contact police.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to York Serious Crime Team.

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
