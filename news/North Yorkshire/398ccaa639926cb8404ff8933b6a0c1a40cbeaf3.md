Scarborough police are appealing for information after damage was caused to a grey Vauxhall Corsa parked on Grosvernor Road.

Overnight between 6pm on Tuesday 31 March and 8am on Wednesday 1 April 2015, the offenders attempted to prise open the driver and passenger doors of the vehicle, damaging both of them in the process.

Police are appealing to anyone who has any information that could help their investigation to contact them, in particular, if you saw anyone loitering around vehicles at the time or have any information that can assist Scarborough Police in tracking down the offender(s).

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jon Kenworthy. You can also email jonathon.kenworthy@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
