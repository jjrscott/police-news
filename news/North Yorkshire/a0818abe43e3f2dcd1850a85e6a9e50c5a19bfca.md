Police executed a warrant under the Misuse of Drugs Act just before 1pm today at a property in Willow Glade, Huntington, York.

A cannabis grow was located in a garage, which officers made safe and dismantled.

A 30-year-old woman was arrested on suspicion of cannabis production. She remains in custody at this time.
