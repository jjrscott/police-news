Police are appealing for information following a collision on A19 at Riccall on Monday 20 July 2020.

The incident happened shortly before 2pm north of Riccall near Hollicarrs, and involved a white Vauxhall combo (small van) and a pedestrian.

Sadly the pedestrian, a 25 year old man from York, was pronounced dead at the scene.

The road remained closed while emergency services worked at the scene. It re-opened at 8.30pm.

Anyone with any information is asked to dial 101, press 2 and ask for Gemma Brett. Alternatively, please email gemma.brett@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12200123843 when passing on any information.
