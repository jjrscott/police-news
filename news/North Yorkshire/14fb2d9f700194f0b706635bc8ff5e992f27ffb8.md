North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Skipton.

The assault occurred at 5.30pm on Monday 12 November 2018 near to the mini roundabout on Keighley Road, Skipton (at the junction with the Snaygill Industrial Estate).

The victim, a 15-year-old boy, suffered a broken nose and a cut lip as a result of the assault. The victim attended Airedale Hospital where he was treated and discharged following treatment.

In particular, we are appealing for any members of the public who may have witnessed this incident to come forward.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
