Last modified: 7 November 2018 at 10:10am

Police are appealing for witnesses to an altercation in York that left a man in hospital.

They were called to reports of a number of men fighting the Clifton Hotel public house, on Water Lane.

It happened at around 7.45pm on Friday (2 November). One man in his 20s was taken to hospital with injuries that were not believed to be serious.

A man in his 40s was arrested in connection with the incident and bailed while enquiries continue.

A short time later, there was a disturbance outside McColls shop on Crichton Avenue, which police believe may be linked.

A man in his 20s was arrested in connection with the second incident and bailed while enquiries continue.

Officers are asking for anyone who witnessed either incident, or has information that could help their enquiries, to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Investigation Hub, York.

Please quote reference numbers 12180205421 in relation to the first incident and 12180205330 for the second.
