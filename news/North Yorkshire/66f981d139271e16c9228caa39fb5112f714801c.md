North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Theft that occurred in B&M; Bargains in Scarborough

It happened at B&M Bargins on Seamer Road Retail Park at 1630 hours on Tuesday 10th March 2015. The suspect is believed to be a black or mixed race man aged between 20-25 wearing a dark blue hooded top and dark coloured jogging bottoms.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help identify the suspect pictured.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Ben Brittain. You can also email ben.brittain@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150039564.
