Here is our statement in relation to video released by the League Against Cruel Sports relating to alleged illegal hunting in the Buttercrambe area.

A police spokeswoman said:

"North Yorkshire Police received video footage from the League Against Cruel Sports on the morning of Tuesday 26 January 2016 relating to alleged illegal hunt activity in the Buttercrambe area and will be investigating the matter."

26 January 2016
