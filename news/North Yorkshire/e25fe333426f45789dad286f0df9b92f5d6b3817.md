Police investigating a collision which occurred on Sunday 16 October near Keighley, are appealing for witnesses.

The collision happened at 12.25pm on the A6068 between Cowling and Glusburn and involved a Silver VW Polo and a white Mercedes. Both vehicles were travelling in the direction of Glusburn.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, or who saw either vehicle prior to the collision, is asked to contact police. In addition, officers would like anyone who saw a white VW Golf travelling in the opposite direction to get in touch. Officers are also keen to trace a Large Goods Vehicle which was in the area at the time and the driver may have vital information.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to TC Mark Brook, or email Mark.Brook@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number NYP-16102016-0427 when passing on any information.
