North Yorkshire Police are appealing for the owner of a Karcher jet wash to come forward after it was recovered in the Barlow area, near Selby, on Monday 17 December 2018.

The item is believed to have been stolen recently.

If you think the jet wash is yours or you know who it belongs to, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Craig Davies or Dave Pegg. Please quote reference number 12180233326.
