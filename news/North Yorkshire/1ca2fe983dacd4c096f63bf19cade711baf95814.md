This is an update following the incident in Knaresborough reported at 8.48am on the morning of Saturday 9 May 2020.

To clarify, the location was on Farnham Lane between Boroughbridge Road and Farnham village, although Borougbridge Road was closed for a short period of time.

Officers responded to initial reports of what was believed to be a collision after a man was found collapsed at the side of the road. Sadly the man was pronounced dead at the scene .

Police are investigating the circumstances surrounding the incident but at this stage there is no suggestion any vehicle has been involved and no suspicious circumstances.

A post mortem is due to be carried out to establish the cause of the manâ€™s death which will help to inform further inquiries.

Or email Andrew.morton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk please quote incident number 97 of 9 May.
