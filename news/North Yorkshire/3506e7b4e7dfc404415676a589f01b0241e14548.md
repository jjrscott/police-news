Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:04am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Skipton town centre on Saturday night.

It happened at Coopers Café, Belmont Street, Skipton some time overnight of Saturday 11 and Sunday 12 August 2018, before 1.20am. This involved the offender(s) smashing a window to gain entry and the till being stolen from inside.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for anybody who may have witnessed any suspicious activity around the Belmont Bridge area from around midnight onwards.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Martin Carson. You can also email Martin.Carson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-12082018-0056
