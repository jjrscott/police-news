Last modified: 22 March 2021 at 04:58pm

Police have issued CCTV of a man they would like to speak to following a theft in Whitby.

It happened at Harrison's Garage, Upgang Lane at 3pm on 7 February and involved a man stealing several packets of cigarettes.

Officers are asking members of the public to get in touch if they recognise the person in the images as they believe they may have information that will help the investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC 1212 Williams or email marie.williams1212@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12210054906 when passing on information.
