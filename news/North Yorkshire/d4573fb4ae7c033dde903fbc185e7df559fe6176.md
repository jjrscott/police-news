Police are appealing for information following an assault and criminal damage in Harrogate.

In the early hours of Thursday 6th August 2015 a male was assaulted on Century Walk, Harrogate leaving him with facial injuries. There was also significant damage caused to his property.

A male was arrested at the time however was bailed pending further enquiries being carried out.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from people who witnessed the assault or heard any disturbance at the time

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 311 George Frost or email George.frost@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150135726
