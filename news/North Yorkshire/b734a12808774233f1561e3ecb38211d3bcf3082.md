North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information about a group of people riding motorcycles on the road and pavement, without lights or number plates, in Knaresborough.

At 11.45pm Saturday 8 December 2018 around the Halfpenny Lane, Hay-A-Park estate area of Knaresborough, three people were seen riding motorbikes on the road and pavement without lights or registration plates, causing a danger to legitimate road users and pedestrians and causing a noise nuisance.

Police are appealing for information to determine who the riders are and are appealing for information from any witnesses to the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC187 Marie Scott. You can also email marie.scott@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180228585.
