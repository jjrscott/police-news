North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Norton near Malton

It happened on Welham Road in Norton between 1520 hours on Saturday 4th July 2015 and Wednesday 8th July 2015 at approx 1000am. Entry was gained to the property by a side window being smashed.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Emma Collins. You can also email emma.collins@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150115601.
