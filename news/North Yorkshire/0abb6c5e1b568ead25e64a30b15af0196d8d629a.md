Police are appealing for information after a teenager was approached and spoken to inappropriately by a man driving a small white van in Hellifield, near Skipton.

The 16-year-old victim was walking on the pavement next to the A65 / Otterburn Road in Hellifield on the evening of Monday 12 November 2018. She was left extremely shaken by the incident.

Officers are urging anyone who witnessed the incident, or has any information about it, to get in touch. They are particularly keen to speak to any drivers with an active dash cam who were driving through Hellifield on Monday between 5.10pm and 5.20pm.

Anyone with information should call North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference 12180212498.
