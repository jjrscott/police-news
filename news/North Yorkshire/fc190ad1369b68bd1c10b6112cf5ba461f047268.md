Last modified: 8 November 2018 at 12:13pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Skipton.

The burglary occurred at a residential property on Jubilee Close between 4.35pm and 7.40pm on Wednesday 7 of November 2018 when the offender/s broke in by breaking the lock of the rear door.

Officers are appealing for information, in particular, about a suspicious silver car that was sighted on Jubilee Close at approx. 1pm earlier that day. The vehicle left and was seen to return to the street a short time later with the occupants paying attention to houses within the cul-de-sac.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Gasson. You can also email david.gasson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180208660.
