North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a child's push bike and Scooter that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Victoria road between 14:30hrs and 16:00hrs on 28.06.2018.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the potential whereabouts of the stolen items.
