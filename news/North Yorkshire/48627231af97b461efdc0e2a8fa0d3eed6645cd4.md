Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:24am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about burglary that occurred Camblesforth.

It happened at Selby Salads/"P3P" Brigg Lane Ltd at Brigg Lane, Camblesforth at approximately 9pm on 13 March 2018.

Offenders removed a window of the premises and cut through a wall to obtain access. A large amount of copper cable was cut and removed from the site. Offenders would have required a vehicle to remove the cable from the locality.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Alison Baker. You can also email alison.baker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-14032018-0162
