Police are appealing for witnesses after significant disorder during Wednesday night's football match between Whitby Town and FC United of Manchester.

Police are appealing for witness after significant disorder during Wednesday night's football match between Whitby Town and FC United of Manchester.

The disorder predominantly involved the home supporters, with flares being set off and thrown into the crowd and onto the pitch.

Officers believe that the flares were hidden inside the Turnbull Ground at some point before the match.

An 18-year-old local man has been arrested in connection with the disorder and the police are working to identify other people who were involved.

Anyone who was at the match and witnessed the disorder is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police with information.

In particular, officers would like to hear from anyone who filmed or took photos of the disorder.

If you have any footage, please send it via email to rebecca.lalor@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

You can provide information about the disorder by calling North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for Sgt Rebecca Lalor or Whitby police.

If you prefer not to give you name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Young people can report information anonymously online at https://www.fearless.org/give-info-anonymously

Please quote reference number 12150053066 when passing information about this incident.
