Last modified: 9 May 2018 at 10:40am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Kirkbymoorside.

It happened on West End at approximately between 11.15am and 11.40am on 5 May 2018, and involved an unidentified man, who smashed a side window gaining entry to the premises and then made off on foot.

Later the suspect was seen to discard his clothing in a nearby hedge on the A170 and get into a silver small vehicle possibly a Ford Fiesta (new shape) car.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Emma Collins. You can also email emma.collins@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180077692.
