North Yorkshire Police is appealing for the owner of two cycles to contact them.

The first appeal is to the owner of a cycle whose wheel was removed when it was locked up at St Andrewgate cycle racks in York city centre overnight of Tuesday 2nd August to Wednesday 3rd August, to contact them.

The second appeal is to the owner of a red and black Trek 4000 which has been recovered and is currently in police possession.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC112 DRAYCOTT. You can also email roseanne.draycott@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-03082016-0023.

Date 3 August 2016
