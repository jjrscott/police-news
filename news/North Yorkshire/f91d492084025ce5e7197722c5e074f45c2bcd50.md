Last modified: 9 May 2018 at 10:42am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after fuel was syphoned from six wagons parked at Gallowfields Industrial Estate in Richmond.

The incidents happened on two occasions between Thursday 24 August and Monday 28 August 2017. On each occasion, damage was caused to the fuel tanks and a large quantity of fuel was syphoned from the vehicles.

Police are appealing to anyone who saw any suspicious vehicles, people or activity in the area over the weekend or has any other information that could assist the investigation to contact them.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should call 101, select option 2, and ask for PC James Bowie. You can also email james.bowie@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170153576.
