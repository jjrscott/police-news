North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a violent assault that occurred in York.

It happened near Burdyke Avenue in York approximately 03.30hrs on Sunday 17 March 2019 and involved the assault of a 31 year-old man. The man was treated at hospital with a fractured cheekbone and serious wounds to the back of his head requiring staples before being discharged later that day.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the assault which took place as the man was cycling near to Burdyke Avenue in Clifton.

The offenders are believed to be a group of two males and one female under 30.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact York CID on 01609 643279. You can also email CIDYork@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
