Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a spate of criminal damage to cars on Bondgate Green, Ripon.

The incidents occurred 10 January 2017 between 3pm and 5pm. A number of motor vehicles that were parked at this location were scratched causing damage to the body work.

We are conducting enquiries and are appealing to anyone that witnessed these incidents to contact the police.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1720 Dave Furlong . You can also email david.furlong@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170005314
