North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a collision involving a cyclist and car earlier this month.

The incident occurred on Monkgate, York at around 3.30pm on Wednesday, 12 September.

The cyclist was taken to hospital for treatment to their injuries but has since been released.

Anyone who has information on the collision or the events surrounding it is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, selecting option 2 and ask for PC Ellison. You can also email david.ellison0041@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12180174019 when providing information.
