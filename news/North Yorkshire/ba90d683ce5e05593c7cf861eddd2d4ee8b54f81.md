North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Robbery that occurred on Walkers Passage, Harrogate, between Park View and Park Parade.

The incident happened at around 9.30pm on Sunday 21 August 2016, when the victim was walking from the Stray side of Walkers Passage towards the town centre when he was approached by a man on a bicycle who skidded to a stop, blocking the victims path before threatening him with a knife.

The offender is described as a white man, aged 20-25 years old, thin build, clean shaven with short dark hair and spoke with a local accent. He was wearing dark trousers and a light coloured top and was riding a mountain bike with a light coloured/yellow bike frame. The knife is described as a 7-8 inch black handled kitchen knife.

CCTV enquiries have been carried out and there is a sighting of a man matching the description close to where the offence took place in the minutes prior to the incident.
