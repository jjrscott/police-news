The family of the the 46-year-old man who died recently in York has been located following an appeal. Thank you to all who shared our appeal.

**Original release: Appeal to trace York man’s next of kin**

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for help to find the next of kin of a man who recently died in York.

Colin Meggitt, sadly passed away at his home address on Windsor Garth in Acomb aged 46. His death is not being treated as suspicious.

It is believed that Mr Meggitt volunteered at the Scope charity shop in Acomb. He may also have lived in or around #Pocklington at some point prior to 2008.

If you have any information about Mr Meggitt or his family please contact the Police Coroner’s Office on 01609 643123.
