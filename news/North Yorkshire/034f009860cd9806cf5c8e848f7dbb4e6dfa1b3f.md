The incident happened sometime between 1 and 7 September 2015 when the owners of the property were on holiday. Offenders gained entry by smashing a conservatory door.

A number of high value items were stolen in the burglary, including a limited edition Brietling GBW Superocean white chronographic watch, marked as 09 of 100. The watch, serial number 899162, is worth around £7,500. Several items of jewellery were also taken.

An amount of cash, including £250 cash float belonging to a registered local pre-school charity raised through fundraising events, was also stolen.

Anyone with any information which could assist the investigation is asked to contact police as a matter of urgency. Dial 101, press option 1 and pass information on to the force control room.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150157906 when passing on information about this incident.

Thursday 10 September.
