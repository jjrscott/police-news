North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an Arson that occurred at the rear of Cash convertor and British Heart Foundation charity shop, Gowthorpe, Selby in the early hours of Tuesday 18th August 2015.

During the early hours of Tuesday 18th August 2015, offenders have entered the yard area of Cash Convertor and started a fire, causing significant damage to premises.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, police are appealing for any witnesses that were in the area of Gowthorpe and Finkle Street at around 4:25am on 18 August.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, press 1 and pass information onto the force control room.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Wednesday 19 August 2015.
