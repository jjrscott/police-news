North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a series of shed burglaries that occurred in Fadmoor and Gillamoor between 10pm on Sunday 11 January 2016 and 7am on Monday 12 January 2016.

It happened in the Boonhill road area and nearby at around 11.30pm and into the early hours at nearby addresses on Monday 11 January 2016 into Tuesday 12 January 2016 and involved several sheds being broken into and property stolen, one of the items stolen was a distinctive child's quad bike.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, and quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160006300.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
