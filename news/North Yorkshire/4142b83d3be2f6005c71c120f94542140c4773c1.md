Police investigating after a man was assaulted outside a nightclub in Northallerton have released CCTV footage of a number of witnesses they would like to trace.

View image

The group stopped to help the victim and officers are urging them to come forward as they may have information vital to the investigation.

The victim, a 23-year-old local man, sustained a cut and bruising to his eye in the incident which occurred at sometime between 3am and 3.30am on Sunday 16 November 2014.

Officers are appealing to the people shown in the CCTV footage and anyone else who witnessed the assault to come forward with information.

Anyone who can help the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for PC Peter MacMillan or Northallerton police.

Alternatively, information can be emailed to peter.macmillan@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you prefer not to give your name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12140198124 when passing information about this incident.
