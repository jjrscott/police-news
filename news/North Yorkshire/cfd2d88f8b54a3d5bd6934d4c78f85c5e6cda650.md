Last modified: 20 November 2019 at 04:33pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and dash-cam footage following a serious collision on the A64, near Malton.

It happened at around 9.34am, on Wednesday 20 November 2019, when a green-coloured Iveco wagon overturned on the eastbound carriageway.

The driver of the wagon was seriously injured and taken to hospital by air ambulance.

Police enquiries are ongoing and officers are appealing for witnesses to come forward to assist the investigation.

In particular, anyone who witnessed the collision, was travelling in the area at the time, or had dash-cam in operation prior to the collision is urged to contact them.

If you can help, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101 and pass information to the Force Control Room or email Road Policing Sergeant Neale Rees at Neale.Rees@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12190213454 when providing details about this incident.

The stretch of road was closed until approx. 4pm.
