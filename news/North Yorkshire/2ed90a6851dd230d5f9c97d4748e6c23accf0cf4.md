Last modified: 24 April 2019 at 04:54pm

North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary in Lothersdale near Cross Hills.

It happened close to the centre of the village between 6.00pm and 9.20pm on Tuesday 23rd April 2019 and involved entry being forced to the rear of a property.

A number of cameras, items of jewellery, gold "Elgin" pocket watches and other items were stolen.

Officers have released an image of a distinctive double-sided gold, necklace and are asking anyone who may have been offered it for sale, or has any other information at all regarding the break in, to please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 12190073452.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
