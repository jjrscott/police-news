North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a stolen Ford Transit Tipper Van in Yellow with Harrogate Borough Council livery, registration number YF57OZX. The van had a Yellow coloured SHLIESING 480EX Woodchipper in tow

The van was stolen from Fewston Crescent, Harrogate at approximately 11am on 7 october 2015.

The offender is describes as a white man, approximately 5 foot 11 inches in height with a slim face. He was wearing dark coloured trousers, a long sleeved dark coloured shirt and a black beanie hat.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting assistance form the public to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from any witnesses who may have seen this vehicle, or the man of the above description, in the area at the time of the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Brendon Firth, or email brendon.frith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
