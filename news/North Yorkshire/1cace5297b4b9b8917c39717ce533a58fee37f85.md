The man that officers wanted to speak to after a moped was damaged in York has now been identified.

Thank you to all who shared our appeal and provided information to assist the investigation.

**Original release: CCTV appeal after moped damaged in York**

York police are asking for the public's help to identify a man they want to speak to after a moped was damaged.

It happened on Blake Street at 5.24am on 7 October 2018 when the moped was kicked over, causing damage to it.

Anyone who can help identify the man is asked to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Ross Garside. You can also email ross.garside@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180187277
