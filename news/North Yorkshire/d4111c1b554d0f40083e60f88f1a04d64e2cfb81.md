North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a motor vehicle that occurred in Huntington, York.

A City of York council vehicle was stolen from outside a property on Cleveland Way in Huntington. It is believed the vehicle travelled through York between the times of 11.35pm to midnight on the Wednesday 23 May 2018. Police believe the vehicle has also travelled through the Tang Hall and Osbaldwick areas.

The vehicle is described as a "tipper" type van, white in colour with the City of York Council motif on it.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any sightings of the van around or near Metcalfe Lane, as well as any sighting of anyone acting suspiciously.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Jackson 425. You can also email nikki.jackson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-24052018-0043
