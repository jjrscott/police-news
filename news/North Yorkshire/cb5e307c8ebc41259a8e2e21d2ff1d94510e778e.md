Police in Ryedale are looking for information regarding a burglary at Pickering Recreation Club.

In the early hours of Wednesday 12 October 2016, the recreation club on Mill Lane in Pickering was broken into. A great deal of damage was caused by the offenders, who broke into gaming machines, pool tables and the bar area.

Anyone with any information is urged to contact Police. Dial 101, press 1 an pass information onto the force control room. Alternatively, anonymous information can be passed via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
