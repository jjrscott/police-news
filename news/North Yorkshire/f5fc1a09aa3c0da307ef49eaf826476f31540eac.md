Police are appealing for witnesses to a collision involving a car and motorbike.

The rider and pillion passenger were injured in the collision, which took place on the B6451 near Dob Park, Norwood, between Harrogate and Otley.

It happened at around 7.05pm on Thursday 13 July 2017 and involved a red Peugeot 2008 and a white Aprilia Dorsoduro 750 motorcycle.

Police are appealing for anyone who was in the locality at the time to contact them if they can provide information.

Please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for David Minto or email at david.minto@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk quoting reference 12170123032.
