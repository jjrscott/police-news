North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Auborough Street between 6pm and midnight on 27 January 2016 and involved unknown persons gaining entry to a building via a rear window from Tollergate

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information if anybody saw or heard anything suspicious between the times of the offence.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Steve Ashley PC 390. You can also email stephen.ashley@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160015969
