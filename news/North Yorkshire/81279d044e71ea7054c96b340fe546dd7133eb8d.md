North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road rage incident on the A169 between Malton and Pickering.

It happened at about 1.40pm on 2 April 2019 on the A169 between Malton and Pickering and involved a large black vehicle and another vehicle. The drivers of both of the vehicles were involved in a confrontation and one man suffered a punch to the face, sustaining minor facial injuries.
