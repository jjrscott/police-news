Police are appealing for information after a motorcyclist died in a single-vehicle collision in Catterick Garrison last night.

Just before midnight on Friday 7 August 2015, a white Kawasaki ZX636 motorcycle was travelling east on Leyburn Road, Catterick Garrison, when it left the road and collided with a lamppost. The rider, a 24-year-old man from Northallerton, was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Police are appealing for anyone who saw the collision, or the motorcycle involved before the collision, to come forward.

Anyone with information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for the Major Collision Investigation Team, or email MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12150137181.

**8 August 2015**
