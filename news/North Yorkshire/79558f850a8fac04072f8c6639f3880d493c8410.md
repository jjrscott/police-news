Last modified: 17 October 2018 at 04:33pm

Police have issued CCTV images of man they would like to speak to following the attempted theft of a motorcycle in York.

At around 8.50pm on Saturday 9 July 2016 a man was captured on CCTV trying to move a motorcycle from a multi-storey car park on Rowntree Wharf, York. The motorcycle was moved only a few metres before a group of people are seen to leave the car park.

A few hours later, a group of people return and remove the motorcycle from the car park. The motorcycle was then left on the side of the road damaged.

Police would like to speak to the man pictured on the CCTV stills as he may have important information that could assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises him should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for the Force Control Room.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160121806.
