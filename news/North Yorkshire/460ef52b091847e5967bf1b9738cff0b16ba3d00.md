Last modified: 6 November 2017 at 12:46pm

Police have released CCTV footage of a man they want to speak to in relation to the theft of a pedal cycle.

The cycle was left at 6.30am Wednesday 25 October 2017 at the bike racks at the front of the Travelodge on Eboracum Way in York. When the owner returned half an hour later at 7am, the bike had been taken.

Police are keen to speak to the man captured in the CCTV images or anyone who could help identify the man.

Anyone with any information which could assist the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to Nikki Jackson. Alternatively you can email Nikki.Jackson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170191271.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
