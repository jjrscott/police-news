North Yorkshire Police is asking for the public's help to locate 34 year-old Katie Tuff who is wanted on suspicion of causing harassment.

She has connections in York and Malton and officers believe she could be in one of the two areas – although there’s a possibility she could be elsewhere.

If you have seen Tuff, or know where she is, please call 101 with the reference number 12200242076.
