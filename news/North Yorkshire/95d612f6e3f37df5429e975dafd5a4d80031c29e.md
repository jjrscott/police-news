Police are appealing for information following an aggravated burglary at Screwfix in Selby.

It happened just after 8pm on Thursday 8 November 2018 when a group of men threatened a member of staff as they were closing the store on Bawtry Road.

Thankfully the member of staff was not physically injured during the incident.

Police are appealing to anyone in the area at the time who noticed a disturbance at the store or saw any suspicious people or vehicles in the area of Bawtry Road Industrial Estate and Staynor Hall, to contact them.

It is believed there were around six or seven people in the group, three of them are described as wearing black clothing, two with a face-warmer covering part of their face with a skull design on it, and one wearing a balaclava and sunglasses. All were around 6 feet tall.

Anyone with any information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for York Serious Crime Team, or email SCTYork@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12180209400 when passing on information.
