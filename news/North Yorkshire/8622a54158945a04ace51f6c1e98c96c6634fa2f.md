A 24-year-old man has been charged with attempted theft of a pedal cycle and going equipped to steal, after being spotted on CCTV trying to steal a bike from a bike rack on St Andrewsgate in York.

Police were contacted on Monday 26 June 2017 at around 7.50pm to a report of a man with wire cutters, who had been seen attempting to cut through a bike lock to release a bike from a bike rack.

Officers attended the area and located a man matching the suspectâ€™s description, still attempting to cut the bike free from its lock. He was arrested on suspicion of theft and was found to be in possession of a set of wire cutters.
