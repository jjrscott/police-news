Police are appealing for information regarding a serious assault which left a man with facial injuries.

Ambulance services alerted police to the incident which took place at around 3.30am on Saturday 30 March 2019 at the Moko Lounge on Kings Road in Harrogate.

A 26-year-old man from Oxfordshire was the victim of an assault which left him with facial injuries. He was taken to Harrogate District Hospital for treatment and later transferred to York District Hospital for specialist treatment.

Police are keen to speak to anyone who may of witnessed the assault or who has any information which would assist their investgation.

Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to PC George Frost. You can also email george.frost@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk - please quote reference number 12190057122.

Alternatively you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
