North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after two day time burglaries took place in Nidderdale.

During the early afternoon of Wednesday 17 October, 2018 two houses were burgled in Nidderdale. Both of the burgled premises were detached houses near to the B6165 and B6265 main route through Nidderdale, from Wharfedale towards Ripley. The burglars used force on rear patio doors to try and gain entry to the houses.

Police suspect the occupants of a grey Audi A4 estate car may have been involved. They may have visited other premises in the area.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1 and be ready to quote reference 381 of 17 October. You can also email bill.hickson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
