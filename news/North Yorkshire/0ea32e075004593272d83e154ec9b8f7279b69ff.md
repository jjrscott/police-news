North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a bike theft that occurred in Scarborough.

A silver Muddy Fox bike was stolen from outside the Everyone Active Leisure Centre.

It happened on Sunday 12 August 2018 and officers appealing for information from anyone who may have been in the area between 9.30pm and 10.30pm, who may have witnessed something that could assist police with the investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12180151035 when passing on information.
