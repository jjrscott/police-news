Last modified: 9 June 2016 at 12:44pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a generator that occurred in Rillington, Malton.

It happened on Sands Lane, Rillington between 9pm 20/05/15 and 6.30am 21/05/15 and a large super silent vent generator in blue was stolen. The generator will have been moved using a large vehicle.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Louise Neville-Beck. You can also email Louise.Neville-Beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-21/05/2015 0054

Thursday 21 May 2015.
