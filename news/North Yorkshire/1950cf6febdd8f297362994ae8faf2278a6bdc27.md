North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a vehicle collided with a house in York.

It happened between 6.15pm and 6.45pm on Thursday 7 Jun 2018 when a white Peugeot Expert van drove into a wall of a house on Scarcroft Road.

No injuries occurred to the occupants of the house.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle(s) prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the identity of the driver and the movements after the collision.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Gareth Foster. You can also email gareth.foster@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180101119
