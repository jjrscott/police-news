Last modified: 13 June 2016 at 10:45am

Police in Settle are appealing for witnesses and information following the theft of a specialist railway drill.

It happened outside a property on West View between midnight and 6am on Friday 23 October 2015.

The suspects made off with the the railway drill - stamped STG TS-02 - and numerous other Stanley and Draper tools worth hundreds of pounds which were inside a Mitsubishi pick-up truck.

Officers are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help identify the offenders and recover the tools, which are specific to the railway maintenance.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Harry Carpenter. You can also emailHarry.Carpenter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.

Please quote reference number 12150187956 when providing details about this incident.
