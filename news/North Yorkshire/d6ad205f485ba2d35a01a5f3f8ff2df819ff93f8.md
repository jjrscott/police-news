At around 11am on 2 March 2016, two people entered Hoppers, Market Place Malton.

They each placed a Yankee Candle into their bags and left the store without making any attempt to pay for them items.

Anyone who recognises the individuals in the CCTV or who has any information is urged to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Garnett, collar number 788.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
