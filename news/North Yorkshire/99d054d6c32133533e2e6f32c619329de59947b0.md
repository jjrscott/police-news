Police have identified a man they want to speak to about an incident in Banyan, Harrogate when a woman was hit by a glass.

However, offices are still appealing for witnesses to the incident to come forward.

At around 10.30pm on Saturday 25 March 2017 the woman was hit in the face by a glass, leaving her with lacerations to her face and nose which required hospital treatment.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, was in Banyan on Saturday night, or has any information which could assist the investigation, please call 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to PC Michael Haydock. Alternatively you can email _Michael.Haydock@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

Please quote reference 12170050910.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
