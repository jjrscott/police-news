Police have issued photographs of three men they would like to speak to about a theft at a Selby mobile phone shop.

At about 1pm on Monday 19 March 2018, three men entered the O2 store on Gowthorpe in Selby and pulled two display phones from their stands, before running out of the store.

Officers would like to speak to the men pictured on the CCTV images as they may have information to assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises any of these men is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Richard Stagg, or email richard.stagg@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180047126.
