Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:17am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of power tools in Great Ayton overnight between 14 and 15 September 2017.

It happened at a location just off A173 and involved a break-in to a storage cabin.

During the incident, power tools marked with the letters "CLS" were stolen, along with several keys for plant machinery.
If anyone is offered these power tools with these markings, or has information in relation to the theft, should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Graeme Gale. You can also email graeme.gale@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170165543
