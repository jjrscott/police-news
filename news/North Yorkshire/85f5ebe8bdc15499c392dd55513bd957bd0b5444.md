York police are investigating after a man was verbally and physically attacked by another man in York.

The incident, which occurred on Blossom Street in York at 8pm on Saturday 4 March involved the victim, a 27-year-old Asian man, being racially abused and physically attacked by another man.

The suspect is described as white and was wearing a blue long-sleeved top at the time.

If you recognise the man or have any information that can assist the investigation please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Nicola Manning. Or email Nicola.Manning@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 .

Please quote reference number 12170037068when providing details about this incident.
