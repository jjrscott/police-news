Last modified: 14 March 2019 at 04:41pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an altercation that took place on the A172 near Stokesley between two drivers.

At around 5.30pm on Tuesday 26th February 2019 an assault took place at the side of the A172 Thirsk Roam, as you enter Stokesley.

The driver of a silver Vauxhall Vivaro was involved in a physical altercation with the driver of a Nissan Quashqai ,which left one of the men with head injuries.

Police are appealing to motorists who may have witnessed this incident, particularly motorists with dash cam footage, to contact them, so they can establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC93 Roche or email Alex.Roche@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190035974
