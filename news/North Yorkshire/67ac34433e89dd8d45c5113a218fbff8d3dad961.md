Last modified: 8 January 2020 at 02:59pm

Officers have launched an investigation after three incidents in Easingwold on New Year's Eve and the early hours of New Year's Day.

At around 10.15pm on New Year's Eve, five windows were broken at Clarks Bakery, Long Street (incident ref 12190238610).

Around half an hour later, a man was threatened by another man at the Horse Shoe, Long Street (incident ref 12190238649).

Then, around 12.05am on New Year's Day, a window was broken at the Tipsy Fox, Spring Street, Easingwold (incident ref 12200000269).

Police believe all three incidents may be linked.

Anyone with information is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Lucy Tate, 0r email Lucy.Tate@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the reference numbers listed above when passing on information.
