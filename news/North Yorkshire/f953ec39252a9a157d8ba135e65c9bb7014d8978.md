North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a bicycle theft that occurred in Richmond. The suspect had taken a mountain bike which is described as an ORANGE FIVE 29 with a black frame and white decals. The suspect was last seen heading from Victoria Road, onto Cravengate and onto Newbiggin and out of sight.

The incident happened on Westfields at approximately 20:30 hours on 10th May 2018 where a bicycle had been taken from the front of an address on Westfields, Richmond.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 645 Stephen Lifton. You can also email Stephen.lifton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180081693
