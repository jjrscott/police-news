Last modified: 17 October 2018 at 04:33pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information about a theft of cycle that occurred in the Melbourne Street area of York overnight on 15 October 2017.

The cycle that was stolen was double-locked to fencing in a secluded area off Melbourne Street.

It is a Nuke Proof Mega TR in black, white and yellow. It has Rock shock forks in white, cycle lights and a Rock Shock Hydraulic seat dropper which not standard. This style of cycle is unusual.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Liz Welburn. You can also email liz.welburn@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170185521
