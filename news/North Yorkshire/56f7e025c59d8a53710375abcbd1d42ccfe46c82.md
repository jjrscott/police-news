Scarborough Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from a parked car in Scarborough.

The theft occurred at around 5.30pm on Saturday 21 February 2015 when the offenders smashed a window to a parked car on Gladstone Lane, before stealing a purse from inside and making off in an unknown direction.

Anyone who may have been in the area at the time of the incident and seen or heard anything unusual is urged to contact Scarborough Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Liam Cromack.

You can also email liam.cromack@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can also pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150029221.
