The incident in Nathan Court has now been brought to a safe conclusion. Thank you to the local residents for their support and patience.

There is an ongoing police incident in Nathan Court off Scalby Road in Scarborough.

Specialist units attended the address after a man was found with serious injuries near Falsgrave Road, at around 8.40am today (Saturday 26 September). The man has been taken to hospital.

The road has been closed and members of the public are asked to avoid the area.

Anyone who saw an injured man in the Falsgrave Road area this morning is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 1 and pass information to the Force Control Room, quoting reference number 0101 of 26 September.
