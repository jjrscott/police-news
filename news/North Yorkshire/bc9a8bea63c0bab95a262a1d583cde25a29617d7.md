Police in Northallerton are seeking two vital witnesses to an incident that happened at around 9.30pm on the night of Friday 19 February.

The first appeal relates to the occupants of a car who had to stop near to the pelican crossing between Tesco and the library on East Road when a youth was pushed in front of their car.

The second appeal relates to a man who spoke to a group of boys and a man on a mountain bike at around the same time.

If you are the motorist or the man who spoke to the group, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Caroline Pugh or email _caroline.pugh@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160030183.

A 34-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of assault and released on police bail while the investigation continues.
