Last modified: 9 June 2016 at 07:25pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in the Eastfield area of Scarborough.

It happened on Hopper Hill road between 10.35pm on the 3 July and 2.35am on the 4 July and involved a local business been entered and a large quantity of Hugo Boss, Adidas and Grenson shoes been stolen from within.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information which would assist in locating the stolen property and if anyone has been offered similar items for sale

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Philip Giblin.

You can also email Philip.Giblin@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

**7 July 2015**
