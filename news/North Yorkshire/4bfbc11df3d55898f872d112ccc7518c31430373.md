Officers are appealing for information following a collision in Acomb.

The collision occurred at around 8.50am on Monday, 10 July and involved a cyclist and a small black car. The cyclist suffered minor injuries following the collision and members of the public who stopped to provide first aid are thanked.

Officers are keen to speak with anyone who witnessed the collision or the events surrounding the incident.

If you can help with the investigation, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 41 Ellison. You can also email david.ellison0041@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12180129390 when providing information.
