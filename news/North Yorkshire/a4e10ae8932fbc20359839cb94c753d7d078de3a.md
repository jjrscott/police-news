North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Settle.

It happened on Marshfield Road between 7pm and 7.30pm on Wednesday 22 January 2020 when a pair of Ugg boots were stolen from the property.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Burgess. You can also email David.Burgess@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200012447
