Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:37am

Officers are appealing for information following a two vehicle collision in Strensall.

The incident occurred at around 10.30am on Monday, 17 April on Flaxton Road and involved a burgundy coloured Audi and a gold coloured Volkwagen Golf.

No injuries were reported but there is an ongoing investigation into the events surrounding the collision.

Anyone with information which could assist with the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, selecting option 2, and asking for Louise Harness. Information can also be provided by emailing louise.harness@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, information can be provided to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference 12170065320 when providing information.
