A man has died in hospital following an suspected attempted murder at a property in Sowerby, Thirsk.

George Francis Kidson, 85, from Sowerby, Thirsk passed away on Monday 9 November at James Cook Hospital.

Following police enquiries Ryan Brady Campbell, a 29-year-old local man, was arrested and appeared in Northallerton Magistrates Court yesterday (Mon 9 November).

He was charged with attempted murder and theft and will appear at Teesside Crown Court on 23 November 2015.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing to anyone who has any information which could assist the investigation to contact them on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to the Major Crime Unit.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
