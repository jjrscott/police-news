North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following an attempted theft at Sainsbury's, Scarborough. worth £322

It happened at 5.16pm on 21 December 2020 and involved an attempted theft of a trolley of goods worth more than £300.

Officers are appealing for information to help identify the woman in the CCTV image as it is believed she has information which could assist the investigation.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for 546 Gascoyne. You can also email Helena.Gascoyne2@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200242682.
