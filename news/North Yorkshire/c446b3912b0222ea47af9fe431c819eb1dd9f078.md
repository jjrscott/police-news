Four people have been arrested this morning in connection with an assault in York.

Police were called at 11.15pm on Friday 12 August 2016 to reports of a man with serious injuries in Burnholme Drive, York. The man, in his forties, had been assaulted and sustained serious head injuries. He was taken to Leeds General Infirmary.

On Monday 15 August, two men aged 21 and 30 from the York area, and a 28-year-old man and a 22-year-old woman both from the Malton area, were arrested in connection with the incident.

All four remain in custody while enquiries continue.

Anyone with any information that could assist the investigation is asked to phone 101, select option 2 and ask for York Serious Crime Team. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12160145406.
