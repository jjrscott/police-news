Police are appealing for information to help identify three people in the CCTV images following a theft of a drill from B&Q.;

The two men and one woman in the CCTV were seen entering the B&Q store on Hurricane Way in York shortly after 3.30pm on Monday 12 June 2017.

Anyone with any information who can help officers identify the two men and the woman is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Ricci Haslam or email ricci.haslam@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170102494 when passing on any information.
