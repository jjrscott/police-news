Officers are appealing for information following the theft of a motorcycle in Selby.

Officers are appealing for information following the theft of a motorcycle in Selby.

The motorcycle was stolen on Flaxley Road between midnight and 1am on Thursday, 7 June. It is believed two offenders were involved who are believed to have wheeled the vehicle along Flaxley Road on to Long Trods before riding it back along Flaxley Road in the direction of Scott Road.

The motorcycle is a red and white Honda CBR600 with the registration N565 0EW.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area or who may know the where abouts of the stolen motorcycle.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Richard Stagg. You can also email Richard.Stagg@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Information can be passed anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12180100564 when providing information.
