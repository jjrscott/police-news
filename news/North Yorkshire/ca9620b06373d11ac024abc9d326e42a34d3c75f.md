Police are appealing for witnesses after a man was assaulted in a York bar.

At about 1am on Sunday 2 October, a man was assaulted in Vodka Revolution on Coney Street. The victim, a man in his 20s, suffered a gash to his head which required hospital treatment.

The bar would have been busy at this time, and police are urging anyone who witnessed the incident to contact them. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for the York Investigation Hub, quoting reference 12160179048.

A 29-year-old York woman has been arrested in connection with this incident, on suspicion of assault occasioning actual bodily harm. She has been released on bail pending further enquiries.
