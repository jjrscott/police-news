Police have issued CCTV images of a man they want to speak to in connection with a burglary at a jewellery shop in Easingwold.

The break-in happened in the early hours of 18 February 2015 when the window of Snowdon's jewellers was smashed and a large amount of jewellery was stolen including

gold bracelets, rings, necklaces and earrings, Timex watches and a brooch.

Officers want to speak to the man shown in the images as he could have important information that could help the investigation.

Anyone who can help identify the man is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Kate Jones or email kate.jones@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150027039 when passing on information.
