North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a minor collision that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Wreyfield Drive at around 6pm Sunday 2nd October 2016 and involved a motorbike and a white car. A number of other vehicles that were parked nearby have been damaged as a result of this collision.

It is believed that a male rider of the motorcycle was taken to Scarborough hospital with a foot injury. We would urge this male and the driver of the white car to come forward.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle(s) prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.
