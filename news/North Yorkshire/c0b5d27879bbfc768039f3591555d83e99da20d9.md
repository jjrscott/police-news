Police are appealing for information after the theft of a moped in Scarborough.

A Baotian black/white moped with a rear helmet box was stolen from the back garden of a property on Colescliffe Road, Scarborough, between 9pm on 2 May and 1.55pm on 3 May 2015.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Caroline Scholes. You can also emailCaroline.Scholes1830@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150072778.

**5 May 2015**
