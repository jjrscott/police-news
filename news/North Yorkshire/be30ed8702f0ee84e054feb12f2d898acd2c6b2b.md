Police have issued a photograph of a man they want to speak to in connection with a racist incident in York.

At about 11pm on Saturday 22 October 2016, a man was racially abused by another man in George Hudson Street, York, outside Popworld. It is believed the suspect is from the West Yorkshire area.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured on CCTV, as he may have important information that could assist them with their enquiries.

Anyone who recognises the man should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - please select option 2 and ask for PC Jonathan Weeks. Alternatively email Jonathan.Weeks541@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Or contact Crimestoppers anaonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12160192503 when passing information.
