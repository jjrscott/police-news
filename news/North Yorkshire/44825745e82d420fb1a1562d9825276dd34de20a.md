Police are appealing for witnesses after a burglary in Darley, near Harrogate.

It occurred at a property on Valley Road, Darley, some time between 1pm and 10pm on Tuesday 11 April 2017. Electrical items, jewellery and medication were stolen.

In particular, police are asking for any information about a military medal, described as a Falklands War military medal with blue and green ribbon. It has a silver crown with then Queen's head on the front and a Falklands crest on the back, and is engraved with the name 'Alan Mould'.

Anyone with information about the burglary is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Gary Anderson or Pateley Bridge Police Station, or email gary.anderson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12170062015 when passing information.
