Here, PCSO Claire Routh based in Whitby tells what made her decide to become a PCSO and what three words she'd use to describe the role:

#####

##### <br style="clear:both;"></br>

##### Q: How long have you been a PCSO?

##### _A: 2 years 9 months_

##### Q: Where are you based?

##### _A: Whitby_

##### Q: What did you do before you became a PCSO?

##### _A: Credit Controller for insurance company_

##### Q: What do you do in your spare time?

##### _A: Spanish lessons, Running, Ariel Hoop, going out with friends and spending time with family._

##### Q: What made you decide to apply to become a PCSO?

##### _A: Something I’d always wanted to do_

##### Q: What was your first day like?

##### _A: Scary, Exciting and nerve wracking_

##### Q: What is your favourite thing about being a PCSO?

##### _A: No two days are ever the same_

##### Q: What is the worst thing about being a PCSO?

##### _A: Restrictions of the role occasionally._

##### Q: What three words would you use to sum up what being a PCSO is really like?

##### _A: Expect the unexpected._

##### Q: Name one thing that people wouldn’t know about PCSOs?

##### _A: The variety of the job._

##### Q: What advice would you give to anyone who is thinking of becoming a PCSO?

##### _A: Go for it!_

Good luck!
