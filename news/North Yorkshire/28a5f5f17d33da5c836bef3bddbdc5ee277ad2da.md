Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a serious assault that occurred in York.

The incident happened on Low Ousegate between 9pm and 10pm on Tuesday 17 December and involved two groups of people; one made up of males, the other a mixed group of males and females. It is believed the group of males were aged in their twenties.

It is believed that a female was assaulted and pushed to the ground by a male in the other group, whilst a male in the mixed sex group of friends was assaulted by another male in the all-male group. A glass was thrown towards this male and he was taken to York District Hospital by ambulance where he was discharged following treatment.
