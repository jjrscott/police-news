Police are investigating a serious sexual assault which took place in Ripon on the morning of Tuesday 24 March.

The incident happened at the Quarry Moor Nature Reserve.

The victim, a woman in her thirties, is being supported by specially trained officers.

Police are appealing for anyone who was in the nature reserve between the hours of 9am and 1pm on Tuesday 24 March to contact them as a matter of urgency as any information they have could assist with this investigation.

Dial 101 and ask to speak to Harrogate CID.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150050009 when passing on any information.
