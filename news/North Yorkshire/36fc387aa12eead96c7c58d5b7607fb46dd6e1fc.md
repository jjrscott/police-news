North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred at Flamingo Land, Kirby Misperton near Malton.

It happened between 1610hrs on Wednesday 6th May 2015 and 0650hrs on Thursday 7th May 2015. Offenders approached a large outbuilding, forced entry into the building and removed various gardening tools. The tools are mainly Husquarna Orange petrol powered units including strimmers, a leaf blower, two chainsaws and a hedge trimmer.

We are appealing for anyone who saw a vehicle on the outskirts of Kirby Misperton close to the Flamingo Land park, or any one who has had any petrol-powered gardening equipment offered to them for sale.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andy Pomfret. You can also email andrew.pomfret@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150074915
