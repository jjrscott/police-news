North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary on Edgerton Drive, Tadcaster.

The incident is believed to have taken place between the Wednesday 18 January and Thursday 19 January 2017 and involved lead being stolen off the roof of Edgerton Lodge and the property being broken in to.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone acting suspiciously or any vehicles which were in the grounds of Edgerton Lodge at this time.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Alison Baker. You can also email Alison.baker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-19012017-0151
