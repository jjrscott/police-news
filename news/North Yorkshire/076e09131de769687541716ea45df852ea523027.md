North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a collision which occurred on the A63 near Osgodby, Selby at 5pm on Friday 12 February 2021. A black Mazda 6 was travelling west bound along the road towards the A19 when it collided with a white Jaguar XF travelling in the opposite direction.

The female driver of the Jaguar suffered serious injuries and was transported to hospital with a young child who was a passenger in the car. The male driver of the Mazda suffered serious injuries and was also transported to hospital.

Police are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or seen either vehicle driving along the A63 prior to the collision. If you have any information or dashcam footage please call 101 and ask for TC 1532 Richard Harrison or email: Richard.harrison@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.
