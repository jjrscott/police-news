Police are investigating the death of an 88-year-old lady who was admitted to hospital on 29 October after being found with injuries at her home in Carleton near Skipton.

Sadly the lady died in hospital in the early hours of Monday 2 November.

At this stage, it is too early to say how she sustained her injuries and officers are treating the circumstances as unexplained.

Enquiries are continuing into the events that led to her injuries and a post mortem will be carried out to determine the cause of her death.

We will provide more information when we are able.

We understand that the incident has caused some concern among the local community. Although the events that led to the lady's death are still to be determined, it is recommended that everyone follows our crime prevention advice at all times of the year.
