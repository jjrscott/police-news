Last modified: 4 May 2017 at 09:05am

Detectives in York are appealing for witnesses to two incidents in the city after a man sustained serious head injuries during an assault.

In the first incident, between 9pm and 9.30pm on Saturday 29 April 2017, a man was assaulted in toilets in The Micklegate at 127 on Micklegate in York. He sustained serious head injuries and was taken to hospital.

Officers want to hear from anyone who was in the pub at the time or has any other information that could assist the investigation to contact them.

In the second incident, officers are appealing to anyone who witnessed a group of men fighting on Castlegate a few hours later at around 1am on the morning of 30 April.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option two and ask for York Serious Crime Team, or email marie.hall@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12170073420 when passing on information.

Officers believe the two incident are linked.

Two men have been arrested in connection with the incident and released while under investigation.
