**Original release: CCTV appeal following a theft of cash at Morrisons in Selby**

_Published on Monday 5 August 2019_

Police have issued CCTV of a woman they would like to speak to following a theft by finding at Morrisons in Selby on Friday 14 June 2019.

At around 6.45pm a man had withdrawn Â£40 cash back at the self checkout till and had forgotten to take the money. CCTV captured a person approaching the checkout and removing the cash.

Officers are asking members of the public to get in touch if they recognise the person in the image, as they believe they will have information that will assist the investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Thomas Longhorn or email thomas.longhorn@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12190107513 when passing on information.
