Police in Skipton are appealing for witnesses following an attempted theft at the O2 store in Sheep Street.

It happened at around 1.45pm on Thursday 9 August 2018 and when a group of men entered the store and approached the display stands before being challenged by staff and leaving the store empty handed.

Officers are appealing to anyone who was in the area at the time and witnessed the incident or saw a group or men acting suspiciously.

In particular, they are appealing for information about the identity of the men.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1838 Andrew Middlemiss. You can also email ANDREW.MIDDLEMISS@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number12180147371
