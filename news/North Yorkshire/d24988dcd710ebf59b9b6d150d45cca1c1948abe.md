Police have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to trace in connection with two burglaries at a clothes shop in Malton.

Atom Clothing in Market Place was broken into overnight between 21 and 22 January 2015 and more recently between 9 and 10 March 2015. Around Â£2,000 worth of stock was stolen in the incidents.

Officers are appealing to anyone who recognises the man pictured in the image to get in touch.

Anyone who can help officers with their enquiries is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for PC Nick Ham or Malton police.

Alternatively, information can be emailed to nicholas.ham@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you prefer not to give your name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
