North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempted theft that occurred in Selby.

It happened at Sainsburys, Abbey walk, Selby at 15:40 on 23/02/2015 and involved 2 males selecting items from shelving and placing at other locations within the store to be collected by a second male, both were noticed with almost Â£1000 worth of stock in the trolley and left the shop leaving the stock behind.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Lee Smith. You can also email Lee.Smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
