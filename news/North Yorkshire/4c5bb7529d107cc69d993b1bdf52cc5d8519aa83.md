North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information after a Secma Fun Quad was stolen from Lythe.

It was stolen from Lythe Caravan and Camping Park at around 2.55am in the early hours Tuesday 12 May 2015.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help locate the stolen quad and identify those responsible for the theft.

The quad is a convertible type and was stolen without the roof and doors as shown in the pictures.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Melanie Smith. You can also email melanie.smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150078168.
