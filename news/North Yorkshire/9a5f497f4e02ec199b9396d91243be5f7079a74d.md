Last modified: 17 October 2019 at 01:03pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Knaresborough.

It happened at a property on Park Row, Knaresborough, at some time between 6.10pm and 8.30pm on Wednesday 16 October 2019 and involved an unknown offender(s) gaining entry into the address by smashing the rear window and then once inside removing a black 42″ TV.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Nicholas Woods. You can also email Nicholas.Woods@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190191582.
