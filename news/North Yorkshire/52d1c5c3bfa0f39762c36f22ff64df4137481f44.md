Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:04am

Skipton police are appealing for witnesses and information that could lead to the identification of four males believed to be responsible for tatgetting vehicles on Burnside Avenue and Crescent in Skipton.

Between 11.45pm and midnight on Tuesday 1 November 2016, four males were responsible for theft from at least one vehicle parked on a driveway on Burnside Avenue, Skipton. It is believed that the group tried the door handles of numerous locked vehicles while in the area. It is believed that they walked, as a group along both Burnside Avenue and Crescent as well as Branch Road, before continuing in the direction of Brooklands School.
The males are described as being white, aged late teens to early thirties.
Anyone who has information in respect to the identity of these males is requested to make contact with North Yorkshire Police.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Robert HUTCHINSON You can also email robert.hutchinson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160198989
