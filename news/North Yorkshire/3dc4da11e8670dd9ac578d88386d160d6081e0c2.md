At 1.35am Thursday 15 February 2018 a member of the public contacted police and reported a silver Audi having collided with a lamp post on the A63 at Osgodby.

The impact of the collision had severed the lamp post, leaving it lying in the road obstructing the carriageway and the silver Audi in a ditch.

The member of the public reported seeing the driver get out of the car and walk away from the scene, and was able to pass a good description to police.

An area search commenced and at 1.50am the driver was located on York Road in Barlby.

He failed a roadside breath test and was arrested and taken back to the police station, where he was tested again and returned a further reading of 90 micrograms of alcohol per 100 millilitres of breath â€“ over two and a half times the legal limit of 35 micrograms of alcohol per 100 millilitres of breath.

He was charged with drink driving and will appear at York Magistrates on Wednesday 14 March 2018.
