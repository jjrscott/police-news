North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary at an office on Pavilion Terrace in Scarborough.

Between 4.15pm and 5pm on Wednesday 30 December 2015, entry was gained to a business premises at Pavilion Terrace, Scarborough, by offenders who broke a rear window to gain entry.

It is requested that anything suspicious seen by the public be brought to the attention of the investigating officer PC 604 Andy Grant by dialling 101, or pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150231320
