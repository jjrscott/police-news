North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information about an incident in Cowling involving a man with an axe.

It happened on the A6068 at Cowling near to the local village shop on the 4 May 2016 at 9.45pm.

The incident involved a local youth who is alleging that he was verbally threatened by an man who was carrying an axe.

The youth was not injured in the incident but visibly shaken . The alleged offender is said to have made off in a black vehicle in the direction of Acre Mews, Cowling .

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed this incident to come forward with information.

Please contact Pc 1484 David Foyston on 101 quoting crime reference 12160079901.

If you prefer to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
