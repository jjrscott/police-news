It has come to the attention of officers from the Yorkshire and the Humber Regional Cyber Crime Unit that exhibitors at The Commercial Vehicle Show 2016 at the Birmingham NEC between 26-28th April 2016 may be targeted by online criminals trying to extort money with the threat of Denial of Service Attacks against their websites and others trying to claim they are collecting outstanding money on behalf of The CV Show.

If you are in any doubt as to the authenticity of a communication please contact The CV Show organisers directly via accounts@cvshow.com or call +44 (0) 20 3857 9008.

If you have received any communication containing threats, demands for payment or have suffered any losses in relation to this matter please log the incident with your local police force and record any Log or Crime Reference Numbers provided.

If your business is located in the policing areas of North, South or West Yorkshire or Humberside please also make contact with the Yorkshire & the Humber Regional Cyber Crime Unit who are investigating viaregional.cyber@westyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
