North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a Burglary that occurred in Ravensworth, Richmond

It happened at Ravensworth nurseries, Richmond at around 1350Hrs on 6/7/2015. At this time the door of the Nursery shop was forced open and a quantity of cash taken from a locked till. A member of staff reports seeing a Black 56 plate Fiat Punto leaving the area at this time. This same member of staff recalls seeing a male leaving the shop area, described as Asian, 40 years old, average build with short black hair, tidy in appearance, wearing a brown jacket.

In particular we would like to speak to this male and the occupants of this Fiat Punto.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Roche. You can also email Alex.Roche@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150114444.
