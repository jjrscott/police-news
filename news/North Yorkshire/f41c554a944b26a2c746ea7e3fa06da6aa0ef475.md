North Yorkshire Police have issued new CCTV images of two men they would like to speak to in connection with the theft of a Dyson vacuum cleaner from the Sainsburys store on Wetherby Road in Harrogate on 28 March 2017.

The two men returned to the store on Saturday 6 May 2017 and were seen in the shelved area where the vacuum cleaners were previously on display.

Police would like to speak to the two men about the original theft of the Dyson vacuum cleaner and also a further theft of toilet roll and two boxes of cider.

Anyone who recognises the men or who has information which would assist the investigation is asked to contact PC Adam Smith on 101 or email Adam.Smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170054483

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111
