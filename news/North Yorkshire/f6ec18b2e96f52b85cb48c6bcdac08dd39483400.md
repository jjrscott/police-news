Last modified: 11 September 2017 at 11:36am

Police are appealing for witnesses to an altercation outside the New Globe Inn, Yorkersgate in Malton to come forward.

At around 9.30pm on Saturday 9 September 2017, an altercation between a group of men took place outside of the pub, which left one man with injuries to his head and another with facial injuries and a punctured lung.

A 41-year-old man, a 51-year-old man and a 47-year-old man, all from Malton were later arrested on suspicion of wounding with intent and currently remain in police custody.
Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward, to determine the full circumstances around the incident.
Anyone who has any information which could assist the investigation is asked to contact police on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to Graeme Boast. Alternatively you can email Graeme.boast@northyorkshire.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170162082.

Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.
