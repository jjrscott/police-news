Police are appealing for information after several vehicles were damaged overnight in Northallerton

Between 11pm on Monday 14 March and 7am on Tuesday 15 March, a number of vehicles suffered damage to their windscreen wipers and tyres in Bylands Place.

Police have so far received five separate reports of damage, and it is believed about eight vehicles have been attacked.

Officers are appealing for anyone who saw or heard anything suspicious in the area to get in touch. Please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference number NYP-15032016-0065, or alternatively Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

**15 March 2016**
