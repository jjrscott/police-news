North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a number of shed burglaries in the Stokesley area.

The thefts happened between 5pm on 3 August and 8am on 4 August 2015. They involved sheds and containers been broken into at a fishing lake near Great Ayton, a shed in Station Road, Great Ayton and a farm building near Carlton in Cleveland.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incidents.

In particular, we are appealing for information about suspicious vehicles and people seen in the area, and if anyone is offered Sthil petrol strimmers and leaf blowers along with horse tack for sale.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Chris Villiers.

You can also email chris.villiers@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-04082015-0073/0062 or 0081.

**4 August 2015**
