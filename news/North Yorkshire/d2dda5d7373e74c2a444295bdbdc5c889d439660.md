Police are appealing for witnesses to an incident in which an 18-month-old girl was attacked by a dog at a pub in Malton.

The incident occurred at 3.30pm on 2 May 2015 in The Yard public house and involved a grey and white Old English sheep dog which bit the young girl around the head and neck causing minor injuries.

PC Stephen Ashley, of Malton Police, said: "I am appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or may know the owners of the dog in question to come forward."

The owners were a middle aged couple who also had with them a smaller dog. They left the establishment after the incident but did not leave any details.

If you have information which could assist this investigation, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Stephen Ashley or Malton Police.

**6 May 2015**
