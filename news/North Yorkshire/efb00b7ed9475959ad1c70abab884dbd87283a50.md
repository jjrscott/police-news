North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempted burglary which occurred in York

It happened on Newby Terrace between 10.10am and 12.30pm on Sunday 10 November 2019..

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious persons or activity in the Newby Terrace area at this time.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1356 GOBBY. You can also email philip.gobby@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190207412.
