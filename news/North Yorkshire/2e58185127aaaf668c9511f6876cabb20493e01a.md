North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Harrogate

It happened on Knaresborough Road Harrogate at approximately 9.45pm on Sunday 19th April 2015 and involved three men and a woman.

One of the men who was with the woman sustained an injury to his face , however left the area prior to police obtaining details

Another man, aged 22, was arrested at the time and is currently on bail pending further enquiries

We are conducting enquiries and requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the injured man who left the scene.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Wayne Hutchinson. You can also email wayne.hutchinson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150064360.

Date 29th April 2015.
