North Yorkshire Police are asking for information to help identify a man who could assist the police with the investigation of an assault which occurred in York on Tuesday 24 January 2017.

The incident happened on East Parade in York between the hours of 6.30am and 7am on Tuesday 24 January 2017

Police are appealing for any witnesses to come forward or anyone that can help identify a male who is described as being in his 50's or 60's, of average height and build, wearing a black beanie type hat, green jacket with cream/white fur around the collar, glasses, stubble and has distinctive tattoo's on both hands.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC541. You can also email jonathan.weeks541@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170013325.
