North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident of criminal damage in Hawes.

The incident happened some time between 10pm on 25 June and 9am on 26 June 2020 when damage was caused to the shop door of the Penny Garth Cafe and also a wooden bench outside the premises.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the identity of a suspect who caused the damage.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for 710 Steve Gardner. You can also email Steven.Gardner@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200108060.
