Last modified: 13 July 2020 at 01:20pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a minor injury collision that occurred on the A64 Seamer Bypass, Scarborough.

It happened at 6.30pm on Sunday July 12 2020 and involved a white Audi A4 car and a silver Vauxhall Antara marked police vehicle.

Damage was caused to both vehicles and the police officer driving the Vauxhall required a hospital assessment for a minor injury to her neck.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicles prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Antonia Porter. You can also email Antonia.Porter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200118590.
