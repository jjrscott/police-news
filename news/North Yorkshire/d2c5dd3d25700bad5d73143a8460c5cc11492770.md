North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a property was vandalised in West Ayton.

It happened on Friday 27 April 2018 between 6pm and 11pm when a pot of red paint was thrown at a property hitting a window. The action caused the pot of paint to explode all-over the window and driveway.

Anyone with information regarding this incident or similar incidents in the West Ayton area should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jamie Manson. You can also email jamie.manson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180073083.
