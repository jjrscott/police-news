Officers are appealing for help in identifying a man following an incident in York where a man was assaulted and a public order offence committed.

The offence occurred at the Asda store, Layerthorpe at around 6.30pm on Monday, 11 May.

If you can identify the man in the image or have any other information which may assist with the investigation please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Sophie Simpson. Alternatively you can email sophie.simpson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to provide information anonymously then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote ference 12200078553 when providing information.
