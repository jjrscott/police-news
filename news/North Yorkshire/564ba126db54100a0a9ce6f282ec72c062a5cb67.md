Police are appealing for information regarding a burglary at a property on Castlegate in Pickering, where a families set of bicycles and a child's 'tag along' bike were stolen.

Four bicycles and a child's 'tag-along' bike were stolen from an outbuilding at the property at some time overnight on Tuesday 13 February 2018 to Wednesday 14 February 2018.

The bikes are described as a man's Giant: Escape mountain bike in blue, which a noticeably large model, a pink and white ladies' Claude Butler mountain bike, a man's Shogun: Jackal XTC mountain bike in blue and a child's Grant: XTC in red, white and blue. Also stolen was a young child's Spokey Joe 'Tag-along' bike in red, described as having one wheel and attaches to an adult's bike.

The bikes were the cherished property of the family and the burglary and their theft has left the children in some distress.

Police are appealing for the public's help to trace the bikes and anyone with information which would assist the investigation is asked to contact them on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to Alan Robson. You can also email alan.robson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk - please quote reference 12180026046.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800555111.
