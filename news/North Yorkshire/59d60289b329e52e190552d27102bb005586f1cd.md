North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision which occurred on the A170 between Kirkbymoorside and Pickering.

It happened on A170 at about 6.30pm on Monday 19 June 2017 and involved a motorcycle, which was within a group of three motorcycles, which collided with a car heading in the opposite direction.

The motorcyclist suffered a serious foot injury and were taken by ambulance to Scarborough Hospital where they were treated and discharged following treatment.
