Officers are appealing for information following an assault, which resulted in a 37 year old man being taken to hospital with a head injury and cut to his eye.

The incident happened at around 6pm on Monday 13 November 2017 in the Navigation Road / Walmgate area of York.

Two men, aged 37 and 34, have been arrested in connection with the incident. Both men have been released on bail pending further enquiries.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or who has any information, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to the York Investigation Hub. Or call the hub directly on 01904 643294.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 08000 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170204298 when passing on any information.
