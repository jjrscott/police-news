North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic collision that occurred on Westbourne Grove in Scarborough at about 3.40am on Friday 18 September 2020.

A parked Vauxhall Astra was hit by an unknown vehicle. The driver failed to stop at scene and had left prior to the arrival of police.

The parked vehicle has suffered extensive rear end damage and the force of the collision moved the vehicle into the centre of the road.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the second vehicle involved. If you were driving this vehicle or know who was, please contact the police. If you witnessed the incident or saw a vehicle driving around Scarborough with front end damage at about 4am on 18 September 2020, we would also like to hear from you.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Simon Hunter. You can also email simon.hunter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200163760.
