Last modified: 22 June 2020 at 12:10pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a commercial burglary in Baldersby near Thirsk.

It happened between 8pm on Thursday 11 June and 6.45am on Friday 12 June 2020 when the following items were stolen:

 * Two white Honda CRB125 motorbikes
 * A Yamaha R6 motorbike
 * Two Honda generators

Following enquiries, one of the Honda motorbikes was in recovered in Middlesbrough last week however officers are still appealing for information about the burglary or whereabouts of the remaining items.

In particular, they would like to hear from anyone who has been offered a motorbike or generator for sale under suspicious circumstances.

Anyone who witnessed suspicious activity in the area or has information is asked to email Graham.Truman@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 12200098788.
