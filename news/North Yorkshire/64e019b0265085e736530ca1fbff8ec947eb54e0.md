Last modified: 2 December 2019 at 05:46pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic incident that occurred at the roundabout off the High Street in Northallerton at the entrance of an unnamed road leading into the Applegarth Carpark.

It happened at around 9am on Saturday 23 November 2019 when a 72-year-old female who was crossing the road to the entrance of the Applegarth Car park, Northallerton when she was hit by a silver car.

The female sustained injuries to her hand and ankle and was taken to the local hospital for treatment.

Police are appealing for witnesses to the incident to contact them. Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 618 SHARRAD. You can also email amy.sharrad@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190215334
