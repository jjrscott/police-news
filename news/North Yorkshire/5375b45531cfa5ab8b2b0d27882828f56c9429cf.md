Police are appealing for witnesses and information in relation to two incidents of arson at a farm in Stokesley.

??????????????

Whitehouse Farm in Stokesley has suffered two indcidents of Arson in the past week.

The first incident happend at aorund 5.30am on Monday 6 April 2015, when bails of straw were set on fire. During the incident, the barn was also covered in graffiti by the offender (s).

The second incident happened at around 6.15pm on 8 April 2015 when an amount of logs were set on fire.

Anyone who saw anything suspicious in the area around the times of these incidents, or who has information to assist the investigation into these crimes, is asked to contact the police.

Also, anyone who recognises the graffitti "tagging" (pictures attached) is asked to pass on information to the police.

Dial 101 and ask to speak to PC Blair Taylor, or email blair.taylor@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, information can be passed on anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference numbers 12150057240 and 12150055667.

Friday 10 April 2015.
