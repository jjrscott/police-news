Police are appealing for witnesses to a serious collision on the A172 to contact them.

At 12.15am on Monday 2 September 2019 a white Nissan Navara pick-up truck and a JCB tractor, which was towing a trailer, collided on the A172 near to Chestnut Farm, Busby, near Stokesley.

The driver of the Nissan Navara sustained serious injuries and was taken to James Cook Hospital for treatment.

Police are appealing for anyone who either witnessed the collision or saw either vehicle prior to it, to contact them. They are particularly interested to speak to anyone who may have captured dash cam footage.

Please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and ask to speak to the Major Collision Investigation Unit. You can also email MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk - please quote reference 12190162453.
