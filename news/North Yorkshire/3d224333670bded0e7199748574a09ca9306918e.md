On Saturday 11 June 2016 at around 4.50pm, a road traffic collision happened when a 48 seater coach left the road and went into some trees. The incident happened on the A171 near Cloughton Bank.

The coach was on a weekend excursion to the east coast and had travelled across from Southport and in was travelling towards Scarborough when the collision happened.

Four passengers and the driver sustained minor injuries in the crash. They were taken to Scarborough Hospital and released later that same evening.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or who saw the coach prior to the crash, or who has any information is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to TC283 Gareth Gilleard.
