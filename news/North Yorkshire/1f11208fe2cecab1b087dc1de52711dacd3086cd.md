North Yorkshire Police is appealing for the public’s help to locate 53 year old Deighton man, Gerrard Burrell-Hodgson who has not been seen since 10am at his home on Deighton Lane, Deighton yesterday (1 March 2021).

Gerrard is 6ft 1 in height with a stubble beard and short dark hair. He is believed to be driving a grey Land Rover and could be in the Northallerton/Thirsk area.

Officers are extremely concerned for Gerrard’s welfare.

If you have any information which could help locate him please call 101 or 999 for an immediate sighting.
