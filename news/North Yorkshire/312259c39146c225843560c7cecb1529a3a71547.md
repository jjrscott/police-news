North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Burglary that occurred on St Winifreds Avenue West in Harrogate.

It happened on St Winifreds Avenue West in Harrogate between 10pm on the 18th August 2015 and 5.30am 19th August 2015. Offenders have gained entry to a residential property in that area and personal belongings removed.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 46 Brendon Frith. You can also email Brendon.Frith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150144929
