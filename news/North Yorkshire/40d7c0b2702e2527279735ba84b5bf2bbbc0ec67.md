Police in Harrogate have issued CCTV images of two men they want to trace in connection with a theft from Sainsbury's store.

On 28 March 2017, a Dyson vacuum cleaner was stolen from the shop on Wetherby Road.
Officers want to the speak to the men about the theft and are appealing to anyone who can help identify them to get in touch.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Adam Smith, or email

adam.smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170054483 when passing on information.
