North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a bicycle that occurred in Selby.

The bike was stolen from the front of Abbey Lodge on Leeds Road, Selby between 7am and 6pm on Sunday 30 July 2017.

The stolen bike is a man's "Mongoose" BMX with a silver frame and purple trims.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1944 Alison BAKER. You can also email Alison.baker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-30072017-0518.
