North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary in Boroughbridge.

It occurred on New Row between 12:15pm and 2.15pm on Thursday 4 January 2018. The suspect(s) have broken into the house, taken the car keys and stolen the homeowners car which was parked on the drive.

The stolen car is a Mercedes CLS-350 in matt grey.

Police are appealing for anyone who was in the area at the time of the incident and saw anything suspicious to get in touch.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 614 Ross Baldwin. You can also email ross.baldwin@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180002048.
