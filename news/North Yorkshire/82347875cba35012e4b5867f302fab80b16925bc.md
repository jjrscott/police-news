Police are appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision in Richmondshire.

It happened on Monday 16 February at around 10.30am on the B6267 near to the Kirklington crossroads.

A vehicle, believed to be blue in colour, left the road, colliding with a wooden post. It failed to stop at the scene and would have had significant damage caused to it as a result of the collision.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Katie Shaw or email katie.shaw@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-16022015-0074
