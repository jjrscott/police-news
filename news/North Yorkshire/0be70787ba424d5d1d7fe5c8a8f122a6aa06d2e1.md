Last modified: 9 May 2018 at 10:45am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a minor collision that occurred on Skinner Street, Whitby.

It happened at approximately 4.15pm on Friday 21 October 2016 and involved a black Mitsubishi Shogun travelling the wrong way along Skinner Street which then collided with a pedestrian near to The Granby Hotel.

The pedestrian was not seriously injured and did not require hospital treatment.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

In particular, we are appealing to any eyewitnesses who can provide vehicle details.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Neil Graham at Whitby Police Station. You can also email neil.graham@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160192236.
