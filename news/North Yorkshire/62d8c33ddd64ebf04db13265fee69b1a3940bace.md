Last modified: 25 March 2019 at 02:24pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary at Admiral Casino, Selby.

Two men gained access to the casino at 2.20am and returned at 4.30am on Wednesday, 13 March. They damaged gaming machines and stole cash.

Police are appealing for any witnesses who may have seen anything suspicious in the area at these times to contact them.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Tom Longhorn. You can also email Thomas.longhorn@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190045306.
