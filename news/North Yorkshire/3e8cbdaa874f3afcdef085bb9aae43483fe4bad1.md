Overnight between Friday, 21 April and Saturday, 22 April a large quantity of tack was stolen during two burglaries in Flixton and officers need your help in finding those responsible.

Overnight between Friday, 21 April and Saturday, 22 April a large quantity of tack was stolen during two burglaries in Flixton and officers need your help in finding those responsible.

The stolen horse riding tack included:

 * A navy Gatehouse Hickstead velvet hat (pictured)
 * A velvet Charles Owen H2000 hat (pictured)
 * Blue hat bag (pictured)
 * A diamante head collar (pictured)
 * A 15 inch pony saddle
 * A 16 inch pony saddle
 * Horse clippers (pictured)
 * Jeffries Pelham English leather bridle with plaited browband
 * Tools including bolt croppers and gardening tools

In particular officers are keen to speak with anyone who saw a vehicle in suspicious circumstances or any other unusual activity in the area to contact them with information. If you have recently purchased similar items through online market or auction sites which you believe may connect to the theft, please also contact officers.

Information can be provided directly to police by calling 101, selecting option 2, and asking for Harriet Green. Information can also be provided by emailing harriet.green@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous please contact the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please provide reference 12170068470 when providing information.
