Police are appealing for information to help identify the man in the e-fit. Officers want to speak to the man in connection with a sneak-in burglary that occurred in Aysgarth, Leyburn on 27 September 2016.

View image

The incident happened between 3.30pm and 3.45pm, when the occupier of the property saw a man inside the house who quickly walked off.

Anyone who can help identify the man in the image, or who saw anything suspicious in the area at the time and has information to assist the investigation, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Andy Foster or email andy.forter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160176261 when passing on information.
