North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Harrogate around midnight on Thursday 22 December 2016.

It happened at the Viper Rooms Night Club on Parliament Street and involved a group of men assaulting a 25 year old man. Other people were also assaulted. Two men, a 23 year old and a 26 year old, both form Leeds, were arrested in connection with the incident. Both have been released on bail pending further enquiries.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the names of other victims who are yet to come forward, along with details of any other offenders.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Chris Hind. You can also email Chris.Hind@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160230130.
