North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred in Elvington, York.

It happened between 5pm on Wednesday 3 July and 8.45am on Thursday 4 July 2019 at Harrier Court, Airfield Industrial Estate, Elvington, and involved persons cutting into a commercial property and removing stock from within, taking between £10,000 and £20,000 worth of blenders, juicers and dehydrators.

The stolen items include:

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any vehicles, suspicious activity or persons in the area of the industrial estate between the times above. We are also interested if anyone tries to sell large numbers of blenders and juicers in your area.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Chris Dalby. You can also email chris.dalby@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190078002.
