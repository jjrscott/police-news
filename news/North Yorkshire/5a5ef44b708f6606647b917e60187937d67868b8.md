North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information in realtion to a break into a caravan parked on Sawmill Lane in Helmsely

Police are asking for the assistance of the public in relation to a caravan that was broken into over the past two weeks.

The caravan was parked in a compound on Sawmill Lane, Helmsley when the offender forced entry and stole several items from inside.

Officers are carrying out enquiries and are appealing to anyone who has any information about who is responsible.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1 and pass information to the Force Control Room.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160160140 when passing on information.
