A man from North Yorkshire has been handed a Criminal Behaviour Order preventing him from drinking alcohol in public areas throughout the county.

A man from North Yorkshire has been handed a Criminal Behaviour Order preventing him from drinking alcohol in public areas throughout the county.

Tim Morris, 35, has caused issues around in the area due to excessive alcohol consumption. He now banned from consuming alcohol in public places, except licensed premises within North Yorkshire.

Morris must also attend 10 sessions with Changing Lives over a four month period.

Anyone who witnesses a breach of the Criminal Behaviour Order should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101.
