Officers from North Yorkshire Police's Firearms Support Unit were called to Merry Lees Caravan Park at Staxton, Scarborough, this afternoon (Saturday 14 September 2018) to detain a distressed man who was threatening to harm himself with a knife.

The incident was reported to the police by concerned members of the public at 12.37pm.

Local officers responded initially and then called for back-up from armed officers when it was confirmed the man was carrying a knife.

The incident was brought to a safe conclusion at 2.30pm when the 62-year-old local man was detained outside a nearby pub.

He has been taken into custody on suspicion of possessing an offensive weapon in a public place. He is receiving medical support.
