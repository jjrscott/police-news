North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about two burglaries which occurred in West Witton, Leyburn.

The first burglary happened on Chantry Garth between 4 and 7 March 2020. It involved unknown offender(s) smashing the rear window of the property, before performing an untidy search, taking some jewellery and exiting the property.

The second burglary happened The Wensleydale Heifer Hotel on Main Street overnight between 15 and 16 March 2020. It involved unknown offender(s) entering the premises and taking the cash safe, staff tips and till float, before exiting the premises.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incidents.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious vehicles or circumstances between the key times.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Holly Grosvenor. You can also email holly.grosvenor@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference numbers 12200039842 or 12200045015.
