North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a serious of frauds that have occurred in York and the surrounding areas.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the identity of a woman suspected of obtaining property and services from various beauty salons in York and the surrounding areas.

The woman is described as an attractive, slim, well dressed lady, around 60-years-old. She is around 5ft 6in tall, with a grey or blonde bob hair style, and is also described as having distinctive piercing blue eyes and an educated southern accent.

In addition, the woman is believed to drive a black Jaguar convertible with possibly a personalised number plate.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for the Safer Neighbourhood Service Desk.

You can also email sntservdesk@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-24042015-0456
