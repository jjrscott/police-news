North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft and criminal damage to a motor vehicle on Sandybed Lane, Scarborough.

It happened on Sandybed Lane between 2000hrs on the 2nd of February 2015 and 1050hrs on 3rd of February 2015 whereby a motor vehicle had the passenger side door lock removed and the driver's side door lock damaged, some items were also removed from the vehicle.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any person or persons who may have been seen in the area at the time acting suspiciously.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Adam Gallimore. You can also email adam.gallimore@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150018643.
