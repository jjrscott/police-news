Police are appealing for witnesses and information about the death of 14 sheep near Huttons Ambo.

The sheep were fine at 6.30am on Wednesday 7 June 2017 - in a field at the top of Golden Hill close to the A64 - but were found dead on Thursday 8 June 2017 at 9.15am.

It is believed that the sheep, 11 ewes and three lambs, died after being chased around their field by dogs, causing them to become distressed and run into boundary fences.

Officers are investigating the incident and are appealing for anyone who witnessed the sheep being chased to get in touch.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 168 David Meer You can also email david.meer@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170099759.
