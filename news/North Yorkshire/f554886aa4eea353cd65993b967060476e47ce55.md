Police in Tadcaster continue to appeal for information after a woman was assaulted in the early hours of Sunday 19 May 2019.

The incident happened on Bridge Street, Tadcaster at around 2am when a woman was assaulted by another woman and sustained bruising to her face and wrists and a fracture to the base of her thumb.

A 23-year-old woman has been arrested in connection with the incident and released on conditional police bail.

Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward including a woman who came to the victim's aid and two men in the area at the time who may have witnessed the assault.

If you are the woman who helped or have any information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Katie Swift, or collar number 6-4-3-7. You can also email Katie.Swift@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190089825.
