Following this appeal, Jean Love’s next of kin has come forward. Thank you all for your help in sharing the appeal.

**Original release: York Coroner’s Office next of kin appeal for Jean Love**

York Coroner’s Office have issued a next of kin appeal to trace family members of a woman who has passed away.

Jean Love, aged 75, from Nursery Road, Nether Poppleton, York, was found at her home on Monday 11 November 2019.

The death is not being treated as suspicious.

Enquiries so far have not yet established any next of kin for Ms Love.

York Coroner’s Office is appealing for people to come forward who may be able to find her relatives.

If you can help please contact 01609 643123.
