Police are appealing for information after the windscreen of a Vauxhall Vivaro van was damaged in Harrogate.

It happened on Hookstone Chase at around 5am in the early hours of 16 July 2018. The suspect was walking along the road, paused at the vehicle before walked off in the direction of Morrisons.

If you saw the suspect or have any information please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 547 Sharon Leverton.

You can also email Sharon.Leverton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180129532

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
