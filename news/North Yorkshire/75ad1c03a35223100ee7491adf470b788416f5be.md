North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a car was vandalised on Dean Road, Scarborough.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a car was vandalised on Dean Road, Scarborough.

The damage was caused to the vehicle just before 7.30pm on Thursday, 21 February and officers are asking anyone with CCTV or dashcam footage of the area around this time to contact them.

Anyone who can assist with the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police quoting reference 12190032989.

If you wish to provide information anonymously then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 111 555.
