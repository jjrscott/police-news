North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in York.

It happened at the junction of South Bank Avenue and Bishopthorpe Road around 4.45am on Sunday 20 May 2018, and involved a lone male who was attacked and left unconscious on the street.

The victim suffered minor lacerations to his face and chest. He attended York Hospital and has since been released.

Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward with any information about the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Sarah Jobe of York Serious Crime Team. You can also email sarah.jobe@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180088209.
