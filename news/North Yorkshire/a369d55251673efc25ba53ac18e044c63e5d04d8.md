Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:24am

Selby Police are investigating an attempted burglary at a house on Barlby Road.

It happened between 5pm on Saturday 17 February and noon on Sunday 18 February 2018.

The offenders tried to gain entry to the house by smashing the top section of the kitchen window at the rear of the property. An attempt was also made to jemmy open the UPVC window frame. Nothing was stolen.

Anyone with information about this incident or anyone who possibly recalls suspicious behaviour in the area around this time, are urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, option 2, and ask for Tim McGreevy. Or email tim.mcgreevy@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Please quote reference number 12180029141 when providing details about this incident.
