Last modified: 30 September 2019 at 06:09pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for anyone who recognises this jewellery, in particular those who may have travelled to Scarborough on holiday recently.

Extensive enquiries have taken place following a number of suspected thefts from a hotel in Scarborough.

With the investigation is in its final stages, no victims have identified in relation to these items

If you think any of this jewellery belongs to you, please use the following details to contact DC Amy McDougall.

In any contact please state; your full name, address, contact number and any information you can provide regarding when and where you think this Jewellery might have been taken from.

Please note, before any items are released you will be required to show proof of ownership.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for AMY MCDOUGALL. You can also email amy.coverdale@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190078361.
