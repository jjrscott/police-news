North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of four iPhones from the O2 shop on Gowthorpe, Selby.

It happened on Monday 19th October 2015 at 13:25hrs and involved two males entering the shop and cutting the security wires from the phone before running from the shop with four iPhones.

The suspects are both white males, approximately 18-20 years old, both of slim build and approximately 5'10\. Both males were wearing grey tracksuit bottoms, one had a grey hooded top on and one had a black jacket one. One of the males was wearing a black baseball cap.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1944 Alison BAKER You can also email alison.baker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-19102015-0257.
