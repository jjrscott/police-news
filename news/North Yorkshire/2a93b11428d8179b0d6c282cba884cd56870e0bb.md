North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of catalytic converter on Burton Green, York.

It happened on Burton Green York at 12.45pm on Saturday 12 December 2020 when three males wearing face coverings and head wear attended the location in a dark grey Volkswagen Golf. They stole a catalytic converter from the victim's car and one of them threatened the victim with a metal bar.

Officers are appealing to anyone who was in the area at the time and saw the grey Golf or anyone acting suspiciously.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Antony Lockey Smith. You can also email antony.lockey-smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200227679
