North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic incident that occurred on the A59.

It happened near Flaxby around 5.30pm on Sunday 27 November 2016.

A road traffic incident occurred on the A59 near Flaxby, Knaresborough at around 7.30pm on Sunday 27 November 2016 involving a blue Volvo V70 Estate and a white Skoda Fabia, whereby the Volvo has collided with the front bumper of the Skoda.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle(s) prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Lynn Maguire. You can also email lynn.maguire@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160216527
