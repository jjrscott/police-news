Last modified: 6 March 2020 at 04:28pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a serious road traffic collision that occurred at Gillamoor Bank, Kirbymoorside.

It happened on Gillamoor Bank at approximately 11am on Thursday 5 March 2020 and involved a red Honda CB500 motorcycle and what a witness describes as a small green or blue van, similar to a Peugeot Partner.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision, anyone who recalls seeing the vehicles prior to the collision or has any dash camera footage of the vehicles, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the green or blue van, as the occupants may have important information which would assist the investigation.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to PC 974 Isaac Carter. You can also email Isaac.Carter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200038531.
