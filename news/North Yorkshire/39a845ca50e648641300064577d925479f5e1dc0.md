Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:24am

Police have issued CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with a theft from a shop in Selby.

At 7.40pm on Saturday 13 January 2018 a man entered the Sainsburys store on Abbey Walk, Selby.

The man removed the tags and concealed two bottles of Jack Daniels spirit in his bags before leaving the store without making an attempt to pay for them.

Officers would like to speak to man pictured on CCTV, as he may have important information that could assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises him is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for Nicola Knowles or email nicola.knowles@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12180008054 in connection with this appeal.
