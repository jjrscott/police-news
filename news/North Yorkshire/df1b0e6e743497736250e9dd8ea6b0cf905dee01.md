Last modified: 1 February 2021 at 05:00pm

Police have requested information about, or CCTV footage of, a commercial burglary that occurred at a garage in Albion Road, Scarborough.

The incident happened at about 3am on Saturday 30 January 2021. High-value tools and equipment were stolen, including an off-road pit bike.

Officers are asking members of the public to get in touch if they have information that will help with the investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Sophie Milner or email sophie.milner@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12210048530 when passing on information.
