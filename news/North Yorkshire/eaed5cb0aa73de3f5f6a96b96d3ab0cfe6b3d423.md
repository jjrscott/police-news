SKipton police are appealing for witnesses and information following an assault on a local man on Keighley Road Skipton at approximately 3 pm on Tuesday 16 August 2016.

The incident involved 2 men, one of whom was riding his pushbike into the town centre when a confrontation took place in the lead up to the assault.

The offender left on foot leaving the victim to make his way into the town centre seeking help .

As a result of the assault, the victim has suffered minor injuries to his face.
