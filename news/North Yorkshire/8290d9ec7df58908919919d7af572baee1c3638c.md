North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred near Great Ayton.

It happened at a farm on Dikes Lane, Great Ayton between 11.30pm on Saturday 15 April and 9.40am on Sunday 16 April whereby a number of items were taken including a red 2001 Honda TRX quad bike, a red and white BETA EVO ZT 250 off road motorcycle, two NX13 EKZ, Specialized mountain bikes - one a full suspension in black and silver, the other a hard tail in red and white. Also taken were two chainsaws - a Husquarvana and a Stihl and a Stihl petrol strimmer.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious vehicles seen in the area.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Chris Villiers. You can also email chris.villiers@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
