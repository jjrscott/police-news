Police have issued a renewed appeal for information following an incident in Long Preston near Skipton in which a manâ€™s jaw was broken.

It happened on the morning of Friday 11 December 2020 at around 9.30am in the area of The Green near to some temporary traffic lights that were in place at the time.

The incident involved two men and resulted in one of them sustaining a broken jaw.

Police previously appealed for information shortly after the incident and have thanked those members of the public that have come forward so far. Officers are appealing to anyone who may have information but has not yet come forward to contact them, including anyone who was passing through along the A65.

Anyone with information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and ask for Harrogate Investigation Hub. You can also email InvestigationHubCounty@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200225840.
