Last modified: 8 March 2019 at 10:57am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a motorcycle in Scarborough.

It happened on Tennyson Avenue between 5.50pm and 8.30pm on 5 March 2019.

The motorcycle was found in an alleyway on Tennyson avenue with a substantial amount of damage the following day.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul O'Neill. You can also email Paul.O'Neill@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-05032019-0475.
