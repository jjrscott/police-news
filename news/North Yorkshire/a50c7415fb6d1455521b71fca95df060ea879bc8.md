North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of horse saddles that occurred on the outskirts of Leavening village, North Yorkshire.

It is believed to have taken place on the evening of Saturday 20 May and involved the theft of three horse saddles from a farm building on Hanging Hill Lane.

Two of the saddles were rare Ortho-flex endurance long distance saddles (pictured). They are described as being similar to Western or Australian type saddles but without the saddle horn.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who might have seen or been offered the saddles.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul Fricker.

You can also email Paul.Fricker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170102813.
