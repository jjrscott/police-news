North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a house burglary that occurred in Otley.

It happened on Riverside Drive between 7am and 4.20pm on Tuesday 8 March 2016. Entry to the house was gained from the back which looks out onto the main road, Farnley Lane.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for any potential witnesses to the offence or any persons / vehicles acting suspiciously in that vicinity between those times.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Gary Anderson.You can also email gary.anderson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160041270
