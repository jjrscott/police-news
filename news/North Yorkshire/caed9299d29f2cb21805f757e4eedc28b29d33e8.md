Last modified: 23 January 2020 at 05:05pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following an attempted vehicle theft that occurred on West Square, Scarborough.

It happened overnight between the hours of 9pm on 15 January and 12pm on 16 January when a silver Mercedes Sprinter Van was broken into and tampered with in an attempt to steal the vehicle.

We are appealing for witnesses who may have been in the area at that time or seen anyone acting suspiciously around vehicles in West Square.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Natalie Marshall. You can also email natalie.marshall@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200008866.
