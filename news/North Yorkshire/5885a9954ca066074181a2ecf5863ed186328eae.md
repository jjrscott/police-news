North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about thefts from vehicles that occurred in Sea View Drive, Scarborough.

It happened on Sea View Drive between 02.00-06.30 hours on Sunday 5th April 2015 where two parked vehicles were entered and items stolen from them.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who saw anyone suspicious in the Sea View Drive area of Scarborough or anyone who knows any information regarding the theft.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 277 Ryan Lyth. You can also email ryan.lyth@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150055042
