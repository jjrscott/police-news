Ripon Neighbourhood Policing Team and Harrogate District Comunity Safety Partnership are launching operation Ottawa on Wednesday 16th November 2017 which is an operation aimed at improving home security and reducing domestic burglary.

With the nights drawing in, unoccupied houses can be left vulnerable and targets for opportunist criminals.

In order to make houses less attractive to this type of offence Ripon Neighbourhood Policing Team have joined forces with the Harrogate District Community Safety Partnership to supply and fit timer switches in the Ripon District.

PCSO Glen Cooper and members of the Ripon Neighbourhood Policing Team will be in the residential areas of Ripon from Wednesday, identifying vulnerable premises, offering crime prevention advice and fitting free timer switches.
