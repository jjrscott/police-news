North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Commercial Burglary that occurred in East Heslerton near Malton.

It happened at Wolds View Farm, East Heslerton at 00:55hrs on Wednesday 8 July 2015. Offenders entered a secure stable block where a quantity of horse tack was stolen. They made off in a white panel van in the direction of Malton

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the white panel van that left the scene.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Nick Ham. You can also email nicholas.ham@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
