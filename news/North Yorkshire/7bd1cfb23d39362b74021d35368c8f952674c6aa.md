Four men have been charged in connection with an attack on a 27-year-old man in Sherburn in Elmet on 27 December 2015.

All four men have been charged with wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm and are due to appear at Leeds Magistrates' Court today (Friday 22 January 2016).
They are:
Lee Cormack, 34, from Sherburn in Elmet
Ross Michael Nicholson, 28, from Micklefield, Leeds
Aaron George Pinkerton, 26, from Sherburn in Elmet
Gareth Stephen Roberts, 29, from Biggin, Leeds

The victim remains in Hull Royal Infirmary in a critical condition.
