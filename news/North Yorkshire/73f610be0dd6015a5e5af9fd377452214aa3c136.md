Police have seized drugs after a warrant was executed in York today.

At 9.30am, officers forced entry to a property on Dale Street, off Nunnery Lane. Inside the house, they located an amount of cannabis, and syringes containing a brown substance.

The warrant was executed by the York South Neighbourhood Policing Team, with support from the force's Operational Support Unit. Police were acting on information received from the local community.

The investigation is ongoing.

A spokesperson for North Yorkshire Police said: "Anyone with information about activity connected to illegal drugs should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101. As this morning's warrant shows, we can and will act on community concerns and take proactive enforcement action."
