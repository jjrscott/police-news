North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from a building development site that occurred in Low Worsall near Yarm. North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from a building development site that occurred off Manor Close, Low Worsall.

It happened off Manor Close, Low Worsall, between 6.25pm and 7.05pm on Saturday 16 January 2021 and involved offenders entering the site and removing the following items:

• Clarke 5.5KVA petrol generator
• A set of electricians yellow step ladders with ‘NEE’ wrote on them in marker pen
• A box of general electrical items

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious vehicles or persons sighted in the area around the time stated above in the Low Worsall area particularly near the Public House car park on the B1264.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1255 Jack Dodsworth. You can also email Jack.Dodsworth1255@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12210024849.
