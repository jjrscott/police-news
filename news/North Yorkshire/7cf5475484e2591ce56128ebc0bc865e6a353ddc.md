The 58-year-old York man who was charged with wounding following an assault in an alleyway near the Fulford Arms on Fulford Road in York, has now been charged with murder following the death of the victim.

The victim, 45-year-old Benjamin James Traherne of Ordnance Lane, York, died in York District Hospital on Monday 7 March after being taken there following the assault at around 9.45pm on the night of Friday 19 February 2016.

Kenneth Dunnington from York was originally charged with wounding Mr Traherne and had been remanded in custody since appearing in court earlier this month.

He was today (22 March 2016) charged with the murder of Mr Traherne.

He is due to appear at York Magistrates' Court tomorrow morning (23 March 2016).
