Police have released images of a man they want to speak to after a stolen bank card was used in a shop in York.

The transactions were made in an Argos shop in York at 9.20am on Thursday, 23 November 2017 - a day after the card was stolen.

Police want to speak to a man pictured by CCTV in connection with the incident.

If you recognise him or have any information that could help enquiries, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Christopher Bolton.

You can also contact PC Bolton on Christopher.Bolton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12170210212 when passing on information.
