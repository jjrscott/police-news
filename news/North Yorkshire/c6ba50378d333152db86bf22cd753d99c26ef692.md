We are appealing for information about the theft of a caravan from Egton.

Some time between 5pm on Friday the 26 June 2020 and 10.30am Sunday the 28 June 2020 a caravan was stolen from a caravan site in Egton, Whitby.

The caravan is an Elddis Buccaneer, it is mainly white in colour with a grey front and it is not expected to be displaying any registration plates at this time.

If anyone has any information to offer on the location of this caravan please can they contact North Yorkshire Police and quote reference number 12200109365.
