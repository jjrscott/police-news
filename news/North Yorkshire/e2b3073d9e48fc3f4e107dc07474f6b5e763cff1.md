Last modified: 10 October 2018 at 04:58pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for any witnesses to a serious road traffic collision which occurred on the A629 Airevalley Trunk Road between Steeton and Kildwick.

It happened at at approximately 8.40pm on Tuesday 9 October 2018 on the Kildwick bound carriageway just prior to the roundabout at Kildwick.

It involved a blue Vauxhall Corsa which was travelling towards Kildwick from Steeton.

The 20 year-old driver was taken by air ambulance to Leeds General Infirmary. She is described as being in a stable condition.

The three passengers were taken to Airedale Hospital by road ambulance.

Traffic Constable Mark Brook of the Roads Policing Group said:

"I would urge any witnesses to the incident or any person/s within vehicles in the vicinity, that may have been witness to the collision, to come forward to assist with the investigation."

Please call 101 and ask for Mark Brook or email Mark.Brook@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk quoting reference no 12180189147.
