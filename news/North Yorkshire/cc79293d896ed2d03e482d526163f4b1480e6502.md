North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following a burglary at a property in Copmanthorpe.

The incident occurred between 1.15pm-2.10pm on Thursday, 23 August at a detached property on Moor Lane.

If you saw anything suspicious around the time the offence occurred or have any further information which may assist police with their investigation please contact us with information.

if you have information which may assist officers please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101. If you wish to remain anonymous then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180157917.
