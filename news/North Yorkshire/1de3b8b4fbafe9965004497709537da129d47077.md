The investigation into the whereabouts of Cleo, the dog believed to have been stolen from a rescue centre in Whitby has concluded and it has been deemed no criminal offences had occurred.

It was initially believed Cleo, who is a black and white German shepherd and a collie cross dog, was taken from the shelter between Tuesday, 28 March and Wednesday, 29 March. Following a number of enquiries it was established she had been removed from the centre lawfully.

Thank you to everyone who assisted with the appeal for information.
