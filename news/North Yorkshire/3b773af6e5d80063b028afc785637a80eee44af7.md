North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Alma Parade, Scarborough.

Overnight on the 8th to the 9th of September 2015 the Alma Public House was burgled. Offenders broke a side window and entered the premises stealing two charity boxes containing an unspecified amount of cash . It is requested that any information which may lead to the apprehension of the persons responsible for this offence be forwarded to North Yorkshire Police, for the attention of PC 604 Andy Grant.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150158752.

12 September 2015
