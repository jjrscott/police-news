Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision on the A64 near York.

The collision occurred on Friday 26 February 2021, at about 2.20pm, at the Towthorpe crossroads.

It involved a white Transit van and a red Audi. The Audi was travelling from the Malton area towards York, and the Transit was travelling from Strensall towards Stockton-on-the-Forest.

Two people suffered serious injuries in the collision.

Anyone who saw the collision, or either vehicle prior to it, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police by phone on 101, or email glen.robinson470@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk Please quote incident reference 12210067383 when passing information.
