Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward following a fatal collision in York earlier today, Sunday 6 May 2018.

Officers investigating the incident are also asking anyone with dash cam footage to send it to police.

The collision happened shortly before 10am on the A64 East Bound at Grimston Bar.

It involved a Triumph Rocket Motorcycle and a black VW Passatt .

Sadly, the rider of the motorcycle, a man in his early fifties from the Nottingham area, died at the scene.

The pillion passenger, a woman in her early fifties, was taken to the LGI by air ambulance, with serious injuries.

The occupants of the VW Passatt from the Durham area were uninjured in the incident, but extremely shaken.

Anyone who witnessed the collision is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to TC Cobb in the Major Collision Investigation Team.

Anyone who has dash cam footage is asked to send it to MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number NYP-06052018-0203 when passing on any information.
