North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary in the centre of Skipton.

The incident occurred sometime between the afternoon of Wednesday 18 November and the afternoon of Thursday 19 November from garages located near Water street in the centre of SKIPTON, North Yorkshire.

A ladies Cannondale Trail 7 bike in midnight blue (a dark navy metallic) was stolen.

Any sightings or information please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 12200204169.
