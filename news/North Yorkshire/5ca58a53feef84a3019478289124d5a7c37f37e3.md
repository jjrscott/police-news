North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred on the edge of Hardcastle Moor, near Merryfield Mines, West Lane, Bewerley.

The incident ocurred on 23rd September 2015 at around 9pm. Unknown offenders broke into a barn and stole three quad bikes. The offenders then rode the bikes all the way along West Lane and through Bewerley village around 9.30pm.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who may have seen the quad bikes being driven through Bewerley or in the area. In addition anyone who may have seen a van or vans or trailer parked up in the area or who saw any unfamiliar person or vehicle on West Lane or any of the tracks and paths in that area earlier in the evening.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Bill Hickson. You can also email bill.hickson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150168906.
