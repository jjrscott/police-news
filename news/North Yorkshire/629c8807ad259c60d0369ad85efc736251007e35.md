North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a shed burglary at a farm on the outskirts of Ugthorpe, near Whitby, at around 10.45pm on 17 August 2015.

The offenders removed tools and were disturbed before making off, possibly in a vehicle towards the A171.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for LOUISE NEVILLE-BECK. You can also email louise.neville-beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150144279.
