Last modified: 23 October 2019 at 04:10pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred in Marks and Spencer on Friarage Street, Northallerton, at around 4pm on Tuesday 3 September 2019.

The incident involved two men and two women who entered the store with empty trollies and filled them up with items to a value of around £150. They left the store without offering payment.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the identity of these individuals.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Bradley Moss. You can also email Bradley.moss@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190164324.
