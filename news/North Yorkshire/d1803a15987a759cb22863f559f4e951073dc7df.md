North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a disturbance outside the Waterhouse pub in Scarborough.

It happened on St Thomas Street at approximately 0230hrs on 17/12/2017 and involve allegations that a man used derogatory language regarding a woman's ethnic origin.

In particular, we are appealing for information about what was said to the woman.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andrew Metcalfe. You can also email Andrew.Metcalfe473@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170224654
