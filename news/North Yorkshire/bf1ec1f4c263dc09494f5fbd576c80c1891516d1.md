Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:14am

Police were called at about 4.15pm today with a report of a person in the river at Knaresborough.

Police, fire and ambulance attended the scene and sadly the body of a man was recovered from the water a short time later. Investigations are ongoing, but it is not believed there are any suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

We are not in a position to identify the man at this time.
