North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following a burglary at a care home in York.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following a burglary at a care home on Haxby Road in York.

The offence occurred at a care home in Haxby between Saturday, 5 January and Saturday, 12 January.

Several items, including the following were taken during the burglary:

 * A decorative china plate with a thick brown rim and decorated with a river scene
 * A black marble effect medal with a brass rim and a white rose like figure in the centre
 * A red brandy glass
 * An amber glass vase fluted around the top with a turquoise frill

Anyone who has information on the offence or who believes has seen the items since they were taken is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, selecting option 2, and asking for PC Joanne Shanley. Alternatively you can email joanne.shanley@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to provide information anonymously the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12190009283 when providing information.
