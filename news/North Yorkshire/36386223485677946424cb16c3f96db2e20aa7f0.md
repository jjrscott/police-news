North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Cowling.

It happened at Park Lane at 1.50pm on Thursday 19 February 2015 and involved an outbuilding building being broken into, on Park Lane. The offenders made off after being disturbed by an alarm.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to identify the people involved.

In particular, we are appealing for information about a silver vehicle seen leaving the road at the time of the offence towards Crosshills

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andrew Ingram.

You can also email Andrew.Ingram@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150027646
