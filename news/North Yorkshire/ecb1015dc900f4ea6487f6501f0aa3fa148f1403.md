Last modified: 15 October 2020 at 02:46pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in the village of Swainby, whereby motorcycles, a pit bike and electric bicycles were taken after entry was made to a residential garage in the area.

It happened in the village of Swainby, off the A172, in the early hours of Saturday 3 October 2020, between 1am and 6am.

A Honda CRF 150 off-road pit bike in red was stolen from a garage, along with a black and orange Specialized Keveno electric bicycle with Troy Lee graphics and Fox 36 orange forks. A KTM electric bicycle in black and orange was also taken along with a white ARAI full head motorcycle helmet .

Two other motorcycles were also taken, but recovered nearby a short time later.

Anyone with information regarding the location of any of the above property or any other information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Stephen Lifton. You can also email Stephen.lifton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200173685.
