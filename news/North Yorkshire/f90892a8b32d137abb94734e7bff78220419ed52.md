Officers are appealing for witnesses following a collision in York in which a 15-year-old girl suffered injuries.

Officers are appealing for witnesses following a collision in York in which a 15-year-old girl suffered injuries.

The collision occurred on Poppleton Road with the junction with Lindsay Avenue which involved the girl who was travelling on her bike and a vehicle which officers want to trace. The incident occurred at aroun 7.30pm on Wednesday, 5 September.

The girl was left with injuries which are believed to be minor but did require hospital treatment.

Anyone who has information which may assist with this investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police. Information can be provided to the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference NYP-06092018-0162 when providing information.
