North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about after a man was injured by a air pellet.

It happened at Wentworth Street carpark, Malton at between 4.30pm and 4.45pm on Saturday 29 February .

The man suffered injury to his head that required medical treatment.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Pc70 NEVILLE-BECK. You can also email louise.neville-beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200035771.
