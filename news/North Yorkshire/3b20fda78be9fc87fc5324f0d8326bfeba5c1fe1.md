A brief update in relation to our investigation following the tragic death of Leah Heyes on Saturday 11 May 2019.

Last night (13 May) a second arrest was made by officers investigating the circumstances of Leah's death. An 18-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of class A drugs. He has since been released while under investigation.

The 17-year-old who was arrested previously has also been released while under investigation.

The investigation continues.
