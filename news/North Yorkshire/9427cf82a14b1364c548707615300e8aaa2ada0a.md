Edit this information to suit your appeal North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempt burglary that occurred in Norton

Police are appealing for information about an incident that took place on St Peters Street, Norton, between 12.45pm and 3.30pm on Sunday.

The suspects are believed to have used an implement to force open the window of a building.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Louise Neville-Beck. You can also email louise.neville-beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP22022016-0012
