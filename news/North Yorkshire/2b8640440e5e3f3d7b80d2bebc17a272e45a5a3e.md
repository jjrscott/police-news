49 year-old Brian James from Northallerton was travelling along the A19 around 5.30am on Thursday 8 October 2020 when he was involved in a two-vehicle collision.

The collision happened on the southbound carriageway near South Kilvington when the two vehicles, a black Peugeot driven by Mr James and a white Audi A4, collided and left the road. Mr James was sadly pronounced deceased at the scene. The driver of the Audi was uninjured.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for anyone who was in the area at the time and who may have information or dashcam footage of the collision. If you can help the investigation please email: emma.drummond@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or call North Yorkshire Police on 101 quoting the reference: 12200176606.

Police are currently at the scene of a two vehicle collision on the A19 at South Kilvington.
