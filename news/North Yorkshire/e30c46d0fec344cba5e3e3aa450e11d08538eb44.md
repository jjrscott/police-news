North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from a motor vehicle that occurred in Scarborough

A grey Ford Transit van was parked on Westbourne Grove between 2230hrs 07/03/15 - 0730 hrs 08/03/15. During this time the front driver's side window was smashed and several items stolen from the van

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 246 Gemma Johnson. You can also email Gemma.Johnson246@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150038218
