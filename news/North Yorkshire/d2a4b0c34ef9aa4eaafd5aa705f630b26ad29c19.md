A man has been arrested on suspicion of stealing over Â£3,000 worth of meat from a butcher's shop in Boroughbridge.

At sometime overnight between 24 and 25 January 2015, a burglary occurred at J & L Meats in St James' Square.

Following police enquiries, a 19-year-old man from Northumbria was arrested on Thursday 12 February 2015, on suspicion of burglary.

He has been released on police bail as enquiries continue.

Officers are appealing to anyone with information about the burglary to contact them with information.

Anyone who can help officers with their enquiries is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for Harrogate CID.

If you prefer not to give your name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150013640 when passing information about this incident.
