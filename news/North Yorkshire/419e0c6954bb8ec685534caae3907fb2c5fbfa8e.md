North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage to signs belonging to the gliding club at Rufforth Airfield.

It happened between 12 July and 27 July 2018 and involved an unidentified person causing damage to two signs on the public footpath at the gliding club.

The damage is to two signs that are in place for public safety whilst walking on the airfield.
