North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an injury collision that occurred on Keighley Road at Crosshills.

It happened at 3.10pm on 20 June 2017 and involved a single motor vehicle and a pedestrian.

The pedestrian suffered a fractured left elbow and received treatment at Airedale Hospital

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision and in particular the driver of the vehicle involved which stopped at the time but didn't leave details

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andrew Ingram You can also email Andrew.Ingram@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170109299
