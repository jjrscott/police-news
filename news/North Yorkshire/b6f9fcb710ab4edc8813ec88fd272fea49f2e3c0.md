North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred on Station Road, York.

It happened on Station Road, York at approximately 5.20pm on Tuesday 8 December 2020.

It is reported that two men approached the victim near to a bus stop between Lendal Bridge and the railway station. One of the men has assaulted the victim, a man in his 20s, by pushing him to the chest and attempting to punch him several times. It is reported some of the incident occurred on the road and traffic had to avoid the pair.

The man is described as a white, aged approximately in his early 30s with fine, dark stubble and was wearing a dark blue hoody and grey tracksuit bottoms at the time of the incident. He was described as having a plastic bike lock around his neck and was in company with a white male, aged approximately late 20s with brown hair and a burgundy sweatshirt, riding a cycle at the time of the incident.
