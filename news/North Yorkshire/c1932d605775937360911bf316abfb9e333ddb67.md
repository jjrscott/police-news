Last modified: 31 July 2017 at 12:52pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information regarding a road traffic collision that occurred on Boroughbridge Road, at the junction with Water End, York at approximately 10.40pm on Friday 28 July 2017.

The collision involved a black Toyota Prius, driven by a 59-year-old woman from York and a 19- year-old male pedestrian from York.

The pedestrian was taken to York District Hospital with serious injuries. He was later transferred to Hull Royal Infirmary where he remains in a serious condition.

The Toyota driver was unhurt and is assisting police with their enquiries.

Officers are appealing for any witnesses to contact them, so they can establish the full circumstances around the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, or who recalls seeing the male pedestrian who was wearing blue jeans and a black coat, or the black Toyota Prius prior to the collision, is asked to contact police as soon as possible, to assist the investigation.

Please dial 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to either TC 1470 Gemma Wood or TC67 Mark Mullins.

You can also email Gemma.Wood@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or Mark.Mullins@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170133424
