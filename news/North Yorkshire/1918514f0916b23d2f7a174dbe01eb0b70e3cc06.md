North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempt burglary that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Greylands Park Avenue at 16:45 on 10th April 2015 and involved two men attempting to break into a garage.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any males spotted acting suspiciously in this area. One of the males wearing a bobble hat and brown coat and the other wearing grey tracksuit bottoms.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC RUSHWORTH. You can also email jacob.rushworth@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150058557
