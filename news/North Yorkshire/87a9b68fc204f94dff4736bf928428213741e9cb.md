Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:08am

Harrogate police are appealing for witnesses and information after a man riding a Deliveroo motorcycle was targeted by car driver during an incident on Wetherby Road in Harrogate.

It happened at around 6.50pm on Saturday 17 June 2017 when the male driver of a silver Honda Civic became aggressive towards the motorcycle rider.

Police are appealing in particular to a member of public who witnessed the incident and stopped to assist the Deliveroo rider - a 23-year-old man - who, although not physically injured, was very shaken up by the incident.

Officers are also appealing for any further witnesses who recall seeing the two vehicles on Wetherby Road or witnessing the altercation between the two, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Sheree Evans. You can also email sheree.evans@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170107508
