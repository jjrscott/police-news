North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident of indecent exposure that occurred in Harrogate Town Centre.

The incident happened at the Cenataph, Parliment street in Harrogate on Tuesday 20th September between 4:45pm and 5.10pm and involved an elderly man exposing himself to two teenagers. The male was walking between the Cenataph and the Bus Stop several times while commiting the offence. A male was identified and arrested at the scene on the day of the offence.
