Police are appealing for information after a spate of van break-ins in Richmond.

Officers are investigating six incidents overnight between 16 March and 17 March 2015.

The vans were targeted in the areas of Reeth Road, Nuns Close Car Park and Quaker Lane. Thieves smashed windows and stole tools from inside the vehicles.

Officers are appealing to anyone with information about the thefts to get in touch.

If you can help the investigation please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for PC Helen Lavender or Richmond police.

Alternatively, information can be emailed to helen.lavender@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you prefer not to give your name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
