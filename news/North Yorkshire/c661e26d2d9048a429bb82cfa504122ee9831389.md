A police warrant was executed at an address on Lang Avenue at around 8am on Sunday 19 July.

A quantity of cannabis plants and other paraphernalia were seized from the the property.

A 30 year-old man from Lang Avenue was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in production of a Class B controlled drug (cannabis). He has been released under investigation whilst enquiries continue.

Reference: 12200122954
