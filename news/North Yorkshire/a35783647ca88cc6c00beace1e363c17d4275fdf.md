North Yorkshire Police is reissuing an appeal for witnesses to an assault in Pickering to come forward and make contact.

North Yorkshire Police is reissuing an appeal for witnesses to an assault in Pickering to come forward and make contact.

The incident took place on the Ropery in Pickering between 12.50pm and 1.20pm on Saturday 15 February 2020 and involved a man and a woman. It is believed that three members of the public, two men and one woman, intervened in the incident and officers are appealing for them to come forward and make contact, as they may hold information which is important to the investigation.

Officers are also keen to speak to anyone else who may of witnessed the incident. Anyone with information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to PC Louise Neville-Beck. You can also email louise.neville-beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12200026936.
