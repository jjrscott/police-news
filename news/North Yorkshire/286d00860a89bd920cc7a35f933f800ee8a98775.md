North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage to a vehicle that occurred in Copperfield Close, Malton

It happened on Copperfield Close, Malton at 11pm on Saturday 18 April 2020 and involved the front tyres of a vehicle being slashed.
