Police are appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in York city centre.

The incident took place on Grosvenor Terrace between 7 - 7.15pm on Saturday 14 April 2018.

It is alleged that a group of four youths attacked an older man with planks of wood. The youths are described as between 14 and 16 years old. Two were wearing green tracksuits and one had ginger hair. Another of the group was wearing a white t-shirt and black jeans with short dark hair. One of the youths may have been carrying a bike tyre at the time of the offence.

The victim sustained an injury to his eye and was taken by ambulance to York District Hospital where he was treated and discharged following treatment.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information which may help us to identify the four youths and would encourage anyone who saw them or who could identify them them to contact North Yorkshire Police.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1 and pass the information to the Force Control Room. Please quote reference number 12180063695.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
