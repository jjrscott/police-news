North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Westhouse, Ingleton during the early hours yesterday (Monday, 22 June).

General crime appeal

It happened between 2am and 3am and involved several motorbikes and mountain bikes being stolen from a garage. The items are pictured below.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Burgess. You can also email David.Burgess@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200105132.
