North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and dashcam footage following an RTC near Rawcliffe Bar.

The collision occurred around 4.15pm on Thursday 5 November 2020 at the roundabout of the Rawcliffe Bar Park and Ride on the A19 and A1237 junction. A black Skoda Fabia and a white Ducati motorbike collided. The motorcyclist came off his bike and sustained serious injuries. He was taken to hospital where he is receiving treatment.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding this incident.

In particular, we are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision or who may have dashcam footage of it taking place to contact police. We are also appealing for the off-duty doctor who stopped and administered initial first aid to get in touch.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for 0694. You can also email Joseph.schramm@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
