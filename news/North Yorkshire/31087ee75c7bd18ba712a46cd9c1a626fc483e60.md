North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Scarborough town centre.

It happened on Foreshore Road between 10.30pm and 11pm on Saturday 10 February 2018 and involved a 29-yearold man and 32-year-old woman. They also had a boy believed to be around 13-years-old with them.

The man suffered minor injuries to his face as a result of the incident.

Police are appealing to anyone who was in the area at the time and witnessed the incident to contact them.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Joe Gallagher. You can also email joe.gallagher@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180024408.
