North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about damage to a vehicle that occurred in Brayton, near Selby.

It happened overnight between the 28 and 29 April 2015 on the corner of Ashtree Drive and Maple Close and involved all four tyres of a vehicle being punctured.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Alison Marshall.

You can also email alison.marshall@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-29042015-0248
