Updated: Woman arrested for failure to attend court

Adele Evans was arrested at 4.40pm on Friday 11 November and remanded to appear at York Magistrates at 9.15am on Saturday 12 November. Many thanks to everybody who assisted our appeal to find her.

**Previous appeal**

Evans, of no fixed address but believed to be in the Skipton area, failed to attend Skipton Magistrates Court on Tuesday 8 November 2016 in connection with the theft of a vehicle, and road traffic offences.

Anyone who thinks they might have seen Evans, or who has any information about her whereabouts is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for Craven Intelligence Unit.

If you prefer to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160203228.
