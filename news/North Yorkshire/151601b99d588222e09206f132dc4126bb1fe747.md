Police have released a CCTV image of a woman who is suspected of stealing two charms from a jewellery shop in Scarborough.

The incident occurred at Pandora, on Westborough, at around noon on Thursday 23 November 2017.

The woman approached the desk and is greeted by a member of staff who assists with selecting various bits of jewellery.

She selects and pays for two charms.

However, the woman is seen to conceal another two charms prior to leaving the store without making any effort to pay for them.

If you can help identify the woman, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Nick Simpson. Or email Nicholas.Simpson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.

Please quote reference number 12170210543 when providing details about this incident.
