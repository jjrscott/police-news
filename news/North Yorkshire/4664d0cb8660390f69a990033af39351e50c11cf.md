Police attended a collision involving a bus and cyclist on The Village, Strensall.

The cyclist was taken to hospital by ambulance with injuries to his head which are not believed to be life threatening.

The road was closed whilst emergency services responded but has now reopened.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or has any information is asked to call North Yorkshire police on 101 quoting reference 12200165995
