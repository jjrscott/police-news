Last modified: 28 January 2020 at 03:35pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a serious road traffic collision near Selby.

The incident happened on Hull Road, Hemingbrough, at 8.50am on 28 January 2020 and involved a beige Fiat 500 and a pedestrian.

The pedestrian, a 20 year old man, sustained a head injury and was taken by air ambulance to Leeds General Infirmary. He is currently in a serious but stable condition.

The stretch of road was closed for two and a half hours while officers investigated the scene and to allow the vehicle to be recovered.

Local diversions were put in place by highways.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle or pedestrian prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC465 Mike Rowan. You can also email Michael.Rowan@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200015648.
