North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a garage burglary that occurred in Skipton.

The burglary took place at a property on Hayton Way, just off Granville Street, Skipton and occurred between 4.00pm on Friday 16 December and 8.00am on Monday 19 December.

The burglars forced entry to the garage and stole a quantity of tools and plant equipment.
