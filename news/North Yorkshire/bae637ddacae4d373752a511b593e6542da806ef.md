Police are appealing for witnesses after a member of the public was bitten by a dog in Harrogate.

On Wednesday 11 July 2018, a member of the public was walking their dog along Saltergate Drive in Harrogate when they were approached by a woman.

The woman was also walking her dog, which jumped up towards the member of the public and bit their left arm, causing a small injury.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or who has any information about it, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101. Please select option 2 and ask for PC Nicholas Woods, or email nicholas.woods@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk - quote reference 12180141381.
