North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred in Skipton.

he incident happened at Boots chemists High Street Skipton on the evening of Thursday 31 March 2016 and we are appealing for any witnessed and information to come forward.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious people or vehicles seen in the area close to the Boots store between 9.30pm and 10.45pm.

We would be very interested if anybody has been seen carrying a ladder in the area around that time. Or if any members of the public have been offered perfumes at a reduced price.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1771 Andrew Hanks. You can also email andrew.hanks@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160056017.
