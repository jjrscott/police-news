Last modified: 13 March 2018 at 12:18pm

Police in York have issued a CCTV image of a woman they want to speak to following the theft of a purse.

On 19 January 2018, the victim was shopping in Quality Stitches on Goodramgate in York with her handbag hung over the handlebars of her pram. On leaving the shop, she discovered her purse had been taken from her handbag.

Police would like to speak to the woman shown in the CCTV image as she could have information that could assist the investigation.

Anyone who can help to identify the woman is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Chris Dalby or email Chris.Dalby@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12180010924 when passing on information.
