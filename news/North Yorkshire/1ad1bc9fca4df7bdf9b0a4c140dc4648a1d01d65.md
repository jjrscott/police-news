North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information to help locate three medals - a Pacific Star, a WW2 Star and a WW2 medal.

The three medals, which belonged to the late father of the victim and are of "high sentimental value", were stolen in a recent burglary at an address in Scarborough.

North Yorkshire Police is hoping to locate the medals so that they can be returned to the owner.

If you know anything about these medals, have been offered them or seen them for sale, please get in touch.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Steph Poole. You can also email investigationhubscarborough@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180134562 .
