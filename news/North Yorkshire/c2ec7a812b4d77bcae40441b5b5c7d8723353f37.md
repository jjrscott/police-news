Police are appealing for witnesses to a collision near Otley in which an 88-year-old man sustained serious injuries.

It happened at around 6.15pm on Wednesday 3 May 2017 at the junction of Askwith Moor Road and Moor Lane near Otley.

It involved a blue Skoda Yeti driven by an 88-year-old from Ilkley travelling from the direction of Blubberhouses to Otley, and a blue Audi A3, driven by a 69-year-old man from Skipton, travelling in the opposite direction.

The driver of the Skoda was airlifted to Leeds General Infirmary with serious injuries, where he currently remains.

The Audi driver was uninjured.

Officers are asking anyone who saw either of the vehicles prior to the crash or who witnessed the collision to contact them.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Harrogate Roads Policing Group, or email david.minto@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12170075926 when passing on information.
