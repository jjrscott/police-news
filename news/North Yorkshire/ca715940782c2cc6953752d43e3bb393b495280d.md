North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information in relation to the theft of a push bike in Scarborough.

It happened on Colescliffe Road at around 4pm on Sunday 4 December 2016 when the victim was walking with his push bike down the street with his group of friends. The male suspect approached the victim, got on the bike and took it towards Wreyfield Drive.

The stolen bike is described as being a BT WIN Rockrider in dark grey with white lettering along the frame.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for anyone who may have seen or been offered a bike matching this description for sale recently.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Liam Cromack. You can also email liam.cromack@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160218971.
