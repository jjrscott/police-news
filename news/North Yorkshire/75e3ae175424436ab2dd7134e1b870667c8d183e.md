North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following the theft of an off-road motorbike, pedal cycle and tools from a shed in Sherburn in Elmet.

The items were taken from a shed in Eversley Mount, Sherburn in Elmet between 0100hrs and 0330hrs on Wednesday 24 July 2019 with the padlock to the shed being cut.

The items stolen include a KTM 85 off road motocross bike and a Voodo Bantu pedal cycle, grey in colour with orange writing and flashes.

Police are appealing for witnesses, particularly if anyone saw a dark or black coloured 4×4 vehicle – possibly a Land Rover Discovery – in the vicinity of Eversley Mount, Garden Lane or Kirkgate, Sherburn in Elmet between those times. If you can help with our enquiries or have any information on the location of the stolen property then please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Steve Holloway or Dave Pegg quoting job number:12190134273

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 08000 555 111.

A reward of £250 of motorcycle parts has been offered for information that results in the recovery of the stolen vehicles and/or the conviction of the offender/s.
