Last modified: 13 February 2017 at 01:39pm

Police in York have issued CCTV images of two men they want to speak to following theft from a jeweller's store on Stonegate.

It happened on 13 January 2017 when three gold rings were stolen.

Police are appealing to anyone who can help identify the men to contact them.

Both men are described as Asian, the first man was in his mid-thirties, 5ft 9in tall, of broad build. He was wearing a black cap, Armani glasses, a dark coloured parka jacked,dark coloured jeans and was carrying a Levis bag.

The second man was around, 5ft 5in tall of skinny build and was wearing a blue parka jacket and light coloured jeans. He had blacked slicked back hair.

Both men are believed to have a West Yorkshire accent.

Anyone who can help identify the men is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Leon Dryden. Or email Leon leon.dryden0090@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 1217007228 when passing on information.
