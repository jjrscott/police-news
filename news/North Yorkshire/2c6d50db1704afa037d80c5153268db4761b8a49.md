North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information about a wanted Selby man.

James Sebastion Thomas, 28, failed to appear at York Crown Court last year, after being found guilty of assault. A warrant was issued for his arrest and extensive enquiries have been ongoing to locate him.

Although he is from Selby, he could be anywhere in the country.

Anyone who sees him, or has information about where he is, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference number 12190153359.
