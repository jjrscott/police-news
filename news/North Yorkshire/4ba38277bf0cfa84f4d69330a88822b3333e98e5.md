A 17-year-old girl from Harrogate and a 24-year-old man of no fixed address have been charged with wounding with intent after a man was found with stab wounds in Cambridge Road, Harrogate on the morning of Monday 20 August 2018.

The two appeared at York Magistrates’ Court on 21 August and were remanded in custody to appear at York Crown Court on 24 September 2018.

Officers believe there were a number of people who passed the area of St Peter’s Church on Cambridge Road between 7.30am and 8.30am on the morning of Monday 20 August and officers are appealing to them to come forward.

Please contact DC Sam Harding of Harrogate Serious Crime Team on 01609 643143 quoting reference 12180155086.

The injured man has since been released from hospital.
