Police are appealing for witnesses to a collision on the B6451 at Farnley, near Otley, which left a motorcyclist requiring hospital treatment.

The collision occurred near Farnley Primary School on Sunday 6 August 2017 at around 2.30pm.

The 41-year-old male rider, on a blue Honda motorcycle, was travelling from the Otley direction towards Farnley, and left the road to the nearside, avoiding a black 4Ã—4 vehicle travelling in the opposite direction.

The rider received serious, but not life-threatening, injuries as a result.

Police are requesting anyone who saw the collision, or either vehicle involved prior to it, to get in touch.

Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for TC 117 Steve James, or email steve.james117@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk, quoting reference number 12170138844.
