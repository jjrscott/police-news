Police investigating a suspected motoring offence want to trace a Porsche driver who may have important information.

The Porsche was travelling along the A64, near Whitwell Hill, towards Scarborough between 3.10pm and 3.50pm yesterday (Saturday 4 November).

A green Volkswagen Golf estate failed to stop for a police vehicle and it is believed that Porsche driver may have witnessed the incident.

A man in his 30s has been arrested in connection with the incident and released under investigation while enquiries continue.

If you were the Porsche driver, or have any other information about the incident, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Andrew Metcalfe. You can also email PC Metcalfe on Andrew.Metcalfe@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170198305 when sharing information.
