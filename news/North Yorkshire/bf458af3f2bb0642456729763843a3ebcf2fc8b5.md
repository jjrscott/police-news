North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a tractor that occurred in Whorlton, Swainby

It happened on Sunday 24th May at approx 1pm near Church House Farm, Swainby.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about an old style blue van in the area at the time of the offence.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Blair Taylor, You can also email Blair.Taylor@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150086523
