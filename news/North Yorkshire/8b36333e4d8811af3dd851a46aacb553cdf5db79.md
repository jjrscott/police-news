North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a shed burglary that occurred in Pickering.

It happened on RUFFA LANE, PICKERING between 9.00 and 9.53pm on Monday 14 November 2016 and a shed at the rear of a property was approached by offenders. The lock on the shed was forced and a large saw was removed. The offenders were seen to carry the saw along RUFFA LANE and hide it in the garden of a nearby property. They were seen to make off on foot down FOREST ROAD, PICKERING.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about two young males aged late teens/ early twenties wearing dark hooded tops and trousers.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Kay Campbell. You can also email kay.campbell@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160206796
