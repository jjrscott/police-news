A man has been arrested on suspicion of drug driving after a collision on Flaxley Road, Selby.

At about 3am this morning, officers on patrol located an unattended black Vauxhall Corsa which had left the road and crashed into an outbuilding.

A 19-year-old man was spoken to by officers a short distance away. He failed a roadside drugs test, and was arrested on suspicion of drug driving.

He remains in custody at this time, as enquiries continue.
