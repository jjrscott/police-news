Police are trying to identify a vehicle following a commercial burglary in Hinderwell.

Officers want to identify the vehicle to assist the investigation into a commercial burglary in Hinderwell.

The incident happened on Monday 2 October at Hinderwell Service Station, Hinderwell.

At approximately 11.45pm on Monday 2 October, the vehicle was seen approaching the service station from the Coronation Avenue area and parks along Browns Terrace.

Moments later, two offenders broke into the service station by smashing one of the large front windows, entered the property and stole Â£2,000 worth of cigarettes and tobacco.

The same vehicle was then seen travelling on the A174 towards Whitby.

The headlights of the car and are distinctive and the car has five spoke alloy wheels.

If you have any information please contact North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask for Jonathan Formstone.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted annonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170177037 when passing on any information.
