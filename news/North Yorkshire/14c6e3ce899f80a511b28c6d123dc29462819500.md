Today, 50 year-old Sharron Roustoby of Rowntree Avenue, York, has been charged with the murder of John David Clarke.

John David Clarke, known as David, of Huntington Road, York, died aged 43 in April 2007. His body was recovered from the River Foss near Towthorpe in April 2007.

In April 2020, David Roustoby, 44, of Rawcliffe Lane, York, was also charged with the murder of Mr Clarke.

Both Sharron Roustoby and David Roustoby are remanded in custody awaiting a future court date.
