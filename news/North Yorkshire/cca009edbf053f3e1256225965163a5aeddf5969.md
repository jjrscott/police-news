North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempt theft from motor vehicle in Scarborough.

It happened around 0100 hours on Monday 19th October at Hillside Gardens when the suspect approached a parked Land Rover and tried to open the door.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone seen around that time in the Seamer Road area wearing dark clothing and a Mckenzie stripped bobble hat.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Georgina Hanson. You can also email Georgina.Hanson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150186295.
