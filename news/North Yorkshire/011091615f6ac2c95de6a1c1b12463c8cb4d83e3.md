Officers are appealing for witness and information about a van after it was stolen from a car park in Richmond.

The van, a white Ford Transit, VRM HV61 FTT, with Kate Schindler Garden Maintenance written on the side was stolen between 8.30pm on 27 August and 7.30am on 28 August form the Holly Hill pub car park.

Anyone in the area at the time who saw anything suspicious, or anyone with any information is asked to contact police.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC 1460 Emma Taylor.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170153521 when passing on any information.
