Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:47am

Police in Ripon are appealing for witnesses and information after a large trailer-carried advertisement board was deliberately set on fire.

The incident occurred on Harrogate Road between 3.20am and 3.25am on Saturday (17 February 2018).

If you have any information as to who is responsible or if you saw the incident personally, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, option 2, and ask for Michael Spittlehouse. Or email michael.spittlehouse@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.

Please quote reference number 12180027888 when providing details about this incident.
