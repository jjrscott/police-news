Police are appealing for information following an aggravated burglary in Ripon which left a man in hospital.

The incident occurred between 4.20pm and 7pm yesterday (Tuesday 10 March 2015) at an address in Bondgate, Ripon.

Two men, who had their faces covered with scarves, entered the property and assaulted the 44 year old victim, punching and kicking him to the floor.

Police are asking anyone with any information to contact them as a matter of urgency. Anyone who may have seen anything suspicious in or saw two men acting suspiciously in Bondgate between 4.20pm and 7pm yesterday is urged to get in touch.

Dial 101, press option 1 and pass information to the force control room, quoting reference number 12150039630.
