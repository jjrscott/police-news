North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of two mobile phones from a shop in Skipton town centre on Tuesday 7 November 2017.

The incident took place on Sheep Street in Skipton at 2.10pm on Tuesday 7 November 2017. A man entered a mobile phone shop in the town centre and asked to view two Iphone 7 mobile phones.

When handed them by the shop assistant, the man made off from the shop with the mobile phones. He was seen to run through the town centre, along Sheep Street and towards Caroline Square, where the shop assistant lost sight of him.

He is described as a black male aged in his mid 20's, with facial stubble. He was dressed in a black puffa style short jacket, black skinny jeans and a bright green baseball cap.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1389 Anne Jefferson.

You can also email Anne.Jefferson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170200249.
