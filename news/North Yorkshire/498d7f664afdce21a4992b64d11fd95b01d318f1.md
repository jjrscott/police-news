North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following a burglary in York.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following a burglary in York.

The offence occurred on the junction with Boroughbridge Road (A59) and Carr Lane at around 12.45pm on Friday, 15 February.

In particular officers are appealing to anyone who may have seen a man leaving a property through the front window, then crossing the A59 and walking down Carr Lane. Also, officers would like to speak with anyone who has CCTV or dash-cam footage of this area around the time of the offence.

The suspect is described as wearing all black clothing with hood up and carrying a black back pack and a white carrier bag.

Anyone with information on this offence is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Simpson. Alternatively you can email sophie.simpson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 121900286628 when providing information.
