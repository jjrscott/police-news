North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses to a 'road rage' incident on Fishergate on Friday 14 December 2018.

Were you in the area of Fishergate, York, on Friday 14 December 2018 between 6pm and 6.15pm? Did you see a road rage incident between the driver of a white moped and the driver of a dark blue Audi convertible?

An incident occurred between Cemetery Road and Grange Garth, and we are looking for anyone who may have witnessed what happened.

If you saw anything or have any dashcam footage, please contact PC291 Armstrong on 101 quoting reference 12180232355. Or email John.Armstrong291@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
