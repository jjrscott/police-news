Police are investigating following a fire at Thirsk Athletic Club yesterday.

It was reported shortly before 1pm on Sunday 31 May 2020, and resulted in a portable cabin and its contents being destroyed.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12200091595 when providing information.
