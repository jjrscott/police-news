After a report of suspicious incident near Keighley Campus at Keighley on 27 August 2020, the suspected stolen yellow mountain bike in the attached photograph was recovered by officers.

If you recognise this bike please get in touch as soon as possible.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Joanne Ollerenshaw. Or email Joanne.Ollerenshaw@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200149228 when providing details.
