Last modified: 9 October 2017 at 12:35pm

North Yorkshire Police can confirm that the 12-year-old girl who was seriously injured when she was hit by a car in Byram near Knottingley has sadly died in hospital.

Her name was Kaitlin Mitchell of Byram.

The collision happened at around 3.30pm on Thursday 28 September 2017, when Kaitlin got off her school bus, crossed the road and was hit by a red Vauxhall Astra that failed to stop at the scene.

Police enquiries led officers to arrest a local man in his thirties and recover the suspect vehicle in Knottingley.

He has been released on bail while enquiries continue.

Officers are renewing their appeal for witnesses and dash-cam footage to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Mark Mullins or the Major Collision Investigation Team. Or email MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12170174154
