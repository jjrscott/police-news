North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a collision between a car and motorbike

The collision happened on Clay Bank near Great Broughton at around 7.45pm on Monday 26th August 2019 when a black Suzuki motorbike collided with a grey BMW car.

Both vehicles were travelling down Clay Bank towards Great Broughton when the collision occurred. The rider and pillion passenger of the motorbike were both taken to James Cook Hospital to be treated for their injuries.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses or dash cam footage to help establish the full circumstances surrounding this incident, in particular we are looking for information about a tractor that was travelling up Clay Bank toward Chop Gate at the time of the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Chris Graham-Marlow. You can also email: Chris.Graham-Marlow@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190158042
