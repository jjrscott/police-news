Police are appealing for information to help identify the man in the CCTV following an incident of criminal damage in York.

Officers want to speak to the man in connection with the incident, which happened shortly before 1.30am on Saturday 15 July 2017.

The glass in a panelled shop front door on Peter’s Lane was smashed, causing £430 worth of damage.

**** Anyone who can help identify the man or who has any information that can assist the investigation is asked to contact police.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Oliver Lewis.

Please quote reference number 12170124079 when passing on any information.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.
