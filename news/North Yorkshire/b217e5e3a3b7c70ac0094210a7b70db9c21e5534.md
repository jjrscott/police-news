Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:47am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred on Harrogate Road, Ripon, whereby a ground floor flat was broken into and property stolen.

The burglary occurred at Grove House, Harrogate Road, Ripon, between 6 and 10 April 2017, and involved the offender smashing a ground floor window and then going on to steal a quantity of electrical items and cash.

North Yorkshire Police are conducting enquiries and requesting the public's assistance to help identify the offender.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 429 Lana Beardsley You can also email lana.beardsley@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170061174.
