North Yorkshire Police are appealing for information following a sexual assault in York.

North Yorkshire Police are appealing for information following a sexual assault in York.

The incident occurred on Monday, 13 August between 11pm and 11.30pm.

The victim was walking along an alleyway between Nicholas Street and Milton Street when she was approached by a man unknown to her. The man inappropriately touched the woman before she was able to leave the scene.

The offender is described as a white man, around 6ft tall, of medium build and with a grey beard. He was wearing a dark blue or black t-shirt and grey tracksuit bottoms. He spoke with a local accent.

Anyone who has information which may assist with this investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Lucy Watson. You can also email lucy.watson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous then the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12180150883 when providing information.
