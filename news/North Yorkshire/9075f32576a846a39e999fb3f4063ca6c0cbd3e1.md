North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision that occurred at 9.15am on Tuesday 29 November on Main Street, Cayton

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident. In particular, we are appealing for the driver of the vehicle involved to come forward.

The incident involved a blue long wheel base ford transit van and a parked Ford Mondeo causing damage to the rear.

The male driver of the van stopped to observe the damage caused to the Mondeo and then drove off. He was wearing a beanie style hat and puffa jacket.

We are appealing for the driver of the van to get in touch.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and pass information on Crimestoppers.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
