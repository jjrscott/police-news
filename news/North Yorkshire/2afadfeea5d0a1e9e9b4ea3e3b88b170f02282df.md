Police are appealing for witnesses after a teenager was robbed in Scarborough.

At about 9.45pm on Wednesday 14 October 2015, a 14-year-old boy was approached by two men at the junction of Cross Street and Friars Way in the town. The two men took the boy's wallet after threatening him with violence.

One of the men is described as white, 5ft 7ins to 5ft 10ins tall, of skinny build, aged 16 or 17. He was wearing a white Adidas hoodie with the hood up. The other man is described as white, 5ft 7ins to 5ft 10ins tall, also of skinny build but larger than the other man. He was wearing a black hoodie with the hood up.

Anyone with information about this incident, or who recognises the descriptions of those involved, is asked to contact Scarborough Police on 101, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12150182475 when passing information.
