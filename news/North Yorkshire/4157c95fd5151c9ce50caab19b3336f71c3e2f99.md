North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage caused to Scarborough Town Council's front door.

Shortly before 1am on 16 March 2020 a man approached the glass front door to Scarborough Council's Customer First Centre before beginning to repeatedly kick in the glass door.

We have issued CCTV of a man we would like to identify as part of our investigation.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for James Moody . You can also email james.Moody@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200045040.
