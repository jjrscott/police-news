Police are appealing for information to help identify the man in the CCTV following a theft at the Co-op on Stokesley High Street.

Officers want to speak to the man in connection with the incident, which happened at 9.40pm on 2 January 2018 when a man entered the store, filled a bag with joints of meat and left the store without making any attempt to pay for the items.

Anyone who recognises the man in the CCTV image, or who has any information, is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Michael Wilson or email Michael.wilson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote crime reference number 12180001380 when passing on any information.
