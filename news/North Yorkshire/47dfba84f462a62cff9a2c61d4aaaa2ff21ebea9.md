Officers are urging residents in the Malton and Norton areas to be vigilant following a series of burglaries over the weekend.

Two properties in Burdale Close, Norton were targeted over the weekend, whereby offenders smashed conservatories windows and doors to gain entry in the properties. Items of jewellery were stolen from the properties, along with other high value items.

It is believed the burglaries happened sometime during the daytime, between 9.45am on Friday 21 October 2016 and 6.30pm on Sunday 23 October 2016.

A property in Chestnut Grove, Malton, was also targeted between 9am on Thursday 20 October and 1pm on Saturday 22 October 2016.

Residents are urged to be vigilant and report any suspicious circumstances to the police.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Nick Coning or email nick.coning@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
