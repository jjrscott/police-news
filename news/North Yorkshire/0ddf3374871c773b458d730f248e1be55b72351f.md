North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a Wheelie bin fire that occurred in Scarborough

It happened around 22.45 hours on 15/05/2016 on Tindall Street in Scarborough and involved a wheelie bin being set alight.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who witnessed the wheelie bin being set alight or from anyone who saw anyone acting suspiciously in the area or fleeing the scene.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 277 Lyth. You can also email ryan.lyth@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160084882
