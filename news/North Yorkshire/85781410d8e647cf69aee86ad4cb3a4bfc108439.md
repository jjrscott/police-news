North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a collision in which a motorcyclist sustained serious injuries.

Around 3.27pm on Sunday 11 October 2020, a man was travelling on the A59 near East Marton towards Skipton when his red Honda motorbike left the road. The 50 year-old motorcyclist was taken to hospital by air ambulance but sadly later died as a result of his injuries.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision or who may have dashcam footage to contact them on 101 quoting reference 12200178871 or email: laura.cleary@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
