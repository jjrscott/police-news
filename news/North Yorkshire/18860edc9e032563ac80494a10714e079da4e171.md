North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a teenager was touched inappropriately on a bus in York.

It happened on a First York bus at 4.15pm on Friday 2 November 2018, and involved a 17-year-old girl being sexually assaulted, by being touched inappropriately, and comments of a racially abusive nature by an unknown male suspect.

The bus journey went from Ousegate to Foxwood Lane in Acomb.
