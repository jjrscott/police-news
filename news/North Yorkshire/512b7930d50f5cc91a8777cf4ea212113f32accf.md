North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Burglary that occurred in Cowling.

General crime appeal

It happened on Royd Street, Cowling between 22:00hrs - 07:00hrs on Friday 1st July 2016 involved unknown offender/offenders enter property via rear door and stole items from within.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 352 Michael Smith. You can also email Michael.smith352@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
