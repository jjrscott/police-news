Police are appealing for witnesses and information after an incident on Walmgate in York.

Between 6.30pm and 6.55pm on Friday 13 January 2017 an altercation involving two men took place outside and opposite the One Stop shop, on both sides of the road.

The victim, a man in his forties from York, received serious injuries, including stab wounds, requiring hospital treatment.

A 43-year-old man from York was arrested nearby on suspicion of wounding, and has been released on bail as enquiries continue.

DC Robert Brook, of York Serious Crime Team, said: “This was a very busy time of day, and members of the public will have walked past or seen what was going on. I would ask them to contact the police or Crimestoppers with any information.”

Anyone who saw any part of the incident is asked to phone North Yorkshire Police on 101. Please select option 2 and ask for DC Robert Brook or York Serious Crime Team. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12170007299 when passing information.

Police patrols in the Walmgate area have been conducted to provide reassurance to the local community.
