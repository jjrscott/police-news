North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a sexual assault that occurred in Selby.

The female victim was touched inappropriately by two men on cycles as they rode past her on the pavement. The incident took place on the pavement outside the Town Hall in Selby at around 8.30pm on Monday 19 June 2017.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 81 Mark Gaskin. You can also email mark.gaskin@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170107457.
