Last modified: 2 March 2020 at 06:30pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a building site burglary in Malton.

It happened over the weekend of 22 - 23 February 2020 when the suspects have entered the Lindum Group building site on Rainbow Lane Malton and removed boilers, towel rails and cooker hobs from the houses.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jane Jones . You can also email jane.jones@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200031867.
