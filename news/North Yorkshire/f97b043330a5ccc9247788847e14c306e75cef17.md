North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about an attempted burglary that occurred in Killinghall, Harrogate.

It happened on Otley Road, Killinghall between Sunday 12 February and Thursday 16 February 2017 and involved offenders repositioning outdoor lights and motion sensors to prevent them shining on the property.

If you, or anybody else has had lights or sensors repositioned, or have any information which could help assist our investigation, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Sarah Danby. You can also email sarah.danby@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170027800.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
