North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a flock of Swaledale sheep that have been stolen from Keasden Moor.

Around 20 sheep and 10 lambs were taken at some time between 30 July 2015 and 12 August 2015.

Police are appealing for anyone with information to come forward.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PCSO Helen Montgomery.

You can also email helen.montgomery@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150140204.
