North Yorkshire Police are appealing for any information regarding the theft of a car, taken from a garage in Newland near Drax, Selby.

The car, a burgundy 4×4 Izuzu Rodeo with the registration 'YX04 NNU' is believed to have been taken between 5pm on Tuesday 20 September and 9am Wednesday 21 September 2016.

Police are appealing for any witnesses who may have seen the 4×4 vehicle in the area to contact them. They are particularly interested to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the stolen car at around 8pm on Wednesday evening driving in the area in convoy with a white 4×4 vehicle.

Anyone with any information which could assist the investigation, or any further details about the white 4×4 is asked to call 101 and select option 2 and ask to speak to PC Alison Baker. Please quote reference 12160171949. You can email Alison.Baker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively Crimestopppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
