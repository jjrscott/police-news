The family of the the 84-year-old man who died recently in Ripon has been located following appeal. Thank you to all who shared our appeal.

**Original release 27 February - ****Appeal to trace Ripon man 's next of kin**

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for help to find the next of kin of a man who recently died in Ripon.

Brian Unwin, sadly died at his home address on Glebe Meadow, Sharow, Ripon, aged 84.

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

It is believed he has an ex-wife and two daughters in the UK and a son in Australia. It is believed he previously worked in the army as a Civil Engineer.

If you have any information about Brian or his family please contact the Police Coroner's Office on 01609 643181.
