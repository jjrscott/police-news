Police are working on behalf of the Coroner to find relatives of a woman who died in Selby.

Susan Jones, 71, died at her home address in Selby on Monday 1 March 2021. The death is not being treated as suspicious.

At present, no next of kin have been identified.

Anyone who believes they are related to Susan, or can offer contact details of a relative, is asked to contact Su Gregson, Coroners Officer, on 01609 643168.
