North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a woman had her mobile phone snatched from her hand in Scarborough.

The theft occurred at around 9.15pm on Tuesday, 28 March as the woman was walking on West Street.

Officers are keen to identify a white man, around 5ft 9ins in height, of slim build, with short dark hair. He was wearing dark clothing and dark trainers and was seen in the area around the time of the offence.

The offender is believed to have approached the victim from behind from the direction of Avenue Victoria and returned in this direction following the incident.

Information can be provided by calling 101, selecting option 2, and asking for PC Leah Gillon (596). Information can also be provided by email on leah.gillon@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous please contact the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12170052442 when providing information.
