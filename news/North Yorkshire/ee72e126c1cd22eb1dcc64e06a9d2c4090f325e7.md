Police in Scarborough are appealing for witnesses and information after a gate was damaged.

It happened at an address on Prospect Mount Road between 5pm and 6pm on Sunday 17 December 2017.

An unknown man approached a house and ripped the gate of its hinges before making of in the direction of Scalby Road.

Officers are appealing for information about the suspect, who was wearing black trousers with a black hooded top. The hood was up to cover the offender's face.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul O'Neill. You can also email Paul.o'neill@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-17122017-0347
