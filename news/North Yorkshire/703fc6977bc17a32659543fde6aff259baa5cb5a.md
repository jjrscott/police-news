North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred on Harrogate Road, Ripon

Between 9am and 6pm on Monday 14 January 2019, unknown offenders entered a property on Harrogate Road, Ripon, via the rear patio door.

An untidy search was made and a number of rare coins were taken from the property, including a 007 James Bond 10p coin.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1995 Hargreaves. You can also email sarah.hargreaves1995@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-14012019-406.
