Harrogate police are appealing for information and a potential eyewitnesses to come forward after a12-year girl was assaulted in Harrogate.

It happened at approximately 9pm on the evening of Sunday 7 August 2016 on 'Oaker Bank' near to The Old Spring Well pub in Harrogate when the 12-year-old victim was assaulted at the roadside by a group of similar aged girls. The victim suffered bruising as a result of the attack.

It is believed that a woman with dark brown, bobbed hair and glasses who was driving a red car, stopped at the scene in the aftermath of the attack and enquired after the victim's wellbeing, and that she may therefore have witnessed the incident taking place.

If you are the woman who stopped, or if you witnessed the incident or have any other information that could assist this investigation, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1067 Walker. You can also email daniel.walker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160147829.
