Police were contacted by the Go Outdoors store on Foss Bank, York at 4.35pm, Thursday 7 September 2017, to report two suspected shoplifters who had just left the store, who had also been seen visiting their Wakefield store earlier that day.

The store provided details and a description of the vehicle the suspects had left in.

Through the use of ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition) cameras, the vehicle was located and successfully stopped by police on the A64 near Tadcaster Road, York.

Following a search of the vehicle, a number of high-value items including waterproof jackets and trousers were located, all suspected to have been stolen from the Wakefield store.

North Yorkshire Police arrested two women aged 47 and 54, both from Liverpool, on suspicion of theft from a shop. They were later transferred into the custody of West Yorkshire Police who will lead the investigation.
