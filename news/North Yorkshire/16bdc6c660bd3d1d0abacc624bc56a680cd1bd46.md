North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses after a 68-year-old man was assaulted in an unprovoked attack in York.

It happened on Burton Stone Lane at around 10pm on Wednesday, 2 May 2018.

The man was sitting at a bus stop when three male youths assaulted him.

The victim suffered minor cuts to his head and face. A pair of glasses owned by the victim were also broken.

Enquires are ongoing to trace the three youths involved.

Officers are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, they are appealing for information about any sightings of three male youths, aged between 14 to 18 years, dressed in mainly dark clothing.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Nikki Jackson (collar number 425). You can also email Nikki.Jackson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180076041.
