North Yorkshire Police appealing for witnesses after a collision between a van and motorbike

Around 9pm on Wednesday 4th December 2019, a collision occurred between a 125cc motorbike and a white van on the A61 just north of Melmerby Green Lane. The motorbike had been heading towards Ripon and the Ford towards the A1M â€“ junction 50 when the collision occurred.

The rider of the motorbike was a 22 year old man from Ripon. He sustained serious injuries and was taken by ambulance to James Cook Hospital. The driver of the van, a man from the north east was uninjured.

The road remained closed for several hours whilst police investigated.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the collision or may have information or dash cam footage which could help with the investigation. Please call 101 or if you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

There was an HGV travelling along the A61 towards the A1M at the time that was not directly involved but it is believed the driver may have witnessed the collision.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190222656.
