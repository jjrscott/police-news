Three people have been arrested following an incident in Scarborough tonight.

Police were called to reports that two women had been assaulted on Queen’s Parade in Scarborough at about 9.30pm. Both victims have been taken to hospital for treatment for serious injuries.

A man and two women have been arrested on suspicion of wounding. They remain in custody at this time.

A section of Queen’s Parade has been closed and will remain so for some time. Members of the public are asked to avoid the area to allow police enquiries at the scene.

Anyone witnessed the incident, or has any information about it, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference 12160146641.
