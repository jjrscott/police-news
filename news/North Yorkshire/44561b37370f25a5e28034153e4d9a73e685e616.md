Gillygate, York is now open. Thank you for your patience whilst we responded to the incident.

**Original release: Incident on Gillygate in York - please avoid the area**

We received a report at 5.45am this morning of an assault on Gillygate, York.

A man was taken to York District Hospital with serious injuries.

It is unknown at this stage how the injuries happened.

Enquiries are ongoing to locate a man believed to be involved in the incident.

The road is currently closed until further notice. Please avoid the area until further notice.
