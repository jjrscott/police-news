North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a shed burglary that occurred in Keldholme village near Kirkbymoorside.

It happened on the main street in the village sometime between 2300 - 0600 hrs on Monday 6th April and Tuesday 7th April 2015 and involved suspects gaining entry to a locked shed and stealing numerous power tools making off unseen and unheard

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help find the people responsible.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone who was seen in the area acting suspicious or any vehicles seen acting suspiciously.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1634 Emma Collins.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150056543.
