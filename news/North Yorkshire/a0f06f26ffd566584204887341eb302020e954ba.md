Last modified: 8 June 2016 at 11:20am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a day time burglary that occurred in Harrogate.

It happened on Albany Road between 10am and 12 noon on Thursday 25 February 2016, whilst the owners were out. A large sum of cash and a CCTV system was taken as part of the burglary.

One man was arrested and has been bailed pending further enquiries.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information regarding who carried out the burglary, where the stolen items are now and any information regarding suspicious activity on Albany Road, Harrogate at the stated times.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Corrina Graham-Merrett. You can also email corrina.graham-merrett@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160031072.
