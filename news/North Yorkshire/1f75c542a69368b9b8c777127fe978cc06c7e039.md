North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a violent disorder incident that occurred in Crossgate.

It happened between 5.30pm and 6.20pm Cayton Low Road, Crossgate on Tuesday 5 May 2015 and involved a number of men fighting in the grassed field opposite the public house and on the road between the pub and the bridge over the A64.

A number of people have been arrested in connection with this incident.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101 and ask for the Scarborough Investigation Hub.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150074258.
