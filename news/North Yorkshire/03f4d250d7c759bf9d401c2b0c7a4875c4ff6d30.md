Last modified: 19 November 2018 at 11:49am

North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about attempt attacks on ATM cash machines that occurred in York and Strensall in the early hours of Friday 16 November 2018.

The ATM at the CO-OP store on Beckfield Lane, Acomb, York, was targeted between 4.30 and 5.15am on Friday 16 November 2018. Suspects have tried to extract money from the ATM through by destructive methods (ref 12180214026)

Another similar incident was reported with the ATM at Martin House in Strensall, York which was targeted at approximately 2pm on Friday 16 November. Suspects have again tried to extract money from the ATM through destructive methods (ref 12180214302)

Police are appealing for information about any vehicles, or persons who were seen in the areas of interest during the times of both incidents and would specifically like to speak to anyone who may have heard the suspects attempting to gain access to these ATM cash machines

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 13 STOREY, or PC 1147 BAILEY. Please quote the relevant reference number when passing on information.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
