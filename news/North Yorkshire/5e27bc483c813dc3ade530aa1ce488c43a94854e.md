Last modified: 23 October 2018 at 02:13pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred at Cliffe Pre-school on Main Street, Cliffe, near Selby.

It happened between 15:30hrs on Friday 19 October 2018 and 08:30hrs on Monday 22 October when the suspects gained entry by forcing a window at the premises and stole items from inside.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1167 Chris Rawlins. You can also email Chris.Rawlins@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180197529
