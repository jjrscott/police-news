Police are appealing for information to help identify a man involved in two thefts from Boots retail store, High Street, Skipton.

On 6 October 2015, an unknown man entered the store and taken a Phillips toothbrush, worth £250 without attempting to pay for the item. The same man then visited the store again, on 7 October, and took another Phillips Toothbrush worth £250 and a Phillips Epilator worth £100, again without making any attempt to pay for the items.

The man is described as white, in his mid-twenties, approximately 6′ tall. He is of thin build with short ginger hair, shaved at the sides, wearing grey jogging bottoms, grey hooded top and white trainers.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, press option 2 and ask to speak to PC David McManus.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150177531 when passing on information about the incident.

Monday 12 October 2015.
