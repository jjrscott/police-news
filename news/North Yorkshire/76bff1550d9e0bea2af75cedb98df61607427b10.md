Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:08am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary at Bass and Bligh camera shop on Beulah Street in Harrogate.

At some point in the early hours of Monday 23 April 2018 the shop was targeted by thieves, who cut the shutter to gain entry.

A number of items were stolen before thieves left the scene.

Police would like to speak with anyone who was in the area of Beulah Street, who witnessed anything suspicious, between the hours of 2.00am and 5.30am.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to James SKAITH.

You can also email James.Skaith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180069235
