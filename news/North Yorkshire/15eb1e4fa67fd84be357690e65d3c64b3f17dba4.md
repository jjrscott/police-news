Police are urging people in York to contact them with information about a wanted man.

Alex David Kenneth Ogden, 22, was released from HMP Hull on 5 June 2018, after being jailed in August 2016 for assault occasioning actual bodily harm.

However, he has since been recalled to prison because he failed to attend an appointment with his probation officer and allegedly committed further offences (theft).

Ogden is believed to be in York.

Anyone who sees him, or knows where he is, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference 12180101547.
