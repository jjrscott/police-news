North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from an unattended motor vehicle that occurred in Whitby, North Yorkshire.

It happened when a BMW motor vehicle was parked near to Whitby Abbey between 04/04/2015 and 06/04/2015.

The vehicle had its front seat head rests stolen. These are black leather and contain television head sets and may have electrical leads attached.

We are requesting the public's support and assistance to determine the full circumstances of the incident. In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious persons or vehicles that were seen near to Whitby Abbey between the dates stated. We are also appealing for any who recognises the property, has been offered the sale of it or who has seen it for sale to come forward.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Nicola Knowles.

You can also email nicola.knowles@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150056151.
