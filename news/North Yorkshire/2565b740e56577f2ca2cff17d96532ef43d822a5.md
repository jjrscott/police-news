North Yorkshire Police is appealing for two drivers to come forward with information following the collision at The Odeon Car Park, Station Avenue, Harrogate at 10:00am on Monday 9th March 2015 in which an 82 year old woman died.

Two cars travelling along Station Avenue from Odeon roundabout towards the Old Police Station stopped just after the collision and both drivers offered help to the injured pedestrian.

One of the cars was silver and the driver was wearing black trousers & a black jacket with short light coloured hair. The other car was a black convertible and the driver wearing a brown jacket which was taken off and offered to the pedestrian.

Police urge these two kind drivers to come forward. Even though they may not have witnessed the actual collision, any information they do have could assist with the investigation.

Anyone who may have seen the collision, or the pedestrian or silver Kia Picanto prior to the collision is asked to come forward and contact TC970 Mark Hutchings of the major Collision Investigation Unit on 101 or email Mark.Hutchings@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote incident number 12150038748 when providing information about this incident.
