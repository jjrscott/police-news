Last modified: 19 November 2018 at 04:47pm

Police have released CCTV footage of a man they would like to identify, following a report of a concern for the safety of a woman in York City Centre.

The report regarding the woman was made to police at 4.30am on Saturday 17 November 2018.

Police believe the man captured in the CCTV footage may hold information that would assist their investigation.

He is described black and aged between his late 20s and early 30’s, wearing a white t-shirt, blue jeans and light coloured trainers. He had a beard which is described as ‘flat to his face’ with short, shaven hair.

The man was first seen in the Christmas Market area of York near to the christmas tree moving toward Market Street. He was then seen in the area of Spurriergate and Coppergate, heading in the direction of King Street.

Anyone who recognises the man, or who has any information which would assist the police with their investigation is asked to dial 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to Stephen Eyres. Please quote reference 12180214851.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
