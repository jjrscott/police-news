Last modified: 24 May 2016 at 02:05pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a dwelling burglary that occurred in Castleton.

Between 9.30am on Wednesday 23 December 2015 and 1pm on 31 December 2015, an offender or offenders approached the rear of a property in Castleton North Yorkshire, and disabled security lighting. They gained entry by smashing a rear bedroom window and climbing through. Once inside an untidy search of all rooms was carried out and property stolen.

The stolen property includes wrist watches and stamps.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Neale Graham. You can also email neale.graham@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 1250231711.
