Police investigating a house burglary in Harrogate have released an e-fit of a man they want to identify.

The offence took place between 7.25pm and 7.45pm on Monday (20 March 2017) on East Parade, Harrogate.

The man pictured below is described as white, around 6ft tall and in his late 20s. He is slim and was wearing a dark hooded top and dark trousers.

If you recognise him, saw anyone acting suspiciously in the area or have any other information that could help the investigation, contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for "Investigation Hub, Harrogate".

Please quote 12170047318 when passing on information.
