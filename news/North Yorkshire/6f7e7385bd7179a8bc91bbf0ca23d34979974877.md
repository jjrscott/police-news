North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses regarding an assault that took place at around 7pm on Tuesday 14 February 2017 on Westborough, Scarborough, opposite Lord Roseburys Public House.

The assault involved an unknown male assaulting the female victim. During the incident a member of the public intervened stopped any further assault. The offender then walked off towards Northway.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public’s assistance to identify the offender who has been described as around 5'5″ tall, about 40 years old and a medium build. The offender is also described as having short brown hair and was wearing a dark coloured jacket blue jeans and brown timberland boots.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Phil Hazlewood. You can also email philhazlewood@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
