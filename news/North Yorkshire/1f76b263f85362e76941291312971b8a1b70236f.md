Do you have information which can help use identify those responsible for setting a car alight in Thirsk?

The offence occurred in an enclosed courtyard off Thirsk Market Place, behind Orchard Houses at around 11.40pm on Monday, 10 April. The car involved was a silver Audi A3.

Officers are keen to speak with a man who was seen carrying a large piece of sandstone in the area around the time of the offence as it is believed he may have vital information.

If you can assist with the investigation please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, selecting option 2, and asking for Lucy Tate. You can also email information to _lucy.tate@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_.

If you wish to remain anonymous, then information can be provided through the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170061348.
