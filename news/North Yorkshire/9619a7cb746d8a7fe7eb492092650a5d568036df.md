Four people have been seriously injured in a collision near Scarborough.

Police are appealing for witnesses or dashcam footage the moments leading up to the incident on Coastal Road, Burniston.

At 7.40pm on Monday (16 December), a red Hyundai I20 with three occupants collided with a white Seat Ibiza, which had two occupants.

Four of the five people involved were seriously injured.

Officers investigating the collision are particularly interested in hearing from anyone who witnessed it, or saw Red Hynudai prior to the collision, as it travelled from Scarborough direction, or anyone with dashcam footage.

Please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Huw Walkey. You can also email Traffic Constable Walkey on Huw.Walkey@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Quote incident number 12190230033 when sharing information.
