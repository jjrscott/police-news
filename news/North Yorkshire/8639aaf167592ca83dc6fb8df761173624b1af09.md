North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of razors from Savers on the High Street in Skipton that occurred on 23 September 2016.

On 23 September 2016, two men entered the Savers store on Skipton High Street and whilst one man distracted staff with questions, the other helped himself to razors from the display before leaving the store without making any attempt to pay for the items.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Suspect 1 is a white male , aged in his mid 30s, clean shaven, wearing a grey/white baseball cap , and grey fleece adidas tracksuit .

Suspect 2 is a white male, also aged in his mid 30s ,unshaven appearance, about 5ft 8ins tall, wearing a khaki-coloured short jacket and blue jeans, with a blu drawstring bag over his shoulder.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Anne Jefferson. You can also email Anne.jefferson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
