Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:23am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a pedal cycle that occurred in Selby.

It happened between midday on Thursday 15 and 4am on Friday 16 February 2018 when the black framed Claude Butler racing style cycle with white inner wheels was stolen from the cycle rack on Abbey Walk.

It had been left locked and secure but the offender or offenders cut the lock before taking the cycle.

If you have any information regarding this incident or witnessed anything suspicious in the area around this time please contact Tim McGreevy by phoning 101, and selecting option 2 or via email: tim.mcgreevy@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk, quoting crime reference number 12180027606.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
