North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred in Whitby on the morning of Thursday 28 July 2016 at approximately 8am. We are asking for the public's help to identify the male who is suspected to have stolen money from a milk float.

On Thursday 28 July 2016 at approximately 8am a small plastic tub containing between £40 -£50 in mixed coinage was taken from a milk float as it was parked on Baxtergate, Whitby.

We are conducting enquiries and would like to speak to the man pictured in relation to the theftm and are requesting the public’s assistance to help identify him.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Michael Cope You can also email michael.cope@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 1216013460229 August 2016
