North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage to a motor vehicle that occurred on Scarborough seafront.

The incident took place at Sandside, Scarborough seafront at 2pm hours on 24 September 2016 and involved a woman who was trying to rest her motorised bicycle against a post outside the Make It Personal shop. The bicycle was reported to have fallen over three times against a White Suzuki Swift car, causing minor damage to door panels. The woman was then seen to enter the shops prior to leaving the area, without leaving any details for the car owner.
