North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that took place in Falsgrave Road, Scarborough, on 26 December 2015.

Between 7pm and 7.30pm on Saturday 26 December 2015, a woman walking her dog was assaulted by being grabbed around the neck and then pushed to the floor by a group of unidentified women.

It is requested that any witnesses or anyone with information that may assist the police with their enquiries pass this information to PC 604 Andy Grant of Scarborough Police, on 101 quoting crime reference 12150229927.
