North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information in relation to an incident that occurred at St Andrew's Church, Middleton, near Pickering.

It happened at St Andrews Church between 12pm and 5pm on Saturday 13 August 2016. Offenders tried to gain entry to the vestry and caused damage to the donation box.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Louise Neville-Beck. You can also email louise.neville-beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-14082016-0246.
