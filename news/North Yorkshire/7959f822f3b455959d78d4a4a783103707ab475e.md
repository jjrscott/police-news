Officers arrested a woman after a Ford Fiesta collided with a signpost in Eastfield.

Police attended the scene of the collision just before 6am on Sunday 22 November 2020.

A woman in her 20s was arrested on suspicion of aggravated vehicle taking and dangerous driving, drink driving, driving without a licence, failing to stop after a collision, and failing to report a collision.

She has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
