Police have released images of a man they would like to speak to regarding the use of fraudulent £50 notes to purchase items from Hobbycraft, Starbucks and Sainsburys supermarket at Monks Cross Shopping Park in York.

The images were captured at Hobbycraft on Thursday 8 February 2018, when a fraudulent Bank of Ireland £50 note was used to purchase a number of low value items.

The man also visited Starbucks and Sainsburys and purchased items from the stores, allegedly using a fraudulent Bank of Ireland £50 note each time.

Anyone who recognises the man, or has information which could assist police with their enquiries is asked to dial 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to PC 189 Claire Hudson. Alternatively you can email Claire.Hudson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800555111

Please quote reference number 12180025529
