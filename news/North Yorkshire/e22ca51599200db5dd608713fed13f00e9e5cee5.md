Police have issued a CCTV stills three men and woman they would like to speak to following an incident of criminal damage in Scarborough.

At about 9.15pm on Saturday 14 October, three men and woman entered Coldyhill Lane in Scarborough from Scalby Road.

One of the men smashed a window on the side of a local tea room business.

Officers would like to speak to the group pictured on CCTV, as they may have important information that could assist the investigation.

If you recognise him, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Mick Barker or email Michael.Barker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170185130 when passing information.
