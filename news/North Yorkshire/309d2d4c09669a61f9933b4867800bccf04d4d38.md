Police investigating an assault which took place in the early hours of Sunday 2 August are appealing for witnesses to come forward.

Officers were called to a property in The Haven, Selby, at around 1.15am with reports that two men had been assaulted. Upon arrival at the scene, officers found two men who had suffered knife wound injuries.

The injured men, one aged 22 and one aged 38, both from Selby, were taken to hospital for treatment and later released.

Police arrested two men on suspicion of section 18 wounding in the area shortly after the incident. The two men, one aged 29 and one aged 35, both also from Selby, have been released. No further action is being taken against them at this time, however the enquiries into the incident are continuing and police would like anyone who witnessed the incident or who may have some information to assist the investigation, to contact them.

Dial 101, press option 2 and ask for the Harrogate Serious Crime Team.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150132931 when passing on information about the incident.

Monday 3 August.
