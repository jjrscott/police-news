North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a bag that occurred at Selby Bus Station.

It happened at 7.25pm on 31 March 2016 and involved a male gaining entry to a unattended bus and removing a bag.

The suspect is a white male, aged approximately 40-50 years old and of slim build. He was wearing a black hooded jacket with blue/red logos on the front and rear shoulder, dark jeans and black/white trainers.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1944 Alison Baker. You can also email alison.baker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160055875
