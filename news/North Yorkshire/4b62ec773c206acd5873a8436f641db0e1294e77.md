North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a street altercation in Acomb, York.

The altercation happened between a man and a woman at 3pm on Saturday 17 March outside the Co-operative store on York Road.

The woman was in a small silver car parked near to the junction of York Road and Manor Drive South when the man approached the vehicle and an argument ensued.

The man briefly left, but returned minutes later in a green car and appeared to block the female in before driving away.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have seen this incident or the movement of the two vehicles involved.

Anyone with information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jack Judge or email jack.judge@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12180046050 when providing any information.
