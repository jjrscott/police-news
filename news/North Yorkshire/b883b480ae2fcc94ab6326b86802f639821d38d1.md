North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Station Square, Whitby.

It happened at sometime between 1.30pm and 1.40pm on 21 April 2015 and involved a man and a woman, who were both white and wearing blue hoodies. Both parties were aged in their late teens to early twenties.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anybody who observed a physical assault occur between the parties.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Tom Rollinson.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150065305.
