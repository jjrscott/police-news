A Somerset woman has been charged with two counts of theft following reports of shoplifting in York and Helmsley.

On the afternoon of 10 January 2019, police received a report of theft from Asda in Monks Cross, York, followed by a second report of theft from Fat Face in Helmsley.

Observations were circulated for a vehicle linked to the suspects which was intercepted at around 2.30pm in Helmsley market place.

A man and a woman, both from Watchet in Somerset were arrested. The woman, 33, has been charged with two counts of theft and is due to appear at York Magistratesâ€™ Court on 14 February 2019.

The man, 34, remains in police custody.

The vehicle was seized along with a number of items of suspected stolen clothing.
