Last modified: 7 May 2019 at 12:15pm

North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about an incident of criminal damage which occurred during the early hours of Tuesday 23 April 2019 in the High Street, Northallerton.

At around 2am on Tuesday 23 April 2019, on the roundabout between the High Street and Friarage Street in Northallerton, some signage has been damaged.

Police are appealing to anyone who has witnessed this incident or has any information about the identity of the people responsible to come forward.

In particular, Police are keen to speak with three men, one wearing a dark coloured gilet, one in a white shirt and one in a grey shirt wearing dark coloured rimmed glasses.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC521 Mitson. You can also email: karen.mitson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190073249.
