North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident that occurred on Tadcaster Road in York.

It happened at approximately 1.30am on Wednesday 1 July when three 18 year-old men were attacked on Tadcaster Road on their way home from the Knavesmire by two unknown males believed to be approximately 16-18 years-old.

The incident occurred directly opposite the Tadcaster Road entrance to Hob Moor. One of the 18 year-olds was sprayed with incapacitant spray and shot with a BB gun. The BB gun was also used on one of the other 18 year-olds. Both men sustained bruising in the incident.

Police are appealing for information which may lead to identifying the males responsible and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
