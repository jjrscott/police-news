Police investigating the theft of a bottle of vodka from a Crosshills shop have releases a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to.

At around 4pm on Friday 1 December 2017 a man entered the Co-op store on Main Street, Crosshills and, after a confrontation with members of staff, left with a bottle of vodka without making any attempt to pay for it.

Police have issued a CCTV still of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the incident as he may have important information that could assist the investigation.

If you recognise him or have any information that can assist our investigation please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for **David Foyston** or email **david.foyston@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk**

Alternatively, you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number **12170215377**
