Police investigating a theft from a York shop have released CCTV footage of a man they want to speak to.

A man entered the Spar shop on Lowther Street and allegedly put a number of items in a bag and his pockets before leaving without paying.

It happened on 13 May and despite extensive enquiries, officers are still trying to trace the man pictured, as they believe he may be able to help with their investigation.

Anyone who recognises him or has any further information about the incident is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Ricci Haslam or email Ricci.Haslam@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote 12170082236 when passing on information.
