Last modified: 3 January 2020 at 11:34am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident where a car was reportedly driven at someone on Muston Road (A1039), Filey near the level crossing of the railway line.

It happened between 2am and 2.30am on Monday 27 December and involved an altercation between two people in a Blue Ford Ka that was stopped in the middle of the road. It was further reported that the car was later driven at one of those involved.

In particular, we are appealing for the driver and occupants of a car that passed the couple while the incident was ongoing to come forward.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andrew Metcalfe. You can also email andrew.metcalfe@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190235923.
