North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a hit and run road traffic collision.

It happened on the junction of Tadcaster Road onto Knavesmire Road in York at around 10.45pm on Tuesday 5 March 2019

The collision involved a dark coloured estate car and a pedestrian who was crossing the road at the time. Immediately after the collision the driver did not stop.

Police and ambulance attended and the 55 year-old pedestrian was taken to York Hospital for treatment for mild facial injuries and cuts to his arms and legs. He has since been discharged.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who may have witnessed the collision or have details of the driver or vehicle involved

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
