North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred on Wedderburn Avenue, Harrogate.

The incident occurred on Wedderburn Avenue between 10.30pm on Thursday 14 April and 7.15am on Friday 15 April and involved unknown person(s) entering the property whilst the occupant was asleep upstairs and removing various items, including a gold plated carriage clock and a gold ring containing three diamonds and two rubies.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the location of property stolen during the burglary and those involved.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Kerrie Roworth or email Kerrie.Gray@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-15042016-0098
