North Yorkshire Police is investigating an assault that occurred at North Street Car Park in Scarborough over the weekend.

The incident, which was originally reported as a stabbing but now being treated as an assault, happened at around 1.30am on Saturday 19 September.

Two men received treatment by paramedics for minor injuries.

Enquiries are ongoing to establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with any information that can assist the investigation is asked to email John.Axcell@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or call North Yorkshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 12200164472
