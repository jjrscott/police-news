North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about an alleged assault that occurred outside The Barbican on Paragon Street, York, on Thursday 07/11/2019.

The incident occurred outside The Barbican between 7pm and 9.30pm on 7 November 2019 and involved a man and woman in their late twenties.

An injury was sustained to the female of a small cut just above her left eye and swelling. No medical assistance was required in this instance.

A male in his late twenties was arrested and bailed with conditions pending further enquiries.
