Skipton police are appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a charity box from H Samuel Jewellers in the town.

It happened at around midday on Monday 1 May 2017 in the High Street store, when a Clic Sargent collection box was taken from the shop while staff were busy with customers.

Officers are asking for help to identify the man shown in this CCTV image. He is described as white, aged 50 to 60, of stocky to large build, with a bald head and a grey and dark goatee beard. He was wearing a black waist length jacket, blue denim jeans and dark coloured shoes.

Officers are also appealing to anyone who may have found a discarded charity box marked with Clic Sargent to contact them.

Anyone who can help identify the man or has any other information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Rich Sutcliffe. You can also email richard.sutcliffe@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
