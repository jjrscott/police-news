Police are appealing for sightings or information about a missing Craven man.

Missing: David Freeman

North Yorkshire Police are becoming increasingly concerned regarding the whereabouts of 40-year-old David Freeman.

He left his home address in the Glusburn area of Craven on Boxing Day and is believed to have travelled into Leeds.

He is described as white with short dark brown hair, medium build and is 5ft 8 inches tall.

There have been fears raised about his physical wellbeing and North Yorkshire Police would like to hear from anyone with information about his current location and welfare.

Please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and select option 1 to speak to the Force Control Room.

Please quote 12200245489 when sharing information about this appeal.
