North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in an alleyway behind Bridge Street in Helmsley.

The incident happened near to Bridge Street, Helmsley around 8pm on 9 July 2015 and involved a number of men in their twenties.

The victim, a 28 year old man form Helmsley, suffered minor injuries and had some jewellery stolen during the incident.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Police are appealing for any witnesses to this incident, in particular, we are appealing for information about a man who offered assistance to the victim.

A 25 year old man form Helmsley was arrested on suspicion of theft. He has been bailed until 18 August 2015 pending further enquiries.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Investigator Nick Skelton.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150116751 .
