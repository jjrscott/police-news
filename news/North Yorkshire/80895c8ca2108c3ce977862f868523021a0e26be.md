Police have issued CCTV images of two men after spirits were stolen from a shop in Whitby.

At about 2pm on Wednesday 29 June 2016, two men entered the Co-op store on Endeavour Wharf.

They took several bottles of spirits, of a value totalling more than Â£250, and left without offering payment.

Officers are keen to speak to the two men pictured in the CCTV stills, as they may have important information that could assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises them is asked to phone North Yorkshire Police on 101. Please select option 2 and ask for PC Joseph Schramm. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference 12160114451 when passing information.
