North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred at Cragg Hall Farm, Ravenscar.

It happened in the early hours of the 19th January 2016.

A number of out buildings were broken into and damage caused to the doors.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspect vehicles or people who may have been seen in the area in the early hours of the 19th January 2016.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 598 Laverick. You can also email david.laverick@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160010748 .
