Police are appealing for witnesses and information following an assault at Scarborough Fair which resulted in a 30 year old woman being assaulted.

The incident happened at around 9pm on Thursday 28 September 20017 at the fair on Dean Road, Scarborough. Two women and one man were involved in an altercation which resulted in the 30 year old victim suffering a cut to her eyelid which required several stitches.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or who has any information, is asked to contact police.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Andrew Metcalfe or email Andrew.metcalfe@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170176094 when passing on any information.
