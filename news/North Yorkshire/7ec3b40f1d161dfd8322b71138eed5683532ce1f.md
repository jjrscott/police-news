North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a motor vehicle at Brompton on Swale, near Richmond.

A red Mercedes-Benz Sprinter van was stolen from a premises on Gatherley Road on the morning of Saturday 26 May 2018.

The vehicle has registration number of NA59 PXJ and is distinctive due to having some old Parcel Force signage displayed above the driver cab.

It is believed that the van was stolen at around 10am and was subsequently driven down Gatherley Road before turning left at the crossroads in the direction of Scorton.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any sightings of the van around the local area, as well as any sightings of anyone acting suspiciously.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Amy Price. You can also email amy.price@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180092385.
