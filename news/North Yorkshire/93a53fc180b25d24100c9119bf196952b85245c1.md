North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following the theft of forestry machinery near Richmond.

It happened at Aske Moor Saw Mill between Thursday 30 March 2017 and Monday 3 April 2017 and involved theft of an Alstor 8 x 8 mini -forwarder (see image of same make/model).

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious vehicles or people that were in in the area at the tiem.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Gary Hankin. You can also email gary.hankin@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170055924
