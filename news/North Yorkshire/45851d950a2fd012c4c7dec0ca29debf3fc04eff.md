Police investigating the theft of a bottle of wine and a child’s bicycle from Scarborough have issued CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to.

At around 4.20pm on Friday 8 December 2017 a man entered the B&M store on Seamer Road, Scarborough. He then left the store with a bottle of wine without making any attempt to pay for the item.

Upon leaving the store, he spotted an unlocked child’s mountain bike outside the store and put in the boot of his car, a black Vauxhall Astra, before driving away.

Police have issued a CCTV still of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the thefts, as he may have important information that could assist the investigation.

If you recognise him or have any information that can assist our investigation please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for Stephanie Palmer or email Stephanie.Palmer@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number **12170209294**
