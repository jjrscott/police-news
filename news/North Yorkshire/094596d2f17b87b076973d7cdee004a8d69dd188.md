Police are appealing for information following a serious assault on a man in The Viper Rooms, Harrogate.

The incident happened sometime between 12.30am and 1am on Sunday 20 March 2016.

A 21 year old man was standing at the bar in the upstairs area of the club when he was punched in the face by another man. The victim received a serious eye injury requiring significant surgery.

Anyone who has any information that could assist the investigation is asked to contact the police.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to DC Quita Passmore or email _quita.passmore@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160048494 when passing on information about the incident.

**23 March 2016**
