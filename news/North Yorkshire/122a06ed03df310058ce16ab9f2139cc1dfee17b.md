North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary that occurred at a farm with commercial outbuildings, near Sheriff Hutton and West Lilling.

The incident took place between 7pm on Friday 9 August 2019 and 8am on Saturday 10 August 2019 when several outbuildings on the site were broken into.

Two vehicles, a motorcycle, tools and equipment have been stolen, including a large amount of diesel. During the burglary, two dogs were attacked causing injuries which required treatment by a vet.

Officers are appealing for information which would assist their enquiries and are interested to speak to anyone who may of seen the vehicles stolen from the yard - a white Ford Transit panel van with the registration number YA67 JFX , a white Ford Transit tipper van with the registration number YG18 CBF and Yamaha Enduro Motorcycle with the registration YN55 LXC.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, and quote the reference number 12190146813.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
