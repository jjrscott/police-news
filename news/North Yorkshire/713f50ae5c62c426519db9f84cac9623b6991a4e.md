A man has appeared in court charged with attempted sexual assault by penetration against a woman following an incident in Fairburn near Knottingley on 18 June.

The 29-year-old man was charged on Tuesday 21 July and appeared at York Magistrates Court later that day where he was remanded in custody to appear at Leeds Crown Court on 24 August.
