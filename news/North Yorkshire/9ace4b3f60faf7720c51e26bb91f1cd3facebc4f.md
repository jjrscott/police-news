North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in York.

It happened at Popworld, George Hudson Street, York around 2am on 2 June 2017 and involved a 24-year-old man from York who was hit in the face and fell to the floor sustaining an injury.

The victim was taken to York District Hospital by an ambulance where he was treated for a deep vertical cut above his eyebrow.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about two males who were speaking with the victim just prior to the assault. The first male is described as around 5ft 8ins, white, thin build with short clipped hair. The second male is described as tall, white and thin.
