Three people were arrested in Harrogate yesterday following a drugs warrant at an address on Skipton Road.

Local officers and a dedicated Neighbourhood Support Team attended and recovered a large quantity of Class A drugs from the property.

A 19 year old man from Leeds was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply. A 39 year old woman and a 35 year old man, both form Harrogate, were arrested for being concerned in the supply of controlled drugs.

Sergeant Amy Hunter of Harrogate Response team said:

"We are committed to making the county of North Yorkshire an even safer place for our residents. We are dedicated to disrupting criminal activity, and bringing those responsible to justice.

Friday 26 June 2015
