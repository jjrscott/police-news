North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Richmond market place.

An unprovoked assault occurred at the taxi rank in Richmond market place at around 11.40pm on Saturday 5 May 2018, and involved a man in his 50s who sustained an eye injury which required hospital treatment.

In particular, we are appealing for information about two men suspected of assaulting the victim. Both are described as being in their late 20s, with very short hair and tattoos on their arms. One was wearing a white t-shirt, the other a black t-shirt.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Gary Hankin. You can also email gary.hankin@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180078152.
