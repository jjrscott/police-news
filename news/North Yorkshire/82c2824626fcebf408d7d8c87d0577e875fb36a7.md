North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a spate of damage to vehicles in Scarborough.

It happened on Sunday 25 December 2016 on Elmeville Avenue after 5pm and involved a large amount of vehicles being scratched by a sharp implement which were parked up on one side of the road.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious behaviour that has been seen or heard, and requesting that if anyone has any CCTV covering the area that they contact the police.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to PC Charlotte Coles. If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160231706.
