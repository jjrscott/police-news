A man in his 20s has been arrested after an indecent exposure in Whitby.

A teenage girl reported that a man indecently exposed himself on the footpath on New Bridge.

It happened between 8.20am and 8.40am on Friday (6 March).

Following a police investigation, a local man was arrested and has been released on bail pending further enquiries.

Police are urging anyone who saw the incident to get in touch if they have not already done so.

Contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200039153.
