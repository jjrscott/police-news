Police are investigating a serious collision that happened in York today.

Officers were called to reports of a collision involving a grey estate car and a pedestrian in the early hours of this morning.

It happened on Station Road, close to Rougier Street.

The road was closed for a number of hours to allow emergency services to deal with the incident.

An investigation remains ongoing today and the road has been reopened.

If you witnessed the incident or have dashcam footage, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1 and quote reference number NYP09022020-0057.
