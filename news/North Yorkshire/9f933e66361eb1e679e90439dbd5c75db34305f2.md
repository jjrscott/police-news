North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information after a motorcyclist was injured on the B1257 at Newgate Bank between Helmsley and Chop Gate.

It happened at around 6pm on Thursday 18 August and involved a blue and white Suzuki motorcycle and a silver Chrysler Voyager.

The motorcyclist, a 46-year-old man from Stockton on Tees had been traveling from Stokesley towards Helmsley with a group of other riders. The Chrysler was traveling in the opposite direction and the two vehicles collided on a bend.

The motorcycle sustained serious injuries and was air lifted to James Cook Hospital in Middlesbrough by the Yorkshire Air Ambulance.

The occupants of the Chrysler were uninjured.

Police would like to speak to anyone who say any of the vehicles leading up to the collision and to anyone who witnessed the collision itself.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Traffic Constable Chris Graham-Marlow. Or email Chris.Graham-Marlow@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12160149444 when passing on information.
