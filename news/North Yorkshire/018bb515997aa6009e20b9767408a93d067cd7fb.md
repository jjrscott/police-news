North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident of criminal damage that occurred in Appleton Wiske.

A bench was removed from Appleton Wiske Primary School playground and was later found in the skate park. The bench had been set alight and had fire damage to one of the seats.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Catherine Skelton.

You can also email catherine.skelton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150061614
