Police have issued CCTV images of a woman they want to speak to following the theft of alcohol from the Cooperative store in Sherburn in Elmet.

On 7 April 2018, two bottles of vodka were taken from store without payment.

Police are appealing to anyone who can help identify the woman to contact them.

Anyone with information is asked to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Derek Bone, or email Derek.bone@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12180059025 when passing on information
