At 5pm on Tuesday 11 September 2018, police were called to reports of an unprovoked attack on an elderly woman, which had taken place outside the Co-op store on Beckfield Lane in Acomb, York

Police attended and arrested a 22-year-old man from York on suspicion of wounding with intent. He currently remains in police custody.

The victim, a 71-year-old local woman, was taken to York District Hospital where she received treatment for her injuries, which thankfully are not as serious as first thought. She remains in hospital and is receiving support from specially trained officers.

Officers believe that this was isolated incident and extra patrols are being conducted in the area, to reassure the local community.
