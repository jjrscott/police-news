Detectives are appealing for information after a commercial burglary in York city centre.

Thieves took more than 30 bottles of fragrances when they smashed their way into Savers, on Goodramgate, using a metal drain grate, at around 1.10am on Tuesday 17 March 2015.

Two suspects were seen to leave the scene on pedal cycles.

Officers are linking this incident to previous burglaries in York city centre at Links of London, Bang hairdressers, and the Whisky shop.

Police are appealing for anyone with information about the incident, or anyone who has been offered any stolen items for sale, to get in touch.

Anyone who can help officers with their enquiries is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for York CID.

Alternatively, information can be emailed to YorkCID@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you prefer not to give your name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150043360 when passing information about this incident.
