A 36-year-old man from Selby was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply class A drugs, after officers from the Roads Policing Group stopped his vehicle on the A645 near Hensall at 2.45pm on Friday 21 April 2017.

Officers conducted a search of the BMW car and seized a large amount of cash and drugs from within the vehicle.

The man has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1, and speak to the Force Control Room.

If you wish to remain anonymous, please pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.

Please quote reference number 12170067988 when providing details about this incident.
