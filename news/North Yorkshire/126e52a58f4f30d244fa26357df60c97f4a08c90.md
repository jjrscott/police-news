Overnight on 3-4 February 2016 offenders approached an address at Clairemont, Filey and stole the occupants mountain bike from the garden.

Police are appealing to anyone who was in the area at the time or has any other information to contact the police.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Neale Graham.

You can also email neale.graham@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160023037.
