Last modified: 15 October 2018 at 04:03pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary in Skipton.

It happened on Princess Drive between 6pm Saturday 6 October and 10am on Monday 8 October 2018 and involved offender/s attempt to gain entry to a property by forcing a rear window, which resulted in the window being smashed.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 352 Michael Smith. You can also email Michael.Smith352@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180187969.
