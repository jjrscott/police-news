Police are appealing for witnesses after a woman was hit by a taxi in York.

The woman, aged 29 and from Middlesbrough, was hit by a white minibus outside the Royal York Hotel on Station Road at around 8.20pm on Saturday 10 January 2015.

The taxi did not stop at the scene of the collision.

The woman, was treated at the scene by paramedics but was not seriously injured.

Officers are appealing to anyone who witnessed the collision to come forward with information.

Anyone who can help officers with their enquiries is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for PC Gareth Vaughan or York police.

Alternatively, information can be emailed to gareth.vaughan@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you prefer not to give your name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150005479 when passing information about this incident.
