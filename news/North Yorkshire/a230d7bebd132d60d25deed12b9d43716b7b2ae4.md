Last modified: 15 October 2018 at 12:48pm

Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision on the B1363 near York between Wigginton and Sutton-on-the-Forest this morning.

The collision occurred at about 7.30am and involved a Jaguar, which was travelling south, and a VW Polo.

The male driver of the Jaguar suffered serious injuries, and was taken to hospital by air ambulance.

Officers are appealing for anyone who saw the collision, or either of the vehicles involved prior to it, to get in touch. Dial 101, select option 2 and ask for TC Martin Hayes, or email martin.hayes@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Please quote reference number NYP-15102018-0060.

The road remains closed while the vehicles involved are recovered. It is likely to remain closed until about 2pm, and so motorists are urged to find alternative routes.
