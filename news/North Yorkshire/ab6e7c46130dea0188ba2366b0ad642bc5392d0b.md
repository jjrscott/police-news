Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:24am

Police are appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a valuable tractor trailer.

It occurred at a farm in the Eggborough area between 3pm on 26 August and 7am on 27 August 2018.

The blue trailer is 28ft long and has “Philip Watkins” written in red down the side and is displaying the registration plate YN65 DMX on the back.

It is believed the trailer was removed using a tractor.

Police enquiries are ongoing to investigate the full circumstances of the incident.

Members of the public are asked to keep an eye out for the trailer and to report information to the police as soon as possible. Officers would also like to hear from people who recall any suspicious behaviour in the area, particularly around farm land and building.

If you can help, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Graham Smith. Or email Graham.Smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, contact Crimesroppers anonymously on 0800 555111.

Please quote reference number 12180160733 when reporting details about this incident.
