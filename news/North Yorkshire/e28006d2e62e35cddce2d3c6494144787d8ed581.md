Detectives are appealing for witnesses following a violent incident in York.

A 20-year-old man suffered a broken nose in the disorder, which occurred at McDonald's on Blake Street at around 3am on Monday 12 January 2015.

Police and ambulance services attended the incident which took place both inside and outside McDonald's.

A 19-year-old man was arrested in connection with the incident and has been released on police bail as enquiries continue.

Officers are appealing to anyone who witnessed the incident to contact them with information.

Anyone who can help officers with their enquiries is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for Detective Constable Vicky Hildred or York CID.

Alternatively, information can be emailed to vicki.hildred@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or YorkCID@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you prefer not to give your name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150006114 when passing information about this incident.
