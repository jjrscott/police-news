North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred on Skipton Street in Harrogate.

It happened between 8:20am and 16:40pm on Tuesday 29 September 2015 and involved offenders entering a terraced property and conducting an untidy search of the victims home before then leaving with stolen items.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Janine Walker. You can also email janine.walker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150172509.
