Can you help officers investigating a disorder incident which occurred outside Pizza Roma, Selby on Friday, 31 March?

The incident occurred at around 9pm and officers are keep to speak with anyone who witnessed the incident. In particular if you saw a man acting in an agitated manner or a black Saab car with occupants inside outside the venue on Flaxley Road.

Two men have been arrested in connection with this incident and have been bailed while the investigation continues.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact police by calling 101, selecting option 2, and asking for Hazel Simms. You can also email _hazel.simms@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

Information can be provided anonymously through the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170054444 when providing information.
