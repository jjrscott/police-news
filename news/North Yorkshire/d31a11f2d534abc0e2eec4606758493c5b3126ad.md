North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a knife crime incident that occurred on the cycle track between Wigginton Road and Haxby Road, York.

It happened approximately 13:20hrs on Friday 10 of August 2018 and involved a number of youths from the local area engaging in a verbal altercation.

No injuries occurred but one of the youths involved produced a knife which was concealed in his trousers.

In particular, we are appealing for information to help identify the boy with the knife, who was described as being as white and aged between 13 to 16 years old.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 425 Nikki JACKSON. You can also email nikki.jackson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180148194.
