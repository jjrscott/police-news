Scarborough police are appealing to the public to keep an eye out for a car which they are looking for.

Officers are searching for a silver Honda Jazz - registration number YD55BCF.

The car belongs to an elderly resident who is keen to get her vehicle back.

Anyone who has any information about the whereabouts of the car is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for Traffic Constable Vince Maltby or Scarborough Roads Policing Group.

Please quote reference number 12150026745 when passing information about this incident.
