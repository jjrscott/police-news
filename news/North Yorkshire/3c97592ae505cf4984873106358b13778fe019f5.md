North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about damage caused to vehicles in Topcliffe, Thirsk.

Several incidents of criminal damage to cars have occurred on East Lea, Topcliffe, Thirsk. This damage is in the form of vehicle tyres being slashed and cars damaged with spray paint. Malicious messages have also been left on the damaged vehicles.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anybody acting suspiciously in the area or around any vehicles in or around the street.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Kate Jones. You can also email Kate.Jones@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160000773.
