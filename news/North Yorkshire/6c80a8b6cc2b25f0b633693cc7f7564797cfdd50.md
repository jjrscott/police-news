North Yorkshire Police are appealing for any information in relation to a burglary that has occurred on the evening of Thursday 29th March 2018 at City Electrical Factors on Finkills Way in Northallerton.

Between 8.49pm and 8.55pm on Thursday 29 March 2018, a burglary occurred at City Electrical Factors on Finkills Way in Northallerton where a number of tools and cash have been stolen.

Entry has been gained by smashing the window and forcing the shutters at the front of the premises.

Two men have been observed at the scene, one in all dark clothing and the other in dark trousers and a grey hoody with white gloves.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for any information in relation to this burglary at these business premises.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Karen Mitson. You can also email Karen.mitson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180053707.
