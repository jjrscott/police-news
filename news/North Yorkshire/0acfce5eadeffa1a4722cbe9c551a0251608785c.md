Last modified: 5 December 2019 at 03:41pm

Police in York are appealing for witnesses and dash-cam footage following a collision on Fulford Road involving a car and two pedestrians.

It happened at near the Iceland store around 10am on Wednesday 4 December 2019 when a Volkswagen Touran collided with an 86-year-old man and an 84-year-old woman. The woman was treated for minor injuries at hospital and later discharged.

The man suffered serious but non-life threatening injuries and was taken to hospital.

The driver of the Volkswagen was uninjured. We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC 667 JAMES or email Rebecca.james@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk .

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12190222202 when passing on information.
