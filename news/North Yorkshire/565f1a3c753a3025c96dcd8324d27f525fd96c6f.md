North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following an incident of criminal damage in Scarborough.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following an incident of criminal damage in Scarborough.

The incident occurred on Columbus Ravine at around 3.30pm on Saturday, 1 September.

It is believed two men were involved and caused damage to a brick wall after kicking it.

Two men have since been released and released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Anyone with information which could assist the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jordan Galey. You can also email Jordan.Galey@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12180163596 when providing information.
