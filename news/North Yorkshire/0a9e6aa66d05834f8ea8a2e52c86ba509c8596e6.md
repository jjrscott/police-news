Last modified: 21 December 2020 at 10:56am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for any witnesses or dashcam footage following the collision on Burniston Road.

The collision occurred at 3.10pm on Saturday 19 December 2020 outside the Open Air Theatre on Burniston Road in Scarborough. A female pedestrian suffered injuries after being struck by a silver Kia Ceed. The Yorkshire Air Ambulance attended the scene and the female was transported by air to hospital.

Officers are asking anyone who witnessed the collision or who may have information which could help establish the full circumstances of the incident to get in touch. We are also particularly requesting anyone with dashcam footage to contact police.

Anyone with any information that could assist should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC566. Alternatively you can email Nicholas.Patrick@NorthYorkshire.PNN.Police.uk

When contacting, please quote the reference number 12200241345
