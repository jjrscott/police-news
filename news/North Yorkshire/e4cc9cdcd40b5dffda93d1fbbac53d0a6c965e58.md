North Yorkshire Police are appealing for information after a number of vehicles were damaged and had items stolen from them on Scarcroft Road in York.

The damage occured at some time overnight from Monday 12 October to Tuesday 13 October 2015.

Ten vehicles along the road had windows smashed, wing mirrors kicked off and items stolen from inside including sat navs.

The damaged vehicles include a red Ford Fiesta, a blue Jaguar X Type, a black Mini, a grey Nissan Micra, a white Nissan Pathfinder, a red Peugeot 206, a white Vauxhall Astra, a dark blue Vauxhall Astra, a blue Vauxhall Vectra and a black Volkwagen Polo.

Police are appealing to any witnesses to contact them on 101, select option 1 and ask to speak to Force Control Room. Please quote reference number 12150181238.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Tuesday 13 October 2015
