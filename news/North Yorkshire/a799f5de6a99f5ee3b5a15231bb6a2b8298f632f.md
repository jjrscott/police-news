North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision which caused damage only, in Belle Hill in Giggleswick.

This collision happened on Belle Hill in Giggleswick at 15:15hrs on 3rd August 2016 and involved a silver vehicle colliding into a dark grey Ford Fiesta. The silver vehicle left the area and made no attempt to speak to the driver of the Fiesta.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Burgess. You can also email David.Burgess@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160139009.
