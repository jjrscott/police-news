Police are appealing for witnesses to contact them following a distraction burglary at a mini market in York, where a safe was stolen.

A large group, consisting of two men and five females entered the store at 5.30pm on Monday 26 March 2018.

It is believed that the men engaged the shop workers with enquiries to distract them whilst the women carried out the burglary.

The group is thought to have made off in a large black people carrier style vehicle.

Police are appealing for anyone who was in the area of the time of the burglary, who may have seen a vehicle matching the description, the group of people, or anything which aroused suspicion, to contact them.
