Last modified: 5 December 2016 at 05:10pm

Police are appealing for witnesses after a woman has died in a collision near Borougbridge.

Police were called at 12.24pm on Monday 5 December 2016 to a serious collision on the A168 just south of Boroughbridge involving a red Ford Ka and a green lorry.

The driver of the Ka, a woman in her thirties, was taken to Harrogate District Hospital where she sadly died.

The driver of the lorry was uninjured but very shaken up.

Anyone who saw either of the vehicles before the collision or witnessed the incident is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for the Major Collision Investigation team or email mcit@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12160219432

Police, fire and ambulance services attended the scene. The road was closed to allow emergency services to deal with the collision and to allow collision investigators to examine the scene. It reopened 4.05pm.
