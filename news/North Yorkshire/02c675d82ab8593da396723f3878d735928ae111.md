Police were called at 9.25am Monday 9 November to reports of the sudden death of a woman at St John Playing Field, Windmill Lane, York.

Officers attended and upon arrival found the body of a 23-year-old woman who was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Enquiries are currently ongoing, but there are not believed to be any suspicious circumstances surrounding the woman's death.
