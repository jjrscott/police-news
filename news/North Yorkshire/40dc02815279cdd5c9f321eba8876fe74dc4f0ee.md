North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a criminal damage that occurred on Victoria Road, Scarborough, to the Sewing Solutions shop

It happened between 5.10pm on 11/09/15 and 10am on 12/09/15

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the damage to this small, independent business.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone who saw or heard anything.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 219 Crane. You can also email helen.crane@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150160843
