North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an offence of criminal damage in Skipton on Tuesday 27 February 2018.

On the evening of Tuesday 27 February 2018 at approximately 6.45pm, it is believed that a group of youths were involved in an incident outside Pinder House located in the bus station in Skipton.

During this incident a male was seen to kick the glazed main entrance door causing the glass to smash. A member of staff was standing near to the door and was lucky to avoid injury.
