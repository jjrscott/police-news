Last modified: 22 June 2020 at 03:22pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after the communal areas of two blocks of flats in York were targeted in two burglaries.

Overnight on Thursday 18 June to Friday 19 June, over twenty post boxes belonging to residents were forcibly opened in the communal hallway of flats at Rowntree Wharfe. Enquiries are ongoing to establish if anything was taken from within them.

On Friday 19 June, a parcel containing approx. £95 of clothing was taken from the communal area of a block of flats on Palmer Lane.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who has any information that could assist their investigation.

In particular, they are keen to speak to anyone who is aware of incidents of parcels being stolen or letterboxes entered in similar circumstances.

Anyone who witnessed suspicious activity in the area or has information is asked to email Will.Leah@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk and David.Pegg@northyorkshire.police.uk

Alternatively, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101 quoting the relevant incident number below:

 * 12200103276 – Rowntree Wharfe burglary
 * 12200103627 - Palmer Lane burglary
