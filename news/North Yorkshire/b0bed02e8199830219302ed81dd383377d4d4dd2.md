Officers are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary in Harrogate.

The incident happened Thursday 29 October 2020 between 3pm and 11.30pm, when offender(s) gained entry to a property on Hookstone Avenue,

Anyone who saw anything suspicious in the area between the times state above is asked to contact police.

In addition, if anyone has any dashcam footage of the area at the time, officers would like to hear from you.

Dial 101, press 1 and pass information to the force control room, quoting reference number 12200190265.
