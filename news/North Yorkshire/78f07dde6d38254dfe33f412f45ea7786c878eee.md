North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Selby.

It happened at a residential property on Abbots Road, Selby overnight between 11pm on Sunday 21 October 2018 and 3am on Monday 22 October 2018.

The offender entered the property and stole among other things a black and pink handbag and purse, money, a Samsung Galaxy tablet and a gold and silver bracelet.

On Monday 22 October 2018, a 30 year-old man was arrested on suspicion of burglary. He was released under investigation pending further enquiries.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious activity in the Abbots Road area overnight on Sunday 21 October and any information about the stolen items.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Alastair Foy. You can also email alastair.foy@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180197525.
