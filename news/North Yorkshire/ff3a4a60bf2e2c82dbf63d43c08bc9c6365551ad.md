Police want to identify a woman pictured by CCTV after two pairs of shoes were stolen from a shop in York.

The items were taken from Schuh at Monks Cross on the morning of 6 August 2017 by a woman and a man who tried to trick shop staff and conceal the goods.

Police want to speak to the woman pictured by CCTV in connection with the thefts.

If you recognise her, contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Nikki Jackson. You can also email PC Jackson on Nikki.Jackson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
