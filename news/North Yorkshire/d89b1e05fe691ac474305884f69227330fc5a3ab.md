North Yorkshire Police is seeking witnesses to criminal damage caused to a motor vehicle in Whitby.

It happened on Abbots Walk at approximately 10pm on the evening of Monday 19th October 2020,.

Two male teenagers, aged 16-17yrs, were seen in the immediate vicinity to where three tyres have been slashed on a car. The same two teenagers were then seen running off towards Abbots Road.

The teenagers were described to be wearing all dark clothing and both wearing a hooded top.

If anybody has any information in relation to this incident, or saw anything suspicious in the area at the time, please contact North Yorkshire Police.Dial 101, press 1 and ask to speak to Jonathan Formstone or email Jonathan.formstone@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk, quoting incident number 12200183830.
