Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:24am

We are appealing for witnesses and information about an attempt residential burglary that occurred in Brayton, Selby.

It happened on Ashtree Drive, Brayton, Selby between 1pm and 4.15pm on Wednesday 14 February 2018 and involved the offender attempting to gain entry to a detached house by forcing the rear patio doors.

A male in a high visibility coat was seen on Ashtree Drive and police would like to speak to this man to determine the circumstances around the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation or identify the male in the locality should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for of officer, Alison Baker. You can also email alison.baker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-14022018-0326
