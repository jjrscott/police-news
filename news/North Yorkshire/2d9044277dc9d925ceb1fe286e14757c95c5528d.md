North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred on the evening of 24 January 2016 into the early hours of 25 January 2016

The incident occured between 11pm and 5amon Northstead Manor Road, Scarborough. Unknown offender(s) have attacked a detached garage at the rear of the property and stolen a Red CORGI folding motorbike registration FPY 103. It is unknown if the offenders left on foot or in a vehicle.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for 119 SHARP. You can also email Kelly.Sharp@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160014002
