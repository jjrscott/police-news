Police are asking for help to identify the man in this CCTV images following three incidents of fuel theft.

They happened at the Spar Garage in Hutton Rudby on 25 January, 14 February, and 19 February 2021 when a convertible Vauxhall Astra was filled up with fuel that was not paid for.

Officers are asking for the public's help to identify the man in the image as he could have information that would assist the investigation.

Anyone with information that should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Charlotte Lancaster. You can also email charlotte.lancaster2@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12210040169.
