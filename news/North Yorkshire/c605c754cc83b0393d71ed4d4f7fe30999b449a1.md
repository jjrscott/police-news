Last modified: 13 March 2020 at 01:23pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a fatal pedestrian collision at Aysgarth, near Leyburn.

The incident occurred on Church Bank at around 12.30pm on Thursday (12 March 2020).

The collision involved a heavy goods vehicle and a man aged in his 70s.

The man suffered serious injuries and was pronounced dead at the scene. Next of kin have been informed but we are not in a position to name him at this time.

The driver of the HGV, a 28-year-old man from Cheshire East, was arrested on suspicion of causing death by careless driving. Following questioning he was released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Witnesses or anyone with information about the incident are asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Julie Brown.

Alternatively, email the Major Collision Investigation Team via MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote reference number 12200042833 when providing details about this incident.
