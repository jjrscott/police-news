Police are appealing for witnesses and information after a cyclist was injured in a collision near Eden Camp in the Malton area.

It happened at around 7.15am on Tuesday 9 August 2016 on the roundabout at the A64 interchange with the A169 and B1257.
The cyclist, a 59-year-old local man and a black Vauxhall Zafira were in collision.
The cyclist was airlifted to James Cook Hospital in Middlesbrough with serious but non-life threatening injuries.

Police are appealing to anyone who saw either the Zafira or cyclist prior to the collision, or who witnessed the actual collision, to contact them.
Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Scarborough Roads Policing Group or email Gareth.gilleard@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
Please quote reference number 12160142774 when passing on information.
