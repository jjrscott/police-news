Shortly after 8.15pm yesterday (Monday 7 September 2020) police received a report from the ambulance service of a man seriously injured at an address on Ouseburn Avenue in Acomb, York. The 27 year-old man was transported to hospital and is described as being in a stable condition.

Officers have arrested two men in connection with this incident and both remain in police custody at this time. Police are not currently looking for any further suspects in connection with this incident.

If anyone witnessed the incident or has any information which could help police establish the full circumstances, then please call 101 quoting ref: NYP-07092020-0497
