North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of 25 to 30 Swaledale Gimmer Hogg sheep from a field adjacent to Newby Moor, Clapham.

Between Monday 20 March 2017 to Friday 24 March 2017, 25 to 30 Swaledale Gimmer Hogg sheep were stolen from Hardacre, Bentham. The sheep are believed to have been taken via Newby Moor.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Barbara Parsons. You can also email Barbara.parsons@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170049726.
