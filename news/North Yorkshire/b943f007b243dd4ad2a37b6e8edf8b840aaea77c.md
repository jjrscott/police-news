North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a fail to stop road traffic collission that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened at Holbeck Road, Scarborough near to a chicane between 10.30pm on Thursday 31 March 2016 and 8.30am on Friday 1 April 2016.

An unknown silver vehicle has collided with a parked silver corsa resulting in damage. The silver casing to a wing mirror was left behind at the scene.

We are appealing for any witnesses who saw the collision to contact police.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Georgina Hanson. You can also email Georgina.Hanson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160055984.
