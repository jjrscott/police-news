North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a criminal damage involving two vehicles that occurred in Osgodby, Scarborough

It happened on Osgodby Bridge at around 9pm on Saturday 28 October 2017 when two men and two women were involved in an altercation on the bridge.

During the incident a Peugeot 306 a Vauxhall Vectra were present.

There were a number of vehicles passing the location at the time of the incident and officers are appealing to anyone who was passing and witnessed the incident.

In particular, they are appealing for information about anyone seen kicking a vehicle, potentially causing damage.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Harriet Green. You can also email Harriet.green@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170193785

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

A man has been arrested in connection with the incident and release while under investigation.
