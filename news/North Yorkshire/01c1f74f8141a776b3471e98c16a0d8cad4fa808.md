North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a number of burglaries in Pickering.

The burglaries happened on Shepherd's Hill, Pickering, at sometime between 2.45am and 6am on 20 April 2015 and involved a break-in at a house as well as a garage and a number of garden sheds.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incidents.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspect persons or vehicles seen in the area of Shepherd's Hill, Whitfield Avenue and Ruffa Lane areas of Pickering overnight from Sunday 19 April into Monday 20 April 2015. The offender left the area with stolen property including a white mountain bike and a large bag.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Paul Storey.

You can also email paul.storey554@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150064536.
