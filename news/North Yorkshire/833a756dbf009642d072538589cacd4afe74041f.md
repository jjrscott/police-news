North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred at at builders merchants in Selby.

It happened at BM SELBY, The Waterfront Complex, The Old Ship Yard, Carr Street, Selby between 8pm on Saturday 17th and 10am on Sunday 18th June 2017.

Offenders have smashed the front door of the builders merchants to gain entry to the premise and have stolen items from within the shop and yard.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1944 Baker. You can also email alison.baker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170106341.
