North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in York.

The incident happened near to the cross road separating Monk Bar and Monkgate at approximately 11.40pm on Saturday 11 July 2015.

A man walking home from York City Centre was assaulted by an unknown man, sustaining significant facial injuries.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.
