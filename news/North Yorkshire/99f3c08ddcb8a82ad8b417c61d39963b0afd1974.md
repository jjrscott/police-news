North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a shed burglary that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Cropton Rise at 4:20am on 4 July 2015.

We are appealing for anyone who may have seen anything suspicious in the area at the time to come forward.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 219 Crane. You can also email helen.crane@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150121341when passing on information about this incident.

Friday 17 July 2015.
