Police are appealing to the public for help to identify the men in the CCTV following a theft from Go Outdoors in York.

Officers want to speak to the men in connection with the incident which happened at 1.20pm on 27 February 2018.

Two men entered the Go Outdoors store on Fossbank in York and took a number of jackets believed to be worth a total of Â£1000. They left the store without making any attempt to pay for the items.

Anyone who recognises the men or who has any information, is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Nikki Jackson or email nikki.jackson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12180034443 when passing on any information.
