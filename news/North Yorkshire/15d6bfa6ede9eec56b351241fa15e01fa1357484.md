Officers are appealing for information after a man was found in injured in the road in the early of hours Friday 11 December 2020.

The incident happened at 4.30am when a 22 year old man from Knaresborough was found on the A1246 between BROTHERTON and FAIRBURN (the stretch of road in between the roundabout leaving Brotherton and roundabout approaching Fairburn).

It is believed the man was hit by a vehicle which did not stop.

Anyone who was in the area at the time is asked to contact police.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Nicola Knowles or email Nicola.knowles@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12200225698 when passing on any information.
