North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a violent incident that occurred in Filey.

It happened on Ash Grove and Scarborough Road between 9.30pm and 10.15pm on Tuesday 8 November 2016 and initially involved a violent disturbance outside an address, later progressing onto Scarborough Road

A man was arrested at the time and is currently bailed pending further enquiries.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information relating to a disturbance outside of a residential address and, subsequently, any incident relating to the use of a burgundy / red Ford estate on Scarborough Road and surrounding areas.

Additionally, if anyone finds a discarded knife in the surrounding area please contact police immediately.

The suspect is described as a white man, 6ft 2ins, of proportionate build, short dark hair, dark jacket, dark trousers and black trainers.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Nick Durkin. You can also email nick.durkin@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
