North Yorkshire Police Serious Crime Team are investigating a serious assault on a teenage girl in Strensall which occurred at approximately 8pm on Sunday 13 March 2016.

The incident occurred in woodland adjacent to a play park between Pasture Close and Kirklands in Strensall.

Police are looking to trace witnesses to the attack, in particular a number of teenage youths who were playing football nearby, one of whom had with him a white coloured moped.

Members of the public who witnessed anything that night or can assist with names or identities of the youths playing football at approximately 8pm are asked to contact DC Foy of the Serious Crime Team on 101 quoting incident number 12160044426.
