A man has been charged in connection with a robbery at a jewellers in Northallerton earlier this month.

A 38-year-old man from Lazenby, near Middlesbrough, has been charged with robbing Bradley's Jewellers on 11 March 2019.

He was arrested in Fareham, Hampshire, on 16 March 2019.

He is due to appear at York Magistrates Court this morning.
