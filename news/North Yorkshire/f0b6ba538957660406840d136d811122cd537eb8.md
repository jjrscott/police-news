North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after damage was caused to the front door of a hotel in West Ayton.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after damage was caused to the front door of a hotel in West Ayton.

The incident occurred at around 11.45pm on Wednesday, 22 August at the venue on Pickering Road.

It is believed the offender has used a large concrete slab to cause the damage.

Anyone with information which could assist with the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul O’Neil. You can also provide information by emailing Paul.O’Neil@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
