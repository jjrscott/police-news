North Yorkshire Police are trying to reunite a corn snake with its owner.

Police received a call on Tuesday 3 May at 1.20pm from a concerned member of the public who had seen a one metre long orange snake slither along the path on Holgate Road, York.

Police attended and found a corn snake taking refuge in a near by hedge.

The snake, currently nicknamed Sammy, is currently being looked after by a local pet shop.

If you believe Sammy could be yours, please contact the Force Control Room on 101 and quote reference 12160076442.
