North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a house burglary that occurred in Sutton in Craven.

It happened on Hazel Grove, Sutton in Craven overnight on 1-2 February 2016 and involved the suspects gaining entry via forcing the lock on the back door then making a search of the downstairs area, believed to be looking for the occupants vehicle keys before leaving empty handed.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Susan Marklew. You can also email susan.marklew@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160018964.
