Police are appealing for witnesses following an altercation between a man and a woman in York.

The incident happened between 1.30am and 2.30am on Saturday 21 January 2017 on Micklegate.

A 21 year old man from York was arrested in connection with the incident. He has been released on bail pending further enquiries.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press and ask to speak to PC 1488 Jo Bell.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote crime reference number 12170014206 when passing on any information.
