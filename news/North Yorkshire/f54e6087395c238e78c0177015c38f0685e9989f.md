North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a Land Rover that occurred in Ickornshaw, Cowling.

It happened at Town Head Farm between midnight and 05.50 hours on 23rd February 2015 and involved the theft of a twin cab Land Rover in green registration number YC62WTG

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for James Dykes. You can also email James.Dykes@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150029982
