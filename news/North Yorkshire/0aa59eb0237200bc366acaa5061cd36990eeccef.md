Last modified: 15 July 2020 at 04:42pm

Police in Scarborough are appealing for witnesses and information about a a violent altercation on Albemarle Crescent.

It happened at around 7.25pm on Tuesday 14 July 2020 and involved a group of men.

Officers believe the area was busy at the time and are appealing to anyone who was in the area and saw a group of men involved in the incident and to anyone else who has any other information that could help the investigation, to please get in touch.

Anyone with information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 222 Emaleigh Simeson You can also email emaleigh.simeson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200119996.

Three men have been arrested in connection with the incident and released on bail while enquiries continue.
