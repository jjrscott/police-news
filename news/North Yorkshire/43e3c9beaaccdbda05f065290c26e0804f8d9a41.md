Two men have died following a light aircraft crash near Castle Howard in the Ryedale area of North Yorkshire.

North Yorkshire Police received a number of calls from members of the public at around 10.40am on the morning of Saturday 30 April 2016.

The aircraft crashed in fields between Castle Howard and the A64.

The Air Accident Investigation Branch have been informed.

Roads in the area have been closed to allow emergency services to deal with the incident and members of the public are advised to keep away from the area.

We will release further information when we are able.

30 April 2016
