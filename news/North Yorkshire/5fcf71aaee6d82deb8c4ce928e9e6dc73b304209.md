North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision that occurred on Musham Bank, Eastfield, Scarborough.

The incident happened at approximately 1pm on Monday the 17of July 2017 when a grey Citroen Picasso was hit by another vehicle. The other vehicle failed to stop at the scene and police are now trying to trace this vehicle and it's owner.
Upon arrival at the scene a witness spoke with police, who possibly had dash camera footage of this incident. This witness left the location before any details were exchanged and police would like to speak with this person as a matter of urgency.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 250 Mark Walton. You can also email mark.walton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170125491.
