North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident involving two men at the rear of St Peters School, Clifton in York.

The incident happened between 5.45pm and 6.15pm on Wednesday 24 March 2021 and involved two male teenagers having a verbal altercation, during which one of the males appeared to be in possession of a knife and threatening the other male.
