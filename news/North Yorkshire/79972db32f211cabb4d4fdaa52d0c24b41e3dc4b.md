Police in Filey area appealing for witnesses and information about a shed burglary and the theft of garden items from a property in the village of Hunmanby.

It happened on Simpson Avenue on three seperate occasions and involved the theft of decorative garden ornaments.

In particular, we are appealing for information whether anyone has seen anyone acting suspiciously in the early hours of 11/08/2016 and 16/08/2016

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Lee Ridsdale. You can also email lee.ridsdale221@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160147670.
