North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Burglary that occurred in Skipton at a town centre shop.

It happened at Tog 24 on the High Street in Skipton between the hours of 5pm Sunday 13 November and 9am Monday 14 November.
The offenders are believed to have gained entry via the roof and have stolen cash and a quantity of stock.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for any person to come forward who witnessed anything out of the ordinary at Tog 24 between the above mentioned times.
We are also appealing for anybody who may have been offered the sale of new looking Tog 24 jackets to come forward.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Ben Brittain.
You can also email PC Brittain on ben.brittain@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160206435.
