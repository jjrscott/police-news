North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred in Harrogate.

The burglary took place at Readings Autos on Freemans Way at some point overnight between 10pm Tuesday 2nd April and 6am Wednesday 3rd April 2019.

Offenders have gained entry to the building and have stolen a large quantity of motor mechanics tools and diagnostic equipment along with two motor bikes - one of which a Honda CB600F is pictured.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any vehicles seen in the area, which were acting suspiciously or any one selling used tools, specifically SNAP-ON branded items.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for James Skaith. You can also email James.Skaith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190059585
