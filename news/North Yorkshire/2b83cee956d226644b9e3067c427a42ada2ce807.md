Last modified: 30 September 2020 at 12:49pm

Police are appealing for witnesses and dash-cam footage after a man died in a collision near Settrington in the Malton area.

It happened at around 5.15pm on Tuesday 29 September 2020, when a black MGB and a silver Mitsubishi Outlander travelling in opposite directions, collided on the unclassified road between Back Lane and Scarlet Balk Lane.

Sadly, the driver of the MGB, a man in his 20s from the Malton area, was pronounced dead at the scene of the collision.

The driver of the Mitsubishi was airlifted to hospital but later discharged.

Police are appealing to anyone who saw either of the vehicles prior to the crash or saw the incident happen, including anyone with any dash-cam footage of the vehicles or the collision.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for the Major Collision Investigation Team or email Julie.Brown@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote incident number 12200171579 when passing on information.
