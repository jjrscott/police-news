Officers want to speak to two men after goods were stolen from a Harrogate chemist.

Two men entered Lloyds Pharmacy on Kings Road and took several slimming products from the shelves.

They placed them into one of the men's jackets before leaving the shop without making payment.

Despite detailed enquires police have been unable to identify those involved.

They have now released CCTV images of two men they would like to talk to about the incident, which happened on 21 October 2017.

If you recognise the male or have any information that may assist, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Matthew Reeve.

You can also email matthew.reeve@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170188926.
