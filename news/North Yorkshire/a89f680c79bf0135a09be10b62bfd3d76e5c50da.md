Last modified: 24 December 2019 at 12:26pm

Scarborough police are appealing for witnesses including a group of women to come forward following an assault at Sanctuary Bar on St Nicholas Street.

It happened between 3.10am and 3.15am in the early hours of Saturday 21 December at a table near the front of the premises - which were previously known as the Blue Lounge.

A 19-year-old man was assaulted by another man and sustained a cut to his face.

There were a number of woman sat around the table where the assault happened and officers are appealing to them to come forward along with anyone else who witnessed the incident.

Anyone with any information is asked to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Scarborough Investigation Hub. Or email zac.waterman@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12190232770 when passing on information.

A man in his 30s has been arrested in connection with the investigation and release on bail while the investigation continues.
