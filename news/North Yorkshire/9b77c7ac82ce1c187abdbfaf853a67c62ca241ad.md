Four people have been charged on suspicion of drug offences relating to the supply of heroin and cocaine in Harrogate.

Two of them appeared at York Magistrates’ Court on 10 June, they are:

A 34-year-old woman of no fixed address was charged with being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine and heroin.

A 17-year-old boy from Bradford was charged with money laundering and being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine and heroin.

The case was sent to York Crown Court for trial on 8 July 2019.

A third suspect, a 38-year-old man from Harrogate, has been charged with being concerned in the supply of heroin and cocaine. He is due to appear at Harrogate Magistrates Court on 4 July.

In a separate investigation, a 26-year-old man from Bradford, was charged with possessing crack cocaine with intent to supply and possessing heroin with intent to supply. He appeared at York Magistrates‘ Court on 10 June where the case was sent to York Crown Court for trial on 8 July 2019.
