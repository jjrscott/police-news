Last modified: 30 October 2020 at 09:28pm

Police have issued CCTV images of a woman they would like to speak to in connection with a series of thefts at Boots in Catterick Garrison.

The thefts happened at the store on Prince's Gate between 5.30pm and 5.45pm on Thursday 1 October 2020 and between 4.10pm and 4.25pm on Friday 2 October.

In both incidents, a woman entered the store and took a number of items without making an attempt to pay for them.

Officers are asking members of the public to get in touch if they recognise the woman in the images as they believe she might have information that could help the investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Emma Naylor or email emma.naylor@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12200173684 when passing on information.
