Last modified: 9 March 2020 at 10:17am

Police are appealing for any information relating to the theft of an Ifor Williams trailer stolen from a farm in Warthill.

It happened overnight on Tuesday 3 March.

The trailer is a 16-ft long, two-axel flat bed. It has a hydraulic lift at the front which allows for easier loading. This is an unusual feature.

We are appealing for anyone who saw anything suspicious or has any other information to get in touch.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any sightings of the trailer on March 3 or 4, or any other information leading to its whereabouts.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police using the details below.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200037910
