North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a criminal damage incident that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Columbus Ravine at the junction with Murchison Street at 4.43am on 22 August 2015 and involved a rock being thrown at a side window of Kebabland, causing damage.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone seen in the area at the time.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul Gibson-Hodges.

You can also email Paul.Gibson-Hodges@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150147177.
