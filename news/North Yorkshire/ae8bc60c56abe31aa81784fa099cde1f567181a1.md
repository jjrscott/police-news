North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information in relation to the theft of a Land Rover Defender taken from a farm at Whixley, North Yorkshire.

View image

It happened between 7pm on Wednesday 6 July and 7.50am on Thursday 7 July 2016, and involved offenders entering a farm yard and removing the vehicle by unknown means, as all keys are accounted for.

The stolen vehicle is a metallic red/maroon Land Rover Defender 90, registration number MD08 MTE. It has a van-type back with a high roof and small windows at the top of the roof, an additional single seat has been added in the rear and after-market LED lights have been fitted to the front bumper.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Neil Roworth. You can also email neil.roworth@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-07072016-0077.
