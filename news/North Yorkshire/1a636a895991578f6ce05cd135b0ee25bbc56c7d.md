Emergency services are at the scene of an incident at a property on Marlborough Street, Scarborough.

Officers responded to reports from members of the public and ambulance at 5.40pm today, Wednesday 1 May 2019.

Upon arrival at the scene, officers sadly discovered the body of a man in his seventies.

Officers are working at the scene to establish the full circumstances surrounding the death of the man.
