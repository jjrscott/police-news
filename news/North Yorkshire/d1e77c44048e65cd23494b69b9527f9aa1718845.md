North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident of criminal damage that occurred in Milton Street, Skipton.

It happened between 8pm on Sunday 6th August 2017 and 9.10am on Tuesday 8th August 2017.

The front and rear offside tyres of a Landrover Discovery have been damaged.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Liz Rutter. You can also email liz.rutter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170140547
