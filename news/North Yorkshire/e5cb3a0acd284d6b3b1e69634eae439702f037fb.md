Appeal for witnesses and information following theft of a blue Mitsubishi L200 pickup

At 6.20pm on 5 October 2016. The owner of the Mitsubishi L200, registrationYG15WMV, parked it on a dead end road close to the village of Buttercrambe while he carried out tree surgery with a colleague.

Three men, all described as white and aged between 20 and 40 years of age, were seen in the area driving a metallic light blue saloon type car. They appear to have paid close attention to the victim and his vehicle and a short time later, the pickup was stolen with the keys and made off towards Buttercrambe.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we would like to speak to anyone who may have seen three males acting suspiciously in or around the location of Buttercrambe on 5 October 2016 or anyone who may have seen the vehicle being driven after 6.20pm.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC434 Will Lane You can also email will.lane@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160181498.
