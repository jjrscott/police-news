Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal road traffic collision on the A171 near Whitby overnight.

The incident happened at around 2.20am today, Tuesday 11 August. A black Vauxhall Corsa, travelling east towards Whitby on the A171, left the road and hit and wall and a building.

The driver of the vehicle, a 27 year old man received fatal injuries and was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or who saw the vehicle prior to the collision is asked to contact the police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask for TC Martin Mulcaster in the Major Collision Investigation Team or email

MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
