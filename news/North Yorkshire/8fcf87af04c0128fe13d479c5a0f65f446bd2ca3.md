Last modified: 16 December 2020 at 12:40pm

Police in York are appealing for witnesses and information after a black Ford Fiesta was taken without the owner's consent in the Clifton area.

The incident happened on Horner Street at around 7.50pm on Monday 7 December 2020.

Two teenage boys wearing hooded tops entered the vehicle and it is believed they drove it down Burton Stone Lane and onto Burton Green where it was abandoned.

The car was later returned to the owner.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should email 000863@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200222436.
