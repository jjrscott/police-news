North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a street Robbery that occurred in York on Tuesday 10th March 2015.

We are appealing for witnesses in relation to a street robbery which took place at around 4.15pm on Haleys Terrace, on the end of the ginnel which leads to the rear of Nuffield Hospital.

A man approached a woman, who was walking alone, grabbed her by her jumper and took her property from her pockets.

We are conducting enquiries and appealing for the public's help to determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

The victim saw a female jogger run past her immediately after the incident took place. We are asking for anyone with information about the jogger, or the jogger herself, to contact us as a matter of urgency - your help might assist the investigstion.

We are also appealing for information about a man in his 50's with grey hair wearing a 3/4 length Army Green coloured jacket who was also seen in the area at the time.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should dial 101, select option 2, and ask for DC Beverley Hoban in York CID. Alternatively you can emailbeverley.hoban@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-10032015-0389.
