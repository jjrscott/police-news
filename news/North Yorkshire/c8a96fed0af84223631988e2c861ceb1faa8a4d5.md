North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Bedale.

It happened on Market Place, Bedale between 11.20am and 11.25am Sunday 6 May 2018 and involved two men having a heated altercation in the vicinity of Bedale Post Office.

The first man is described as white, early 20s in age, of slim build with short dark hair wearing dark coloured clothing and he was in company with a husky style dog.

The second man is described as white, early to late 20s in age, medium build, with short fair hair and was wearing a grey coloured t-shirt, blue coloured shorts, sunglasses and he was carrying a dark coloured backpack and pushing a mountain bike.
