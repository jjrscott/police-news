North Yorkshire Police are appealing to any witnesses to fail to stop incident on Bootham to contact them.

Police were called to reports of a fail to stop incident which occured at 10.30am Thursday 14 January 2016 outside Bootham Park Hospital in York.

It is alleged that a car struck a 29-year-old female pedestrian, injuring her leg, but failed to stop.

Police are appealing for anyone who saw the incident or who has any information which would help the investigation to contact them on 101 and quote reference 12160007580. You could also ask to speak to TC Lee Cobb or email Lee.Cobb@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Crimestoppers can also be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote the same reference 12160007580.
