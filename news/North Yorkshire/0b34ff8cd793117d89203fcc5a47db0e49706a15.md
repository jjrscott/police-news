North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an arson that occurred at Grey Leys Farm, Elvington.

At around 4pm on Monday 14 August 2017, unknown offender(s) have set fire to a pile of tyres located at the rear of the field adjacent to Elvington Lane.

Six fire engines attended the scene, swiftly gaining control of the fire and therefore protecting neighbouring properties including farm houses and building containing livestock.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about how the fire was started and who was involved.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170144132 when providing details about this incident.
