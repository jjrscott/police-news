Police in York are appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that happened at Vue Cinema on Clifton Moor.

It happened at approximately 10.20pm on the on 23 July 2019.

A group of youths and an adult male had waited outside the cinema for four people who the youths had had an encounter with earlier that evening.

The offenders surrounded the victims as they left the cinema, one of the group was surrounded and assaulted by the youths and the adult male. A female victim was also assaulted when trying to aid her friend.

One of the victim's received a minor injuries to his face.
