Last modified: 1 April 2021 at 04:56pm

Police have arrested a suspect and recovered a number of cycles stolen from a secure compound at Bootham Green off Newborough Street, York.

The theft happened at 8.20pm on 6 February 2021, and involved suspects wearing dark clothing taking bikes from the compound.

One suspect was located nearby on a Scott mountain bike and arrested. He has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

All of the bikes have been recovered and are in the process of being returned to their owners, with the exception of the Scott mountain bike. Officers would like to locate the owner of this bike.

Anyone who believes it is theirs, or has information that could assist the investigation, should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 935 Harl Pattison. You can also email Harl.Pattison@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12210054551.
