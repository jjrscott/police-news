Police are appealing for information following a road traffic collision in which a silver/grey Porsche collided with a boy who was crossing the road.

The incident occurred outside Jennyfield Styan Community Centre on Grantley Drive, Harrogate at 8.15pm on Thursday 5 February 2015. The boy sustained minor leg injuries.

The driver of the Porsche stopped to establish if the child was OK however left the scene without leaving any details.

Police are looking to trace the driver of the vehicle. Could anyone with any information, or anyone who witnessed the incident, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, press option 2 and ask for Sergeant Amy Hunter. Alternatively you can email at amy.hunter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

When passing on information please quote reference number NYP-05022015-0413.
