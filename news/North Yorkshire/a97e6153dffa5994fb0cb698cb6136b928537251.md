North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a garage burglary that occurred in Skipton.

It happened on Hayton Way, Skipton between the times of 4.30pm on Wednesday 15 of February 2017 and 8.15am on Thursday 16 of February 2017.

The suspects have forced entry into a single garage stealing a large amount of power tools and equipment.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
