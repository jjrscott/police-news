North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a smashed window that occurred on the Barrowcliffe estate in Scarborough.

The incident happened on Hatterboard Drive, Scarborough around 8.55pm on Tuesday 8 November 2016 and involved an offender throwing a piece of broken paving at a residents front window causing it to smash.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information to assist in identifying the offender. Moments after the incident a silver coloured car drove past the scene, traveling towards Briercliffe. Police would like to speak to the driver and any passengers of the car, to determine if they witnessed the offender running towards Wreyfield Drive.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should call 101, select option 2, and ask for Georgina Hanson. You can also email Georgina.Hanson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
