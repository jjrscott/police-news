North Yorkshire Police are appealing for information about the theft of a quad bike from a farm near Brimham Rocks Road in Nidderdale on Friday 5 May 2017.

The owner used the red Honda 500 quad bike for early morning stock checks before parking it in the farm yard. Some time between 6.30am and 8.30am thieves approached the yard unseen, unhitched the trailer and rode the bike away across a field towards Brimham Rocks Road.

The police are particularly keen to identify a red panel van (described as ex-Parcel Force) which had been parked in the layby opposite Brimham Hall Farm during the days leading up to this crime.

If anyone in the area of Brimham Rocks Road saw anything they think may be connected with this crime please contact North Yorkshire Police using the 101 non-emergency telephone number, choose option 1 and be ready to quote the reference 12170076952.
