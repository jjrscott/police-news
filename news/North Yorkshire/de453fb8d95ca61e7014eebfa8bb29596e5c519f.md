Officers are appealing for information following the theft of a bike in York.

Officers are appealing for information following the theft of a bike in York.

The incident occurred in the Picadilly area at around 4.30pm on Thursday, 28 February.

The suspect is described as very tall, of slim build and wearing a grey tracksuit. The bike which was stolen is a navy blue Voodoo Canso bike which was locked up to a bike rack near the bus stops and opposite to Primark.

Anyone who can assist with the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire police on 101 or by emailing joanne.shanley@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to provide information anonymously then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12190037454 when providing information.
