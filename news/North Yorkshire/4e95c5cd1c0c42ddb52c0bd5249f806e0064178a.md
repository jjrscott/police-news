North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a a house burglary in Leeming Bar.

The burglary occurred at a property on Harkness Close at sometime between 11am and 7pm on Monday 16 February 2015.

The offenders smashed a window in order to access and made a search of the property, stealing items of jewellery.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for DC Gary SmithOwen.

You can also email Gary.SmithOwen@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150026323
