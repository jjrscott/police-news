Police in Whitby are appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of candles from the B&M; store on Stainsacre Lane.

The incidents occurred on 28 and 29 August 2020.

Officers are requesting the public's help to identify the two men in the attached image.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jonathan Formstone. Or email jonathan.formstone@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you would prefer to make an anonymous report, contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555111 or go to www.crimestoppers-uk.org.

Please quote reference number 12200150975 when providing details about this appeal.
