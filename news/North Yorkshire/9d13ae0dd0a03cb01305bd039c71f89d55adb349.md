Three boys have been arrested in Sherburn in Elmet after being found to be in in possession of cannabis.

Officers stopped three 17-year old boys in Eversley Park in Sherburn in Elmet at 2.48pm on 17 August 2017. This was in response to an increase in patrols in the area by the local Neighbourhood Policing Team following concerns from local residents about possible drugs activity.

Two were arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply a class B drug. One was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of a Class B drug.

They have been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
