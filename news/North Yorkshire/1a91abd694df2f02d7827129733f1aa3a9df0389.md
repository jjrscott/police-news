Police have issued CCTV images of four people they would like to speak to in connection with the suspected fraudulent use of stolen credit and debit cards.

Some time between 13 June and 1 July 2016, an apartment was entered in Prince of Wales Mansions, Harrogate. Valuables including numerous credit and debit cards were stolen.

The cards were subsequently used in the Seacroft and Harehills areas of Leeds.

Officers would like to speak to four people, two men and two women, pictured on CCTV, as they may have important information that could assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises the people should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101. Please select option 2 and ask for PC Hayley Goodlad or Harrogate Police. Quote reference number 12160115989. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
