Police are appealing for information after a shop window was smashed on Yorkersgate, Malton.

Shortly after 1am on Saturday 30 December 2017 a large window at an office supplies store on Yorkersgate, Malton was smashed.

Enquiries are being conducted to determine the circumstances around the incident and police want to speak to any witnesses who were in the area at the time, or anyone who can assist the investigation.

Members of the public are asked to contact police via 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to Alan Robson. Alternatively you can email alan.robson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555111.

Please quote reference 12170231876.
