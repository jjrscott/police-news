North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision that occurred on the A19 near Thirsk.

It happened on the southbound carriageway between 12pm and 1pm on 11 December and involved a motorcyclist and a car. Fortunately, nobody was seriously injured.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision, or anyone who recalls seeing either of the vehicles prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible.

In particular, we are appealing for any dash cam footage of the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Peter Maw. You can also email peter.maw@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190229113.
