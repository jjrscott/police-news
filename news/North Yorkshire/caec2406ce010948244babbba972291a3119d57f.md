North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Burglary that happened in the early hours of Sunday 4 October 2015.

It happened at Harley Place, Cowling, between 1am and 6am on Sunday 4 October 2015. A property has been entered and a number of items stolen, including two Marinda mountain bikes.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the two bikes one of which is a ladies Marinda mountain bike in green and white and a men's Marinda mountain bike in blue and white.

A number of items which have little monatry value such as business documents have been taken from the address and we would ask that residents and dog walkers in the Cowling area to call the police if they come across any items dumped in gardens or nearby fields which they think may be out of place.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1771 Andrew Hanks You can also email andrew.hanks@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150175489.
