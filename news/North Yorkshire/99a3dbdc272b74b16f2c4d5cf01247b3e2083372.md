North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses , information and Dash Cam Footage about a vehicle that failed to stop for police that occurred in Snainton, Scarborough.

It happened on the A170 shortly before 7.20pm on Tuesday 2 March 2021 and involved a Silver/Grey vehicle failing to stop for police, before overtaking numerous vehicles and contravening a double white line system prior to entering the village of Ebberston.
