North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a the theft of a car that occurred in Sutton in Craven.

It happened at Crofters Mill at sometime between 11.30pm and 9.30am overnight between 30 December and 31 December, when a dark blue VW Golf was taken from the driveway of an address.

The theft took place on a quiet residential cul de sac and we are requesting any information in relation to suspicious behaviour by persons in the area over this time.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1771 Andrew Hanks. You can also email andrew.hanks@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150231559.
