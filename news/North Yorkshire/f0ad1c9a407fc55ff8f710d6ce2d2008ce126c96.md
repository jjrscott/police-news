North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident of criminal damage that occurred in High Bentham.

Between 1700hrs on Sunday 20th December and 09:00hrs on Monday 21st December 2015, graffiti was sprayed on the front door of a house on Wenning Avenue, High Bentham. Offensive images and the letters 'BTS' and 'YA' were sprayed on in gold spray paint.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information about who was responsible should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Barbara Parsons or email _snacraven@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150225863.
