North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred near Raskelf.

It happened at the Tholthorpe Business Park between midnight and 2am on Thursday 21 September 2017.

In particular, we are appealing for information about an Adelphi Diagnostic system and Asus laptop that were stolen.
Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Antony Kent . You can also email Antony.kent@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170169263
