Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:09am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary on Beckwith Crescent in Harrogate.

It happened between 5pm on Sunday 11 December and 4pm on Monday 12 December and involved someone entering a property and removing items to the value of up to £6000.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public’s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious individuals and/or vehicles in the area around the times of the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Sarah Danby. You can also email sarah.danby@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number: NYP-12122016-0320
