York City Police are appealing for the owner of a blue Sirrus Specialised bicyle which was stolen from Parliament Street in the city centre on Friday 1 March 2019, to contact them.

Police were able to recover the bike shortly it had been taken from a bike rack outside of Marks and Spencer on Friday 1st March 2019. A 44-year-old man has been arrested in connection with the incident.

If anybody recognises the bicycle, or has any information regarding the theft, please contact 101 and ask to speak to PC Oakes, quote reference 12190037881. You can also email Alicia.oakes@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
