A motorcyclist has died after being involved in a collision with a car at about 12.25pm on Sunday April 10 on the B1249 at the junction with an unclassified road to Kilham.

The motorcyclist, a 50-year-old East Yorkshire man, was riding a white BMW S1000XR motorcycle when he collided with a grey Ford Fiesta at the junction.

The motorcyclist was taken by air ambulance to Hull Royal Infirmary, but sadly died.

****

The car driver, a 72-year-old man, and his passenger, a 75-year-old woman, suffered minor injuries and were taken to Scarborough Hospital for treatment.

****

Witnesses are sought in relation to the collision. Anyone with information is asked to contact Humberside Police on 101 referring to log 251 of April 10.
