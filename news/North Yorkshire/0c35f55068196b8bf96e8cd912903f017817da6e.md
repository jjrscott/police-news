Last modified: 7 December 2020 at 12:53pm

Police in York have issued CCTV of a man they would like to speak to following a public order incident in McDonald's at Monks Cross.

It happened between midnight and 12.05am on 18 August 2020 and involved a man allegedly shouting homophobic language at another person within the restaurant.

Officers are asking members of the public to get in touch if they recognise the person in the image as they believe they will have information that will help the investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Philip Gobby. Or email philip.gobby@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12200143240 when passing on information.
