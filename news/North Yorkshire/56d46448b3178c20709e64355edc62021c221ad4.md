Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:08am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision between a cyclist and an unknown vehicle that occurred on Coach Road in Harrogate on Wednesday 17 May 2017.

At approximately 9.30am a collision occurred on Coach Road, Harrogate involving a cyclist and a dark coloured vehicle.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation and in particular, we are appealing for information about the car involved.

If you have any information which could assist the investigation, please contact police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Sheree Evans.

You can also email sheree.evans@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170085736
