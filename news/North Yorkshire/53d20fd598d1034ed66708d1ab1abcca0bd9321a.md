Last modified: 4 December 2017 at 05:21pm

Police in York have issued CCTV images of a man they want to identify following the theft of credit and debit cards from a hotel room in York.

It happened on 22 November 2017 at The Mount Royal Hotel when a man entered another guest’s hotel room and left with three credit and debit cards.

One of the cards was used the following day in an Argos store at 9.21am.

Officers are asking for the public’s help to identify the man shown in the images as they believe he may have important that could assist their investigation.

Anyone who has any information that could help to identify him or has any information about the theft is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Chris Bolton.

You can also email PC Bolton a Christopher.Bolton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote incident number 12170210212
