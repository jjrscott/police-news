Police are appealing for information and witnesses after a serious assault in York.

At about 1.30am on Sunday 25 September, officers were called to a report of an incident on Hull Road, York, where three men had been assaulted by a group of men. The incident took place between the Beeswing pub and the petrol station on Hull Road. One of the victims suffered a fractured jaw which required surgery.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident, or has any information about it, to contact them. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for York Serious Crime Team, or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Quote reference number 12160174587.
