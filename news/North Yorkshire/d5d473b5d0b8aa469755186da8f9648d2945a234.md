Officers are appealing for witnesses following a fail to stop between Duffield and Bubwith.

Officers are appealing for witnesses following a fail to stop between Duffield and Bubwith.

The collision occurred on the A163 at around 7pm on Wednesday, 27 February and involved a pedal cycle which was hit by a vehicle which failed to stop at the scene. The vehicle then continued to travel along the A163 towards North Duffield.

The cyclist, a man in his 50s, sustained a fractured clavicle as a result of the incident and was taken by ambulance to York District Hospital for treatment.

Anyone who can assist the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Traffic Constable Andrew Kirby. You can also email Andrew.kirby@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to provide information anonymously then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12190036774 when providing information.
