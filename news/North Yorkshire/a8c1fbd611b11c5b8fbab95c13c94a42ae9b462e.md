A wanted man has been arrested and will be returned to jail, after he was spotted by police while they were patrolling on bikes in York.

North Yorkshire Police officers spotted the man in the Burton Stone Lane area shortly before 1pm today.

The man, in his 20s, had been recalled to prison the previous day, after being released from a jail sentence for theft. He had failed to reside at an approved premises.

Recognising him, and knowing that he had been recalled to prison, officers arrested him. He will be passed to the prison authorities.
