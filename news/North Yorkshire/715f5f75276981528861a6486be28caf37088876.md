Update following appeal issued in June.

North Yorkshire Police is issuing an e-fit following an incident which occurred on Fourth Avenue, Tang Hal in York.

The incident occurred between 5.45pm and 5.50pm on Monday, 25 June when a man asked a 16-year-old girl to get in his car. At no point did the man make physical contact with the girl.

The girl was left shaken following the incident and officers are investigating the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

It is believed the man was driving a small grey saloon car.

If you believe you can identify the man in the e-fit or have any other information which may assist with the investigation please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Alternatively, if you can provide information but wish to remain anonymous then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12180113773 when providing information.
