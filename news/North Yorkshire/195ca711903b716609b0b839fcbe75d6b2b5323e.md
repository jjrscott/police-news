North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occured at Stan James Bookmakers on Fishergate in Boroughbridge which occurred in the early hours of 17th December 2015.

The offence happened at about 02:10hrs on 17th December 2015 and involved a dark coloured Ford Focus containing a male occupant.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about a dark coloured Ford Focus motor vehicle and the male driver of this car.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1720 Dave Furlong. You can also email davidfurlong@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150223022
