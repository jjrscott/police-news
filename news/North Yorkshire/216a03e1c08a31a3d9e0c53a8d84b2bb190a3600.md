Police are appealing for information and witnesses to an incident in Harrogate.

A collision occurred in slow-moving traffic on Skipton Road at about 3pm on Thursday 6 August 2015, when a grey Ford Focus collided with the back of a silver Suzuki Alto.

The two female occupants of the Ford got out, and one of them verbally abused the female driver of the Suzuki. They drove off without leaving their details.

The incident left the occupant of the Suzuki shaken, and with damage to the rear of her vehicle.

Police are appealing for witnesses to the incident to contact them. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for PC 1365 Stephen Ayres or extension 8910. Please quote reference 12150138773 when passing information in connection with this incident.

**10 August 2015**
