Police are trying to trace the owner of a mountain bike which was found in Scarborough.

The Trek 3500 bike was found near Glen Bridge on Monday 5 January 2015.

Officers are urging the rightful owner of the bike to come forward with proof of ownership so it can be returned to them.

If the bike belongs to you please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for PC Mike Holden or Scarborough police.

Alternatively, email mike.holden@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
