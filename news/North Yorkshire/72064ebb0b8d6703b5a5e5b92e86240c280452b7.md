A man has been charged following a serious assault in York on Saturday night (23 September 2017).

Police were called at around 9.40pm to the incident on Station Road near the Principal Hotel which resulted in a man in his sixties sustaining serious head injuries. He was taken to hospital where he remains.

A 26-year-old man and a woman in her sixties sustained minor injuries.

A 33-year-old man from North Shields was arrested and later charged with wounding and assault, He is due to appear at York Magistrates' Court on 26 October 2017.

Anyone who was in the area between 9.15pm and 9.45pm and witnessed the incident is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for York Serious Crime Team.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12170171019 when passing on information.
