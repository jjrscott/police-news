North Yorkshire Police are appealing for information to the whereabouts of a young woman seen in distress at around 1pm on Hob Moor, York.

The woman is described as around 20-years-old, approximately 5ft 6in tall and of medium build with blonde hair tied back into a pony tail. She was wearing a black coat with a hood with fur trim, black trousers and carrying a black handbag over her shoulder.

The woman was last seen walking in the direction of Windsor Garth, towards some new build houses.

Police are concerned for her safety and are requesting anyone who has seen a woman matching this description, who is seen to be unsteady on her feet or in distress to contact them.

Please quote reference NYP-07052017-0231.
