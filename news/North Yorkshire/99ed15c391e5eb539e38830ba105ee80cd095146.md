Police attended a property on Martins Court, York, at 6.30pm on Sunday 3 May 2020, following a report of a suspected cannabis grow there.

Two people, a 30-year-old man and a 28-year-old woman, were arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the production and supply of cannabis. They have been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Officers remained at the scene until about 2am to secure the premises and make it safe.
