Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a crash that occurred on Butts Lane, Lumby, near Sherburn-in-Elmet, at around 8.30am on Thursday 18 May 2017.

It involved a black Audi that was travelling from Lumby towards the A63 junction, which collided with a tree. The only occupant of the Audi a local man, aged 69 years-old, sustained serious injuries and was taken by ambulance to Leeds General Infirmary.

The road was closed until 12.30pm to allow specialist collision investigators time to examine the car and scene.

Police would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed the crash, or seen a black Audi in the village. The investigating team are particularly keen to identify a small panel van which was also believed to be in the area which may have some key information.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Richard Harrison.. You can also email Richard.Harrison@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170085343.
