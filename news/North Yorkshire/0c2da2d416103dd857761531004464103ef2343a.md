A 28-year-old man from Nottinghamshire has been charged with murder following an incident in Whitby last month.

The charge relates to an incident which occurred around 12.05am on Sunday, 24 February outside Porto Pizza, Windsor Terrace, in which a 47-year-old man was assaulted, taken to hospital but later died on Thursday, 28 February.

The man charged has been remanded in custody to appear at Scarborough Magistrates Court on Monday, 18 March.
