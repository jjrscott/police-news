Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident at Primrose Valley, near Filey.

Around 11.30pm on Monday 22 May 2017, police were called to attend Primrose Valley due to a report of a car being driven erratically.

Upon arrival, a man in his 20s had been detained by security staff. He was taken to hospital for treatment, where he remains at this time.

Police are appealing for any witnesses who saw a car being driven erratically around that time on the site, and anyone who may have witnessed a man being detained in the grounds of the park.

Please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Scarborough Serious Crime Team. Please quote reference number 12170088431.
