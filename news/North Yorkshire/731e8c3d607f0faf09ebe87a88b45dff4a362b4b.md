North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a garage burglary that occurred in Sheriff Hutton near York.

It happened between 1630hrs 17/05/15 and 1745hrs on 19/05/15 and a quantity of fishing gear was stolen from a garage in Laurels Garth. A number of items were stolen including rods, holdalls and tackle boxes.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Pc Louise Neville-Beck You can also email Louise.Neville-Beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150082809
