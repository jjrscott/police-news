North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage to a car that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Eastborough at approximately 3.30am on Monday 14 of March 2016. A white Skoda Fabia was parked, unattended near to the Blands Cliff junction when it was significantly damaged by an unknown individual.

Police are hopiing that the man and woman shown in the CCTV image may be able to assist with their enquiries. If you can identify these two people then please contact police.

Anyone with any information that could assist the investigation, or who saw anything suspicious in the area around the time of the incident, should contact police. Dial 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 257 Christie. You can also email Victoria.Christie@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160044568
