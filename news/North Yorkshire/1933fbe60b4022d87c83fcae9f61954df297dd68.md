North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from a motor vehicle that occurred in Stokesley.

It happened on Hebron Road between 14:30hrs and 22:00hrs on Thursday 17th September 2015 when the Fair was present and involved a red Skoda Citigo which had its passenger window smashed and a Skoda specific Sat Nav stolen from the dashboard, this will be useless to any other vehicle.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any persons or vehicles seen in the Hebron Road area].

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Chris Villiers. You can also email chris.villiers@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150164617.
