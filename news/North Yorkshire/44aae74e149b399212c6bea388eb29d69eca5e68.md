North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Linton On Ouse during which a distinctive scrambler motorbike was stolen.

The scrambler style bike is a 'TM 125â€². It is blue in colour and has the numerals '42' written on the rear. The bike also has yellow trim above the rear wheel and the name 'Kev' written near the number 42.

It was stolen between 8PM on 3rd October and 7AM on 4th October 2015 from an agricultural building near Linton On Ouse.

If any member of the public spots this bike either out and about or for sale then they are requested to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1 and pass information to the Force Control Room quoting incident number 12150175485.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
