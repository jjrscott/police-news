Last modified: 3 September 2020 at 09:50am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an alleged assault that occurred in Heworth in York.

It happened outside The Coffee House on East Parade in Heworth, at around 12pm on Thursday 27 August 2020 and involved an altercation between a man and woman with three young children and a group of adults. Two adults were assaulted resulting during the incident resulting in a woman sustaining minor injuries.

Police are appealing to anyone who was in the area at the time and witnessed the incident to contact them.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Kerrie Roworth. You can also email kerrie.roworth@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-27082020-0234.

A man and a woman have been arrested in connection with the incident and released while under investigation.
