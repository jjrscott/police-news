North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in York

It happened at Bartle Garth at around 3.30pm on Friday 8 June 2018 and involved a white man, aged in his late 40s or early 50s, around 5ft 8in tall, skinny build, with grey stubble and a wrinkled forehead. He was wearing a brown flat cap, a quilted jacket similar to a Barbour-style and stood next to a silver bike.

He entered the property and stole a Milwaukee battery drill from within and left the area.
