Police are appealing for information following a burglary at a property in Merchants Row, Scarborough.

An incident occurred on 3 August 2015, between 6pm and 6.30pm, when a woman entered a property and made an untidy search prior to leaving. She is believed to have spent about 20 minutes in the property, and nothing is believed to have been taken.

She is described as aged between 20 and 30 years old, with black hair and dressed all in black with a black-coloured Labrador.

Anyone with any information that could assist our investigation is asked to phone 101 and quote reference 12150134169.

**17 August 2015**
