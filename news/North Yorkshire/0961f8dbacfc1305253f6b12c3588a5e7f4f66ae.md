Officers from North Yorkshire Police's Rural Taskforce are appealing for information about a suspicious vehicle in the rural Whitby area.

A white pick-up truck with Truckman rear cab was sighted in Hinderwell and surrounding areas overnight between 24 April and 25 April 2016.

PC Tom Rollinson, Rural Taskforce officer for Scarborough and Whitby, said: "The vehicle is believed to have been involved in several thefts or attempted thefts, and so I would like to hear from anyone who saw it or anything suspicious in the area."

Anyone with any information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Tom Rollinson. You can also email tom.rollinson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160071358.

You can follow the work of North Yorkshire Police's new Rural Taskforce on Twitter at @NYPRuralTF.
