North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an arson that occurred just outside Leyburn.

It happened at a site on Wensley Road in the early hours of Sunday 4 March 2018, where a Haulotte Cherry Picker and an Ifor Williams Cattle Trailer were set alight, causing a significant amount of damage to the value of £17,000.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for James Bowie. You can also email james.bowie@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180038571.
