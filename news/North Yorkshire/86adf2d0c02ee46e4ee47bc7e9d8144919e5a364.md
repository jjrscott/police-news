North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a man acting suspiciously in Norton, Malton.

It happened at between 7.30pm and 7.45pm on Sunday 20 August 2017. It was reported that a man was acting suspiciously in the woods in the Westfield way and Scarborough Road areas of Norton. He was shouting and waving what is believed could have been a knife.

The man was described as having rough looking grey hair and beard. He was wearing a ripped grey t-shirt and dark trousers.
