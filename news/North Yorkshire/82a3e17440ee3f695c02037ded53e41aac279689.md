Police have appealed for information after an amount of cash was found in Selby.

The cash, which is in the hundreds of pounds, was found by officers outside Selby Police Station in Portholme Road on Tuesday 13 October.

Police are investigating where the cash came from and are appealing for anyone who has any information to contact them.

Anyone with information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Selby Police. Alternatively, you can pass information to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150181733 in connection with this investigation.

**15 October 2015**
