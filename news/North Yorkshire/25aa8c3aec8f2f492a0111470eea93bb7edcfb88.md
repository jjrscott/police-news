North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred in Gilling East

It happened overnight 04/05/16 and 05/05/16 at a field on the edge of Oswaldkirk - Gilling East when 20 sheep gates, 3 sheep feeders, 3 cattle feeders and general fabrication was stolen.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Pc 70 louise Neville-Beck. You can also email louise.neville@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number nyp-05/05/2016-0601.
