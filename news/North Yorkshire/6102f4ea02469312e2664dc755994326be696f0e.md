North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened near Matalan on St Thomas Street at approimately 1:30am on 27 September 2015 and involved a 34 year old man being assaulted.

There are believed to be three suspects described as white men aged about 18 and wearing dark clothing.

The victim received a deep cut to the head and was taken to Scarborough Hospital.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 596 Gillon. You can also email leah.gillon@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150170674
