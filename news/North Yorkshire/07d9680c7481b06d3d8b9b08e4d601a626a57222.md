North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a disturbance that occurred in Appleton Le Moors.

It happened at The Moors Inn at about 10pm on Friday 6 July and involved a small group of men who are believed to be from the local area attending the location and assaulting a number of customers before leaving prior to police arrival.

One man was treated at the scene and a second man was taken to hospital for treatment.
