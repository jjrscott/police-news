North Yorkshire Police is asking for the public’s help identify a person shown on a CCTV image.

Officers want to talk to the suspect about an incident that took place on Thursday 26 January at the Whitefriars Petrol Station in Settle.

At 5.48pm the driver of a silver Mercedes filled up with £58 worth of fuel and left without making any attempt to pay for it.

If you have any information which could help us to identify the man then please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for David Burgess or email David.Burgess@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote incident number 12170017079.
