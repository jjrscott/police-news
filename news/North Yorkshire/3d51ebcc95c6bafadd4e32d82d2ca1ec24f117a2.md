Police investigating an assault near to the Fulford Arms pub on Fulford Road in York on 19 February 2016, have charged a man with wounding with intent.

The 58-year-old man who was arrested on Monday (1 March), was charged yesterday and remanded in custody to appear at York Magistrates' Court (sitting at Leeds) today (3 March 2016).

Police appealed for potential, vital witnesses to come forward who reported an assault to pub staff. They have now been traced.

The victim remains in York District Hospital in a serious condition.
