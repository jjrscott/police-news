North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred in Boots at St James Retail Park, Knaresborough.

It happened on Thursday 20 September 2018 between 12.30pm and 1pm, and involved a man and a woman entering the store and leaving with bags of cosmetics. They left in a grey Skoda estate car which was stopped by police.

A man and a woman were arrested and one man made off. The arrested man has since been released with no further action - the arrested woman has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the theft in Boots and if anyone witnessed the offence.

The man who made off from police is described as white, 6ft tall, medium build, dark hair, wearing a green tracksuit and a cap.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Daniel Crawford. You can also email Daniel.Crawford@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180176105.
