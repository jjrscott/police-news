Police are urging the public to share information after a cat found dead with severe head injuries.

The pet was found dead on the bonnet of a parked vehicle on Monkton Road, York.

It was let out by its owner, who lives nearby, at 1.00am on Sunday (1 October).

A vet who examined the cat concluded it had suffered a massive head trauma that is not consistent with a traffic collision.

It was reported to police at 11.40am the same day (Sunday).

Anyone with information is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Ruth Atkins.

You can also email PC Atkins at Ruth.Atkins@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170175999.
