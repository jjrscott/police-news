Police are appealing for information following a burglary in Scarborough on Monday 7 November 2016.

The burglary took place between 2.40pm and 4.30pm on Hampton Road, Scarborough.

The owner of the property has been out with family at the time.

Offenders gained entry and stole a number of items which include a retirement watch and jewellery of significant sentimental value. A large jar containing savings for a special family trip has also been taken. It is not known at this time the identity of the offenders.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Nicholas Patrick. You can also email Nicholas.Patrick566@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160202541
