North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred at around 8pm on Sunday 13 December at Skipton Rubber Mouldings Ltd, Snaygill Industrial Estate, Skipton.

Offenders have forced entry through some locked gates and attended in the rear yard of the premises. Once there they have stolen 29 large plastic storage containers (as per attached photograph).

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for any information about a large white Luton style van that was used by the suspects at the scene.

We are also appealing for any information about the current whereabouts of the above mentioned stolen storage containers.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Gasson. You can also email david.gasson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150221218.
