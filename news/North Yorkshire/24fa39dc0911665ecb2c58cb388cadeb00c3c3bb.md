North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after suspects threw a brick through the bedroom window of a bungalow.

It occurred at approximately 11:26pm on Tuesday 29 May 2018 at an address on Shireburn Grove, Selby.

Officers are appealing to anyone who either witnessed the incident or has any information about the people responsible to contact them.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask Lucy Hammond. You can also email lucy.hammond@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180094783.
