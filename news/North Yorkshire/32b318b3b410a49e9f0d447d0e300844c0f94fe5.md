Police are appealing for information to help identify a man believed to be involved in incidents of exposure.

Officers are investigating an incident along the river Wharfe near the viaduct in Tadcaster and Church Hill, Selby. In both incidents, the description of the man has been the same. Both incidents happened on Monday 19 June 2017, however there have been other reports of this nature recently along the river in Tadcaster.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Steve Shaw.

Alternatively, contact Crimestopppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170107029 when passing on any information.
