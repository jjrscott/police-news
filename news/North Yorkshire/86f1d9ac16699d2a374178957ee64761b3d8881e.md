Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident in King Street, York, in the early hours of Monday 17 April.

Officers attended the scene at 12.40am. The incident involved a group of women, one of whom, aged in her 20s, was assaulted and dragged to the ground. Her injuries required hospital treatment.

A 23-year-old woman from Scarborough was arrested on suspicion of affray and released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Anyone who saw the incident, or has any information about it, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Please dial 101, select option 2 and and ask for DC Tracey Pearson, DS Matt Dodson or the York Investigation Hub. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12170065113 when passing information.
