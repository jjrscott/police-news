North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Commercial Street between 2000hrs on Sunday 18th October 2015 and 0630hrs on Monday 19th October 2015 and involved two bicycles being stolen from a shed at the rear of a property.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone seen acting suspiciously or anyone who has recently purchased a bike similar to the attached photographs.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Daniel Thompson. You can also email daniel.thompson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150185103.
