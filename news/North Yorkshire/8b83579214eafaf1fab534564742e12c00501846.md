North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a hate crime that occurred on County Bridge between Malton and Norton.

At around 10:00am on Saturday 15 September 2018 a blind man was verbally abused in regards to his disability, causing him distress.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information to assist us in locating the offender, who is described as having a local accent and had a child with him. They were both riding bicycles.

Police would also like to speak to a possible witness, who spoke to the victim after the altercation occurred. The witness is described as having a foreign accent and said that he recognised the offender.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1486 Adam Blackburn. You can also email adam.blackburn@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk - please quote reference 12180174070.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
