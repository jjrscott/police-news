Last modified: 7 November 2017 at 09:46am

Police in York have issued CCTV images of a man they want to identify following the theft of a poppy appeal box from the foyer of the Premier Inn on Blossom Street.

The box was taken on the morning of Thursday 2 November 2017 and contained an unknown amount of cash.

Officers are asking for the public's help to identify the man shown in the images.

Anyone who has any information that could help to identify him or has any information about the theft is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Emily Clark .

You can also email PC Clark at Emily.Clark@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote incident number 12170197218
