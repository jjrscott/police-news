North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision that occurred on the A19 near Thirkelby, south of Thirsk,.

The collision **** occurred just before 9pm on Saturday 22 July 2017 and involved a Blue VW Passat, which was travelling towards York, and a black VW Golf , travelling towards Thirsk.

A man from the Saltburn area sustained slight injuries for which he was treated and later discharged from James Cook Hospital.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for anyone who witnessed the two vehicles prior to the collision to contact police. In particular, we would also like to speak to the occupants of an unidentified car which was travelling towards York which is believed to have been in the process of being overtaken by the VW Passat car.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Mark Thompson. You can also email Mark.Thompson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170129396.
