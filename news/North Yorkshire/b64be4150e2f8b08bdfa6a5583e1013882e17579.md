Allegations were reported to the police that the man, who was walking the dog at the time, was seen hitting, dragging and shouting at the dog near Thirsk swimming pool at around 7.15pm on the night of Monday 5 January 2015.

View image

Police need to speak to the man and are asking anyone who recognizes him from the CCTV images to contact them. He was riding a bicycle and had the dog on a lead at the time.

The dog is described as small and brown.

Anyone who can help identify the person shown in the CCTV is asked to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Thirsk police station.

If you prefer to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12150002545 when passing on information.

6 January 2015
