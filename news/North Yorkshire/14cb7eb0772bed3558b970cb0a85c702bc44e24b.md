North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Scarborough.

The burglary took place at a house on Commercial Street in Scarborough at some time between 09:00 hours and 10:40 hours on 18th October 2015 and involved someone entering a home address and removing two bags and an amount of money whilst the occupant of the address was elsewhere in the house.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance with any help or information as to the identity of the person responsible.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Gary Evans. You can also email Gary.Evans@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150184646.
