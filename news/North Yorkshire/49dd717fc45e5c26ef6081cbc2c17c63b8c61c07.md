Last modified: 24 January 2018 at 09:32am

Police in Scarborough have arrested three men aged 20, 26, and 31, after a man was found on Valley Road with stab wounds just after 11.17am on 23 January 2018.

The man, 28, has been taken to hospital for treatment. An investigation has been launched and police remain at the scene.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, and pass information to the Force Control Room.

Please quote incident number 144 of 23 January.

You can also pass information anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

**UPDATE 5.23pm 23 January 2018**

The injured man remains in hospital and the arrested men remain in police custody at this time.

**UPDATE 9.30am 24 January 2018**

The three men arrested in connection with the incident have been released while under investigation. Police enquiries are continuing.

The injured man remains in hospital in a serious condition.
