North Yorkshire Police is appealing for recent burglary victims to come forward if they recognise any of these items (pictured).

The items include cufflinks, watches, a silver engraved tray, a metal tin and a Superdry jacket (pictured) and they were recovered following the search of a house in the Craven area.

Officers are investigating whether the items are stolen and are appealing for any recent burglary victims to come forward if they believe any of the items could be theirs.

If you recognise any of the items pictured, or if you have any information that could help with the investigation, please email Andrew.Palmer@northyorkshire.police.uk. You can also call North Yorkshire Police on 101 and pass information on to the Force Control Room.

Please quote reference number 12190018573 when providing any information.
