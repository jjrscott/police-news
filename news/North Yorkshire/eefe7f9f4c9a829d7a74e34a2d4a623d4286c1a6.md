Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:36am

Police are appealing for witnesses and information about an assault which took place around 2.15pm - 2.30pm on The Village, Haxby, on Thursday 12 July 2018.

At that time, a woman in an electric wheelchair and her husband were verbally abused by two men in the street. One of the men then pushed the woman.

The two men had been moving a broken down vehicle along the road in the direction of Wigginton just beforehand, and then moved the vehicle in that direction again immediately after the assault.

In particular, officers are appealing for two women who stopped and spoke to the victim to come forward and assist with police enquiries. A third man also drove past, after the assault, and stopped to assist the suspects move their vehicle down the road. That man was driving a forest-green Fiesta-style car and could also assist officers with the investigation.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and quote 12180126444.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
