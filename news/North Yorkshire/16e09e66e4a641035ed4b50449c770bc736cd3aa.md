Police in York are appealing for witnesses, CCTV and dash-cam footage following a criminal damage incident at a bus stop in Heslington.

It happened on Field Lane between 1am and 1.45am on Saturday 16 January 2021.

Numerous panes of glass were smashed at the bus stop.

If you can help please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and quote reference 12210024359. Or email 001282@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
