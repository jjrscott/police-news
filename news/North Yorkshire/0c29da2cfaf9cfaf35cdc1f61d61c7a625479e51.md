Last modified: 9 May 2018 at 10:40am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Thornton Dale.

Between 5pm on 6 April and 9am on 7 April 2018, unknown offenders have smashed the windows of two shops on Maltongate. They have gained entry and stole cash.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jane Jones. You can also email jane.jones@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180058837.
