Police in the Scarborough area are appealing to taxi drivers who may have transported a couple just before 6.30pm on Thursday 18 February 2016.

A 30-year-old woman was found in a distressed state by police on Valley Road following what officers believe was a domestic incident.

It is believed the woman had been in a taxi with her partner prior to being found by the police.

Officer are appealing to any taxi drivers who recall transporting a couple, possibly from the Eastfield area during the relevant time period.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact the police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Rosie Rogers. You can also email rosie.rogers@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160029539.
