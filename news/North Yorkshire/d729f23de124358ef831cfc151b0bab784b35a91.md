Last modified: 25 March 2019 at 10:47am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road traffic collision in Malton.

Police received reports of a 4×4-style vehicle colliding with two pedestrians on Norton Road between 10.35am and 10.45am on Thursday.

No serious injuries were sustained. We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Louise Neville-Beck. You can also email louise.neville-beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-21032019-0174.
