North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses after a member of staff was assaulted at a shop in York.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses after a member of staff was assaulted at a shop in York.

The incident occurred at around 6.55pm on Monday, 25 February at the Morrisons store on Foss Island.

The offender is described as aged in her late 30s/early 40s, of slender build, with a pale complexion and mousey coloured hair. She was wearing a light coloured waist length hooded jacket and carrying a handbag and rucksack.

Anyone who can assist with the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Shanley. You can also email joanne.shanley@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12190035283 when providing information.
