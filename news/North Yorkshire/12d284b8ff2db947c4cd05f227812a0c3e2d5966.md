North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a disturbance in the street in Scarborough.

It happened about 9pm on Thursday 28 May2015 at Belvedere Place, Scarborough,

A group of men were seen threatening each other and acting in a disorderly manner.

Three men were arrested at the time by the police. Two of the men are currently on bail pending further enquiries.

We are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul Bray.

You can also email paul.bray@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150088540 when passing information about this incident.
