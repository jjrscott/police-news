A man has died after falling from a window on Falsgrave Road in Scarborough.

It happened at around 10.20pm on Saturday 19 September.

The victim, a 26 year-old man, was taken to hospital where he sadly died.

Enquiries are ongoing to establish the circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with any information that can assist the investigation is asked to email CIDScarborough&Ryedale@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or call North Yorkshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 12200165083
