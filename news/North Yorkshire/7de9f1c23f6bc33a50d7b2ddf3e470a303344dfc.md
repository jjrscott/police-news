Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:37am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault in the Clifton area of York.

A man in his 30s was walking his dog when he was assaulted by three youths on bicycles.

It happened on Crombie Avenue near to Lady Mill Garth at 6.30pm on Wednesday (11 January 2017).

The victim sustained head injuries which, fortunately, were not believed to be serious. He did not know his attackers.

The ringleader is described as a white male, 15 to 16 years old, with short dark hair and dark eyebrows. All three offenders were on bikes and were wearing dark tracksuits.

Anyone who knows the identity of the attackers or has information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for John Armstrong (collar number 291). You can also email John.Armstrong291@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170006010.
