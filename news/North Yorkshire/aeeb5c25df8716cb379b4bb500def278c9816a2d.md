Police are appealing for information to help identify the man shown in the CCTV images.

Officers want to speak to the man in connection with an assault which happened in Skipton.

The incident happened on Newmarket Street 3.45 am on Saturday 10 December 2016. An 18 year old man was assaulted in an unprovoked attack. The victim was punched in the face by another man, sustaining a broken jaw.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who can identify the man in the CCTV is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press and ask to speak to PC Martin Carson or email martin.carson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160222573 when passing on information.
