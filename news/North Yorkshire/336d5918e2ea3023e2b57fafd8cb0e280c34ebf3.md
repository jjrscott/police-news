North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a robbery that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Waterhouse Lane outside TK Maxx at around 2:30pm on Tuesday 1 August 2017. A 57 year old man was walking past TK Maxx when he was approached by two men who engaged him in conversation before pushing him to the ground and stealing property from his pockets.

Both offenders then made off in the direction of Westborough.
