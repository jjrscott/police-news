North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident involving a dog in Reighton Gap near Filey.

It happened on the footway leading past the Beach Cafe heading away from the beach and involved a man with a young son who were walking their spaniel.

During the incident the spaniel was bitten by another dog, leaving a puncture wound.

The suspect dog is described as similar to the spaniel in size but stockier and black and white in colour, and is reported as having been in company with four adults and a child.

The incident happened at 4pm on Saturday, 4 July 2020 but was not reported to police until some weeks later.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the circumstances leading to the dog bite and the identity of those involved..
