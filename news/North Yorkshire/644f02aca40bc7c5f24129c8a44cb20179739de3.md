Officers are asking for witnesses or dashcam footage after entry was forced to a property on Leadhall Lane

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a residential property on Leadhall Lane, Harrogate was broken into between 8.50am and 4.20pm on Wednesday 11 March 2020.

Due to the high volume of traffic on Leadhall Lane, officers are appealing for anyone who might have seen anything suspicious or might have dashcam footage of the road between the above times, particularly around the area of Leadhall Crescent.

If you can help with information or dashcam footage please contact: Dean.Barrett@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or call 101, press option 2 and ask for Dean Barrett.

Please quote reference number: 12200042425
