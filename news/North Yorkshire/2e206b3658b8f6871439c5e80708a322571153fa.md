North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a sneak in burglary in the Scarborough area.

The incident happened sometime between 1am and 7am hours on Tuesday 1 September 2015. A handbag was stolen from a property in Givendale Road in Scarborough.

This appears to be a sneak in burglary where the offender/s have entered through an insecure door.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for neighbours to come forward if they have seen or heard anything unusual during the times indicated.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Scarborough investigation hub.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150154046

Wednesday 2 September 2015.
