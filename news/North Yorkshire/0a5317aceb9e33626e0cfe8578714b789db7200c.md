Last modified: 9 May 2018 at 10:46am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of animal medication from a unit on the Sidings Industrial Estate in Settle.

The medication consisted of a large amount of sheep and horse wormers, and sheep antibiotics which were taken from the unit on the Sidings Industrial Estate, Settle, on Friday 9 September.

If you are offered any medication that doesn't come from your usual reputable source or you have any other information regarding the theft, please contact the police. Please also bear in mind that the antibiotics that have been taken require refrigeration so any you buy from a non-official source may not be effective if they haven't been refrigerated.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jayne Carpenter. You can also email snacraven@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160164546.
