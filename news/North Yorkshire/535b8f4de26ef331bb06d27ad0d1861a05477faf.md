North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred at Aston's Pharmacy in Scarborough.

It is believed to have happened around 3am on Monday 17 February 2020 when the front window of the pharmacy on Gladstone Road was smashed and items, including several charity boxes, were taken from within.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help identify who may be responsible.

Investigating officer, PC Liam Cromack, said: "I am appealing for anyone who was in the area during the early hours of the morning, and who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in that area, to come forward. Even the smallest piece of information could be of help."

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Liam Cromack. You can also email liam.cromack@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200027948.
