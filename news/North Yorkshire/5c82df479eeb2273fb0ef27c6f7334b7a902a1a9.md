North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information regarding the theft of a horsebox in Oulston.

It happened between quarter-past midnight hours and 5.45am on Friday 13 May 2016.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help identify the offenders responsible.

In particular, we are appealing for information from members of the public who may have seen the vehicle being driven away from the area during the times above.

The vehicle is a black coloured Ford Iveco with an aluminium coloured horsebox on the back.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jonathan Harrison. You can also email jonathan.harrison@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160083050.
