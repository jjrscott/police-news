Last modified: 21 November 2018 at 12:48pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a minor collision that occurred on Jockey Lane at Monks Cross, York.

It happened at about 6.45pm on 10 November 2018 and involved a cyclist who has come off his cycle by an unknown means at this time.

The cyclist was taken to hospital via ambulance but he received only minor injuries.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing any vehicle or vehicles prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Sophie Simpson. You can also email sophie.simpson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP- 10112018-0424.
