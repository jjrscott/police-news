North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about two burglaries that occurred in Embsay near Skipton.

Both happened on Mill Holme Rise, Embsay between 7 December 2016 and the 8 December 2016 and involved offenders removing valuables from each property.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 352 MIchael Smith at Skipton Police Station. You can also email Michael.Smith352@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160221217
