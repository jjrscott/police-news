The van was hit by a stolen red Mazda 3 in Wigginton, York, in the early hours of Tuesday 14 February 2017.

The driver of the stolen car did not stop at the scene, and the police are now trying to trace the van and driver.

A 33 year old man from Great Ouseburn has been arrested in connection with the stolen Mazda. He has been released on bail pending further enquiries.

If you are the driver of the van hit by the Mazda in Wigginton, or have any information, please contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Nicola Pattinson.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170025618 when passing on any information.
