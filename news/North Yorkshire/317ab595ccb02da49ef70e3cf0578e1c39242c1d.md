North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Tesco store in York.

It happened in the store on Low Ousegate at 8pm on Saturday 15 of July 2017 and involved a man stealing an item from behind the serving counter.

The man is described as medium build with brown hair.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Mark Cole. You can also email mark.cole@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170124671
