Police investigating the theft of alcohol from a shop in Skipton have issued CCTV images of a number of men they would like to speak to.

On three occasions – Tuesday 24 January, Wednesday 8 February and Tuesday 14 February 2017 – suspects have stolen from the Marks & Spencer Simply Foods store in Thanets Yard, Skipton.

Three suspects are believed to have been involved, and up to three bottles of spirits were taken on each occasion.

Police have issued CCTV stills of a number of men they would like to speak to in connection with the thefts, as they may have important information that could assist the investigation.

If you recognise any of the men pictured on the CCTV, please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Adam Steventon or Skipton Police, or email adam.steventon@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12170025894.
