Police in York are appealing for a man and woman who helped a man push a car into Clifton Bingo Club car park to come forward.

The incident happened at around 5.30am on Tuesday 5 April 2016 when the two passers-by helped push the red Volvo S40, unaware that it had been reported as stolen from the Malton area the previous day.

Officers are appealing for the two people to come forward as they could have vital information that will help their investigation.

If you believe you are the people we want to trace, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Detective Constable Jezz Pratt. Or email Jeremy.pratt@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12160058614.
