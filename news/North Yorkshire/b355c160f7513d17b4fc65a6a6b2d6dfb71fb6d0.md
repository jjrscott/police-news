North Yorkshire Police is appealing to locate the owner of a Yamaha Kodiac Quad Bike.

The bike was loacted in the Stockton area of Cleveland partially burnt out on Friday 10th April 2015.

The images of this quad show a distinctive devil sticker attached to one of the wheel arches.

Anyone with information that could assist in the identification of the owner of the quad should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Melanie Smith.

You can also emailmelanie.smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
