North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following spate of damage to vehicles in Scarborough.

Vehicles parked on Trinity Road, Westbourne Grove, Oriel Crescent and Cromwell Terrace were damaged overnight on Wednesday 17th August. In total nearly thirty vehicles have received damage to their tyres and windscreens.
