Police are appealing for information after a man was arrested on suspicion of stealing a beer keg in York.

Officers from York police and North Yorkshire Police's Rural Taskforce located a man with a barrel of Black Sheep Ale on the riverside opposite Rowntree Park at about midday today.

He was unable to account for how he came to be in possession of it, and was subsequently arrested on suspicion of theft.

Police are appealing for witnesses to help trace the rightful owner of the barrel, and are asking anyone with information to get in touch. Dial 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Adam Lindsey, or email adam.lindsey@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Please quote reference number 12160069579.

The man, aged 52 and from Darlington, remains in custody at this time.
