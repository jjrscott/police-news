North Yorkshire Police is investigating a robbery after a large amount of money was stolen as security staff filled up a cash machine in Sherburn in Elmet this morning.

The incident was reported at 8.18am on 21 August at the Cooperative Store on Finkle Street after two men wearing balaclavas and carrying knives approached security staff. The men left the scene in a white vehicle.

No-one was injured during the incident.

Anyone who was in the area at the time and witnessed the incident is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 104 of 21 August.
