Police are appealing for information to help identify a man following a theft from Morrisons in York.

Officers want to speak to the man shown in the CCTV in connection with the incident, which happened at around 10.30am on 20 October 2017.

A man entered Morrisons on Foss Island Retail Park in York, filled a trolley with groceries and left the store without making any attempt to pay.

Anyone who can help identify the man is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 ans ask to speak to PC Nikki Jackson or email nikki.jackson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170188234 when passing on any information.
