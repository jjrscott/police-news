North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Commercial Burglary that occurred in Thirsk.

It happened at the Thirsk Squash Club, Newsham Road in Thirsk between 8.30pm Friday 11 December and 7.30am Saturday 12 December 2015.

It is believed that this was an act of general and unnecessary vandalism by youths, whereby vital fire fighting equipment was damaged.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about who might be responsible for this act that resulted in the Club being closed for a short time.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andy Grainge. You can also email andy.grainge@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150220041.

Date 12 June 2015.
