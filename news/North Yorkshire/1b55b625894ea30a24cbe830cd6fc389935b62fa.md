North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred about 7:00pm on Wednesday 28 March within Blue Dolphin Holiday Park with three males involved.

Police are investigating an alleged assault at the Blue Dolphin Holiday Park in Filey.

The incident happened near to the reception area at around 7pm on Wednesday 28 March 2018.

It involved three local men aged 29, 29 and 31.

The 31-year-old man and one of the 29-year-old men were arrested and released under investigation following questioning.

Enquiries are ongoing to establish the circumstances surrounding the incident.

Witnesses or anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Colin Irvine. You can also email colin.irvine@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180053006.
