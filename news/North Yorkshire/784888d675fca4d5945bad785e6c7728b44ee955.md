Police are appealing for information regarding the burglary of a property on Hurrs Road in Skipton.

Police are appealing for information regarding the burglary of a property on Hurrs Road in Skipton.

At some point overnight on Tuesday 14 March 2017 to Wednesday 15 March 2017, thieves gained entry to the property via rear patio doors. They then went on to steal a quantity of cash and jewellery.

Police are appealing for any witnesses who saw anything suspicious in the area, or anyone who has any information which would assist the investigation to contact them on 101 and ask to speak to PC Caroline Watson. Please quote reference number 12170043608.

Alternatively you can email C _aroline.Watson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
