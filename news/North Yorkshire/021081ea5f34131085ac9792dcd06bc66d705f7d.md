North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a man was assaulted

Shortly before 8pm on 21 November 2019 a man was found in the road on Westbourne Grove, Scarborough, having suffered head and facial injuries as a result of being assaulted.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information or anyone who may have witnessed the assault to help us establish the full circumstances of this incident.

In particular, we would like to speak to a woman described as wearing a red coat who may have witnessed the incident but left the scene before police arrived.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police by calling 101. If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190214427.
