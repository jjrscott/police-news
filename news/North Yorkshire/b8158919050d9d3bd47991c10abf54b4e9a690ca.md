North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred at Le Raj restaurant, Ousegate, Selby between 11.30pm on 22 February and 5.30pm on 23 February 2016.

Damage was caused to the front door of the premises and entry gained via the back of the building.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Alison Baker. You can also email alison.baker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160033151.
