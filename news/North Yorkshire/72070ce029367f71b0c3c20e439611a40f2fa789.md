North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempt burglary that occurred in Copmanthorpe

It happened on St Nicholas Crescent around 10pm on Tuesday 21 April 2020 and involved one or possibly two offenders shining torches through a side window which was slightly opened. No access was gained but it is believed this was their intention

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone seen in the area behaving suspiciously or any vehicles which appeared suspicious at, or leading up to that time

With the nice weather we are urging all residents to take care to lock and secure windows overnight to improve the level of home security

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Dave Ellison. You can also email david.ellison@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
