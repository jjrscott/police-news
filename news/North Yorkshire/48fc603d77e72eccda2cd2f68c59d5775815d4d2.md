North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses after an altercation on the dancefloor of a pub in Malton

The incident has occurred at the Gate Inn at around 12.45am on Saturday (26 January).

A man in his 20s from the Malton area has been arrested on suspicion of disorder offences and released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Police believe nobody was injured.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information that could help the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Jack Waterton. You can also email PC Waterton at jack.waterton294@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12190015336 when sharing information.
