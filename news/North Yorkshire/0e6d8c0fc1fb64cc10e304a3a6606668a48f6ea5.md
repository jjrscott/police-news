North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision that occurred near to the village of Ingleton

It happened on Bentham Road close to Ingleton village at 1025hrs on Sunday 20th September and involved a pedal cycle and a Red BMW.

The cyclist suffered a serious arm injury was taken to Lancaster Royal Infirmary for treatment before being released with a cast.

Emergency services worked to examine the scene and enquiries are ongoing to identify the Red BMW involved which did not stop at the scene.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the pedal cyclist or vehicle prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the Red BMW and anyone who may have information as to the identity of the driver or dashcam footage from BENTHAM ROAD between 10am and 10.25am.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andy Ingram . You can also email Andrew.Ingram@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200165444
