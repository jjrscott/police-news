Last modified: 26 March 2021 at 11:33am

Police are appealing for witnesses or dashcam footage following a collision which occurred on the A59 Skipton near to the Broughton Road junction.

The incident happened at approximately 6.30am on Wednesday 3 March 2021 and involved a White Ford Transit Van overtaking a HGV near to the Broughton Road Junction, when the van caused damage to the HGV. Both the HGV and the Van were driving on the A59 heading in the direction of the Little Chef roundabout (The A59/A65 Roundabout). It has been reported that the white van has driven on the wrong side of the road, head on into oncoming traffic.

Police are appealing for any witnesses to this collision and specifically if you were the driver of the vehicle which the van was driving head on towards to come forwards. Any dashcam footage would also be appreciated.

The safety of road users is of paramount importance to North Yorkshire Police so we ask that anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Jon Perrett. You can also email Jon.Perrett@northyorkshire.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12210070245.
