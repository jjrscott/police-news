Following a report by a member of the public, officers located the remains of an adult body at a property on Bondgate in Helmsley at around 6pm on Tuesday 25 September 2018.

The death is currently being treated as unexplained and enquiries are ongoing to establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

A 75 year-old woman, 46 year-old man and 51 year-old woman who were arrested in connection with the incident, have all since been released on conditional bail.

North Yorkshire Police reference 12180179672
