Police are appealing for information to help identify the man In the CCTV images.

View image

The man was in possession of what is believed to be a stolen storenet radio when he attended Kans nightclub in Selby, at around 3am on 15 October 2016. The radio was seized by door staff at the club.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Lucy Hammond. Alternatively, you can email lucy.hammond@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12160188202 when passing on any information.

Alternatively, Crimestopppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.
