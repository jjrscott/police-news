North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred in Scarborough.

An electro-plated 18 carat gold Raymond Weil Watch was stolen from the St. Catherine's Hospice Charity Shop, Falsgrave Road, Scarborough. It happened sometime betwen Monday 29 February and Wednesday 2 March 2016.

The watch serial number is 5514-2 and was fitted with a new Swiss battery serial number 371.

Anyone with information about the watch or who has any information that could assist the investigation should contact us. Dial 101, select option 2, and ask for PC423 MacFarlane. You can also email andrew.macfarlane@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160037204.
