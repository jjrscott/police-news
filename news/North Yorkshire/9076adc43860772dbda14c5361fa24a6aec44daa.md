North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a house burglary that occurred in North Duffield, Selby.

It happened at 27 Green Lane, North Duffield, Selby some time between September and November 2015 and involved a sneak in burglary during which a quantity of jewellery and medals were stolen.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the stolen property - which includes a pocket watch and medals - and its current location

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Julie Carter. You can also email Julie.Carter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150198114.
