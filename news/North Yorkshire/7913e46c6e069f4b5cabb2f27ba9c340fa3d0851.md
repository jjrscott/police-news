North Yorkshire Police is appealing for identification of a female responsible for a racially aggravated assault which took place in Scarborough town centre on the evening of 8 July 2016.

View image

The incident took place in a town centre pub at just before 11.00pm on Friday 8 July 2016. A 29 year old local male was punched in the face by a female, after being racially abused.

Police have obtained images of the female responsible and are keen to identify her.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Belinda Williamson. You can also email belinda.williamson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160121973
