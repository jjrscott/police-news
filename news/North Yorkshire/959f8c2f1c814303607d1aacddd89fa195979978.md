A man in his 30s has been charged with possessing an imitation firearm, namely a BB gun, after police were called to a York pub.

Police were called to the pub in Goodramgate at around 5.50pm yesterday.

A man was arrested at the scene. He has been bailed to appear at York Magistrates' Court in April.
