Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision which left an elderly woman with serious injuries.

The collision occurred at about 11am on Friday 16 October 2015. A white Mercedes Sprinter van was parked on College Square, Stokesley, when a woman in her nineties, being assisted by her female carer walking with linked arms, crossed the car park behind the van. The van reversed and collided with the pedestrians, knocking them to the ground.

The elderly woman was taken to hospital with serious injuries. She remains in a stable condition in hospital.

Police are carrying out an investigation into the circumstances surrounding the collision, and have appealed for any witnesses to get in touch.

Please phone North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for TC Simon Hudson. Quote reference number 12150183351 when passing information about this incident.

**20 October 2015**
