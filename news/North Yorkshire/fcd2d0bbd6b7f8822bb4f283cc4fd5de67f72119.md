Last modified: 6 December 2017 at 05:42pm

Police have released CCTV images of two men they want to speak to in relation to a burglary at the Potting Shed pub on Northallerton High Street on Sunday 12 November 2017.

At around 9.50pm that evening, a locked office at the pub was broken into and an amount of money stolen.

Police are appealing to anyone who recognises the men, or anyone with information which could assist the investigation to contact them on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to DC Janine Mitchell. You can also email Janine.Mitchell@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170203782.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously 0800 555 111.
