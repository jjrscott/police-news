Officers are appealing for information following a burglary in Settle.

The offence occurred at a property on Ingfield Lane at some point over the past four weeks.

Those responsible for the break-in caused a substantial amount of damage at the rear of the property after smashing a UPVC door to gain entry. It is believed once inside the home a search was conducted before small pieces for jewellery were taken.

Officers are keen to speak with people who saw anything suspicious in the area during the period when the offence has occurred or who can assist them by narrowing down the timescale for when the break-in took place.

If you have any information about this offence, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, selecting option 2, and asking for PC957 Ann Earnshaw. Information can also be provided by emailing ann.earnshaw@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish the remain anonymous, the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the reference number 12170075174 when providing information.
