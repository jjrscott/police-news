North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a criminal damage incident that occurred in Thirsk.

Between Friday 23rd October 2015 and Monday 26th October 2015 unknown person(s) have entered the squash courts on Newsham Road Thirsk and have caused damage to furniture.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help identify those responsible.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1835 Clare Mayes. You can also email Clare.Mayes@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150189690.

Date 26th October 2015
