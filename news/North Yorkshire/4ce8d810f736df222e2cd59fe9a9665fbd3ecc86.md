Police are appealing for information after the theft of a boat trailer from Staithes, near Whitby.

Sometime between midnight on 14 September 2020 and 8pm on 16 September 2020, a 'Rapide Roller 11' boat trailer was stolen from the Staithes Bank top car park, in Staithes, near Whitby.

The trailer was fitted with a wheel clamp and a trailer hitch lock. Its serial number is RR1154.

Anyone with information about the theft, or who knows where the trailer is, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference 12200166202.
