Shortly before 8pm on Thursday 19 January 2017, an altercation occurred between a group of people on Coney Street, York.

During the incident, which involved a group of men and women, a man in his early fifties sustained cracked ribs. He subsequently visit York District Hospital for treatment.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or who has any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Dial 101, press 1 and ask to speak to PC Daniel Kirven or email daniel.kirven@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170010842 when passing on information about the incident.
