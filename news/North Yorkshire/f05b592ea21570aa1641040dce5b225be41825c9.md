Police are appealing for witnesses and information after a womanâ€™s handbag was snatched in York.

At about 5pm on Friday 19 May 2017, a woman was walking to work along the cycle path from Crichton Avenue towards Haxby Road. Around two thirds of the way to Haxby Road, two men approached her on cycles. One of them pulled the handbag from the victim, and they both made off in an unknown direction.

The suspects are described as white, aged under 20, and wearing dark-coloured hooded tops.

Anyone who was in the area at the time of the incident, or knows anything about it, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Louise Harness, or York Police. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12170086214 when passing any information.
