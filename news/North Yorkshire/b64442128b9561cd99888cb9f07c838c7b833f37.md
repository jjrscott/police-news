North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following a theft that occurred at a Skipton jeweller.

It happened at Fusion Jewellers in Craven Court on the High Street at around 11am on Tuesday 22 August 2017.

The offender snatched a white gold ring from the shop counter immediately after it had been tried on by a genuine customer.

After collecting CCTV from the area, police are now seeking the public's help to identify the man pictured below.

If you recognise him, or if you are offered a ring similar to the one pictured above, please contact North Yorkshire Police, quoting incident number NYP-22082017-0170.

Telephone 101, select option 2, and ask for Ben Brittain. You can also email PC Brittain on ben.brittain@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
