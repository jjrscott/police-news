Last modified: 8 November 2018 at 12:29pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempted burglary that occurred in Barlow.

It happened on Laburnum Court, Barlow between 10:10am and 2:10pm on Wednesday 7th November 2018 when the offender/s attempted to gain entry to the property via the front door, damaging the door and door frame.

It is also believed that the suspects attempted to break in at the rear of the property as well, but thankfully were unsuccessful.

Officers are appealing to anyone who saw any suspicious activity or people in the area or witnessed the incident, which was in broad daylight, to contact them.

Anyone with any information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Thomas Longhorn or email Thomas.longhorn@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12180208454 when passing on information.
