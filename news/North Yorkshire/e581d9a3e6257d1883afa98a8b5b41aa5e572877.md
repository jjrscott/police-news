North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in York.

It happened at around 4am on Wednesday 9 September 2015.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about a dark coloured van sighted on Holgate Road at the time of the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Amy Price. You can also email amy.price@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150158775.
