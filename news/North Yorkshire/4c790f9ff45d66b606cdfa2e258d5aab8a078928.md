Police have issued CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to after the theft of meat from a York shop.

On Thursday 13 July 2017 a man entered the BP Service Station on Boroughbridge Road, York wearing a blonde wig and glasses.

He took a large amount of meat before leaving th e store without making an attempt to pay for the items.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured on CCTV in the store, as he may have important information that could assist the investigation.

If you recognise him, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Nikki Jackson. You can also email Nikki.Jackson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170122972 when passing information.
