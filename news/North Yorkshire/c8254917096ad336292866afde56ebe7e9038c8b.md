North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following the theft of high value machine plant that that occurred on a building site in Cloughton.

It happened over the weekend of 10 and 11 February 2018 on Hight Street in Cloughton.

We are appealing for anyone who saw anything suspicious on the site or any movements on the site that weekend. A large hydraulic lift had been accessed and operated on the site in order to steal the heavy machinery.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Jonathan Dillon. You can also email jonathan.dillon@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180024953
