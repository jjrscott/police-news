North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Rufforth, York.

It happened at around 3.45pm 1 January 2016 on Tinker Lane, Rufforth, near to the Yorwaste site, and involved a dog walker who at the time was walking two dogs. The two dogs are described as a black Alsatian and a black Terrier-type dog.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the owner of these dogs, who is described as female of a large build with blonde almost white hair. She was wearing distinctive purple trousers when this incident occured.

Anyone with information that could identify the owner of the two dogs or assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 837 Foster. You can also email gareth.foster@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-01012016-0640.
