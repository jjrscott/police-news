Last modified: 11 February 2019 at 12:02pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Acomb, York.

It happened on Holgate Road, York, at 4.45am on Monday 4 February 2019, and involved an offender throwing a brick into the lower door window pane of the a hair salon. The offender then entered the premises and took six GHD Hair straighteners worth £120 each, £200 worth of hair stylist products and £50 worth of shampoo. The offender then left the area in an unknown direction.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident, in particular anyone with dashcam footage who was driving on Holgate Road around the time of the offence.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andrew Copeland.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190020943.
