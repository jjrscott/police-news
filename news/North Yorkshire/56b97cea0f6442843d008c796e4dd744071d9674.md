North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in in Bentham.

It happened on Grasmere Drive overnight between the 11 and 12 October 2015 and involved the theft of a wallet, a tablet and a lap top form a living room.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about a black Nissan Qashquai which was seen driving in the area in the early hours of the morning.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1838 Middlemiss. You can also email andrew.middlemiss@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
