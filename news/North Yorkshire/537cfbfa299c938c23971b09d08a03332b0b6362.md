Police have issued CCTV of a man they want to speak to after alcohol was stolen from a shop in Whitby.

On the afternoon of Saturday 30 July 2016 a man - described as being white, about 5ft 5ins tall, in his late twenties, with short dark hair and wearing blue zip-up jacket - entered Bargain Booze on Windsor Terrace and picked up a 15-pack carton of Carlsberg lager before leaving the store without making payment.

PC Jonathan Formstone, of Whitby Police, said: "We do not tolerate this behaviour in Whitby, and I encourage the public to come forward with information in order for us to take positive action."
