North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a hate crime incident in York.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a hate crime incident in York.

The incident occurred at around 11.55am on Tuesday, 29 May in the Morrisons car park in Foss Islands. A man and woman were seen to verbally abuse another man before the victim was pushed by the male suspect. No injuries were reported.

Anyone with information which could assist with the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Ellison. Alternatively you can email david.ellison0041@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to provide information anonymously, please contact the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the reference 12180094315 when providing information.
