Last modified: 10 March 2021 at 01:42pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in the Jennyfields area of Harrogate.

It happened at a residential address between the B6161 Otley Road and A61 Ripon Road in Harrogate between 17.30hrs - 21.30hr 5.30pm and 9.30pm last Wednesday (3 March).

Suspects broke the lock on the front door, gained entry and took a number of valuable items.

Jewellery including wedding rings, necklaces and Gucci watches were stolen. Thieves also took a Givenchy bag and Dior Air trainers.

Police are appealing for any information about the suspects in the area at the time of this burglary or the stolen items.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Elizabeth Estensen. You can also email elizabeth.estensen@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12210070785 .
