North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft from motor vehicle that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Castle Road beneath Scarborough Castle overnight between 2200h 29/07/2015 and 1245h 30/07/2015 and involved a silver Vauxhall Vectra.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any one seen acting suspiciously in the area over night 29/07/2015 to 30/07/2015, or if anyone heard the sound of smashing glass.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1779 Tom WILKINSON. You can also email Thomas.Wilkinson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150131138.
