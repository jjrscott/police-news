Last modified: 2 March 2020 at 04:34pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a Kawasaki Mule that occurred near to Agglethorpe, Leyburn.

It happened at a farm near to Agglethorpe, Leyburn between 11.30pm on Saturday 29 February 2020 and 05.30am on Sunday 1 March 2020.

The vehicle, which is a lightweight utility vehicle similar to a quadbike with a flatbed-style pickup on the back, bearing the registration NX67CPF, was stolen from an outbuilding.

Anyone who saw any suspicious activity in the area or has any other information that could assist the investigation, should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for James Bowie. You can also email james.bowie@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200036075.
