Officers are appealing for information following a collision near York involving two cars. North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision that occurred on the Fleet Lane to Tockwith Lane crossroads near Tockwith.

It happened at approximately 11.55am on Saturday 29 December 2018 on the Fleet Lane / Cattal Moor Lane junction at Tockwith, york, and involved a black Ford Focus and a black Ford Fiesta.

The driver of the Fiesta, a woman in her fifties, suffered minor injuries and was taken by ambulance to Harrogate district hospital.
