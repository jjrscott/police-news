North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a shop theft that occurred in Malton.

It happened at the Asda store in Norton Road, Malton, around 3pm on Saturday 10 October 2015. Two men entered the store and took a number of high value items.

Asimilar incident occurred at Morrisons store in Malton on the same day.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance.

In particular we are requesting a possible witness who attended the store following the offence and who advised staff at the Asda store to contact police.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1288 Nick Ham.

You can also email nicholas.ham@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150173353
