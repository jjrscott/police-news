Officers are appealing for information after a woman was sexually assaulted in Northallerton last week.

The incident happened at around 1.15am on Wednesday 22 August 2018 on Grammar School Lane at the junction of Hutchinson Drive.

A woman in her fifties was walking in company with a man when he sexually assaulted her by touching her.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to the Northallerton Serious Crime Team.

Please quote reference number 12180159193 when passing on any information.
