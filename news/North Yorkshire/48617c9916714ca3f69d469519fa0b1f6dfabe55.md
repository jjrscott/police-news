Police are appealing for help to identify the man and woman in the CCTV images.

Officers want to speak to them in connection with a theft at the BP petrol station on Lawrence Street in York.

The incident happened at around 1.50am on 20 January 2017 when three bottles of wine were stolen from the shop at the petrol station.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Nikki Jackson.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please use reference number 12170012022 when passing on any information.
