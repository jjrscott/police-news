North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a serious assault that occurred in Ripon.

The assault took place outside Gianni's Pizzeria on Westgate, Ripon between 1-1.25am on Saturday 11 July 2020 and resulted in a 21-year old man receiving injuries which have left him with a fractured skull, a bleed on the brain and a loss of hearing.

Officers are appealing to any witnesses to the incident to contact them. They are particularly interested to speak to a group of three males and one female, who following the assault, were seen to get into a dark coloured hatchback car. One of the men is believed to have been wearing a grey Armani jumper.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation, or who could help officers locate the group seen leaving the scene, is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Lynn Maguire. You can also email lynn.maguire@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200117545
