North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses after a 17-year-old girl was found collapsed outside the George Hotel, Market Place, Easingwold.

At about 4am on Saturday 11 February, Yorkshire Ambulance service responded to a report that a woman had collapsed in Easingwold.

The woman was taken to hospital for treatment where she remains in a critical condition.

North Yorkshire Police are investigating to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident and conducting enquiries in the area.

Anyone who was in the area at the time or who has any information which could assist our investigation is urged to contact us.

Please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for York Serious Crime Team. You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170024089 when passing on information.
