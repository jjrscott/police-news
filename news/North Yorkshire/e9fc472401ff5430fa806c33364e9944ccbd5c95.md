North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary which occurred on Gilling West High Street, Richmond.

It happened on Gilling West High Street between the evening of 24/08/2015 and the morning of 25/08/2015.

Unknown offenders entered the property by smashing a window on the back door of the house before carrying out untidy searches of the living room and upstairs bedrooms. A large amount of jewellery was taken.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspect people or vehicles on the evening of 24/8/2015.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC93 Roche. You can also email Alex.Roche@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150149372
