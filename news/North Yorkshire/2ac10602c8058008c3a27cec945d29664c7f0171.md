Last modified: 8 August 2017 at 05:38pm

Police have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to regarding an assault which took place at the Stone Roses Bar, Kings Street in York.

At around 12.30am on Saturday 20 May 2017 an altercation took place between two men, which resulted in one man being assaulted and left with significant injuries to his leg.
Following extensive enquiries, police are appealing for the public's help to identify the man pictured, as he may have information which would assist the investigation.

Anyone who witnessed anything, or anyone with information which could assist the investigation is asked to contact police on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to PC Francis Hawthorne. You can also email francis.hawthorne@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12170087954.
