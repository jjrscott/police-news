A man from Whitby has been arrested on suspicion of drink driving.

It follows an incident at around 3am on Thursday 20 August where a car crashed into a wall on Helredale Road.

A 30 year-old local man was arrested on suspicion of driving a motor vehicle when alcohol level above limit and driving whilst unfit through drink.

He remains in police custody for questioning.
