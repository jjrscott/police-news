Police are appealing for witnesses to a robbery that resulted in injuries to two victims at St Sampsons Square in York.

The incident occurred in the early hours of Wednesday 27 June 2018 between 1.10am and 1.45am, near a kebab stand. A small quantity of cash was stolen and two victims were punched.

A 20-year-old York man was arrested on suspicion of robbery. He has been released under investigation as enquiries continue.

If you witnessed the incident, or have any information about it, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and quote incident number 12180114633, or ask for DC 1807 Nicola Gill.
