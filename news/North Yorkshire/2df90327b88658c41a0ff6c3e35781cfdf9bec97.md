A man has died after he entered the River Foss in York over the weekend.

Police were called to a report that a man had entered the River Foss at about 4pm on Saturday 21 July 2018, near the Q-Park car park in Garden Place, York. He was pulled from the river by a member of the public and taken to York Hospital for treatment.

Sadly, the man, who was in his 20s and from York, died in hospital the following day.

Enquiries are ongoing into how the man came to enter the river. Anyone who has information that could assist this investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference number 12180133086.
