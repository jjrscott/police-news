Police in York are appealing for the owner of a stolen mountain bike to come forward.

The orange Carrera Vengeance and was recovered from a bike rack in York city centre during the early hours of Saturday 10 November 2018.

A 37-year-old local man has been arrested and released under investigation in connection with the suspected theft.

If you are the owner of the mountain bike or think you know who it belongs to, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for the Investigation Hub York. Please quote crime reference number 12180210258.
