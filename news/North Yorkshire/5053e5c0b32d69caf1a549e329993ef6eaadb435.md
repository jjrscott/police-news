Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:18am

North Yorkshire Police can confirm that the 56-year-old female cyclist involved in the collision on the A168 in Thornton-le-Street, Thirsk last week has sadly died at James Cook Hospital.

It happened at 9.15am on Tuesday 3 October 2017 when the cyclist was in a collision with a silver Nissan Micra.

We are not in a position to name her at this time.

Officers are renewing their appeal for witnesses and dash-cam footage to assist the investigation.

In particular, they are appealing for the drivers of two vehicles which overtook the cyclist prior to the collision to come forward.

If you can help, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Hamish Holloway or the Major Collision Investigation Team. Or email hamish.halloway@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12170177189
