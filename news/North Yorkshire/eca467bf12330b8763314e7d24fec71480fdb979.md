Thank you to everyone who shared our appeal, Donald's family have been in touch.

Previous appeal:

The coroner's office is appealing for information to find the family of Donald Alexander Meegan, 73, of Falsgrave Road.

Sadly Donald collapsed near his home on 17 October and died.

Officers have not been able to trace next of kin and are appealing to anyone who has information that could help find them.

If you knew Donald or can help trace his family or next of kin, please contact the coroner's office on 01609 643614 or email coroner@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Donald's death is not being treated as suspicious.
