North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a man sustained minor injuries when his mobility scooter was involved in a collision.

It happened between 0700-0715 hrs on Tuesday 8th December 2015 at the junction of Crabmill Lane and The Market Place, Easingwold and involved a male on a mobility scooter who was involved in a collsion with a white coloured transit style van. It is believed that the van had Russells written on it. The male was thrown from his scooter on impact and recieved minor injuries.

The road was closed and local diversions were put in place by highways.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle(s) prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the identity of the the van involved.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Katie Shaw. You can also email katie.shaw@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150217473.
