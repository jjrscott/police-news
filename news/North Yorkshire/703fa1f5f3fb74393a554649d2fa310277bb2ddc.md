This is a brief update in relation to the on-going investigation into the death of Rina Yasutake who was found dead at a property in Helmsley on 25 September 2018.

The three people who were arrested as part of the investigation into her death have answered bail and have been released while under investigation to allow further enquiries to be carried out.

Officers are awaiting the results of post mortem tests and do not yet have a cause or time of death.
