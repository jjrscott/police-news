North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a handbag from a motor vehicle that occurred in Skipton.

It happened on Fisher Medical Centre car park between 10:35 and 11:20 am on Thursday 15th October 2015 and involved a person smash a passenger window to a parked Citroen Picasso and stole a white and black handbag from the front passenger seat.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about a white male in the area who was possibly wearing a light grey tracksuit and had a dark coloured motor vehicle.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Mark Browne. You can also email mark.browne@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150182674.
