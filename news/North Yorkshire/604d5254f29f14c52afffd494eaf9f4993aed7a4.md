North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a residential burglary at a property on Westbourne Avenue Harrogate.

Thieves gained entry to the property on Westbourne Avenue, Harrogate between 1-6am on Tuesday 28 July 2020.

They took the victims car keys and stole two vehicles from the driveway; a Audi Q7 registration YH66 MHR and a black Toyota Hilux registration YF69 EYF . The victims handbag was also taken, but later located outside the property, but was missing a deep red Kate Spade purse and a distinctive make up bag.

Since the theft, officers have successfully located the Audi. However, they are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident and to assist them in locating the thieves and tracing the Toyota.
