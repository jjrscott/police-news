Police in Harrogate are appealing to potential victims of a sexual assault to come forward.

It follows a report of a man putting his hand on a woman's leg as he bent down to pick up some money from a supermarket floor.

Officers believe there may be other woman who may have been touched in a similar way.

If you believe this may have happened to you, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Stephen Peachman or email Stephen.peachman@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12180140773 when calling.

A man has been arrested in connection with the incident and remains on police bail while the investigation continues.
