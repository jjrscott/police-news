Police in Scarborough are appealing for witnesses after a car wing mirror was smashed on Belvedere Road in Scarborough.

The incident happened at around 9.10pm on Friday 22 April 2015.

Officers particularly want to speak to a man and a woman who were seen on Filey Road before splitting up, with the man going towards Queen Margarets Terrace and the woman going towards Granville Road.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation is urged to contact PC 128 Mick Barker at Scarborough Police on 101, quoting the reference number 12150084826.

Information can also be passed on anonymously to Crimestoppers by calling 0800 555 111.

**26 May 2015**
