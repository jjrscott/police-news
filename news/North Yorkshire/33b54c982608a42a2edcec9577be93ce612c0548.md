North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a damage only Collision that occurred on Raincliffe Street at Selby.

It has happened at approximately 2255 hrs on the 12th September 2015 at "The Health Clinic" car park on Raincliffe Street, and involved a Ford Vehicle and a male driver, who has reversed into part of the building causing minor damage. The vehicle then leaves and parks on Raincliffe Street for a short period.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle on Raincliffe Street, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Julia Allen. You can also email Julia.Allen@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150162885.
