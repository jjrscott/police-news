North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Selby.

It happened on Millgate near the junction with Friendship Court, between approximately 10.30pm and 10.55pm on Friday 3 November 2017 and involved a man being assaulted in an unprovoked attack.

As a result of the attack, the victim, a local man in his early thirties, attended hospital and received stitches to his face before being discharged.
