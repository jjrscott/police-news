Police are appealing for witnesses after two people were injured when a Renault Clio hit a telegraph pole and a tree on Ox Carr Lane at Strensall.

Officers want to hear from anyone who was travelling through Strensall at around 2am on Tuesday 25 July 2017 and saw a blue Renault Clio leave the road at Ox Carr Lane after its junction with Moor Lane, or saw the Clio travelling in the direction of Strensall from Strensall Common.

The occupants of the car, a 30-year-old man and 28-year-old woman, both from the York area, were taken to hospital following the collision. Both remain in a serious condition.

Anyone with any information should call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for TC Martin Hayes of the Tadcaster Roads Policing Group. Alternatively, email martin.hayes@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote reference number 12170130759 when passing on information.

The road was closed to allow emergency services to attend to the casualties, examine the scene and to recover the vehicle. It reopened shortly after 9am.
