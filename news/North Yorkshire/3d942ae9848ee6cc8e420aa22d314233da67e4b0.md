North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident of public disorder in Malton.

It happened on Yorkersgate, Malton around 11.30pm on Saturday 7 October 2017 and involved a four people, two Eastern European men and two women, who were arguing in the street

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about violence witnessed and/or any objects seen during the incident.

Two men, aged 21 and 29, were arrested in connection with incident. They have been released under investigation whilst enquiries continue.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Georgina Hanson. You can also email Georgina.Hanson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170180229.
