The man who detectives wanted to speak to about a serious sexual assault in York has been identified following a North Yorkshire Police appeal. Thank you to all who shared the appeal.

**Original release: Serious sexual assault in York â€“ CCTV released in appeal for witness to come forward**

Detectives in York have released CCTV of a man they would like to speak about a serious sexual assault in York.

The incident occurred in the early hours of Thursday 12 July 2018 in the Walmgate area when a 19 year-old woman was sexually assaulted.

Officers have released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to as they believe he could have important information that could assist the investigation.

He is described as a White male, of stocky build, with broad shoulders and stubble/a beard. He was wearing chino style shorts and a polo top.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured on CCTV or can help with this investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police straight away: please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for York Serious Crime Team or email SCTYork@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously via crimestoppers-uk.org/give-information. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court.

Please quote reference number 12180125799 when passing information about this incident.
