North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Scarborough town centre.

The incident took place outside the Opera House Casino on St Thomas Street at 12.40am Sunday 23 September 2018.

It involved two men approaching two other men who were punched causing them to lose consciousness and fall to the floor.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Nick Simpson. You can also email nick.simpson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.
