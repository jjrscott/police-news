Police are appealing for information to help identify the men in the CCTV following an incident of criminal damage at Tesco, Northallerton.

Officers want to speak to the men in connection with the incident, which happened overnight between 11 and 12 December 2017 when a number of trollies were damaged. The incident was reported at around 9.30am on 12 December.

Anyone who recognises the men in the CCTV image, or who has any information, is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Graeme Gale or email Graeme.gale@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote crime reference number 12170221605 when passing on any information.
