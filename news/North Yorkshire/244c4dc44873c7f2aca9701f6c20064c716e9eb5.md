North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary that occurred at a shop in Sessay, near Thirsk.

Sometime overnight on Monday 18 November 2019, access has been gained to Oaks Tackle Shop and a number of items of fishing equipment removed from within.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Karen Mitson. You can also email karen.mitson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
