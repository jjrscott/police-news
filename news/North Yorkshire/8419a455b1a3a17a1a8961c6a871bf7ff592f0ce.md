North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about criminal damage that occurred in Eastfield.

The damage took place on Caymer Road, Eastfield, Scarborough overnight from 7th into 8th February 2016. A black Audi A4 was damaged.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing to anyone who saw or heard anything.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101 and select option 1.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160022879
