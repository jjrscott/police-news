Police in York have issued a CCTV image of a man they want to speak to following a number of scratchcard thefts at convenience stores around the city.

The cards were stolen from Sainsbury's, Tesco Express, Spar and Martin's stores between December and January.

The man, or anyone who can help identify him is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Abigail Bambridge. You can also email Abigail.Bambridge@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180005473.
