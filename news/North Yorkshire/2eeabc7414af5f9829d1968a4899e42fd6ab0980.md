North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a car in York.

It happened on Heslington Road at around 1.45am on Wednesday 2 December 2020.

The 51-plate silver Toyota Yaris was stolen while the driver was making a food delivery nearby.

The vehicle made off along Green Dyke Lane, Melrosegate and headed in the direction of Heworth.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for anybody who may have CCTV of the vehicle, or have possibly seen the car since the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Amy Herrick. You can also email amy.herrick@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200214666.
