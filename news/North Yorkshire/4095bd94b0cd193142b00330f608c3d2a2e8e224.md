Last modified: 28 September 2020 at 04:17pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a garage burglary that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on Raleigh Street overnight on Saturday 26 September into Sunday 27 September.

In particular, officers are appealing for information from anyone who has seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area in the early hours of Sunday morning.

Also anyone who may have noticed items missing from their garage or outbuildings.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Simon Garnett. You can also email simon.garnett@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200169847.

Two men have been arrested on suspicion of a number of offences and have been released on bail while the investigation continues.
