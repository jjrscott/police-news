North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about some reported criminal damage that occurred in Easingwold.

Between 6pm on 9 December and 4pm on 10 December 2017, four private motor vehicles parked on Galtres Drive, Easingwold, were damaged damaged by having paintwork deliberately scratched.

Police are appealing for anyone with any information that could lead to the identification of the suspects to contact them.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Matthew Balmforth PC 205. You can also email Matthew.Balmforth@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170220857.
