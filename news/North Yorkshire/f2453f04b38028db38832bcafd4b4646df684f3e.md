North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a man who made lewd comments towards the same woman on two occasions.

The first incident occurred on Foss Island Road, York, close to the Red Tower beside the bar walls, on Monday 19 June 2017 at about 12.15pm.

The second incident took place on Parliament Street York, close to the fountain area, on Thursday 22 June at about 7.25pm.

The victim was a 22-year-old woman. On the second occasion, the man also made lewd comments to other women in the area.

Anyone with information about either incident is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for Lee Wilson, or email lee.wilson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
