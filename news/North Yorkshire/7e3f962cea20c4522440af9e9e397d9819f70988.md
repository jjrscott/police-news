North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a Burglary that occurred in Lower Dunsforth near Boroughbridge.

It happened at Mount Pleasant Farm overnight Tuesday 10th February to Wednesday 11th February 2015 involved the removal of an unregistered red and white Honda Cr250 Motocross bike .

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for any witness and anyone who saw any suspicious vehicles or activity in the area, and any sightings of the distinctive stolen motorcycle .

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Mick Place 884. You can also email mick.place884@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150023348.
