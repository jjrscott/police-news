The incident happened around 1.20pm on 23 February 2021 at the Premier Express shop in Morton-on-Swale. Two men took items from the shop shelves, including some protein powder, and left without making payment.

A witness initially came forward to provide a description of the suspects but did not leave any details.

Police are keen to speak to this witness and anyone else who may have information which could help establish the full circumstances surrounding this incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and quote collar number 156. You can also email 000156@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12210065194.
