North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempted burglary that occurred in Kirk Smeaton.

It happened on Main Street, Kirk Smeaton at 5.20am on Monday 15 October 2018 and involved offender/s attempting to gain entry to a barn by forcing a door open, which resulted in the door being damaged.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 754 Tom Longhorn. You can also email thomas.longhorn@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
