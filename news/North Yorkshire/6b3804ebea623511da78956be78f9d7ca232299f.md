A man has been arrested and suspected heroin seized after a vehicle was stopped in York.

At about 10am on Wednesday 20 April, officers stopped a car on the A1237 at Rawcliffe.

A 28-year-old man from the Leeds area was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply a Class A drug.

He has been released on bail pending further enquiries.
