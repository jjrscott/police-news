Last modified: 1 March 2021 at 02:22pm

North Yorkshire police has issued CCTV images of three men they want to speak to following a theft from a service station in Great Ayton.

It happened at the Esso Petrol Station on Guisborough Road, on 25 January 2021 and involved three males who entered the store and stole a large amount of items from inside.

Officers are asking members of the public to get in touch if they recognise or can provide any information regarding the men involved.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Scott 986 or email michael.scott2@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12210040179 when passing on information.
