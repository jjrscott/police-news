North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the cruel killing of a calf that occurred near Tadcaster.

At about 11.30 pm on 21 December 2016, cruel thieves attended a farm near Tadcaster and attacked a young cow, towed it out of its barn on the back of a quad bike then left it in the field, it was also shot and has subsequently died. The thieves stole the farmer's trailers and caused other damage before leaving the scene on a quad bike.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Nicola GILL PC1087 You can also email Nicola.Gill@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160229555
