North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about an alleged assault that occurred in Irton near Scarborough.

The alleged assualt happened on Main Street, Irton around 9:45pm on Sunday 25 October 2015 and involved a male and female in their late teens who are local to the area.

North Yorkshire Police are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Lee Ridsdale. You can also email lee.ridsdale221@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150189575
