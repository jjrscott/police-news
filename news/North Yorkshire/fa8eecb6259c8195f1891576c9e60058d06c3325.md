Police investigating a fuel theft from a Ripon filling station want to talk to a woman pictured by CCTV.

It happened at the BP Garage on North Road at 10.25pm on Friday, 12 January.

Anyone who recognises the woman pictured, or has any further information, should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Lana Beardsley.

You can also email PC Beardsley on lana.beardsley@northyorkshirepolice.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, call Crime stoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12180007019
