York police are appealing for information following a robbery on the night of Saturday 14 March 2015

It happened between 7.30pm and 8pm on Salisbury Terrace in York, when a 17-year-old boy was approached by a man wearing dark glasses who threatened him with a knife before fleeing the scene with the victim's Nokia Lumia mobile phone. The boy was not injured during the incident.

The suspect is described as white, around 30 years of age, about 6 foot tall, of stocky build and was wearing black clothing including black jogging bottoms, a black Nike hooded top with a small red and white Nike logo, pulled up around his face with draw strings, and grey Nike trainers.

Anyone who witnessed the attack or can help identify the suspect is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for York CID. Or email YorkCID@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150042202 when passing on information.
