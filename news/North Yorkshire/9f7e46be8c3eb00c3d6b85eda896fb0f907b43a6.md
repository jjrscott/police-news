Police are appealing for witnesses and information following damage caused to a phone box in Chain Lane, Knaresborough.

The damaged phone box is near the junction of Chain Lane and Stockwell Lane in Knaresborough. It happened at around 2.40am on 11 February 2016.

Officers are particularly interested in speaking to a slim man believed to be in his late teens or early twenties, who was wearing a black parker style jacket with a fur lined hood, who was seen in the area. If you have any information about this man or the incident please contact 101 and quote job reference 12160024663 or email PC 311 George Frost at george.frost@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

17th February 2016
