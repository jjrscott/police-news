North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a public order incident that occurred at a pub in Whitby.

It happened in the front open area of The Dolphin on Bridge Street at approximately 8:20pm on Friday 22 June 2018 and involved two males who had been refused to be served any more alcohol and began fighting with door staff from the pub.

Two males were arrested and have been currently released under investigation pending further enquiries.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about a woman present who had recorded the incident on their mobile phone or any other persons who may have recorded the incident or witnessed the two men's behaviour.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Gary Evans. You can also email Gary.Evans@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
