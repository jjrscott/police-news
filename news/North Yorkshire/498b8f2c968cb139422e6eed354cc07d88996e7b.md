Police in York have charged a 52-year-old man with attempted murder, wounding with intent, and false imprisonment.

It follows an incident that occurred on the third floor of a block of flats in Fourth Avenue in York on the evening of Wednesday 1 May 2019 at around 6.40pm, when a 47-year-old York man sustained head and pelvic injuries.

Police are appealing to anyone who witnessed the incident and has not already spoken to the police, to contact them.

The suspect was remanded in custody following his arrest on 1 May and is due to appear in court on Friday 3 May 2019

Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and pass information for incident number 12190078740.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
