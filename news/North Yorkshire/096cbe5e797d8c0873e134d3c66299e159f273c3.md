Last modified: 9 May 2018 at 10:40am

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about two burglaries that occurred in the Bishopdale area of Leyburn.

The incidents happened at two separate properties in Bishopdale, Leyburn overnight between 4 and 5 March 2018.

At one property, a garage has been forced open and a Red Honda TRX 500 has been stolen. At another property, a Red Honda TRX 500 has been stolen after entry was forced into a portacabin type building.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for James Bowie. You can also email james.bowie@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
