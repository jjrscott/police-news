North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about three dwelling burglaries in Bentham.

They were committed over the weekend of 17 - 18 October at Barghs Meadow, Ashbank Villas and Grasmere Drive, Bentham. Officers are reminding residents to check their home security, and would also like to hear from anyone who may have disturbed any persons in their home in the area.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 1, and quote reference number 12150184105.

**19 October 2015**
