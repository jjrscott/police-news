We are sharing an appeal for information to trace the next of kin of a 54-year-old man from Malton.

Mr Gerald Scott lived on Wells Lane in Malton and died on Saturday 23 November 2019.

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who has any information which would assist the Coroners Office trace Mr Scott's next of kin is asked to contact them directly on 01609 643168.
