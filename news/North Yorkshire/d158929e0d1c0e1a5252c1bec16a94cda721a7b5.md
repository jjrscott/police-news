Last modified: 17 October 2018 at 04:32pm

Police are investigating a road traffic collision which occurred between a cyclist and black car on Melrosegate in York on Tuesday 1 May at around 3:30pm which left the cyclist with minor injuries.

Police are appealing for any witnesses to the collision, or anyone who capture dash cam or CCTV footage of the incident to contact them.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC41 ELLISON. You can also email david.ellison0041@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180082785
