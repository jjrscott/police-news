North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a commercial burglary that occurred at the Little Chef on Gargrave Road, Skipton.

t happened at the Little Chef on Gargrave Road between 11.30pm and 12.30am overnight on 8 and 9 April, and involved the restaurant being entered and a small metal safe being removed which was later recovered by the roadside in Farnhill near to Cononley Lane Ends.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious activity, persons or vehicles around those time and locations .

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1838 Middlemiss. You can also email andrew.middlemiss@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160061142.
