Police have released CCTV images of a woman they would like to speak to in connection with a shop theft in Harrogate.

At around 10.15am on Friday 8 May 2015, a woman stole a pair of blue suede shoes from Monsoon on James Street.

Officers are investigating the incident and are appealing to anyone who recognises the woman in the images to contact them with information.

Anyone who can help officers with their enquiries is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 and ask for PC Adam Smith or Harrogate police.

Alternatively, information can be emailed toadam.smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you prefer not to give your name, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150076471 when passing information about this incident.
