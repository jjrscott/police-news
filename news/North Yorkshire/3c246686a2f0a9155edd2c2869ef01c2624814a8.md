Last modified: 7 May 2017 at 05:05pm

North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses to a serious road traffic collision between a 23-year-old woman from York and a grey Skoda Octavia, to contact them.

The collision occurred on Ouse Bridge in York at 11pm on Saturday 6 May 2017.

The woman pedestrian sustained life threatening head and serious pelvic injuries and was taken to Hull Royal Infirmary where she remains in a serious condition. The 79-year-old male driver of the Skoda was unhurt. He attended the police station as a voluntary attendee and has been released whilst enquiries continue.

Police are appealing for any witnesses to come forward and contact them to assist them in determining the full circumstances surrounding the collision.

Anyone with any information which would assist the investigation is asked to contact TC Andy Tucker on 101 or email Andy.Tucker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170078047.
