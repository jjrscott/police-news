North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident that occurred at West Bank Park, York.

It happened at about 1.30pm on Saturday 7 March 2020 when an altercation occurred between a man and a woman which resulted in the woman being taken to a local hospital by ambulance with minor injuries.

Prior to this, it is believed that members of the public helped the woman at both West Bank Park and Windsor Garth in the city.

A 30 year- old man has been arrested in connection with this incident and he has been bailed pending further enquiries.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, officers are appealing for witnesses who saw the altercation or who may have assisted the woman to come forward.

Anyone with information, including CCTV, which could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101. You can also email Lejli.Scrowston@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
