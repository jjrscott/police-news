Four Ford Transit Tippers were stolen in the Huntington Road area of York between 10pm on 9 March 2018 and 8am on 10 March 2018.

While enquiries are ongoing to trace the suspects, North Yorkshire Police is urging owners of this type of vehicle to ensure that it is kept secure at all times, removing all goods and, where possible, extra measures are taken such as using wheel clamps, steering wheel locks and fitting tracking devices to enhance security.
