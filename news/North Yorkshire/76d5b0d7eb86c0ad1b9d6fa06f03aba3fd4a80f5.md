Officers are appealing for information to help identify the man in the CCTV image.

Officers want to speak to the man in connection with a shop theft in South Milford, Selby.

The incident happened at the Southlands Garage on Friday 12 January at around 8.40pm, when a man entered the store, placed bottles of alcohol in a basket and left the store with the basket and its contents without attempting to pay for the items.

Anyone who recognises the man, or who has any information, is asked to contact police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Derek Bone or email drek.bone@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12180006819 when passing on any information.
