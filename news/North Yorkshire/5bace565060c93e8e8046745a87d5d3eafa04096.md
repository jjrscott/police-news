Police in York have arrested two men in connection with two burglaries at Links of London jewellers store on Stonegate.

Both men are from York and are aged 32 and 24. They were arrested on 8 February and have been released on police bail while the investigation continues.

The shop was burgled on 29 and 31 January when the suspects threw a metal grate through the door.

Police are appealing to anyone who has been offered Links of London jewellery for sale. The jewellery includes watches, bracelets and necklaces and the watches are all marked with the name "Links of London".

Anyone with any information is asked to contact York CID on 01904 669453. Or email YorkCID@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
