North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about damage to a vehicle that that occurred in Skipton

It happened on Whinny Gill Road between 1pm on 23 December and 1pm on 24 December 2017 when the windscreens to the front and rear of a red hatchback were smashed.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Liz Rutter You can also email liz.rutter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170229132].
