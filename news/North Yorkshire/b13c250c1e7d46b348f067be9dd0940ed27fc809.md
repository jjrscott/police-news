North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a goat that was on the loose in Pickering.

On the 5 and 6 of July 2016 police received a number of reports about a goat that was on the loose in the Pickering area. The goat was located and has temporarily been housed .
If this is your goat, please contact Malton police station reference number 12160119577 with proof of ownership.

Anyone with information that could assist should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for louise neville-beck. You can also email louise neville-beck northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160119577
