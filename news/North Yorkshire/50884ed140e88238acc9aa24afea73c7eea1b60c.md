North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses after a dangerous driving incident on the A169.

It happened between Pickering and Whitby at around 2.25pm on Friday 29 January when it is reported that a grey BMW 1 series was forced off the road by a black Audi TT Quattro.

The Audi was travelling from Pickering towards Whitby and the BMW was travelling in the opposite direction.

Did you see what happened or did you witness a vehicle being dangerously or carelessly in the area at the time?

Officers are also particularly keen to speak to the driver of a Ford Transit that travelling from Pickering towards Whitby and was overtaken by the Audi.

Anyone with any information, including any dash-cam footage , that could assist the investigation is asked to please email lewis.grainge@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or call North Yorkshire Police on 101.

Please quote reference number 12210048589 when providing any information.
