North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a an assault that occurred on Micklegate Walk, Selby.

It happened on Micklegate Walk, off Finkle Street, Selby between approximately 00.00hrs and 01.00hrs on Saturday 11th July 2015. As the victim walked home from a night out he was assaulted in an unprovoked attack. Offenders hit the victim to his head and body with a blunt weapon causing him to lose consciousness.

The victim was treated in hospital due to the injuries sustained.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Alison Marshall. You can also email alison.marshall@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150117989.

**15 July 2015**
