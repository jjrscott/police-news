North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Hellifield.

The incident took place at some time between Friday 15 and Saturday 16 November 2019. The suspects have forced entry into an outbuilding and children's motorbikes and motorbike clothing have been taken. Garden tools, a patio heater and BBQ have also been removed.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone seen to be acting suspiciously in the area at the time or who were seen loading items into a vehicle.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Burgess. You can also email David.Burgess@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190210997.
