Four youths have been arrested following the theft of a vehicle in York in the early hours of Monday 1 May 2017.

Police received a call from a concerned member of the public at around 5.20am, reporting that there was a suspicious vehicle on Common Lane in the Dunnington area of York.

Officers attended and located the vehicle, a silver Vauxhall Corsa, parked on Common Lane. There were no occupants in the vehicle. Officers approached four youths who were nearby and questioned them. The youths, two aged 14 and two aged 15, were subsequently arrested on suspicion of theft of a vehicle.

All four youths have been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
