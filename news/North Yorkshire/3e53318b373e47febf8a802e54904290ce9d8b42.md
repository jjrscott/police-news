Police are appealing for information after a sentimantal piece of jewellrey was stolen, amongst other items, in a burglary.

The stolen item is similar to the one in the picture, with a Buddha engraved into the bangle, however the actual colour of the stolen item is white metal with a safety chain attached.

Anyone with any information about the item of jewellrey, or who has been offered to buy the item, is asked to contact the.

Dial 101, press option 2 and ask for DC Matthew Mitchell.

Alternatively Crimestoppers can be contacted annonymously on 0800 555 111.
