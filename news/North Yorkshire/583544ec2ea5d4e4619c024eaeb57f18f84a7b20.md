North Yorkshire Police are appealing for information after a burglary at Skipton Golf Club.

The suspect entered the kitchen at about 3.30pm on 28 April 2016 and was taken through to the main part of the building. Whilst waiting for the Manager, he has entered the main office, and removed an envelope containing the days cash takings.

The male is described as white, 6ft tall, and slim build, between 35 and 40 years old, wearing a dark coloured wooly beany hat and and a high visibility sleeveless jacket over dark coloured clothing.

Please are appealing for anyone who may have information regarding this, or any other businesses who may have had similar occurrences.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Jane CARPENTER. You can also email Jane.Carpenter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 1216073828.
