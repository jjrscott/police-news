North Yorkshire Police is appealing for anyone who has any information about a white Boardman CXR road bike stolen from the Richmond area on 19 April 2016.

If anyone has been offered for sale a Boardman CXR Roadbike as seen in the attached pictures they should contact police on the number below.

The bike is described as Boardman CXR, White with saddle bag attached, rear mudguard, SPD pedals and a light set with its own power supply with cables which are taped to the top tube with black tape.

Any of these items could have been removed and offered for sale separately, if anyone has been offered any of these items please get in touch.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Matthew Mitchell. You can also email matthew.mitchell@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160068025
