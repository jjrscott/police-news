North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault in Scarborough.

It happened in the Queen Street/Granby Place area between 9.15pm and 9.30pm on Tuesday 26 June 2018.

A man in his twenties was assaulted by another man and sustained a cut to the back of his head. He was taken by ambulance to Scarborough Hospital where he was treated and discharged following treatment.
