North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident in Scarborough in which two men had an altercation in the street during which an attempted theft has taken place.

It happened on Victoria Road, Scarborough within the last three weeks and involved a middle aged man, a middle aged woman and a young man in his 20s.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

The younger male is described as wearing a light grey tracksuit top, a light coloured shirt underneath, blue jeans and white Reebok trainers.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Adam Gallimore. You can also email adam.gallimore@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150115272.
