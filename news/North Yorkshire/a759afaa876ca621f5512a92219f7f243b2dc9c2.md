Officers are appealing for witnesses after a blue 4x4 was damaged on Lowfield in Eastfield.

The incident occurred between the morning of Sunday, 26 March and Monday, 27 March. It is believed the damage was caused by someone scratching the vehicle.

Anyone who can assist officers with their investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, selecting option 2, and asking for J-S Coleman. You can also email _john-simon.coleman@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_.

Information can be provided anonymously to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170051424.
