Police in Ryedale have reissued an appeal to trace a woman who flagged down a police car in Old Malton Gate in the early hours of 17 January 2016.

The woman reported criminal damage to a window of a residential property in the street that had happened moments earlier.

Officers are appealing to her make contact as she left without leaving any contact details.

If you are the woman who flagged down a police car with two officers in it, please contact the police.

Anyone else who has any information about the incident is also asked to get in touch.

Please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Jade Gallagher-Barrass or email _Jade.Gallagher-Barrass597@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
