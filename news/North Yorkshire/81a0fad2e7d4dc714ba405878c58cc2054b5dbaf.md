North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about after a property was damaged on Castle Road, Scarborough.

Several windows on the occupied property were smashed during the evening of Wednesday 8th April 2015.

Police have recovered numerous metal ball bearing from the scene and believe a sling shot may have been used to cause the damage.

A group of youths were seen in the area at around the time of the incident.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

We are appealing for any person that could assist the investigation to contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Ben Brittain. You can also email ben.brittain@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150057760.
