Police are appealing for witnesses to a serious collision in York involving a woman pedestrian and a double-decker bus.

The incident occurred outside the Missoula restaurant on Bridge Street just after 9pm on Saturday (30 June 2018).

The 26-year-old woman, from the Oldham area, sustained a serious head injury.

She was treated at the scene by paramedics before going to Leeds General Infirmary by ambulance. She is in a stable condition.

Traffic and pedestrian closures were put in place in the Ouse Bridge and Micklegate areas for around four and half hours while officers from the Roads Policing Group and Forensic Collision Investigation Unit conducted enquiries at the scene.

Police are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, option 1, and speak to the Force Control Room. Please quote reference number 12180117552.
