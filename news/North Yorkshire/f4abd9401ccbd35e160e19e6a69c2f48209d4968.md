Last modified: 22 February 2017 at 03:05pm

Police in Skipton are asking for the public’s help to identify the man shown in these CCTV images in connection with a fraud investigation.

On 11 April 2016, a fraudulent cheque for £3,600 was cashed at Natwest Bank in Skipton, the following day, an attempt to cash another cheque for £8800 was made, but failed.

Officers are appealing to anyone who can help identify the man to contact them.

Anyone with any information is asked to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC David Gasson or email david.gasson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12160063268 when passing on information.
