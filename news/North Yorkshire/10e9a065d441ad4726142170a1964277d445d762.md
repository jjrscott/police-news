North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information following two fuel thefts that occurred in Selby.

Both thefts happened on the A63 between Selby swing bridge and the A1041/A63 roundabout.between approximately 7pm Tuesday 20 February and 5.30am on Wednesday 21 February 2018.

A large quantity of fuel was stolen from two lorries parked in the lay-by at the side of the A63.

It is believed the offenders accessed the lay-by by vehiucle through an adjacent field from Thief Lane, Selby. It is believed that offenders have crossed the A63 between lay-bys to target vehicles.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Alison Baker. You can also email Alison.baker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-21022018-0035.
