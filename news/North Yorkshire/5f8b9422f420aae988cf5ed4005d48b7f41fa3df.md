We can now provide more information regarding one of the motorcyclists who died in the collision on the B1257 between Malton an Amotherby on Friday 2 October.

One of the riders has been named as 62-year-old Stuart Hansen from Bridlington, East Yorkshire.

We can confirm that the other motorcyclist involved was an off-duty police officer from Humberside Police. We are not currently in a position to release his identity.

Police are appealing for any witnesses who saw the incident or the events leading up to the collision to contact them on 101, select option 2 and ask for the Major Collision Investigation Team or email MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
