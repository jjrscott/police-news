North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a black Ford puma car was badly damaged whilst parked on Church Street in Whitby. The damage occurred between 4pm - 5pm on Saturday 3 October.

If you have CCTV or dashcam footage which could assist the investigation, or witnessed anything taking place please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, and quote reference number 12200174358. You can also email Jessie.landers@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
