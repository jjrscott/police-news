Officers have issued an urgent appeal to help find a distressed man in Selby.

He was last seen walking towards McDonalds from the Jet petrol station in Selby at 1.30am on Sunday 13 May 2018.

He is described as White, in his 30s, short dark hair thinning on top, wearing all dark clothing, dark coloured trainer type shoes with white soles. He walked with a distinctive limp….

Officers are growing increasingly concerned for the man’s welfare and are appealing to the public for information as to his whereabouts.

If you have seen the man pictured or think you may be able to help us identify him please contact North Yorkshire Police.

Dial 101, press 1 and pass information to the force control room. If you have an immediate sighting of him, please call 999.
