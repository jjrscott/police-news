Police are appealing for information regarding the manner of driving of a silver Ford Focus estate car, that was seen to be driving around the town centre area of Malton.

At around 6pm on Thursday 23 November 2017, the night Malton Christmas lights were switched on, a silver Ford Focus estate car was seen driving from the direction of Wheelgate, along to Market Place and the top of Finkle Street and then turn in the direction of Spital Street and Newbiggin.

Police are appealing to any witnesses who saw the car, to contact them on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to Sergeant Darren Coles. You can also email Darren.Coles@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170210594.
