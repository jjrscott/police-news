Police in Skipton have issued CCTV of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the theft of a bag from a Cancer Research charity shop.

It happened at Cancer Research in Caroline Square Skipton at 3pm on Tuesday 17 February 2015 when a brown leather Ralph Lauren bag priced at Â£99.95 was stolen.

The man police want to speak to was dressed in smart, casual dark coloured clothing and was carrying a leather man bag across his body and entered the store with a woman.

Police are appealing to anyone who can help identify the man shown in the CCTV or for the man to contact the police himself.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for David McManus. You can also email davidmcmanus@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150026880.
