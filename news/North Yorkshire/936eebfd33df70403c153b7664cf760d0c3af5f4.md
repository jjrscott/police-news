North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a road traffic collision between Duggleby and Setterington.

It happened at 1.15pm on 10 May 2015 and involved a cyclist and a blue Skoda Fabia estate.

It is believed that the cyclist sustained minor injuries as a result of the collision.

Police are trying to trace the cyclist and are appealing for witnesses to the collision, or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicles prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Malton police.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150077112.

**11 May 2015**
