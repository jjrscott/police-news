Police have located a 15-year-old girl from York who had not been seen by her family for five days.

Thank you to everyone who has supported the appeal. Your care and support is greatly appreciated as always.

Now that the missing person appeal has been cancelled, we ask that all photographs and details of the girl are removed from websites and social media as soon as possible.
