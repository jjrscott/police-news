We have had a number of calls from members of the public about animal welfare following the release of film footage by Animal Aid of Bowood Farms Ltd (T/A Bowood Yorkshire Lamb) abattoir near Thirsk.

'The investigation into alleged breaches of animal welfare is being carried out by the Food Standards Agency who have the necessary powers to take any legal action in respect of animal welfare issues.

Here is a link to the Food Standards Agency website : http://www.food.gov.uk/news-updates/news/2015/13519/fsa-statement-on-footage-released-by-animal-aid
