Police are appealing to the public to help identify the man in the CCTV images.

Officers want to speak to the man in connection with a theft at a York Hotel which involved two members of staff having their purses stolen.

The incident happened at around 1.30pm on Saturday 21 January 2017 at Hotel 53 on Piccadilly in York.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Police. Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to PC Hanneke Hart.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please use reference number 12170011877 when passing on any information.
