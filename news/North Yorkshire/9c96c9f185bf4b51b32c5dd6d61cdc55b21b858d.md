Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a single vehicle collision on Thornton Lane, near High Marishes, Malton.

At around 9.30am on Sunday 22 April 2018 a single vehicle collision involving a black Ford Fiesta occurred on Thorton Lane, between High Marishes and Thornton-Le-Dale.

The 19-year-old female driver and 17-year-old male passenger sustained minor injuries as a result of the collision.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-22042018-0136

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
