North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred in Harrogate town centre.

It happened at a retail premises on Prospect Crescent in Harrogate at around 10.35am on Saturday 8 October 2016. A woman entered the store with a child and stole property from the store.

We are seeking assistance from the public in order to identify the female responsible for this theft.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Janine Walker. You can also email janine.walker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160183356.
