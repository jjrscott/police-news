North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a shed burglary that occurred in Whitby.

It happened at the Cemetery Lodge on Larpool Lane in Whitby between 6.30pm on 20 July and 7.30am on 21 July and involved a shed being broken into and three 'STHIL 360' grass strimmers being stolen.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Katie Walker.

You can also email katie.walker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150124871.

**21 July 2015**
