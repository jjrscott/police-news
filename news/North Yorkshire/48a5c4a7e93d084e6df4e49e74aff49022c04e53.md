The motorcyclist who died in a collision on the B6255 between Hawes and Ingleton on 15 August has been named as Stuart Clifford Reynolds, 52, from Bingley in West Yorkshire.

Stuart’s family have thanked the members of the public who stopped to assist at the scene and have issued the following statement:

"We would like to extend our thanks to all the people who stopped and assisted Stuart that day.

“He will be very sadly missed by all who knew and loved him.”

The family have asked that their privacy is respected during this sad time.
