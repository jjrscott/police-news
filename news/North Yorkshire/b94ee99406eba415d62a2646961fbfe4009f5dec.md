Last modified: 22 March 2021 at 04:56pm

Police are appealing for information as they investigate an attempted burglary in Kirkbymoorside.

It happened between Thursday 18 March and Saturday 20 March on West Pasture.

A window was removed from the property.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 70 Neville-Beck. You can also email louise.neville-beck@northyorkshire.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12210081616.
