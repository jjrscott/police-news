The 51-year old Scarborough man who was arrested on suspicion of murder yesterday (27 December) remains in custody for questioning.

Officers attended a property in St Johns Avenue, Scarborough, at about 6.25pm yesterday. They located a man, who was pronounced dead at the scene by paramedics.

A 51-year-old Scarborough man has been arrested on suspicion of murder. He remains in custody as enquiries continue.

We are not yet in a position to identify the victim, a local man, aged in his late 40s, at this time.
