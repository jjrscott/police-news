North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision which occurred in Cowling Village.

It happened at 5.30pm on Wednesday 19 July 2017 on the A6068 in Cowling and involved a black VW Golf, a silver Ford Focus and lso a a white BMW.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision or saw the vehicles prior to the collision to get in touch. We'd also like to speak to anyone who has any dash-cam footage or CCTV which may have captured the vehicles up to, and including, the collision.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Caroline Watson. You can also email Caroline.Watson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170127197.
