North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Spofforth near Harrogate.

It happened on Manor Garth between 8am and 7.30pm on Wednesday 8th November 2017.

The suspects entered the property via a rear glass door which was smashed and a thorough, untidy search of the property was carried out and a large amount of jewellery was taken.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
