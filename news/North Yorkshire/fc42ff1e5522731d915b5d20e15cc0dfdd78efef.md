Last modified: 16 October 2018 at 11:47am

Police have issued a CCTV image of a man they want to speak to following the theft of bank cards from a room in Swinton Park Hotel near Masham.

The cards were stolen from a guest's room on the night of 11 December 2018.

The cards were subsequently used to buy goods in various shops in the Hambleton and Cleveland area.

Anyone who can help identify the man in the CCTV image is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for PC Robert Howe or email Robert.howe@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12170221692 when passing on information.
