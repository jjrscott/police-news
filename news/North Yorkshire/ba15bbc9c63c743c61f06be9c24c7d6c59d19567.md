Officers are trying to locate family members of a York man.

Jonathan Reavill, aged 52, died at his home address in York on 21 March 2021.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death. However, enquiries so far have not revealed any next of kin for Mr Reavill.

Anyone who believes they are related to Jonathan, or can help find one of his relatives, is asked to contact the Coroners Office on 01609 643168.
