North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following an assault on a woman in York.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following an assault on a woman in York.

The incident occurred at around 8pm on Wednesday, 11 July on Bishopthorpe Road near to the Winning Post Public House.

The victim was talking to a man who is believed to then have grabbed her by the neck and attempted to kiss her.

The victim was left extremely shaken following the incident.

The suspect is described as a white man, aged in his 30s, of average build, with short spiked light brown hair. The man had a short beard and was dressed in a white t-shire and bottoms.

Anyone with information can contact North Yorkshire Police on 101.

If you would prefer to remain anonymous then the independent charity Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111.
