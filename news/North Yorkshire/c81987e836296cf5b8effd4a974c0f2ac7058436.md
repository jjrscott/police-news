North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a collision that occurred on the roundabout on the A64 above A1 junction 44, Bramham.

The collision took place at 1:35pm on Wednesday 13 January 2016 and involved a white coloured Citroen relay van and a 45 year old male cyclist on a red coloured Giant bicycle. The collision occurred on the roundabout on the A64 at Bramham, over the A1, junction 44.

The cyclist was injured at the scene andthey were taken by air ambulance to Leeds Hospital. They were released later that day with cuts and bruises.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision or anyone who recalls seeing the vehicle(s) prior to the collision, to get in touch as soon as possible to assist the investigation.

If you can help, please contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 350 Paul Stamp.

You can also email paul.stamp@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160007245.
