Police are appealing for information in relation to a distraction burglary of a lone, 87 year old woman.

It happened at around 11am on Friday 5 August 2016 at a property on the Fairway, Selby.

A man purporting to be fixing a roof, attended the victims address and managed to talk his way inside. When there he was left alone for a matter of minutes and managed to make off with items of jewellery including a gold chain and earrings.

The man was driving a white Flat-bed type van with ladders in the back.

Police are appealing for anyone who saw the van in the area at the time of the incident or thinks they might have been visited by the same man, is asked to contact police.

Dial 101, press 2 and ask to speak to DC Peter Wilson or email _peter.wilson@northyorksire.pnn.police.uk_

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number NYP-05082016-0374 when passing on any information.
