North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a robbery in York yesterday (Tuesday, 5 March).

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses following a robbery in York yesterday (Tuesday, 5 March).

The incident occurred just before 1pm on St Benedicts Road when the victim, a man in his 20s, was walking along the road when he was approached by an older man, possibly aged in his 40s who subsequently robbed him.

Officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or the events surrounding it to contact them with information.

Anyone with information which could assist with the investigation is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Michelle Gaunt. You can also email michelle.gaunt@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymously, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference 12190040238 when providing information.
