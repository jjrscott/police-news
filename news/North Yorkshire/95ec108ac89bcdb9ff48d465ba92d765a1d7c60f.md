Police investigating the robbery at Montpellier Antiques in Harrogate on the morning of Monday 16 March 2015, have arrested and charged a man.

A 24-year-old man from Manchester was arrested in Manchester on Monday 30 March 2015. He has been charged with robbery and possessing an offensive weapon.

He is due to appear at Manchester City Court today, Tuesday 31 March 2015.
