The police are looking for information in relation to a cycle theft at Yearsley Baths in York on 16 January 2020.

The bike is described as a Scott with white and brown decals and a white handle bar.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, option 2, and ask for Michael Parker. Or email michael.parker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200009068
