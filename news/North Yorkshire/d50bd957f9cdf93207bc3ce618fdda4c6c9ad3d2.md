Officers are appealing for information following an aggravated burglary in Great Ayton.

It happened in Cleveland Street at around 6.45pm on Tuesday 20 February 2018 when two offenders entered a property, threatened the occupant and left with an amount of cash.

A 16 year old boy was arrested shortly after the incident and he has subsequently been charged with aggravated burglary.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
