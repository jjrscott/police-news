Police are issuing a further appeal for witnesses of a serious collision that occurred on Long Gate in Sutton in Craven on Thursday 30 April.

The incident occurred at around 6pm on Thursday 30 April 2015 and involved a Blue Skoda Citigo and a red Honda XR125.

The driver of the Skoda, a 29 year old man from the Keighley area, sustained serious injury and was taken by ambulance to Airedale hospital. He has since been released from Hospital.

The rider of the Honda, a 17 year old man from Haworth, sustained serious injury and was conveyed by Air ambulance to Leeds general infirmary where he remains in a stable condition.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, or who saw the vehicle's being driven prior to the collision, is urged to contact the police. Dial 101, press option 2 and ask to speak to the Major Collision Investigation Team, or email MCIT@northyorkshire.police.pnn.uk.

Wednesday 13 May 2015.
