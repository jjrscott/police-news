Officers are at the scene of a serious incident in Selby.

Police attended a property in Finkle Street just before 9am this morning, where they located a man who had suffered serious injuries. A woman was also found injured.

A man in his 30s has been arrested in connection with the incident.

Members of the public are asked to avoid the area while forensic enquiries continue at the scene.
