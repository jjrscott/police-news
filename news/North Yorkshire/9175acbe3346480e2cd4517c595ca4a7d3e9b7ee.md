North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a public order incident that occurred on St Nicholas Street at Scarborough.

It happened on St Nicholas Street at 0240hrs on Thursday 6th August 2015 and involved a man in his fifties who was carrying a walking stick. A number of people were stood on St Nicholas Street and potentially witnessed the incident.

The man was arrested and has been released on bail pending further enquiries.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who witnessed the incident or had contact with the man.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Daniel Thompson. You can also email daniel.thompson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150135727.
