Update in relation to the fire at a block of flats in Fossway, York.

**Update 6.30pm on 17 March 2021**

Thankfully, none of the residents were injured in the fire.

Emergency services have now left the scene and the building has been boarded up and secured.

We would like to thank local residents for their patience and co-operation.

The woman who was arrested in connection with the incident remains in police custody at this time.

************************************

**Original message 2.30pm on 17 March 2021**

Fossway in York is currently closed at the Montrose Avenue and Pottery Lane junctions.

Emergency services are working at the scene of a fire in property on Fossway, and a number of flats have been evacuated.

A woman in her thirties has been arrested in connection with the incident.

Members of the public are urged to avoid the area.

Thank you for your co-operation at this time.
