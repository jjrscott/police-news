North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an arson that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on the building site off Blueberry Way at some time between Sunday 6 August and 1am on Monday 7 August 2017.

Two building site vehicles were set on fire causing extensive damage.

We are appealing for information about anyone that was seen acting suspiciously in the area at this time.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Peter Beard. You can also email peter.beard@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170139096.
