North Yorkshire Police would like to speak to the woman pictured about a handbag that was stolen from Boyes store in Malton on Saturday 30 July 2016.

The handbag was left mistakenly on the floor and CCTV captured images of the woman pictured picking up the bag and leaving the shop.

Anyone with any information which could assist the investigation, or anyone who recognises the woman pictured is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quote reference 12160136250 and ask to speak to Louise Neville-Beck or email Louise.Neville-Beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous you can pass information on Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
