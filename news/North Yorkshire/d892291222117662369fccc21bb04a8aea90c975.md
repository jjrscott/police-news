The following is a breakdown of the number of fine notices issued in York and North Yorkshire for breaches of the Coronavirus regulations

As of 11 May 2020 North Yorkshire Police had issued 926 fixed penalty notices during 509 incidents. Of those, 351 of the recipients had previously come to the attention of the police.

58 percent of the notices were issued to non-residents of York and North Yorkshire.
