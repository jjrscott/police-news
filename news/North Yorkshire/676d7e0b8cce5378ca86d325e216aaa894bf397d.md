North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Skipton.

It happened on the downstairs dance floor of Kooky Nightclub, Keighley Road, Skipton at 01.30am on 5 July 2015.

A 27 year old man from Lancashire was punched by an unknown person, causing an injury to his lip. The victim attended Airedale General Hospital where he was treated and subsequently discharged.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who may have witnessed the assault.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 235 David Gasson. You can also email david.gasson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150114242.
