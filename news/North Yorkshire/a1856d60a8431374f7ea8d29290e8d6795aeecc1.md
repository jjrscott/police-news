North Yorkshire Police have released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with a violent incident that took place in York.

At about 11.45pm on Saturday 4 February 2017, a fight involving several men took place inside the Three Cranes pub in St Sampsons Square, York. During the incident a number of men were ejected by pub staff.

Shortly after midnight a further incident of violence took place outside the pub in St Sampsons Square. Police attended and several men were arrested and have been bailed pending further enquiries.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured on CCTV as he may have important information that could assist the investigation.

He is described as having a shaven head and was wearing a long-sleeved beige or white coloured top at the time.

Anyone who recognises him is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for Roland Burnett or email Roland.Burnett@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12170020310 when passing information.
