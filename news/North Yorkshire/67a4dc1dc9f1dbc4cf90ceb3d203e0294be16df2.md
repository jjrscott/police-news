Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision in Acaster Malbis today.

At 11.35am on Sunday 14 August, a yellow MG Midget car was travelling on Intake Lane, towards the village of Acaster Malbis, York, when the driver, who was alone in the vehicle, lost control, left the road to the offside and collided with the hedgerow.

As a result of the collision the driver, a 35-year-old man from the Hovingham area, suffered fatal injuries.

North Yorkshire Police are asking for anyone who saw the MG prior to the collision, or the collision itself, to get in touch. Please dial 101, select option 2, ask for the Major Collision Investigation Team and quote reference 12160146392.
