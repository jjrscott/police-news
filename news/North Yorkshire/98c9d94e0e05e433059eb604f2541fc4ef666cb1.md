We can now release the name of the woman who was found dead in St John's Playing Field in York on Monday 9 November 2015.

She has been named as Azusa Nose, aged 23, from Japan. She was a student at the University of York.

An inquest into her death was opened and adjourned on Friday 13 November 2015.

Police believe there are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.
