Police are appealing for information regarding a collision between a female cyclist and a blue Jewson's lorry which occurred at around 8am Wednesday 8 March 2017 at the junction of James Street and Hazel Court in York.

The blue Jewson’s lorry was turning left from Hazel Court onto James Street and the cyclist was travelling along James Street towards Lawrence Street, when the collision occurred.

The cyclist, a 25-year-old woman from the York area suffered serious injuries and was taken by air ambulance to Leeds General Infirmary where she remains in a serious condition.

The driver of the goods vehicle did not sustain any injuries and voluntarily attended the Police station to assist officers with the investigation.

The road was closed until 1pm to allow police to examine the scene and the vehicles involved.

Police are appealing for any witnesses to the collision or anyone who saw the Jewson’s lorry or cyclist who was riding a mountain cycle approaching the junction to contact them on 101 and speak to TC1532 Harrison of the Tadcaster Road Policing Group. Please quote reference 12170038948.
