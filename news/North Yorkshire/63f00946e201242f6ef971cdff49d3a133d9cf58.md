North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred in Harrogate.

It happened on Crab Lane at 7.45am on Monday 18 March and involved two males wearing balaclavas stealing items from a delivery vehicle.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the identities of the two male suspects and their whereabouts leading up to and following the incident.

One suspect has been described as a white man in his 20s, approximately 6ft 1in wearing a grey coat, black trousers, black gloves and a black balaclava.

The second has been described as a white man in his 20s, approximately 5ft 11in with ginger hair and wearing a black hoodie, black gloves a hi-vis vest and a black balaclava.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Sarah Weatherstone. You can also email sarah.weatherstone@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190048499.
