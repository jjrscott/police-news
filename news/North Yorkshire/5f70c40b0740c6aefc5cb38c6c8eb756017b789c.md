North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Harrogate.

The incident happened at approximately 4am on 3 July 2015 outside the Viper Rooms.

The incident involved a group of men some of which have already been arrested or identified. There were a number of assaults during this incident and North Yorkshire Police are looking for any further witnesses or victims that have not yet come forward.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident. If you were assaulted at this time or witnessed the incident, please contact us.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Samantha Sutcliffe.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150112015.
