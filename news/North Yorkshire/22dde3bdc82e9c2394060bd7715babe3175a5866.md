Police were called at 11.15pm on Friday 12 August to reports of a man with serious injuries in Burnholme Drive, York.

The man, in his forties, had been assaulted and sustained serious head injuries. He was taken to Leeds General Infirmary.

Officers have been conducting house-to-house enquiries and searches in the area. Anyone with any information that could assist the investigation is asked to phone 101, select option 2 and ask for York Serious Crime Team. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote reference number 12160145406.
