North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Scarborough.

It happened on the cinder track below Cross Lane Bridge and Princess Close at about 6.30pm on Sunday 23 April 2017, and involved a man assaulting a woman.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who may have been on the cinder track at that location at that time and anyone who may have witnessed the incident.

Witnesses also reported that a woman on a bike stopped to assist the female victim. Were you the woman on a bike?

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 21 Katie Walker. You can also email katie.walker@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
