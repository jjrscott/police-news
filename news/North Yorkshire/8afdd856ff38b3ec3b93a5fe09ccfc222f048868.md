North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an attempt theft of farming equipment that occurred on a farm near Kirk Deighton

It happened near Deighton Bank near Kirk Deighton and Wetherby between the hours of 22:18 on the 17th September 2015 and 06:28 hours 18th September 2015 and involved an attempt theft of a high value tractor and sprayer.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding this.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any vehicles or persons seen acting suspiciously along Wetherby Lane over night on the dates mentioned.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 46 Brendon Frith. You can also email Brendon.Frith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150164798
