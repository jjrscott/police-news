Single vehicle collision occurred on Knott Road in North York Moors.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information after a serious single car collision which occurred around 1pm on Saturday 2 May 2020. The collision took place on Knott Road, north of Rosedale Abbey in the North York Moors and involved a blue Audi A3.

One of the vehicle’s occupants, a 21 year old male from Middlesbrough was transported to James Cook Hospital by air ambulance with serious injuries. Two other occupants of the vehicle were arrested and released under investigation.

If you witnessed the vehicle prior to the collision, have dashcam footage or any other information which could assist the investigation, please contact North Yorkshire Police’s Major Collision Investigation Team on 01609 643185 or by email: Julie.brown@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk quoting reference number: 12200072984.
