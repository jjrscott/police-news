North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of quad bikes from Thornton in Craven.

It happened at Thornton Hall farm overnight on 22/08/2015 and involved the theft of five Suzuki 50cc children's quad bikes from a container at the farm and then moving them across a field to the main Church Road in Thornton in Craven before disappearing.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone who saw any suspicious people or vehicles in the area of Church Road near to Thornton Hall Farm over the night of 22 to 23 August 2015.

Also, is anyone is offered the bikes for sale in suspicious circumstances, please contact the police.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1838 MIDDLEMISS You can also email andrew.middlemiss@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150147918
