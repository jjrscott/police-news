Police have issued a CCTV still of a man they would like to speak to following an incident of criminal damage in Knaresborough.

At about 1.50am on Friday 30 June 2017, a man and a woman climbed over a wall to gain access to Knaresborough Bowling Club on Park Parade, Knaresborough.

Whilst in the grounds of the bowling green the man kicked open a secure door and broke the lock.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured on CCTV, as he may have important information that could assist the investigation.

If you recognise him, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Pierre Olesqui or email Pierre.Olesqui@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170116855 when passing information.
