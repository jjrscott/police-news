North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault in York that left a man with a broken nose.

The victim, in his 20s, was headbutted by an unknown man in what is believed to be an unprovoked attack.

It happened on Duncombe Place at around 3.50am on Sunday (10 December 2017).

The victim attended hospital and was treated for a broken nose.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information from anyone who was in the area at that time and saw the assault.

The suspect is described as a white male, approximately 5ft10, aged between 20 and 30 and was wearing a red hat.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Harriet Shillito. You can also email harriet.shillito@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170222419.
