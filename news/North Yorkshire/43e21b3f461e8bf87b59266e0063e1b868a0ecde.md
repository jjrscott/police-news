Police are appealing for information about a serious collision which has left a 47-year-old woman with serious injuries..

The collision happened at around 1.45am on Sunday 28 July 2019 on Railway Street in Malton involving an unknown vehicle and a 47 year-old woman.

The collision left the woman with a fractured ankle and bruising and she was taken to hospital for treatment.

The vehicle, described as a small car, did not stop at the scene.
