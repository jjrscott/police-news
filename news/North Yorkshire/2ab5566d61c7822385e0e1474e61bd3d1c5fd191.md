Police investigating an incident in York when a man was forced into a car on Butcher’s Terrace on Bishopthorpe Road, have issued a fresh appeal for information.

Detectives specifically want to hear from anyone who witnessed an altercation involving a group of men who arrived at the Winning Post pub on Bishopthorpe Road in two vehicles at around 5pm on 24 April 2017.

Anyone who can assist the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for York Serious Crime Team. Or email adam.parkinson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12170070137 when passing on information.

To recap, the original incident was reported by members of the public at around 5.28pm on Monday 24 April 2017, after a car pulled up in Butcher’s Terrace, Bishopthorpe Road, followed by another containing three men.

The three men forced the driver of the first car to into their car and drove off.

Four men have been arrested in connection with the incident and released on bail while the investigation continues.
