Last modified: 18 February 2020 at 05:03pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident that occurred in Brough with St Giles.

It happened on Catterick Road at around 1.20pm on Monday 10 February 2020 whereby an altercation occurred between three men.

We are appealing for information from any eyewitnesses to establish the full circumstances of the incident.

Were you driving or walking on Catterick Road, Brough with St Giles near to the mini roundabout and LIDL?

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC James Crawford. You can also email James.Crawford@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200023842.
