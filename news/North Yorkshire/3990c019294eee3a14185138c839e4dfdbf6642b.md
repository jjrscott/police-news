North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a theft and an affray which occurred at Marks & Spencer in Skipton.

It happened at about 4.50pm on Sunday 12 March 2017, and involved a man running from the store towards the main car park before getting into a white Ford Focus estate.
