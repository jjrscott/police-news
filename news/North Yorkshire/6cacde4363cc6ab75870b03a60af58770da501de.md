Police are investigating a road traffic collision involving a coach that hit the central reservation on the A64 near to Grimston Bar in York.

It occurred on Thursday 9 November at around 4.40pm.

Two children were hurt but fortunately their injuries were not serious.

Did you witness the incident and do you have any dashcam footage?

If so please contact North Yorkshire Police via telephone 101 and select option 1, quoting incident number 12170205463.
