At around 4.30pm on 24 December 2020, police were called by the ambulance service to the sudden death of a woman in her 50s at Tesco in Selby after she was found collapsed and unresponsive.

Police believe there are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

A post mortem is due to take place to help determine the cause of her death.
