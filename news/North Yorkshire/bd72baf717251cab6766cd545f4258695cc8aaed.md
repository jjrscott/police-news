UPDATE: The male worker at the plant has been taken to York District Hospital for treatment. The cordon has now been lifted and the incident has now been passed onto the Health and Safety Executive.

Emergency services are currently dealing with an incident at the Industrial Chemicals Group Ltd in Canal View, Selby

Police were informed about the incident at 10.15pm today by the ambulance service, who were at the scene treating a worker who had fallen ill.

There is a cordon in place and specialist teams are working at the scene. This is due to necessary safety precautions in relation to the chemicals at the plant.

Police would like to reassure the public there is not believed to be any threat to the safety of the wider community.
