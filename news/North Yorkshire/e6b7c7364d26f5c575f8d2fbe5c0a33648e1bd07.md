At around 8.30pm on Thursday, 14 June, North Yorkshire Police received a call regarding the welfare of a teenage boy from Egton.

An immediate search of areas around Egton and Whitby took place in a bid to locate the 17-year-old.

To assist in the search for the boy a number of enquiries at local venues which the boy had connection to were carried out.

At around 10.20pm the boy was located at Blakey Ridge in Castleton. He was pronounced dead at the scene and his next of kin has been informed.

The death is being treated as unexplained and an investigation to establish the full circumstances is now being carried out.
