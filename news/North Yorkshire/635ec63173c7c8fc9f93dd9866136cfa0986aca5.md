Police are appealing for witnesses and dashcam footage after a fatal collision in York city centre.

Officers were called to reports of a collision involving a grey Skoda Octavia and a pedestrian at about 2.30am on Sunday 9 February 2020.

It happened at the junction of Station Road and Rougier Street.

Sadly the pedestrian, a man in his 30s from Leeds, was pronounced dead at the scene.

The road was closed until about 10.30am while emergency services worked at the scene.

Police are appealing for any witnesses – or anyone who has dashcam footage that may assist the investigation – to contact them. Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask for the Major Collision Investigation Team or the officer in the case TC Gemma Brett. Alternatively email MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Please quote reference number 12200022915 when passing information.
