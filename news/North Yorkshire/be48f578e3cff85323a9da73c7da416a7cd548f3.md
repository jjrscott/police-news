North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a injury collision that occurred on the A170 between Allerston and Ebberston.

It happened at 12.25pm on 29 December 2015 on the A170 between Allerston and Ebberston and involved a male motorbike rider from Scarborough receiving serious injuries as a result of the collision.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about a beige-coloured motor vehicle that pulled out of the junction of Hagg Side Lane and travelled towards Pickering.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Steve Ashley. You can also email stephen.ashley@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150230512.
