North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred in Selby.

It happened at O2, 3 Gowthorpe, Selby at 15:40 on 26/01/2016 and involved an individual who entered the store, selected a demo Sony Z5 Premium Gold mobile phone with IMEI: 354321070032513 from the display and exited the store without paying.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the identity of the suspect or anyonwe who has purchased a phone matching this description and corresponding IMEI number.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC122 SMITH. You can also email Lee.smith@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160015561.
