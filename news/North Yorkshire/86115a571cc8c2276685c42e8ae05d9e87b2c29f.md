North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a criminal damage that occurred on Nelson Street in Scarborough.

It happened between 9:30pm on Wednesday 22nd March 2017 and 8:30am the following morning when the victim awoke to find one of the tyres on her vehicle had been slashed.

The vehicle was a black Ford Focus that had been parked on the Columbus Ravine end of Nelson Street.

We are in the process of conducting enquiries in an attempt to identify those responsible for the damage.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Liam Cromack. You can also email liam.cromack@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170048811.
