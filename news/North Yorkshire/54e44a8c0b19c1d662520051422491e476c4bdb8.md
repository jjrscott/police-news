Detectives in York are investigating a serious assault which left a man in his 40s with a broken leg.

The victim needed extensive hospital treatment after an ambulance was called to assist him on Foss Island Road, near the former Carpet Right store.

The incident happened on 20 February 2019 but was only recently brought to the attention of police.

Detectives are keen to hear from anyone who helped the 47-year-old victim after the incident, or witnessed what happened to him.

Anyone with information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for York CID.

You can also email Beverley.Hoban@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12190047350 when sharing information.
