A man has been charged to court following a sexual assault in Selby town centre in the early hours of 6 November 2016.

He was charged with sexual assault and remanded in police custody to appear at York Magistrates' Court on Thursday 10 November 2016. Following this first appearance, he was again remanded in custody to appear at court again the following day (Friday 11 November 2016).
