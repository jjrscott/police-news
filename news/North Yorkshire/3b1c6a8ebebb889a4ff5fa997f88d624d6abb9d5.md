North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a Malton pub was burgled.

It happened at the New Globe public house, Yorkersgate Malton at around 5.30am on Monday 5 January 2015.

A suspect wearing distinctive camouflage trousers, a beanie hat and had their face covered with a bandana, forced entry to the pub via a kitchen window and stole some cash.

Officers are appealing to anyone who saw any suspicious people in the area or have any other information which could assist the investigation, to contact them.

Anyone with any information should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC William Knapp, or email William.Knapp@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you would prefer to remain anonymous, information can be passed to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150002275
