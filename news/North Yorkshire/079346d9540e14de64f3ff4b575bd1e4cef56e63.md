Last modified: 23 September 2019 at 04:03pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a road-related incident that occurred on Sunday 15 September 2019.

It happened between 7.25am and 8am on the A1 northbound between the Catterick Garrison interchange at Junction 52, and Junction 53 Scotch Corner.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident, which relate to a vehicle’s manner of driving. The involved vehicle of interest is a black Renault Megan with two occupants.

In particular, we are appealing for witnesses and any dashcam footage.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Steven Gardner. You can also email at steven.gardner@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk
If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190171285.
