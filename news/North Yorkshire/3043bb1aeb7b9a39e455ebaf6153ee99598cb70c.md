North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred in Malton.

It happened on Horsemarket Road between 6.55pm and 7.10pm on Saturday 3 March 2018. A large white Luton style van is believed to have reversed into the car park of the business premises. The offender then loaded two calving gates into the rear of the man and quickly drove off from the area.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about anyone who saw the van in the area at the time, has any cctv that may assist or who has had any calving gates offered to them for a small price.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Pc &0 L Neville- Beck. You can also email louise.neville-beck@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
