Police investigating a theft from a motor vehicle are appealing for the rightful owner of a Makita DHP482 power drill to come forward.

The incident occurred on Scotchman Lane at Flaxton, Ryedale, between 11am and 5.30pm on 21 December 2019.
The Makita power drill was recovered as part of ongoing enquiries.

Anyone who believes the drill belongs to them or someone they know, please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Adam Marshall. Or email adam.marshall@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Please quote reference number 12190233209.
