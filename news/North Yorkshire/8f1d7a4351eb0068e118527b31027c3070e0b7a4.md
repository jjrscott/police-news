Last modified: 2 April 2019 at 01:26pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information in relation to a spate of thefts and attempted thefts from large goods vehicles at Barton Services and the A66 Eastbound near to Gilling West, Richmond.

The incidents happened between 2am and 2.15am, during which time, the vehicle curtain sides were cut and property stolen.

Officers are appealing for anyone who was in the area at the time to get in touch.

In particular, we are appealing for any persons with dash cam footage to give us a call.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Holly Grosvenor. You can also email holly.Grosvenor@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190058728
