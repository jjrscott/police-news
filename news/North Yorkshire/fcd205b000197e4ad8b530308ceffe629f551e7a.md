Police are appealing for witnesses to alleged assault on Stokesley High Street to contact them.

At 1pm on Monday 17 July 2017 a verbal altercation between two men started outside Cooplands bakery on the High Street. The altercation continued as the two men walked down the street, towards the Natwest Bank.

One male then got into a parked black Mercedes car, when he was allegedly physically assaulted by the other man through the open car window.

Police are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the two men arguing or the alleged assault to contact them, so they can determine the full circumstances around the incident.

Please dial 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to PC 1924 Michael Wilson. You can also email Michael.Wilson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170125592

Alternatively you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
