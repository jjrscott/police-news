Last modified: 26 June 2019 at 12:51pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Strensall, York.

It happened on The Willows between 4pm and 5pm on Sunday 23 June when a group of three or four men who had their faces covered were seen leaving the property and driving off in a blue Volkswagen Golf R at speed.

Officers are appealing for witnesses to the incident including anyone who can help identify the men in the Golf.

A large amount of jewellery was stolen including Asian gold jewellery and officers are also appealing to anyone who has been offered it for sale to contact them.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 731 SIMPSON You can also email sophie.simpson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190113168.
