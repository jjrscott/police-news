Police want to trace two people pictured by CCTV following a house burglary in Selby.

The incident happened at around 6.55pm on Saturday, 7 October 2017 in Fordlands, Thorpe Willoughby.

Intruders broke in through a first-floor window and stole jewellery.

They made off when the burglar alarm sounded.

Police investigating the incident want to speak to two people pictured in the area by CCTV.

If you recognise them, or if you have any other information about the incident, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Robert Campbell.

You can also email Sergeant Campbell at robert.campbell@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

You can also call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12170180483 when sharing information.
