Police are appealing for information and witnesses to an attempted handbag robbery in Harrogate, to come forward.

At around 11.25am on Sunday 17 June 2018 the victim was walking down Duchy Avenue, when she was approached from behind by a man. The man grabbed hold of her handbag and attempted to take it from her.

After struggling with the victim for a couple of seconds, the man gave up and released the bag and made off from the area, back up Duchy Avenue, in the direction of Glebe Road. The victim, a 34-year-old woman from Harrogate, was left unhurt but very shaken by the incident.

The man is described as in his late 20â€™s, around 5 feet 11 inches tall and clean shaven. He is described as having a large, chubby build and was wearing a dark coloured baseball cap, dark coloured tracksuit bottoms and an orange hoody with a dark coloured jacket over the top.

Officers are appealing for anyone who has any information which could assist the investigation to contact police on 101, select option 2 and ask to speak to PC Kelvin Troughton. Alternatively you can email Kelvin.Troughton@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk Please quote reference 12180107736.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
