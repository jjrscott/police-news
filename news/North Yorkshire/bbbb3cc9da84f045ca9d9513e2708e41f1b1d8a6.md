North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in York.

It happened on Wilberforce Avenue at approximately 4.45pm on Sunday 20 September 2020, and involved a 32-year-old woman from York who was punched whilst with her 15-year-old daughter.

The woman was left understandably shaken by the incident but escaped serious injury.

Three women aged 22, 23 and 25 have been questioned in relation to the matter, and released under investigation whilst enquiries continue.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the assault and anybody who may have witnessed it.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Herrick. You can also email amy.herrick@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12200165576.
