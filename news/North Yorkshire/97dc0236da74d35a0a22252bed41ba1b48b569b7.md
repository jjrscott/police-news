Police, fire and ambulance are at the scene of a serious collision on the A1 northbound near junction 50 (Ripon/Baldersby).

It was reported just before 7am on Tuesday 22 May 2018 and involves a car transporter and another lorry.

The road is closed with diversions in place.

It is expected to remain closed for most of today.

Drivers are urged to avoid the area.

Witnesses to the collision should call North Yorkshire Police on 101, and pass information to the Force Control Room for incident number 60 on 22 May.
