North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a cyclist reportedly behaved aggressively towards a bus driver in the city.

**Thank you everyone for your assistance, we have now identified this man.**

The incident occurred on Bishopgate Street at approximately 3.50pm on Thursday 5 December 2019 and involved a male cyclist reportedly stopping a double decker bus by standing in front of it and shouting at the bus driver.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for collar number 1404 Luke Steele. You can also email luke.steele@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190223064.
