North Yorkshire Police are appealing for witnesses and information about a shoplifting offence that occurred in Skipton.

It happened at Rackhams department store at 2.40pm on Sunday 2nd August 2015 and involved a woman with black hair pushing a pram who stole two bottles of perfume. Members of the public are asked to view the picture of the offender the police have obtained and try and identify her.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC 1969 Adam Steventon. You can also email adamsteventon@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150133278.

Date 3rd August 2015.
