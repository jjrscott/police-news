Police in Scarborough are appealing for help to find the owner of a suspected stolen mountain bike found in Scarborough.

It was handed into the police by a member of the public who thinks it may have been stolen and dumped outside of their property.

It has a black, green and white frame with "Earl Raster" printed on it.

If you can help, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Lorna Pearcey. Or email Lorna.Pearcey@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number: 12200106492.
