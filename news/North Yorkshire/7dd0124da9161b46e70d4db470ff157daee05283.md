North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a "2-in-1" burglary that occurred on Regents Crescent, Skipton.

It happened overnight on 23/24th April 2015 and involved the taking of a blue Volvo V70, registration RA05XXE, after the property had been entered.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the whereabouts of the blue Volvo and any information about the offender(s).

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Andrew Middlemiss. You can also email andrew.middlemiss@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
