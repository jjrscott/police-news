Police have arrested three men on suspicion of theft from a van, after a report of suspicious behaviour was made by a member of the public.

Police were alerted at 2.07am, Friday 8 September 2017 to a suspected vehicle break-in and tools having been stolen from a van on Duncombe Drive in Strensall.

The member of the public gave a description of two men in the area, acting suspiciously and being dressed in dark clothing, with hoods up. They also reported a vehicle driving loudly and at speed in the area around 1.45am.

As enquiries were being made, an officer from the Roads Policing Group, conducted a search of the local area and stopped a vehicle. Following a search of the vehicle, suspected stolen tools were recovered.

A 21-year-old man from York, a 31-year-old man from York and a 31-year-old man from Strensall were arrested on suspicion of theft from a motor vehicle and currently remain in police custody.

Our thanks go to the vigilant member of the public who contacted police.
