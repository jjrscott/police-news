Police are investigating a serious single-vehicle collision at Suffield in the Scarborough district.

A silver BMW car collided with a tree on Swang Road at around 7.45pm on Saturday (2 June 2018).

Emergency services were called to the scene to recover the driver, a local man aged in his late 20s, from the badly damaged car.

He was airlifted to Hull Royal Infirmary where he is in a critical but stable condition.

Local diversions were put in place by highways while the police investigated the scene and to allow the safe recovery of the car.

The road re-opened at around 12.30am on Sunday.

Witnesses or anyone with information that could assist the investigation are urged to call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 1, and speak to the Force Control Room. Please quote reference number 12180097627.
