North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an incident of public disorder that occurred at 2pm -2.15pm on Sunday 17 January 2016 at the junction of Highfield Road and Birch Avenue, Malton.

Any witnesses or anyone with information is asked to contact PC Andy Grant by dialling 101 and selecting option 2. Alternatively, email andy.grant@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Please quote reference number 12160009602.
