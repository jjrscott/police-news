North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Selby.

The incident happened close to the retail park on Scott Road at approximately 5.35pm on 18 October. It involved three teenagers, all male, who were all on bicycles. Two of the teenagers assaulted the other teenager. All three teenagers are from the Selby area.

No injuries were caused to the victim, aged 19, during the incident.

Two teenage boys, aged 15 and 16 years of age, have been arrested in connection with the incident. They have been released on bail pending further enquiries.
