Police in the Skipton area are asking for witnesses and information following an incident involving the driver of a van and a 10-year-old boy.

It happened between 6pm and 6.15pm on 10 May 2018, when the boy was walking through the centre of Threshfield near the Old Hall Public House. A man in a white van, described as old and rusty, pulled up next to him.

The man offered the boy a sweet and a lift home but he refused and ran home to tell his parents. The van eventually drove off towards Skipton.

Police are asking for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or who saw a rusty white van in Threshfield on the evening of 10 May to contact them.Anyone with any information is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 and pass information to the Force Control Room.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 12180083228 when passing on information.
