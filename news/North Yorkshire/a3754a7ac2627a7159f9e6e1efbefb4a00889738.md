North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a minor road traffic collision that occurred on Norwood Street, Scarborough.

It happened on Norwood Street, Scarborough, at around 6.35pm on Tuesday 25 August 2015. A beige or gold-coloured estate car hit a Ford Galaxy on the street. The estate car failed to stop at the scene after damaging the Galaxy.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about the beige or gold-coloured estate.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Helen Crane.

You can also email helen.crane@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150149687.
