Police have released CCTV images of two women they would like to speak to regarding the theft of a number of items from Smyths Toys on Clifton Moor retail Park in York.

Two women were seen entering the store, accompanied by a boy, at around 2.30pm on Saturday 10 June 2017.

A black and orange push scooter, a toy tool set and workbench and a number of minecraft booksets were selected. The two women and boy then left the store, having made no attempt to pay for the items.

Police are appealing to anyone who recognises the two women pictured to contact them on 101 and ask to speak to PC Nikki Jackson. Alternatively you can email Nikki.Jackson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference 12170104433.

Alternatively Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.
