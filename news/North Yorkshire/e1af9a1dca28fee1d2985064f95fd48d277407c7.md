North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Skipton.

At about 7pm on Tuesday 30 May 2017 Police attended on Moorview Way at the junction of Shortbank Road, Skipton, to a report of a male having been assaulted.

A group of youths were seen to be involved in the incident and made off from the scene prior to police arrival.

The victim subsequently received treatment from ambulance staff for injuries to his face and head.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident to come forward.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Gasson. You can also email david.gasson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170093859.
