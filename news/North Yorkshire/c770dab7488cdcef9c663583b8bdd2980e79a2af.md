North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a child's tablet that occurred in York.

The incident occurred at the Home Bargains store on Foss Islands Road on Saturday 27 July 2019 at approximately 10.30am.

A child's had taken their tablet into the store, and had mistakenly left it in a basket. When the family realised the child's tablet was left behind they returned to the store. However, it had been removed fromt he basket before they had chance to retrieve it.

Police have released a CCTV image of a woman they would like to speak to, as they believe she holds information which would be vital to their enquiries.

Anyone who recognises the woman pictured, or who has information that could assist the investigation is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC Alicia Oakes. You can also email alicia.oakes@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12190136827
