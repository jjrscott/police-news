North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a burglary that occurred in Whitby.

It happened around mid-afternoon on Friday 27 March 2015 at a property on Westbourne Close.

Offenders approached a detached bungalow, walked into the rear garden via an insecure gate at side of house and entered the property through an open bedroom window.

Once inside the offenders stole a quantity of jewellery.

The stolen property included:

Gold link bracelet.

Gold link bracelet with heart lock.

Gold solid bangle.

Gold thick rope bracelet.

Gold crucifix on chain.

Rose gold belcher chain.

Gold chain with St. Christopher.

Half gold sovereign.

Platinum solitaire engagement ring.

Silver jet ring.

Gold russina puzzle ring.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Neale Graham (Whitby CID).

You can also email neale.graham@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150049783.
