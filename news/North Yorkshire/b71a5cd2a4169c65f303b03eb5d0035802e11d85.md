Last modified: 25 March 2021 at 08:57pm

Emergency services are at the scene of a fire in Avenue Grove, Harrogate.

It was reported to North Yorkshire Police at around 8.20pm on Thursday 25 March.

North Yorkshire Fire and Rescue Service are in attendance along with Yorkshire Ambulance Service.

Properties nearby have been evacuated and a cordon has been put in place.

Members of the public are advised to avoid the area while emergency services deal with the incident.

Two people are being treated for smoke inhalation. There are no reports of any further injuries at this time.
