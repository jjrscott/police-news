Last modified: 21 February 2020 at 12:33pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a suspected fail-to-stop collision on the A19 at Crathorne.

The incident occurred on the north-bound carriageway at around 12.05am on Friday (21 February 2020) and it involved a black Vauxhall Corsa and an unknown vehicle which did not stop at the scene.

The driver of the Corsa, a 23-year-old man from Hartlepool, suffered a slight cut to his head and whiplash after his car came off the road and overturned in a ditch. He was treated by paramedics.

The car had to be recovered and the road was clear by 1.15am. The Highways Agency assisted with the incident.

Police are seeking witnesses to the collision or for the driver of the other vehicle to come forward to help the investigation.

Please call North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Steven Wood. Or email Steven.Wood161@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk. Please quote reference number 12200030423 when providing details.
