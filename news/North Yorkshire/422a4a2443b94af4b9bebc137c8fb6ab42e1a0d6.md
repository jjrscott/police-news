Last modified: 14 March 2019 at 05:06pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses to two house burglaries in the Weeton area.

Between 7.45pm on Monday 11 March 2019 and 8am Tuesday 12 March 2019, two burglaries occurred at properties on Weeton Lane in Weeton.

A Silver Audi car registration number YA14NLL was stolen from the driveway of one of the houses, along with an amount of cash and items of jewellery

Police are appealing for any witnesses who may have seen anything suspicious in the area at the time to contact them.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask to speak to Elizabeth Estensen. You can also email elizabeth.estensen @northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference numbers 12190044371 or 12190044572.
