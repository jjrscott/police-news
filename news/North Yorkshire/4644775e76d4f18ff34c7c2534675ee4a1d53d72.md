Police are appealing for information to locate a wanted York man.

Danny Hughes, 30, is wanted on suspicion of harassment, assault and public order offences.

Officers have been searching for him in the York area, where he is believed to be, but he has not yet been arrested.

Anyone with information about Hughes' whereabouts is asked to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, quoting reference 12200224933.
