The incident happened at around 3.55pm yesterday, Sunday 17 April 2016, on Brackenhill Close.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or who has any information which could assist the investigation, is asked to contact police.

Police are particularly interested in events leading up to the altercation and if anyone saw or heard people shouting or making threats.

Two women, aged 38 and 27, and a man, 26, all from the Brayton area, have been arrested in connection with the incident. All have been bailed pending further enquiries.

Dial 101, press 1 and pass information to the force control room.

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contact anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number NYP-17042016-0319 when passing on information.

Extra police patrols, in partnership with Housing Officers, will remain in the area over the coming days.
