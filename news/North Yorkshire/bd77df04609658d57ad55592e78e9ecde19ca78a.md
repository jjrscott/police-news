Police in York are appealing for witnesses and information after a woman was arrested on suspicion of assaulting a man.

The incident happened on Haxby Road when the 30-year-old woman and 29-year-old man had left The Punchbowl at around 10.30pm on Saturday 29 August 2020.

Following police enquiries, the woman was arrested for assault and resisting arrest. She has been released on conditional police bail to mid-September while enquiries continue.
