North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft that occurred in Skipton Town Centre.

It happened at Providence Place, Skipton- the old Skipton Building Society site off Court Lane overnight of the 10th/11th March 2015 and involved the theft of electrical cable.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about any suspicious activity noted and any suspect vehicles seen in the area.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for PC1831 Martin CARSON. You can also e.mail Martin.carson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150039957.
