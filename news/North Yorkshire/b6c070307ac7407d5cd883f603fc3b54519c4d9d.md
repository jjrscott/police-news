Last modified: 17 October 2018 at 04:32pm

Police are appealing for information following an aggravated burglary at a property on Bramham Road, York.

The incident happened at 12.20pm on Tuesday 16 January 2018.

Three masked suspects entered the address, which was occupied by a woman and her three-year-old child, and made various demands of the victim before making their escape.

The victim sustained minor injuries to her leg as a result of the incident and has been left in shock. Fortunately the child was not hurt during the ordeal.

Officers are appealing for anyone whom may have seen anything suspicious, or who has any information about the incident, to contact police.

Please dial 101, press 2 and ask for the York Serious Crime Team or Andrew Yates. Alternatively, you can e-mail SCTYork@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or Andrew.Yates@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12180008585 when passing on information about the incident.
