North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Acomb

It happened on Barkston Road in Acomb on Tuesday 29 of October 2019 at around 6.50pm and involved a man in his fifties being punched in the face by an unknown male. The incident resulted in the victim receiving swelling to his face.
