Police were notified at 4.30pm Saturday 13 October 2018 to a fire at the B&M; store on Clifton Moor in York

Emergency services are in attendance and the A1237 has been closed between the Wigginton roundabout and Tesco.

Due to thick smoke, visibility is limited and driving conditions are hazardous. Motorists are advised to avoid the area.

Advice has been issued to residents in the Clifton Moor, Haxby, Wigginton and Strensall areas to stay indoors and close doors and windows as the smoke from the fire passes over.
