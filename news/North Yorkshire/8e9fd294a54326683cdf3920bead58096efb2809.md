Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a serious road traffic collision in York.

The incident occurred at around 7.15pm on Monday 21 September on Monkgate, York, and involved a blue Ford Transit van and two pedestrians.

The driver of the van, a 27 year old man form York, was arrested on suspicion of drug driving. He has been released on bail pending further enquiries.

The two pedestrians, a man and a woman, both 52 form York, were taken to York District Hospital. Their injuries are not believed to be life threatening.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision, or who saw the blue Transit van, which was travelling into the city centre from Heworth, to get in touch.

Dial 101, press option 2 and ask to speak to TC Mark Patterson or emailmark.patterson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference number 12150166598 when passing on information about this incident.
