Police in York are continuing to investigate a serious assault on a local 20-year-old man at a York nightclub.

The incident occurred around the front doors of Club Jalou on Micklegate at approximately midnight on 30 December 2017.

The victim was punched once to the face by a man aged in his late teens. He suffered several fractures to the jaw, a split lip and a bloodied nose. He was taken to hospital and is still recovering from his injuries.

We are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the assault and is willing to provide a statement in relation to the incident.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Ben Hepworth. You can also email ben.hepworth@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12180002773 when providing details about this incident.
