North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a number of incidents that occurred in York City Centre.

Officers are appealing for witnesses to come forward following a number of incidents that occurred around the Exhibition Square and Clarence Street area of York.

The incidents took place between 10pm and 10.30pm on Tuesday 18 April 2017, when it is believed a man attempted to steal property from a number of women.

The man is described as mixed race, in his early 20's, with hair that had a shaved line down the side. He was wearing a black tracksuit with white stripes.
