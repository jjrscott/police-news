Two men from Scarborough have been arrested on suspicion of money laundering.

Officers stopped the car they were travelling in on the Whitby to Guisborough Road at Scaling Dam earlier today (10 April).

After searching the car, officers discovered a large amount of cash - believed to be several thousands of pounds, hidden in cavity.

The pair, who are aged 28 and 29 were arrested on suspicion of money laundering.

The driver (28) was also arrested on suspicion of drug driving.

They both remain in police custody at this time.
