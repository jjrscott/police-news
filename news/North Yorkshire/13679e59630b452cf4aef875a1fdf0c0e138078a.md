Officers in York are investigating after a cash machine in Acomb was damaged.

Police were called to the Co-op shop on Beckfield Lane at around 5.10am today.

Enquries and forensic tests are currently taking place and it is not currently believed that any money was stolen.

Officers are asking anyone who saw anything suspicious in the area, or who has any other information that could help the investigation, to call 101 quoting reference number 12180214026.
