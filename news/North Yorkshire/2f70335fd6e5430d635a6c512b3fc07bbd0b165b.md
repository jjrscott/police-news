Police in Colburn near Catterick have arrested two men on suspicion of supplying class B drugs.

On the evening of Wednesday 3 July 2019, officers executed a warrant under the Misuse of Drugs Act at a property in the Woodside Chase area.

Two men aged 27 and 33 were arrested at the property on suspicion of possessing class B drugs with intent to supply.

Offices also seized a significant quantity of suspected illegal drugs along with items believed to be used in their supply

The two men remain in police custody at this time.

Anyone who suspects drug dealing is happening in their area should report it. Every piece of information, no matter how small, could help us piece together a bigger picture and help to shape our police activity.

Please look out for the following signs:

Increased callers at a property
Increase in cars pulling up for short periods of time
Different accents at a property
Increased antisocial behaviour at a property
Not seeing the resident for long periods of time
Unfamiliar vehicles at the property
Windows covered or curtains closed for long periods
Call North Yorkshire Police on 101, if you prefer not to speak to the police and want to remain anonymous, you pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, or online at crimestoppers-uk.org
