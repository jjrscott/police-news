North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of a cycle that occurred in Scarborough.

View image

It happened 6.30pm hours on Longwestgate, Scarborough, and involved two teenagers entering the communal area of a block of flats via an insecure door and stealing a cycle secured to a barrier.

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
