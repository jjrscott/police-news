North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information after a ;Land Rover Defender 110 from Coxwold near York.

It happened in Byland Road, Coxwold, between 8pm on Saturday 16 July 2016 and 9am the following day. Unknown persons have attended a garage where they have caused damage to a Landrover and have stolen another. The stolen vehicle is a Land Rover 110 TDI in grey with a green bonnet. Registration number YB04 XNN.

We are conducting enquiries and are appealing to anyone who has saw any suspicious activity, people or vehicles in the area in the run up to the theft, to contact us.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for zero two zero five. You can also email Matthew.Balmforth@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12160126560
