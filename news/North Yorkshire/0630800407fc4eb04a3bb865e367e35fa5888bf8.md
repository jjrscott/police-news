North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred on Tanner Row in York.

It happened on Tanner Row between 12.30am and 1am on 1 January 2018 and involved an altercation between a man and a group of eight other men and a woman.

During the altercation, a 34 year old man was assaulted by another man. He suffered a black eye and bruising to his face and went to hospital for treatment.
