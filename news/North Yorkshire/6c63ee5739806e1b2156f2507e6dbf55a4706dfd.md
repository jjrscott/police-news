North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a theft of a quad bike that occurred in Carlton in Cleveland

It happened overnight on the 6th into 7th May 2015.Whereby the offenders have broken into a barn and stole a quad bike. The offenders have then made of across Sexhow Lane, then Tunstall Lane before heading towards Flatts Lane.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help locate the persons responsible.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Blair Taylor you can also email Blair.Taylor@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150074935
