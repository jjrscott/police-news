Police are appealing for witnesses after a road traffic collision on the A1M.

The collision involving a white Peugeot 308 and a white Mercedes Sprinter van occurred north of junction 47 at Allerton Park at around 6am on Sunday 7 June 2015.

The Peugeot caught fire and two men from Stockton on Tees were injured. A 36-year-old man was taken to Leeds General Infirmary where he remains in a serious but stable condition.

The other man, aged 43, is currently in a stable condition in Harrogate Hospital.

Officers are appealing to anyone who witnessed the collision to come forward with information.

Anyone who can help officers with their enquiries is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101 - select option 2 - and ask for Traffic Constable Andy Macleod.

Alternatively, information can be emailed toandrew.macleod@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote reference number 12150094707 when passing information about this incident.
