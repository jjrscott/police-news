Last modified: 13 December 2017 at 06:01pm

Police investigating an altercation in York in which a woman sustained leg injuries, have released a CCTV image of another man they would like to speak to.

The man pictured was using the pedestrian crossing on Bridge Street when an altercation took place with the driver of a red Mini Cooper.

The Mini driver was hit in the leg by her car door, causing bruising.

The incident happened just before 5.30pm on Thursday, 30 November 2017.

Police would like to speak to the man pictured by CCTV, or anyone who was in the area at the time who witnessed the incident.

If you recognise him or have any information that could help officers with their enquiries, please contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2 and ask for Emily Clark.

You can also contact PC Clark on Emily.Clark@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

Please quote incident 12170215193 when sharing information.
