Police are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision in the Craven area.

At about 12.35pm on Tuesday 19 January 2021, a collision occurred on Colne Road between Cross Hills and Cowling, involving a Volvo XC60 and a Kia Picanto.

A 21-year-old woman sadly died as a result of the collision.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision, or has dashcam footage of the vehicles involved, to phone North Yorkshire Police on 101, or email laura.cleary@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk or MCIT@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk.

Please quote incident reference 12210030233 when passing information.
