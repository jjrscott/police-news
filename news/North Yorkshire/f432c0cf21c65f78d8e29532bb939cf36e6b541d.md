Last modified: 7 October 2016 at 11:15am

Emergency services remain at the scene of a serious collision on the A66, eastbound carriageway, approximately 400 metres west of Scotch Corner. The collision involves a HGV and a Nissan 4 x 4, both travelling in the same direction at the time of the collision.

The driver of the Nissan has been taken to James Cook Hospital in Middlesbrough with serious head injuries.

The collision was reported at 5.46am on Friday 7 October 2016, the road is currently closed to allow emergency services to deal with the incident, examine the scene and recover the vehicles.

Any witnesses to the collision are asked to contact Traffic Constable Chris Coleman of Richmond Roads Policing Group on 101, or email _chris.coleman@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk_ Please quote incident number 62 of 7 October.
