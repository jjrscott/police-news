North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about an assault that occurred in Skipton.

It happened on Keighley Road, Skipton near to the junction with Lower Union Street at around 5pm on Wednesday 17 of May 2017.

A cream hatchback car has been pulling out of a cobbled side street onto Keighley Road when it has struck a pedestrian. The car has then stopped and the male driver has remonstrated with the pedestrian, assaulting him in the process.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the publicâ€™s assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident to come forward and contact us.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for David Gasson. You can also email david.gasson@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12170084948.
