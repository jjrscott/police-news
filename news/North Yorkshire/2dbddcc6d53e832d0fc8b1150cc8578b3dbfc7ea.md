Two men from the Malton area appeared in court in June 2017 charged with Hunting Act offences relating to allegations of illegal fox hunting in the Malton area in January 2017. One man was also charged with a public order offence from the same date. They will appear again for trial in October 2017 at York Magistrates Court.

Two men from the Durham area appeared in court in April 2017 charged with Hunting Act offences relating to allegations of illegally killing a fox and animal cruelty offences in the Hambleton area, in December 2016. They will appear again for trial in June 2017 at Northallerton Magistrates Court.

Three men from the Scarborough area appeared in court in May 2017 charged with Hunting Act offences relating to allegations of illegal fox hunting. They will appear again for trial in September 2017 at Scarborough Magistrates Court.
