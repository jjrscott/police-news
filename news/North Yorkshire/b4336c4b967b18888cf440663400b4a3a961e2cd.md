Police are appealing for information to locate wanted man Darren Plant, aged 37, from Harrogate.

Mr Plant failed to appear at court in relation to two charges of burglary and one charge of theft from a shop.

Despite extensive enquiries, Mr Plant remains outstanding.

Anyone who could assist police with their enquiries is asked to dial 101, select option 1 and pass the information to the Force Control Room. Please quote reference 12170097910.

Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111
