Last modified: 17 May 2018 at 12:30pm

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information in relation to some criminal damage caused to shop window that occurred in Scarborough

The incident took place on Victoria road between midnight and 1.30am on Tuesday 8 May 2018. A shop owner has arrived to open his shop to find that a large glass window had been smashed over night. The window is valued at over £1000.

We are requesting the public’s assistance to help establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident and identify the suspect.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Paul O'Neill. You can also email paul.o'neill @northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number NYP-08052018
