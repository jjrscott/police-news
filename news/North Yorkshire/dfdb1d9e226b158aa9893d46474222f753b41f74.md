North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following an assault which occurred on Blue Bridge Lane in York.

North Yorkshire Police is appealing for information following an assault which occurred on Blue Bridge Lane in York.

The incident occurred at around 5pm on Wednesday, 13 June and is believed to have involved a woman in her 40s and a man in his 30s.

Officers need to ascertain the full circumstances surrounding the incident and are asking anyone who can help the investigation to contact them with information.

The first suspect is a white woman, with blonde hair and was wearing light blue jeans, a white t-shirt and a grey cardigan at the time of the incident. The second suspect is a white man, with a beard and at the time of the assault was wearing blue trousers, a blue and cream coat and a blue cap.

Anyone with information which may assist officers is urged to contact North Yorkshire Police on 101, select option 2, and ask for Zoe Coutts. You can also email zoe.coutts@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote reference 121800105361 when providing information,
