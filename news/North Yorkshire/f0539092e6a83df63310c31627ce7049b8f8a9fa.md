North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about the theft of six bottles of perfume from Rackhams on Skipton High Street.

It happened at around 5pm on 31 August 2017 and a female suspect was captured on CCTV leaving the shop.

Staff noticed her place the items into a bag and she made no effort to pay for the goods.

She is described as small in height, hair tied up in an unkempt style, and she was wearing a black floral top, black pants and beige Ugg-style boots.
