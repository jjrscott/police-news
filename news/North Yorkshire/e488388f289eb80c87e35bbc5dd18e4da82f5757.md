Officers are at the scene of a serious collision on Selby Road, Monk Fryston.

Police were called at 3.30pm today to a report of a collision involving a lorry and a bus.

Two people have been taken to hospital for treatment.

A spokesperson for North Yorkshire Police said: "Please avoid the area while emergency services work at the scene of the collision. The roads around Monk Fryston are extremely busy, and motorists should make alternative arrangements for their journeys."
