North Yorkshire Police is appealing for witnesses and information about a criminal damage that occurred in Scarborough.

The incident happened on Castle Road between the evening of Thursday 24th September 2015 and 10am on Friday 26th September 2015 and involved what appears to be pellets being shot at a property, causing damage to the render.

The property was under renovation and thankfully no-one was injured.

We are conducting enquiries and are requesting the public's assistance to help determine the full circumstances surrounding the incident.

In particular, we are appealing for information about who may have been involved.

Anyone with information that could assist the investigation should contact us on 101, select option 2, and ask for Liam Carter. You can also email Liam.Carter@northyorkshire.pnn.police.uk

If you wish to remain anonymous, you can pass information to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Please quote the North Yorkshire Police reference number 12150172011.
