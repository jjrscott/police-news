Police are releasing images of recovered items in a bid to trace their rightful owners.

The items, which are believed to have been stolen, were recovered by officers near Buntingford on Tuesday, June 4.

PCSO Aaron Pender, from the local Safer Neighbourhood Team, said: "If you recognise the items pictured, please get in touch with us; we're keen to reunite them with their owners."

Anyone who does recognise them should contact PCSO Pender via email to or by calling the non-emergency number 101. You can also report information online or speak to an operator in our Force Communications Room via our online web chat, which can be launched here
