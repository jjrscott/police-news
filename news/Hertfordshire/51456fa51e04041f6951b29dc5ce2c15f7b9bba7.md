* ### Decorative planters were damaged in The Parade.

 * ### The incident happened in the early hours of June 22.

 * ### Police would like to speak with the people pictured for help with their enquiries.

Police investigating an incident of criminal damage in Watford have released an image of three people they would like to speak to for help with their enquiries.

At around 1.20am on Friday, June 22, damage was caused to decorative planters situated in The Parade, near the pond area.

Officers would like to speak with the people pictured as they may have information which could assist with the investigation.

Anyone with information is asked to contact PC Ryan Doyle via email to ryan.doyle@herts.pnn.police.uk or call the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/11295/18. You can also report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
