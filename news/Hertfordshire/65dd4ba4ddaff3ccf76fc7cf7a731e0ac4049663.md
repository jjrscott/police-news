* Did you witness physical altercation on Maidenhead Street in Herford?
 * It happened between 12.30pm and 12.45pm on Saturday, December 9
 * Please call 101 quoting A1/17/4101 if you have information

Officers investigating an affray in Hertford are appealing for information and witnesses.

It happened in Maidenhead Street between 12.30pm and 12.45pm on Saturday, December 9.

Three men were reported to have been involved in a physical altercation with another man, who then left the scene with a woman and a child.

It occurred at a busy time of day when there would have been a lot of people in the immediate area.

If you witnessed what happened, or have any further information that could assist the investigation, please call Hertfordshire Constabulary on the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference A1/17/4101 or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report

Alternatively, if you have any information about this incident you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
