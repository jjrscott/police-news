* ### Police are trying to trace the owners of this property, believed to be stolen.

 * ### It includes a Musket gun and distinctive jewellery.

 * ### A man has been arrested in connection with burglaries in St Albans, Borehamwood and Luton.

A Musket gun, distinctive jewellery, collectors' coins, sunglasses and a watch are some of the items that have been seized by police as they are believed to have been stolen during a series of burglaries in St Albans, Hertsmere and Luton.

"We are trying to trace the owners of this property," said Detective Constable Paul Cullinan. "The property is distinctive and includes some sentimental items. If you recognise any of these items, please get in touch with us, so we can return them to you."

If you recognise any of the property, please call 101 quoting reference 41/18541/19.

 * Azariah Morgan, age 35, of no fixed abode, has pleaded guilty to committing four burglaries in St Albans, Borehamwood and Luton. He is due to be sentenced at St Albans Crown Court on May 31.
