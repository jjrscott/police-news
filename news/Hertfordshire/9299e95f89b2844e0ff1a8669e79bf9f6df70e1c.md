* ### Appeal for wintess following attempted robbery in Cheshunt

 * ### The incident happened on Downfield Road at around 3.30pm on Monday, June 6

 * ### Contact 101 quoting crime reference 41/4253/18 if you have information

Police are appealing for witnesses and information following an attempted robbery in Cheshunt on Monday, June 4.

The incident occurred around 3.30pm when two boys aged around 16 approached the victim in Downfield Road. One of the boys, described as tall and white, tried to search the victim, but the victim managed to push him away. Nothing was taken and the victim was not hurt during the incident.

Anyone who has any information about what happened is asked to contact Hertfordshire Constabulary via the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference Crime Ref 41/4253/18 or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report. Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court
