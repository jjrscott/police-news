Officers arrested two people of suspicion of drugs offences in Hatfield last week.

Police stopped a vehicle in Cavendish Way on Wednesday, March 6, after it appeared to evade officers.

Officers searched the vehicle and found 16 bags of suspected cannabis.

A 21 year old man from London was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply.

A 22 year old man from London was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply and driving whilst under the influence of drugs.

Both have been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

If you are worried about drugs activity in your local area, call the non-emergency number 101 or report information online.

Remember, always call 999 if a crime is in progress.
