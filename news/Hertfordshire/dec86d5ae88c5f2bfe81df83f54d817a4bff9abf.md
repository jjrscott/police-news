* ### Following a call from a member of the public, police arrested a 34-year-old man for conspiracy to burgle.

 * ### He was also arrested on other matters.

 * ### If you suspect a burglary is in progress, call 999.

A 34-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to commit burglary in St Albans on Saturday (June 2).

He was arrested on suspicion conspiracy to commit burglary, possession of an offensive weapon and driving whilst disqualified and going equipped to steal in Chivenor Place.

Following a call to police from a member of the public, police quickly arrived with dogs at Drakes Drive, Camp Road, Chivenor Place and Hill End Lane to try and trace the offenders. Enquiries are ongoing to trace the remaining suspects.

If you see any one acting suspiciously or suspect a burglary is in progress, call 999. If you are reporting a crime retrospectively, call 101 or report online.
