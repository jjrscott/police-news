Officers investigating a burglary in Wheathampstead are appealing for anyone with information to get in contact.

Between 8.40pm and 8.55pm on Sunday, January 13, Wheathampstead Golf Course in Harpenden Road was broken into and an untidy search was conducted.

A Stihl leaf blower and two charity boxes were stolen, and three CCTV cameras were damaged.

Any witnesses or anyone with information should contact PC Lee Osborne by emailing or the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/4127/19. You can also report information online

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form.
