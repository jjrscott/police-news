Following the arrest of a 19 year old man in Bishop's Stortford by the Metropolitan Police Counter Terrorism Command, East Herts Chief Inspector Gerry McDonald said: "We are aware of the arrest that took place in Bishop's Stortford yesterday evening (Wednesday, May 23) and are liaising with The Metropolitan Police.

"The man remains in police custody at this time and searches at the address in Bishop's Stortford are now complete.

"At this stage there is no suggestion that there will be any further police activity in the area in connection with this investigation however, anyone who lives locally and feels concerned or worried can contact their local Safer Neighbourhood Team."
