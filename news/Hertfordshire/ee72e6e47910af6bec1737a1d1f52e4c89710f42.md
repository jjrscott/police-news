Detectives from the Bedfordshire, Cambridgeshire and Hertfordshire Major Crime Unit have arrested a man following a re-examination of 47 year old Diane Stewart's death at her home in Bassingbourn on June 25, 2010.

The 57 year old man from Bedford was arrested on Tuesday, August 21 and questioned on suspicion of murder before being released under investigation.
