 * ### Police are appealing for anyone who may have seen the rings to come foward.

 * ### Two people have been arrested in connection with the investigation.

Detectives are appealing for the public's help to trace five rings which were stolen from an address in Aston, East Herts.

The rings (some of which are pictured) were reportedly taken some time between 2pm and 5.45pm on Thursday, March 7, along with a small quantity of cash.

Detective Sergeant Samantha Alcock, from the East Herts Local Crime Unit, said: "I am appealing for anyone who thinks they may have come across the rings to please get in touch. I would also like to hear from anyone who may have any other information about the incident."

Anyone with information should contact DS Alcock via email to samantha.alcock@herts.pnn.police.uk or by calling the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/22291/19. You can also report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
