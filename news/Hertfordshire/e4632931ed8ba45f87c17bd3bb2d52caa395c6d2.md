A 42 year old man has been arrested and charged following after an altercation in Hitchin on Thursday 16 July.

Paul Wood, of Wedgewood Road in Hitchin, was arrested on Saturday 18 July and charged with a racially aggravated Section 4a public order offence and ABH, after allegedly assaulting an associate of his, in Brand Street.

The victim was knocked unconscious and members of the public intervened to administer first aid.

Wood's next court appearance is on Monday 17 August at Hatfield Magistrates Court .
