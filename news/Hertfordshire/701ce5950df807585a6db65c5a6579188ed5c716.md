* ### Detectives have made arrests following series of robberies in Harpenden

 * ### Two teenage boys from Luton were taken into custody

 * ### Nine offences have been recorded in total

Detectives investigating a series of robberies and attempted robberies in Harpenden have made two arrests.

A 15 year old boy and a 16 year old boy, both from Luton were arrested in connection with nine offences which occurred between Saturday, October 28 and Tuesday, October 31. They have since been released under investigation.

Officers are carrying out further investigations into the series.

 * An attempted robbery which took place on Saturday, October 28 between 4pm and 5pm in Rothamstead Park
 * A robbery which took place on Saturday, October 28 between 4pm and 4.20pm in Rothamstead Park
 * Two robberies which took place on Saturday, October 28 at 5.50pm in Rothamstead Park
 * An attempted robbery which took place on Sunday, October 29 at 5pm in Station Road
 * Two robberies which took place on Sunday, October 29 between 4pm and 4.20pm in Rothamstead Park
 * A robbery which took place on Tuesday, October 31 at 7pm in Station Road
 * An attempted robbery which took place on Tuesday, October 31 at 9pm in the High Street

Anyone who has information that could assist the investigation can contact the St Albans Local Crime Unit via Hertfordshire Constabulary's non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference F2/17/2092 or report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
