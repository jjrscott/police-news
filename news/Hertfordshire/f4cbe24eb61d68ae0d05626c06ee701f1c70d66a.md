* **Police called to a fight in Tring**
 * **Four people suffered injuries**
 * **Appeal for witnesses to come foward**

Police were called at 11.17pm on 12th October to reports of an altercation involving a group of around eight men outside a shop which is being refurbished on Frogmore Street in Tring.

A few minutes after the call, the offenders left the scene.

Officers attended and found four people had been injured. One man had suffered a broken jaw. Damage was also caused to the shop window.

Despite searches of the area, no arrests have been made.

Having reviewed CCTV footage, officers are aware there were a number of people in the area and are keen to speak to these people or anyone else who could assist the investigation.

 Anyone with information is asked to contact is asked to call the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/45813 or report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report.

 Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
