Police in East Herts are appealing for help and information in order to find two women they believe could assist them with their investigation into a burglary in Bishop's Stortford.

The offence happened in Norfolk Way on Saturday 10th February in the early hours of the morning. Car keys, computer equipment, a handbag, bank cards and cash were taken.

Officers would like to speak to the women pictured in the photo, as they believe they may have information which could help their inquiry.

Anyone with any Information is asked to contact DC Aby Page in the Local Crime Unit on 101 quoting Crime reference number A2/17/255.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or submit information through their anonymous online form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court.
