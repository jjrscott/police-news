* ### Do you recognise these people?

 * ### Police would like to speak to them as part of their enquiries following the theft of DVDs at WH Smith in Hertford.

 * ### Anyone with information is asked to contact officers.

Officers investigating a theft in Hertford are releasing CCTV images of two people they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

At around 3.15pm on Wednesday, March 14, 45 DVDs were stolen from WH Smith in Bircherley Green.

Anyone who recognises the people pictured is asked to contact PCSO Stephen Blanks by emailing, call the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference A1/18/845 or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
