* ### Three bicycles were stolen from a garden shed in Broxbourne.

 * ### They are a black Scott (910 model) road bike, a black Boardman CX Comp bike and a black Scott CR1 bike.

 * ### Anyone who has seen them or believes they've been offered the bikes for sale is asked to contact police.

Officers are appealing for information and witnesses after three bicycles were stolen from a garden shed in Broxbourne.

The black Scott (910 model) road bike, pictured, was among the bicycles stolen from Baas Lane between Monday, August 6 and Wednesday, August 8.

A black Boardman CX Comp bike and a black Scott CR1 bike were also taken.

Anyone who believes they have seen the bikes or thinks they may have been offered them for sale is asked to contact PCSO Lauren Arnold by emailing, calling the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/25588/18 or report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
