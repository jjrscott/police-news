* ### Witnesses are being sought following an assault in Hoddesdon.

 * ### lt took place around 4pm last Wednesday (24 February) on Burford Street near Morrisons.

 * ### Call 101 quoting **41/13771/21 if you can help.**

We are appealing for witnesses and information following an assault in Hoddesdon.

It has been reported that at around 4pm last Wednesday (24 February) a teenage girl was assaulted by another girl on Burford Street near to Morrisons. She suffered a cut lip.

PC Ross Brown, who is investigating, said: "If you were on Burford Street at the time of the incident and witnessed it then please get in touch.

"Similarly, if you have CCTV which covers the area and shows anything of relevance which could assist our enquiries then we would like to hear from you.

"If you are able to help I can be contacted directly via email at ross.brown@herts.pnn.police.uk"

You can report information online at herts.police.uk/report, speak to an operator in our Force Communications Room via our online web chat at herts.police.uk/contact or call the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference **41/13771/21**.

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at crimestoppers-uk.org.
