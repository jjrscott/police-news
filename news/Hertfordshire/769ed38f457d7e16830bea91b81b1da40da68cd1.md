 * ### Mountain bike stolen on Christmas Day

 * ### Theft happened at Huron Road, Turnford

 * ### Anyone with information please contact Herts Police non-emergency number 101 or report online quoting crime references K1/17/3290

Police are appealing for information following the theft of a pedal bike in Turnford on Christmas Day.

A dark blue Saracen mountain bike was stolen from an address in Huron Road, at around 10.15pm.

Anyone who has any information about the theft or has been offered a bike matching the description for sale is asked to contact the Herts Police non-emergency number 101 or report online quoting crime references K1/17/3290.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
