* ### Man injured during assault at The Flag pub in Watford last month

 * ### Did you see anything?

 * ### Men pictured man be able to assist police enquiries

Officers have released CCTV images of two men they would like to speak to in connection with a serious assault in Watford.

At around 10.30pm on Friday, January 12, a 23 year old man was punched to the head and then had two glasses thrown at the back of his head in The Flag public house in Station Road. He sustained injuries which required hospital treatment.

Police would like to speak to the men pictured as it is believed they may have witnessed what happened or may have information that can assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises the men, or has any other information is asked to contact PC Adam Bates on adam.bates@herts.pnn.police.uk or via the Hertfordshire Constabulary non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference C1/18/243. You can also report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
