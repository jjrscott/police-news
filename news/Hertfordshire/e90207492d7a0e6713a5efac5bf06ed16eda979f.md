Police have arrested a man in connection with two alleged attempted robberies in Bishop's Stortford.

At 10pm on Saturday, December 15, it is alleged that a man entered Lee Garden takeaway in Elizabeth Road, Bishop's Stortford, and demanded money. When the victims refused, he walked out of the restaurant.

At 7.16pm on Sunday, December 16, police received a call reporting an attempted robbery on a bus in Villiers-Sur-Marne Avenue, Bishop's Stortford. A man had entered the bus and demanded money. When the victims refused, he exited the bus.

In both incidents, the man was reported to have been in possession of a gun. Nobody was harmed during either incident.

 * A 36 year old man from Bishop's Stortford has been arrested on suspicion of attempted robbery. He remains in police custody at this time.
