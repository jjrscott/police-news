* ### Did you see alleged assault in Letchworth?

 * ### A woman was reportedly pushed to the floor and repeatedly kicked and punched in Hawkfield.

 * ### Police would like to speak to anyone who saw the incident and, in particular, a member of the public who help the victim to her car.

Officers investigating an alleged assault in Letchworth are appealing for information and witnesses.

At around midday on Thursday, May 24, a woman was allegedly pushed to the floor by a man and repeatedly kicked and punched in Hawkfield. The victim suffered bruising.

Both parties are believed to be known to each other.

Detective Constable Paul Ambrose said: "I'm appealing for anyone who saw the incident to get in touch. In particular I'd like to hear from a woman or man who helped the victim back to her vehicle after the alleged assault."

Anyone with information is asked to email DC Ambrose at paul.ambrose@herts.pnn.police.uk, call him on 01438757785, call the non-emergency number 101, quoting 41/538/18, or report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.

 * A 40 year old man from Letchworth has been arrested in connection with the incident. He has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
