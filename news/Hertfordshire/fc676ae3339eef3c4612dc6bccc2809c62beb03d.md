* ### Have you seen missing Leigh Headland from Stevenage?

 * ### He was last seen in Six Hills Way at 10pm on Monday, October 30

 * ### If you see him or know where he is, please call 101 quoting ISR 257 of November 1

Police are appealing for the public's help to trace a man who has gone missing from Stevenage.

Leigh Headland, aged 51, was last seen on Six Hills Way at 10pm on Monday, October 30.

He is described as white, of slim build, with dark hair.

He was last seen wearing a red T-shirt, a grey hoodie and either dark blue jeans or black combat trousers.

Officers are growing increasingly concerned for Leigh's welfare.

Anyone who has seen him or who knows of his whereabouts should call Hertfordshire Constabulary's non-emergency number 101, quoting ISR 257 of November 1.
