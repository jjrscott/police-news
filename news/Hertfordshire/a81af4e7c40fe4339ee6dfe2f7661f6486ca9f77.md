* ### Do you recognise this man?

 * ### Officers would like to identify him as part of their enquiries following a burglary at Aldenham Social Club.

 * ### Offenders gained access to the club in the early hours of Monday, July 9 and took money from the till.

 * ### Anyone who recognises the man pictured is asked to contact PCSO Tim Conway.

Officers investigating a burglary in Aldenham are releasing a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

Between 1.57am and 2.10am on Monday (July 9) offenders gained entry to the Aldenham Social Club in Church Lane where they carried out a search inside and took money from the till.

Officers would like to identify the man pictured as he may have information that could assist the on-going investigation.

Anyone with information is asked to contact PCSO Tim Conway by emailing, calling the non-emergency number 101 quoting crime reference 41/15747/18 or report information online

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
