 * ### Drivers are advised to remove all valuables from their vehicles when leaving them unattended.

Police investigating a theft from a motor vehicle in Brookmans Park are releasing CCTV images of people they would like to identify as part of their enquiries.

Vehicles parked outside an address in Brookmans Avenue were targeted overnight between Monday, July 2 and Tuesday, July 3.

A pair of designer sunglasses was stolen.

Vehicles were also targeted in nearby Peplins Close on the same evening but nothing was taken.

Officers would like to speak with the people pictured as they may have information which could assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises them is asked to report information online or call the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/13771/18.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
