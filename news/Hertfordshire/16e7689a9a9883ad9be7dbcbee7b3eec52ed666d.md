 * ### Police would like to hear from anyone who may have been offered the bike for sale, seen it abandoned or has any further information

 * ### If you can help, please contact police quoting reference 41/99974/20

Officers investigating the theft of a stolen mountain bike in Hemel Hempstead have released an image of it as part of their enquiries.

The bike was stolen from a secure car park on Selden Hill, off St Albans Road, sometime between Sunday 6 December and Saturday 12 December.

PC Attila Cseh, who is investigating, said: "I am keen to hear from anyone who thinks they may have been offered the bike - which is a GT Avalanche - for sale, or may have seen it abandoned somewhere.

"Any information, however small, could be vital in helping us reunite the bike with its rightful owner. Please get in touch."

You can email PC Cseh at attila.cseh@herts.pnn.police.uk, report information online at herts.police.uk/report, talk to one of our Force Communication Room operators via web chat at herts.police.uk/contact, or call our non-emergency number 101 quoting reference **41/99974/20**.
