Officers investigating a robbery in Bishop's Stortford are appealing for anyone with information to get in contact.

At around 2am on Friday, April 19, a 22-year-old woman walked past a small group of people on Riverside after which she was assaulted and her bag containing her passport, bank cards and cash was taken. She sustained scratches and bruises.

Any witnesses or anyone with information should contact Investigator Nickie Morris on nicola.morris@herts.pnn.police.uk or the non-emergency number 101, quoting reference 41/35385/19. You can also report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
