* ### Do you recognise this woman?

 * ### Do you recall an assault in Stevenage Old Town?

 * ### Officers investigating an assault are appealing for witnesses

Police in Stevenage are appealing for the public's help to identify a woman they would like to speak to in connection with an assault.

On Saturday 5 October, a woman was assaulted by three other women following a dispute about a taxi in the Old Town High Street. Officers would like to speak to the woman pictured in the CCTV as they believe she was in the area at the time and may have information which could help their investigation.

Anyone with any information should contact PC Poppy Goldman via email to poppy.goldman@herts.pnn.police.uk or by calling the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime number 41/91319/19. You can also report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report _._

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.

For over 30 years, Crimestoppers has always kept its promise of anonymity to everyone who contacts them.
