* ### Have you seen a motorcycle stolen from Letchworth?

 * ### The bike is royal blue.

 * ### It was stolen following a burglary.

Police are appealing for witnesses and information after a motorcycle was stolen in a burglary in Haymoor, Letchworth between 3pm on Thursday, April 26 and 9.30am on Tuesday, May 1.

The stolen motorcycle is a Sherco ST300 2016 model and was locked and secure in the garage of the property at the time it was stolen. Offender(s) forced their way into the garage, stole the bike and then made off in an unknown direction. The bike is mainly royal blue in colour.

It has been reported to police that the stolen bike may have been seen in the area of Norton Common.

Anyone who has seen the bike or has information in relation to this incident is asked to contact SNTLetchworthandBaldockTown@herts.pnn.police.uk or call Hertfordshire Constabulary's non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference G2/18/995. Crime can also be reported online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
