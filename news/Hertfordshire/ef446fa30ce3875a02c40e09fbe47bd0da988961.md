Police are appealing for the public's help to trace a man who is wanted.

William Riley, aged 18 and whose last known address is Down Edge in St Albans, is wanted in connection with threats to kill.

He also has connections to Borehamwood.

Anyone who has seen William or knows of his whereabouts is asked to contact Hertfordshire Constabulary via the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/47397/18.
