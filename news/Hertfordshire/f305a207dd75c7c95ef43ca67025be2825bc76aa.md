* ### **The bike was taken from the garage of a house in Southfields**

 * ### **It happened on Saturday, November 16 between 12.45pm and 16.30pm**

 * ### **Anyone who has seen it or has information should contact PC Matt Clark on 101 or at _matt.clark2@herts.pnn.police.uk_ quoting crime reference 41/57109/18. **

Officers investigating a burglary in Letchworth Garden City have released a photo of a bike that was taken in a bid to trace it.

Between 12.45pm and 16.30pm on Saturday, November 17, a bike was taken from the garage of a house in Southfields.

It is a blue Voodoo Bantu mountain bike that has had several upgrades from the standard model, including a drink holder and a different saddle.

PC Matt Clark, who is investigating, said: "Have you seen this bike? Have you been offered it for sale?

"If you think you have, or if you saw the burglary happen, please get in touch with me on either the non-emergency number 101 or through email at _matt.clark2@herts.pnn.police.uk_ quoting crime reference 41/57109/18."

You can also report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. In over 30 years, Crimestoppers charity has always kept its promise of anonymity to everyone who contacts them.
