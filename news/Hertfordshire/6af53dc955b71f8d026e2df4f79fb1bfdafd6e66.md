Police investigating a theft in Berkhamsted have released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to for help with their enquiries.

At around 4.10pm on Friday, October 5 a cosmetics gift set was stolen from a pharmacy in the High Street.

Officers would like to speak with the person pictured as he may have information which could assist their enquiries.

Anyone with information is asked to contact PCSO Lauren Jackson via email or by calling the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/43603/18. You can also report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
