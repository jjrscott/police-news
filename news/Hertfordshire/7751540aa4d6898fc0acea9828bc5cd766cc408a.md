* ## Police recover gold bean.

 * ## Owners sought.

 * ## Man arrested on suspicion of theft.

Police in Stevenage have released a photo of an unusual item of jewellery which they believe may have been stolen.

The gold coffee bean was found after a routine stop in the town on Monday, July 16.

PC Benn Swapp who is investigating said: "I am appealing for anyone who has had an item like this stolen or lost, to get in touch. You may have information which could help our investigation."

A 29 year old man has been arrested on suspicion of theft and has been released under investigation.

Anyone with information is asked to contact PC Ben Swapp via the non-emergency number 101. You can also email or report information online

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
