* ### Police would like to speak to this woman as part of their enquiries following a racially aggravated assault.

 * ### It happened on May 2 at JRC Global Buffet in the town centre.

 * ### The victim suffered injuries to her wrist and arm.

Police are releasing CCTV images of a woman they would like to speak with in connection with a racially aggravated assault in Watford.

The incident occurred at 8pm on Wednesday, May 2 at JRC Global Buffet, the Parade, High Street, when a staff member - a woman in her 20s - was approached by the offender. Having requested a refund which was decline, the offender picked up an object and threw it at the victim causing injuries to her wrist and arm.

PC Kat Murray is investigating and said: "This was a vicious attack and I am keen to hear from anyone who was in the restaurant at the time of the incident and who may have seen what happened or anyone who can identify the woman pictured as I would like to speak with her as part of my investigation."

If you have any information about the incident, please contact Hertfordshire Constabulary via the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference C1/18/2609. You can also report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
