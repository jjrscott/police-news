* ### Teenager Tyrone Kafeero was last seen in Bedford on Wednesday, March 28

 * ### He often travels by rail and it is believed he may be in the Bedford area

 * ### Contact police if you have seen him or know where he may be

Police are appealing for the public's help to trace a teenager from Watford who is missing.

17 year old Tyrone Kafeero was last seen in Bedford on Wednesday, March 28.

Tyrone is described as black, 5ft 4in tall, of a slim build and with brown eyes.

Tyrone often travels by rail and it is believed that he may be in the Bedford area. He has links to Enfield.

If you have seen Tyrone since he was reported missing or have information about where he has been, please call Hertfordshire Constabulary's non-emergency number 101 quoting ISR 620 of April 12. If you believe you are with him now or have seen him the last few moments, please call 999 immediately, quoting ISR 620 of April 12.

You can also report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report .

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
