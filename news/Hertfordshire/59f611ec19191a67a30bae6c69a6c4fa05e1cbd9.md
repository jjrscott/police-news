* ### Do you recognise these people? Police would like to speak to them as part of their enquiries into a burglary in Sawbridgeworth

 * ### It happened at the Village Pharmacy in the early hours of Monday July 30

 * ### If you can help, please call 101 quoting crime reference 41/22536/18

Police in East Herts are looking to identify two men who they believe could help them with their investigation into a burglary at the Village Pharmacy in Sawbridgeworth.

The offence took place in the early hours on Monday morning (July 30) at around 3.40am. Offenders smashed the front window of the premises and removed items from within.

The two men were in the area at the time and thought to be driving an old silver BMW 3 series.

Anyone who thinks they recognise these men is asked to contact PC Shelly Marshall on 101 quoting crime reference number 41/22536/18 or report online at www.herts.police.uk/report

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
