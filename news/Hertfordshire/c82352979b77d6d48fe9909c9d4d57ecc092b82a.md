* ### 57-year-old Michael Walkerdine from Ware charged with sexual assault.

 * ### Former Devon and Bristol resident appeared in court on Monday.

 * ### Please call 101 if you have information that could assist the investigation.

Fifty-seven-year-old Michael Walkerdine from Ware has been charged with sexual assault against a boy.

Walkerdine, who formerly lived in the Devon and Bristol areas, was charged on Saturday (February 17) and appeared at Hatfield Remand Court on Monday (February 19).

He was remanded until a further hearing on March 23.

The offence relates to an allegation that he sexually touched a 15-year-old boy in Ware on Saturday January 13.

Anyone with information that could assist this investigation is asked to contact police quoting crime reference A1/18/535, or report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
