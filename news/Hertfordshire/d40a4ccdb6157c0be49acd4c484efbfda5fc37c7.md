* ### Detectives are investigating a burglary in Deadman's Ash Lane in Sarratt

 * ### It happened between 1pm and 10pm on Sunday 28 June

 * ### Police would like to know if anyone saw a white Ford Transit-style van in the lane between the times stated

Detectives investigating a burglary in Sarratt are appealing for information and witnesses.

It happened on Deadman's Ash Lane between 1pm and 10pm on Sunday 28 June.

Offender(s) broke in via a ground-floor window and searched the property before taking cash and jewellery.

Detective Constable Nicola Simons, from the Three Rivers Local Crime Unit, said: "We have already carried out extensive enquiries and we are now appealing to anyone who saw any suspicious activity in the area between the times stated.

"In particular we would like to hear from anyone who saw a white Ford Transit-style van on Deadmans Ash Lane around the time of the burglary."

If you have any information you believe could assist the investigation, you can email DC Simons at nicola.simons2@herts.pnn.police.uk, report information online at herts.police.uk/report, talk to one of our Force Communication Room operators via web chat at herts.police.uk/contact, or call our non-emergency number 101 quoting reference **41/50600/20**.

Alternatively you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or visit their website www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
