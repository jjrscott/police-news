Police are appealing for the public's help to trace a man who is wanted.

Johnathon Robinson, aged 26, whose last known address is in Welwyn Garden City, is wanted in connection with an incident in Welwyn Garden City that happened between Sunday, February 3 and Monday, February 4 involving controlling and coercive behaviour, and common assault.

He has links to Welwyn Garden City and Hatfield.

Anyone who sees Johnathon, or has any information about their whereabouts, is asked to contact Hertfordshire Constabulary via 101 or report information online _quoting crime reference_ 41/11538/19 _._

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form.

For over 30 years, Crimestoppers has always kept its promise of anonymity to everyone who contacts them.
