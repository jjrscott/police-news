Police are releasing CCTV images of three females they would like to speak to as part of an enquiry following an incident of shoplifting in Berkhamsted.

Officers believe the three females pictured may have information that can assist the investigation following the incident which happened at around 11.20am on Saturday, November 17 in Jojo Maman Bebe on the High Street.

Anyone who recognises them or has further information is asked to contact PCSO Lauren Jackson by emailing _lauren.jackson@herts.pnn.police.uk_ , calling the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/61889/18 or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
