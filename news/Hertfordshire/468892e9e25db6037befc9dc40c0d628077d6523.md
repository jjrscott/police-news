* ### Collectable Car Stolen from Dane End.

 * ### Green Caterham Seven Super Sprint Taken.

 * ### Police appeal for information.

Officer in East Herts are investigating the theft of a Caterham Seven Super Sprint car from Dane End near Ware.

The vehicle, which is believed to have been taken in the early hours of Wednesday November 29, from Windy Rise.

PSCO Karen Broad who is investigating said: "We believe the vehicle may have been removed on a trailer and we are appealing to anyone who may have seen a green Caterham Seven car being transported in this way.

"We would also like to hear from you if you have seen this car for sale anywhere. Any information you may have could help our inquiry.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact 101 quoting crime reference number A5/17/768 or report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
