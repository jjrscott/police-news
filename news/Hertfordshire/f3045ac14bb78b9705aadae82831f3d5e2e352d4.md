A man has been charged in connection with two knifepoint robberies in Watford.

Ryan King, aged 18, of Thorpe Crescent, Watford, has been charged with two counts of robbery.

This is in connection with a robbery in The Willows, Watford, on August 21 and a further robbery in Deakin Close, Watford, on August 23.

In both cases, the victims were takeaway delivery drivers.

King has been remanded in custody and is next due to appear at court on September 24 for a case management hearing.
