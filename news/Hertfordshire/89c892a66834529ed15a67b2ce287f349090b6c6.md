* ### Do you recognise the people pictured?

 * ### Police would like to speak to them as part of their enquiries following the theft of a bank card in Watford

 * ### If you are pictured, or you know someone who is, please contact police quoting 41/25691/19

Officers investigating the theft of a bank card in Watford have released images of a number of people they would like to identify and speak to as part of their enquiries.

Between 10.30pm and 10.45pm on Friday, March 15 the victim, a woman in her 20s, had her debit card stolen while on a night out in the town centre.

It was later used for a number of contactless transactions in locations across Watford, totalling more than Â£100.

PC Laura Grant, who is investigating, said: "I am keen to identify the people pictured as we believe they may have information that can help progress our enquiries. If you are pictured, or you know someone who is, please contact me as soon as possible."

If you can help, please contact PC Grant on _laura.grant@herts.pnn.police.uk_ , report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report or speak to an operator in our Force Communications Room via online web chat, quoting reference 41/25691/19.
