* ### We are investigating a theft of hammers from Travis Perkins in Bishop's Stortford on Monday 2 December.

 * ### The incident took place at around 12.30pm.

 * ### Call 101 quoting 41/108676/19 if you have information about this crime.

We have released the images of two individuals we would like to speak to for help with our enquiries following a theft in Bishop's Stortford.

The incident occurred on Monday 2 December at 12.30pm at Travis Perkins, Woodside, where hammers were stolen.

Officers would like to speak to the men pictured as they may have information which could assist the investigation.

If you recognise these men you can report information online at herts.police.uk/report, speak to an operator in our Force Communications Room via online web chat at herts.police.uk/contact or call our non-emergency number 101 quoting reference 41/108676/19.

Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or report information online at crimestoppers-uk.org.
