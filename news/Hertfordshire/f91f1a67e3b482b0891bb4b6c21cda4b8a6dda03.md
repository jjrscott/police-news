Officers have released CCTV images of two women they would like to speak to following an attempted theft in Broxbourne.

The incident happened in Sainsburys, High Road on Sunday, April 29 at around 1.40pm.

Two women attempted to leave the store without paying for numerous items they had taken. They were stopped by shop staff and then made off without the items.

Officers would like to speak to the two women pictured as it is believed they were in the area at the time so could have vital information that will assist officers with the investigation.

Anyone who recognises them or has any other information should contact Hertfordshire Constabulary on the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference K1/18/1072.

You can also report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report .

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
