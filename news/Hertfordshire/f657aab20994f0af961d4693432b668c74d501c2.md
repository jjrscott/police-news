Police have now charged a man in connection with the armed robbery that allegedly occurred in Much Hadham on Monday (December 3).

The offence reportedly happened at around 1.50pm at the Londis store in Tower Hill.

Mark Johnson (46) of Pitmans Field in Harlow has been charged with robbery and possession of an imitation firearm following his arrest on Friday, December 7.

He appeared at Hatfield Magistrates court today (Monday December 10) and has been remanded in custody until January 7, when he is due to appear at St Albans Crown Court.
