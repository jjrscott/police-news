A 28-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of possession of drugs with the intent to supply after a vehicle was stopped in Hoddesdon.

At around 7pm on Thursday, February 21, the man, who is from Broxbourne, was stopped in a car by Operation Scorpion* officers in Cock Lane. After searching the car, the man was arrested.

An address in Broxbourne was subsequently searched by officers.

A substantial amount of cash was found, along with items suspected of being Class A drugs.

The arrested man has been released under investigation.

*Operation Scorpion targets burglars, robbers and those suspected of being involved in drug and vehicle-related crime. The proactive team is focused on those criminals causing the most harm to our communities.
