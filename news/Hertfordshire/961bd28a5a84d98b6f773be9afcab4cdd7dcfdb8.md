Officers investigating a burglary in Broxbourne are appealing for information and witnesses.

It happened in McKenzie Road between 2pm and 2.30pm on Friday, November 24.

Offender(s) have gained access to the property's rear garden via the side gate before smashing a downstairs window.

They searched several rooms and took a pearl necklace, a Rolex watch and a quantity of cash before making off.

If you saw any suspicious activity in the area at the time stated, or have any further information that could assist the investigation, please email Detective Constable Rebecca March at rebecca.march@herts.pnn.police.uk, call Hertfordshire Constabulary on the non-emergency number 101 quoting crime reference K1/17/3025 or report information online.

Alternatively, if you have any information about this incident you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
