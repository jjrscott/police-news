 * ### Appeal for information after theft from Vodafone in Waltham Cross

 * ### Two iPhones were stolen on Wednesday, April 4

 * ### Anyone with infor,ation is asked to call 101 and quote reference K2/18/1594

Officers investigating a theft from a shop in Waltham Cross have released images of people they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

The thefts occurred in Vodafone in the High Street at 3.10pm on Wednesday, April 4, when two iPhones were stolen.

If you recognise any of the men or have any information that could help the investigation please contact us online at www.herts.police.uk/report or call 101, quoting crime reference number K2/18/1594.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
