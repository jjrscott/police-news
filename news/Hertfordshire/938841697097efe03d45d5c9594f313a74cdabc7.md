Police in East Herts have released an image of a man they believe could assist them with their investigation into a burglary in Sawbridgeworth.

On Saturday February 3, at around 5.45am offenders smashed the glass at the front of the Bliss Budget store in West Road and entered the premises.

Officers would like to speak to the man in the photo, as they believe he was in the area at the time and may have information which could help their inquiry.

Anyone with any Information is asked to contact PC Shelly Marshall on 101 quoting Crime reference number A4/18/83 or report information online.

Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or submit information through their anonymous online form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will not go to court.
