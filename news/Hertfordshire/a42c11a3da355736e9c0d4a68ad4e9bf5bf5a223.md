* ### Do you recognise the two people pictured?

 * ### Police would like to speak to them as part of enquiries into a burglary in Chandler's Cross

 * ### If you know them, please contact police quoting reference 41/3886/19

Officers investigating a burglary in Chandler's Cross have released images of two people they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

It happened at The Grove Hotel just after 1am on Saturday, January 12.

Offender(s) broke into the hotel's restaurant before stealing a quantity of cash and an iPad.

PCSO Dean Redhead, from the Rickmansworth and Croxley Green Safer Neighbourhood Team, said: "We appreciate some time has passed since the incident however we have only recently managed to gain access to the CCTV footage from the day in question.

"Therefore we are appealing to anyone who thinks they may know the people pictured, as we believe they may have information that could help progress our investigation."

If you know the people pictured, or have any further information that could assist police, please contact PCSO Redhead on _dean.redhead@herts.pnn.police.uk_ , report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report or speak to an operator in our Force Communications Room via online web chat, quoting reference 41/3886/19.

Online web chat can be launched here: www.herts.police.uk/Information-and-services/About-us/Contact

You can also call the non-emergency number 101, and ask for Herts Police.

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org
