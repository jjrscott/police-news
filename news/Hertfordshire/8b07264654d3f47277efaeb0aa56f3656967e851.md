* ### Police would like to speak to the man pictured as part of enquiries following an incident where a man was threatened.

 * ### It happened at around 10.30pm on Tuesday, January 23 at the Sella Supermarket in Parkhouse Court, Hatfield.

 * ### Anyone who recognises the man is asked to contact police.

Officers investigating an incident where verbal threats were made in Hatfield are releasing CCTV footage of a man they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

At around 10.30pm on Tuesday, January 23, a man entered the Sella Supermarket in Parkhouse Court and became verbally abusive towards the victim - a man aged in his 50s.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured is asked to contact PC Stephen Carter by emailing stephen.carter@herts.pnn.police.uk, call the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference B2/18/356 or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
