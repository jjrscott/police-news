* ### Do you recognise this person? Police would like to speak with them as part of enquiries into the theft of a purse in Smallford

 * ### It happened at Notcutts Garden Centre on Hatfield Road at 11.10am on Monday, August 27

 * ### If you know the woman pictured, please call 101 quoting crime reference 41/32144/18

Officers have released two images of a woman they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries into the theft of a purse in Smallford, near St Albans.

It happened at Notcutts Garden Centre on Hatfield Road at 11.10am on Monday, August 27.

The victim, a woman in her 70s, was shopping when her purse was stolen from her handbag.
