Three men have been charged in connection with an alleged burglary in Chorleywood.

Christian Serban, aged 27, of no fixed address, Robert Constantine Neagu, aged 23, of no fixed address and Daniel Cava, aged 23, of Sydenham Road in London, were arrested on October 23 and charged with burglary.

They appeared before Hatfield Remand Court on Thursday, October 25 where they were bailed to appear for a hearing at St Albans Crown Court on Monday, November 26.

Between 2pm and 2.40pm on October 23, a property in The Crump was allegedly broken into.
