Could you be extra-ordinary and volunteer your time as a Special Constable?

Special Constables have full police powers, uniform and protective equipment and work alongside the regular force.

We're holding an information session for anyone interested in finding out about becoming a volunteer Special Constable.

**The information evening is taking place on Thursday, September 27 at 7pm at Police Headquarters. Stanborough Road, Welwyn Garden City, AL8 6XF.**

You'll be able to find out more about what the role entails and gain valuable advice about the application process. There will also be an opportunity to speak with serving Specials.

To book a space at the event, please email _specialsrecruitment@herts.pnn.police.uk_ _._ Parking is available on site.

For more information about the role visit www.hertspolicespecials.co.uk
