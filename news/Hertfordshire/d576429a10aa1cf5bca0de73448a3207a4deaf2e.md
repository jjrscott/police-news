On December 18, a woman's bank card was stolen and subsequently used in Edmonton.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured as they believe he may have been in the area when the bank card was used and may have information that could assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises him is asked to contact Detective Constable Vicky Cornelius by emailing vicky.cornelius2@herts.pnn.police.uk, calling the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/67280/18 or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report _._
