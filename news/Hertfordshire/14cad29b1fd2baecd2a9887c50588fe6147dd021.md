* ### Do you recognise this person?

 * ### Police would like to speak to them as part of their enquiries into theft from cars in Northwood

 * ### If you can help, please contact police quoting 41/15521/19

Officers investigating a theft from two cars in Northwood have released an image of a man they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

It happened in Bourne End Road between 5.15am and 5.20am on Sunday, February 10.

Offender(s) gained entry to a black Mercedes B200 and a white BMW X5 before taking a pair of earphones, an iPod and a set of wireless earphones.

PC Carly Fry, who is investigating, said: "We are keen to identify and speak to the man pictured as we believe he may have been in the area at the time and have vital information that could assist our investigation. I appreciate his face is not clear but maybe you recognise his clothing?"

If you know this person, or have any other information, please contact PC Fry on _carly.fry@herts.pnn.police.uk_ , report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report or speak to an operator in our Force Communications Room via online web chat, quoting reference 41/15521/19.

Online web chat can be launched here: https://www.herts.police.uk/Information-and-services/About-us/Contact

You can also call the non-emergency number 101, and ask for Herts Police.

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
