Wanted Marjo Marku

 * ### Appeal to trace wanted Marjo Marku from London

 * ### He is wanted in connection with a burglary in Cheshunt

 * ### Anyone with information should call 101 quoting reference K2/18/307

Officers are appealing to members of the public to help trace a wanted man.

Marjo Marku, who is also known as Mario, aged 29, of Caladonian Road, London is wanted in connection with a burglary which occurred in Cheshunt on January 18.

It is believed that he may be in the Broxbourne, Cheshunt, Waltham Cross or Enfield areas.

Anyone who sees Marjo, or knows of his whereabouts is asked to contact PC Nicola Howard via the Hertfordshire Constabulary non-emergency number 101 or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report quoting crime reference: K2/18/307.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
