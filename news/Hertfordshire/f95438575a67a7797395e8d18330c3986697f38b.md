Police are releasing images of a man they wish to speak with as he may be a potential witness following a serious sexual offence which occurred in Croxley Green.

A woman was assaulted at the Premier Inn in Ascot Road, during the late evening of September 24.

Officers are keen to trace the man pictured as he may be able to assist the investigation.

Anyone with information is asked to contact is asked to contact the non-emergency number 101, quoting 41/42895/18 or report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
