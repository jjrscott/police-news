* ### Two people have been arrested following a stabbing incident in Watford.

 * ### A 17 year old boy and 19 year old man remain in custody at this time.

 * ### The victim, who suffered stab injuries to his wrist, is being treated in hospital.

Detectives have arrested two people following an incident in Watford yesterday (Thursday) where a 23 year old man suffered stab injuries to his wrist.

Officers have today arrested a 17 year old boy and 19 year old man in connection with the incident. They remain in police custody at this time.

The victim remains in hospital.

Anyone with information that could assist the on-going investigation is asked to contact Hertfordshire Constabulary via the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference C1/18/1344, or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
