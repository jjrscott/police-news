* ### We are trying to locate Wanted Paul Collins.

 * ### Paul is from Baldock and is wanted for criminal damage, threatening behaviour and harassment.

 * ### Anyone who sees him is asked to call police via 101.

Police are appealing to the public for their help in locating a wanted man from Baldock.

Thirty-five-year-old Paul Collins, of Nightingale Way, Baldock is wanted for criminal damage, threatening behaviour and harassment.

It is believed that Paul could be in either Hitchin or Baldock.

Anyone who sees Collins or who has any information about his whereabouts is asked to contact Hertfordshire Constabulary immediately via 101 and ask for Sergeant Jon Vine.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
