Detectives investigating a robbery in Hoddesdon have arrested two men.

The incident occurred at Ladbrokes in Stanstead Road, at around 8.45pm on Saturday, January 27.

Cash was allegedly stolen after staff members were threatened.

A 24-year-old man from Hoddesdon and a 26-year-old man from Broxbourne have been arrested on suspicion of robbery and remain in police custody at this time.

Police are still appealing for anyone with information about the incident to contact the Broxbourne Local Crime Unit on the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference K1/18/218. You can also report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
