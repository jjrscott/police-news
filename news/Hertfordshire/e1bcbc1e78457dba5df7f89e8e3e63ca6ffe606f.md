* ### Appeal to find wanted man Aaron Moore.

 * ### He was last seen in London and may be in the Bethnall Green area.

 * ### He has been residing in Stevenage.

 * ### Call 999 if you see him.

We are appealing for the public's help to trace a man who is wanted.

Aaron Moore, aged 33, has been residing at the Cygnet Hospital in Graveley Road, Stevenage, and was last seen in London this morning (Sunday, 4 October).

He was last seen wearing a black coat with a fur hood, dark blue t-shirt, dark blue jeans and black Air Max trainers. He was wearing a black face mask with a skull on it and also had a black rucksack and JD Sports draw string bag. It is believed he may be in the Bethnall Green area.

If you have information on his whereabouts, please report it.

You can report information by:

 * Filling out our online reporting form.
 * Speaking to an operator in our Force Communications Room via online web chat.
 * Calling our non-emergency telephone number **101** (in an emergency, always dial **999).**
 * Contacting Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111** or visiting the Crimestoppers website.
