Police in North Herts are trying to trace a cyclist after an incident in Whitwell High Street and Lawrence End Road in Peters Green, in which a number of motorists were spat at.

The offence happened on Sunday 17 May at 12.15pm and 12.30pm. Officers believe the man in the picture was in the area at the time and could have information which will help their investigation.

If you recognise this man, or have any information which will help the investigation, please contact PC Gary Pugsley via email.

You can also report information online, speak to an operator in our Force Communications Room via online web chat or call our non-emergency number 101 quoting reference 41/38535/20. If a crime is in progress or someone's life is in danger, call 999 immediately.

Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or report information online.
