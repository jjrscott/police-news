* ### The theft happened at Vodaphone in South Street on Friday, August 24.

 * ### Officers believe the men pictured could help them with their investigation.

 * ### If you recognise them, or have other information, please call 101 quoting crime reference 41/30597/18.

Officers investigating a theft in Bishop's Stortford have released CCTV images of three people they would like to speak to.

At around 2.55pm on Friday, August 24, three men entered EE in South Street and attempted to take an iPad before they were interrupted.

They left the shop and then entered Vodaphone next door where they took three iPhones.

Officers believe the men pictured may have been in the area at the time and could have vital information that will help with the investigation.

If you recognise them or have any other information about the theft, please contact Hertfordshire Constabulary on the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/30597/18.

You can also report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
