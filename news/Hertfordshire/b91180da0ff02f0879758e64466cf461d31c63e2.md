* ### A woman aged in her 70s had her handbag stolen as she shopped in Tesco.

 * ### Her bank cards were then used to make purchases.

 * ### Police would like to speak with the woman pictured as she may be able to assist with the investigation.

Police investigating the theft of a handbag in Rickmansworth have released an image of a woman they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

Between 1.45pm and 2.15pm on Tuesday, May 29 a woman aged in her 70s had her handbag stolen from her trolley as she shopped in Tesco, Harefield Road.

Her bank card was then used to purchase items.

Officers would like to speak with the woman pictured as she may have information which could assist with their enquiries.

Anyone who recognises her, or has information about the theft, is asked to contact Sgt Neil Canning via the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/2130/18. You can also report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
