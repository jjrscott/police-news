* ### A blue Honda CRV was damaged at Tesco in Oldings Corner, Hatfield.

 * ### The front and rear passenger doors were scratched.

 * ### Anyone with information is asked to contact PC Rebecca Cannon.

Police are appealing for witnesses and information following an incident where a car was damaged in Hatfield.

Between 11am and 11.30am on Saturday, June 2, a blue Honda CRV which was parked at Tesco, Oldings Corner, was scratched to the front and rear passenger doors.

Anyone who was in the car park between these times and who may have seen what happened or who has any information which could assist the investigation is asked to contact PC Rebecca Cannon by calling the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference number 41/ 4147/18 or report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
