* ### A 14-year-old girl has been charged in connection with an assault in Bishop's Stortford

 * ### She has been released on conditional bail until 24 October

 * ### This relates to an assault in Sworders Field on the evening of 13 September

A 14-year-old girl has been charged with three offences in connection with an assault in Bishop's Stortford.

The teenager was yesterday (19 September) charged with assault by beating, section 4a public order and criminal damage.

She has been released on conditional bail and is next due to appear at Stevenage Youth Court on Thursday 24 October.

This is in relation to an incident at 5pm on Friday 13 September, where a 14 year old girl was assaulted in Sworders Field, off The Causeway.

Safer Neighbourhood Inspector Chris Hunt said: "We understand this incident has understandably caused some upset within the community but I want to reassure residents that we take reports of this nature extremely seriously, and that we are supporting the victim and her family.

"I would urge people to refrain from speculating about the incident on social media, or sharing any related footage, and instead let us the police and judicial process take its course."

If you have any information about the incident and have not yet spoken to us, please get in touch quoting crime reference 41/84128/19.

You can email _SNTBishopsStortfordandSawbridgeworth@herts.pnn.police.uk_ , report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report, use online web chat here or call 101.
