* ### Damage was caused to a house window, rear door and conservatory by offender(s) attempting to gain access to the property in Springhall Lane, Sawbridgeworth.

 * ### The attempted burglary happened between 11.30pm on Wednesday, July 11 and 8am the following day.

 * ### Anyone who saw anything suspicious in the area is asked to contact PC Tom Leask.

Police are appealing for information and witnesses following an attempted burglary in Sawbridgeworth.

The incident happened in Springhall Lane between 11.30pm on Wednesday 11 July and 8am on Thursday 12 July.

During this time, offender(s) attempted to gain entry to a house, causing damage to a window, rear door and conservatory.

PC Tom Leask, who is investigating said: "Did you spot anyone acting suspiciously in the area during this time?

"I am appealing for any witnesses or anyone with information to get in touch with me, quoting crime reference 41/16801/18. You can contact me by calling the non-emergency number 101, or emailing."

You can also report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
