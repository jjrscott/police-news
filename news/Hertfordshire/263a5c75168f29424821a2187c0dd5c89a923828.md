Police have released images of two people they would like to speak to following the discovery of a cannabis farm in Waltham Cross.

Officers were called at 2.48pm on Tuesday, April 10 to assist the fire service in the High Street where the roof of a property was alight.

A cannabis farm was subsequently discovered at the property and officers have now launched an investigation.

Police would like to speak to the two people pictured as they may have information about the cannabis farm that could assist officers with the investigation.

Anyone who recognises the people, or has any other information about the cannabis farm, should contact PC Sian Wilkins from the Hertfordshire Constabulary Operational Support Group on the non-emergency number 101, quoting Operation Aplustre. You can also report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report .

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
