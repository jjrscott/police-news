* ### Do you recognise these women?

 * ### Police would like to speak to them as part of their enquiries into the theft of items at Co-op in Rickmansworth

 * ### If you know them, please contact police quoting 41/26890/19

Officers investigating a theft from a shop in Rickmansworth have released images of three women they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

It happened at the Co-op on Watford Road just after 6.10pm on Friday, March 22.

Offender(s) entered the store and put a number of food and drink items in a basket before leaving the store without making any attempt to pay.

PCSO Jessica Hill, from the Rickmansworth and Croxley Green Safer Neighbourhood Team, said: "We would like to identify the people pictured because we believe they may have vital information that could assist our investigation. If you recognise them, please contact me."

Any witnesses or anyone with information should contact PCSO Hill on _jessica.hill@herts.pnn.police.uk_ , report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report or speak to an operator in our Force Communications Room via online web chat, quoting reference 41/26890/19.

Online web chat can be launched here: www.herts.police.uk/Information-and-services/About-us/Contact

You can also call the non-emergency number 101, and ask for Herts Police.

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
