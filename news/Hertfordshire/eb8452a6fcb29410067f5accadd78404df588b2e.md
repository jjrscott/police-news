* ### Five more people have been charged following Operation Retuned.

 * ### Operation Retuned was a six month proactive operation in St Albans targeting the supply of Class A drugs.

Following on from Operation Retuned which saw a number of warrants carried out across St Albans to target the supply of Class A drugs, the following people have now been charged:

 * Donal Kelliher, aged 48, of Charrington Place, St Albans has been charged with supplying Class A drugs. He has been released on bail and will appear at St Albans Magistrates Court on Tuesday, January 2.
 * Kamrul Islam, aged 27, of Napier Close, London Colney, has been charged with supplying Class A drugs. He has been released on bail and will appear at St Albans Magistrates Court on Tuesday, January 2.
 * Damian Alexander, aged 39, of Mayflower Avenue, Hemel Hempstead, has been charged with conspiracy to supply Class A drugs. He has been released on bail and will appear at St Albans Magistrates Court on Tuesday, January 2.
 * Philippa Havard, aged 56, of Ladies Grove, St Albans, has been charged with being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs. She has been remanded and will appear at St Albans Magistrates Court on Friday, January 5.
 * Charles Oluyede, aged 27, of Theobald Street, has been charged with being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs. He has been remanded and will appear at St Albans Magistrates Court on Friday, January 5.
