* ### Do you recognise this person? Police would like to speak to them as part of enquiries into the theft of money from a shop in Welwyn Garden City

 * ### It happened at McColls newsagents in Howardsgate on Saturday, August 25

 * ### If you know who this is, please call 101 quoting crime reference 41/30925/18

Officers investigating a theft from a newsagents in Welwyn Garden City have released an image of a person they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

It happened at McColls newsagents in Howardsgate just after 5pm on Saturday, August 25.

The offender reached over to the till while another customer was being served and stole a quantity of cash before leaving the shop.

PCSO Cameron Abraham, from the Welwyn Hatfield Safer Neighbourhood Team, said: "We would be very keen to speak to the person pictured as we believe they may have information that could assist our investigation.

"I appreciate the image quality leaves a lot to be desired but if you think you know who this is, please contact us on 101 quoting crime reference 41/30925/18. Thank you."

You can also report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report
