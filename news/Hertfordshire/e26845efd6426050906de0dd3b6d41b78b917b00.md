A man who was wanted by Hertfordshire Constabulary has been arrested and charged.

Gary Jakeman, aged 28 and of no fixed address, was arrested yesterday and has been charged with the following:

 * Four counts of fraud by false representation across Stevenage

 * Vehicle interference in Cottonmill Lane, St Albans on Saturday, March 24 2018

He has been remanded to attend Hatfield Remand Court today.
