* ### Did you see anything suspicious activity in Colney Fields Shopping Park?

 * ### Police are investigating a theft of fuel from a lorry on Thursday February 15.

 * ### Please call the Constabulary's Investigation Management Unit on 01707 354470 with information.

Police are appealing for witnesses and information following a theft of fuel in Colney Fields Shopping Park, London Colney.

Between 3.45pm and 5pm on Thursday February 15, an offender or offenders have stolen 120 to 150 litres of diesel from a lorry.

The vehicle was parked in Sainsbury's car park directly in front of the store when the offence occurred.

Anyone who saw anything suspicious is asked to contact Hertfordshire Constabulary's Investigation Management Unit on 01707 354470, quoting crime reference F3/18/383, or report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
