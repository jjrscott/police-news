* ### Warrant executed at Stevenage Address.

 * ### Class A drugs seized.

 * ### Investigation into drug activity at the property continues.

Officers from the Stevenage Operation Scorpion Team executed a warrant in Homestead Moat this morning (Wednesday September 12).

At around 7am the team entered a property and a search was carried out. A quantity of what is believed to be class A drugs was subsequently seized.

Enquiries into the nature of the drugs and the activity at the address continue. Anyone with any information about drug crime in the area is asked to contact 101 or report online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
