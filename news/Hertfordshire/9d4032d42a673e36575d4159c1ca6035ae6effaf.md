 * ### Appeal to find wanted Michael Barnet from Cheshunt

 * ### Appeal in connection with shoplifting offences in Cheshunt

 * ### Anyone with information should call 101 or report online

Police are appealing to the public for their help in locating a wanted man.

Michael Barnet, aged 36, of Turners Hill, Cheshunt, is wanted in connection with shoplifting offences in Cheshunt.

He is believed to be in the Cheshunt area.

Anyone who sees him, or has any information about his whereabouts, is asked to contact PC Charlotte Byers on the Hertfordshire Constabulary non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference K2/17/4787 or by emailing charlotte.byers@herts.pnn.police.uk.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
