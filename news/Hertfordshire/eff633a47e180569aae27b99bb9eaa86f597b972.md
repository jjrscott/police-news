 * ### Suspected stolen power tools, generators and bike recovered in Cheshunt

 * ### If you recognise these items please call 101 or 01992 533277 quoting crime reference 41/7784/18

 * ### A 43 years old man has been arrested on suspicion of handling stolen goods

Police in Cheshunt have recovered a haul of suspected stolen items and would like to reunite them with their owners.

The recovered items include dozens of power tools, a Makita dab radio, power generators and a black Scott bicycle.

recovered generators

PC Richard Partridge, who is investigating, said: "We would like to reunite these goods with their rightful owners, there's hundreds of pounds worth of tools and equipment, so if you recognise any of these items, please call us on 01992 533277 or 101 quoting crime reference 41/7784/18."

A 43-year-old man from Cheshunt has been arrested on suspicion of handling stolen goods. He has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
