A woman has been charged in connection with a distraction burglary in Hitchin.

Tracey Southgate, aged 50, of Maytrees, Hitchin, has been charged with burglary dwelling and theft.

She has been remanded in custody and is next due to appear at St Albans Crown Court on Monday 18 November.

This is in connection with an incident in Hollow Lane on the evening of Thursday 10 October, where a man in his 90s was burgled.

DC Michael Hardiman, from the North Herts Local Crime Unit, said: "I would like to thank the public for their assistance with the investigation so far, and for taking the time to contact me.

"We now await the outcome of the pre-trial preparation hearing scheduled for next month."
