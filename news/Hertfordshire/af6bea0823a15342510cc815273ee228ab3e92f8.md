 * ### Keep an eye on your neighbourhood and call police if you see anyone acting suspiciously.

Police are asking for members of the public's help in identifying two men captured on CCTV in St Albans.

They were seen in Puddingstone Drive and Newfield Way in Highfield Park, St Albans and may have vital information about attempted thefts from motor vehicles in the area.

Anyone who recognises the men is asked to contact police on 101 quoting crime reference F3/18/424, speak to Detective Sergeant Karen Lewis on 01707 806386 or report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
