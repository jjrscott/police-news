* ### Appeal for information after handbag stolen in Waltham Cross.

 * ### The theft occurred on Saturday August 11 in Iceland.

 * ### Anyone who recognises the person pictured or has information is asked to call 101 quoting reference 41/26442/18.

Police investigating the theft of a handbag in Waltham Cross have released images of a woman they would like to speak to for help with their enquiries.

The handbag was stolen from a shopping trolley at Iceland in Waltham Cross High Street on at around 10.14am on Saturday August 11.

Officers would like to speak with the person pictured as they may have information which could assist the investigation.

Anyone with information is asked to contact PCSO Steve Jones on the Hertfordshire Constabulary's non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/26442/18. You can also report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
