* ### Officers found the jewellery on Thursday, April 12, near Junction 17 of the M25, between Chorleywood and Mill End.

 * ### A total of 31 items were found.

 * ### If you believe this jewellery is yours or you recognise it, contact PC Matt Perrin on 101 or matt.perrin@herts.pnn.police.uk

Officers are releasing photos of items of suspected stolen jewellery in an attempt to trace the rightful owners.

Officers found the jewellery on Thursday, April 12, near Junction 17 of the M25, between Chorleywood and Mill End.

PC Matt Perrin said: "31 items of jewellery were found discarded and we believe that they may have been stolen. I am asking people to take a look at these photos and contact me if they believe this jewellery is theirs or they recognise it as belonging to someone they know.

"You can email me or on the non-emergency number 101. You can also report information online."

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
