* ### Teen charged with attempted murder in connection with stabbing in Watford

 * ### Remanded at court today

 * ### Second man released under investigation

A 17 year old boy from Edgware has been charged with attempted murder in connection with a stabbing which occurred in Watford on Thursday (8th March).

The victim, a 23 year old man, suffered injuries to his wrist following in incident following an altercation in the High Street at around 2pm.

The 17 year old appeared before Hatfield Remand Court this morning (10th March) where he was remanded into custody.

A 23 year old man who was also arrested in connection with the incident has been released under investigation.
