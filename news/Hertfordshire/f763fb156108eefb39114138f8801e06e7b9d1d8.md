* ### An estimated £50,000 worth of cannabis resin seized by police.

 * ### Two men from Scotland charged.

 * ### They are back in court next month.

An estimated £50,000 worth of cannabis resin was seized by police after they stopped a vehicle on the A405 St Albans Road at 12.55pm on Tuesday (July 10).

Laurence Phee, aged 47, of Carlisle Road and Patric Hatti, age 49, of Lammermuir Way, both in Airdrie, Scotland have been charged with possession with intent to supply controlled drugs and they appeared at Hatfield Remand Court yesterday (Wednesday July 11).

They have been released on conditional bail to reside in Airdrie and are due to appear at St Albans Crown Court on Friday August 10.
