Police are appealing for the public's help to trace a man who is from Stevenage.

Billy Smith, aged 21 from Sish Lane is wanted in connection to an assault (ABH) which took place in the town.

Anyone who sees Billy or knows of his whereabouts is asked to contact Hertfordshire Constabulary via the non-emergency number 101, quoting reference 41/67605/19 or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
