* ### Officers want to speak to the man pictured following a burglary in Bushey.

 * ### A home in The Squirrels was burgled and a Mini was stolen from outside the property.

 * ### The man pictured may have information that could help the on-going investigation.

Officers investigating a burglary where a vehicle was stolen in Bushey have issued images of a man from a door bell camera that they wish to speak to as part of their enquiries.

A grey Mini was stolen from an address in The Squirrels between 11pm on January 5 and 8.15am on January 6. Offender(s) gained access to the property via the front door where they stole car keys and cash before taking the Mini.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured is asked to contact Sergeant Dave Smith quoting crime reference J2/18/28, via the non-emergency number 101, or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
