Officers investigating the theft of a bike in Bishop's Stortford have released a photo of a man who might be able to help them with their enquiries.

Between 2.15pm and 2.25pm on Wednesday, September 5, a purple bicycle was taken from outside Wilkinsons in Jackson Square.

PCSO Adam Pemberton, who is investigating, said: "Thankfully, the bike has now been found and it's been returned to its owner.

"We believe the man pictured was in the area around the time of the theft and he could have vital information that will help the investigation.

"If you recognise the man, or have information about the theft, please call me on the non-emergency number 101 or email me at adam.pemberton@herts.pnn.police.uk quoting crime reference 41/33997/18."

You can also report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
