Police are releasing a CCTV image of a man they would like to identify as part of their enquiries following the theft of post in Bushey Heath.

Between January 27 and February 16, post has been repeatedly stolen from a post box at a property in Fuller Close.

Officers would like to speak to the man pictured as they believe he may have information that can assist their investigation.

Anyone who recognises him or has information that could assist police is asked to contact PC Gerard Handscombe by emailing _gerard.handscombe@herts.pnn.police.uk_ , calling the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/15444/19 or online at www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can stay anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
