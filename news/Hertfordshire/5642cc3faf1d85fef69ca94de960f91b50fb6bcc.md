Jake Wheeler

 * ### Appeal to trace wanted Jake Wheeler from Wormley

 * ### He is wanted in connection with an assault

 * ### Anyone who knows his whereabouts is asked to contact us via the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime ref: K2/18/1816

Officers are appealing for the public's help to trace a 23-year-old man who is wanted.

Jake Wheeler, from Macer's Court, Wormley is wanted in connection with an assault.

Anyone who sees him or knows of his whereabouts is asked to contact Hertfordshire Constabulary via the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference K2/18/1816 or report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
