 * ## Appeal for information following shoplifting offences in Cheshunt

 * ## Items of clothing were stolen from New Look at the Brookfield Retail Park

 * ## Images released of people who may be able to help with police enquiries

Police investigating shoplifting from New Look at the Brookfield Retail Park in Cheshunt are releasing CCTV images of two women they would like to speak to for help with their enquiries.

The theft occurred at 7pm on Monday October 22, when items of clothing were stolen from the store. Officers would like to speak with the people pictured as they may have information which could assist the investigation.

Anyone who recognises them, or has other information, is asked to contact PCSO Kirsty Smith by calling the non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/49352/18. You can also report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
