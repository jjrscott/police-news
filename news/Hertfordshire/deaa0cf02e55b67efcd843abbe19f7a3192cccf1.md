Officers investigating a vehicle interference offence in Potters Bar have released two images of a man they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

Sometime between 6.30pm on February 16 and 3.31pm on February 17, a man approached a vehicle in Pinewood Drive and attempted to open the driver side door before making off.

The vehicle was parked off the street on a driveway.

Police would like to speak with the man pictured as he may have information which could assist enquiries.

If you recognise the man pictured or witnessed the incident, please contact PCSO Ramdeen on christopher.ramdeen@herts.pnn.police.uk or the non-emergency number 101, quoting reference 41/16063/19. You can also report information online at www.herts.police.uk/Report

Alternatively, you can stay 100% anonymous by contacting the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or via their untraceable online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.

For over 30 years, Crimestoppers has always kept its promise of anonymity to everyone who contacts them.
