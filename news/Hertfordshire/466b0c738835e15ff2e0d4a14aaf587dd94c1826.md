* ### Hatfield man arrested and charged following arrest in Stevenage.

 * ### Man stopped after seen acting suspiciously in Stevenage.

 * ### Robert Chubb arrested and charged under Proceeds of Crime Act.

A 30 year old man from Hatfield has been charged following his arrest in Stevenage in the early hours of Monday November 6.

Robert Chubb of Goldings Crescent in Hatfield was stopped in Valley Way, Stevenage at around 2.45am, by officers carrying out a routine patrol of the area. They had observed him behaving suspiciously around some of the properties in the road and he was also found to be in possession of several bags of items.

Chubb was arrested on suspicion of theft and taken into custody at Stevenage Police Station.

He was subsequently charged with being in possession of stolen property under the Proceeds of Crime Act and appeared at Hatfield Remand Court later that day (Monday November 6.)

Chubb was granted bail and is due to return to Stevenage Magistrates Court on January 8.
