* ### Did you witness arson in Hitchin town centre?

 * ### We'd like to identify three girls who may be able to assist police with their enquiries

Officers investigating an arson which occurred at Starbucks in Market Place, Hitchin yesterday morning (July 16) have released an image of three girls they would like to speak to as part of their enquiries.

The incident happened between 12.30am and 1.05am when rubbish bags at the side of the property were deliberately set on fire, causing damage to the window and guttering of the property.

If you can identify the people pictured, or have any further information that could assist the investigation, please contact Detective Sergeant Sarah Gilbertson at sarah.gilbertson@herts.pnn.police.uk or via the non-emergency number 101. You can also report information online at www.herts.police.uk/report. Please quote crime reference 41/17928/18.

Alternatively, if you have any information about this incident you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
