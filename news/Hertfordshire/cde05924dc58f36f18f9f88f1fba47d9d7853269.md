* ### Appeal after two motor cross bikes stolen in Waltham Cross.

 * The bikes were stolen from Bulls Cross Ride on August 9.

 * ### Anyone with information is asked to contact the Hertfordshire Constabulary non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/25773/18.

Police are releasing images of two motor bikes that were stolen during a burglary at a home in Bulls Cross ride.

The burglary occurred between midnight and 12.45 am on Thursday, August 9, where offenders have broken into a container in the victim's garden and stolen two motor cross bikes and tools. The stolen bikes were a green Kawasaki and an orange KTM.

Anyone who has information about this offence or who has seen the bikes pictured is asked to contact the Hertfordshire Constabulary non-emergency number 101, quoting crime reference 41/25773/18 or report information online.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
