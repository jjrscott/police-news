* ### Officers investigating an assault in Hatfield are appealing for witnesses.

 * ### The incident happened in Whitebeams on Thursday 21 November.

 * ### 2 men, who are believed to be known to eachother, were involved in a physical altercation while sitting in a stationary vehicle.

Officers are appealing for witnesses and information following an assault in Hatfield.

The incident happened at around 12.40pm on Thursday 21 November, in Whitebeams.

Following a verbal disagreement, two men were involved in a physical altercation while sitting inside a stationary vehicle. Both men sustained minor facial injuries.

The two men are believed to be known to each other.

PC Callum Walford, of the Welwyn Hatfield Safer Neighbourhood Team, said: "We're appealing for anyone who was in the area who may have witnessed this incident taking place, or may have any information, to please get in contact.

"Any information, no matter how minor it may seem, could be invaluable to our investigation."

Anyone with information can contact PC Walford directly via email at Callum.Walford@herts.pnn.police.uk.

You can also report information online, speak to an operator in our Force Communications Room via online web chat or call our non-emergency number 101 quoting crime reference 41/105088/19. If a crime is in progress or someone's life is in danger, call 999 immediately.

Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or report information online.
