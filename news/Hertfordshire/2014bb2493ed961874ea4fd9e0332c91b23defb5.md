* ### A shop in the high street was broken into.

 * ### Mobile phones, laptops and tablets were stolen.

 * ### Police would like to speak with the people pictured for help with their enquiries.

Police investigating a burglary in Rickmansworth have released an image of two people they would like to speak to for help with their enquiries.

Overnight between June 8 and June 9, a phone shop in the High Street was broken into and mobile phones, laptops and tablets were stolen.

Officers would like to identify the people pictured as they may have been in the area at the time and could have information that would assist the investigation.

Anyone with information is asked to contact PCSO Alan Waller the non-emergency number 101 or by email to alan.waller@herts.pnn.police.uk, quoting crime reference 41/5841/18. You can also report information online at https://www.herts.police.uk/Report.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or through their Anonymous Online Form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org. No personal details are taken, information cannot be traced or recorded and you will never need to go to court.
