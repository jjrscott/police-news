Country Watch officers carrying out patrols in Hart's rural districts arrested a man last night on suspicion of going equipped for theft.

The arrest happened just before midnight on May 13 in Hook Road, Hook, after a suspicious vehicle was stopped and searched by the officers. The vehicle and items within, including a machete, have been seized.

The 26 year old man, from Bracknell, has been arrested on suspicion of being in possession of an offensive weapon and going equipped

He remains in custody at this time.
