Detectives investigating a robbery in Chandlers Ford on Saturday morning (28 November) have charged a woman.

It comes after a distraction burglary, which took place on Saturday 28 November around 9.50am at a property in Belmond Road, Chandler's Ford.

Ruby Ventham, 27, of Cox Row, Chandlers Ford, has been charged with robbery - she was remanded to appear at Southampton Magistrates Court today, Monday, 30 November.

A handbag was taken but has not yet been located and officers believe it may have been discarded in the area.

If you see a navy blue leather Hotter handbag - which included an amount of cash, cards and photographs - or a blue tartan purse and brown wallet in the area, please contact us on 101 quoting 44200460326
