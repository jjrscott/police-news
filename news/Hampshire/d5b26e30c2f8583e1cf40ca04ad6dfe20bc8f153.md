Officers have charged Chorley Laxton, 18, of Taranto Road, Southampton with 15 offences including, burglary and vehicle taking.

He was remanded in custody last night and is due to appear at Southampton Magistrates Court today (Monday 15 July.)

He has been charged with:

• Three counts of burglary relating to incidents where houses were burgled and cars were taken in Romsey, Fareham and Southampton.

• Five counts of aggravated vehicle taking

• Two counts of fraud by false representation

• Assault on an emergency worker

• Drive a motor vehicle dangerously

• Breach of Criminal Behaviour Order (CBO)

• Driving whilst disqualified

• Use a motor vehicle with no insurance
