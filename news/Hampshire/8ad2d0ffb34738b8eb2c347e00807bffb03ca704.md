A woman has been charged as part of an investigation into an assault on a 63-year-old woman in Bishop's Waltham.

Officers were called to an address in Oak Road at 11.30am on Monday 22 July.

Paula Jane Ayres, 51, of Willow Road, Bishop's Waltham, has been charged with attempted murder.

She was remanded in custody and is due to appear at Portsmouth Magistrates' Court today (Wednesday 24 July).
