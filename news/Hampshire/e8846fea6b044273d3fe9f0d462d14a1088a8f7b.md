Can you help us identify this woman?

We are looking to speak to her in connection with an assault that happened at the Injoy Play Centre, Ocean Village, Southampton, this month.

Between 6pm and 6.30pm on Sunday 2 February a woman in her 40s was assaulted in front of families. The victim sustained facial injuries.

The woman is described as being white, between the ages of 25 and 30. She is approximately 5ft 3ins tall and has a slim build.

Notably she is described as having dyed red hair with a bar through her lip and lots of facial piercings.

She was wearing jeans and tan coloured boots.

Do you recognise the woman in this CCTV image?

Anyone with information can call 101, quoting 44200040547. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
