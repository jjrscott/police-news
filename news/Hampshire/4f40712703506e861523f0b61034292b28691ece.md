We are investigating an incident that happened between 5am and 5.30am on the forecourt at Woolston Service Station on Portsmouth Road in Southampton on Monday 1 February.

It is believed this man may have been in an altercation with a group of men in a car who reportedly were chasing him with weapons.

No one was reported as injured.

The weapons have been described as machetes and the car the group were in has been described as white Ford Kuga.

We are carrying out a number of enquiries in relation to this incident, but do you know the man pictured in this CCTV image?

Furthermore, is this you?

We are trying to ascertain the exact circumstances what happened and would therefore ask anyone with information about who was involved to get in contact with us.

Call 101 and quoting 44210037867.
