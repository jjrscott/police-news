Officers responding to reports of a drunk man lying unconscious in a road in Hythe, have made an arrest.

The incident occurred at 10.54pm on Sunday 27 September on Fawley Road, Hythe.

Officers escorted the man to the side of the road where he became abusive and aggressive towards them and members of the public in the vicinity.

A 24-year-old man from Southampton was arrested for on suspicion of committing a public order offence.

He then assaulted two police officers as they attempted to engage with him and was subsequently further arrested on suspicion of assaulting emergency service workers.

He was later released under investigation pending further enquiries.
