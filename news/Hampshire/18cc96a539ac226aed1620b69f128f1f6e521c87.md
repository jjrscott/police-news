**Do you recognise this man? We want to speak to him in connection with a robbery in Basingstoke.**

**The incident occurred at around 6.50pm on Tuesday, October 22. The victim, a 26 year old man from Basingstoke, was walking from underneath the Victory Roundabout onto a pathway into Brookvale Park.**

**As the suspect walked past him, he asked for a cigarette paper. When the victim said he didn 't have one, the suspect showed him a knife before stealing the victim's bag containing a grey Dell E6440 laptop with charger, black headphones, white phone cable, work papers and other items.**

** ** **He also took the victim 's black leather wallet containing cash, cards and driving licence, before making off.**

** ** **If you think you recognise the person in the E-fit image or if you have any information about this crime, please get in touch quoting reference number 44190378883.**

** ** **Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.**
