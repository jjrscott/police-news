Officers investigating a non-dwelling burglary have charged a man.

Dariusz Serej, 41, of Millbrook Point Road, has been charged with non-dwelling burglary with intent to steal.

The charge comes after a man was seen breaking into a shed at an address on Arthur Road, Southampton in the early hours of Thursday, 14 January.

Serej has also been charged with receiving stolen goods, burglary and attempted burglary relating to separate incidents at two addresses on Northlands Road in October 2020.

He appeared at Southampton Magistrates' Court today, 15 January and has been remanded to appear at Southampton Crown Court on Friday, 12 February.
