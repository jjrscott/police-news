We are appealing for witnesses and information after the front of a restaurant was sprayed with yellow paint.

The incident occurred between 11.40pm and 11.50pm on October 24 at the Jas Indian Restaurant in Heath End Road. The restaurant is due to open shortly.

Two suspects were seen wearing face coverings and one was carrying a spray can.

If you can help, please get in touch by calling 101 and asking for PC John Hayes from Tadley Police Station. The reference number is 44200415201.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
