A 20 year old man has been arrested and charged with possessing Class A drugs with intent to supply in Basingstoke.

Errol McLean of Lower Addiscombe Road, Croydon, was arrested in Hackwood Road, Basingstoke on April 2.

He was charged with possessing Class A drugs, namely heroin and crack cocaine, with intent to supply and is appearing before Basingstoke Magistrates Court today, (Friday).
