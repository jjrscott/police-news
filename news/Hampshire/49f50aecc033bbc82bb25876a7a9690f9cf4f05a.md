We are investigating a robbery which took place at Central Stores in Avenue Road, Sandown, last night (Wednesday 27 November).

It was reported that a man entered the store at around 8.30pm and told staff to empty the till.

The man, who had his face covered, was holding an item described as a pellet gun.

He then left the store with a quantity of cash.

Enquiries are ongoing.

If you have any information about this incident, please call us on 101, quoting 44190427405.

Alternatively you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their anonymous online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org/
