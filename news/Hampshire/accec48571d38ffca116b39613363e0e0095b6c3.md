We are appealing for witnesses after a report of a burglary at an address in Thyme Avenue, Whiteley.

The incident happened some time between midnight on Sunday 29 November and 2.30pm on Tuesday 1 December.

It has been reported that jewellery was stolen from the property during the burglary.

Officers are investigating the incident and are keen to hear from anyone who has any information.

Maybe you saw something suspicious in the area of Thyme Avenue on these days?

Anyone with information should call 101, quoting the reference 44200464542.
