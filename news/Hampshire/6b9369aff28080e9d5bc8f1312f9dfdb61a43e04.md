Officers investigating an indecent exposure incident in Fleming Park have last night (18 August) arrested a man.

The incident occurred on Thursday 6 August at around 4.20pm.

A 24-year-old man from Fareham was arrested on suspicion of indecent exposure and was released under investigation pending further enquiries.

As part of this investigation, we are reviewing a number of other indecent exposures that have happened in the area. Enquiries remain ongoing in connection with these incidents.
