Officers investigating drugs offences in Southampton have arrested two men.

Officers stopped a car in Melbourne Street, Southampton, yesterday afternoon (10 June) and arrested a 19-year-old man from Southampton and a 20-year-old man from the New Forest on suspicion of possession of a class A drug with intent to supply.

They have both been released from custody but remain under investigation as our enquiries continue.

If you have concerns about drug-related activity in your area please call us on 101, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Tackling drug-related harm in our city is a priority for us and information from residents and businesses in the area can help us ensure we are doing all we can to disrupt this type of activity within our communities.
