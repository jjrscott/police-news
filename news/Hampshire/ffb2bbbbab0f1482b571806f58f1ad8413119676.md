Officers investigating a burglary and two attempted burglaries have charged a man.

Joshua Taylor, 23, of no fixed abode, has been charged with burglary, burglary with intent to steal, attempted burglary and going equipped for theft.

The charge comes after a number of power tools were taken from a shed in Witt Road, Eastleigh, in the early hours of Saturday, May 9.

It was also reported that around the same time, between 2.45am and 3.30am, attempts were made to break in to sheds in Spring Close and Summerlands Road, Fair Oak.

Mr Taylor has been remanded in custody to appear at Southampton Magistrates' Court this morning (May 11).
