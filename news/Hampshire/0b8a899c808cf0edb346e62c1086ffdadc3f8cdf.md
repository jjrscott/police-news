We are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at a flat in Portsmouth early on the morning of Friday 20 November.

We were called just after 6.05am to the incident, in which a man broke into a flat on Nelson Road and stole a backpack.

We would like to speak to a man in connection with the incident. He is described as follows:

 * Around 20-years-old
 * Approximately 5 feet 6 inches
 * Black
 * Slim build
 * Wearing a dark, hooded top

We would also like to speak to anyone that may have been in the area at the time or anyone that may have CCTV footage of the area.

Anyone with information is asked to call 101 with the reference 44200449165.

Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
