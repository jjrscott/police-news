Police officers have charged Todd James Edward Cooper, aged 26, of Whitelands, Thorney Hill, Bransgore with;

• one count of ABH assault
• two counts of using abusive or threatening behaviour causing harassment, alarm or distress
• one count of causing criminal damage

The charges come after a woman was assaulted in Sway on Tuesday 8 September 2020 and threats to kill were made towards another woman in New Milton on Sunday 8 November 2020.

He has been remanded in custody and will next appear at Southampton Magistrates Court on Friday 26 February.
