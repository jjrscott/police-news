We're appealing for information following a public order incident in Andover.

The incident took place in King George Road, Andover, at around 11.45pm on Tuesday 11 August.

It was reported that there was an altercation amongst a group of people. Officers attended.

Two people, a 52-year-old man and a 26-year-old woman, were taken to hospital with minor injuries.

A third person, a 28-year-old man, sustained a minor injury but did not require hospital treatment.

Officers are investigating the circumstances of this incident and would like to hear from anyone who saw what happened.

No arrests have been made.

Anyone with information should call police on 101, quoting the incident number 44200305741.

Alternatively, people can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
