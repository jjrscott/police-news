Two teenagers have been arrested following an attempted knifepoint robbery at a Basingstoke store.

The incident occurred at 6.15pm on Saturday, March 15, at Bhuller store in St Peter's Road.

Both boys were arrested on suspicion of attempted robbery.

A 19 year old from Basingstoke has been given conditional bail.

A 17 year old from Basingstoke has been released under investigation.

Police enquiries are ongoing. If you have any information about the crime, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200096999.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
