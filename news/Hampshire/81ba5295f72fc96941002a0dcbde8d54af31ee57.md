Police investigating a fire at an address on Priestley Road have charged a man with arson with intent to endanger life, and criminal damage.

The incident happened at around 1.50pm on Monday 6 April.

Dean Puttock, 36, of Priestley Road in Basingstoke was charged last night and has been remanded in custody.

He will appear at Basingstoke Magistrates Court today (8 April).
