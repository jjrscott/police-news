Two teenagers have been charged following a robbery at a Co-op store in Southampton.

A 15-year-old boy from Southampton has been charged with robbery and possession of a firearm with intent to cause fear of violence.

A 14-year-old boy from Southampton has been charged with robbery and possession of a bladed article.

It comes following a robbery at the Co-op store on Portsmouth Road, Sholing just after 9pm on 7 March, where cigarettes and tobacco were stolen.

Both teenagers appeared at Southampton Youth Court this morning (9 March) and have been released on conditional bail to appear again on Friday, 20 March.
