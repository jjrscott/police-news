We are now in a position to name the man who died in Havant on Saturday 23 May.

George Allison, aged 21, of Riders Lane, Havant, died after he suffered fatal stab wounds at an incident in Tichborne Grove.

His family has released this image of George to pay tribute.

Georges family ask for privacy at this very sad time.

Officers are continuing to investigate the incident that happened at 11pm on Saturday 23 May, whereby a 27-year-old woman was also attacked. She was taken to hospital with serious but not life threatening injuries.

If you have any information that you think might help with this investigation, please get in touch by calling 101, quoting 44200184002.
