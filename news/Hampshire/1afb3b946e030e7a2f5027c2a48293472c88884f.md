We have charged two men following a report of an aggravated burglary at a home on Tatwin Crescent just after 5am yesterday (September 17).

Jack Daniel Cove, aged 21 of no fixed address and Donald John Haisman, aged 22, of no fixed address have both been charged with aggravated burglary.

Jack Cove has also been charged with three further offences

• Aggravated burglary at a home on Adelaide Road, Southampton on March 23
• Aggravated burglary at a home on Winchester Road, Botley on May 12
• Burglary of a home on Walker Gardens, Hedge End on August 5.

Both men will appear before Southampton Magistrates Court today (Sept 18).
