We are appealing for witnesses or information after a bronze statue of a boxer dog was stolen in Cliddesden, Basingstoke.

The statue went missing between 6pm on April 10 and 7.30am on April 11. The life size statue was in the garden at the property in Basingstoke Road.

We are appealing for anyone who saw anything suspicious or who may recognise the statue from the photograph. If you can help, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200130600.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
