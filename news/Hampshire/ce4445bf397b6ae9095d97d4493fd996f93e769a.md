We are appealing for information as part of a house burglary investigation in Andover.

It took place in Camelot Close, sometime between 9pm and 10pm on Friday 23 August.

Nothing was stolen.

It is believed that the offender ran from the house. This person is described as being of muscular build, and wearing a black, zipped up hooded top and leather gloves.

The incident was reported to police five days later.

We appealing for information to assist in the identification of the offender.

Do you remember being in this area at the time? Did you see anything suspicious?

Perhaps you were driving past and may have some dashcam footage that could help our investigation?

If so, please call us on 101, quoting the crime reference number 44190306173.

Alternatively, people can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
