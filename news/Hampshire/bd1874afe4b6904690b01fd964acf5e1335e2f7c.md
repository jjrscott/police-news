Three men have been arrested on suspicion of non-residential burglary.

The three, a 38 year old man of no fixed abode, a 35 year old man and a 31 year old man, both from Basingstoke, were arrested on suspicion of burglary at a building site in Meudon Avenue, Farnborough on November 7.

They have all been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
