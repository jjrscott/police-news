Officers investigating a burglary in Southampton have charged a man.

Brian Mitchell, 44, of Lyon Street, Southampton has been charged with burglary other than dwelling.

It comes following a burglary at E & J Jarvis Ltd, on Onslow Road, between 11.11pm on November 9 and 12.48am November 10.

Nothing was stolen during the burglary.

Brian Mitchell was due to appear at Southampton Magistrates Court today, Monday December 14.
