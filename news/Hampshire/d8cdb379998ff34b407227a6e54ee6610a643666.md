Do you recognise this man?

We would like to speak to him after money was stolen from the till at the Lymington Community Centre cafe in New Street.

It took place sometime between 3.30pm and 4pm on Tuesday 6 August.

A man entered the cafe while it was still open. He leant over the counter and took £120 cash from the till, before leaving.

The man is described as:

• white
• aged between 45 and 60 years old
• of plump build
• balding

We have released a CCTV image of a man we would like to speak to in connection with this incident.

Do you recognise him?

If so, call us on 101, quoting the crime reference number 44190278312.
