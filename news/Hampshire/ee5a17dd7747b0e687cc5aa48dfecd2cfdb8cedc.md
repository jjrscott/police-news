We are appealing for the public's help in tracing this distinctive green Landrover Defender, which was stolen off the owner's driveway in Basingstoke on Thursday night.

The Landrover, whose index number's last three digits are 'RYT', has a distinctive white roof and was taken from Purcell Close at around 10pm on October 10.

If anyone has seen this vehicle or thinks they recognise the description, please get in contact by calling 101 and quoting 44190363408.

Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111
