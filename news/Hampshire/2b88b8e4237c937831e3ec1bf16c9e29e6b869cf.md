Three men have been charged with conspiracy to supply Class A drugs in Basingstoke.

The three were arrested following a warrant at a property in The Moorings at around 4.45pm on December 14.

They are:

* Darren Bryant, 36, of Skippetts Gardens, Basingstoke

* Terry Hall, 38, of Quilter Road, Basingstoke

* Saran Man Uam, 29, of The Moorings, Basingstoke

All three were remanded in custody to appear before Basingstoke Magistrates Court today, (Wednesday, December 16).
