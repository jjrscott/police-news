A number of people have been arrested in connection with an altercation in Queens Road, Farnborough.

Four have been arrested on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm following the incident near the Co-op store on Queens Road at around 8.34pm and 8.50pm on Tuesday, August 27.

They are three men in their 20s from Ash and a man in his 30s from Ash Vale.

All have been released on conditional bail.

A fifth man, a man in his 20s from Farnborough, was also arrested on suspicion of attempted grievous bodily harm, affray and money laundering and has been released under investigation.

A man has also been charged in connection with the same incident. Gregory Small, 27, of Guildford Road East, Farnborough, is charged with possessing Class A and Class B drugs with intent to supply.

He was remanded in custody to appear before Basingstoke Magistrates Court today, (Wednesday).
