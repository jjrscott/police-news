Officers have arrested two teenagers following a public order incident in Thornhill, Southampton yesterday (24 Sept).

At around 4.45pm on Thursday 24 September, it was reported that two groups of teenagers were fighting in the street outside the Co-op on Warburton Road.

A 16-year-old boy from Southampton has been arrested on suspicion of possession of an offensive weapon in a public place.

A 17-year-old boy from Southampton has been arrested on suspicion of affray and criminal damage.

Both remain in police custody at this time.
