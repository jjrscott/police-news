Do you recognise the man in this image?

We would like to speak to him after a 35-year-old man was assaulted in Plush in London Street, Andover.

It took place around 12.30am on Saturday 15 June.

An altercation took place in the bar after which the 35-year-old man was sat on the floor against a wall.

He was then approached by an unknown man, who punched him in the face.

The victim sustained fractures to his cheekbone and eyesocket and has had to undergo reconstructive surgery.

The man is described as:

• white
• aged between 20 and 30 years of age
• around 6ft tall
• of medium build
• dark hair
• had extensive tattoos on his left arm, and others on his right

We have released a CCTV image of a man we would like to speak to in connection with this investigation.

We would also like to speak to anyone who was in Plush that night and witnessed what happened.

Anyone with information can call us on 101, quoting the crime reference number 44190206058.

Alternatively, people can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
