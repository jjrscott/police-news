Officers have arrested a 23-year-old man from Southampton on suspicion of three offences of thefts of motor-vehicle.

It comes after officers attended an address in Mortimer Road, Botley on Monday, 16 November and recovered three stolen motorbikes.

The 23-year-old was interviewed and released under investigation while our enquiries continue.

The three motorbikes that were recovered have been returned to their owners.
