We would like to speak to the man pictured in the two CCTV images in connection with an assault at a Southampton pub which left a man with a broken jaw.

The incident occurred at around midnight outside the Mountbatten Pub in Lordshill District Centre on Saturday, June 1.

An altercation began between two males in a group stood outside of the pub, which was closed at the time and the victim was assaulted.

The victim, aged 33, and from Southampton, ran away from his assailant and later underwent surgery for a broken jaw.

The suspect is described as being a white male wearing a baggy white t-shirt. His hair is described as being shorn at the sides with slightly longer hair on the top.

If you have any information about this incident, or recognise the man in the image, please call 101 and quote reference 44190191902.
