Callum Robert Knox, aged 33, of no fixed address has been charged with two counts of assaulting a police officer following an incident at a property on Linacre Road in Southampton, on 3 May 2019.

He has also been detained for failing to appear at Southampton Magistrates' Court on Monday 17 June 2019, where he was due to face a charge of breaching a suspended sentence, and failing to appear at Slough Magistrates' Court, Berkshire, on 18 November 2019 in relation to a drugs offence.

He will appear before Southampton Magistrates Court on Wednesday 25 March.
