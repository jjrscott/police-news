Officers investigating the death of a woman in Portsmouth have charged a man.

Mark Brandford, 48, of Kingston Crescent, Portsmouth, has been charged with murder.

It comes following the discovery of a woman's body at an address in Kingston Crescent on the evening of December 17.

The 32-year-old's next of kin have been informed and are being supported by specialist officers.

Mark Brandford is due to appear at Portsmouth Magistrates Court on Monday morning (December 23).
