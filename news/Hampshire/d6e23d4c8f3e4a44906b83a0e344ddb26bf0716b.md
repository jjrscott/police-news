Do you recognise this man?

Officers investigation an assault in Rockbourne near Fordingbridge wish to speak to him in connection with the incident.

The incident occurred at 12.50pm on Saturday 11 July, where a road rage incident led to a man being assaulted outside of the Rose and Thistle pub on Rockbourne Road, causing cuts to his head.

The suspect was identified as white, medium build and wearing a blue polo top and blue jeans.

Enquiries to identify the man have been ongoing, but we are now asking the public to help us identify this man in the CCTV image.

If you recognise him or have any information relating to the incident, please call 101, quoting 44200256100.

Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
