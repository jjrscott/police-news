We are appealing for witnesses and information after two door staff were assaulted at an Aldershot nightclub.

The incident occurred between 2am and 2.30am in the early hours of Sunday, November 10.

Two men were asked to leave the vicinity of Truth Nightclub in Victoria Road. An altercation followed and two members of door staff were assaulted. Both required hospital treatment for injuries to their hands.

Were you in the area at the time? Did you witness the altercation or were you driving past and have dash cam footage?

If you have any information, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44190403516. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Two men have been arrested in connection with this incident and have been bailed.
