We are appealing for information or witnesses following a serious assault at the One Stop Shop in Milbrook at the weekend.

The incident occurred between 5.15pm and 5.30pm on Saturday, April 4 at the shop in Cumbrian Way.

Two males have entered the shop and one has assaulted a 23 year old man, resulting in him suffering a broken wrist, dislocated shoulder and bruising. He was taken to hospital following the incident.

If you saw anything on the day or have any information about this incident, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200121016. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Three people have been arrested in connection with the incident on suspicion of conspiracy to cause grievous bodily harm. A 42 year old woman, a 22 year old man and a 16 year old boy, all from Southampton, are on conditional bail while the investigation is ongoing.
