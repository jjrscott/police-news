We want to trace him following an incident at a home on Cranbury Road in Eastleigh on July 15.

At 6.30am it was reported two men approached the home and caused damage to the doors and fencing, it was believed to try and gain entry to the property.

Both men made threats of violence throughout the incident.

One man, a 21-year-old man from Eastleigh was arrested in connection with the incident and remains under investigation.

However, officers would now like to trace the man pictured.

Do you know him?

He is described as white, aged between 18 and 20-years-old with a medium build and short dark hair. He is described as wearing black trousers and jacket and was carrying a black rucksack.

Any information, please call 101 and quote 44190246445.
