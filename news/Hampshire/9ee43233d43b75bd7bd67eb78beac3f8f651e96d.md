We have released CCTV images of two men that we would like to speak to in connection with fraudulent activity.

A bank card has been used in a number of locations, predominantly supermarkets, across Southampton between 10am on Saturday 1 August and 3am on Sunday 2 August.

Do you recognise the men pictured?

Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200295716.

Alternatively, call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
