Officers investigating the attempted abduction of a child in Basingstoke have charged a man.

Mohamed Jarboui, 51, of New Street in Basingstoke, has been charged with attempted child abduction for the incident on 4 February at Eastrop Park, Basingstoke.

He has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Basingstoke Magistrates Court this morning, February 8.
