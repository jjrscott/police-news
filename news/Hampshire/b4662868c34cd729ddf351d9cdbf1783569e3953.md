We are investigating a fatal collision which took place on the A33 Terminus Terrace in Southampton this morning (12 June).

Officers were called to the road at around 8am after a lorry and a bicycle collided.

The cyclist, a 43-year-old man from Southampton, died as a result of the collision.

His next of kin have been informed and are being supported by specialist officers.

We continue to ask anyone who witnessed the collision, or perhaps captured the incident on DashCam, to contact us on 101, quoting 44200211336.
