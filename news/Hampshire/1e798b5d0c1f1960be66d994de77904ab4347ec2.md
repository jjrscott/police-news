We are appealing for information after a cyclist was found collapsed in the road in Romsey.

Officers were called at 10.05am on Friday 8 May following the incident in Cupernham Lane.

The cyclist, a man in his 80s, was taken to Southampton General Hospital.

An investigation is ongoing as to how the incident occurred.

Anyone who saw what happened or has dashcam footage should call police on 101, quoting the reference number 44200162569.
