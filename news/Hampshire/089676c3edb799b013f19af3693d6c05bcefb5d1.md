Three men have been charged and appeared before court today in connection with cannabis production in Southampton.

Fatjon Hidri, 36, of The Polygon, Southampton, Ottian Lleshi, 21, of the Polygon and Jurgen Spahiu, 18, of no fixed abode, have been charged with being concerned in the production of a controlled drug of class B \- cannabis.

Fatjon Hidri also faces a further two charges of possession / control a false / improperly obtained / another person's identity document.

It comes after officers from Southampton's High Harm Team executed a warrant at an address in The Polygon, Southampton, on Wednesday 15 January.

All three men appeared at Southampton Magistrates Court today Friday, 17 January and have been remanded to appear again at the same court on Monday, 20 January.
