Do you recognise this man? We would like to speak to him in connection with a burglary in Aldershot.

We realise the photo isn't very clear but you may recognise some of the characteristics of the person featured.

The incident occurred overnight between 1pm on August 16 and 9am on August 17 in Canning Road.

The suspect gained entry to the address via an insecure window and stole a set of keys from within.

If you can help, please contact 101, quoting reference 44190291817. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
