Officers investigating a sexual assault in the Blackfield area over the weekend, have arrested a man in connection with the incident.

At sometime between 11.20am and 11.50am on Saturday 2 January, a woman was attacked while out walking in woodland off of Row Dow, between Roughdown Lane and Kings Copse Road.

A 30-year-old man from Blackfield has been arrested on suspicion of attempted rape.

He remains in custody at this time.
