We can confirm we were called to an incident in which a 25 year old man was found in Farnborough with a number of injuries yesterday evening.

The incident occurred at 8.32pm yesterday evening (March 25) in Coombe Way.

Police and ambulance attended and the victim was found to have two or three superficial wounds and a puncture wound to his side.

It was reported to police there were three or four people were also involved in the incident. They were travelling in a small black car and were wearing face coverings.

The victim refused hospital treatment and did not provide police with a statement.

Officers will be conducting patrols in the area today and carrying out house to house enquiries.

Neighbourhood Sergeant Mark Woodhead said: "If you have any concerns, don't hesitate to speak to officers. You can get in touch by emailing us at Rushmoor.police@hampshire.pnn.police.uk.

If anyone has any information about this crime, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200110706.
