Officers from Southampton's High Harm Team arrested three people in Morris Road, Southampton today on suspicion of drugs offences.

A 19-year-old man from Southampton was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class A drugs and possession with intent to supply Class B drugs.

A 27-year-old man from Plymouth was arrested on suspicion of possession of a controlled drug of Class B.

A 23-year-old man of no fixed abode, was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class B.

All three remain in police custody at this time.
