Officers investigating a burglary in Portsmouth have charged a man.

Jason Marshall, 44, of no fixed address, has been charged with burglary.

It comes following a burglary at an address in Stanley Street, Portsmouth, between 10am on December 29 and 1.30pm on December 30.

Thousands of pounds worth of items were stolen from the address, including Bose music equipment, jewellery and a laptop.

Jason Marshall was due to appear at Portsmouth Magistrates Court today (February 24).
