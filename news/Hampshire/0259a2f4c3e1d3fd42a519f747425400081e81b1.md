A 21 year old man has been arrested on suspicion of theft from a motor vehicle.

The incident occurred at around 7am on July 21 in Beecham Berry, Basingstoke. Police were called after a resident found her car had moved off her driveway and was abandoned in the road.

The man currently remains in custody.
