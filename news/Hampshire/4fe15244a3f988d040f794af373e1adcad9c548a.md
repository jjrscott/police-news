Updated News Post

Officers investigating the burglary at the London Road Brew House have charged a man.

Karl Alexander Fawcett, 37, of no fixed address, has been charged with burglary and has been remanded in custody to appear at Southampton Magistrates' Court this morning.

The 35-year-old man arrested on suspicion of handling stolen goods has been released with no further action.

************************************************************************************************

Two men have today, Wednesday 1 April, been arrested following a burglary at the London Road Brew House, Southampton.

The burglary took place just before midnight on 31 March, in which a quantity of alcohol was stolen.

A man, aged 35, has been arrested under the suspicion of handling stolen goods and another man, aged 37, has been arrested under the suspicion of burglary.

They remain in police custody at this time as our investigation continues.
