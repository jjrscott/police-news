A 52 year old man from Basingstoke has been charged with three residential burglaries and will appear before Winchester Crown Court on October 23.

Mark Thorne, of St Peter's Road, Basingstoke is charged with:

 * Burglary with intent to steal on August 22 at Hampshire Close, Aldershot
 * Burglary on August 9 at Lower Farnham Road, Aldershot
 * Burglary on August 3 at Brighton Road, Aldershot

He has been remanded in custody.
