We are appealing for information following a robbery in Havant on Tuesday 27 October.

At 4.45pm, a woman was pulled from her electric scooter and assaulted several times on Stockheath Lane at the junction with Barncroft Road.

The suspect then road off on the scooter.

The woman suffered minor injuries.

We would like to speak with a man in connection with the incident. He is described as follows:

 * Between 16 and 19-years-old
 * Around 5 feet 8 inches
 * White
 * Slim build
 * Wearing a black army jacket, black tracksuit trousers, a black hoody with the hood pulled up and a bag on his shoulder.

We would also like to speak to any witnesses that may have been in the area at the time or anyone that may have CCTV footage of the area.

Anyone with information should call police on 101, quoting the crime reference number 44200417654.

Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
