Officers in Gosport have arrested a man on suspicion of a robbery which took place in Alverstoke.

At around 9am on Wednesday 5 February, a woman in her 60s was approached by a man who stole her phone after trying to steal her handbag.

This took place in the churchyard of St Mary's Church.

We would be keen to hear from anyone who witnessed this incident. If you have any information, please call us on 101 quoting 44200044864.

A 31-year-old man from Gosport was arrested on suspicion of robbery. He has been bailed until 4 March while we make further enquiries.
