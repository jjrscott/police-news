Police officers investigating a firearms incident in Yateley have today (20 March) charged a man.

The charges come after a firearm was discharged at a black Mercedes on Reading Road at the junction with Darby Green Road on Tuesday 16 March.

Macari Fullard, 21, of Prospect Road, Ash Vale has been charged with one count of attempted murder.

He will appear at Basingstoke Magistrates Court this morning (20 March).
