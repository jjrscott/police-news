Can you help?

We are trying to identify the driver of a white Ford Mondeo after the driver is reported to have deliberately driven the vehicle at a boy on a bike.

The incident happened on Launceston Drive, Eastleigh, near to Denbigh Close between 12pm and 3pm on Thursday 30 January.

The boy was crossing the road on his bike when it is reported the driver a white Ford Mondeo deliberately nudged the bike tyre of the boys bike, causing him to fall off and suffer an injury to his wrist, hand and face.

At this time, we would like to trace the driver to ascertain exactly what happened.

He is described as Asian, aged between 40 and 50 years old, had a beard and was wearing a turban.

If you can help us identify the man described, or if this is you, please call 101 and quote 44200040271.
