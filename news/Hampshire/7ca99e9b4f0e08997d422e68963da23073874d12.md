We're appealing for witnesses following an incident in Portswood Road, Southampton.

Officers were called to reports of an air gun being fired from an address on Portswood Road between 8.14pm on Monday 2 November and 3am Tuesday, 3 November.

Officers attended and seized a BB gun.

A 37-year-old man from Southampton was arrested on suspicion of possession of an imitation firearm with intent to cause fear of violence. He has been released from custody but remains under investigation.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who may have information.

Did you see or hear anything around the time of the incident?

Anyone with information should call us on 101 quoting 44200425826.

Alternatively people can call Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
