Two 17 year old boys have been arrested on suspicion of possessing Class A drugs with intent to supply in Blunden Road Recreation Ground, Farnborough.

The pair, who are from Mitcham and Wembley in London, were arrested in a woodland area in the park on November 10.

Officers seized suspected Class A substances and a quantity of cash. They have both been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
