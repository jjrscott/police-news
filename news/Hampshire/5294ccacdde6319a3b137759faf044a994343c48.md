Officers investigating a collision after a school bus collided with a railway bridge in Winchester have charged a man.

Martin Robert Walker, 36, of Burke Drive in Southampton, has been charged with three counts of causing serious injury by dangerous driving.

The incident happened on Well House Lane, just after 8am on 10 September 2020.

Walker is due to appear before Basingstoke Magistrates Court on 6 April.
