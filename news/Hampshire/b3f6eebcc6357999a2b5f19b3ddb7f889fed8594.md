We have released a CCTV image of a man we would like to speak to after jewellery was stolen from a home in Sarisbury Green.

It took place in Barnes Lane, sometime between 12.35pm and 1.30pm on Thursday 3 October.

Glass on the front door was smashed to gain entry, and jewellery and watches were stolen.

We have released a CCTV image of a man we would like to speak to as part of our investigation.

Do you recognise him? Do you have any other information that could help our enquiries?

If so, please call 101, quoting the crime reference number 44190353369.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
