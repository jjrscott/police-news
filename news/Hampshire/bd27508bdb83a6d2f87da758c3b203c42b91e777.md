Aaron Morgan, aged 28, of Sydney Avenue, Hamble-Le-Rice has been charged with conspiracy to commit burglary today in connection with five burglaries at Tesco Express stores across Hampshire last year.

On each occasion, cash boxes within the stores were taken.

The following stores were broken into overnight between August 17 and October 4, 2019.

Tesco Express, Charlton, Andover on August 17
Tesco Express, Pennington, Lymington on September 27
Tesco Express, Farlington, Cosham on October 1
Tesco Express, Brockenhurst on October 1
Tesco Express, Eastleigh on October 4

Today, Morgan was charged conspiracy to burgle and was remanded into custody,

He is due to appear at Southampton Crown Court on June 15.
