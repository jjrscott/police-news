Officers are investigating the circumstances of an incident in Farnborough where a man in his 20s received a serious stab wound.

The victim attended hospital on June 24 at 10.30pm with a single stab wound that required surgery. He remains in a serious but stable condition. At this time, the location and circumstances of the assault are not known.

If anyone has any information about this crime or saw or heard anything suspicious that could be connected during the evening of June 24, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200230088.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Neighbourhood Inspector Phil Mayne said officers will be conducting investigations and carrying out extra patrols around Farnborough. Please don't hesitate to speak to an officer if you have any concerns.
