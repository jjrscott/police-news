A man has been charged following a robbery in Romsey.

It's alleged that at around 7.12pm on 16 March, money was snatched from the till at the Southern Co-Operative store on Botley Road.

Tomas David Lloyd, 29, of no fixed abode, was yesterday (17 March) charged with robbery and remanded in custody.

He is due to appear before Southampton Magistrates Court this morning.
