A 19 year old teenager has been arrested on suspicion of robbery following an incident in Basingstoke town centre.

The 19 year old, from Hook, was arrested in connection with an alleged robbery on a 16 year old boy in Market Place, Basingstoke, on February 19.

He has been released from custody on conditional bail.

Two other teenagers, a 17 year old from Basingstoke and a 19 year old from Basingstoke, were released with no further action.
