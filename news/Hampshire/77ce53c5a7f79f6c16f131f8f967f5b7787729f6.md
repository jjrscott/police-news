Two teenage boys have been arrested following the theft of two mopeds in Southampton.

At around 9.45pm yesterday, Tuesday 6 October, two grey mopeds were reported stolen from Domino's Pizza in Mercator Close, Southampton.

One of the mopeds was later found in the Tanners Brook area.

Two 16-year-old boys from Southampton have been arrested on suspicion of theft of a motor vehicle.

Both remain in police custody at this time while our enquiries continue.
