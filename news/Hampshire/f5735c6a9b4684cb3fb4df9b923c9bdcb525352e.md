**A 14 year old boy has been charged with attempted robbery and assault and will appear before Basingstoke Youth Court on February 7.**

** ** **The youth, who cannot be named for legal reasons, is charged with the attempted robbery of an 18 year old man in Alencon Link, Basingstoke, on January 21.**

** ** **He is further charged with assault by beating on the same date and at the same location.**
