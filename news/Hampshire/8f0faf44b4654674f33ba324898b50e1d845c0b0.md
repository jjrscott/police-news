We are appealing for witnesses following the death of a motorcyclist on the B3400 in Hurstbourne Priors yesterday evening (Tuesday, September 17).

The incident occurred at 5.20pm on the B3400 west of Hurstbourne.

The rider, a man in his 20s from Andover, was pronounced dead at the scene.

Were you travelling in the vicinity around this time? Did you see a blue Suzuki GSF motorbike? If you can help, and particularly if you have any dash cam footage, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting Operation Trainer.
