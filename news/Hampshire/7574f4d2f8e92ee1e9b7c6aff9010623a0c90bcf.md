Detectives have charged two people following an investigation into a report of an attempted robbery in Hedge End.

It took place at a house in Elliot Rise at around 2am on Wednesday 24 July.

Two men were arrested nearby.

Alan David Bloomer, 25, of Anson Drive, Southampton, has been charged with:

• attempted robbery
• having an imitation firearm with intent to commit an indictable offence

Ben McCash, 25, of Commercial Road, Southampton, has been charged with:

• attempted robbery
• having an imitation firearm with intent to commit an indictable offence
• driving a motor vehicle dangerously
• driving a motor vehicle otherwise in accordance with a licence
• using a motor vehicle on a road/public place without third party insurance

The men are due to appear at Southampton Magistrates' Court today (Thursday 25 July).
