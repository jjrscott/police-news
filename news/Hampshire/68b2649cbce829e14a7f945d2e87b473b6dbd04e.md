Officers continue to investigate the circumstances surrounding the rape of a 19-year-old woman in Southampton.

The woman reported she was raped between 8.40pm and 9.20pm on Sunday 11 August in the park area between Union Road and Summers Street.

A 45-year-old man was arrested in connection with the investigation and questioned by officers. He has since been released under investigation.

Officers continue to investigate the circumstances of the incident.

Anyone with information regarding this incident is asked to call 101 and quote 44190284325.
