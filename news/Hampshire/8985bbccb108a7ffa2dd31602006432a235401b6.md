We are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed a robbery on Guildhall Walk, Portsmouth, at around 1.30am on Tuesday 15 October.

Following an altercation involving a group of men and women close to the Astoria nightclub, a man in his 20s was threatened with a kitchen knife.

His phone and watch were taken and he sustained minor injuries.

If you have any information about the altercation or the robbery, please call 101 quoting reference 44190368730.

Alternatively you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
