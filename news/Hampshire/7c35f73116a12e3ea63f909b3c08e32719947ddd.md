Police officers investigating reports of a burglary in the Hightown area of the New Forest have arrested two men.

The incident occurred around 4pm on Thursday 3 December where entry was gained to a garage in the Hightown area. A wide-range of power tools were stolen from the garage along with a Blue petrol twin seater off-road buggy and an arcade cabinet.

A 24-year-old man from Ringwood has been arrested on suspicion of burglary.

While a 46-year-old man from Ringwood has also been arrested on suspicion of burglary.

Both men remain in custody at this time.
