A 31 year old Aldershot man has been arrested on suspicion of possessing Class A drugs with intent to supply.

Officers stopped a vehicle the man was driving in Alexandra Road, Aldershot, just after 12.30am on September 19. They seized the alleged drugs and a quantity of cash.

He has been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
