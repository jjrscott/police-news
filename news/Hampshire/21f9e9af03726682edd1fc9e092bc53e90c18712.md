We are appealing for witnesses and information following a burglary at The Burgate School, Fordingbridge.
A blue Ford 2600 tractor was stolen from the school grounds between 2.35am and 4.00am on Tuesday 22 October.
Two males are believed to have entered the grounds by cutting the chains on the gate with an angle grinder.
Anyone with information is asked to call 101 and quote 44190377928.
Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
