Do you recognise any of these items? They were found in a field adjacent to The Firs in Odiham.

The items include a number of crow bars, wrenches, numerous other tools including a jet washer and a Stihl garden strimmer.

If you have had a similar item stolen recently, or if you have any information about this incident, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44190386250.
