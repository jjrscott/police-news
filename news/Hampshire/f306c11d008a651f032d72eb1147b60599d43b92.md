Police officers investigating a disturbance at a block of flats in Eastleigh have arrested and charged a man.

The charges come after a number of residential properties and a lift were damaged at a block of flats in the early hours of Sunday 10 January on Southampton Road.

Aaron Callaway, aged 39, of Southampton Road, Eastleigh has been charged with two counts of causing criminal damage and one count of possessing an offensive weapon.

He remains in custody at this time.
