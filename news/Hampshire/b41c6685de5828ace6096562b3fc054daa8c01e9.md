Officers have released CCTV images of a man they want to speak to following an incident of arson in Eastleigh.

During the early hours of the morning on Saturday, 6 February two vehicles were set alight in Sandmartin Close.

PC Scott Pigott, said: "I am appealing for anyone who recognises the man in the CCTV, or anyone who has any other information that could help our investigation, to get in touch with us."

Three men, aged 26, 25 and 23 from Eastleigh, and a fourth man, aged 28, from Southampton, have been arrested on suspicion of conspiring to commit arson and have been released under investigation.

Anyone with information should call Hampshire Constabulary on 101 quoting 44210045401.
