Two 17 year old boys were arrested on suspicion of drug and weapon offences after being stopped by police in Aldershot.

The boys, who are both from Aldershot, were stopped in Elms Road, Aldershot on November 6. They were searched and a quantity of suspected cannabis and two lock knives were seized.

Both have been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
