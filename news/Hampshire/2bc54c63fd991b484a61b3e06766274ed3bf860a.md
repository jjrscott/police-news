We are appealing for witnesses or information after an alleged arson outside a property in Farnborough.

A car was extensively damaged following the incident on New Year's Day (January 1) at around 1am in Farnborough Road. The proximity of the car to a property also caused smoke damage to the building.

Did you see or hear anything suspicious? If you can help, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200000467.

Alternatively, please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

A 24 year old man from Farnborough has been arrested and released under investigation in connection with this incident.
