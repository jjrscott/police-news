Officers have arrested two men following a report of a break in at a Lidl in Southampton.

We were called at around 1.40am this morning, Wednesday, 3 March to a report of a burglary at Lidl on The Avenue, Southampton.

Officers attended and arrested two men aged 21 and 26, both of no fixed abode.

The 21-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of burglary and possession of a Class B drug.

The 26-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of burglary.

Both remain in police custody at this time.
