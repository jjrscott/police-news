Do you recognise this man?

Officers have released a CCTV image and are appealing for witnesses after a teenage girl was assaulted on Pound Tree Road in Southampton on Monday 2 November.

The victim, a 16-year-old girl from Southampton, was walking along Above Bar Street at around 8.35am and turned onto Pound Tree Road while looking at her phone and wearing headphones. As she passed JD Sports she was punched in the face by a man unknown to her.

She suffered minor injuries which required stitches to her nose.

Officers investigating have made a number of enquiries and are now turning to the public for help.

Do you know this man? Were you in the area at the time and saw what happened?

Anyone with information is asked to phone 101, quoting reference 44200424723.

Alternatively, you can contact independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
