We are now in a position to release CCTV images of three men we would like to speak to in connection with an assault in Andover.

A man in his 20s was assaulted by a man on Winchester Street at about 3.10am on Saturday 10 August.

The victim was then chased by four men to Westbrooke Close and assaulted again.

He received minor cuts and bruises.

The men are described as follows:

Suspect 1

 * White
 * 20-30 years old
 * Heavy build
 * Short brown hair and beard
 * Wearing black shirt and blue jeans

Suspect 2

 * White
 * 20-30 years old
 * Heavy build
 * Short brown hard and beard
 * Wearing dark blue v-neck top, light coloured trousers and white trainers

Suspect 3

 * Black
 * 20-30 years old
 * Slim build
 * Long dreadlocks pulled back
 * Wearing white long-sleeved top with large skull-like logo on the back, blue jeans and white trainers

Suspect 4 (no image)

 * White
 * 20-30 years old
 * Slim build
 * Wearing dark coloured shirt and blue jeans

There were a lot of people in the area at the time - if you have any information, please call us on 101 and quote 44190285401.

Alternatively you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
