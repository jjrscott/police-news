We are appealing for witnesses after a robbery in the Portswood branch of McDonald's just before midday today (12 October).

A G4S driver was assaulted by two suspects after collecting money from a safe within the McDonald's branch at approximately 11.50am. A cash box containing a quantity of money was subsequently stolen, with the suspects fleeing from the scene.

Did you see what happened? Were you in the area at the time? Did you capture the incident on a mobile phone or have dash cam footage?

Anyone with information in relation to this incident is asked to call 101, quoting 44200395964. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
