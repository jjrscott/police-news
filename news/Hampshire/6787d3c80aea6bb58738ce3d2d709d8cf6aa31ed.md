We are appealing for witnesses following a fail to stop collision on the A27 (east bound) at Farlington on Tuesday 5 November.

The collision, that happened between 5.20pm and 5.30pm, just after the A27/Eastern Road interchange involved five cars including a white Audi.

The Audi failed to remain at the scene.

No serious injuries were reported to police.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who witnessed the incident who may have information or dash cam footage that will help identify the driver of the Audi.

Anyone with information is asked to contact us on 101, quoting 44190397455, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
