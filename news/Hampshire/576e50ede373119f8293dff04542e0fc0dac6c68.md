UPDATED

Three teenagers have been arrested on suspicion of arson following a fire at an empty commercial premises in Aldershot High Street over the weekend.

Officers were called at 3.53pm on Saturday, February 22. Hampshire Fire & Rescue extinguished the fire and there were no reports of injuries.

Two 14 year old boys from Aldershot and a 15 year old boy from Ash, were arrested and have been released under investigation.

If anyone has any information about this incident, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200068506.

Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
