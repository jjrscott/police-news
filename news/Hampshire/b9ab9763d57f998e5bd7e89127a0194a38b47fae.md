We have released an image of a man we would like to speak to in connection with three incidents in Dibden Purlieu on Wednesday 6 May.

 * Entry was gained to a rear garden on Oakenbrow at 2.30am
 * Between 6am and 6.30am, an attempt was made to enter a vehicle and garden on Malvern Drive, but both were secure
 * At about 9.25pm, a car was entered on Oakenbrow

Nothing was taken in any of the incidents.

If you recognise the man or have any other information about these incidents, please call 101 quoting 44200160026.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Residents are advised to be vigilant and to report any suspicious activity to us.
