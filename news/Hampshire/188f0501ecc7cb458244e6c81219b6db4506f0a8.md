We are appealing for witnesses and dash cam footage following a collision involving a grey Audi A3 and red Yamaha motorcycle in Hayling Island last week.

Police were called just before 8.30pm on Thursday 30 July to the collision, which happened in Havant Road close to the Maypole pub.

The motorcyclist, a man in his 30s, suffered a broken hand.

Officers investigating the exact circumstances of the collision are keen to speak with anyone who witnessed it or who may have dash cam footage.

Anyone with information can call 101, quoting the reference 44200286405.

Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
