Two men have been arrested on suspicion of possessing crack cocaine and heroin with intent to supply after being stopped near Basingstoke Train Station.

An 18 year old man from Kensal Green, London, and a 27 year old man from Brent, London, were arrested on November 26 after officers stopped a vehicle at the train station.

A search was carried out and suspected heroin and crack cocaine were seized along with a quantity of cash.

Both have been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
