A Basingstoke shop owner will be appearing before Basingstoke Magistrates Court on January 7 next year to face allegations relating to the importation of cannabis and possessing cannabis with intent to supply.

Fraser Bracey, 27, of Goldney Road, Camberley, will face allegations of:

Possessing Class B Cannabis with intent to supply at the Vedas Cafe and Shop in Church Street, Basingstoke.

Being knowingly concerned in the fraudulent evasion of a prohibition on the importation of a bag of Class B Cannabis.
