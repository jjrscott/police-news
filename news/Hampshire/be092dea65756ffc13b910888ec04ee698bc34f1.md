Officers responding to reports of a theft from a local shopping centre have issued a community resolution order to a local teenager.

Police were called to an incident at 3.55pm on Tuesday 20 October on Wells Road, whereby a teenage girl was seen by CCTV shoplifting from a local store.

Upon searching the teenager, officers recovered stolen items.

A 16-year-old girl from Southampton was issued with a community resolution order.
