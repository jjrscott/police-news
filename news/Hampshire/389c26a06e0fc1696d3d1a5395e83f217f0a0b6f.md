Do you recognise these men? We want to talk to them in connection with a burglary at Currys in Farnborough.

The burglary occurred between 2.52am and 5.11am on February 29 at Currys in Blackwater Retail Park in Farnborough.

The suspects have gained entry by smashing a hole in the wall at the rear of the premises and then broke into a mobile phone storage unit. A number of mobile phones were taken.

Do you think you recognise either one of them? If you have any information that can help our enquiries, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200077038.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
