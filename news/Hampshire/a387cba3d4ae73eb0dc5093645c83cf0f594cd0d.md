We're appealing for information after two cars were stolen following a burglary in Lee-on-the-Solent.

Overnight on Monday 9 March, the vehicles were stolen from a driveway on Studland Road.

The cars are described as a white Jaguar XF Luxury V6 with a registration number ending in JMO, and a bronze-coloured Seat ALTEA with a registration number ending YTY.

If you have seen these vehicles, or saw any suspicious activity in the area at the time, then please contact us.

Four people have been arrested on suspicion of burglary and remain in custody at this time.

They are a 19-year-old man, a 16-year-old boy, a 23-year-old man and a 20-year old woman, all from Southampton.

Anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101, quoting 44200090226.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
