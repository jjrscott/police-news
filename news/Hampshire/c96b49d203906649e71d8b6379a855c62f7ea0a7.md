We are appealing for witnesses and information after a 54 year old man was assaulted in Southampton, resulting in him sustaining a broken hip.

The incident occurred between 4pm and 4.30pm on April 10 in Bursledon Road, near to the BP garage.

The victim was taken to hospital following the altercation which involved a woman and two other men. As well as a broken hip, he also sustained bruising to his head and concussion.

Did you witness the incident? If you have any information, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200127968.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

A 53 year old woman from Southampton has been arrested in connection with this incident.
