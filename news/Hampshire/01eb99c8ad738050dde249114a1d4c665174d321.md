We are appealing for witnesses after a woman was assaulted on Ham Lane, Gosport.

The incident happened at 7.10am this morning (Monday 14 December).

The victim, an 18-year-old woman from Fareham, was walking on Ham Lane when she was followed and assaulted by a man.

She sustained injuries during the assault, they are not considered serious or life-threatening.

We would like to speak to a man in connection with the incident. He is described as follows:

 * White
 * Aged between 20 and 25-years-old
 * Slim build
 * Between 5feet 2inches and 5feet 4inches tall
 * Curly, dark brown, scruffy hair, around ear length
 * Pale complexion with cuts to his mouth and chin
 * Red eyes
 * Very hairy arms and hands
 * Wearing dark grey baggy tracksuit bottoms, a blue, ripped t-shirt and trainers with white bottoms

Were you in the area at the time? Perhaps you saw a man matching that description?

Anyone with information is asked to phone 101 with reference 44200481032.

 Alternatively, you can phone Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111.
