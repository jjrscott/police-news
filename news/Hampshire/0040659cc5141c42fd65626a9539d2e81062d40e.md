Detectives are appealing for witnesses after a man has been left in a critical condition following an altercation in Alton.

The incident happened just after midnight (26 July), on the High Street, Alton, close to the Vodafone store.

Following an altercation, a 24-year-old man was taken to Southampton General Hospital where he is being treated for serious head injuries.

Officers will be at the scene today continuing with their enquiries.

Anyone who has any information about this incident or anyone who witnessed the incident is asked to call us on 101, quoting Operation Infringe or 44200279176.

Alternatively people can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
