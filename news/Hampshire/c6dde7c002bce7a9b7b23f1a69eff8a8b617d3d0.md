Krystian Kwarta, aged 20, of Keyhaven Road, Milford-on-Sea has pleaded guilty to two counts of rape and one count of assault occasioning actual bodily harm.

Kwarta entered the plea at Southampton Crown Court today (March 20) following an investigation by our Operation Amberstone team based at Southampton Central Police Station.

He admitted he attacked a 21-year-old woman within the perimeter of Westquay shopping centre just before 8am on the morning of Saturday 15 February.

He will be sentenced on April 30.
