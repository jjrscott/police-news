Two people arrested after police searched a property in Mauritius Close in Basingstoke yesterday, (Tuesday), have been released under investigation.

The 25 year old woman from Basingstoke and a 38 year old man from Basingstoke were arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs.
