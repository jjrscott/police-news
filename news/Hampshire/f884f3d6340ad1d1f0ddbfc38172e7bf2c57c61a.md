Police officers investigating a break-in at a shop on Lymington High Street have arrested two men.

The incident occurred on Sunday 27 September at 11.55pm on the High Street.

Officers had been in attendance at a business premises following a break-in earlier that evening, when they heard another business premises in the High Street being broken into.

Two men were subsequently arrested by officers within the premises.

A 37-year-old man from Ringwood was arrested on suspicion burglary, while a 45-year-old man was also arrested on suspicion of burglary.

The suspects remain in custody at this time pending further investigation.

As part of this investigation, we are reviewing a number of other business burglaries that have happened in the area. Enquire remain ongoing in connection with these incidents.
