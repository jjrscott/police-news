Officers in Southampton have arrested two men following a robbery that took place in Woolston, Southampton.

At around 8.30pm on Tuesday, 12 January, an 18-year-old man was approached by two men who threatened him before stealing his phone, bank cards and a gold chain.

This took place close to Newtown Road in Woolston.

Two 19-year-old men from London have been arrested on suspicion of robbery.

They remain in custody at this time.
