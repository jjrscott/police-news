An 18 year old Andover man will appear before Basingstoke Magistrates Court on January 14, charged with robbery.

Ardurel Hamzaj of Junction Road, Andover, is charged with stealing cash and cigarettes from a man in his 20s in Winterthur Way, Basingstoke on September 28.

He is also charged with using threatening words or behaviour towards the same victim.
