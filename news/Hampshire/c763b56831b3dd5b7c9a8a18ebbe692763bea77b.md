A man has been charged following non-dwelling burglaries in Southampton.

Alexander Dibden, 37, of Wallace Road, Southampton appeared before Southampton Magistrates Court yesterday (4 June).

The charges relate to offences in Southampton:

 * Two burglaries on 4 June at One Stop on Peartree Avenue where cash was stolen from the till.
 * A burglary on 5 April at One Stop on Peartree Avenue where cash was stolen from the till.
 * A burglary on 9 April at One Stop on North East Road where the till and cash were stolen.
 * A burglary on 17 May at Oxford Brasserie on Oxford Street where the till and cash were stolen.

Dibden has been remanded into custody.

He is next due to appear at Southampton Magistrates Court on 11 June.
