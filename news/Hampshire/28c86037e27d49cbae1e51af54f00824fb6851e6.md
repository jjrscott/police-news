A man and woman have been arrested on suspicion of Class A drug offences in Basingstoke.

The 29 year old woman and 26 year old man, both from London, were arrested after a vehicle was searched in Oakridge Road at around 4pm on December 2.

A quantity of suspected heroin and crack cocaine were seized.

Both have been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
