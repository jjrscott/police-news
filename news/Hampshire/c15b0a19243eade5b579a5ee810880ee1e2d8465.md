A woman has been arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to rob following a robbery at New Century Bingo, Southampton.

At around 8am on Sunday 24 November, two men wearing motorcycle crash helmets broke into the New Century Bingo hall using a crowbar. They locked the cleaner in a room and waited for other staff to arrive before stealing approximately Â£15,000 from the safe.

The woman was questioned by officers and has been released under investigation. Enquiries are ongoing.

Officers investigating the robbery continue to appeal for information.

Were you in the area around this time on Sunday 24 November? Did you see two people wearing high visibility clothing riding a moped or motorcycle? If you witnessed anything unusual or have any CCTV or dash cam footage which may assist our investigation, please call 101 and quote 44190422425.
