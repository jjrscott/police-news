A 32 year old woman and a 31 year old man have been charged in connection with a series of vehicle crimes in Farnborough.

Sarah Fuller, 32, of Lynchford Road, Farnborough and Jason Kennedy, 31, also of Lynchford Road, have been bailed conditionally to appear before Basingstoke Magistrates Court on June 30.

Fuller faces five charges of theft from motor vehicles and three charges of interfering with vehicles

Kennedy faces four charges of theft from motor vehicles and three of interfering with vehicles.

All offences took place in Farnborough in March and April 2020.

Kennedy also faces three charges of shoplifting in Queens Road, Farnborough and one offence of being in possession of an offensive weapon in Lynchford Road.
