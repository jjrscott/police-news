A 35 year old Basingstoke woman has been arrested in connection with the supply of Class A drugs.

The woman was arrested at 1.30pm on July 5 in Chineham after being stopped by officers. She was arrested on suspicion of possessing Class A drugs with intent to supply.

A 19 year old man from London was also arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs and possessing Class B cannabis.

An 18 year old man from London was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs.

All have been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
