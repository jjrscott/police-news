Two men have been charged with interfering with a motor vehicle.

The pair were arrested after police were called to Worting Road, Basingstoke, in the early hours of July 22.

Andrew Pepper, 45 and Benjamin John, 47, both of Attwood Close, Basingstoke, were bailed pending an appearance before Basingstoke Magistrates Court on September 17.
