A man in his 20s has been arrested on suspicion of possessing Class B drugs with intent to supply after a warrant was executed at a Basingstoke address.

The man was also arrested on suspicion of possessing criminal property. He has been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.

The warrant was conducted on July 16 at Salisbury Gardens. A quantity of Class B drugs and a quantity of cash were recovered from the property.
