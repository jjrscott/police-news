Officers investigating an assault at the Ham Lane/Elson Road junction in Gosport have arrested a man.

We'd received a report that an 18-year-old woman from Fareham was assaulted at around 7.10am on Monday 14 December.

She sustained injuries during the assault, but they are not considered serious or life-threatening.

This morning (Tuesday 15 December) officers arrested a 21-year-old man, of no fixed abode, on suspicion of assault occasioning actual bodily harm.

He remains in custody at this time.

We continue to ask anyone with information about this incident to call 101 and quote incident number 44200481032.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111.
