A man has been arrested on suspicion of a drugs supply offence in Canada Road, Wellow.

On the morning of Sunday 25 October, officers from the Test Valley Rural Neighbourhood Policing Team arrested a 28-year-old man from Salisbury, Wiltshire, on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of a Class B drug.

The man has been released from custody but remains under investigation. Enquiries are ongoing.

The arrest followed reports received from residents about drug-related activity taking place near Canada Common.

Anyone with information on drug-related activity can report it online via our website: https://www.hampshire.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/

Reports can also be made by calling 101. Alternatively, people can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
