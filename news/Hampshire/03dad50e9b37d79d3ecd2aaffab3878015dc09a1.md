Two men have been charged following a robbery at the Co-op service station in Southampton.

Dwain Ward, 36, of no fixed abode and John Eden, 31 of Millbrook Road West, have both been charged with robbery.

It comes following a robbery at the Co-op service station on Millbrook Road West shortly after 10am on Saturday 25 April, where coffee and wine was stolen.

Both men are due to appear at Southampton Magistrates Court today (April 27).
