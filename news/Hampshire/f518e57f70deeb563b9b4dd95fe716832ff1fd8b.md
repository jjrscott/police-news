We can confirm that the body found just off The Needles last Saturday (20 June) is that of Matthew Doyle.

His family have been informed and are being supported.

The death is not being treated as suspicious and a file is being prepared for the Coroner.

We would like to thank everyone who has offered to help with searches for missing Matthew, and who has shared the appeals and supported our investigation.
