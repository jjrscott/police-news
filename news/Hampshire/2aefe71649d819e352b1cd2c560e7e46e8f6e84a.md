## Man dies after shooting near Andover

A man who was shot during an incident in Upper Enham has died.

James Nash, 42, from Upper Enham, sustained serious head injuries following the shooting outside his home. Officers were called to the address at 3.01pm on Wednesday 5 August.

He died in hospital in the early hours of today (Saturday 8 August). His family are being supported by specialist officers.

The death is now being treated as a murder investigation. Enquiries to establish the circumstances of what took place are ongoing.

Anyone with information should call police on 101, quoting Op Plaque.
