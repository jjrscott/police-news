We are appealing for witnesses following a fatal road traffic collision in Havant.

The single vehicle collision took place just after 9pm on Friday 10 July in Portsdown Hill Road.

The vehicle, a grey Morgan Plus Four, was in collision with a lamppost on a crossing island and overturned.

The driver, a 70-year-old man, was pronounced dead at the scene. His next of kin have been informed.

We are investigating the circumstances of this incident and would like to hear from anyone who saw the collision or the vehicle prior to the collision.

Anyone with information should call us on 101, quoting the reference number 44200255290.
