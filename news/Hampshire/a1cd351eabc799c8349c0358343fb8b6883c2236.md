Officers have charged a man in connection with a robbery that took place in Sholing.

It is alleged that a man carrying a knife entered the Best One shop on Station Road, between 12.15pm and 12.30pm on Thursday 19 December, before stealing cash and tobacco.

We would like to hear from any witnesses to this incident, in particular the driver of a 4x4 vehicle who is believed to have followed a man who had left the shop.

If this is you, or you have any information about this incident, please call us on 101, quoting 44190455244.

Gary Samuel Moule, 33, of no fixed abode, was charged with robbery. He was remanded in custody to appear at Southampton Magistrates' Court tomorrow (21 December).
