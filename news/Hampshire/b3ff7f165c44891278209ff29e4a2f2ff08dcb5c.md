We're appealing for information after a member of a bar's door staff was assaulted in Basingstoke.

The incident took place outside The Tea Bar, in London Street, Basingstoke, sometime between 9pm and 9.15pm on Saturday 20 July.

A man approached the victim and punched him in the face.

The victim sustained bruising to his right eye.

The man is described as:

• white
• aged between 40 and 50 years of age
• around 5ft 8ins tall
• black/grey hair
• wearing a white Ralph Lauren shirt, black trousers and a burgundy suit jacket.

Were you in London Street on that night? Did you witness what happened? Do you have any other information that could help our investigation?

Anyone with information should call the police on 101, quoting the crime reference number 44190256184.

Alternatively, people can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
