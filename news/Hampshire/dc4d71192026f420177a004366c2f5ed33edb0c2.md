We are appealing for witnesses after a vehicle was set alight on Buckingham Green, Portsmouth.

The fire was set to the white Mercedes A180 SE between 11pm and 11.30pm on Monday 15 February.

Officers investigating are keen to speak with anyone who may have been in the area at the time or who has any information.

Were you in the area at the time? Perhaps you have dash-cam footage of the area or the car?

Anyone with information is asked to phone 101 with the reference 44210057954.

Alternatively, you can phone Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111.
