Eighteen people have been given conditional cautions in relation to a protest at an abattoir in Peabody Road, Farnborough on Wednesday, October 16.

Three people had been charged with obstruction or disruption of a person engaged in lawful activity but they have subsequently accepted conditional cautions and the charge has been withdrawn against all of them.

The conditions of the caution are that they must not trespass in the next three months.
