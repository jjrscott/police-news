Police officers investigating reports of a disturbance outside an address in Holbury have arrested three men.

The incident occurred on Sunday 7 March at around 4.35pm in Beechwood Road, Holbury.

It was reported that three men pulled up in a vehicle on Beechwood Road, approached a man in his 20s and assaulted him, causing minor injuries.

The three men then fled from the scene in a black Audi, which was located nearby by officers a short while later. Three men matching the description given by the victim were located in the vehicle and were subsequently detained.

A 31-year-old man and a 49-year-old man, both from Fawley, were arrested on suspicion of assault causing actual bodily harm.

While a 26-year-old man from Fawley was arrested on suspicion of assault causing actual bodily harm and possession of a Class B drug.

They have all been released under investigation, pending further police enquiries.
