Three men and a woman have been charged as part of a county lines intelligence-led operation to tackle the supply of drug dealing of Class A drugs from London into Southampton.

Bailey Dooling, 18, Harmony Place, Southwark, London has been charged with concerned in the supply of Class A drugs, heroin and crack cocaine.

Jahmell Francis, 26, Barkworth Road, Southwark, London has been charged with the supply of Class A drugs, heroin and crack cocaine.

Aimee-Rose Malone, 21, Bramcote Grove, Southwick, London has been charged with supply of Class A drugs, heroin and crack cocaine.

Raheem Simpson, 26, Cumbrian Avenue, Bexley, London has been charged with supply of Class A drugs, heroin and crack cocaine.

A series of warrants were executed on Tuesday, 2 June with officers from Hampshire, The Met and Kent Police. Five were arrested at an address in SE16. Four were charged the following day, all have been remanded in custody and appeared at Southampton Magistrates Court today (4th June).

A 44 year-old man from Southampton has been released under investigation.
