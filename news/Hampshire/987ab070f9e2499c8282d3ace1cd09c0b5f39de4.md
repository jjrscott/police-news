Officers, attending a disturbance at an address in Eastleigh, have charged a man in possession of a knife, causing criminal damage and assault.

Laurence Benjamin Cuthbert, 32, of Alexander Square, Eastleigh, was arrested.

He was remanded in custody and appeared at Southampton Magistrates Court on Monday 17 August.

Laurence pleaded not guilty and was remanded in custody until 25 September where he will appear at Crown Court.
