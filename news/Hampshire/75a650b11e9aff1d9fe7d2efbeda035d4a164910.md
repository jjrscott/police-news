Officers investigating a robbery at Golden Touch amusement arcade in Southampton are appealing for witnesses.

The incident happened at between 8.55am and 9.12am on Wednesday, 5 August at Golden Touch amusement arcade in the High Street, Southampton.

A member of staff was opening up the venue when a man entered and threatened her with what has been described as a black gun, before demanding money from the safe.

The man is described as being between 20-50-years-old, around 5'10" - 5'11" tall and of slim build.

He was wearing blue jeans, a blue T-shirt under a grey hoody with the hood worn up over a cap, trainers and a face mask.

Anyone who witnessed anything or saw anyone acting suspiciously in the area at the time or has any information is asked to call us on 101, quoting 44200294513.

Alternatively people can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
