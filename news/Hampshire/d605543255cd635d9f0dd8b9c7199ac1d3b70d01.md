Officers investigating an incident that took place in a restaurant in Southampton are looking to trace three witnesses.

It happened in Tang's Chinese restaurant in Above Bar Street between 6pm and 6.39pm on Monday, 20 January.

It was reported a man entered the restaurant and harassed another two women and a man, who then left.

We're appealing for these three people to get in touch as we believe they may have information that could assist our investigation.

If this is you, please call us on 101, quoting 44200024173.

A man has been charged with two sexual assaults, assault by beating, assault by beating of an emergency worker and affray in relation to this incident.
