A man has been charged with drugs offences in Southampton.

Iaos Zammit, aged 18 of Dean Road, Southampton, has been charged with two counts of possession with intent to supply a Class A drug, namely heroin and cocaine and possession of a Class B drug, cannabis.

He appeared at Portsmouth Magistrates' Court on Saturday morning (October 7) and is next due to appear at Portsmouth Crown Court on 7 December.

This forms part of Operation Fortress, Hampshire Constabulary's campaign to reduce the harm of drug-related violence.

If you suspect drug related activity to be happening in your neighbourhood please contact us. Every call you make helps us build up an intelligence picture which enables us to take action. Call 101, or call Crimestoppers and leave information.
