Police officers investigating reports of dangerous driving in Eastleigh, have arrested four men in connection.

The incident occurred yesterday evening (25 Feb) at around 9.25pm on Bishopstoke Road, whereby officers spotted the vehicle driving dangerously. A short pursuit followed and continued onto Stoneham Lane whereby officers were able to use specialised tactics to stop the vehicle.

The four occupants and the vehicle were searched, with a quantity of drugs located and seized as a result.

A 19-year-old man from Eastleigh was arrested on suspicion of taking a motor vehicle without the owners consent, possession of a Class A drug and possession of a Class B drug.

A 15-year-old boy from Eastleigh and a 19-year-old man from Southampton were both arrested on suspicion of possession of a Class A drug and possession of a Class B drug.

A 17-year-old boy from Eastleigh was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply of a Class B drug.

They all remain in custody at this time, while police enquiries continue.
