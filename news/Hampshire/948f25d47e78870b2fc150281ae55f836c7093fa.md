Do you recognise this person? We want to speak to him in connection with a burglary at a business premises in Greenwich Way, Andover.

The incident is believed to have occurred overnight between 7pm and 8pm on Sunday, July 5.

Two suspects are believed to have got into the yard and forced access to a storage container. A flatbed van was also broken into. A number of power tools were believed to have been stolen.

If you have any information about this crime or you think you recognise the person in the images, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200249952.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
