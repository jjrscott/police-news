Do you recognise this man? We want to speak to him in connection with a burglary at the Groundlings Theatre in Portsmouth.

The incident occurred between 5am and 8.50am on Sunday, September 29.

Suspect(s) entered the theatre in Kent Street by forcing a door and stole cash, electrical items and computer equipment.

A 38 year old man has been arrested in connection with this offence and released under investigation.

If you recognise the man pictured, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44190347621.
