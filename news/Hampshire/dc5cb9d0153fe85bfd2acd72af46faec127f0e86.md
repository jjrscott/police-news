Updated News Post

Officers investigating the sexual assault of a woman on Stoke Road, Gosport, have made an arrest.

The incident took place some time between 9.50pm and 10.20pm on Tuesday 2 February this year.

A 30-year-old man from Gosport has been arrested on suspicion of sexual assault. He has been bailed with conditions until 13 March while our enquiries continue.

We continue to encourage anyone who has information about this incident to get in touch with us on 101, quoting 44210046979.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
