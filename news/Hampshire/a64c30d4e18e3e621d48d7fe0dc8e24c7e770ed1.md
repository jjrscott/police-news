Officers investigating a house burglary in Southsea on August 2, this year, have charged a man.

We were called to a report of a burglary at an address in Auckland Road West, Southsea, which occurred around 6.30am.

Michael James Boswell, 36, of Heyward Road, Southsea, has been charged with burglary and seven counts of fraud by false representation.

He was remanded in custody to appear at Portsmouth Magistrates' Court today and will next appear before Portsmouth Crown Court on September 7.
