Four people have been arrested on suspicion of drugs offences after police carry out a search at an address in Southampton.

Officers from Southampton's High Harm Team carried out the search at an address in Kent Street yesterday, 30 June.

A 32-year-old woman and a 23-year-old woman, both from Southampton, were arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs.

The 23-year-old woman was also arrested on suspicion of being in possession of Class A drugs.

A 36-year-old woman from Portsmouth and a 54-year-old man from Southampton were both arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class A drugs.

All four were released from custody but remain under investigation while our enquiries continue.

If you are concerned about drugs in your area, please contact us. Every call you make to us helps us build up an intelligence picture which enables us to take action.

Anyone with information should call police on 101. Alternatively, people can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
