Have you been offered any of the items pictured?

Officers from Hampshire Constabulary found the images of unusual jewellery and scrambler motorbikes on a phone that was seized from a suspect.

They believe the items pictured may have been stolen and would like to speak to anyone who may have been approached and offered the items.

Or they would also like to speak to anyone who thinks the items belong to them but have since been stolen.

If you recognise any of the items, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting 44190351817.
