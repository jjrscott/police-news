Police officers investigating a series of indecent exposures in Eastleigh in 2019 have charged a man.

The charges come following a series of indecent exposure incidents which occurred in the Fair Oak and Chandler's Ford area between 5 September and 10 October 2019.

Virendra Asharafi Val, aged 42, of High Street, Eastleigh has been charged with five counts of indecent exposure.

He pleaded guilty to all five counts and will appear before Southampton Magistrates Court for sentencing on Monday 29 March.
