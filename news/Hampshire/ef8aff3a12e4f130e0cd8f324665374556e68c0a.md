We are appealing for witnesses and information after a 16 year old boy was robbed in Station Road, Petersfield.

The incident occurred at around 10.15pm on Saturday, November 16. The victim had been walking with three friends when they were approached by a large group of young males.

The victim was threatened and punched before a quantity of cash was taken. He has sustained bruising and a suspected broken nose.

Two of the suspects are described as being white, of skinny build, around 15 - 17 years old. They were both wearing black Nike hoodies and tracksuit bottoms and one had a black body warmer.

Were you in the vicinity of Station Road on Saturday night? Did you see a large group of young males?

If you have any information about this crime, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44190413565.

Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
