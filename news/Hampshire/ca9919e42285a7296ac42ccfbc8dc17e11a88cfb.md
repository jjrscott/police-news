We are appealing for witnesses or information - in particular dash cam or cctv footage - following an assault in Londesborough Road, Southsea on Saturday, December 19.

The incident occurred at around 9.40pm. The 21 year old victim opens his front door to a male suspect. A verbal altercation ensues and the suspect uses some kind of weapon to damage the front door before making off with another male. The victim sustains a cut to his hand which is not described as a serious injury.

Were you in the vicinity around this time? If you have dash cam footage, Ring Doorbell or CCTV that may help our investigation, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200489035, or you can report online via www.hampshire.police.uk.

You can also report anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or www.crimestoppers-uk.org.

A 42 year old man from Southsea has been arrested in connection with this incident.
