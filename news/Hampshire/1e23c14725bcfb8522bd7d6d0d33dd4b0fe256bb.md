The family of a woman who died following a collision on the A32 in Droxford have paid tribute to her.

Police were called just around 8pm on Thursday 4 February to the incident, involving a grey BMW and a silver Volkswagen Golf.

The passenger of the BMW, Melissa Ann Orsborn, 20, of Salmons Road, Odiham, was taken to hospital, but she was sadly pronounced dead later that evening.

Today, her family have paid tribute to her.

They described Melissa as: "A wonderful, beautiful, loving and caring young woman. She will sadly be missed by family and friends."

Enquiries into the exact circumstances of the collision are ongoing.
