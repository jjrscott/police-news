Officers investigating a commercial burglary in Southampton have charged a man.

Leigh Armsden, 45, of no fixed address, has been charged with burglary other than dwelling.

It comes following a burglary at Trespass in Above Bar Street, Southampton, shortly before 2am on May 2.

Various items of clothing were stolen.

Leigh Armsden was due to appear at Southampton Magistrates Court today (May 4).
