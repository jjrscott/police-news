We are investigating a report of a middle aged man indecently exposing himself in Fordingbridge.

The incident occurred at 3.20pm on Saturday, January 25, near the sewage works on Frog Lane.

A 32 year old woman saw him masturbating in a field near where she was walking her dogs.

He is described as being a six foot tall white male, aged between 40 and 50, with grey, shoulder length hair. He was wearing tracksuit bottoms, baseball cap and rucksack.

If you have any information about this incident or you recognise the description of the suspect, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200031028.
