Can you help us identify this man?

Officers would like to speak to him in connection with a burglary that happened at the Standing Order Pub on the High Street, Southampton, earlier this month.

At around 1.50am on Wednesday, 16 September, a man has gained entry to the pub and taken cash from three charity tins along with a number of bottles of alcohol before leaving.

Do you recognise the man in this CCTV image?

If you have any information that can help our enquiries, please get in touch by calling 101, quoting reference 44200358270.

Alternatively you can phone Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
