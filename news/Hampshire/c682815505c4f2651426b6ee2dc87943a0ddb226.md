Detectives investigating the death of 18-year-old Taylor John Williams in Basingstoke have tonight charged a man with murder.

Olamide Soyege, 28, of Mayswood Gardens, Dagenham, Essex, has been charged with murder.

He has also been charged with robbery, conspiracy to rob and possession of a bladed article.

As part of the same investigation, detectives have charged Paige Taylor, 23, of St Michael's Road, Basingstoke, with robbery and conspiracy to rob.

Both will appear before Basingstoke Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Thursday 5 September).

Taylor, of Winlaton Road, Bromley, London, was found injured an address in Shooters Way, Basingstoke, on Saturday 31 August. He was taken to hospital where he subsequently died.
