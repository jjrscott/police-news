Police detectives investigating a fire outside a property in Eastleigh have arrested two men in connection with the incident.

At around 4.10pm on Sunday 21 February, a pair of trainers were set alight outside a property on Sand Martin Close. This resulted in fire damage being caused to the front door of the property.

A 59-year-old man and a 17-year-old boy, both from Eastleigh, have been arrested on suspicion of arson with intent to endanger life.

They both remain in custody at this time.
