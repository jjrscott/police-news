We're appealing for anyone with information regarding a collision which took place in Kingsclere earlier today (Thursday 31 December) to make contact with police.

At around 2pm this afternoon, a silver Nissan Qashqai collided with a pedestrian on Swan Street.

The pedestrian, a man in his 40s, became trapped between the Nissan and another vehicle as a result and was taken to Southampton General Hospital for treatment to serious and potentially life-threatening injuries.

Closures were put in place while emergency services worked at the scene, but were lifted shortly before 6pm.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or has any information, is asked to contact police on 101, quoting 44200501176.
