Have you seen this bike?

It was stolen in a burglary in Kensington Road, Copnor between 10.30pm on Thursday 8 August and 6.30am on Friday 9 August.

Two high value bicycles were taken, one of which has been recovered.

Access appears to have been gained through the rear of the house.

Computer equipment, headphones, sunglasses and car keys were also stolen. The vehicles outside the house were searched.

If you have seen the bike pictured - a Giant Propel Advanced SL2 - or have been offered it for sale, please call 101 quoting 44190281188.

You may have other information about this incident, including CCTV or dash cam footage.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

A 17-year-old boy from Rochdale in Lancashire was arrested on suspicion of burglary and theft. He has been released while investigations continue.
