**Do you recognise this man? We would like to speak with him in connection with an incident in which an 18 year old woman was grabbed by a man on a footpath in Basingstoke.**

** ** **The incident occurred at around 10.40pm on July 18.**

** ** **The woman reported to police in September that the man had followed her onto the footpath at the rear of Asda in Brighton Hill. He grabbed her arm but she struggled free and ran away.**

** ** **The man is described as being aged between 45 - 50, around six foot tall, of slim build and wearing a grey hooded jacket and glasses.**

** ** **If you think you recognise the person in the E-fit or have any information regarding this incident, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44190326039.**

** ** **Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.**
