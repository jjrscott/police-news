We are appealing for information after units at a Chilbolton trading estate were broken into.

The burglaries took place at Stonefield Park, in Martins Lane sometime between 2am and 3am on Saturday 22 February.

Two businesses were broken into, and attempt was made on a third. Two cars, a Mazda MX5 and a Volkswagen Passat, were stolen. The Passat was later recovered in Wiltshire.

Tools and diagnostic equipment were also stolen.

Enquiries are ongoing.

We are appealing for anyone with information on what happened to come forward.

Were you in this area at the time? Do you remember seeing anything suspicious?

If so, please call us on 101, quoting the crime reference number 44200067642.

Alternatively, people can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
