Two teenage boys have been arrested after a 15 year old boy was threatened with a knife before being robbed in Brighton Hill, Basingstoke.

A 14 year old and a 15 year old, both from Basingstoke, were arrested on suspicion of the robbery which occurred between 4.45pm and 5.10pm on Saturday, January 2 as the victim walked along the footpath running between Brighton Hill School and Quilter Road.

Both teenagers currently remain in custody.
