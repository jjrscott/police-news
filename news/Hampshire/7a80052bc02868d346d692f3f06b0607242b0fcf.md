Officers investigating a public order incident at Southampton General Hospital have charged a man.

David Holmes, aged 38, of St Denys Road, Southampton, has been charged with three assaults, one assault on an emergency worker, one racially aggravated public order offence and one public order offence.

It comes following an incident outside Southampton General Hospital at around 8.30pm on Sunday, 12 July.

Holmes appeared at Southampton Magistrates Court yesterday, (Tuesday 14 July).
