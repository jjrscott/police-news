A burglar has pleaded guilty to breaking into two homes in Portsmouth.

Charlie McGowan, 20, of Finchdean Gardens, was charged with two counts of burglary.

He appeared at Portsmouth Magistrates Court on October 8, where he pleaded guilty to the charges.

McGowan admitted to breaking into an address in Jessie Road and Francis Avenue in the early hours of September 26.

He is due to be sentenced at Portsmouth Crown Court on November 8.
