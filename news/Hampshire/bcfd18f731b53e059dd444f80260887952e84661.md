Two 17 year old boys have been arrested on suspicion of possessing Class B drugs with intent to supply.

The two boys were arrested after being approached by officers at a grassed area in Churchill Way, Basingstoke, on September 4. A quantity of suspected cannabis and cash were seized from the pair.

Both have been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
