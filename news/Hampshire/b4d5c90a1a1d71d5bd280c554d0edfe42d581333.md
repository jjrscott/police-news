Do you recognise this man? We want to speak to him in connection with an assault on a staff member at a Co-op store in Southampton.

The incident occurred at 3.41pm on April 25 at the Co-op in Blechynden Terrace. The 37 year old victim was struck several times and sustained bruising and lacerations to his face and head.

The suspect is described as being aged in his mid 20s, around six foot two inches tall, with dark hair and a strong accent.

If you have any information about this incident, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200146062. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
