Detectives have tonight (Friday 13 September) charged a third person in connection with the death of 18-year-old Taylor John Williams in Basingstoke.

Terrance Maccabee, aged 18, of Radnor Road, Peckham, has been charged with:

 * Murder
 * Robbery
 * Conspiracy to rob
 * Possession of a bladed article in a public place

He will appear at Southampton Magistrates' Court tomorrow (Saturday 14 September).

Taylor, of Winlaton Road, Bromley, London, was found injured in the street at Shooters Way, Basingstoke, on Saturday 31 August. He later died in hospital.
