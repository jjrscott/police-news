Three people have been arrested after police carried out a search at a property in Southampton.

Officers from Southampton's High Harm Team and the Southampton West Neighbourhood Policing Team carried out the search at an address in Castle Street yesterday, Wednesday 17 June.

A 20-year-old man from Southampton and a 17-year-old boy from Portsmouth were arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply a Class B drug.

A third man, aged 18, from Southampton, was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply a Class A drug.

All three were released from custody but remain under investigation while enquiries continue.

We continue to ask anyone who suspects drug related activity to be happening in their neighbourhood to contact us. Every call you make to us helps us build up an intelligence picture which enables us to take action. Call 101, or call Crimestoppers and leave information anonymously on 0800 555 111.
