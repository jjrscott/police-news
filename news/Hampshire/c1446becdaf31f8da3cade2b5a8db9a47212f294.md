We are appealing for witnesses and information after a large scale altercation in Aldershot in the early hours of Saturday morning.

We are appealing for witnesses and information after a large scale altercation in Aldershot in the early hours of Saturday morning.

The incident took place between 3am and 4am on July 6, between The Famous Door and Fusion in the High Street, Aldershot.

Around 30 people were involved in the disturbance. We understand that at least one person was injured and we would like to identify him - we believe he has sustained facial injuries.

We would also like to speak to a male who is described as being of Asian appearance, wearing a long sleeved red polo shirt and black jeans.

If you have any information about this incident, please call 101, quoting reference 44190233893. Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
