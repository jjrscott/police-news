**Three men charged following County Lines warrants in which a green Lamborghini was seized.**

**Following the warrants executed between the Met and Hampshire Police on Tuesday, 12 May, the following men have been charged:**

**Calvin Hutchinson, 34, of Rigby Close, Croydon, was charged with being in concerned in the supply of Class A drugs; being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs and acquisition, use and possession of criminal property contrary to the proceeds of crime act (2002).**

**Aubin Earl Hutchinson, 38, of Rigby Close, Croydon, was charged with being in concerned in the supply of Class A drugs; being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs and acquisition, use and possession of criminal property contrary to the proceeds of crime act (2002).**

**Gavin Byron Griffiths, 41, of Derwent Road, SE20, was charged with being concerned in the supply of class A drugs and one charge of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs**

**They have been remanded to appear at Portsmouth Magistrates Court today.**

**A 25-year-old female and 51-year old man were also arrested as part of the warrants, they have been released under investigation.**
