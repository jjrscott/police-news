A 36 year old London man has been arrested on suspicion of possessing Class A drugs with intent to supply after police stopped a vehicle in Worting Road, Basingstoke.

He was also arrested on suspicion of possessing criminal property.

The vehicle was stopped in Worting Road at around 8.20pm on November 27. A large wrap of a substance suspected to be Class A drugs and a quantity of cash were seized.

The man, who is from Bow, East London, has been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
