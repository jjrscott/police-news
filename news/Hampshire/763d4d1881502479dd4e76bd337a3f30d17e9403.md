We are investigating a report of a serious sexual assault on a 15 year old girl that occurred in Aldershot Park on Sunday, July 26.

The incident took place in the park in Guildford Road, between 6.30pm and 8pm.

The suspect is described as a white male, aged around 15-16, with dark hair, wearing beige jogging bottoms and a hoody. He was with another male who was also white, around the same age, with blond hair, wearing a grey tracksuit.

If anyone has any information about this crime - or recognises the description of the suspect and the other male, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200282486.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
