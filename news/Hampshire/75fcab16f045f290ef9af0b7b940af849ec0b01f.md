A 37 year old man has been charged with theft and burglary of a home in Farnborough.

Scott Dickson, of Alexandra Road, Aldershot, is charged with burglary of a home in Elles Close, Farnborough on September 14.

He is further charged with theft of a quantity of cash from Betfred in Victoria Road, Farnborough on October 13.

He has been remanded in custody to appear before Basingstoke Magistrates Court today, (Wednesday).
