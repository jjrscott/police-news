A 33 year old woman from Reading has been arrested on suspicion of dangerous driving and assaulting police officers following an incident in Basingstoke yesterday.

A 27 year old man, also from Reading, was also arrested on suspicion of possessing Class B drugs with intent to supply and assaulting police officers.

Both currently remain in custody.

The incident occurred at around 8.15pm yesterday, August 25, at the car park at the Skyline Plaza, Alencon Link, Basingstoke.
