Tribute has been paid to a mum and grandmother who died following a collision on the M27 at Cadnam.

Zoe Eleanor Maclennan, 90 of Ashley Heath, Dorset, died following the single vehicle collision on Monday, 23 October.

Today her family have paid tribute to her. They said: "We are deeply shocked and saddened by her sudden passing, not long after the bereavement of losing her husband.

"Zoe will be greatly missed by her son and daughter, granddaughters and all those who loved her.

"We would like to take this opportunity to thank the Hampshire emergency services for all their efforts after arriving promptly at the scene."

Investigations into the exact circumstances of the collision are ongoing.

Anyone with information can call us on 101, quoting 44190379830, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
