A third man has been arrested following an incident where a 35 year old man was found with stab injuries in Farnborough.

Police were called just after 5:19pm (Wednesday, 15 July) by the ambulance service to assist with reports of an assault inside an address in Vickers Close, Farnborough.

The victim remains in hospital where he is described as being in a stable condition.

A 21-year-old man, and a 22- year-old man both from London, were arrested on suspicion of wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm.

A third man, a 41 year old man from Crondall, has also been arrested on suspicion of wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm.

All three have been released under investigation.

Anyone who has information that may assist with our enquiries is asked to phone 101, quoting 44200262735. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
