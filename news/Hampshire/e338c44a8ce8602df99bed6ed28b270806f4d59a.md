Two men have been charged in connection with the robbery of a taxi driver in West Street, Fareham.

Gary Crawley, 31, of Queens Road, Fareham and Ryan Keen, 25, of no fixed abode, are charged with the offence at West Street on September 30, 2019.

They have been remanded in custody to appear before Portsmouth Crown Court on November 4.

A 23 year old woman from Fareham was also arrested and released under investigation.
