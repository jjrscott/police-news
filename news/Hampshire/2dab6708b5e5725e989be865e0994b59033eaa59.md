Seven men have been re-bailed following the maritime security incident that took place on board the Nave Andromeda off the coast of the Isle of Wight on Sunday 25 October.

The seven arrested men, who are all Nigerian nationals, and who remain detained under Border Force powers, have now been re-bailed until Monday 25 January 2021 in relation to the criminal investigation, whilst investigators continue their enquiries.
