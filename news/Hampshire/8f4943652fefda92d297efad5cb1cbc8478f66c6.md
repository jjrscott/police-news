A 19 year old man has been charged with two offences of robbery against two 17 year olds in Fleet.

Aaron Hubbard, 19, of Montgomery Road, Farnborough, has been charged with two counts of robbbery in Albert Street, Fleet, on November 14.

He was remanded to appear before Basingstoke Magistrates Court today, (Tuesday, November 17).

Two other men, a 20 year old from Aldershot and a 50 year old man from Farnborough, have been released under investigation in connection with the same incident. Police enquiries are ongoing.
