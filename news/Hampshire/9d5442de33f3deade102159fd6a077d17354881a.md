Officers have made three arrests after executing two warrants in Basingstoke in relation to an ongoing burglary investigation.

The warrants were executed this morning (29 May) at an address on Buckland Avenue, Basingstoke, and Kiln Road in Sherborne St John.

Two men aged 60 and 51-years-old and a female aged 22 have been arrested in connection with a burglary on Worting Road on 9 January 2020.

All three remain in custody as enquiries continue.

We would like to thank the local residents for their patience as we completed our enquiries.
