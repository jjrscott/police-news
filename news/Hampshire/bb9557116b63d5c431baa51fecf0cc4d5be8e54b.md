Have you seen this ride-on mower recently?

If so, you could help our investigation into a burglary in Lymington.

The burglary took place at an address in the Woodside area, sometime between 1pm on Friday 29 November and 9am on Monday 2 December.

Machinery was stolen from an outbuilding, including:

• A Honda lawnmower
• A John Deere ride-on lawn mower
• A John Deere tractor

Have you seen these items since the weekend?

Or perhaps you were in this area at the time and remember seeing something suspicious?

If so, call us on 101, quoting the crime reference number 44190432805.
