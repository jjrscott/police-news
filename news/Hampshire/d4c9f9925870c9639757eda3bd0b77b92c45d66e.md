We are appealing for information after three men with their faces covered entered Crookham Village General Stores and demanded keys to the post office.

The incident happened on Saturday 24 August between 4.20am and 4.35am.

The men smashed the glass door and threatened the owner inside. One man was carrying a crow bar.

The owner did not hand over the keys and the men left empty handed, leaving the scene in a silver coloured Seat Ibiza or similar type vehicle.

The owner was assaulted during the incident and suffered a cut to his face.

The men have been described as white aged between 25 and 35-years-old.

One man stood 6ft tall and was wearing a khaki coloured hooded top and a dark neck warmer than covered his face. Another man is described as being 5 feet 10 inches and wore a dark grey hooded top with a black neck scarf over his face. The other was smaller, about 5 feet 6 inches and was in a dark coloured top.

Officers deployed to the area and conducted a search, No one was located.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to call 101 and quote 44190300452.
