The family of a 41-year-old man, whose body was found in a car parked in Corhampton, have paid tribute to him.

The body of Gurinderjit Rai, from Eastleigh, also known as G, was found in a car parked in Shepherds Farm Lane on the morning of Saturday 13 July.

His family said: "Gurinderjit was a devoted father to his two young children and a loving fiance to his partner. He adored his parents.

"He had an infectious sense of humour and would always find the best of any situation.

"He was a principled man and would always be the first to offer help to those that needed it. He will be missed dearly by all his family and friends."

**Appeal**

Detectives continue to investigate the circumstances of Gurinderjit's death.

Six men have been arrested in connection with the death.

They have been released on police bail, pending further enquiries.

Detectives would like to hear from anyone with information about what happened.

Information, including dashcam footage, can also be uploaded via this link: https://mipp.police.uk/operation/44HC19N02-PO1

People can call the 101 non-emergency number, quoting Operation Coach.

Alternatively, people can provide information anonymously by calling Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
