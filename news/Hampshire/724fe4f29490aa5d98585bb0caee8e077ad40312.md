We are appealing for witnesses after a man exposed himself in front of a 14-year-old girl in Eastleigh on Monday afternoon (June 1).

The girl was walking on Wilmer Road when the man exposed himself as walked past her in the opposite direction just after 3pm.

He is described as being in his 40s, wearing all black clothing, including shorts, and with a black cap on.

Officers investigating the incident are keen to speak with anyone who saw a man matching the above description in Eastleigh on Monday.

Anyone with information can call 101, quoting the reference 44200197353. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
