Three teenage boys have been arrested this morning following the theft of a vehicle.

A silver Citroen Picasso was reported stolen from the driveway of a property on Chilworth Drove overnight between May 17 and May 18.

This morning, two 15-year-old boys from Southampton and one 14-year-old boy from West End have been arrested on suspicion of theft of a motor vehicle.

The Citroen Picasso was later found in Fullerton Road, Wherwell.

Officers would to hear from anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in either Chilworth Drove between May 17 and May 18 or Fullerton Road where the car was located.

Anyone with information should call 101 and quote 44200175428.

Enquiries are ongoing.
