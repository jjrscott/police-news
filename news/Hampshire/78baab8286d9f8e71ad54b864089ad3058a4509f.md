Peter Goodchild, aged 43, of no fixed abode, has been charged with two counts of burglary.

The first burglary took place at an address in Borrowdale Road, Southampton between 7pm Thursday, 6 February and 1pm Sunday, 9 February.

The second burglary took place at an address in Siskin Close, Southampton, shortly after 1.20pm on Friday, 21 August.

Goodchild appeared at Southampton Magistrates' Court on Monday, 24 August and has been remanded in custody to appear at Southampton Crown Court on Friday, 25 September.
