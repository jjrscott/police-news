We have charged a man from West Wellow with drink driving and driving without third party insurance after a red BMW failed to stop for officers on Sunday night.

James Nicholas Bragg, 37, of Wheatears Drive, West Wellow, was arrested and a red BMW 325 CI seized by officers.

He has been bailed to appear at Southampton Magistrates' Court on October 20.
