Police officers investigating a burglary in Eastleigh have today (25 August) charged a man.

The charges come after two pushbikes were stolen from a residential property on Saturday 22 August on Shakespeare Road.

Andruis Vasiljevas, 39, of Charden Road, Gosport has been charged with one count of burglary.

A 42-year-old man from Gosport was also interviewed in connection with the incident, but has been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

VASILJEVAS has been bailed and is due to appear before Southampton Magistrates Court on Thursday 8 October.
