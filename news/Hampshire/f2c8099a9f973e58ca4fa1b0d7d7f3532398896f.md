Officers investigating a robbery in Gosport are appealing for witnesses.

The incident happened between 8.10pm and 8.20pm on Friday, 22 May in Rambler Drive, Gosport.

A 27-year-old man was approached by two men who assaulted him before stealing his bag and wallet which contained a number of cards including a bank card.

The victim sustained a minor head injury during the assault.

One of the men is described as being white, aged between 20-30 years-old, 5 feet 6 inches tall, slim with short black hair and wearing a zip up, waist length leather jacket.

Were you in the area around this time? Did you see two men acting suspiciously or did you hear anything?

Do you have any private CCTV that may have captured something in the area?

If you think you can help us please call 101, quoting 44200182655, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
