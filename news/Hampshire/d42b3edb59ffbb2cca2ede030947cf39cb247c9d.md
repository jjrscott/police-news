Two men and a woman have been arrested on suspicion of being involved in a conspiracy to supply Class A drugs in Basingstoke.

The three were arrested after officers attended a property in Basingstoke Town Centre on June 26.

A 35 year old woman from Basingstoke was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs.

A 23 year old man from South East London was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs.

A 23 year old man from South East London, was arrested on suspicion of possessing Class A drugs with intent to supply.

Police also seized suspected Class A drugs and a quantity of cash.

All three have all been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
