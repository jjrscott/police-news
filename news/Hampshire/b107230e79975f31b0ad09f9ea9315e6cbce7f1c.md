A man has been arrested in connection with drugs offences after officers carried out a stop search in St Marys Place, Southampton, on Friday, 13 November.

An 18-year-old man from Southampton was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class B drugs and possession of an offensive weapon.

A quantity of suspected Class B drugs, a metal baton and cash were seized.

He has been released from custody but remains under investigation while our enquiries continue.

If you have concerns about drug-related activity in your area please call us on 101, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
