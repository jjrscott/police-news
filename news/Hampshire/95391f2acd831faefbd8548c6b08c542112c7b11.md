We have charged a man with assault by penetration and attempted rape following an investigation into a serious sexual assault in Winchester.

The charge comes following a report of a serious sexual assault on a woman in her 20s in the area of St George's Street at around 5am on Saturday 12 October.

Jason Chester, aged 43, of Campbell Way, Fair Oak is due to appear before Winchester Magistrates Court today (October 18).
