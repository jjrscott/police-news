A man has been charged and appeared before court today in connection with cannabis production in Southampton.

Puan Hoang, 26, of Prince of Wales Avenue, Southampton, has been charged with producing a controlled drug of class B - cannabis.

It comes after officers from Southampton's High Harm Team executed a warrant at an address in Prince of Wales Avenue, Southampton on Monday, 15 June.

The pre-planned warrant was carried out under Fortress, Hampshire Constabulary's campaign to reduce the harm of drug-related violence.

Hoang was remanded and appeared at Southampton Magistrates Court today Tuesday, 16 June.

If you're concerned about drug related activity in your area, please call 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
