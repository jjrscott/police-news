A 20 year old Aldershot man has been arrested in relation to drugs and firearms offences after being stopped by police in Bordon.

He was arrested on suspicion of possessing Class A drugs with intent to supply and possession of a firearm after being stopped in Conde Way, Bordon, on June 16.

He has been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
