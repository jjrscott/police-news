Four people have been arrested in connection with drugs offences after officers from the Southampton High Harm team attended an address in Northam Road, Southampton, this morning (7 January).

Three men aged 19, 24 and 56 and a girl aged 17, all from Southampton, have been arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of Class A drugs.

The 56-year-old man has also been arrested on suspicion of possession of Class A drugs.

A quantity of suspected Class A drugs were seized.

All four remain in custody at this time.

If you have concerns about drug-related activity in your area, we want to know. Please call us on 101, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 to report it.
