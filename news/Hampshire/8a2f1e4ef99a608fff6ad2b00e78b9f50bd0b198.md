Five people arrested in connection with a serious assault on a 31 year old man in Basingstoke, have been released under investigation.

All five were arrested following the assault on a 31 year old man in Gershwin Road, on October 31. Officers attended and found the victim in a rear garden of a residential property in Gershwin Road with serious head injuries.

The victim remains in hospital where his injuries are currently described as life changing.

The five arrested are two men in their thirties from Basingstoke and three men in their twenties - two from Basingstoke and one from Middlesex.

They were all arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to commit murder.
