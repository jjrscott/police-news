Officers investigating the rape of a 21-year-old woman in Southampton have charged a man.

The attack happened within the perimeter of Westquay shopping centre just before 8am on the morning of Saturday, 15 February.

Krystian Kwarta, aged 20, of Keyhaven Road, Milford-on-Sea has been charged with two counts of rape and one count of assault occasioning actual bodily harm.

He will appear before Southampton Magistrates Court tomorrow (Wednesday 19 February).
