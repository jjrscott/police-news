Can you help us identify the man in this e-fit?

We are looking to speak with him in connection to an assault on Salisbury Road, Totton, which happened on Tuesday 21 July at around 10pm.

The victim, a 16-year-old girl from Totton, was approached by an unknown man who grabbed her by the arm and held onto her.

The man is described as being white, around 50 to 55-years-old, around 5ft 9ins and of a large build, with dark hair that is long top and shorter on the sides and facial hair.

He was described as wearing a tight fitting black t-shirt, and light blue stonewashed jeans. He was riding a dark bike that had a silver basket and a speaker system on the back.

Enquiries to identify the man have been ongoing, but we are now asking the public to help us identify this e-fit.

If you know him, or have any information that may assist our investigation, please contact 101 with the reference 44200272781. Alternatively, you can phone Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
