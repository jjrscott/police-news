A 56 year old man has been charged with stealing a car from a petrol station in Farnborough while its owner paid for fuel.

David Fordham of Heath Lane, Farnham, is charged with taking the Toyota Yaris without consent from the Shell petrol Station on Farnborough Road on November 8.

He is also charged with driving the vehicle without insurance and failing to provide a specimen of breath for analysis.

Fordham has been remanded to appear before Basingstoke Magistrates Court today, (November 9).
