Two 16 year old boys have been arrested in connection with an attempted knifepoint robbery in Aldershot.

The incident is alleged to have occurred in Deadbrook Lane, Aldershot, at 8.30pm on November 3.

One of the 16 year olds was arrested on suspicion of attempted robbery and possession of an offensive weapon. The second 16 year old was arrested on suspicion of attempted robbery. Both currently remain in custody.

The two victims were not physically injured during the incident.
