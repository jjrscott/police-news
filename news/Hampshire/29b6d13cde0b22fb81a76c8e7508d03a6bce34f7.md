We are appealing for witnesses and information after two teenagers were threatened and assaulted by a group of three males outside Iceland in Farnborough town centre.

The incident occurred at around 5pm on Tuesday, December 15. The two victims, both aged 18, were approached by the three youths outside the supermarket in Queensmead. They have threatened the two boys and used a lighter to burn the hand of one of the victims.

One of the suspects was wearing a green tracksuit and another was wearing a red jacket.

If you can help, please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting reference 44200483599.

Alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or report online at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
