A 22 year old man has been jailed for 18 weeks after admitting to using stolen bank cards that he stole from a car in Farnborough.

Daniel Jankovic, of Fernhill Road, Farnborough, pleaded guilty to theft from a motor vehicle in Brownsover Road, Farnborough, on September 26 after appearing at Basingstoke Magistrates Court yesterday (Wednesday, October 13).

He further admitted:

 * Fraud by using a stolen bank card at JD Sports in Guildford, on September 26.
 * Fraud by using a stolen bank card at Asda in Westmead, Farnborough, on September 26.
 * Fraud by using a stolen bank card at McDonalds in Farnborough Road, Farnborough on September 26.

Jankovic asked for a further 11 offences of theft from motor vehicles and vehicle interference to be taken into consideration. The incidents all occurred in the Southwood area of Farnborough on the night of September 26.
