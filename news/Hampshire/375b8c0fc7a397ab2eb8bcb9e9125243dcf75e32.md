Officers investigating a burglary at a business premises in Lymington have today (3 September) arrested four men.

The arrests come after reports of 'loud banging' from a member of the public between 9.30 and 10.30pm at Mole Country Stores on Mount Pleasant Lane on Wednesday 2 September.

\- A 22-year-old man from Salisbury was arrested on suspicion of burglary
\- A 23-year-old man from Ringwood was arrested on suspicion of burglary
\- A 23-year-old man from Hythe was arrested on suspicion of burglary
\- A 33-year-old man from Marchwood was arrested on suspicion of burglary

All remain in custody at this present time, while we continue our investigation.
