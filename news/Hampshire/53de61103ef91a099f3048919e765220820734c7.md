Officers investigating the rape of a 22-year-old woman in Eastleigh in January 2019, have today (30 July) charged a man.

Police were contacted after a woman was approached by a man and raped in Whyteways in the early hours of Wednesday 9th January 2019.

David Howse, aged 32, of The Quadrangle, Eastleigh has been charged with one count of rape.

He will appear before Southampton Magistrates Court on Tuesday 6 October .
