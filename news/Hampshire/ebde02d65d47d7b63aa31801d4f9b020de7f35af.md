We are investigating reports of criminal damage to 13 vehicles in Bransgore overnight from Saturday 14 November into the following morning, Sunday 15 November.

It has been reported vehicles had been broken into or damaged in the following roads:

 * Betsy Close
 * Brookside Road
 * Elmers Way
 * Hill Close
 * Rosehill Drive
 * Shackleton Square
 * Shearsbrook Close
 * Tyrrells Court

Among those vehicles damaged was a first responder's car.

Officers are making enquiries and are keen to speak with anyone who saw anything suspicious or who has any information about the damaged vehicles.

They are particularly keen to speak with anyone who may have dash cam or CCTV footage from these roads.

Anyone with information should call 101, quoting 44200442389.
