A man in his 20s was arrested on suspicion of possessing Class A drugs with intent to supply after being stopped by officers in Aldershot.

The man, who is from London, was stopped in the High Street at around 11.30am on October 19. Following the search, around 40 wraps containing substances suspected to be Class A drugs, were seized.

He has been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.
