Two men were arrested on suspicion of hare coursing after a report of trespassers in a field in Whitchurch.

A 22 year old man from Gillingham in Dorset and a 31 year old man from Salisbury, Wiltshire, were arrested following the report at around 10.15am on Sunday, November 22.

A vehicle and two dogs were also seized by police officers. The dogs are being cared for in a secure location.

The two men have been released under investigation while enquiries are ongoing.
