We are appealing for information after a burglary in Fordingbridge.

Entry was gained to house on Salisbury Road between about 8.20am and 12.45pm on Monday 20 January.

A number of items were reported stolen, including jewellery, sunglasses, a television and computer equipment.

If you have any information about this burglary, please call 101 quoting 44200023921 or contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

A 29-year-old man from Fordingbridge was arrested on suspicion of burglary and has been released while investigations continue. A 31-year-old man of no fixed address and a 16-year-old boy from Fordingbridge have also been arrested on suspicion of burglary. They remain in custody at this time.
