We are investigating following a report of a robbery at the One Stop store in Five Heads Road, Horndean.

It took place around 4.15pm on Monday 27 January.

A man entered the store and stole cash and cigarettes.

No injuries were reported.

The man is described as being dressed in a black coat with a hood up, with tight dark jeans/tracksuit bottoms and white trainers.

He was also described as having a pair of tights over his head and carrying a blue Lonsdale Bag for Life.

We are keen to hear from anyone who has information about what happened.

Were you in the area at the time? Do you remember seeing someone acting suspiciously?

Perhaps you have been offered cigarettes for sale at a cheap price since this incident?

We would also like to identify the driver of a red and white Mini seen close to the shop at the time. This person may hold vital information on what happened.

Anyone with information should call police on 101, quoting the crime reference number 44200033501.

Alternatively, people can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
