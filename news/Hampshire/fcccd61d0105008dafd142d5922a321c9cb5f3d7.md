Officers are appealing for information after a woman was assaulted in Greatham, near Liss.

The incident happened at some time between 9.45pm and 10.30pm on Thursday 21 November at the Greatham roundabout. An 18-year-old woman had got out of a grey Toyota Hilux truck. A man then also exited the vehicle and assaulted the woman.

She suffered bruising to her face and cuts to her back.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who witnessed the incident, or anyone who may have been driving through the area that may have dashcam footage.

A 49-year-old man from Bordon has been arrested on suspicion of attempting to cause grievous bodily harm and occasioning actual bodily hard. He has been released on police bail until 20 December.

Anyone with information should call police on 101 quoting 44190419563.
