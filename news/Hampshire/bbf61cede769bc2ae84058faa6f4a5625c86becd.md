Do you recognise these men?

We'd like to speak to them in connection with two racially aggravated assaults that took place in Aldi on Bevois Valley Road, Southampton.

It happened between 2.55pm and 3.30pm on Friday, 22 November.

Two men entered the store and became verbally abusive and threatening towards staff and customers.

One of the men assaulted two members of staff whilst being racially abusive.

Do you know these men? Did you witness this incident?

If you have any information that could help with this investigation please call us on 101, quoting the crime reference number 44190420363.

Alternatively, people can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where information can be left anonymously.
