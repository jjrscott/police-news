A 19 year old and a 20 year old who were arrested after five teenagers were robbed in a park in Farnborough, have been released under investigation while police enquiries continue.

The incident occurred at around 9.20pm in Queen Elizabeth Park, Farnborough. Five friends, all aged 15, were in between the pond and play area when they were approached by two suspects. Property including two mountain bikes were stolen.

If anyone has any information about this crime or if you were in the area and have dash cam footage, please get in touch by calling 101, quoting reference 44200441928.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting the same reference.
