We are appealing for witnesses after a man suffered serious injuries after an assault on King Arthur's Way, Andover.

The incident happened at between 5.15pm and 5.35pm on Saturday, 28 November.

The victim, a 39-year-old man, was walking through the car-park for The Merlin Pub when he was in an altercation with a group, the victim was then assaulted by a man in the group.

He was taken to North Hampshire Hospital to be treated for an injury to his head.

A second victim, a 39-year-old man, was also assaulted and received minor injuries.

Officers have conducted a number of enquiries and are now turning to the public for help.

Were you in the area at the time? Perhaps you have camera or dash-cam footage that could assist in our investigation?

Anyone with information is asked to phone 101 with the reference 44200460818. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers 100% anonymously on 0800 555 111.
