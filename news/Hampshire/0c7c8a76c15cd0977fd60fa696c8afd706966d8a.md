Do you recognise this distinctive jacket? We would like to speak to the wearer in connection with a robbery at a convenience store in Aldershot.

The incident occurred at around 7.45pm on Saturday, September 28. The suspect was wearing black sun glasses and a black ski mask when he walked into the Best One shop in Andover Way.

The suspect has pointed an object that was hidden inside a red or orange plastic bag at the member of staff before making off with a quantity of cash.

The suspect made off out of Andover Way and ran along Lower Farnham Road towards Badshot Lea.

Did you see anyone matching this description in the vicinity before or after the incident? Were you in the area? Do you have any dash cam or private CCTV in that location?

If you can help, please call 101, quoting reference 44190347233.
