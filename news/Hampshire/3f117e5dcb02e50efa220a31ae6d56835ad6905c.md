We are now in a position to name the motorcyclist who died following a collision on the A343 near Hurstbourne Tarrant.

The collision took place sometime at around 5pm on Wednesday 5 August.

Alex Sartain, 34, from Upper Enham, was pronounced dead at the scene.

His next-of-kin are being supported by specialist officers.

Anyone who has information about this collision and has not yet contacted the police, should do so by calling 101 quoting Op Crystal.
