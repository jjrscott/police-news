A 42 year old Basingstoke man has been charged with a burglary that occurred in Old Worting Road in October this year.

John Shrubshall of no fixed abode, is charged with residential burglary at the property on October 12. Items including jewellery, a laptop, a kindle and two tablets, were stolen.

Shrubshall appeared before Basingstoke Magistrates Court on November 28 and has been committed for a pre-trial hearing at Winchester Crown Court on December 23.
