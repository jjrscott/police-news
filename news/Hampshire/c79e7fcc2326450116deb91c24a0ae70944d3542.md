Police officers conducting a concern for welfare check at a property in Eastleigh, have arrested a man in connection with drug supply.

The incident occurred in the early hours of Tuesday 2 March after officers attended an address in Toynbee Road. Upon entering the property, officers located a large quantity of Class B drugs which were seized as a result. A subsequent search of the property found a quanity of Class A drugs, drug-related paraphanelia and mobile devices - all which were seized as evidence.

A 59-year-old from Eastleigh was arrested on suspicion of possession of a Class B drug, possession of a Class A drug with intent to supply and possession of a Class B drug with intent to supply.

He was later released under investigation pending further police enquiries.
