Updated News Post

Three men who were arrested in connection with the murder of Kelly Anne Case in Gosport have been released with no further action.

The 27-year-old's body was found by firefighters following a fire at her home in Grange Crescent on Tuesday 30 July.

A 32-year-old man from Gosport, a 26-year-old man of no fixed abode and a 28-year-old man from Gosport were arrested but have since been released without charge.

Brendan Nicholas Rowan-Davies, 28, of Haslar Road, Gosport, has been charged with murder and is due to go on trial on 14 January 2020.

We are asking members of the public to not speculate or share information of this case on social media, and would advise anyone with information to contact police in the first instance.
