A 14-year-old boy is due to appear in court today in Hull, charged with offences relating to an incident on Wednesday, 25 November, when a teenager took to a roof in Thorndale.

The boy – who cannot be legally named – is charged with three counts of criminal damage, four counts of assaulting an emergency worker and using threatening words or behaviour.
