A 17-year-old boy has been arrested in connection with our ongoing efforts to tackle county lines drug dealing operations in Hull after officers spotted a person behaving suspiciously in Anlaby Road, Hull.

The incident happened at 1.35am this morning (Thursday, 10 December) and drugs, a knife and a large sum of cash have been seized.

A 17-year-old remains in our custody on suspicion of possession with intent to supply drugs (other than class A) and possession of a knife as we continue our investigation.
