Today (Wednesday 16 December) at approximately 2:30am, officers were called to a reported burglary in progress at a property on Spring Bank West, Hull.

Upon entering the property, officers uncovered a large cannabis factory with over 40 mature plants estimated to be inside.

A man has been arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the production of cannabis, and remains in custody whilst we continue with our investigation.
