A man and a teenage boy have been arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class A drugs this morning, Monday 18 January, following a warrant in Reedness, Goole.

Upon searching the property and car, wraps of suspected heroin, cannabis, a quantity of cash and multiple mobile phones and tablets were discovered and seized.

The two men remain in our custody whilst we progress our investigation.
