A 37-year-old wanted man from Hull has been arrested by #OpGalaxy officers yesterday, Sunday 25 October.

Officers had been wanting to speak with him in connection to a number of shop thefts around East Hull and an assault on a shop worker.

He was also arrested on suspicion of possession of Class A drugs.

He remains in our custody at this time whilst we continue our investigations.
