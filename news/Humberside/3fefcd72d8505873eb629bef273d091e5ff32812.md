Three people have been arrested in connection with the reported stabbing of a man in Doncaster Street, Hull, on Saturday, 3 October

Two men, aged 38 and 48, along with a 36-year-old woman, remain in our custody, assisting us with our enquiries.

We were called at around 10.15pm and the area remained cordoned off into Sunday, as officers continued their investigation.

The man remains in hospital in a serious condition.

Detective Inspector Andy Crawforth said: “We appreciate the impact that this kind of incident can have on residents but I can reassure you that this is believed to have involved people known to each other and that there is no risk to the wider public.”

Anyone with information is asked to call police on 101, or anonymously contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555111, quoting log number 516 of 3 October.
