On Friday 23 October officers conducted a drugs warrant at a property on the junction of Freeman Street with Garibaldi Street in Grimsby.

On entry thousands of cannabis plants and growing equipment were discovered on three floors of the property.

Our officers are gathering evidence and dismantling the grow and the electricity supply is being made safe. No one has been arrested in connection with the investigation at this time and our enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone with information about the premises and the activities within should call us on our non-emergency number 101 quoting log 265 of 23 October 2020.

If you don't want to speak to the police but have information you want to share you can call Crimestoppers, an independent charity, anonymously on 0800 555 111. The information you provide is anonymous and you could be in line for a reward.
