Khalid Aadan (18) and Mohamed Aadan (19) of Pendrill Street, Hull, and a 17 year-old boy who cannot be named for legal reasons, have today been charged with the murder of 20 year-old Abdullah Balouchi on Peel Street on Wednesday 7 October 2020.

They appeared at Hull Magistrates Court today and were remanded in custody.

One man remains in police custody in relation to the murder as we continue our investigations. Others who were arrested in connection with the incident have now been released without charge.
