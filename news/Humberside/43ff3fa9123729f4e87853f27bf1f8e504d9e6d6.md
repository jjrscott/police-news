A 43 year-old woman has been arrested on suspicion of burglary and possession of a controlled drug in South Cave this morning.

We were called at 2:09am today (Wednesday 23 December) following reports of a suspected burglary at one property on Plantation Drive, South Cave.

Officers attended the scene and a 43 year-old woman has been arrested on suspicion of burglary and possession of a drug. She remains in our custody whilst investigations are ongoing.
