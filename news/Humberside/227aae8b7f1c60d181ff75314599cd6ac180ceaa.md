This afternoon a teenage boy (aged 16) has been arrested in connection with the robbery of a woman at 7.30pm on Endyke Lane in Cottingham on Monday 11 January.

The boy remains in custody where he will be questioned in connection with the incident.

The victim is recovering well and is thankful for the messages of support from the public.

Her handbag and contents have been recovered apart from her purse and contents, described as navy coloured purse with a red tab on the front and held bank cards, driving licence and cash.

Anyone who can help with our ongoing investigation should call our non-emergency number 101 quoting log 423 of 11 January 2021.
