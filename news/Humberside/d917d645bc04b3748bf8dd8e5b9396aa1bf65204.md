Brandon Hynes (26) of Eden Grove, Doncaster has been charged with possession with intent to supply Class A drugs, possession of bladed articles and possession of an offensive weapon.

He appeared at Grimsby Magistrates court Tuesday 15 December and has been remanded in custody to appear in court at a future date.

Eagle eyed officers stopped a suspicious looking vehicle on Fraser Street, Grimsby at approximately 10:20pm on Sunday 13 December. When searching the vehicle, officers discovered a large number of drugs, cash and knives.

Believed to be linked to an address in the area, officers carried out an additional search at a house on Torrington Street, Grimsby where they uncovered drugs, and equipment associated with the supply of drugs.

A second man and a woman were arrested in connection with the incident and released under investigation whilst we continue with our enquiries.
