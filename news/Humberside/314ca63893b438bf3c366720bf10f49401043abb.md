A Brigg man has been charged with 10 counts of burglary, following a number of break-ins at homes and businesses in the town.

Joshua Barnard, 26, of no fixed abode, was remanded to appear before Grimsby Magistrates Court today (Saturday, 16 January) where he stands accused of four burglaries at a local football club; four burglaries at a school and one at a private house.
