One man has been charged in connection with a burglary in Wenlock Street, Hull, on Wednesday 9 December after an elderly resident reported that a wallet containing a large sum of money had been stolen.

Christopher Harrison, 37, of Albany Street, Hull has been charged with burglary and has been remanded in custody to appear at Hull Magistrates Court today (Monday 14 December).
