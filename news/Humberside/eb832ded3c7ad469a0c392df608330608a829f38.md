A man has been charged with affray, two counts of assaulting an emergency worker, attempts to assault causing actual bodily harm, driving without due care and attention, driving without insurance and driving without a license following an incident on Crosby Road Scunthorpe, on Thursday 11 March.

Following his appearance at Grimsby Magistrates Court, the 27-year-old man has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Grimsby Magistrates Court tomorrow (Tuesday 16 March).

A man was reported to have detached a petrol tank from an uninsured motorbike and whilst on fire thrown it at officers.
