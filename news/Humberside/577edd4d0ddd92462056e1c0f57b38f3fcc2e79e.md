A Grimsby man has been found guilty today of the murder of 47-year-old Shaun Lyall in Cleethorpes last year.

Craig Whittle, 45, of Corporation Road in Grimsby was charged with murdering Mr Lyall who was found with multiple injuries at a house on Sidney Street on Tuesday 17 July 2018.

Today at Hull Crown Court he has been found guilty of murder and is due to be sentenced tomorrow (Wednesday 18 December).
