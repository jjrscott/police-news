More than 150 cannabis plants have been recovered from a property on Cemetery Road in Scunthorpe, on Thursday 14 January.

Whilst searching the property for suspected cannabis cultivation, officers found and seized £30,000 worth of herbal cannabis bush, £10,000 worth of cannabis plants, £20,000 in cash, £3,000 worth of watches, a side handled baton, controlled medication and high value electronics which are associated with growing cannabis.

As a result, two men have been charged and remanded.

Robert Kobierowski, 38, of Cemetery Road and Sebastian Janczak, 38, of Cemetery Road have been charged with production of a controlled class B drug and possession of criminal property.

They are both due to appear at Grimsby Magistrates Court today, Friday 15 January.
