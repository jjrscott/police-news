Yesterday (Thursday 4 March) a man was charged with assault, two counts of robbery, two counts of possession of a bladed article and six counts of theft following a number of reports in February.

The 36-year-old man is due to appear at Grimsby Magistrates Court today (Friday 5 March).

It was reported several shops had items and cash stolen and in a separate incident, it was reported cash had been taken from a man at an ATM.
