A third man has been charged following a robbery at a shop on Wellington Street, Grimsby on Friday 20 November.

Robert Mobbs (26), of Harlow Street, was yesterday charged with robbery. He is due to appear at Grimsby Magistrates Court today.

Two other men, Wayne Price (39) and Adrian Mobbs (42) are due to appear at Grimsby Crown Court on 21 December 2020 in connection with the same robbery.

We would encourage anyone who has not yet spoken with us and may have information that could assist our investigation to call 101 quoting log 514 of 20 November. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 to remain anonymous.
