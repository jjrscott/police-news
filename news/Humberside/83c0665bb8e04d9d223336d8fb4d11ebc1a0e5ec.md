A cannabis factory has been shut down at property in Farebrother Street, Grimsby on Sunday, 29 November where an estimated 300-400 plants were discovered by officers.

Renzi Domi, 24, of Preston Road, Birmingham; Beher Xhediku, 38, of Farebrother Street Grimsby;Doris Merdini, 24, of no fixed abode, and Vilson Ciku, 30, of no fixed abode have all been charged with the production of cannabis and are due to appear before Grimsby Magistrates Court today.
