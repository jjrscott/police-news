Detective Chief Inspector Rhod Troake has this morning confirmed that a murder investigation has been launched following the discovery of the body of 17 year-old Connor Lyons, on Tuesday, 19 January in Ennerdale, Hull.
One man is in custody, with another assisting with our enquiries.
We are supporting his family at this very difficult time and out of respect for Mr Lyons and his family, we ask that their privacy is respected.
Our thoughts are with his loved ones.
If you have any information that could assist our investigation, please call our non-emergency 101 line, quoting log number 80 of 19 January.
