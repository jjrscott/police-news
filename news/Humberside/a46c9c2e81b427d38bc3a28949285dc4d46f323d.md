A 21-year-old Hull man has tonight been charged with the murder of Bransholme teenager Connor Lyons (Friday, 22 January).

Cole Jarvis, of Stroud Crescent West, has been remanded to appear before Hull Magistrates Court tomorrow morning.

Sadly, Connor’s body was found on the banks of the River Hull at Ennerdale on the morning of Tuesday, 19 January.

His family has been informed of the development in the case and are being supported by specialist officers.

Detective Chief Inspector Rhod Troake said: “Once again, I would like to thank everyone who came forward with information to assist us in our investigation.

“Our thoughts remain with Connor’s family and loved ones.”
