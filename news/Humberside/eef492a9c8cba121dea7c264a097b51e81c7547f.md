The cordon has now been lifted in Market Place, Pocklington, after our colleagues at the Explosive Ordnance Disposal team confirmed there is no risk to the public.

We would like to thank all those in the area for their patience and co-operation whilst we brought the incident to a safe conclusion.

Officers will remain in the area to provide reassurance, so please speak to them if you have any concerns.
