Two men have been arrested after officers executed a warrant under the theft act at a property on Sidney Street, Cleethorpes.

Wednesday 10 February officers descended on a property in Sidney Street, Cleethorpes. Whilst inside the property they discovered a large quantity of high value mountain bikes, fishing equipment and electrical items.

A 31-year-old man and a 35-year-old man have been released under investigation whilst we continue with our enquiries.

To report any stolen bikes, or other related crimes, please call our non-emergency number 101 and the relevant teams will look into these reports.
