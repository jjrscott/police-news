We arrested Alan Macgilvray yesterday (Wednesday 13 January) after he was circulated as wanted for a number of recent serious incidents.

Alan Macgilvray (24), of no fixed abode, has now been charged with a number of offences in Hull including the rape and assault of a woman in November 2020, of two counts of criminal damage In December 2020 and of two counts of assault and coercive control in January 2021.

He is due to appear at Hull Magistrates Court today.
