A man has been arrested on suspicion of burglary and theft from a vehicle, after a man was spotted breaking into a business premises on Newtown Road, Scunthorpe at 8pm last night, Monday 15 March.

Officers attended the scene and discovered a catalytic converter hidden under a vehicle. They also found a rucksack with tools such as an angle grinder in.

The man remains in our custody at this time.

Anyone with any information, who has not yet spoken to us, is asked to call our non-emergency number 101 quoting log 569 of 15 March.
