Scott Wright, 24, of Hargrave Street, Grimsby, has been charged with robbery, posession of a bladed article and criminal damage following an incident at a store in Gilbey Road, last night (Thursday, 14 January).

He has been remanded to appear before Grimsby Magistrates Court tomorrow (Saturday, 16 January).
