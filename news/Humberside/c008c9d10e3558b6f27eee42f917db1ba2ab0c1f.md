Two drugs warrants were executed yesterday Monday 4 November at separate addresses in Scunthorpe as part of Operation Galaxy.

Both were under section 23 of the Misuse of Drugs Act. One was at a property on Grasby Road, the other on Salmonby Road. A car was also searched.

Prepared bags of class B drugs were seized along with £60 worth of what are thought to be class A drugs, between 600 and 800 Pregabalin tablets, £100 in cash and drugs paraphernalia.

Three women and two men were arrested on suspicion of possessing an illegal drug with intent to supply.

They have been released under investigation while investigations continue.

Please keep the information coming in relation the anything you might be suspicious of in your communities.

Op Galaxy is made up of many different specialist teams who are proactively tackling those responsible for crime all over the force area.
