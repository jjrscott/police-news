Two Hull men are due before court today in connection with the stabbing of a man in Doncaster Street on Saturday, 3 October.

Campbell Higinbotham, 38, of Chester Grove, has been charged with assisting an offender and possession of a knife.

Richard Hamilton, 48, also of Chester Grove, is also charged with assisting an offender.

Both are remanded to appear before Hull Magistrates Court today (Thursday, 8 October).

A 19-year-old man arrested on suspicion of attempted murder, remains in our custody.

A 36-year-old woman arrested in connection with the incident earlier this week has been released without charge.
