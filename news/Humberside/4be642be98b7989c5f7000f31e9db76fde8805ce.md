Two men have been charged following an alleged theft on The Square, Dringhouses, York, Monday 7 December. It was reported that two cars and a wallet had been stolen after the property had been broken in to.

Rhys Bunce, 26, of Hutton Close, Hull, has been charged with burglary; making off without paying for fuel; two counts of theft of a motor vehicle; dangerous driving; driving whilst disqualified and driving without insurance.

Maccauley Hatch, 25, of Royal Mews, Hull, has been charged with burglary; two counts of theft of motor vehicle; making off without paying for fuel; dangerous driving and driving without insurance.

Both men have been remanded in custody to appear at Hull Magistrates Court today (Wednesday 9 December).
