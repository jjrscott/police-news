Three men were arrested following a burglary at a commercial garage in Melbourne near Pocklington on Friday 30 October.

We received a call at 11.25pm reporting a burglary in progress at the garage. Our officers were immediately deployed and stopped a vehicle nearby. Three suspects were arrested on suspicion of burglary.

Following our enquiries Thomas Smith (26) of James Street, York, Paul Brown (38) of Rowntree Avenue, York and Simon Smith (29) of James Street, York were all charged with burglary and theft of a motor vehicle.

They have been release on bail to appear at Hull Magistrates Court on 7 January 2021.
