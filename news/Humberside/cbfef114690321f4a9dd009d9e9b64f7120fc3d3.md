A nineteen year-old and three 16 year-old boys have been arrested in connection with a burglary on Rokeby Park in Hull on Friday 6 November.

They were stopped by our roads policing officers on Walker Street in Hull yesterday afternoon on suspicion that the car they were driving in was stolen in the burglary last week.

Although all of the suspects tried to run from the car they were caught by our officers and taken into custody. All four have all been arrested on suspicion of burglary and theft of a motor vehicle

The car has been recovered and will be returned to its owner once it has been forensically examined for evidence.
