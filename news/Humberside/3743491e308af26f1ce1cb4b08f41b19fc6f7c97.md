A Hull teenager and three men from Sheffield have been arrested on suspicion of stealing a Mercedes AMG in the early hours of this morning.

The grey vehicle was seen travelling at high speed by our officers, who brought it to a stop on Priory Road, Hull, at 2.15am today (Friday, 18 December).

Three men aged 23, 19 and 21 and a 16-year-old boy remain in our custody.
