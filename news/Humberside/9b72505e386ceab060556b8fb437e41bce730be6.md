A 33-year-old man has been charged with grievous bodily harm following an incident on Edgecumbe Street, Hull on Sunday 21 March.

It was reported that a woman had been assaulted and sustained serious injuries. She remains in hospital at this time.

Following his appearance in Hull Magistrates Court on Tuesday 23 March, he has been remanded into custody to appear in Hull Crown Court on Wednesday 21 April.

A second man who was arrested in connection with the incident has been placed on conditional bail whilst we continue our enquiries.

We are keen to speak to any further witnesses, who may not yet have come forward. Please call us on our non-emergency number 101 quoting log 568 of 21 March if you have any information, or you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
