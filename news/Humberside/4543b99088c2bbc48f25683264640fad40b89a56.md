Two men have been charged following a reported burglary on Amethyst Road, Hull last month.

Shaun Sussam (31) of no fixed abode has been charged with burglary, fraudulent use of bank cards and handling stolen goods. He has been bailed to appear in Hull Crown Court on Thursday 31 December.

Daniel Huteson (22) of Holland Street, Hull has been charged with burglary, and fraud, and is due to appear in Hull Magistrates Court today (Monday 7 December).

It was reported that jewellery and bank cards had been stolen after the property was broken into.
