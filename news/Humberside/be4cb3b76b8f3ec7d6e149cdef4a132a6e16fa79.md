Following a collision on the A165 between Leven and Brandesburton at 7.30am on Thursday 28 January, sadly one of the drivers has now died.

He has been named as 23-year-old Harry Thomas. His family have released this picture and ask that they are given privacy at this extremely difficult time. They continue to be supported by our specially trained officers.

The second driver who was injured in the collision remains in a critical but stable condition.

We continue to appeal for anyone who witnessed the collision, or has dash cam footage, to call us on our non-emergency number 101 quoting log 63 of 28 January.
