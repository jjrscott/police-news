19 year-old Chloe Fewster, reported missing on Boxing Day (Saturday 26 December), has been located and has been seen and spoken to by our officers.

Chloe was reported missing at 11am on 26 December prompting an extensive police operation to find her. She was located in Hull last night (Monday 28 December).

We would like to thank everyone for sharing our appeals to find Chloe.
