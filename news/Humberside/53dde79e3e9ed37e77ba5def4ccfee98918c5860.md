A woman has been charged with burglary, robbery and fraud following an incident on Morpeth Street on Saturday 6 March.

The 23-year-old woman appeared at Hull Magistrates Court on Saturday 13 March and was remanded in custody to appear at Hull Crown Court on Monday 12 April.

An elderly resident reported that his wallet and bank cards had be stolen, after the stolen cards were used to make multiple purchases.
