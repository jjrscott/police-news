A man has been charged following a reported robbery on Saxon Crescent, Cleethorpes

John Cooper (34) of Grimsby Road, Cleethorpes has been charged with grievous bodily harm, robbery, theft, possession of an offensive weapon and seven counts of fraud by false representation.

Cooper is due to appear at Hull Magistrates Court, today (Monday 25 January).

It was reported that a man had been assaulted and had his bank card stolen, after the stolen card was used multiple times to make purchases.
