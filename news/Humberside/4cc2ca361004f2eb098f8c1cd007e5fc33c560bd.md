A man and woman have been arrested on suspicion of money laundering by officers from our Road Crime Team.

Their vehicle was stopped by the team as they drove towards Grimsby at around 9pm last night and a large quantity of cash was found.

The 31-year-old man and 29-year-old woman both remain in our custody, assisting us with our enquries.
