Following our media appeal last week, two teenagers, aged 13 and 14, have been charged with a robbery which happened on Wednesday 18 November at a convenience store on Greenwich Avenue in Hull.

The two teenagers, who cannot be named for legal reasons, have both been charged with robbery and possession of an offensive weapon and are due to appear at Hull Youth Court on 1 December 2020.
