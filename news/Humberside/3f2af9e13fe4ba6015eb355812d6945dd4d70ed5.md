Nicholas Royston (42) of Rosmead Street in Hull has today been charged with the assault of two police officers on the A63 yesterday.

The officers were at the scene of a fatal road traffic collision shortly after 12.15pm yesterday (Friday 11 December) when they were assaulted. One officer sustained facial injuries which required hospital treatment.

Nicholas Royston has been remanded to appear at Hull Magistrates Court today.
