A man has been arrested and subsequently charged after officers executed a misuse of drugs act warrant at a property on Wath Grove, Hull Yesterday (Thursday 21 January 2021).

Richard Myers (52) of Wath Grove, Hull was charged with possession with intent to supply class B drugs and two counts of possession with intent to supply class A drug.

Myers is due to appear at Hull Magistrates Court today (Friday 22 January).

Your local teams are continuing their action against drugs in your area and continue to encourage anyone with information relating to the production of cannabis, or any drug, to call 101 and report it to us.
