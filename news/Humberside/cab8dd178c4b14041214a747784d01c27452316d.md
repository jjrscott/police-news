Two men have today been charged with the murder of a man at Beech Close in Hull on Tuesday 9 March.

Hull man, Jason Joseph Clark (40) was found with fatal injuries by emergency services outside a flat on Beech Close in Hull. Jason’s family are being supported by specially trained officers and have asked for privacy at this time.

Patrick William Clayton, aged 54 and 26 year-old Patrick Sean Clayton have been charged with murder and are due to appear before Hull Magistrates Court today Friday 12 March 2021.

Assistant Chief Constable Darren Downs said: “A tragic incident of this nature has been a shock to the local community, and our neighbourhood team continue to be on hand to speak to residents about their worries. If anyone has any concerns, please talk to one of our officers.

“We would ask that anyone with any information who has not yet spoken with us to call our non-emergency number 101 quoting log 80 of 9 March 2021. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 if you wish to remain anonymous.”
