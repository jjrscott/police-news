Following two warrants in Brough by the #OpGalaxy team on Friday 19 February, two people have been arrested in connection with the supply of drugs.

A 48-year-old man and 42-year-old woman were arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply drugs.

A quantity of cash and suspected drugs were recovered and seized from the properties.

The two have been released under investigation whilst enquiries continue.
