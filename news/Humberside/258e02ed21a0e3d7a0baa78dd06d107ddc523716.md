A 40-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class A drugs, after eagle-eyed officers spotted a man acting suspiciously on Earl Street, Grimsby at 8.45pm last night (Thursday 3 December).

Wraps of what is thought to be cocaine and cash have been seized.

The man remains in our custody whilst we continue our investigation.
