On Friday two people were arrested and taken into custody following warrants served at premises under the Misuse of Drugs Act.

A house off Boothferry Road in Hull and a second in Hessle were raided on Friday 26 February and £200 worth of Class A drugs, over £17000 worth of Class B drugs, drug cultivation equipment, £1000 in cash and several weapons were seized.

Two men arrested at these properties have been released from custody under investigation as we continue our enquiries.
If you have information about drug dealing or cultivation in your area call us on our non-emergency number 101 and let us help make our communities safer for everyone.
