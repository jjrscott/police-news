Officers investigating a collision between a car and motorbike on Staithes Road, Preston which occurred just before 7.15am this morning, Tuesday 2 March, are appealing for any witnesses who have not yet spoken to us to get in touch.

It was reported that a dark silver Vauxhall Astra went onto the wrong side of the road, colliding with a motorcyclist on a blue Yamaha.

Sadly the motorcyclist was declared dead at the scene. His family are aware and are being supported by specially trained officers.

A man has been arrested in connection with the collision, including on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving. He remains in custody at this time.

A road closure remained in place until 3.30pm, the road has now reopened.

If you saw either the collision itself, the vehicles prior to the collision or anyone nearby behaving in a suspicious manner after the collision, please call our non-emergency number 101 quoting log 48 of 2 March.
