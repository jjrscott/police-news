A second man has been charged following a robbery at a shop on Wellington Street, Grimsby on Friday 20 November.

Wayne Price, 39, of Wellington Street has been charged with robbery and remanded into custody to appear in Grimsby Crown Court on Monday 21 December, following his first court appearance yesterday (Tuesday 24 November).

The first man charged has also been remanded and will be appearing alongside Price in Grimsby Crown Court.

The third man arrested in connection with the robbery has been bailed with conditions, pending further enquiries.

We would encourage anyone who has not yet spoken with us and may have information that could assist our investigation to call 101 quoting log 514 of 20 November. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 to remain anonymous.
