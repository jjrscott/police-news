On Sunday 24 January officers were made aware of a video circulating social media showing a cat being put out of a roof window.

We can confirm that the cat is back with his owner and thankfully, did not suffer any injuries.

An investigation has been launched by the RSPCA and is currently ongoing, we will support them to take the appropriate action against any identified individual/s
