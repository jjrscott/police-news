A man aged 34 has been arrested following a vehicle stop yesterday (Thursday 11 March), on Henderson Avenue, Scunthorpe.

Upon searching the vehicle officers discovered a sum of cash, and a large quantity of drugs.

The 34-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply and theft of a motor vehicle.

The man remains in custody whilst we continue with our enquiries.
