A man and woman have been arrested on suspicion of attempting to break into a Goole supermarket this morning.

We were called 4.45am today (Thursday, 19 November) following reports a window had been smashed at the Boothferry Road store and two people had been seen behaving suspiciously.

Officers attended the scene and a 43-year-old man and a 23-year-old woman were arrested. Both remain in our custody, assisting us with our enquiries.

If you have information that could assist our investigation, please call our non-emergency 101 line, quoting incident number 16/118287/20.
