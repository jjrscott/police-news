A man and a woman have been arrested in the early hours of this morning after a robbery at a petrol station on Grange Lane South in Scunthorpe.

We received a call shortly before 4am to report that a robbery had taken place and the two suspects had fled having stolen scratch cards and cash.

A man and a woman were later arrested nearby on suspicion of robbery and they remain in custody.

Anyone with information that would help with our investigation should call our non-emergency number 101 quoting reference 16/125615/20.
