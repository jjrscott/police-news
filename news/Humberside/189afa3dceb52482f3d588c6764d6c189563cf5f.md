Three men have been arrested, with two subsequently charged, after officers from our North Hull Community team, alongside partner agencies, executed a misuse of drugs act warrant on Beckside Street, Hull yesterday (Monday 21 December).

Leon Durrant (50) of Beckside Close, Hull was charged with two counts of possession with intent to supply class A drugs and assaulting a police officer. Durrant is due to appear at Hull Magistrates Court today (Tuesday 22 December).

Andrew Moodie (29) of Ryde Street, Hull was charged with assaulting a police officer and released on bail to appear at Hull Magistrates court, 11 February 2021.

The third man has been released under investigation whilst officers continue to conduct their enquiries.

Your local teams are continuing their action against drugs in your area and continue to encourage anyone with information relating to the production of cannabis, or any drug, to call 101 and report it to us.
