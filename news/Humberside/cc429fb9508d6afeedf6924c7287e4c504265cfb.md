A man and women have been charged following an alleged assault on Harley Street, Hull, on Saturday, 5 December, which resulted in a man suffering serious injuries.
Natalie Plummer, 41, of Beverley Road, Hull and James Medlam, 37, of Beverley Road, Hull have both been charged with wounding with intent to cause Grievous Bodily Harm and possession of a knife in a public place.
Both have been remanded in custody to appear at Hull Magistrates Court today (Wednesday, 9 December).
