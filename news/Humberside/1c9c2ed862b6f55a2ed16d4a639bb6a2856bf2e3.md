A man has been charged following the discovery of a suspicious item whilst executing a pre-planned warrant at an address on Market Place, Pocklington on Monday 14 December.

Linas Ponomariovas, 26, of Market Place, has been charged with making an explosive substance for an unlawful purpose and has been remanded to appear in Hull Magistrates Court today, Thursday 17 December.

A cordon and a number of officers remained in place for several hours of the day to ensure the safety of all those nearby whilst the Explosive Ordnance Disposal team assessed the item and confirmed the area safe for the public.

We want to be clear and provide reassurance that this incident is not believed to be terrorism related.
