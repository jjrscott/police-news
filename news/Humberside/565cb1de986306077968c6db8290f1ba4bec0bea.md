On Friday 5 March, a man was charged with criminal damage and possession of an air weapon with intent to cause fear of violence following an incident in Kilham, Driffield.

The 45-year-old man appeared at Hull Magistrates Court on Saturday 6 March and has been remanded to appear at Hull Crown Court at a later date.

A man was reported to have caused damage to a property whilst brandishing a weapon. Thankfully nobody was harmed.
