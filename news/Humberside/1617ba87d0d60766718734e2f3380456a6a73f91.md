A man is in custody following a warrant on Main Street in Burstwick shortly after 8.30am this morning, Friday 8 January.

#OpGalaxy officers descended on the property and discovered a quantity of cannabis, as well as samurai swords, hunting knives, a cross bow and an air rifle.

The drugs and weapons have been seized and the man remains in custody whilst we continue our enquiries.

We would encourage anyone with information about drugs or weapons in their area to contact us on our non-emergency number 101 so we can investigate and take the appropriate action.
