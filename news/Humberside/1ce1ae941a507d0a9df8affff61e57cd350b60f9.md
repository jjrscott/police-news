A local man has been charged in connection with three robberies in Bransholme, Hull reported to have occurred in the last week.

Carl Fullard, 41, of Oakington Garth was arrested on the evening on Sunday 13 December and subsequently charged with three counts of robbery and two counts of possession of a bladed article.

On Wednesday 9 December, a woman reported a man stealing her handbag containing a mobile phone and bank cards on Wickenby Garth. On Saturday 12 December a delivery driver reported a man stealing a number of parcels from his van on Hucknall Garth. Finally on Sunday 13 December, a woman reported a man getting inside her car on Goodhart Road, threatening her and stealing it. The car was later recovered.

Fullard has been remanded into custody to appear in Hull Magistrates Court today, Tuesday 15 December.
