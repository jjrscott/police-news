A further man had been arrested in connection with the reported stabbing of a man on Doncaster Street, Hull, on Saturday, 3 October.

The 19-year-old has been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder and remains in custody.

The two men, aged 38 and 48, arrested earlier this week remain in our custody assisting with our investigation and the woman has been released without charge.

The injured man remains in hospital in a serious but stable condition.

Officers are still out and about in the area to offer reassurance to the public.

Anyone with information, who has not yet spoken with us, is asked to call police on 101, or anonymously contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555111, quoting log number 516 of 3 October.
