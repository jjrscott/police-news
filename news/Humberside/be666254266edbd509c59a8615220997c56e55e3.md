Detectives have today charged 28 year-old Joshua Esberger of Cranbeck Close, Bridlington in connection with a burglary and violent assault on Stockshill Road in Scunthorpe on 16 April 2018.

The incident took place shortly after 8pm on Monday 16 April 2018. A man was working at the back of his van when he was violently assaulted, leaving him with life-changing injuries.

Following a wanted appeal, Joshua Esberger handed himself into Bridlington police station yesterday (Friday 6 November) and was today charged with Section 18 assault causing grievous bodily harm and burglary.

He has been remanded in custody to appear at Hull Magistrates Court on Monday.
