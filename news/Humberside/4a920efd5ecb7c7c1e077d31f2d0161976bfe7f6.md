Following a collision on Tuesday 24 November on Dunswell Lane, the driver of the van has sadly passed away in hospital.

The collision happened on Dunswell Lane, between Cottingham and Dunswell at around 3am on the morning of Tuesday 24 November.

We are still appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the collision, or has dash cam footage covering it, or the moments before or after, to get in touch with us on our non-emergency number 101 quoting log 27 of 24 November.
