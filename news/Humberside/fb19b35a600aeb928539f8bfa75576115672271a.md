Following a pursuit around the East Marsh area of Grimsby in the early hours of this morning, Wednesday 7 October, an 18-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of possession of an offensive weapon.

Officers on proactive patrol in the area spotted a suspicious man on Hamilton Street who was seen to be wearing a balaclava. During a short pursuit, a bag was reported to have been thrown into a garden on Mansel Street, which was discovered to contain a machete. A man was arrested close by on Oxford Street.

The man remains in custody whilst we continue our investigation.
