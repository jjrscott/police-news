Two men have been charged in connection to a number of reports of arson and criminal damage in the Yarborough area of Grimsby last month.

A 30-year-old man and 31-year-old man have been charged with conspiring to commit arson with intent to endanger life on Holly Mews, conspiring to commit arson on Chelmsford Avenue and three counts of conspiring to destroy or damage property on Chemlsford Avenue.

Both men have been remanded into custody following their first court appearances on Friday 5 and Saturday 6 February respectively.
