We are investigating a reported arson at a house on Wellowgate in Grimsby, which was reported to us at 1am yesterday (Thursday 4 February.)

A man was arrested on suspicion of arson and assault, and has been bailed with conditions whilst enquiries continues.

Detective Inspector Tom Kelly said: “Over the coming days, the community may see an increased police presence in the surrounding area of Wellowgate, as we continue our active investigation.

“Officers are there to provide reassurance and listen to any concerns.

“I want to also provide reassurance that this incident is believed to be an isolated one, with no wider threat.

“I would encourage anyone with information about the incident to contact us on our non-emergency number 101, quoting log 19 of 4 February.

“Alternatively, if you wish to remain anonymous, Crimestoppers are a completely anonymous reporting tool and can be contacted on 0800 555 111.”
