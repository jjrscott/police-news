11:01 10/06/2020

Do you know these men?

We have issued CCTV images of two men we want to speak to following two burglaries at a bar in Liverpool city centre.

The burglaries happenened at around 7.30pm on Thursday 21st May and 11.20pm on Monday 25th May at The Celtic Corner, Ranlegh Street.

Damage has been caused to the property inside on both occasions including a juke box, pool table and fruit machine.

Officers would like to speak to these two men as they may be able to assist with their enquiries.

Anyone who knows the two men or can assist with the investigation is asked to DM @MerPolCC or @CrimestoppersUK on 0800 555 111 quoting reference 20000310760.
