11:44 29/01/2021

Detectives have arrested and charged a Wirral man with drug supply conspiracy offences.

Yesterday morning, officers attended an address in Barnston and detained a man, who was taken to a police station for questioning.

49-year-old Brian Connis of Thorncroft Drive has now been charged with conspiracy to supply Class A and Class B drugs.

Connis was remanded in custody to appear at Wirral Adult Remand Court this morning, Friday 29 January.

The charges are linked to the ongoing national investigation Operation Venetic.
