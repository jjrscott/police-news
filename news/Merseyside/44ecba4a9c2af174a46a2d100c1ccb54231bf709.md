16:46 12/02/2021

We are appealing for help in tracing a 12-year-old boy who is missing from home in St Helens.

Jack Cliffe was last seen in the Doulton Street area of St Helens at around 12.30pm on Tuesday 9 February.

He is described as a white male, 5ft 3in tall, of stocky build with short dark hair and a pale completion.

When last seen he was wearing a grey baseball cap, black North Face hoodie, black tracksuit bottoms and trainers. He also has a gold and black BMX bike with the word 'voodoo' written on it.

He is known to frequent the St Helens and Warrington areas.

Anyone who has seen him or knows of his whereabouts is asked to DM @MerPolCC, call 101 or contact @missingpeople on 116 000.
