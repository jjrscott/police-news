11:54 02/02/2021

Officers recovered three bullets during an open land search yesterday (Monday 1 February) in the area of Marsden Street in Kensington as part of our ongoing investigations into gun crime on Merseyside.

Officers are responding to intelligence in relation to the use and storage of weapons as part of our commitment to remove guns from our streets.

Anyone who has information about who is using or storing guns in their area or has any information which could assist with our enquiries is asked to DM @MerPolCC or contact @CrimestoppersUK anonymously on 0800 555 111.
