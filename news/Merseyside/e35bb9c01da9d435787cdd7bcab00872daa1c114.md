11:42 10/07/2020

Sefton Coroner's Office is appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a man who recently died in Seaforth.

79-year-old Joseph Andrew Gordon Gaskell passed away at his home address on Wednesday, 8 July.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who knows of Mr Gaskell's next-of-kin or can assist is asked to contact Coroners Investigation Officer Alby Howard-Murphy on 0151 777 3481.
