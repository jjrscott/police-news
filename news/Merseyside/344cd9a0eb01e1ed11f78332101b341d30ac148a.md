We are appealing for help in finding the next of kin of a 72 year-old man who sadly passed away at his home in Allerton.

John Evans sadly passed away at his home address in Booker Avenue on Monday 5th October.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who knows of Mr Evans' next of kin or can assist is asked to contact Coroner's Investigation Officer Lisa McElvogue on 0151 233 0128 or email lisa.mcelvogue@liverpool.gov.uk
