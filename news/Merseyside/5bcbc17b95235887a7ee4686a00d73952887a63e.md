11:54 30/01/2020

We can confirm a 65-year-old Wirral man has been charged with a number of sex offences against children.

Trevor Ainslie of Berrylands Close, Moreton, has been charged with 17 offences including 12 counts of indecent assault on a boy under 14, two counts of indecent assault on a boy under 16, gross indecency with a boy under 14, and two counts of inciting a child under 16 to commit an act of gross indecency.

All charges relate to offences which occurred between 1976 - 2004.

Ainslie will next appear at Wirral Magistrates Court on Wednesday, 19 February 2020.
