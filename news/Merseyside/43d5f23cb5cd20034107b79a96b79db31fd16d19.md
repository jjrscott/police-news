15:16 08/04/2020

Detectives investigating the murder of Hassan Haadi have charged a man in connection with the offence.

24-year-old Elvis Duruaku of Barker Avenue North, Sandiacre, Nottingham was charged with murder in the early hours of this morning, Wednesday 8 April.

Duruaku will appeared at Liverpool Magistrates Court this morning and was remanded in custody to next appear at Liverpool Crown Court on Thursday 14 May.

We continue to encourage anyone with information to send it via the following link: https://public.digital-policing.co.uk/merseyside/appeal/police-appeal-murder-investigation
