09:51 14/05/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a 72 year-old man.

John Payne, who was born in Liverpool but lived in the Formby area, passed away at his home on Monday 11th May.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Mr Payne wasn't married and didn't have any children but is known to have had a brother called Roger who sadly died in the Rhyl area of North Wales eight years ago.

Anyone who is able to offer any contact details for Mr Payne's next of kin are asked to contact, coroners investigating officer, Clair Sajewicz by calling 0151 777 3754 or via email at Sefton.Coroners.Office@merseyside.police.uk
