Can you help Wirral Coroner's Office to find the next of kin of a Wirral women who recently passed away?

Ann Georgina Lee, aged 70, was sadly found deceased last Thursday, 13 February at her home in Bebington Road, Rock Ferry. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Ann may have a niece and nephew but their whereabouts are unknown.
