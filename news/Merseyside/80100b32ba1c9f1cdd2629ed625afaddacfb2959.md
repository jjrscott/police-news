12:47 26/03/2020

Merseyside Police have charged a 27 year old man from St Helens with multiple offences following an altercation earlier this week.

Adam Pendlebury, of Bruce Street, was arrested after the incident and charged with affray, criminal damage, threatening a person with a bladed article and possession of a controlled drug.

He will appear in court on 21st April. #OpTarget
