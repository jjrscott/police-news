Can you help Sefton Coroner's Office find the next of kin of a Waterloo man who recently passed away?

55-year-old Paul Flannery sadly died on Friday 29 October at his home address on Oxford Road. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Paul is believed to have a sister in Liverpool.

If you have any information, please contact James Martindale via James.Martindale@Sefton.gov.uk or call 0151 777 3530.
