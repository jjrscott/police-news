16:11 17/10/2019

Liverpool & Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for help to trace the next of kin of a man who recently died at Whiston Hospital.

Neil Lloyd, aged 60, of Hunts Cross Avenue, Woolton died in hospital on 9 October.

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone with information to help trace his next of kin is asked to contact Coroners Investigating Officer Jackie Horner on 0151 777 2606 or Jacqueline.A.Horner@merseyside.police.uk.
