09:27 30/06/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a 77 year-old Wirral man who has died.

Peter John Chambers sadly passed away at his home address in Hoylake on Sunday 28th June.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who knows of the whereabouts of Mr Chambers' family is asked to contact Coroners investigation officer Elise Fahey on 0151 233 0132 or email elise.fahey@liverpool.gov.uk
