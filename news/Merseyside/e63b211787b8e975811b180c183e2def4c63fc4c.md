20:00 05/02/2020

We can confirm that a 24-year-old man has today, Wednesday 5th February, been charged with the murder of a 22 month-old boy.

Jonathan Simpson, of no fixed abode, but originally from the Winsford area of Cheshire, was charged with the murder of Jacob Marshall.

Jacob was taken to hospital from an address in Belsford Way in Speke on the afternoon of Friday 12th July 2019. He later passed away in hospital.

Simpson is due to appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court tomorrow, Thursday 6th February.
