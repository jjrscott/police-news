11:56 23/12/2020

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for information to help trace the next of kin of a man who recently died in Liverpool.

Kenneth McCarrick, aged 59, sadly died at his accommodation address in Strathmore Road on Tuesday, 22 December.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information to help trace his next of kin is asked to contact Coroner's Officer Lesley-Anne Rogers on 0151 233 0137 or Lesley-Anne.Rogers@liverpool.gov.uk.
