12:55 24/11/2020

Can you help? Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is looking to find the next of kin of a Wallasey woman who sadly recently passed away.

Irene Clare, nee Irene Jones, 66, sadly died at home in Falkland Road, Wallasey on Saturday 21 November. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone with information that may help locate Mrs Clare's next of kin can call Coroners Investigation Officer Jacqueline Gallagher on 0151 233 0143 or email jacqueline.gallagher4@liverpool.gov.uk or hmcoroner@liverpool.gov.uk.
