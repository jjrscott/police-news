16:55 26/02/2021

Have you seen this woman? We're urgently appealing for the public's help in finding missing Helen Evans, also known as Pat.

Helen, aged 78, was last seen in Chetwynd Road, Prenton at around 3pm today, Friday, 26 February.

She is described as a white female, around 5ft 4in tall, of medium build, with grey hair.

When last seen, she wearing a powder blue jacket, a blue jumper, black trousers and black trainers.

Helen is known to frequent the Parkhill Road area of Toxteth and areas of Wirral.

Please contact 999 if you see Helen, or pass any info urgently to @MerPolCC or 101 or via Missing People on 116 000.
