Wanted

05:00 12/12/2020

Do you know this man? Lee O'Shea, 28, from West Derby is wanted on recall to prison after breaching his licence conditions.

He was released from on 3 August 2020 after serving part of a seven years and 10 months sentence for aggravated vehicle taking.

Extensive enquiries are ongoing to trace him.

O'Shea is white, 5ft 6in tall, of slim build, with brown hair and blue eyes. He speaks with a Liverpool accent and has a scar on his lip and forearm.

We will be issuing one wanted appeal per day for the next 12 days to support the work of our Force Fugitive Team, so help us track these people down before Christmas.

The public are warned not to approach O'Shea, and anyone with information on his whereabouts or any of our wanted people is asked to contact @MerPolCC or 101 with reference 20000640416.

You can also pass information anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. You can also contact them via their online form at: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/give-information/give-information.
