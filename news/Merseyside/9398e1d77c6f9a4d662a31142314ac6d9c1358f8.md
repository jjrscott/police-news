09:06 25/11/2020

Can you help? Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is looking to find the next of kin of a Wirral man who sadly recently passed away.

Alan Scott, 78, sadly died at home in Sunfield Road, Moreton on Friday 20 November. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone with information that may help locate Mr Scott's next of kin can call Coroners Investigation Officer Liam Moss on 0151 233 0125 or email liam.moss1@liverpool.gov.uk.
