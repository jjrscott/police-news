12:45 20/08/2020

Detectives investigating a stabbing Liverpool City Centre at the weekend have arrested three males and are continuing to appeal for information.

At around 11.30am, officers were called to Paradise Street, Liverpool to reports of a stabbing. A 25-year-old man was taken to hospital. He has since been discharged.

A total of three arrests have now been made, as follows:

A 21-year-old man from Runcorn was arrested on suspicion of affray and released on conditional bail.
A 14-year-old boy from Huyton was arrested on suspicion of Section 18 wounding and released on conditional bail.
A 16-year-old boy from Walton was arrested on suspicion of affray and released on conditional bail.

Enquiries continue, so if you have any information, CCTV or other images, please contact us @MerPolCC, 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously with reference 20000488802.
