10:46 30/03/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a 55 year-old man from Bootle.

Robin James Connolly, who was from the Milton Street area, died at his home on Saturday 14th March. There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed Mr Connolly had three daughters, one who is believed to reside in the Southport area by the name of Sally.

it is believed his other two daughters, Michelle and Georgina, live in the south of England.

Anyone with contact details for the daughters are asked to contact the Southport Coroners Office, Coroners Officer, Clair Sajewicz on 0151 777 3754 or via email Clair.S.Sajewicz@merseyside.police.uk
