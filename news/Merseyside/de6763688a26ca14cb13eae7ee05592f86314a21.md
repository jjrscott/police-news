Wanted

05:00 19/12/2020

Do you know this man? Scott Grace, 32, from Liverpool is wanted on recall to prison.

He was given a six-year prison sentence for supply of drugs at Chester Crown Court on 15 October 2014, before being released on license on 11 April 2019.

He has been wanted on prison recall since 15 August 2019, and extensive enquiries have been ongoing to trace him since.

Grace is white of medium build, 5ft 9in tall, has blue eyes, short brown hair, and speaks with a Liverpool accent.

He has connections to South Liverpool, Cheshire, Wigan and Plymouth.

We are issuing one wanted appeal per day for the next 12 days to support the work of our Force Fugitive Team, so help us track these people down before Christmas.
