Please share and help us find two missing teenagers from Aigburth who are missing from home.

16-year-old Vuong Voy Vo (top) and 17-year-old Thinh Hung Ho (bottom) were last seen on Wednesday 29 April.

Vuong Voy Vo is Asian, 16 years old, 5ft 2in tall, of medium build, with short black straight hair. When last seen, he was wearing black tracksuit pants and a black t-shirt. He has a dragon tattoo on his left shoulder/bicep.

Thinh Hung Ho is Asian, 17 years old, 5ft 10in tall, of slim build, with short dark brown hair and a moustache. When last seen, he was wearing black tracksuit pants and a black long sleeved top.

They both have links to the Bootle area of Sefton.

Anyone who has seen them or has any information as asked to contact @MerPolCC, 101 or Missing People on 116 000.
