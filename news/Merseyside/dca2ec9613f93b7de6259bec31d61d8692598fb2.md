11:33 22/10/2019

Can you help? Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing to find the family of Mr Roy Sanders, aged 80, of Wellgreen Road, Childwall.

Mr Sanders sadly died in Liverpool on Monday 15 October 2019. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone who can help the Coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call Katy Porter on 0151 233 0130 or email katy.porter@liverpool.gov.uk.
