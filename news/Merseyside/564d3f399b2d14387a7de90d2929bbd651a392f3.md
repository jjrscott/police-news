15:00 15/12/2020

Detectives investigating the murder of 18-year-old Kyle Whitley in April have arrested another male on suspicion of murder yesterday, Monday 14 December.

Kyle was fatally stabbed in Vincent Road, Litherland on the afternoon of Monday 13 April.

Following continued enquiries into the murder, a 17-year-old male from St Helens was last night arrested on suspicion of murder and possession with intent to supply Class A and B drugs. He has been taken to a police station for questioning.

To date five people have been arrested in connection with Kyle's murder, three of whom remain under investigation.

Anyone with information on the murder of Kyle is asked to DM @MerPolCC or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting reference 20000215637.
