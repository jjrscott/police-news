15:40 08/03/2021

We can confirm the 26-year-old woman injured in a collision in Huyton on 1 March has sadly died.

Emergency services attended Court Hey Road, Huyton (Monday 1 March) following the incident.

At around 12.45pm it was reported that an off road bike was in collision with a pedestrian.

The pedestrian was treated for serious injuries at the scene, and taken to hospital for further treatment. She sadly died from her injuries on Friday 5 March.

Her next of kin have been informed and are being supported by officers.

The rider of the bike, a 29-year-old man, was taken to hospital where he remains receiving treatment. His condition is described as serious but stable.

CCTV and witness enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone who saw the incident or was passing and has any dashcam footage or information is asked to contact us via @MerPolCC on Twitter with reference 21000132231.
