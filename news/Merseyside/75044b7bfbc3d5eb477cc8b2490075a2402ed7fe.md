10:21 10/12/2020

Two men have been charged with Production of a Controlled Drug after a large cannabis farm was discovered on Newstet Road, Kirkby on Tuesday afternoon.

Dawid Ryba, 21 and Tomasz Wisniewski, 24, both of Doncaster Road, Leicester will appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court today (10th December).

Anyone that knows of other people involved in the supply of distribution of any drug, should get in touch @MerPolCC or by contacting @CrimestoppersUK anonymously.
