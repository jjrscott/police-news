09:43 16/12/2019

Sefton, St Helens and Knowsley Coroner's Office is appealing for the family of a woman from Southport who recently passed away to come forward.

Barbara Harvey, aged 70, of Scarisbrick Street, sadly died on Saturday, 30 November this year.

Her death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who can help the coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call the Coroner's Office on 0151 777 3480.
