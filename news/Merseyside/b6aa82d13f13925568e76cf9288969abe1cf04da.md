14:26 29/01/2020

We are issuing the image of a man and woman who may have information after a theft from a vehicle in Stoneycroft and fraud in Walton earlier this month.

On Saturday 18 January, items including bank cards and electrical items were stolen from a car parked outside a home on Baden Road.

An attempt was made to use a bank card later the same morning at Asda in Utting Avenue, Walton.

CCTV and witness enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone who recognises those pictured or has any information is asked to contact @MerPolCC, 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, with reference number 20000037960.
