11:47 18/06/2020

We have charged a 31 year-old man with assault, burglary, criminal damage and warrant offences.

Gary Stanley, of no fixed abode but originally from the Walton area, was arrested in the Burscough area of Lancashire yesterday by officers from Merseyside Police's Fugitive Team.

Stanley has been charged with a number of offences including s39 assault, burglary, harassment with violence, possession of Class B controlled drug, two counts of criminal damage and two warrant offences.

He was remanded into custody to appear at Liverpool magistrates court today, Thursday 18th June.
