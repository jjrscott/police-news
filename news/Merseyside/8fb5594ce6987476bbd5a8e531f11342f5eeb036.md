10:44 23/10/2020

We're appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a man who recently died in Southport.

Gary Waterhouse, aged 57, sadly died at Ascot Nursing Home, Chambers Road, Southport on Monday, 5 October.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information to help trace Mr Waterhouse's next of kin is asked to contact 0151 934 4737.
