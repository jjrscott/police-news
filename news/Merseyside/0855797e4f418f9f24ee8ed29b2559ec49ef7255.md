14:08 30/04/2020

We have charged a 40 year-old Knowsley man with being concerned with the supply of Class A drugs.

Officers on patrol in Olney Street, Walton, yesterday morning, Wednesday 29th April, spotted two males acting suspiciously.

The males were searched and cash and two mobile phones were recovered. Searches were also carried out at a number of addresses and cash and suspected Class A drugs were recovered.

Kevin Cook, of Roughwood Drive, Kirkby, was also charged with possession of criminal property and remanded into custody to appear at Liverpool magistrates court today, Thursday 30th April.

A 44 year-old man, also arrested on suspicion of being concerned with the supply of Class A drugs was released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Anyone who has information about drugs or drug supply in their area is asked to DM @MerPolCC or contact @Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
