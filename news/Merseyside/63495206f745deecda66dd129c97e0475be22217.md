14:30 03/06/2020

Detectives investigating the fatal stabbing of 18 year-old Kyle Whitley in Litherland in April have arrested two people.

A 19 year-old woman and a 20 year-old man for the Litherland area were arrested yesterday on suspicion of conspiracy to murder. They have both been released under investigation as enquiries continue.

Three people previously arrested in connection with the murder have all been released on conditional bail.

We are continuing to appeal for anyone who witnessed the incident, which took place on Vincent Road, at 2.30pm, to contact detectives as they may have information which is vital to the investigation.

Anyone with information is urged to get in touch by DM @MerPolCC, calling 101 or reporting anonymously via @CrimestoppersUK on 0800 555 111 quoting log 378 of 13th April.
