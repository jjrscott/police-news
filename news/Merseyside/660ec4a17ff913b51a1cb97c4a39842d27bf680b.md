16:19 23/11/2020

Please share and help Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office find the next of kin of a New Brighton man who recently died.

David Charles Smith sadly passed away at his home in Seymour Street last Thursday, 19 November. He was 73. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone with information to help trace Mr Smith's next of kin is urged to contact Karen Gregory on (0151) 233 0989 or email Karen.Gregory@liverpool.gov.uk.
