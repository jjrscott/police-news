12:00 22/04/2020

Can you help? Liverpool Coroner's Office is looking to find the next of kin of a Toxteth man who recently passed away.

65-year-old Martin Kelvin Cummins lived on Princes Road and sadly passed away on Saturday 4 April at hospital. His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Please call Coroner's Investigation Officer Lisa McElvogue on (0151) 233 0128 or email Lisa.McElvogue@liverpool.gov.uk.
