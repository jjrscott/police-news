14:03 18/11/2020

Officers on the Wirral have arrested and charged a 23 year old man for a burglary offence earlier this year.

Dominic McHugh, 23, of Parkend Road, Tranmere was arrested earlier this week on suspicion of a burglary which took place on 15th September.

He was taken to custody and later charged with the offence. He appeared at court on Monday 16th November where he was remanded until 14th December. **#OpTarget**
