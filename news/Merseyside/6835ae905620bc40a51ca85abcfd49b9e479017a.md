15:24 20/02/2020

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for help in find the family of Claire Gilbert, of River Avon Street, Toxteth.

Miss Gilbert, 43, sadly died in Liverpool on Wednesday 19th February2020. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone who can help the Coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call Katy Porter 0151 233 0130 or email katy.porter@liverpool.gov.uk.
