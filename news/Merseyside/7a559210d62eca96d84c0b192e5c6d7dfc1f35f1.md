21:22 27/03/2020

Detectives investigating an absconded prisoner from Aintree University Hospital on Wednesday, 25 March have charged a man and woman in connection with the incident.

30-year-old Patrick Garrett of Vescock Street, Liverpool has been charged with escaping from lawful custody and has been remanded in custody to appear at South Sefton Magistrates Court tomorrow, Saturday, 28 March.

22-year-old Elle Lavelle of Vescock Street, Liverpool has been charged with assisting an escaped prisoner and possessing identity documents with intent. She has been released on bail.
