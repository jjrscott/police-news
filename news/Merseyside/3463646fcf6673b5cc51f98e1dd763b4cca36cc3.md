13:46 08/12/2020

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for information to help trace the next of kin of a man who recently died in Upton.

William Leathwood, aged 75, sadly died at his home address in Moreton Road on Monday, 7 December.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information that may help trace Mr Leathwood's next of kin is asked to contact Coroners Officer Lea Cripps on 0151 233 0150.
