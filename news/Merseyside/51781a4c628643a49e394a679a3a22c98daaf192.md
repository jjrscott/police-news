16:54 13/07/2020

Have you seen this man? We are appealing for help in tracing Richard Caswell who is wanted on recall to prison.

Caswell, 38, from the Kirkdale area, is wanted for breaching the terms of his license after he was released from prison.

He is described as a white male, of medium build, with brown hair and blue eyes.

He is known the frequent areas of north Liverpool, Manchester and London. He sometimes goes by the name of Will Caswell.

Members of the public are advised not to approach Caswell directly but to call 999 if they see him.

Anyone with information on his whereabouts is asked to contact @MerPolCC, 101 quoting ref. 20000372636 or Crimestoppers, anonymously on 0800 555 111.
