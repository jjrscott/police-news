10:57 20/09/2019

Detectives have charged a man with firearms offences following the search of a flat in Huyton on Wednesday, 18 September 2019.

At around 3pm, officers carried out the warrant at the property on Primrose Drive.

Officers forensically recovered two pistols and ammunition during the search.

49-year-old Alan Mallon from Primrose Drive has been charged with two counts of possession of a firearm and one count of possessing ammunition for a firearm without a certificate.

Mallon was kept in custody to appear at Bootle Magistrates Court this morning, Friday 20 September.

If you suspect that firearms or other weapons are being stored in your community, contact us @MerPolCC or you can pass information anonymously via the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
