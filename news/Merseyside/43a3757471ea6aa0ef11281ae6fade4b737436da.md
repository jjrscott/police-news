10:53 20/11/2020

We have charged a 32 year-old man with firearms offences.

Wesley Seddon, of Lance Close, Everton, has been charged with possessing a firearm when prohibited for life and possessing a shortened shotgun.

He was remanded into custody to appear before Liverpool magistrates court today, Friday 20th November.
