10:24 20/09/2019

Detectives working on Operation Castle, our ongoing response to burglary across Merseyside, have charged a man in connection with an ongoing investigation.

53-year-old Antonio Hall from Longmoor Lane in Fazakerley was charged with conspiracy to commit burglary.

The charge refers to an ongoing investigation into burglary offences between March and August across Merseyside.

Hall has been remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court this morning, Friday 20 September.
