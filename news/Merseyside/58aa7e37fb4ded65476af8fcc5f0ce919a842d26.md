16:28 10/05/2020

Emergency services are currently in attendance of Upper Duke Street, Liverpool this afternoon (10th May) following reports of a road traffic collision.

It was reported that a Police vehicle and a motorcycle were involved in the collision at around 2:15pm.

The motorcyclist is said to be in a serious condition and has been taken to hospital.

Road closures are in place on Duke St, Upper Duke St, Roscoe St, Great George St, Berry and Leece St and Hardman St. Please avoid the are if you are making any essential journeys.

The incident will be referred to the IOPC for investigation.

Anyone that witnessed the incident is advised to contact us quoting log 420 of 10th May 2020 **.**
