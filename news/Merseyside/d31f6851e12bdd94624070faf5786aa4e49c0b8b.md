10:54 02/03/2021

We are appealing on behalf of Liverpool Coroner's office to find the next of kin of a 63 year-old man who sadly died in Wirral.

Roger Paul Crabtree died in Spital on Monday 22nd February. There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death. Mr Crabtree had been living in Chester prior to his death and had previously lived in Oldham.

Anyone who can help is asked to contact Coroner's Investigation Officer Lisa McElvogue on 0151 233 0128.
