Updated News Post

Following the arrest of seven people on Wednesday, 10 March as part of a Project Medusa operation into County Lines, three people have been charged.

Brady Cole, 31 of East Lane, Runcorn has been charged with conspiracy to supply Class A drugs and will appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court.

Daniel Bullock 31 years of Raleigh Street, Barrow in Furness and Tony Miller 34 years of Annan Street, Barrow in Furness have been charged with conspiracy to supply Class A drugs, they will appear at Furness and District Mags, Barrow.

Four others arrested in connection with this investigation have been released under investigation.
