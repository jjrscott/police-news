07:59 06/03/2020

Can you help us find the next of a kin of a 64 year-old man who passed away in hospital?

George Caldwell, of White Rock Street, Everton, died at the Royal Liverpool Hospital on Friday 28th February.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed that Mr Caldwell may have had family living in the Kirkby area.

Anyone with information should contact Coroner's investigation officer Elaine Jordan at Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office on 0151 233 0124 or via email Elaine.Jordan@liverpool.gov.uk
