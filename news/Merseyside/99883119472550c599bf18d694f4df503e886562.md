17:43 14/10/2019

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a 64-year-old man from Wirral.

Stephen Lawrence Kinealy passed away at his home address in Melrose Avenue, Hoylake on Tuesday, 8 October.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who knows of the whereabouts of the next of kin of Mr Kinealy is asked to contact Coroner's Investigation Officer James Martindale on 0151 233 0129 or james.l.martindale@liverpool.gov.uk.
