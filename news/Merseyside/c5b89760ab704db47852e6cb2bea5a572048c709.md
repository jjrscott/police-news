10:53 14/12/2020

We have charged a 41 year-old man with burglary and escaping lawful custody.

Officers were called to a property in Court Hey Avenue in Huyton on Friday 11th December following reports of a burglary. A male was detained by officers nearby.

Michael Trevillion, of Barkbeth Road, Huyton, has been charged with burglary dwelling.

He has also been charged with escaping lawful custody in relation to an incident in Whiston on Saturday 12th December.

He was remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool magistrates court today, Monday 14th December.
