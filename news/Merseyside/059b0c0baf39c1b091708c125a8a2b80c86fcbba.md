10:51 13/07/2020

Officers have arrested another man following disorder in Liverpool City Centre on Friday 26th June.

On Friday 26 June, several thousand people attended the Pier Head, and a number of criminal offences were reported, including drug offences.

An extensive investigation is ongoing and as part of those enquiries, a man, aged 23 Liverpool was arrested on suspicion of theft on Sunday, 12th July. He was taken to custody and has now been released under investigation, along with the 24 year old man that was arrested on Friday 10th July.

We would like to speak to the man pictured and urge the public to get in touch if they recognise him.

If you have any information that could help with our continued investigation, please contact us using email: Operation.Precinct@merseyside.police.uk, and send any images and video via: https://unitedkingdom1cpp-portal.digital-policing.co.uk/merseyside/appeal/20000379360.
