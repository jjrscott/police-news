14:18 24/02/2020

We are appealing for help in finding the next-of-kin of a 59 year-old man who passed away at his home in Liverpool.

Andrew Lewis McKenna, passed away at St Anne's Court, on Cheadle Avenue, Old Swan, on Sunday 23rd February.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed Mr McKenna may have had a brother who lives in the Scarborough area.

Anyone who knows the next-of-kin or has any information which could assist is asked to contact HMCoroner@liverpool.gov.uk or contact the coroner's investigation officer on 0151 233 0143.
