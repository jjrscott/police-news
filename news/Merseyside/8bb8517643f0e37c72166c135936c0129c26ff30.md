16:23 15/01/2021

Detectives investigating a burglary at a shop in Anfield have issued a CCTV image of a man they believe may have information to assist enquiries.

It was reported that in the early hours of Thursday, 26 November 2020, unknown offenders forced entry to the Co-op, Utting Avenue, before making off with a quantity of cash and tobacco.

An investigation is underway and detectives are keen to speak to anyone with information.

Detective Inspector Jennie Beck said: "We are particularly keen to speak to the man pictured, as we believe he may have vital information to assist the investigation, and would ask him, or anyone who recognises him, to please come forward.

"Likewise if you have any CCTV or dashcam footage from the area, or any further information which could assist our enquiries, please let us know."

Anyone with information is asked to contact @MerPolCC, 101 quoting ref. 20000718484 or Crimestoppers, anonymously on 0800 555 111.
