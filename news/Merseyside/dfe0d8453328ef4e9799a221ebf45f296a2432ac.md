Please share and help Liverpool Coroner's Office find the next of kin of a Walton man who recently passed away.

68-year-old Phillip Kelly was sadly found deceased at his home on Utting Avenue on Monday 26 October.

There are no suspicious circumstances around his death.

Mr Kelly's next of kin or anyone who holds information is asked to call (0151) 233 0143, email Jacqueline.Gallagher4@liverpool.gov.uk or HMCoroner@liverpool.gov.uk.
