08:30 04/02/2020

We're issuing the image of a mountain bike recently stolen from an address in West Derby.

Between 10pm on Friday 31 January and 10am on Saturday 1 February, access was forced to a shed at an address in Stalisfield Avenue and a Scott Aspect 680 mountain bike was stolen (pictured).

Enquiries are ongoing to reunite the bike with its owner.

If you have seen or been offered this bike, saw anyone acting suspiciously in the area in recent days, or captured anything on home CCTV or dashcam, let us know via@MerPolCC, 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
