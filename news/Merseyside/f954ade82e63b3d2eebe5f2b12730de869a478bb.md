11:06 22/10/2019

Detectives have charged a man with firearms offences following the search of a property in Kensington on Thursday, 17 October 2019.

At around 12pm, officers carried out the warrant at the property on Minto Close.

Officers forensically recovered one pistol during the search.

25-year-old Thomas Jennings from Alexander Walk, Walton, has been charged with possession of a firearm and ammunition without a certificate.

Jennings was remanded into custody to appear at Liverpool Crown Court on 18 November.

If you suspect that firearms or other weapons are being stored in your community, contact us @MerPolCC or you can pass information anonymously via the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
