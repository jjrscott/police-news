11:23 28/10/2020

We're appealing for information following a serious injury road traffic collision in Maghull last night, Tuesday 27 October.

At around 10.40pm, emergency services were called to the junction of Northway and Eastway to reports of a collision involving a Range Rover and a Mercedes.

A passenger from the Mercedes, a 7-year-old girl, was taken to hospital with serious head injuries. She remains in a critical but stable condition. The drivers of both vehicles received minor injuries.

Road closures were in place for several hours as initial investigations were carried out. Enquiries are ongoing.

If you saw this incident or have any dashcam footage which may be of assistance, please contact our Roads Policing Unit on (0151) 7775747, or pass information via our social media desk @MerPolCC on Twitter, or 'Merseyside Police Contact Centre' on Facebook, with reference 200000652389.
