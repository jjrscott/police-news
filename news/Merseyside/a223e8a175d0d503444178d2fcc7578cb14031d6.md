13:24 01/06/2020

We can confirm that enquiries are ongoing following a fail to stop collision in Eccleston on Saturday, 30 May.

At around 6.30pm, officers were called to reports of a collision between a Volvo and a stationary Nissan Qashqui on Hewitt Avenue. Damage was caused to the Nissan and the Volvo failed to stop at the scene.

Enquiries are ongoing to locate the occupants of this car.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has home CCTV or dashcam can contact us via our social media desk on Twitter @MerPolCC or Facebook 'Merseyside Police Contact Centre', with reference 20000313002.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, or via their online form at: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/give-information/give-information.
