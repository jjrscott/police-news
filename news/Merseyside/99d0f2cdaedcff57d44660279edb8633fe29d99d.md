18:42 18/02/2020

We can confirm that emergency services are in Seaforth following a serious RTC this evening, Tuesday 18 February.

At 5.50pm, we were called to reports of a collision between a bus and a female pedestrian at the junction of Church Road and Seaforth Road.

The pedestrian was taken by ambulance to hospital in a critical condition. The bus driver stopped to assist with enquiries.

Officers remain on the scene to carry out an initial investigation and a road closure is in place in both directions. Please avoid the area if possible.

If you witnessed this incident, please call (0151) 7775747 with reference 743, or you can pass info via @MerPolCC or 101.
