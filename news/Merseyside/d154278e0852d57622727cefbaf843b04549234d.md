A 21 year-old Liverpool man has been given a three year football banning order after being found guilty of a public order offence ahead of the Liverpool v Everton fixture on 2nd December last year.

Everton fan John O'Brien, of St Domingo Road, Liverpool, was heard shouting offensive comments prior to the derby game.
