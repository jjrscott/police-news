13:52 25/02/2020

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing to find the family of Mr Ronald Crawford, of Stonedale Crescent, Croxteth.

Mr Crawford, 86, sadly died in Liverpool on Saturday 22nd February 2020.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who can help the Coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call Katy Porter on 0151 233 0130 or email katy.porter@liverpool.gov.uk.
