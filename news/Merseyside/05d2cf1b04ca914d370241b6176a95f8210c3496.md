11:14 04/09/2020

We have charged three men with violent disorder following an incident close to Anfield football ground on the evening of Wednesday 22nd July.

At approximately 11.25pm violence broke out in the area around Walton Breck Road.

An investigation was carried out and a number of warrants were executed yesterday.

William Duggan, 21, of Broadfield Avenue, Beechwood, Birkenhead; Jake Robinson, aged 20, of Glenfield Close, Beechwood, Birkenhead and Jake Duffy, aged 20, of Arley Close, Beechwood, Birkenhead, have been charged with violent disorder.

All three were remanded in custody to appear in court this morning, Friday 4th September.
