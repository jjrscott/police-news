Please share and help us find missing teenager Frances Lewis from Old Swan.

15-year-old Frances was last seen on Tuesday 15 December in the Old Swan area. Frances is white, 5ft 3in tall, slim, with shoulder length dark brown hair in a ponytail, and brown eyes. She was wearing and black and white snakeskin full outfit, black leather jacket, black puffer coat and black trainers.

Frances is known to frequent the Speke Retail Park and Anfield areas.

Please call 101 with any sightings or information, or contact us via @MerPolCC on Twitter, 'Merseyside Police Contact Centre' on Facebook, or via the charity Missing People on 116 000.
