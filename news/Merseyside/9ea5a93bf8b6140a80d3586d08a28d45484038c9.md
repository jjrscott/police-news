12:11 25/03/2020

 We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a man who recently died in Aigburth.

54-year-old John James Gallagher, passed away at his home address in Alwyn Close on Thursday, 19 March.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information to help trace Mr Gallagher's next of kin is asked to contact Coroner's Investigation Officer, Simon Knuckey at simon.knuckey@liverpool.gov.uk.
