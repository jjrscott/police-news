12:23 18/03/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of an 83 year-old man who passed away at home in Woolton.

Kenneth Shakeshaft, passed away at his home in Dovercroft.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed Mr Shakeshaft has a nephew called David Shakeshaft.

Anyone who can help is asked to contact Coroner's Investigation Officer Nicola Griffiths on 0151 233 0143 or email Nicola.griffiths@liverpool.gov.uk
