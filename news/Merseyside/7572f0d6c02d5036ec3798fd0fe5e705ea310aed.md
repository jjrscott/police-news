12:21 27/05/2020

We have charged a 44 year-old man with assault, criminal damage and racially aggravated public order following an incident in Wirral.

Officers were called to a house in Clover Drive in Birkenhead, at 12.05am on Tuesday 26th May, to reports of a disturbance. A man was detained at the scene.

Robert Bowe, 44, of Clover Drive, has been charged with assaulting an emergency worker x 4, criminal damage and racially aggravated public order.

He was remanded in custody to appear before Wirral magistrates court today, Wednesday 27th May.
