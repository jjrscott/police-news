21:32 17/02/2020

A 36-year-old man from Wavertree will be questioned by detectives after handing himself in at St Anne Street police station in possession of a handgun and ammunition.

When officers searched his bag they found an automatic Colt handgun containing a number of rounds of ammunition.

During a subsequent search of his home officers also found a knife and crossbow, which have been seized.
