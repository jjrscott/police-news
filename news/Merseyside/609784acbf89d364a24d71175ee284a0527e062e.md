10:49 23/03/2020

Merseyside Police have charged a 21 year old man from Bootle following a warrant last week (19th March).

Sonny Teaney, 21 of Hawthorne Drive, Kirkby was charged with possession of a firearm, possession of ammunition, domestic assault, assault and threatening a person with a blade/ sharply pointed article in public place.

He appeared at Sefton Magistrates Court on 20th March 2020 and has been remanded in custody until a later date. #OpTarget
