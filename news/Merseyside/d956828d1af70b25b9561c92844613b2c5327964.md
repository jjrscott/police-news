17:50 17/09/2019

Liverpool & Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for help to trace the next of kin of a man who recently died in Wirral.

David Charles Halton, aged 77, died at his home address in Kendal Road, Poulton today (Tuesday 17 September).

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone with information to help trace his next of kin is asked to contact Coroners Investigating Officer Lisa McElvogue on 0151 233 0128 or Lisa.McElvogue@liverpool.gov.uk
