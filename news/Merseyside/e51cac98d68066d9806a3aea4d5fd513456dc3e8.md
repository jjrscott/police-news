10:08 04/12/2020

The family of Edward 'Ted' Bevan, 77, who sadly died following an RTC earlier this week in Bebington, Wirral, have issued this short tribute:

"Today is a very sad and devastating day for our family. Our dad set off to walk into town & never came back. He was a real family man. A loving husband, dad & grandad and will be very sorely missed."

The collision happened at around 12.45pm on Wednesday, 2 December, on Broadway at the junction with Kings Road.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has CCTV or dashcam is asked to contact the Roads Policing Unit on 0151 777 5747, @MerPolCC or 101 quoting reference 20000732506.
