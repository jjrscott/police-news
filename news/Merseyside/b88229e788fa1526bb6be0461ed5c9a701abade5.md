17:46 01/03/2021

Emergency services are in attendance on Court Hey Road, Huyton this afternoon (Monday 1 March) following a road traffic collision.

At around 12.45pm it was reported that an off road bike was in collision with a pedestrian.

The pedestrian, a woman believed to be in her 20s, is being treated for serious injuries at the scene.

The rider of the bike, a 29-year-old man, has been taken to hospital.

Road closures are in place on Court Hey Road and drivers are advised to avoid the area.

CCTV and witness enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone who saw the incident or was passing and has any dashcam footage or information is asked to contact us via @MerPolCC on Twitter with reference 21000132231.
