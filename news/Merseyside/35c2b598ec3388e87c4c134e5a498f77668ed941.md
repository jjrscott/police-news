11:53 16/01/2020

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's office is appealing for help in tracing the family of John Daryll Dwyer.

Mr Dwyer, 75, of Peel House Lane, Widnes, sadly passed away of natural causes on Saturday 11th January 2020 at Liverpool Heart and Chest Hospital.

Anyone who can help the Coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should contact Elise Fahey 0151 233 0132 or email Elise.Fahey@liverpool.gov.uk
