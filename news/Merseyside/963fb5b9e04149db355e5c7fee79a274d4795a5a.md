12:43 05/05/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a 75 year-old woman who sadly passed away at her home in Waterloo.

Joyce Ditchfield died yesterday, Monday 4th May, at her home in Wellington Street.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone who knows of Mrs Ditchfield's next-of-kin or can assist is asked to contact Coroners investigation officer James Martindale via james.l.martindale@merseyside.police.uk
