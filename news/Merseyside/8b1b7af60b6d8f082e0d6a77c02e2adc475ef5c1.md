09:45 01/02/2021

Detectives investigating a burglary in Rainford have issued CCTV images of three men they believe may have information to assist enquiries.

At around 2.30am on Monday, 4 January, it was reported that four men had gained entry to an address in Maggots Nook Road before making off with a HGV containing a large amount of food goods.

An investigation is ongoing and detectives are appealing for anyone with information to get in touch.

Detective Inspector Jackie Guinness said: "A significant amount of goods were stolen following a burglary at an address in Rainford and I would ask anyone with information to come forward.

"We are particularly keen to speak to the three men pictured as we believe they may have vital information to assist the investigation, and would ask them, or anyone who recognises them, to please come forward."

If you have any information, CCTV or dashcam footage from the area, please contact @MerPolCC, 101 quoting ref. 21000007183 or Crimestoppers, anonymously on 0800 555 111.
