We are appealing for help in finding a 17 year-old girl who is missing from home in Liverpool.

Charlotte Walker was last seen at 6.45am on Saturday 30th January in the Fairfield area.

She is described as a white female, 5ft 6in tall, of slim build with long, straight brown hair and blue eyes. She speaks with a Liverpool accent.

When last seen she was wearing a black T-shirt, a black jacket and black jogging bottoms.

She is known to frequent the St Helens and Liverpool areas.

Anyone who has seen Charlotte or knows of her whereabouts is asked to DM @MerPolCC or @missingpeople on 116 000.
