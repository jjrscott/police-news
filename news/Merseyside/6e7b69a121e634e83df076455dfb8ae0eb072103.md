12:05 25/09/2020

Can you help us find the next of kin for James Simpson Logan, aged 78, who recently passed away in Southport.

Mr Logan died on Thursday 10 September at Southport Nursing Home in Albert Road. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Mr Logan previously lived in Harrow, London and has two sons, Joseph and Jason, who are believed to be between 35 and 40.

Anyone with information is asked to call Jodie Richardson on (0151) 934 4033 or via 0345 140 0845. You can also email Jodie.Richardson@sefton.gov.uk.
