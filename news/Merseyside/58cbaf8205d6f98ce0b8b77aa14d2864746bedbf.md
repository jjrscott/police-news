16:00 07/03/2020

****UPDATE: SATURDAY 7 MARCH, 4PM****

Detectives investigating the death of a woman in Woolton yesterday (Friday 6 March) have arrested a second person.

Emergency services were called to a house on Kings Drive just before 1am where a woman in her 60s was sadly pronounced dead.

A 33-year-old woman from Wirral has been arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to murder and taken to a police station for questioning.

A 37-year-old man from Wirral, who was yesterday arrested on suspicion of murder, remains in custody.

 ****Original release****

We can confirm that officers attended a house in Woolton today, Friday, 6 March, following the death of a woman.

Emergency services were called to a house on Kings Drive just before 1am where a woman in her 60s was sadly pronounced dead.

We are in the process of formally identifying her and informing her next of kin.

A 37-year-old man from Wirral has been arrested on suspicion of murder and taken to a police station in Merseyside to be interviewed.

Her death is currently being treated as unexplained.

A post-mortem will be conducted to establish the cause of death.
