Updated News Post

Detectives investigating a cannabis supply conspiracy have charged four men following warrants and activity in Merseyside and West Yorkshire on Friday, 11 December.

A total of seven people were arrested. The following four men have been charged with conspiracy to supply cannabis and will appear at Sefton Magistrates Court this morning, Monday 14 December:
24-year-old man Bertold Cara; 24-year-old man Jurgen Miha; 25-year-old man Arber Disha; all of Canon Road, Anfield; and 32-year-old Xhyla Erion of no fixed abode.

A fifth person, a 25-year-old-woman from Hackney, London was released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Two more people arrested by West Yorkshire Police in Bradford have also been charged and will appear at Bradford Magistrates Court this morning, Monday 14 December.
