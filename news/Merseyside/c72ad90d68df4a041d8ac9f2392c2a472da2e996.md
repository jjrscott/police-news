22:57 26/06/2020

Officers have today (26th June) charged a 15 year old boy with attempt murder in relation to a shooting on Station Road, Prescot on 20th January where a 17-year-old boy was shot resulting in injuries to his abdomen.

The 15 year old, who cannot be named for legal reasons, has been remanded in custody and will appear at Liverpool Court tomorrow (27th June).
