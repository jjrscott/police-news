13:33 19/05/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a 78 year-old Wirral man.

Harold Furness, of Reedville, Prenton, sadly passed away at Arrowe Park Hospital on Sunday 17th May.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who has information about Mr Furness' next of kin is asked to contact Nicola Griffiths, Coroner's Investigation Officer, on 0151 233 0131 or by email Nicola.Griffiths@liverpool.gov.uk
