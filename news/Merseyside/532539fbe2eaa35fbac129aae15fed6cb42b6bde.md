We are appealing for help in finding a 16 year-old girl who is missing from home in St Helens.

Megan Jones was last seen on Sherdley Road at 11pm on Saturday 13th March.

She is described as a white female, 5ft 6in tall, with very long brown hair, normally worn in a top knot.

When last seen he was wearing a green T-shirt, black pants, black shoes with white soles and carrying a green hooded top.

She is known to frequent the St Helens, Warrington and L5 areas.

Anyone who has seen Megan or knows of her whereabouts is asked to DM @MerPolCC or @missingpeople on 116 000.
