Updated News Post

We have charged a 51 year-old man with arson following an incident at a house on Kingsway in Huyton on the evening of Monday 27th January.

Ian Ronald Hunter, of Kingsway, was charged with arson being reckless as to whether life would be endangered and s39 assault.

Hunter was remanded into custody to appear at Liverpool magistrates court today, Wednesday 29th January.
