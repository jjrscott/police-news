17:21 01/04/2021

We have charged a 35 year-old Liverpool man as part of a Class A drugs conspiracy.

Jordan Alvis, of Trent Close, Croxteth Park, has been charged with conspiracy to supply Class A drugs (heroin); conspiracy to supply Class B drugs (ketamine) and conspire to convert criminal property.

His arrest came as part of the national Operation Venetic.

Alvis was remanded into custody to appear at Liverpool adult remand court on Saturday 3th April.
