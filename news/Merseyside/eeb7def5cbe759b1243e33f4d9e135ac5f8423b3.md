10:33 18/02/2021

Officers have charged a 58-year-old man with drugs offences following a warrant in St Helens yesterday, Wednesday 17 February.

At around 9.30am, officers attended an address on Corporation Street and seized between 200-300 cannabis plants.

Fitim Bashaj, 58, of no fixed abode, was arrested at the scene and has since been charged with being concerned in production of a Class B drug.

He was remanded in custody to appear at Wirral Adult Remand Court on Thursday 18 February.
