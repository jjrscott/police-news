10:13 09/07/2020

Can you help? We are asking for help in finding the next of kin of a 59 year-old Liverpool man who has died.

Navid Masih, sadly passed away at his home in Alford Street in Fairfield on Friday 3rd July.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed Mr Masih has links to Pakistan and siblings living in the London area.

Anyone who can help is asked to contact Coroner's investigation officer Simon Knuckey on 0151 233 0134 or email simon.knuckey@liverpool.gov.uk
