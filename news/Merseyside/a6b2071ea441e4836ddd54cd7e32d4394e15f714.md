09:22 26/05/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a man who recently died in Bootle.

75-year-old Mansfield James Jones, also known as Charles Harrigan-Jones, passed away on Tuesday, 19 May at his home address in Longfellow Street, Bootle.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed Mr Jones may have had relatives from the London area.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Coroners Investigation Officer David Woodroofe on 0151 777 4534 or email David.R.Woodroofe@merseyside.police.uk.
