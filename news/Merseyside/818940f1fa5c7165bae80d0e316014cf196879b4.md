14:41 18/03/2021

We have today, Thursday 18th March, arrested a 19 year-old male on suspicion of burglary.

The teenager, who is from the Norris Green area, was arrested by officers from the designated Op Castle and Force Fugitive team in relation to four burglaries which occurred at properties between August and September last year.

He has been arrested on suspicion of burglary at a property in West Derby on 19th August 2020 in which a VW Polo was stolen; a burglary at a property in Southport on 23rd September 2020 in which a Renault Kango Panel van was stolen; a burglary at a property in Kirkby on 23rd September 2020 in which a VW Golf was taken and a burglary at a property in Norris Green on 25th September 2020 in which an X Box and cash was stolen.

The male is currently in custody where will be questioned by detectives.
