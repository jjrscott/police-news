11:46 27/09/2020

Detectives investigating a sexual assault on a teenage girl in Orrell Park last Monday (21 September) have charged a man.

30-year-old Dean Lockley of no fixed abode was charged with kidnap; false imprisonment; and sexual assault.

He has been remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court tomorrow, Monday 28 September.
