15:18 14/08/2020

Officers have today (14th August) charged a 26 year old man from Liverpool for drugs offences.

Bradley Butler, of Melling Road, Liverpool has been charged with possession of cannabis resin with intent to supply after the car he was driving was stopped yesterday on Simonswood Lane.

He has been remanded in custody and will appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court tomorrow (15th August).
