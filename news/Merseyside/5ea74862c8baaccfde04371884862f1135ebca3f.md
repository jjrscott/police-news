14:51 08/11/2019

Can you help? Liverpool Coroner's Office need to find the next of kin for a Walton man who recently passed away.

68-year-old Dennis Sadler lived in Ireton Street and sadly passed away on Wednesday 6 November. His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who can assist with tracing Mr Sadler's family is asked to contact Elise Fahey on (0151) 233 0132 or email elise.fahey@liverpool.gov.uk.
