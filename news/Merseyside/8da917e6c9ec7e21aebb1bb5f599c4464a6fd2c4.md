13:00 12/03/2021

Please share and help Liverpool Coroner's Office find the next of kin for an Everton man who recently passed away.

87-year-old William Osbourne sadly died at his home on Beacon Lane on Monday 1 March. There are no suspicious circumstances. Mr Osbourne also had links to an address on York Terrace.

Anyone who can help the Coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call Katy Porter 0151 233 0130 or email HMCoroner@liverpool.gov.uk.
