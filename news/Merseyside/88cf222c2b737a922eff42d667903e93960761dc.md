19:43 16/02/2020

A 28-year-old man will appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court tomorrow (Monday, 17 February) following the recovery of a submachine pistol at a house in Everton yesterday.

Jake Rogan from Patmos Close, Everton, has been charged with possession of a prohibited weapon.

A 25-year-old woman from Everton, who was also arrested, has been released under investigation.
