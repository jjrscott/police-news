11:45 18/09/2019

Can you help? Wirral Coroner's Office is looking to find the next of kin for a Wallasey man who recently died.

78-year-old Derek Lewis from Seaview Road sadly passed away yesterday (Tuesday 17 September). There are no suspicious circumstances.

Mr Lewis previously lived on Northwood Road in Prenton.

Please contact Coroner's Officer Lea Cripps on (0151) 233 0150 or email Lea.Cripps@liverpool.gov.uk
