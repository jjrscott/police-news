10:39 13/11/2019

We have charged a 35 year-old man with robbery following an incident on Grasmere Street in Anfield on the evening of Tuesday 5th November.

Paul Pollard, of Birchfield Drive, Walton, has also been charged with possession of a bladed article and criminal damage to a motor vehicle.

The charges relate to a robbery involving a private hire taxi driver.

Pollard was remanded into custody to appear at Liverpool magistrates court today, Wednesday 13th November.
