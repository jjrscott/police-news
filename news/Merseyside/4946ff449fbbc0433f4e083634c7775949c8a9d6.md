13:48 22/04/2020

The Merseyside Coroner's Investigation Office are appealing for the publics help to locate the next of kin of a woman who sadly passed away in the Huyton area.

Barbara Hewitt, 68, died at her home address on Gort Road, Huyton on 18th April.

Her death is not being treated as suspicious. If you were friends with Barbara or know her relatives, please get in touch via 34600@merseyside.police.uk or calling 01517773481
