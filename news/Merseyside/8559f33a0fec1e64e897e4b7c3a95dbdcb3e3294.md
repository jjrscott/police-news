12:53 29/01/2021

Can you help? We're appealing for information to help find Chantelle Roberts, who is missing from home.

Chantelle, aged 17, was last seen at around 5pm on Saturday, 23 January in Kirkby.

She is described as a white female, around 5ft 11in tall, with long mousey brown hair usually worn in a ponytail, a fair complexion and blue eyes.

When last seen, Chantelle was wearing a dark grey top with a leopard print 'P' in the middle, light grey pants with a white pattern, light grey trainers and a grey/black coat with fur around the hood.

She is known to frequent areas of Warrington, Kirkby, St Helens, Newton-le-Willows and Southport.

Anyone who has seen Chantelle, or has any information on her whereabouts is asked to contact @MerPolCC, 101 or @missingpeople on 116 000.
