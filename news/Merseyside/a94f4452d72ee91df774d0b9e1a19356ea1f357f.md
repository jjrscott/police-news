12:25 25/02/2020

Liverpool Coroner's Office is appealing for information to help trace the next of kin of a woman who recently died in Kirkdale.

Maureen Daley, 74, passed away on Tuesday, 18 February at her home on Tolpuddle Way. There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone who can help trace her next of kin is asked to contact Coroners Investigation Officer, Paula White on 0151 233 0126 or paula.white@liverpool.gov.uk.
