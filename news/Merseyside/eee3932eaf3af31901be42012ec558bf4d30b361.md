15:23 25/03/2021

We have charged a man, 25, and a woman, 32, with drug offences following a warrant in St Helens yesterday (Wednesday 24 March).

Officers executed a Misuse of Drugs Act warrant yesterday at an address on Borough Road St Helens and located a cannabis farm

Bafti Beluli, aged 25, and Blemira Shpata, aged 32, both of Antonio Street, Bootle have been charged with cultivation of cannabis.

They have been remanded in custody and will appear in Wirral Adult Remand Court today, Thursday 25 March.

If you have any information about suspected drug production in your area, please contact @MerPolCC, 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Always call 999 if a crime is in progress.
