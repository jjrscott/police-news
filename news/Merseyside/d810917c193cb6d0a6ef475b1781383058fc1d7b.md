10:38 11/03/2020

Sefton Council is appealing for help in tracing the next-of-kin of a man who passed away at his home in Maghull.

John Edward Sprung, aged 81, of Deyes Lane, sadly died on Tuesday 25th February.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who has any information about Mr Sprung's next of kin is asked to contact Jodie Richardson on 0151 934 4033 or email Jodie.richardson@sefton.gov.uk
