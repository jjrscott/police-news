07:31 03/03/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a 61 year-old man who passed away at his home in Liverpool.

Alec Patterson was sadly found deceased on Friday 28th February 2020 at home in Alfred Stocks Court, Turner Close, Dingle.
His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who can help is asked to contact Nicola Griffiths, Coroner's Investigation Officer, on 0151 233 0143 or email Nicola.Griffiths@liverpool.gov.uk
