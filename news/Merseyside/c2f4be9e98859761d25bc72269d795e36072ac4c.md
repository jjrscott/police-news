17:56 18/03/2021

We have charged a 23 year-old Liverpool man with firearms and drugs offences.

Lewis Langfield of Renshaw Street, Liverpool city centre, was charged with conspiracy to transfer firearms, conspiracy to supply cocaine and handling stolen goods.

He was remanded into custody to appear in court tomorrow, Friday 19th March.

The charges come as part of the national Operation Venetic.
