Merseyside Police has this evening (30th June) charged a male with murder following the death of Dre Estridge in Toxteth on 26th June.

The 17-year-old male from Wavertree, who cannot be named for legal reasons, has been charged with murder and will appear at Liverpool Crown Court on 1st July.
