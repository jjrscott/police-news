16:16 28/11/2019

Can you help the Coroners Office find the next of kin for a Southport woman died last week?

68-year-old Kathleen Wenton sadly passed away last Thursday 21 November. Her death is not being treated as suspicious.

As well as Southport, Kathleen had links to L6 and L8 in Liverpool.

Any family members or anyone with info are asked to please make contact with Coroners Officer Dave Woodroofe on 0151 777 4534, or email David.r.woodroofe@merseyside.police.uk.
