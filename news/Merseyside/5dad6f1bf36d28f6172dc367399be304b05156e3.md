15:14 09/04/2020

We are appealing to find the next of kin of a man who sadly passed away earlier this week.

Stephen Walker who was 58 and from Catherine Street, Liverpool was found deceased on 6th April.

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Although we have contacted a member of Mr Walkers family, we believe Stephen has a son who we are keen to get in touch with. If you know his details, please contact Coroner's Investigation Officer Nicola Griffiths on 0151 233 0143 or email Nicola.Griffiths@liverpool.gov.uk.
