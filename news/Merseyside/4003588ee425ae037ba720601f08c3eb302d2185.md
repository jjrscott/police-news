Can you help? Sefton Coroner's Office are looking to find the next of kin of a Southport woman who recently passed away.

Christine Collis, 64, sadly died at her home at Brentwood Court, Southport yesterday (Wednesday 23 September). There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone with information to find Ms Collis's next of kin can call Coroners Investigation Officer David Woodroofe on 0151 777 4534 or email David.R.Woodroofe@merseyside.police.uk
