Can you help? Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office are looking to find the next of kin of a Liverpool man who recently passed away.

Paul Anthony Brown, 63, sadly died at home on East Prescot Road on Friday 16 October. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone with information that may help locate Mr Brown's next of kin can call Coroners Investigation Officer Paula White on 0151 233 0126 or email Paula.white@liverpool.gov.uk
