17:32 04/10/2019

We have arrested an 18 year-old male on suspicion of drugs supply following a stop search in the Walton area this morning, Friday 4th October.

Officers were on patrol in the Lind Street area when they detained the teenager. They also recovered a flick knife and numerous wraps of white powder and bags of cannabis.

The 18-year-old was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class A and B drugs and possession of a bladed article.

He is currently in custody where he will be questioned by officers.
