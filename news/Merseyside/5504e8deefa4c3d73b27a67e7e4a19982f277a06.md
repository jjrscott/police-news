12:33 19/06/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of an 80 year-old man who passed away in Liverpool.

David Healiss, of Hillview, Mossley Hill, sadly passed away at his home address on Thursday 18th June.

There are no suspicious circumstances and a post mortem will be held to establish the cause of death.

Anyone with information is asked to contact coroner's investigation officer Simon Knuckey on 0151 233 0134 or email simon.knuckey@liverpool.gov.uk
