16:39 28/10/2020

We can confirm that emergency services are in Walton after a woman was found injured this afternoon, Wednesday 28 October.

At 2.50pm, officers and the ambulance service were called to the Rice Lane flyover to reports of a woman in her 20s having fallen.

She has been taken to hospital for assessment of her injuries.

A road closure will be in place as we deal with the incident. Please take an alternative route and thanks for your patience.
