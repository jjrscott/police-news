21:26 02/04/2020

Two people have been charged after a man was stabbed at flats on Laird Street, Birkenhead, yesterday afternoon (Wednesday, 1 April 2020).

Graham Byrne, 50 years, of Laird Street, has been charged with Section 18 assault and Annette Heath, 41, of Laird Street, Birkenhead, has been charged with Section 18 assault and possession of a bladed article.

They will both appear at Wirral Magistrates Court tomorrow morning (Friday, 3 April 2020).
