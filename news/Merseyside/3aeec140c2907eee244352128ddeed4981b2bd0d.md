16:40 02/09/2020

Can you help? Merseyside Coroner's Office is looking to trace the next of kin of a Whiston man who recently passed away.

Derek Kewley, 81, sadly died at Whiston Hospital on Saturday 29 August. His death is not being treated as suspicious. Mr Kewley may have a daughter and son, from whom he was estranged.

Anyone with information is asked to email Clair.Sajewicz@sefton.gov.uk or call 0151 777 3754.
