11:28 22/02/2021

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's office is appealing for the public's help in tracing the next of kin of a man who recently died in Liverpool.

Leslie Sefton, aged 70, sadly died at his home address in Newlyn Road, Croxteth, on Tuesday, 16 February.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information to help trace Mr Sefton's next of kin is asked to contact Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's office on 0151 233 5770 or email Coroner@liverpool.gov.uk
