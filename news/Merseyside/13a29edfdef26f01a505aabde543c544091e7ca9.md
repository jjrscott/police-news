21:22 16/08/2020

Following an extensive investigation into an incident that occurred on Moorland Road, Maghull yesterday (15th August), Merseyside Police can confirm that this incident is now being treated as tragic street injury.

The man arrested has been released with no further action to be taken against him.

No further suspects are being sought.

Thank you to the media and the public for your cooperation and support whilst we dealt with this incident.
