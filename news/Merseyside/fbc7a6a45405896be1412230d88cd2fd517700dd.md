00:01 11/08/2020

Merseyside Police can confirm officers attended Fox Street, Liverpool this evening (Monday, 10 August) to reports a car was being damaged.

Officers attended Fox Street at around 6.10pm to reports of criminal damage of a Vauxhall Astra. On arrival the officers found the car to be empty and there are no reports of anyone being injured at this time. The car and a Yamaha motorbike were recovered from the scene for forensic examination.

The scene was cordoned off and officers recovered what is thought to be a stun gun from the area. This has also been recovered for forensic examination.

Enquiries are on-going and CCTV from the area is being examined. Anyone with any information about this incident is asked to get in contact @MerPolCC or call 101 quoting reference 876 of 10.08.20 or contact @CrimestoppersUK anonymously on 0800 555 111.
