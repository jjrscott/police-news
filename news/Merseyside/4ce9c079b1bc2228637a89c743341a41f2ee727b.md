16:06 14/07/2020

The family of Warren James Glover, 33, have issued a tribute after he died on 7 July from injuries sustained in an assault on Saturday 13 June in French Street, Thatto Heath:

"As a family, we are heartbroken and truly devastated with the loss of Warren.

"He was a genuinely kind-hearted and caring Dad who adored his family and friends.

"He was much loved and loyal; his fun, genuine and empathetic character will be sorely missed by all who knew him especially his Mum."
