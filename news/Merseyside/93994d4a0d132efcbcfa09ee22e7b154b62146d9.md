08:15 05/03/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next-of-kin of a 39 year-old man who passed away in Liverpool.

Michael Andrew Marshall, who lived in the Anfield area, passed away at a house in Toxteth on Friday 28th February.

There are no suspicious circumstance surrounding his death.

Anyone with information about Mr Marshall's next-of-kin is asked to contact Coroner's Investigation Officer Liam Moss on 0151 233 0125 or email liam.moss1@liverpool.gov.uk
