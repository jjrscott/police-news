20:30 30/01/2021

We can confirm that a 19 year-old man has been charged with the murder of a woman in Wavertree.

Mohammed Diakite, also known as Nigel Diakite, of Prince Alfred Road, Wavertree, was charged with the murder of N'Taya Elliott-Cleverley.

The body of Ms Elliott-Cleverley , 20, was found at a property in Prince Alfred Road in the early hours of yesterday morning, Friday 29th January.

Diakite has been remanded into custody to appear in court on Monday, 1st February.
