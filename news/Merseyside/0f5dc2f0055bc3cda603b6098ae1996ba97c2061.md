11:57 07/10/2020

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office are appealing for information to help trace the next of kin of a man who recently died in Wirral.

David Taaffe, aged 79, sadly died at his home address in Pickering Road, New Brighton, on Wednesday, 30 September.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

It is believed Mr Taaffe may have family in the Scarborough area.

Anyone who has information about Mr Taaffe's next of kin is asked to contact Coroner's Investigation Officer, Karen Gregory on 0151 233 0989 or email Karen.Gregory@liverpool.gov.uk.
