15:00 10/08/2020

Detectives are issuing the CCTV image of a man who may have information in connection with a sexual assault in Liverpool City Centre last month.

At around 12.40am on Friday 24 July, a woman was at McCooleys on Temple Court, Mathew Street when she was sexually assaulted by a man outside the unisex toilets.

Detective Constable Nicholas Glascott-Tull said: "We think this man may have vital information in connection with this incident. If you recognise him, let us know directly or anonymously via Crimestoppers.

"We're supporting the victim in this distressing time as our extensive enquiries continue."

Anyone with information is asked to contact our social media desk via Twitter @MerPolCC or Facebook 'Merseyside Police Contact Centre' with reference 20000436245.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or via their online form at: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/give-information/give-information.
