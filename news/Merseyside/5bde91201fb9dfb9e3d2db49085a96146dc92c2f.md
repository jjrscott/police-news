Can you help? Liverpool Coroner's Office is looking to find the next of kin for a West Derby man who recently passed away.

80-year-old Patrick Blood died at his home on Galemead last Thursday, 13 February. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Any family members are requested to contact Lesley-Anne Rogers, Coroner's Investigation Officer on 0151 233 0137 or email Lesley-Anne.Rogers@liverpool.gov.uk.
