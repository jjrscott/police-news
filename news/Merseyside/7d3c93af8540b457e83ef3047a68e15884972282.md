14:59 21/11/2019

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for the family of a man from Wallasey who recently passed away to come forward.

Michael Burgess, 65 years, lived in Rice Lane and sadly died on Monday 18 November on Sunday 17 November.

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who can help the coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call James Martindale on 0151 233 0129 or email james.l.martindale@liverpool.gov.uk
