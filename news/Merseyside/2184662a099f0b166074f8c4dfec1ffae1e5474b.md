09:23 27/11/2019

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for the family of a woman from Liverpool who recently passed away to come forward.

67-year-old Kathleen Bigley passed away at her home address in Heathergreen Court, Walton on Sunday, 24 November.

Her death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who can help the coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should contact Elaine Jordan on 0151 233 0124 or email Elaine.Jordan@liverpool.gov.uk
