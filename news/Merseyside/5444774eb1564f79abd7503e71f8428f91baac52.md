13:25 17/12/2020

Can you help? Sefton Coroner's Office is appealing for information to help trace the next of kin of a man who recently died.

Stephen Longbottom, aged 68, sadly died at his home address in Little Crosby on Saturday, 12 December.

There are no suspicious circumstance surrounding his death.

Mr Longbottom was born in Leeds, and it is believed he may have a brother in the Nottingham area.

Anyone with information to help trace his next of kin is asked to contact Coroner's Officer Clair Sajewicz on 0151 777 3754 or Clair.Sajewicz@Sefton.gov.uk.
