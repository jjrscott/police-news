13:09 20/09/2019

Updated News Post

Officers are asking the public for their help to locate wanted Bootle man Sonny Redmond.

26-year-old Redmond is wanted after he breached his gang injunction and failed to attend court in relation to drugs offences.

He is described as white, around 5'8 tall, with Hazel coloured eyes and brown hair.

If you have seen him or know where he is, please get in touch @MerPolCC or by calling 101.
