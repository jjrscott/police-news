16:27 20/01/2020

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for the family of a woman from Anfield who recently passed away to come forward.

Marie Lyon, 70 years, died at Rowan Garth Care Village on Wednesday 15 January.

Her death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who can help the coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call the Coroner's Office on 0151 233 0124 or email Elaine.Jordan@liverpool.gov.uk.
