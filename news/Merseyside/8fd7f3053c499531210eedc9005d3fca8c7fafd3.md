21:05 19/11/2020

We have this evening, Thursday 19th November, charged a Liverpool man with firearms offences.

Michael Lyons, 33, of no fixed abode, has been charged with three offences of possession of a firearm without a certificate.

He has been remanded into custody to appear at Liverpool magistrates court tomorrow, Friday 20th November.
