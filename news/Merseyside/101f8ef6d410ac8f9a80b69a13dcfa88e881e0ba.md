16:01 19/02/2021

The Liverpool & Wirral Coroner's Office are appealing for contact information regarding the next of kin for the late James Lindeman - known as Jim.

The 77 year old male from Rice Lane, Walton, Liverpool sadly died on Monday 15th February.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

If anyone has any contact information regarding a next of kin please contact Karen Gregory on 0151 233 0989 or email hmcoroner@liverpool.gov.uk
