12:38 15/01/2021

We have issued pictures of items we would like to trace following a suspected theft in the Brighton Le sands area.

It was reported that between 2am and 8am on Thursday 14 January, offenders forced entry to a garage and shed in the rear garden of a family home on River View.

Two bikes are reported missing: a red and white YT Jeffsy and a black Specialized Stumpjumper.

CCTV enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Merseyside Police quoting reference 21000027559 - you can DM us @MerpolCC, message us on Facebook at 'Merseyside Police Contact Centre' or call 101. You can also call @CrimestoppersUK anonymously on 0800 555 111.
