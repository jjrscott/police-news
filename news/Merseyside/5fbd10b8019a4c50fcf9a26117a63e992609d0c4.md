15:23 26/03/2020

Liverpool & Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for help to find the family of Ian Sterling from Toxteth.

Mr Sterling, aged 68, sadly died in Liverpool on Friday 20th March 2020. There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who can help the Coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call Katy Porter on 0151 233 0130 or email katy.porter@liverpool.gov.uk.
