Wanted

14:18 17/03/2021

We are appealing for help in finding a 30 year-old Liverpool man who is wanted on warrant.

Phillip Lam, who is originally from the Dingle area, is wanted for failing to appear at Liverpool Crown Court on 9th March 2021.

Lam was sentenced in his absence to four years and nine months for conspiracy to commit fraud in relation to credit card fraud. A no bail warrant has been issued.

He is described as being 5ft 3in tall, of heavy build with dark hair and brown eyes.

Anyone who has seen Lam or knows of his whereabouts is asked to DM @MerPolCC or @CrimestoppersUK on 0800 555 111.
