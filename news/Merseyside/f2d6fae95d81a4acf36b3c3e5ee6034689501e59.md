09:58 26/03/2021

Detectives have charged 11 men with drug supply offences following a strike day yesterday, Thursday 26 March.

Ten warrants were carried out at addresses in Liverpool and Wirral as part of our ongoing commitment to tackling Class A and Class B drug dealing.

In total, 11 men were arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to supply Class A and Class B drugs and taken into custody for questioning. All have now been charged as follows:

Sean Burrell, 29, of Peacehaven Close, Childwall has been charged with conspiracy to produce cannabis; conspiracy to supply cocaine; conspiracy to supply heroin; conspiracy to supply ketamine; and conspiracy to supply cannabis
James Wright, 35, of Brewery Gardens, Gateacre Brow has been charged with conspiracy to supply cocaine; conspiracy to supply heroin; conspiracy to supply ecstasy; conspiracy to supply ketamine; conspiracy to supply cannabis; and money laundering
Martin McCoy, 42 of 5 Bewey Close, Toxteth has been charged with conspiracy to supply cocaine; conspiracy to supply heroin; and conspiracy to supply cannabis
Michael Joseph, 55, of Rock Lane East, Rock Ferry, Wirral has been charged with conspiracy to supply cocaine; and conspiracy to supply heroin
Marvin Porcelli, 46, of Gray Street, Bootle has been charged with conspiracy to supply cocaine; and conspiracy to supply heroin
Thomas O'Brien, 42, of Finch Lea Drive, Dovecot has been charged with conspiracy to supply cocaine; conspiracy to supply heroin; conspiracy to supply ketamine; and conspiracy to supply cannabis
Steven Sinclair, 53, of Pilch Lane, Huyton, has been charged with conspiracy to supply cocaine; conspiracy to supply heroin; conspiracy to supply ketamine, and conspiracy to supply cannabis
David Conroy, 42, of Luke Street, Toxteth has been charged with conspiracy to supply cocaine; and conspiracy to supply heroin
Kieran Meehan 30 of Queens Drive, Wavertree has been charged with conspiracy to produce cannabis; and conspiracy to supply cocaine
All the above will appear at Wirral Magistrates court today, Friday 26 March.

Thomas Penrith, 33 of no fixed abode, has been charged with conspiracy to supply cocaine; and conspiracy to supply heroin
Andrew Webb, 46, of no fixed abode, has been charged with conspiracy to supply cocaine; and conspiracy to supply heroin
The above will appear at Liverpool Magistrates court on Tuesday 30 March.
