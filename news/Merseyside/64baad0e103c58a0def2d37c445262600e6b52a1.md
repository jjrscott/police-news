09:49 14/12/2020

We have charged a 24 year-old man with assault by beating following an incident on Dale Street in Liverpool city centre on Wednesday 9th December.

Theo Maulba, of no fixed abode, was also charged with possession of a controlled drug Class B.

He was remanded into custody to appear before Liverpool complex case adult remand court today, Monday 14th December.
