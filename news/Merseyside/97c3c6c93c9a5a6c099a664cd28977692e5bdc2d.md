We are appealing on behalf of the Coroner's Investigation Office to trace the next of kin of a man who sadly passed away yesterday (13th May).

Mr William McCarthy, 51 of Maplewood Close, Liverpool died at his home address.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone that knows Mr McCarthy or his family members should get in touch with Coroner's Investigation Officer Katy Porter on
