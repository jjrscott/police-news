12:27 04/03/2021

Detectives charged three men yesterday, Wednesday 3 March, on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class A drugs.

Shortly before 2.30pm, a warrant was executed at a property in Moorfields, Liverpool, where a large quantity of Crack Cocaine was seized.

Mohammed Awad, 35, of Linacre Road Litherland, has been charged with possession with intent to supply Class A drugs

Devere Ogungboro, 31, of Goschen Street, Old Swan has been charged with possession with intent to supply Class A drugs and breaching a civil injunction.

A 16-year-old boy was also charged with possession with intent to supply Class A drugs.

All three males were taken into police custody and will appear at Birkenhead Magistrates Court today.
