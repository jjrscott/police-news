09:19 05/01/2021

We are appealing for help in finding a 43 year-old man who is missing from home in Liverpool.

Anthony Hall was last seen in Long Lane, Aintree, at 10pm last night, Monday 4th January.

He is described as a white male, 6ft 2in tall, with short red hair and stubble.

When last seen he was wearing black North Face tracksuit bottoms, a grey top, a black Adidas bubble coat and blue Nike trainers.

He is known to frequent Liverpool city centre, Fazakerley, Norris Green, Southport and St Helens.

Anyone who has seen Anthony or knows of his whereabouts is asked to DM @MerPolCC or @missingpeople on 116 000.
