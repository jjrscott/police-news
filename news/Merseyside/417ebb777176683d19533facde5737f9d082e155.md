15:13 18/03/2021

Please share and help us find missing Knowsley teenager Sophie Francis.

Sophie, 15, was last seen on 17 March at Darlington Train Station and extensive enquiries are ongoing.

Sophie is described as white, 5ft 6in tall, of slim build, with shoulder length straight dark hair, blue eyes, and speaks with a Liverpool accent.

When last seen, she was wearing a black bubble parka coat with a fur collar and a pink, hooded bubble jacket underneath. Sophie wore a multi-coloured pink, purple and blue gym top with matching leggings and black trainers with a thick white sole, and silver sparkly earrings.

Sophie is known to frequent areas of Halewood, Kirkby and Kensington.

Please pass any sightings and information via our online form: https://www.merseyside.police.uk/ro/report/mp/v2/report-sighting-of-missing-person-form/ or you can contact us @MerPolCC on Twitter, 'Merseyside Police Contact Centre' on Facebook or 101.
