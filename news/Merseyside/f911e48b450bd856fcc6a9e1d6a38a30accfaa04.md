12:13 19/03/2021

We've charged a 19 year-old man with burglary and dangerous driving.

Lucas Devereux, of Ranworth Place, Norris Green, has been charged with a burglary at a house in Norris Green in September 2020 and dangerous driving in relation to a stolen car which was used during the burglary.

He will appear in court today, Friday 19th March.
