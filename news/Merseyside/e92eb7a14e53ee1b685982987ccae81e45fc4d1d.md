18:51 07/05/2020

Detectives have charged three men with the murder of Stephen Maguire in Birkdale on 16 March, 2020.

Karl Corson, 27 years, of Columbine Close, Melling; Jack Higgins, 21 of Waresley Crescent, Fazakerley, and Ryan Smith, 21 years, of Colquitt Street, Liverpool city centre, were all charged with murder, two counts of kidnap, possession of a firearm, possession of ammunition and robbery.

They will appear at Liverpool, Knowsley, Sefton and St Helens Magistrates Court tomorrow (Friday, 8 May 2020).
