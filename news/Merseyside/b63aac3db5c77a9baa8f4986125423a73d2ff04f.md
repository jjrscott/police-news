12:55 05/02/2020

Knowsley, St Helens and Sefton Coroner's Office is appealing for the family of a man from Huyton who recently passed away to come forward.

Paul Hughes, aged 70, of Belfield Crescent, sadly died on Saturday 25 January this year.

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who can help the coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call 0151 777 3480 or email sefton.coroners.office@merseyside.police.uk
