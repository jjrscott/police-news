14:28 03/02/2021

We have charged a 27 year-old man with violent disorder following an incident outside Liverpool's Anfield football stadium on 22nd July 2020.

Jack Stuart Hayes, of Brook Street, Birkenhead, was remanded into custody to appear in court and will appear at south Sefton magistrates court today, Wednesday 3rd February.
