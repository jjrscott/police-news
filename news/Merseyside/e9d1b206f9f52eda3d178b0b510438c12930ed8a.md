Can you help? Wirral Coroner's Office is looking to find the next of kin of a Birkenhead man who recently died.

70-year-old William John Walker from The Dell, Birkenhead sadly passed away on New Year's Eve. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Coroner's Investigation Officer Elise Fahey on (0151) 233 0132 or email Elise.Fahey@liverpool.gov.uk.
