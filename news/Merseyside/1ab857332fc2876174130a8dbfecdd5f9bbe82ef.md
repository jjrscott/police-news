21:49 25/06/2020

 We have charged six people as part of an investigation into the supply of drugs in Wirral.

Officers attended seven addresses across Wirral on Thursday (25 June), arresting seven people and seizing cash, Class B drugs, jewellery and clothes.

Five men and one woman have been charged with conspiracy to supply class A drugs:

Liam Richard Bradley, 29 years, of Temple Road, Prenton
Thomas Willis, 28 years, of no fixed address
Mark Fitzpatrick, 41 years, of Borough Road, Birkenhead
Gary Andrews, 46 years, of Priory Wharf, Birkenhead
Stephanie Jones, 38 years, of New Ferry Road, New Ferry
Gary Clare, 58 years, of Ethel Road, Wallasey

They have been remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court on Friday 26 June.

A 31-year-old woman from Prenton who was arrested on suspicion of money laundering has been released pending further enquires.
