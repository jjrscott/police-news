10:57 10/01/2020

Officers are issuing CCTV images of a man they wish to speak to in connection with an incident of criminal damage in Birkenhead on Saturday, 14 December 2019.

It was reported that at around 10.55pm, a man damaged the night pay kiosk window at Shell Garage on Borough Road, after punching out at the employee behind the window. No one was injured during the incident.

CCTV enquiries have been ongoing and officers are keen to speak to the man pictured, as he may have vital information to assist the investigation.

If you recognise this man or have any information about the incident, please contact @MerPolCC, call 101 with reference 19100731422 or you can contact @CrimestoppersUK anonymously on 0800 555 111.
