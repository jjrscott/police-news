15:09 17/09/2019

We can confirm officers attended a care home in Speke this morning (Tuesday 17 September) following reports of a sudden death.

Police and the North West Ambulance Service were called to the premises, on Parade Crescent, just before 6.45am where a man in his 70s was sadly found deceased.

At this time officers are treating the death as unexplained.

Enquiries are ongoing to establish the circumstances of his death, and to formally identify the man. We are in the process of informing his next of kin.
