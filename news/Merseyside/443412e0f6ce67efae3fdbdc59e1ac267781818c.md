16:07 16/03/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of an 84 year-old man who passed away in Wirral.

William Samuel Lively sadly passed away at his home on New Chester Road on Tuesday 10th March.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who can help the Coroner trace any potential next of kin should call Elise Fahey on 0151 233 0132 or email Elise.Fahey@Liverpool.gov.uk
