10:00 04/03/2020

Merseyside Police have charged a 34 year old from Manchester after he was found in possession of a knuckle duster on Speke Road.

David Millward, 34 of Wildbrook Road, Little Hulton was stop checked in the car he was driving yesterday (3rd March).

During a search, he was found in possession of a knuckle duster and controlled drug (amphetamine). He was consequently arrested and later charged with these offences.

Millward has been bailed and will appear at Liverpool Crown Court later this month.
