10:53 16/02/2020

A man has been charged with possession of a firearm following an incident on Rainbow Drive, Melling, on 3 September last year, which resulted in the recovery of a sawn off shotgun and a long barrelled shotgun and two rounds of ammunition.

Stephen McHugh, 25 years of Ashbrook Drive, Fazakerley, has been charged with possession of a firearm and will appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court on Monday, 17 February 2020.
