14:13 15/10/2020

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's office is appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a woman who recently died in Everton.

72-year-old Carol Anne Bowness sadly died at her home address in Northumberland Terrace on Monday, 12 October.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Anyone with information to help trace her next of kin is asked to contact Corners Investigation Officer Paula White on 0151 233 0126 or Paula.white@liverpool.gov.uk.
