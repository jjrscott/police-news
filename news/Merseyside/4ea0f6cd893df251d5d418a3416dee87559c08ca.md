12:02 03/03/2021

Have you seen this man? We're appealing for information to trace Neil Tootle, 40, from Liverpool, who is wanted on warrant.

He is wanted by police after failing to appear in court to answer charges in relation to dangerous driving and aggravated vehicle taking.

Tootle is described as a white male, around 5ft 5in tall, with receding fair hair and blue eyes.

He has links to the Walton and Anfield areas of Liverpool.

Anyone who has seen Tootle, or has information on his whereabouts, is asked to contact @MerPolCC, 101 quoting ref. 20000717260 or Crimestoppers anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
