17:38 01/04/2021

The family of a 57 year-old man who was found dead at a house in Wavertree last month have issued a tribute.

Emergency services attended at the address on Bishopgate Street on the morning of Wednesday 24th March where they sadly found the body of 57 year-old Adrian Swift.

In a statement issued by Mr Swift's family they said:

"Such a huge shock at the tragic loss of our much loved brother and uncle.

"Will be sadly missed by his sister Rachel, family, and friends - a talented musician gone too soon, Rest In Peace Ade x"

Three people arrested in connection with Mr Swift's death have been released on bail pending further enquiries.
