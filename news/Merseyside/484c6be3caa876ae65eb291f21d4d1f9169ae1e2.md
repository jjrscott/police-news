12:02 15/09/2020

Can you help? Wirral Coroner's Office are looking to find next of kin of a Wirral man who recently passed away.

71-year-old Philip Sibley sadly died at his home on Town Meadow Lane, Moreton yesterday (Monday 14 September). There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone with information to find Mr Sibley's next of kin can call Lisa McElvogue on 0151 233 0128 or email Lisa.McElvogue@liverpool.gov.uk.
