Please share. Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing to find the family of Aileen Scott, aged 72, of Clipper View, New Ferry.

Aileen sadly died in New Ferry on Friday 21st July 2020. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone who can help the Coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call Katy Porter 0151 233 0130 or email katy.porter@liverpool.gov.uk.
