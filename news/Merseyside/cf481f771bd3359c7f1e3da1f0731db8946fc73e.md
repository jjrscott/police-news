11:40 12/04/2020

Officers are currently investigating reports of a firearms discharge last night (11th April).

We were called to Finborough Road, Walton just before midnight to reports that gun shots had been heard.

Armed officers were deployed to the scene where they found damage consistent with a firearm discharge to the door of a property.

Thankfully no injuries have been reported and enquiries remain ongoing.

Anyone with information should contact us quoting: 20000213389
