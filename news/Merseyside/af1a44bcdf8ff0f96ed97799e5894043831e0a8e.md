17:57 28/01/2020

We have today, Tuesday, 28 January, arrested and charged a Liverpool man with a vehicle offence.

 At around 4.10am, officers were called to Central Drive, Liverpool 12 following reports a man had been seen attempting to open a car door.

 35-year-old Mark John Pascoe from Lower Breck Road was arrested and has now been charged with vehicle interference.

 He will appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court tomorrow, Wednesday, 29 January.
