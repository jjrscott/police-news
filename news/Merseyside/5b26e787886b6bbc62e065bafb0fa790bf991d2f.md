14:03 13/12/2019

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for the family of a man from Tranmere who recently passed away to come forward.

Stephen Galloway, 57 years, sadly died at home on Whetstone Lane, Tranmere on Tuesday 10 December this year.

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who can help the coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call the Coroner's Office on 0151 233 0128 or email Lisa.McElvogue@liverpool.gov.uk.
