12:39 14/10/2019

Merseyside Police are appealing on behalf of the coroners office to find the next of kin of a woman who sadly passed away.

Susan Jones, 57 of Dorrit Street, Liverpool was found deceased at her home address on 12th October.

Her death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone that knows her next of kin and could put us in touch with them should call 0151 233 0150 or email Lea.Cripps@liverpool.gov.uk.
