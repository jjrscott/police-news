Wanted

11:55 22/10/2020

Detectives are continuing to appeal to the public for help find a man who may have vital information about the supply of Class A drugs in Merseyside.

Officers believe 29-year-old Amar Alsoraimi could have information that would assist their enquiries.

Extensive enquiries have been ongoing to find him and anyone with information is urged to come forward.

Alsoraimi is described as 5ft 7in tall with black hair and brown eyes and of medium build.

Anyone with information is asked to contact our social media desk @MerPolCC on Twitter or 'Merseyside Police Contact Centre' on Facebook, call 101 or you can provide information to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
