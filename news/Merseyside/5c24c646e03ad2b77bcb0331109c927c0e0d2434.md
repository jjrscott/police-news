14:11 25/02/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next-of-kin of a man who died in Liverpool.

Jonathan Colin Wilkins, 52, of Bowsell Street, Toxteth, passed away in Cressington, on Friday 21st February.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's investigation officer Simon Knuckey on 0151 233 0134 or email simon.knuckey@liverpool.gov.uk
