09:29 28/02/2020

Detectives investigating a firearms incident in Birkenhead yesterday (Thursday 27 February) have a charged a man.

Officers were called to the area of Lansdowne Road this morning, 27 February, just after 7.30am, to reports a man was brandishing a firearm in the street. Officers responded quickly and detained the man shortly after, along with a gas powered BB gun.

25-year-old Daniel Rowlands from Lansdowne Road was charged with possession of an imitation firearm with intent to cause fear of violence; and possession of an imitation firearm in a public place.

He has been remanded in custody to appear at Wirral Adult Remand Court this morning, Friday 28 February.

Any info on the storage and use of firearms and other weapons. Always call 999 if a crime is in progress, or tell us what you know @MerpolCC, 101 or anonymously through Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
