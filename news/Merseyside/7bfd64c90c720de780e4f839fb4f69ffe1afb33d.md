12:27 11/11/2019

We have charged a 32 year old man following a stabbing in Greasby which occurred on Saturday evening, 9th November.

At around 11.45pm, patrols were called to Greasby Road, Greasby to a report that a woman had been stabbed and child has been assaulted at a residential address.

The victims, a woman in her 30s and a seven year old child, were both taken to hospital for treatment. Thankfully, both victims injuries are not thought to be life threatening.

32-year-old Stephen Farrell of Upton Road, Moreton has been charged with attempted murder and assault. He was remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court, Derby Square today, 11th November.
