Wanted

05:00 14/12/2020

Do you know this man? James Hitchmough, 30, from Roby is wanted on recall to prison after breaching his licence conditions.

He was released on 7 March 2017 after serving part of a nine-year sentence for wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm.

Extensive enquiries are ongoing to trace him.

Hitchmough is white, 6ft tall, of medium build, with brown hair and brown eyes. He speaks with a Liverpool accent.

We are issuing one wanted appeal per day for the next 12 days to support the work of our Force Fugitive Team, so help us track these people down before Christmas.

The public are warned not to approach Hitchmough, and anyone with information on his whereabouts or any of our wanted people is asked to contact @MerPolCC or 101 with reference 20000518241.

You can also pass information anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. You can also contact them via their online form at: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/give-information/give-information.
