13:42 12/11/2019

We are appealing for help in finding the next of kin of a 75 year-old man.

William Kenneth Horwell, of Coulsdon Place, Dingle, died in the Royal Liverpool University Hospital on 7th November 2019.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anybody with information with regards to Mr Horwell's next of kin is asked to contact Coroner's Investigation Officer, Liam Moss, at the Coroner's Office on 0151 233 0125 or email liam.moss1@liverpool.gov.uk
