Can you help? Sefton Coroner's Office want to hear from the family of a Bootle man who recently died at home.

62-year-old Stephen John Greenhalgh passed away at his home address in Mount Avenue last Friday, 20 September.

Any family members or people with information are asked to ring (0151) 777 1921 or email sefton.coroners.office@merseyside.pnn.police.uk.
