16:09 22/12/2020

Detectives investigating the murder of Kyle Whitley in Litherland in April have arrested another man on suspicion of murder yesterday, Monday 21 December.

A 23-year-old man of no fixed abode but from Walton was arrested on suspicion of murder and conspiracy to supply Class A drugs. He has been released on conditional bail.

To date five people have been arrested in connection with Kyle's murder, three of whom remain under investigation.

Anyone with information on the murder of Kyle is asked to DM @MerPolCC or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111, quoting reference 20000215637.
