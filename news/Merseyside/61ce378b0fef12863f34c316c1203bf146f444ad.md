An 18-year-old man has been charged with possession of a firearm following an incident in November last year.

James Foy of Rossini Street, Seaforth, was charged with the possession of a firearm following an incident on Longfield Road, Litherland, on 3 November 2019.

He appeared at Liverpool Magistrates Court today (Thursday, 16 April).
