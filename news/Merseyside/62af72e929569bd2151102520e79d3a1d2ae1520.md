17:12 06/10/2020

We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a 48 year-old man from Tuebrook.

Darren Mather sadly passed away at his home address on Green Lane on Friday 25th September.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who has any information about Mr Mather's next of kin is asked to contact Coroner's Investigation Officer Liam Moss on 0151 233 0125 or email liam.moss@liverpool.gov.uk
