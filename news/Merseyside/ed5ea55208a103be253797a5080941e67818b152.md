10:35 28/05/2020

Detectives investigating an assault in Liverpool city centre have released CCTV images of two men they would like to speak to in connection with the incident.

Officers were called to Temple Court, at the junction with Victoria Street, at just after 4am on Friday 7th February 2020, to reports a man had been punched to the head. The victim sustained fractures to his nose, eye socket and sinus.

Detective Constable Rachel Gregory said "We are keen to speak to the two men in the CCTV images as we believe they may have information which will assist our ongoing investigation. I would also ask the public to contact us if they know who these individuals are or who have information about their whereabouts."

Anyone with information is asked to contact the Investigation Team on 0151 777 4945 or @MerPolCC or @CrimestoppersUK anonymously on 0800 555 111 quoting the reference 20000081308.
