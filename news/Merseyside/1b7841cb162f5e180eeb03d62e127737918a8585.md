14:41 19/09/2019

Sefton Coroner's Office is appealing for help to trace the next of kin of a man who recently died in Bootle.

Alan Evison, aged 66, died at his home address in Irlam Road, Bootle on Monday 16 September.

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone with information to help trace his next of kin is asked to contact the Coroners Office on 0151 934 2746.
