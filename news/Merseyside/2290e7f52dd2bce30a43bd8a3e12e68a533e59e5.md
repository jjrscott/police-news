16:29 11/03/2021

We are continuing our appeal for help in finding a 54 year-old Wirral man who is missing from home.

Darren Hyde, who is from Egremont, was last seen in Ellesmere Port on Saturday 27th February.

He is described as a white male, 5ft 9in tall, of proportionate build, with short grey hair, blue eyes and facial stubble.

When last seen he was wearing a thin black jacket with red on the bottom and dark jeans.

He is known to have links to the Liverpool, Wirral, Ellesmere Port and Chester areas.

Anyone who has seen Darren or knows of his whereabouts is asked to DM @MerPolCC or @missingpeople on 116 000.
