17:46 08/09/2020

We can confirm that detectives are investigating following the unexplained death of a woman in Fazakerley today, Tuesday 8 September.

At around 9am, officers were called to Dereham Crescent to reports of a woman in her 60s having been found with serious injuries. She was taken by ambulance to hospital where she was sadly pronounced deceased a short time later.

Enquiries are ongoing to establish the circumstances, and her death is currently being treated as unexplained. Her next of kin have been made aware and a post mortem will take place.

If you saw anything or have CCTV or dashcam which could assist, please contact @MerPolCC with reference 20000541569.
