Can you help? Liverpool Coroner's Office need to trace the next of kin of a Toxteth man who died last month.

Anyone with information is asked to call Elaine Jordan at Liverpool Coroner's Office on (0151) 233 0124 or email Elaine.Jordan@liverpool.gov.uk. Thanks.
