12:31 11/11/2020

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for information to help trace the next of kin of a man who recently died in Liverpool.

79-year-old John Colclough sadly died at his home address in Paul Orr Court, Liverpool on Wednesday, 4 November.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone with information that could help trace Mr Colclough's next of kin is asked to contact Coroners Investigation Officer Liam Moss on 0151 233 0125 or email Liam.Moss1@liverpool.gov.uk.
