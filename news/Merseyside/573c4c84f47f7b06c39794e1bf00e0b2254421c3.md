16:53 08/01/2020

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for the family of a woman from Waterloo who recently passed away to come forward.

Alison Hardman, 42 years, of Sandon Street sadly died at home on Wednesday 1 January.

Her death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who can help the coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call the Coroner's Office on 0151 233 2606 or email Jacqueline.A.Horner@merseyside.police.uk.
