15:10 25/09/2019

Detectives investigating a high-value theft from a Liverpool City Centre jewellers have arrested a man in connection with the offence.

At around midday on Sunday 31 August, jewellery was stolen from Beaverbrooks in St Johns Precinct, leading to extensive enquiries, including a CCTV appeal.

A 51-year-old man from Widnes in Cheshire has now been arrested on suspicion of theft and taken to a police station for questioning.

Anyone with information on this offence or any theft can contact @MerPolCC or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
