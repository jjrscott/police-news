14:01 17/09/2019

We are appealing for helping in tracing the next of kin of a man who died at his home in Wirral.

Eric Hiatt, 87, resided at Clifford Grange Sheltered Accommodation in Tranmere.

He sadly passed away at home on Wednesday 11th September.

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who knows of Mr Hiatt's next of kin is asked to contact the coroner's investigation officer on 0151 233 0143 or email Nicola.griffiths@liverpool.gov.uk
