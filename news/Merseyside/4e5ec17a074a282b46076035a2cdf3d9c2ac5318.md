08:54 22/09/2020

We can confirm a 21-year-old man has been charged in connection with the murder of Paul Morgan in Toxteth on Saturday, 19 September.

Joshua Morgan, of Prince William Street, Liverpool, was yesterday (Monday, 21 September) charged with murder and possession of a knife blade/sharp pointed article in a public place and remanded in custody to appear at South Sefton Magistrates Court today (Tuesday 22 September).

A post mortem revealed Paul Morgan, 39, died as a result of a stab wound to the chest.

An investigation is ongoing and anyone with information is asked to contact @MerPolCC or 101 quoting reference 20000568640. Alternatively you can contact Crimestoppers, anonymously on 0800 555 111.
