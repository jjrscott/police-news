12:03 16/02/2021

Police have charged a 24-year-old man with burglary after a house was broken into in Huyton last year.

At around 4am on Thursday 17 December, entry was gained to a house on Stanley Road. The offender made off empty-handed after making considerable damage to the property.

Following forensic and witness enquiries, detectives arrested and charged 24-year old Andrew Harris of no fixed abode with burglary on 14 February.

Harris was remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court on Monday 15 February.
