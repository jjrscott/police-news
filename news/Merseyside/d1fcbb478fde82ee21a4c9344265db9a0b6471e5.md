10:20 11/03/2021

Recognise this male? Detectives think he may have information following an incident in Waterloo earlier this month.

At around 4.30pm on Monday 1 March, a fight broke out involving males inside Mainstore newsagents in Waterloo. One person was punched and a second received an arm injury.

Enquiries are ongoing and two males, aged 31 and 17, have been arrested and remain under investigation.

If you recognise the male pictured or witnessed this incident, please contact us @MerPolCC on Twitter, 'Merseyside Police Contact Centre' on Facebook with crime reference 21000132844.

Information can also be passed on via 101 or the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 and online at https://crimestoppers-uk.org/give-information/forms/give-information-anonymously.
