****UPDATE \- 11PM FRIDAY 1ST MAY**

We have charged a 40 year-old man with theft by employee.

Gary Edwards, of Coxheath, Rock Ferry, Wirral, was remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool magistrates court tomorrow, Saturday 2nd May.**

ORIGINAL

A 40 year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of theft by employee.

Officers attended at an address in Bromborough, today, Friday 1st May, where a quantity of personal protective equipment (PPE) was seized.

The man is currently in custody where he will be questioned by detectives.

Detective Inspector Nick Suffield said: "Despite the Coronavirus lockdown our investigation work is ongoing and we will continue to act on information supplied to us."
