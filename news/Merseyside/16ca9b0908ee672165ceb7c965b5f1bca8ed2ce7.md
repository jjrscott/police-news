00:02 28/01/2020

We have arrested a man in connection with a suspected arson in Huyton tonight, Monday, 27 January.

At around 8.10pm, it was reported that a house was on fire in Kingsway. Emergency services attended and 51-year-old man from Runcorn was arrested on suspicion of arson with intent to endanger life and also on suspicion of assault and was taken to a local police station for questioning.

Enquiries are ongoing and anyone with information is asked to contact @MerPolCC, call 101 with reference 0943 of 21/1/20, or you can provide information to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
