11:36 06/07/2020

We are appealing for help in locating the next of kin of a Wirral man.

David Crosbie, 68, sadly passed away at his home address in Sidney Street, Birkenhead, on the afternoon of Thursday 2nd July.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding his death.

Anyone who knows of Mr Crosbie's next of kin is asked to contact Coroner's investigation officer Paula White on 0151 233 0126 or email paula.white@liverpool.gov.uk
