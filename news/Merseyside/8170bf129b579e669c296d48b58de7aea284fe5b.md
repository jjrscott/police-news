16:22 22/03/2021

Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing to find the family of Catherine Threlfall, aged 89, of Church Street, Wallasey.

Catherine sadly died on Saturday 20 March. There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone who can help the Coroner identify or trace any potential next of kin should call Katy Porter on 0151 233 0130 or email HMCoroner@liverpool.gov.uk.
