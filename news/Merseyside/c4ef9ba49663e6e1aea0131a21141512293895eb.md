Merseyside Police can confirm that a man has been arrested and charged following an incident in Bootle on Monday, 2 March.

At around 7.25pm, a call was received from a woman alleging a sexual offence had taken place.

52-year-old Rodney Chidi Akomas from Fairfield, was charged on suspicion of sexual assault on a female.

Akomas was remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court on Wednesday, 13 May 2020.
