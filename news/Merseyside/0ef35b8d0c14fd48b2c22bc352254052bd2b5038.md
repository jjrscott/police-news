13:32 08/10/2019

Liverpool & Wirral Coroner's Office is appealing for help to trace the next of kin of a man who recently died at the Royal Liverpool University Hospital.

Brian Gould, aged 82, of North Drive, Wavertree died in hospital on 3 October.

His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone with information to help trace his next of kin is asked to contact Coroners Investigating Officer Elaine Jordan on 0151 233 0124 or Elaine.Jordan@liverpool.gov.uk.
