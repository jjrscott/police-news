Updated News Post

**Update:**

**Merseyside Police can confirm that the 46-year-old woman who died in Stockbridge Village on Wednesday (1 January 2020) was Magdalena Pacult.**

Orignal statement:

We have charged a man in connection with the death of a 46-year-old woman at a house in Stockbridge Village on Wednesday, 1 January.

Officers attended at the address in Custley Hey following a report of concern for safety of one of the occupants.

When officers attended they found the body of the woman.

A Home Office post mortem confirmed the cause of death was blunt force trauma, mechanical asphyxiation.

48-year-old Piotr Cichy of Custley Hey, Stockbridge Village has been charged with murder. He has been remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court today, Friday, 3 January.

An investigation is ongoing and anyone with information is asked to contact @MerPolCC, 101 quoting reference 712 of 1 January 2020 or @CrimestoppersUK, anonymously on 0800 555 111.
