10:45 25/01/2021

We are appealing for help in finding a 14 year-old boy who is missing from home in Liverpool.

Connor Beswick, was last seen on 11.50pm on Tuesday 19th January in Hornsey Road, Anfield.

He is described as a white male, 5ft 6in tall, of medium build with shoulder length dark brown hair and blue eyes. He speaks with a Liverpool accent.

When last seen he was wearing a black North Face coat, black North Face tracksuit bottoms and black/grey Nike trainers.

He is known to frequent the Liverpool and Knowsley areas.

Anyone who has seen Connor or knows of his whereabouts is asked to DM @MerPolCC or @missingpeople on 116 000.
