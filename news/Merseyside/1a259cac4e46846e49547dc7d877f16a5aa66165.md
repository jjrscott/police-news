We are appealing for help in tracing the next of kin of a 69 year-old man from Wirral.

Peter Williams, died at his home address in Castle Street, Birkenhead, on Saturday 28th December 2019. His death is not being treated as suspicious.

Anyone who can assist with the appeal is asked to contact Coroner's officer Lesley-Anne Rogers on 0151 233 0137 or by emailing Lesley-anne.rogers@liverpool.gov.uk
