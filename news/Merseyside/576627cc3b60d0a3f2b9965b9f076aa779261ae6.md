20:11 26/02/2021

Detectives have charged a man in connection with an investigation into the supply of class A drugs.

53-year-old Garry Reid, of Ackers Road, Woodchurch, has been charged with conspiracy to supply Class A drugs (cocaine and heroin) and conspiracy to supply Class B drugs (cannabis).

The charge comes as part of national Operation Venetic.

He has been remanded in custody to appear at court tomorrow, Saturday, 27 February.
