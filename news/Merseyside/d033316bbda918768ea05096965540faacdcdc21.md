23:05 26/06/2020

Officers have today (26th June) charged a two men with attempt murder following a shooting at the McDonalds Drive-thru on 18th June.

James Nolan, 30 of Thomas Drive, Prescot and James O'Neil, 33 of Leyfield Road, Liverpool have been remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool Crown Court tomorrow morning.
