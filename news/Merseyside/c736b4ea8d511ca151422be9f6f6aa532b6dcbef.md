13:28 30/09/2020

Can you help? Liverpool and Wirral Coroner's Office are looking to find the next of kin of a Wirral man who recently passed away.

Joseph Ashley, 73, sadly died at home at Castleway South, Moreton on Wednesday (23 September). There are no suspicious circumstances.

Anyone with information that may help locate Mr Ashley's next of kin can call Coroners Investigation Officer Nicola Griffiths on 0151 233 0131 or email Nicola.Griffiths@liverpool.gov.uk
