17:30 29/01/2021

We are appealing for help in finding a 17 year-old girl who is missing from home.

Jade Johnson was last seen in the Huyton area at 6pm on Tuesday 26th January.

She is described as white, 5ft 2in tall, of slim build with shoulder length brown hair worn in a ponytail, a pale complexion and blue eyes. She also wear glasses.

When last seen she was wearing a purple coat with fur around the hood, a black hooded top, pale blue jeans, white and pink Nike trainers and she was carrying a bag.

She is known to frequent the Huyton and Kirkby areas as well as the L4, L20 and L21 areas.

Anyone who has seen Jade or knows of her whereabouts is asked to DM @MerPolCC or contact@missingpeople on 116 000.
