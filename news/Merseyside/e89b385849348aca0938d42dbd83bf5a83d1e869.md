16:12 23/12/2020

We're appealing for witnesses following a road traffic collision in Prenton on Monday, 21 December.

At 5.40pm, officers were called to Woodchurch Road near the junction with Palmwood Close following reports a blue BMW had collided with a traffic light.

Emergency services attended and the driver, a man in his 30's, was taken to hospital for treatment to minor injuries.

An investigation is underway to establish the full circumstances and officers are keen to speaking to anyone who witnessed the incident, or has any dashcam or CCTV footage from the area.

Anyone with information is asked to contact the Roads Policing Unit on 0151 777 5747, @MerPolCC, or 101 quoting ref. 20000774701. You can also contact Crimestoppers, anonymously on 0800 555 111.
