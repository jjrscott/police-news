21:52 17/06/2020

We can confirm the man taken to hospital following an incident in Netherton on Monday, 15 June, has passed away.

Emergency services were called to Park Lane West at around 7.45pm following reports a 51-year-old man had collapsed and sustained a serious head injury.

He was taken to hospital and sadly died today, Wednesday, 17 June.

Following enquires, the death is not being treated as suspicious and a police investigation has concluded.

A file will be prepared for the coroner.
