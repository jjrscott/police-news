A 37-year-old man from Anfield, arrested by #OpTarget officers, has been charged with perverting the course of justice, possess articles for use in fraud, possession of criminal property, disqualified driving, driving a motor vehicle not in accordance with a licence and two counts of driving a motor vehicle with no insurance.

Mark Taylor, 37, years from Newsham Drive, will appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court today(Wednesday, 19 February).
