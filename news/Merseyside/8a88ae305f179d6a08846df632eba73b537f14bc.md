17:49 15/12/2020

A man has been charged after warrants were executed in Kirkby yesterday (Monday 14 December) as part of an investigation into County Lines drug supply.

Kevin McCabe, 28, of Pershore Road, Southdene, Kirkby was charged with three counts of disqualified driving, three counts of driving with no insurance, dangerous driving, possession with intent to supply a Class A drug, two counts of making an offer to supply drugs and assault of an emergency worker.

He has been remanded in custody to appear at Liverpool Adult Remand Court on Wednesday, 16 December.

A 28-year old woman from Kirkby arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class A drugs and a 50-year-old man from Kirby arrested on suspicion of being concerned in making an offer to supply controlled drugs have both been released under investigation.
