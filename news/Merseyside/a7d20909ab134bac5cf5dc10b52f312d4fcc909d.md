12:25 25/11/2020

We can confirm that officers have been in St Helens following an RTC this morning, Wednesday 25 November.

At around 8.30am, emergency services were called to Park Road to reports of a collision involving a Ford Fiesta Zetec and a 14-year-old girl pedestrian. The girl was taken to hospital for assessment of injuries which have now been confirmed as minor.

The road has been reopened.
