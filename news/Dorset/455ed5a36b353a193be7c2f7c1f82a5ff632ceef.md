# Witness appeal following reported assault in Bournemouth

08 April 2019

* * *

Detectives investigating an alleged assault in Bournemouth town centre are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

At 1.34pm on Saturday 6 April 2019 officers were called to an incident in The Square.

A man aged in his 40s, who claimed he had been assaulted, suffered a minor puncture wound and was taken to hospital for treatment.

A 51-year-old man of no fixed abode was arrested on suspicion of assault and has been released under investigation as detectives continue to make enquiries.

Detective Constable Michelle Lawrence, of Bournemouth CID, said: "I understand there were a number of members of the public in the area at the time of this incident and I would ask any witnesses who have not already spoken to police to please contact us."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190051806. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
