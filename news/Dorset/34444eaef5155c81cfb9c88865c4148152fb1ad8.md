# Witness appeal after man found with serious injuries in Bournemouth

15 June 2017

* * *

Detectives are appealing for witnesses to come forward after a man was found with life-threatening injuries in Bournemouth.

At 12.32am yesterday, Wednesday 14 June 2017 the ambulance service called Dorset Police to inform them that a man had been found outside an address in Carnarvon Road. It is understood the man fell from a height.

The local man aged in his 40s was taken to Southampton General Hospital with life-threatening injuries. His next of kin have been informed.

Detective Constable Sarah Close, of Bournemouth CID, said: "Enquiries are underway to establish how this man sustained his injuries and we are trying to find out if he fell or whether someone else was involved.

"I would urge anyone who was in the Carnarvon Road area in the early hours of yesterday morning and witnessed what happened to please contact Dorset Police."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170088638. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
