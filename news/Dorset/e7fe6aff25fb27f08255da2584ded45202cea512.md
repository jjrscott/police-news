# Witness appeal following burglary in Weymouth

10 August 2018

* * *

Officers investigating a burglary in Weymouth are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

It was reported that between 7pm yesterday, Thursday 9 August 2018, and 12.45am today, Friday 10 August 2018, a break-in occurred at a home in Alexandra Road.

Entry is believed to have been gained through a ground floor window and an untidy search was carried out. Two Asus laptops, a purse containing cash and cards and various items of jewellery were taken from the property.

Police Constable Katherine Rupp, of Weymouth police, said: "We are carrying out a number of enquiries, including house to house and examination of the crime scene, as we investigate this burglary.

"I am appealing for anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity in the area of Alexandra Road around the relevant times or who has any information about this burglary to please contact us."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180128204. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
