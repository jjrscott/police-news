# Arrests made following alleged robbery at Bournemouth store

18 December 2018

* * *

Detectives have arrested two men in connection with an alleged robbery at a Bournemouth store.

At around 5.20am on Saturday 15 December 2018, it was reported that a man entered Costcutter on Wimborne Road in Winton, approached the counter allegedly brandishing a large knife and stole a quantity of cash from the till.

No one sustained any physical injuries.

An 18-year-old Bournemouth man was arrested on suspicion of robbery. A 19-year-old Bournemouth man was arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to commit robbery. Both men are assisting officers with enquiries.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180200851. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
