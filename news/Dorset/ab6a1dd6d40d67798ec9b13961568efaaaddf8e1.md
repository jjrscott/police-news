# CCTV appeal following burglary in Weymouth

03 February 2021

* * *

Officers investigating a burglary at Weymouth College are issuing a CCTV image of the suspect.

The incident occurred at around 8.10am on Saturday 30 January 2021. A man entered the college grounds and used a stone slab to break a window panel before entering a building. It is not believed anything was taken.

Police Constable Will Harris, of Weymouth police, said: "We are continuing to make enquiries into this burglary and we have obtained a CCTV image of the suspect. I would urge anyone with information as to his identity to please contact us.

"I am also releasing an image of the distinctive stone slab that was used to break the window as it is not believed to have come from the college site and I am appealing to anyone who recognises it to get in touch."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55210019176. Alternatively, to stay 100 per cent anonymous, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers online at Crimestoppers-uk.org or call Freephone 0800 555 111.
