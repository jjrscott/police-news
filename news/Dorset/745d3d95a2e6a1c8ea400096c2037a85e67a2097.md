# Witness appeal after a reported assault in Bournemouth

03 August 2017

* * *

Detectives are appealing for witnesses after a woman reported an assault in Bournemouth.

Police received a report at 9.42pm on Tuesday 1 August 2017 that a woman had been assaulted and sustained an injury in Heathlands Walk, which is a footpath between Mandale Road and Maclean Road in the West Howe area.

The woman, aged in her 50s, was walking her dog when she was knocked to the ground by a man, causing a fracture to her shoulder. She was taken to hospital for treatment.

Detective Constable Chris Waters, of Bournemouth CID, said: "We are investigating the circumstances around the incident. I would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed what happened or saw anything suspicious in the area around the time of the incident to please contact Dorset Police."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170118262. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
