# Witness appeal after serious assault in Bournemouth

21 April 2017

* * *

Detectives are appealing for information after a serious assault in Bournemouth.

The incident in Charminster Road near to the Golden Supermarket was reported by a third party to officers at around 5.50am today, Friday 21 April 2017, with the victim having left the scene.

It was later reported that the victim, a 43-year-old man of an unknown address, had been admitted to hospital with serious neck and head injuries.

A 44-year-old Bournemouth man has been arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm with intent and is assisting officers with enquiries.

Detective Inspector Wayne Seymour, of Bournemouth CID, said: "This victim has suffered very serious, potentially life-changing injuries.

"Our enquiries into this incident are continuing and I am keen to here from any witnesses who may have seen this assault in the early hours of this morning in the Charminster Road area."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting incident number 21:44. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
