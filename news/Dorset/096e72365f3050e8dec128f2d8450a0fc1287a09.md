# Witness appeal after reported assault in Poole

26 March 2018

* * *

Detectives investigating an alleged assault in Poole are appealing for any witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

The incident was reported to have occurred at around 3.45pm on Friday 23 March 2018 along Adastral Road in Canford Heath.

The victim - a local man aged in his 30s - was walking along when he was allegedly assaulted and punched to the head. He suffered a broken jaw and was taken to hospital following the incident, but has since been discharged.

A 30-year-old man from Poole was arrested on suspicion of assault and has been released under investigation.

Detective Constable Taylor, of Bournemouth CID, said: "Following a number of enquiries I am appealing for anyone who was in Adastral Road at the relevant time and witnessed the incident or has any information that could assist with the investigation to please contact Dorset Police.

"Finally, I would ask any motorists who were driving in the area at around 3.45pm on Friday 23 March 2018 and have dash cams fitted in their vehicles to please make contact."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180043864. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 55511 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
