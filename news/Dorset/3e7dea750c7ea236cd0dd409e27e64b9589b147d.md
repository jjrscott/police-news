# Appeal for witnesses following criminal damage to car in Bournemouth

16 September 2019

* * *

Officers investigating criminal damage to a car in Bournemouth are appealing for any witnesses to come forward.

Between 7pm on Friday 13 September 2019 and midday on Saturday 14 September 2019 a grey Jaguar S-Type was damaged on Pembroke Road near to the junction with Alumhurst Road.

The damage included four slashed tyres, smashed windows and wing mirrors, scratched doors, damaged windscreen wipers, the number plates and badge were removed and liquid poured over the vehicle.

Police Constable Tom Ringer, of Bournemouth police, said: "I am appealing to anyone who witnessed the criminal damage incident to please get in touch.

"I would also like to hear from anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area at the relevant time."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190144999. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
