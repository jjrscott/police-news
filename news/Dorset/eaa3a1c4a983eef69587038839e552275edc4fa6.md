# Appeal following alleged assault in Bournemouth

05 February 2019

* * *

Officers are appealing for witnesses following an alleged assault in Bournemouth.

The incident happened at around 6.40pm on Monday 4 February 2019 on Wimborne Road in Winton between St Luke's Road and Alma Road.

It was reported that an altercation occurred between a man and a woman. During the incident it is reported that the woman was assaulted. She did not require hospital treatment.

Both the man and woman were believed to be known to each other.

A 35-year-old local man was arrested on suspicion of assault and is assisting officers with enquiries.

Police Sergeant Sophie Williams, of Bournemouth police, said: "I understand that there were a number of people in the area at the time of the incident and am appealing to anyone who witnessed what happened to please come forward."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190018282. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
