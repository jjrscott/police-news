# Witness appeal following indecent exposure in Dorchester

15 November 2016

* * *

Detectives are appealing for witnesses following an indecent exposure incident in Dorchester.

At around 6.45am today, Tuesday 15 November 2016, a woman was in an address off Buckingham Way when she spotted a man outside the rear of the property. The offender indecently exposed himself before running away.

The offender is described as being of medium build, approximately five feet nine inches tall and was wearing jogging bottoms and a long-sleeved navy blue top. His face was covered.

Detective Constable Sarah Desmond, of Western CID, said: "I am appealing to anyone who saw a man matching the description given in the area at the relevant time to please contact Dorset Police urgently."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55160171627. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
