# Woman arrested after reported robbery in Bournemouth

05 April 2018

* * *

Officers have made an arrest after a woman on a mobility scooter was reportedly robbed in Bournemouth.

The victim - a vulnerable woman aged in her 70s - was riding her mobility scooter along Carbery Avenue at around 4.15pm yesterday, Wednesday 4 April 2018.

She became aware of a cyclist approaching her from behind who is alleged to have grabbed a handbag and plastic bag from the basket on her mobility scooter before pushing the mobility scooter, causing it to topple over.

The victim was taken to hospital, where she was treated for a broken arm before being discharged later that evening.

A 31-year-old woman from Bournemouth has been arrested on suspicion of robbery and is assisting officers with enquiries.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180049956. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
