# Witness appeal following theft of car from Gillingham

07 June 2019

* * *

Officers are appealing for witnesses to come forward after a car was stolen from a hotel car park in Gillingham.

Sometime between 5pm on Wednesday 5 June and 6.20am on Thursday 6 June 2019 a grey Seat Leon with the registration YC64 SJY was stolen from the Red Lion pub car park on the High Street.

Inspector Simon Perry, of North Dorset police, said: "We are carrying out a number of enquiries in relation to this incident, including house-to-house visits and CCTV checks, and I would appeal to anyone who knows who was responsible for this theft or knows of the whereabouts of the vehicle to please contact Dorset Police."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk, or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190085217. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
