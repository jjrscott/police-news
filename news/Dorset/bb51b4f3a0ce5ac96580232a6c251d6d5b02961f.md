# Witness appeal following theft of tractor in West Dorset

03 March 2017

* * *

Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward after thieves stole a tractor from a farm in West Dorset.

Sometime between 5pm on Friday 24 February and 7am on Saturday 25 February 2017 a new blue Holland Tractor T50/60 with the registration of HF11 FSS was stolen from a farm near Halstock.

The tractor was driven out of the farm and southbound toward Winyards Cap on the A358.

Prior to reaching the A358, the tractor was driven through a padlocked metal farm gate before continuing toward the A358.

Police Constable John Poe, of Dorset Police, said: "I am appealing to anyone who saw or heard anything suspicious in the area of Halstock between the relevant times to please contact me.

"I am also keen to hear from anyone who witnessed the tractor being driven toward the A358 or knows of its current whereabouts.

"Dorset Police is committed toward tackling rural crime and we would advise businesses and residents to please visit dorset.police.uk for crime prevention advice and how to contact their local officers."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170028503. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
