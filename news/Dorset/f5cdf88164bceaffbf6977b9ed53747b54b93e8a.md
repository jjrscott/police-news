# Man arrested in connection with reported theft of bicycles at Royal Bournemouth Hospital

07 April 2020

* * *

Officers investigating the alleged theft of bicycles belonging to staff at the Royal Bournemouth Hospital have arrested a man.

Three bicycles were reportedly stolen on Monday 30 March and Tuesday 31 March 2020 from outside the hospital.

Following enquiries by officers from the local neighbourhood policing team, a 23-year-old man from London was arrested on suspicion of theft and has been released under investigation.

Inspector Nick Lee, of Dorset Police, said: "We continue to support our NHS staff who are working tirelessly to treat those suffering from COVID-19 (coronavirus).

"Our investigation into these thefts is ongoing and I would urge anyone with information to please contact us."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55200046968. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
