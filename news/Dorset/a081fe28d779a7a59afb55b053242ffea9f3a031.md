# Burglary in Poole â€“ witness appeal

06 April 2017

* * *

Officers are appealing for witnesses to come forward following a burglary in Poole - with images being released of some of the stolen items.

Sometime between Thursday 30 March and Sunday 2 April 2017 offenders gained access to a property on Canford Cliffs Road in the town.

A large quantity of cash and a number of items of jewellery and silver were stolen.

The stolen items of jewellery include a diamond encrusted cross necklace, a Boodle and Dunthorne diamond bracelet, a white and gold diamond ring with matching earrings, a white gold garnet and diamond ring and a large pearl choker-style necklace.

Police Constable Steven Donaldson, of Poole Neighbourhood Policing Team, said: "I am releasing pictures of some of the items of stolen jewellery and am appealing to anyone who has seen these items for sale under suspicious circumstances to please contact Dorset Police.

"I would also like to hear from anyone who saw someone acting suspiciously around Canford Cliffs Road during the relevant times."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170047904. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
