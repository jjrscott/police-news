# Update - appeal after man found with head injury in Bournemouth

21 August 2018

* * *

Detectives investigating the circumstances around an incident where a man was found with a head injury in Bournemouth have made an arrest.

Dorset Police was called by the ambulance service at 2.24am today, Tuesday 21 August 2018, reporting that they had conveyed an injured man to hospital. He had sustained a potentially serious head injury.

He was located on Carysfort Road near to the junction with Hamilton Road.

A 32-year-old man from Bournemouth has been arrested on suspicion of committing grievous bodily harm with intent and is assisting officers with enquiries.

A cordon remains in place to allow officers to examine the scene.

Detective Inspector Wayne Seymour, of Bournemouth CID, said: "We continue to investigate the circumstances around how the man came to sustain the injury and I am appealing to anyone who witnessed anything suspicious in the area or saw what happened to the man to please come forward. The timeframe we are specifically looking at is sometime between 11pm on Monday and the early hours of this morning.

"If you have any information that could assist with the investigation, please contact Dorset Police urgently or call Crimestoppers anonymously."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk, or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180134798. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
