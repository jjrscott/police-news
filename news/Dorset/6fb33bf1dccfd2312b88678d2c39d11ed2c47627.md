# Witness appeal following burglary in Poole

03 February 2017

* * *

Officers are appealing for witnesses to come forward following a burglary in Poole - with images being released of some of the stolen items.

Sometime between Tuesday 10 January and Tuesday 28 January 2017 offenders gained access to a property in The Avenue in the town.

A number of items of jewellery and a gold-coloured iPhone 5S were stolen.

The stolen items of jewellery include three rings, one of which was sapphire, a diamond bracelet and two men's Ebel watches which are described as steel and gold.

Police Constable Mark Palmer, of Dorset Police, said: "I am releasing pictures of some of the items of stolen jewellery and am appealing to anyone who has seen these items, or any of the other items described, for sale under suspicious circumstances to please contact Dorset Police.

"I would also like to hear from anyone who saw someone acting suspiciously around The Avenue during the relevant times."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170014398. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
