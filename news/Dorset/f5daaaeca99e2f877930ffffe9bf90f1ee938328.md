# Witness appeal following collision in Weymouth

08 April 2020

* * *

Officers are appealing for witnesses to come forward following a collision in Weymouth.

At around 7.30pm on Monday 6 April 2020 it was reported that a light blue Ford Ecosport was in collision with a stationary dark blue Land Rover Discovery on Dorchester Road.

It was reported that the Ford Ecosport then drove away in the direction of Upwey.

Both cars sustained damage in the incident.

Police Constable Ryan Lloyd, of Weymouth police, said: "We are continuing to investigate this incident and I am keen to hear from any witnesses to the collision, or the manner of driving prior to it, to please contact us.

"I would also urge anyone who was driving in the area and has dashcam fitted to their vehicle to check their footage for anything relevant."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55200049672. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
