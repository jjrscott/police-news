# Witness appeal after serious road traffic collision in Bournemouth

27 April 2017

* * *

Officers are appealing for witnesses after a serious road traffic collision in Bournemouth.

At 11.45am yesterday, Wednesday 26 April 2017, police received a report of a collision in Southbourne Road involving a pedestrian and a car.

As a result of the collision the pedestrian, a Bournemouth woman aged in her 80s, suffered serious injuries to her legs and head.

The car involved was a silver Fiat Multipla.

Road closures were put in place while emergency services dealt with the incident.

Police Constable Craig Tatton, of the traffic unit, said: "I would ask anyone who witnessed the collision who has not already spoken to officers to please contact me."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting incident number 26:134. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via crimestoppers-uk.org.
