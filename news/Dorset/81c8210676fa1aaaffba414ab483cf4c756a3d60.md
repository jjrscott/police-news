# Witness appeal following burglary at Bournemouth store

15 November 2019

* * *

Officers investigating a burglary at a store in Bournemouth are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

At around 11.30pm on Monday 11 November 2019 a break-in occurred at the Boots store in Wimborne Road.

A group of four or five people are believed to have forced open the front door and stole a quantity of perfume and aftershave. The exact value of the goods stolen has not yet been confirmed, but is believed to be several thousand pounds.

The offenders are believed to have left the store through a side door heading onto Leslie Road.

Police Constable Chloe Chapman, of North Bournemouth police, said: "We are continuing to investigate this burglary and I would urge anyone with information to come forward.

"I am keen to speak to anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity in the area on the night of Monday 11 November 2019."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190178278. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
