# Witness appeal after alleged assault in Swanage

08 June 2017

* * *

Detectives investigating reports of an assault in Swanage are appealing for witnesses to come forward.

The incident, involving two 15-year-old local boys, happened in an alleyway off Marsh Way at around 9.30pm on Sunday 28 May 2017.

It was reported that the victim was pushed to the ground before being punched five times to the head and kicked to the legs and stomach.

The altercation stopped when a nearby resident was alerted to the noise and went into the alleyway to see what was happening.

The victim did not require any hospital treatment.

A 15-year-old boy from Swanage has been arrested on suspicion of assault in connection with the incident and has been released under investigation.

Detective Constable Lucy Cummings, of Weymouth CID, said: "We are continuing to investigate this incident and I would ask anyone who witnessed the assault, or who saw the two teenagers in the alleyway around the relevant time, to please contact us."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170079173. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
