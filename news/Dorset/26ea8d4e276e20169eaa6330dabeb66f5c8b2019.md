# Witness appeal following reported assault in Bournemouth

18 November 2019

* * *

Detectives investigating a reported assault on a bus in Bournemouth are appealing for witnesses to come forward.

At approximately 11.10pm on Friday 15 November 2019 it was reported that an assault occurred on a bus that was stopped on Palmerston Road.

The alleged assault involved four boys and one man.

The man, aged in his 20s and from Christchurch, was taken to hospital for treatment to head and facial injuries.

Three boys from Verwood and one from Blandford, all aged 16, were arrested on suspicion of inflicting grievous bodily harm without intent. All four boys have been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Detective Constable Laura Smith, of Bournemouth CID, said: "I believe there were four male passengers on the bus at the time of the alleged assault and I am appealing to those men to come forward as they may have information to assist my investigation."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190180882. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
