# Witness appeal following indecent exposure in Poole

29 January 2018

* * *

Officers investigating an incident of indecent exposure in Poole are appealing for any witnesses to come forward.

The victim, a local woman aged in her 40s, was at the junction of Lakeside Road and Tower West Road at around 2.35pm yesterday, Sunday 28 January 2018, when a man exposed himself in front of her.

The offender is described as white, aged around 30 years old, five feet nine inches tall and of medium build. He wore a dark fleece and grey tracksuit bottoms.

The victim left the scene in her car before contacting the police.

Police Constable Scott Thorne, of Poole police, said: "This was obviously a distressing incident for the victim and an investigation is underway to identify the offender.

"I am appealing for any witnesses to the incident, or anyone who saw a man matching the description given in the area around the relevant time, to contact police."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180014597. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestopppers-uk.org.
