# Witness appeal following criminal damage in Bournemouth

09 January 2020

* * *

Officers investigating an incident of criminal damage of two vehicles in Bournemouth town centre are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

At around 3am on Monday 6 January 2020 it was reported that a man pushed over a motorbike onto a parked car on Madeira Road at the junction with Old Christchurch Road.

Both vehicles were damaged in the incident.

The man was reported to be intoxicated.

Police Constable Heidi Nielsen said: "We are continuing to make enquiries into this incident.

"I am appealing to anyone who witnessed the criminal damage to please get in touch."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55200002229. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
