# Update â€“ man arrested following reported commercial burglary in Poole

09 November 2018

* * *

A man has been arrested in connection with a reported commercial burglary by two men on a moped in Poole.

The incident occurred at around 8.15pm on Monday 29 October 2018 at Central Fast Food Supplies on the Technology Road Industrial Estate in Poole.

It was reported that two people riding one moped used their vehicle to ram the doors of the unit and force entry. A quantity of cash was allegedly taken from inside the premises.

Following enquiries, a 38-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of burglary and has been released under investigation.

Anyone with information about the incident is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180175196. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
