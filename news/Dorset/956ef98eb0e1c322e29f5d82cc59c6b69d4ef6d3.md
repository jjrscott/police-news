# Appeal for witnesses after reported assault in Bournemouth

01 August 2017

* * *

Detectives are appealing for witnesses following an alleged assault in a Bournemouth street.

Police received a report of an assault in Wootton Gardens at around 10.30pm yesterday, Monday 31 July 2017.

A man, aged in his 50s, was found in a property near to the scene of incident with a serious head injury.

He was taken to hospital for treatment to his injury, which is not thought to be life threatening.

A 58-year-old Poole man has been arrested on suspicion of inflicting grievous bodily harm with intent and is assisting officers with their enquiries.

Detective Constable Nicky Sharpe, of Bournemouth CID, said: "Our investigation is ongoing to establish exactly what happened and we are keen to hear from any witnesses or anyone with information about this incident."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170117709. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
