# Witness appeal following burglary at store in Wareham

16 April 2018

* * *

Detectives are appealing for witnesses to come forward following a break-in at a store in Wareham.

At 2.19am on Monday 16 April 2018 Dorset Police was called to reports of a burglary involving two offenders at Horsey's Newsagent in North Street.

Officers attended and carried out a search of the area, assisted by the NPAS helicopter.

A 42-year-old man of no fixed abode was arrested nearby on suspicion of burglary and has been released under investigation. No other arrests have been made.

Detective Sergeant Nicky Jenkins, of Weymouth CID, said: "A full investigation is underway into this incident and enquiries are being conducted to establish what, if anything, was stolen.

"I would urge anyone who saw or heard anything suspicious in the area of North Street in the early hours of Monday morning to please contact Dorset Police.

"I am also keen to hear from anyone who finds bags or other items discarded in the local area. Please do not touch the items but call us immediately."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180056071. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
