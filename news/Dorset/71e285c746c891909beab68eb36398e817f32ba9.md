# Public appeal following indecent exposure incident in Poole

27 September 2016

* * *

Police in Poole are appealing for the public's help following an indecent exposure incident in the town.

At around 9.55pm on Monday 19 September 2016 a woman was walking along Ashley Road when a man indecently exposed himself. The offender was stood in an alleyway between the Co-operative food store and E-Cigarette and Vaping Centre.

The offender is described as white, approximately 26 years old, five feet 11 inches tall, of medium build and with short light-coloured hair. He was wearing grey tracksuit trousers that were gathered at the bottom and a grey hooded top with a pouch at the front.

Police Constable Alexandra Kimmins, of Poole police, said: "I am appealing to anyone who was in Ashley Road at the relevant time and saw a man acting suspiciously close to the Co-operative food store to please contact Dorset Police.

"I would also like to hear from anyone who recognises the man from the description given."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55160142248. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
