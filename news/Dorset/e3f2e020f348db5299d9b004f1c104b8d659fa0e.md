# Witness appeal following alleged assault in Christchurch

20 March 2018

* * *

Officers investigating an alleged assault on a woman in Christchurch are appealing for witnesses to come forward.

The incident occurred sometime between 10pm and 11pm on Saturday 17 March 2018 outside Arcado Lounge on High Street.

It is alleged the victim - a 27-year-old woman from Bournemouth - was pushed into a street junction box and assaulted.

A 31-year-old man from Bournemouth, who is known to the victim, was arrested on suspicion of assault and has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Investigations officer Lucy Wickham, of Bournemouth CID, said: "I am appealing to anyone who witnessed the incident to please contact Dorset Police.

"I am particularly keen to hear from some passers-by who went to the victim's aid during the incident as I believe they may have important information that could assist my investigation."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk, or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180040639. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
