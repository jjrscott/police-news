# Witness appeal following arson in Bournemouth

03 September 2019

* * *

Officers investigating a suspicious fire on heathland in Bournemouth are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

At approximately 5.45pm on Thursday 29 August 2019 a fire was reported on heathland below East Overcliff Drive.

The area was cordoned off while emergency services were at the scene.

The fire service extinguished the blaze and there were no reports of injuries or damage to any buildings.

Police Constable Paul McKenna, of Bournemouth police, said: "We have been carrying out a number of enquiries into this incident and I would like to hear from anyone who may have seen suspicious activity in the area prior to the incident.

"This fire took place in a busy part of the town during the Bournemouth Air Festival. If you saw anything that may assist my investigation, please get in touch."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190136120. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
