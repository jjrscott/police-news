# Witness appeal after assault in Bournemouth

27 July 2019

* * *

Detectives investigating an assault in Bournemouth are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

The incident, which happened at an address in Exeter Park Road, was reported 4.52pm on Saturday 27 July 2019.

An altercation took place involving three men. One of the man sustained stab wounds to his shoulder, which are not believed to be life-threatening. The other two men left the location before officers attended.

Detective Inspector Gavin House, of Bournemouth CID, said: "We are making a number of enquiries to establish who the men involved were. Therefore people will see an increased police presence in the area.

"I am appealing for any witnesses to the incident or anyone with information about those involved to come forward."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk, or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190116399. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
