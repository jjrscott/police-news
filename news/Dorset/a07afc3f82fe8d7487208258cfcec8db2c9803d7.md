# Witness appeal following serious collision in Blandford

17 November 2016

* * *

Police are appealing for witnesses and anyone with information to come forward following a serious road traffic collision on the A357 in Blandford.

The collision, involving a car and a pedestrian, occurred along the A357 in Durweston at around 10.39am today, Thursday 17 November 2016.

A black Peugot 107 collided with a local woman aged in her 70's.

As a result of the collision, the pedestrian suffered life-changing injuries and was taken to Southampton General Hospital by air ambulance. The driver of the Peugot was uninjured.

The road remained closed for several hours while emergency services dealt with the incident.

Dorset Police would like to thank members of the public for their patience during the road closure.

Police Sergeant Phil Lee, of Dorset Police's Road Policing Team, said: "I am appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision or the manner of driving of the Peugot prior to the incident to contact Dorset Police."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting incident number 17:130. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
