# Update â€“ witness appeal following alleged serious assault in Poole

18 October 2017

* * *

Detectives investigating an alleged serious assault in Poole have arrested a man on suspicion of assisting an offender.

An investigation was launched following an incident at Sterte Close at around 2.20am on Monday 9 October 2017.

Emergency services attended and two men were found with stab wounds to their backs. A 34-year-old man of no fixed abode sustained injuries that were initially believed to be life threatening and a 45-year-old man from Poole was seriously injured. Both have now left hospital.

Following enquiries by officers, a 43-year-old man of no fixed abode has been arrested on suspicion of assisting an offender.

Detectives are continuing to investigate the incident and are appealing for anyone with information who has not already spoken to police to come forward.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170157393. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
