# Appeal after reported assault in Bournemouth

06 August 2018

* * *

Detectives are appealing for witnesses following a reported assault in Bournemouth.

The incident happened at around 3am on Saturday 4 August 2018 near to the pedestrian crossing on Exeter Road by the Royal Exeter Hotel.

It is alleged an altercation took place between two groups of men.

One man sustained a broken cheekbone and wrist during the incident.

A 25-year-old man from Bournemouth was arrested on suspicion of inflicting grievous bodily harm and has been released under investigation.

Detective Constable Kate Rhodes, of Bournemouth CID, said: "I am appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information that could assist with the investigation to please get in contact.

"I'm also particularly interested in speaking with anyone who was driving past at the time and may have relevant dashcam footage."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180124447. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
