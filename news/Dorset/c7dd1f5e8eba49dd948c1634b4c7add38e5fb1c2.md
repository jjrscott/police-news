# Notification of movement of a police escorted abnormal load

17 August 2016

* * *

An abnormal load will be travelling with a police escort tomorrow, Thursday 18 August 2016, from the A31 at Ringwood to Brittany Ferries at the docks in Poole.

The transport of a 39m long boat will start at 10am and continue along the A31, A350 and B3068 to the docks.

Motorists are being advised that there will be some delays along the route.

Officers would like to thank everyone for their patience during this time.
