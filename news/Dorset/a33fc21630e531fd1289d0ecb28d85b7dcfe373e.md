# Appeal for information following commercial burglary in Bournemouth

04 November 2019

* * *

Officers investigating a break to a store in Bournemouth are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

At around 7am on Sunday 19 October 2019 offenders gained access to Digitek on Old Christchurch Road. A number of items, including laptops and phones were stolen.

Police Constable Kim Ward, of Bournemouth police, said: "We are continuing to investigate this break-in and I would urge any witnesses or anyone with information that can assist our enquiries to please contact us.

"I am keen to hear from anyone who saw two men in the vicinity of the store at the relevant time or anyone who has seen electrical items offered for sale in unusual circumstances.

"This is a small, independent business that has been targeted and we are very keen to locate the suspects and recover any stolen items."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190165139. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
