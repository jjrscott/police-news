# Witness appeal following burglary at Weymouth pub

04 November 2016

* * *

Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward following a burglary at a Weymouth pub.

Sometime between 11pm on Sunday 30 October and 7.15am on Monday 31 October 2016 an offender gained access to the Park Hotel on Grange Road and stole a quantity of cash from tills and cash boxes.

The occupiers were asleep when the offence occurred.

Police Constable Neil Fitch, of Weymouth police, said: "I am appealing to anyone who saw someone acting suspiciously in the area of the pub during the relevant time to please contact me."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55160164032. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
