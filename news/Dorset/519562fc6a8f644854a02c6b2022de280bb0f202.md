# Update - Appeal following sexual assault in Weymouth

28 October 2016

* * *

A man has made himself known to police following a CCTV appeal in Weymouth.

Detectives investigating a sexual assault in the town issued a CCTV image on Wednesday 26 October 2016 of a man they believed may hold vital information regarding the incident.

A 67-year-old from Weymouth came forward to police and has been helping them with their enquiries. No arrests have been made.

The investigation relates to a sexual assault that occurred in an alleyway near to the bowling green in Greenhill Gardens at around 12.45am on Friday 21 October 2016.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55160159338. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
