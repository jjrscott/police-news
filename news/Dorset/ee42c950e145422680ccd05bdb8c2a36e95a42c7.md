# Witness appeal following altercation in Bridport

06 December 2019

* * *

Officers investigating an altercation in Bridport are appealing for any witnesses to come forward.

The incident occurred at around 5pm on Sunday 1 December 2019 in North Allington. Two women aged in their 50s, who are known to each other, were reportedly involved in an altercation during which one of them was reportedly carrying a broom.

Police Constable Rebecca Barnes, of Bridport police, said: "We are continuing to investigate this incident and have spoken to both parties involved. No arrests have been made.

"I am now appealing for any independent witnesses who have not already spoken to police to please contact us."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190189221. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
