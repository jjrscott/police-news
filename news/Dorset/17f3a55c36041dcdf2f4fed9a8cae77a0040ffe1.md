# Witness appeal following burglary in Wimborne

24 January 2017

* * *

Officers are appealing for witnesses and anyone with information to come forward following a burglary at a craft centre in the Knobcrook area of Wimborne.

Sometime between 8.45 and 9.30pm on Sunday 22 January 2017 offenders gained access to Walford Mill Crafts on Stone Lane by smashing open a front window.

The offenders then smashed a glass cabinet containing a large amount of jewellery which was stolen during the incident.

Police Constable Dave Hassell, of Wimborne police, said: "I am appealing to anyone who saw or heard anything suspicious in the Knobcrook area during the relevant time to please contact Dorset Police.

"I am particularly keen to hear from anyone who has seen or been offered items of jewellery for sale in suspicious circumstances since the time of the incident."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170010996. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
