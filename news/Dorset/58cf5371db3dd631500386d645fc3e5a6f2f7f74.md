# Witness appeal following burglary in Poole

03 February 2021

* * *

Officers investigating a burglary at an address in Poole are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

Sometime between 4.20pm and 4.50pm on Saturday 30 January 2021 the rear doors to a property in St Osmunds Road were smashed and entry was gained.

A number of items of jewellery and watches were stolen, as well as items of clothing and car keys.

Police Constable Cara Bradley, of Poole police, said: "We have been carrying out a number of enquiries to identify who was responsible for this burglary, including house-to-house visits in the area.

"I am appealing to anyone who witnessed any suspicious activity around the relevant time to please get in touch."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55210018710. Alternatively, to stay 100 per cent anonymous, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers online at Crimestoppers-uk.org or call Freephone 0800 555 111.
