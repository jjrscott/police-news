# Woman charged in connection with alleged stabbing incident in Bournemouth

24 April 2017

* * *

A woman has appeared in court charged in connection with an alleged assault in Bournemouth in which a man sustained stab injuries.

At around 5am on Friday 21 April 2017 police received reports of a woman with a knife at an address in Wootton Gardens.

It was reported that the woman had entered the flat of another man and assaulted him.

A 53-year-old Bournemouth man suffered stab injuries during the incident, which are not believed to be life-threatening. He was taken to hospital for treatment.

A 36-year-old Bournemouth woman was arrested and taken to hospital for treatment to minor injuries. She has been charged with attempting to cause grievous bodily harm with intent and appeared before Poole Magistrates' Court on Saturday 22 April 2017. She will next appear before Bournemouth Crown Court on a date yet to be fixed.
