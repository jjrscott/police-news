# Update â€“ further arrested following reported assault in Poole

10 December 2018

* * *

A second man has been arrested in connection with an altercation in Poole where a man was reportedly assaulted.

The incident happened at around 8.20pm on Saturday 8 December 2018 on Aspen Gardens.

Officers attended and upon arrival located a man with stab wounds. He was taken to hospital for treatment to minor injuries and has since been discharged.

Following a search of the area, a 22-year-old man from Wimborne was arrested on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm and affray. He has been released under investigation.

After further enquiries, a 25-year-old Wimborne man was arrested on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm and has been released on police bail.

Detectives are still appealing for any witnesses to the incident who have not already spoken to police to come forward.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55180197183. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
