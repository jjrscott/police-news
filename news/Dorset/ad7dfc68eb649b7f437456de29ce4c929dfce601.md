# Appeal for witnesses following commercial burglary in Poole

29 September 2017

* * *

Detectives investigating a break-in at a car dealership in Poole are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

Dorset Police received a report at 7.24am today, Friday 29 September 2017, of a burglary at the Westover Car Group in Wallisdown Road overnight.

Seven cars were stolen and damage was caused to a number of other vehicles on the premises.

Detective Constable Tom Alsford, of Bournemouth CID, said: "We have made a number of enquiries into this incident and I would urge any witnesses to the burglary, or anyone with information that may assist our investigation, to contact us."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170151946. Alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
