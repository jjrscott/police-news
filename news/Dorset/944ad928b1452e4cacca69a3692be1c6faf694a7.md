# Witness appeal following assault in Bridport

10 February 2020

* * *

Officers investigating an assault in Bridport are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

Between 3am and 3.30am on Saturday 25 January 2020 a man and a woman were approached by a group of people near to the Esso petrol station on East Road. An altercation occurred, in which the woman was kicked and punched by another female. The man she was with went to intervene and was himself punched and head-butted by a member of the group.

The woman did not suffer any injuries in the incident. The man suffered a minor injury to his face, but did not require hospital treatment.

Police Constable Neil Gauden, of Bridport police, said: "We are continuing to investigate this incident and are making enquiries to identify those responsible.

"I am appealing to anyone who may have witnessed the assault or who has any information to please contact us."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55200012464. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
