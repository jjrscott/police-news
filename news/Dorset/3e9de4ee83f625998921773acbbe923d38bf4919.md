# Witness appeal following suspected arson in Weymouth

29 August 2019

* * *

Officers investigating a suspicious vehicle fire in Weymouth are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

At approximately 4.20am on Thursday 22 August 2019 Dorset Police was called by the fire service to a report of a vehicle on fire at the rear of a property on Littlemoor Road.

Damage was caused to five cars and a shed.

Police Constable Luke Kerslake, of Weymouth police, said: "It is believed that the fire was started deliberately. I am appealing to anyone who may have witnessed any suspicious activity in the area at the relevant time to get in touch.

"I would also like to hear from anyone who may have taken any photos or videos of the fire on the night of the incident."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190131474. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
