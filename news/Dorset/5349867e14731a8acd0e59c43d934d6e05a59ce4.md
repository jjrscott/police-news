# Witness appeal following reported serious assault in Bournemouth

28 May 2019

* * *

Detectives are appealing for witnesses to come forward following a reported serious assault in Bournemouth.

At 2am on Sunday 26 May 2019 it is alleged an altercation occurred between a group of people in New Road near to the Northbourne roundabout.

During the incident it is reported a 19-year-old local man was assaulted. He sustained several fractures to his face and was taken to hospital for treatment.

Two local 17-year-old boys have been arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm with intent and have been released on police bail while enquiries continue.

Detective Constable Neil Causebrook, of Bournemouth CID, said: "I am appealing to anyone who was in the New Road area at the relevant time and saw what happened to please contact Dorset Police.

"I am particularly keen to hear from any motorists who drove past the incident with dashcam fitted. I am also aware that a taxi driver was in the area at the time and I would ask them to please come forward."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190079357\. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
