# Witness appeal following road traffic collision in Poole

09 March 2020

* * *

Officers investigating a road traffic collision in Poole are appealing for witnesses or anyone with relevant dashcam footage to come forward.

At around 6.15pm on Tuesday 3 March 2020 a collision occurred on Pearson Avenue at the junction with Ashley Road involving a blue Vauxhall Corsa and a pedestrian.

The pedestrian - a man aged in his 50s - sustained injuries to his head, neck and leg. His injuries are described as serious and he remains in hospital receiving treatment.

The driver of the Vauxhall was uninjured.

Police Constable Adam Carr, of Poole police, said: "I am appealing to anyone who was travelling in the area at the time and witnessed the collision to please get in touch.

"I would also like to hear from any motorists who may have captured anything relevant on dashcam footage."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55200033569. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
