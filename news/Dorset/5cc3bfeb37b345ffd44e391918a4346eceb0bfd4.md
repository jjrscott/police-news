# Indecent exposure in Weymouth â€“ witness appeal

26 April 2019

* * *

Officers are appealing for witnesses to come forward after a man indecently exposed himself in Weymouth.

Sometime between 7.30pm and 8pm on Thursday 11 April 2019 a man indecently exposed himself to a man and woman who were inside Batida Bar in St Thomas Street.

The suspect is described as white, six feet two inches tall and bald. He was wearing a blue jumper and grey tracksuit bottoms.

Police Constable Gareth Morris, of Weymouth police, said: "Following initial enquiries, I have just been in the position to speak to one of the victims to ascertain what happened.

"I am appealing to anyone who was in the area at the relevant time and recalls seeing a man matching the description given to please contact Dorset Police."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55190054851. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
