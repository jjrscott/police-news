# Update - appeal after reported suspicious incident in Bournemouth

04 September 2017

* * *

A man has been arrested following a CCTV appeal in connection with a reported suspicious incident in Bournemouth.

The incident reportedly happened at around 12.05pm on Friday 18 August 2017 in Greggs on Old Christchurch Road.

It was alleged that a man took a photograph up the skirt of a local woman aged in her 20s while she was being served at the counter.

A 41-year-old Christchurch man was arrested on suspicion of voyeurism and is currently assisting officers with their enquiries.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55170127971. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
