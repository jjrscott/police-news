# Witness appeal following dangerous driving incident on Portland

27 February 2020

* * *

Officers investigating reports of a dangerous driving incident on Portland are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.

It was reported that at around 10.15pm on Wednesday 26 February 2020 a grey BMW car was seen driving at excessive speed and overtaking other vehicles in a dangerous manner heading north along Portland Beach Road, near to the Fine Foundation Chesil Beach Centre.

Sergeant Billy Taylor, of Weymouth and Portland police, said: "We are making further enquiries into this incident and I would urge anyone who witnessed a grey BMW driving at excessive speeds or in a dangerous manner in the area at the relevant time to please contact us.

"I would also ask anyone who was driving in the vicinity and has dashcam fitted to their vehicle to check their footage for anything relevant."

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55200030752. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
