# Update â€“ arrest made following reported arson in Weymouth

18 May 2020

* * *

Detectives investigating an alleged suspicious fire in Weymouth have arrested a man.

At around 1am on Monday 18 May 2020 Dorset Police was called by the fire service to a report of a fire in a wheelie bin outside an address on Franklin Road.

It was also reported that damage was caused to the front door of the property.

Following enquiries, a 62-year-old Weymouth man has been arrested on suspicion of arson with intent to endanger life. He is currently assisting detectives with their enquiries.

Officers are continuing to make enquiries into this incident and are appealing for any witnesses to come forward.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Dorset Police at www.dorset.police.uk, via email 101@dorset.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101, quoting occurrence number 55200069875. Alternatively, to stay 100 per cent anonymous, contact the independent charity Crimestoppers online at Crimestoppers-uk.org or call freephone 0800 555 111.
