Detectives from the City of London Police have released images of a man they want to speak to in connection with a burglary at a business in the City.

It is suspected that the man forced entry into the building at Smithfield Market, between 18:00 and 18:07 on Saturday 18 April 2020, and stole two Apple Macs.

CCTV images were captured at the premises and officers from the City of London Police are keen to speak to anybody who recognises the man in the photos.

**Detective Constable Ufuk Ekbic, who is leading the investigation for the City of London Police, said:**

"Burglary and theft are very significant crimes and ones we take seriously in the City. To help further our enquiries in this investigation, we would like to hear from anyone who recognises the man in the CCTV images released today."

If you can help the investigation, please contact the City of London Police by calling 020 7601 2222. You can also report crime to us online or by dialing 101 . Or report crime anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. In an emergency dial 999.
