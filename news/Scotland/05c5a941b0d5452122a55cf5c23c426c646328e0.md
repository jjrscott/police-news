With reference to our appeal for information following a road crash in which Amanda Boag, 39-years-old, from Buchlyvie died after her VW Golf and a gas tanker collided on the A811, near to Arnprior Village, on Tuesday, 25 February, 2020, please note:

A 62-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with the incident. He has been released to appear at court at a later date and a report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.
