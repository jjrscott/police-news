A 42-year-old man has been arrested in connection with an assault and robbery at L&M Stores in Hutcheon Street, Aberdeen on Thursday, 2 July, 2020. Officers working on this inquiry would like to thank members of the public for their co-operation and assistance.

The 42-year-old man was released on an undertaking and is due to appear at Aberdeen Sheriff Court on Wednesday, 9 September, 2020. A report has been submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.
