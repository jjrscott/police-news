Police in Glasgow have released CCTV images of two individuals they wish to trace in connection with a disturbance, during which two people were seriously assaulted, in the Haghill area of Glasgow.

The incident took place around 5.15pm on Sunday, 13 September, 2020, on Cumbernauld Road before continuing onto Aberfeldy Street, Glasgow.

The men officers are eager to trace are both described as white with Scottish accents.

The first is of slim build with a beard and is pictured wearing a black baseball cap, a hoodie with a blue torso and grey sleeves, grey jogging bottoms and dark trainers.

The second is of slim to medium build with brown hair and is pictured wearing dark clothing and white trainers.

Detective Constable Gillian Burns, of Shettleston Police Station, said: “We’re eager to trace these men as part of our enquiries and urge anyone who recognises them to contact us or Crimestoppers anonymously as soon as possible.”

Anyone with information which could help can contact Police Scotland via 101, quoting incident number 2137 of 13 September, 2020, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.”
