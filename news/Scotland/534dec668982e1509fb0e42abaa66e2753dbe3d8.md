With reference to our previous release regarding a fatal road crash involving one car on the A93, Spittal of Glenshee on Sunday, 3 January, 2021, please note:

The 23-year-old passenger in the BMW 3 series, who died following the crash can now be named as Reece Tucker of Kirkton, Dundee.

Two boys, aged 9 and 5 years, who were passengers in the car were taken by air ambulance to the Queen Elizabeth University Hospital in Glasgow. Hospital staff describe their condition as serious.

The 31-year-old driver was taken by ambulance to Ninewells Hospital in Dundee where he was treated for minor injuries.

A full report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal.

Sergeant Fraser Cameron, of the Perth Road Policing Unit, said: “The family has requested privacy at this time.

“Our investigation into the cause of this crash on Sunday afternoon is continuing. I would ask any witnesses to the crash who have not yet spoken to officers to contact us.

“I would also appeal to anyone driving on the A93 near to its junctions with the B950 and B951 around 4.15pm who has dash-cam footage to come forward.”

Anyone with information should contact the Road Policing Unit through 101 quoting reference number 2130 of 3 January.
