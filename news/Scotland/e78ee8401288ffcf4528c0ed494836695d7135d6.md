Police Scotland can confirm that two males, ages 16 and 17, have been arrested and charged in connection with the serious sexual assault of a 16-year-old female.

The incident took place in the Glasgow Green area on Saturday, 15 August.

The two males are expected to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court today (Tuesday, 18 August).
