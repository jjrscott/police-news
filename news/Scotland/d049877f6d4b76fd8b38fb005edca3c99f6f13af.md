**Published 6 March 2020**

With reference to our previous releases regarding the death of 34 year-old Morgan Dunn in Marigold Square, Ayr on Saturday, 8 February, 2020, please note:

A 32 year-old woman has been arrested and charged in connection with the death. She is due before Ayr Sheriff Court on Monday, 9 March.

A full report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal.
