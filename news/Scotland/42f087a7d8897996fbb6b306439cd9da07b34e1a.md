A 43-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with the death of Daniel Greer in Airdrie.

The 33-year-old was found within his home at Rankin Street in the Greengairs area of the town around 12.55pm on Monday, 19 October, 2020.

A report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal and the 43-year-old is expected to appear at Airdrie Sheriff Court on Monday, 26 October, 2020.

The family of Daniel Greer has issued the following statement through Police Scotland.

"'He was a much loved member of our family and he will be sadly missed. My Darling boy Daniel , life will never be the same without you. We will always love you.'

Detective Superintendent Kevin Jamieson of Police Scotland’s Major Investigation Team (East) said: “I’d like to thank the local people in the Greengairs and Airdrie communities for their assistance during this inquiry and to assure them that a significant police presence will remain in the area at this time.”
