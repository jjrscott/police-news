Detectives have released an image of a male they believe may be able to assist with their enquiries in relation to an incident which occurred on Friday 22 May 2020.

The incident occurred at around 11.30pm at East Lane, Paisley, and resulted in a male being injured and requiring hospital treatment.

The male in the images is described as being around 35 to 40 years old, medium build, clean shaven, wearing dark clothing, and carrying a hold-all.

Anyone who recognises the male or who has any information is asked to contact Police Scotland on 101 for the attention of DC Paul Fanning of Paisley CID. Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111, where anonymity can be maintained.
