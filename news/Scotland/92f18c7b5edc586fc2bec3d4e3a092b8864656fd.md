With reference to our previous release regarding a 27 year-old woman who was assaulted and robbed in Ashgrove Road, Aberdeen on Thursday, 6 August, please note:

A 41 year-old woman has been arrested and charged in connection with this incident. She is due before Aberdeen Sheriff Court on Monday, 10 August, 2020.

A full report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal.
