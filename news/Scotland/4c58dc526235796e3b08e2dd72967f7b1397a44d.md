A 47-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a robbery in Leven town centre.

The incident happened on a path between North Street and Nairn Street around 9am on Tuesday, 3 November.

He is due to appear before Kirkcaldy Sheriff Court on Thursday, 5 November, and a report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.
