**Published 17 July 2020**

Police Scotland can confirm that a 17-year-old male youth and a 19-year-old male youth have been arrested and charged in connection with the death in Springbank Road in Paisley.

The body of Ryan Low (32), was found in his flat on Monday, 29 June, 2020.

Both are expected to appear at Paisley Sheriff Court on Monday, 20 July.
