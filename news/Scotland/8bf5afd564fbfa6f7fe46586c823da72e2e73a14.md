Detectives are releasing an image of a man who they believe may be able to assist them in their enquiries in respect of an incident which occurred at around 11:40pm on Saturday 18 July 2020, in Shettleston Road, near to Ardholm Street, Glasgow. During the incident two males were assaulted and required hospital treatment as a result.

Detective Constable Paul Fraser said, “I am appealing to anyone who may recognise the man in the image, or who has any information, to get in touch.”

Anyone with information is asked to call Police Scotland via 101 quoting incident number 4793 of 18 July, 2020. Alternatively, please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 where information can be given anonymously.
