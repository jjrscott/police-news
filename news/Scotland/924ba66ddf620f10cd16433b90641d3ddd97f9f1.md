Police Scotland can confirm that two men aged 21 and 25 have been arrested and charged in connection with an attempted murder in Dundee.

The incident happened around 1.35am on Monday, 4 January, 2021 when a 20-year-old man was stabbed on Cleghorn Street. He was taken to Ninewells hospital for treatment of serious injuries.

Chief Inspector Ross Fitzgerald, of Tayside Police Division, said: "I would like to thank the members of the public who came forward and provided vital information towards the investigation."

Both men are due to appear at Dundee Sheriff Court on Wednesday, 6 January, 2021.
