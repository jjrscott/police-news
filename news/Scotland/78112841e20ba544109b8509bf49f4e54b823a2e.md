Police Scotland can confirm that a 21-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a robbery at a property near Kiltarlity in Inverness-shire.

The incident happened around 7pm on Monday, 19 October, 2020.

The man is expected to appear at Inverness Sheriff Court on Monday, 11 January.
