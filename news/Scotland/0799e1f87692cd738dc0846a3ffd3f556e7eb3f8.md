Officers in Glasgow are appealing for information following a serious assault in the Garrioch Road area of Maryhill on Friday, 13 November, 2020.

The incident happened around 8.15pm when a 23-year-old man sustained a serious head injury. He was taken to the Glasgow Royal Infirmary by the Scottish Ambulance Service.

Detective Sergeant Gillian Alexander, of Maryhill CID, said: “We are trying to establish the full circumstances of what happened and I am appealing to anyone in the area, or nearby streets, who may have seen something to come forward.

“There are a number of takeaways in the area and it would have been busy at that time of night. Also, if anyone was driving in the area and has dash-cam footage that may help with our enquiries, then please get in touch.

“If you can help please call Police Scotland on 101, quoting incident number 3623 of Friday, 13 November, 2020, or make a call anonymously to the charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.”
