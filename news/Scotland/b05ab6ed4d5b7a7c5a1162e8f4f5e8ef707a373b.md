Police in Sutherland are appealing for information after a break-in at a business in the Migdale area near Bonar Bridge.

Entry was forced to Migdale County Stores sometime between 9.30pm on Monday, 5 October and 7.30am on Tuesday, 6 October.

A significant number of items of clothing and footwear were stolen from the shop.

Detective Constable Aggie Paluch said: "Our enquiries are ongoing and I would urge anyone with information which could aid our investigation to come forward.

"Have you noticed any suspicious activity or vehicles operating in the area?

"If you can help then please call 101, quoting incident 0584 of 6 October, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
