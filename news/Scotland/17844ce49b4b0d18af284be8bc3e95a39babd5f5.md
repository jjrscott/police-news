**Published 3 January 2020**

Police in Glasgow have released an image of a woman they wish to speak to as they believe she may be able to assist with their enquiries in relation to a serious assault in the city centre.

The incident took place within a flat in Albion Street between 7.00pm and 12.00am on Wednesday, 28 August 2019, when a woman in her forties was attacked.

The woman pictured is described as white, in her mid 20s, 5ft 1in tall, with a Northern English accent. She is of large build, has long blond hair and a tattoo on her foot.

Detective Constable Kieran Bewick, of Greater Glasgow's Public Protection Unit, said: “We believe the woman in the image may be able to help with our ongoing investigation and we are appealing for her, or anyone who may know her, to make contact with us as soon as possible.”

Those with information can call Police Scotland on 101, quoting incident number 1968 of 29 August, 2019, or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
