**Published 16 April 2020
**

Police in Glasgow are investigating a theft by housebreaking that occurred in Bearsden, Glasgow.

The incident took place in Jubilee Gardens around 0955hrs on 31 October 2019 during which a property was broken into and various items were stolen.

Detectives have released CCTV images of a male they believe may have information that can assist them with their investigation.

He is described as being white, aged between 30-40 years old, of slim build and with short dark hair. He is pictured wearing a “Good for Nothing” black jacket with a white butterfly logo, black Armani joggers and black trainers with white soles.

Detective Constable Andrew Lambie of Glasgow City Centre CID said

"We are keen to talk to the man in the images released today as he may be able to help with our enquiries.

"I would urge anyone with information as to the identity of the man pictured to contact Police Scotland via 101, quoting incident number 0993 of 31 October 2019. Alternatively, please contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where anonymity can be maintained."
