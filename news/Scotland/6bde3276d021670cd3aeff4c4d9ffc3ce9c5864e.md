Police in Glasgow are appealing for witnesses after a man was found injured in the Castlemilk area.

Around 7.20am on Saturday 12 September officers received reports of a man lying in Croftfoot Road.

The 53-year-old was taken to the Queen Elizabeth University Hospital where he is receiving treatment for his injuries.

Enquiries are continuing to establish how the man came about his injuries and anyone with any information is asked to contact 101 quoting incident number 0893 of 12 September 2020.
