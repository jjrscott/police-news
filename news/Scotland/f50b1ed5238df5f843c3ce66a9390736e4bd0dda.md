Detectives in Forth Valley have arrested and charged three male youths, aged 15, 14 and 12, in connection with an assault and robbery in Alloa.

The incident happened at Braehead Golf Course around 8pm on Saturday, 26 September, 2020, when a 51-year-old man was walking his dog on the course and was assaulted and robbed by the youths.

The man was taken to Forth Valley Royal Hospital with head and back injuries.
