Police Scotland can confirm that a 29-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a shooting in Coatbridge.

The incident happened around 4.05pm on Saturday, 21 November, 2020 when a firearm was discharged at a red Seat Leon car on Marnoch Drive.

The man is due to appear at Airdrie Sheriff Court on Thursday, 3 December, 2020.
