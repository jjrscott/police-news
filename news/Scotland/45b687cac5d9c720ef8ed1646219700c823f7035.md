**Published 23 February 2020**

With reference to our previous appeal regarding a serious assault in Panmure Place, in the Tollcross area of Edinburgh on Friday, 21 February, please note:

A 47 year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with this serious assault and he is due before Edinburgh Sheriff Court on Monday, 24 February.

A full report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal.
