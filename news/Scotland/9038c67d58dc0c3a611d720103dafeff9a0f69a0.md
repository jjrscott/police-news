A 50-year-old man has now been arrested and charged in connection with the death of a 33-year-old man in the Townhead area of Glasgow.

The body of Tomasz Lipiec was found in a property in Dobbies Loan Place on Monday, 7 September

A 31-year-old man was arrested and charged on Friday, 11 September, 2020.

Both men are due to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court on Monday, 14 September, 2020.

A report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.
