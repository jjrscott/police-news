Police are appealing for information following a serious road crash in Dundee on Tuesday, 22 September, 2020.

Around 9.10 pm, a 34-year-old man was seriously injured after being struck by a white Citroen C1 whilst crossing Coupar Angus Road, near its junction with Landsdowne Gardens, Dundee.

He was taken by ambulance to Ninewells Hospital in Dundee where he remains for treatment. Medical staff describe his condition as serious but stable.

The 23-year-old male driver of the Citroen and his 25-year-old male passenger were not injured.

Sergeant William Strachan, Tayside Roads Policing Unit, said:

"Our enquiries into the crash are continuing and we are appealing to anyone who saw the incident, particularly motorists who were in the area and who have dash-cam footage, to get in touch. Information can be passed to officers at the Roads Policing Unit via 101. Please quote reference number 3464 of 22 September 2020 when calling."
