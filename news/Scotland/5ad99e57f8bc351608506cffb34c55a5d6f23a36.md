**Published 12 February 2020**

Police Scotland have released images of a man they wish to speak to as it is believed that he may have information that will assist their investigation in relation to an assault on a 22-year-old man which occurred around 3am on Thursday, 14 November, 2019 in the Bamboo Nightclub, West Regent Street, Glasgow.

The man is described as in his 30s, of Asian appearance, 6ft in height with dark hair and was wearing dark coloured clothing.
Anyone who recognises the man in the image or who has any information is asked to contact Police Scotland via 101, quoting incident 0269 of 14 November 2019, or report this anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
