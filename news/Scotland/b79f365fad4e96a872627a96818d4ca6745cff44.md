Sometime between 7.45pm and 8pm on Monday, 9 November, 2020, a car was set on fire while parked in the driveway of a house in Boydston Road. The vehicle has been damaged and the fire was extinguished by the Fire and Rescue Service. Nobody was injured in the incident.
