With reference to our previous appeals for information on the following Glasgow robberies:

 * Greggs, Dumbarton Road on Saturday, 28 November
 * One-o-One store in Aikenhead Road on Sunday, 15 November
 * Shop robbery on St Vincent Street on Saturday, 7 November
 * Royal Bank of Scotland, Kilmarnock Road on Friday, 6 November

A 42 year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with the above and also in connection with an assault with intent to rob on Millbrae Road, Glasgow on Sunday, 15 November, and a robbery at a shop on Alexandra Parade on Tuesday, 24 November, 2020.

He is expected to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court on Wednesday, 16 December, 2020.

A full report will be forwarded to the Procurator Fiscal
