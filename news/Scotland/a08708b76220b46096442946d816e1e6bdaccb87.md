With reference to our previous appeal after three cars were stolen from a car showroom in Elgin on Thursday, 14 January, 2021, please note:

A 25 year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with theft by housebreaking. He is due before Inverness Sheriff Court on Thursday, 28 January, 2021.

Police enquiries are continuing.

A full report will be forwarded to the Procurator Fiscal.
