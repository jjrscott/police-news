Police in Lanarkshire are appealing for information following a serious road crash near Bothwell.

The incident happened on the M74 southbound, near Bothwell, around 6.35pm on Sunday, 13 December, 2020, and involved five vehicles.

Two women aged 20 and 46 were taken to University Hospital, Wishaw as a precaution.

Another 45-year-old woman sustained a serious, but not life-threatening, injury and was also taken to hospital.

The road was closed for around three hours for an investigation at the scene and re-opened around 9.40pm.

Constable Jonathan Lyall, of Motherwell Road Policing Unit, said: “We are carrying out enquiries to establish the full circumstances which led to this crash and would urge anyone with information to come forward.

“We would be keen to speak to anyone who witnessed the crash or who may have seen the vehicles involved beforehand. Anyone who was on the road around the time of the incident and may have dashcam footage which could assist with our investigation is asked to contact police.

“Any information can be passed on by calling 101, quoting incident 2697 of 13 December.”
