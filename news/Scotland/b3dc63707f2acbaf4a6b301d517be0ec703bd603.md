Police Scotland can confirm that two people have been arrested and charged following our previous appeal regarding an elderly man robbed twice at his home in Hamilton.

The incidents took place at a flat in the May Gardens area on Sunday, 27 September, and Monday, 28 September.

The pair - a 50-year-old woman and a 51-year-old man - are due to appear at Hamilton Sheriff Court today (Tuesday, 6 October).

Detective Sergeant Kevin Clark, from Lanarkshire CID, said: "I'd like to thank the public for their assistance in this investigation, allowing us to bring some peace of mind to the victim."
