**Published 18 July 2020**

Police in Fife can now confirm the names of the two men who died following a serious road crash in Leven on Friday, 17 July, 2020.

Daniel Allardyce (29) from Leven and Terrence Thornber (27) also from Leven both sustained serious injuries in the one vehicle collision which happened in the early hours on Windygates Road and were pronounced dead at the scene.

Sergeant Nicola Young from Fife's Road Policing Unit said: "Our thought's remain with the family and friends of Daniel and Terrence at this very difficult time.

"Enquiries into the crash are ongoing and I would like to thank everyone who has assisted with these. A report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal."
