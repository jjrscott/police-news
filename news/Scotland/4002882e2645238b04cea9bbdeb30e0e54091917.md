A 30-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a series of fire raising incidents in Edinburgh.

He has been charged in connection with a number of fires in the Silverknowes area of the city in the early hours of Wednesday, 27 January.

He is expected to appear at Edinburgh Sheriff Court on Thursday, 28 January.
