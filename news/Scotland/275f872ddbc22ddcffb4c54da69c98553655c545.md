Police in Glasgow are appealing for witnesses following a robbery at a shop on Cross Arthurlie Street, Barrhead.

A 25-year-old man was working within the Premier Store at around 9.25am on Sunday 3rd January 2021, when two men threatened him with a knife and stole some items.

Both suspects are described as being white, in their early 30s, approximately 5'10 in height.

One was wearing a blue jacket with white zip, black under armour trousers and grey Air max trainers.

The second was wearing a blue jumper with black hood, black adidas jogging bottoms with white stripes down both legs and black Nike trainers.
