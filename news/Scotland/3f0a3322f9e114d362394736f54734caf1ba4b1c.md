Police Scotland can confirm that a 21-year-old man has been charged in connection with a serious sexual assault in Clydebank on a 23-year old woman in the early hours of Saturday, 1 August, in a lane off Alexander Street in the town.

He is due to appear at Dumbarton Sheriff Court on Tuesday, 4 August, 2020.
