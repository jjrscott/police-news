Police Scotland can confirm that a man has been arrested and charged in connection with an assault and robbery in Glasgow.

The incident happened on Anniesland Road in the city around 4.15pm on Monday, 10 August.

A report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal and the 38-year-old man is expected to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court at a later date.
