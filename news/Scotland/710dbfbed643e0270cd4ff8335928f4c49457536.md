Police in Paisley are appealing for witnesses after a wilful fire raising at a property in the town.

Officers were called to an address on West Buchanan Place around 6.30am on Wednesday 9 December following reports of a fire. It was extinguished quickly and nobody was injured.

Constable Dougie MacFarlane from Paisley Police Station said: “This was a frightening incident and it was lucky there were no injuries.

“We are checking local CCTV, and asking if anyone has any information to contact us via 101 quoting reference number 0403 of 9 December 2020.”
