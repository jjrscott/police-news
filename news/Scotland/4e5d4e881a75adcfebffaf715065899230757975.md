**Published 19 March 2020**

olice Scotland can confirm that a second man has been arrested and charged in connection with the death of Alan Ritchie (61), which occurred in Kennishead Avenue, Carnwardric, Glasgow, on Tuesday, 10 March, 2020.

The 49-year-old man is expected to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court today (Thursday, 19 March).
