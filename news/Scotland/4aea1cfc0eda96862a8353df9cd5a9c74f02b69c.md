Police in Irvine are appealing for information following an assault with intent to rob.

The incident happened around 00.35am on Thursday, 1 October, 2020 at Ravenspark Filling Station on Kilwinning Road.

A 43-year-old woman was withdrawing money from the ATM when she was approached by a woman and threatened.

A member of the public intervened and the suspect made off in the direction of Fleming Terrace.

The suspect is described as white, around 5ft and slim. She was wearing dark clothing, a dark face mask and a snood.

Detective Constable Lee Duncan, of Saltcoats CID said: “The victim was not injured during the incident but left shaken.

“We are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident or who intervened and who haven’t already spoken to officers to come forward.

“We would also appeal to anyone who was around the garage at the time to get in touch with police if they have any information which may assist us. I would also ask anyone who was driving in the area to check dashcam footage.”

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police on 101 quoting incident 0082 of 1 October, 2020.
