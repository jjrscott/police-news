Police in Ayr are appealing for information following a serious assault in the Whitletts area.

The incident happened around 3.50am on Sunday, 4 October, 2020, when a 20-year-old man was approached by a male and female on Glenmuir Road at its junction with Glenmuir Place, the male then assaulted the victim.

The man received treatment for a serious facial injury at hospital.

The male suspect is described as being in his late 20s, of slim build, with short black hair and a beard and was wearing a dark jacket. The female he was with is described as small with long blonde hair.

Detective Constable Barry Gibson, of Ayr CID, said: “We have been conducting enquiries since the incident was reported and we are now appealing for witness to assist with our investigation.

“Anyone with any information or who may have seen the male and female around the time of the incident, should contact police by calling 101 and quoting incident number 0636 of 4 October.”
