**Published 8 May 2020**

Detectives in Fife can confirm that, following their recent appeal regarding a robbery on North Street, Leven, a 46-year-old man has been arrested and charged.

He is expected to appear at Falkirk Sheriff Court on Monday, 11, May.

Detective Inspector Chris Mill said: “I’d like to thank the public for their positive response and for coming forward with information which helped us progress our enquiries.”
