Police Scotland can confirm that two men aged 26 and 29 have been arrested and charged in connection with an attempted murder in Edinburgh.

The incident happened around 6.45pm on Sunday, 13 September, 2020 when police and emergency services were called after a 34-year-old man was seriously injured during an altercation in Panmure Place, Edinburgh.

His condition is currently described as stable.

Both men are due to appear at Edinburgh Sheriff Court on Monday, 21 September, 2020.

A report has been submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.
