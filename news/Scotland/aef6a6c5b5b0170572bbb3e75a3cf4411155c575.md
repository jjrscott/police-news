**Published 9 May 2020**

Police in Dundee are continuing to investigate an assault which took place on Sunday, 15 March, 2020, around 3:30am at Grovesnor Casino, Marketgait, Dundee.

A man required hospital treatment as a result of the incident.

Today police released images of a man they believe could assist in their enquiries and would appeal to anyone who recognises him to contact them.

The man is described as white, in his mid-twenties to mid-thirties with short hair shaved on the sides and wearing a long sleeve blue top, dark jeans and white trainers.

Anybody with information with regards to what happened or who can identify the man is asked to contact Police Scotland on 101 quoting incident number 0652 of 15 March. Any information can also be passed anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
