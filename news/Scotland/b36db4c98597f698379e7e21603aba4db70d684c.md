Police in Inverness are appealing for witnesses after a woman was knocked down on Thursday, 3 December, 2020.

The 52-year-old was at the pedestrian crossing at around 3pm at Shore Street roundabout when she was struck by a blue Seat Leon.

Sergeant Stuart Fitzpatrick from Burnett Road police station said: "This has been a frightening experience for those involved. We are appealing to anyone who was in the area at the time, or who may have dash cam footage to come forward.

"Anyone with information can call local officers on 101 quoting number 1903 of 3 December 2020."
