**Published 5 January 2020**

Police at Maryhill are continuing enquiries and appealing for information after a man was found with serious injuries in Royston Rd, Glasgow on Saturday, 4 January, 2020.

Around 9.45 pm on Saturday, 4 January the 41-year-old man was discovered with stab wounds in Royston Road near to the rear of the Garngad public house.
He was taken by ambulance to the Glasgow Royal Infirmary where he was treated for stab wounds.

Detective Alistair Donaldson at Maryhill CID said:

“We are still trying to establish the motive and exact circumstances surrounding this incident and are reviewing CCTV in the area.

“Anyone who was in the area of Royston Road around 9.45 pm last night and may have witnessed a disturbance of some sort is asked to contact police immediately.”
Anyone with information should contact police at Maryhill via telephone number 101 quoting incident number 3539 of 4 January 2020. Alternatively calls can be made to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 where anonymity can be maintained.
