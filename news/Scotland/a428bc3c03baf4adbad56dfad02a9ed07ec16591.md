**Published 18 February 2020**

Police in Galashiels have issued images of three males they believe may have information that can assist them with their investigations into an incident that occurred in the early morning of Wednesday, 28 August 2019, in the Co-op on Edinburgh Road, Lauder.

Two of the men in the images are described as wearing dark clothing with balaclavas covering most of their faces. The third man is described as wearing a grey top and a balaclava.

Anyone who recognises any of the males in the images is asked to contact Police Scotland on 101, quoting incident number 0238 of the 28 August 2019. Alternatively, Crime Stoppers can be contacted by calling 0800 555 111 where information can be given.
