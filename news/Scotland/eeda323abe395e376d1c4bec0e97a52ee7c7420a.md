**Published 19th June 2020**

With reference to our previous releases appealing for information to help identify the woman who was recovered from the River Forth, off Society Road, west of South Queensferry on Saturday, 13 June, 2020, please note:

The woman has now been identified as 30-year-old Veronica Frincu and her relatives have been informed of the death. There are no apparent suspicious circumstances and a report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal. Members of the public are thanked for their response to our appeal.
