**Published 10 January 2020**

Following a CCTV appeal issued on Monday, 6 January regarding an assault on Saturday, 6 April, 2019, in Glasgow, a 56-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with the incident.

A report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal and the man is due to appear in court at a later date.
