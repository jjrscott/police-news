**Published 19 February 2020**

Police Scotland is appealing for information following a serious road crash on Ballater Street, Glasgow, on Tuesday, 18 February, 2020.

The incident happened around 5.15pm at the pedestrian crossing between the junctions of Laurieston Road and Florence Street. It involved a 22-year-old woman and a grey Toyota Yaris.

Emergency services attended and the woman was taken to Queen Elizabeth University Hospital for treatment to serious injuries.

The road was closed until 9pm while police carried out their investigations.

I am particularly keen to speak to anyone who may have dash cam footage which could help with our enquiries."

Anyone with information should contact Police Scotland on 101, quoting reference number 2700 of 18 February.
