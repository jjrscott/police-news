**Published 27 April**

Police in Glasgow are appealing for witnesses following a robbery in the Lambhill Bridge area.

The incident happened around 2pm on Wednesday, 1 April, 2020, on Balmore Road. A 38-year-old man was assaulted by two other men, who stole a low three-figure sum from his pocket.

The suspects are described as both being white, with one wearing a cream coloured jacket and the other in dark clothing.

PC Kirstie Black, from Govan Police Office, said: “We have been carrying out enquiries in the area and are keen to hear from anyone who either witnessed the robbery take place, or saw anything suspicious in the area during the afternoon of 1 April.

“Thankfully, the victim only sustained minor injuries and did not require medical treatment, but this was a shocking incident and anyone with information should get in touch with us.

“You can contact Police on 101, quoting incident 1918 of 1 April. Alternatively, you can contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, if you wish to remain anonymous.”
