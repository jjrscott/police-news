Man arrested and charged in connection with a robbery and attempted robbery, West End, Glasgow.

Officers in Glasgow can confirm that a 40-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a robbery at a petrol station and an attempted robbery at a bookmakers in the city.

The robbery happened around 2.55am on Saturday, 26 September, at the BP Petrol Station on Great Western Road.

The attempted robbery happened around 7.50pm on Friday, 9 October, 2020, at a bookmakers on Crow Road.

The man is due to appear at Glasgow Sheriff court on Wednesday, 14 October, 2020.
