Police in Fife are appealing for information following a report of a man indecently exposing himself near to Tayport Primary School.

The incident took place at around 11am on Wednesday, 16 September.

Sergeant Matt Spencer, from St Andrews Police Station, said: "We are carrying out extensive enquiries into this incident and are appealing for anyone with information to please come forward.

"We have also increased our police patrols in the local area and will continue to engage with the local community."

Anyone who may have information or saw anything suspicious can contact police on 101, quoting incident 0805 of 17 September.
