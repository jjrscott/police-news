Police in Glasgow are appealing for information after a man was assaulted in the south side of the city.

The 34-year-old was found after being attacked with a knife in the Gorbals area just before midnight on Friday 1 January 2021. It’s not known how many people were involved in the incident.

He was taken to Glasgow Royal Infirmary for treatment to his injuries and was later discharged.

Detective Constable James Johnston from Police Scotland said: “This was a frightening incident for the man involved and has left him with serious injuries.

“We were looking to speak to anyone who was near Caledonia Road or Oregon Street around the time of the incident or those with dash cam footage from the area at that time, to come forward.”

Anyone with any information can contact 101 quoting reference number 0015 of 2 January 2021.
