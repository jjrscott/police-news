**Published 28th June 2020**

Police Scotland can confirm that a 34-year-old woman has been arrested and charged in connection with the death of a man in Clydebank.

The incident happened around 3.50am on Friday, 26 June, 2020 at an address on Singer Street. The 38-year-old man was pronounced dead at the scene.

The 34-year-old woman is due to appear at Dumbarton Sheriff Court on Monday, 29 June, 2020.

Detective Inspector Erin Renwick, of the Major Investigation Team, said: “A police presence will remain in the area for the time being to allow enquiries to be carried out.

“This incident is now being treated as murder.

“Although this happened in the early hours of the morning, I would urge that if you have any information surrounding this incident to get in contact with officers.

“Police Scotland can be contacted on 101 quoting incident number 0719 of Friday, 26 June, 2020, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
