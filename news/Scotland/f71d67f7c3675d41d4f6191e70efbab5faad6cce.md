**Published 2 January 2020**

Police Scotland can confirm that three men, aged 24, 23 and 22, have been arrested and charged in relation to three separate attempted robberies which all took place in the Raploch area of Stirling between 9.00pm and 10.00pm on Saturday, 28 December.

They appeared before Stirling Sheriff Court on Monday, 30 December and have been remanded in custody.

Detective Inspector David MacGregor, of Stirling CID, said: “I am aware of the impact violent crime can have on our communities.

"It is fundamental that people of all ages feel safe, especially around this time of year, and I would like to reassure everyone that we will respond to any report and continue our work to keep offenders off of the streets.”
