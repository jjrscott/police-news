**Published 21 April 2020**

Detectives in Glasgow are continuing to investigate a serious assault and are today releasing images of two men they believe could assist with their enquiries.

The incident occurred around 12.20am on Sunday, 23 February, 2020 on Paisley Road, Glasgow.

The first man is described as white, early 20s, 5ft 10ins, stubble, possibly missing front teeth and was wearing an orange Nike baseball cap, black jacket, black Nike jogging trousers and grey and orange trainers, possibly Nike.

The second man is described as white, early 20s, 6ft 2ins, clean shaven, wearing all black and a black baseball cap.

Anyone who believes they could assist in this enquiry is asked to contact Detective Constable Natalie Timothy at Govan Police Station via 101, quoting incident 0223 of 23 February.
