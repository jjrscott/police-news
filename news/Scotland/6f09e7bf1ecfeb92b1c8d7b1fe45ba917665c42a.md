Police in Forth Valley are appealing for information following a serious single vehicle road crash in Maddiston, Falkirk, on Friday, 21 August, 2020.

Around 10.20 pm last night, a 44-year-old man was seriously injured after his purple Mini Cooper overturned whilst travelling East on Vellore Road, near to Almond Road, Maddiston.

He was taken by ambulance to Edinburgh Royal Infirmary where he remains for treatment. Medical staff describe his condition as serious but stable.

The injured man was the sole occupant in the car.
