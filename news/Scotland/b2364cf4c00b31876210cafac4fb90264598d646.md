A 47-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with an assault in Edinburgh earlier this year.

The incident occurred around 9.30pm on Friday, 17 April, 2020, when a man was assaulted with the Tesco Express store on Stenhouse Place East.

Officers would like to thank those who shared our previous appeal for information.
