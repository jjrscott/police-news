**Published 16 March 2020**

With reference to our previous articles regarding the death of Alan Ritchie (61) within a flat in Kennishead Avenue, Carnwardric, Glasgow on Tuesday, 10 March, 2020, please note:

A 31-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with the death and a report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal.

He is expected to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court on Tuesday, 17 March, 2020.
