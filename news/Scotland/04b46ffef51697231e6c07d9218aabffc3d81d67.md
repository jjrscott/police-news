Police Scotland can now confirm a 22-year-old man has been charged in connection
with the attempted murder of a 23-year-old woman in woodland near to Broad Street,
Cowdenbeath, on Sunday, 30 August 2020.

He is due to appear at Kirkcaldy Sheriff Court on Tuesday, 1 September, 2020.

A full report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal.
