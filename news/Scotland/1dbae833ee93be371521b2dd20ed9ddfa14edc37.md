Police Scotland can confirm that a 40-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with an assault which happened in High Street, Perth around 3am on Sunday 8 November 2020.

He is due before Perth Sheriff Court on Monday, 30 November, 2020.

A full report has been forwarded to the Procurator Fiscal.
