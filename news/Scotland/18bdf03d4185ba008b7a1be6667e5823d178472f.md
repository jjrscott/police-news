Officers in Keith are appealing for witnesses after 6 vehicles were damaged in a deliberate fire.

Police were called to the property off the A96, near North Bogbain, Keith just after 7am on Thursday 15 October 2020.

Detective Constable Lynne Mackenzie from Elgin CID said: “It was fortunate nobody was injured in this incident.

“The road this happened on leads to Mulben. We are looking to speak to anyone who was in the area at the time, or may have dash cam footage from yesterday morning, to contact us so we can trace whoever is responsible.”

Anyone with any information is asked to contact 101 quoting reference number 0546 of 15 October 2020.
