Two men have been arrested and charged in connection with a fire raising incident in Arbroath.

The incident happened at Sidney Street in the town around 5am on Monday, 5 October.

The two men, aged 22 and 19, are expected to appear at Dundee Sheriff Court on Tuesday, 13 October.
