Police in Glasgow are appealing for information following a serious assault in the Panmure Street area.

The incident happened around 6.15pm on Sunday, 11 October, 2020, when a 33-year-old man was assaulted by a male on Panmure Street.

The man sustained serious facial injuries and was taken to Glasgow Royal Infirmary where he remains in a stable condition.

The suspect is described as a white male, early 30s, of slim build with dark scruffy hair and stubble. He was wearing a grey/black jumper and dark jeans.

Detective Constable Paul Corr, of Maryhill CID, said: “We are now appealing for witnesses to assist with our investigation.

“Anyone who was in the area around the time of the incident or who may have dash-cam footage, should contact police by calling 101 and quoting incident number 2832 of 11 October.”
