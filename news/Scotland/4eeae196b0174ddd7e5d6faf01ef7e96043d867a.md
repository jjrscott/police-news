Police in Clydebank are appealing for witnesses after an indecent incident near a school in the town.

It happened around 2.25pm yesterday (Thursday 8 October) on Kirkoswald Drive.

A man exposed himself to passing teenagers before carrying out a sexual act, he then made off from the area.

The suspect is described as white, between 30 and 40 years old, with dark hair. He was wearing black combat style trousers and a dark green jacket.

Detective Sergeant David Lavery said: “This was a distressing experience for those involved and enquiries are ongoing to identify the male. We are appealing to anyone who may have seen a man matching this description, in or around the area at the time, to contact us via 101 quoting incident number 1709 of 8 Oct 2020.”
