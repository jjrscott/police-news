**Published 29 May 2020**

Two men have been charged following the execution of a drugs search warrant at an address in Raploch, Stirling on Friday, 29 May, 2020.

Officers seized quantities of crack cocaine and heroin with a street value of Â£2,500.

Two men, aged 50 and 23, have been arrested and charged in connection and will appear from custody at Falkirk Sheriff Court on Monday, 1 June, 2020.

Detective Inspector Norrie Petrie, of Forth Valley CID, said: "We are committed to tackling the illegal supply of drugs in our communities and the tragic impact they bring. We will continue to work tirelessly to bring those involved to justice.

"We rely on members of the public to provide us with information which we act on. We would encourage anyone with information about suspicious activity to contact Police Scotland on 101, or if you wish to remain anonymous, Crimestoppers on 0880 555 111."
