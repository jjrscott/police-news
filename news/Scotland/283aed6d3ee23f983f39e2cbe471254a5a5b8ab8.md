Police Scotland can confirm that a third man has been charged in connection with the death of a man in Kirkintilloch.

Robert Cunningham, 29, died on Monday, 25 August after the incident within a property on Braehead Street in the town on Saturday, 22 August.

A 34-year-old man has now been arrested and charged in connection with the incident. He is due to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court on Wednesday, 26 August.

Two men - aged 31 and 28 - were previously charged and appeared in court on Tuesday, 25 August.
