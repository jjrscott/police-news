**Published 12 February 2020**

Police in Aberdeen area appealing for information following a fire in the Gramps area of Kincorth.

The fire was reported to police shortly after 9.20am on Wednesday, 12 February.
It was extinguished by the Scottish Fire and Rescue Service and nobody was injured.
Following an examination of the area the fire is now being treated as deliberate.

Sergeant Kevin Souter, of Nigg Police Station, said:

"Deliberately starting a fire is extremely reckless - you only have limited control over how it may develop.

"I'd appeal to anyone with information or may have seen anything suspicious in the area to let us know.

"You can contact police by calling 101, quoting incident 0859 of 12 January, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
