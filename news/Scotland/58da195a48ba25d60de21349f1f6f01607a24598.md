Around 11.20am on Tuesday, 10 November, 2020, officers carried out a search of a property in Feus Road, Perth.

Within the property a cannabis cultivation with an estimated street value of £20,000 was recovered.

Herbal cannabis with an estimated street value of £5,500 was also found.

Constable Matthew Bull from Perth Police Station said: “Enquiries are ongoing to identify and trace the person or persons involved in this drugs activity and I would appeal to anyone with information that could assist officers to get in touch.

“Drugs have absolutely no place in our community and officers are working every day to gather intelligence and target offenders.

“Information can be passed to Police Scotland through 101, quoting incident number 0979 of Tuesday, 10 November. Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.”
