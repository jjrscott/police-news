A man who died following a fatal road traffic collision on St Margaret Street in Dunfermline can now be named as Steven Kennedy.

The incident happened around 9.50pm on Wednesday, 19 February, 2020, when a white Mercedes Benz A-Class on St Margaret Street collided with a 33-year-old man and stopped. Emergency services attended and he was pronounced dead at the scene.
