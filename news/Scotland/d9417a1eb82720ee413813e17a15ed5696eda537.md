**Published 23 January 2020**

Road Policing officers in Dingwall are appealing for witnesses after a motorcyclist was forced to leave the road due to an approaching vehicle which was overtaking in to his path. The rider sustained minor injuries and his motorcycle was damaged.

The incident occurred around 5.00pm on Saturday, 18 January on the A832 at Rosehaugh, with a number of other vehicles reported to have been present on the road at the time.

Officers are looking for any information about the car involved in the incident and are appealing for witnesses. The offending vehicle was heading towards Avoch.

Anyone who may have been in the area and witnessed the incident, or anyone who may have dashcam footage, is urged to contact Police Scotland on 101, quoting incident 2841 of 18 January.
