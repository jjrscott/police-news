Police Scotland can confirm that a third man, aged 26, has been arrested in connection with the death of Billy McGuire in Clydebank.

The incident took place around 12.05am on Monday, 21 December, 2020 when 25-year-old Billy McGuire was found with serious injuries on Perth Crescent. He later died in hospital.

Detective Chief Inspector Alan McAlpine of the Major Investigation Team said: “We continue to conduct enquiries surrounding Billy’s death and I would urge anyone who has any information to come forward and speak to Police.

“I would also like to thank the community for their assistance with our investigation so far.

“If anyone has any information I would urge that they get in touch with police by calling 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.”
