Following an investigation, officers have arrested a 27-year-old man in connection with the death of a 32-year-old man in Glasgow.

Omer Sadiq (32) was found seriously injured on Boydstone Road around 1.25pm on Tuesday, 15 September. He was taken to the Queen Elizabeth University Hospital where he died a short time later.

A 27-year-old man has been arrested in connection with his death and is due to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court on Friday, 2 October, 2020. A report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.
