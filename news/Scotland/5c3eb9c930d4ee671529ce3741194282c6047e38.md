**Published 11 March 2020**

Police in Glasgow are releasing image of a male who they believe may be able to assist them with their enquiries in relation to a robbery which occurred at Your Secret Desires, 359 Argyle Street, Glasgow.

The incident occurred around 1437 hours on Thursday the 26th of December 2019. A knife was presented towards the victim and a sum of money was stolen.

Officers wish to trace the male shown in the image who they believe may be able to assist them with their enquiries. The male is described as White-Scottish, with a Glaswegian accent, approximately 5 foot 7 in height, in his late 20s and of slim build. He is shown in the image wearing a dark skip cap, black bubble jacket, dark navy blue joggers and dark blue/grey trainers.

Anyone with information which could help should contact police as soon as possible on the 101 number, quoting incident number 2112 of 26th of December 2019.

Anyone wishing to pass on information and remain anonymous can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
