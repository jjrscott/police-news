Police Scotland has released images of two individuals they would like to identify and speak to. It is believed that the man and woman shown in the images may have information that could assist an ongoing police investigation into a fraud that occurred in the Aberdeen area on Tuesday, 11 August, 2020.

The man and woman are believed to be from the Tollcross area of Glasgow.

Detective Constable Mark McKenzie, of North East Division, said: "I would appeal to anyone who recognises the man and woman in these photographs to please contact us as soon as possible as they may have information which could assist us. Likewise, I would appeal to them directly to get in touch.

"Anyone with information should contact police on 101, quoting CF0183530820 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 if you would prefer to remain anonymous."
