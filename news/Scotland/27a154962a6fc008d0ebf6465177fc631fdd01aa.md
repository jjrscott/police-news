**Published 28 July 2020**

A 35-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a wilful fireraising.

The incident happened around 5pm on Tuesday, 28 July, 2020, when a barn at Setonhill Farm, Longniddry was set on fire.

Fortunately no one was injured and the fire was extinguished by the Scottish Fire and Rescue Service.
