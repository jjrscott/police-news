Officers from the Road Policing Unit at Dingwall are continuing their enquiries and appealing for witnesses after a fatal road crash on the C1016 near Alness on Sunday, 29 November, 2020.

Around 0040 hours, police were called to a report of a road crash involving a Toyota RAV4 car on the C1016 between Alness and Achandunie, north of Millcraig Road. The 56 year-old female driver of the car was pronounced dead at the scene.

A full report will be forwarded to the Procurator Fiscal.

Sergeant Kate Park from the Road Policing Unit said: “We are working to establish the exact time that this road crash took place. I am urging anyone who was driving on this road between 10.30pm on Saturday evening, and 12.40am on Sunday morning who witnessed this take place, or who has information that may assist our continuing enquiry to contact us through 101 quoting reference number 0161 of 29 November.”
