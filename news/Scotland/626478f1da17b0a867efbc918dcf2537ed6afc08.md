Officers in Dundee are appealing for witnesses following a serious road crash involving a motorbike on the A92 near Arbroath.

The incident took place at around 7.35pm on Saturday, 17 October, 2020, on the A92 eastbound near the Balcathie junction.

The 17-year-old male motorcyclist was taken to Ninewells Hospital with a leg injury.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or anyone with dash-cam footage which may be able to assist enquiries are urged to contact police via 101, quoting incident 3574 of 17 October.
