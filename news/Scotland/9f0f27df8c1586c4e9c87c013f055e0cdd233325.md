Police Scotland can confirm that a 15-year-old boy has been charged in connection with making inappropriate sexual comments towards young females in Dundee.

The incidents happened in Americanmuir Road, Derwent Avenue and Gilburn Road between Monday, 26 October and Saturday, 31 October.

The 15-year-old will be reported to Youth Justice Assessor.

Chief Inspector David McIntosh, Local Area Commander for Dundee said :

“I’m aware that local people will be concerned by these incidents and I would like to assure them these are not connected to any other incidents which happened in the Kirkton area of the city over the weekend."
