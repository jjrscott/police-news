A 30-year-old man and 36-year-old woman have been arrested and charged in connection with an attempted robbery in the Kirkcaldy area.

The incident, involving a 14 year old boy, happened on Hendry Road in Kirkcaldy at around 10.55am on Saturday, 14 November, 2020.

Both are due to appear before Kirkcaldy Sheriff Court on Tuesday, 17 November, and a report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.
