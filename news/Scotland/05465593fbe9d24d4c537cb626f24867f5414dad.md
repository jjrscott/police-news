Officers in Renfrewshire are appealing for information following a robbery in Paisley.

Two men approached a man while he was in his car on Waverley Road, Foxbar, around 9pm on Tuesday, 1 September.

They threatened him with violence and stole his wallet, including a two figure sum of cash, before heading towards the footpath at Amochrie Road.

The men are described as white, between the age of 30 and 40. One was wearing a black zipper and the other a grey t-shirt.

Detective Constable Malcolm Clark said: “Thankfully no-one was injured as a result of this incident but the man was left badly shaken.

“We are looking to trace the men involved and are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident or two men matching this description in the area at the time to come forward.”

Anyone with any information is asked to contact 101 quoting incident 3545 of 1 September, 2020.
