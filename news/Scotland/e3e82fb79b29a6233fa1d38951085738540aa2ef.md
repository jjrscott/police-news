Police Scotland attended the A859 south of Ardhasaig, Isle of Harris, around 9.05am today (Thursday, 24 December, 2020) following a crash involving one vehicle.

Police Sergeant Kate Park of the Road Policing Unit in Dingwall said :

"The 29-year-old male driver was pronounced dead at the scene. His next of kin have been made aware and our thoughts are with them at this difficult time.
