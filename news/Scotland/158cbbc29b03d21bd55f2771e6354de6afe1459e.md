The woman who died in a fatal road crash on Barrhead Road, Glasgow, on Monday, 14 December, has been named.

Connie McAvoy (46) was from the local area and her relatives are aware.

The incident happened around 5.35pm on Barrhead Road close to its junction with Boydstone Road and involved a blue Subaru Impreza.

Connie was taken to the Queen Elizabeth University Hospital where she died a short time later.

Sergeant Stewart Taylor, of the Road Policing Unit, said: “Our thoughts remain with Connie's family at this tragic time.

"Enquiries are continuing into the circumstances surrounding the crash and we are urging any witnesses, or motorists who may have dash-cam footage which could be of assistance, to please get in touch.

“Anyone with information is asked to contact Police Scotland through 101, quoting incident number 2483 of 14 December.”
