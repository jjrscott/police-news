A 23-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a number of vehicle related crimes in Livingston.

The incidents happened in the Dedridge and Murieston areas of the town on the morning of Tuesday, 19 January.

The man is expected to appear at Livingston Sheriff Court on Monday, 25 January.

Officers would like to thank the victims and members of the public who have assisted with this investigation.
