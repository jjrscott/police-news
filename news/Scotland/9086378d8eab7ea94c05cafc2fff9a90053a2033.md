**Published 11 March 2020**

Today, Wednesday 11 March, 2020, police in Glasgow have released images of two men they believe may be able to assist with their enquiries in relation to an investigation into an assault and robbery.

The incident took place on Wednesday, 18 December, 2019, at around 19:15 at Burrells Lane, near to High Street in the city centre. A man suffered minor facial injuries and had a sum of money stolen.

Officers believe the men pictured may have information to assist their investigation and anyone who recognises either of them is urged to come forward.
