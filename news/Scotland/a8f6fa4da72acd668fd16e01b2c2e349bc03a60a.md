**Published 14 March 2020**

Police in Edinburgh have arrested and charged a man in connection with a serious assault that took place in October last year.

The incident happened at around 2.20am on Tuesday, 15 October, 2019, in St Andrew Square.

A 19-year-old man is expected to appear at Edinburgh Sheriff Court at a later date and a report has been submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.

Officers with to thank everyone who assisted with the earlier appeal for information.
