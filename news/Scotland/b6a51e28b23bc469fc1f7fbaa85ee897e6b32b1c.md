**Published 11 March 2020**

Police Scotland can confirm that 32-year-old Andrew Weir, an inmate at HMP Castle Huntly, Dundee, who went missing during home leave, has been traced.

Weir was reported missing on Friday, 6 March, and traced and arrested in the Killin area on Monday, 9 March.

Officers would like to thank all those who supported the appeal for information to trace Weir.
