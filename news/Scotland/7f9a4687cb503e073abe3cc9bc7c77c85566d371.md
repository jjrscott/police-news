Police are appealing for information after a theft by housebreaking at a shop in the Dumbarton area.

The incident occurred between 2am and 4am on Sunday, 25 October at USAVE on Brucehill Road. Entry was forced to the shop and a four figure sum of cash and tobacco products were stolen.

Detective Constable Robert Struthers of Clydebank CID said: “This incident has had a significant financial impact on a local business. We are appealing to anyone who may have been in the area at the time who observed anything suspicious or any vehicles in the area to contact police.

“Likewise anyone who has dashcam or private CCTV footage I would urge you contact us”

Anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101 quoting incident number 0919 of 25 October, or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
