**Published February 2020**

Police in Inverness have released images of a male they believe may be able to assist with their investigations following an incident whereby a male was assaulted on Sunday, 10 November 2019.

The incident occurred on Academy Street, Inverness at around 3am when a man was assaulted and sustained serious injuries as a result.

The man in the images is described as white, around 5ft 9in and of slim build, in his early 20s and with short brown hair. He was wearing black “Vans” trainers, light coloured trousers and a dark, long-sleeved jumper with a zip half way down the front.

Police Scotland request that any members of the public who recognises the male in the images or have any other information relating to the incident should contact them on 101, quoting incident number 0768 of 10 November 2019. Alternatively CRIMESTOPPERS can be contacted on 0800 555 111, where anonymity can be maintained.
