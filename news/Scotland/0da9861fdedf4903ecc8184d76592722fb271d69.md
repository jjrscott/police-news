Police Scotland have arrested and charged a man in connection with an assault around 12pm on Tuesday, 26 January.

The incident happened within a Post Office on Albert Street and involved a 70-year-old man.

A 47-year-old man was arrested and charged this morning (Friday, 29 January) and a report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal. We are not looking for anyone else in connection with this incident.

Officers would like to thank members of the public who assisted with enquiries and those who helped during the incident, it emphasises the strong community spirit that exists in the area.
