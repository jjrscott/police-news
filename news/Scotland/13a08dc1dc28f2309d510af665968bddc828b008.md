**Published 28 February 2020**

Police Scotland can confirm that a 36-year-old male cyclist has died after a road traffic collision on Friday, 28 February.

The collision happened at 6.30am on the A912 Bridge of Earn to Aberargie road and involved a car and the cyclist.

Inspector Greg Burns of the Road Policing Unit said : ‘The man’s next of kin have been informed and our thoughts are with them at this difficult time.

‘The road is closed to allow crash investigations to be carried out and we would appeal to anyone who witnessed the collision to get in touch with Police Scotland on 101 quoting 0500 of 28 February.
