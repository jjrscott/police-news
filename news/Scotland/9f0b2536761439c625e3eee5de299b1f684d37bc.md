Police are appealing for information following the theft of power tools and computer equipment from a premises in Fraserburgh.

The break-in, to a premises north of Whitewell, Tyrie, is believed to have happened between 5.30pm on Wednesday, 30 September and 6.30am on Thursday, 1 October.

The power tools and equipment taken have a total estimated value of £2000.
Constable Craig Bruce of Fraserburgh CPT said: “We are appealing for anyone who may have seen anything suspicious or any unusual vehicles in the area to contact us.

“We would also like to speak to anyone with information on individuals potentially trying to sell such items.

“Rural crime is a priority to us and I encourage anyone with information in relation to this incident to contact Police Scotland.”

Anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101 quoting incident 0397 of 1 October. Alternatively, information can be passed to Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111.
