Police Scotland is releasing an image of a man they wish to speak to, as it's believed he may be able to assist with an investigation into a fraudulent transaction.

It occurred within a jewellers in Glasgow on 14 December, 2019, at around 10.10am.

The man in the image is described as black, aged between 20-25 years of age, with black hair. He was wearing a blue zip-top, white t-shirt and black jeans.

Anyone who recognises him, or who has any information, is asked to contact officers via 101, quoting reference 2300 of 12 December, 2019, or to report this anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
