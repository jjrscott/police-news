Police Scotland can confirm that a 52 year old man has been arrested and charged in connection with an incident which saw a man threatened with a weapon and a three figure sum of cash stolen.

This happened on Friday, 10 July, 2020 at Gorgie Farm, Gorgie Road, Edinburgh.

He is due to appear at Edinburgh Sheriff Court at a later date.

We would like to thank the public for their assistance following our appeal for information.
