**Published 1st June 2020**

Police Scotland is looking to trace a man who may have been injured during an assault at Morgan Academy grounds in Dundee.

The incident took place between 10.15pm and 10.30pm on Sunday, 31 May.

Police Constable Jo Greenlaw said:

"We were made aware of an assault on footpath on the Pitkerro Road side of the school grounds. The victim made off from the scene and we need to trace the man to ensure he was not injured and that he is safe and well.

"He is described as being in 60s, with grey hair and wearing a blue t-shirt. We are appealing to the man to get in touch to confirm his welfare.

"The incident was witnessed by members of the public and we are following a positive line of enquiry as to who the person responsible is.

"If you are the man who may have be injured or you know who he is, please get in touch with Police Scotland on 101 quoting incident 4352 of Sunday 31 May or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
