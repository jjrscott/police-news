Man arrested and charged in connection with assault in Union Square in Aberdeen

A 39-year-old man has been arrested in connection with an assault in Aberdeen.

Around 1am on Saturday, 3 October, 2020, a 26-year-old woman was assaulted near to Union Square Shopping Centre in Civic Square, Aberdeen.

The man was arrested and charged on Tuesday 2 February 2021. He has been released on an undertaking to appear at a later date.

Officers would like to thank members of the public for their assistance in this inquiry.
