Police Scotland can now confirm a 24-year-old man has today (Wednesday, 2 September, 2020) been arrested and charged in connection with the fatal shooting of 26-year-old Graham Williamson in Caithness Street, Blantyre, which happened on Monday, 17 August, 2020.

He is due to appear at Hamilton Sheriff Court tomorrow (Thursday, 3 September, 2020) and a report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal.
