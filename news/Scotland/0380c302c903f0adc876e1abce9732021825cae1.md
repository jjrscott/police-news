Police Scotland continues to investigate the deaths of two people in Bishopbriggs.

Around 4.10pm on Wednesday, 23 December, 2020, a 16-year-old male youth and a 40-year-old man were found dead within a house in Grainger Road, Bishopbriggs.

They can be named as Antoni Thomas (16) and Seweryn Thomas (40).

Detective Inspector Stevie Watson, of Kirkintilloch CID, said: "Our thoughts are with Antoni and Seweryn's family at this very difficult time and we are providing them support.

"The incident is being treated as murder and enquiries are ongoing to establish the full circumstances. These enquiries are still at an early stage, however, we would like to speak to anyone who was in the vicinity of Grainger Road around 4pm on Wednesday, 23 December, and anyone who might have any information that could assist with our enquiries. Please contact police via 101 quoting incident number 2311 of 23 December, 2020."
