**Published 25 January 2020**

Police Scotland can confirm that a man arrested abroad on a European Arrest Warrant in connection with the murder of Jamie Lee in 2017 is expected to appear in court next week.

The 25-year-old, who was arrested in December 2019, is anticipated to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court on Monday, 27 January.

Mr Lee, 23, died following an incident in the Castlemilk area of Glasgow on Saturday, 8 July, 2017.
