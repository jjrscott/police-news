Police Scotland can confirm that a 33-year-old woman has been charged following an assault in Edinburgh last year.

The incident occurred in Greenside Place on Saturday, 7 December, 2019. During the incident, a man was assaulted.

Officers would like to thank those who shared the earlier appeal for information.
