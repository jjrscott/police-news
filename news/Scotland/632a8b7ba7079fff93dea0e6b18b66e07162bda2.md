Police Scotland can confirm a 38-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a number of reported incidents of indecent exposure in Wishaw on Sunday, 4 October, 2020.

He was due to appear at Hamilton Sheriff Court on Wednesday, 28 October, 2020.

We would like to thank the public for their assistance following our appeal for information.
