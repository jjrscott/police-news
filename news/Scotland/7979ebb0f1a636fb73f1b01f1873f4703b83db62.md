Police Scotland can confirm that a second man, aged 25, has been arrested and charged in connection with the death of 25-year-old Billy McGuire in Clydebank.

Officers attended an address on Perth Crescent around 12.05am on Monday, 21 December, 2020 where two men were found seriously injured.

Both men were taken to hospital where 25-year-old Billy McGuire was pronounced dead a short time later.
