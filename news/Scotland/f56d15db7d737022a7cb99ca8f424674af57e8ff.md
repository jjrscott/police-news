**Published 24th June 2020**

With reference to our previous release regarding a woman robbed within her flat in Dumbarton Road, Glasgow on Sunday, 3 May, 2020, please note:

A 21 year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with this robbery and he is due before Glasgow Sheriff Court on Thursday, 25 June 2020. Police enquiries are continuing.

A full report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal.
