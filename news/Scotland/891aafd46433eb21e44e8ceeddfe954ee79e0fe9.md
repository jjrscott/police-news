Police Scotland can confirm that a 26-year-old man is due in court today (Friday, 6 November) in connection with the death of Graham Williamson in Blantyre.

Mr Williamson, also 26, died following an incident at Caithness Street on Monday, 17 August, 2020.

The 26-year-old was arrested on a European Arrest Warrant in October and has now been charged. He is expected to appear at Hamilton Sheriff Court.

Two other men, aged 24 and 23, have previously been charged and appeared at court in connection with the death of Mr Williamson.
