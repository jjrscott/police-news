**Published 7 March 2020**

Police Scotland is appealing for information regarding the whereabouts of 32-year-old prisoner Andrew Weir.

Weir is an inmate at HMP Castle Huntly, Dundee, and has breached the conditions of his home leave.

It is believed that he has connections in the Bathgate and wider West Lothian area.

He is described as being 5ft 7in, medium build with short dark hair. When he was last seen he was wearing a grey tracksuit top and bottoms and grey trainers. He was also carrying a black holdall.

If seen, members of the public should not approach him but contact Police Scotland on 101, quoting incident 0933 of Friday, 6 March, 2020, or call the charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
