Police Scotland is appealing for the assistance of the public to help trace a 44-year-old man who failed to attend an appointment in the Tayside area.

Steven Dunn did not attend a pre-arranged meeting in Forfar on Thursday, 29 October, 2020.

Mr Dunn is from the Stoke-on-Trent area and was last seen at an address in Stoke on Thursday, 5 November, 2020.

He is described as white, around 5ft 8 tall, medium build with grey hair and grey facial hair. He speaks with an English accent and when last seen he was carrying a large light blue holdall.

Detective Inspector Gail Hill of Police Scotland said: "Mr Dunn does not have access to a car and is believed to have used public transport to travel to Stoke-on-Trent.

"We are keen to trace him as soon as possible. Members of the public who may have seen him, or have any information regarding his present whereabouts, should contact police on 101, quoting incident number 1612 of 18 November, 2020.
