Police Scotland can confirm that the body of a man was found in the Clydebank canal around 5.20pm on Thursday, 15 October, 2020.
The family of local man Scott Anderson, missing since Thursday, 1 October, have been made aware of the discovery.
Formal identification is yet to take place and a post mortem examination will be carried out in due course to establish the exact cause of death. The death is not being treated as suspicious.
