Police Scotland is continuing enquiries in relation to a theft in Glasgow City Centre.

Around 4.50pm on Sunday, 1 November, 2020, a motor vehicle was broken into on West George Street, Glasgow, and valuable items from within were stolen.

Officers have released an image of man who they believe can assist them with their investigation.

He is described as a white male, 6ft in height, medium build and was wearing a black hooded top, red skip cap, grey jogging bottoms and black trainers.

Anyone who recognises this man or has any information in connection is asked to contact Police Scotland on 101, quoting 3036 of 1 November, or if they wish to remain anonymous, can contact independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
