A 28-year-old man and a 31-year-old woman have been arrested and charged following a break-in at a clothing store in The Green, Aberdeen.

The incident happened sometime between 8pm on Monday, 30 November and 10am on Tuesday, 1 December, 2020.

Both are due to appear at Aberdeen Sheriff Court on Monday 7 December 2020.

A report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.
