A 35-year-old man has been arrested and charged following the attempted murder of a 35-year-old man in a supermarket car park in Main Street, Ayr on Sunday, 27 September.

The man will appear at Ayr Sheriff Court on Monday, 5 October 2020.

The victim has since been released from hospital.

The public are thanked for their assistance with this matter.
