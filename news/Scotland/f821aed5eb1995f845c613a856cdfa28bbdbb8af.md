**Published 23 July 2020**

Police Scotland can confirm that two men have been charged in connection with an attempted murder in Glasgow.

The 30-year-old and 53-year-old men were arrested following the incident which happened around 5.50pm on Tuesday, 21 July, on Devonshire Gardens.

Both men are due to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court today (Thursday, 23 July, 2020).
