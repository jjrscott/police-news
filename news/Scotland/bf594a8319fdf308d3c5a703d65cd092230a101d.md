**Published 24 March 2020**

Police in Aberdeenshire can confirm that two boys, aged 14 and 15, and a 13-year-old girl have been charged in relation to a wilful fireraising, which took place in derelict building in the Commercial Street area of Macduff on Sunday, 22 March.

A report will be submitted to the Youth Justice Management Unit.
