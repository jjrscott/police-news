**Published 17 July 2020**

Police Scotland can confirm a 34-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a serious assault in the Milton area of Glasgow in January.

A 69-year-old woman was injured in the incident which happened at a property on Castlebay Street around 3.25pm on Monday, 27 January, 2020.

The man is due to appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court today (Friday, 17 July).
