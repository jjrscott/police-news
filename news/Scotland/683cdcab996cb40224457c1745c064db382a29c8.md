Detectives are releasing an image of a man they believe may be able to assist in their investigation into an incident in Glasgow that is being treated by them as attempted murder.

Around 0020 hrs on Friday 15 March 2019, a 21 year old man was seriously assaulted in Duke Street, Glasgow.

Officers have released an image of a man they believe may be able to assist them with their enquiries and would ask any members of the public who recognise him to contact police.

The man is white, medium build, with dark hair. At the time of the incident, he was wearing a dark coloured hooded top, dark tracksuit trousers and dark trainers.

Anyone with information is asked to contact the officers at Shettleston Police Station via 101 quoting incident number 0090 of 15 March 2019. Alternatively, please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, where anonymity can be maintained.
