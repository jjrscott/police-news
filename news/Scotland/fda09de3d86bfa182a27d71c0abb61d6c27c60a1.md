**Published 18th June 2020**

Police Scotland can confirm that two men have been charged in connection with a fatal road traffic collision on the A9.

Gregor McIntosh, 16, from Inverness died in the incident at the Munlochy Junction on the Black Isle around 6.55pm on Friday, 20 December, 2019.

The two men - aged 18 and 48 - have been charged with causing death by careless driving and reports have been submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.

They are both expected to appear in court in due course.
