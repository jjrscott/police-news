A man has been arrested and charged in connection with an attempted murder in Greenock.

The incident took place on Cumberland Road on Saturday, 19 September, when an item, filled with accelerant, was thrown at a house.

The 24-year-old man was arrested and charged on Friday, 6 November. He is due to appear at Greenock Sheriff Court on Monday, 9 November.
