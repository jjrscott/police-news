Detectives at Maryhill are continuing with enquiries into a robbery that took place at a newsagents in Celtic Street, Glasgow around 12.50pm on Tuesday, 14 April, 2020. Officers have issued an image of two men they believe may be able to assist with their ongoing investigations.

The two men officers wish to trace are white and were both wearing dark-coloured clothing. One of them had on a dark-coloured jacket and tracksuit trousers with grey and white trainers. The other was wearing a dark-coloured hooded top with white stripes on the shoulders and arms and a white logo on the chest. He was also wearing black tracksuit trousers and black trainers with white soles.

Anyone who recognises these two men, or who has information on this robbery should contact officers at the Community Investigation Unit based at Helen Street Police Station through 101 quoting reference number 1476 of 14 April. Alternatively Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111, where anonymity can be maintained.
