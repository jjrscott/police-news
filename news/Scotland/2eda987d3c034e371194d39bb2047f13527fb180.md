Detectives are releasing images of a man they believe may be able to assist them with their enquiries in relation to an ongoing investigation into an assault which occurred at around 9.30pm on Sunday, 24 May 2020, in Lancefield Quay, Glasgow.

As a result of the incident a man required hospital treatment.

The man in the images is described as having sallow skin, 16 to 20 years of age, medium build, short dark hair, 5 ft 10ins and was wearing a black tracksuit. He was in possession of a mountain bike.

If anyone has any information or recognises the above description, please call officers at Drumchapel CID via 101, quoting incident number SP-20200525-0248. Alternatively, please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
