Police Scotland can confirm that a 42-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with a serious sexual assault in Kelty.

The incident was reported around 11.15pm on Monday, 6 July, 2020 when a woman was found in the Limepark Crescent area and disclosed to officers that she has been attacked shortly before within a car.

The man is due to appear at Dunfermline Sheriff Court on Tuesday, 18 August, 2020.
