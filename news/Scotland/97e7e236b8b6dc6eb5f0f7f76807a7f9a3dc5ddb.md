Police have named the 56-year-old lady who died following a road crash on the C1016 near Alness on Sunday, 29 November, 2020, as Karen McKeddie from Alness. Her family are aware.

Mrs McKeddie died at the scene after her Toyota RAV4 car crashed on the C1016, between Alness and Achandunie, north of Millcraig Road.

Sergeant Kate Park from the Road Policing Unit said:

"We are still appealing to anyone who was on this road between 10.30pm on Saturday evening and 12.40am on Sunday morning and who witnessed this crash happen or who may have dash-cam footage from the area to come forward.
