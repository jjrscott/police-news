**Published 20 February 2020**

Police Scotland can confirm that a 26-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with the death of 34-year-old Morgan Dunn.

The incident happened around 4pm on Saturday, 8 February, 2020, at an address in Marigold Square, Ayr.

The man is due to appear at Ayr Sheriff Court on Thursday, 20 February, 2020.

A report will be sent to the Procurator Fiscal.
