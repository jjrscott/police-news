Police Scotland can confirm that a 31-year-old man has been arrested in connection with the death of a man in Townhead, Glasgow.

The body of Tomasz Lipiec, 33, was found in a property in Dobbies Loan Place, around 6.45 pm on Monday, 7 September, 2020.

Enquiries are continuing.
