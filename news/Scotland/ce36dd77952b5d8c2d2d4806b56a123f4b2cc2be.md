Police in Aberdeen can confirm that two teenage boys, aged 14-years-old, have been charged in connection with a recent wilful fireraising in the city.

The incident happened on Sunday, 20 December when a fire was set within a derelict building on Old Stonehaven Road, resulting in significant damage.

Both youths will be subject to a report to the Youth Justice Management Unit.

Officers would like to thank members of the local community for their assistance with this enquiry.
