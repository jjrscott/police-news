**Published 3 February 2020**

Officers have released an image of a man who officers wish to speak to after a bottle was thrown at the Celtic and Hearts match on Saturday 25 August 2019.

It is believed he may have information that will assist their investigation in relation to an incident which occurred around 3.40pm on Saturday, 25 August 2019 within Celtic Park Football Stadium during the football match between Celtic and Hearts when a bottle was thrown onto the pitch.

Anyone who recognises the man in the image or who has any information is asked to contact Police via 101, quoting incident number 3100 of Sunday 26 August 2019. Alternatively, Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111 where information can be given anonymously.
