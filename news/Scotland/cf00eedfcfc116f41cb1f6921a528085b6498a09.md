MAN ARRESTED AND CHARGED

We can confirm that a 23 year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with the fatal shooting of 26-year-old Graham Williamson in Caithness Street, Blantyre, which happened on Monday, 17 August, 2020.

He is due before Hamilton Sheriff Court on Wednesday 26 August 2020 and a report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.
