**Published 10 July 2020**

In connection with the investigation relating to the alleged mistreatment of residents at Springvale Care Home in Lennoxtown, three women, aged 55, 57 and 62, have been arrested and charged.

A report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal. Relevant families and partners have been made aware.
