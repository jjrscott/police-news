A boy from Peterborough has been arrested and charged in connection with an incident at a McDonalds restaurant yesterday afternoon (Sunday).

The 17 year old was arrested at about 5.15pm after being located in Star Mews, Eastgate, and has since been charged with possession of a knife and using threatening language or behaviour with intent to cause fear.

The boy, of Bluebell Avenue, Dogsthorpe, has been remanded in custody to appear at Peterborough Magistrates' Court today (17 February).
