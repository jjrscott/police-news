A 36 year old man was arrested yesterday (Thursday, 7 November) in connection with numerous keyless car thefts across Cambridgeshire.

Paulius Puras was arrested following a warrant at his home address in Woodhurst Road, Peterborough, and has since been charged with conspiring to steal vehicles.

He has been remanded in custody to appear at Peterborough Crown Court on 6 December.
