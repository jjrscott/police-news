Officers have released CCTV images of a man and woman they would like to speak to following an assault at the Milton Arms pub in Cambridge.

Milton Arms CCTV appeal

At about 12.15am on Saturday, 23 June a member of staff was assaulted and left with minor injuries after trying to remove the woman from the pub.

PC Ed Allison said: "This took place in a busy pub and I'm keen to speak to anyone who was there at the time or who may recognise the man or woman pictured."

Anyone with information should call police on 101 quoting 35/8260/18 or visit www.cambs.police.uk/report. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555111 or via www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
