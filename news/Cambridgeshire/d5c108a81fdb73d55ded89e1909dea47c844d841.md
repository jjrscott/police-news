Officers investigating a serious collision on the A10, Royston Road, on Saturday evening (9 November) are appealing for witnesses.

At about 11.30pm a Volkswagen Polo struck a pedestrian who was walking along the road.

The pedestrian, a man, was taken to Addenbrooke's Hospital and remains in a serious, but stable, condition.

The road was closed while the incident was dealt with and no arrests have been made.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, or has any information about build up to it, is asked to call police on 101 quoting incident 0609 of 9 November.
