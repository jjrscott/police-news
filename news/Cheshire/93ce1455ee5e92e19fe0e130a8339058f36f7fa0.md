11:19 28/11/2019

**Detectives have charged four people with burglary offences in the Chester and North Wales area.**

All four have been remanded into custody and will appear at Crewe Magistrates Court on Friday 29 November.

Anna Trepczyk, aged 28, of Watery Road in Wrexham has been charged with conspiracy to commit burglary.

Sebastian Mozerys, aged 23, of no fixed address has been charged with conspiracy to commit burglary.

Paulina Majczak, aged 26, of North Road, Ponciau in Wrexham has been charged with conspiracy to commit burglary.

Patryk Korybolski, aged 26, of North Road, Ponciau in Wrexham has been charged with conspiracy to commit burglary.

The charges relate to arrests made in Wrexham on Thursday 28 November in-connection with an operation involving around 80 rural burglaries.
