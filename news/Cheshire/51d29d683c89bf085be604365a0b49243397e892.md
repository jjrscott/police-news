08:27 26/03/2020

A 40-year-old man has been charged with murder in relation to the death of Warrington man Mark Bradbury.

Daniel Shovelton, of Longbarn, Warrington, was arrested at his home address on Tuesday 24 March.

He has been remanded into custody and will appear at Warrington Magistrates' Court on Thursday 26 March.

At 10pm on Sunday 4 August Mark Bradbury's body was found at his home on Station Road, Padgate, Warrington .

Mark, who was 58-years-old, had suffered significant head injuries in a brutal attack.

Cheshire Police would ask anybody with information regarding this offence to contact the enquiry team on 101 or via the Major Incident Public Portal https://mipp.police.uk/operation/07CH18D08-PO3.

Information can also be provided anonymously by calling Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
