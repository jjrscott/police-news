11:21 17/08/2020

**Police have released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with criminal damage at shops in Prestbury.**

Between 00.50am and 1.10am on Wednesday 29 July a number of premises had their windows smashed in the village.

Officers have been following a number of lines of enquiry and would like to speak to the man in the CCTV image.

They are asking anyone who recognises him or has information regarding his whereabouts to get in touch.

If you can assist officers with their investigation please contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 766256 or through our website.

Information can also be provided anonymously by calling Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
