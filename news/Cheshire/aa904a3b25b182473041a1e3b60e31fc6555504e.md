11:41 02/12/2019

Police have charged a man in-connection with drugs found at an address in Widnes.

Van Binh Doan, aged 37, of no fixed address has been charged with being concerned in the supply of cannabis. He has been remanded and will appear at Warrington Magistrates Court on Monday 2 December.

A 17-year-old boy was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of cannabis and has been released on conditional bail.

 The charge relates to a warrant that took place at an address on Frank Street at 3.40pm on Saturday 30 November.
