12:09 08/08/2020

A man who was arrested following a disturbance in Chester has been remanded in custody.

Alex Mills was arrested on Saturday 8 August following an incident on Francis Street.

The 24-year-old has since been charged with possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life.

Mills, of Whitchurch Road, Christleton, appeared at Chester Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 11 August where he was remanded in custody.

He is next set to appear at Chester Crown Court on Tuesday 8 September.
