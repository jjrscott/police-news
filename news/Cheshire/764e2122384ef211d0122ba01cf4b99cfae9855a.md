17:58 10/11/2019

**Police have charged two men with murder following the death of a man in Macclesfield.**

Daniel Whitmore, aged 25, of Mayfield Avenue, Macclesfield, and Daniel Mayers, aged 38, of Glegg Street, Macclesfield, are due to appear before Crewe Magistrates' Court on Monday 11 November.

Mayers was arrested on the evening of Friday 8 November and Whitmore was arrested on Saturday 9 November and both have subsequently been charged and remanded into custody.

The victim - Peter Robson (aka Archie), aged 59, from Macclesfield - died on Saturday 9 November.

He was taken to Macclesfield Hospital the previous night after being found with serious injuries at an address in Glegg Street.

Officers remain in the local area to provide reassurance and anyone with any concerns is urged to speak to them.
