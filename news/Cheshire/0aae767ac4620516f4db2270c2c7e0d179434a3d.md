14:58 31/07/2020

Detectives investigating a serious assault in Nantwich have arrested a 17-year-old boy.

The teenager was arrested in Stockport on suspicion of attempted murder and is currently in police custody waiting to be questioned by police.

At 8.20pm on Sunday 27 July police were made aware of a man inside a flat on Tailors View, Arnold Street who had suffered severe injuries to his head.

A 42-year-old man has been charged with attempted murder and is due to appear at Chester Crown Court on 27 August.

A 50-year-old man has been released on conditional bail.
