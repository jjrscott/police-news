15:20 25/01/2021

**Officers are appealing for more information following a fatal collision in Middlewich.**

At around 6.08am on Sunday 24 January police were called to King Street following reports of a collision involving a black BMW and a white Nissan van.

The driver of the black BMW, a 26-year-old man from Middlewich, died at the scene.

His next of kin have been informed.

The driver of the Nissan van, a man, was taken to hospital for checks. He has since been discharged.

Police are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed the collision or may have any dashcam footage of the vehicles before the incident to come forward.

Anyone with any information is urged to call 101 quoting IML 902218 or report it via our website.

Alternatively anyone with information can report to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via the Crimestoppers website.
