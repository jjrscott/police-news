14:47 10/03/2020

**UPDATE** \- Officers investigating a number of thefts from motor vehicles in Northwich have now charged a man in connection with the incidents.

Jason Burke, of no fixed abode in Northwich, was arrested on Tuesday 10 March following a number of incidents in the town.

The 37-year-old has since been charged with seven counts of theft from a motor vehicle and three counts of vehicle interference.

The charges relate to incidents in David Street, Rosemary Drive, Northway, Naylor Court, Flower Street, Romanes Street, Queensgate and Penrhyn Road.

He has been remanded in custody and is set to appear at Chester Magistrates' Court on Thursday 12 March.
