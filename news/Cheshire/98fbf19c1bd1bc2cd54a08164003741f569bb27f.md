09:29 25/11/2020

**A 22-year-old man from Widnes has been charged following a robbery at a supermarket in the town.**

Daniel Flanagan, of Rivenmill Way, was arrested in Mill Lane, Widnes, at around 8.30pm on Monday 23 November.

He has subsequently been charged with robbery and possession of a class B drug (cannabis).

The charges relate to an incident at a supermarket off Derby Road that occurred shortly before 8.30pm that night.

Flanagan appeared at Warrington Magistrates' Court, via a video link, on Wednesday 25 November.

He was remanded in custody and is set to appear at Liverpool Crown Court on Monday 21 December.
