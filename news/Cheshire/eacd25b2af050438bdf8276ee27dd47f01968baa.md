14:59 23/03/2020

**The family of a 24-year-old man from Middlewich who died following a collision on Tuesday 17 March have paid tribute to him.**

Metin Mustafov was taken to Warrington Hospital with serious injuries after being involved in a collision on the A559 Northwich Road in Whitley.

Sadly, Metin passed away a short time later.

Metin's family have now paid tribute to him, they said: "Loving son and brother to Firdes, Beychet and Beyhan.

"The family would like to thank the emergency services and members of the public for their help during this incident and ask for respect at this difficult time."
