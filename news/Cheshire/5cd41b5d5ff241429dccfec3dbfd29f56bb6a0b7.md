14:19 01/05/2020

**A 25-year-old man from Widnes has been charged with three offences after officers conducted a stop and search in the town.**

Jack Feeney, of Green Oaks Path, was arrested in Page Lane at around 1.45pm on Thursday 30 April.

He has subsequently been charged with possession of class A drug cocaine and class B drug cannabis.

Feeney has also been charged with possession of a bladed article in a public place.

He appeared at Warrington Magistrates' Court via a video link on Friday 1 May.

Feeney has been remanded in custody and is set to return to the court on Friday 29 May.
