11:01 19/12/2019

Detectives from Macclesfield have charged four men following an armed robbery at an address in Higher Hurdsfield.

Rebanne Cantrill, John McLeod, Jordan Morrisey and Devon Patterson were all arrested on Tuesday 17 December following a robbery at a house on Swanscoe La

ne.

Cantrill (18) of Manchester; McLeod (19) of Back Haslam Street, Bury; Morrisey (18) of no fixed abode, Manchester and Patterson (20) of Stan Jolly Walk, Manchester, were all subsequently charged with robbery and possession of an offensive weapon.

The men have been remanded in custody and are set to appear at Chester Magistrates' Court on Thursday 19 December.
