14:30 30/01/2020

**A 19-year-old man has been charged following a robbery in Wilmslow.**

Rilwan Mboob was arrested on Westbank Road in Manchester shortly after 7pm on Tuesday 28 January.

He has since been charged with robbery.

The charge relates to an incident that occurred on Station Road in Wilmslow at around 3.30pm on Tuesday 28 January.

A 13-year-old boy from Wilmslow had his mobile phone stolen during the incident.

Mboob, of Westbank Road, Manchester, appeared at Chester Magistrates' Court on Thursday 30 January.

He has been remanded in custody and is set to appear at Chester Crown Court on Thursday 27 February.
