11:45 18/11/2019

Officers from Northwich LPU are appealing for information from the public following two burglaries and an attempted burglary in Rudheath.

At 9.30pm on Saturday 16 November officers were contacted by a member of the public who reported suspicious activity on Wentworth Close.

Officers swiftly attended the scene and found that three unknown men had attempted to break into an address by forcing open a window.

The suspects had fled the scene prior to their arrival.

Officers conducted a search of the local area to locate the men and discovered that two other nearby homes had also been burgled.

Two of the suspects were describes as white men, around 5'10" to 5'11" tall and of average build.

The third suspect was described as a man, around 5'5" - 5'6" tall, of stocky with dark eyes brows and a Mediterranean complexion.

Enquiries in relation to all three incidents are ongoing and anyone with any information which may assist the investigation is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 566786

Information can also be passed anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0500 555 111.
