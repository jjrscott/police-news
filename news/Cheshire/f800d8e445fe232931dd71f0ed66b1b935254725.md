11:27 30/09/2020

**A 28-year-old man and a 30-year-old man have been charged in connection with drug offences.**

Alexander Hewson, of Weaver Road in Nantwich, and Michael Davies, of Prospect Vale in Liverpool were arrested at around 5am on Tuesday 29 September.

They have both been charged with conspiracy to supply class A and B drugs.

Hewson has also been charged with conspiracy to possess a firearm.

They have been remanded in custody and are due to appear at Warrington Magistrates Court on Wednesday 30 September.

The charges relate to early morning warrants executed on Tuesday 29 September in connection with serious and organised crime.
