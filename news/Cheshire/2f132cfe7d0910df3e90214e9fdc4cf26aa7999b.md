15:02 23/03/2021

**Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Warrington.**

Officers were called just before 4.30pm on Monday 22 March to Enfield Park Road following reports of a collision involving a cyclist and a pedestrian.

The pedestrian, a woman in her 80s, was taken to hospital with life-threatening injuries. She remains in a critical condition.

The cyclist stopped at the scene and is assisting police with their enquiries.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have captured CCTV, dashcam footage or witnessed the incident.

Anyone with information can contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 944773 or report it via the Cheshire Police website.
