16:46 04/03/2020

Three men have been charged with drugs offences following an investigation by Crewe detectives into drug dealing in the area.
The charges followed warrants at addresses in Crewe on 27 February.
Reece Polles, 21, from Cranborne Road in Crewe, was charged with two counts of possession with intent to supply Class A drugs (cocaine and heroin)
Darren Lawton-Jones, 21, from no fixed address was charged with three counts of possession with intent to supply class A drugs (cocaine, crack cocaine and heroin) and acquiring or possessing criminal property.
Troy Jones, 22, from Crabtree Grove in Crewe was charged with three counts of possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class A (heroin, crack cocaine and cocaine.)
They all appeared at Crewe Magistrates on 29 February and will next appear at Chester Crown Court on 30 March,
Two women age 39 and 64 and a man aged 39 were also arrested but were released under investigation pending further enquiries.
