**Police are asking the public to help them trace a 43-year-old man from the Lache area of Chester who is wanted for failing to appear at court.**

A warrant was issued for the arrest of David Beach after he failed to attend Chester Magistrates' Court on Thursday 5 March.

He is believed to be in the Chester area.

Beach is 5' 9" tall with a proportionate build. He is white and has short dark brown hair and green eyes.

Anyone who sees Beach, or has information regarding his whereabouts, is asked to call Cheshire Constabulary immediately on 101, quoting 20000140772.

Alternatively, information can be given anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
