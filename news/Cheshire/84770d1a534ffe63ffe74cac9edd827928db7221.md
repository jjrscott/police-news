15:38 01/04/2021

**A 26-year-old man from Crewe has been charged with drug dealing offences after officers conducted a stop and search in the town.**

Mohammed Harun Miah, of Samuel Street, was arrested in Flag Lane at around 3pm on Tuesday 30 March.

He has subsequently been charged with possession with intent to supply class A drugs heroin and cocaine.

Miah has also been charged with possession of criminal property in connection to a quantity of cash seized by officers.

He appeared at Warrington Magistrates' Court on Thursday 1 April.

Miah was remanded in custody and is set to appear at Chester Crown Court on Thursday 29 April.
