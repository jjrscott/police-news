09:17 24/07/2020

Detectives have charged three men with firearm and drug offences.

Danny Hanley, aged 29, of Lorenzo Drive in Liverpool has been charged with conspiracy to supply firearms and ammunition and conspiracy to supply cocaine.

Jack Wymer, aged 26, of Brookwood Road, Huyton in Liverpool has been charged with conspiracy to supply firearms and ammunition and conspiracy to supply cocaine, heroin, ketamine and cannabis.

Lewis Fitzpatrick, aged 25, of Eldersfield Road in Liverpool has been charged with conspiracy to supply firearms and ammunition and conspiracy to supply ketamine and cannabis after being interviewed in prison.

The charges relate to two arrests made on Thursday 23 July following a number of warrants in Liverpool as part of an operation targeting serious and organised crime.

All three men are due to appear at Warrington Magistrates Court on Friday 24 July.
