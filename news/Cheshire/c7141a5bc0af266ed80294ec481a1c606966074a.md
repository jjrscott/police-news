17:36 31/01/2020

A 31-year-old man from Chester has been charged with class B drug offences.

Terry Busch, of no fixed address, has been charged with possession with intent to supply a class B drug (Spice) and being concerned in offering to supply a class B drug (Spice).

He was arrested by officers after a warrant took place at an address on Queens Avenue on Thursday 30 January.

Busch has been remanded into custody and is due to appear at Crewe Magistrates' Court on Monday 3 February.
