11:33 31/12/2020

Detectives investigating a burglary at a residential house in Widnes have charged a man in connection with the incident.

Jamie Horgan was arrested on Tuesday 29 December at an address On Stapleton Avenue in Liverpool.

The 25-year-old has since been charged with burglary, escaping lawful custody and recall to prison.

The burglary charge relates to an incident at an address on Kingsway, Widnes, which occurred on 21 October 2020.

Horgan, of no fixed abode, has been remanded in police custody and is set to appear at Warrington Magistrates' Court on Thursday 31 December.
