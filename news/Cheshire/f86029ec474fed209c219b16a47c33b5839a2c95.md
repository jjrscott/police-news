13:14 06/08/2020

Detectives investigating a large disturbance in Widnes have now charged three men.

Luke Price, aged 52, Rueben Price, 47, and Rueben Price Jnr, 25, were all arrested by officers following an incident which occurred at around 12.30pm on Monday 3 August on Factory Lane, Widnes.

Luke, of Southworth Road, Newton-le-Willows, Rueben, of Factory Lane, Widnes and Rueben Jnr, of Regent Street, Newton-le-Willows, have all since been charged with one count of violent disorder and two counts of having a firearm with intent to commit an indictable offence.

The trio have been remanded in custody and are set to appear at Warrington Magistrates Court today, Thursday 6 August.
