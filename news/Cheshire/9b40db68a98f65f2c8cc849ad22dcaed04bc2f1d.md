12:07 07/04/2020

A man arrested by detectives in Runcorn has pleaded guilty to four counts of attempted burglary.

Lewis Johnson was arrested on Thursday 2 April following a series of attempted burglaries at addresses on Fisher Court earlier that day.

The 25-year-old, of Churchill Mansions on Coopers Street, Runcorn, was subsequently charged with four counts of attempted burglary.

Johnson appeared at Warrington Magistrates' Court on Monday 6 April where he pleaded guilty to the four offences.

He was remanded in custody and is due to be sentenced at Chester Crown Court on Tuesday 5 May.
