10:14 21/10/2020

**A 58-year-old man from Ellesmere Port has been charged following a robbery in the town.**

Kenneth Brown was arrested on Heathfield Road at around 8.15pm on Monday 19 October.

Brown, who lives on Heathfield Road, has subsequently been charged with robbery and possession of a bladed article in a public place.

The charges relate to an incident at a petrol station on Whitby Road that occurred at around 8pm on Monday 19 October.

Brown appeared at Warrington Magistrates' Court, via a video link, on Wednesday 21 October.

He was remanded in custody and is set to appear at Chester Crown Court on Wednesday 18 November.
