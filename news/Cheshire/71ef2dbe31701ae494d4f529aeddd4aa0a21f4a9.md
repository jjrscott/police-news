15:59 27/01/2021

**A 37-year-old man has been charged after a cannabis farm was discovered in Crewe.**

Edlir Zorbaj, of no fixed address, was arrested in Badger Avenue in the town at around 11.15am on Tuesday 26 January.

He has subsequently been charged with production of a class B drug.

The charge relates to a cannabis farm being discovered by officers at an address in Badger Avenue that morning.

The large quantity of cannabis plants inside the address were seized by officers, as was equipment used to cultivate them.

Zorbaj appeared at Warrington Magistrates' Court, via a video link, on Wednesday 27 January.

He was remanded in custody and is set to appear at Chester Crown Court on Wednesday 24 February, again via a video link.
