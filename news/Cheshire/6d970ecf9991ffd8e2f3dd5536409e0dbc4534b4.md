16:11 07/01/2021

Police have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to following an assault in Northwich.

At 6.15pm on the 14 December 2020 a 15-year-old boy was sitting on a wall outside McDonalds in High Street, Northwich when he was approached by a man who pushed him off the wall and assaulted him.

PC Thomson said: "The teenager was left shocked by the incident but relatively unharmed. As part of our enquiries into this incident we have released a CCTV image of man we'd like to speak to. If you recognise the man, or witnessed an assault take place please contact us."

Anyone with information should contact PC Thomson on 101 or via www.cheshire.police.uk quoting IML 873696.

Alternatively information can be given anonymously by contacting Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
