14:23 29/01/2021

**Two men have been charged with drug offences in Warrington. **

John Palmer, 38, of Kentmere Road in Timperley and Ewan Burrows, 20, of Davenport Avenue in Latchford have been charged with three counts of possession with intent to supply heroin, crack cocaine and cannabis.

Both men appeared at Warrington Magistrates Court on Thursday 28 January and were remanded in custody until they are due to appear at Liverpool Crown Court on Thursday 25 February.

The charges relate to a warrant executed, by officers from Warrington BIT, at an address on Knutsford Road on Tuesday 26 January.
