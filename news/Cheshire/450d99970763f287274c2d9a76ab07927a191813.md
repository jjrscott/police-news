14:44 15/10/2020

**A 29-year-old man from Runcorn has been charged with class A drug dealing offences.**

Ricky Hope, of Caithness Court, was arrested in Cooper Street in Runcorn town centre at around 11.15am on Wednesday 14 October.

He has subsequently been charged with possession with intent to supply cocaine and being concerned in the supply of the class A drug.

The charges relate to a warrant that was executed in Cooper Street on the morning of Wednesday 14 October.

Hope appeared at Warrington Magistrates' Court, via a video link, on Thursday 15 October.

He was remanded in custody and is set to appear at Chester Crown Court on Thursday 12 November.
