11:16 17/01/2020

At 2.05am this morning (Friday 17 January) police were called by Cheshire Fire and Rescue Service to a flat fire in Parkgate Road, Neston.

While firefighters were tackling the fire and searching the premises, the body of a man was discovered in one of the rooms.

Police and fire officers are investigating the circumstances and enquiries are in the early stages.

Next of kin have been informed.

Anyone with any information in relation to the incident is asked to contact Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 613997 or visit https://www.cheshire.police.uk/tua/tell-us-about/ .

For more information about the fire visit: https://www.cheshirefire.gov.uk/news-events/incidents/ongoing-flat-fire-in-neston .
