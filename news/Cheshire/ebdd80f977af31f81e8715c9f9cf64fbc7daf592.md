13:54 16/12/2019

**A 23-year-old man has been charged after a large quantity of ecstasy tablets were seized at a motorway service station.**

Connor Lewis was arrested at Sandbach Service Station on the M6 shortly after 5pm on Wednesday 13 December.

He has since been charged with possession with intent to supply a class A drug (MDMA).

Lewis, of St Levan Road, Plymouth, appeared at Crewe Magistrates' Court on Friday 13 December.

He has been remanded in custody and is set to appear at Chester Crown Court on Friday 10 January.
