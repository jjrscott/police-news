14:31 16/12/2020

Detectives have released images of jewellery stolen in a burglary at an address in Appleton.

Sometime between 5pm and 6pm on Monday 7 December a house on Delphfields Road, Appleton was broken into and jewellery stolen.

DC Chris Little said: "I'd urge anyone who recognises the jewellery or has been offered similar items for sale to contact us.

"We are making a number of lines of enquiry and are keen to hear from anyone who may be able to help with our investigation."

Anyone with information should contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 868436.

Alternatively information can be given anonymously by contacting Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
