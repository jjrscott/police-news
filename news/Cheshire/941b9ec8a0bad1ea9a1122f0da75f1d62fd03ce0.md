20:09 07/05/2020

Two men have been charged with attempted murder and possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life in connection with an incident in Warrington.

Aaron Bretherton, aged 23, of Netherfield Road South in Liverpool and 25-year-old Lewis Fitzpatrick, of Eldersfield Road in Liverpool, have been remanded in custody.

Bretherton has also been charged with possession of a prohibited firearm, ammunition and possession of controlled drugs with intent to supply.

They are due to appear at Warrington Magistrates Court on Friday 8 May.

The charges relates to an incident at 8.50pm on Friday 24 April when a number of shots were fired outside an address on Poplars Avenue in Orford.

A 55-year-old man sustained serious injuries to his leg and he remains in hospital in a stable condition.

Bretherton and Fitzpatrick were arrested at separate addresses in Liverpool on Wednesday 6 May and were subsequently charged on Thursday 7 May.

On Tuesday 28 April 22-year-old Anthony Morris was charged with attempted murder and possession of a firearm.

Morris has been remanded and is due to appear at Liverpool Crown Court on Wednesday 27 May.
