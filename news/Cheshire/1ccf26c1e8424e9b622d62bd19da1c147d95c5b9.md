18:36 01/12/2019

Police investigating a firearm being discharged in Ellesmere Port have arrested two men in-connection with the incident.

Both men, aged 26, have been arrested at separate addresses, one in the Merseyside area and the other in Cheshire for firearms offences and are now in custody. Searches are ongoing at several locations.

At 2.25pm on Saturday 30 November a man was shot in the leg on Enfield Road and taken to hospital where he remains in a stable condition.

Police would like to thank the community for their support following this incident.

If you witnessed the incident or have any information please contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 577560. Information can also be passed on anonymously by calling Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or visiting their website www.crimestoppers-uk.org/
