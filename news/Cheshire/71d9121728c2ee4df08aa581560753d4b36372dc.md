**A 35-year-old man has been charged following a burglary at a bakery in Runcorn.**

Michael Farrer, of Lydgate Walk, Whitefield, Bury, Greater Manchester, was arrested in the Bury area at around 10.45pm on Wednesday 12 August.

He has subsequently been charged with burglary.

The charge relates to an incident at a Poundbakery store in Church Street, Runcorn. It occurred between 5pm on Saturday 1 February and 9.30am on Sunday 2 February.

Farrer was issued with a postal requisition ordering him to appear at Chester Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 12 January.

He was released from the court on unconditional bail and is set to appear at Chester Crown Court on Tuesday 9 February.
