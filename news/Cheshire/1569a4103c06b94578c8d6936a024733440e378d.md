15:54 08/12/2020

Detectives are appealing for witnesses following a burglary at an address in Appleton.

Sometime between 5pm and 6pm on Monday 7 December a house on Delphfields Road, Appleton was broken into and jewellery stolen.

DC Chris Little said: "I'd urge anyone who has seen anything that didn't quite seem right or seemed out of place in the area recently to contact us. If you were in the area of Delphfields Road on the day of the burglary and have dashcam footage that my help us please get in touch.

"We are making a number of lines of enquiry including house to house and CCTV and are keen to hear from anyone who may be able to help with our investigation."

Anyone with information should contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 868436.

Alternatively information can be given anonymously by contacting Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
