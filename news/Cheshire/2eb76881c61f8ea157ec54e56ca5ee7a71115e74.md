10:25 16/01/2020

Two men arrested following an altercation at a pub in Tarporley have now been charged.

Timothy Kirkham, 37, and Ben Starkey, 30, were both arrested on Saturday 2 November 2019 following an incident at the Rising Sun Public House.

The victim in the case, a 46-year-old local man, sustained serious injuries as a result of the incident

Kirkham, of Wrexham Road, Ridley, and Starkey, of Daleford Lane, Whitegate, have now been charged wounding/ inflicting grievous bodily harm without intent.

The pair are set to appear at Chester Magistrates Court on Friday 14 February.
