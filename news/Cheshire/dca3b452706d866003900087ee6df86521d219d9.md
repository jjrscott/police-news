12:05 08/02/2021

**Detectives investigating a burglary at a house in Elton are appealing for information from the public.**

The burglary occurred at some time between 3pm on Monday 1 February and 10.30pm on Monday 1 February on Parkland Drive.

Two men, wearing masks and gloves, have entered and searched the home. It is unclear if anything was stolen.

Enquiries have been ongoing and police are encouraging anyone who with CCTV or dashcam footage that may assist the investigation to get in touch.

Detective Constable Paul Boneham, from Chester Local Policing Unit, said: "Since the burglary was reported we have been following a number of lines of enquiry including speaking to witnesses and viewing CCTV footage.

"We are particularly keen to hear from anyone who saw anyone or any vehicles acting suspiciously in the parkland Drive and Ince Lane areas.

"In addition to this I am encouraging anyone, who has not yet been in touch with police, to come forward if they have any information, CCTV or dashcam footage which could provide useful to the investigation."

If you have any information please contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 908559 or Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111.
