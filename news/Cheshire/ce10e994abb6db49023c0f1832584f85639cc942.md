16:32 27/02/2020

A 21-year-old man from Birmingham has been charged in connection with an incident of courier fraud.

Marcello Gaspar was arrested on Wednesday 26 February following an incident at a property in Macclesfield.

Gaspar, of Crocodile Court, Birmingham, has since been charged with one count of fraud by false representation.

He has been remanded into custody and is due to appear at Crewe Magistrates' Court on Friday 28 February.
