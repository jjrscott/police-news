14:13 06/11/2020

Detectives are appealing to the public to help locate the rightful owners of a number of items of suspected stolen property.

On Thursday 5 November police were called to reports of a burglary and a number of attempted burglaries in the Holmes Chapel area.

Officers also believe that there may be more victims in the area who have not yet come forward.

A 27-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of burglary and he is currently assisting officers with their enquiries.

A number of items distinctive jewellery have also been recovered.

Anyone who believes that they may have been a victim, or anyone with any information which may aid the investigation, is urged to get in touch with the team at Crewe on 101 quoting, ILM 844678.
