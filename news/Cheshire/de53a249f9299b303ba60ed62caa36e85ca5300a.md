13:57 26/06/2020

The Serious and Organised Crime Unit have charged three men in-connection with drug offences.

Lee Preston, aged 21, of no fixed address has been charged with conspiracy to supply crack cocaine and heroin.

Connor Robins, aged 26, of Clover Lane, Lache in Chester address has been charged with conspiracy to supply crack cocaine and heroin.

Joshua Burns, aged 24, of Flint Court in Ellesmere Port has been charged with conspiracy to supply crack cocaine and heroin.

All three men appeared at Warrington Magistrates Court on Friday 19 June and have been remanded until Wednesday 15 July where he will appear at Chester Crown Court.

A 27 year-old man was arrested on Thursday 26 June on suspicion of possession with intent to supply cannabis and dangerous driving. He has since been released under investigation.

The charges are in connection with an operation which took place on Tuesday 16 June where 16 people were charged following warrants in Ellesmere Port, Chester and Wirral.
