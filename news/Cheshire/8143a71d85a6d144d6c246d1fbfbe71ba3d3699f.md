**Police are asking the public to help them trace a man who is wanted on recall to prison.**

Michael Hill is from Winsford and is believed to be in the town.

The 35-year-old is white and around 5'11" tall with a proportionate build. He has receded brown hair and brown eyes.

Anyone who sees Hill or has information regarding his whereabouts is asked to call Cheshire Constabulary immediately on 101, quoting IML 940650.

Alternatively, information can be given anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
