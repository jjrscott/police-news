09:24 06/11/2020

**A 33-year-old has been charged after two other men from Widnes were assaulted in the town.**

Liam Waite was arrested at his home on Hale Road at around 10pm on Wednesday 22 July.

He has subsequently been charged with wounding with intent and assaulting a person and causing them actual bodily harm.

The charges relate to an incident that occurred at an address in Page Lane, Widnes, at around 8pm on Wednesday 22 July.

A man in his 30s sustained a serious injury as a result of the incident and was taken to hospital in an ambulance. He has since been discharged after receiving treatment.

A man in his 40s suffered a minor injury as a result of the incident that did not require hospital treatment.

Waite appeared at Warrington Magistrates' Court on Friday 24 July, via a video link.

He was remanded in custody and is set to appear at Liverpool Crown Court on the week commencing Monday 11 January.
