13:57 26/03/2021

**Police are appealing for information following a collision in Warrington.**

Just after 4pm on Saturday 20 March, officers were called to Penketh Road following reports of a collision involving a green Mazda and three pedestrians.

One pedestrian, a woman, was taken to hospital with serious injuries. She has since been discharged from hospital.

The two other pedestrians, both women, were taken to hospital for minor injuries.

The driver of the car stopped at the scene and is assisting police with enquiries.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or may have captured dashcam footage can contact Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 943236 or report it via the Cheshire Police website.
