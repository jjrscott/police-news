14:41 09/11/2020

**Detectives have charged a man in Ellesmere Port with drug offences.**

Peter Carruthers, of Wolverham Road, has been charged with possession with intent to supply heroin and possessing criminal property.

The 55-year-old appeared at Crewe Magistrates Court on Saturday 7 November and remanded in custody.

He is due to appear at Chester Crown Court on Monday 7 December.

The charges relate to a warrant executed on Wolverham Road at 11.30am on Friday 6 November.
