22:40 08/08/2020

A following reports of a domestic incident in the Brookvale area of Runcorn has now been charged.

The man was arrested at around 8.30pm on Saturday 8 August following an incident at an address on Cambourne Close.

The 50-year-old has since been charged with sending a false message by public electronic communication network to cause annoyance, inconvenience or anxiety.

The man, who resides on Cambourne Close, has been released on bail and is set to appear at Warrington Magistrates on 8 September 2020.
