17:33 28/07/2020

**Police are appealing for witnesses following a serious assault at a bar in Runcorn.**

At 1.43am on Saturday 25 July, police were called to Alchemy in High Street, Runcorn following reports of an altercation.

On arrival paramedics were treating a 21-year-old man who was taken to hospital. He remains in a serious but stable condition.

DC John Ellis said: "We're still piecing together events from that night and are appealing to anyone who saw the incident to contact police. During the incident the victim's mobile phone also went missing. If anyone found a Huawei P6 in the pub or High Street, please make contact with police."

As part of enquiries into the assault a 21-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of a section 18 assault.

Anyone with information should contact Cheshire Police on 101 or via our website, quoting IML 763112.

Alternatively information can be given anonymously by contacting Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
