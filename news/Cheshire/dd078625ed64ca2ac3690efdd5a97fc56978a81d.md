10:56 04/05/2020

Officers are appealing for information following an arson in Warrington.

At approximately 10pm on Friday 20 March a large plastic bin was deliberately set on fire outside a community centre on Grasmere Avenue.

Police have released CCTV footage of a man they would like to speak to in-connection with the arson.

Enquiries in relation to the incident are ongoing and officers are urging anyone who believes they may know the identity of the offender in the CCTV footage to get in touch.

Anyone with information that may help the investigation should call Cheshire Police on 101 quoting IML 665258 or report it through the website https://www.cheshire.police.uk/

Alternatively, information can be reported to Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or by visiting their website www.crimestoppers-uk.org/
