09:59 24/12/2020

**Police are appealing to the public for help in finding a missing 38-year-old from Warrington.**

James Miller was last seen on Slater Street in Latchford at 11pm on Tuesday 22 December but hasn't been seen since.

He is described as 5ft 8ins tall with bright blue eyes, of a medium build and with a distinctive scar on the side of his head. He is wearing a white t shirt and grey jogging bottoms and is believed to be on foot.

PC Angela Bahn, of Warrington Local Policing Unit, said: "We're becoming increasingly concerned for James as time goes on and are continuing to search the area he was last seen.

"I am encouraging anyone who thinks they may have seen James, or know of his whereabouts, to please contact us. I would also ask residents to check their sheds and outbuildings and report any information to us.

"James, if you see this please get in touch with us on 101 as your family are really worried about you."

Anyone with information should contact police on 101 quoting IML 880492.
