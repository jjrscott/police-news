17:40 17/01/2020

Detectives investigating a series of high value burglaries in Cheshire have charged three men in connection with the incidents.

Nicholas Orlando Carrasco Oviedo, 23: Alexis Miguel Carrasco Oviedo, 24, and Joel Ureta, 42, were all arrested on Wednesday 15 January following an investigation by detectives from Macclesfield CID.

The trio, who are all of no fixed abode, have since been charged with three counts of burglary and theft.

The charges relate to three burglaries which occurred in the Knutsford and Prestbury areas between Saturday 11 January and Wednesday 15 January.

All three men have been remanded in custody and are next due to appear at Crewe Magistrates Court on Saturday 18 January.
