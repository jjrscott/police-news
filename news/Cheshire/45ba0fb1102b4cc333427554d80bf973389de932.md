15:34 12/12/2019

**Detectives from Cheshire Constabulary investigating ATM attacks in Cheshire, West Yorkshire and Shropshire have charged four men.**

Sean Devereux, 30, Liam Doherty, 39, Mark Edwards, 47, and Peter O'Neill, 41, were all arrested on Tuesday 11 December following a series of warrants in Merseyside.

Following questioning, Devereux of Quarry Green, Northwood; Doherty of Hansby Drive, Liverpool; Edwards of Martland Road, Belle Vale, and O'Neill of Heathfield Road, Wavertree, have all been charged with conspiracy to commit burglary with intent to steal.

The charges relate three ATM burglaries which occurred in Warrington, Wakefield and Telford between Thursday 3 October and Thursday 21 November.

The men have all been remanded in custody and are set to appear at Warrington Magistrates' Court on Thursday 12 December.
