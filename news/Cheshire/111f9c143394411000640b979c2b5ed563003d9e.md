14:25 07/05/2020

Officers are appealing to the public for help in tracing a 34-year-old man from Ellesmere Port who is wanted in connection with criminal damage.

A warrant was issued for the arrest of Thomas Donoghue following an incident on 18 April in the town.

Anyone who sees Thomas Donoghue, or has information on his whereabouts, is asked to call Cheshire Police on 101, quoting IML 685294 or visit https://www.cheshire.police.uk/

Alternatively, information can be given anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or through their website www.crimestoppers-uk.org/
