10:46 15/07/2020

**A man from Widnes has been charged following an assault in the town.**

Connor Wright, of Avondale Drive, was arrested on Liverpool Road in the Hough Green area of Widnes shortly after 12.30pm on Sunday 12 July.

The 24-year-old has subsequently been charged with assaulting a person and causing them actual body harm.

He has also been charged with possessing a bladed article in a public place.

The charges relate to an incident at the entrance to Hough Green Railway Station.

A 22-year-old man from Liverpool sustained minor injuries as a result of the incident, which occurred shortly before 12.30pm on Sunday 12 July.

He did not go to hospital.

Wright appeared at Warrington Magistrates' Court on Monday 13 July, via a video link.

He has been remanded in custody and is set to appear at Liverpool Crown Court on Monday 10 August, again via a video link.
