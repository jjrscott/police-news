**Cheshire Constabulary is asking the public to help the force trace a man from the Northwich area who is wanted on recall to prison.**

As well as Northwich, Bradley Thomas has links to Winsford.

The 20-year-old is white and around 6' 3" tall with a slim build. He has brown hair and blue eyes.

Anyone who sees Thomas is advised not to approach him but to instead call the police as soon as possible on 101, quoting IML 710085.

Alternatively, information regarding Thomas' whereabouts can be reported anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
