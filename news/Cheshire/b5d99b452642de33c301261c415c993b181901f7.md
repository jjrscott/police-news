12:33 03/12/2019

**Cheshire Constabulary is appealing for witnesses to come forward after a man died following a collision in Betchton.**

The collision on New Inn Lane in Betchton, which is between Sandbach and Alsager, involved a black Ford Fusion car and a pedestrian.

It was reported to police at around 6.20pm on Thursday 28 November.

Officers attended the scene and discovered that the pedestrian had sustained serious injuries.

Sadly, the 54-year-old man from Hassall was pronounced dead at the scene by paramedics.

His family have been informed and are being supported by specialist officers.

The driver of the car stopped at the scene following the collision.

Enquiries in relation to the incident are ongoing and no arrests have been made.

Officers want to hear from anyone who witnessed the collision or has any information or dashcam footage which may aid the investigation.

Anyone with information or dashcam footage in relation to the incident should call Cheshire Constabulary on 101, quoting IML 576177, or give the details via https://www.cheshire.police.uk/ro/report/
