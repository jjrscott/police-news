11:09 10/11/2020

**Detectives have charged three people with firearm and drug offences in Crewe.**

Givemore Mutevera, aged 39, of Laura Street in Crewe and two 17-year-old boys, one from Crewe and one from Doncaster, have each been charged with possession of a firearm, possession of a knife and two counts of possession with intent to supply cocaine and heroin.

Mutevera was arrested on Gresty Road in Crewe at 2pm on Friday 6 November.

The two 17-year-old boys were arrested on Crewe Street at around 2.25pm on Friday 6 November.

They appeared at Warrington Magistrates Court on Monday 9 November and have all been remanded.

The charges relate to three people stop and searched by police on the afternoon of Friday 6 November.
