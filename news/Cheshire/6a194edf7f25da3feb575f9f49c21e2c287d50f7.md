09:15 08/01/2020

A man from Chester has been charged following a robbery in the city.

Karl Kent was arrested at his home in Raymond Street shortly before 6.30pm on Friday 3 January.

The 43-year-old has subsequently been charged with robbery.

The charge relates to an incident in Garden Lane, Chester, which occurred at around 5pm on Saturday 7 December.

A 55-year-old man from Malpas had his watch stolen during the incident.

Kent appeared at Chester Magistrates' Court on Saturday 4 January.

He was remanded in custody and is set to appear at Chester Crown Court on Monday 3 February.
