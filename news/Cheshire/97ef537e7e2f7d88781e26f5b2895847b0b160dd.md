13:21 21/02/2020

**A 46-year-old man from Crewe has been charged with class A drug offences.**

Wayne Moss, of Ruskin Road, was arrested at Crewe Railway Station shortly before 5pm on Wednesday 19 February after getting off a train.

He has subsequently been charged with two counts of possession with intent to supply class A drugs (heroin and cocaine).

Moss has also been charged with possession of a class A drug (crack cocaine).

He appeared at Crewe Magistrates' Court on Friday 21 February.

Moss has been remanded in custody and is set to appear at Chester Crown Court on Friday 20 March.
