18:00 18/09/2020

A 48-year-old man from Liverpool has been charged following a burglary at shop on Congleton Retail Park.

At around 11.15pm on 5 September officers were called to Barn Road following reports of a burglary at a retail shop.

A quantity of perfume was taken during the incident.

Terence Brady, of Conleach Road in Liverpool, has been charged with burglary.

He was remanded into custody to appear at Liverpool Magistrates Court tomorrow (Saturday 19 September).
