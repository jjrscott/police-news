A CCTV image has been released of a man who may be able to help officers investigating a reported assault in **Folkestone**.

Kent Police was contacted on **Monday 1 March 2021** and it was reported that a member of staff at a shop in **Park Farm Road** had been assaulted by a customer.

## Appeal

Officers are investigating and have released an image of a man who may be able to assist enquiries.

Anyone with any information is urged to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference 46/34438/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
