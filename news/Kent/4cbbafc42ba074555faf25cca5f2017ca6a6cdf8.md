Two men have been arrested in connection with an assault in **Rochester High Street.**

At around **1am** on **Saturday 25 January 2020** , Kent Police was called to reports a man had been approached by a group of people and assaulted. The victim, who is in his 20s, was taken to hospital with a head injury. He remains in hospital.

## Arrests

On Saturday 1 February 2020, a 23-year-old man from Sittingbourne and a 24-year-old man from Gillingham were arrested on suspicion of assault. They were later released pending further investigation.

Investigating officers continue to appeal for anyone who may have witnessed the incident to come forward.

## Can you help?

Anyone who saw what happened, or has any information, is asked to call Kent Police on **01634 792 209** quoting reference 46/15110/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555 111** or on the Crimestoppers website.
