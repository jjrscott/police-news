A man has been charged in connection with spitting in a woman's face in **Tunbridge Wells.**

It is reported that at around **12pm** on **11 October 2020** , the victim was in **Victoria Road** when she asked a man who she did not know to move away, as she felt he was walking too close to her.

It is alleged the suspect responded by insulting the victim, before throwing the contents of a beer can at her and then spitting in her face and on her clothing.

## Charged

Kent Police issued a media appeal for information and following this **Paul Greaves,** aged 52, of Willington Street, Maidstone was arrested.

He was later charged with assault and bailed to appear before Sevenoaks Magistrates' Court on 1 April 2021.
