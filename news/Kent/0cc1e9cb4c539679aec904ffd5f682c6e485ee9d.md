## Ashford man receives driving ban for drink driving in Sittingbourne

Carl Lubin, aged 39, of Willesborough, Ashford, was sentenced on 27 January 2021 at Maidstone Magistrates' Court.

He pleaded guilty to driving a motor vehicle above the legal drink drive limit, on the A251, Sittingbourne, on 18 December 2020. When stopped he gave a breath test of 83 microgrammes of alcohol in 100 millilitres of breath. The legal limit is 35 microgrammes.

He was disqualified from driving for 20 months and fined £120\. He was also ordered to pay a surcharge of £34 and £85 of costs.
