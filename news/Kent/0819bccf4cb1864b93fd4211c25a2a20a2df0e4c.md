Witnesses are sought after a woman is reported to have caused a disturbance at a **Maidstone** store.

At around **4.40pm** on **20 November 2020** , a woman who had been acting suspiciously in Tesco Express on **Tonbridge Road** was challenged by an employee.

## Abusive

It is alleged the suspect then became abusive and spilt a noxious substance on the member of staff.

The victim did not suffer any injury and a 42-year-old woman from Maidstone was arrested on suspicion of possessing an offensive weapon in a public place and committing a public order offence. She was later bailed to return to police on 14 December.

## Can you help?

Investigators are urging anyone who saw the incident and has not yet spoken to officers to call the appeal line on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/207619/20.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** or complete the online form.
