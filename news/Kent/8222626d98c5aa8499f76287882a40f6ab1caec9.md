The owners of these items are being sought.

The owners of antiques believed to have been stolen are being urged to come forward by Kent Police.

Officers in **Dover** came into possession of the items on **Friday 26 February 2021** as part of an investigation into possible burglaries in the area.

## Appeal

Photos of the items have now been released in the hope of tracing victims of theft who may not yet have reported their property stolen.

Anyone who recognises any of the items or believes one or more of them may belong to them should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference 46/81576/20.

Anyone who recognises the items should contact police.
