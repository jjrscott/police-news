A suspect is to appear in court after allegedly assaulting a police officer in **Maidstone**.

At around **5.05pm** on **Sunday 7 March 2021** , a patrol was called to concerns for the welfare of a person in Sandling Road.

An officer attended **Staceys Street** where a woman was seen to walk in front of moving vehicles.

## Arrest

As she tried to get her to return to the pavement the officer was then reportedly kicked and bitten. The woman was arrested and taken into custody.

**Timea Orban** of Barton Road, Maidstone was later charged with assaulting an emergency worker by beating.

Ms Orban, aged 31, appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 9 March where she was bailed to a hearing at the same court on 16 April.
