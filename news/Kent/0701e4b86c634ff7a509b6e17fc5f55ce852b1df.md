Investigating officers are appealing for witnesses after a man was assaulted in **Ashford**.

The victim is reported to have been punched and kicked by a group of four men while walking in an alleyway close to a field in **Norwood Gardens** at around **5pm** on **Friday 17 July 2020**. He sustained injuries to his face and chest.

A 28-year-old man and a 17-year-old boy, both from Ashford, have been arrested on suspicion of assault and remain in custody as enquiries continue.

It is believed some members of the public may have seen the incident taking place and they could have information that can assist officers. Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting **46/122862/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** or using the anonymous online form.
