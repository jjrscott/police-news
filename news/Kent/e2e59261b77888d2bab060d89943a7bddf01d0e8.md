Detectives are appealing for witnesses following a robbery at a shop in **Folkestone**.

Kent Police was called to the premises in **Cheriton High Street** at around **6.30am** on **Friday 4 September 2020** , shortly after the incident had taken place.

A man and a woman dressed in dark clothing had entered the shop and demanded money from the till.

They stole a quantity of cash and tobacco before leaving in the direction of Marler Road.

Officers attended the scene and enquiries are ongoing.

## Can you help?

Anyone who saw anything suspicious in the area or has information about the robbery is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 04-157.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form.
