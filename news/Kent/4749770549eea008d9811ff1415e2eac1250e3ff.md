A suspected drug dealer has been charged after being arrested by officers in **Faversham**.

Police were patrolling through the town centre on 1 December 2020 when they saw a man sitting on a stationary E-Scooter in **East Street** and became suspicious of his behaviour.

During enquiries at the scene, officers reported uncovering a quantity of cocaine and heroin in the man's possession and he was arrested. Enquiries into any offences associated with the use of the E-Scooter are ongoing.

**Lewis Cady** , 34, of Perry Wood, Selling, Faversham has been charged with possession with intent to supply heroin and cocaine.

Mr Cady appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on 3 December 2020 where he was remanded in custody to appear before Maidstone Crown Court on date to be determined.
