Officers are appealing for information following a burglary in **Ashford**.

Kent Police was called to **Lakemead** after a man was seen stealing tools from a garage at around **6.40pm on Monday 23 November 2020**.

The suspect, who is described as wearing a dark hoodie, jumped over a fence after being disturbed by the victim.

Officers are carrying out enquiries and are appealing to anyone who saw anything suspicious, or who may have CCTV or dashcam footage which could assist the investigation, to contact them.

Anyone with any information should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference **46/209237/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form at Crimestoppers-uk.org
