CCTV image

CCTV images have been released by officers of a man they believe may have important information regarding hundreds of pounds-worth of cosmetics stolen in **Ashford and Tenterden**.

Officers are investigating a theft of more than £500 worth of Vichy Dermablend products from a business in **Tenterden High Street on 3 August 2020** , at around **11.15am**.

A second theft was reported on **20 October 2020** at **10am** from a business in **Mill Court, Ashford** , where around £300 worth of Rimmel make-up was stolen.

Anyone with information should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting **46/188039/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form at Crimestoppers-uk.org
