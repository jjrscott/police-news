Missing man Nathan Cherry

Kent Police officers are appealing for information to locate a missing man from **Dartford**.

Nathan Cherry, 37, was last seen in the area of **Darenth Wood Road** at around **1am** on **Monday 1 March 2021**.

He is described as being around five feet, three inches tall, of slim build with very short hair and fair stubble. He was last seen wearing a red cap, blue surgical mask, a green and beige hooded top, grey tracksuit bottoms with a red stripe and black trainers with white soles.

Officers are concerned for his welfare and ask anyone with information regarding his whereabouts to call 101, quoting reference 01-0048.
