Kent Police is appealing for information following the burglary of two shops in **Whitstable**.

Officers were called on the morning of Monday 20 January 2020 by staff at shops in **Harbour Street** and **Tankerton Road**.

The premises in Harbour Street is believed to have been burgled between **midnight** on **Sunday 19 January** and **9am** on **Monday 20 January** and money has been stolen.

The shop in Tankerton Road is believed to have been broken into between **4.30pm** on **Saturday 18 January** and **10am** on **Monday 20 January** and property was damaged.

Enquiries into both incidents are ongoing and officers are carrying out a review of CCTV from the surrounding areas.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting 46/12435/20 for the Harbour Street burglary or 46/12548/20 for the Tankerton Road burglary.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
