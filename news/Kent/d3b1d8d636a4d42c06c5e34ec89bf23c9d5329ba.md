Image of robbery suspect

The computer-generated image of a suspect has been released following a robbery reported in **Dover**.

At around **8pm** on **Wednesday 28 October 2020** a man was walking through a car park in **Maison Dieu Road** , when he was approached by four other men.

He reported being threatened by the group and assaulted by one of the men.

The victim, in his 40s, had cash stolen from his back pocket before the men left the area in separate directions. Detectives have made a number of enquiries since the incident and now have an e-fit of one of the suspects.

Any witnesses or anyone who recognises the man in the image is asked to come forward with information. He is described as being in his 40s, over 6 feet tall, large build, and having dark hair with a dark beard. He was believed to have been wearing a black jacket, a blue jumper and black jeans at the time of the incident.

# Witnesses

Anyone who recognises the description, the man in the image or has any other information is urged to call 01843 222289 quoting reference 46/193754/20.

Witnesses can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form at Crimestoppers.
