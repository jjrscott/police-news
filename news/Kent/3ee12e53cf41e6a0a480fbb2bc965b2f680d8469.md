Officers investigating two incidents of criminal damage in a car park in **Dartford** are appealing for witnesses.

During the evening of **Thursday 12 November 2020** , two cars were reportedly damaged when they were parked at **Darenth Valley Hospital in Darenth Wood Road.**

Both vehicles, a white Skoda Yeti and a Mazda 2, had their windows smashed but nothing was taken.

It is believed they were damaged between 6.30pm and 11pm.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Kent Police on 01474 366149 quoting 46/203199/20.
Alternatively you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or by using the anonymous online form.
