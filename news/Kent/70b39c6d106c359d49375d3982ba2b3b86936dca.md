Officers investigating the death of a woman in **Lyminge** have arrested a man.

An investigation was launched by the Kent and Essex Serious Crime Directorate after officers were called at **11.38am** on **Sunday 14 February 2021** to reports of a woman's body found in **Station Road**.

Officers currently remain at the scene while enquiries are ongoing to establish the circumstances of the death.

A 36-year-old man from Deal was arrested on suspicion of murder and taken in custody.

Anyone with information is urged to contact Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 14-0337.
