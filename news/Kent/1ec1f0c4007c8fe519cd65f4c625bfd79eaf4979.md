An appeal for information has been issued after around sixty birds were stolen from a property in the **Ashford** area.

**Between 2.20am and 2.50am** on **Friday 18 December 2020** , an outbuilding in **Little Chart** was broken into and the birds, including canaries, redpolls, chaffinches and goldfinches were taken.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information regarding the burglary or the location of the birds, should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/222856/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
