CCTV images have been issued following allegations of theft and an assault against a shop worker in **Maidstone.**

At around **7.30pm** on **Wednesday 17 June 2020** a man entered the Sainsbury's Local store in **Tonbridge Road**.

It is reported he placed a number of meat items into a bag but then made no attempt to pay.

The suspect was challenged by a member of staff as he attempted to leave the store.

It is alleged he pushed the employee and made repeated threats to punch him, before leaving the scene with the stolen items.

## Do you recognise him?

Officers would like to identify the person pictured in the CCTV images, who they believe can assist with their enquiries.

Anyone with information is asked to call the west Kent appeals line on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/105930/20.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111, or by completing an online form.
