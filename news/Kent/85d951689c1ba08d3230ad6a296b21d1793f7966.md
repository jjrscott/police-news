Stolen: The Austin Vanden Plas Princess

Witnesses are sought after a classic car was stolen from **Herne Bay**.

A black and grey Austin Vanden Plas Princess car was taken from a property in **Thornden Wood Road** **between 8.15am and 9.35am** on **Friday 5 February 2021.**

## Can you help?

Anyone with information regarding the stolen vehicle should contact Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/19119/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting the website.
