A suspect is to appear at court charged with burglaries at two addresses in **Sevenoaks**.

A break-in was reported at a house in **St Botolphs Road** on **12 September 2020** during which mobile phones, computer tablets, bank cards and a quantity of cash were stolen.

A further report was received by Kent Police that a premises in **Amherst Hill** was targeted between **Sunday 13** and **Monday 14 December** , and a computer tablet, mobile phone and a camera were stolen.

## Arrest

**Harry Healey-Bowen** of Vine Court Road, Sevenoaks was arrested by officers from the Chief Constable's Crime Squad on Wednesday 16 December and later charged with two burglaries. He was also charged with one count of criminal damage at Tonbridge police station.

Mr Healey-Bowen, aged 21, appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on Thursday 17 December where he was bailed to a hearing at Maidstone Crown Court on a date to be confirmed.
