Two charges have been authorised against an alleged **Rochester** drug dealer.

**Torron Sapsed** was first arrested by Kent Police on 29 October 2020 after a quantity of cocaine and cannabis was reported to have been found at an address in Mooring Road, Rochester. He was then released, pending further enquiries.

The 24-year-old was arrested again on **4 December** , after officers became suspicious of his behaviour while they patrolled the **City Way** area.

He was later charged with one count of being concerned in the supply of cocaine and one count of being concerned in the supply of cannabis.

Mr Sapsed, of Mooring Road, Rochester, was remanded in custody to appear at Medway Magistrates' Court on Saturday 5 December 2020.

At the hearing, he was further remanded in custody and will next appear at Maidstone Crown Court on Monday 11 January 2021.
