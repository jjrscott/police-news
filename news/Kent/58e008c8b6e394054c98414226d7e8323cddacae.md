CCTV images have been issued of a man who may have important information about a reported robbery in **Maidstone**.

Kent Police received a report that at around **9.30pm** on **Tuesday 3 March 2020** , a man carrying a knife went into a shop on **Plains Avenue** and assaulted a customer and a member of staff.

## Cigarettes stolen

He allegedly stole a quantity of cigarettes from behind the counter before leaving the premises and heading towards **Cranbourne Avenue**.

A customer and a shop assistant both suffered injuries and the customer, a man in his 50s, went to a local hospital for medical attention.

Following the completion of a number of enquiries including forensic examination at the scene and a review of CCTV footage, investigators are now releasing images of a man who may be able to help them with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604 100** , quoting reference **46/40613/20.**

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** , or by using the anonymous online form.
