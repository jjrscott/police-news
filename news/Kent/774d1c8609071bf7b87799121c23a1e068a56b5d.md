Detectives are appealing for information after two men tried to steal a tan-coloured lurcher dog from a street in **Gillingham on 18 March 2021**.

The two men got out of a black estate car on **Grange Road, near Hazlemere Drive** in the town, at around **9pm** and tried to steal the dog from its owner.

They drove off when an unknown man in a white transit van drove past and shouted at them.

The two white men had English accents and were aged either in their late 30s or early 40s. The man who got out of the car's passenger seat wore jeans and a hoodie.

## Can you help?

Officers are interested in hearing from anyone who may have seen the incident or who may have recorded it via CCTV, dashcam or mobile phone.

Anyone with information should contact Kent Police on **01634 792209, quoting reference 46/44939/21**.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete an online form at www.crimestoppers-uk.org
