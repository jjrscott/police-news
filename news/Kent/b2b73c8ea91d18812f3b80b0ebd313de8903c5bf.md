Businesses in **Tunbridge Wells** are warned to be on alert after an opportunist thief distracted staff to steal cash and a mobile phone.

Officers have issued CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the offences, which took place on **Tuesday 23 June 2020**.

At around **3.45pm** a man entered Sankey's in **Mount Ephraim**.

The suspect is reported to have engaged staff by asking about when the pub would reopen, before requesting a piece of paper to write the details down. When he left it was discovered a phone had been stolen.

Around 30 minutes later, the suspect is believed to have entered a shop in **London Road, Southborough**.

During this incident it is alleged he distracted a worker by asking for a postcode to an address. A quantity of cash was later found to have been taken.

## Can you help?

Enquiries have revealed the suspect had also been seen visiting other premises in the local area.

If you recognise the man pictured or have any other information which may assist the investigation call **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/10681/20.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers, anonymously on 0800 555111, or by using the online form.
