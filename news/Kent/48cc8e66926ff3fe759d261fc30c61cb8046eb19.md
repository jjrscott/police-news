Information is sought after a mobile phone and a games console were stolen during an aggravated burglary in **Canterbury**.

A man in his 30s reported he was woken by three people entering his home in **Knight Avenue** at around **4am on Thursday 3 September 2020**.

He was assaulted and a number of items were stolen, including Apple AirPods, an iPhone XR, Xbox One S, a quantity of cash, a silver chain and a bank card.

No serious injuries were reported by the victim.

The three offenders were all described as white and wore surgical masks, gloves and dark clothing. One man was reported to be 6ft 6ins tall.

## Can you help?

Anyone who witnessed anything suspicious in the area at the time or has any information regarding the stolen items, should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/156994/20.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form.
