Officers are seeking information to help find a bicycle stolen from **Sevenoaks** town centre.

It was taken from outside an area of shops in **Brewery Lane** at around **1pm,** on **Monday 21 December 2020.**

The bike, described as a black Specialized model, has sentimental value as parts of it were given to the victim by their late father.

## Second bike found discarded

Enquiries have revealed that prior to the theft the suspect travelled into the town from Sevenoaks train station, using a different bike, also suspected to have been stolen _(pictured below)._

This was found discarded nearby. After the theft, it is believed the suspect headed back in the same direction.

## Can you help?

Anyone who can assist the investigation is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/224456/20.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers, anonymously on 0800 555111 or by using the online form.
