An investigation into a robbery in **Dartford** has led to officers charging four men.

The incident was reported to have happened inside a flat in **Spital Street** at around **midnight** on **29 January 2021**.

A group of men were alleged to have broken into the property, where they were reported to have assaulted a man with a weapon before stealing a large quantity of cash along with boxes of cigarettes.

As part of the investigation the following four men have been charged with one count each of robbery and one count each of possession of an imitation firearm with intent to cause fear of violence.

• Arkan Saber, 20, of Westminster Road, North London.
• Mahmood Abubakr, 23, of Oakfield Gardens, North London.
• Dogan Yarligac, 23, of no fixed address.
• Farman Fattah, 22, of Church Street, Greater London.

All four men appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on 1 February where they were remanded to appear before Maidstone Crown Court on 1 March.
