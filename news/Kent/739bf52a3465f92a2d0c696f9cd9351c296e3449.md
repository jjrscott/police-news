A CCTV image has been issued by officers investigating allegations of attempted shoplifting at a store in **Maidstone.**

It is reported that on **Wednesday 9 December 2020** , a woman tried to steal multiple items of clothing from Marks and Spencer in **Week Street**.

The suspect entered the store between **2pm** and **3pm**.

After selecting the items of clothing, it is alleged she attempted to leave without paying.

A member of security staff challenged the suspect, who was able to leave the scene after a man she was with also intervened.

## Can you help?

All the clothing items were recovered.

Officers are now looking to trace the woman pictured, who they believe may have important information.

Anyone who can assist the investigation is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/218025/20.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111, or by using the online form.
