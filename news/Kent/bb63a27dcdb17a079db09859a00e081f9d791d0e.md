A pair of suspected cocaine dealers from **Herne Bay** and **Whitstable** have appeared at court, following an investigation by the Kent and Essex Serious Crime Directorate.

**Gavin Calloway** of Fairoaks, Herne Bay, and **Charles Hodges** of Kent Street, Whitstable, were arrested on Wednesday 6 January 2021 after search warrants were executed at their home addresses.

Investigators seized three mobile phones at Mr Calloway's house and a quantity of cash at premises occupied by Mr Hodges.

## Charged

Mr Calloway, aged 42, and Mr Hodges, aged 33, were later charged with conspiracy to import cocaine into the United Kingdom between **1 March and 12 June 2020** , conspiracy to supply cocaine and conspiracy to supply diazepam.

On Thursday 7 January, they were both remanded by Folkestone Magistrates' Court to appear at a hearing before Canterbury Crown Court on 8 February.
