Witnesses who have not yet spoken to officers about an attempted assault in **Ashford** are asked to come forward.

Kent Police is investigating a report that following a disturbance in **Bailey 's Field** at **8am** on **15 June 2020** , a bystander who was sitting in his van nearby, was threatened with a knife.

The suspect was verbally abusive and reportedly pushed his arm and the knife through the open window of the vehicle, but did not injure the victim. The victim was then able to safely close his window and leave the area.

## Suspect

The suspect was described as a white man with a small to medium build, 5ft 8in tall. He had ginger hair and was wearing a grey North Face hooded top and black tracksuit bottoms.

A 20-year-old man from Lydd was arrested in connection with the incident and has since been released without charge.

Officers are urging anyone who recognises the suspect's description, or any witnesses who have not yet spoken to officers, to call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting 46/100729/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
