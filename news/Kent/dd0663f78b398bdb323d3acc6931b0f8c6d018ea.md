A suspected burglar has been charged after a restaurant was broken into in **Rochester**.

**Kevin Ward** , 45, of no fixed address, has been charged with burglary following the incident which happened in the high street just after **12am** on **16 February 2021**.

During the burglary the front door had been forced in and cash was stolen along with electronic items and alcohol.

Mr Ward was also charged with theft from a shop which happened at a store in **Frindsbury Road, Rochester** , on the afternoon of **19 February**.

He has additionally been charged with vehicle interference following a report that a man was seen attempting to enter a vehicle parked in **Grange Road, Rochester** at around **1.40am** on **18 February**.

Mr Ward was charged with the offences on 24 February and appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on 25 February where he was bailed to appear before the same court on 5 March.
