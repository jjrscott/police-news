Information is being sought following the theft of a Land Rover from **Broadstairs.**

It was reported the vehicle was stolen whilst parked on the roadside in **Edge End Road** between **5pm** on **Monday 3 February 2020** and **5.30am** the following day.

The khaki green Land Rover Series 3 was subsequently found in a damaged state later in the day on **Tuesday 4 February.**

## Can you help?

Officers would like to speak to anyone with information about the theft, or if they witnessed something suspicious in the area which might assist enquiries.

Witnesses are asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222 289** quoting 46/21514/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** or using the anonymous online form.
