Investigators are appealing for information following a reported burglary in **Hythe**.

Kent Police was called at **7.45am** on **Sunday 25 October 2020** following a report that a business in **Douglas Avenue** had been broken into.

A door had been damaged before cash and other items were stolen in the burglary, which is thought to have taken place between **3am** and **4am** that morning.

## Can you help?

House to house enquiries and a review of local CCTV are being carried out and anyone with any information that may assist the investigation is urged to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference 46/191205/20.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form at Crimestoppers-uk.org
