A disturbance in a block of flats in **Chatham** has led to a man being charged with various offences.

Kent Police was called at **1.30pm** on **12 February 2021** to a report of a disturbance in **Teeshot Mews**.

Officers arrived and a man was arrested in connection with the incident.

As part of the investigation **Lee Whiting** , 33, of Teeshot Mews, was charged on 14 February with affray, attempted arson and possession of a bladed article in a public place.

He appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on 15 February 2021 where he was remanded to appear before Maidstone Crown Court on 15 March.
