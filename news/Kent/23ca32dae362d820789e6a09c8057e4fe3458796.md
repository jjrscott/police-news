Log splitter

The image of a log splitter believed to be stolen has been released by officers from Kent Police's Rural Task Force in **Canterbury**.

The trailer-mounted machine was discovered during the early hours on **Monday 2 March 2020** in the area of **St Martin 's Lane**.

Officers have carried out a number of enquiries since then to establish who it belongs to and have not yet been able to trace its owner.

A picture has now been released in the hope that someone will come forward.

## Contact us

Anyone who has information or believes this may belong to them should call the East Kent appeals line on 01843 222289, quoting reference 02-0142 and PC Daniel Perry.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or via their website.
