Officers are appealing for information following an incident in **Chart Road, Ashford** at around **9pm** on **Friday, 12 February 2021**.

A Volkswagen Golf was involved in a collision with a silver Mercedes C-Class which reportedly failed to stop at the scene.

Police are keen to hear from anyone who may have information about the incident, including two witnesses who spoke to police shortly after the collision, however they left before officers were able to take their details. Investigators would also like anyone who may have dashcam footage to come forward.

A man was arrested in connection with the incident and has been released under investigation.

If anyone can help police with the investigation please contact Kent Police on 01843 222289, quoting reference 12-1334.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form at Crimestoppers-uk.org
