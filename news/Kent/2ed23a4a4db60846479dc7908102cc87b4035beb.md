Detectives from the Kent and Essex Serious Crime Directorate have charged a man with murder following the death of a woman in **Canterbury**.

Kent Police was called at **11.25am** on **Tuesday 29 December 2020** to a report that a woman had suffered injuries at a property in **Long Acre Close**.

Emergency services attended and a 50-year-old woman was found with injuries consistent with stab wounds. She was pronounced deceased at the scene.

## Charge

**David Creed** , 52, of Long Acre Close, was arrested and has been charged with murder.

He appeared before Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Thursday 31 December and was remanded in custody to appear at Maidstone Crown Court on Monday 4 January 2021.
