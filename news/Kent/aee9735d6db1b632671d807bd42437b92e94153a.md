Three men will appear in court charged with two robberies in **Kent**.

On **Friday 12 March 2021** , Kent Police received a report of a robbery in progress at a commercial premises in **Joy Lane** , **Whitstable** , at around **3pm**.

A member of staff was assaulted and cash and cigarettes were stolen. Police stopped a car in **Hengist Way** near **Ramsgate** the same day and three men were arrested.

## Charged

• **Neil Clarke** , 37, of Paragon in Ramsgate, has been charged with the robbery, possession of an offensive weapon, failing to stop for police and driving without insurance or a licence.

• **Zymon Elsom** , 19, of King Street in Ramsgate, has been charged with the robbery and possession of an offensive weapon.

• **Kane Sampson** , 20, of no fixed address, has been charged with the robbery and possession of an offensive weapon.

### Second robbery charge

All three individuals have also been charged with another robbery and possession of a knife, following an incident in which a business in **Queens Road** , **Broadstairs** , was targeted on **Tuesday 9 March 2021**. Staff reported being threatened with a knife and cigarettes and alcohol were taken.

The trio are due to appear at Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Monday 15 March 2021.
