A CCTV image has been issued by officers investigating two separate thefts of alcohol in **Lenham**.

At around **8pm** on **29 March 2020** , a man entered a service station on **Ashford Road** where he reportedly stole bottles of beer and champagne.

The same man returned to the premises the following day and it is alleged he stole further bottles of alcohol.

## Image released

Investigators have now identified a CCTV image of a man who may be able to assist with their enquiries.

Anyone who recognises him is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting crime reference 46/56693/20.

You can also call Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form.
