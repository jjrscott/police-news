An investigation into the exploitation of children who became involved in a county line drug supply chain has resulted in charges being authorised against a man from London.

Enquiries were made by officers after children were reportedly brought from the London area to **Chatham**.

**Daniel Garcia** , 33, previously of Morton Gardens, London, was charged on Tuesday 1 December 2020 with three counts of facilitating the travel of a person with a view to exploit them.

He appeared before Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Thursday 3 December 2020 and he was remanded in custody. He is due before Maidstone Crown Court on Friday 15 January 2021.
