Cannabis, mobile phones and more than one thousand pounds in cash were seized after police stopped a car on the **A2** in **Gillingham**.

Officers signalled for the vehicle to stop at around **2.40pm** on **Monday 30 November 2020**. Patrols smelt cannabis coming from the car and carried out a search under the Misuse of Drugs Act, which is when the phones, cash and drugs were discovered.

## Arrested

Two men, both aged 22 from Medway, were arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply. They have since been released pending further investigation.
