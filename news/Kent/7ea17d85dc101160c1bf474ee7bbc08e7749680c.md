Rimas Vilcinskas, aged 62, of Clare Way, Sevenoaks, was sentenced on 30 November 2020 at Sevenoaks Magistrates' Court.

He pleaded guilty to driving whilst above the legal drink drive limit, in Sevenoaks, on 13 September 2020. When stopped he gave a breath test of 71 microgrammes of alcohol in 100 millilitres of breath. The legal limit is 35 microgrammes.

He was disqualified from driving for a total of 18 months' and fined £230\. He was also ordered to pay a victim surcharge of £34 and costs of £85.
