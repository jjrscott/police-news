Information is being sought by officers investigating an attempted robbery in **Ashford**.

Kent Police was called on **Saturday 27 February 2021** following a report that a teenage girl had been approached by three people on a **path between Rutherford Road and Beecholme Drive** that evening.

One of the men had demanded the girl give him her belongings and followed her briefly when she refused.

No items were stolen in the incident, which took place at around at around **7.40pm** , and no injuries were reported.

Officers are investigating and are appealing to anyone who saw three men in the area around that time, or who may have domestic CCTV that could assist.

The male who demanded the victim's belongings is described as at least six feet tall, wearing a black face mask and a black coat with the hood up.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting 46/32819/21.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111, or by completing an online form on their website.
