Witnesses are sought by officers investigating a serious collision in **Maidstone**.

Kent Police was called at **5.40pm** on **Friday 1 January 2021** following a collision involving a white Volvo XC60 and a teenager riding a BMX bike, on **Sutton Road** at its junction with **Cranborne Avenue.**

South East Coast Ambulance Service also attended and the 18-year-old cyclist was treated for injuries. He was taken to a London hospital where he remains in a serious condition.

## Can you help?

Anyone who saw the incident, or who has CCTV or dashcam footage which may assist the investigation, is urged to call the Serious Collision Investigation Unit on **01622 798538** or email them, quoting reference 01-1190.
