Officers investigating a reported assault have released this CCTV image

Officers investigating a reported assault in **Folkestone** have released a CCTV image of a man who may be able to assist enquiries.

A member of staff at a shop in **Park Farm Road** spoke to a man following a reported theft shortly before **6pm** on **Friday 29 January 2021**.

The man is reported to have headbutted the member of staff before leaving the store.

## Appeal

Enquiries into the incident are ongoing and officers are releasing a CCTV image of a man they believe may be able to help the investigation.

Anyone with information should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference 46/15022/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting crimestoppers-uk.org
