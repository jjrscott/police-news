A wallet containing cash and cards was reported stolen during a robbery in **Dover**.

At around **8pm** on **Sunday 12 January 2020** a man was robbed by two people as he walked along an underpass in **Bench Street**.

The victim, in his 50s, was threatened and then assaulted before the suspects left with his personal belongings, including cash, cards and vehicle documents.

## Can you help?

The suspects were both described as having short dark hair, being around 5ft 7ins tall and wearing dark jackets.

A number of enquiries have been carried out including the review of CCTV opportunities.

Detectives investigating the reported robbery would like to hear from anyone who witnessed the incident or has information that could assist.

Please call the Kent Police appeal line on 01843 222289 quoting reference 46/7207/20.

Alternatively call the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555111.
