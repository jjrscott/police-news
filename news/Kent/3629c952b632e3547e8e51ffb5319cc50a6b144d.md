Witnesses are being asked to contact Kent Police following a fatal collision in **Canterbury**.

A white Ford Transit Connect van was travelling along the **A257, St George's Place** , Canterbury when it collided with a pedestrian at around **5.35pm** on **Wednesday 11 November 2020**.

The incident happened near a pedestrian crossing outside of a supermarket between Lower Bridge Street and New Dover Road.

The pedestrian, a 79-year-old woman, was taken to a local hospital by ambulance but was later pronounced deceased.

## Appeal

Officers from Kent Police's Serious Collision Investigation Unit (SCIU) are carrying out enquiries and wish to hear from any witnesses.

Anyone who has dashcam or CCTV footage of the incident, or of the vehicle involved in the moments before the collision, is also urged to call SCIU on **01622 798538** or email sciu.td@kent.pnn.police.uk, quoting reference HW/DJ/100/20.
