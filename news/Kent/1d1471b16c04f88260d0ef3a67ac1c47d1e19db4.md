Kent Police is appealing for information following a reported robbery in **Chatham**.

The incident happened in an alleyway between **Luton Road** and **Magpie Hall Road** , near the Luton Arches roundabout, between **11.30pm** and **midnight** on **Saturday 19 December 2020**.

It was reported that a man in his fifties was assaulted and may have had items stolen from him.

The victim suffered injuries to his head and face and does not recall what happened. He was taken to a local hospital for treatment but has since been discharged.

The suspect is described as being a man in his forties wearing black trousers, a black coat, black Nike trainers and a red baseball cap.

Witnesses or anyone with information is urged to call the appeal line on 01634 792209 quoting reference 46/223647/20. You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form at this link.
