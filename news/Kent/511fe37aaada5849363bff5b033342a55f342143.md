A suspect is to appear in court charged with carrying out a hate crime in **Canterbury**.

Kent Police attended the city centre on **Tuesday 9 February** following a report that a man had been assaulted on two occasions, in **St George 's Street** and in a bakery. No injuries were reported.

**Ben Tilling** , 42 of Tivoli Road, Hounslow, was arrested and charged with two counts of racially aggravated assault, and two accounts of assault by beating.

He appeared at court on Thursday 11 February where he was bailed. The case will next be heard at Folkestone Magistrates' Court on 25 June.
