Detectives are investigating a burglary in which a car was stolen from outside a **Folkestone** property.

A black VW Scirocco car was taken during the burglary in **Archer Road** , which reportedly took place **between 11am and around 1pm** on **Sunday 24 January 2021**.

## Can you help?

Anyone who saw or heard anything suspicious in that area at the time of the incident or has CCTV or dashcam footage which may assist, should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/12286/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting the website.
