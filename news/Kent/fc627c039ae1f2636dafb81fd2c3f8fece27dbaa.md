Witnesses are sought after a woman was assaulted in an early morning incident in **Dover**.

The woman in her 50s reported that she was walking along **Folkestone Road** **shortly before 7am** on **Tuesday 20 October 2020** when she was approached by a man who spoke to her and then assaulted her.

She sustained bruising and other injuries which required hospital treatment.

An 18-year-old man from Dover was subsequently arrested in connection with the incident. He has been released on bail until 8 November while enquiries continue.

Detective Inspector Cara Ferguson said: 'The assault took place near a set of a temporary traffic lights in Folkestone Road, so we believe there are motorists who were in the area and might have seen something which could assist our enquiries.'

## Can you help?

Any witnesses, or motorists who may have dashcam footage, should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/187904/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
