A robbery suspect has been remanded in custody after a delivery driver reported having their van stolen in **Strood**.

**Kevin Tandoh** , 31, of Styles Garden, London, has been charged with one count of robbery.

The charge was authorised by the Crown Prosecution Service in connection with an incident in **Kingswear Gardens, Rochester** , which happened at around **12.35pm** on **22 December 2020**.

A delivery driver reported being approached by two men who then robbed him of the keys and drove away in the vehicle. The vehicle was later found abandoned in Windmill Street, Strood, and more than 100 parcels were reported to have been stolen from inside.

As part of the investigation Mr Tandoh was arrested on 13 January 2021 and later charged with the offence.

He appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on 14 January where he was remanded in custody to appear before Maidstone Crown Court on 11 February.

Enquiries to locate the remaining suspect are ongoing.
