Witnesses and dashcam footage are being sought by officers investigating a collision on the **A258** near **Deal**.

Officers were called to the collision near to **Bridge Hill** involving a white Audi A5 car and a grey Alfa Romeo car shortly before **8pm** on **Sunday 16 February 2020**.

A man suffered an injury and was taken to a local hospital for treatment.

## Did you see anything?

Anyone who witnessed the collision, saw either vehicle beforehand, or may have captured dashcam footage of the incident, is asked to call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting 16-1442.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
