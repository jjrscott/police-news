CCTV images have been issued by officers investigating a theft at a **Maidstone** shop.

On **Saturday 28 November 2020** , Kent Police received a report that a quantity of beauty products had been stolen from Boots the Chemist in **King Street** , as the store was opening at around 8.30am.

Officers are now able to issue a CCTV image of two people who they believe could help with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone who recognises them or has information that may assist the investigation is urged to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/212339/20.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form.
