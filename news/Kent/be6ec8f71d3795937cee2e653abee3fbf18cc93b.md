Information is being sought following the theft of a trailer from **Ashford**.

It was reported that **between 1pm and 2pm on 17 March 2021** , a trailer was stolen from a site in **Sheepfold Lane, Kingsnorth**.

The trailer is described as a heavy plant trailer able to carry up to three tonnes.

## Can you help?

Officers are appealing to anyone who has seen it, or knows of its whereabouts, to contact Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/43581/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or by using the anonymous online form.
