## Man receives driving ban in West Kent

Jevgeni Kruglov, aged 37, of no fixed address, was sentenced on 25 January 2021 at West Kent Magistrates' Court.

He pleaded guilty to driving a motor vehicle above the legal drink drive limit, in the Lamberhurst bypass, Tunbridge Wells, on 10 October 2020. When stopped he gave a breath test of 95 microgrammes of alcohol in 100 millilitres of breath.

The legal limit is 35 microgrammes.

He was disqualified from driving for 24 months and fined £440\. He was also ordered to pay a surcharge of £44 and £85 of costs.

## Drink driver receives disqualification

Rimantas Jazukevicius, aged 34, of Spring Walk, Tunbridge Wells, was sentenced on 21 February at Sevenoaks Magistrates' Court.

He pleaded guilty to driving a motor vehicle above the legal drink drive limit, on the A21, Tonbridge, on 4 October 2020. When stopped he gave a breath test of 178 microgrammes of alcohol in 100 millilitres of breath. The legal limit is 35 microgrammes.

He was disqualified from driving for 36 months. He was also ordered to pay a surcharge of £95 and £85 of costs.
