## Maidstone man receives disqualification

Roman Miko, aged 22, of King Edward Road, Maidstone, was sentenced on 5 March 2021 at Maidstone Magistrates' Court.

He pleaded guilty to failing to provide a specimen for analysis, at Folkestone Police Station, on 3 May 2020.

He was disqualified from driving for two years and ordered to carry out 100 hours of unpaid work within the next 12 months. He was also ordered to pay a surcharge of £95 and costs of 85.

## Maidstone man receives driving ban

Jamie O'Connell, aged 29, of St Johns Road, Faversham, was sentenced on 9 March 2021 at Maidstone Magistrates' Court.

He pleaded guilty to being in charge of a motor vehicle when above the legal drink drive limit, and driving a motor vehicle when above the legal drink drive limit, in Boxley, Maidstone, on 2 February 2021. When stopped he gave a breath test reading of 89 microgrammes of alcohol in 100 millilitres of breath. He was disqualified from driving for 20 months and fined £120.

He was also ordered to pay a surcharge of £34 and £85 of costs.
