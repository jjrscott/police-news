A CCTV image has been issued by officers investigating the theft of a car from **Eynsford**.

The Audi Q2 was reportedly stolen at around **5pm** on **Tuesday 26 May 2020** , following the theft of the keys while the victim was distracted in the **Riverside** area.

## Vehicle stolen

The vehicle which was parked nearby was seen to leave towards **Dartford**.

Enquiries in the area have led officers to identify a CCTV image of a man who may have important information.

Investigating officer, Police Constable Sally du Preez said: 'While the image is blurred, we are hoping that somebody will recognise this person and contact us.'

## Can you help?

Anyone who recognises him is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting crime reference 46/88652/20.

You can also call Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form.
