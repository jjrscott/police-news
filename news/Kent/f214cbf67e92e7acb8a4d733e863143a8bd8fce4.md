Three men have been charged with offences relating to the supply of heroin and crack cocaine in **Gillingham**.

Plain clothed Kent Police officers were targeting an area known for county line drugs supply on the afternoon of **Thursday 7 January 2021**.

During the patrol, they became suspicious of a man who was seen leaving an address in **Louisville Avenue**.

The individual was stopped and searched before the officers carried out additional checks at the property, where a further two men were located.

As part of their enquiries, officers have reported seizing around 70 wraps containing suspected heroin and crack cocaine.

**Subham Limbu** , 20, of Green Lane, Morden, London, **Kyle Smith** , 30, of Louisville Avenue, and **Thuvakaran Srithar** , 34, of Sydenham Road, Croydon, have since each been charged with possessing heroin and crack cocaine with intent to supply.

They have all been remanded in custody to appear at Medway Magistrates' Court on Friday 8 January 2021.
