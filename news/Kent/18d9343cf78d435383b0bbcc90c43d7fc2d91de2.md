Investigators have relased a CCTV image of a man who may have important information following a burglary in **Leysdown**.

The incident happened in **Park Avenue** on **Tuesday 16 February 2021** , when cash, electrical items and bottles of alcohol are reported to have been stolen.

## Can you help?

Anyone who recognises the man is urged to contact Kent Police on **01795 419119** , quoting crime reference 46/25426/21.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** or complete an online form on the website.
