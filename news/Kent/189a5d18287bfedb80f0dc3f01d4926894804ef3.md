A suspected county line dealer has appeared in court charged with drug supply offences in **Tunbridge Wells**.

On **Monday 22 March 2021** , a man was arrested in **Colebrook Road** following an investigation by Kent Police's County Line and Gang Team.

Officers also seized a mobile phone he was carrying and two further phones during a search at his home address in High Brooms Road.

## Charged

**Ashley Inglis** , aged 29, was charged later the same day with being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine and being concerned in the supply of heroin.

He was also charged with criminal damage in connection with a separate incident at Tonbridge police station.

Mr Inglis appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 23 March where he was remanded to a hearing at Maidstone Crown Court on 20 April.
