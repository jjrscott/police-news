Investigators have issued a CCTV image of a man who may have information regarding the theft of power tools in **Southfleet**.

The tools, which include a leaf blower and a cordless pole saw, are reported to have been stolen from a property in **Hook Green Road** , on **Wednesday 13 January 2021.**

Following a review of available evidence, including CCTV, officers are now in a position to release a picture of a man who may be able to assist with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone who recognises the man is asked to call Kent Police on **01474 366149** , quoting crime reference 46/6639/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously **on 0800 555 111** or complete the online form on their website.
