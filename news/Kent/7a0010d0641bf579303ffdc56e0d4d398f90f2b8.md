Witnesses are sought after a man was assaulted in **Margate** during the bank holiday weekend.

The victim, aged in his 20s, has reported that four men assaulted him while he walked down an **alleyway near Byron Avenue and Dane Park** at around **8.50pm** on **Saturday 29 August 2020**.

He suffered an arm injury which required hospital treatment.

Officers would like to speak to anyone with information or CCTV footage which may assist their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Witnesses should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/154363/20.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form.
