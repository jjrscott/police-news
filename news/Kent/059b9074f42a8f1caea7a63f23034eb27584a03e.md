Witnesses are being urged to come forward following an altercation in **Folkestone**.

Kent Police was called at **2.45am** on **Wednesday 31 March 2021** after a reported altercation inside and then outside a property in **Bayle Street**.

Officers attended and two men were taken to a local hospital with head injuries and have since been discharged.

## Arrests

A 21-year-old man and a 22-year-old man, who are both from the Folkestone area, were arrested in connection with the incident and have been bailed while enquiries continue.

An investigation is ongoing and officers are urging anyone who saw what happened, or who has any information, CCTV or dashcam footage which may assist, to contact Kent Police on **01843 222289**, quoting reference 46/52420/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555111 or using the anonymous form at crimestoppers-uk.org
