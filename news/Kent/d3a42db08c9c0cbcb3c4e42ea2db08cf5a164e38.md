A teenager has been arrested in connection with reports of beach hut break-ins in **Herne Bay**.

Kent Police was called to **Spa Esplanade** in the town shortly before **11pm** on **Sunday 20 December 2020** to reports of torchlights seen around the beach huts nearby.

Officers found a beach hut had been broken into and a second hut had also been tampered with.

A total of six burglaries were reported to have taken place in huts during the same weekend.

Officers arrested a 16-year old boy from Herne Bay in connection with the break-ins. He was further arrested on suspicion of possessing a bladed article and riding a motorbike otherwise than in accordance with a licence.

He has since been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
