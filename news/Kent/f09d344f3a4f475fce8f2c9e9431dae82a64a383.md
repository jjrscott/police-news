Witnesses are being sought after a rare three-wheeled electric cycle was stolen from a garage in **Margate**.

It was reported that the burglary took place **between 8pm on Sunday 3 January 2021 and 11am the following morning** , when the domestic garage in **Dent de Lion Court** was broken into.

The tricycle is described as an orange Hase three-wheeled pedal cycle with an electric assist motor.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information regarding the burglary or the missing tricycle should call Kent Police **on 01843 222289** quoting 46/1780/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting the website.
