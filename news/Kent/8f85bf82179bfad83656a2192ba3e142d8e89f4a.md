A suspect has appeared in court following a reported distraction theft in **Canterbury**.

The victim reported that his wallet was missing after a woman came to his house in the **Castle Street** area of the city on **Monday 7 December 2020**.

Officers from Kent Police's Chief Constable's Crime Squad investigated and **Carolyn Gleadall** was arrested in Canterbury on Tuesday 22 December.

## Court

Ms Gleadall, of no fixed address, was later charged with theft and two counts of fraud, relating to the alleged use of a stolen bank card.

The 35-year-old appeared before Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 23 December and was remanded in custody to appear again at the same court on Wednesday 30 December.
