CCTV images have been issued by officers investigating a theft at a **Larkfield** supermarket.

On **Friday 22 January 2021** , Kent Police received a report that a quantity of groceries and alcohol had been stolen from Tesco Express in **London Road** , at around **3.15pm**.

It is alleged a member of staff intervened and recovered some of the stolen items as two suspects attempted to leave the store without paying for them.

Officers are now able to issue CCTV images of two people who they believe could help with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone who has information that may assist the investigation is urged to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/11436/21.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form on their website.
