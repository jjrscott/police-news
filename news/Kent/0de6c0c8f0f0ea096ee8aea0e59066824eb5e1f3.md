Detectives investigating burglaries in **Sevenoaks** have released images of two men they would like to speak to.

Between **Wednesday 4 March** and **Monday 9 March 2020** , eight break-ins were reported during the afternoon and early evening at properties in **Hillfield Road, Chipstead Park, Lake View Road, Madison Way, Oakdene Road** and **Cavendish Avenue.**

## Stolen

Property stolen from premises included cash and jewellery.

Investigating officer, PC Alex Peacock said: 'We are asking anybody who thinks they may recognise the men from the clothing they are wearing in the CCTV images, to call the appeal line.

'I would also urge members of the public to remain alert and contact us on 101 if they see anybody acting suspiciously, or call 999 if they believe a crime is being committed.'

## Can you help?

Anyone who has information that may assist the investigation should call Kent Police on **01622 604 100** quoting crime reference **46/44634/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** or using the anonymous online form.
