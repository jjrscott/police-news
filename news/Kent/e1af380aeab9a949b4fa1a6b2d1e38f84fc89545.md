Witnesses are sought in connection with a robbery in **Folkestone**.

A man in his forties was in **Guildhall Street North** , near Broadmead Road when he was approached by a woman who reportedly assaulted him.

The victim ran away but fell over and the woman, who was joined by three men, approached him a second time where he was assaulted.

During the incident, the victim's cash and mobile phone were stolen.

The robbery took place **between 9.10pm and 10pm on Sunday 6 December 2020**.

A 27-year-old woman from Folkestone was arrested in connection with the incident and has since been released without charge.

The review of available CCTV footage and house-to-house enquiries have been carried out by officers and anyone with information regarding the incident who has not yet come forward is urged to get in touch.

## Can you help?

Witnesses should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/216567/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting the website.
