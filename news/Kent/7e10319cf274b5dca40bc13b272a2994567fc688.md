Officers are appealing for information following reports of criminal damage in **Whitstable**.

Kent Police was contacted on the evening of **Monday 7 December 2020** and it was reported that the windows of three buses had been damaged in the **St John's Road** area.

The incidents took place between **6pm** and **7pm**. Police attended and an investigation is ongoing.

## Patrols

Officers have since been carrying out increased patrols in the area and travelling on buses between Herne Bay and Whitstable.

Anyone with any information, or with CCTV or dashcam footage which may capture the incidents or perpetrators, is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference 46/217147/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
