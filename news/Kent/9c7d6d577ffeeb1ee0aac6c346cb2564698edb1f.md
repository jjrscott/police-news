Information is being sought after two people were assaulted in **Westgate-on-Sea.**

Kent Police was called at **1.50am** on **Friday 26 February 2021** following a report that a man and a woman had been assaulted in **St Crispin 's Road**.

Officers attended along with South East Coast Ambulance Service and a man with arm injuries and a woman with a head injury were taken to a local hospital. The male victim has since been discharged.

It is reported that, at around 1.40am, two men in balaclavas had assaulted the two victims outside a property in the street before running away.

One of the assailants is described as white, around five feet, eleven inches tall and was wearing dark sportswear clothing. The second man is described as wearing similar clothing, but was taller and of larger build.

An investigation into the incident is ongoing and officers are urging anyone who saw what happened, who has dashcam or CCTV footage which may assist, to call them on 01843 222289, quoting **46/31912/21**.

You can also call Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or visit their website crimestoppers-uk.org
