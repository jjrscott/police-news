Information is sought following a report of a burglary at a **Canterbury** business.

It was reported that at around **2.20am** on **Thursday 5 November 2020** a burglary took place at a shop in **Burgate near Butchery Lane** in the city centre.

A window was damaged during the incident.

Enquiries are ongoing to establish what was stolen from the premises.

The suspect was described as wearing dark-coloured clothing, with a hood up and a face covering.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information regarding the burglary should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/198206/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
