Officers have issued CCTV images following arrests made as part of an investigation into an armed robbery in **Ramsgate**.

An investigation was launched by Kent Police following a report received regarding a robbery which took place at a property in **Claremont Gardens** on **15 June 2020** where the victim had his phone stolen.

During the **morning of Tuesday 12 January 2021** , officers recovered two imitation firearms and subsequently a 22-year-old man from Broadstairs was arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to commit robbery and a 36-year-old man from Snodland was arrested on suspicion of aggravated burglary and possession of a firearm with intent to cause fear of violence.

They have both been released under investigation.

Detective Inspector Julien Lawton said: 'We are continuing to investigate this incident, and would like to identify the two men pictured, as they may have information which could assist our enquiries.

'We urge anyone who recognises them to get in touch with Kent Police.'

Witnesses should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/101243/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting the website.
