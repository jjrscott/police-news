Two people have been charged in connection with a robbery in **Herne Bay**.

During the afternoon of **Thursday 11 February 2021,** a teenage boy reported he had items including an electric scooter, a mobile phone and an Apple watch stolen during a robbery in **Pettman Close**.

Following an investigation by Kent Police's Chief Constable's Crime Squad, 18-year-old **Hayden Knight,** of High Street, Chatham, and 22-year-old **Ellis Willis** , of High Street, Herne Bay, were each charged with robbery and possession of a bladed article.

## Court

Mr Knight was remanded in custody to appear at Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Thursday 18 February.

Mr Ellis was released on bail to appear at Margate Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 15 April.
