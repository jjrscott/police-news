Officers would like to speak with this man

A CCTV image of a man who may have important information on the theft of a bicycle in **Dartford** has been released by Kent Police officers.

Investigators believe he can assist with their enquiries after a bike, which had been locked to a bike stand outside **Littlebrook Hospital** , in **Bow Arrow Lane** , was reported to have been stolen on **2 June 2020**.

The stand was cut to remove the property, which is described as being a white and grey mountain bike with a carbon frame. It was manufactured by Cube.

Anyone who recognises the man, or who has been offered a bike matching its description for sale in suspicious circumstances, is asked to call Kent Police on **01474 366149** quoting **46/93121/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** , or using the anonymous online form.
