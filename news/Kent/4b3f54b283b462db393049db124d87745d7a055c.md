Information is being sought after cash was stolen during a burglary at a **Folkestone** business.

Between **2.30am and around 6.10am on Saturday 27 March 2021** , a window was damaged and cash was taken from the commercial premises in **George Lane**.

Detectives would like to speak to a man seen walking in the area at the time of the burglary. He is described as being a slim white man in his late twenties to thirties, with dark spikey hair. He was wearing a black leather jacket.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information, CCTV or dashcam footage of the area should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/49937/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form.
