An amount of cash has been reported stolen following a robbery in **Broadstairs**.

As well as the money, a quantity of cigarettes and alcohol were stolen from the business in **Queens Road** at around **7.40pm** on **Tuesday 9 March 2021** , after staff were threatened with a knife.

The three suspects are described as men or teenage boys dressed in grey tracksuit bottoms and dark coats. They all wore face coverings.

## Appeal

Anyone with information regarding the robbery, or dashcam or CCTV footage which may assist enquiries, should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting 46/38629/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
