Information is sought to help locate a vulnerable man missing from **Maidstone**.

Kiane Trotman was reported as missing from **Hermitage Lane** , Maidstone on **Wednesday 29th July** at **7.30pm**.

The 33-year-old is described as 5ft 10ins, of stocky build with dark brown hair, stubble and blue eyes. He has a large tattoo across his right shoulder, chest and neck as well as a tattoo on his left and right wrists.

## Can you help?

Anyone who knows of Kiane whereabouts should call Kent Police on 101 quoting 29-1412.
