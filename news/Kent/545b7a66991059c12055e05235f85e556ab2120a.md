A large amount of jewellery has been reported stolen from a property in **Eythorne** following a break-in.

The burglary took place between **1pm** and **8pm** on **Wednesday 8 July 2020** where damage was caused to the window of a property in Wigmore Lane.

A number of items of gold jewellery were reported stolen, including a gold pocket watch _pictured below_ , following a messy search of the house.

Detectives investigating the burglary urge anyone who witnessed anything suspicious in the area or has information about the incident to call the Kent Police appeal line on 01843 222289 quoting reference 46/116552/20.

Alternatively, you can call the independent charity, Crimestoppers in Kent, anonymously, on 0800 555111.
