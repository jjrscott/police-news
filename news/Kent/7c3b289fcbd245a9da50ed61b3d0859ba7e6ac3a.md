Suspected class A drugs have been seized after a police patrol stopped a car travelling in **Maidstone**.

At around **1pm** on **Friday 19 February 2021** , officers from the Maidstone Task Force were on patrol in **Surrey Road, Shepway** , where they stopped a Honda Civic.

## Drugs seized

Suspecting the driver was involved in the supply of drugs, the officers completed a search and recovered cannabis and cocaine from inside a drinks flask.

They also seized a quantity of cash and the driver, a 41-year-old-man from Maidstone, was arrested.

## Further arrest

Officers then searched an address in Penenden Heath where they seized a further quantity of suspected cocaine, cash and some electronic scales. A 41-year-old local woman was arrested and taken into custody.

Both suspects were later released pending further investigation.
