Investigators are appealing for information following the theft of power tools and other equipment from **Kennington, near Ashford**.

Kent Police was called at 4.15pm on Sunday 25 October 2020 and it was reported that a garage in **Ulley Road** , Kennington had been broken into.

A ride-on mower, power tools and other equipment is reported to have been stolen between **2.30pm** on **Saturday 24 October** and **4pm** on **Sunday 25 October.**

## Can you help?

Anyone who witnessed anything suspicious in the area, or who has since seen the stolen items, is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference 46/191664/20.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** or complete the online form.
