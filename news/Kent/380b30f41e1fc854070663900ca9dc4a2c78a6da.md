An appeal has been issued by investigators following a report of a robbery in **Maidstone**.

The incident happened at around **12.30pm** on **Friday 18 December 2020** in **Willington Green**.

It is alleged a woman in her 30s was walking with her child from shops in **Willington Street** when a teenage boy pulled a bag from her shoulder and ran off.

## Description

The victim saw the suspect running along **Huntingdon Walk** towards **Westmorland Road** with another teenager. They are described as being aged between 17 and 18 years old and were wearing tracksuit tops with the hoods up.

Police Constable Chris Welham of Maidstone CID said: 'While investigators are completing enquiries in the area, including the examination of CCTV footage, we would like to hear from anybody who witnessed the incident or saw the suspects.
'I would also appeal to motorists who were in Willington Street or Willington Green between midday and 1pm with dashcam, to check for any footage that may assist our investigation.
'We have issued a photo of the missing handbag and are asking anyone who finds it discarded to contact us.'

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is urged to call the appeal line on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/223389/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555111** or using the anonymous online form.
