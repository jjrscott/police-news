Detectives are appealing for information following the theft of a cash machine from a service station on the **A299 Thanet Way**.

The incident at the Shell Lychgate garage near **Dargate** was reported at around **1.50am** on **Monday 1 March 2021**.

A CAT digger was used to remove the cash machine, causing a considerable amount of damage.

It was then loaded onto the back of a grey flatbed Ford Transit, which is reported to have left the scene in convoy with a dark-coloured Mitsubishi Shogun.

The incident is being linked with an unsuccessful attempt to steal a cash machine from the wall of a Tesco Express store in **High Street** , **Dymchurch** , at around **2.15am** on **Sunday 28 February 2021**.

Both offences are being investigated by detectives from the Kent and Essex Serious Crime Directorate.

## Can you help?

Witnesses or anyone with information is urged to call the appeal line on 01843 222289 quoting reference 01-0066. You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555111 or complete the online form by clicking here.
