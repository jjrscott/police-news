A green BMW M5 was seized by Kent Police officers along with around £20,000 found hidden inside the engine compartment.

The high performance vehicle was stopped at the outbound controls of the Channel Tunnel terminal in **Cheriton** , near Folkestone, at around **7.30pm** on **Monday 8 February 2021**.

A further £2,000 cash was found inside a rucksack and £3,000 inside a Louis Vuitton handbag.

Officers arrested a 29-year-old man and a 22-year-old woman from Birmingham on suspicion of money laundering. Both have since been released from custody pending further enquiries.
