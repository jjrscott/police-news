A suspect is due to appear in court charged with the theft of parcels in **Ashford**.

Kent Police was contacted after two packages were reportedly stolen in the **Repton Avenue** area of the town on the morning of **Friday 29 January 2021**.

Officers from Ashford's Victim Based Crime Team investigated and arrested a man on Wednesday 3 February.

## Charged

**Oliver Hilden** , of Newenden Close, Ashford, has since been charged with two counts of stealing parcels.

The 39-year-old has been released to appear before Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 9 March.
