Information is sought after a quad bike was stolen from a farm near **Canterbury**.

The burglary took place **between 9pm on Sunday 29 November 2020** and **6.45am the following morning**. The 2012 Kubota bike was reported stolen from a barn situated on the farm site in the **Ickham area**.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information regarding the theft should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/212694/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.

## Staying safe

Officers are reminding people there are some steps they can take to protect themselves from burglary and reduce the risk of becoming a victim of crime.

 * Install a burglar alarm on any building where equipment is kept.
 * Consider using hitch locks, wheel clamps or ground anchors.
 * Keep a record of all valuable items.
