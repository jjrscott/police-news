Kent Police is appealing for information to locate a teenage girl reported missing from **Harrietsham** near Maidstone.

Mya McMullen, aged 14, was last seen at around **6.30pm** on **Saturday 4 April 2020** after boarding a train from Harrietsham station.

Officers are concerned for her welfare and believe she could be visiting areas where she has connections to, in south east London.

## Description

Mya is described as being around 5ft tall and of slim build and with long brown hair, brown eyes and a scar to the left side of her nose.

Anyone with information is urged to call **101** quoting reference 04-1175
