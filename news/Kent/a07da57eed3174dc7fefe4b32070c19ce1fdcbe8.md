Two suspected shoplifters have been charged following the theft of cosmetics valued at almost Â£1,000 in **Maidstone**.

At around **12.50pm** on **Tuesday 15 December 2020** , Kent Police received a report that a quantity of beauty products had been stolen from Boots the Chemist in **King Street**.

## Arrest

Officers attended the scene and two people were arrested.

**Michael McDonaugh** , aged 19, of Homerton Road, Homerton, Greater London and a 15-year-old boy, also from Homerton, Greater London, were later charged with theft.

Both were bailed to attend Maidstone Magistrates' Court on 23 February 2021.
