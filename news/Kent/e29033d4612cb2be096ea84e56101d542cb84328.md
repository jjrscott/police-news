CCTV image

An image of a woman has been released by officers investigating a report of theft in **Canterbury**.

It was reported that a number of Lego sets were stolen from **B &M store in Marshwood Close between 12.30pm and 1.15pm on 13 July 2020.**

Officers would like to identify the woman pictured as she may be able to assist their enquiries into the shoplifting.

Anyone who recognises the woman, or has any information regarding the incident, should call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting 46/120290/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111, or using the anonymous online form.
