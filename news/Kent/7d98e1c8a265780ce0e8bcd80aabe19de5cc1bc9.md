A CCTV image of two people has been released by officers investigating a report of criminal damage in **Folkestone**.

Shortly after **8am** on **Thursday 28 January 2021** , a substance was thrown by a number of people over the gate and nearby pavement of **Shorncliffe Camp** , causing damage to the property.

The suspects reportedly left the scene by car.

A 36-year-old man from the Dover area and a 46-year-old man from Folkestone were arrested on suspicion of causing criminal damage.

They have been released on bail until 22 February 2021.

## Can you help?

Officers would like to identify and speak to the people pictured, as they may have information which could assist their ongoing enquiries.

Anyone with information regarding the image or the incident itself, should contact Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/14178/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting the website.

Officers want to identify these two people.
