Four ferrets have been reported stolen from a property in **Ashford**.

On the morning of **Thursday 25 February 2021** the owner of four ferrets discovered them missing from a run at the back of his property in **Ashford Road**. The housing had been tampered with and it is believed they were stolen at some point during the night between **10pm** and **8am**.

## Previous incident

This follows a separate incident at a property in **Willingdon** on **Tuesday 16 February** where a shed was broken into and two ferrets were reported stolen between **6.30pm** and **7.30pm**.

Ferret owners in the Ashford area are advised to be vigilant to this offending and ensure they have adequate security measures in place to protect their animals. Anything suspicious should be reported to Kent Police.

Anyone with information concerning the thefts can call 01843 222289 quoting 46/31485/21 for Ashford Road or 46/25538/21 for Willingdon. Information can also be reported online at www.kent.police.uk or by calling Kent Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or visiting their website.
