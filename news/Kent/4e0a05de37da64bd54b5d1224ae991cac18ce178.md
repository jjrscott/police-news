Officers from the Serious Collision Investigation Unit are appealing for witnesses following a collision involving a cyclist and a car in **Otford**.

The collision occurred on **Saturday 21 November 2020** at about **10.05am** on **Filston Lane.**

The cyclist was travelling along Filston Lane towards Shoreham when the collision occurred.

A man in his fifties was taken to a London hospital where his condition is described as serious but stable.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or who may have dashcam footage is asked to contact Kent Police's Serious Collision Investigation Unit on 01622 798538, quoting HW/RF/103/20. Alternatively, email sciu.td@kent.pnn.police.uk
