A CCTV image of a man who may have important information on an assault in **Dartford** has been released by Kent Police.

Investigators believe the man can assist with their enquiries into a report a shop worker was assaulted at the **Sainsbury 's** supermarket, in **Priory Market Place** , on **Wednesday 10 February 2021**.

No serious injuries were sustained.

Anyone who recognises the man is asked to contact Kent Police on **01474 366149** quoting **46/22251/21**.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** or complete an online form, which is available via this link.
