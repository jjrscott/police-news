An investigation into suspected county line drug dealing in Medway has led to three men being charged with class A drug offences.

The three men were charged on 31 January 2021 as part of an ongoing investigation into the supply of crack cocaine and heroin from **London** into **Medway**.

As part of the investigation the men were each charged with being concerned in the supply of heroin and being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine between **25 September 2020** and **5 January 2021**.

The trio were arrested on 30 January and charged the following day.

They are:

• **Haroon Rahim** , 18, of Sheringham Avenue, Manor Park, East London.
 **• Mohamed Hussein**, 21, of Hockley Avenue, East London.
• **Rudolph Roberts** , 18, of Charter Street, Gillingham.

Mr Roberts was additionally charged with possession with intent to supply crack cocaine and possession with intent to supply heroin.

The three men appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on 1 February. Mr Rahim and Mr Roberts were both remanded in custody.

Mr Hussein was bailed following his court appearance. All three men are due to appear before Maidstone Crown Court on 1 March.
