Information is sought following a report a man attempted to rob a girl in **Gillingham**.

The offender is said to have approached the child and attempted to grab a case she was carrying while she walked in **Crown Street** at around **3.35pm** on **Wednesday 25 November 2020**.

The victim was unhurt and able to kick the suspect in the shin, prompting him to run away towards **Saunders Street**.

The offender is described as being in his late thirties and tall, with dark skin. He is also reported to have been wearing dark green trousers.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01634 792209** quoting 46/210464/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111.
