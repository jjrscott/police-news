Witnesses continue to be sought by officers investigating a fatal collision in **Maidstone**.

Kent Police was called at **5.40pm** on **Friday 1 January 2021** , following a collision involving a white Volvo XC60 and a teenager riding a BMX bike, on **Sutton Road** at its junction with **Cranborne Avenue**.

South East Coast Ambulance Service also attended and the 18-year-old cyclist was treated for injuries. He was taken to a London hospital for further medical attention where he died on 12 January.

## Appeal

Anyone who saw the incident who has not yet spoken to officers, or who has CCTV or dashcam footage which may assist the investigation, is urged to call the Serious Collision Investigation Unit on **01622 798538** , quoting reference 01-1190.

You can also make contact with investigators by email.
