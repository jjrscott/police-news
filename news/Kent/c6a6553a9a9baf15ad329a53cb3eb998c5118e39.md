Officers from the Serious Collision Investigation Unit are appealing for witnesses following a collision involving a pedestrian and a car in **Hawkhurst**.

The collision occurred on **Saturday 31 October 2020** at about **8.50pm** along **Rye Road**.

A Jeep Cherokee was travelling along **Rye Road at the junction with Queens Road** going towards **Sandhurst** when the collision occurred.

A 14-year-old boy was taken to a London hospital where his condition is described as serious.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or who may have dashcam footage is asked to contact Kent Police's Serious Collision Investigation Unit on 01622 798538, quoting SM/DJ/95/20. Alternatively, email sciu.td@kent.pnn.police.uk
