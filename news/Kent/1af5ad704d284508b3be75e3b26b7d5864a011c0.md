Witnesses are being sought by officers investigating a burglary in **Deal**.

A property in **St Richard 's Road** was broken into **between 2.30am and 5.45am** on **Tuesday 25 August 2020** while the victims were sleeping.

Nothing has been reported stolen currently but enquiries into the burglary are ongoing.

## Can you help?

Anyone who saw or heard anything suspicious in the area is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 25-0182.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555111 or using the anonymous online form.
