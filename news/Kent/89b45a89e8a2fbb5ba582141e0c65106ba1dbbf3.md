Investigators are appealing for information following an attempt to steal a cash machine in **Northfleet**.

The incident at Morrisons supermarket in **Coldharbour Road** was reported to Kent Police at around **2.40am** on **Sunday 31 January 2021**.

It was alleged a JCB was driven into the front of the building, causing a substantial amount of damage. Four or five suspects are thought to have left the scene empty-handed in two 4x4 vehicles and a flatbed truck.

The incident is being investigated by detectives from the Kent and Essex Serious Crime Directorate and enquiries are ongoing.

## Can you help?

Witnesses or anyone with information are urged to call the appeal line on **01474 366149** , quoting reference 46/16388/21.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers on **0800 555111** or complete the online form on their website.
