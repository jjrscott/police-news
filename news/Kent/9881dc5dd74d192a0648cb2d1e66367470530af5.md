Stolen: some of the missing archery equipment.

Information is being sought after a range of specialised archery equipment was stolen from a property in **Hythe**.

The burglary took place between the **evening of Thursday 26 November 2020** and **around 3.20pm on Saturday 28 November** , from an outbuilding in **Tanners Hill Gardens**.

Equipment stolen included the following bows: a white left-handed PSE Perform X with green strings; a blue left-handed PSE Supra Focus XL LD; a black left-handed Mybo Edge 39 with orange strings; a purple left-handed Mybo Edge 35 with green strings; an orange right-handed Hoyt Prevail 37; and a red Bowtech Fanatic SD with yellow and orange strings.

Two bow bags were taken, described as a green legend bag, and a custom blue bow bag with 'DOVER' embroidered on it.

Two quivers were taken, a PSE black quiver, and a custom white HCH quiver.

Numerous sets of arrows were also stolen, as well as a number of new sets of Gold Tip arrows still in their packaging.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information regarding the burglary or the stolen items should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/211959/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
