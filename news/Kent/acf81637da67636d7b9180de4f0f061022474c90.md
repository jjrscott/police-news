Officers in **Ashford** are appealing for information after three mountain bikes were stolen in a burglary.

The thefts, from a property in **Kingsnorth Road** , are believed to have taken place between **midday** on **Saturday 7 November** and **10.30am** on **Sunday 8 November 2020**.

The three bikes taken are a blue, yellow and black Giant mountain bike, a blue Diamondback mountain bike with 'XCT' on the front fork, and a black Carrera bike, with grey writing on the frame.

A gas cylinder was also reported stolen following the incident.

Anyone with any information, or who believes they may have seen the bikes since the thefts, is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference **46/200277/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** or using the anonymous online form available via this link.
