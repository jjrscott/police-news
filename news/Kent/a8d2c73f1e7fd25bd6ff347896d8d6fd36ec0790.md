A suspected drug dealer has appeared in court following his arrest in **Tunbridge Wells.**

On **Saturday 13 February 2021** , a search warrant issued under the Misuse of Drugs Act was executed at an address in **Medway Drive**.

Officers seized a quantity of cocaine, some herbal cannabis and electronic scales. They also recovered a quantity of cash and a man was arrested.

## Charges

**Louie Bailie** was later charged with possessing cocaine with intent to supply, possessing cannabis and possession of criminal property, namely the cash seized.

Mr Bailie, aged 26, appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on Monday 15 February where he was remanded to a hearing at Maidstone Crown Court on a date to be confirmed.
