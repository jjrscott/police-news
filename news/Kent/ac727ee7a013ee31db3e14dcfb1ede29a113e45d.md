A police dog located a man who reportedly ran away from the scene of a road traffic collision where drugs were seized.

Kent Police was called to **junction 11 of the M20** , **Hythe at 5.23pm on Tuesday 23 March 2021** following a report of a road traffic collision.

The passenger of one of the vehicles reportedly ran away from the scene, he was promptly located by Police Dog Yankee on a nearby bridge and arrested after class A and B drugs were found during a search.

The man, who is in his thirties and from the **Folkestone** area, was arrested on suspicion of possessing class A and B drugs with intent to supply. He has been released under investigation.
