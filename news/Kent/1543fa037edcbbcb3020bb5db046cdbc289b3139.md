Kent Police is appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision on the M20 near **Ashford**.

A man in his 30s was taken to a London hospital after his black Mercedes collided with a bridge at **Drovers roundabout** , Junction 9, at around **4.40am** on **Friday 19 March 2021.** The man died the following morning, Saturday 20 March 2021.

A diversion was put in place whilst officers from the Serious Collision Investigation Unit carried out enquiries at the scene. The road was re-open by around 1.20pm.

### Can you help?

Officers would like to hear from any witnesses who saw the vehicle prior to the incident. They would also like to hear from anyone with dashcam footage that may assist their investigation.

Anyone with information is urged to call the appeal line on 01622 798538 quoting reference **DB/YP/21/21**. You can also email sciu.td@kent.police.uk.
