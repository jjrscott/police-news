Dashcam footage is sought following a report a motorist threw a bag of fishing bait at a woman in **Tonbridge**.

The incident is reported to have taken place in **Cannon Lane** at around **4.40pm** on **Monday 23 November 2020** , with no serious injuries sustained.

The item is said to have been thrown from a dark blue, or green, van that was travelling towards Hadlow Road.

The victim, a woman in her 20s, was stood on a traffic island in the middle of the road, next to Cannon Lane Retail Park.

Officers would now like to hear from motorists who may have dashcam footage that was filmed in Cannon Lane between 4.35pm and 4.45pm on 23 November.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** quoting **46/209536/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
