A suspect has appeared in court charged with a robbery in **Lydd** town centre.

Kent Police was called at **4.50pm** on **Sunday 14 February 2021** and it was reported that a woman had been robbed in the town's **High Street**.

It was reported that the woman had been approached by a man holding a craft knife, who had then taken money from her purse.

## Arrest

Following an investigation by Kent Police's Chief Constable's Crime Squad, **Christopher Banks** was arrested on Monday 22 February.

Mr Banks, of Salisbury Road, Chatham, has since been charged with robbery, possession of a knife and possession of cannabis.

The 24-year-old appeared before Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 23 February and was remanded in custody to appear at court on a date to be arranged.
