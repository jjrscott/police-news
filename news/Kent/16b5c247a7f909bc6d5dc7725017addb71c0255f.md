CCTV images have been issued following a serious assault in **Maidstone**.

At around **11pm** on **Saturday 18 July 2020** , a man in his 40s was in a bar on **Bank Street** when it is reported he was punched and struck over the head with a glass by two men.

## Injuries

The victim suffered head injuries and was required to attend a local hospital for medical attention.

Police Staff Investigator, Amanda Lissenden, of Maidstone C.I.D. said: 'We are releasing two images of a person who may be able to assist us with our enquiries.

'While they are slightly blurred, we are hoping somebody recognises the man and contacts us.'

## Can you help?

Anybody with information should contact Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting crime reference 46/123777/20.

You can also call Kent Crimestoppers, anonymously on **0800 555111** or by using the online form.
