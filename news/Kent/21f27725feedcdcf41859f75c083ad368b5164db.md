Detectives investigating a disturbance in **Hildenborough** are appealing for information.

The incident happened at around **4pm** on **Tuesday, 2 March 2021** after it is alleged a man went to an address in **Mount Pleasant Court** and threatened a man who lived there.

## Altercation

It is reported both men were then involved in a collision between the cars they were driving in **Mount Pleasant** and a subsequent altercation after they got out of the vehicles.

Officers are appealing for witnesses and are asking anyone who has not yet spoken to the police to contact the appeal line.

They are also urging any motorists with dashcam who were in the area at the time and residents with doorbell cameras, to check for footage that may assist the investigation.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is urged to contact Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/34498/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on **0800 555 111** or by using the online form.
