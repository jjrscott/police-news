Detectives investigating a reported assault in **Deal** are appealing for witnesses to come forward.

The incident happened in **South Street** , near the junction with Middle Street, shortly before **2.30am** on **Saturday 25 January 2020**.

It was reported that two men had a confrontation in the street resulting in one, aged in his 20s, suffering injuries consistent with being stabbed.

He was taken to a local hospital for treatment but has since been discharged.

Enquiries to establish the circumstances surrounding the incident and to identify the person responsible are ongoing.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is urged to call the appeal line on 01843 222289 quoting reference 46/15130/20.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form.
