Officers from the Serious Collision Investigation Unit are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision on the **London-bound carriageway** of the **M20** on **Saturday 31 October 2020**.

At about **2.20pm** , between **junctions six and five** , a white Mercedes-Benz E220 collided with the rear of a blue Mitsubishi L200 which was parked on the hard shoulder. This had been involved in an earlier collision with a white Ford Kuga that had collided with a crash barrier.

A 22-year-old woman in the Mercedes-Benz was declared deceased at the scene. Her next of kin have been informed.

A 50-year-old woman received serious injuries and was taken to a local hospital for treatment.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or who may have dashcam footage is asked to contact Kent Police's Serious Collision Investigation Unit on 01622 798538, quoting SM/DC/094/20. Alternatively, email sciu.td@kent.pnn.police.uk
