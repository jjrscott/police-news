Investigators are appealing for information after receiving a report of criminal damage in **Tunbridge Wells**.

The incident happened on **7 December 2020** , when it is alleged a man damaged a barrier at an underground car park in **Culverden Park Road**.

Having made a number of enquiries into the incident, officers are now able to issue a CCTV image of a person who they believe could help with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone who has information that may assist the investigation is urged to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/217971/20.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form on their website.
