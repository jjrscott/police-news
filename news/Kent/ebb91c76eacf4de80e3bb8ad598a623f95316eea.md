Do you recognise this woman?

A CCTV image has been issued of a woman officers would like to speak to in connection with shoplifting offences in **Canterbury**.

A number of recent thefts have been reported at the Marks and Spencer store in **St George 's Street**, where bottles of alcohol have been repeatedly stolen.

The offences are believed to have been committed by the same person. The latest incident was reported to have taken place on **5 March 2020**.

## Can you help?

An image is now being released of a woman who may have important information. If you recognise her please call the east Kent appeals line on **01843 222289** , quoting reference 46/42476/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111, or by using the online form.
