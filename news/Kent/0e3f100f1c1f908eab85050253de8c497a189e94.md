Detectives from the Kent and Essex Serious Crime Directorate are investigating a report that gunshots were fired at a **Maidstone** address.

Kent Police was called to a property in **Massey Close, Tovil** at around **7.55pm** on **Sunday 16 February 2020** , where a window had reportedly been damaged by shots discharged from outside the premises. Patrols, including firearms officers, attended the scene where nobody had been injured.

An investigation to establish the full circumstances is underway including forensic examinations at the scene and the review of CCTV in the area.

## Can you help?

Investigators are urging witnesses or anyone with any information to contact Kent Police on **01622 604 100** , quoting crime reference **46/30075/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by dialling **0800 555 111** or by completing the online form.
