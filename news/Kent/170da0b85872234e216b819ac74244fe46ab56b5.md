Missing woman Margaret Rossiter

Kent Police is appealing for information into the whereabouts of a woman from **Dover**.

Margaret Rossiter, 45, was last seen at **Dover Priory Railway Station** at around **midday** on **Thursday 17 December 2020** , and officers are growing increasingly concerned for her welfare.

## Description

Margaret is described as white, with shoulder length brown hair and has a slim build.

She was last seen wearing a thin red diamond quilted jacket with a black fur trim to the hood and grey leopard print leggings, light socks and black fluffy sliders. She may also be carrying a black handbag and a bright orange carrier bag.

Anyone who sees Margaret or knows of her whereabouts is urged to call 999 quoting reference 17-0856.
