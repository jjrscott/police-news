A new CCTV image has been issued by officers who are continuing to appeal for information regarding the assault of a bus driver in **Whitstable**.

The victim reported that he was verbally abused and assaulted through his window while driving the bus **shortly after 2.30pm on 28 December 2020** in **Harbour Street**. The wing mirror of the vehicle was also damaged during the incident.

Police have obtained a new CCTV image as part of their enquiries, and would like to identify the man pictured as he may have information which could assist the investigation.

## Can you help?

Anyone who witnessed the incident should also contact Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/227644/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting the website.
