Do you recognise this woman?

A CCTV image of a woman has been released by officers investigated a reported theft from a shop in **Maidstone**.

The incident happened at Card Factory in **The Mall** at around **10am** on **Friday 11 December 2020**.

It was reported that the victim, a woman in her 80s, left her purse containing a bank card and driving licence on the counter. The bank card was later reportedly used by someone without her permission.

Enquiries have been carried out and officers would now like to hear from anyone who recognises the woman in the CCTV image, as they believe she may be able to assist them.

Please call the appeal line on 01622 604100 quoting reference 46/219131/20. You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form at this link.
