A **Maidstone** police community support officer has assisted in the arrest of a man suspected of a knifepoint robbery.

At around **5pm** on **Monday 30 November 2020** , a man in his 20s reported he had been approached by a suspect with a knife in **Lynley Close**.

It is alleged he demanded cash before leaving empty handed.

## Arrest

A local PCSO responded to the incident and, after spotting a man near Sutton Road, was joined by officers who made an arrest. Officers also seized two knives.

**Jason Barnes** , of no fixed address, was later charged by investigators from the Chief Constable's Crime Squad with attempted robbery and two counts of possession of a knife. He was also charged with one count of assaulting an emergency worker relating to an incident at the time of his arrest.

## Court

Mr Barnes, aged 47, was remanded in custody to appear at Maidstone Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 2 December.
