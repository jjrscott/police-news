A suspected drug dealer is due to appear in court after Kent Police seized drugs and cash in **Canterbury**.

Officers attended an address in **Long Meadow Way** on the morning of **Wednesday 2 December 2020** and carried out a warrant under the Misuse of Drugs Act.

Around 100 wraps of suspected cocaine, a quantity of cash and mobile phones were seized as part of the investigation.

## Charge

**Tre Brooks** , of Vassall Road, Kennington, south London, has been charged with possession of cocaine with intent to supply.

The 21-year-old is due to appear before Margate Magistrates' Court on Thursday 3 December 2020.

Four other people who were arrested as part of the investigation have been released without charge.
