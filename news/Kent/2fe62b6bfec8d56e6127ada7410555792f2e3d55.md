A man has been charged with a murder in **Margate**.

Kent Police was called to a property in **Godwin Road, Cliftonville shortly before 11am on Thursday 28 January 2021** , where a woman was found fatally injured.

Following an investigation by the Kent and Essex Serious Crime Directorate, a charge of murder was authorised by the Crown Prosecution Service against 57-year-old **Abdelaziz El Msseyah** , of Godwin Road, Margate.

He remains in custody and is due to appear at Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Friday 29 January.
