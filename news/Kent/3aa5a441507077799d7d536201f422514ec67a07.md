The cannabis cultivation in St Margaret's Bay

Kent Police officers have seized cannabis plants and suspected stolen bikes from a property near **Dover**.

Officers attended the property in **The Droveway, St Margaret's Bay** on the afternoon of **Thursday 7 January 2021** following a report that a stolen bike had been seen in the area.

When the property was searched, four bikes were found and seized, along with around 20 cannabis plants and two bags of cannabis.

## Arrests

A 35-year-old man and a 24-year-old woman were arrested as part of an ongoing investigation and have been released while enquiries continue.

Officers are working to establish the owners of the bikes and return them.
