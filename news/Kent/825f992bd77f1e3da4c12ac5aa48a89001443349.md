An appeal has been issued by officers investigating a collision in **Maidstone**.

It took place at around **7.50pm** on **Friday 5 March 2021** , when a car was involved in a collision with a child in **Sutton Road** before its junction with **Willington Street**.

It is reported the vehicle did not stop but continued towards **Chart Sutton**.

## Injuries

The pedestrian, a teenage girl, was taken to a local hospital for treatment to a head injury and bruising, and was later discharged.

Investigating officer, Police Sergeant Andy Ferguson said: 'The vehicle is described as a silver or light blue small hatchback and we would like to speak to the driver.
'We are appealing for anybody who witnessed the collision to contact our appeal line.'

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is urged to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 5-1377.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** or by using the online form on their website.
