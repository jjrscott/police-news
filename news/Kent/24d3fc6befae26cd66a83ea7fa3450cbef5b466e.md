A burglary charge has been authorised against a man who is alleged to have stolen a bank card, tobacco and food from a property in **Canterbury**.

**Jeremy Martin** is alleged to have entered a flat in **Craddock Road** at around **10.40pm** on **Wednesday 6 January 2021** , before stealing the items.

The 52-year-old, of Craddock Road, Canterbury, was arrested on the same night and later charged.

He appeared at Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Saturday 9 January and has been remanded in custody to appear at Canterbury Crown Court on Monday 8 February.
