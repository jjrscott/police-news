Witnesses are sought following a collision involving a car and an elderly pedestrian in **Maidstone**.

The collision happened at around **12.15pm** on **29 December 2020** in **Milton Street** , close to the junction with Bazalgette Rise.

The victim, aged 69 and from Maidstone, was treated for his injuries at hospital.

He also received care for a separate medical condition and has since died at the hospital. His next of kin are aware.

It is reported the car involved in the collision was a **silver Ford Focus** and that it failed to stop and left the scene.

Shortly after the incident officers arrested a 53-year-old local man on suspicion of driving offences. He was later released, pending further investigation.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information who has not yet spoken to police is urged to call the Serious Collision Investigation Unit on **01622 798538** , quoting reference 11620. You can also email sciu.td@kent.pnn.police.uk

Motorists who may have been in the area are urged to also check any dashcams which may assist the investigation. Residents with CCTV or doorbell security systems are also asked to check for any footage.
