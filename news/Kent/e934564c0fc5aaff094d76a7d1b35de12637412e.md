The cause of a fire in a derelict building in **Whitstable** is being investigated by Kent Police.

Officers were called to the scene in **Graystone Road** at around **4.20am** on **Thursday 1 October 2020**.

Crews from Kent Fire and Rescue Service were already in attendance and extinguished the flames.

It is understood the building was unoccupied at the time of the incident.

Enquiries are ongoing into the cause of the fire which is believed to be suspicious.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information or who witnessed anything suspicious in the area, is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/175288/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
