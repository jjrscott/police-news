Investigators have issued an appeal for information following a suspected arson in **Sevenoaks**.

Kent Police was called at around **11.15pm** on **Saturday 26 September 2020** to a report a car was on fire whilst parked on a driveway on **Garth Road**.

Officers attended the scene along with fire crews who extinguished the blaze.

Police Constable Jamie Webb of Tonbridge CID said: 'We are appealing to anyone who was in the area between 10.20pm and midnight and saw anything suspicious or unusual to contact us.

'In addition, we are asking motorists who may have been in the area to check for any dashcam footage and local residents to view home security CCTV or doorbell cameras.'

## Can you help?

Anyone with information should call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting crime reference 46/172822/20.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on **0800 555111** , or complete the online form.
