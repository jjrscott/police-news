Two suspected county line dealers have appeared in court following their arrest for drug supply offences in **Tunbridge Wells**.

On **Tuesday 16 February 2021** , search warrants were executed at two addresses in London and Tunbridge Wells

At the London address, officers seized a mobile phone, two wraps of cocaine and a quantity of cash. At the premises in Kent they recovered three sets of electronic scales. Two men were arrested.

## Charges

**Connor Martin** , of no fixed address, and **Henry Muggridge,** of North Farm Road, Tunbridge Wells, were later charged with conspiracy to supply crack cocaine and conspiracy to supply heroin.

Mr Martin, aged 19, and Mr Muggridge, aged 31, both appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court the following day, where they were remanded to a hearing at Maidstone Crown Court on 17 March.
