Commercial units have been targeted in a rural location near **Cranbrook**.

Six premises off **Chalk Lane** were reportedly broken into between **6pm** on **Saturday 25 July** and **6am** on **Sunday 26 July 2020**.

Inspector John Betts of the Tunbridge Wells Community Safety Unit said: 'At this stage it is believed those responsible for the burglaries left empty-handed.

'We regularly patrol rural areas and I would urge residents to call us immediately if they see anybody acting suspiciously, particularly during the hours of darkness.'

## Can you help?

Officers investigating the incidents are asking anyone with information to contact Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting crime reference 46/130445/19.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** , or using the anonymous online form
