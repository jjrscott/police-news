Witnesses are sought following a fatal collision near **Folkestone**.

At around **5pm on Wednesday 3 February 2021** , a black Nissan Qashqai car was involved in a collision with a pedestrian walking a dog in **New Dover Road** , near the junction with Helena Road in **Capel-Le-Ferne**.

A 79-year-old man was confirmed deceased at the scene. The dog also died following the collision.

Officers from the Serious Collision Investigation Unit are appealing to anyone who saw the collision, or has CCTV, doorbell or dash cam footage, to come forward.

## Can you help?

Witnesses should contact the SCIU appeal line on **01622 798538** quoting reference RY/DC/8/21.

You can also email sciu.td@kent.police.uk.
