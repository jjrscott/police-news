Witnesses are being sought to a report of threatening behaviour in Margate.

It was reported at around **5.50pm** on **Wednesday 15 January 2020** , a man, who was standing in **Margate High Street** near the junction with The Centre, was approached by another man and threatened with a knife.

The victim was able to leave the area without further issue and call police. Both parties are known to each other.

## Can you help?

A 46-year-old man of no fixed address was arrested on suspicion of threats to kill and possession of a bladed article on 15 January, and was released on bail until 3 February 2020 while enquiries continue.

Anyone who was in the area at the time and witnessed the disturbance is asked to call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting 46/9242/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
