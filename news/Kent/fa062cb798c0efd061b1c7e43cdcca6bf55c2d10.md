Officers in **Folkestone** have recovered three suspected stolen mopeds and arrested a man.

Kent Police was called to **Coolinge Road** at **4.30am** on **Thursday 18 February 2021** following a report of moped riders with no crash helmets.

Officers attended and recovered two mopeds from an alleyway near **Foord Road** and another scooter in **Broadmead Road**.

## Arrest

All of the vehicles are suspected to have been previously stolen and enquiries are ongoing to trace their owners.

An 18-year-old man from Folkestone was arrested on suspicion of theft of a motor vehicle. He has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
