A second woman has been arrested following a disturbance in **Ramsgate** that left four people with injuries.

Kent Police officers were called to **Plains of Waterloo** on **Saturday 11 July 2020** at around **9.20pm** where two of the victims had suffered injuries which required hospital treatment.

## Two arrests

A 21-year-old woman was arrested at the scene on suspicion of assault and has since been released on police bail until 6 August. On Monday 13 July a second woman, aged 20, was arrested and has also been released on bail until 3 August.

### Witnesses still sought

Officers continue to investigate the circumstances of this incident and are still seeking witnesses who have not yet been in touch. Anyone with information should call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting reference 11-1585.

Officers would particularly like to hear from anyone with CCTV or dashcam footage.

Anyone with information can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
