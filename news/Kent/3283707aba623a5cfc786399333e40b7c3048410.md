A suspected drug dealer has appeared in court following his arrest in **Gillingham**.

On **2 October 2020** officers seized 30 wraps of heroin and crack cocaine from inside an address in **Saunders Street**.

As part of the ongoing investigation a search warrant was executed by officers from Kent Police's county line and gang team at an address in **Richmond Road** on **2 February 2021**.

Inside the premises they seized around 150 heroin and crack cocaine deals and a man was arrested on suspicion of drug offences.

**Sean Naylor** , of no fixed address was later charged with two counts of possession with intent to supply heroin and possession with intent to supply crack cocaine.

Mr Naylor, aged 26, appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on 4 February where he was remanded to appear before Maidstone Crown Court on a date to be determined.
