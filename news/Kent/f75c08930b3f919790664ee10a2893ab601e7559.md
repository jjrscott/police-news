Witnesses are being sought following a serious collision in **Ashford**.

A white lorry was travelling along the **A2070 Kennington Road, Willesborough** , towards Canterbury at around **9.50am on Monday 7 December 2020** when it was involved in a collision with a pedestrian.

A man suffered serious injuries and was taken to a London hospital.

An investigation into the incident is being carried out by Kent Police's Serious Collision Investigation Unit (SCIU).

## Can you help?

Anyone who witnessed the collision or has dashcam footage should call SCIU on **01622 798 538** or email here quoting DS/SW/109/20.
