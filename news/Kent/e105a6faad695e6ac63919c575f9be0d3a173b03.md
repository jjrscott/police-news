Information is being sought following a burglary at an address in **Strood** where a number of medals were reported stolen.

Officers were called at around **7.20pm** on **Monday 27 January 2020** to reports of a break-in at a property in **Daffodil Road**.

During the break-in, which is believed to have happened between 2.15pm and 7.15pm, an untidy search took place and a number of medals and items of jewellery were allegedly stolen.

Officers are asking for anyone with information that may help to locate the medals to come forward.

Details of the medals are as follows:

 * General Service Medal (silver medal with mauve and green ribbon and Northern Ireland bar)
 * Long Service Medal (silver medal with a burgundy and white ribbon)
 * Queen Elizabeth II Golden Jubilee Medal (gold colour medal with blue and red ribbon)

## Can you help?

Anyone with any information regarding the incident is asked to call the Kent Police appeals line on 01634 792209 quoting reference 46/17042/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by dialling 0800 555 111 or completing the online form.
