A suspected drug dealer has been charged after a proactive arrest by officers in **Tunbridge Wells.**

At around **5.30pm** on **Monday 11 January 2021** , a police patrol in **Eridge Road** stopped the driver of a Vauxhall Corsa they suspected of being involved in the supply of drugs. The officers completed a search of the vehicle and seized 36 deals of cocaine and a quantity of cash.

## Charged

**Viujan Leka** , of no fixed address, was later charged with possession of cocaine with intent to supply, driving a motor vehicle otherwise than in accordance with a licence, driving without insurance and possession of criminal property, namely the cash seized.

Mr Leka, aged 26, appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 12 January, where he was remanded to a hearing at Maidstone Crown Court on a date to be confirmed.
