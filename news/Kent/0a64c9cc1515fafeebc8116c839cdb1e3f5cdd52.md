A suspected thief from Medway is to appear in court charged in connection with a disturbance in **Ashford**.

The charges follow an incident in which police were called to a report of theft of alcohol and threats to staff at a petrol station in **Beaver Lane, Ashford** during the **evening of Thursday 25 March 2021**.

**Christopher Leech** , of Tupman Close, Rochester, was arrested and charged with theft, possession of a knife, and a public order offence.

The twenty-eight year old appeared at Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Saturday 27 March and was bailed to next appear at the same court on Thursday 29 April.
