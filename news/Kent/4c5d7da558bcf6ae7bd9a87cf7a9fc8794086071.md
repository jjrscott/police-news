Investigators are appealing for witnesses to a serious injury collision in **Edenbridge**.

The incident took place at around **5.50pm** on **Thursday 25 June 2020** on **Main Road** at its junction with **Hillcrest Road** , where a silver Skoda Octavia was involved in a collision with a black Suzuki motorcycle.

The motorcyclist, a man in his 30s, was treated for injuries and then conveyed to a London hospital where he remains in a serious but stable condition. The motorist also received medical attention.

## Can you help?

Officers are working to establish the circumstances of the incident and are urging anyone who saw the motorcycle or the car in the moments leading up to the collision, or who have dash cam footage from the area around the time, to contact them.

Witnesses can call the Serious Collision Investigation Unit on **01622 798 538** , quoting reference RY/JG/57/20.

Alternatively, email sciu.td@kent.pnn.police.uk.
