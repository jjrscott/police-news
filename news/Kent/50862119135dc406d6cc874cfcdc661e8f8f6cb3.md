An arrest has been made after Kent Police received more than 20 reports of criminal damage taking place across **Lenham**.

Between **31 January** and **21 March 2021** , the Maidstone Community Policing Team received 26 reports of glass jars being thrown at homes across the village.

Officers within the team reviewed the circumstances of each report and carried out a number of plain-clothed patrols.

As part of these enquiries, a man in his 20s, from the village, was arrested on suspicion of criminal damage on the morning of Friday 26 March.

He has since been released on bail, pending further enquiries, until Monday 26 April.
