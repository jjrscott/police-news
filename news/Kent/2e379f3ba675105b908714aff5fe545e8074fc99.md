CCTV images have been issued by officers investigating a reported fraud at a **West Malling** store.

On **Wednesday 17 February 2021** , it is alleged a woman went to Tesco in the **High Street** and fraudulently obtained a quantity of cash and a gift card, in connection with a refund.

Officers are now able to issue CCTV images of a person who they believe may be able to help the investigation.

## Can you help?

Anyone who recognises the woman is urged to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/29302/21.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form on their website.
