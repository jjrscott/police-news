Improvised weapons with knives and spikes have been seized during the latest drugs enforcement activity in **Tunbridge Wells.**

The weapons were discovered along with a cannabis cultivation, when police executed a search warrant at a property in **Holmewood Road.**

Officers from the Tunbridge Wells Community Policing Team entered the address at **6.30am** on **Tuesday 9 February 2021.**

Quantities of cannabis were initially found in the kitchen, before 35 plants were discovered underneath the stairs.

## Weapons, drugs and arrests

Among weapons seized, included three wooden implements with knives and spikes taped to them.

Cash and several mobile phones were also confiscated, including one device alleged to have been thrown from a window of the property as police entered.

Two men, aged 29 and 59, were arrested on suspicion of the cultivation and supply of drugs.

Both were later released pending further investigation.
