Officers are appealing for the owner of a stolen mountain bike to come forward following the arrests of two men in **Dartford**.

The grey and purple Hawk Lady Reaction bicycle is believed to have been taken from outside a supermarket in **Home Gardens** at some point on **Monday 1 December 2020**.

A 33-year-old man and a 35-year-old man were arrested in Priory Hill at around 11.30pm the same day and have since been released pending further enquiries.

The owner of the bike or anyone who witnessed it being stolen are encouraged to call Kent Police on 01634 792209 quoting reference 46/213216/20. You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form at this link.
