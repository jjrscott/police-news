Information is sought following a burglary in **Deal**.

It was reported that a number of items, including a 43-inch Samsung television, GoPro camera, Xbox, a Breitling watch, a boxed Nespresso coffee maker and a gold large Cuban link chain were taken from a domestic property in **Thornton Road, Tilmanstone** , **between Christmas Eve and Monday 4 January 2021**.

A bag containing designer clothes including a blue Kenzo jumper and a black Moshino hooded top were also reported stolen.

## Can you help?

Anyone who might have information regarding suspicious activity in that area, should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/2129/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting the website.
