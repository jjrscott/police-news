A van was stopped in **Ditton** where officers from the Rural Task Force arrested a man for drug and driving offences.

On **Wednesday 2 December 2020** at around **9am** the driver was stopped as he was leaving a petrol station on **London Road**. He was searched and police found a quantity of cannabis in his rucksack. The vehicle was found to have no insurance and was seized.

# Charged

**Aaron Pennells** , 23, of no fixed address has been charged with possession of class B drugs and driving without a licence or insurance.

He has been bailed to appear at Maidstone Magistrates' Court on Friday 5 February 2021.
