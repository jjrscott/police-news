An appeal for information has been issued in relation to a number of reports of criminal damage in **Canterbury**.

Officers are investigating the damage caused to the windows of seven vehicles as well as a damaged window to a property **overnight between Monday 4 January and the following morning**.

The offences are reported to have taken place in **Hales Drive, Field Avenue, and** **South Street**.

There have also been reports of some vehicles sprayed with paint.

Police Community Support Officers have been in the area speaking to residents and thank everyone who has already come forward with CCTV.

If anyone else has CCTV or dashcam footage which covered the late evening, they are urged to contact Kent Police.

In particular, police are keen to speak to the owner of a light coloured saloon car seen in the area that evening.

## Can you help?

People can report via **01843 222289** quoting ZA CSU.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting the website.
