An appeal has been issued by officers investigating a series of reported arsons in **Tunbridge Wells** which caused damage to parked cars.

Between **1.45am** and **3.45am** on **Tuesday 16 June 2020** two vehicles sustained damage after fires were started in **Sherwood Road** and **Kingswood Road.**

## Further fires

A short time later rubbish bins and street signs were set alight in **Pembury Road** and also in **Nelson Road, Hawkenbury**.

Police Sergeant James Page said: 'It is possible these incidents are linked and suspects may have walked between the four locations.

'We have completed local enquiries and are now asking residents in the area with private CCTV or motorists with dashcam who were driving through the neighbourhood at the time, to get in touch.'

## Can you help?

Anyone with information that may assist the investigation is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/101217/20.

You can also call Kent Crimestoppers, anonymously on **0800 555111** or by using the online form.
