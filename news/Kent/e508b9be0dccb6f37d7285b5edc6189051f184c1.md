This motorbike was stolen from Fordwich, near Canterbury

Police are appealing for information following the theft of a motocross motorbike from Fordwich, near Canterbury.

Officers were called after the bike was reported stolen from a garage near **Fordwich High Street** between **6pm** and **9.30pm** on **Friday 17 January 2020**.

Two men were seen pushing a bike in the area wearing dark hooded jumpers, lighter tracksuit bottoms and trainers.

## Can you help?

It is reported the motorbike was loaded into a white Transit-style van before being driven off.

Kent Police are releasing images of the stolen bike, which may have since been modified, and are asking anyone with information to call 01843 222289, quoting reference 46/10898/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
