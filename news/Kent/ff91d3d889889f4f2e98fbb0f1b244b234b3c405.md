Information is being sought after jewellery was stolen during a suspected burglary at a **Tenterden** property.

It was reported that on **Friday 2 October 2020** a resident of **Rothley Close** left their home and when they returned a window was open. They later noticed that jewellery was missing.

Items reportedly stolen include a gold wedding ring, a gold signet ring, a small gold ring, a pair of gold cross earrings, a pair of silver earrings and a gold ankle bracelet.

Anyone with information regarding the burglary or if the jewellery has been seen or offered for sale, is asked to call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting 46/196635/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** or using the anonymous online form available via this link.
