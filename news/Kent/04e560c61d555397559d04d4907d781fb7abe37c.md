Investigators are appealing for information following a reported theft from an ambulance in **Sevenoaks.**

It is alleged a bag was stolen from the ambulance as paramedics were attending an address and dealing with a medical incident.

The incident happened in **Greatness Lane** , between **12.30am** and **1.15am** on **Thursday 25 March 2021**.

District Commander, Chief Inspector Mark Stubberfield said: 'It is completely unacceptable that staff from South East Coast Ambulance Service have been targeted whilst responding to an emergency. 'This bag contained personal items and we are appealing for witnesses or anybody with information to contact us.'

## Can you help?

Officers are also asking drivers with dashcam who were in the area and residents with CCTV to check for footage that may assist the investigation.

Anyone with information is urged to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 25-0092.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111 or by using the anonymous online form.
