Information is sought by detectives investigating a spate of reported burglaries in the **Bidborough** area.

Between **9am** and **9.45am** on **Thursday 24 September 2020** , three houses were targeted in **Bidborough Ridge** and **London Road**.

Damage was caused to windows and doors and jewellery was stolen from one of the premises.

## CCTV sought

Investigators from the Chief Constable's Crime Squad are appealing for drivers who may have been in or around the area between 8am and 10am to check dash cams for anything suspicious.

Residents in the area with CCTV or doorbell cameras are also urged to review any footage which may assist enquiries.

Two or three men are believed to have been acting suspiciously in the area and may be connected to a black hatchback seen in the vicinity at the time of the break-ins.

## Can you help?

Witnesses and anyone with information are asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/171043/20.

You can also call Crimestoppers, anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by using the online form.
