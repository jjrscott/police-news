Construction equipment was reported stolen following a burglary at an outbuilding near **Canterbury**.

The suspects reportedly broke into the premises in **Canterbury Road, Barham** , between **the evening of Monday 24 August and 10.20am the following day.** A plate compactor, two auger drills and a mower with hammer blades were taken.

## Can you help?

Anyone who witnessed anything suspicious in the area and has seen similar items for sale, is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/151684/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
