A police dog has assisted officers with locating a suspected thief after several roof tiles were removed from a property near **Tonbridge.**

Kent Police was called at **10.35pm** on **Wednesday 6 January 2021** to a report of two trespassers trying to enter a building in **Half Moon Lane, Tudeley.**

The suspects were said to have removed around 30 tiles from the roof of the building.

Officers attended and with the help of PD Biggy carried out a search of the area.

A 33-year-old man from Pembury was located in a nearby field. He was arrested and later released pending further investigation.

## Can you help?

The second suspect remains outstanding.

Anyone with information which may assist the investigation is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/2935/21.

You can also contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111, or by using the online form.
