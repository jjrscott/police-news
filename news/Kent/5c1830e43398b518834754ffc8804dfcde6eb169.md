Witnesses are sought after a man reported being assaulted in **Borstal**.

The victim was walking along **Borstal Street** between **9.40pm** and **10pm** on **Thursday 1 of October 2020** when he fell over.

While he was on the ground he has reported that two men are said to have approached him and kicked his chest, arms and head.

The victim sustained arm and head injuries which required treatment at a local hospital and he has since been discharged.

Both suspects are described as being black, with slim builds and aged between 20 and 25.

A man, who is in his 20s and from Rochester, was arrested on suspicion of assault on 2 October and has since been released, pending further enquiries.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01634 792209** quoting **46/175977/20**.

Alternatively you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on **0800 555 111**.
