An appeal to identify a man who may have information regarding a burglary in **Ashford** has been issued by officers.

A quantity of cash was reported to have been stolen from a property in **Drummond Grove, Willesborough,** **between 12.15pm and 2.30pm** on **Monday 17 February 2020.**

## Can you help?

If you recognise the man pictured, or believe you have other information that can help officers investigate, please call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/31384/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
