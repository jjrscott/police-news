Information is sought by officers investigating the theft of a motorbike during a burglary near **Maidstone**.

Kent Police received a report that on **22 October 2020** , a garage door was forced open at an address in **Egremont Road** , and a motorbike and electric scooter were stolen.

Investigating officer, PC Connor McCarthy said: 'We have completed enquiries in the area, including the examination of any CCTV opportunities, and are now appealing for anybody with information that may assist our investigation, to get in contact.
'I would particularly like to hear from anybody who has seen the distinctive bike shown in the photograph or has been offered it for sale since the burglary.'

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/190110/20.

You can also call Crimestoppers, anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by using the online form.
