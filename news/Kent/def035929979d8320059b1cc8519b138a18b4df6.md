A CCTV image has been issued by officers investigating a report of damage to a car in **Maidstone**.

Kent Police received an allegation that at around **1.15am** on **Saturday 5 December 2020** , the driver's window on a car parked in **Hart Street** was smashed and a quantity of loose change was stolen from inside.

Officers would like to identify the person pictured in the CCTV image who they believe may be able to assist them with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/215885/20.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form.
