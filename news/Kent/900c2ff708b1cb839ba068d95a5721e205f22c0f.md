Police have released the image of a man that may have important information about a racially motivated incident that happened at a supermarket in **East Street, Sittingbourne** at **6.07pm** on **18 January 2021**.

It is reported two men were in the store when an altercation took place and a racist comment was made towards someone who attempted to intervene.

## Can you help?

Anyone who may have information that will help police with this investigation is asked to call the appeal line on 01795 419119, quoting crime reference 46/9476/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555111 or using the anonymous online form on their website.
