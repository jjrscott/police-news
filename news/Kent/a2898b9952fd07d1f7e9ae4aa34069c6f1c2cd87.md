CCTV images have been issued of two men who may have important information about a suspected fraud in **Sevenoaks**.

Officers are investigating a report that on **16 January 2020** , two men and a teenage boy went to an address in the town centre area and falsely claimed they were council employees. It is alleged they then obtained a pensioner's bank card before stealing a quantity of cash from their bank account.

## CCTV images

Following the completion of a number of enquiries including examination of CCTV footage, investigators are now releasing images of two men who may be able to help them with their enquiries.

Investigating officer, PC Matthew Thomas said:

'While the images are a little unclear, we are hoping somebody will recognise these men and call us.'

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604 100** , quoting reference 46/10217/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** , or by using the anonymous online form.
