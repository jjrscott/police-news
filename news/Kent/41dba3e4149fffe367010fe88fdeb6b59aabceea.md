The owners of these three bikes are being sought

The owners of three bikes which are believed to be stolen are being asked to come forward and identify their property.

The bikes were recovered when police attended a property near **St Margaret's Bay** , near **Dover** on **Thursday 7 January 2021**.

A 35-year-old man and a 24-year-old woman were arrested as part of an ongoing investigation and have been released while enquiries continue.

## Appeal

Pictures of the bikes have been released in the hope of tracing victims of theft who may not yet have reported their property stolen.

Anyone who recognises any of the bikes and believes one or more may belong to them should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference 46/3452/21.
