A suspected county line drug dealer has appeared in court following an arrest in **Folkestone**.

Officers on patrol in the town on the afternoon of **Saturday 6 February 2021** seized suspected class A drugs, cash and a mobile phone after spotting suspicious activity near **Foord Road**.

## Court

**Joshua Begg** , of Mount Pleasant Road, Folkestone, was arrested and later charged with possession of heroin and cocaine with intent to supply.

The 28-year-old appeared before Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Monday 8 February and was remanded in custody to appear before Canterbury Crown Court on Monday 8 March.
