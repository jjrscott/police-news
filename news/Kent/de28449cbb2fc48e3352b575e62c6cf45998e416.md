Witnesses are being sought after computer equipment and jewellery were reported stolen following a burglary in **Chatham**.

At **around 3am on Sunday 14 March 2021** , a woman woke up to find a man in her property in **Mount Road**. It was reported that he demanded her phone, and was believed to be holding a weapon.

During the burglary, items including an Apple watch with an olive green strap, a gold ring with a blue stone, an iPhone, an iPad, an Apple laptop and a purse, were stolen.

The suspect is described as around five feet, five inches tall; aged between thirty and forty years old, and was wearing dark clothing.

## Can you help?

Detectives would like to hear from any witnesses, or anyone who has dashcam or domestic CCTV which could assist enquiries.

People with information regarding the burglary should call Kent Police on **01634 792209** quoting 46/41226/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
