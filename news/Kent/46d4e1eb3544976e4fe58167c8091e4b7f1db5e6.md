A suspected burglar is to appear in court charged in connection with a burglary in **Margate**.

Kent Police received a report that a property in **Kent Road** had been burgled between **9 and 10 January 2021**. Items including alcohol, Christmas presents and the contents of a fridge-freezer were reported missing.

**Lewis Tedham** , 22, of Laleham Road in Margate was charged with one count of burglary on Sunday 10 January.

He was also charged with receiving stolen goods, namely a blue disabled parking badge, **between 6 and 10 January**.

He appeared at Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Monday 11 January. The case will next be heard at Folkestone Magistrates' Court on 28 January.
