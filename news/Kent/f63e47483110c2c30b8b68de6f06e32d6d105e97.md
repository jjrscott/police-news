CCTV images have been issued by officers investigating a reported fraud at a **Maidstone** supermarket.

At around **2.30pm** on **29 January 2021** , it is alleged a woman entered the Co-op in **Week Street** and gained a refund for items, including alcohol, she had not purchased.

Officers are now able to issue CCTV images of a person who they believe could help with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone who has information that may assist the investigation is urged to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/15121/21.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form on their website.
