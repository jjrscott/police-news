Information is being sought by detectives following a reported sexual assault in **Maidstone.**

It is alleged a woman was targeted by a man as she walked home from the town centre, during the early hours of **Saturday 18 July 2020.**

The victim had been passing through **Waterlow Road** at around **12.30am** when the incident took place.

The suspect is said to have followed the victim for a short distance, before carrying out the assault close to a car park for flats at Walsingham House.

## Description

He is described as being white, aged in his 20s, around 5ft 8ins tall and of slim build, with a dark goatee style beard and short cropped hair.

He was wearing a dark coloured top and shorts.

Officers are continuing to carry out a number of enquiries, including forensic checks, identifying any possible CCTV opportunities and speaking to local residents.

Detective Constable Paul Wells, of the West Kent Vulnerability Investigation Team, said: 'The suspect was first seen approaching the victim in Waterlow Road, from an area containing garages and a large skip bag for waste. After the assault he then headed in the direction of Wheeler Street.

'He has a fairly distinctive description and we are urging anyone who may have seen him or who has any other information to call us.'

## Can you help?

If you can help, contact the west Kent appeals line on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/123021/20.

You can also call Kent Crimestoppers, anonymously on 0800 555111.
