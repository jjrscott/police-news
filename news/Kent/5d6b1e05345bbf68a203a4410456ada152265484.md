Witnesses are being sought after a storage premises in **Hartley** was destroyed in a fire.

Kent Police officers are treating the fire in **Ash Road** , which took place on **Thursday 4 June 2020** at around **5.20pm** , as suspicious. House-to-house enquiries have been carried out and available CCTV has been reviewed.

Any witnesses who have not yet come forward are urged to call and provide any information that could assist with enquiries. The incident is believed to have caused thousands of pounds' worth of damage.

Anyone in the area at that time or who has information should call Kent Police on 01622 604100 quoting crime reference 46/100347/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
