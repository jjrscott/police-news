Two men have been arrested on suspicion of firearms offences as part of an investigation into a disturbance in **Ashford**.

Officers were called to **Monkton Close** at **midnight** on **Wednesday 30 September 2020** following a report that a suspected weapon had been heard during an altercation in the street between two groups of people.

A 20-year-old man from Ashford was arrested a short time later. He has been released on bail until 21 October.

Later on the same day, a 24-year-old man from Ashford was arrested in connection with the disturbance and was taken into custody where he currently remains.

A catapult and some blank rounds of ammunition have been recovered as part of the ongoing enquiries in to the incident.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information or who witnessed anything suspicious in the area, is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/174645/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
