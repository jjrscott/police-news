Witnesses are sought after a man was found injured in **Gillingham**.

Kent Police was called at **5.59am** on **12 October 2020** to a report a man had been found unconscious in **Canterbury Street**.

Officers attended along with South East Coast Ambulance Service and the man was taken to a local hospital with head injuries, not reported to be life threatening. He has since been released.

The incident is believed to have happened at around 3am and as part of enquiries a man in his 30s was arrested on suspicion of assault. He has been bailed until 4 November pending further enquiries.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the incident or who has information which can assist their enquiries.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on 01634 792209 quoting 46/182616/20.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or by using the online form.
