Witnesses are being sought following a serious assault in **Chatham.**

The incident is reported to have taken place in an alleyway which joins **Thorold Road** and **Luton Road,** Chatham between 4.30pm and 5.10pm on **Friday 27 March 2020.**

During the disturbance a 47-year-old man from the Chatham area suffered an injury to his stomach consistent with a stab wound. He was taken by air ambulance to a London hospital where he remains in a stable condition.

## Can you help?

Officers have since carried out a number of enquiries and would like to hear from any witnesses who are yet to make contact.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01634 792209** quoting reference number **46/55360/20.** ** **You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
