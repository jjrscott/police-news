Drivers in **Tunbridge Wells** are being urged to check dash cam footage following the theft of a car from a driveway.

At around **5.40pm** on **Wednesday 1 July 2020** a white Audi S3, 2019 model, was stolen after a property was burgled in **Sandhurst Park**.

The suspects forced entry through a door and stole jewellery and car keys, before taking the vehicle from the driveway.

They are believed to have arrived in a silver or grey coloured BMW X3, before leaving the scene and heading in the direction of High Brooms.

The BMW was reportedly later seen being driven erratically in Yew Tree Road.

## Footage sought

Detectives are appealing to motorists who may have been in these areas to check for possible evidence on their vehicle dash cams.

Residents with CCTV and doorbell security cameras are also being asked to check for any footage which may assist the investigation.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/111607/20.

You can also call Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111, or by using the online form.
