Gardening tools have been reported stolen following a burglary in **Tenterden**.

The tools, including chainsaws, strimmers, a leaf blower and a backpack-style hedge cutter, were taken during a break-in to a workshop of commercial premises in **Smallhythe Road** between **5pm** on **Wednesday 17 February 2021** and **5.30am** the following morning.

## Appeal

Officers are appealing for witnesses, or anyone offered the items for sale, to contact Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting 46/26183/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting crimestoppers-uk.org
