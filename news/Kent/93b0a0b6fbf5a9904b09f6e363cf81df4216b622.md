A suspect has been charged after a child was seriously injured in a collision in **Margate**.

Kent Police was called at around **2.05pm** on **Sunday 10 January 2021** following a collision between a van and a car in **Dane Valley Road**.

A boy, who had been a passenger in the van, was taken to a London hospital and remains in a serious but stable condition.

**Jacek Jarosz** has since been charged with driving whilst unfit through drink and causing serious injury by dangerous driving.

The 49-year-old, of William Avenue, Margate, is due to appear before Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Tuesday 12 January 2021.
