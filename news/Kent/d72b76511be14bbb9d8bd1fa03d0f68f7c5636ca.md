CCTV

CCTV images have been released following a hate crime incident in **Gillingham**.

Two women were shopping inside Tesco in **Courteney Road** , when they reported being targeted by three other people.

The offenders were alleged to have used homophobic language towards the victims who had to seek refuge inside a security office inside the store.

Since the incident, which happened at around **3.15pm** on **31 January 2021** , officers have carried out several enquiries to locate the suspects, and are now in a position to release CCTV images of women they are wishing to talk to.

Anyone who recognises them should call Kent Police on 01634 792209, quoting reference 46/16658/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or complete the online form at Crimestoppers.
