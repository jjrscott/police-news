## Man receives ban for attempted drink driving

Arturo Zoffman, aged 47, of Dover, was sentenced on 5 February 2021 at Folkestone Magistrates' Court.

He pleaded guilty to attempting to drive a motor vehicle when above the legal drink drive limit, in the Channel Tunnel, Folkestone, on 4 February 2021. When stopped he gave a breath test of 61 microgrammes of alcohol in 100 millilitres of breath. The legal limit is 35 microgrammes.

He was disqualified from driving for 20 months and fined £300\. He was also ordered to pay a surcharge of £34 and £85 of costs.

Drug dealer sentenced

Joshua Callender, aged 25, of Grange Road, Ramsgate, was sentenced on 25 January 2021 at Sevenoaks Magistrates' Court.
