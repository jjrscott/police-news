CCTV images have been issued after a man was assaulted and racially abused in **Cranbrook.**

The incident happened at a kebab shop in **Carriers Road** at around **7.15pm** on **Sunday 23 February 2020**.

It is reported that three suspects were involved in the assault.

It is alleged the victim was punched in the face and then beaten with a weapon believed to be a snooker cue. He was also subjected to racist abuse.

## Can you help?

Officers are now able to issue images of a man who they would like to identify.

Anyone who recognises him or who may have information is urged to call **01622 604100,** quoting reference 46/35086/20.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete an online form.
