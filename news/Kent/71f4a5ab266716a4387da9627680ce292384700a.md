Investigators are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision on the **A21** near **Lamberhurst**.

At **12.09pm** on **Monday 29 March 2021** , a blue DAF lorry was travelling south along the A21 between **Lamberhurst Quarter** and the **Forstal Farm Roundabout** , when it was involved in a collision with a pedestrian.

The pedestrian, a man in his 30s, was taken to a London hospital where he remains in a serious but stable condition.

## Can you help?

Anyone who saw the incident who has not yet spoken to officers, or who has dashcam footage which may assist the investigation, is urged to call the Serious Collision Investigation Unit on **01622 798538** , quoting reference XX/DC/023/21.

You can also make contact with investigators by email here.
