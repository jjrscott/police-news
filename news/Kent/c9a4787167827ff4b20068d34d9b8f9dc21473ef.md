Images have been issued of suspected stolen items believed to be connected to burglaries in and around the **West Kingsdown** area.

Residents are being asked to check whether they recognise possessions recovered, which include three distinctive watches and a number of pieces of jewellery.

They were seized on **Tuesday 7 April 2020** from a property close to **Church Road, West Kingsdown.**

Officers also located other suspicious items including car keys for a Kia, Mazda and a Vauxhall.

## Do you recognise anything?

Anyone who recognises anything in these images or has any information which may assist the investigation is asked to call the west Kent appeals line on **01622 604100,** quoting 46/60978/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555111.
