A suspected drug dealer has been charged after cocaine and heroin was seized at a **Swanley** address.

At around **4.30am** on **Sunday 6 December 2020** , officers attended a house on **London Road** following a report of a disturbance.

Once inside the premises, the officers saw two suspected deals of cocaine on the floor.

## Arrest

A search led to the seizure of further quantities of cocaine as well as heroin and cannabis, and a man was arrested.

**Nicholas Jebb,** of London Road, Swanley, was later charged with possession of cocaine with intent to supply, possession of heroin with intent to supply and possession of cannabis with intent to supply.

Mr Jebb, aged 31, appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on Monday 7 December. He was remanded to a hearing at Maidstone Crown Court on 4 January 2021.
