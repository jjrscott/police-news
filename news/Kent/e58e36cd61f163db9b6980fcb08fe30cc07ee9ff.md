A suspect is to appear in court charged with drugs and driving offences following an arrest in **Dover** in September.

Officers pursued a car after it failed to stop when requested to do in **Salisbury Road** at **11.10pm** on **Saturday 5 September 2020**.

The pursuit came to an end in **Buckland Terrace** , where the car made contact with a patrol car.

## Court

A man from Dover has now been summonsed to appear at Folkestone Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 9 December 2020.

The 20-year-old will face charges of possession of cocaine, dangerous driving, failing to stop when required to do so, driving without insurance and driving without the correct licence.
