A suspected drug dealer was arrested after officers uncovered a cannabis cultivation while on duty in **Dover**.

Kent Police officers were carrying out enquiries in the **London Road** area of the town on the morning of **Tuesday 2 February 2021** when the discovery was made.

Cultivation equipment including fans and lights were recovered, along with bags of cannabis and cash.

## Arrest

A man was arrested on suspicion of cultivating cannabis, being concerned in the supply of a controlled drug and abstraction of electricity.

The 29-year-old, who is from the Dover area, has since been released under investigation.
