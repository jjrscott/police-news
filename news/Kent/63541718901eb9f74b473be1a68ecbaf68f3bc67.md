Around 200 cannabis plants have been seized and two arrests made during Kent Police action near **Ashford**.

Officers carried out a search of a property near **Flood Street, Mersham** on the evening of **Friday 5 March 2021**.

As well as the plants, packaged cannabis and fertiliser were seized as part of an ongoing investigation.

## Arrests

A 43-year-old man and a 47-year-old woman, both from the Romney Marsh area, have since been arrested on suspicion of cultivating cannabis.

Both have been released under investigation while enquiries continue.
