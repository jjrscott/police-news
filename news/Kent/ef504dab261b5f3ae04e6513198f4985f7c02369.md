Dash cam footage and witnesses are being sought by officers investigating a collision on the **M20** near **Ryarsh**.

The incident happened at around **12.35pm** on **Sunday 1 November 2020** on the **Londonbound carriageway** between **junctions 3 and 2** , and involved an white Audi S4 and a grey Nissan Qashqai.

Investigators are appealing for information following reports of dangerous driving before the collision, and would like to obtain dash cam footage that shows the vehicles prior to and in the moments leading up to the incident.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information who has not yet spoken to the police should call **01622 604100** , quoting reference 1-0579.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by using the online form.

## Arrests

Two men aged 33 and 28 from Dartford and Forest Hill, Greater London respectively, were arrested on suspicion of dangerous driving and driving whilst unfit through drugs.

They were later released pending further investigation.
