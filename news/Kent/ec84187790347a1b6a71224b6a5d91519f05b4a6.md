CCTV

Officers investigating an attempted burglary in **Dartford** have released the image of a man who may have important information that will assist them with their enquiries.

The incident was reported to have taken place between **6.45pm** and **7pm** on **10 February 2021** in **Squires Way, Dartford.**

It is said that three men attended the address in a black Audi. One man stayed in the vehicle, two got out and entered the property. The men were disturbed by someone nearby and they ran away. Damage was caused in the incident and cash was stolen.
