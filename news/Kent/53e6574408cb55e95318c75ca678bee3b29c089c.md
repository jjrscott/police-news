CCTV images have been issued of three men who may have important information about a suspected theft in **Maidstone**.

Officers are investigating the reported theft of packets of cigarettes and bottles of spirits from a service station on **Chatham Road** on **Tuesday 31 March 2020**.

Investigators have reviewed available CCTV footage and are now releasing images of three men who may be able to help them with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference **46/57153/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** , or by using the anonymous online form.
