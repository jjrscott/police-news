A fourteen-year-old teenage girl from **Gravesend** who was reported missing has been safely located.

The teenager was missing from **The Sandpipers area** of the town at about **8pm on Sunday 20 December 2020** and was found safely by officers on the morning on **Monday 21 December**.

Thank you to everyone who shared our appeal.
