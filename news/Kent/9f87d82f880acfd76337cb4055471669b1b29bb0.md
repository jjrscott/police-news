CCTV images have been issued by officers investigating a theft at a **Maidstone** supermarket.

On **Thursday 17 December 2020** , Kent Police received a report that bottles of spirits had been stolen from Tesco in **Grovewood Drive North, Grove Green** , at around **12.30am**.

## CCTV Images

Officers are now able to issue CCTV images of two people who they believe could help with their enquiries.

Investigating officer, PC Saskia Jenner said: 'While the man and the woman in the photographs are wearing face masks, we are hoping that somebody recognises one or both of them and gets in contact with our appeal line.'

## Can you help?

Anyone who has information that may assist the investigation is urged to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/222262/20.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form.
