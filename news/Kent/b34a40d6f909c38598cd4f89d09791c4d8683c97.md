Richard Padun, aged 44, of Woodall Close, Chessington, was sentenced on 4 December 2020 at West Kent Magistrates' Court.

He pleaded guilty to exceeding a 50 mph speed limit as he drove through the Dartford Tunnel on 5 November 2019 and 22 January 2020.

He was disqualified from driving for 28 months' and fined £384\. He was also ordered to pay a victim surcharge of £38 and costs of £110.
