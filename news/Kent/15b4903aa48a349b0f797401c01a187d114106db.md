Kent Police has located a man who was assaulted in **Dartford** and are continuing to appeal for information from members of the public.

The incident took place at 2.07am on **Saturday 8 August 2020** and reportedly saw a man assaulted as he lay on the ground, following a disturbance in the High Street.

A suspect for the assault was located by officers in **Lowfield Street** a short time later and he has been released on bail, pending further enquiries, until **2 September**.

The victim was initially unknown to Kent Police and officers issued an appeal to locate him. They have since established he is safe, well and not seriously injured.

Anyone who can help should call Kent Police on **01474 366149** , quoting reference **46/137862/20**.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent, anonymously, on **0800 555 111**.
