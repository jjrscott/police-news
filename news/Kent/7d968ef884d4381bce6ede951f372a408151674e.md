A CCTV image of a man who may have important information on a robbery in **Strood** has been released by Kent Police.

It is reported that between **6.40pm** and **7pm** on **Saturday 7 November 2020** a member of staff at a newsagent in **Carnation Road** was threatened with a knife, before being made to hand over a quantity of cash.

Following a review of available evidence, investigators believe the man in the picture will be able to assist them with their ongoing enquiries.

Although it does not clearly show his face, officers believe those who know him would be able to identify him.

Anyone who recognises him is asked to call Kent Police on **01634 792209** quoting **46/199987/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on **0800 555 111** or complete the online form via this link.
