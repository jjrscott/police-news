A suspected drug dealer has been remanded in custody after Kent Police officers seized cocaine, heroin and cannabis in **Dartford**.

The substances are said to have been found inside a car which was stopped in **Heath Gardens** at around **10.45pm** on **Monday 30 November 2020**.

**Joshua Barzey** was arrested in connection with the seizures and is said to have refused to comply with requests from officers for him to be searched.

The 30-year-old, of no fixed address, has since been charged with possessing cocaine, heroin and cannabis with intent to supply and two counts of obstructing a constable in the execution of their duties.

He appeared at Medway Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 2 December 2020 and has been remanded in custody to appear at Woolwich Crown Court on Tuesday 5 January 2021.
