A suspected county line drug dealer has appeared at court following his arrest in **Maidstone**.

At around **12.45pm** on **Wednesday 3 February 2021** , officers from Kent Police's County Line and Gang Team stopped a man on an electric scooter in the **High Street**.

Suspecting he was involved in the supply of controlled drugs, the officers arrested him and seized a mobile phone.

## Cash seized

**Jamie Little** , of Pudding Lane, Maidstone was later charged with being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine and being concerned in the supply of heroin.

On Wednesday 8 February, Mr Little, aged 21, appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court where he was remanded in custody to appear at a hearing at Maidstone Crown Court on date to be confirmed.
