Information is sought after a French carriage clock and other items were stolen during a burglary in **Ramsgate**.

Between **6.30am and 6.30pm** on **21 December** , a property in **Borrowdale Avenue** was burgled. As well as cash, a number of distinctive belongings were reported stolen, including a HP Pavillion Notebook laptop, a 1963 men's Garrard wristwatch with a leather strap and jewelled movement, and a French brass cased carriage clock described as having a rectangular white enamel dial with black roman numerals and enclosed in its own fitted leather case.

## Can you help?

Anyone who has seen these items, or who has any information regarding the burglary, is asked to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/226016/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting the website.
