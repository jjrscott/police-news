Two men have been charged with carrying out a violent assault in **Tonbridge**.

Officers were called to **Welland Road** at **11.30am** on **Saturday 12 December 2020** , following reports of a disturbance. A man in his 20s was taken to a local hospital with head injuries.

## Arrest

**Callum Found** , aged 18, of no fixed address was arrested and later charged with wounding with intent and with possessing an offensive weapon (namely a metal wrench) in a public place.

**Harry Mansfield** , 22, also of no fixed address, was also arrested and charged with the same offences. He was further charged with threatening a person with a bladed article in a public place and with assault occasioning actual bodily harm.

Both suspects appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on Monday 14 December where they were remanded to a hearing at Maidstone Crown Court on 6 January 2021.

## Appeal

Investigators are continuing to appeal for witnesses and urge anyone with information to contact Kent Police on **01622 604100** quoting reference 12-412.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on **0800 555 111** or complete the online form.
