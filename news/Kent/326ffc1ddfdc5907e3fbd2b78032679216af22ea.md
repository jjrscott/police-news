Robbery investigators are appealing for information following a report a motorist was targeted in **Dartford**.

At around **11.30am** on **Sunday 27 December 2020** , a white Kia is reported to have stopped in **Priory Place**.

The driver is then said to have approached a grey Citroen, which had stopped behind his car, before opening a passenger door and grabbing a handbag.

The victim, a woman in her 50s, then confronted the man and regained possession of her bag following an altercation. During the altercation, she sustained an injury to her finger.

The offender is described as being black and was wearing a red jumper and black trousers at the time of the incident.

At present, no arrests or charges have been made however a forty-eight-year-old man from Northfleet, the suspect for the offence, has since attended a police station for a voluntary interview.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01474 366149** quoting **46/173244/20**.
