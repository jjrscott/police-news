An appeal has been issued by officers investigating a report of a robbery in **Hartley**.

The incident happened at around **8pm** on **Wednesday, 17 February 2021** in an alleyway leading from **Porchester Close**.

It is alleged two men assaulted a woman in her 30s as she walked into the alleyway, and stole a quantity of cash.

The men are described as being around six feet tall and wearing hooded tops with the hoods up.

Investigating officer, PC Abbie Brookes, said: 'This type of offence is not common in this area and we are appealing for witnesses who saw two men acting suspiciously in the vicinity of Porchester Close, to contact us.'

## Can you help?

Anyone who saw the incident or who has information that may assist the investigation is urged to call **01622 604100** , quoting crime reference number 46/26627/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, by calling **0800 555111** or using the anonymous online form.
