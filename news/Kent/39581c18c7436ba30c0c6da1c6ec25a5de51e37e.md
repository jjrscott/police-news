A suspected drug dealer is to appear in court after officers recovered cannabis-infused edible products in **Ramsgate**.

Officers from Thanet Community Policing Team and the PCSO Proactive Policing Team were in the **Bellevue Avenue area** of the town during the **afternoon of Tuesday 26 January 2021** , when they became aware of suspected criminal activity in the area. As part of their enquiries, packets of cannabis-infused edibles and a quantity of cash were recovered.

**Bradley Mayo** , 30, of no fixed address, has been charged with possessing cannabis and being concerned in the supply of cannabis.

He was released on bail to appear in Margate Magistrates' Court on 2 March.
