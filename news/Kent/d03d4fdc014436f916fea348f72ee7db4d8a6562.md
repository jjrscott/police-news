Detectives from the Chief Constable's Crime Squad have charged a third person following a robbery in **Rochester**.

Kent Police was called at about **9.15pm** on **4 February 2021** to **Mercury Close** after the occupants of a property reported a robbery. Items including money and computer consoles were reported to have been stolen. Officers attended but the suspects had left the scene.

On the same day of the incident officers stopped a car on the A2, near Bluewater and two people were arrested.

**Mahei Ahmad** , 29 from London was charged with robbery, possession of an offensive weapon, possession of a Class B drug, driving a vehicle with no license and no insurance.

**Mohammed Maow** , 26, also from London was charged with robbery, possession of an offensive weapon and possession of a Class B drug.

As part of the investigation on 8 February **Thami Ngqobongwana** , 28, Trafalgar Street, Gillingham, was charged with robbery. He appeared before Maidstone Crown Court on 9 February where he was remanded in custody.

All three men were remanded in custody and are due to appear at Maidstone Crown Court on 8 March.
