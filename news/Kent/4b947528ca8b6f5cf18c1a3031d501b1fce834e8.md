A suspected **Maidstone** drug dealer is to appear in court following his arrest in London.

On **Tuesday 23 February 2021** , officers from Kent Police's County Line and Gang Team and the Metropolitan Police executed a search warrant at an address in Southwark.

They seized a quantity of heroin, two mobile phones and electronic scales.

## Arrest

They also recovered a watch and some jewellery, and a man was arrested.

**Kai Lewin Cuffy** , of Bourne Walk, Southwark, Greater London, was later charged with being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine, being concerned in the supply of heroin and possession of heroin with intent to supply.

Mr Lewin Cuffy, aged 20, was remanded in custody to appear before Medway Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 24 February.
