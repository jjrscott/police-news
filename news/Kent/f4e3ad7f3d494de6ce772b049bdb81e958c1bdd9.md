A suspected arsonist has been arrested by officers investigating a series of bin fires in **Sevenoaks**.

Ten rubbish bins were reported to have been set alight in the town centre between **17 September and 26 October 2020**.

## Arrest

Following enquiries, including the examination of CCTV in the area, a 31-year-old woman from Sevenoaks was arrested on Wednesday 4 November on suspicion of arson.

She was taken in custody while the investigation continues.
