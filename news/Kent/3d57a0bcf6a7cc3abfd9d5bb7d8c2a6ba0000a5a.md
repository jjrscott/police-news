A suspected sex offender has been arrested following multiple allegations of indecent exposure in **Sevenoaks.**

It is alleged that between **Tuesday 2 February** and **Friday 5 February 2021** , a man had been seen exposing himself in the **Seal Hollow Road** area.

Police have received reports of four separate incidents over this period, during which the suspect is also said to have carried out indecent acts.

Officers are also investigating further possible offences in the area.

A 61-year-old man from Sevenoaks has since been arrested and later released, pending further enquiries.

## Report offences

Anyone who can assist the investigation and who has not yet spoken to police is asked to call the west Kent appeals line on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/18459/21.

You can also contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111, or by using the online form.
