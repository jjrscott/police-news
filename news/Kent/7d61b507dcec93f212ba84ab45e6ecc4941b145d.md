Do you recognise this man?

Detectives from Kent Police who are investigating an attempted burglary at a property in **Ashford** have released the image of a man who may be able to help them with their enquiries.

**Between 3pm and 3.20pm** on **Saturday 30 December 2019** an attempted break-in was reported at a premises in **Highfield Road**. The suspect tried to open the door but left without success. Nothing was reported stolen and nothing was damaged.

Officers making enquiries into the incident believe the man in the CCTV image may have information important to their investigation.

## Can you help?

Anyone who recognises this man or has information should call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting 46/247574/19. Alternatively contact Kent Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555111.
