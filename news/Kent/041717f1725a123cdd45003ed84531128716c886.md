Information is sought after a man was found injured **Gravesend**.

At around **11pm** on **Friday 18 December 2020** , a man in his 30s was found injured in a car park in **Overcliffe** - between **Lennox Road** and **St James ' Avenue**.

He was taken to a local hospital and disclosed that an hour before he was found, he had an altercation with a man.

As part of ongoing enquiries, a 28-year-old man from Gravesend was arrested on suspicion of assault the next day. He has since been released on bail until Tuesday 12 January 2021.

Officers are keen to hear from any witnesses who may have information but are yet to come forward.

In particular, they would like to obtain dash cam footage from vehicles that were travelling in the area between 10pm and 11pm.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01474 366149** quoting **46/223143/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on **0800 555 111** or complete the online form available via this link.
