Investigators are appealing for information after a pensioner was injured in **Gravesend**.

The victim, a man in his 70s, is said to have been assaulted by an unknown man in **Whitehill Parade** , near the junction with **Whitehill Lane** , at around **7.40pm** on **Saturday 2 January 2021**.

He sustained injuries to his face and arms which required treatment at a local hospital.

The suspect is described as being white, in his mid-20s and around 5ft 10ins tall with a stocky build. He was wearing a grey hooded jumper, with the hood up, and matching grey jogging bottoms.

He was also wearing black trainers and was last seen running towards Whitehill Lane.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01474 366149** quoting **46/1008/21**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on **0800 555 111** or complete the online form, available by clicking here.
