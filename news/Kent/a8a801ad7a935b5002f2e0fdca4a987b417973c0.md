A CCTV image has been issued by officers investigating a reported theft at a store in **Tunbridge Wells**.

Between **4.10pm** and **4.20pm** on **29 October 2020** , a group of women entered Boots the Chemist in **Calverley Road** , where it is alleged they stole a quantity of cosmetics.

Officers are now able to issue a CCTV image of three people who they believe could help with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone who recognises them or who has information that may assist the investigation, is urged to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/212822/20.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form.
