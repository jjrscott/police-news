Information is sought after rings were stolen from a woman in the **Dumpton Gap** area of **Broadstairs**.

It was reported that the victim, a woman in her forties, was walking in an alleyway between **Montefiore Avenue** and **Seacroft Road** at around **2pm** on **Thursday 3 December 2020** when the robbery took place.

The victim was pushed against a wall and threatened into handing over her jewellery to the offender, who then left the scene towards Montefiore Avenue.

The suspect is described as a white man in his thirties, with a large build and approximately 5 feet 9 inches tall. He was wearing black trainers, dark jeans, a black padded jacket and gloves and black hat. He also wore a blue surgical mask.

Anyone with information should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting **46/214786/20**.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling **0800 555 111** or using the anonymous online form available via this link.
