CCTV images have been issued by officers investigating a report of racist abuse in **Wouldham** , near Rochester.

It is alleged that between **1.30pm** and **2pm** on **Monday 18 January 2021** , a man wrote offensive language on the door of a property in the **High Street**.

The suspect had previously knocked on the door of the address and is believed to have then left the scene on an electric scooter.

Police believe the person pictured may have important information.

## Can you help?

Anyone who recognises him or can assist the investigation is asked to call **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/6370/21.

You can also contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or use the online form.
