Witnesses are sought by officers investigating a report of inappropriate behaviour in **Herne Bay**.

A teenager reported she was waiting at a bus stop outside **Herne Bay railway station** at around **10am** on **Thursday 10 December 2020** when a man walked past and touched her leg.

Nothing was said and the girl walked away without further issue.

## Can you help?

The man was described as a short, middle-aged white male, wearing dark clothing. He was reportedly walking a small dog on a lead at the time.

Anyone with information regarding the incident is asked to contact Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting 46/218594/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
