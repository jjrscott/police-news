Detectives are appealing for information following the theft of a delivery van in **Ashford**.

Kent Police was called at **5.35pm** on **Saturday 5 December 2020** following a reported robbery in **Orion Way**.

A delivery driver was reportedly hit with a metal bar by a man who then drove off in the van at around **5.30pm**.

The victim was taken to a local hospital, from where he has since been discharged.

## Can you help?

The van is a silver Vauxhall Vivaro with three distinctive blue-tinted spotlights attached to the front.

Officers are investigating and urge anyone with any information, or who has seen the van since its theft, to call Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference 46/215994/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or by using the anonymous online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
