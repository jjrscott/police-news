A suspect has been charged with multiple offences following aggressive behaviour towards a shop worker in **Dover**.

Kent Police was called on **Friday 15 January 2021** after a suspected shoplifter reportedly threatened a member of staff outside a store in **Charlton Green**.

Following an investigation by Dover's Victim Based Crime Team, **Jason Cox** was arrested on Wednesday 24 February.

## Charges

Mr Cox, of Park Street, Dover, has since been charged with assault, three counts of shoplifting in the town between Wednesday 13 January and Thursday 11 February, and possession of heroin and cannabis.

The 48-year-old is due to appear before Medway Magistrates' Court on Friday 26 February.
