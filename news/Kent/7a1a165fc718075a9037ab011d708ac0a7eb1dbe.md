Witnesses are sought after a man reported being robbed in **Gillingham**.

The incident is alleged to have happened at **12.45am on 19 July 2020** , in **Rosebery Road** at the junction with Burnt Oak Terrace.

Officers were called after a person reported being approached by a man who then threatened him with a knife. The suspect took the victim's mobile phone before leaving the area.

The offender is described as black, male, 5ft 9ins and of slim build. He was believed to be wearing a black hooded top with black jogging bottoms. He was also reported to be wearing a covering over his face.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01634 792209** quoting 46/123742/20.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or complete the online form.
