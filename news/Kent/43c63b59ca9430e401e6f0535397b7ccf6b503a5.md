Information is urgently sought to help locate a man who has been reported missing from **Lydd**.

David Hill has not been seen since he left his home to go running on the afternoon of **Wednesday 30 December 2020**.

The 52-year-old is last known to have been in the **Dennes Lane** area at around **2.10pm** on that date, and has previously completed runs across country towards **Old Romney.**

## Description

David is described as being six feet tall, of stocky build, with receding grey hair. He was last seen wearing a red Adidas coat and black Adidas tracksuit bottoms.

Anyone with information that could assist is asked to call Kent Police on **101** quoting 30-1225.
