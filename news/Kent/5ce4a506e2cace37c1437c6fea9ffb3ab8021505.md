Information is sought to help locate a woman who has been reported missing from **Gillingham**.

**Michele Talty** was last seen in the **Gillingham Park** area, on the **Park Avenue** side, at around **2.30pm** on **Friday 8 January 2021**.

The 54-year-old is described as being five feet seven inches tall with short, brown hair.

She was last seen wearing a navy puffer jacket (as shown in the picture on the top left) with dark jogging bottoms and a black woolly hat.

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **101** quoting **08-1311**.
