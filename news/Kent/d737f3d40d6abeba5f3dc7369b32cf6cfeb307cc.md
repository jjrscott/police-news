Witnesses are being urged to come forward following a series of fuel thefts from vehicles in **Thanet**.

Four thefts, and one attempted theft, were reported to Kent Police between **Friday 28 February** and **Tuesday 3 March 2020** in **Minster** , **Ramsgate** and **Manston**.

On Friday 28 February, two lorries were targeted overnight on the **A229** in **Monkton**.

A further fuel theft was reported at **6am** on Sunday 1 March at Minster Services and on Tuesday 3 March, fuel was reported stolen from two more lorries at around 2.45am on the coastbound **A229.**

## Can you help?

Anyone who witnessed the thefts or has information should call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting reference 03-0079.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or complete the online form.
