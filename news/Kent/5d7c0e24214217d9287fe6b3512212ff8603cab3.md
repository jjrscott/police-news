A CCTV image has been released by officers investigating the theft of a purse from a car in **Maidstone**.

The victim reported their vehicle had been targeted in **Park Avenue** between **10pm** on **29 June 2020** and **7.40am** the following day.

## Card used

A bank card from inside the purse was later used to make a purchase at a shop in **Bower Place.**

Officers would like to identify the person pictured in the CCTV image, who they believe may be able to assist with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01622 604100** , quoting reference 46/110784/20.

You can also contact Kent Crimestoppers anonymously on **0800 555111** , or by completing an online form.
