CCTV image

A CCTV image has been issued after a reported robbery in **Gravesend**.

At around **5.30pm on 28 July 2020** the victim reported that he had been approached in Woodlands Park, Dashwood Road by an unknown man and his electric scooter had been stolen as a result.

Officers have been conducting enquiries since the incident, including reviewing CCTV footage from the surrounding areas and are now in a position to release an image of a man they believe may help them with their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information is asked to call Kent Police on **01474 366149** quoting reference **46/131293/20**. You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111 or using the anonymous online form at Crimestoppers-uk.org
