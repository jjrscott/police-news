Crack cocaine supply charges have been authorised against two men after a quantity of the substance was reportedly seized from a **Sheerness** home.

Due to suspected drug dealing in the area, officers from the Swale Proactive Team attended an address in the **Richmond Street** area on the afternoon of **Wednesday 10 March 2021**.

Thirty-nine wraps of the class A substance were recovered.

**Ashleigh Sharpe** , 26, of Richmond Street, Sheerness, and **George Wright** , 32, of Church Road, Sittingbourne, have each been charged with possessing crack cocaine with intent to supply.

Mr Wright has also been charged with an additional count of possessing amphetamine with intent to supply in connection with a separate search of a car.

Both have been remanded in custody to attend Medway Magistrates' Court on Friday 12 March.
