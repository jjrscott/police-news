An appeal for witnesses has been made after a mobile phone was stolen during a robbery in **Margate.**

It was reported that at around **1.30pm on Saturday 1 February 2020** a boy and a girl were walking along **Marine Gardens** when a group of boys approached them.

The boy was assaulted and his phone, a gold coloured Alcatel mobile phone, was stolen.

The group were seen to walk away towards the **High Street**.

One of the suspects was described as aged 13 or 14 years old, with a medium build, and black short straight hair. He wore a black padded zip-up coat with the hood up, and black tracksuit bottoms.

A second suspect was described as being of a similar age, with a slim build and short ginger hair. He wore a grey hooded top and grey trousers.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information regarding the robbery is asked to call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting 46/20066/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
