A suspected drug dealer has appeared in court following his arrest in **Ditton**.

On **Wednesday 10 February 2021** , a search warrant issued under the Misuse of Drugs Act was executed at an address in **St Peters Road**.

Officers seized a mobile phone and arrested a man on suspicion of drug supply offences.

A rucksack containing a quantity of cannabis and electronic scales was also recovered from the rear of the premises.

## Charged

**Harry Adlington** , of St Peters Road, Ditton, was later charged with possessing cannabis with intent to supply.

He was also charged (in connection with two previous arrests) with being concerned in the supply of cocaine, being concerned in the supply of cannabis and possession of amphetamine.

Mr Adlington, aged 18, appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court on Thursday 11 February where he was remanded to a hearing at Maidstone Crown Court on 11 March.
