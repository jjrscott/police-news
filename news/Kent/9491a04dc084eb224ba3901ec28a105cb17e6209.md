Jewellery images

Diamond rings, brooches, earrings and necklaces are just some of the items reported stolen following a burglary in **Ashford**.

Detectives have released images of some of the sentimental jewellery that was taken when a property in **Egerton House Road** was burgled on **Tuesday 31 December 2019** at around **6.15pm**.

The pictures show a turquoise and rose diamond ring, a pearl and diamond heart-shaped brooch, a pearl and turquoise pendant, a set of pearl earrings and a necklace.

Thieves are believed to have broken into the house through a back window and stolen a large amount of jewellery including a Calvin Klein watch with four diamonds and an engraved gold-plated 1968 Roamer watch.

## Did you see anything?

Any witnesses to the burglary or anyone who may have been offered the items for sale should call Kent Police on 01843 222289 quoting reference 46/247704/19.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
