A woman has been arrested following a fatal collision in **Dover**.

Kent Police was called at **9.40pm** on **Thursday 17 December 2020** following a report that a man had suffered serious injuries in **Crabble Hill**.

Officers attended along with South East Coast Ambulance Service and a 59-year-old man from south-east London was pronounced deceased at the scene.

Officers from Kent Police's Serious Collision Investigation Unit (SCIU) believe a grey Ford Focus collided with the man, who was a pedestrian, in Crabble Hill at around 9.39pm.

The vehicle was not at the scene when police arrived.

Enquiries have led to the arrest of a 50-year-old woman from Folkestone who remains in police custody.

## Witnesses

Anyone who witnessed the incident, has information, CCTV or dashcam footage which may assist enquiries, is urged to contact SCIU on **01622 798538** or email sciu.td@kent.police.uk quoting reference SN/CF/112/20.
