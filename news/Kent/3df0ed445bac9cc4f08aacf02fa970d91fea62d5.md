An arrest has been made after a man sustained injuries following an assault in **Broadstairs.**

Kent Police was called to **Pierremont Park** following the incident shortly after **11am on Saturday 13 March 2021**.

It was reported that the victim, a man in his 20s, was assaulted following an altercation with another man. He suffered an arm injury and was taken to a local hospital for treatment.

A 21-year-old man from Margate was arrested on suspicion of assault. He has since been released on police bail until 31 March 2021.

## Can you help?

Detectives would like to hear from anyone who saw or heard anything during the incident and has not yet spoken to an officer.

Witnesses should call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/40921/21.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111 or using the anonymous online form.
