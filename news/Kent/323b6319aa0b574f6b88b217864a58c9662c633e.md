Assault charges have been authorised against a man from Sittingbourne.

**Rhys Seager** has been charged with two counts of assaulting an emergency worker, criminal damage, driving without insurance and failing to provide a specimen for analysis.

The charges were authorised following his arrest in **High Halstow, Rochester,** on **17 October 2020** after officers received a report of a gate to farmland being damaged.

The 27-year-old of Church Road, Sittingbourne, has additionally been charged with actual bodily harm relating to an incident in Sheerness on 4 October.

He appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court by video link on 19 October and has been remanded in custody to appear at Maidstone Crown Court on 16 November.
