Officers are appealing for information following a reported burglary at a shop in **Chatham**.

Kent Police was called on the morning of **Sunday 13 December 2020** after a business in **Military Road** was burgled.

The incident is thought to have taken place between **12.45am** and **1am** on **Sunday 13 December**.

## Can you help?

A number of items were stolen and an investigation into the circumstances is ongoing.

Anyone with any information, or with CCTV footage which may assist enquiries, is urged to call Kent Police on **01634 792209** , quoting reference 46/219947/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111 or complete the online form at crimestoppers-uk.org
