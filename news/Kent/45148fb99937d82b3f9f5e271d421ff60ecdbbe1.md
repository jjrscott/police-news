This CCTV image has been released by officers in Ashford

A CCTV image has been released by officers investigating a reported failure to pay for a hotel.

On **Friday 27 November 2020** , a guest left a hotel on the **A20** near **Ashford** without paying his bill.

Officers are investigating and are now releasing a CCTV image of man who may be able to assist their enquiries.

## Can you help?

Anyone with any information, or who recognises the man, should contact Kent Police on **01843 222289** , quoting reference 46/211403/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers in Kent anonymously on 0800 555 111, or by visiting crimestoppers-uk.org
