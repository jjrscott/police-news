A teenager has been charged with a shop robbery in **Strood**.

The 14-year-old boy has been charged with one count of robbery and one count of possession of a knife in a public place.

The charges were authorised by the Crown Prosecution Service after a member of staff at a newsagent in **Carnation Road** reported being threatened with a knife, before being made to hand over a quantity of cash.

The incident was reported to have happened between **6.40pm** and **7pm** on **7 November 2020** and following a review of evidence Kent Police issued a CCTV appeal in connection with the incident.

On 1 February 2021 the teenage boy from Rochester was arrested before being charged.

He appeared before Medway Magistrates' Court, sitting as a youth court, on 2 February where he was bailed to appear before Medway Youth Court on 8 March.
