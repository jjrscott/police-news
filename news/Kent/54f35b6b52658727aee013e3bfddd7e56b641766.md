Two arrests have been made by officers investigating an assault in **Charing, Ashford.**

Kent Police was called at **6.49pm** on **Sunday 9 August 2020** to a disturbance in a field near **Downsway**.

Three people were reported to have been injured following an altercation and two were taken to a local hospital for treatment.

Following enquiries, a 20-year-old man from Lydd was arrested on Wednesday 12 August on suspicion of assault and malicious communications. A 45-year-old man from Lydd was also arrested on suspicion of assault.

They were both released on bail until 3 September while the investigation continues.

## Can you help?

Anyone with information on the incident can call Kent Police on **01843 222289** quoting 46/138985/20.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously by calling 0800 555 111, or using the anonymous online form.
