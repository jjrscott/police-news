**We are appealing for information following an assault on a woman in the ASDA store in Dunstable, at around 10.25am on Sunday, 21 January.**

The victim was shopping at the store on Court Drive when she had an argument with another woman. Following an altercation, the offender pushed her trolley hard at the victim, resulting in her suffering a broken arm and requiring hospital treatment.

Anyone with information is asked to call us on 101 quoting reference number JD/03543/2018. You can also use the online reporting centre.

Alternatively, people can call Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
