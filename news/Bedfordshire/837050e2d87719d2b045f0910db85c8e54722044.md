**We are appealing for witnesses, after receiving reports of a man indecently exposing himself in Marston Moretaine last week.**

At around 9.50am on Monday, 16 July, reports were received of a man indecently exposing himself whilst sitting on a bench in the Marston Vale Millennium Country Park, near Station Road.

The offender is believed to be aged between 25 and 30, with short brown hair and is thought to have been wearing a black polo shirt and black trousers.

Anyone who believes they have seen a man matching the description, or has any information, is asked to use the online reporting tool on the force's website or call 101, quoting crime reference number 40/13651/18.
