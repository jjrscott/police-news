### A 74-year-old man from Luton has pleaded guilty to four counts of exposure, following a number of incidents in the Wigmore Park area of Luton at the end of July.

Badder Loy, 74, of Sowerby Avenue, Luton, appeared at Luton Crown Court on Tuesday (4 August), in connection with the incidents in the Wigmore Park area of Luton between 28 July and 3 August.

Loy has been bailed ahead of his sentencing on 1 September.

Police Constable James Cremore from Bedfordshire Police said: "I'm pleased that Loy has chosen to plead guilty, saving the victims the additional angst of a trial.

"These incidents cause great distress and anxiety, and I hope this case shows that we take these types of crimes very seriously, and will continue to protect our community from these offenders."

Anyone who may have witnessed a similar incident in the Wigmore Park area is asked to call police on 101 or report via our website.
