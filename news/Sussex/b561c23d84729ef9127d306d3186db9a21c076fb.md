A man has been arrested after a vehicle failed to stop for police near Bexhill.

Officers were made aware of a car travelling at excess speed around 7.15am on Friday (November 20).

Police quickly carried out numerous enquiries to trace the vehicle's movements and as a result, it was sighted again around 8.20am.

A short pursuit occurred before police made tactical contact with the car to bring it to a safe stop on Combe Valley Way. No injuries have been reported.

A man was arrested on suspicion of failing to stop for police, dangerous driving and theft of a motor vehicle. He remains in police custody at this time.

A section of the road was closed while the vehicles were recovered.
