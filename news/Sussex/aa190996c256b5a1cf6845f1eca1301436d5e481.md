A man has been charged with causing public nuisance following a police investigation into calls made to a public phone box in Littlehampton.

Mark Furnevel, 51 unemployed, of Oyster Quay, Shoreham, will appear in custody at Brighton Magistrates Court on Saturday 15 August charged with causing a public nuisance by making persistent calls to a phone box in Littlehampton High Street.

If you can help or have any information which could assist with the investigation, please report online or ring 101 quoting Operation Entire.
