Do you recognise this man?

Police would like to speak to him in relation to a robbery at the Co-op store in Framfield Way, Eastbourne.

A man was reported to have entered the store with a metal object around 9.30pm on Tuesday (February 23) demanding money from the till. A quantity of cash was handed over. No one was injured but staff were left shaken by the incident.

The man was wearing a distinctive purple baseball cap with the word 'Boston' on the front, and had a blue scarf covering his face.

Anyone who recognises him, or who has any other information about the robbery, is asked to contact police online or by calling 101 quoting serial 1318 of 23/02. Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
