Officers investigating a robbery at Tesco Express in Seaside Road, Eastbourne, on Saturday night (20 February) have released CCTV images of three individuals they would like to identify.

Cigarettes and alcohol were stolen in the robbery, which took place at approximately 9.55pm. Police were alerted when staff activated a security alarm.

Police would like to identify three men captured in the shop's CCTV images at that time. The individuals are described as over 5'9" in height, of medium build and possibly of mixed-race _._ They were wearing dark-coloured clothing and balaclavas.

If you recognise the men or have any relevant information or dash cam or CCTV footage, please contact police by reporting online or by calling 101 quoting serial 137 of 20/02.
