Have you seen missing 16-year-old Darcie Goobie from Bexhill?

She was last seen on Tuesday afternoon (9 March) leaving her home to go to the shops but did not return. She is believed to have travelled to London and could now be in Birmingham.

Darcie is 5'4', of mixed race with shoulder length brown hair and was last seen wearing a black puffa coat, blue jeans, Disney T-shirt and black Nike trainers.

We are concerned for her welfare so if you see her please call 999 or call 101 quoting serial 1085 of 09/03 if you have information about her whereabouts.
