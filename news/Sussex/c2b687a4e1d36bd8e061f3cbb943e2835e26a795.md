Sussex Police is appealing for witnesses to a burglary at a college in Burgess Hill.

Between 2am and 3am on Wednesday (17 March) St. Paul's Catholic College in Jane Murray's Way a man broke into the building and stole a quantity of cash.

Police are investigating lines of enquiry and would like to speak to the man in the CCTV footage in connection with the incident.

If you witnessed any suspicious behaviour or recognise the man in the image please make contact with Sussex Police either online or call 101 quoting serial 170 of 17/03.
