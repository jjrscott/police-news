Do you recognise this man?

Police would like to speak to him about the theft of a bicycle in Horsham.

The custom-built red Norco 4 suspension 29 inch bike was locked up to a bike rack outside PHD Barbers in Bishopric when it was stolen around 5.10pm on October 19.

Officers believe the man pictured could help with their enquiries. Anyone who recognises him, or who has any other information about the theft, is asked to contact police online or by calling 101 quoting serial 1433 of 20/10.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
