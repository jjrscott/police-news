A man has been identified following a police appeal published on social media yesterday (Tuesday 29 September).

The man was sought in connection with an incident at Pets at Home, in Lottbridge Drove, Eastbourne, on 28 August.

Enquiries are ongoing, and we would urge the public not to speculate as our investigation continues.
