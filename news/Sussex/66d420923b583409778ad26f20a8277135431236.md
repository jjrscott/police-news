Police investigating a robbery at the Coral betting shop in Sterling Parade, Rustington, on Tuesday (17 December) have charged Graham Baker, 44, unemployed, of Shadwells Road, Lancing, with the offence.

On Thursday (19 December) he appeared before Crawley magistrates and was remanded in custody to appear at Portsmouth Crown Court on Friday 17 January, 2020.

A second man thought to have been involved, aged 43, from Sompting, has been arrested on suspicion of robbery and released under investigation while enquiries continue.
