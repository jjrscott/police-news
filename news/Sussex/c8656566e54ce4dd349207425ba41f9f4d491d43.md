Police are appealing for witnesses after a car was damaged in Eastbourne.

Officers were called on Sunday (July 19) after the owner of a blue Kia Picanto noticed damage to the vehicle while it was parked in Borough Lane.

The damage - a crack on the right hand side of the windscreen - happened between 9am and 10am that day.

Anyone who saw what happened, or has any other information relating to the matter, is asked to contact police online or by calling 101 quoting serial 1376 of 19/07.
