Police investigating a collision in Birdham are trying to trace witnesses from the scene.

Officers were called to the junction of the A286 and A259 Sidlesham Lane around 8.45pm on Tuesday 14 April to reports of a collision between a black Peugeot and a black Hyundai.

A number of people are known to have stopped and helped at the scene, and police are urging them to get in touch to help with the investigation.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police online or by calling 101 quoting serial 1366 of 23/04.
