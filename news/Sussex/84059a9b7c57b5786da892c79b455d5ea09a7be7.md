Police investigating a road traffic collision involving a car and a pedestrian in Worthing are appealing for information.

Officers were called to Sompting Avenue prior to the junction with Beaumont Road at 7.10am on Tuesday (2 February).

The pedestrian - a 60-year-old man - suffered serious but not life-threatening injuries and was taken to hospital for treatment.

Police are seeking information about the injured man, who did not have identification on him, and is described as being white with grey hair.

Anyone who saw either party prior to the collision, or who witnessed what happened or who may have relevant dash-cam footage is urged to come forwards.

Information can be reported online or by calling 101, quoting 168 of 02/02.
