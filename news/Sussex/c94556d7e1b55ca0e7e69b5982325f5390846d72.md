Sussex Police are seeking the identity of the woman in the photograph, who they would like to speak to in connection with shoplifting.

Police received a report on Tuesday, 4 August, regarding the theft of a large quantity of meat from from Budgens in Orion Parade, Hassocks, around 2.45pm on Friday, 31 July.

The woman is described as white, in her mid-30s, with long black hair and she was wearing a black and red Adidas t-shirt and black shorts.

If anyone recognises the woman or has information to assist our investigation please report online or call 101 quoting serial 1569 of 04/08.
