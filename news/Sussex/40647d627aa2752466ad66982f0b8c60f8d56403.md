Police are concerned for the welfare of missing Hove man, Andrew Stell.

Andrew, 56, was reported missing on Friday (4 December) and is likely to be in the Brighton and Hove area.

He is described as 6' 5" tall and of heavy build, with mousey brown, short hair and blue eyes.

The photos were taken some time ago and Andrew may look slightly fuller in the face now.

Anyone with information on his whereabouts is asked to to report online or

call 101, quoting serial number 1057 of 6/12.
