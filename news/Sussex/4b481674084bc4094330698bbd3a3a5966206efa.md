Police are investigating a sexual assault in Horsham.

A woman was running along the Downs Link, Southwater, at the junction of Marlpost and Two Mile Ash Road, around 7.20am on Tuesday (27 October) when she was pushed to the ground by a man. The man ran off when a member of the public walked past.

The victim has been taken to hospital and she is being supported by specialist officers.

The suspect was riding a bicycle and was wearing brown trousers and a distinctive red tartan padded jacket.

If you witnessed the assault, recognise the description of the suspect or have information to assist the police investigation please report online or call 101 quoting reference Operation Morning.
