Matthew Day, 30, who had been reported missing from his home in Cuckfield, has sadly been found dead.

Matthew was last seen by his family on Tuesday, 22 December, and his body was found at Beachy Head near Eastbourne on Thursday afternoon, 7 January.

The death is not being treated as suspicious and the Coroners Officer is continuing enquiries.
