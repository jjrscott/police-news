Police have issued a renewed appeal for information to a fatal collision near Arundel.

At 7.14am on Tuesday 11 August, police were called to a collision on the A29 Fairmile Bottom involving a Suzuki motorcycle and a Ford Tipper van, both travelling north.

The rider of the motorcyclist was sadly pronounced dead at the scene. He has been identified as 60-year-old Allan Young from Chichester.

Officers investigating the collision would still like to hear from anyone who witnessed the collision but has yet to come forward. In particular, they would like to hear from people who may have captured the collision or the events leading up to it on their dash cam.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact police by calling 101 or emailing collision.appeal@sussex.pnn.police.uk quoting Operation Oakhaven.
