Updated News Post

A man has been charged in connection with a Selsey burglary.

Benjamin Hunter, 28, of no fixed address was charged with burglary other than dwelling and will appear at Worthing Magistrates' Court on November 12.

Police received a report of a window being smashed and jewellery being stolen in High Street, Selsey, around 10.15am on Wednesday (October 16).

Officers, assisted by the police helicopter, conducted an area search and detained a man matching the same description nearby.
