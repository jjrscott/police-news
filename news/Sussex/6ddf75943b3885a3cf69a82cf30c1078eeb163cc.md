A man has been arrested and charged with two counts of rape and a sexual assault in Brighton.

Sam Kesete, 21, of Canterbury Drive, Brighton, appeared at Brighton Magistrates' Court on Monday (14 December).

He is charged with two counts of rape against a 21 year-old woman at an address in Canterbury Drive near The Level on 28 November and sexual assaulting an 18-year-old woman by touching at Kings Road Arches on 18 September.

Kesete was remanded in custody and will appear at Lewes Crown Court on 11 January.
