Fiona McKay, 51, who was reported missing from Eastbourne on 2 January, has sadly been found deceased.

Fiona was located on Tuesday (12 January) at an address in Eastbourne and next of kin have been informed.

The death is not being treated as suspicious and the Coroners Officer is continuing enquiries.
