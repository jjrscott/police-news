Police investigating a burglary in Brighton have arrested a suspect.

Officers were called to Montpelier Street at 6.15pm on Tuesday (16 February) to a report that a man had entered a basement floor property.

Fast-time enquiries were carried out by Response teams and a suspect was swiftly identified.

A 56-year-old local man was arrested on suspicion of burglary and remains in custody for questioning.

Police urge anyone with information, or who has witnessed any suspicious behaviour in the area, to report it online or by calling 101, quoting 1014 of 16/02.
