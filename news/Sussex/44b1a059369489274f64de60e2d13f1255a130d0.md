Police are appealing to locate Matthew Day, 30, who has been reported missing from his home in Cuckfield.

Matthew was last seen by his family on Tuesday, 22 December, and officers are concerned for his welfare.

He is described as a white male, 6', of a skinny build with a shaved head and dark brown beard.

Anyone who sees him is asked to report online or call 101, quoting 428 of 30/12.
