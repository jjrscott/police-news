Police are investigating the circumstances surrounding a fight in Hailsham town centre.

Officers became aware of the altercation in High Street around 8.40pm on Thursday (July 30).

Police attended the scene but the group dispersed. Enquiries are now ongoing to establish what happened and to identify those involved.

Anyone with any information about the fight, or anyone who may have any CCTV or dash cam footage from the area at the time, is asked to contact police online or contact 101 quoting serial 1148 of 30/07.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
