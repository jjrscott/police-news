Police have now identified a driver who was sought after a collision in Seaford.

Officers were called to the A259 East Dean Road shortly after 9am on Tuesday (June 23) to reports of a collision involving three vehicles.

An appeal was issued to trace one of the drivers who did stop at the scene, but left before police arrived. The driver has now been identified.

Anyone who has any information about the collision is asked to get in touch with police, either online or by calling 101 quoting serial 327 of 23/06.
