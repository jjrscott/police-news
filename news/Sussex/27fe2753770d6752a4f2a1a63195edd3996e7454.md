Police are concerned for Eric Wakanena, 16, who has been reported missing from South Chailey.

He was last seen about 10.45am on Tuesday, 5 January and police are concerned for his welfare.

Eric has links to Newhaven, Staines, Stanwell, Feltham and Watford

He is described as a mixed race male, 6', of a medium build, with short brown hair, brown eyes. He was wearing a wet look Michael Kors jacket, navy tracksuit bottoms and bright blue Nike trainers.

Anyone who sees him or who has information on his whereabouts is asked to report online or call 101, quoting 387 of 05/01.
