Police have made two arrests in connection with the theft and fraudulent usage of bank cards in Bognor Regis.

Officers are investigating after a purse was reported stolen and several bank cards from it were used in supermarkets and service stations in the Shripney area on 6 January.

An appeal was issued to obtain any further details in relation to the incident, and as a result of information received from the public, officers made the two arrests on Friday 28 January in Bognor town centre.

Two men - aged 37 and 41 - were arrested on suspicion of theft from a person. They have been released under investigation.

Anyone who has any information is asked to contact police by reporting online or calling 101 quoting serial 0787 of 06/01.
