Police are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Hastings.

Officers received report of a man being hit with a cricket bat during an altercation in Bohemia Road at 2.30pm on Tuesday (13 October).

The victim - a 35-year-old man - suffered an injury which required hospital treatment.

A 59-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of wounding with intent and has been bailed while enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone with information can report online or call 101 quoting 738 of 13/10.
