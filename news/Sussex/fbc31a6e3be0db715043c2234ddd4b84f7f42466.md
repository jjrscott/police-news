A man has been arrested on suspicion of affray after a disturbance in Crowborough on Thursday afternoon (August 13).

Police were called around 2.50pm to reports of a man with a knife threatening others inside a property on Crowborough Hill.

Officers attended and arrested a man in nearby Burdett Road. No one was injured, and police are not looking for anyone else in relation to the matter.
