Police have released new images of a man they would like to speak to in relation to a sexual assault in Worthing town centre.

The victim reported being assaulted in Warwick Street and Montague Street between 00:12am and 1am on Tuesday 28 July.

Officers investigating the matter are keen to speak to anyone who witnessed either assault, or who saw any suspicious behaviour in the area at the time.

They are particularly keen to speak to the man pictured, as they believe he can help with their enquiries.

He is described as black, in his late 20s, between 5' 2" and 5' 6" tall, of skinny build and with dark hair.

Anyone who recognises him, or who has any other information relating to the incident, is asked to contact police online or by calling 101 quoting serial 59 of 28/07.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
