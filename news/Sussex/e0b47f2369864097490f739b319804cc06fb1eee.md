A man has appeared in court charged with burglary in Brighton.

Abubaker Deghayes, 52, an estate manager, of Arundel Drive East, Saltdean, Brighton, has been charged with two offences of burglary at a premises in Dyke Road, Brighton, on Tuesday 24 November.

He appeared in custody at Brighton Magistrates Court on Wednesday 25 November and the case was adjourned for a further hearing at the same court on 28 April 2021. Deghayes was granted court bail.
