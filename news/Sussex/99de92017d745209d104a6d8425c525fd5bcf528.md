Police in West Sussex are searching for Stephen Maurice Best, 32, who they want to interview about an attack on a woman in Worthing.

The offence was reported to have happened in Worthing on 23 March.

Best is described as white, 5'9", with short brown hair and blue eyes. He has a tattoo of a bulldog on his right arm.

He is known to frequent the Worthing, Lancing and Pulborough areas of West Sussex as well at the Coddenham area of Suffolk.

Detective Constable Emily Turner of the West Sussex Safeguarding Investigations Unit said; "We want to interview Best about an offence of causing actual bodily harm. If you have seen him or have any other information his whereabouts, please contact Sussex Police on 101, or 999 if he is present, quoting serial 769 of 23/03."

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

The woman and Best are known to each other.
