Police investigating a burglary in Barcombe are appealing for witnesses to come forward.

An outbuilding belonging to a property in Spithurst Road was broken into on Friday (19 March) and tools including a lawnmower and a strimmer were reported to have been taken.

The incident is believed to have taken place between 9.15am and 12.15pm.

A white Ford Transit was reported to have been seen acting suspiciously within the area.

Police are keen to speak with anyone who witnessed what happened, or knows further details about the vehicle seen to be having suspiciously.

Anyone with information can report online or call 101, quoting 524 of 19/03.
