Police are investigating an aggravated burglary in Littlehampton.

Shortly after 7.30pm on Thursday 11 February, officers responded to a report of a disturbance at a house in Southfields Road.

Two men entered the premises, assaulted a male occupant - who received treatment for head and foot injuries in hospital - and threatened others.

One suspect is described as white British, over 6', of skinny build and wearing a dark hoody. He was carrying a metal pole.

The second suspect is described as white British, about 5'10", with fair, curly hair and wielding a knife. Both were wearing snoods.

A Volkswagen Passat car may have been involved which the attackers left the area in.

The investigation is ongoing and the motivation for this incident is currently unknown.

Anyone with any information is encouraged to report it online or call 101, quoting serial 1131 of 11/02.

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or report it online.
