Five people have been charged with drug offences following simultaneous warrants in Bognor Regis and London.

Officers from Sussex Police, assisted by officers from the Metropolitan Police, conducted two simultaneous drug warrants on Friday 28 August at a property in Berrymill Close, Bognor Regis and the second in Pitman Gardens, Ilford.

A total of five people were arrested and a large quantity of class A drugs and cash were seized from both locations.

The five arrested were later charged with being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine and concerned in the supply of heroin. The five are:

 * Liban Abdulle, 29, of Pittman Gardens, Ilford.
 * Igors Gogu, 36, of Ellasdale Road, Bognor Regis.
 * Abubakar Sahal, 29, of Leytonstone Road, Stratford.
 * Adam Braggs, 32, of Berrymill Close, Bognor Regis.
 * Holly Gittings, 22, of Berrymill Close, Bognor Regis.

All five, apart from Braggs, were remanded in custody and they will all next appear at court on 28 September.

To report drug issues in your community please go online or call 101.
