Sussex Police are appealing for witnesses to a fatal collision in Rudgwick to come forward.

Around 10.45am on Wednesday (22 July) officers responded to reports of a road traffic collision involving a cyclist and a Renault HGV tipper lorry on the A281 Guildford Road, Rudgwick.

The cyclist, a man in his 20s from Morden in Surrey, was pronounced dead at the scene. Next of kin have been informed.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision or who has dash cam footage to make contact with us. You can report information at collision.appeal@sussex.pnn.police.uk or by calling 101 quoting Operation Oakford.
