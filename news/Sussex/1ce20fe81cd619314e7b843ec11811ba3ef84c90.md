Police are appealing for witnesses following a robbery in St Leonards.

Officers received report that a man and a woman had their phones stolen in a pathway off Bexhill Road, St Leonards at 7.45pm on 12 August.

The suspect is described as a black man, about 6', wearing an Adidas tracksuit which is black with bright coloured design on and black Nike Huaraches.

Police are appealing for witnesses to come forwards and are particularly keen to speak to a cyclist who may have seen what happened.

Anyone with information can report online or call 101, quoting 1463 of 12/08.
