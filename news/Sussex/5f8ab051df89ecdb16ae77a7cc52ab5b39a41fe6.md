Sussex Police is appealing for witnesses to a fatal collision in Groombridge.

At 9.54am on Monday (29 March) officers attended the B2188 Cherry Gardens Hill following a collision with a cyclist and a silver Iveco Daily Tipper vehicle.

The cyclist, a woman in her 30s, sadly died at the scene as a result of her injuries. Next of kin have been informed.

Police are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision or has any dashcam footage to make contact with them via e-mail or call 101 quoting Operation Stereo.
