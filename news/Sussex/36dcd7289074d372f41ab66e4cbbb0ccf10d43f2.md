Police are searching for Shouidur Rahman, 33, who is wanted for failing to appear in court.

Rahman was charged with one count of criminal damage after a car was keyed in Portslade.

He failed to appear at Brighton Magistrates' Court on 27 November and a warrant of arrest was issued by the court for failing to appear.

He is described as an Asian man of a slim build, with brown eyes and long dark hair.

It is thought he remains in the local area.

Anyone who sees him or who has information on his whereabouts is asked to report online or call 101, quoting 47200152241.
