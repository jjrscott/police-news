Police are searching for a man who threatened a counter assistant and stole cigarettes from a shop in Bexhill.

At 9.50pm on Thursday 4 March the man entered the One-Stop shop in Turkey Road, Bexhill, and stole a small quantity of cigarettes.

He is described as white, 6', of medium build, with light brown hair which was long, wearing a jacket with a hood which was turned inside out, with a cammo top underneath and wearing a face mask.

Detective Constable Nick Arthur said; "Do you recognise this man, who put the assistant in fear by his aggression and threats. If you were in the area at the time or if you can help in any way, please contact us either online or by calling 101, quoting serial 767 of 04/03."

You can also contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
