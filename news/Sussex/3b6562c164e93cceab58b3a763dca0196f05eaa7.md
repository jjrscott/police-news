A man has been arrested on suspicion of criminal damage after the window of a police car was smashed in Eastbourne. The incident happened at about 5.35pm on Monday (22 March).

Witnesses reported seeing a man throw a rock at the vehicle, which was parked and unattended outside Grove Road Police Hub. Nobody was injured.

A 34-year-old man from Eastbourne was arrested on suspicion of criminal damage and has been released under investigation.
