Updated News Post

A man has died in a serious collision near Slinfold, Horsham.

At 8.20am on Wednesday (27 January) police were called to a two vehicle collision on the A264 Five Oaks Road in Itchingfield involving a silver Ford Focus and a black Mitsubishi L200.

The driver of the Ford Focus, a 37-year-old man from Horsham, sadly died at the scene. Next of kin have been informed.

The driver of the Mitsubishi, a 39-year-old man, suffered minor injuries and was taken to hospital by ambulance.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or either vehicles before the collision, or anyone who has any dashcam footage are asked to contact Sussex Police by emailing collision.appeal@sussex.pnn.police.uk quoting Operation Poet.
