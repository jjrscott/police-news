Police investigating a report of the theft of a motocross in Goring are appealing for witnesses to come forward.

At 5.30am on Thursday (14 January), a suspect was seen on CCTV outside a property in Sunny Close. About 6am, a suspect is seen to take the vehicle away.

A distinctive helmet was also taken from the victim's garage.

Police are appealing to to anyone who knows who the suspect may be to come forwards and report it.

Anyone with information is asked to report online or call 101, quoting 157 of 14/01.
