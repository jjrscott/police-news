A body found on the beach off George V Avenue in Worthing on Thursday morning (December 17) has sadly been identified as that of missing man Gerald Thompson.

Gerald, 67, had been reported missing from his home in Broadwater, Worthing, on Monday (December 7) having last been seen around 10pm the evening before.
