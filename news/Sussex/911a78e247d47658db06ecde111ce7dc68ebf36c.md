Sussex Police have arrested three men in connection with a Bognor Regis stabbing.

At 6.32pm on Tuesday (25 August) officers received a report of a man in his late 20s in Ockley Road, with a stab wound to the head. The victim was taken, by land, to hospital for treatment.

Officers, including firearms officers, conducted an area search and swiftly arrested two men in Linden Road and a third man in Richmond Avenue.

The three men, two 45 and one 58-year-old, have been arrested and remain in custody while enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone who witnessed the assault or has information to assist the police investigation should report it online or call 101 quoting reference 1156 of 25/08.
