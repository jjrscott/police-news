Sussex Police is seeking the rightful owners of stolen jewellery seized in a warrant.

Officers attended Pelham Place, Crawley on Wednesday 11 November following reports of a bicycle being stolen.

A warrant was then conducted at an address in Boswell Road, Crawley and officers discovered a number of stolen items, including 82 pieces of jewellery.

Police would like to hear

from anyone who has recently had items of jewellery stolen from them or anyone who recognises any of the items to make contact with them.

If you can help Sussex Police reunite the items with their owners' please report information online or call 101 quoting reference 1316 of 11/11.

A 34-year-old man from Crawley was arrested on 11 November on suspicion of handling stolen goods, four counts of burglary dwelling with intent to steal and one count of possession of cannabis. He was released on police bail until 10 December.
