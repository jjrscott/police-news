A body has been found believed to be that of missing Tom Jennings.

Just before 12.50pm on Tuesday (9 March) officers responded to reports of a body being found outside a basement flat in Norfolk Square, Brighton.

Chief Inspector Nick Dias said: "Sadly a body has been found believed to be that of missing Tom Jennings who was also known as Tom Wright. Our thoughts are with his family at this difficult time."

The family of Tom said: "We are absolutely devastated by the outcome. We would like to thank everyone who has helped us search for our loving son."

Police continue to investigate the circumstances surrounding the death and urge anyone with information to contact us online or call 101 quoting serial 262 of 05/03.
