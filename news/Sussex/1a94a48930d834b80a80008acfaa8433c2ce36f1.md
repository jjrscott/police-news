Three motorcyclists have been charged with dangerous driving following a collision in Chilgrove.

The three riders, all off-duty police officers, were travelling south towards Chichester at 1.20pm on Saturday, 11 July. Two of the motorcyclists suffered multiple injuries when they were in collision with a red Ford Fiesta. The driver of the red Ford Fiesta involved, an 80-year-old man from Chichester and his female passenger sustained minor injuries.

PC James Latter, 30, based at Lewes; PC Ahmet Yapicioz, 53, based at Gatwick and PC Alan Harris, 30, based at Lewes have been summonsed to court to face the charge of dangerous driving. Yapicioz and Harris were also summonsed with driving at a speed exceeding 60 miles an hour.

The three will appear at Worthing Magistrates' court on 9 February.
