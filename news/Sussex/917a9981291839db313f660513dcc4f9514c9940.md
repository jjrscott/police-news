Police have confirmed a man's body was sadly recovered following a fire at a storage facility in Ferry Road, Littlehampton, yesterday afternoon (Thursday 22 October).

His next of kin have been informed, and our thoughts are with them at this difficult time.

The death is being treated as unexplained at this stage, and we're urging anyone with any information to report it online or call 101, quoting serial 535 of 22/10.
