Kamil Kaminski has been arrested and charged by police in Brighton.

The 22-year-old man was was arrested by police in Newhaven on Thursday evening (September 10) and was charged on Friday afternoon (September 11) with assault occasioning actual bodily harm in connection with an alleged assault at Co-op in St James's Street, Brighton, on July 9.

He has been remanded in custody to appear before Brighton Magistrates' Court on Saturday (September 12).
