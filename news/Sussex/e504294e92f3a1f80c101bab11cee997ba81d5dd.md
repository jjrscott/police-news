Police were called to Downland Drive, Crawley, shortly after 5.30pm on Monday (November 30) to reports of a young man being forced into a dark coloured car outside of the Tesco Express store.

Officers are conducting an area search and carrying out fast-time enquiries to try and establish the full circumstances of the matter.

Anyone who saw what happened or who has any information is asked to contact police online or by calling 101 quoting serial 967 of 30/11.

Alternatively, you can contact the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
