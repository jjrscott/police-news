Police are looking for witnesses to a collision which occurred on Wednesday (23 December) near Fontwell which left the driver with serious injuries.

At 5.54am a Blue Volkswagon Polo travelling west on the A27 West of Fontwell left the road and collided with a tree. The driver suffered leg injuries and was taken to Royal Sussex County Hospital, Brighton, for treatment.

PC Andy Green, Sussex Police Roads Policing Unit, said: "We're really keen to speak to anyone who may have seen what happened in the lead up to this incident.

"If you have any information which you think may help us, please contact us online at https://www.sussex.police.uk/contact/af/contact-us/us/contact-us/provide-more-information-to-be-added-to-a-crime-report/ or by phoning 101 quoting CAD 153 of 23/12."
