Police are appealing for witnesses following a report of an explosion at a public toilet in Bognor Regis.

Officers were called to Hotham Park, Upper Bognor Road at 5pm on Tuesday (20 October).

The doors to the building were blown off and smoke damage was found inside.

No serious injuries have been reported.

Two men - aged 37 and 23 - were arrested at the scene on suspicion of arson and have been taken into custody for questioning.

The cause of the fire is currently under investigation.

Police are keen to speak to anyone who was in the area at the time or who saw anything suspicious to report online or call 101, quoting 1021 of 20/10.
