Police are appealing for witnesses after a pedestrian was seriously injured in a crash in West Wittering.

The incident involving a silver Ford Fiesta occurred on the B2179, close to the junction with Itchenor Road, about 6.19am on Monday 8 February.

The pedestrian - a local man aged 56 - was taken to hospital with head injuries, where he remains at this stage. No other injuries were reported.

Anyone with any information about the collision - including anyone with any dash cam or CCTV footage which may have captured what happened - is urged to email collision.appeal@sussex.pnn.police.uk quoting serial 108 of 08/02.
