Officers have identified a girl they wished to speak to about a trespassing incident at Sheffield Park Gardens in Uckfield.

Police issued an appeal for information after reports of people trespassing in the park on September 12.

The girl was identified and assisted police with their enquiries, and no further action will be taken.
