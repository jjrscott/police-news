James Robus, 31, of All Saints Crescent, Hastings, has been found not guilty of three charges in relation to an assault on a woman on the West Hill in Hastings.

He had been charged with attempted robbery, wounding with intent to do grievous bodily harm, and wounding / inflicting grievous bodily harm without intent, but was acquitted of all three.

A jury returned its verdict at Lewes Crown Court on Friday (December 11) following a week-long trial.

The charges related to an assault around 9.30pm on December 27, 2019, in which a woman suffered serious facial injuries.
