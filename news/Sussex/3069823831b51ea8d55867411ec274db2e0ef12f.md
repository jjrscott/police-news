Police are seeking witnesses to a robbery in Crawley.

At about 7.15pm on Sunday 7 February, a 65-year old man was outside the Co-op Texaco garage in Balcombe Road, Crawley, when he was pushed to the ground and had his wallet taken from his back pocket.

There were three suspects, described as being younger white males in dark clothing.

They ran away towards Turners Hill Road, discarding the wallet from which they took some cash.

The victim sustained cuts and bruises.

If you saw what happened, please contact Sussex Police either online or by calling 101, quoting serial 27 of 08/02.
