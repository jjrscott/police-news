Updated News Post

An Eastbourne man has been charged with the murder of Jacqueline Hoadley.

The body of 58-year-old Mrs Hoadley was discovered in her home in Broad Oak Close just before 2pm on Sunday (July 5).

On Thursday afternoon (July 9), Raymond Hoadley, 62, of Willowfield Road, Eastbourne, was charged with her murder and he appeared before a virtual court later in the day.

He was remanded to appear at Crown Court on Monday (July 13).
