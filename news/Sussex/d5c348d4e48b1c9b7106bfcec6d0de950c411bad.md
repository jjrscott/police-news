A man arrested in South London on Wednesday, 16 September, as part of an operation targeting 'County Lines' offending, is to appear in court in Sussex.

Cameron Halton, 20, of Brighton Terrace, London SW9, has been charged with being concerned in the supply of crack cocaine and heroin and is to appear in custody at Crawley Magistrates' Court on Thursday (17 September).

Officers from the Metropolitan Police and Sussex Police had carried out a search warrant at the address on Wednesday morning.
