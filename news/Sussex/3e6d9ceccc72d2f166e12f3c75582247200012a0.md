Sussex Police has launched an investigation into an assault in Worthing.

Just after midday on Wednesday (23 December) officers received a report of a woman being assaulted in an alleyway in Wallace Avenue near Courtland Way, West Worthing.

The 26-year-old woman sustained a serious slash wound to her hand and has been taken to hospital.

Detective Inspector Antony Leadbeatter added: "Officers are conducting an area search for the suspects involved in the assault.

"We believe this to be an isolated incident and do not believe there is any wider threat to the public. High visibility patrols will be increased in the area while the search continues.

"We are appealing for anyone with information to make contact with us."

If anyone witnessed the assault or has information to assist the police investigation please report online or call 101 quoting reference 491 of 23/12.
