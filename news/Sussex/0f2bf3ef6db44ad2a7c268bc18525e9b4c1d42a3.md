Police are appealing for witnesses to a robbery in Crowborough.

Officers were called to Buller Close at 3pm on Saturday, 5 December where a 19-year-old man was assaulted by two suspects who took his mobile phone.

The suspects are described as two males, aged around 15, wearing dark clothing with their hoods up. It is believed the victim and suspects may be known to each other.

The victim suffered minor facial injuries which did not require hospital treatment.

Detective Constable Daniel Payton said: "We are appealing to anyone who witnessed what happened or who saw two suspects behaving suspiciously around the time of this incident.

"If you have any information that could assist with our enquiries please report it to us online or by calling 101, quoting 812 of 05/12.
