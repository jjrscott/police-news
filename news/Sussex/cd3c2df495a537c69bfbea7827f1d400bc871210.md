Sussex Police is searching for missing teenager June Smith.

June, 18, was last seen on Tuesday, 15 December in the Worthing area however it is believe she has travelled to Southampton by train.

She is described as white, 5' 4", of slim build. June was last seen wearing blue jeans, a black jumper, a black coat with fur on the arms and hood, black pool sliders and black socks.

She has links to Worthing and Southampton.

If you see June or have information on her whereabouts please report online or call 101 quoting reference 1512 of 11/12.
