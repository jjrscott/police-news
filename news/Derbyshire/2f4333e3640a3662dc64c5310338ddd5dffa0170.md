15:56 20/03/2020

We've released an image of a man we'd like to speak to in connection with an assault on a woman in Derby.

The woman was approached by a man in Leopold Street at around 4.20am on Saturday 29 February and he threatened and assaulted her.

Detectives has now released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to the incident.

Do you recognise the man pictured? If so please contact us quoting reference number 20*0115471 and the name of the officer in the case Detective Constable Greg Palfreyman.
