News

08:55 07/08/2019

Do you recognise these two people? Officers would like to speak to them about the alleged theft of a purse from a shop in Sadler Gate, Derby.

The theft is reported to have happened at around 3pm on Thursday, 25 July.

Two people entered the store and one spoke to the shop assistant. When both people had left, the shop assistant noticed her purse had been stolen from her handbag. The large black leather Mulberry purse contained her driver's licence, bank cards and a quantity of cash.

We would like to identify the two people pictured, as they may be able to help with our enquiries.

If you have any information which may assist with our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods, quoting reference number 19*390644:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary

 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
