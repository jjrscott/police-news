11:28 19/08/2019

A Volkswagen Golf owner was shocked to see thieves making off with his bumper and is warning other drivers about this type of crime.

At around 11.15pm on Sunday, August 11, a man heard a noise outside his home in Rugby Drive, Stonegravels, and looked out of the window.

He saw two men on his driveway and asked them what they were doing. The pair ripped the front bumper from his silver VW Golf and ran off with it towards the town centre.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact officer in the case PC Rebecca King, quoting the reference number 1900425252 in any correspondence.
