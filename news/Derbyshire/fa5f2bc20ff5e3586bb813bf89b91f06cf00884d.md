20:55 04/04/2020

A man who was found dead at property in Langley Mill yesterday morning has been formally identified.

Officers were called to attend a flat in Frost Avenue at around 2am on Friday 3 April and found Craig Morse, who was 33, with a fatal stab wound.

Mr Morse's family have been informed and are being supported by specialist officers at this difficult time.

A 28-year-old woman was arrested on suspicion of murder yesterday and she has since been charged.

Tonya Crabtree, of Frost Avenue, Langley Mill was charged with murder this evening (Saturday 4 April) and remanded into prison custody.

She is due to appear in court on Monday 6 April.
