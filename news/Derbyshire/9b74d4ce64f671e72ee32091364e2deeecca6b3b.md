09:53 25/10/2019

A man has been taken to hospital following a collision in Long Eaton, this morning.

It happened around 7.25am on Friday 25 October 2019. The man, who was driving an Audi A6, and was the only person in the vehicle, is believed to have been in collision with a barrier at the side of the road.

He remains in hospital, where he is receiving treatment.

Officers are keen to hear from anyone who may have captured dash cam footage of the crash.
