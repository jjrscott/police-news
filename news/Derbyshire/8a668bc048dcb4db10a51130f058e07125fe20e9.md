10:45 09/02/2020

Two men have been seriously injured during an assault in Derby.

The incident happened in Cheapside at around 1.15am on Saturday 8 February.

One 20-year-old man, who cannot recall the details of what happened, suffered a fractured skull while his friend, who is also 20, remains unconscious in hospital with facial injuries.

The street would have been busy at this time on a Saturday morning and we are urging witnesses to come forward and help us identify what happened to these men.
