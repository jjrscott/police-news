News

19:30 25/11/2020

Officers in Derby would like to hear from anyone who may have been offered a second-hand bike recently, or noticed them for sale.

It is as they investigate three robberies in the area around Infinity Park Way, which happened on Thursday 5 November and Friday 6 November.

Inquiries are ongoing into the incidents, but we would be particularly interested to hear from anyone who may have information about the whereabouts of the bikes.

The bikes which were stolen include a Forme Alport HTE Electric, a Carrera Vengeance and a Carrera Sulcata.

Do you have any information which could help? If so, please contact us on the non-emergency contact methods below.

Please quote reference 20*587933 and the name of the officer in the case, DC Joanne Bateman, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
