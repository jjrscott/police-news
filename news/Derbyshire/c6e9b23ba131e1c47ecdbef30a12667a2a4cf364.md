Below is a list of roads that are currently closed due to the snow fall in the north of the county.

Across the High Peak heavy snowfall has closed a number of roads and made driving conditions treacherous.

The following roads are closed as of 5.15pm 4 December.

 * A57 Snake Pass - closed at the Royal Pub, Glossop
 * A628 - closed at the Gun Inn, Hollingworth and at the junction of Church Road
 * A53 - closed at the Grinlow Road junction
 * Monk Road, Buxton
