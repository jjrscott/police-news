News

15:00 12/08/2020

We're appealing for information following a crash between two motorcycles which has left a man suffering with serious injuries.

It happened on the A57 Snake Pass, about a mile away from Glossop, at around 11.45am on Sunday 2 August.

The rider of one bike has appears to have come off his bike and collided with a rider travelling in the opposite direction.

Both riders were taken to hospital for treatment following the collision, and a 33 year old man remains in hospital having suffered serious injuries.

We would like to hear from anyone who witnessed the collision, or if they were travelling along Snake Road in either direction around the time and may have dashcam footage.

If you have any information which could help with our inquiries please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 20*399038 and name of the officer in the case, DC Richard Marshall, in any correspondence.
