18:07 16/02/2020

A 23-year-old woman has been charged with possession of a bladed article after she allegedly left a house in Normanton carrying a knife, yesterday afternoon.

Officers were called to the Lower Dale Road area at about 2.40pm on Saturday (15 February) following allegations of a domestic dispute.

Huda Hadi, of Great Northern Road, Derby has also been charged with possession of a class b drug and assault.

She has been remanded into custody and is due to appear at Southern Derbyshire Magistrates Court tomorrow morning, Monday 17 February.
