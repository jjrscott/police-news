10:06 03/02/2020

Officers who have been investigating an assault in Ilkeston have now released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to about the incident.

An 18-year-old man reported that he had been assaulted on the dance floor at the Market Inn pub, Market Place which left him with a broken nose.

Since the incident happened in the early hours (around 1 am) of Sunday 5 January, officers have been following a number of lines of enquiry and are now seeking help from the local community to identify the man pictured.

If you recognise this man, or have any other information about this incident, please contact us quoting reference number 20*07755.
