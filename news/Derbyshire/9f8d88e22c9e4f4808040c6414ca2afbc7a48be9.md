14:18 03/07/2020

We're appealing for witnesses after a man was assaulted in Derby on Friday 26 June.

The incident happened shortly before 10.15pm in St Peter's Street, near to McDonalds when a 29-year-old man was assaulted by another man.

The attacker is described as bald, and was wearing cream shorts and no top.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or who saw someone in the area fitting the description above is asked to contact PC Craig Peat, quoting reference number 2*326172.
