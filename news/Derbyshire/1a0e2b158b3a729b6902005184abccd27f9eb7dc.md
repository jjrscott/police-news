17:17 26/05/2020

We're investigating a serious assault which took place in Normanton.

At around 9.30pm on Monday 25 May, a woman was assaulted in Dale Road, Normanton. She remains in hospital.

Four people have been arrested in connection with the incident and we are appealing for anyone who witnessed anything to come forward.

We would like to assure local residents that this incident is not connected to the alleged burglary at the nearby Gurdwara and the associated assault at the Polanica shop, in Normanton Road.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using the below methods including reference 1656 of May 25.

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
