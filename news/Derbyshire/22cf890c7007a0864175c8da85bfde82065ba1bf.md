News

14:59 04/07/2019

Officers investigating an aggravated burglary at a house in Somercotes have released images of two men they want to speak to in connection with the incident.

At around 7.20pm on Saturday, June 15, two men entered a house in Queen Street and assaulted a man.

The man required hospital treatment for minor injuries.

One of the men was described as white and was wearing dark coloured clothing and the other was wearing a red hoody.

They left the house in what is believed to be a grey Nissan Juke.

If you recognise the men pictured, or have any information which may help our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.
