09:09 16/07/2019

Officers are appealing for information following an armed robbery in New Mills.

Just before 4pm on Sunday, 14 July, the man pictured walked into the Premier Sunny's Convenience Store in Low Leighton Road, New Mills, and threatened staff with a knife.

A customer in the shop wrestled with the man, who then ran off.

Officers want to speak to the member of the public (pictured above) who confronted the robber and want to hear from anyone who knows the identity of the man who is suspected of carrying out the crime.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using one of below methods - including reference 19000367566 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
