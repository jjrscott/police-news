16:56 19/02/2020

We're appealing for witnesses after a robbery at a shop in Derby yesterday morning.

A man is reported to have entered the Markeaton News store, in Bromley Street at around 5am on Tuesday 18 February. He threatened the shop assistant and demanded cash. He stole a quantity of cash, cigarettes and alcohol.

The man is described as white, between 5ft 10ins and 6ft tall and of average build. He was wearing a hooded top and had his face covered with a black scarf. He was also wearing brown jeans.

If you have any information about this incident, or recognise the description of the man, please contact us quoting reference number 20*093540 and the name of the officer in the case Detective Constable Rorry Head.
