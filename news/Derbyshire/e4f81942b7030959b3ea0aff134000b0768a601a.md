News

16:30 01/03/2021

We're issuing CCTV images of two people who may be able to help officers investigating the theft of a woman's wallet in Church Gresley.

CCTV appeal may help officers investigating theft of wallet

A woman, aged in her 80s, had been shopping in Sainsbury's and also visited the Church Gresley fish and chip shop.

As she was making her way home along Brunel Way she was approached by a woman and a man who allegedly stood in front and behind of her and loudly asked her which way she was going.

The pair then left the area and the woman later discovered her wallet, containing bank cards, had been stolen.

Officers would like to speak to the trace the people pictured in the images as they may be able to help with their inquiries.

If you are pictured, if you recognise those in images, or if you have any information please contact us using the non-emergency methods below.

Please quote reference 21*93347 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Megan Jones in any correspondence.
