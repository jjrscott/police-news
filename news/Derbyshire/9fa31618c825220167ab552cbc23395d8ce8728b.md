News

15:22 26/06/2020

Officers are appealing for the public's help tracking two young men who featured in a video shared widely on social media.

The video shows two men fighting close to Chatsworth House surrounded by a large number of young people.

Officers had been called to various incidents in the area around Chatsworth, including reports of parking issues and disturbances, on Wednesday 24 June.

If you are one of the young men pictured, or you know who they are, then officers are asking you to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference number 1010-240620 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
