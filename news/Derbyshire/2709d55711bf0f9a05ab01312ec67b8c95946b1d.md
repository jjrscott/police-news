16:16 20/08/2019

We have released images of two men we want to speak to about the theft of cash from a gaming machine.

At around 12.20pm on Thursday, August 15, two men went into the Norfolk Arms Hotel, in Norfolk Square, Glossop, and broke into a gaming machine in the bar.

They emptied it of cash and left.

We would like to speak to the men pictured.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact PC Patrick Haley, quoting reference number 19*432632.
