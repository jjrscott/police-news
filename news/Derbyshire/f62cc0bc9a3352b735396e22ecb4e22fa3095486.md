14:12 16/02/2020

We're appealing for information about Craig Lewis who is missing from his home in Shirebrook, as we have concerns for his welfare.

Craig was last seen at 2.30pm on Wednesday 12 February in Manor Court Road, Bolsover.

He is described as white and of slim build, with a shaved head. He was wearing a red puffa jacket, grey t-shirt, pale blue jeans and blue Adidas trainers when last seen.

Craig is also thought to have links to the Wolverhampton area.

If you have seen Craig, or know where he may be, please contact us quoting reference number 801 of 15 February.
