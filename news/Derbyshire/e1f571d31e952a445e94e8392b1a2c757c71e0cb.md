12:44 24/05/2020

We are investigating a fire at a phone mast in Breadsall Hilltop and are appealing for witnesses to come forward.

Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service called us shortly before 2am today (Sunday 24 May) to report that they were dealing with a fire at the mast in Scarborough Drive.

We believe the fire was started deliberately and we are investigating.
