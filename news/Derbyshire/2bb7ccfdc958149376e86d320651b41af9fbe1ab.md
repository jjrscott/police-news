12:28 22/04/2020

Officers investigating a burglary in Brimington have released CCTV images of a man they would like to speak to about the incident.

A house in Coronation Road was burgled sometime overnight between 10pm on Wednesday 25 March and 5.30am on Thursday 26 March, after a rear door was forced open.

A number of items were stolen including a television, mobile phones, jewellery a laptop and two bank cards.

The bank card was later used at an ATM machine in New Whittington and then in a Spar shop in Sheffield Road, Chesterfield.

Officers have now released images of a man who they believe may be able to assist with their enquiries into the incident.

Do you recognise this man?

Did you witness anything suspicious in Coronation Road overnight on 25 March?
