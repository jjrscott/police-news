15:44 30/10/2019

Can you help identify these men? Officers would like to speak to them in relation to the theft of a bike from a home on Occupation Road, Newbold.

Between midnight and 7:15am on Sunday 20 October, a GT Force Elite push bike was taken from a locked shed.

The bike has bright yellow suspension, gold coloured handle bars, and a black seat.

If you recognise these men, have any further information, or have seen a similar bike for sale, please contact us using one of the following non-emergency methods, quoting reference number 19*560811.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to this page
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
