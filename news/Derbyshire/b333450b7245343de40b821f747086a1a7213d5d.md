08:16 25/01/2020

We are appealing to the public for information after a woman was assaulted on Warner Street in Derby.

We received a call from a witness yesterday who stated that a woman had been assaulted on the street at around 6.45pm on Thursday, 23 January.

This was near the George Cross pub, following the incident the woman is said to have walked off towards Abbey Street.

The man is described as black, in his 20s and was wearing a black and white tracksuit.

If you have any information which may assist with our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the incident number 989 of 23 January and the name of the officer in the case DC Ryan Wilson, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
