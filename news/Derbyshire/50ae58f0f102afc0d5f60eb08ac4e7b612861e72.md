16:17 14/08/2019

We are releasing this image of a woman we want to speak to after hundreds of pounds worth of goods were stolen from shops across Chesterfield.

Officers are investigating four crimes where large amounts of steak and salmon have been stolen from supermarkets.

The latest crime happened just after 2pm on Tuesday, August 13 when a woman went into the Co-Op in Littlemoor and took steak and salmon from display.

The same products were taken from the Co-Op in High Street, Brimington, at 8pm on Thursday, August 8.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact Sgt Mark Webb, quoting reference number 19000428607.
