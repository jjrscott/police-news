News

16:42 31/03/2021

Officers investigating the theft of a distinctive Audi from a High Peak home are appealing for information.

The white Audi A3 S Line TDI Quattro, which has distinctive black wing mirrors, was stolen between 9.15pm on 29 March and 6.10am on 30 March after a house in Manchester Road, Chapel-en-le-Frith was broken into.

Anyone with information about the whereabouts of the Audi, the number plate of which begins S99, is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods.

Of particular interest are any homeowners with CCTV that covers the area and may have caught the car as it was driven away.

If you have information please quote reference 21*175492 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
