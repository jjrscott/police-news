News

19:01 10/05/2020

A woman has been taken to hospital following a collision off London Road, Alvaston.

Officers were called to the scene, opposite Eden Street, at 3pm today (10.05.20) to reports that a woman had been injured following a collision between her and a car.

The woman, whose age is unknown at this time, was airlifted to hospital with serious injuries and is undergoing treatment.

Officers remain at the scene and are keen to speak to anyone who may have been in the area at the time of the incident and may have dashcam footage that can help with enquiries.
