News

13:37 22/09/2020

We have released some images of a man we want to speak to about an alleged assault at a pub in Derby

Images released as part of investigation into an alleged assault at a Derby pub

The man in the pictures may have information which could help our inquiry.

The incident happened at around 5.30pm and 6.30pm on Saturday 12 September. A 29-year-old man was allegedly assaulted as he was leaving the toilets at Bar Sport on Queen Street.

He suffered a broken nose.

We know that the images are not the most clear, but we hope someone may recognise the man pictured or his clothing.

Do you recognise the man in the image or have any information which could help with our inquiries?

If so, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 20*484884 and name of the officer in the case, PC Andrew Smith, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
