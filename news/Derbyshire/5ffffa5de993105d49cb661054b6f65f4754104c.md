Safer Neighbourhood

05:30 06/06/2020

A man has been arrested on suspicion of the production of drugs following a search at a property in South Normanton.

Man arrested following warrant at a property in South Normanton

Officers from the local Safer Neighbourhood policing and investigation teams carried out the warrant on The Common on Wednesday 3 June.

They discovered more than 150 suspected Cannabis plants.

The 29 year old, who is from the local area, has been questioned and released on police bail while inquiries continue.

Safer Neighbourhood Team Sgt. James Bowler said: "We are continuing to work hard across the Amber Valley to target drug misuse, crime and anti-social behaviour, and to keep our communities safe.

"We will always look to develop any information we receive about possible illegal drug activity and investigate further if appropriate. I would like to thank the local community for their support and would encourage anyone with information about drug misuse or suspicious activity to get in touch."

If you have information about drug use in your community you can contact us on one of the following options:
