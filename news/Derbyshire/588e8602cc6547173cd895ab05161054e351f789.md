14:50 04/02/2021

Thieves have stolen several bicycles from a shop in Derby.

We Buy Cycles, in Full Street, was broken into sometime between 9.30pm last night (Wednesday 3 February) and 2.30am today.

Initial inquiries suggest that three people went into the shop and stole a number of bicycles. Staff are compiling details of the stolen bikes for our officers.

We want to hear from anyone who saw suspicious activity in the area at the time, or anyone who has information about the burglary.
