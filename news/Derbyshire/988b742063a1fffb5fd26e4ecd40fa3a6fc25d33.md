14:08 04/07/2019

A man from Derby has been charged and remanded over alleged sexual offences.

Toby Yates, 31, of Merevale Way, Stenson Fields was charged with adult abuse of position of trust to incite sexual activity with a girl aged 13-17, and two counts of possessing indecent photography, on Tuesday, July 2.

He appeared at Southern Derbyshire Magistrates Court yesterday morning (Wednesday, July 3) and was remanded into custody.

He will next appear at Derby Crown Court on Wednesday, July 31 at 10.30am.
