10:48 01/06/2020

We are appealing for witnesses to a minor collision involving a Ford Focus and a Subaru Forester on Chesterfield Road, Alfreton.

The incident happened between 11.30am and 11.40am on Saturday 23 May on a stretch of the A61 as it approaches Alfreton from Shirland.

Traffic was backed up due to roadworks when the collision took place. There was also a verbal dispute between the occupants of the cars.

Were you in the traffic when the incident took place?

Did you witness it, or do you have dashcam footage recorded at the time?
