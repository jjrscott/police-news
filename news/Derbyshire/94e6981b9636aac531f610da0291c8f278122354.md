15:49 12/10/2020

Officers investigating a report about a woman being followed in Derby are appealing for information.

The woman reported that she was walking along Queen Street at 1.15pm on Saturday 10 October when she noticed a man nearby who appeared to be touching himself inappropriately. The man continued to walk close by.

The woman returned to her car in River Street and the man walked off.

He is described as white, 5ft 6ins tall, aged in his late thirties or early forties with a bald head and had several teeth missing. He was wearing baggy, black jeans with large white circles on each of the back pockets. He also wore a baggy black coat.

Local officers will be on patrol in the area and they will be happy to speak with anyone who has information or concerns about this incident.

Alternatively, anyone who may be able to assist with our enquires can also contact PC Alanna Griffiths quoting reference number 2*534430, via one of the following methods.
