News

13:51 07/02/2021

Officers are appealing for help to find a missing 15-year-old from Derby.

Jahmar Campbell was last seen in the Sinfin area of Derby on Thursday 4 February at around 10am.

Jahmar, who is 5ft 11ins tall and has short black hair, was last seen wearing a black Nike tracksuit, a blue puffer coat and black, white and red Nike Jordan trainers.

Anyone who has any information about the whereabouts of Jahmar is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 1228-040221:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
