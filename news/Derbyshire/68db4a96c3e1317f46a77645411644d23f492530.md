09:40 24/02/2020

We want to hear from drivers who may have dash cam footage of a pursuit in Chesterfield.

At around 10.10am on Wednesday 19 February, officers in unmarked police cars were following a grey BMW 2 series between the Hornsbridge and Lordsmill roundabouts.

Anyone who was driving in the area at the time and captured the incident on dash cam should contact us quoting reference 20*95926.
