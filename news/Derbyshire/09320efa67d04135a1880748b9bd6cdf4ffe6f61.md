News

11:27 10/02/2021

Officers investigating a collision that saw a man run off from the scene are appealing for drivers with dashcam footage to come forward.

The collision between the Ford Focus and a Vauxhall Vectra occurred in Hills Lane, Stanton by Bridge at 10.45am on 7 February.

The driver of the Vauxhall left the scene of the collision prior to police arriving and information is sought in order to try and identify the man. The driver of the Ford was left with minor injuries.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who was in the area at the time, in particular any drivers with dashcam that may have captured the incident and the driver leaving the scene.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 21*077698:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
