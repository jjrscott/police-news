16:25 14/01/2020

Yesterday it was reported to police that a Mansfield Town player has received racist abuse on Twitter.

The matter was investigated by Nottinghamshire Police and the alleged Twitter account was found to belong to an individual in Derbyshire.

The individual involved has contacted the force and in conjunction with Nottinghamshire Police the investigation is ongoing.
