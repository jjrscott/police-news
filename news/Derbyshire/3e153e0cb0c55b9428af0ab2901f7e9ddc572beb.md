16:43 05/11/2020

Two teenagers have been robbed in Darley Abbey.

The boys, aged 16 and 15 were in the alleyway at the side of St Benedict Catholic Voluntary Academy, Duffield Road, when they were approached by a youth with a moped, who threatened them and demanded their phones.

The friends handed over their phones and the older boy was punched in the face.

The robbery happened at about 10.45pm on Saturday 31 October.
