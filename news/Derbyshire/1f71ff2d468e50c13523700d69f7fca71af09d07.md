10:35 17/04/2019

Officers want to speak to anyone who may have witnessed a dog-on-dog attack in Crich.

The incident took place at around 9am on Monday, April 8, in the Chatsworth Avenue/Bullin Lane area of the village.

Witnesses have reported a number of dogs loose on the road - one of which has attacked a smaller, terrier breed dog, which has died.

Anyone with any information about the incident is asked to call Derbyshire police on the 101 number with reference 19000178025.
