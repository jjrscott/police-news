News

12:30 01/12/2020

Officers are appealing for information after a man indecently exposed himself to a child in Kilburn.

The incident occurred at around 4pm on Thursday 19 November in Belper Road, Kilburn.

A white car, believed to be a BMW, pulled up to ask a child, under the age of 14, for directions. While giving the man directions the child has noticed that the man has exposed himself. The child ran away and the man drove off.

The driver of the car is described as being younger than 40 years of age, white, with a bald or shaved head and had a beard.

Officers are appealing for anyone who may have been in the area at the time of the incident and may have dashcam or CCTV footage that may have captured the car on film.
