13:42 23/09/2020

A car has been damaged by a fire which we believe was started deliberately.

We are appealing for witnesses to the fire, which happened at around 9.30pm last night (Tuesday 22 September).

A BMW parked at a house in Alfred Road was set alight, causing significant damage.

Witnesses or anyone with information can get in touch via the following methods. Please quote reference 20*499490;
