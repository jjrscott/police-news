10:28 20/12/2019

Officers investigating an assault in Ripley want to speak to this man in connection with the incident.

The assault took place in Ripley market place at around 10pm on Saturday, 14 December.

The victim, a 32-year-old man, was punched to the face causing a fractured jaw.

Officers want to speak to the man pictured who was in the area at the time of the offence.
