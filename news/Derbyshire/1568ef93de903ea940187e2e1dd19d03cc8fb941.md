16:58 19/08/2019

A serving prisoner who had absconded from HMP Sudbury has been arrested and charged with escaping lawful custody.

Kieron Moss, 33, was reported to have absconded from HMP Sudbury on Sunday, 11 August. He was arrested by officers in Nottingham on 15 August and has subsequently been charged with escaping lawful custody.
