12:24 12/01/2021

Officers have released a number of images of suspected stolen items, in a bid to find their owners.

The various items which include bikes, a watch, and power tools were seized from a property in the Rose Hill area of Derby on 6 December.

Do you recognise any of the items pictured as belonging to you, or do you know someone who has had something similar stolen in the last few months?

If you believe any of the items pictured may belong to you, or someone you know, please contact PC Sue Smith detailing the relevant image number.

Please also quote reference number 20*644404.
