News

11:19 28/05/2019

Officers investigating a burglary at a Post Office in Alfreton are appealing to the public for further information.

The incident took place at around 3.30am on Thursday, May 2, at the Post Office in Nottingham Road, Somercotes, Alfreton.

The offenders broke into the building and stole cash, cigarettes and a large quantity of postage stamps.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who has have been offered quantities of stamps for sale in recent weeks, or anyone with any further information that may help with their enquiries.

Please include reference number 19*222366 and the name of the officer, DC Marshall, in any correspondence.
