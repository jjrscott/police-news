News

15:00 11/08/2020

An investigation is underway after a catering van parked in a layby was destroyed by fire yesterday (Monday 10 August).

Catering van destroyed in suspected arson in Hilton

Officers and fire crews were called to the scene at around 6.20am on the A516 between Etwall and Hilton.

It is believed the fire was started deliberately and we would like to hear from anyone who was in the area around 6am that day and may have seen or heard anything suspicious.

If you have any information which could help with our inquiries please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 20*413126 and name of the officer in the case, PC James Williams, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
