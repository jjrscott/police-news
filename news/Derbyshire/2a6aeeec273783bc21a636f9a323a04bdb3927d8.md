16:15 31/03/2020

We have issued a picture of Graham Walker Redfearn, who we would like to speak to about allegations of a breach of a court order.

The 47-year-old moved to Grangemill, near Matlock, in May 2016 and worked in Elton.

He has previously lived in Shrewbury and has connections to different parts of Wales. He is also thought to have connections to Ireland.

He is 5ft 9ins tall, slim and has light brown hair, which is thinning. He has many tattoos, including three tigers and a woman on his left arm and a symbol tiger in Japanese at the top of his neck.
