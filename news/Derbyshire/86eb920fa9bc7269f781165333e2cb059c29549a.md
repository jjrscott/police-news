News

12:00 20/08/2020

We're appealing for help to find out what happened to an elderly man who was found injured and unconscious on a street in Derby.

Paramedics and officers were called to Shakespeare Street, near the junction with Macauley Street, in Sinfin at around 11am on Saturday 25 July.

An 81 year old man had been found on the road with an injury to his head. He was taken to hospital for treatment.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have been in the area and perhaps saw what happened, or noticed the man or any vehicles just before or after the time of the incident.

If you have any information which could help with our inquiries please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 20*382347 and name of the officer in the case, PC Amreen Khokhar, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
