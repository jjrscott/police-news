20:38 09/07/2019

The body of a man, believed to be that of missing John Chalmers-Brown, has been found near Ravensdale Cragg in Cressbrook, on the Monsal Trail.

Mr Chalmers-Brown, 62, was reported missing from his home in Ravensdale Cottages in the early hours of this morning. Officers searching for him located the body of a man at around 10.30am this morning.

Mr Chalmers-Brown's family have been made aware and our thoughts are with them at this sad time.

Formal Identification is yet to take place.

There are thought to be no suspicious circumstances surrounding the death.
