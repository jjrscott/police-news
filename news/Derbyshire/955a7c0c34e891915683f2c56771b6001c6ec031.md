15:38 01/12/2019

Officers are currently in attendance at a serious collision on the A623, near Tideswell and the road has been closed between Tideswell and Wardlow.

Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service, the Air Ambulance and East Midlands Ambulance Service are also in attendance.

The road may be closed for some time so please avoid travelling unless your journey is absolutely essential. For those who do need to travel local diversions will be in place, but please consider taking alternative routes ahead of starting a journey. Thank you in advance for your patience.

A further update will be issued once the road is reopened.
