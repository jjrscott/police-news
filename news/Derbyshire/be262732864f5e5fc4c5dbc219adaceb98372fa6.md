16:39 06/11/2019

Detectives investigating an alleged stabbing in Chesterfield are appealing for witnesses to the incident to come forward.

Officers were called to Arundel Close, Newbold, to reports that a man had been stabbed at around 6am this morning (6 Nov).

On attendance a 32-year-old man was found with serious but not life threatening injuries to the side of his head. He was taken to hospital for treatment.

A 19-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of assault.

Anyone who was in the area and has any information about the incident, especially those with CCTV or drivers with dashcam footage, are asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods with reference 19*593621:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to this page
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us
 * Phone - call us on 101

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
