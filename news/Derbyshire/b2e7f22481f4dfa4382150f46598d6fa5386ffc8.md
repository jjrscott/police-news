13:06 27/02/2020

We have released an image of a man we want to speak to about a burglary.

A woman was at her home in Queensway, Derby, at about noon yesterday (Wednesday 26 February) when she heard the sound of smashing glass.

She went to investigate and disturbed a burglar downstairs. He ran off along the footpath leading to Markeaton Street.

We want to speak to the man pictured in case he can help with our inquiries.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact us quoting reference number 20*109745.
