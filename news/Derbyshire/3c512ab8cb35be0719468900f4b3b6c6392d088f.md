08:24 27/11/2019

Officers investigating an attempted burglary and theft at a Swadlincote business are appealing for the public's help to identify the men pictured.

Police were called to reports that an attempt had been made to break into a business in George Holmes Way, Swadlincote, and that a laptop and electrical machinery had been stolen from a works vehicle between 7.15pm and 7.45pm on 27 October.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who has information about the incident or recognises the man pictured.

If you have information please contact Derbyshire police using any of the following methods, including reference number 19*575607 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
