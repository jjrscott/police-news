News

11:36 29/04/2019

Officers investigating a burglary at The Mease Pavilion, Hilton, have released images of a man they would like to identify, in connection with the incident.

We were called to reports of a burglary at around 8am on Friday, April 12. It is believed that the break-in happened between 1am and 5am.

Significant damage was caused to the changing room area, main room, kitchen and bar.

CCTV footage shows that the offender returned several times during the night. The alarm also sounded.

If you recognise the man pictured, or you have any information which may assist with our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19000185595 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Mark Rose, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
