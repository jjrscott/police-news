News
Safer Neighbourhood

11:51 20/10/2020

We are appealing for information and dashcam footage following a collision involving a cyclist and a car near Swadlincote.

An 18 year old woman was knocked off her bike after a small black car allegedly clipped her wheel as she was travelling along Burton Road, at Midway, between 7.50pm and 8pm on Tuesday 29 September.

She injured her knee and had to receive hospital treatment. The driver did not stop.

Officers have been making inquiries, but are now appealing to the public for help to trace the driver.

Did you witness the incident, or were you in the area around the time of the incident and have dashcam footage which may be useful?

If you think you can help which please get in touch get in touch using one of the non emergency methods below.

Please quote reference number 20*513125 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Matt Boyer, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
