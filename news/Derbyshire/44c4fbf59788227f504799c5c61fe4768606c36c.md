15:53 14/10/2019

Witnesses are being sought to a collision which left a pedestrian and her dog with serious injuries.

At around 4.40pm on Sunday 22 September, a 61-year-old woman was walking along Heage Road, Ripley when she was in collision with a black Mitsubishi L200.

She suffered a fractured pelvis and bruising while her dog needed stitches for a lacerated shoulder.

The collision happened close to Heage Industrial Estate.

Anyone with any information should contact PC Colin Baker using any of below methods with reference 19000506817.
