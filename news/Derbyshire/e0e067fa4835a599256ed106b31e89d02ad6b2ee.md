17:16 16/07/2020

We're appealing for witnesses after an altercation in a park in Church Gresley, in which an 18-year-old man was threatened.

The incident is reported to have happened in Maurice Lea Memorial Park, in Common Road at around 10.15pm on Tuesday 14 July.

The victim had been walking down York Road when he became involved in a disagreement with a group of youths.

He was then threatened and followed by a member of the group who is described having a shaved head, and was wearing grey jogging bottoms, a white top and grey shoes.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or with any other information is asked to contact PC Leanne Bull quoting reference number 2*361274.
