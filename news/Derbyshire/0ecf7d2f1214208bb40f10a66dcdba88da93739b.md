News

17:52 01/02/2020

Two more people have been arrested on suspicion of murder following the death of Andrew Jackson in Shirebrook last weekend.

Mr Jackson who was 55-years-old, was discovered at around 10am on Sunday 26 January at an allotment, off Prospect Drive.

A 26-year-old man and a 37-year-old man were arrested this afternoon (Saturday 1 February). This takes the total number of those arrested to six.

The five men and one woman all remain in police custody.
