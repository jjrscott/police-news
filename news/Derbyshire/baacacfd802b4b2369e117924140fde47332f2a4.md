News

15:03 22/12/2020

Officers are appealing for information after a roller was stolen from a building site in Chellaston.

The Hamm roller, similar to that pictured, was stolen from a building site off Swarkestone Road, Chellaston, between 4.30pm on 5 December and 9.30am on 7 December.

Officers investigating the theft are appealing for anyone who was in the area between the 5 and 7 December and may have information that can help their enquiries.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference number 20*647751:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
