09:12 19/12/2019

We're becoming increasingly concerned for the welfare of missing man Hayden Marshall and have released a new image of him, in a bid to jog the memory of anyone who have may have seen him.

Hayden was last seen in Derby Market Place at around 1.15am on Tuesday morning (17 December).

Hayden, who is 28, is described as white, 6ft 1ins tall and of slim build. He has short dark hair and a beard, and sometimes wears glasses. Hayden was wearing a checked blue and white shirt, blue jeans and a black leather jacket when last seen.

This latest image shows the jacket Hayden was wearing at the time he was last seen and also his glasses, which he is likely to be wearing.

If you have seen Hayden or know where he may be, please contact us quoting reference number 23 of 17 December.
