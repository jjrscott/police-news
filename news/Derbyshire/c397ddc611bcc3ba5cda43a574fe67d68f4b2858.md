13:24 20/08/2020

Nearly 200 suspected cannabis plants were found in a house in Stonegravels.

Officers attended the house in William Street yesterday (19 August) and found 180 suspected cannabis plants inside the address.

Marjus Cjapi was arrested at the address on suspicion of the production of a controlled class b drug and has been charged and remanded to appear at the Chesterfield Justice Centre today (August 20).

Anyone who suspects that drug dealing or production is taking place in their community can contact Derbyshire police in confidence using any of the below methods:
