16:01 22/10/2019

Police are hunting a suspect who assaulted two men in the city centre.

Police are hunting a suspect who assaulted two men in the city centre.

It happened between 3 - 4am on Saturday 12 October 2019, outside Brigdens in Sadler Gate, as the two friends were approached by a group of men.

One of the victims, 18, was struck from the side, causing him to fall to the floor and sustain a broken jaw.

The other victim was punched to the face.

The offender and those he was with, ran off towards the Thomas Leaper pub / Standing Order.

Anyone with any information, who knows who is responsible or who witnessed the assault is asked to contact us as soon as possible.

Please quote the reference number 19000546622 and the name of the officer in the case, DC Jo Bateman in any correspondence.
