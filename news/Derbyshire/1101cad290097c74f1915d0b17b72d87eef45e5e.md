14:46 15/10/2019

We are releasing pictures of two men we want to speak to about the theft of money from a church in Derby.

Sometime between noon and 1pm on Sunday 22 September, cash was stolen from St Joseph's Church, in Burton Road.

The money had been collected earlier that day during mass.

We hope the two men pictured could help us with our inquiries.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact officer in the case PC Sophie Salloway, quoting reference 19*506917.
