15:35 06/03/2020

Detectives investigating an assault at a pub in Hasland are appealing for witnesses to the incident to come forward.

The victim was in the Devonshire Arms in Mansfield Road at around 7.15pm on Friday, 31 January when he was knocked unconscious by two men.

The 36-year-old man was found to have suffered a fractured cheekbone and eye socket - he is recovering well from him injuries.

The two suspects are described as:

1 - 6ft 2ins tall, large build and wearing blue jeans

2 - 5ft 6ins tall, small build and wearing black trousers and light coloured top

Officers would like to speak to anyone who has any information about the incident or who knows who the two men are.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*059763 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
