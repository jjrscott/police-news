News

12:00 09/11/2020

We're appealing for help to help us identify the driver of car involved in a collision in Hatton.

It's after a vehicle allegedly collided with a van which was parked near the Co-op store on Station Road at around 7.10pm on Friday 6 November.

The driver of the vehicle, which is believed to have been a black car, left the scene and our officers would like to trace them.

No one suffered any injuries but the rear of the van was damaged.

Officers would also like to trace the driver of a yellow van who stopped shortly after the incident to then to the driver and passenger of the van which had been damaged.

We would like to hear from anyone who may have seen the incident, or with dashcam or CCTV footage from the area shortly before or after the incident.

If you have any information which you think could help with our inquiries please contact us using one of the following methods.

Please quote reference number 20*588960 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Joe Stafford.
