13:50 11/08/2020

Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service are currently dealing with a fire in the Woodthorpe area, near to Staveley in Chesterfield.

Fire at Woodthorpe

Local residents are advised to keep windows and doors closed due to thick smoke in the area.

The A619 Worksop Road is currently closed in both directions in between Mastin Moor and Staveley. Please avoid the area and find an alternative route.
