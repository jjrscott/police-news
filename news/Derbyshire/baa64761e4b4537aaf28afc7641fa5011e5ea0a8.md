17:19 14/06/2019

Officers investigating an incident at the Aldi store in Burton Road, Derby, want to speak to a man in relation to the incident.

We were called to reports that a man was shoplifting from the store and had been detained by staff.

The man threatened staff and left the store with a number of items.

Officers want to speak to the man pictured - who was wearing a distinctive hooded top with a camel on the front - in relation to the incident, which took place at 8am on Wednesday, June 12.

Anyone with any information that can help officers with their enquiries should get in contact immediately, either directly with the force, or via Crimestoppers.

In any correspondence please include reference 19000301531.
