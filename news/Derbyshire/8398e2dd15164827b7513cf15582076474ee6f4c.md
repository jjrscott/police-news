16:49 09/12/2019

We're reissuing an appeal to locate a wanted man, who officers would like to speak to about allegations of harassment and breaching a restraining order.

We first released an appeal to find 34-year-old Craig Smith, from Derby, in September and despite a number of calls from members of the public, have not yet been able to trace him.

If you have seen Craig, or have any information about where he may be, please contact us using one of the following methods.

Please quote reference number 19*259366 in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
