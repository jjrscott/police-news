14:40 20/12/2019

Officers investigating an assault in Derby are asking the public to help identify the man captured on CCTV.

A woman was assaulted on Bass Rec at around 5pm on Tuesday, 13 August, and officers are keen to trace the man pictured who was in the area at the time of the offence.

Detectives have been following a number of lines of enquiry since the incident, however, are now appealing for the public's help to identify the man pictured.

Anyone with any information that can help should contact Derbyshire police using any of below methods with reference 19*429215:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 or online at www.crimestoppers-uk.org.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
