11:42 23/12/2020

Several air rifles and a crossbow have been stolen from a house in Normanton.

A house in Randolph Road was broken into sometime between 3.30pm and 5.20pm on Saturday 19 December.

We want to hear from witnesses, or anyone who has been offered an air rifle for sale.
