News

10:17 23/07/2020

Officers are re-releasing an image of Tai Saide, from Buxton, who they would like to speak to about allegations of dealing Class A drugs.

It is believed that the 22-year-old may be in the Manchester area.

If you have seen Tai recently, or have information about where he may be, please contact us using one of the following methods, quoting reference number 19*100317 in any correspondence

\- Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary

\- Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact

\- Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.

\- Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
