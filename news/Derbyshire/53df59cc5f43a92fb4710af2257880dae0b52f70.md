News

13:58 17/08/2020

Officers investigating the theft of a scooter are appealing for witnesses to the incident to come forward to help their enquiries.

The incident occurred at 9.40pm on Friday 7 August in Tutbury Avenue, Littleover.

The 19-year-old owner was riding a red Lexmoto scooter when a motorcycle pulled in front of him and the rider and pillion forced him off his scooter and rode off.

A number of cars were in the area at the time of the incident and officers want to speak to any drivers who witnessed the incident - in particular any drivers with dashcam footage.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*409180 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
