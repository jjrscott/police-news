News

07:15 10/12/2020

We're appealing for help to identify a man in connection with a robbery at a building society in Derby.

CCTV appeal after man robs building society in Derby

The incident happened between 2.20 pm and 2.30 pm on Friday 20 November.

A man walked into the Nationwide Building Society on Albion Street. He threatened a cashier and demanded cash.

The cashier handed over a quantity of cash and the man fled in the direction of St. Peter's Street. He is thought to have run along Babbington Lane and was last seen walking past the Intu Centre towards London Road.

Officers have now released the image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with the incident. Do you recognise him?

If you have any information which could help with our inquiries please contact using any of the below methods, including reference 20*615295 and the name of the officer in the case, Temp. DC Lauren Alderson, in any correspondence.
