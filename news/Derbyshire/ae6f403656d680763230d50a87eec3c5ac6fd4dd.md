12:11 24/03/2021

We are investigating a burglary at a business in Derby where a credit card was stolen and later used to make purchases at several shops.

The burglary took place in a property on Friar Gate on Friday 5 March where a number of items were stolen which included a credit card, this was then used in some shops in the local area. The credit card was used between Saturday 6 March and Monday 8 March.

We would like to speak to the two men pictured as they may have information that could help with our inquiry. If anyone recognises these men then please contact us using any of the below methods and include reference 21*131317 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
