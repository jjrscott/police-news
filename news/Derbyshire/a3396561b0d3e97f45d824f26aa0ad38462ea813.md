13:00 27/11/2019

We received a call at just after 5.05pm to report that a grey Volkswagen Transporter had collided with a man in Langwith Road.

East Midlands Ambulance Service attended and the man was taken to Queens Medical Centre for treatment to his injuries, which are understood to be serious and potentially life-threatening.

We'd like to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time and may have information.

Anyone with dash cam fitted, who was travelling nearby yesterday evening, is asked to download any footage that may be of use to officers onto another device and store it safely for them to view.

Please quote the reference number 19*634282 in any correspondence.
