10:45 16/07/2019

A 25-year-old man has been charged with possessing an imitation firearm, cannabis and cocaine after being arrested in Hollingwood on Saturday, 13 July.

Dale Walker-Marsh, 25, of Hornbeam Close, Hollingwood is alleged to have been involved in a number of incidents of road rage in Brimington on Saturday.

Armed officers were deployed to the area at around 12.30pm following reports of a man driving around with a handgun.

Mr Walker-Marsh has been released on bail and is due to appear at Chesterfield Magistrates Court on Tuesday, 30 July.
