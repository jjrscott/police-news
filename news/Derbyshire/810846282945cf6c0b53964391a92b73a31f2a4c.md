16:34 12/07/2019

Detectives have released an image of a Canon camera and a photo found on the camera, in a bid to trace the owner.

The camera was found inside a rucksack alongside a number of other items, including some which have been traced back to a robbery at a Jet garage in Wilsthorpe Road, in Breaston in October last year.

Is this camera yours or are you in the photograph?

Do you recognise the women pictured?
