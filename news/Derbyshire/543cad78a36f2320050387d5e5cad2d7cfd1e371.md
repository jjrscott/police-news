News

10:28 09/11/2020

Officers have now traced a man they wanted to speak to in connection with an alleged assault at a taxi office in Chesterfield.

The appeal was issued on Friday 6 November and was in connection with an incident at the A-Line Taxi office on Stephenson Place on Thursday 8 October.

Thank you to all those who shared our post on social media or responded to our appeal.
