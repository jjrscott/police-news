09:12 11/09/2019

Officers investigating the burglary of a significant amount of gardening equipment are appealing for information.

Between 12.20am and 12.30am on Wednesday, 14 August, three offenders forced the doors to an outbuilding at the Breadsall Priory Country Club open and stole a number of pieces of gardening equipment.

The thieves used a black Honda CRV to escape with the following items:

 * 8 Stihl chainsaws
 * 4 Stihl strimmers
 * 3 Stihl hedge cutters
 * 2 Stihl garden blowers

Officers want to speak to anyone who has any information about the men pictured or the vehicle that was used.

In particular officers want to speak to anyone who was driving in the area around the time of the incident and may have dashcam footage of a vehicle similar to that pictured.

If you have any information which may assist with our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19*429776 in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
