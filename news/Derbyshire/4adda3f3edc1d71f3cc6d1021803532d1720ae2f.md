17:13 09/12/2019

Officers were called to reports of a serious assault in Uttoxeter New Road, Derby, at 1.25pm on Sunday, 8 December.

A 43-year-old man was found at the scene with serious injuries and is being treated at hospital.

Officers are keen to speak anyone who was in the area at the time and may have information that can assist with their investigations.

They are also keen to hear from anyone with dash cam footage of this incident, or anyone who was travelling nearby and may have video that can help officers.

If you have footage which may be of use please download it onto another device and store it securely ready for officers to view.

Please quote incident number 19*656439 in any correspondence.
