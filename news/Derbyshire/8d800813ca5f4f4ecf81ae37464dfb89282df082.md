14:40 05/02/2020

A tray of rings has been stolen from a jeweller in Glossop.

At around 12.45pm on Monday 3 February, a man went into Paul David Jewellers in High Street and asked to view some diamond platinum rings.

A member of staff brought him a tray of rings to view and he snatched it, running out of the shop.

We have released these images of two of the rings and are asking people to contact us if they are offered rings for sale.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact DC Julie Shaw via the following methods, quoting reference 20*064212:

•Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
