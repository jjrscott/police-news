News

12:03 05/02/2021

Officers investigating reports that a woman was assaulted on a footpath in Chellaston are appealing for witnesses to come forward to help with their enquiries.

The force was made aware today of a report that a woman was assaulted on a footpath between Infinity Parkway and Lowes Lane, Chellaston, on Tuesday 2 February between 2.30pm and 3.30pm.

At this time, it is believed this is an isolated incident and enquiries are ongoing.

Detectives want to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time, in particular any dog walkers who were in the area between 2.30pm and 3.30pm on Tuesday.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 21*072827:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
