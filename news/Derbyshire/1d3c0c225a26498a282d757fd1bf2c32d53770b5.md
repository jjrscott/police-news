News

14:18 22/04/2020

Officers investigating the theft of a dumper truck in Dronfield are appealing for the public's help.

The vehicle, a one-ton yellow Thwaites dumper truck, was stolen from a property in Carr Lane, Dronfield Woodhouse, at 7.40pm on Thursday 16 April.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who was in the area at the time - in particular any drivers with dashcam that may have captured the incident or the moments after.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*196012 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
