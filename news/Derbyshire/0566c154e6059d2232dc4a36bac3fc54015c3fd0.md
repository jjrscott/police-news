12:11 15/04/2020

Officers investigating the theft of a Volkswagen Transporter van are appealing for the public's help to find those responsible.

The incident occurred in Linkmel Road, Langley Mill, between 11am and noon on Wednesday 8 April.

The Mercedes car transporter pictured pulled up in front of the Volkswagen and loaded the van on to the back. The Mercedes is then believed to have headed into Nottinghamshire.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who witnessed the incident - in particular drivers with dashcam footage - as well as anyone who may have information about the whereabouts of the Volkswagen or the car transporter.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference number 20*182662, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
