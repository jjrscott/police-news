09:53 03/04/2020

Officers searching for missing man Hayden Marshall would like to re-appeal for anyone with information to contact us.

Hayden (28) was last seen in Derby city centre at around 1.15am on Tuesday 17 December. He was wearing a checked blue and white shirt, blue jeans and a black leather jacket when last seen.

Our investigation to find Hayden remains open and we would ask for people to remain aware and to get in touch should they be able to assist with our enquiries.

Anyone with information should contact us quoting reference 20*674880.
