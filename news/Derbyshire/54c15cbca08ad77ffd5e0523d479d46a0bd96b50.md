News

13:47 13/08/2020

Officers investigating a card skimming fraud want to speak to these men in relation the crime.

The victim, a 27-year-old woman, was using an ATM at the Co-op in Grasmere Road, Long Eaton, when her card was swallowed by the machine on the afternoon of Sunday 9 August.

After contacting her bank, she was informed that a number of a transactions had taken place using the card in the Long Eaton area.

Officers want to speak to the men pictured in connection with the incident.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*412232 in any correspondence:
