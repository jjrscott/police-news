11:08 28/11/2019

An 81-year-old woman has died following a collision in Darley Bridge, near Matlock.

Officers were called to an incident in Main Road, Darley Bridge, at around 2pm on Friday, 15 November.

The woman, who was a pedestrian, suffered multiple, serious injuries as a result of the collision with the vehicle. She was taken to the Queens Medical Centre where she died on Monday, 25 November.

Our thoughts are with her family at this time.

The investigation into the incident is ongoing and there have been no arrests.
