13:17 17/12/2019

We're concerned for the safety of Hayden Marshall who last seen in Derby Market Place at around 1.15am this morning (Tuesday 17 December).

Hayden, who is 28, is described as white, 6ft 1ins tall and of slim build. He has short dark hair and a beard, and sometimes wears glasses. Hayden was wearing a checked blue and white shirt, blue jeans and a black leather jacket when last seen.

If you have seen Hayden or know where he may be, please contact us quoting reference number 23 of 17 December.
