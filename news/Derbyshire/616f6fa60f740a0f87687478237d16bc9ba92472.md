15:47 18/02/2020

We're appealing for information after a robbery at a hair salon in Alfreton.

Officers investigating the robbery have released an image of two men they would like to speak with about it.

At around 6.15pm on Tuesday 11 February two men entered the salon in High Street and demanded money from a member of staff. The men left with a quantity of cash.

If you recognise the men in the image, or have any other information, please contact us quoting reference number 20*081446.
