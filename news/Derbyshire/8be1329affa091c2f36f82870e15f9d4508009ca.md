News

16:03 24/03/2021

Officers are appealing for help finding a missing 16-year-old boy who was last seen in Derby.

Officers concerned for missing 16-year-old boy in Derby

Hoang Nguyen was last seen to run out of the Aldi store in Alvaston, on to Harvey Road, just after 11 am on Sunday 21 March. He left in the direction of The Blue Peter pub.

Hoang is Vietnamese and speaks very little English. He is described as being around 5ft 5in, slim, with short black hair, with a golden earring in a loop on his left ear.

He had been wearing black trainers and white socks, black tracksuit bottoms with white stripes down them, a tracksuit top and a colourful face mask.

Anyone who may have seen Hoang, or with any information about his whereabouts, is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, with reference 349-210321:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
