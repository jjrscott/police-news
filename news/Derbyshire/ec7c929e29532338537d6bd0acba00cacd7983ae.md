News

13:30 17/03/2021

Have you recently lost a bag in the Kingsway area of Derby?

We're appealing to trace the owner of a black bag which was handed in to police.

It was found in the Sainsbury's car park at the Kingsway Retail Park on Friday 12 March. Inside the bag was a large amount of cash.

If you've recently lost a bag, or if you think it may be yours, please contact us on any of the following methods, quoting reference 461 of 12 March.
