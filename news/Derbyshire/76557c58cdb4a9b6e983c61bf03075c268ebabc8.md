16:45 18/04/2019

Officers investigating the theft of a bank card from an elderly man have released an image of a man they would like to speak to about the incident.

The incident is thought to have happened sometime between Thursday, January 31 and Saturday, February 2 and the bank card was taken from the coat pocket of the victim, in the Royal Derby Hospital.

Following the theft, the card was used to make a number of transactions at pubs and shops.

Officers have been following a number of lines of enquiry but would now like to ask for the public's help to find the man pictured, as they believe he may be able to aid them in their investigation.

If you recognise the man, or have any other information, please contact us quoting reference number 19*66384.

Please reference the name of the officer in the case, PC Francesca Dolby, in any correspondence.
