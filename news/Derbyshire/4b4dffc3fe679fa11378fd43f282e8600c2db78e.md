09:54 10/02/2020

Officers investigating the theft of a quantity of cash from an elderly woman have released images of two women they would like to speak in connection with the incident.

The victim was shopping in Derby on the afternoon of Thursday 9 January between midday and 2.30pm with the cash stored in her shopping trolley.

On returning home she realised the money had gone.

Officers have been following a number of lines of enquiry and are now seeking your help to identify the women pictured.

Do you recognise these women, or do you have any other information which may be useful to officers?

If so, please contact us quoting reference number 20*022674 and the name of the officer in the case PC Matt O'Neill.
