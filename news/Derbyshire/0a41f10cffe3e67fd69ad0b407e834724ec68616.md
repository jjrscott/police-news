News

13:09 20/08/2020

We're appealing for information following a serious collision on the A38 between Ripley and the Watchorn island at 5.20am today (Thursday 20 August).

The collision involved a cyclist and an HGV.

The cyclist, a 44-year-old man, has been taken to the Queens Medical Centre, Nottingham, with serious injuries.

The driver of the HGV, a 34-year-old man, has been arrested on suspicion of causing serious injury by dangerous driving.

The northbound carriageway of the road remains closed at this time (1pm) and we would advise people to use alternative routes. The southbound carriageway is now open.

Any drivers who witnessed the incident, in particular anyone with dashcam footage that captured the collision or the moments before or after, should contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*433673 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
