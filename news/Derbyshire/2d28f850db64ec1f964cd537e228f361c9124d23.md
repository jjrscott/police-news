14:06 27/05/2019

A joint investigation into the cause of the Church Gresley fire has been launched with Derbyshire Fire & Rescue Service

Last night, Sunday, May 26, emergency services were called to Church Lane in Church Gresley to reports of a large fire.

When the fire service arrived they found a large pallet fire. Our officers attended to assist keeping local resident's safe and putting a cordon in place.

A joint investigation into the cause of the fire has now been launched with Derbyshire Fire & Rescue Service.

Anyone with information relating to the fire is asked to contact us on 101 quoting incident number 1147 of May 26.
