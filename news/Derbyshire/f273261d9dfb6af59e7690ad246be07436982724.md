News

16:45 22/12/2020

Officers investigating the theft of a gold chain from a shop in Derby want to speak to these two men in relation to the incident.

At around noon on Saturday 19 December a gold necklace was stolen from a jewellery shop in Sadler Gate, Derby.

The two men pictured were in the area at the time of the offence and officers want to speak to them in relation to the offence.

They are also keen to hear from anyone who recognises either of the men.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference number 20*670000:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
