A man has been charged with drugs offences and possession of a bladed weapon, after a stop and search was carried out.

Jack Brown, 18, of Welshpool Road, Derby, has been charged with possession with intent to supply heroin and crack cocaine and possession of a bladed weapon. A lock knife was also discovered during the search in James Close, Derby.

He has been remanded into custody and is set to appear at court on 21 January 2020.
