15:29 02/09/2019

Do you recognise the man in this picture? Officers are keen to speak with him about a burglary in Staveley on Thursday, 22 August.

It is reported that at around 4.15pm that day, two men entered a dental practice in Church Street and whilst one distracted the receptionist the other stole an iPhone from a staff room.

The men are described as Asian.

If you have any information about the incident, or you know the man in the image, please contact us quoting reference number 19*447733 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Ryan Gill.
