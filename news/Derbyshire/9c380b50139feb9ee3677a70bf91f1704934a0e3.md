10:42 04/01/2020

A woman has been assaulted while out jogging in Spondon.

She was in Brunswood Recreation Ground at around 8.30pm yesterday (Friday 3 January), on the path that runs between Reader Street and Rudyard Avenue, when she was approached from behind and assaulted.

She has told officers she saw someone walking their dog at around the time she was assaulted. We want to speak to that dog walker in case they have witnessed anything.

The park is closed this morning while we carry out inquiries.

We want to hear from witnesses, or anyone with information about the incident. You can contact us via the following methods, quoting reference 1083-030120.

•Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
