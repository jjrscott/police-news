12:34 14/03/2020

Four people have been arrested by officers investigating a stabbing in Derby.

We were called to Breadsall Hilltop at about 11.20pm last night (Friday 13 March) to reports of a disturbance at Cambourne Close.

Officers attended and found a 29-year-oldman in nearby Roe Farm recreation ground with a stab wound to his abdomen.

The man remains in hospital in a stable condition.

Two women aged 22 and 27 have been arrested in connection with the incident along with a 14-year-old boy and a 26-year-old man.

Officers remain in the area today carrying out inquiries.
