10:50 11/03/2021

We are appealing for information to help trace a man who exposed himself in a park in Buxton.

Officers were called to the park in Byron Street at about 3.30pm on Thursday 4 March to reports of a man exposing himself near a bench.

He is described as white, in his 30s and of heavy build. We wore a grey coat and grey jogging bottoms. He had an orange carrier bag.
