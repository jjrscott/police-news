News

10:52 02/10/2020

Officers are appealing for the public's help after a burglary in Derby.

The incident took place in Queensbury Chase, Littleover, Derby, between 10.30am and 11.30am on Wednesday 30 September.

Two men entered the property and stole gold from inside.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who recognises the two people pictured or the black VW Golf which was seen in the area at the time.

Anyone has any information, please get in touch using one of the non-emergency methods below, quoting reference number 20*515087:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
