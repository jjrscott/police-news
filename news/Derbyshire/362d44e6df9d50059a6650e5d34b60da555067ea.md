21:35 17/02/2020

A woman has been charged with murder after a man was found dead at a property in Derby.

On Saturday, 15 February we were called to a house on Manchester Street at 1.10pm after reports that the body of a man had been found.

A murder investigation was launched after a man was found dead inside the property.

Magdalena Kissova, aged 47 of Manchester Street will appear at Southern Derbyshire Magistrates Court tomorrow.
