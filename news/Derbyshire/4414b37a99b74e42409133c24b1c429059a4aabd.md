News

15:12 26/05/2020

We are appealing for witnesses after a collision yesterday (Monday, 25 May) involving a car and motorbike in Tansley.

The collision happened at around 3.15pm on Alfreton Road, The Cliff, Tansley, when a motorbike travelling towards Alfreton, was in collision with a VW Golf car.

The motorcyclist received chest and head injuries and was taken to the Queen's Medical Centre where he is currently stable but sedated.

We have started an investigation in to the collision and would like to speak to anyone who was in the area at the time or has dash-cam footage.

If you have any information, please contact Sergeant Darren Parkin, quoting reference number 2*263771.

Please ensure any video footage is downloaded to a separate, secure device ready for officers to view.
