08:56 31/03/2020

We are appealing for witnesses to a burglary at a post office in Langley Mill.

Sometime between 8.30pm and 9pm on Saturday 21 March, a burglar broke into the post office in Station Road through the back door.

They attacked the till, taking some cash before leaving over the railway bridge and possibly onto North Street.

We want to hear from witnesses or anyone who lives in the area who has CCTV footage which may have captured the burglar.

Witnesses or anyone ­­­­­with information should contact us quoting reference 20*157072.
