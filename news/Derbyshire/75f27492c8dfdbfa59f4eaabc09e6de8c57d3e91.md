18:16 25/09/2019

We are concerned for the safety of Marsha Jefford who is missing from her home in Derby.

Marsha was last seen in Norwood Close, Mackworth, at around 2.40pm and has links to the village of Milford and the Markeaton area of Derby.

Marsha is around 5ft 6ins tall and was last seen wearing a black top, black leggings and a fluffy burgundy jacket. She drives a black Vauxhall Astra with a number plate that begins PF57.

Anyone with any information about Marsha's whereabouts should contact Derbyshire police using the 999 number immediately.
