News

14:48 15/05/2019

Can you identify these two people? Officers would like to speak to them regarding a break-in at Swadlincote Snowsports Centre.

The incident is reported to have happened at around 2am on Tuesday, April 23.

Two intruders entered the site on Sir Herbert Wragg Way and broke into one of the sheds, causing damage to the lock and a shed panel. They then removed equipment from the shed and used it on the slopes.

Nothing was reported as being stolen.

If you recognise either of the two people pictured on CCTV, or have any information which may help with our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19000205880 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Lauren Alderson, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
