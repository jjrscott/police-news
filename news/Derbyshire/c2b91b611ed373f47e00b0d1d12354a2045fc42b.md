News

16:30 21/09/2020

We are releasing an image of a man we would like to speak to after two men were verbally abusive at a takeaway in Derby.

CCTV appeal to trace man in connection with abuse at a Derby takeaway

The incident happened at around 12.30am on Saturday 5 September at Sarrys on Cheapside.

Do you recognise the man in the image or have any information which could help with our inquiries?

If so, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 20*463848 and name of the officer in the case, PC Andrew Smith, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
