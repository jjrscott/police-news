09:54 04/08/2019

Emergency services have arranged a further meeting for residents of Whaley Bridge where they can be updated on the current situation regarding the Toddbrook Reservoir.

This is due to be held at 5pm on Sunday, 4 August at Chapel School, Long Lane, Chapel-en-le-Frith, High Peak, SK23 0TQ.

Representatives from the police, fire & rescue service, Office of the Police and Crime Commissioner and the Canal and River Trust will be in attendance.

It is crucial that emergency services have contact details of all evacuated residents. If residents are not able to attend the meeting we would ask that they ring the helpline number on 01629 533190 from 9am-10pm.
