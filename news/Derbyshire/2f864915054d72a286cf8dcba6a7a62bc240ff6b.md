14:50 10/02/2020

We have launched a murder investigation after a man who was allegedly assaulted in Chesterfield later died in hospital.

We issued an appeal for information on Saturday 8 February following an incident which took place at Vibe, in Holywell Street, at around 3.50am that morning.

A 23-year-old man was allegedly struck over the head with a bottle.

He was taken to hospital but sadly died at 11.30pm last night (Sunday 9 February).

In light of his death, we have launched a murder inquiry. The 24-year-old man who was arrested on Saturday remains in custody.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact us via the below methods quoting reference 20*074751;
