News

16:32 13/01/2020

Officers are concerned for the safety of missing man Mohammed Ishtyaq.

Mohammed was last seen at the scene of a collision on Swarkestone Bridge at 10.45pm on Saturday, 11 January. It is unknown if he suffered injuries as a result of the collision and officers are concerned for his wellbeing.

The 37-year-old was last seen wearing a blue jacket, black trainers and a grey tracksuit - he was also wearing a gold ring with a green flag on it.

Mohammed, who is from the Normanton area of Derby, is described as 5ft 5ins tall, of stocky build and has black hair.
