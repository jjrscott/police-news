12:45 11/09/2019

We were called at 3.45pm yesterday (Tuesday, 10 September) after a passer-by found a woman and her dog being attacked by cows, in a field in Sheldon, near to Magpie Mine.

Officers attended, as did an air ambulance, and the woman was taken to hospital in Sheffield, where she remains in a critical condition. Her dog had been killed by the cows.
