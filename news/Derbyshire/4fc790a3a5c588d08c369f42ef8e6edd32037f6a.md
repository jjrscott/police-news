09:58 21/09/2019

Officers investigating an arson in Ripley last night (Friday, 20 September) are appealing for anyone with information to come forward.

Derbyshire Fire and Rescue Service attended an address in Outram Court, where there was a fire in the door way of a property. Fire investigations determined that the source of the fire was likely to be deliberate.

The incident is believed to have happened at around 10.50pm.

If you were in the area at the time and witnessed anything suspicious, or live or work nearby and have CCTV installed, please contact us quoting reference number 19*503789.
