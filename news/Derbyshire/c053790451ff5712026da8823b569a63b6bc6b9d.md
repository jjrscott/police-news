10:13 07/03/2020

We were called to an address in Town Street, Sandiacre last night, Friday, 6 March, at around 10pm to reports of a man lying in the street.

On arrival a man was found and was pronounced dead at the scene.

An investigation has been launched into the circumstances surrounding his death and the man is yet to be formally identified.

A 42-year-old man has been arrested and remains in police custody.
Further detail will be released when available and the investigation is in its early stages.

Anyone with information that would help our enquiries is asked to contact us using the following methods and quoting incident number 1327 of 6 March.

•Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
•Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
•Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
