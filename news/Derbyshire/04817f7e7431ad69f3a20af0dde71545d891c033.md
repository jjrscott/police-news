News

16:00 12/11/2020

Officers investigating an incident on the West Bars roundabout in Chesterfield are appealing for witnesses and dashcam footage to assist in their enquiries.

The incident occurred at around 3pm on the West Bars roundabout. A woman in a black Vauxhall Insignia was threatened by a man travelling in a green Range Rover - which has then driven off in the direction of Markham Road.

Anyone who was in the area at the time and may have dashcam footage of the incident is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*592969 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
