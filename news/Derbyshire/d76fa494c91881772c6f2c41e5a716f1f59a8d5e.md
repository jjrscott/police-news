16:37 21/02/2020

A 29-year-old man from Leicestershire has been charged with numerous offences after he was searched in his vehicle in Derby city centre.

Nasir Habib of King Street, Loughborough was searched by officers on Netherclose Street and a quantity of drugs and weapons were found.

He was charged with possession with intent to supply Class A drugs and possession of an offensive weapon and he will appear for the alleged offences at Derby Crown Court on 12 March.
