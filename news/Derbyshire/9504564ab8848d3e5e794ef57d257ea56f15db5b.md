News

14:15 02/07/2019

Do you recognise this man? Officers investigating a theft from a newsagent in Normanton Road, Derby have released CCTV images of a man they would like to identify.

A man entered the shop and stole a dispenser containing a large number of scratch cards. He left on foot towards Hartington Street in the direction of Osmaston Road.

He is described as being white, around 6ft tall and of slim build with distinctive tattoos on his wrists.

The theft was reported at around 7.50pm on Monday, May 13. Since the incident, officers have been following a number of lines of enquiry, but are now seeking the public's help to identify the man pictured.

If you recognise him, have been offered a quantity of scratch cards for sale or have any information which may assist with our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19000244191 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Atkin, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
