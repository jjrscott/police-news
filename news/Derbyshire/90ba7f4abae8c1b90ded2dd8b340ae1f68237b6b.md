News

16:03 22/11/2019

Officers are concerned for the safety of missing 14-year-old Kyra Priest Levett.

Kyra was last seen in Glossop at 10pm on Tuesday, 19 November, and is 5ft 5ins tall with dark brown hair and brown eyes.

It is believed that Kyra is currently in the Derby area.

Anyone who may have seen Kyra, or has information about her current whereabouts, is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods with reference 200-201119:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
