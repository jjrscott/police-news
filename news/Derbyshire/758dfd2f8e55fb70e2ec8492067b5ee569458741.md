News

13:56 11/02/2021

Officers are appealing for information after eight dachshunds were stolen from a property in Burton Road, Acresford, near Swadlincote.

Three male and five female dogs, which are all miniature wire-haired dachshunds, were taken from the property between 11.30pm and midnight (10 February).

To view a gallery of images of the dogs click here.

Anyone with any information about the whereabouts of the dogs is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 21*084142:
