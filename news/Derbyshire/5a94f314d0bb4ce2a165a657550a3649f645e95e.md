19:15 26/10/2019

A 31-year-old man from Allenton has been charged with firearms offences.

 Michael Paul Hamblett-Sewell of Marlborough Road, was arrested on Thursday evening (24 October) after an alleged incident in which officers stopped a car in Harvey Road, Allenton and recovered a suspected firearm.

He was charged with possessing a firearm and possessing prohibited ammunition last night (Friday 25 October) and has been remanded into prison custody.

He is due to appear at court on Monday 25 November.

A 37-year-old man who was also arrested on Thursday has been released under investigation.
