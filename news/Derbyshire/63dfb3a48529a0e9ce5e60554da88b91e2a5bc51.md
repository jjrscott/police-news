10:29 09/01/2020

Officers investigating the assault of woman in Derby want to speak to this man in connection with the incident.

The woman was on a night out in Derby city centre when an assault is alleged to have taken place, resulting in minor injuries.

The man pictured was seen in the area at the time of the offence, which took place in the early hours of 8 September, 2019. Since that date officers have been undertaking a number of lines of enquiry but these have proved negative.

Anyone with any information about the man pictured should contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 19000479694 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
