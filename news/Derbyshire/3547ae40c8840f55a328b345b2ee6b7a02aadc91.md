17:17 08/03/2021

We're concerned for the safety of Emma Priest who is missing from her home in Ripley.

Emma, who is 37, was last seen at her home in Argyll Road on 27 February.

She is described as 5ft 2ins tall, and of slim build with shoulder length dark brown hair.

She is known to spend time in Derby city centre and Ripley.

If you have seen Emma or know where she may be, please contact us quoting reference number 404 of 4 March.
