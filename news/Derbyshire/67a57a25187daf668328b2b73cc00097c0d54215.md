14:12 10/06/2019

We are appealing for information following an armed robbery in Breaston.

At around 4.30pm yesterday afternoon (09.06.19) a man entered the Co-op in Main Street, Breaston.

The threatened staff and demanded cash and cigarettes. He made off in a silver Renault Daily van, registration LV09 OVZ, which drove off down Blind Lane towards Stevens Lane.

This van was stolen from a property in Long Eaton overnight from around 10pm on June 3.

Detectives are keen to speak to anyone who may have seen the van parked up between June 4 and June 9 - as well as anyone with any dashcam or CCTV from around the Breaston area.

If you have video footage of the incident please ensure that you download and keep safe the video for an officer to view.

Anyone with any information that can help detectives with their enquiries should get in contact immediately, either directly with the force, or via Crimestoppers.

In any correspondence please include reference 19*296695.
