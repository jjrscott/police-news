News

17:56 21/12/2020

Officers searching for missing Joshua Jones have ruled out a man hitchhiking in the Matlock area as being Joshua.

Following Joshua leaving his home in Hurst Rise at 11.30am on Saturday 12 December the force has received multiple calls regarding a man matching his description hitchhiking towards Chesterfield that afternoon.

Following extensive enquiries, officers have now been able to rule out the hitchhiker as being Joshua.

Joshua, who was seen on CCTV in Matlock town centre at the Boyes shop at around noon on 12 December, was last seen wearing a pair of blue jeans, a grey Superdry coat which is darker on the sleeves with a hood that is bright green on the inside.

Officers are still very keen to hear from anyone who may have information, no matter how small, in relation to Joshua's disappearance and would ask anyone with details to contact the force using any of the below methods, quoting reference 518 of 12 December:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
