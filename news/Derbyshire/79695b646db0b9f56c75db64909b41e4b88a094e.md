News

13:23 01/05/2019

Officers attended Bailey Street in Normanton at around 10.30pm last night (Tuesday, April 30) to reports of an assault.

It was reported that a man was assaulted by two men whilst walking along the street.

A group of men then arrived in a Volkswagen Golf, got out of the vehicle and assaulted another man and a woman. None of the assaulted people required hospital treatment.

Officers pursued the Golf which collided with another vehicle at the junction of Reginald Street and Sale Street.

Two men were detained and arrested (a 26-year-old and a 31 year-old) and a further man ran from the scene.

If you have any information which may assist with our enquiries or dash cam footage, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.
