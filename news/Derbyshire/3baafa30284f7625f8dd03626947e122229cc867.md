13:49 19/11/2020

We are releasing an image of watches, stolen during in a burglary, in a bid to find them and return them to their rightful owner.

At around 6.30pm on Friday 30 October, a burglar broke into a house in Manor Road, Littleover and stole a collection of eight watches.

The stolen watches are, six of which are pictured here;

 * A Breitling watch
 * A men's rose gold Rolex with a black face
 * A men's Armani gold watch with a black face
 * A Diesel watch in matt black
 * A gold Michael Kors ladies watch
 * A ladies gold Rolex watch with flowers on a white face
 * A men's silver Armani watch
 * A ladies silver Armani watch

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact us quoting reference 2*576725;
