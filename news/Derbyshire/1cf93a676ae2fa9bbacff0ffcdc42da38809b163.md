17:35 28/01/2021

We're appealing for help to identify the person in this picture. It's in connection with an alleged robbery in Stenson Fields, Derby.

Appeal following alleged robbery in Stenson Fields, Derby

The incident happened around 1.40pm on Saturday 23 January at an underpass on Grampian Way in Stenson Fields, near to the Grampian pub.

A teenager was allegedly threatened and a speaker was stolen.

Officers are particularly keen to trace someone who has passed by a few moments before the incident and may have noticed who was in the area.

Do you have any ideas who the person in the image could be, or were you in the area?

If you have any information which you think could help with our inquiries, please contact us on the methods below quoting reference 21*50917 and the name of the officer in the case Temporary DC Hannah Price.
