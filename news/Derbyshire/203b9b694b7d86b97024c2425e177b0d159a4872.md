News

17:37 11/01/2021

We're concerned for the welfare of 17-year-old Jordan Opoku-Baah who is missing from his home in Derby.

Jordan Opoku-Baah

Jordan was last seen leaving his home in Normanton at 3pm on Friday 8 January.

Jordan is described as black, 5ft 8ins tall with short braided hair.

If you have seen Jordan, or know where he may be, please contact us using the below methods.

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
