16:17 21/08/2020

The incident happened at around 3pm on Wednesday 5 August in Crewton Way, when a blue Audi A4 collided with a 25-year-old woman.

The woman attended hospital and was discharged the same day. Her injuries were not described as serious.

A 24-year-old man from Derby was arrested on suspicion of assault occasioning actual bodily harm (ABH) and was later released on police bail.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time and may have witnessed the incident. They are also keen to speak to anyone who may have dash cam footage or private CCTV footage.

If you believe you have footage which may be of use to officers please ensure it is downloaded to a separate, secure device ready for them to view.
