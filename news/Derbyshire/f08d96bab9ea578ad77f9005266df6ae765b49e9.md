News

13:30 11/08/2020

We've released a CCTV image of a man we would like to speak to in connection with an incident in Chesterfield where a woman was allegedly spat at.

Can you help us trace this man?

It happened outside a store on Derby Road, Birdholme, at around 1.10pm on Saturday 25 July. A woman was allegedly verbally abused by a man who then spat at her and her car.

Do you recognise this man or did you see the incident taking place?

If you have any information which could help with our inquiries please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 20*382437 and name of the officer in the case, PC Dave Cropper, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
