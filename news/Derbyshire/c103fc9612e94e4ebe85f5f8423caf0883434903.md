09:47 09/07/2019

We are appealing for information about two people who we want to speak to in relation to the robbery of an opticians in Oakwood.

At around 2.10am on Monday, June 24, the front window of the Oakwood Eye Care store in Oakwood District Centre, Danebridge Crescent, was smashed and a man and a woman entered the store.

A considerable number of glasses were stolen by the pair who exited through the front window.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who may have been in the area at the time and have any information that could assist with enquiries is asked to contact Derbyshire police using the below methods - please include reference 19000325175 in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
