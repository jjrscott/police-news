13:51 21/01/2020

We are appealing for witnesses to a suspicious incident in Ripley in which a man was assaulted.

Shortly after 4pm on Monday 20 January, two men were in Ash Crescent when a black Ford Mondeo ST hatchback with a 55-plate pulled up.

Three men got out and approached the two men, assaulting one of them. The group then got back into the car and drove off.

The victim suffered facial injuries.

Witnesses, or anyone who saw the Mondeo in the area at the time, should contact us via the below methods, quoting reference 20*037465;

•Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary

•Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact

•Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.

•Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
