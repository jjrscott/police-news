News

11:06 26/06/2019

Do you recognise this woman? Officers would like to speak to her following a substantial theft from the Derby County Megastore at Pride Park.

On Monday, June 10, at around 1pm, a woman entered the Megastore, selected a number of football shirts and left the shop without paying.

She was seen driving away from Pride Park in a black Vauxhall Insignia.

The woman pictured was seen in the shop at the time. If you recognise her, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19000298245 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Zoe Milner, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
