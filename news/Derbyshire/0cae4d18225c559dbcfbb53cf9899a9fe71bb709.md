16:33 22/10/2020

A convict from the West Midlands has absconded from HMP Sudbury.

Curtis Paul Douglas, also known as Paul Curtis, left the open prison today.

The 34-year-old was convicted at Birmingham Crown Court in 2011 and sentenced to life for wounding with intent.

Douglas is white, 5ft 10ins tall and of medium build. He has brown eyes and brown hair and has a Celtic tattoo on his right arm.

He has links to the Great Barr, Erdington and Minworth areas of Birmingham.

If you see Douglas, or know where he is, you are advised not to approach him but to contact us immediately quoting reference 491 of October 22;
