News

14:32 17/07/2019

Officers investigating a burglary in Sinfin have released CCTV images of two men they would like to speak to in connection with the incident.

We were called to reports of a burglary at a business unit on Sinfin Commercial Park, Sinfin Lane at around 1am on Sunday, June 23. A large quantity of computer ink was stolen.

CCTV footage from the time of the incident shows several men loading boxes into a white Mercedes van.

If you recognise either of the men pictured, or have any further information that may help our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19000323525 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Lindsay Rose, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
