13:27 15/08/2019

Officers investigating a number of car break-ins want to speak to this man after he was caught on CCTV.

The man, who is around 5ft 8ins tall, can be seen trying car doors along High Street, Newhall, on CCTV captured at 3.45am on Friday, 2 August.

As he leaves the scene of the crime he can be seen wearing a grey DC baseball cap which had been stolen from one of the vehicles.

Anyone who recognises the man, or who has any information about the incident, should contact Derbyshire police using one of the following methods - including reference 19*405903 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
