News

12:00 23/01/2021

It's after the car allegedly collided with a van on the westbound carriageway, near to the junction with Cole Lane, just before 7.45am on Tuesday 19 January.

The driver of the vehicle, which is believed to have been a red Skoda, did not stop at the scene and our officers would like to trace them.

We would like to hear from anyone who may have seen the incident, or with dashcam footage shortly before or after the incident.

If you have any information which you think could help with our inquiries please contact us using one of the following methods.

Please quote reference number 21*043586 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Ryan Barrell.
