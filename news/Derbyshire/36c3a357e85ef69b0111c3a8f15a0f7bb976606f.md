12:04 04/02/2020

We are seeking witnesses to an incident in Allenton where scrap metal is alleged to have been stolen and criminal damage caused to a van.

Officers are investigating allegations that an altercation took place between a group of men at the junction of Jubalton Close and Upper Moor Road over scrap metal.

A van was then damaged.

The incident happened between 9.25am and 10am on Friday 31 December.

Were you in the area at the time? Did you witness anything or do you have dash cam footage?

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact PC Matthew Ward via the following methods, quoting reference 20*058652:

•Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary

•Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
