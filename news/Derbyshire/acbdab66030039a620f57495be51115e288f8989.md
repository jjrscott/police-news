11:34 13/08/2020

We are releasing an image of a man we would like to speak to after a bus driver was allegedly racially abused.

At around 9am on Saturday 27 June, an Arriva bus driver collected a passenger in Derby city centre. The driver alleges that the passenger racially abused him and got off on Stockbrook Road.

The man we want to speak to is white, in his 50s or 60s, about 6ft tall and wore a checked shirt and white or cream trousers.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact us via the below methods quoting reference 20*327297;

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
