10:45 06/11/2020

A 28-year-old man has been charged with a number of alleged drug and firearms offences.

Marlon Dean was arrested by officers after a search of a black Audi A3 in a layby off the A516 Etwall Road on Monday, 3 November found a loaded handgun, a lock knife and a quantity of suspected cannabis. A search of Mr Dean at the scene also found a substantial amount of cash.

After his arrest, a further search of Mr Dean, of no fixed abode, found quantities of suspected heroin and cocaine.

He was subsequently charged with:

 * Possession of a firearm
 * Possession of prohibited ammunition
 * Possession with intent to supply a controlled Class A drug
 * Possession of a Class B controlled drug
 * Possession of a knife in a public place
 * Possession of criminal property

He was remanded in custody and appeared at Southern Derbyshire Magistrates' Court on Wednesday 4 November where he was remanded to prison and will next appear at Derby Crown Court on 2 December.
