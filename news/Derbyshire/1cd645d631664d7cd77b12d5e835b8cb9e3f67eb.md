10:32 17/04/2019

Officers were called to attend a report of a serious collision at 9.15am this morning in Derby Road, Sandiacre.

The following roads are currently closed and will remain so for the foreseeable future whilst accident investigation work takes places.

Bostocks Lane to the junction of Derby Road

King Edward Street

Derby Road to the junction with Town Street

Longmoor Lane

Traffic diversions are currently in place via Town Street and Stapleford, however motorists are advised to take alternative routes and avoid the area where possible.
