15:41 02/02/2021

A 53-year-old man has been charged after allegedly drink driving in Derby, on Monday evening.

Officers stopped a car in Uttoxeter New Road at around 9pm yesterday (1 February) after a receiving a report that a man had been seen drinking alcohol before getting in a car and driving off.

Joy Chacko, of Uttoxeter New Road, was arrested on suspicion of failing to provide a specimen at the roadside and was later charged with drink driving in custody.

He is due to appear at Southern Derbyshire Magistrates Court on 7 June.
