14:25 08/02/2020

A man has suffered a serious head injury after he was allegedly struck with a bottle.

We were called to Vibe in Holywell Street, Chesterfield, at around 3.50am today to reports that a 23-year-old man was injured.

He has been taken to hospital where he remains in a serious condition.

We have arrested a 24-year-old man in connection with the incident and he remains in custody.
