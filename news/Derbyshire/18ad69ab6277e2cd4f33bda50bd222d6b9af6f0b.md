15:47 09/04/2019

An 18-year-old man and two 16-year-old boys have been charged with wounding with intent by joint enterprise following an alleged incident in Derby last November, in which a man was found with serious injuries.

On November 25 2018, officers were called to the The Morledge in Derby city centre to reports that a man was injured. The man was taken to hospital with critical injuries but has since returned home where he continues to recover.

Keenan Carter, 18, of no fixed abode and the two teenage boys are due to appear at Southern Derbyshire Magistrates on Tuesday, April 30.
