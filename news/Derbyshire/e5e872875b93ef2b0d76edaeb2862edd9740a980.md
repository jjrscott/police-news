News

10:09 06/11/2020

Officers investigating an alleged assault in Ilkeston are appealing for witnesses to the incident to come forward to help with enquiries.

The incident took place between two men on the forecourt of the Co-op filling station in Ladywood Road at around 1.45pm on Sunday 1 November. There were no serious injuries as a result of the incident.

Anyone who was in the area at the time and witnessed the incident is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*576853:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
