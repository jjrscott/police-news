Witness appeal following altercation in Trent Meadows car park

Our officers are appealing for witnesses to an altercation at Trent Meadows picnic area at the weekend.

We were called after the alleged dispute between two men and a woman at about 1pm on Saturday 9 May.

It took place in the car park of the picnic site, which is off Meadow Lane in Long Eaton.

We are aware that there were several people in the car park at the time and are urging any witnesses who have not yet spoken to us to get in touch.

If you were there, please contact us using one of the following non-emergency methods and quote reference 20*235783.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
