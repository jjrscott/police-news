17:52 13/08/2020

We are trying to trace witnesses to a collision which left a motorcyclist injured.

Shortly before 11.20am on Thursday 13 August, a Kawasaki motorbike collided with a wall in Nottingham Road, Borrowash.

The man riding the bike suffered serious injuries and was taken to hospital.

Witnesses, or anyone who has dashcam of the incident or the motorbike prior to the crash, should contact us quoting reference 20*420565;
