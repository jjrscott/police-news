09:46 07/10/2019

One Swadlincote teenager has been found while another is still missing.

On Friday, we appealed for information about the whereabouts of Leon Penman and Jesse Sanders, who were last seen in the town on Thursday, 26 September.

Jesse (17) has been found safe and well in Bedfordshire.

Leon (15) is still missing. He is 5ft 4ins tall.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods with reference 1153-260919:

 * _Facebook_ - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * _Twitter_ - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * _Website_ - complete the online contact form _derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us_.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
