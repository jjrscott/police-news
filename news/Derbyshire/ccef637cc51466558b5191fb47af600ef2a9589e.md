13:12 24/02/2020

A car fire in Heanor is being treated as arson by detectives.

Firefighters and police officers were called to Eames Close at around 12.50am today to reports that a car was alight and flames had spread to a neighbouring house, damaging the windows.

A 29-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of arson. He remains in custody.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact us quoting reference number 20*104681.
