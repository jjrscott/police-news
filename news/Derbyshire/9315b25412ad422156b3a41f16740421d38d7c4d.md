News

17:43 11/03/2021

Officers are investigating reports of fake Rolex watches being sold on online selling sites.

Over the past five months, it is believed that a number of high-value Rolex watches have been sold to unsuspecting customers.

Anyone who has bought a Rolex in the past five months from an online marketplace is asked to first take the watch to a reputable watch specialist to verify its authenticity.

Anyone who believes they may have been sold a fake watch is asked to contact DC Thor Long using any of the below methods:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
