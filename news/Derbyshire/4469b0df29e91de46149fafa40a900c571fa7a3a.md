12:05 02/12/2019

A coach has left the carriageway and has struck a low wall on the A6 between Darley Dale and Rowsley, and the road is currently closed in both directions to allow vehicle recovery to take place.

The coach was transporting passengers, who have been checked over at the scene, but there are not believed to be any serious injuries.

Please avoid the area, taking alternative routes where possible. A further update will be posted once the road is reopened.
