News

17:26 25/09/2020

Officers investigating a collision that saw a telegraph pole felled are appealing for help tracking down those involved.

The incident in Whitemoor Lane took place at around 3.50am today (25 September) and saw a Fiat Punto collide with a telegraph pole before coming to rest on its side.

The driver and passenger both ran off from the vehicle and officers are keen to speak to anyone who may be able to identify either of those involved.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*504065 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
