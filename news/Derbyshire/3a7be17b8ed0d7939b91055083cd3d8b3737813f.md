09:27 24/02/2020

We are appealing for witnesses to a robbery at Darley Park.

At around 11pm on Wednesday 19 February, a 52-year-old man was walking through the park when he was approached by two men.

They threatened him and demanded his phone before stealing his phone, bus pass and earphones.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact us quoting reference number

20*095342 and the name of the officer in the case PC Ella West.
