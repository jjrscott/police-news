News

14:14 03/06/2019

We are appealing to the public for help, following a collision between a black Audi TT and a cyclist.

The incident happened around 4.30pm on Wednesday, May 15, outside the Dog and Duck pub, in London Road, Shardlow.

The driver of the Audi, described as a blonde-haired woman, stopped at the scene and spoke to the cyclist. However, she did not leave any contact details and we would like to trace her to help with our enquiries.

The cyclist, a 36-year-old woman, required hospital treatment for a leg injury.

If you witnessed the incident, or have any information which my help us identify the Audi driver, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19*249724 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Hiatt, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
