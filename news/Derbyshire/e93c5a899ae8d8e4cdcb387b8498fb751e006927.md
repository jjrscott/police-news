News
Safer Neighbourhood

17:02 21/01/2020

We are releasing a CCTV image of a man we would like to speak to after a man was assaulted at a shop in Ripley.

CCTV appeal after assault at a store in Ripley

The incident took place between 6.35pm and 6.50pm on Friday 10 January at the Co-op store on Derby Road.

A member of staff was kicked after trying to stop a man they thought had been trying to leave the store without paying for items.

We think the man in the picture may have information which could help with our inquiries. Do you recognise him?

If you think you can help please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 20*20321 and name of the officer in the case, PCSO Carl Mott, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
