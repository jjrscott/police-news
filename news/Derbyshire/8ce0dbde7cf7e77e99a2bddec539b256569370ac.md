14:04 03/05/2019

Officers want to speak to witnesses to an assault in Glossop.

The incident took place at around 8pm on Sunday, April 21, in Market Street.

A large group of young people were in the area with reports that a 14-year-old girl was assaulted by around four other youths.

It is understood that a number of people witnessed the incident - in particular we would like to speak to a couple who attempted to separate those involved.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or has any information, should contact us using one of the below methods - in any correspondence please include reference number: 19*202666.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
