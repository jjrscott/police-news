News

09:21 28/01/2020

Officers investigating a series of burglaries in the Normanton area of Derby earlier this month, have released an image of a man they would like to speak to.

Over a two-week period from Thursday, 9 January we believe around a dozen house burglaries in the area to be linked. Officers investigating these crimes would like to speak to the man in this image, taken Saturday, 18 January on Rowan Park Close, Derby.

Do you recognise him or perhaps the clothes that he is wearing?

If so please make contact with us using any of the following non-emergency contact methods, quoting reference number 20*30148:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org
