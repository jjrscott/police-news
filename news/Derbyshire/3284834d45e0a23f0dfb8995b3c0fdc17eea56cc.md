16:14 31/05/2019

Officers investigating a robbery in Matlock have released images of two men they would like to speak to about the incident.

The robbery is reported to have happened in High Tor Road, Matlock sometime between 2.45pm and 3pm on Friday, April 19.

A 29-year-old man and a 28-year-old woman were approached by two men who threatened them, before making off with a wallet and two iPhones.

The wallet contained a bank card which was later used in a number of shops in Belper.

The robbers are described a follows:

Robber 1 -- white, slim build and around 5ft 5ins tall with cropped dark hair and aged between late teens and early twenties. He was wearing a grey tracksuit with black on the bottoms.

Robber 2 - white, slim build around 5ft 8ins tall and of slim build. He was wearing a baseball cap with the hood pulled up and a tracksuit.

Officers are keen to speak with anyone who recognises the men pictured, or with any information about this incident.

Please quote reference number 19*199790 and the name of the officer in the case, Detective Constable Jane Cyples in any correspondence.
