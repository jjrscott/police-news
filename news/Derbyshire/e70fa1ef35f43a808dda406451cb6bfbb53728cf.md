News

09:31 25/12/2020

Detectives investigating an incident in which a woman was injured after being allegedly struck by a car in a Chesterfield street are appealing for witnesses to the incident to come forward.

Officers were called to reports that a woman had been struck by a vehicle in Derby Road, Chesterfield, at around 6.45pm on Wednesday 23 December.

The woman, 26, suffered minor injuries in the incident and a 27-year-old man from Chesterfield has been arrested and released on police bail.

A number of people witnessed the incident, a number of whom are pictured, and officers are keen to speak to them.

If you are one of the people, or you know who they may be, please contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*678155 in any correspondence:
