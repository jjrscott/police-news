News

12:09 13/09/2020

Officers investigating an alleged assault in Chesterfield are appealing for a witness to the incident to come forward.

The incident occurred at around midnight on Thursday 13 August in The Shambles, Chesterfield.

A woman in her 30s was allegedly assaulted by a man who was then chased by another man who witnessed the incident. Officers want to speak to this man as a witness to the incident.

A 49-year-old man from Chesterfield has been arrested on suspicion of causing actual bodily harm and released on police bail.

If you were the man who witnessed the incident, or know who he may be, please contact us using any of the below methods, including reference 20*461603 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
