19:16 16/02/2020

An investigation has been launched after a man was found dead at a property in Manchester Street, Derby yesterday afternoon.

We were called to report the incident at 1.10pm on Saturday (15 February).

An inquiry into the circumstances of the death is in its early stages and three people have been arrested in connection with the incident.

Detectives would like to hear from anyone who was in the area around the time and may have information that could assist them.

A scene remains in place around the property and officers will be visible in the area carrying out enquiries

Anyone with information should contact us quoting reference number 20*089287.
