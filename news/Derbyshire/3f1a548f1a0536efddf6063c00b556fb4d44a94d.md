15:57 27/01/2020

A man suffered a broken nose and cuts to his face and head during an alleged assault in Buxton.

At around 11.50pm on Monday 20 January, an 18-year-old man was assaulted in South Street by a group of men.

A 43-year-old man has been arrested and a 16-year-old boy voluntarily interviewed as part of the investigation.
