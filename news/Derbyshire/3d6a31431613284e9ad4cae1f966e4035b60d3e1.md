16:47 18/02/2020

A man who was found dead at a property in Derby at the weekend has been named.

David Allsopp, who was 67, was found at a property in Manchester Street at around 1.10pm on Saturday 15 February.

Following his death, a murder investigation was launched and a 47-year-old woman was arrested and charged with murder.

Magdalena Kissova, of Manchester Street, appeared at Southern Derbyshire Magistrates Court this morning and has been remanded into prison custody.

She is next due to appear at Derby Crown Court tomorrow morning (Wednesday 19 February).
