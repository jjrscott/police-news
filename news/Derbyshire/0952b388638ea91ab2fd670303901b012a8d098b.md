16:26 18/07/2019

We're attempting to trace a prisoner who absconded from Sudbury open prison yesterday at 12.30pm.

Steven Jones, 37, was convicted for burglary and a number of theft offences in July 2018 at Gloucester Crown Court.

He is described as white, 5ft 5ins tall and of slim build. He has blue eyes and brown hair and was clean shaven when last seen. He also has a birth mark on his neck.

Steven has links to Cheltenham.

If you have seen Steven, or know where he may be, please contact us quoting reference number 1039 of 17 July.
