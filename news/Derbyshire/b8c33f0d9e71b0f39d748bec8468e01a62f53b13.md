14:07 13/02/2020

Officers were called to a house in Long Eaton on Tuesday (11 February) after a young child was bitten by a dog.

Police and ambulance crews were called to a house in Beech Avenue at about 5.40pm.

A one-year-old girl was treated at the scene and then taken to hospital for further treatment. She remains in a serious condition.

The dog has been seized by officers and an assessment will be taking place to determine its breed.

Enquiries into the incident continue.
