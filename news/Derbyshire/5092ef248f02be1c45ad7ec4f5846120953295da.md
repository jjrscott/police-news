15:29 28/02/2020

A man who was arrested following the death of a man in Shirebrook has been released on police bail.

The 26-year-old was arrested on Tuesday morning (February 25) on suspicion of the murder of Andrew Jackson and has since been released on police bail pending further investigations.

Mr Jackson who was 55-years-old, was discovered at around 10am on Sunday 26 January at an allotment, off Prospect Drive.

Nine people have now been arrested and released on bail while enquiries continue.
