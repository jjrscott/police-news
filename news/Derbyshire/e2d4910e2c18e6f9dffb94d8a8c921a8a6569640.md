12:00 13/03/2021

Officers investigating the theft of two high-value bikes in Chapel-en-le-Frith are appealing for the public's help locating them.

Both the bikes were taken from homes in the town overnight between Saturday 6 March and Sunday 7 March.

One bike, a Specialized Status 2020 mountain bike, was stolen between 10.30pm on 6 March and 8am the following day from a shed in Beckets Wood.

A second mountain bike, a Trek Fuel Ex 15 2015, was stolen from a shed in Netherfield Road, between 1.30am and 11am on 7 March.

Officers investigating the incident are asking for anyone who may have any information, in particular CCTV or dashcam footage, that may be able to help with their enquiries to get in touch.

Anyone with information is asked to contact the force using any of the below methods with reference 21*130117 for the Trek bike stolen from Netherfield Road, and reference 21*129921 for the Specialized bike stolen from Beckets Wood.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
