News

16:30 10/07/2020

Officers investigating a burglary at a bar in Chesterfield are appealing for help to trace a man in connection with the incident.

The burglary took place at Monk Bar, Corporation Street, Chesterfield, 8 July at around 6.15am and a substantial sum of money - as well as a tip jar and iPad - were taken.

Officers are keen to speak to the man pictured in connection with the incident and, while the photos do not show a face, it is hoped that someone recognises the distinctive clothing.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using an of the below methods, including reference 20*349619 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
