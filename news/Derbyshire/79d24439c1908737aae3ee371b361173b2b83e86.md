News

10:45 01/05/2019

Officers investigating a burglary at Ashbourne Bowls Club, Wyaston Road, have released images of two men they would like to identify, in connection with the incident.

We were called to reports of a burglary at around 8am on Saturday, April 13. It is believed that the break-in happened between 12am on Friday, April 12 and 8am on April 13 and a small amount of cash was stolen.

If you recognise the men pictured, or you have any information which may assist with our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19000187574 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Debbie Holmes, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
