14:42 23/03/2020

We are treating a fire at a house in Brimington as arson.

Officers were called to a house in Cornwall Drive at around 3.30am today. The homeowner was woken by her smoke alarm and when she went to investigate, she found a lit object had been pushed through the letterbox.
