News

12:24 02/02/2021

An investigation is ongoing following a robbery at a store in Derby.

CCTV images released following a robbery at a store in Derby

At around 7.40 am on Friday 26 January, two men entered the Todays Local store on Elton Road. They pushed a member of staff out of the way and stole cash and cigarettes.

The offenders are believed to have left the shop and headed over a footbridge across the railway line and on to Balfour Road.

Officers are now releasing CCTV images of two men they would like to identify as they may be able to help with their inquiries.

Do you recognise these men or did you notice any suspicious activity in the area around the time?

If you have any information which could help our inquiries please contact us on the contact details below, with reference 21*54713 and the name of the officer in the case, Temporary DC Joseph Lovelock.
