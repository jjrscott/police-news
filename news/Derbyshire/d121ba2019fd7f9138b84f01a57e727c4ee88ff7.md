16:45 29/01/2020

Officers investigating a collision between a pedestrian and a car are appealing for two people understood to have witnessed the incident to come forward.

At around 4pm on Tuesday, 28 January, in Radbourne Lane, Derby, a collision between a VW Polo and a ten-year-old girl happened.

The girl suffered minor injuries as a result of the incident, which happened close to the junction with Samuel Road, and the driver of the VW has been spoken to by officers.

It is understood that two adults were at the scene and witnessed the incident. Officers are keen to speak to these two people and they should contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*053730:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
