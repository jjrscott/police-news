16:31 14/02/2020

A man was arrested on suspicion of robbery after allegedly stealing cash from a post office in Long Eaton before running off.

Police were called to the branch in College Street at about 10.30am today following reports that a man had gone in with a hammer.

Officers arrested a 51-year-old man a short time later.

He remains in police custody on suspicion of robbery.
