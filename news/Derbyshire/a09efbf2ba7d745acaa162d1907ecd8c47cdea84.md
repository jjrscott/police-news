13:55 23/11/2020

A business in Clay Cross has been broken into and a caravan stolen from inside.

Sometime between 5.30pm on Wednesday 18 November and 9am the next morning, thieves broke into Cross Car Sales, in High Street, Clay Cross.

They stole a Bailey Phoenix caravan, registration AV58 KCY.

We are releasing these images of the caravan in a bid to trace it and would urge anyone with information, or anyone who has been offered the caravan for sale, to come forward.

You can contact officer in the case PC Kirby Kern, quoting reference 20000612561;
