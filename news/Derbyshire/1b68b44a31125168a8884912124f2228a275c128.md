10:18 25/02/2020

An unusual bicycle is one of the items stolen during a burglary in Horsley Woodhouse.

The victim's 2016 GT Fury downhill bike was stolen - thought to be one of only about 15 that have been imported to the country.

The bike has a broken upper slider on the rear derailleur and two marks on the frame.

Several Ryobi tools were also taken during the burglary, which happened at a house in Fairfield Road sometime between 7pm on Monday 17 February and 6am the next day.

Witnesses or anyone who is offered the bike or tools for sale should contact us quoting reference number 20*93610 and the name of the officer in the case PC Grant Weller.
