News

14:46 07/12/2020

A joint police and fire investigation into the fire at All Saints' Church, Mackworth, is still ongoing and is yet to determine a cause.

The 17-year-old boy who was arrested on suspicion of arson has been released on police bail as enquiries continue.

Detectives are still keen to hear from anyone with any information that may be able to assist in their enquiries - especially any drivers in the area at the time who may have dashcam footage or homeowners with private CCTV.

Anyone who may have information about the incident is asked to contact Derbyshire police, using any of the below methods, including reference 20*639983:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
