17:01 19/07/2019

Officers investigating the theft of a motorbike from Chesterfield have released images of two men they'd like to speak to about the incident.

The incident happened at 7am on Friday, 12 July in Holland Road when a red Honda CRF motorbike was stolen.

The men pictured were in the area at the time and it is believed they may be able to assist officers with their enquiries.

If you recognise the men, or have any other information, please contact us quoting reference number 19*362749.
