News

15:00 19/01/2021

Can you help our officers as they investigate a report of an alleged indecent exposure? It happened around 1.30pm on Thursday 7 January at Alfreton Park.

The man was described as being white, with short grey hair and aged in his 40s. He had been wearing a lightweight brown coat and jeans and spoke with a Nottingham accent.

Did you notice this man, or witness the incident?

It is not known if there are any connections with an incident reported in October on Institute Lane, which an appeal was put out for yesterday (Monday 18 January).

If you have any information which could help with our inquiries please contact us on the methods below, quoting reference number 21*11881 and the name of the officer in the case PC Michael Jones.
