News

17:01 06/09/2019

Police have issued an appeal for witnesses after two men were assaulted during a disturbance in a Littleover street.

The incident happened outside flats in Cloudwood Close at about 7.45pm yesterday.

Two men were treated for injuries by ambulance crews at the scene and one was taken to hospital for further treatment.

Two men, both aged 52, were arrested in connection with the incident.

Officers investigating the nature of the incident are appealing to anyone who witnessed it to get in touch.

If you have any information which may assist with our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19*474497 in any correspondence.
