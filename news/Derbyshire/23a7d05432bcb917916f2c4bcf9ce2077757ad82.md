19:41 13/09/2020

A man is in hospital and another is in custody this evening following an alleged assault in Newton.

We were called at 4.30pm on Saturday 12 September to reports that two men had had an altercation on the playing fields off Town Lane and that one man had been assaulted.

A 65-year-old man is in hospital following the incident and a 51-year-old man has been arrested.
