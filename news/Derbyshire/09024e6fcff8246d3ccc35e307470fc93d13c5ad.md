13:53 01/02/2020

Four people have been arrested on suspicion of murder following the death of Andrew Jackson in Shirebrook last weekend.

Mr Jackson who was 55-years-old, was discovered at around 10am on Sunday 26 January at an allotment, off Prospect Drive.

A 51-year-old woman and a 44-year-old man were arrested on Thursday night (30 January).

Two further people, a 39-year-old man and a 27-year-old man, were arrested last night (31 January) and this morning (Saturday 1 February) respectively.

All of those arrested remain in police custody.
