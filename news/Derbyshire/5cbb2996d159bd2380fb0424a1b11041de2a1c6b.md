15:33 05/03/2021

A 30-year-old man who was wanted on suspicion of assault and harassment has been arrested, charged and remanded to prison.

Kevin McLean, of no fixed address, was charged with assault, harassment and malicious communications in the early hours of this morning (Friday 5 March).

He then appeared at Southern Derbyshire Magistrates Court at 9.30am today, and was remanded into prison custody.
