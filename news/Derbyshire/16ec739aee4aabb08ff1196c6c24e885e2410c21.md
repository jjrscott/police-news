13:06 25/02/2020

Detectives investigating the death of a man in Shirebrook have arrested another person on suspicion of murder.

The 26-year-old man was arrested this morning on suspicion of the murder of Andrew Jackson.

The man remains in custody and is being questioned. This takes the total number of arrested people to nine. The other eight have all been released on bail while enquiries continue.

Mr Jackson, who was 55-years-old, was discovered at around 10am on Sunday 26 January, at an allotment, off Prospect Drive.
