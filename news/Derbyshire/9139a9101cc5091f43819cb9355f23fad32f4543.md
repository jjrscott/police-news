News

11:03 27/06/2019

Officers investigating a burglary in the Rose Hill area of Derby are appealing for witnesses.

We were called to reports of a burglary in Alder Walk at around 1.45pm on Wednesday, June 19.

On arrival, officers found that a ground floor window had been smashed and an untidy search of the house had been made. A quantity of cash was stolen.

The burglary is believed to have happened between 10.30am and 1pm. Were you in the area at the time? Did you see or hear anything suspicious?

If you have any information which may assist with our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19000316321 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Anwar, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
