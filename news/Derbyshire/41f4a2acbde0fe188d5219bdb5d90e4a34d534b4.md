11:20 15/01/2020

Officers investigating a burglary in Barlborough are asking for the public's help.

Between Saturday 4 January and Monday 6 January a house in Park View was broken into and a number of items of jewellery were stolen.

The pieces, which were stolen in a beech coloured, green velvet lined, jewellery box with a lift up lid and four drawers, include a gold coloured snake bracelet with ruby eyes, a three stone diamond gold ring, a ladies Skargen gold coloured watch and numerous other items.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who may have been in the area at the time as well as anyone who may have seen items matching those described above for sale.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Derbyshire police with reference 20*009640 using any of the below methods:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
