News
Safer Neighbourhood

14:20 10/04/2019

Officers from our Long Eaton Safer Neighbourhood Team have released CCTV footage of a theft of a bike and are asking for your help in identifying the suspect.

The theft happened at around 3.30pm on Monday, April 1, outside the West Park Leisure Centre, Wilsthorpe Road, Long Eaton.

Officers would like to speak to the man in the footage, do you recognise him?

If so please make contact with the team using any of the following contact methods, quoting reference number 19*165355:

 * Facebook – send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter – direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website – complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone – call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org
