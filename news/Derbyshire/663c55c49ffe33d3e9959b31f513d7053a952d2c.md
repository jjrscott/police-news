News

15:28 20/04/2020

Officers investigating a burglary of a house in Derby are appealing for public's help in tracing three bikes stolen during the incident.

The three men pictured broke into the garage of the house in Lucerne Road, Oakwood, Derby, just midnight on Thursday 9 April.

The trio stole three bikes:

A matt black Cannondale Synapse road bike. This carbon fibre is a 56cm size, is fitted with Shimano 105 gears and a Mavic front wheel/Shimano rear wheel combination.

A matt orange Felt Dispatch 960 mountain bike. This 20 inch framed bike is equipped with Shimano Deore gears, Sram cranks and 29 inch wheels fitted with Maxxis Ardent tyres.

A grey Carrera Vengance mountain bike. Understood to have 18 gears and has red and white accents on the frame.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who may have been in the area at the time and may have seen the three men pictured.

They are also want to hear from anyone who may have been offered the bikes for sale or has seen any bicycles on any selling sites.
