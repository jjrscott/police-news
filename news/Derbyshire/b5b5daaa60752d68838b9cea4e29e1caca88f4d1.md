News

16:00 31/03/2020

We are releasing the image of a gold coloured bracelet stolen in a Derby robbery, have you been offered it for sale?

The incident happened at around 12.50pm on Monday, 9 March on Beaumont Walk in Sunnyhill, where a man was threatened with violence and so handed over this gold coloured bracelet to the offender who then ran off towards Caxton Street.

The offender was described a man, around 6' tall, large build, aged 35+ years with bushy eyebrows. He was wearing a black hooded jumper, black, sheeny coat with black shoes or trainers.

Have you been offered anything looking like this item for sale or know anything about the robbery?

If so please contact us, using any of the following non-emergency contact methods and quoting reference number 20000133602:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
