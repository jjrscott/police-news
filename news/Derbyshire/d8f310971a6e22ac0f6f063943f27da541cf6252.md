16:22 03/03/2020

We are releasing pictures of a rifle and other items stolen from a car in Tapton.

The distinctive rifle was stolen sometime between 11pm on Sunday 1 March and 1pm the next day.

It was taken from a Peugeot 107, which was parked in Brimington Road.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact PC Justine Parsons via the below methods, quoting reference 20*119724.

* Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
