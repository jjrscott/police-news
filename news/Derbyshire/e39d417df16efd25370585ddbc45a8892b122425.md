09:51 01/10/2020

Officers investigating the theft of a bike and TV from a shed in Heanor have released this picture in a bid to trace the stolen Boardman.

A shed in the garden of a house in Wellington Street has been broken into and a Panasonic TV and an orange Boardman bicycle stolen from inside.

The theft happened shortly after 1am on Friday 25 September.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact PC Emma Erskine via the below methods, quoting reference 20*504127;
