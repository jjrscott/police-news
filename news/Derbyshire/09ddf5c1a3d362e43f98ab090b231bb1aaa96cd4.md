News
Safer Neighbourhood

16:30 29/10/2020

We're releasing CCTV images of a man officers would like to trace in connection with some suspicious activity around a house in Apperknowle.

Can you help us identify this man?

Officers are investigating a number of reports of suspicious behaviour around a property on High Street.

This man has been seen in the area, and in local shops in the Coal Aston area.

If you recognise the man, or have seen him in the area, please contact us using one of the non-emergency methods below. He may be able to help with our inquiries.

Please quote reference number 20*401389, and the name of the officer in the case, DSI Sarah Price, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
