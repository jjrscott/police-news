News

12:40 22/09/2020

Officers investigating a burglary at a store in Derby are asking the public for help identifying the man pictured.

The incident occurred at the Intu centre, Derby, between 5pm on Sunday 13 September and 8am the following morning.

During the incident a large number of watches - between 10 and 20 - along with as many as 40 non-working mobile phones were stolen.

The man pictured was seen in the area at the time and is hoped to be able to help officers with their enquiries.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*482903 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
