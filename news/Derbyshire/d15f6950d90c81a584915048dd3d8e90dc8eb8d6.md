16:36 23/08/2019

Officers in Shirebrook are appealing for information after a suspicious incident was reported yesterday evening.

A member of the public rang to say that they had witnessed a suspicious incident in Vernon Street, Shirebrook, involving a large, dark coloured car at around 8pm.

Officers are keen to speak to anyone who has information about the incident - in particular any drivers with dashcam footage.

Anyone with footage should download footage and ensure it is stored in safe place for an officer to view.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Derbyshire police on any of the below methods including reference 1225-210819 in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
