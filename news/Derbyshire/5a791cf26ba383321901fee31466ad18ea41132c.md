News

16:52 01/12/2020

Officers investigating two burglaries in Buxton are appealing for information to help with their enquiries.

The first took place at between 9.30pm on Sunday 29 November and 5.30am on Monday 30 November and saw a house in Holmfield broken into. The thieves searched the property, took keys for a van which they then tried to steal but were disturbed and left the area.

The second incident took place between 11.30pm on Sunday 29 November and 8am on Monday 30 November in Heath Park Road. Nothing was stolen in the incident.

Officers investigating the two incidents want to hear from anyone who may have been in the area at the time, in particular anyone with CCTV or dashcam footage which may have captured the areas mentioned above between these times.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20000632514 for the Holmfield incident and reference 20000632686 for the Heath Park Road incident.

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
