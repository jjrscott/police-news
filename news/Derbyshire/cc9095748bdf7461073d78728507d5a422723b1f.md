21:00 14/01/2021

A man has been charged with possession with intent to supply and money laundering.

Peter Swain of Kingfisher Way, Glossop, was arrested yesterday following a number of warrants in the Glossop area.

Suspected drugs and cash were seized in the searches, which were carried out by our officers as part of an investigation led by Greater Manchester Police.

The 42-year-old appeared at Tameside Magistrates' Court today (Thursday 14 January) and was remanded to next appear at Minshull Street Crown Court in February.
