14:41 20/05/2020

Detectives investigating the alleged firing of a gun in Littleover have made another two arrests.

Police were called to Moorway Lane just before 6pm on Tuesday 12 May to reports that a firearm had been discharged during a dispute between two groups of men.

The two groups were then reported to have fled the scene at speed in separate cars.

Two more people have now been arrested, taking the total number of arrests in this case to nine.

Two men aged 21 and 25 have now been arrested and are in police custody.

The men previously arrested have been bailed or released under investigation while our inquiry continues.

Officers investigating the incident would like to thank everyone who has come forward with information to date. We are acting on all information we receive and we are urging people to come forward if they can help. You can contact us via the following methods, quoting reference 20*240776;
