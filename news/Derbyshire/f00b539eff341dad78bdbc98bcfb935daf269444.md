12:16 21/04/2019

Detectives have launched a murder investigation following an alleged incident in Chaddesden Lane, Chaddesden yesterday evening (Saturday, April 20), and have confirmed a 16-year-old boy has been arrested on suspicion of murder. **
**

Officers were called at 9.45pm and on arrival found a man injured in a car park, near to Chaddesden Park. The man was taken to hospital where he sadly died a short time later.

Formal identification of the man is yet to take place.

The 16-year-old remains in police custody.

Detectives would like to hear from anyone with information about the incident, or who may have been in the area at the time.

If you can assist with their enquiries, please contact us quoting reference number 1291 of April 20 and the name of the senior investigating officer DI Simon Cure.
