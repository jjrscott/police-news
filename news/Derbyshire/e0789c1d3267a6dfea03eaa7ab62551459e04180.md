News

17:27 24/02/2021

We are releasing an image of a man we would like to trace in connection with an alleged dog bite incident in Hilton.

Image released following alleged dog bite incident in Hilton

A woman was walking along a lane at Hilton Fields around 8.40 am on Friday 5 February when a dog ran up to her and has allegedly bitten her arm.

The woman didn't suffer any injuries, but there is concern that the dog was not on a lead or under control.

Do you recognise the man in the picture? If so, or if you have any information which could help please contact us using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote reference number 21*73881 in any correspondence.
