News
Safer Neighbourhood

10:24 03/09/2019

An officer from our City Centre Safer Neighbourhood Team has released a picture of a man he would like to speak to after an alleged sexual exposure.

The incident happened at around 1.20pm on Monday 15th July outside the TK Maxx store on Albion Street.

It was reported that the man was rude to staff in the shop before going on to the street and exposing himself.

Do you recognise the man?

If so please contact PC Harry Wood using any of the following contact methods and quoting reference number 19000369406:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
