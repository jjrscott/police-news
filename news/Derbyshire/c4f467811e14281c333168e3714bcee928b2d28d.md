10:45 07/10/2019

We are releasing pictures of two men we want to speak to about the theft of a bicycle from outside a house in Derby.

At around 6.30pm on Thursday, 5 September, a bicycle was taken from the front garden of a house in Blackmore Street, Normanton.

The woman's bike was black with no other markings or stickers on the frame.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact officer in the case PC Neil Haywood, quoting reference 19*474370.
