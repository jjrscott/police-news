News

16:37 15/06/2020

Officers want to speak to the woman pictured as a witness to a serious assault in Woodville.

A 30-year-old man was left with a cut to his head after a glass was allegedly thrown at him during the incident which took place at the Nelson Inn in High Street at around 1am on 8 March.

Since that date officers have been following a number of lines enquiry but are now appealing for the woman pictured to come forward as they believe she may have information that can help the investigation.

If you are the woman, or you recognise her, please contact Derbyshire police using the below methods, including reference 20*131108:

Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
