News

16:30 03/12/2020

We're appealing for information after an excavator was stolen from a cemetery in Glossop.

The digger, which is a Takeuchi Compact Mini Excavator like the one in the picture, was taken from a lock-up container which was broken in to at Glossop Cemetery.

It happened sometime overnight between Monday 30 November and Tuesday 1 December.

If you noticed anything suspicious in the area, have noticed a digger like this for sale second-hand, or have any information please contact us

Please quote reference 20000634904, and DC Nicola Kirk, in any correspondence.
