11:16 28/10/2019

Officers investigating an alleged incident where a man suffered stab wounds are appealing to the public for information.

On Saturday 26 October, at around 7.45pm, officers were called to a flat on Hanbury Close in Holme Hall, Chesterfield.

On arrival a man was found with a number of injuries and was taken to hospital where he remains.

A 21-year-old man and a 23-year-old man were arrested on suspicion of attempt murder and possession of a class B drug.

A 32-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply a Class B drug and a 24-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of attempt murder.

All of the men remain in police custody.

If you have any information which may assist with our enquiries, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19000574023 and the name of the officer in the case, DC John Bowerman in any correspondence.

 * _Facebook_ - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * _Twitter_ - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * _Website_ - complete the online contact form _www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us_.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
