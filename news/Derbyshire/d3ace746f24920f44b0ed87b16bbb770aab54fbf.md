09:32 20/12/2019

Officers are continuing the search for missing 28-year-old man Hayden Marshall.

Hayden was last seen at around 1.15am, on Tuesday, December 17,

Hayden is white, 6ft 1ins tall and slim. He has short dark hair and a beard.

When he was last seen he was wearing a checked blue and white shirt, blue jeans, a black leather jacket and was also wearing glasses - as shown in the picture above.

If you have seen Hayden or know where he may be, please contact us quoting reference number 23 of 17 December.
