16:38 13/09/2020

A woman has died in a flat fire in Newbold.

We were called to a block of flats in Seaton Court, Devonshire Close, just before 9pm last night.

The 34-year-old woman sadly died at the scene.

Investigations to determine the cause of the fire are ongoing but we are asking witnesses or anyone with information to come forward.
