News

12:54 25/02/2020

Officers investigating a burglary at a bungalow in Sleetmoor Lane, Swanwick are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the incident to come forward.

The owners of the bungalow (a man and woman) were out during Thursday, February 20 between 11am and 4.30pm when someone forced entry via the bathroom window and left via the lounge window.

A quantity of jewellery and cash was stolen.

The officer in the case would like to hear from anyone who saw anything suspicious between those times.

If you have any information please contact us using any of the following non-emergency methods, quoting reference number 20*99032:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
