15:02 14/08/2020

A woman from Shirebrook has been charged with a number of offences and was remanded into prison custody earlier this week.

Melissa Mikosz, 30, of Church Drive has been charged with actual bodily harm (ABH), intimidating a witness and assaulting an emergency worker.

The charges relate to three separate incidents which happened between 4 July and 11 August.

Ms Mikosz will next appear at Derby Crown Court on 9 September.
