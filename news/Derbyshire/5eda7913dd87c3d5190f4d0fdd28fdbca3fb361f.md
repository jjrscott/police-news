News

18:47 18/01/2021

Officers investigating a report of an alleged indecent exposure in Alfreton have now released an image of a man they would like to speak to.

Image released in connection with an indecent exposure in Alfreton

It happened on Saturday 10 October on Institute Lane, at the back of Jack Fulton Foods.

The man in the image may be able to help officers with their inquiries.

If this is you, or if you recognise the man in the image, please get in touch with us using the contact details below.

Please quote reference 20*534072 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Adam Hardy, in any correspondence.
