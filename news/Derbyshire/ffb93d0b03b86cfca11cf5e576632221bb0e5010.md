14:28 15/06/2020

We are concerned for the safety of a 15-year-old boy who has gone missing from his home in Normanton.

Isaac Aljdi left home on his bicycle on the afternoon of Saturday 13 June. He has not been seen since.

Although Isaac has longer hair in this photo, he has recently had it shaved off.

If you have seen Isaac, or have any information as to his whereabouts, please contact us quoting reference 818 of June 13;

* Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
