10:34 25/03/2021

Residents living in the Dronfield area are asked to be vigilant after a distraction burglary.

At around 3pm on Wednesday 24 March, a man visited the home of a 90-year-old woman in the town, claiming that he needed to check her upstairs taps.

The woman later realised her bedroom had been searched. It is not known at this time if anything was stolen.

The man is described as white, aged about 30 and with curly brown hair.
