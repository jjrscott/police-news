12:48 16/03/2020

We've issued an image of a man we'd like to speak to about an assault in a Derby nightclub. Do you recognise him?

On Saturday 7 March a 19-year-old man was on the dancefloor in MOSH nightclub in Friar Gate, when he was assaulted by another man.

The victim was left requiring stitches in his lip following the incident, which happened at around 2.25am.

If you recognise the man pictured, please contact us as we believe he may be able to assist with our enquiries.

Equally, if you have any other information that may help our investigation please also get in touch.

You should quote reference number 20*0129385 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Matt Titterton, in any correspondence.
