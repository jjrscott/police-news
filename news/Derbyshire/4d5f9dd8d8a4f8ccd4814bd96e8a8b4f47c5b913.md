News

17:39 31/08/2020

Officers are appealing for information after a fight in a Chesterfield street.

Police were called to reports of a fight involving around 20 people outside the Spotted Frog pub in Chatsworth Road, Chesterfield, at around 10.15pm on Sunday 30 August.

At the scene a 30-year-old man was found with injuries to his face that required hospital treatment.

Detectives are keen to speak to anyone who was in the area at the time and witnessed the incident - as well as drivers with dashcam footage that may have captured the incident.

Anyone with information or footage is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*453889 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
