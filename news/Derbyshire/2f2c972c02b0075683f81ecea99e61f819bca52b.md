News

11:26 09/12/2020

Officers investigating a burglary in Whaley Bridge, in which a van, tools and a substantial amount of cash were taken, are appealing for information.

The incident took place between 12.55am and 1.45am on Tuesday 1 December in Bings Road, Whaley Bridge.

The burglars broke into the house, stole a set of keys to a silver Mercedes Sprinter van, a substantial amount of cash and several work tools which were in the back of the van.

Officers want to hear from anyone who may be able to help with information about the incident - in particular drivers who may have dashcam from the area at the time or homeowners with CCTV in the area.

Anyone with any information is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*634381 in any correspondence:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
