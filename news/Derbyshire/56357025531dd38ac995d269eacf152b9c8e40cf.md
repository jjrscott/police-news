News

18:35 05/12/2020

Our detectives are appealing for witnesses after a man was hospitalised following an assault in Alfreton.

The incident happened in Birch Close at about 2.40am this morning and involved two groups who are thought to be known to each other.

During the dispute, an 18-year-old man was assaulted and was taken to hospital to be treated for his injuries, which were not thought to be life-threatening.

We are appealing for witnesses and want to hear from anyone who lives in the Birch Close area and has private CCTV.

Anyone who can help should contact us using one of the below methods and quoting reference 20*642277.
