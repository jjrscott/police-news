14:06 14/08/2019

We are asking drivers with dashcam footage to come forward following a collision between a car and a motorbike on the A38.

The incident happened northbound, just after the Markeaton island, at about 6.20pm on Sunday, August 11.

A blue Ford Focus and a grey Suzuki GSF were involved. Neither driver was injured.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact PC Karl Swanson, quoting reference number 19*424950.
