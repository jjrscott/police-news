10:33 12/08/2020

A woman has pleaded guilty to the murder of David Allsopp in Derby earlier this year.

Magdalena Kissova, 47, of Manchester Street appeared at Derby Crown Court on Friday 7 August.

Mr Allsopp, who was 67, was found dead in his home in Manchester Street on 15 February this year.

Ms Kissova was arrested and charged on 17 February.

She is next due to appear at Derby Crown Court on 16 September.
