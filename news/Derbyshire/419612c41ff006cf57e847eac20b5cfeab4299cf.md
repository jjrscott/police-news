15:27 04/01/2021

A convict has absconded from HMP Sudbury open prison.

Joseph John Hickinbottom

John Joseph Hickinbottom, who is 54, absconded from the prison on Saturday 2 January.

He is serving an indeterminate sentence for public protection.

Hickinbottom has links to the West Midlands and Gloucestershire, and is described as being white, around 6ft tall with blue eyes and brown hair.

If you have seen him or know of his whereabouts, please do not approach him, instead, contact us immediately quoting reference number 980 of 2 January.
