09:56 14/08/2020

A man who died after a collision between the motorcycle he was riding and a van on the A515 on Wednesday has been named.

Wayne Poxon, who was 51, and from Derby was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

We were called shortly before 11am to report the collision between a black Kawasaki motorcycle and a white Citroen Relay van near Biggin.

Both vehicles had left the carriageway during the incident.

Detectives investigating the collision would like to hear from anyone who may have information about what happened.

If you were travelling in the area at the time and witnessed the incident, or in particular, if you have dash cam installed which may have captured it please contact Detective Sergeant Darren Muggleton quoting reference number 20*418338.

If you have any footage which may be useful to officers please ensure it is downloaded to a separate, secure device ready for them to view.
