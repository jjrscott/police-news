News

11:25 29/08/2020

Officers investigating an assault in Ripley are appealing for witnesses to the incident to come forward.

A 38-year-old man was punched to the face outside the White Lion pub in Ripley at around 11pm yesterday (28 August).

The man was taken to hospital where he was found to have suffered a broken jaw.

Anyone with information about the incident is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 1228-280820:

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.
