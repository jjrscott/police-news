News

15:08 20/07/2020

Officers are appealing for help identifying a man they want to speak to in connection with a burglary in the Hope Valley.

An Audi SQ2, which was stolen from a house in Calver, in the Hope Valley, on 10 June, was recently recovered in Hyde, Manchester, and a camera - which had also been stolen during the burglary - was found to contain a number of pictures taken after the vehicle was stolen.

Officers are keen to speak to the man pictured or to hear from anyone who recognises him. If you have any information then please contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including the name of the officer PC Froggatt 15091 and reference 20*293298, in any correspondence:

 * Facebook- send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter- direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website- complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also anonymously contact the independent charity Crimestoppers, on 0800 555 111 or by visiting www.crimestoppers-uk.org.
