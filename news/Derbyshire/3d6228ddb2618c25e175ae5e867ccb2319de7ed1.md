15:49 19/08/2020

Officers who discovered five bikes during a drugs warrant in Derby are hoping to reunite them with their owners.

Following the enforcement, which took place at a house in Thorn Street, Derby, on 12 August, a 33-year-old man from Derby was arrested and released under investigation on suspicion of possession with intent to supply class A drugs.

At the address five bikes that are believed to have been stolen were found and officers are now looking to reunite them with their owners.

If you recognise any of the bikes then please contact Derbyshire police using any of the below methods, including reference 20*418707 in any correspondence:
