News

11:22 25/06/2019

Do you recognise these teenagers? Officers would like to speak to them in connection with the theft of food and drink from a restaurant in Chesterfield.

On Tuesday, June 4, at around 5pm, a group of teenagers left Frankie and Benny's in Alma Leisure Park, Chesterfield without paying their bill.

We would like to identify the four people pictured to help with our enquiries.

If you recognise them, please get in touch using one of the following non-emergency contact methods.

Please quote the reference number 19000286928, number of the photo and the name of the officer in the case, PC Carl Davies, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
