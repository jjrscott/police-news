15:21 28/04/2020

We are re-issuing an image of Azad Shafiq, from Derby, who officers would like to speak to about allegations of possessing a firearm and possession of Class A drugs with intent to supply.

Officers understand the 28-year-old has links to the West Yorkshire area, in particular the city of Bradford.

Anyone who may have seen Azad Shafiq, or know where he may be, please contact us via one of the following methods.

• Facebook - send us a private message to this page
