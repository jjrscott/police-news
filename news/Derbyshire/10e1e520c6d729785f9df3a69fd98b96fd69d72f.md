16:34 03/05/2019

Officers want to speak to anyone who may have witnessed a burglary at a Post Office in Alfreton.

The incident took place at around 3.30am on Thursday, May 2, at the Post Office in Nottingham Road, Somercotes, Alfreton.

The offenders broke into the building and stole cash and cigarettes.

Officers want to speak to anyone who may have been in the area at the time and witnessed the incident - in particular anyone who may have dashcam or CCTV footage that may have captured those responsible is asked to download the video and secure it for an officer to view.

Please include reference number 19*222366 and the name of the officer, DS Grainger, in any correspondence.
