News

15:11 25/07/2019

Officers are appealing for information following a burglary in Chisworth in which the four motorbikes, pictured, were stolen.

We were called to reports of a burglary in Marple Road at around 4am on Saturday, 20July.

Four men stole four motorbikes from an outbuilding, loaded them into a white Ford Transit van and drove off towards Marple.

The Honda motorbikes - including a red ANF 125, a black NC 750 SA, and a silver VFR 1200 - are quite distinctive and officers are appealing for anyone who knows of their whereabouts to get in touch.

If you have any information about the burglary which may assist with our enquiries, contact using one of the following non-emergency methods.

Please quote the reference number 19*379293 and the name of the officer in the case, PC Doyle, in any correspondence.

 * Facebook - send us a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
 * Twitter - direct message our contact centre on @DerPolContact
 * Website - complete the online contact form derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us.
 * Phone - call us on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Please note in the event of an emergency you should always call 999.
