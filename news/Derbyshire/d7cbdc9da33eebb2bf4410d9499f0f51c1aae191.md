12:39 24/02/2020

Witnesses are being sought to a collision in Allenton.

The collision happened at about 12.15pm on Tuesday 18 February on Spider Island.

We want to speak to anyone who has dash cam footage of the collision, or of the two female drivers outside their cars following the incident.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact us quoting reference number

20*094480 and the name of the officer in the case PC Amy Abbott.
