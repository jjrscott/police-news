On Tuesday 22nd December 2020, Police were called to a Road Traffic Collision on Dean Street, Liskeard, Cornwall. The collision involved a single vehicle and a pedestrian.

Police attended the scene along with South West Ambulance. As a result of the collision the pedestrian, a man in his 70s, sustained head injuries considered possibly life changing. He remains in hospital at this time.

Officers from the Alliance Roads Policing team also attended and the road was closed for a number of hours whilst examination and recovery took place.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or may have been in the area at the time and may have dashcam footage are asked to contact police on 101, quoting log number 682 of 22/12/20.
