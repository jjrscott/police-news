Police are concerned for the welfare of 32-year-old Shaun Brown, who has been reported missing from his home address in Kingsteignton.

Shaun was last seen on Thursday 18 March.

He is described as a white male, 6ft 3in tall, with short dark hair and a beard.

It is not known what he was last wearing.

For the last 24 hours, numerous agencies have been assisting police in the search for Shaun, particularly around the chalk mine areas of Kingsteignton.

Anyone who sees Shaun, or has information on his whereabouts, is urged to call police immediately on 999, quoting log 200 of 18/03/21.
