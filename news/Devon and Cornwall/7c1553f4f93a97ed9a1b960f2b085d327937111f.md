A woman, whose body was found in the water at Ilfracombe harbour on Sunday 6 December, has been identified.

Police would like to thank the community for their support and the members of the public who came forward to assist with identifying her.

Police were notified by the coastguard about the discovery at around 9.20am. Sadly the woman, a local woman in her early 70s, was pronounced deceased at the scene.

Her identity was unknown at the time and police released images of her clothing in their efforts to identify her and notify her next-of-kin.

Her death is being treated as non-suspicious and her next-of-kin have been informed.
