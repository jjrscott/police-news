Devon and Cornwall Police are appealing for information in relation to missing person, Efstathios Anthis.

Efstathios, also known as Stan, has been reported missing from Bigbury on Sea Area. He was last seen at the Burgh Island Hotel, Burgh Island, Bigbury on Sea, on the 8 March 2021 at approximately 5am.

He is described as a white Greek male, aged 33, 5ft 7ins tall and of a slim build, with short dark hair and a stubbly beard.

It is believed he may have travelled to the Plymouth area.

If anybody has any information at all, please call 999 quoting log 0450 13/03/2021.
