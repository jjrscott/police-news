Police are appealing for the publics help to locate a man from Plymouth who is wanted on recall to prison.

25-year-old Riki Leeds is described as a white male of slim build with short dark brown hair.

He has links across Plymouth and particularly in the Honickowle area of the city.

The public are asked not to approach Riki Leeds and to contact police immediately.

Anyone who sees Riki or knows of his whereabouts is asked to contact police on 999 quoting log 277 9/2/21.
