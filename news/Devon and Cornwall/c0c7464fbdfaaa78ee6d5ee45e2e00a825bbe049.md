Police are appealing for dashcam footage of a motorist driving the wrong way along the A30 near Okehampton yesterday evening, Tuesday 16 March.

The incident occurred at around 9pm in the Exeter/Eastbound carriageway between Sourton and Okehampton.

A 69-year-old woman from Okehampton was arrested on suspicion of drink driving and her uninsured vehicle was seized. She was released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Any witnesses, or anyone with relevant dashcam footage, is asked to contact police on 101 or via email at 101@dc.police.uk quoting crime reference, CR/020588/21.
