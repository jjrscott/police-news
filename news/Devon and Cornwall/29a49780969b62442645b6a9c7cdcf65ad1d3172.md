Police are appealing for information about two high-value burglaries at the Devonia Industrial Estate, Buckfastleigh in the early hours of this morning, Wednesday 23 December.

Thousands of pounds worth of tools were stolen from a business unit and hundreds of pounds worth of items were stolen from a storage unit after they were broken into between 3am and 4am.

Police would like to hear from anyone who has seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area or any suspicious vehicles, as well as anyone who has CCTV which may have captured the incidents.

Anyone with any information which could assist police enquiries is asked to contact police on 101 or via email at 101@dc.police.uk quoting crime reference, CR/107110/20.
