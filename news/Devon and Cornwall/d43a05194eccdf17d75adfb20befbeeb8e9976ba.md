Police are investigating a road traffic incident which resulted in a boy needing hospital treatment.

Officers received a report that a child had collided with two cars on Delamere Road, Plymouth, at around 5pm on Monday 14 December.

The child was taken to Derriford Hospital and has since been discharged.

The female driver of one of the vehicles – a black car – did not stop at the scene of the incident and police are now seeking to identify this driver.

If anyone has dashcam footage or witnessed the collision, please phone 101 or email 101@dc.police.uk and quote log number 616, 14/12/20.
