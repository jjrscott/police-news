Police are appealing for witnesses after a lone female was attacked in the Mutley Plain area of Plymouth.

The 22-year-old woman was repeatedly hit in the face during the attack, leaving her with bruising to face, neck and wrist.

The woman, who was walking her dog at the time, also reported that her attackers attempted to steal her dog.

The incident happened between 9.30pm and 10pm around the Mutley Plain Lane are.

Officers attended the scene and are seeking two males in relation to the attack.

Police are appealing for any witnesses who may have heard or seen anything.

Anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101 or email 101@dc.police.uk quoting crime reference CR/002880/21.
