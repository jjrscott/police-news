A man has been charged with drug and driving offences after his vehicle was stopped in Stoke-on-Trent on Tuesday afternoon.

Ivan Tarranda Weekes, 38, of Bouverie Parade, Sneyd Green, was stopped in a Honda Civic on Leek New Road at 1.30pm.

He has been charged with possession with intent to supply Class A drugs (crack cocaine), possession with intent to supply Class B drugs (cannabis), driving whilst disqualified, driving without insurance, failure to cooperate with a drugs swipe and criminal damage.

He appeared before magistrates at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre this morning (Thursday 7 January).
