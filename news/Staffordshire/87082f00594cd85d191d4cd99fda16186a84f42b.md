A man and a teenage boy have been arrested following calls that a vehicle was being damaged on Chaplin Road.

Police were called at 6pm yesterday (Wednesday 12 February) to reports that hammers and knives were being used to cause criminal damage.

A 20-year-old man, from Stoke-on-Trent, was arrested on suspicion of affray and possession of an offensive weapon. A 15-year-old boy, also from Stoke-on-Trent, was arrested on suspicion of affray, criminal damage and possession of an offensive weapon. Both of them remain in custody at this time.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 537 of February 12, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
