Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary on Beacon Road in Rolleston-on-Dove.

Officers were called to reports that a garage had been broken into between 15 February and 16 February and several high value tools were stolen.

The offenders forced a panel of a rear PVC door open to get inside the property where they took equipment including; socket sets, ratchet set, torque wrenches and screw drivers.

The items have an engineer's stamp on them with reference number EN10 which makes them identifiable.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 287 of 16 February, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
