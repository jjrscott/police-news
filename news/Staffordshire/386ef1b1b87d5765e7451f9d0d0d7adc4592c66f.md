Officers have discovered a cannabis factory in Birches Head.

The force had been contacted following concerns over activity at the address on Burnley Street.

Today police attended at around 9am and discovered 138 plants. There was nobody inside the property.

An investigation is now underway. Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 0600 of February 22, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
