Officers investigating a sexual assault in the Newcastle area earlier this year have a released an image of a man they would like to talk to.

The incident happened on Sunday 3 February at around 2.30pm at an alleyway near to Orme Road. A man, who was unknown to the female victim, exposed himself and pushed her as he walked past her.

If you recognise this man, please Direct Message us on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident 419 of 3 February or ring 101.
Alternatively, for guaranteed anonymity, please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
