Do you know where this blue scooter is?

Officers are seeking to locate the scooter after it was rode in a dangerous manner in Shelton, Stoke-on-Trent.

Intelligence suggests it is being used in public spaces such as Hanley Park, along canal paths, and near primary schools.

Officers believe the scooter is being used by a group of males and has been linked to incidents of anti-social behavior and crime.

Police believe it is being stored in Shelton and Etruria and urge anyone with knowledge of its whereabouts to get in touch.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter, call 101 or alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 111 555.
