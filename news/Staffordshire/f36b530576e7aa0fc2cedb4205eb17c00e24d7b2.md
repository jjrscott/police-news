Police are appealing for witnesses following a collision on September 7 between a car and motorbike in Norton Canes which left one man with serious injuries.

Officers were called to Brownhills Road at around 11.30am and found the motorcyclist injured in the road. The driver of the car left the scene before the police arrived.

The vehicle is a sliver/blue Ford B Max and the driver is believed to be an Asian male, in his 40s, with a grey beard and wearing a black top.

Anyone with information or CCTV of the collision is urged to call 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111 quoting incident 287 of September 7.
