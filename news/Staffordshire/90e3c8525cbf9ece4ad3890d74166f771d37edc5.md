Detectives are investigating after two men were assaulted on Victoria Road in Fenton on Saturday (4 July) night.

Police were called at 6.40pm to reports of two men with head injuries in an alleyway off Victoria Road.

One of the men was taken to the Royal Stoke University Hospital, where he is still receiving treatment. The other man did not go to hospital.

Officers are interested to hear from anyone who was in the area at the time or anyone who has CCTV footage.

Any witnesses or anyone with any information is asked to contact 101 or report online, quoting incident number 703 of 4 July. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
