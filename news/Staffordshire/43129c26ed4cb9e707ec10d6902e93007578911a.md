An investigation is underway following a robbery at Leek United Building Society in Leek.

Just before 1pm this afternoon (Thursday 2 January), police were called to reports of a robbery at the St Edward Street branch.

A man entered the building society and aggressively demanded cash. A quantity of money was stolen.

He is described as white, 6ft tall with a slim build.

He was wearing a black baseball cap with a white motif, a black top with a small white motif on the left chest, a black waterproof coat with a hood, black trousers with a small motif on the left top pocket, black trainers, a black scarf covering his face and black skiing gloves.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 257 of 2 January, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
