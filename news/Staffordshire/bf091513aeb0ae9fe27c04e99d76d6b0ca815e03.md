Updated News Post

Police are now in a position to name the woman who sadly died following a road traffic collision in Cannock on New Year's Eve.

Sharon Jenkins, aged 62, from Cannock, was driving a mobility scooter when she was involved in a collision with a car at around 6.30pm.

Despite the efforts of emergency service staff, Sharon was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Specialist officers are supporting her family, and they have requested that their privacy is respected at this difficult time.

Detectives continue to investigate the circumstances surrounding the collision. They are appealing for relevant dash-cam footage or any witnesses to contact them on 101 quoting incident 590 of 31 December.
