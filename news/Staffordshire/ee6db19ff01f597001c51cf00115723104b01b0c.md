Three men and a teenager have been charged with drugs offences following a vehicle stop in Shelton on September 9.

The four have been charged with possession with intent to supply cocaine and heroin in Shelton on September 9 and will appear before magistrates this morning (Sept 11).

Marian Durac, aged 19, of Stoke Road, Shelton, Patrick Durac, aged 24, of Burnham Street, Fenton, Christopher Wise, aged 40, of Grange Road, Lincoln, and a 16-year-old boy from Fenton, who cannot be named for legal reasons were charged.
