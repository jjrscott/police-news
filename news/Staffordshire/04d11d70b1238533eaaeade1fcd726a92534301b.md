A Hanley man has been jailed for more than five years for drugs offences.

David Benbow, aged 57, was jailed for 2,045 days at Stoke-on-Trent Crown Court on Friday (Feb 28) after pleading guilty to possession of class A drugs with intent to supply.

Benbow, of Baskerville Road, also pleaded guilty to driving while disqualified in Whitehall Avenue, Kidsgrove, on 24 January.
