Detectives investigating the death of a man in Chesterton have today named him as Jack Sumner, from Newcastle.

Jack was assaulted in an incident in Chesterton in June and sadly passed away on Wednesday (14 October).

Specially-trained officers are supporting Jack's family at this difficult time.
