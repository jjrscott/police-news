Specialist investigators are appealing for information or dash-cam footage following a serious collision in Tamworth.

Officers were called at 7.20pm yesterday (15 April) to a report of a road traffic collision on Glascote Road (B5000) in Tamworth.

The collision involved a white Fiat Punto and a cyclist who were travelling towards Silver Link Road.

The cyclist - a man in his 40s from the local area - was taken to the Queen Elizabeth Hospital where he remains in critical condition.

Officers from the Staffordshire and West Midlands Serious Collision Investigation Unit are appealing for any witnesses to the collision or anyone who saw the car or cycle before the incident to get in touch.

Any witnesses are asked to call 101 or email ciu@staffordshire.pnn.police.uk quoting incident number 661 of 15 April.
