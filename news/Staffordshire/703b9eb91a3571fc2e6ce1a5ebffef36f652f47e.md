Officers have issued CCTV images of a man they want to speak to in connection with a theft from a Tesco Express in Tamworth.

It's after cans of alcohol were taken from the store on Tamworth Road in Amington shortly before 6.30am on 12 February.

A staff member was also hurt after being pushed to the floor during an altercation.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 76 of 12 February, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
