Police are appealing for witnesses after a moped was stolen in Burntwood overnight.

Officers were called to Highfield Avenue just before 1am this morning (Monday 10 January) to reports two offenders had threatened a man with a knife before stealing a black Piaggio moped.

The suspects are then believed to have made off towards Morley Road.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 18 of 10 February, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
