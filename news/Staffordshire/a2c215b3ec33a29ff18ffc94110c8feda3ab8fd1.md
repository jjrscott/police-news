Officers are appealing for whitnesses after a chairty shop in stafford was burgled overnight.

Police were called to Oxfam on Hunters Row at 5am yesterday (Thursday 2 January) to reports of a burglary.

The building had been broken into and a glass case containing jewellery was damaged. Gold and silver jewellery was taken from the store, alongside a sum of cash.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 59 of January 2, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
