Police are appealing for witnesses after four bikes were stolen from a garage in Kinver.

It is believed the unit, on The Compa, was targeted between January 30 and February 14 where the bikes along with a petrol strimmer were taken.

Officers are also keen to speak to an individual captured on February 14 on a doorbell recording in the area. The individual was wearing what appears to be a tracksuit, long coat with the hood up and a face mask.

_**Pictured: Police would like to speak to this individual as part of the investigation**_

The following bikes were stolen:

 * A Specialized Stumpjumper Evo;
 * A grey and orange NS Snabb;
 * A black Giant talon;
 * A black and green BMX.

In addition a Stihl-branded petrol garden strimmer was also taken.Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 0663 of February 15, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
