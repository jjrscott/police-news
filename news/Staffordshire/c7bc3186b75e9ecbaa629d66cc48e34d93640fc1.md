Detectives investigating a man's death last week can now confirm his identity as Christopher Henworth, aged 57.

Mr Henworth, from Longton, was found at his address in Wigmore Place on 17 October.

Detectives are investigating the circumstances around his death which is unexplained.

A man, aged 41 and from the local area, who was arrested on suspicion of murder on October 18 has been released under investigation while enquiries continue.

Detective Chief Inspector Jason Everett, of Staffordshire Police, said: "Our investigation into the circumstances around Mr Henworth's death is continuing. We are keen to hear from anyone who has any CCTV or dashcam footage, or other information. We'd greatly appreciate it if they got in touch."
