Four men have been arrested following a raid on a shop in Smallthorne last night (Sept 8).

Detectives are appealing for information after the incident at the One Stop Shop in Community Drive at 8.45pm.

Armed officers attended after it was reported that offenders were carrying weapons.

Men aged 19, 20, 26 and 38, and all from the local area, were arrested on suspicion of violence against the person and remain in custody.

Detective Inspector Scott McGrath, of Staffordshire Police, said: "We're appealing for anyone with information to assist us as we continue our investigation. This was a violent attack which led to the victim receiving medical treatment."

Anyone with information is urged to contact 101 referring to incident 717 of 8 September.
