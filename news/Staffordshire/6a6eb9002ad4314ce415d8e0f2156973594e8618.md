A 15-year-old boy and 16-year-old boy, both from Stoke-on-Trent, have been arrested on suspicion of robbery following an incident on Malkin Way on Tuesday, October 22.

An 18-year-old was attacked in his vehicle at around 11.30pm and the car was stolen. It was later recovered on Port Vale Street.

Witnesses or anyone with any information are asked to call 101 or private message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 868 of 22/10. Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
