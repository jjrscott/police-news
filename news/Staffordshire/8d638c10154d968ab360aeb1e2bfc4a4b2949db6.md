The driver of a van involved in a fatal road traffic collision on the A5 on Tuesday (10 September) has been named.

Liam Clark, aged 21, from Rosefield Road, Liverpool was pronounced dead at the scene of the collision between the white Citroen van he was driving and a Bateman agricultural vehicle which happened at around 11.30am in Shenstone at the junction of the A5 and Streetway Road.

The road was closed for around three hours to allow specialist collision investigation officers to examine the scene. Anyone who witnessed, or has dashcam footage of the collision is asked to ring 101 quoting incident 228 of 10 September or email ciu@Staffordshire.pnn.police.uk
