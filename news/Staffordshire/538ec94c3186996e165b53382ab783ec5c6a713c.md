A Blurton man has been jailed for 30 months for possessing heroin with intent to supply earlier this year.

Ryan Atkinson, 26, of Ellerby Road, Blurton pleaded guilty to possession of heroin with intent to supply in Trentham on 8 January.

He was stopped by officers riding a scooter in Trentham Road when he was disqualified from driving.

He was disqualified from driving for 63 months and jailed at Stoke-on-Trent Crown Court on 10 February.
