An investigation is underway following the death of a man in his 40s in Tamworth.

Police were called by colleagues from West Midlands Ambulance Service to a property on Coleridge Close shortly after 7:20am this morning (Thursday 4 June).

A 41-year-old man and a 50-year-old woman - both from the town - have been arrested and are helping police with enquiries.

They remain in police custody.

Officers remain in the area and are carrying out door to door enquiries.
