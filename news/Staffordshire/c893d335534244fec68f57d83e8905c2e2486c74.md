Officers investigating the death of a teenager on the A50 last week are appealing for witnesses.

Eighteen-year-old Devon Locker from Longton was killed after being hit by a vehicle - having fallen onto the eastbound carriageway by the Foley Road interchange on Friday 2 October at around 9am.

His family are being supported by specially trained officers and have asked for privacy at this difficult time.

Officers from the Staffordshire and West Midlands Serious Collision Investigation Unit continue to investigate the circumstances of the incident and are appealing for anyone who may have travelling on the Foley Road interchange roundabout or on the westbound carriageway at the time to get in touch.

Anyone with dashcam footage or any information is asked to email ciu@staffordshire.pnn.police.uk or call 101 quoting incident 136 of 2 October.

Devon Locker
