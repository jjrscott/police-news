Officers from the Staffordshire and West Midlands Serious Collision Investigation Unit are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision in Lichfield.

Police were called at 2.30pm this afternoon (Wednesday 24 February) to reports of a two-vehicle collision on Cappers Lane.

The collision involved a red Vauxhall Meriva and a HGV.

Sadly, a man, the driver of the Vauxhall Meriva, died at the scene. The driver of the HGV was not injured.

Specialist officers are supporting the man's family.

Witnesses who may have seen the vehicles before the collision or anyone with relevant dashcam footage to message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 364 of 24 February or call 101.
