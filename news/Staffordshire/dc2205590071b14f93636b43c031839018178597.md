A man has appeared in court charged with serious sexual offences against a child.

Keiron Wallbanks, 37, of Burntwood, has been charged with two counts of indecent assault, six counts of sexual assault, six counts of causing/inciting a child to engage in sexual activity and one count of rape.

Wallbanks appeared at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre this morning.
