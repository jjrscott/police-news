Officers have recovered a number of tools they believe were stolen.

Staffordshire Police received reports of the items being found in a field off Wentlows Road in Upper Tean just after 7.15am on January 29.

A Dewalt heavy-duty box and two green Makita branded holdalls were found. While one bag was empty the other contained some drill bits, tape measures and various tools.

Local officers are carrying out enquiries and checking CCTV in the area.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 0076 of January 29, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
