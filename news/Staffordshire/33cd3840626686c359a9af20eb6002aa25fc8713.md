Updated News Post

Detectives investigating the death of a woman in Audley have today named her as Clare Bell.

Clare, aged 42, was found at an address in Church Street on Sunday 9 August.

Investigations continue into Clare's death and specially-trained officers are supporting her family at this difficult time.

Anyone with any information should call Staffordshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 565 of 9 August or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
