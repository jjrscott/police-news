Police are now in a position to name the male sadly killed in a fatal road traffic collision that occurred on the A5 westbound carriageway, in Glascote, Tamworth on Tuesday 8 December 2020 at around 08:55am.

Nigel Ward, aged 59 years, from the Tamworth area, was pronounced dead at the scene, despite the efforts of the West Midlands Ambulance Service.

His family are being supported by specially-trained officers and they ask for their privacy to be respected at this tragic time and will be making no further comment.

Officers are appealing for any witnesses who may have seen the vehicles involved beforehand or the collision itself to contact them on 101 quoting incident 123 of 08/12/2020 or via ciu@staffordshire.pnn.police.uk
