A man has been charged in connection with a collision where an officer was injured in Tamworth yesterday (Tuesday 21 January).

Dwain Trevor Francis, 35, of Barnwood Road, Quinton, Birmingham, has been charged with failing to stop, dangerous driving, possession with intent to supply crack cocaine and possession with intent to supply heroin.

Francis is due to appear at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre tomorrow morning (23 January).
