Police are appealing for witnesses after hundreds of pounds worth of cleaning equipment and golf clubs were stolen from a garage in Stoke-on-Trent.

The burglary off Eastbourne Road in Northwood was discovered on January 21 where it appears entry was gained to the garage through a smashed window.

Items stolen include two petrol-powered jet washers, a Karcher K4 pressure washer, a Karcher steam cleaner, and a Karcher Wet and Vac hoover. In addition golfing equipment was also stolen which included a blue Nike golf bag with a luminous green tick, Nike Vapor Fly Pro irons, TaylorMade AeroBurner driver and Scotty Cameron putter.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 0635 of January 21, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
