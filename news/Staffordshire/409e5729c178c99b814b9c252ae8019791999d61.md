Detectives have charged a 55-year-old man following a serious assault in The Crown public house in Chasetown yesterday (Friday 31 January).

Brian Patrick Harmon, of Lawnswood Avenue, Burntwood, has been charged with wound/inflict grievous bodily harm with intent and possession of an offensive weapon in a public place.

He has appeared at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre this morning (Saturday 1 February) and has been bailed until his next court appearance.
