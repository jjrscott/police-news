Officers have released a CCTV image of man they are keen to trace.

He is suspected of exposing himself on the 6a bus which was travelling from Blythe Bridge to Hanley on 19 December.

The suspect, who is believed to be white, in his 40s, was wearing a blue jacket, dark-coloured jeans and a woollen hat.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 649 of 21 December, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
