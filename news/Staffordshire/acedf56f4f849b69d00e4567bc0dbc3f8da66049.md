Officers have made two arrests following disorder in Fenton earlier this afternoon (Sept 23).

A report was made that a man armed with a weapon, believed to be an axe, was in Christchurch Street at 1.20pm.

Following the report of weapons a firearms unit was sent to the area and officers arrested a 49-year-old man in King Street on suspicion of possessing an offensive weapon.

A 45-year-old woman was arrested nearby on suspicion of threatening behaviour.

Both are in custody. Anyone with information is urged to contact Staffordshire Police on 101 referring to incident 351 of 23 September.
