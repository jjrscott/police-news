An investigation is underway following a burglary in Newcastle where a number of items of jewellery were stolen.

The force received a report of a break-in on Kingsway West just after 4.30pm on February 5.
A number of high value items were taken during the burglary including jewellery and bags. A men's GT Aggressor pedal bike was also stolen.
Officers attended the scene and conducted house to house enquiries. An investigation is ongoing with witnesses asked to contact the force with any information.
Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 0521 of February 5, or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
