We received reports from the ambulance service that a woman's body had been found at a property in Church Street, Audley yesterday (9 August).

At this time the death of the woman, in her 40s, is being treated as unexplained and enquiries are on-going.

A man, who is in his 40s and from the local area has been arrested on suspicion of murder and remains in custody.
