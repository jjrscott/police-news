A Stafford man has been jailed for 16 months after he pleaded guilty to threatening a man with a knife in the town.

Lea Taylor, aged 31, of no fixed address, admitted threatening the man with a knife, possessing a knife in a public place and affray.

The offences all took place in Newport Road, Stafford, on 29 December.

He was sentenced at Stafford Crown Court on 23 January, after pleading guilty at magistrates' court to threatening a person with an offensive weapon in a public place, possession of an offensive weapon in a public place, and affray.
