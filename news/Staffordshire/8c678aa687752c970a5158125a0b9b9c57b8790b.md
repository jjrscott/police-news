As Operation Disrupt continues, neighbourhood officers from Stoke North have arrested three people following stop searches in Cobridge.

Samantha Davy, 34, of Werrington Road, Bucknall, Stoke-on-Trent was arrested and has been charged with possession of a controlled drug of Class A - heroin and possession of a controlled drug of Class B - amphetamine. She appeared before magistrates at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre this morning.

A 31-year-old man, from Stoke-on-Trent, has been arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class A - cocaine. He has been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

A 25-year-old woman, from Stoke-on-Trent, has been arrested on suspicion of being in possession of monkey dust. Due to the quantity found, she has been given a community resolution.

A vehicle was also seized for no insurance and the driver was reported for driving offences.
