Police have charged a man following an assault at a pub in Stoke-on-Trent.

Charlie Diskin, 24, of Mornington Road, Stoke-on-Trent, was one of two men arrested by officers called to the Terrace Inn on Leek Road on Saturday night (4 January) - where two people were assaulted and garden furniture thrown.

Diskin - charged with criminal damage and using threatening behaviour with intent to cause fear and provoke unlawful violence - has been granted conditional bail until appearing at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre on 21 January.

The second man - arrested on suspicion of criminal damage and assault - has been released with no further action.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 764 of 4 January, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
