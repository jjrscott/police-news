Detectives have released CCTV footage of someone they are keen to trace after an arson attack in Packmoor earlier this year.

The incident on Samuel Street, where graffiti was sprayed onto a trailer and an accelerant was used to set fire to a horsebox and a van, happened at around 1pm on 18 May 2019.

The footage shows someone with a distinctive gait running away from the scene of the fire.

Witnesses or anyone with information should direct message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter or call 101, quoting incident 59 of 18 May.
