Police in Cannock have arrested a man on suspicion of theft in the early hours of this morning (5 November).

Officers received a report at around 1am of a man behaving suspiciously outside a property on Booth Street in Cannock. He was located at the property and officers found he had two satnavs and some bank cards which were believed to have been stolen. The 35-year-old man of no fixed address was arrested and is now in custody for questioning.
