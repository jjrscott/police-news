A man has been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder following an incident in Park Hall, Stoke-on-Trent on Wednesday night (July 8).

Detectives are appealing for information after a man in his thirties was struck by a red Mercedes in Park Hall visitors' centre, Hulme Road at around 10pm.

The man suffered injuries to his head but these are not believed to be life-threatening.

A 29-year-old man has been released on bail while enquiries continue.

Anyone with information is urged to contact Staffordshire Police on 101 referring to incident 751 of 8 July or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.

**UPDATE** : Detectives are appealing for anyone with dash-cam footage from Park Hall Road, on the evening of the 8 July 2020 between approximately 9.30pm - 10.15pm, to get in touch.
