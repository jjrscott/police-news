Detectives in Staffordshire have charged a 23-year-old man following the rape of a woman in Stoke in May 2016.

Jarah Al-Shamari, of Bath Road, Bournemouth, was arrested on Tuesday (28 January) following ongoing enquiries.

He was charged with two counts of rape and appeared at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre this morning (Thursday 30 January).

He was remanded into custody and is due to appear at Stoke-on-Trent Crown Court on 2 March.
