A man has been charged with possession with intent to supply heroin.

Dean Andrew Mason, 36, of no fixed address, was arrested by officers from the Stoke South Neighbourhood Policing Team yesterday (Wednesday 27 January).
Mason is due to appear before magistrates at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre today (Thursday 28 January).

A 22-year-old woman, from Stoke-on-Trent, was also arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply Class A drugs (heroin). She has been released under investigation pending further investigation.
