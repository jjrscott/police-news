A 19-year-old man has appeared in court charged with a number of sexual offences on women and girls.

Wesley Streete, who is currently on remand awaiting trial in relation to other matters, appeared at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre on Saturday (14 March) and was further remanded to appear before Stafford Crown Court on 17 April.

Streete was charged on 13 March with rape of a child under 16, rape of a woman over 16, sexual activity with a girl and three counts of sexual assault.
