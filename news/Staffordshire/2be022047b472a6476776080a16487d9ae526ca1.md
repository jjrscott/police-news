Police are appealing for any dash-cam footage that may have recorded a collision on Horninglow Road in Burton on Tuesday November 1 at around 5pm.

The incident happened near the Navigation pub just past the junction of Shakespeare Road and involved a car colliding with a pedestrian.

A man in his eighties was left with injuries and remains in a serious condition in hospital at this time.

Witnesses or anyone with any information are asked to call 101 or private message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 522 of November 1. Alternatively, you can contact the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
