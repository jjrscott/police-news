Two men have been arrested in connection with a stabbing in Cannock.

A 27-year-old and a 34-year-old, both from Cannock, remain in police custody after being arrested on suspicion of wounding with intent following the incident last night.

Officers had been called to Sycamore Green at 11:30pm last night (Monday 23 November) to reports a man had been stabbed in the hand and head. He remains in hospital with non-life threatening injuries.

Enquiries are underway and we're appealing for anyone with any information to get in touch.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 784 of 23 November, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
