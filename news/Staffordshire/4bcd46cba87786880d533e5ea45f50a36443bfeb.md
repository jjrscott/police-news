Police are appealing for information after a number of valuable watches were stolen during a burglary in Uttoxeter.

The break-in took place at an address in Balance Street sometime between 1pm and 5.30pm on Monday 18 November.

The offender(s) gained entry into the property and made off with thousands of pounds worth of watches and a distinctive engagement ring.

The stolen items include; three Rolex 'Datejust' watches, three Tag Heuer watches, two woman's Breitling watches, a Rolex 'Submariner' watch, a Omega 'Seamaster' watch and an art deco emerald cut platinum engagement ring with a baguette surround.

DC Emma Francis said: "The investigation is on-going and we are appealing for anyone in the Uttoxeter area who saw anything suspicious on Monday afternoon or anyone with dash-cam footage from Balance Street between 5pm and 5.30pm to come forward and speak to officers.

"If you know where the stolen property is, or have been offered these items for sale, please get in touch."

Anyone with any information is asked to contact 101, quoting incident number 638 of 18 November.

You can also report crime by contacting the independent crime-fighting charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
