Three men have been detained over drugs offences in Cannock.

Police spotted two men, aged 52 and 49, acting suspiciously on Stafford Road, Cannock, at 5.40pm on Wednesday (24 March).

The 49-year-old man, from the Cannock area, was found in possession of a small amount of crack cocaine.

A subsequent search of the home address of the 52-year-old man found a quantity of cash, class A drugs, a bladed article and a quantity of cannabis.

A 19-year-old man from Birmingham, who was present at the address, was also detained.

The 49-year-old man was interviewed by police before being issued with a conditional caution.

The 52-year-old man, from the Cannock area, was arrested on suspicion of possession of supplying class A drugs, while the 19-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of class A and B drugs.

Both remain in police custody.
