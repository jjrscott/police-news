Police are appealing for information after around Â£10,000 worth of fishing equipment was stolen from a property.

Officers were called at 10am on Sunday (21 March) to reports of a burglary at an outhouse in the Tamworth area.

Police attended and a large number of poles, rods, reels, luggage bags and a seat box, were reported stolen.

The burglary is believed to have occurred at around 10.15pm on Saturday (20 March).

Officers are currently reviewing CCTV footage and urge anyone who may see items branded Frenzee, Matrix, Drennan or Preston Innovations for sale locally to get in get in touch.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 209 of 21 March.
