Detectives have charged a man following the death of a woman in Tamworth in 2017.

Police launched an investigation into the death of 72-year-old Khin Mar Win on Dorset Close in Tamworth on 26 September.

Win Naing, 79, of Dorset Close, Tamworth, has been charged with manslaughter by gross negligence.

He appeared at Stafford Crown Court today (3 August) and will next appear on 19 October.
