A Tamworth man has been jailed for four years after being caught with cocaine he intended to sell in Stoke-on-Trent.

Wayne Doran, 31, of Alexandra Mews, was jailed on 6 September at Stoke-on-Trent Crown Court after admitting possessing cocaine with intent to supply in Burslem.

Doran was arrested in a Vauxhall Corsa in a pub car park on 23 April this year.
