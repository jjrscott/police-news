Three men have been charged following an incident where mail was stolen in Great Wyrley on Saturday (Oct 24).

Craig Ramsay, 37, of Beakes Road, Smethwick, was charged with theft of mail and assaulting the postman and remanded in custody.

Thomas Smith, aged 30, of Meadow View, West Bromwich, was charged with theft and remanded in custody.

A 17-year-old from Walsall, who cannot be named for legal reasons, was charged with theft and bailed by the court.
