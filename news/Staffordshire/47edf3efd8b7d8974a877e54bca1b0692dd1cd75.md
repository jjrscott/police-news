Officers from Staffordshire Police have called off the search for missing person Robert Thornton, who was last seen in Great Haywood, near Stafford, on Monday 28 September.

Sadly, the body of a man, believed to be 51-year-old Robert, was found near to the railway line in Colwich, near Stafford, at approximately 8.45am this morning (Friday 16 October).

Formal identification is due to take place.

Robert's family are being supported by specially-trained officers and have asked for privacy at this difficult time.
