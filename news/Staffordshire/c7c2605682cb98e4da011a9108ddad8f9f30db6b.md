A woman appeared in court today charged with burglary following an arrest in Burslem yesterday (7 November).

Hayley Macdonald, aged 27 of no fixed address appeared at North Staffordshire Justice Centre today. The charges relate to the theft of perfume from a shop in Hanley on 29 September and a burglary at a shop on Campbell Place, Stoke-on-Trent on 19 August in which clocks and gift items were stolen.
