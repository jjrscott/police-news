A man has been arrested following a house fire in Meir, Stoke-on-Trent.

Police were called by colleagues at the fire service at 1.15am this morning (Wednesday 5 February) to reports of a house fire on Seddon Road. No one was injured.

Colleagues from Staffordshire Fire and Rescue Service attended to extinguish the fire before carrying out their own fire investigation.

A 50-year-old man, from Stoke-on-Trent, was arrested on suspicion of arson and remains in custody.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 39 of 5 February, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
