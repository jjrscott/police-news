Police are appealing for information relating to the theft of a bike from Burton.

The bike is believed to have been taken at around 4pm yesterday (10 September) from outside Asda. It has a distinctive appearance: white frame with 'kim' written on it in orange, Rolls Royce handlebars and specialist tyres and seat.

Anyone with any information is asked to ring 101 quoting incident 617 of 10 September.
