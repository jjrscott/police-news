A man found dead in his home in Maud Street, Fenton, has been named by police as 56-year-old Mark Timmis.

A 48-year-old man who was arrested on suspicion of murder has been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 333 of 2 February, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
