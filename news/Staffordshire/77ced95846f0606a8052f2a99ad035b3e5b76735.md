A woman has been arrested in connection with an assault in Stoke on Thursday evening (19 November).

A man suffered a facial injury following the incident on Vale Street at around 7.20pm and was taken to hospital.

A 34-year-old from Stoke-on-Trent was arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm with intent, possession of a sharp pointed article in a public place, and possession of a controlled drug of Class A. She remains in custody at this time.

An investigation is on-going and anyone with information is asked to call 101 or report online quoting incident 590 of 19 November.

Alternatively, for guaranteed anonymity, call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
