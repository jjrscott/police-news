A teenager from Middleport has been jailed for 30 months for drugs offences.

Abdulrahman Hereka, aged 19, of Newport Street, Middleport, admitted two charges of possessing heroin and crack cocaine with intent to supply on 9 July last year in Stoke-on-Trent.

He also pleaded guilty to assaulting a police officer.

He was sentenced on 13 March at Stoke-on-Trent Crown Court to a total of 30 months in prison.
