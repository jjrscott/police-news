A woman from Stoke-on-Trent has appeared before magistrates this morning (Nov 12) charged with the attempted murder of a six-year-old girl.

Naomi Platt, 41, of Chell Heath, is charged with the attempted murder of the girl in Chell Heath on 26 October.

She was remanded in custody by magistrates in Newcastle-under-Lyme.

The girl has now left hospital and is recovering following treatment.
