The woman found dead at a property on Beresford Street, Stoke-on-Trent, last week (11 September) has been formally identified as 40-year-old Lucy Wright.

A post mortem has taken place but has been unable to establish a cause of death.
Detectives do not believe the circumstances to be suspicious and a file will be prepared for HM Coroner.

Lucy's family have asked for privacy at this difficult time.
