Officers are investigating a burglary in Newcastle yesterday (Tuesday 3 December).

Police were called to Monmouth Place at 10pm to reports that three men had broken into the property.

One of the men had a crowbar and is described as white, of a stocky build, wearing a balaclava and dark coat.

The second offender is described as white, tall and thin. The third man had a tanned complexion with a moustache. Both wore balaclavas with dark clothing and spoke with Liverpool accents.

The intruders carried out an untidy search of the property and stole a handbag from the kitchen counter which contained a sum of cash and personal items.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 808 of 3 December, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
