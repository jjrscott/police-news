A man has been arrested on suspicion of arson following a fire at a guest house in Stafford this morning (6 Feb).

Officers were made aware of the fire in Lichfield Road at 2.20am after colleagues from Staffordshire Fire and Rescue Service attended the scene and extinguished the blaze.

Police cordoned off the area to secure the scene while the fire service carried out their fire investigation.

A man aged 41 from Stafford has been arrested on suspicion of arson and remains in custody.
