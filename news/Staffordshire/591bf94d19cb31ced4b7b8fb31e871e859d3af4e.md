Detectives have released an image of a man who suffered head injuries in an assault in Hanley in a renewed appeal for information.

Cal Burton remains unconscious in intensive care after suffering head injuries in Norville Drive on the evening of Sunday 13 December.

His family have released the image of 23-year-old Cal in the hope that people come forward to assist detectives with their investigation.

Detective Constable Jody Kay, of Staffordshire Police, said: "We're appealing for anyone who has any information about this incident or who may have seen or heard anything in the area between 10pm and 11pm to please get in touch."

A 23-year-old man from Stoke on Trent was arrested on suspicion of assault and is currently on police bail in relation to this incident.

Anyone with information is urged to contact Staffordshire Police on 101 or via social media referring to incident 655 of 13 December 2020.
