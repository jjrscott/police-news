Officers from the Staffordshire and West Midlands Serious Collision Investigation Unit are appealing for information following a road traffic collision in Penkridge this afternoon (14 September).

The incident involving a red Yamaha motorcycle and a pedestrian, took place at around 1.50pm on Wolverhampton Road, near to the petrol service station.

The pedestrian, a 89-year-old woman, was taken to hospital with a serious leg injury.

If you witnessed the collision or have relevant dash-cam footage, please contact 101 or email ciu@staffordshire.pnn.police.uk quoting incident 400 of 14 September
