Officers were called to a property on Rowhurst Crescent in Butt Lane at 4.45pm this afternoon (Saturday 20 March) by colleagues at the ambulance service reporting a man in cardiac arrest.

The man, in his 30s, was taken to hospital where he remains in a critical condition.

An investigation is now underway.

A 43-year-old woman, from Newcastle-under-Lyme, has been arrested on suspicion of Section 23 offences agains the person. She remains in police custody.
