The death of a man whose body was found in Coven is not being treated as suspicious.

Officers were called to Laches Lane, Slade Heath, at 9.50am on Saturday 13 February following concerns about an individual.

A 47-year-old man was pronounced dead by paramedics a short time later.

Officers are continuing to work to understand the man's movements prior to his death and have conducted house-to-house inquiries and reviewed CCTV footage.

The man is described as wearing a navy blue tracksuit top with a white collar, white cuffs, waistband and zip, grey jogging bottoms, black trainers and black man bag.

Formal identification is yet to take place and a file is being prepared for the coroner.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 133 of 13 February.

Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
