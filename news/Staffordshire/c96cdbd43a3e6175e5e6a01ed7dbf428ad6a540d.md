Five men have been arrested following a stabbing in Great Haywood near Stafford.

Just after 1pm this afternoon (Tuesday 18 August) emergency services were called to a property on Abbeyfields.

A 19-year-old man was found in a serious condition and taken to hospital with multiple stab wounds.

Officers stopped a vehicle on Tixall Road a short time later and arrested five men from Stafford.

A 35-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder and driving under the influence of drugs.

A further four men were arrested on suspicion of attempted murder.

They all remain in custody and an investigation is underway.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 288 of 18 August, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
