We were called to reports a man had been stabbed in Norton, Stoke-on-Trent just after 11pm last night (Sept 10).

On arrival an ambulance crew was in attendance at Mallorie Road treating a 19-year-old man for serious stab wounds. He was taken to hospital where he is receiving treatment. His condition is not life threatening.

Detectives are keen to speak to anyone who has information or witnessed the incident. Were you in Mallorie Road or Barks Drive last night? Did you see or hear a disturbance? Please contact Staffordshire Police on 101 referring to incident 773 of 10 September.
