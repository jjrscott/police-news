A motorcyclist is in hospital being treated for serious injuries following a road traffic collision in Leek Road, Stoke-on-Trent on 8 September.

The man, aged 19 and from the Lancashire area, was on a bike involved in a collision with a black Audi at around 9.50pm.

He is being treated for serious injuries. Anyone who witnessed the incident or saw either vehicle beforehand is urged to contact Staffordshire Police on 101, referring to incident 771 of 8 September.
