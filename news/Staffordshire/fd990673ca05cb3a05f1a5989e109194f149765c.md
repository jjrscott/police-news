Detectives are investigating after a man was stabbed in Shelton.

Police were called to Snow Hill at 4.20pm on Monday (28 September) by colleagues at the ambulance service with reports that a 19-year-old man had received stab wounds to his hand and bottom.

His injuries were not believed to be life threatening or life changing and he was taken to the Royal Stoke University Hospital for treatment.

Enquiries are ongoing.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 461 of 28 September, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
