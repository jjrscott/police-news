Power tools have been recovered after a man was arrested in Stoke-on-Trent.

The arrest, of a 24-year-old man from Stoke-on-Trent, took place yesterday (Wed 13 November) as part of an investigation into thefts of tools from vans in the Moorlands.

Officers are now working to try and identify the recovered property and will begin the process of reuniting it with owners.

Anyone with any information please email PC Phillip Nelson on phillip.nelson@staffordshire.pnn.police.uk.
