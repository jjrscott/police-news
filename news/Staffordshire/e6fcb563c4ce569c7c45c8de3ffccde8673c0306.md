Detectives are appealing for information following a robbery where jewellery was taken in Penkridge.

A man was assaulted and robbed by a group of about five men, described as being in their early 20s, in Stratford Grove area at around 11.30pm on Sunday night (18 October).

A gold bracelet was stolen. The suspects - were described as wearing all black, hooded and masked up, one wearing a white Nike jacket. After the robbery the suspects were seen to get into a small dark coloured car and make off.

Anyone who may have seen the incident or a car making off in the Shakespeare Drive/Boscomoor Lane/Wolverhampton Road area is urged to call 101 or via social media, quoting incident 828 of 18 October or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
