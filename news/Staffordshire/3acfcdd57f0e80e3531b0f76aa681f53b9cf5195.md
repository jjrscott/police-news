Police have uncovered a cannabis grow in a house in Packmoor, Stoke-on-Trent.

The discovery was made at a property on Carr Street at around 12.30pm today following a strong smell of cannabis being reported by people living nearby. When they gained entry, officers discovered more than two hundred plants growing in three rooms in the property.

An investigation is underway and officers will remain at the property while this continues.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 314 of 8 November, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
