Police have arrested three men on suspicion of attempted theft of a vehicle following an incident on Hill Street in Rugeley on November 12.

Officers were called at 2am to reports that three men were attempting to steal an Audi from a driveway and had smashed the window to gain access to the vehicle.

A 42-year-old and two 19-year-olds, all from Birmingham, were taken into custody after a vehicle was stopped on Rugeley Road in Armitage.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 26 of 12/11, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
