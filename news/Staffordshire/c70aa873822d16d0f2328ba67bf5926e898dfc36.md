UPDATE: A 22-year-old man arrested in connection with a robbery of a taxi driver in August 2019 has been released on conditional bail pending further enquiries. The investigation remains ongoing. Local officers continue to support the victim and provide community reassurance.

As a result of new evidence brought to the attention of Staffordshire Police, a 22-year-old man, from Cheddleton, has been arrested in connection with a robbery of a taxi driver in August 2019.

He has been arrested on suspicion of robbery and perverting the course of justice and remains in custody.

This is in relation to a video shared recently across social media.

Local officers continue to support the taxi driver and provide community reassurance.

Staffordshire Police takes reports of hate crime seriously and will deal with it robustly.
