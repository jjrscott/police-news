Officers have charged a man with a drug supply offence after they stopped a vehicle in Newcastle on Friday night (2 October).

Following intelligence, officers stopped a car on Cedar Road in Crackley at 10.15pm.

Ghafar Hussain, 24, of Stoner Street, Cobridge, has been charged with possession with intent to supply a controlled drug of Class A (cocaine) and acquire/use/possess criminal property.

Hussain appeared before magistrates at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre this morning.
