A man has been charged after a woman was sexually assaulted in Burton.

It follows reports of an incident on Ashby Road on Tuesday 2 February.

Jason Thomas Peter Hobson, 38, of no fixed address, has been charged with attempted rape, sexual assault, two counts of burglary and robbery.

Hobson is due to appear before magistrates at the North Staffordshire Justice Centre today (Wednesday 10 February).
