Four men and a woman are due before magistrates today charged with theft after eight dogs were stolen.

Danny Price, aged 22, of Tolney Lane, Newark, Natalie Edis, aged 29, of Shelley Avenue, Doncaster, and Jim Lee, aged 40, of Doncaster Road, Wakefield, have all been charged with theft, alongside Kevin Rogers, aged 31, and Creddy Smith, aged 32, both of Common Road, Brierley.

The theft took place at an address on Derby Road in Burton-upon-Trent on Monday 25 May.

They have all been remanded in custody and are due to appear at North Staffordshire Justice Centre.
