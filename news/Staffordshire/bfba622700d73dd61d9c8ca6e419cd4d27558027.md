Police seized suspected class A drugs after searching a car in Burntwood.

And two men are currently in custody following the discovery made by detectives from the Disruption Team South who were supported by the force's tactical department.

A car was searched in Burntwood at around 8.20am this morning where officers went on to discover a large amount of suspected heroin and crack cocaine.

**Pictured: Some of the suspected class A drugs seized**

Subsequently a 52-year-old man, of Burntwood, was arrested on suspicion of possession with intent to supply a class A drug.

As part of the same operation officers then went on to search an address in Burntwood and a 40-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of a class A drug.

The two men remain in custody and investigations are ongoing.
