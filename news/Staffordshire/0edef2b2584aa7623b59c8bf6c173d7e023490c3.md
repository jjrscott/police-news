Officers have arrested a second person on suspicion of murder after a woman's body was found at an address in Burton yesterday afternoon (2 March).

Investigations have continued and have led officers to arrest a woman, aged 44, from Burton.

It follows the earlier arrest of a 39-year-old man from Burton on suspicion of murder.

Anyone with information should contact us via social media or on 101 quoting reference 476 of 2 March.

The man and woman both remain in custody. The incident has been referred to the Independent Office for Police Conduct (IOPC) as is routine following earlier police contact.
