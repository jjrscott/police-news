Updated News Post

Two teenage boys have been arrested as part of an investigation into a stabbing in Stoke-on-Trent.

The incident took place on Sunday 1 December 2019 at a property on Keelings Drive, Penkhull.

A 16-year-old boy was taken to hospital with multiple stab wounds and has since been discharged.

A 15-year-old and an 18-year-old, both from Stoke-on-Trent, were arrested on suspicion of attempted murder on Sunday (22 December).

They have been released on conditional bail pending further enquiries.
