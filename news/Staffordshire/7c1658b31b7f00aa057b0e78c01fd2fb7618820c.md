Detectives have released CCTV footage of a man they would like to speak to in connection with an indecent exposure in Newcastle.

A teenager was walking along Rathbone Avenue at approximately 11am on Sunday 9 February, when a man grabbed her arm before exposing himself.

Enquiries are on-going and anyone who recognises the man captured on CCTV is asked to call 101 or send a private message on Facebook or Twitter, quoting incident number 359 of 9 February.

Alternatively, for guaranteed anonymity, call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
