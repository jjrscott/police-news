At 1.20pm today (Monday) officers were called to Stourbridge Road in Wombourne.

A 68-year-old man was sadly pronounced deceased at the scene. Police do not believe there are any suspicious circumstances surrounding his death and a report has been prepared for HM coroner.

Incident 330 of December 16.
