A Burton man has been charged with five counts of indecent exposure in a park in the town.

Anthony Carr, 55, is charged with indecent exposure at Washlands Sports Club, in Meadow Road, on 12, 13, 14 and twice on 17 September.

He is also charged with causing a child to watch him engage in sexual activity and four counts of breaching a sexual harm prevention order, all in Meadow Road, on 12, 13, 14 and 17 September.

Carr has been remanded in custody and will appear before magistrates this morning (September 18).
