Officers preparing a file for the Coroner following the death of a man in Ashley in June are appealing for information.

Craig Hackney, 37, sadly died when he fell from a ladder while working at a property on Wesleyan Road, Ashley, on 24 June 2020.

It is believed that a woman may have been passing the property on Wesleyan Road at the time of Mr Hackney's fall and may have information that could assist officers.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 260 of 24 June 2020, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
