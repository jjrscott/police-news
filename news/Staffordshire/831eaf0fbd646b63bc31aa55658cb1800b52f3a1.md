A man who died following an incident on Tontine Street in Hanley on Saturday 8 February has been named as Terry Findler.

Mr Findler aged 52, from Knutton, Newcastle-under-Lyme, was taken to hospital following a reported assault but sadly died.

A 28-year-old man from Hanley, initially arrested on suspicion of assault has been arrested on suspicion of manslaughter. He has now been bailed until March 8.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 817 of 8 February, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
