Two men have appeared in court charged with rape following an incident in Cauldon Park, Shelton, earlier this month.

Ondrej Kaleja, 38, and Julius Hanko, 39, both of Stoke-on-Trent, have both been charged with two counts of rape.

They were remanded into custody and will appear at Stoke-on-Trent Crown Court on Monday 16 December.
