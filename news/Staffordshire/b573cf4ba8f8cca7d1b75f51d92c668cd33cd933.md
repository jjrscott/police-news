Police are appealing for witnesses to come forward after a fatal collision near Uttoxeter yesterday (29 September).

The crash happened just after 2pm at the crossroads of Loxley Lane and Quee Lane in Willslock and involved a white Alfa Romeo Mito and a Silver Peugeot 206. sadly, the 57-year-old female passenger from the Peugeot was pronounced dead at the scene. Her family has been told and is being supported by specialist officers.

Officers from the West Midlands Serious Collision Investigation Unit attended the scene and carried out a detailed examination.

Anyone who saw either of the two vehicles before the collision is asked to ring 101 quoting incident 407 of 29 September. Anyone with dashcam footage, please email ciu@staffordshire.pnn.police.uk.
