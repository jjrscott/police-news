Police are appealing for help to find missing teenager Brandon West.

The 16-year-old was last seen on 28 November in Runcorn, Cheshire.

Officers believe he may be in Stoke-on-Trent or Newcastle-under-Lyme.

Brandon is described as white, 5ft 8ins tall, slim build with short (possibly shaven) brown hair. He was last seen wearing a blue and green North Face coat, black jumper with a white logo on the left chest, blue denim jeans - ripped and black trainers.

Anyone who has seen Brandon or has information about his whereabouts is asked to call 101 or direct message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter, quoting incident 598 of 28 November.
