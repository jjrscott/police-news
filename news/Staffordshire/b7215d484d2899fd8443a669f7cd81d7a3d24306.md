Officers are appealing for witnesses after a teenage boy suffered potentially life-changing injuries after being involved in a collision with a car in Longton.

The victim, 15, was struck by the silver Ford Fiesta at around 6.30pm on Sunday (25 October) in Baths Road.

Officers believe the driver and at least three passengers left the scene shortly after the collision.

The boy suffered a fractured skull, a broken thigh bone and a shoulder injury.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Staffordshire Police on 101, or via social media, quoting incident number 602 of 25 October or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
