Detectives are continuing to investigate after the discovery of a man's body in Fenton, Stoke-on-Trent, yesterday (Sunday 2 February).

The death of the 56-year-old man is being treated as suspicious and his family are being supported by specially-trained officers.

A forensic post mortem will take place in due course.

The 48-year-old man, arrested on suspicion of murder, remains in custody.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 333 of 2 February, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
