Detectives are investigating after a man was assaulted in Burslem this afternoon (Saturday 20 March).

Police were called at 12.45pm to reports of a three-vehicle RTC and an assault on Sytchmill Way.

It was reported that a blue BMW 320 was struck to the front by a blue Ford Kuga and to the rear by a silver Mitsubishi Shogun.

Four men got out of the vehicles and a passenger in the blue BMW was assaulted. The men got back into the vehicles and drove off.

The injured man was taken to the Royal Stoke University Hospital with a head injury, which his not believed to be life threatening or life changing.

The men are described as over 5ft 10" tall, three had a slim build and one had a large build. They were all wearing black balaclavas and black clothing.

The Ford Kuga has since been located in the Stoke-on-Trent area.

Officers will continue to offer reassurance and patrols in the area.

Anyone with information, CCTV or dashcam footage of the three vehicles is asked to contact Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 326 of 20 March. Alternatively, contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 111 555.
