Police seized cash, phones and a quantity of a suspected class A drug after stopping a car in Rugeley.

The vehicle was stopped on School Road at around 6.40pm on March 1 where officers carried out a search.

Following the stop officers conducted a search of a property in Wolverhampton where scales and cash were also seized.

Two men - aged 29 and 32 - were arrested on suspicion of being concerned in the supply of a class A drug. They remain in police custody.

Enquiries are ongoing.
