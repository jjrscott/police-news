Detectives are continuing to investigate the sudden death of a woman in Wolverhampton and are appealing for anyone with information to come forward.

Donna Boden, 46, from Tettenhall, was found on Dirtyfoot Lane in Lower Penn at around 1.30pm on Saturday (2 November). Her death is being treated as unexplained at this time.

Detective Inspector John Quilty, senior investigating officer, said: "We would like to hear from anyone who may have seen or heard from Donna recently, particularly, anyone who may have seen Donna between 5.30pm on Friday (1 November) and lunchtime the following day.

"Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 382 of 2 November, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
