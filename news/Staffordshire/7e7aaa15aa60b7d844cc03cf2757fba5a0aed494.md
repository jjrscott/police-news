Police are appealing for witnesses after two women were sexually assaulted in Stoke-on-Trent.

The women, who are in their early twenties, were approached by a man as they walked along Ashford Street in Shelton at around 7pm on Monday 16 September.

He touched them both inappropriately before running down the alleyway next to the post office.

The suspect is described as Asian, between 13-17 years of age, 5ft 2ins tall and of slim build. He was wearing a white cap and dark clothing.

Any witnesses or anyone with any information is asked to email Daniel.Davenport@staffordshire.pnn.police.uk quoting incident number 631 of 16 September.
