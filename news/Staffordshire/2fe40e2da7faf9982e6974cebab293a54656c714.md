A man has been arrested in connection to a sexual assault in Hanley this evening (Friday 10 January).

Officers were called to report of a female who was sexually assaulted near a bus stop on Bethesda Street at around 6.20pm.

The man, a 28-year-old from Stoke-on-Trent, is in custody awaiting questioning.
