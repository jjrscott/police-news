Detectives have charged two men with the murder of 26-year-old Anas Chergat in Stoke-on-Trent last August.

Richard Street, aged 29, and Alan Martyn, aged 45, both of no fixed address, are charged with the murder of Anas on 23 August 2019 in Josiah Wedgwood Street, Etruria.

Street and Martyn will appear before court on April 1. Both men are in custody.
