Two teenage boys have been arrested after a man was stabbed in Tunstall on Friday (13 September).

A 17-year-old boy, from Stoke-on-Trent, has been arrested on suspicion of assault GBH and concerned in the supply of controlled drugs (Class A and B).

A second 17-year-old boy, also from Stoke-on-Trent, has been arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to commit Section 18 assault.

Both remain in custody.
