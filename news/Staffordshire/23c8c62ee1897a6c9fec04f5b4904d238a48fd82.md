Detectives continue to appeal for witnesses to a serious assault in Meir on Friday (1 May) evening.

The assault of a 23-year-old man happened shortly before 11.20pm. He was found on Lansbury Grove with a serious head injury and taken to hospital by the ambulance service.

The victim remains in hospital and is in a serious but stable condition.

A man, from Stoke-on-Trent, has been arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm and remains in custody.

Witnesses or anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 717 of 1 May, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
