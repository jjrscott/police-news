Officers are investigating a collision in Tean last week where a man was left with a broken wrist.

Police were called at 5.20pm on Wednesday 25 November to reports of non-stop collision on Hollington Road, heading towards Draycott, involving a grey Seat Ibiza and a dark blue vehicle. The driver of the blue vehicle did not stop.

The driver of the Seat received a broken wrist and the car was written off due to the level of damage.

Anyone with information, dasham or CCTV footage of the collision should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 573 of November 25, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
