Officers are investigating after Apple products were stolen from Currys PC World during a robbery.

Police were called to Festival Heights Retail Park in Etruria, Stoke-on-Trent, at 4.40pm, yesterday (Thursday 19 December) to reports that three men entered the store and stole Apple products.

In the process the wooden tables were broken and used to threaten the staff.

The men are described as white, wearing dark clothing, including baseball caps. One of the men wore navy blue tracksuit bottoms with a stripe down the side and a black jacket. He was approximately 5ft 4ins with tattoos on both arms and spoke with a Stoke accent.

Anyone with any information should message Staffordshire Police on Facebook or Twitter quoting incident number 516 of December 19, call 101 or alternatively contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
