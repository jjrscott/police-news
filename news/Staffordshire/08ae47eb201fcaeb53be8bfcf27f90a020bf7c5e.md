A man wanted in connection with various offences in Stoke-on-Trent is sought by officers from Staffordshire Police.

We are appealing to the public to get in touch if they have seen, or have any information on the whereabouts of David Hibbs.

The 33-year-old is wanted in connection with a number of offences including making threats to kill, harassment and breach of a restraining order.

He has connections in Hanley and Burslem.

Anyone with any information is urged to contact Staffordshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 118 of 31 of May. Alternatively, please call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
