Two men have been charged in connection with an incident on Apedale Road, Chesterton, on Saturday (14 September).

Jake Pettifer, 22, of Birch House Road, Newcastle, and Callum Weaver, 26, of Applecroft, Newcastle, have both been charged with possession of an imitation firearm with intent to cause fear of violence.

Pettifer has also been charged with harassment.

They appeared at North Staffordshire Justice Centre this morning.
