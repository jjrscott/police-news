A woman has died following a road traffic collision on the car park of B&Q in Meir Park, Stoke-on-Trent.

At just after 2.45pm yesterday (Tuesday 1 October) police were called to reports of a collision between a blue Ford Focus and two pedestrians.

Emergency services attended and one of the pedestrians, a 67-year-old woman from Leek, sadly died on arrival at the Royal Stoke University Hospital. The second pedestrian received minor injuries.

Officers from the Staffordshire and West Midlands Serious Collision Investigation Unit are appealing for witnesses or anyone with relevant dash cam footage to contact 101 or email CIU@staffordshire.pnn.police.uk, quoting incident 422 of 1 October.
