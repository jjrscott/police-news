Detectives from Staffordshire Police have charged two men from Birmingham following a break-in at a convenience store in Kings Bromley.

Khye Salmon, aged 18, of Radleys Walk, has been charged with burglary other than dwelling - theft, driving a motor vehicle which had been taken without owner's consent and driving whilst disqualified.

Anthony Ferrie, aged 25, of Grange Farm Drive, has been charged with burglary other than dwelling - theft, being carried in a motor vehicle which had been taken without owner's consent and possession of a controlled drug (Class B).

They are both remanded in custody and are due to appear at North Staffordshire Justice Centre in Newcastle-under-Lyme this morning (13 November).
