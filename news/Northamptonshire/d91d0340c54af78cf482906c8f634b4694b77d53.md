Detectives are appealing for witnesses after a man was stabbed in Prentice Court, Northampton.

The incident happened last night (Sunday, February 7) at about 8.20pm when a 31-year-old man was stabbed in an incident involving up to five males.

The man was taken to hospital where he remains in a serious but stable condition.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111. Detectives are particularly keen to hear from a male on a bicycle who may have witnessed the incident. If you are this person, please come forward.

**Incident number:** 21000078249
