Police are appealing for witnesses after an incident in Northampton on New Year's Day.

The incident took place at about 11.30pm in Burleigh Road, Semilong, when three men banged on the front door of a property with a tool, which damaged the door.

The three men then gained entry to a communal garden before climbing over a wall and making off.

The first offender was white, in his 20s, 5ft 4ins, slim and wore a black hoody, black tracksuit bottoms and black baseball cap.

The second offender was white, in his 20s, 5ft 6ins, slim and wore a grey hoody and tracksuit bottoms, with black trainers.

There is no description of the third offender.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed this incident or who may have any information regarding it. Witnesses or anyone with information can call police on 101, quoting 21000001692, alternatively contact independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
