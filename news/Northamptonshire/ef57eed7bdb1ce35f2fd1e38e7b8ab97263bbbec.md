A 46-year-old man has died following a road traffic collision in Irchester.

The collision happened at about 5.35pm on Friday at the junction of Gypsy Lane and Wollaston Road.

The driver of the white Ford Focus was given first aid by members of the public as well as paramedics, but was sadly pronounced dead at the scene.

Northamptonshire Police are appealing for witnesses and any dashcam before, during or after the collision.

Anyone with information or who witnessed this incident is asked to call the Drivewatch Hotline on 0800 174615.
