Officers are appealing for information after a burglary in Northampton on Friday, December 11.

Sometime between 1.40am and 2am, the new Costcutter/Post Office store in Farmfield Court was broken into.

The unknown offenders pried open the metal shutter to gain entry. Nothing was stolen.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.

Incident number: 20000653356
