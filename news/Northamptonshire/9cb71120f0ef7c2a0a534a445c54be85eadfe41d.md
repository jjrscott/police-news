Detectives investigating a robbery at a Northampton pawnbrokers in December have released a second image of a man they think may be able to help with their enquiries.

The incident happened at about 4.50pm on Wednesday, December 9, at a business in Regent Square.

An appeal was released at the time with a CCTV image of a man officers believe may have information relating to the incident.

A second image (above) of the man has now been released in order to try and identify and trace him.

Officers are appealing for the man pictured, or anyone who recognises him to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 or alternatively Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
