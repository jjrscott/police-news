A man has been charged with aggravated burglary following an incident in Severn Drive, Kings Heath, Northampton.

During the incident early on Wednesday, December 23, a man was stabbed in the thumb and property was damaged.

Christopher Gibson, aged 45, also from Severn Drive, has been remanded in custody.

He will appear before Northampton Magistrates' Court on Friday, January 22.
