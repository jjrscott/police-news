Police investigating an unprovoked attack on a man in Northampton are appealing for witnesses to get in touch, including three people who helped the victim.

At around 8.50pm on Wednesday, March 24, a man was walking along Harborough Road when he was passed by another man, who then attacked him from behind while shouting at him.

The victim suffered significant injuries during the assault, including a broken leg, before members of the public were able to get his attacker off him.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who saw the incident, especially three young males who helped the victim.

Anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101, or Crimestoppers in confidence on 0800 555111.
