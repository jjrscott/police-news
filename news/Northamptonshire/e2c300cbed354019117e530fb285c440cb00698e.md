Police officers are appealing for witnesses after an attempted robbery at Kingfisher Lake, at the rear of Dairymeadow Court in Northampton.

The incident occurred between 5.10pm and 5.25pm on Sunday, February 7, when a teenage cyclist was approached by a group of between five or six males aged between 14 and 18.

The group threatened the boy and demanded he handed over his bike however, his sister joined him and they both left. He was not physically hurt and no property was stolen. The group continued to walk around the lake.

One of the offenders is described as white, aged 14 to 16, 5ft 7 to 5ft 8in, and of a slim build. A second offender is described as being of mixed race, aged 14 to 16, 5ft 4in, of a medium build with scruffy black hair. He was wearing a large black puffa-style jacket.

Anyone who witnessed this incident or has any information which could assist with the investigation is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000078828. Or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
