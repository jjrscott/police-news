Police are asking for the public's help to identify a woman they believe may have information about an incident in a Northampton store where a store assistant was assaulted.

The incident happened in The Range, St James Retail Park, between 6.30pm and 6.45pm on Thursday, November 26, when three women were spoken to by staff as they were leaving the store. One of the women who was pushing a pushchair then coughed three to four times in the store assistant's face.

Anyone who recognises the woman pictured are asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 20000626956.
