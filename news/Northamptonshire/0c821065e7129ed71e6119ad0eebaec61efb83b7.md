A man has been charged with robbery following an incident in Cottingham Road, Corby, which happened on Sunday, December 13.

Peter Tierney, 39, of Finland Way, Corby, was charged yesterday, January 4, and was today remanded in custody by Northampton Magistrates' court. He will next appear at Northampton Crown Court on January 20.
