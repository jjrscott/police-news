Police are appealing for witnesses following a burglary in Northampton Road, Higham Ferrers.

Offenders broke in sometime between 10pm on Wednesday, December 16 and 8.20am the following day. They took a set of car keys and stole the vehicle, a black BMW X5, which was parked on a verge next to the property.

Witnesses, or anyone with information, are asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101, quoting reference number 20000665747.
