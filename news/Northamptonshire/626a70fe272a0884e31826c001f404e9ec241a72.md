Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a residential burglary in The Pyghtle Road, Wellingborough.

Between 4.15am and 4.30am on Friday, January 22, the occupant was woken by the sound of someone at the front door, and has discovered the property had been broken into. Nothing is believed to have been stolen.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed this incident or who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area.

Anyone who may have any information regarding this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000049217. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
