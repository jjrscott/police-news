On Saturday, November 28 we released CCTV images of a man we were keen to speak to in connection with a suspicious incident in Silverwell Close, Moulton, Northampton.

On Tuesday, December 1 a 19-year-old man was arrested in connection with the incident. He has since been released on bail pending further enquiries.

The investigation into the incident, which happened on Friday, November 27 between 3.30pm and 4pm, and involved a man knocking on a door threating one of the occupant's with violence, is ongoing.

Anybody with any information should contact police on 101 and quote Operation Detail, or crime number 20000628877.
