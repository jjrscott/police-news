Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a woman had her bag stolen in a robbery in Kingsley Park Terrace, Northampton.

The incident took place between 11.15am and 11.25am on Monday, December 28, outside the Kingsley Park Methodist Church as the 78-year-old woman walked along Kingsley Park Terrace.

She was approached by a man who grabbed her arm, hurting her in the process, who then pulled her shopping bag from her and ran off down Milton Street towards Junction Road.

A member of the public quickly came to her assistance and called the police. The offender is described as a white man in his 50s, between 5ft 6 and 5ft 8in, and of a slim build with greying hair and unshaven. He was wearing a brown coat, brown shoes and a grey beanie hat.

Anyone with information or anyone who may have witnessed this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000683055. Or alternatively, if you wish to stay anonymous, call Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.

A 64-year-old man from Northampton has been arrested in connection with this incident and released under investigation pending further enquiries.
