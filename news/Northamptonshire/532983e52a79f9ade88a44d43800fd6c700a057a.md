Police officers have released a CCTV image of a male they wish to speak to after an incident of sexual harassment in Northampton.

The incident happened in New South Bridge Road on Thursday, January 14, at about 9.20am, when a male on a bicycle approached a woman and made inappropriate comments of a sexual nature towards her before riding off.

Officers investigating the incident believe the male in the image could assist them with their enquiries and they are encouraging him or anyone who knows him to contact police on 101.
