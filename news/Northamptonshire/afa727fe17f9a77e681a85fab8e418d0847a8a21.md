On Thursday (August 20), two arrests were made following raids on the Hemmingwell estate.

An 18 year old man has been charged with possession with intent to supply Class A drugs. A boy, aged 16, has been released under investigation pending further enquiries for the same offence.

Crack cocaine and drug paraphernalia was seized from this address.

These warrants form part of Northamptonshire Police's 100 Days of Intense Policing Activity.
