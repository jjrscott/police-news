Officers investigating an incident of indecent exposure in Duston have released an e-fit of a man they would like to speak to.

Shortly after 3pm on Wednesday, December 9, a 17-year-old girl walking along Berrywood Road, towards the junction with Main Road, saw a man in a hedgerow who exposed himself to her as she passed him.

Police have now released an e-fit of a man they would like to speak to in connection with this incident.

Anyone who recognises the man pictured, or has information about this incident, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

**Incident number:** 20000653677
