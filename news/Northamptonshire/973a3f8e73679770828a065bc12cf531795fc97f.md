Police officers are appealing for information following an incident of indecent exposure in Northampton.

The incident happened in Duston, at around 3pm on Wednesday, December 9.

A 17-year-old girl was walking along Berrywood Road when she saw a man in the hedgerow who was exposing himself.

The offender is described as a 50 to 60-year-old white man of average height and large build, with a white or grey beard and wearing a baggy jacket. He had a small dog with him.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
