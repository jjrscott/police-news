Police officers have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to in connection with a residential burglary in The Scarplands, Northampton.

Between 11pm and 11.55pm on Tuesday, March 9, the occupant was woken after his garage alarm was activated and he saw two men using bolt croppers to break into his property.

He then challenged the offenders who fled on foot down The Scarplands and on to Stanfield Road. Nothing was stolen however damage was caused to the garage door and lock.

The first offender was white, in his late teens to early 20s, 5ft 10in and of a slim build. He was wearing a dark coloured hooded top and tracksuit bottoms, and dark coloured trainers with a thick banded sole.

The second offender was also white and in his late teens to early 20s, 6ft and of a slim build. He was wearing a dark coloured hooded top and tracksuit bottoms, and dark coloured trainers.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who can help identify this man, or who may have seen two men fitting this description in The Scarplands area between the stated times.

Anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000135435. Or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
