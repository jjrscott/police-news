Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a residential burglary in Greenfields Drive, Oundle.

Between 1.30am and 2am on Tuesday, December 8, someone attempted to break into the front door of the bungalow of a 70-year-old man. It is thought that dogs barking may have deterred the offender/s.

Anyone who witnessed this or who may have any information about the incident, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000650656. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
