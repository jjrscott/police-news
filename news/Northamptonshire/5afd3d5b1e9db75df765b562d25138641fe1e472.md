Police are appealing for witnesses and information after a man was injured in Brackmills, Northampton.

At 5.25am this morning, Friday, March 19, a man riding a bicycle was in collision with a car which overtook him in Salthouse Road.

The cyclist was taken to hospital suffering injuries which are not believed to be life-threatening.

Anyone with information or who witnessed this incident is asked to call the Drivewatch Hotline on 0800 174615, regarding incident 45 of March 19.
