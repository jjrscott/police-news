Police officers are appealing for witnesses after an incident in Bath Lane, which took place yesterday, Wednesday, December 16.

At around 5pm, there was an altercation between a group of around ten males. A 17-year-old boy and a 19-year-old man were subsequently taken to Kettering General Hospital with non-life threatening stab wounds.

Anyone who witnessed this or who may have any information about the incident, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Incident number: 20000664447.
