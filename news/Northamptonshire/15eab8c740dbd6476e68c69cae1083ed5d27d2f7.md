Police are appealing for witnesses after a 17-year-old was assaulted on Tuesday evening.

The incident took place in Barton Road, Kettering, between 5.20pm and 5.40pm, on Tuesday, December 1, when the teenager was approached by an unknown man who sprayed an unknown substance on him.

The man then grabbed the victim and punched him repeatedly in the back before the victim, who didn't sustain any injuries, managed to get away and call for help.

The offender was a white man, aged in his late 30s to early 40s, around 5ft 9in and of slim build. He had a dark coloured, short beard and was wearing a navy coloured hooded top.

Police would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed this incident or who may have any information regarding it.

Witnesses or anyone with information can call police on 101 quoting 20000637740 or independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
