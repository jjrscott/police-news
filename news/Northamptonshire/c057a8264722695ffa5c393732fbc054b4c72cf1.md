Detectives investigating an incident in the early hours of Saturday, February 13, are appealing for members of the public who may have information to come forward.

The incident happened in the Kingsthorpe area of Northampton between 1am and 5am on Saturday morning when a 31-year-old man was the victim of an assault.

The attack is believed to have taken place around the Harborough Road area with Chalcombe Avenue, Glan-y-mor Terrace and Kingsthorpe Rec particular places of interest.

If you saw anything suspicious during these times or have any pertinent dash cam or doorbell footage, please come forward and ring 101, quoting incident number 191 if 16/02/21.

Alternatively, if you wish to remain anonymous, please call Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.
