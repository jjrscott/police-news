A 15-year-old boy has been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder by detectives investigating a serious stabbing in Northampton.

The teenager, who is from Northampton, was arrested earlier today, Thursday, April 1, after a 19-year-old man suffered life-threatening stab wounds in Bailiff Street, close to the Racecourse, at around 9.30pm on Monday, March 29.

He remains in police custody this afternoon. The victim is recovering from his injuries.

Officers are still seeking witnesses to the incident. Anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101, or Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.
