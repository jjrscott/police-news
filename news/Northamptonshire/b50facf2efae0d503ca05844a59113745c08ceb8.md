Police officers are trying to identify the owner of a paddle mixer which is believed to have been stolen shortly before Christmas.

The Dewalt M14 54-volt paddle mixer has distinctive writing on it in marker pen which the owner will be able to identify.

Anyone who believes the item may be theirs, is asked to contact PC Robert Johnson on 101 or by emailing robert.johnson@northants.police.uk, quoting reference number 20000684832.
