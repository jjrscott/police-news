Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a woman was threatened by a dog walker as she walked her own pet on Bradlaugh Fields, Northampton.

The incident took place between 6pm and 6.15pm on Saturday, December 12, as both dog walkers held on to their pets as they walked past each other. After a short distance, the woman let her dog go, and it returned to the man and his dog.

The woman apologised and the man has become verbally abusive towards her and threatened her with his dog. The man was white and in his 20s, 6ft, of a slim build and wearing a navy tracksuit. His dog is described as a Doberman.

Anyone who witnessed this incident or who may have any information which could help with the investigation is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000656687. Or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
