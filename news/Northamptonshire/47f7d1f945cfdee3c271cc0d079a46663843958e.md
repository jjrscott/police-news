Members of the public are encouraged to come forward if any of the bicycles (pictured) may belong to them.

The bicycles were recovered after a search of a premises in Wellingborough and officers who believe they are stolen are now hoping to reunite them with their rightful owners.

Anyone who has recently had a bicycle stolen is asked to look at the images and make contact via 101 if they think one of the bikes is theirs.
