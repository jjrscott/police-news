Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a Covid-19 banner was defaced outside Falconers Hill Infant School in Ashby Road, Daventry.

The incident took place between 3.30pm on Monday, January 4 and 8.30am on Tuesday, January 5, when a Northamptonshire County Council banner promoting social distancing was vandalised.

The offender/s wrote "no white in poster" and "white lives matter" using a black marker pen and used masking tape to cover the faces of minority groups depicted on the banner.

Anyone with information or who may have witnessed this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000006557. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
