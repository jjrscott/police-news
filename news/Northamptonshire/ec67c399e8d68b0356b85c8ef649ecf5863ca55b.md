Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a residential burglary in Hatton Street, Wellingborough.

Between 3.30am and 3.40am on Friday, November 6, the offender attempted to gain entry to the property via a kitchen window, and broke into the occupant's garden shed.

The offender is believed to have been in his teens and was wearing a black hooded top and black jogging bottoms. After the burglary, the offender headed in the direction of Harrowden Road.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may CCTV or dash-cam footage who may have captured a male walking in the direction of Harrowden Road between the stated times.

Anyone who witnessed this incident or who may have any information which could assist with the investigation is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000587676. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
