Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a serious assault last night (Sunday, November 29) in Mull Drive, Corby.

The incident happened at about 9.30pm when there was an altercation between a group of people which resulted in two men being stabbed.

They were both taken to hospital however their injuries are not suspected to be life threatening or life changing.

Three men aged 22, 24 and 25, and one woman, aged 22, have all been arrested on suspicion of GBH with Intent in connection with the incident and remain in police custody at this time.

Officers are now appealing for anyone who witnessed what happened or anyone who might have information about the incident to come forward.

Please call us on 101 with any information or if you would prefer to remain anonymous, you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.

Detectives are satisfied that this was an isolated incident and that there is no wider risk to members of the public. Police officers will be patrolling the area over the coming days to provide reassurance to local residents.
