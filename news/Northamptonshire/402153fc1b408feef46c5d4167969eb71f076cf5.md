Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a woman was robbed in The Racecourse, Northampton.

The incident happened on Sunday, February 14, between 8.30pm and 9.40pm, as the woman was walking along the path at the back of the houses towards Barrack Road when a man appeared in her path.

He demanded she hand over her handbag and the rings on her fingers before making off.

The suspect is described as a white man with sallow skin, about 30 years old, 6ft, and of an average build. He had short black hair and brown eyes and was wearing a dark jacket and dark blue jeans.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.
