Police investigating a burglary in Northampton have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to identify and speak to.

At some point between midnight and 7.30pm on Tuesday, March 2, a property in Thornton Road, in Queens Park, was broken into.

Items stolen included a bank card which was later used to purchase items elsewhere.

Officers have now issued an image of man they would like to identify, as it is believed he may have information relevant to the investigation.

The man pictured, anyone who recognises him or has information about the burglary is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101, or independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.

**Incident number:** 21000136197

_Officers would like to identify this man - any information to 101._
