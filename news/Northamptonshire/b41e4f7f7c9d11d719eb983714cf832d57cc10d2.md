Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a residential burglary in Marlborough Road, Northampton.

Between 10pm on Friday, November 27 and 5am on Saturday, November 28, the offender gained access to the property through a rear window and stole a number of items including keys to the family's car.

The car - a black Volkswagen Passat - was also stolen. The vehicle has since been recovered. The offender is also believed to have broken into a van parked at the Thomas Beckett car park.

The offender is believed to have been a white man in his early 20s, of a medium build with short dark coloured hair. He was wearing grey jogging bottoms and black T-shirt.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may CCTV or dash-cam footage who may have captured the vehicle travelling around Northampton between the stated times or the offender breaking into the van or property.

Anyone who witnessed this incident or who may have any information which could assist with the investigation is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000629655. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
