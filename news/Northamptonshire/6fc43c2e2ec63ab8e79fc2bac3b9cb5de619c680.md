Police officers are appealing for witnesses after an incident of criminal damage in Olympic Square, Corby.

The incident took place between 7.35pm and 7.45pm on Friday, December 18, when a man damaged two vehicles with a sharp instrument, before he walked off towards Keating Road.

The offender is described as white and in his late 20s, of a stocky build with brown hair and was wearing a black jacket and brown trousers.

Anyone with information or who may have witnessed this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000668612. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
