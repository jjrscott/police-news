Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a burglary at a residential address in Bridgewater Court, Corby.

Between 6.40am and 7.45pm on Wednesday, February 10, the rear door has been forced and entry gained. Once inside, the offender/s have stolen a quantity of cash, a silver Samsung A80 and a blue and copper Curren watch.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed this incident or who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area of Bridgewater Court between the stated times

Anyone who may have any information regarding this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000083671. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
