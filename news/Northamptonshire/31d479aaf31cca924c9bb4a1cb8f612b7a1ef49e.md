Witnesses are being sought after an altercation between two drivers following a road traffic collision at the Abthorpe Roundabout in Towcester.

The incident took place between 2.45pm and 3.15pm on Sunday, January 24, after the drivers of a white Nissan Qashqai and a red Vauxhall Astra became involved in an altercation following the collision.

The Nissan Qashqai driver was a black man, 5ft 11 and of a stocky build. The Vauxhall Astra driver was a white man, 5ft 10 and of a stocky build.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed either the collision or the subsequent altercation, or who may have captured the incident on dash cam footage.

Anyone with information or who may have witnessed this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000052297. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

A 29-year-old man from Towcester has been arrested in connection with this incident, and has been released on police bail pending further enquiries.
