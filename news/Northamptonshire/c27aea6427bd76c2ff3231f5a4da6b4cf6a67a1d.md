Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a red Vauxhall Zafira was targeted twice in Fulmar Lane, Wellingborough.

The first incident took place between 8pm on Sunday, December 31, and 6am on Friday, January 1, when the offender/s used a sharp instrument to puncture one of the vehicle's offside tyres.

Then, between 6pm on Saturday, January 23, and 5am on Sunday, January 24, an instrument, possibly a screwdriver, was used to puncture one of the nearside tyres.

Anyone with information or who may have witnessed either incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 210000057197. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
