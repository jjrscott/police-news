Police are appealing to find two dog walkers who saw a man threatening a couple with a knife in a Wellingborough park.

At around 12.15am on Tuesday, February 16, a man and a woman were in Croyland Park when they were approached and threatened by a man with a knife.

While this was happening, a couple with a small brown dog and a small black dog on leads passed nearby, walking along a footpath by the stream heading towards Kingsway.

Officers investigating the incident are appealing for these witnesses to come forward as they may be able to provide vital information.

If you believe you were one of these witnesses, please contact Northamptonshire Police on 101.
