Witnesses are being sought after a taxi was broken into while parked on the drive of a residential property in Sandhurst Close, Northampton.

Between 8.45pm and 9.15pm on Sunday, February 7, the offender smashed the driver's door window and stole a Timberland bag containing Â£50 in change, which had been left under the driver's seat.

The offender is described as white, 6ft and of a slim build. He was wearing a grey hooded top, jogging bottoms and a beanie hat. He had a cross-body bag and was carrying a torch. He left the scene on foot and headed into the Sandhurst housing estate.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the theft, or who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area between the stated times.

Anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000078277. Or alternatively, if you wish to stay anonymous, call Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.
