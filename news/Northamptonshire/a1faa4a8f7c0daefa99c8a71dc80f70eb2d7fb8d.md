Police officers are appealing for witnesses after an attempted robbery near to the Spring Rise Park play area in Highfield Road, Kettering.

Between 1.30pm and 2pm on Tuesday, March 23, a teenager was walking along a footpath leading to the play area, when he was approached by another male who threatened him with a knife and demanded he turn out his pockets.

The teenager ran away and nothing was stolen. The offender was white, 5ft 6in and of an average build. He was wearing a grey tracksuit with the hood up, white and grey Nike Air Force One trainers and a distinctive Joker style face covering.

Anyone who witnessed this incident or has any information which could assist with the investigation is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000164011. Or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
