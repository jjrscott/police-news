Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a residential burglary in Lindale Close, Northampton.

Between 8am and 4pm on Wednesday, December 9, the offender/s have smashed the glass panel on a rear door to access the property. Once inside they have made an untidy search and stole three gold watches.

Officers are appealing for residents and businesses in the Lindale Close or Coniston Avenue area to check their CCTV or dash-cam footage for anyone who may have been acting suspiciously in the area between the stated times.

Anyone who witnessed this or who may have any information about the incident, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000650656. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
