Officers investigating a Northampton burglary in which jewellery of sentimental value was stolen are appealing for witnesses and information.

Between 6.45pm and 7.30pm on Friday, January 29, a property in North Holme Court, Thorplands, was broken into via the back door.

Once inside the offender/s stole a number of items of jewellery, including diamond rings, gold and silver necklaces, bracelets and earrings, many of which had huge sentimental value to their owner. A Waterford crystal vase was also taken.

Anyone with information about the break-in, or who has been offered jewellery for sale in suspicious circumstances, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

**Incident number:** 21000062772
