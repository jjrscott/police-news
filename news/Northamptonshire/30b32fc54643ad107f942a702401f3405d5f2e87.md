Officers investigating a commercial burglary in Northampton are appealing for witnesses and information.

Between 10.15pm and 10.50pm on Friday, March 12, two unknown offenders triggered an intruder alarm by breaking into premises in Quarry Close, Moulton Park.

The suspects are described as two men, wearing hooded jumpers. A white Ford Transit van was captured on CCTV driving away from the area.

Anyone with information relevant to this investigation is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

Incident number: 21000141587
