Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a residential burglary in Harborough Road, Dingley

Between 10pm on Saturday, January 9 and midday on Sunday, January 10, the offender/s has thrown a brick through a double-glazed window to gain access to the property. A tidy search was carried out but no property was stolen.

The property is situated down a single track off the A427 Harborough Road, and officers would like to hear from anyone who may have seen any vehicles acting suspiciously in the area between the stated times.

Anyone who may have any information regarding this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000016001. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
