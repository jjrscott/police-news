Detectives investigating an alleged assault outside Argos in St Peters Square, Northampton, believe the men pictured may have important information about the incident, which happened at about 11am on Monday, June 15.

The victim was at the Argos depot car park when he was approached and assaulted by one man, sustaining a cut to the back of his head and bruising to his body. Another man stood nearby filming the incident.

Anyone who recognises the men pictured or has any information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 20000302922, or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
