Police officers in Northampton would like to hear from anyone who may have seen a male acting suspiciously in the area of Ashwood Road last week.

On three separate occasions, between 7.30pm on Thursday, March 18, and 8.55pm on Saturday, March 20, a male was seen acting suspiciously in residential gardens and attempting to gain access to garages and sheds.

The male is described as white and was wearing a black jacket, dark jeans and a black beanie type hat. He was possibly using a bicycle.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have seen this male or who may have doorbell or CCTV footage of anyone acting suspiciously in the area over the past week or so.

Anyone who may have any information regarding this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 210000158058. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
