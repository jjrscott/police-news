Police officers are appealing for witnesses after an incident in Kilnway, Wellingborough.

The incident happened on Monday, February 22, at about 7.20pm, when three men broke into a property in the area and threatened the occupants with weapons before making off. Nothing was stolen.

The suspects are described as three men in their early 20s in dark clothing with hoodies on and the hoods worn up. One of the men was white, about 6ft, with blond hair and wearing a his-vis jacket over his hoody. A second man was black and about 5ft 11in.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101 or if you wish to remain anonymous, Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.
