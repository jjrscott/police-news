Police are appealing for witnesses following a fight involving around 10 people outside a Daventry pub.

The public order incident happened at about 9.45pm on Christmas Eve in the car park of The Peppermill pub in Westerburg Square.

Anyone with information about this incident should call Northamptonshire Police on 101, quoting incident 20000679242, or ring Crimestoppers in confidence on 0800 555111
