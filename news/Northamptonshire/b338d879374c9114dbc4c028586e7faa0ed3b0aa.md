Police officers have released a CCTV image of a male who they believe may have information about a suspected arson which took place in Duke Street, Kettering.

The incident happened between 11pm and 11.15pm on Sunday, November 22, when several bins are believed to have been deliberately set on fire.

Officers investigating the incident believe this male may be able to assist with their enquiries, and are appealing for him or anyone who may recognise him, to get in touch.

Anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000619076. Or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
