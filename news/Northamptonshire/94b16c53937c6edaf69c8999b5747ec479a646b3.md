Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a residential burglary in Chaucer Street, Northampton.

The incident took place between 10.30pm on Wednesday, December 23 and 9.30am on Thursday, December 24, when the offender/s gained access via an unlocked front door.

The offender/s stole two wallets - one black and one blue - containing personal documents, credit cards and a bank card - and a set of car keys to the occupants grey Audi A6, which they drove off in.

The vehicle was later found parked in Bourverie Walk, Northampton and officers would like to hear from anyone who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in either Chaucer Street or Bourverie Road between the stated times.

Anyone who may have any information regarding this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000678496. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
