Police officers are appealing for witnesses or anyone who may have any pertinent CCTV footage after an incident in Finedon last night (Sunday, March 21).

The incident happened in Allen Road between 9.45pm and 10pm when it is believed a firearm was discharged in the area, damaging the window of a nearby property.

Enquiries are currently being progressed to ascertain what happened and who was involved. In the meantime, detectives are encouraging anyone with information to come forward.

They are particularly interested in anyone who may have any pertinent CCTV, dashcam or doorbell footage.

Witnesses or anyone with information should contact Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
