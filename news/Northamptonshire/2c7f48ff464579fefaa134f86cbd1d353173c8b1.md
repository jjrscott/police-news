Police in Northampton are appealing for witnesses following a disturbance in Kings Heath in the early hours of New Year's Day.

Officers were called at about 1am on Friday, January 1, to reports of a fight involving 10 to 15 males in the Park Drive area.

An investigation into the incident is underway and anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101, quoting incident 21000000295.

Alternatively, they can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
