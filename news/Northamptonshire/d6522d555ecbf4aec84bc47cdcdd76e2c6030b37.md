Police officers are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Oakley Road, Newton.

The incident happened on Wednesday, January 27, at about 2.10pm when, for reasons yet unknown, the rider of a black Carrera Cross City E-bike became unseated and fell onto the road.

The rider, a 60-year-old woman, was conveyed to University Hospital Coventry where she remains in a critical condition.

Anyone with information or anyone who witnessed this incident or may have any dashcam footage should call the Drivewatch Hotline on 0800 174615. ** **

**Incident number:** 262 of 27/01/21
