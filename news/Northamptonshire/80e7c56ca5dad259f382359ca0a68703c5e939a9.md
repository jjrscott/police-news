Police in Corby are appealing for witnesses after tools and a work bag were stolen from a vehicle in the Shire Lodge area.

The theft happened on Monday, November 30, between 7.45am and 6.30pm, in a parking area on Tyne Road, behind Thurso Walk.

Another vehicle was also broken into but nothing was stolen.

Witnesses or anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101, quoting reference number 20000633475.
