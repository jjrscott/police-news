Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a woman was sexually assaulted in Black Path, Daventry.

The incident happened on Wednesday, January 6, between 7.30pm and 8.30pm, when a woman in her 20s walking in the area was attacked by a man and sexually assaulted.

The suspect is described as a white man, about 40-years-old, tall, with dark eyes and of a stocky build. He was wearing a blue beanie hat with a single white stripe going horizontally across it, blue denim jeans and a brown/khaki-coloured jacket with a rimmed collar.

A 41-year-old man has been arrested in connection with the offence and has since been released on police bail pending further enquiries.

Anyone with any information regarding the incident or anyone with any pertinent CCTV or dashcam footage, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
