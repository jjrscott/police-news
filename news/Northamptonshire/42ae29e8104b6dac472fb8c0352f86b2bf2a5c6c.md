Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a fatal road traffic collision on the A427 between Oundle and Lower Benefield.

The incident happened on Saturday, February 20, at about 2pm, when a white Yamaha R125 motorcycle travelling from Oundle towards Lower Benefield was in collision with a red Mini Cooper travelling in the opposite direction.

As a result of the collision, the motorcycle rider - a 28-year-old man - sadly died at the scene.

Police officers are now appealing for anyone with any information or dashcam footage just prior to the collision or of the incident itself, to come forward.

Anyone with information or who witnessed this incident is asked to call the Drivewatch Hotline on 0800 174615.

**Incident number:** 229 of 20/02/21
