Chief Constable Nick Adderley gave a news conference today (Tuesday, October 22) on the Northamptonshire Police investigation into the death of Harry Dunn, who died following a road traffic collision at Croughton in August.

The news conference was held at Northamptonshire Police Headquarters.

Mr Adderley was joined by Supt Sarah Johnson, head of operations, at the briefing during which they provided an update on the inquiry by the Force.
