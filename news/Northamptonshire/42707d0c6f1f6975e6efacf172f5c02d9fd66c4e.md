Police are appealing for witnesses after a burglary at a stables in Brigstock.

Sometime between 6.30pm on Sunday, November 29, and 6.30am on Monday, November 30, an offender entered the yard in Stanion Road.

The offender entered one of the stables and threw items at the horse, before removing a bandage from the horse's leg.

The horse was left distressed by the incident.

Police would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed this incident or who may have any information regarding it.

Witnesses or anyone with information can call police on 101 quoting 20000632760, or independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
