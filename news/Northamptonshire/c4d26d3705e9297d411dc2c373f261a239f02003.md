Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a an incident of criminal damage to a historic property in Higham Ferrers last month.

Sometime between midday on Monday, November 16 and midday on Tuesday, November 17, stones were removed from the Saffron Road side of the 15th Century English Heritage Chichele College.

The site is located in a busy area of the town and officers are keen to speak with anyone who may have dash cam footage or remember seeing something suspicious at the time.

Anyone who witnessed this or who may have any information about the incident, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
