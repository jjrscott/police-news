Two offenders tried to steal items from a car after breaking the back window of the vehicle which was parked in Hilary Close, Burton Latimer.

The incident happened sometime between 9.15pm and 10pm on Thursday, December 10.

They were disturbed by a nearby resident who heard the glass breaking and shouted at the offenders who ran off in the direction of Pioneer Avenue.

The offenders wore dark coloured tracksuits and had local accents.

Anyone with information can contact Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, they can call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
