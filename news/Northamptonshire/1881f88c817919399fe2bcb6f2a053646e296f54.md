Police investigating anti-social behaviour around service station toilets along the A43 in south Northamptonshire are asking anyone who may have experienced this to get in touch.

Northamptonshire Police has received reports of incidents at BP service stations at Brackley and Towcester between December 1, 2020, and Wednesday, March 17.

Officers looking into these reports are now appealing for anyone else who may have experienced or witnessed anti-social or concerning behaviour while using toilet facilities at these locations to get in touch.

Anyone with information related to this investigation is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

**Incident number:** 21000116829

 * A 36-year-old Banbury man arrested in connection with the investigation is currently on conditional bail.
