Police officers are appealing for information after a man indecently exposed himself on two separate occasions in the village of Thorpe Malsor, near Kettering.

The first incident happened on Monday, November 9, at about 11am, when a man indecently exposed himself to a member of the public in the wooded area of Church Way.

The second incident happened in the same area on Tuesday, November 24, at about 9.20am, when the man indecently exposed himself to another member of the public.

The man is described as a white middle-aged male, about 5ft 8in, of an average/slim build. He was wearing a distinctive black bobble hat, a black bodywarmer and black tights.

Anyone with information should contact Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.

**Incident number:** 2000623613
