Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a motorcycle was damaged during an attempted robbery in Balfour Road, Northampton.

Between 9.30pm and 10pm on Friday, March 19, two males stood in the road and ordered a passing motorcyclist to stop, before striking the vehicle with a metal object.

Despite the motorcycle being damaged, the rider continued and turned into Brockton Street. There is no description of the two males, who were wearing dark clothing.

Anyone who witnessed this incident or has any information which could assist with the investigation is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000155456. Or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
