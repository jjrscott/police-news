Police are investigating an arson attack in Apollo Close, Daventry.

The occupants were out at the time of the blaze, which happened at about midday on Christmas Eve and fire investigators have subsequently confirmed it was an arson attack, probably started using an accelerant in the master bedroom.

Anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101 or ring Crimestoppers, in confidence, on 0800 555111.
