Detectives investigating a robbery in Regent Square, Northampton town centre have released a CCTV image of a man they think may be able to help with their enquiries.

The incident happened at about 4.50pm on Wednesday, December 9.

Officers are appealing for the man pictured, or anyone who recognises him to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference 20000650673 or alternatively Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111
