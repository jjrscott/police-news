It's hotting up for a second weekend of F1 racing at Silverstone!

Superintendent Dennis Murray takes the reins as Policing Commander for the Emirates Formula 1 70th anniversary Grand Prix this weekend.

Fans are reminded to stay away to prevent the spread of Covid-19 and to enjoy the event from the comfort of their homes.

Please remember:

* There is an exclusion zone around the circuit - it will not be possible to watch the races from the perimeter fences.

*All campsites in the vicinity remain closed to the public.

* Dadford Road from the A43 junction to Stowe crossroads and Winterhills will be closed from 6.30am - 7pm over the weekend.

* Drones are prohibited - the Civil Aviation Authority has imposed a flight restriction for the entirety of the Grand Prix season.

Superintendent Dennis Murray is pictured along with some of the members of his team who will be supporting the policing operation this weekend and helping to ensure a safe event with minimal disruption for the local community.
