Northamptonshire Police is appealing for information regarding the whereabouts of 31-year-old Charles Dickie, from Daventry.

An arrest warrant has been issued for Dickie after he failed to appear in court in October 2020, in connection with a common assault.

Anyone who sees Dickie, or has information as to his whereabouts, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 20000495091.
