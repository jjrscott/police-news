Witnesses are being sought after two burglaries in Earls Barton.

The first incident happened in King Street between 3.30pm on Sunday, November 8, and 3pm on Monday, November 16, when the unknown offender/s smashed the patio doors to get inside.

The second incident happened in Manor Road between 11.45am on Saturday, November 14, and 11am on Monday, November 16, when the unknown offender/s also smashed the patio doors to get inside.

It is not yet known what was stolen from either property.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

**Incident number:** 20000606126 and 20000606625
