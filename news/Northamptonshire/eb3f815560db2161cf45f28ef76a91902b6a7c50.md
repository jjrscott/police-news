Following the arrest of two men in East Northamptonshire, officers are appealing for anyone who believes they have had a parcel stolen and yet not reported it to the police to come forward.

A 24-year-old man from Higham Ferrers and a 35-year-old man from Rushden were arrested on Thursday, December 10, following the theft of Amazon parcels in the Rushden and Higham Ferrers area.

Both men have been released under investigation pending further enquiries, and officers are keen to trace all the victims, and are appealing for anyone who has yet to come forward to do so.

Anyone who may have any information which could assist with this investigation, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000652621. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
