Police officers have released a CCTV image of a two men they believe may have information about a robbery in Northampton.

Between 1pm and 1.20pm on Wednesday, December 23, staff were threatened at Ashvin Food and Wine, Quarry Road, Duston and offenders took several items.

The men in the image or anyone who recognises them should call Northamptonshire Police on 101. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.

Incident number: 20000677119
