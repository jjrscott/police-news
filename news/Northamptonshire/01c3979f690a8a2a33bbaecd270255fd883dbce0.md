Members of the public who have had in-car electronic accessories stolen from them are asked to come forward if any of the items pictured may belong to them.

The Garmin Tom Tom (model 4FL60) and a dash-cam, along with leads, were recovered after a warrant in the Kingsthorpe area of Northampton on Friday, December 4.

Officers believe these items could have been stolen in the Northampton area anytime from between October 1 and December 4 this year.

Anyone who recognises these items or knows who they might belong to should call Northamptonshire Police on 101, quoting reference number 20000551953.
