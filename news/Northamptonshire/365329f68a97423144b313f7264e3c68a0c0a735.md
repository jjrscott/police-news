A 40-year-old man has been remanded in custody following an incident in Desborough during which a police officer was injured.

Officers were called to reports of an incident at Neuville Way at about 5.20pm on Friday, November 20.

While dealing with the incident, an officer was assaulted and had to be taken to Kettering General Hospital for treatment.

Marshall John Coe, of Neuville Way, was arrested at the scene and has been charged with wounding with intent and possession of an air weapon with intent to cause fear of violence.

Magistrates remanded Coe in custody to appear before Northampton Crown Court on Wednesday, December 16.
