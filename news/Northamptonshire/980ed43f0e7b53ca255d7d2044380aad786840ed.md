Police officers are appealing for witnesses or anyone with information after £100,000 worth of stock was stolen from a lorry on the A14.

The incident happened sometime between 4.50pm on Thursday, February 25, and 9am on Friday, February 26, when a lorry was broken into near Junction 1 on the A14.

A large amount of candleware, including wax melting equipment, worth about £100,000, was stolen. This equipment is all branded with an "All Seasons Wax Co" mark.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.
