Valuable jewellery and designer items were stolen during a burglary in Sharman Way, Rothwell.

Sometime between 6.45pm and 11pm on Friday, December 4, the offender broke into the property and stole significant amounts of jewellery including Swarovski, Links of London, Forever Diamonds and two traditional Chinese gold bracelets - one for a girl and one for a boy.

The property had been searched and a CCTV control box stolen.

The offender is described as a white man and wearing a light grey hooded top.

Anyone with information can contact Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, they can call the independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
