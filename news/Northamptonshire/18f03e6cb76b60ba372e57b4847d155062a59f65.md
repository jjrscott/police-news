Police officers are appealing for witnesses after an aggravated burglary in Standing Stones, Northampton.

The incident happened on Thursday, March 18, between 7pm and 8pm, when a man broke into the property, threatened the occupant with weapons and demanded they hand over jewellery.

The suspect is described as a tall, skinny man with a deep voice and wearing a hoody and a face-covering with goggles attached to it.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

**Incident number:** 21000153198
