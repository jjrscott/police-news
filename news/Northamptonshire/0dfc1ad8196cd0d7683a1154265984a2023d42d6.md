Police investigating an arson at a property in Northampton are appealing for witnesses and information.

Between 3.30am and 4am on Wednesday, March 17, a mattress was set alight outside a property in Derby Road, Abington, before being pushed up against the front door, causing it to catch fire.

Fire crews attended and police are now investigating the incident.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area at the time, or have information about the incident.

Residents nearby who have CCTV are also asked to review their footage and contact police with any information they may have.

Please call Northamptonshire Police on 101 with any relevant information.
