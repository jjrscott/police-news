A warrant executed at a property in Kettering uncovered a large quantity of Class A drugs, thousands of pounds in cash, designer goods, and mobile phones.

Police officers stormed the property in Crown Street at 9am on Thursday, November 26, and after a search, the drugs were located in various locations across the house.

Cash, phones and designer goods were also seized including Mulberry, Chloe and Christian Louboutin handbags.

Three people - a man, aged 25, and two women, aged 66 and 40, were all arrested on suspicion of Class A drugs with intent to supply.
