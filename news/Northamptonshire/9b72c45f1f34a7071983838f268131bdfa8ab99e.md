Police officers are appealing for witnesses after an assault in the car park at the Tesco Extra store in Oakley Road, Corby.

Between 2.50pm and 3.20pm on Saturday, December 26, a man in his 20s drove into the car park and parked up. As he sat in his car, another male riding a black mountain bike approached him.

The male punched the driver in the face several times, causing significant facial injuries, and rode off. He is described as white, aged 19 to early 20s, of a slim build with mousey brown hair. He was wearing a grey or light green tracksuit.

Anyone who witnessed the assault or who may have any information which could assist with the investigation is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000682935. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
