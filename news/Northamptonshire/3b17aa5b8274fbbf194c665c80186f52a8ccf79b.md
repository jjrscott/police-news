Police officers are appealing for witnesses after an attempted residential burglary in Windsor Road, Wellingborough.

Between 9.30pm and 9.50pm on Saturday, January 9, someone has attempted to force open the front door of the property using an implement to jemmy the lock. Fortunately no entry was gained, and nothing stolen.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed this incident or who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area between the stated times.

Anyone who may have any information regarding this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000015666. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
