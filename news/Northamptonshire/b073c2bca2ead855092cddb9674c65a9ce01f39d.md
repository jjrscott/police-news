Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a fatal road traffic collision in Aynho.

The incident happened in Roundtown at about 9.15pm on Sunday, February 28, when a red Yamaha FJR motorcycle travelling towards Croughton Road was in collision with a blue Ford Fiesta which was travelling towards Banbury Road.

The motorcyclist, a 40-year-old man, sadly died at the scene.

Anyone with information or who witnessed this incident is asked to call the Drivewatch Hotline on 0800 174615.

**Incident number:** 611 of 28/02/21
