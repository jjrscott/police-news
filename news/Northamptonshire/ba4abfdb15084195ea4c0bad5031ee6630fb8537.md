Police investigating the stabbing of a man in Wellingborough are appealing for witnesses and information.

Between 9.20pm and 9.40pm on Friday, March 12, a man was in Burns Road when he was approached by an unknown male and stabbed a number of times in the arm, back and leg.

The offender then ran off, leaving the victim with non-life-threatening injuries which required stitching in hospital.

The suspect is described as a small man wearing a dark grey jacket.

Anyone who saw the incident, or has information about it, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101, or Crimestoppers in confidence on 0800 555111.
