Police officers are appealing for witnesses after an attempted residential burglary in Shelley Road, Wellingborough.

Between 6.15pm and 6.45pm on Friday, January 29, three males have attempted to force access to the property, which resulted in the front door being damaged. No entry was gained.

The offenders are described as being of a slim build and wearing black hoodies and clothing. At least one of them was white.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed this incident or who may have seen three males acting suspiciously in the area of Shelley Road, between the stated times.

Anyone who may have any information regarding this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000062020. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
