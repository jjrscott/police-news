Witnesses are being sought after a man was stabbed in the hand in Brunswick Walk, Northampton.

The incident happened on Thursday, February 18, at about 11pm, when a member of the public witnessed a man being chased by another man. One of the men had suffered hand injuries including a laceration to his palm and the loss of a fingertip as the result of being stabbed.

A 19-year-old man was arrested at the scene and has since been released on police bail pending further enquiries.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.
