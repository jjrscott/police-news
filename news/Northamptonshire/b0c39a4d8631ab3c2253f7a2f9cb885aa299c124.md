Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a residential burglary in Station Road, Earls Barton.

Between 5pm on Sunday, January 3 and 2.40pm on Monday, January 4, the offender/s forced the rear door and once inside, made an untidy search before stealing cash.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed this incident, or who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area between the stated times.

Anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000005543. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
