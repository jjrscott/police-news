Superintendent Sarah Johnson, said: "Our investigation into the death of Harry Dunn continues at pace and in light of this latest development, we are liaising closely with both the Foreign & Commonwealth Office and the International Crime Coordination Centre as to our next steps.

"Northamptonshire Police remains absolutely committed to getting Harry and his family justice and we are doing everything on our side to ensure that a full and thorough investigation, with the assistance of all parties involved, takes place, in order for this to be achieved."
