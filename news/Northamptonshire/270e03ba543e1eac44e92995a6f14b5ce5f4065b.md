Police officers are appealing for witnesses after an assault near to the play area in Charlotte Place, Kettering.

Between 2pm and 3pm on Monday, December 21, an altercation between two sets of two males has taken place, which has resulted in a 20-year-old man sustaining facial injuries and another receiving a cut to his hand.

During the incident, one of the males has produced a knife, causing the two males who had been assaulted to run off. The two offenders were wearing tracksuits with the hoods up and face masks.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed the altercation or who may have caught the incident on CCTV or dash-cam.

Anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000673514. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
