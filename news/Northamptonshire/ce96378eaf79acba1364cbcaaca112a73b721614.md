Police officers are appealing for information following a serious assault in Euston Road, Northampton.

The incident happened between 3am and 4am on Saturday, December 5, as a 20-year-old man walked along the road.

Four males were then seen to exit a nearby vehicle and assault him. As a result of the attack, the man received a stab wound and was taken to hospital, however is expected to make a full recovery.

The suspects are described as four black males.

It is believed that that the assault may have been filmed and anyone who has any pertinent footage is asked to get in touch with police.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
