Witnesses are being sought after a suspected arson which took place next to flats in Melbourne Walk, Northampton on Christmas Day.

The incident took place between 6.45pm and 7.15pm on Friday, December 25, when a sofa, which had been discarded next to the flats, is believed to have been deliberately set on fire.

Officers believe this reckless act of criminal damage endangered the lives of the residents, who had to be evacuated as a result of the fire, which also damaged two flats.

Anyone with information or who may have witnessed this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000680231. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
