Police officers are appealing for witnesses after two males attempted to force entry into a residence in Salisbury Road, Wellingborough.

At about 8pm on Thursday, January 28, a teenage boy was chased by two males in the area of Mill Road, and he has ran to a friend's house in Salisbury Road.

It is believed the two males have followed him to the address, and caused damage to the front door in an attempt to gain access.

The two males were both black and wearing all black clothing. One of the males had short plaited hair or dreads.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed either the boy being chased or the subsequent incident at the house in Salisbury Road.

Anyone who may have any information regarding this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000060280. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
