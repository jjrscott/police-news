Witnesses are being sought after a pair of medals were stolen during a distraction burglary in Vicarage Road, Wellingborough.

At about 5.30pm on Wednesday, December 9, three men entered the address and informed the elderly resident that they were police officers. After they left the man discovered his medals had been stolen.

The medals had been awarded to the man to mark the Queen's Silver and Golden Jubilee, and officers would like to hear from anyone who may have been offered such items for sale in unusual circumstances.

Officers would also like to hear from anyone who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area of Vicarage Road on the afternoon or early evening of Wednesday, December 9.

Anyone who may have information which could assist this investigation is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000651129. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
