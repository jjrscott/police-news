Officers are appealing for information after an incident in Duston on Thursday, December 10.

An 11-year-old girl was walking towards Duston School, Berrywood Road, at 8.15am when a man grabbed her around the waist from behind.

The offender is described as a 5ft 7in, 30-40-year old slim white man with no facial hair, wearing a grey jacket or hoody, a red top, blue trousers and black shoes

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.

Incident number: 20000654127
