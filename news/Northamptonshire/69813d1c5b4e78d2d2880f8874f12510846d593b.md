A man has been charged with criminal damage following an incident in Thrapston last month.

A silent soldier who stood over Thrapston's war memorial in Oundle Road, was destroyed beyond repair during the incident which took place overnight between Thursday, November 12 and Friday, November 13.

The 24-year-old man from Northampton will appear at Northampton Magistrates' Court on Friday, February 22, 2021.
