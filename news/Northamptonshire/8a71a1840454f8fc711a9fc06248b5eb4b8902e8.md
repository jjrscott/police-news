Members of the public who have had tools stolen from them are asked to come forward if any of the items pictured may belong to them.

The tools were recovered after a warrant in the Kingsley area of Northampton on Thursday, November 26, with intelligence suggesting that the tools could have been stolen in the Northampton area anytime from between June and November this year.

Anyone who recognises the tools or knows who they might belong to should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

**Incident number:** 20000620513
