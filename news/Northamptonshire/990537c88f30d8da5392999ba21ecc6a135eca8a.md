A 22-year-old Northampton man has been charged with child sex offences.

Bradley Jones, from Balmoral Road in Kingsthorpe was charged last week with four counts of sexually assaulting a child under 13 by touching and two of engaging in non-penetrative sexual activity with a child aged 13 to 15.

He is due to appear before Northampton Magistrates Court on a date to be determined.
