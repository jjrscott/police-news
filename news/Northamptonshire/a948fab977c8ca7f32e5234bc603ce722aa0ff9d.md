Police in Northampton are releasing CCTV images of a man they'd like to speak to in connection with a robbery in Grange Park which happened at around 10pm on Sunday, November 22.

This incident happened at the top of Williams Way, just past the CO-OP, and involved the theft of a mobile phone and car keys.

The man in the CCTV images or anyone who recognises him should call us.

Anyone with any information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
