Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a burglary in Watson Close, Corby.

The incident happened on Saturday, November 21, between 12pm and 2pm, when the unknown offender/s forced entry into the property via a back door. Once inside, they stole a key to the occupant's Volvo and a HP Laptop.

The homeowner also noticed that someone had been in the Volvo however nothing appears to have been stolen from inside.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

**Incident number:** 20000618018
