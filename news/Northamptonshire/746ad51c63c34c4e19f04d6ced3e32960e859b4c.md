Witnesses are being sought after a woman was threatened with a knife in Goldings, Northampton.

Between 6pm and 6.30pm on Sunday, January 24, a group of 10 to 12 young people gathered outside a home in Dryleys Court and shouted at those inside.

A woman who came out was then threatened with a knife before the group, thought to be aged between 14 and 16, left the area.

Anyone who saw the incident, or has information about it, is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101, or Crimestoppers in confidence on 0800 555111.

**Incident number:** 21000052540
