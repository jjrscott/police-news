Officers investigating the theft of a bicycle in Weston Favell have released a CCTV image of a man they would like to speak to.

The bike was stolen from outside McDonald's after its lock was cut between 10am and 10.30am on Wednesday, February 24.

Police investigating the theft have issued a CCTV image of a man they would like to identify and speak to, as it is believed he may have information relevant to their enquiries.

The man pictured, anyone who recognises him or anyone with information about the theft should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.
