Police are appealing for witnesses to a burglary in Chalcombe Road, Northampton.

The break-in happened between 2am and 4.30am on Wednesday, February 24, with items including a television, alcohol and food stolen.

The suspect is described as male, aged between 20-30, of small build and wearing a grey hooded top.

Witnesses to the burglary or anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

**Incident number:** 21000109187
