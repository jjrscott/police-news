Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a residential attempted burglary in Brecon Close, Kettering.

The incident took place between 11am and 12.30pm on Friday, November 27, when the offender/s attempted to force the front door and caused damage in the process. Entry was not gained

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed this incident who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area of Brecon Close between the stated times.

Anyone who may have any information regarding this incident is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000628987. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
