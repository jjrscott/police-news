Do you recognise this man? Police officers would like help to identify this man who they believe may have information about a burglary in Gravely Street, Rushden.

Between 3.45am and 4.15am on Sunday, March 14, a man broke into a shed and activated the burglary alarm. The resident then confronted the offender, but the offender made off.

The offender is described as white and with brown medium length hair. He was wearing a brown puffer-style jacket, blue jeans, black trainers and a blue surgical face mask. He was riding a bicycle.

Officers investigating the incident believe the man in the image may be able to assist them with their investigation and are appealing for him or anyone who may recognise him, to get in touch.

Anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000143341. Or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
