Police investigating the theft of reels of wiring in Irthlingborough are appealing for witnesses who may have seen two men pushing wheelie bins nearby.

The theft took place at a property in Addington Road between 6.30am and 6.50am on Wednesday, February 24, with the stolen items carried away in wheelie bins.

It is believed the offenders made their way along the A6 towards Station Road, and officers are appealing for anyone who may have seen them at any point on their journey to get in touch.

Witnesses or anyone with information about the thefts should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

**Incident number:** 21000108334

 * A 29-year-old man and a 17-year-old boy, both of Irthlingborough, arrested in connection with the thefts have been released under investigation.
