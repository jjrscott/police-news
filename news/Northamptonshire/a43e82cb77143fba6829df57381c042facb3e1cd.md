Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a man was seen to indecently expose himself in Coronation Park in Elizabeth Street, Corby.

The incident happened on Saturday, November 28, at about 11.30pm, when two members of the public called police to report the man's behaviour.

The suspect is described as a white man in his late 20s, about 5ft 7in, of a medium build, with short dark hair and wearing a grey and red hooded jumper and grey jogging bottoms.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.

A 32-year-old man has been arrested in connection with this incident and released on police bail pending further enquiries.
