Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a summerhouse was deliberately set on fire in Brixworth.

The incident happened at about 9pm on Sunday, November 22, at Brynne Vineyard in open fields off the A508, Brixworth Bypass, when a summerhouse was deliberately set on fire.

A man received facial injuries as a result of the fire and is currently being treated at Birmingham Hospital.

Witnesses or anyone with information as to how the fire started should contact Northamptonshire Police on 101. Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555111.
