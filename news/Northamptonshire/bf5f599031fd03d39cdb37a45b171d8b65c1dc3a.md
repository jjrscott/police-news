Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a horse trailer was stolen from a stable yard in the early hours of Wednesday, December 2.

The incident happened between 2am and 3am and officers are asking anyone who saw a blue Ifor 505 trailer being towed in the area, or anyone in the equestrian world who has seen anything suspicious to get in touch.

Anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 20000638182. Or alternatively call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
