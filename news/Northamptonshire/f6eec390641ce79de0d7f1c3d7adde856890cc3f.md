Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a man was assaulted in Kettering Road, Northampton.

The incident happened today (Wednesday, January 27) at about 10am, near to the junction with Spencer Road, when a man in his 30s suffered injuries to his head. He has been taken to hospital but is expected to make a full recovery.

A 19-year-old man has been arrested in connection with the incident and remains in police custody at this time.

Witnesses or anyone with information should call Northamptonshire Police on 101.
