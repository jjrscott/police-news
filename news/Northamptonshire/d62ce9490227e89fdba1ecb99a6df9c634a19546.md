Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a fatal road traffic collision at the roundabout junction of Lloyds Road and Weldon Road, Corby.

At about 9.30pm on Saturday, January 2, a silver Fiat Stilo was travelling along Lloyds Road towards Weldon Road, when for unknown reasons it has collided with a tree on the roundabout.

As a result of the collision, the passenger - a 76-year-old woman from Corby - was treated at the scene by paramedics, however sadly died at the scene.

Officers would like to speak to anyone who may have witnessed this incident or who may have captured the car either travelling along Lloyds Road or the collision on dash-cam.

Anyone with information or anyone who witnessed this incident is asked to call the Drivewatch Hotline on 0800 174615 or Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 438 of 02/01/21.
