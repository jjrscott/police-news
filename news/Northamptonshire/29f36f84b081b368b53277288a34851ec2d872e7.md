Police officers are appealing for witnesses after a residential burglary in Mander Close, Northampton.

Between 5.50pm and 8.50pm on Sunday, January 5, the offender/s have forced opened the front door and once inside, stole a number of items including a laptop, mobile phone and jewellery.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who may have witnessed this incident, who may have seen anyone acting suspiciously in the area between the stated times or who may have been offered such items for sale in unusual circumstances.

Anyone with information is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101 quoting reference number 21000007880. Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
