Police are appealing for witnesses after a man was attacked by three others in Weston Favell.

Between 6pm and 7pm on Friday, March 26, the man was set upon by three unknown men as he walked by the police station in Pyramid Close.

During the assault he was struck with a hammer, suffering facial injuries.

Anyone who witnessed the attack or has information about it is asked to call Northamptonshire Police on 101.
