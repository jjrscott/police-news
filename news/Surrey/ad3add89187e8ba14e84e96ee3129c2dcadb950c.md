We are releasing images of items that we believe may be stolen in an effort to reunite them with their owners.

The items have been recovered as part of an ongoing burglary investigation in the Shepperton area.

The items include:

 * Europa pocket watch case
 * Europa pocket watch in red case
 * Black Kodak camera in green case
 * Silver Swiss Tek watch
 * Silver and gold Pulsar watch
 * Slazenger watch with black strap
 * Black telescope in black case
 * Henry watch with black strap
 * Maxim watch with black strap
 * Gold Sekonda pocket watch in black box
 * iPad in black leather case
 * iPad in black leather Griffin case with charging cable
 * Black Revelation bag

A 29-year-old man from Ashford has been arrested on suspicion of burglary and released under investigation.

If you were the victim of a burglary and recognise any of the below items, please contact us via webchat on our website or by calling 101 and quoting the crime reference number PR/45210009480. These items could also have been taken from more vulnerable members of your family, so please show them these images.

We'd like to take this opportunity to remind you of the following crime prevention advice:

 * Register your valuables using an accredited property database.
 * Take photographs and keep documents like receipts related to your property so you can prove ownership.
