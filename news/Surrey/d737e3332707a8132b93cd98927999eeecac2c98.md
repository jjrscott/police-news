As part of ongoing work to tackle the issue of drugs in Woking, a 17 year-old was arrested on Tuesday 13 October.

The teenager was charged with the concern and supply of Class A drugs and the concern and supply of human trafficking.

He has been released on bail with conditions, and will appear at Guildford Magistrates Court on Wednesday 28 October.
