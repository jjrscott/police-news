We can now confirm that a pedestrian involved in a collision with a BMW in Ewell earlier today has died.

Officers were called to Kingston Road, opposite Ewell Court Avenue, at 5.07pm following reports of a collision involving a red BMW and an elderly man. The pedestrian passed away at the scene and his next of kin has been informed. All road closures have now been lifted.

Anyone who witnessed the collision or who might have CCTV or dashcam footage, should call 101 and quote the reference number PR/P19294151.
