A man who was wanted has been located and arrested.

40 year old Graham Thomas Lees was arrested by officers in Guildford yesterday (Monday, 6 April). He was remanded and appeared at Guildford Crown Court this morning where he pleaded guilty to breaching his court conditions and sentenced to 6 months imprisonment.
