All roads have now reopened near Enterprise Place in Woking, following reports of debris falling from a building due to the high wind yesterday (9 February).

A structural engineer has assessed the damage to the building and it is not thought to present any further risk to the public at this time.

A minimal police presence will remain in the area this morning.

The following roads have now reopened:

Church Street East

Chertsey Road - junction with Stanley Road

Chertsey Road - junction with Board School Road

Chertsey Road - junction with Duke Street

Thank you to members of the public for their patience while the closures were in place.
