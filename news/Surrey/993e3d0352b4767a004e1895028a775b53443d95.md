We have released some CCTV images of men we would like to speak to in connection with a residential burglary in Cobham on 17 September at around 12pm.

Entry was forced to the property on Birch Vale, and a large quantity of jewellery was stolen from the address.

No one was at home at the time of the offence.

We are keen to identify the men pictured in these CCTV images. If you recognise the people pictured, or you were in the area at the time and have any information which could help, please contact us quoting crime reference number PR/45200099046 via

• Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk

• Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/

• Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, you can also call independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
