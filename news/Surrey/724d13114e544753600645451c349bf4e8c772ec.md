We are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Sunbury this afternoon (April 12).

The collision involving a man on a bicycle and a car happened at the junction of the A308 Staines Road West and the A244 Windmill Road at 2.13pm. The car stopped at the scene.

Road closures are in place either side of the A244 and the A308.

The cyclist is currently in a serious condition.

Officers are appealing for witnesses to get in touch including anyone who may have dashcam footage of the collision.

Anyone with any information should contact Surrey Police on 101 and quote the reference number PR/P20083024.
