We are appealing for witnesses after a woman has been taken to hospital with life threatening injuries following a collision earlier today (Friday, 8 November).

Police were called to the collision on Glenwood Road near the roundabout junction on Park Avenue West at around 6am this morning which involved a blue Ford Focus estate.

The road was temporarily closed but has since reopened.

Anyone who witnessed the collision, or with any dashcam footage, or any other information which could assist our enquiries, is asked to contact us 101, quoting incident reference PR/P19259492
