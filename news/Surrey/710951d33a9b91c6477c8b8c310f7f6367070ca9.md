We are pleased to say that missing man, Patrick Hegarty, who left his family home in Virginia Water on Wednesday morning (June 24), has been found.

Mr Hegarty was found in the London area and will be reunited with his family in due course.

Thank you to everyone who provided information and shared our appeal.
