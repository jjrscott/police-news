We are appealing for witnesses following an assault and robbery in Guildford on Wednesday, 24 February.

Officers were called to Stoke Park following reports of an assault and a robbery which had occurred at around 7.05pm. The victim was walking through Stoke Park from London Road train station towards the Spectrum when they were approached and assaulted by a group of eight men
.
The men then walked off in an unknown direction, leaving the victims with injuries to the head.

Investigating Officer PC Mihai Kerekes said "We are appealing to anyone who may have any more information or could have witnessed anything suspicious in the area to come forward. Please also let us know if you were driving past and have any dashcam footage."

If you were in the area at the time and have any information which could help, please contact us quoting incident/crime reference number PR/45210019428 via:

• Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
• Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
• Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
