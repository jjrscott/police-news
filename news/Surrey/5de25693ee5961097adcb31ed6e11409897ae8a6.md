They've recently been recovered, alongside jewellery that we've linked to a burglary at a property in Walton-on-Thames on 6 December.

It's believed these items may have been stolen, potentially from other properties locally and we're hoping to identify the owners.
If you are the owner of these pieces of jewellery, or you have any other information which could assist us, please call us on 101, quoting reference PR/45190132908.
You can also give information, 100% anonymously, to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111; or through their anonymous online form: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/
If you own any expensive items, please consider registering them on immobilise.com, which is free of charge. You could also photograph and mark your items with ultra violet pens.
For more tips on keeping your belongings safe, visit our website: https://www.surrey.police.uk/cp/crime-prevention/residential-burglary/
