We are appealing for the public's help in finding Luke Smith, 28, wanted in relation to a breach of a court order.

He is described as white, of medium build, with brown hair and brown eyes. He has links to West Surrey and North Hampshire.

If you have seen Smith, or have any information which could help us find him, please call us on 101. Alternatively, if you wish to remain anonymous, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
