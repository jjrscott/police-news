Seven people have been arrested in connection with a stabbing in Oxted earlier this evening (31 December).

Officers were called to the incident on Station Road West, near Oxted train station, at around 5.50pm.

A 46-year-old man was treated at the scene by paramedics and is currently in hospital in a serious condition.

A police cordon remains at the scene.

Five boys and two girls were arrested on suspicion of attempted murder after enquiries by officers located them in the area.

We're still appealing for witnesses and would like to encourage anyone who may have any information which could help our investigation to contact us quoting P20300541 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
