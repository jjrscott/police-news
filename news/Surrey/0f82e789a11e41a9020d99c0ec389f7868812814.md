Surrey Police is appealing for witnesses following an incident of GBH in Byfleet on Friday 7th August 2020.

Officers were called to High Road, Byfleet, shortly before 22:50 hours following reports of a large fight involving multiple people. On arrival, officers found several persons at the scene with injuries who were taken to separate hospitals for treatment. None of the injuries sustained are believed to be life-threatening or life-changing. A 41-year-old man and a 32-year-old man have subsequently been arrested on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm. Both have since been released on police bail and enquiries continue.

We are now looking for anyone who witnessed this incident to assist us with our investigation. If you have any information which could help us, directly witnessed what took place, have CCTV or relevant mobile phone footage, please get in touch quoting incident ref 45200083003.

Options for contacting us include:

• Webchat on our website: surrey.police.uk

• Online at https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/

• Phoning on 101

Alternatively, if you'd rather not speak to police you can also call independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
