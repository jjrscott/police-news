A man who went missing from Ewell yesterday (12 March), has been found.

85-year-old Jack Henry was last seen around 11.45am and was reported missing after he failed to return home.

He was found in the Brixton area and has been reunited with his family.

We would like to thank everyone who shared our appeal and assisted with the search.
