We are appealing for witnesses following a knifepoint robbery at Sheerwater Recreation Ground in Woking, on Friday 18 December.

The incident occurred at around 5pm and involved the victim being threatened with a knife, assaulted and robbed of his phone, wallet and clothes.

Fortunately, the victim sustained no serious injuries.

A 13-year-old boy from Woking has been arrested in connection to the incident and been released on police bail.

If you were in the area and saw anything suspicious, we would like to hear from you. You can contact by quoting PR/45200133437 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
