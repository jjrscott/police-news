We are appealing for witnesses following an assault in Guildford on 8 March.

At approximately 3.30am, we attended reports of a large scale fight in progress outside Charcoal Grill on Park Street.

The victim received a fractured cheek bone from being punched and kicked.

We believe the suspect attended the Thiry3Hz club before the incident, and are asking for any photos or video footage taken inside the premises to send them to us. If you witnessed the incident or have any information which might help, please call 101 and quote ref PR/45200026632.
