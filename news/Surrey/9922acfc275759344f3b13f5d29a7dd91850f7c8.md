We are appealing for help to find Patrick Hegarty who went missing yesterday morning from his home in Virginia Water.

He was last seen on Christchurch Way, Virginia Water in the early hours of yesterday morning (24 June).

Patrick has links to Sunningdale and Longcross and Shepperton Studios where he used to work.

He is described as 5'10" tall with grey receding short hair. He's of medium build and has sunburn. He was last seen wearing a jumper and jeans with blue trainers. Patrick may present as confused.

Anyone with any information should call Surrey Police on 101 and quote the reference number PR/P20145958.
