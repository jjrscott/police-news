We received reports of a stolen vehicle in Whyteleafe overnight between 11 and 12 April.

The suspect(s) forced entry through a padlocked gate and stole the black Ford Mustang from a business premises in Godstone Road. The car has distinctive bright orange wheel trims.

A white low loader van was seen in the area at around 11pm on 11 April.

If you have any information or video footage which could help us with this investigation, please contact 101 and quote ref PR/45200038814.
