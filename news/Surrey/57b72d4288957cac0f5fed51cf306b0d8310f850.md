We are appealing for witnesses following an assault outside Spice Boys takeaway, Horley which has left a man in hospital with serious head injuries.

The incident happened at 12.35am on August 15 after two groups of men were seen arguing outside the takeaway on Victoria Road.

Investigating Officer Detective Constable Jen Trudel said: "This was a serious assault and we are sure there must have been witnesses to what happened. If you were in the area at the time please get in touch by calling 101 and quoting PR/45200085886 if you have any information whatsoever about what happened."

If you have any information which could help, please contact us quoting crime reference number PR/45200085886 via

• Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk

• Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/

• Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
