We are appealing for the public's help in finding missing man James Duddy.

. James, 31 was last seen at around 6pm this evening in Redhill and his family are growing increasingly concerned for his welfare.

James is described as a white man of a slim build with ginger hair and clean shaven. He was last seen wearing a grey and green checked shirt with jeans.

He is likely to be travelling by foot but has possibly got a taxi.

Anyone with information that could help us locate James is asked to contact Surrey Police urgently using the chat function on our website, via social media, or by calling 101, quoting reference PR/P20205034. If you are with her, please call us immediately on 999.
