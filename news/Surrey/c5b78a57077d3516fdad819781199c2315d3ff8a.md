We are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision in Ockley last night (13 November).

Officers were called to the incident involving a pick-up truck and a motorbike, which took place in Ockley Road around 10.30pm.

The motorcyclist, a man in his 30s, was sadly declared dead at the scene around 11.15pm.

His next of kin has been made aware.

The driver of the truck, a 25-year-old man from Dorking, was arrested on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving and has been released under investigation pending further investigation.

We are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision, with any other information or any dashcam footage, to contact us straightaway via quoting incident reference PR/P20264076.
