Officers from Guildford's Safer Neighbourhood Team has secured a full three-month Closure Order against 10a Attfield Close in Ash.

It follows reports of anti-social behaviour and drug dealing relating to the maisonette..

The full Closure Order was granted on Friday 21 February under Section 80 of the Anti-Social Behaviour, Crime and Disorder Act 2014 by Staines Magistrates' Court.

The order means that no-one can enter the maisonette until 20 May 2020.

Borough Commander, Inspector Andy Hill from Guildford's Safer Neighbourhood Team said: "Drug-related anti-social behaviour will not be tolerated in Ash and we are grateful to the community for working with us. The local police team are committed to dealing with this sort of issue and I encourage anyone to contact us if they are experiencing similar"

If you suspect drug dealing is taking place, you can report this by calling 101 or online via www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report. Always dial 999 if a crime is in progress.

You can also report anonymously via https://crimestoppers-uk.org/
