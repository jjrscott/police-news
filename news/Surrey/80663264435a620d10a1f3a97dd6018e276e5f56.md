We are appealing for your help to identify this man in this CCTV Image, following a theft from a motor vehicle in Addlestone.

Officers were called to a residence in Addlestone on Sunday 27 December, following a report of stolen car keys from a vehicle while it was parked on the driveway with the engine running. The suspect approached the car, opened the driver's door and took the car keys.

Later that day, the suspect and another man re attended the address before being scared away by the vehicle owner.

The suspect is described as wearing a grey and black jacket, blue hat and purse.

If you know who this man is, or you have any other information that could help our investigation, please contact us quoting crime reference number PR/ 45200136302 via

 * Webchat on our website
 * Online - https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

Alternatively, if you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
