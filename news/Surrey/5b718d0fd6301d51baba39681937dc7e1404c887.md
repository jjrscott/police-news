We are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision involving a cyclist in Compton yesterday afternoon (9 April).

The collision, which also involved a van, took place in New Pond Road around 4.30pm.

The cyclist, a man in his 60s, was sadly declared dead at the scene. His next of kin have been made aware.

The driver of the van, a man in his 40s, was taken to hospital with minor injuries.

We are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision, or with any other information or dashcam footage, to come forward straightaway. Please contact us on 101, quoting incident reference PR/P20080006.
