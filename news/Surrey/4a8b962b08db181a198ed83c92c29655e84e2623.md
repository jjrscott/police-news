We are appealing for witnesses following a single motorcycle collision on the A240 Reigate Road in Ewell just before 10am this morning (Saturday, 21 March).

Officers were in attendance with an air ambulance and Surrey Fire and Rescue. The motorcyclist sadly passed away at the scene.

An investigation to determine the circumstances is underway and A240 Reigate Road currently remains closed between the A24 and College Road in Ewell.

We are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed the collision or have any further information or dash cam footage to get in touch with us by calling 101, quoting reference number PR/P20065564.
