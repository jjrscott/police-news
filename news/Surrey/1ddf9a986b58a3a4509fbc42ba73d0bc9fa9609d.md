We're appealing for witnesses after a man has been seriously injured following a collision earlier this morning (2 November).

At around 01:10 police contact centre operatives received a 999 call regarding the collision, involving a single blue Mini, on Gatton Park Road near the allotments. The injured man, in his early 30s, was attended to by officers and paramedics at the scene and was transported to hospital at around 02:10.

Gatton Park Road is currently closed between the junction with Colesmead Road and the junction with Carlton Road, while officers complete their investigations.

If you witnessed the incident, have any dashcam footage which could assist our investigation, or were involved in some other way please contact us on 101 quoting incident reference PR/P19254274.
