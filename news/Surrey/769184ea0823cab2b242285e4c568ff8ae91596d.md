We are appealing to the public for help in finding a man who is wanted in relation to multiple ongoing criminal investigations.

Anthony Chieke, 50, of no fixed address is wanted in relation to a number of burglary offences.
He is described as Asian, 6 ft 6 tall, of medium build, with brown eyes and short, black hair. He has a large scar on the right side of his forehead, right brown and scar on upper left arm.
He has links to the Woking area.
If you have seen Chieke, or you have any information which could help us find him, please call us on 101 (999 in an emergency), quoting crime reference PR/45190135602.
Alternatively, if you do not wish to leave your name, call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
