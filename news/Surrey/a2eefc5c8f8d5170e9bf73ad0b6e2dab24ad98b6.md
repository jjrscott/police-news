We have released CCTV image of a man we would like to speak to in connection with a commercial burglary investigation in Farnham on Thursday, 17 December, shortly before 6pm.

Entry was forced to the property on Riverside Park, but no items were stolen from the address.

No one was at the address at the time of the offence.

We are keen to identify the man pictured in the CCTV Image. If you recognise the person pictured, or you were in the area at the time and have any information which could help, please contact us quoting crime reference number PR/45200133172 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
