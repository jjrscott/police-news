We are appealing for witness following an incident between two groups of people at the Stepping Stones on the River Mole on Tuesday (June 23) at around 4.25pm.

The altercation took place when a group of six people came across another two while crossing the river at the beauty spot, leaving one with serious facial injuries.

The first suspect has been described as being of medium build with short dark hair and facial hair. He was wearing a white t-shirt, black shorts and grey trainers.

The second suspect has been described as having a chubby build and dark hair. He was wearing a grey t-shirt, grey shorts and white footwear.

If you witnessed this incident please call 101 and quote ref PR/45200064749. Alternatively, if you prefer to remain anonymous, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
