We are now in a position to name the 52 year-old local man who died at an address on Old Palace Road in Weybridge on Wednesday 8 January.

Glen Hopgood's family has been informed of his death and specialist officers have been providing support to his relatives.

Detective Chief Inspector Colin Pirie said: "Our thoughts are with Mr Hopgood's family at this difficult time. A man has been charged as part of our investigation in relation to Mr Hopgood's death. I would like to thank all of the officers and staff who have worked tirelessly to progress our enquiries over the last forty-eight hours".
