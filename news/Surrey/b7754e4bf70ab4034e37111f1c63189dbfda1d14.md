We're appealing for witnesses after three men were robbed in the common near Weston Green Road yesterday evening (15 September).

The victims were walking through the passageway leading to Weston Green Road at around 8pm when they were approached by two boys.

The suspects demanded their belongings and threatened them with a golf club. One of the suspects also punched the victim in the face before leaving the scene with a small amount of cash.

The suspects are described as:

 * White, around 16 years old, 5ft 11" tall of slim build with blonde curly hair. He was wearing a grey shorts and white t-shirt which he was using to cover his face.
 * White, around 16 years old, 5ft 8" tall of slim build with dark brown hair. He was wearing a full black Adidas tracksuit and was using his hood to cover his face.

If you witnessed this incident or noticed anything suspicious in the area, please contact us quoting PR/45200098158 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
