The body of a man, believed to be that of 32-year-old Leon Ford, was found at an address in Guildford this morning (17 February).

Officers were called to the address in Stoughton around 8am.

Formal identification is yet to take place but the body is believed to be 32-year-old Leon Ford, who was reported missing in the early hours on Saturday (15 February). His next of kin are aware.

Enquiries remain ongoing to establish the circumstances of the death, although it is not believed to be suspicious or that there is any third party involvement at this time.

Thank you to everyone who shared our appeal.
