We're appealing to the public for help in finding a man who is wanted in relation to an on-going criminal investigation.

Dominic Nugent, who also uses the surname of Ellis, is 19-years-old, and of no fixed abode.

He is described as white, of medium build with short brown hair. Dominic Nugent is believed to have links to the areas of Sunbury-Upon-Thames and Redhill in Surrey, Patcham and St Michaels Way in East Sussex, along with areas in Suffolk, Manchester and Yorkshire.

If you have any information which may assist officers in locating Dominic Nugent/Ellis, please call 101 (or 999 in an emergency) quoting reference PR/45190031920 Alternatively, if you do not wish to leave your name, call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
