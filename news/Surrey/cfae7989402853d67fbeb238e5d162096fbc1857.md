The items were stolen from an address in Effingham between the hours of 5pm on Friday 20 March and 4pm on Sunday 22 March.

The stolen items include various Waterford Crystal ornaments and picture frames as well as two nine carat gold children's rings.

If you have seen the items identified in the images, or if you have any information which might help, please call us on 101 and quote ref PR/45200031882. Alternatively, if you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
