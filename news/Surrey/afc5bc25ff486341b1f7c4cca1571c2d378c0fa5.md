We are appealing for witnesses after thieves stole a moped from a flat on Middleton Road in Epsom.

The bike is believed to have been taken around 7pm on 6 June.

The suspect is described as a 17-year-old male with ginger/blonde hair wearing a grey jumper and black waistcoat.

The stolen moped is described as a Stomp 110 in red and white with the words BH London written on the side.

If you witnessed this incident or have seen this bike, please call 101 and quote ref PR/45200058455.
