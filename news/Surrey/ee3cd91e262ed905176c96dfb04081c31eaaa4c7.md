We are appealing for witnesses following a collision between a car and a pedestrian at Church Street in Epsom this afternoon.

Officers were called to an underground car park in Church Street, Epsom just before 2pm this afternoon (Saturday, 25 January) following reports of a collision between a car and a pedestrian. The victim has been taken to hospital with life-changing injuries. All involved were spoken to at the scene.

We are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed the collision, have any dashcam footage or any further information to contact us on 101 quoting reference number PR/ P20019845.
