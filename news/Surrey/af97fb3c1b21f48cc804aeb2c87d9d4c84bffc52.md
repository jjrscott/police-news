We are pleased to confirm a missing 82-year-old man from Woking has been found safe and well.

A concern was raised for his welfare when he was reported missing this morning (15 May).

In order to restore his privacy, we ask that you please do not share this appeal any further and remove any posts referring to the original appeal.

Thank to you all who assisted with the search.
