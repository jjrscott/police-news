A 48-year-old man from Farnborough charged with a number of sexual offences against children is due to appear in court next week.
​

Julius Edwards, of Queens Road, has been charged with nine counts of sexual activity with a child and three counts of attempted penetrative sexual activity with a child.
​

The offences are alleged to have taken place between April and May 2020.
​

Edwards is due to appear at Kingston Crown Court on 27 May 2020.

Anyone with any information which could assist our investigation is asked to contact us on 101, quoting reference number PR/45200048404.

For further support and advice, please go to our web site https://www.surrey.police.uk/advice/advice-and-information/caa/child-abuse/
