We are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Wormley, Godalming this evening (16 November).

Officers were called to Petworth Road at around 7pm following reports of a collision between a van and a motorbike.

The motorcyclist has been taken to hospital with life-threatening injuries.

The driver of the van has been arrested on suspicion of dangerous driving offences and remains in custody.

The road remains closed whilst emergency services are on scene.

We are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed the collision or have any dashcam footage to get in touch.

If you have any information which could help, including CCTV or dashcam footage, please contact us quoting incident reference number P20266279 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
