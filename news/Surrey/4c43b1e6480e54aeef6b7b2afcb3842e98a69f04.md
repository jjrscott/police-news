We are currently dealing with a serious collision involving an electric scooter which took place in Albert Drive, Sheerwater around 7pm this evening (5 August).

The scooter is believed to have been involved in a collision with a silver Toyota Corolla.

The driver of the scooter has been taken to hospital.

Road closures are in place at the junctions with Bateson Way and Forsyth Road while we deal with the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or anyone with any dashcam footage, is asked to contact us using the webchat on our website www.surrey.police.uk or by calling us on 101, quoting incident reference PR/P20182318.
