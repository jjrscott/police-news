Three men have been arrested following a robbery and assault causing ABH in Woking on Saturday night (21st December).

** **

The incident started in Sundridge Road, Woking around 10pm when two men were robbed at knifepoint.

A further incident occurred in Gloster Road, Woking a short while later. During the incident two men were hit by a small hatchback vehicle, leaving them with injuries. They have since been treated and discharged from hospital.

The investigation remains ongoing and we are appealing for anyone who witnessed the incident, or with any other information, to contact us straightaway on 101, quoting crime reference number PR/45190137051. Alternatively, if you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
