Surrey Police is appealing for witnesses following a report of indecent exposure in Guildford on Tuesday afternoon (28 July).

The incident took place at around 3.30pm when the suspect exposed himself at the victim in the Bedford Road car park on Laundry Road.

The man is described as white, around 30 years old, about 5'10" tall, of slim to medium build with short dark brown hair. He was wearing grey jogging bottoms, a black t-shirt with a Union Jack on the front and white trainers.
