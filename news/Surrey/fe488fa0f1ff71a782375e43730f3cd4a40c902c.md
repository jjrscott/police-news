We are appealing for the public's help in helping us find missing man Simon Stokes.

Simon, 47 of no fixed address was last seen on Sunday, 29 November outside the Salvation Army, Woodthorpe Road in Ashford.

He is described as around 5ft 11 with blue eyes and balding brown hair.

There is a possibility he could be sleeping rough or in a homeless shelter and he has connections in Ashford, Staines, Sunbury, Shepperton and Walton-on-Thames.

If you have any information which could help, please contact us quoting crime reference number PR/45200126575 via

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
