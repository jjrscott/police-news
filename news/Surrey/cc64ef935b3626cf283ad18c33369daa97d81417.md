Officers are still investigating following a fatal collision in Ockley earlier this month.

Police were called to the collision, involving a pick-up truck and a motorbike, in Ockley Road on Friday, 13 November around 10.30pm.

The motorcyclist involved in the collision, who was sadly declared dead at the scene, has been named as Jamie Griffiths, 33, of Warninglid in West Sussex.

The driver of the truck, a 25-year-old man from Dorking, was arrested on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving. However, he has now been released without charge and is assisting police as a witness.

If you witnessed the collision, or you have any other information or any dash cam footage, please contact us straightaway quoting incident reference PR/P20264076 via
