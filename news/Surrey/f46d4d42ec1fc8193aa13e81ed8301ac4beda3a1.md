Two wanted people from Stanwell and New Haw have now been found.

Nathan Smithers and Liza Mullan were located by officers in the area of Stoke Poges, South Buckinghamshire in the early hours of this morning following an extensive search.

We would like to thank everyone who provided information and shared our appeal.
