Officers were called to Petworth Road, Wormley, at around 7pm on 16 November, following a collision between a van and a motorbike.

The motorcyclist was taken to hospital with serious injuries, where he remains in a stable condition.

The driver of the van, Adam Harbatowski, 61, of no fixed address, was arrested and charged on suspicion of causing serious injury by dangerous driving.

He has been remanded in custody.
