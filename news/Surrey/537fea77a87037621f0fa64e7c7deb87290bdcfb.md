Officers are appealing for information following a fatal collision on the M3 at Lightwater this morning (9 December).

The multi-vehicle collision, involving six vehicles, including a motorcycle, took place on the northbound carriageway at Junction 3 around 6.17am.

The motorcyclist was sadly pronounced dead at the scene. His family are aware and are being supported by officers.

No one else was injured. ** _

_**The road was closed while emergency services were at the scene, but has now fully re-opened.

We would like to thank everyone for their patience and cooperation whilst officers dealt with the incident and the vehicles were recovered. The investigation is ongoing.
