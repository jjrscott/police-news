Officers are still investigating following a serious injury collision in Ashford last Sunday (7 March).

Officers were called to the collision, involving a Vauxhall Astra and a Mercedes A Class, at the Staines Road West and Ashford Road Junction in Staines around 12:30pm.

The driver of the Mercedes was taken to hospital by air ambulance after suffering from life threatening injuries. Her condition remains stable in hospital and the investigation remains ongoing.

We are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision, particularly anyone with dashcam or helmetcam footage, of either the black Mecedes A140 or the silver Vauxhall Astra between 12:10 and 12:25 on Staines Road West approaching the junction with Ashford Road.

If you witnessed the collision and may have footage, please upload it on the following link: footage appeal collision in Staines or contact us straightaway quoting incident reference PR/ 45210023480 via:
