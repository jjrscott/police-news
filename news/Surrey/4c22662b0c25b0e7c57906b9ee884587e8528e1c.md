A second man has been arrested following an assault in Epsom in December 2019.

The 20-year-old from Epsom was arrested on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm on Sunday (1 March), and has since been released under investigation.

The victim was left with a broken jaw and nose following the incident, which took place on the pavement in an alleyway at the side of Nando's in Waterloo Road between 1.10 and 1.20am on December 21.

A 19 year-old man from Epsom was arrested in January 2020 on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm with intent, and possession of a psychoactive substance with intent to supply after being found with 106 NOS canisters. He is currently on bail.
