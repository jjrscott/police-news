We are looking to identify the owners of these items as part of an ongoing burglary investigation.

The items were recovered after officers stopped a vehicle in Weybridge on Friday, 13 March for driving offences. The driver, a 26 year old man of Brinkley Road, Worchester Park was arrested on suspicion of burglary dwelling, failure to stop a vehicle when required and driving whilst under the influence of drugs. He has been released under investigation.

If you are the owner of these pieces of jewellery, or you have any other information which could assist us, please call us on 101, quoting reference PR/45200028870.
You can also give information, 100% anonymously, to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111; or through their anonymous online form: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/
If you own any expensive items, please consider registering them on immobilise.com, which is free of charge. You could also photograph and mark your items with ultra violet pens.
For more tips on keeping your belongings safe, visit our website: https://www.surrey.police.uk/cp/crime-prevention/residential-burglary/
