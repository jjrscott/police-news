We are appealing for further witnesses following a report that a woman in her 30s was subjected to a serious sexual assault at the park near Millmead Lock in Guildford.

The incident is alleged to have taken place between 11.30pm on Monday 21 September and 12.30am on Tuesday 22 September.

A 19-year-old man from Guildford has been arrested in connection with the incident and released on bail.

Investigating officer Detective Inspector Deborah Birch said: "We would still like to hear from anyone who was in the area on those dates and heard or saw anything suspicious - your information could be vital to our investigation.

"If you wish to report relevant information but prefer to remain anonymous, you can call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."

If you have any information which could help, please contact us quoting crime reference number PR/45200100727 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101
 * If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111
