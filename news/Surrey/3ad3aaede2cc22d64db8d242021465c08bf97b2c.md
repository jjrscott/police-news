We are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision involving a car and a cyclist on Sunday (20 December) in Knaphill.

Officers were called to the roundabout at the junction of Carthouse Lane and Kestrel Way at around 2pm following reports of a collision involving a bicycle and a silver Vauxhall Astra Estate.

The cyclist was taken to hospital with a head injury and remains there in a stable condition. All involved parties stopped at the scene and were spoken to by officers.

We are appealing for to anyone who may have witnessed the collision or have any further information or dashcam footage to get in touch. If you can help, please contact us quoting PR/P20293045 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
