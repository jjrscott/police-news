We are appealing for help to identify a victim following a road traffic collision this morning in Farleigh.

The incident took place at around 6.50am (July 22) when a jogger was in collision with a car on Old Farleigh Road, Farleigh in Tandridge.

At this point in time the identity of the victim is unknown. He has been described as a white male, in his 60's with short grey hair and around 6' tall. He was wearing black Adidas shorts, a grey marble effect top, white towelling socks and grey and brown trainers which are quite old and dirty.

If you think you may know who the victim is, witnessed the incident or have any dashcam footage, please call Surrey Police on 101 and quote reference number PR/P20169842.
