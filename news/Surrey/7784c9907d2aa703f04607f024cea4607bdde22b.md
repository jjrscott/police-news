We are appealing for the public's help in finding wanted man Harvey Drummond.

Drummond is wanted for breaching his bail conditions and is described as a white man, 20 years old, with brown, short hair. He has connections to Spelthorne.

If you have any information on his whereabouts or have any information please contact us quoting crime reference number PR/45200102419 via

• Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
• Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
• Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
