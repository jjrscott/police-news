We are currently at the scene of a serious collision involving three cars in Shepperton.

The road remains closed following the head-on collision, which took place in Gaston Bridge Road around 1.50pm.

Five people have been injured and four of them have been taken to hospital.

The cars involved include a Ford Focus, a Jaguar and a Peugeot.

The road remains closed at the junction with green Lane and Fordbridge Road.

We are appealing to anyone who witnessed the collision, or with any information or dashcam footage, to contact us straightaway on 101, quoting PR/P20169254
