We're appealing for witnesses after a 16-year-old boy was seriously injured during an altercation earlier today (29 July).

Officers were called to Well Path at around 5pm after reports of an incident between a group of teenagers.

The victim has been taken to hospital.

DC Ashton said: "I'd like to reassure the community that we are at the scene and anyone with concerns can speak to uniformed officers in the area. I would urge anyone with information or CCTV footage of the area at the time to please get in touch. Thank you for your patience whilst we deal with the incident."

If you saw anything unusual in the area or have any information which could help, including CCTV or dashcam footage, please contact us quoting incident number P20176229 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
