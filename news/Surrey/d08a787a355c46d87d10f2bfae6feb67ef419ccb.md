We are appealing for witnesses following a collision between a car and two motorbikes on Farnham Road, Guildford which has left one person with serious injuries.

The collision happened between 7.45 and 7.50am on October 23 and involved a VW Golf, a Yamaha MT motorbike and a Honda CBR motorbike.

We are appealing for any witnesses or anyone who may have dash cam footage covering that route.

If you have any information which could help, please contact us quoting incident number PR/45200112487 via

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
