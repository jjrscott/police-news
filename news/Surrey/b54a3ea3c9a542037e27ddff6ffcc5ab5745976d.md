Surrey Police attended an address in Bletchingley, Redhill, around 5pm on Tuesday 29 September, after receiving reports of a large fight taking place.

Two men were taken to hospital with non-life threatening injuries.

Two men, aged 35 and 50 years, and from Redhill, and a 32-year-old woman from Crawley, have been arrested in connection with the assault and are in police custody.

The investigation remains ongoing.
