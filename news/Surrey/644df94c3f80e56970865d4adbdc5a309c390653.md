We are appealing for witnesses after a man was chased down Fir Tree Road in Banstead, by another man holding what appeared to be two large kitchen knives.

The incident occurred at around 8.30am on Friday 25 September.

The victim was unharmed and the suspect was arrested the following day after his vehicle was stopped and searched by officers.

The 29-year-old man from Wandsworth was arrested on suspicion of:

 * Attempted grievous bodily harm
 * Affray
 * Stalking

The suspect has been released on bail and the investigation is ongoing.

As a member of the public, you may have seen or heard something that could help us with our enquiries. If so, please contact us quoting crime reference number PR/45200102124 via

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
