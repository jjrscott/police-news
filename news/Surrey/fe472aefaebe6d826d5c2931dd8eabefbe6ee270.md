We are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision on the A320 Egley Road in Woking yesterday evening (Friday, 29 November).

Police were called at around 8:40pm following reports of a collision between a silver Ford Mondeo and a black Vauxhall Corsa. The driver of the Ford Mondeo was taken to hospital with life-threatening injuries.

The road remained closed between Old Hill and Almond Avenue until around 3am whilst emergency services were on scene.

We are appealing to anyone who witnessed the collision or has any dash cam or CCTV footage of the collision of moments leading up to it to get in contact. If you have any information, please call us on 101, quoting incident number PR/P19277538. Alternatively, you can report online by going to https://www.surrey.police.uk/contact-us/report-online/
