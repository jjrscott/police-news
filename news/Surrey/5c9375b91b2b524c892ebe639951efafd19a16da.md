We are appealing for witnesses following a single vehicle collision on the A25 Reigate Road, Brockham today (May 29) which has resulted in a man suffering serious injuries.

The collision happened at around 7.25am when the motorcycle left the road near The Arkle Manor Public House.

The motorcyclist was flown by air ambulance to hospital with life threatening injuries.

We are appealing for any witnesses to the incident or anyone who saw the manner of the driving of the motorbike prior to the collision, and for anyone who may have dash cam footage to call 101 and quote PR/45200055307.
