We're appealing for witnesses following a single vehicle collision on Hurst Lane, Oxted shortly before 1PM this afternoon (11 August).

Officers attended the scene along with South East Coast Ambulance and Surrey Fire and Rescue.

An 87-year-old woman has been taken to hospital with serious but not life-threatening injuries.

If you have any information which could help, including dash cam or CCTV footage, please contact us quoting incident number PR/P20187245 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
