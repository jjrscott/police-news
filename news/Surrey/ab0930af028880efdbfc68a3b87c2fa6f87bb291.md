We are releasing CCTV image of a man we would like to speak to in connection to a hit and run on Farnborough Road, Farnham on Friday (6 November) where a man was left with significant, life-threatening injuries.

We are keen to identify the man pictured in the CCTV image. If you recognise him, or know of someone matching his clothing and description, or have information which could help, please contact us quoting incident reference number PR/P20258507 via:

 * Webchat on our website police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
