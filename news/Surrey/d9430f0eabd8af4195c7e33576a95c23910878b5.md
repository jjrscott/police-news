We are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision in Douglas Road, Stanwell, earlier today (Tuesday, 10 December).

Officers were called at around 11am following reports of a collision involving a silver Mercedes and a green Fiat 500. The driver of one of the vehicles was sadly pronounced dead at the scene. Enquiries remain ongoing to establish the circumstances of the collision.

We are appealing for anyone who may have witnesses the collision, have any dashcam footage or further information to contact us on 101, quoting reference number PR/19286251. Alternatively, you can report online by going to https://www.surrey.police.uk/contact-us/report-online/

You can also give information, 100% anonymously, to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111; or through their anonymous online form: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/
