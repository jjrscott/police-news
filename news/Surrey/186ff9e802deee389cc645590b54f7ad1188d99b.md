We are pleased to confirm a missing 44-year-old woman from Caterham has been found safe and well.

A concern was raised for her welfare when she was reported missing on 12 May.

In order to restore her privacy, we ask that you please do not share this appeal any further.

Thank to you all who assisted with the search.
