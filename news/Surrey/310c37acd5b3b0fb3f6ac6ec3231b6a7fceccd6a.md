We are appealing for witness following a collision in Capel, Dorking which has left one man with life threatening injuries.

The collision happened at 8.09am today (June 12) on the A24 Horsham Road and involved a white Toyota Corolla and a green lorry.

The driver of the Toyota has been taken to hospital with serious injuries.

We are appealing for witnesses to the collision and for anyone who may have any dashcam footage of the incident to get in touch. Please call 101 and quote PR/P20135356.
