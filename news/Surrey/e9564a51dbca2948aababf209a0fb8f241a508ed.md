The victim was sat with a friend at the skate park when they were approached by three men at around 6:30pm. The men approached from the direction of the Morrison's before stealing the victim's phone and walking away.

The suspects were wearing balaclavas but are described as three men wearing dark clothing.

We are carrying out ongoing enquiries and reviewing CCTV of the area.

If you were in the area at the time and have any information which could help, please contact us quoting incident/crime reference number PR/45200114498 via:

• Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
• Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
• Calling us on 101
