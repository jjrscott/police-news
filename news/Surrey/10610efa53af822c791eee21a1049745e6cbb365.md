We are appealing for the public's help in identifying the man in these CCTV images in connection with an ongoing indecent exposure investigation.

Officers were called to Memorial Park on London Road in Redhill on Thursday, 25 February following reports of an indecent exposure which is believed to have occurred around 7:30am that morning.

The female victim was walking in the park when the suspect engaged in conversation with her before exposing himself.

If you know who he is or have any further information which could assist us in this information please contact us quoting PR/ 45210019584 via:

• Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
• Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
• Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
