Surrey Police would like to thank members of the public after a car drove the wrong way down the M25 last night (Wednesday, 10 February).

The grey Vauxhall Insignia was spotted by members of the public driving erratically on the M25 at Leatherhead between junctions 8 and 9 at around 8.50pm.

The vehicle was eventually brought to a halt when the officers from the Roads Policing Unit have gone head to head with it, with the two stopping less than a metre apart.

The female driver, aged in her late 30's was arrested on suspicion of dangerous driving and driving while unfit through alcohol. She remains in Custody at this time.
