Two teenagers have been charged with robbery following an incident at Watchetts Recreation Ground on Wednesday (August 26).

Officers were called to the recreation ground at 5.15pm following reports of a robbery outside the public toilets.

A 17 year old from Camberley has been charged with robbery and possession of Cannabis and has been remanded in Custody.

A 17 year old from Camberley has been charged with robbery and released with post bail charge conditions.

A third teenager, a 17 year old from Bagshot has been bailed.

All are due to appear at South West Surrey Youth Court on Thursday 3 September.
