We are currently dealing with a serious collision involving four vehicles which took place on Titsey Road, Titsey at around 16:40 this afternoon (9 August).

The driver of one vehicle has sustained serious injuries and was taken to hospital via air ambulance.

Road closures are in place on Titsey Road at the junctions with Pilgrims Lane and Bluehouse Lane while we deal with the incident.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or anyone with any dashcam footage, is asked to contact us using the webchat on our website www.surrey.police.uk or by calling us on 101, quoting incident reference PR/P20185681.
