We are appealing for witnesses after a 20-year-old man was assaulted and robbed of his phone near Nutfield Road at around 6.30pm last night (2 April).

Three of the male suspects are described as white while the fourth suspect is described as being of dual heritage. The suspects are believed to have been wearing hooded tops at the time.

The group also had a silver mountain bike with them.

A 16-year-old and 17-year-old boy have been arrested for robbery in connection with this incident, but our investigation remains ongoing.

If you witnessed this incident or have any information which might help, please call 101 and quote ref PR/45200035236. You can also give information, 100% anonymously, to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111; or through their anonymous online form: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/
