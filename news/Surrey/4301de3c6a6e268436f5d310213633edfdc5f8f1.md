We're appealing for witnesses after two vehicles were involved in a collision with a lorry on the A3 southbound near the Ripley Bypass earlier this morning (12 August).

Officers were called to the scene at around 9.15am. Fortunately, those involved in the collision received minor injuries.

A 22-year-old man has been arrested for dangerous driving and theft of a motor vehicle and remains in custody.

If you saw the collision, witnessed a silver Mercedes driving dangerously in the area or have any CCTV or dashcam footage, please contact us quoting ref P20188022 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
