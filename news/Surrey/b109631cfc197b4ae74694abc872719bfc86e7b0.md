We can confirm that a man has sadly died following a collision on the M25 motorway between junctions 5 and 6 in Godstone late on Sunday night, 3 November.

The collision, involving a silver VW Transporter van, occurred at 23:15 on the clockwise carriageway.

The driver of the van, a 58 year old man, was pronounced dead at the scene. Officers are currently seeking the man's next of kin.

The road was closed in a clockwise direction for five hours for police investigation and was reopened at 05:00 this morning (Monday 4 November).

Police are appealing to any motorists who witnessed the collision, stopped at the scene to provide assistance, or who have any information which could assist our enquiries including dashcam footage, to contact us on 101, quoting incident reference PR/P19255891.
