A 23-year-old man from Guildford has pleaded guilty to manslaughter after appearing in court today (14 November), following a serious assault in Guildford in the early hours of 12 October.

Barney Thomas Christopher Coyle, 23 of Frog Grove Lane, Guildford, is due to be sentenced at Guildford Crown Court on 6 January.

Jamie Smith, 24, from Elmbridge, sadly died in hospital on October 17 following the assault.
