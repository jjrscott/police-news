Officers have charged a man in connection with the rape of a teenage girl in the toilets to the rear of the British Legion on Hollyhedge Road, Cobham on Thursday, 22 August.

Alex Cadwell, 20-years-old, from Greenacres Avenue in Uxbridge was arrested shortly after the incident was reported to police.

He will appear at Staines Magistrates Court this afternoon (18 October) after he was charged with nine offences including rape of a female under 16, possession of indecent images of children, meeting a child following grooming, attempting to incite a child to commit a sex act and attempting to have sexual communication with a child.
