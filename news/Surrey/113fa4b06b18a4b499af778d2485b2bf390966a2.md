We're appealing for witnesses after racist graffiti was found on the Liberty Lane footbridge over the M25 in Addlestone.

We believe that the graffiti was applied overnight between 13 August and 14 August. Surrey County Council has been contacted to remove the graffiti.

We are now looking for anyone who might have witnessed this incident to get in touch. If you have any information which could help, please contact us quoting incident/crime reference number PR/45200085599 via:

• Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk

• Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/

• Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.

Surrey Police takes a zero tolerance approach to hate crime which has no place in our society.
