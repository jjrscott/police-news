A 41 year-old man from Bletchingley has been charged in connection with the ongoing murder investigation in Godstone.

Darren John Robert McClean of Coneybury in Bletchingley was charged with murder after being arrested yesterday (30 April).

The victim, who has been identified as 88-year-old Dennis Kellond, was found dead at an address on Bletchingley Road just before 11am on Monday (27 April).

Officers have stepped up patrols in the area to provide reassurance to the local community.

_Please note an earlier version of this release gave a wrong address for the home address of Darren McLean._
