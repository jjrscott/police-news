We are appealing for witnesses after a farm in Farnham was broken into and thousands of pounds worth of tools and vehicles taken.

Police were called to the address on Wednesday 30 December. The burglary is believed to have taken place overnight between 10.00pm and 01.00am.

The items taken were:

 * One green Honda 420 quad bike
 * One green Kawasaki quad bike
 * One Kawasaki scramble bike
 * Two chainsaws, one strimmer and one toolbox.

We have attached some photos of some of the stolen items. Have you seen any of these vehicles or tools for sale recently? Were you driving through Farnham overnight, particularly in the Folly Hill area?

If you saw or heard anything, or have any CCTV or dashcam footage that might assist with our investigation, please contact us quoting PR/45200136989 via:

 * Webchat on our website police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
