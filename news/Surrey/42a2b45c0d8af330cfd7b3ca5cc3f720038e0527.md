We have been dealing with a serious collision involving a single vehicle and a bus-shelter, which took place on Church Road, Ashford at around 09:30 this morning (12 September).

The driver of a silver Ford Fiesta left the road and collided with a pedestrian and bus-stop. The pedestrian has serious injuries and left the scene via ambulance.

Road closures have been in place between Fordbridge Road and Parkland Grove while we deal with the incident. The closures are likely to be in place for a further hour.

Anyone who witnessed the incident, or anyone with any dashcam footage, is asked to contact us using the webchat on our website www.surrey.police.uk or by calling us on 101, quoting incident reference PR/P20213769.
