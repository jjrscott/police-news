We are appealing for the public's help in locating Joseph Setty, 31, whose last known address was in Camberley.

Setty is wanted in connection with the fraudulent use of a stolen bank card.

He is described as a white man with a stocky build, with brown hair and brown eyes. He also has a distinctive tattoo on his neck of a rose and angel wings.

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
