We have released CCTV images of a man we would like to speak to in connection to an investigation into a burglary at The Bridge Grill in Tadworth.

The break-in is believed to have occurred between midnight and 3.30am on Sunday 29 December.

A front window was smashed and two bottles of vodka were taken from the premises.

PC Clive Farnham, of the Reigate and Banstead Neighbourhood Patrol Team said: "We believe the man pictured may have information that could help us with this investigation, so if you know who he is or have any information about the theft, please report it to us.

Also, if you were in the area at the time and saw anything suspicious, or you have any CCTV or dashcam footage, please contact us."

If you can help, please contact us on 101, quoting incident reference PR/, or alternatively report it via our website: https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/

You can also give information, 100% anonymously, to the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111; or through their anonymous online form: https://crimestoppers-uk.org/
