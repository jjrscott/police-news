We are appealing for witnesses following a collision this morning (15 December) involving a motorbike and a police car on Lower Hampton Road in Sunbury-on-Thames.

Shortly before 2am, Officers in an unmarked police car were in pursuit of a motorbike following reports of suspicious behaviour. During the pursuit, the motorbike has then been involved in a collision with a marked police car on Lower Hampton Road.

The motorcyclist has been taken to hospital with serious injuries.

Officers remain at the scene and Lower Hampton Court Road and Harfield Road are partially closed.

If you have any information or dashcam footage which could help, please contact us quoting incident number P20288659 via:
