Two men are due to appear in court next month after being charged with conspiracy to commit burglary following an incident at a Farnham jewellers in October 2018.

Darren Avis, 36, of Bransgore Avenue, Havant, and Alec Bulloch, 46, of Millbrook Drive, Havant, are due to appear at Guildford Magistrates' Court on 17 March.

Officers were called to Lion and Lamb Way in Farnham shortly after 11.30pm on 18 October 2018 following a report of a ram raid at a jewellers. Jewellery worth £20,000 was stolen, with the damage caused believed to be £12,000.
