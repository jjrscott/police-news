Have you seen wanted man Luke Abraham Smith?

Smith, 28, is wanted on recall to prison.
He is described as white, approximately 5 ft 7 tall, of slender build, with short brown hair and brown eyes.
He was last seen in Guildford on 14 November.
He has links to Surrey Heath, specifically Camberley and the St Michaels and Watchetts areas, as well as Manor Park in Yateley.
If you have seen Smith or you have any information which could help us find him, please call us on 101 (999 in an emergency), quoting crime reference PR/45190122102. Alternatively, if you do not wish to leave your name, call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
