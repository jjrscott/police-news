Connor Higgs, 24, of Gatley Avenue, Epsom, has been remanded in custody after being charged with multiple offences following a collision on Sunday (16 August).

Higgs was arrested following the collision with a telephone box in Riverview Road.

He was subsequently charged with a number of offences, including:

 * Aggravated vehicle taking;
 * Driving whilst disqualified;
 * No insurance;
 * Possession of class B drugs;
 * Fail to provide

He was remanded after appearing at Guildford Crown Court on Monday (17 August).
