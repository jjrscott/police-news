We are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision between a car and cyclist at Hardwick Lane this morning (Sunday, 27 December).

Officers were called to a road Hardwick Lane, Chertsey this morning just after 11am (Sunday, 27 December) following reports of a collision involving a car and a cyclist. The victim has been taken to hospital with major head injuries.

An investigation is underway, including checking CCTV footage in the area. Officers are searching for a red Volkswagen polo believed to be involved in the collision which failed to stop at the scene.

We are appealing to anyone who may have witnessed the collision, have any dashcam footage or any further information to contact us on 101 quoting reference number PR/P20297628 via:

 * Webchat on our website surrey.police.uk
 * Online https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
 * Calling us on 101

If you do not wish to leave your name, please call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
