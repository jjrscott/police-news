A woman has been charged following a fatal collision in Runfold in 2019.

Officers were called to the A31 in Runfold at around 9:30pm on 16 October following a collision involving four vehicles resulting in the death of a moped rider.

20-year-old Conner Traves, from Camberley, sadly died as a result of multiple injuries.

 Victoria Howell, 23 of Park Road, Farnborough has been charged with causing death by careless driving.

She will appear at Guildford Magistrates Court on Friday, 26 February.
