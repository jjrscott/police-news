We are looking to speak to them in connection with a business burglary which occurred on Tuesday, 30 June in Whyteleafe.

Officers were called to the Tesco Express on Godstone Road following a burglary which had occurred around 8pm.

It is believed that one of the suspects distracted the cashier whilst cigarettes were stolen from behind the counter. We are looking to speak to the men in these CCTV images in connection with this investigation. If you know who they are, or have any further information which could help us in this investigation, please contact 101, quoting reference number PR/45200067693.

Alternatively, you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
