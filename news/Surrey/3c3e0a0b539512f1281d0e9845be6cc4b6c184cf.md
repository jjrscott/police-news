We are appealing for help to trace a man from Kent who is wanted in relation to an ongoing Surrey Police investigation.

James Case, 30, from Canterbury in Kent is wanted in relation to an ongoing investigation. He is also wanted on warrant by Isleworth Crown Court and Folkestone Magistrates Court for failing to appear when required.

He is described as 6 ft 2 tall, of medium build, with brown eyes and lightly tanned skin.
He has short, straight, dark brown hair and a dark brown beard. He also has a large tattoo of a name on his right forearm.

He has links to Walton-on-Thames in Surrey, as well as Margate, Canterbury and Sturry in Kent, and Bexley, Lewisham and Eltham in London.

If you have seen him or you have any information which could help us find him, please call us on 101 (999 in an emergency), quoting crime reference PR/45190127476.

Alternatively, if you do not wish to leave your name, call the independent charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
