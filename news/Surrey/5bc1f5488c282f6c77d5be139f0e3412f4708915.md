We are appealing for witnesses following a fatal collision in Hooley on Tuesday, 10 November at around 9.15pm.

The single vehicle collision, involved a black Toyota RAV 4 which was in collision with a bridge southbound on the A23 London Road North in Hooley. The driver, a man in his 40's was pronounced dead at the scene.

We are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision, particularly anyone with dashcam footage, or who may have any other information, to come forward as soon as possible.

If you have any information which could help, please contact us quoting incident number PR/45200119614
