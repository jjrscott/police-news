A woman who went missing has been found safe and well.

A woman who went missing in Egham around 6pm today (30 March), has been found safe and well.

Madeline Siredzuk, 66, was last seen in the Coopers Hill area.

We would like to thank everyone who assisted with the search and shared our appeal.
