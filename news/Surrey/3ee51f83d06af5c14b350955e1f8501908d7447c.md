A man officers were seeking to identify in connection with an assault at a pub in Byfleet has been identified.

The CCTV image was released earlier today (November 14) to identify the man who may be able to assist the investigation.

The alleged assault took place on Saturday 26 October at around 10pm in the Blue Anchor Pub.

The media and public are asked to please delete the original image.
