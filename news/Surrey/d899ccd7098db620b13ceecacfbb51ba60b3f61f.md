We are appealing for witnesses following a serious collision between two cars and a pedestrian earlier this evening (Tuesday, 3 December).

Officers were called to the A3 slip road near Guildford Cathedral at around 6pm following reports of a collision involving two cars and a pedestrian.

The pedestrian was taken to hospital with life-threatening injuries.

We are appealing for anyone who may have witnesses the collision or have any dashcam footage or further information to contact us on 101 quoting reference number PR/P19280703.

Alternatively, you can report online by going to https://www.surrey.police.uk/contact-us/report-online/
