We need your help to locate a man who failed to attend Guildford Magistrates Court on Friday 31 July.

Despite a number of attempts to contact him, twenty-nine year-old Jack Mayle of Croydon Road in Caterham hasn't responded to officers' requests to locate him after he failed to attend court.

Jack is described as:

 * White.
 * Slim to stocky build.
 * Around 5ft 10ins tall.
 * He has a number of tattoos including: a diamond tattoo on his face, a full sleeve tattoo on his left arm, both hands tattooed with "Money Never Sleeps" along with an eye design featuring hands around it and the word "Littles". He also has tattoos saying " TRAP STAR" on his fingers and neck tattoos with warriors and religious figures on horses.
 * He wears glasses.
 * He has curly brown hair, shaved at the sides and a brown beard.

Jack has connections to the Purley, Croydon, Coulsdon, and Caterham areas.

If you have seen him, or you have any information which could help us, please contact us urgently quoting PR/45200079024.

You can contact us by
• using the webchat on our website www.surrey.police.uk
• using the form at https://www.surrey.police.uk/ro/report/ocr/af/how-to-report-a-crime/
• calling us on 101
